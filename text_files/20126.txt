                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1300






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Development of Roads under PMGSY




Ramapati Ram Tripathi














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the roads identified for development in the districts of various states including Uttar Pradesh under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY);(b) 	the amount sanctioned, spent/unspent under PMGSY during the last three years, particularly in the districts of Uttar Pradesh; and(c) 	the manner in which construction were under PMGSY is likely to be accelerated?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	So far under various interventions/verticals(PMGSY-I, II, RCPLWEA & III) of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), the number of road works sanctioned across the country is 1,78,322, out of which 1,61,134 number of roads have been constructed. In the State of Uttar Pradesh, against the total of 19,462 sanctioned road works, 18,530 roads have been constructed. The road works sanctioned in the various districts of the States/UTs includes Uttar Pradesh may be seen at http://omms.nic.in/# > proposals> sanctioned projects. (b)	The funds for implementation of the scheme is released by the Ministry to the state as whole. Further release of funds to the Programme Implementation Units (PIUs) at the district level is done by the respective State Governments depending upon the absorption capacity of the particular district in the State. The districts wise details of expenditure incurred in Uttar Pradesh during the last three years is attached as Annexure.(c)	The funds for works sanctioned under PMGSY are released to the States/UTs timely and there is an unbroken chain of fund release. The progress of implementation of works sanctioned under PMGSY is regularly reviewed by way of Regional Review Meetings (RRMs), the Performance Review Committee (PRC) Meetings, Pre-Empowered /Empowered Committee Meetings with the States and Monthly Review Meetings through Video Conference. At District level, the District Development Coordination and Monitoring Committee (DISHA) headed by a Member of Parliament (LS) monitors the implementation of various schemes of Government of India including PMGSY.  In addition to this, special review meetings/Monthly Review Meetings are also held by Secretary/Additional Secretary/Joint Secretaries, Ministry of Rural Development with the Chief Secretaries/Principal Secretaries of the States. ****** AnnexureAnnexure referred to in part (b) of the answer of Lok Sabha unstarred Question No 1300 to be answered on 9.02.2021                                            (Expenditure in Lakh)
