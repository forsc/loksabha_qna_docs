                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2619






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Food Processing Units in Odisha




Ramesh Chandra Majhi














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of food processing units in Odisha as on date; (b)	whether the Government has any proposal to set up food processing units in      Nabarangpur and Malkangiri districts of Odisha; and  (c)	if so, the time by which these units are likely to be set up?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a):	As per Annual Survey of Industries 2016-17, 1127 food processing units are registered in State of Odisha.  Also, as per 73rd round Survey of NSSO, 2015-16, 77,781 Unincorporated Enterprises manufacturing Food and Beverages units are registered in State of Odisha.  (b) & (c): Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) promotes setting up of food processing units and does not set up food processing units on its own. The projects are selected through the proposals received against the Expression of Interest (EoI) invited by the Ministry from time to time for setting up of food processing units in the Country through eligible entrepreneurs/investors.  *****
