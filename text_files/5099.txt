                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4669






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana




Nayab Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of persons benefited throughout the country with the launch of Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana by the Government so far;(b) whether the benefits of the scheme have been availed in Haryana particularly Kurukshetra district so far and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government proposes to link more and more people with this scheme; and(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a) A total of 1,70,72,858 girl children have benefited in the country under  the scheme since the launch of the scheme upto 31.05.2019.(b) Yes, Sir.  The benefits of the scheme have been availed in Haryana including Kurukshetra District.  A total of 5,68,800 and 9600 accounts have been opened since the launch of the scheme upto 31.05.2019 in the State of Haryana  and Kurukshetra District respectively.(c)&(d) Yes, Sir.  The scheme is being offered to public through Post Offices and Banks throughout the country.
