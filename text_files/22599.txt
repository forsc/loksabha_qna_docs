                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3747






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Krishi Udan Yojana in Gujarat




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the main objectives of Krishi Udan Yojna;(b) the locations from where this service is likely to be started;(c) the names of the districts of Gujarat with which this service is likely to be connected; and(d) whether this service is likely to be started from tribal districts of Gujarat also?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (d): The main objectives of the Krishi Udaan Scheme of the Ministry of Civil Aviation are to improve value realization, especially in North-East and tribal districts, through enhanced competitiveness across the supply chain and improved air connectivity between origin-destination airports at affordable rates on international and national routes. The Scheme envisages resources-pooling through convergence between different schemes administered by various Central Ministries / Departments and those of State Governments for enhanced air transportation of agri-produce. ;        All agri-product clusters, including those of tribal areas, in the State of Gujarat are covered under the Scheme for air transportation and facilitation through various airports as may be feasible.
