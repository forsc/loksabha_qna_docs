                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2987






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Jewish Population




Locket Chatterjee














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is an official count of the population of the Jewish Community in our country and if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and (b) whether the Government has taken any step for the preservation of the Jewish Community in India and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	Office of the Registrar General, India, has intimated that the population of Jews / Judaism religion is published in Census under the head “Other Religion and Persuasions”.  The population of Jews / Judaism religious community at India / State / UT level, as per Census 2011 is available online at the link: http://www.censusindia.gov.in/2011census/population_enumeration.html.  State / UT wise population of Jews / Judaism religious community as per Census 2011 is annexed.(b)	In States like Maharashtra & Gujarat, where Jews have been notified as minorities in the State, the benefits of State Government schemes for minorities are also available to them for their preservation and development.******
