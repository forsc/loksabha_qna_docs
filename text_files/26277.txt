                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1919






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Anganwadi Centres




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether there is any mechanism to monitor functioning record keeping and documentation of anganwadi centres, if so, the details thereof;(b)	the details of anganwadi centres in the country so far, State/UT-wise including Haryana;(c)	the funds provided to States under this scheme during last three years and  current financial year, State/UT-wise including Haryana; and(d)	whether the Government has formulated any separate scheme to set up anganwadi centres in extremely backward districts of the country and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)  (a)	The Anganwadi Services Scheme has an in-built monitoring system since its inception to track the physical progress of the scheme in respect of various input process, output and impact indicators through regular collection of monthly/ quarterly reports maintained in a standardized Management Information System (MIS).  An ICT enabled platform named Poshan Tracker has been designed to capture realtime data on implementation and monitoring of Anganwadi Services across the country. In addition, in order to strengthen the performance of Anganwadi Services Scheme, Government has a 5-tier monitoring & review mechanism at National/State/District/Block and Anganwadi levels  for monitoring the status of Anganwadi centres. (b)	State/UT-wise details of sanctioned & operational Anganwadi Centres across the country including Haryana is at Annexure-I.(c)	State/UT-wise funds released to States/UTs including Haryana during last   three years and current financial year is at Annexure-II.
