                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2148






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Budget Allocation for PG Institute of Yoga and Naturopathy




Rajmohan Unnithan













                                                    Raju Bista
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) the details of the Post Graduate Institutes of Yoga and Naturopathy and Central Research Institutes of Yoga and Naturopathy to be setup in the country with the objective of research in this field as proposed by Ministry, State/UT-wise;(b) the details of the budgetary allocation in the financial year 2019-20 for the construction of these Institutes;(c) whether the Government has allocated any funds of the setting up of Yoga and Naturopathy Institutes in the rural areas, including Kasargod district of Kerala and the time by which the project is likely to be completed, if so, the details thereof State/UT-wise; and(d) the details of the funds released and total expenditure incurred on the construction of these Yoga and Naturopathy Institutes during the last five years, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a):	With the objective of promotion of research in the system of Yoga & Naturopathy, the Central Council for Research in Yoga & Naturopathy (CCRYN), New Delhi, an autonomous body of Ministry of AYUSH establishing Post Graduate Institutes of Yoga and Naturopathy and Central Research Institutes of Yoga and Naturopathy in the country. The following states have provided/ offered land for setting up of Central Research Institutes of Yoga and Naturopathy (CRIYN) to CCRYN:i.	Andhra Pradesh ii.	Kerala iii.	Odishaiv.	West Bengal v.	Rajasthan vi.	Jharkhand vii.	Chhattisgarh viii.	Karnataka ix.	Haryana(b):	Budgetary allocation in the financial year 2019-20 for construction of establishment of Post Graduate Institutes of Yoga and Naturopathy at Jhajjar, Haryana and Nagamangala, Karnataka is as under:               Project 		        Budget Allocated            Nagamangala                           Rs. 22.13 cr                 Jhajjar                                 Rs. 19.37 cr(c) & (d):	The Govt. has allocated funds for Post-Graduate Institute of Yoga and Naturopathy Education and Research of Jhajjar, Haryana & Nagamangala, Karnataka, and one Central Research Institute of Yoga and Naturopathy, Odisha in the rural area. No fund has been allocated for setting up of Yoga and Naturopathy Institute in Kasargod district of Kerala.  The details of the funds released are as under:-                                State                  Project cost               Amount Released    Jhajjar            :       Rs. 59.21 cr                   Rs.56.55 crNagamangala :       Rs. 59.67 cr                   Rs.56.35 crBhubaneswar :       Rs. 1.58 cr                     Rs. 0.09 cr
