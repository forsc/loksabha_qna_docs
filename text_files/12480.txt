                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1317






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya in Bihar




 Gopal Jee Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to set up a Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayain Darbhanga, Bihar;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the names of the areas of Bihar where the Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas arelikely to be set up during the next three years?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d): The Navodaya Vidyalaya Scheme envisages opening of one JawaharNavodaya Vidyalaya (JNV) in each district of the country. All the 38 districts of Biharincluding Darbhanga district have been covered under the Scheme.*****
