                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

238






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






PMFME Scheme




Bhola Singh













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                     Chinta Anuradha
                                                








                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy

                                                    Rajveer (Raju Bhaiya) Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has started scheme of Pradhan Mantri Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) to enhance the competitiveness of existing individual micro-enterprises of the food processing industry;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has recently disbursed Rs.25.25 crore in the first year of the PMFME scheme for meeting seed capital requirements of Self Help Group (SHG), if so, the details thereof and the funds sanctioned to the States, State-wise especially in Andhra Pradesh;(d)	whether the Government is promoting One District One Product (ODOP) and development of Common Incubation Centres (CICs) under the PMFME in the country;(e)	if so, the details thereof and the total number of CICs allotted to the States especially in Andhra Pradesh;(f)	whether the Government has developed an online portal for submission of CICs proposals and an online CICs map; and(g)	the other steps being taken by Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	Yes Sir(b)	As part of Aatmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan, Ministry of Food Processing Industries has launched a centrally sponsored "PM Formalisation of Micro food processing Enterprises (PMFME) Scheme" for providing financial, technical and business support for upgradation of existing micro food processing enterprises. The scheme was approved by the Union Cabinet on 20 May, 2020 and Guidelines for the scheme were issued on 19 June, 2020. It is to be implemented for a period of five years from 2020-21 to 2024-25 with an outlay of Rs.10,000 crore. Two lakh micro food processing units are to be directly assisted with credit linked subsidy.The Scheme adopts One District One Product (ODOP) approach to reap benefit of scale in terms of procurement of inputs, availing common services and marketing of products. One District One Product approach would provide framework for value chain development and alignment of support infrastructure.Support to food processing units, SHGs/ FPOs/ Cooperatives envisaged in the scheme is as under:(i)	Credit linked grant @ 35% of the project cost with maximum grant up to Rs.10 lakh to micro food processing units for capital expenditure;(ii)	Credit linked grant @ 35% of the project cost to SHGs/FPOs/Cooperatives for capital expenditure;(iii)	Seed capital @ Rs.40,000/- per member of SHGs engaged in food processing as a working capital;(iv)	Credit linked grant @ 35% of the project cost for common infrastructure;(v)	Support for marketing & branding up to 50% of the expenditure.(c)	In the FY 2020-21, amount of Rs. 376.67 Cr. had been released to States forPMFME Scheme, which included the amount for Seed Capital. For FY 2021-22, amount of Rs. 91.03 Cr. has been released to States. For the State of Andhra Pradesh in FY 2020-21, the amount of Rs. 34.98 Cr. has been released including amount for Seed Capital. In FY 2021-22, the amount of Rs. 14.15 Cr. has been released for Seed Capital. State-wise details are at Annexure-ITill date 49209 SHG members have been recommended amounting Rs. 139.18 Cr. to State Nodal Agencies(SNAs) by State Rural Livelihood Missions (SRLM) under National Rural Livelihood Mission (NRLM). In the State of Andhra Pradesh 7614 SHG Members have recommended amounting Rs. 29.78 Cr to SNA by SRLM. State-wise details are at Annexure- II.(d)	to (g) Yes Sir.    Under the One District One Product (ODOP) component of thePMFME Scheme, the Ministry of Food Processing Industries has approved ODOP for 707 districts for 35 States and UTs, including 137 unique products as per the recommendations received by States/UTs. The GIS ODOP digital map of India has been launched to provide details of ODOP products of all the States and UTs.54 Incubation Centers have been approved by MOFPI, details are at Annexure-III. One Incubation Center is approved in Andhra Pradesh in Chittoor District at IIT, Tirupati for processing of its ODOP Tomato along with Mango and Citrus. Online portal has been developed by the Indian Institute of Food Processing Technology for the Ministry of Food Processing Industries for submission of Incubation Center proposals. The proposals can be submitted    at: http://iifpt.edu.in/pmfme-cif-application/ and IC    map    may    be    found on: http://iifpt.edu.in/olapp/pmfme/web/incubation.php. For institutes willing to apply for Grant -in-Aid for establishment of IC under PMFME Scheme, assistance and guidance are available at http://iifpt.edu.in/olapp/pmfme/web/incubation.php.*****
