                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2923






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






KV in Maharashtra




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the list of the Central Government Schools in Maharashtra district-wise;(b) the list of number of students enrolled, drop-out rate and teacher-to-pupil ratio at primary, secondary and senior secondary levels for all the schools in the above list gender-wise; and(c) the number of functional classrooms in each school?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a)Presently, 59 Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) and 34 Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs) are functioning in the State of Maharashtra. District-wise details of KVs and JNVs are at Annexure-I.(b) & (c) The gender-wise details of the students enrolled, teacher–to-pupil ratio at primary, secondary & senior secondary and number of functional classrooms in each KV in the State of Maharashtra are at Annexure – II. The details of the gender-wise students enrolled, teacher–to-pupil ratio at secondary & senior secondary and number of functional classrooms in each JNV in the State of Maharashtra are at Annexure – III. No case of dropout has been reported in KVs and JNVs functioning in the State of Maharashtra. *****
