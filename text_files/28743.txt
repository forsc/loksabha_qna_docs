Digital Villages




Kuldeep Rai Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:(a)	the district-wise number and details of villages made digital in the Union Territory of Andaman & Nicobar Islands;(b)	the details of funds allocated during the current and the last three years to the Union Territory of Andaman & Nicobar Islands for making the said villages digital;(c)	the details of various facilities being provided in the said digital villages at present; and(d)	the details of other steps taken by the Government to connect more rural and remote villages digitally?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology [MeitY] had rolled out Digital Village project on pilot basis in 700 villages (with atleast one Gram Panchayat or Village per district per State/UT) in October, 2018 for delivery of various citizen centric services viz Education & Health, Skill & Financial Inclusion and solar lights in 700 villages across India. Tusnabad Gram Panchayat of Ferrargunj Block of South Andaman district of Andaman & Nicobar Islands is covered. (b)	A total outlay of Rs 98.32 Crore has been allocated for implementation of Digital Village Pilot project covering 700 villages. One Gram Panchayat or village from each district of the State/ UT are covered in the project. State-wise/UT-wise specific allocation is not made, an expense to the tune of Rs. 9.99 lakh is spent per village under the project.The list of the 700 villages is enclosed at Annexure –I.(c)	The facilities provided in these villages under the Digital Village pilot project including the G2C and B2C services are as under:-i.	Education Service - Courses offered (i) Basic Computer Course (BCC), (ii) Course on Computer Concept (CCC), (iii) Tally – Kaushal Praman Patraii.	Health Services – Tele-Health &  Tele-Veterinary consultation iii.	Financial Inclusion- Financial inclusion awareness programme  iv.	Solar Street lights (8 solar lights per village).v.	Skill Development in (a) Automotive Technician, (b) Handset repairing, (c) Field Technician- Home Appliances, and (d) Electrical Technician.(d)	The Government of India (Department of Telecommunication) has the following schemes for expansion of Telecom services in rural and remote areas of the country with support from USOF:1.	BharatNet project to create network to connect all the Gram Panchayats (approximately 2.6 lakh) with broadband in the country. 2.	Public Wi-Fi hotspots at the 25,000 rural telephone exchanges of BSNL 3.	Provision of mobile services in Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected areas under Phase-I4.	Provision of 4G mobile services in Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected areas under Phase-II5.	Provision of Mobile Services in Uncovered villages in Assam, Manipur, Mizoram, Nagaland, Tripura, Sikkim, and Arunachal Pradesh (National Highways only) of NER and seamless coverage along National Highway6.	Provision of 4G Mobile Services in Uncovered villages of Meghalaya and seamless coverage along National Highway7.	Provision of 4G Mobile Services in Uncovered Villages of Arunachal Pradesh and 2 Districts of Assam8.	Hiring of 10 Gbps International Bandwidth for Internet Connectivity to Agartala from BSCCL, Bangladesh via Cox Bazar9.	Provision of mobile connectivity in 354 uncovered villages of J&K, Ladakh, Himachal Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Rajasthan, Gujarat, Uttarakhand, Border area & other priority areas10.	Provision of 4G mobile connectivity in 502 uncovered villages of Aspirational Districts in four States (namely Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Madhya Pradesh & Rajasthan)11.	Provision of 4G Mobile Coverage in 85 uncovered Villages and seamless 4G Mobile coverage of NH223 in Andaman & Nicobar Islands12.	Laying of submarine optical fibre cable between Chennai and Andaman & Nicobar Islands13.
