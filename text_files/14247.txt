                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3020






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Ongoing Sports Schemes




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of ongoing and upcoming sportsschemes and projects in Hazaribagh and Ramgarh districts of Jharkhand; and(b) the details of number of female sports personsand disabled persons engaged with Khelo Indiaprogrammes since 2016 in Jharkhand, District-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI KIREN RIJIJU](a) Following sports promotional schemes are being implemented by Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports (MoYAS) through Sports Authority of India (SAI) in Jharkhand:(i) SAI Training Centre (STC)(ii) Army Boys Sports Company (ABSC)(iii) Extension Centre of STC/SAG(iv) National Sports Talent Contest (NSTC) [its Sub-Schemes-INDIGENOUS GAMES AND MARTIAL ARTS SCHOOLS (IGMA)]5 sports centres are functional (2 STCs, 1 ABSC, 1 Extension Centre and 1 IGMA) under these SAI Schemes in Jharkhand, including in Ramgarh District.The Khelo India Scheme is being implemented across the country, including in Jharkhand, with the aim of strengthening the entire sports ecosystem to promote the twin objectives of mass participation and promotion of excellence in sports.One proposal for laying of Synthetic Hockey Turf at Sports Authority of India, SAI Training Centre, Hazaribagh has been received. However, no proposal for any sports project has been received till date from Ramgarh District of Jharkhand.(b) Under the “Talent Search and Development” component of Khelo India Scheme, 27 athletes, including 17 females, have been selected in various sports disciplines from Jharkhand till date.The Khelo India Scheme for persons with Disability was started in 2017. Under this scheme, Special Olympic Bharat (SOB) conducted “District Games” in 150 districts with total participation of 38,022 athletes;“State Games” in 24 states with total participation of 7,038 athletes; and “National Games” with 1100 participants during 2017-18.Further, under “Sports for Women” vertical of Khelo India Scheme, leagues for U-17 Football and U-17 Basketball have been conducted. Leagues for U-21 Hockey are proposed to be commenced. District-wise details of female sportspersons and disabled persons engaged with Khelo India Scheme are not maintained.Participation of female athletes from Jharkhand in Khelo India Games are as follows:Khelo India Youth Games (KIYG), 2018 : 34Khelo India Youth Games (KIYG), 2019 : 92Khelo India Youth Games (KIYG), 2020 : 71Khelo India University Games (KIUG), 2020 : 38.*****
