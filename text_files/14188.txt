                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2956






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






High Speed Broadband Services for e-Class




Talari Rangaiah














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is planning to launch any new affordable communications scheme for the rural areas of the country and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government proposes to connect all the Government schools with high speed broadband systems for online e-classes; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	The following initiatives are envisaged to provide telecom services in rural areas of the country:(i)	Under Left Wing Extremism affected areas Phase-II project, Government has approved installation of 2217 mobile towers for providing mobile connectivity.(ii)	Providing mobile connectivity in 354 villages of uncovered border areas, Ladakh and Kargil Region and other priority areas.(iii)	A Comprehensive Telecom Development Plan for mobile connectivity in the North Eastern Region to provide mobile coverage in uncovered villages, along with National Highways and to strengthen transmission network.(iv)	Laying of submarine optical fibre cable between Chennai and Andaman & Nicobar Islands for providing connectivity to Andaman & Nicobar Islands.(v)	Mobile connectivity to cover uncovered villages and along National Highway (NH 223) in Andaman & Nicobar Islands.(b) & (c)	BharatNet project is being implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity to all the Gram Panchayats (approx. 2,50,000) in the country. As part of BharatNet project, the Last Mile connectivity, through Wi-Fi or any other suitable broadband access technology, is to be provided in all Gram Panchayats.Digital Communications Commission (DCC) on 20.12.2019 approved a strategy to provide high speed broadband connectivity to Government Institutions at Gram Panchayat level. Accordingly, 1 Wi-Fi Access Point in Gram Panchayats, and 5 Fiber to the Home (FTTH) connections to Government Institutions (including Government Schools) at Gram Panchayat level are being provided at the Gram Panchayats of BharatNet Phase-I in all the States, except where Wi-Fi Access Points are provided under other Universal Service Obligation Fund schemes.**********
