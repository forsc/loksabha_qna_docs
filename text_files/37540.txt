                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4590






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Development of Railways in Kozhikode and Malabar Region




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has noted that budgets have shown repeated neglect towards people of Kozhikode and Malabar region since 2014 and if so, the details regarding the measures taken for the development of railways in Kozhikode and Malabar region;(b) 	whether capital expenditure granted to Railways is applicable to railway owned companies also and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government has provided any grants for capital and revenue expenditure of K Rail Development Corporation Limited and if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	whether the Government plans to run any new trains through Kozhikode railway station as per the budget and if so, the details regarding the newly sanctioned trains?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
