                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2754






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Start-up Eco-system




Gangasandra Siddappa Basavaraj














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the country’s start-up eco-system has made it the world’s third largest hub for start-ups and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether a new report of NASSCOM titled: ‘Indian Tech Start-up Eco-system’ says that investments in start-ups were steady at $ 4 billion in January-September 2019 across 450 start-ups at a 5 per cent year-on-year growth and if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto; and(c) 	whether according to the NASSCOM report, many of the incubators and accelerators are active outside tier-1 cities, creating around 40,000 direct jobs during the last five years, with 2019 alone accounting for more than 60,000 jobs and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):	The report of NASSCOM is not available with DPIIT. However, a report titled “Indian Tech Start-up Ecosystem” is available on their website. As per this report, Indian start-up ecosystem continues to be the 3rd largest in the world.(b):	Yes Sir. The report says that investments in start-ups were steady with USD 4.4Bn in January to September 2019 across 450 start-ups at a 5%             year-on-year growth. The contents of the report have been noted.(c):	The report analyses the companies founded between 2009-19. Based on an analysis of all funded and 500 unfunded Startups, the number of direct jobs created are stated to be between 3,90,000 – 4,30,000. The report does not give State/UT wise details. ******
