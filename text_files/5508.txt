                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

448






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Unmanned Level Crossings




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of unmanned crossings in the country, zone and State-wise and in the newly proposed South Coastal Railway Zone; (b)	whether the Railways has decided to advance 2020 deadline to get rid of unmanned level crossings and if so, the details thereof;(c)	the funds/budget allocated for removing unmanned level crossings in the country during the last three years and the current year, year and zone-wise; 	(d)	the details of funds spent and utilized during the above said period, year and zone-wise; and(e)	whether the Government is going to do away with 19,500 manned level crossings and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (e): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.
