                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

149






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






General Budget




A. Ganeshamurthi













                                                    A.K.P. Chinraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has invited various industrial groups and trade/ business      organizations for consultations for preparing the general budget for the year 2020 as well      as requested to give their feedback, suggestions for inclusion in the general budget; (b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has extended the invitation to the farmers/ agriculturists to give      their suggestions and feedback for pre-budget consultations; and (d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (d) Yes, Sir.  The Ministry of Finance holds the Pre-Budget consultations with the various stakeholders including representatives of industrial groups, trade/business organizations and farmers/agriculturists and their suggestions/ideas are noted for preparing the Union Budget. The proposals and suggestions received from industrial groups, trade/business organizations and farmers/agriculturists covered a range of themes/issues which, inter alia, related to revival of growth and investment, promotion of exports, ease of doing business, promotion of agriculture, animal husbandry and agro processing sector and empowerment of farmers.*****
