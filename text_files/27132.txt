                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

242






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Electricity to Poor Households under Saubhagya




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle













                                                    Gautham Sigamani Pon
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has launched various schemes to provide electricity to all poor households in the country including Karnataka under Pradhan Mantri Sahaj Bijli Har Ghar Yojana - Saubhagya;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the achievements made along with the challenges faced by the Government under Saubhagya and also the details of other schemes launched by the Government to provide electrification in urban areas of the country including district-wise in Karnataka;(d) 	the number of households in rural and urban areas which have been provided with power facility in the country during each of the last three years and the current year, category-wise including Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes and others;(e) 	whether the Government has achieved the targets set for providing electrification in rural and far flung tribal and backward areas of the country including Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh and North-Eastern Region; and(f) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF POWER AND NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY (SHRI  R.K. SINGH)(a) to (f) :	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*********** STATEMENTSTATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (f) OF STARRED QUESTION    NO. 242 TO BE ANSWERED IN THE LOK SABHA ON 05.08.2021 REGARDING ELECTRICITY TO POOR HOUSEHOLDS UNDER SAUBHAGYA*********(a) to (c) : Government of India had launched Pradhan Mantri Sahaj Bijli Har Ghar Yojana-Saubhagya in October, 2017 with the objective to achieve universal household electrification by providing electricity connections to all  un-electrified households in rural areas and all poor households in urban areas in the country.Under the Saubhagya scheme, as on 31.03.2021 all the States reported 100% electrification of all the willing un-electrified households, identified before 31.03.2019.  A total 2.817 crore households have been electrified since the launch of Saubhagya, up to 31.03.2021. State-wise electrification of households including Karnataka since the launch of Saubhagya Yojana are at Annexure-I.The details of challenges faced under Saubhagya scheme are as under: i)   	Households scattered in inaccessible & remote areas. ii) 	Difficult & Hilly terrain, inclement weather, Riverine / Marshy / Snowbound.iii) 	Transportation of material by Head loading, Helicopters, Bamboo Bridges, Rafts, Boats, etc. iv)  	In Left Wing Extremism affected areas some hurdles were faced from the extremists. v)  	Forest clearance and Right of Way issues. Ministry of Power (MoP), Government of India launched the “Integrated Power Development Scheme" (IPDS) on 3rd December’14 for strengthening the power sub transmission and distribution networks in urban areas. The R-APDRP (Restructured Accelerated Power Development & Reforms Programme) scheme was carried forward and subsumed under IPDS. State-wise infrastructure created under IPDS is at  Annexure-II(a) and Annexure-II(b). DPRs under IPDS were prepared circle-wise and accordingly projects were sanctioned for each Circle. The details of projects implemented under IPDS in State of Karnataka, circle-wise and district-wise, are attached as Annexure-III.(d) to (f) : All the States have reported 100% household electrification as on 31.03.2021. A total of 2.817 crore households have been electrified since the launch of Saubhagya, upto 31.03.2021, including households of Scheduled Castes / Scheduled Tribes and other categories. State-wise & Year-wise details of household electrification since launch of Saubhagya Yojana are at Annexure-IV.The SAUBHAGYA scheme was launched with the objective of connecting all willing households with electricity.  Projects under SAUBHAGYA are sanctioned across all the States.**********
