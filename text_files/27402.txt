                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3083






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Medical College in Odisha




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether a medical college has been approved to be set up at Bhawanipatna, Kalahandi, Odisha; and(b) the time by which the work of the said college is likely to be started along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) & (b) The Ministry of Health & Family Welfare administers a Centrally Sponsored Scheme namely, ‘Establishment of new medical colleges attached with existing district/referral hospitals’. Under Phase-III of the above Scheme, one medical college at Bhawanipatna, Kalahandi district has been approved in Odisha. The medical college has been approved at a cost of Rs.325 crore to be shared between Central Government and State Government of Odisha in the ratio of 60:40. Out of Central Share of Rs.195 crore, Rs.81.62 crore has already been released to the State Government. As per the scheme guidelines, the State Government is the implementing agency and the planning, execution and commissioning of the project is to be done by the State Government.
