                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2147






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Honorarium to Midwives




Vijay Baghel













                                                    Arun Sao
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Soni
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of midwives (Daayee) identified throughout the country under the scheme promoting revival of local health practices, midwife’s work etc, along with the state/UT-wise details thereof including Chhattisgarh;(b)	whether the Government has fixed the honorarium to be given to midwives under the said scheme; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (c): The Government of India provides support to states for provision of institutional deliveries and deliveries attended by Skilled Birth Attendants (SBAs). As per the NFHS-5, 89.4% of deliveries in the country are attended by Skilled Birth Attendants, including 88.6% institutional deliveries. Government of India does not have any scheme for engagement of midwives (Dayee).
