                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1931






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






AIIMS, Mangalagiri




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the current status of development/completion of AIIMS Mangalagiri, Andhra Pradesh;(b) whether the timeline for completion of AIIMS Mangalagiri was June 2021;(c) if so, the status of works completed under Phase-I and Phase-II, including OPD block, residential complex, hospital, academic campus, hostel buildings, etc.;(d) whether there is a time and cost overrun of the project, if so, the details thereof; and(e) the details of total cost approved, funds sanctioned, released, utilised along with the revised timeline decided for completion of all works for AIIMS Mangalagiri?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(Dr. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (e) The Status of progress in construction work of AIIMS, Mangalagiri is as follows:•	Phase-I: OPD Block & Residential Complex including hostels - 99%•	Phase-II: Hospital and Academic Campus - 71%       There is a slight time over-run in completion of the project and revised time line for completion is September 2021. However, in the meanwhile, MBBS course of the Institute   has started from the academic year 2018-19 and OPD services   in March 2019. Facilities for testing and treatment of Covid-19 have also started.         Sanctioned cost of the project is Rs. 1618 crore, out of which Rs. 922.01 crore have been released so far. Fund utilized till date is Rs. 880.15 crore. There is no cost over-run for the project.
