                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2977






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Monitoring on NHs




Krishna Pal Singh Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to arrange for monitoring/patrolling on all the National Highways (NHs) across the country; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the funds allocated and utilised for the purpose during each of the last three years and the current year, year-wise; and (d) the details of funds sanctioned for the financial year 2021-22, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	 & (b) Provision for Incident Management Services (IMS) (Rescue Ambulance, Patrol vehicle and Tow Away Crane) on National Highways (NHs) stretches entrusted to States / Union Territories (UTs) has been made in all new development projects, Periodical Renewal (PR) / Improvement of Riding Quality Program (IRQP) works as well as Medium term contract maintenance of 3 years or more. For stretches entrusted to NHAI, the responsibility of Incident Management rests with the individual concessionaire /contractor operating the highway stretch, and as a part of incident management in the contract/concession agreements, there is already a provision of patrolling vehicles which patrol the concerned stretch of National Highway under the jurisdiction of NHAI at regular intervals. (c) & (d) No separate allocation is done for monitoring/patrolling of NH sections as this is done as part of the contract of the respective project.*****
