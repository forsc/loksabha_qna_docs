                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4752






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Development of Bagdogra Airport




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the hassles faced by passengers passing through Bagdogra airport due to the lack of bigger airport terminal and if so, the details thereof;(b) the details of the works undertaken for the development of Bagdogra Airport and funds allocated during the last three years;(c) the details of funds allocated and spent under the UDAN scheme, state-wise; and(d) the number of airports and airstrips which are operational under UDAN scheme in the North Eastern Region (NER)?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a): The existing building at Bagdogra Airport is designed for capacity of 0.75 Million Passengers Per Annum(MPPA). However, the actual footfall from 01.04.2021 to 28.02.2022 is 1.75 million. Land has been handed over to Airports Authority of India(AAI)  by Government of West Bengal on 16th March 2022, to construct a new Terminal Building to ease out the congestion.;(b): Allocation of Rs. 11.26 crore has been made for the development of Bagdogra Airport in the last 3 years. The details of work completed/undertaken for the development of Bagdogra airport are as under:i) Expansion work of existing Terminal Building having an area of 1710 sqm (i.e. 810+891 Sqm) in Phase I & II.ii) Work of expansion of existing Terminal Building area of 546 Sqm.;(c): The details of fund allocated and spent under the Regional Connectivity Scheme(RCS)-UDAN(Ude Desh Ka Aam Nagrik) Scheme, state-wise is attached as Annexure.;(d):  Nine(9) airports involved in the awarded routes under UDAN have been operationalized in the States of North Eastern Region by the Selected Airline Operators (SAOs) as on 24.03.2022.****** AnnexureStatement referred to in reply to Lok Sabha USQ No.4752 for 31.03.2022 regarding “Developemnt of Bagdogra Airport”State-wise estimated cost and expenditure of RCS Airports / Water Aerodromes and Heliports as on 10.03.2022  Rs in CroreS.NO	State	Estimated Cost as per VIIIth PEC.
