                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2463






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Setting up of NIPER




Hasmukhbhai Somabhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has approved any proposal for any setting up of National Institute of Pharmaceutical Education and Research (NIPER) in Gujarat State; (b) if so, the time by which it is likely to be opened along with the outlay for setting up of the NIPER in Gujarat State; (c) the details of NIPER established in the country at present, State-wise; and (d) the details of the total cost involved in this regard, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS (SHRI D.V. SADANANADA GOWDA)(a) & (b) The Government has already set up a National Institute of Pharmaceutical Education and Research (NIPER) at Ahmedabad, Gujarat in the year 2007. The institute is currently functioning from a transient, temporary building on a 60-acre site at Gandhinagar, Gujarat since August 2016. It offers masters and doctorate courses in eight different specializations of pharmaceuticals and medical devices. More than 600 students have already graduated from the Institute and are well placed in various pharma industries in India and abroad. (c)      At present, seven NIPERs have been established at NIPER SAS Nagar, Mohali (Punjab), NIPER Ahmedabad (Gujarat), NIPER Guwahati (Assam), NIPER Hajipur (Bihar), NIPER Hyderabad (Telangana), NIPER Kolkata (West Bengal) and NIPER Raebareli (Uttar Pradesh). (d)       The government provides funds to NIPERs for construction of their campuses, purchase of equipments & machinery, salary of faculty & staff, fellowship to students and other administrative and academic expenses.
