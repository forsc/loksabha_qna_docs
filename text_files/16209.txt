                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4701






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Special Assistance to Bihar




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has released any funds to Bihar under the Special Assistance; (b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the eligibility of Bihar and the funds released, year-wise; and (c)	 if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) : Yes Sir. Rs.7393.23 crore has been released to the State of Bihar from 2015-16 to 2018-19 as “Special Assistance” under Demand No. 32/37/40/38 ‘Transfers to States – Special Assistance’ by Department of Expenditure, Ministry of Finance. (b) & (c) : The funds amounting to Rs.22.83 crore were released to the State of Bihar as one time assistance towards support for developing water sources for arsenic and fluoride affected areas in the State and Rs.7370.40 crore as Special Assistance for completion of projects under unspent amount of 2013 Special Plan for Bihar. (BRGF- State Component) now part of Prime Minister’s Package for Bihar, 2015. The year-wise details are as follows:  (Rs. in crore)Particulars 	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	TotalOne time assistance towards support for developing water sources for arsenic and fluoride affected areas	22.83	-	-	-	22.83Special Assistance for completion of projects under unspent amount of 2013 Special Plan for Bihar.   ( BRGF- State Component) now  part of Prime Minister’s Package for Bihar, 2015	1887.53	1329.40	3414.00	739.47*	7370.40Total	1910.361329.40	3414.00	739.47	7393.23* Post adjustment of Rs. 0.10 crore released against the project “Renovation and Modernization of Barauni and Muzzafarpur Thermal Power Station” and Rs. 0.5 crore released against the project “Integrated Community Based Forest Management” from the pending liabilities of Rs.7370.55 crore as on 31.03.2015. *******
