                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

90






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Profits of Public Sector Oil Companies




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the profits of public sector oil companies have greatly increased over the years, if so, the details thereof during the last three years, year-wise; and(b) the details of money collected by the Government through selling the Shares of public sector oil companies in the last five years?
                                                





ANSWER
