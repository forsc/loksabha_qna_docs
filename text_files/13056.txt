                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1881






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






NPDD Scheme




Kotagiri Sridhar













                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes sanctioning grants for establishing enhanced milk chilling facility, milk processing & marketing, bench mark survey, planning and monitoring cost under the National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD) Scheme; (b)	the number of project proposals received and sanctioned so far; and (c)	the amount of funds released under NPDD to Andhra Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (DR.SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	Yes Sir. Department of Animal Husbandry & Dairying (DAHD) is implementing National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD) scheme with the objective to create/strengthen infrastructure for Production of quality milk, Procurement, Processing and Marketing of Milk & Milk Products through the State Implementing Agency.(b)	DAHD has received 164 proposals from various State Governments under NPDD from March 2014 to till date. As on 25.02.2020, 129 new projects have been approved in 28 States and 2 UTs with total outlay of Rs.1535.17 crore (Central Assistance Rs.1249.36 crore) during 2014-15 to 2019-20. (c)	An amount of Rs. 23.57 crore has been released to Andhra Pradesh Dairy Development Cooperative Federation under NPDD scheme during 2014-15 to 2019-20.****
