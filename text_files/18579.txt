                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 46TO BE ANSWERED ON 02.02.2021FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE TO NGOS 46. SHRI MAHESH SAHOO: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the amount of Grants in Aid given by the Government to Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs) working for Scheduled Castes during the last five financial years, year-wise; (b) the details of such NGOs from Odisha given aid during the said period, particularly in the districts of Angul and Dhenkanal, year-wise; (c) whether periodic audit of their work has been done on a regular basis and if so, the details thereof; and (d) the details of NGOs found to have under-performed?
