                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2045






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Cost of cultivation




Ram Kripal Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether a second Inter-Ministerial Committee (IMC) was constituted for comprehensive cost of cultivation scheme under the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare by the Government of India; (b) 	the reasons for non-implementation of the recommendations submitted by the Committee in February 2019 even after approval from the Union Cabinet; and (c) 	the time by which it is likely to be implemented by the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	The Second Inter-Ministerial Committee (IMC) was constituted for comprehensive cost of cultivation scheme under the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare by the Government of India vide order dated 27.3.2014.(b) & (c):	The implementation of the recommendations of the 2nd Inter Ministerial Committee has many financial and administrative aspects that are under consideration.*****
