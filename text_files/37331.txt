                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4494






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






PM Gati Shakti Project




Sanjay Seth














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of schemes being implemented under Pradhan Mantri Gati Shakti project as on date along with the aims and objectives of this project;(b) 	the details of the benefits provided to Jharkhand State under Pradhan Mantri Gati Shakti Project; and(c) 	the details of the schemes being implemented in Naxal affected areas by the Government, district/scheme-wise? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a):    	The PM Gati Shakti is a new approach and mechanism adopted for integrated planning, synchronized implementation, prioritization and optimization of cost and time of infrastructure projects.  Under this approach a GIS based technology platform called National Master is developed and made available, to facilitate informed and technology based decision making for integrated planning and implementation of the different projects proposed or implemented by respective Ministries/Departments of Central and State Governments.   (b) & (c): 	Adoption of PM-GatiShakti approach and the National Master Plan has the potential to benefit  all the  States  including in the Jharkhand state or the Naxal affected areas, through better planning , decision making and implementation of various projects of the the Ministries/Departments of Central and the State Governments.*****
