                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2610






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Railway Hospitals




Vijay Kumar













                                                    S. Gnanathiraviam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the names of places where railway hospitals are located in the country,  State/Zone/Division-wise; (b)	whether the Railways has any proposal to set up Medical Oxygen Plants in all its railway hospitals to meet the shortage of medical oxygen with the outbreak of Covid-19 pandemic in the country;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the time by which the new oxygen plants are likely to be set up?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a)	 to (d) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. *****
