                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

907






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Biometric Access at Airport




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning on biometric based digital processing of passengers at airports;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether this initiative seeks to promote paperless and hassle free air travel across the country;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) whether this facility is linked to Aadhaar and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (e) : Digi Yatra is an initiative of the Ministry of Civil Aviation for providing seamless and hassle-free passenger processing at Indian airports, without the need for verification of ticket and ID at multiple touch points. Passengers can chose to enroll themselves in this program for a seamless travel experience. Identification & validation of his/her ticket is to be done by a biometric system. The system discourages the use of paper , however, option of manual procedure is also envisaged in this programme. This facility does not insist upon use of Adhaar , however, a choice to use Adhaar card as one of the identity proof is available.
