                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

470






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Social Schemes




Pushpendra Singh Chandel














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the social schemes being implemented at present in respect of social issues;(b) whether the Government is considering to review the said schemes; (c) whether the said schemes are lagging behind their schedule; and(d) the steps being taken by the Government to make the said social schemes result oriented?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING (RAO INDERJIT SINGH)(a) to (d): Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation does not implement any social scheme at present. However, there are a number of active social schemes run by Government of India under various Ministries with defined benefits to the beneficiaries. These schemes are continuously monitored and reviewed from time to time by the concerned Ministries/Departments. Most of the schemes contain component of result oriented evaluation framework.                                                ****
