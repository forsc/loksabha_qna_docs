                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

513






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Fund for CSR Project




Sisir Kumar Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government PSU’s have failed to spend 100 percent fund for CSR projects towards local development since last 5 years; (b) if so, allocation of funds and utilization therein since the year 2016-17; (c) whether Government has any specific budget to develop their properties schools, hospitals, parks, etc.through PPP model towards infrastructural development within their local areas thereof; and (d) if so, area-wise details of such PPP models running and allocation of budget and expenditure incurred since 2016-17 by the subsidiaries thereof?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF  PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a):There are certain CSR projects for which 100 percent project expenditure is not envisaged in a single financial year and the unspent amount is carried forwarded to next year(s) for spending.Coal India Limited (CIL) and its subsidiaries have collectively spent more amount than the statutorily required amount towards their CSR activities during each of the last five financial years. NLC India Limited (NLCIL) and its subsidiaries have spent 100% fund for CSR Projects towards local development since last 5 years as per norms except for NTPL in the year 2019-20 wherein the amount of Rs. 1.04 Cr. has been carried forward for 2020-21.(b):The Company wise details of CSR fund and expenditure during the last five years is furnished in Annexure A(c): No Sir.(d): Does not arise in view of answer to part (c) above.*****Annexure referred to part(b) of reply to Lok Sabha Unstarred Question 513 for answer on 16.09.2020Annexure ACSR fund and expenditure by CIL and subsidiaries during last five years                                   Figures in Rs.
