                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1597






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Coal Production from Underground Mines




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is a gap between targets fixed and achievements made by Coal India Limited (CIL) and its subsidiaries in producing raw coal from the underground mines during each of the last three years and the current year and if so, the details thereof, mine-wise;(b)	the contribution of underground mines of CIL and its subsidiaries in coal production of the country during the said period, mine-wise and steps taken by the Government to bridge the gap of targets and achievements of coal production;(c)	the number of workers of CIL who died/got injured in the process of extracting of deep seated coal deposits in the country, mine-wise; and (d)	the details of all mines/projects wherein CIL is a part of or is working on in Assam, mine-wise and the expected production capacity and expenditures involved therein? 






ANSWER




MINISTER OF  PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a)&(b): Mine-wise raw coal production target and contribution made by subsidiaries of Coal India Limited (CIL) from the underground mines during each of the last three years and the current year is at Annexure-I. The contribution of underground mines of CIL and its subsidiaries in coal production of the country during last three year and current year is as under:-Year	Total all India coal production (in MT)    	Total production of UG Mines of CIL (in MT)	% share of UG mine of CIL2016-17	657.87	31.48	4.792017-18	675.40	30.54	4.522018-19	730.35	30.48	4.172019-20 Upto Oct. 2019	348.99	16.83	4.82Mine-wise contribution to total coal production of the country is so minscule that it is difficult to quantify.CIL has made following efforts to achieve the targeted production:•	CIL has initiated mechanization of the UG mines to replace semi-mechanized mines to mechanized mines in a phased manner by introducing Mass Production Technology wherever feasible. •	At present 2 Mines are being worked with powered support Long Wall technology (PSLW) and 10 mines are being worked with 16 nos of Continuous Miner technology (CM). •	Further, a study has been made by independent consultants (ISM-SCCL-PWC Consortium) wherein recommendations were made. Subsequent to the recommendations, works for approval of PRs have started. •	CIL has planned to introduce 26 nos of CM in 19 mines, 2 PSLW in 2 mines and 2 mines have been identified for deployment of High Wall (HW) Miners. •	In the coming 5 years, i.e within 2023-24. Activities for implementation of planned CM, PSLW and HW projects are in progress at different stages.(c): Company-wise list of fatalities, serious injuries in UG mine of CIL from 2016 to 2018 and current year 2019 (uptoOctober) are as under:Company	Fatalities in UG	Serious Injuries in UG	2016	2017	2018	2019	2016	2017	2018	2019ECL	6	5	2	4	38	18	19	11BCCL	1	1	0	1	1	10	4	3CCL	0	2	2	1	2	2	4	2WCL	2	2	4	0	9	8	10	5SECL	7	6	7	3	22	23	16	7MCL	1	0	1	0	2	0	0	0CIL	17	16	16	9	74615328(d):The details of Mines/Projects and mine wise expected production capacity and expenditure involved of CIL in Assam is as under:-SL.No.	Name of the Project	Expected Production Capacity 	Expenditure Involved (Sanctioned Capital)1	Lekhapani OCP	0.25 MTY	Rs. 109.44 Cr2	Tikak Extension OCP	0.2 MTY	Rs. 81.91 Cr3	Tirap OCP	0.6 MTY	Rs. 49.71 Cr ****
