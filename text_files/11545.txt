                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ATOMIC ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

676






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Safety of Nuclear Power Plants




Kapil Moreshwar Patil














                        Will the Minister of





ATOMIC ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the International safety ranking of nuclear power plants of India;(b)	whether a Non-Government group ''The  Nuclear Threat Initiative'' based in Washington has given 23rd ranking to India among 25 countries in terms of nuclear safety systems;(c)	if so, the reaction of the Government thereto;(d)	whether the Government has constituted any team for the study of the safety of nuclear power plants and nuclear material; and(e)	if so, the details thereof along with the steps taken by the Government  for the safety of nuclear material?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES & PENSIONS AND PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR.JITENDRA SINGH):    (a)	There is no established international system of ranking of safety of nuclear power plants.(b)to(e)Government of India ensures safety of Nuclear Power Plants according to International Standards.*****
