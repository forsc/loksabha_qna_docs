                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4598






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Allocation of Land to SSB




Vivek Narayan Shejwalkar














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Special Area Development Authority (SADA) had allotted and handed over 37 acres of land to Sashastra Seema Bal (SSB) on 24.10.2014 for construction of training centre and quarters in Gwalior and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the construction work has not been started by SSB on the said allocated land even after completion of more than six years; and(c) if so, the details thereof and the time by which construction work is likely to begin?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (c):	Special Area Development Authority (SADA) had allotted 37 acres land to SSB on 24.10.2014 for establishment of Reserve battalion headquarters of SSB in Gwalior.  One Semi Permanent Type Barrack for Guard personnel, Toilet and Barbed Wire Fencing has already been constructed on the land by SSB.********
