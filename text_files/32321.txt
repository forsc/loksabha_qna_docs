                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3753






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






NABCONS and NIRD under DDU-GKY




Maddila  Gurumoorthy














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether NABARD Consultancy Services (NABCONS) and National Institute of Rural Development (NIRD) has hired the staff as per MoU/Mandate;(b) 	if so, the number of staff permitted for full time payroll as per mandate and MoU;(c) 	the details of Mandates/MoUs and Staff details along with PF and ESI details working for Deendayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) programme;(d) 	whether the Union Government has released the total sanctioned funds for NABCONS and NIRD; and(e) 	if so, the details of the funds allocated to the respective States?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) to (c) NABARD Consultancy Services (NABCONS) and National Institute of Rural Development & Panchayati Raj (NIRD&PR) have been engaged by Ministry of Rural Development as Central Technical Support Agency (CTSA) for Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY). No mandate has been provided in DDU-GKY guidelines nor any specific MoU executed for hiring of staff by NABCONS and NIRD&PR for the purpose. The details of staff hired by NABCONS for DDU-GKY is given at Annexure-I.The details of staff deployed by NIRD&PR for DDU-GKY is given at Annexure-II. (d) & (e) The DDU-GKY guidelines provide for payment of 1.5% of project cost as fee to CTSA. The funds released to NABCONS and NIRD&PR corresponding to respective States is given at Annexure-III.*******
