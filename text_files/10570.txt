                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3975






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Desalination Plant in Tuticorin




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has started the process of constructing the desalination plant in Tuticorin Port with an installed capacity of 5 MLD, as announced earlier;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the current status of the project and the steps taken by the Government to build the desalination plant sooner?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA) (a) to (c) As informed by V. O. Chidambaranar Port Trust, Ministry of Shipping, M/s Tamilnadu Water Investment Company(TWIC) has submitted the feasibility report with estimated cost of the project Rs. 143.62 Cr, out of which, Rs. 88.11 Crore is installation cost and Rs. 55.51 Crore for operation & maintenance for a period of 7 years. The feasibility report submitted by M/s TWIC has already been approved by the Board of Trusstees of V. O. Chidambaranar Port.
