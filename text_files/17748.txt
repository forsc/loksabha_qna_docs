                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1467






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Data regarding Financial Condition of Farmers




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a):	whether the latest Survey conducted to collect the data regarding financial condition of farmers are as per the NSSO survey in the year 2013 across the country; (b):	whether the Government proposes and recommend to collect the fresh data on this from farmers to supplement the efforts of State Governments as agriculture is a State subject towards the fulfilment of a strategy for doubling of farmers income and to arrive with this strategy; and (c):	the details of the report from the body constituted to monitor and review the progress as per the recommendations submitted by the Doubling Farmers Income (DFI) Committee?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	The  National Statistical Office (NSO) conducted the Situation Assessment Survey (SAS) of Agricultural Households during NSS 70th  round (January 2013- December 2013) in the rural areas of the country with reference period as the agricultural year July 2012- June 2013 to collect information on various aspects relating to farming and other socio-economic characteristics of agricultural households.Subsequently, the NSO conducted a Situation Assessment Survey (SAS) of Agricultural Households during NSS 77th round (January 2019- December 2019) in the rural areas of the country with reference period as the agricultural year July 2018- June 2019.  (c):	With acceptance of the Doubling Farmers Income (DFI) Committee’s recommendations, the Government has constituted an ‘Empowered Body’ to review and monitor the progress on implementation of recommendations. The said body has held several meetings with the implementing agencies/departments.******
