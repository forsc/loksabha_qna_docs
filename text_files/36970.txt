                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4048






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Green India Mission




Durga Das Uikey













                                                    Uday Pratap Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the measures/works/projects undertaken by the Government under various development works for the protection of environment and forest in the country especially in Madhya Pradesh under the Forest Conservation Act 1980; (b)	the details of funds allocated and utilized for the said projects in Madhya Pradesh during the last three years, year-wise(c)	whether the State Government has sent proposals seeking additional central funds to undertake work under Green India Mission and other various projects and if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard; (d)	the funds spent in Madhya Pradesh under the Green India Mission during the last 3 years and the works for which the said funds have been spent along with the amount spent for each work, work-wise details thereof, district-wise;(e)	the cost-wise details of the works done in Betul and Harda, Khandwa district under Betul Parliamentary Constituency under Green India Mission during the last three years and the details of the agencies of NGOs through which the said works have been done; and (f)	the details of the unemployed to whom training material has been distributed through the NGOs under Green India Mission along with the cost of training material distributed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWANI KUMAR CHOUBAY)(a) Any non-forestry activity over forest land is permitted as per the procedure laid in the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 and Rules framed thereunder.  In order to compensate the loss of natural forest areas and ecosystem services, Compensatory Afforestation (CA) over equivalent non-forest land or double degraded forest land is stipulated and Net Present Value (NPV) is levied. Additional mitigation measures in the form Safety zone maintenance, Catchment Area Treatment Plan, Wildlife Management Plan etc. are also stipulated on case to case basis.(b) 	The year wise details of funds allocated and utilized in Madhya Pradesh through CAMPAduring the last three years:-(? in Lakh)Year	Funds allocated	Utilized2019-20	45350.0	34306.02020-21	50000.0	47564.02021-22	61688.0	47900.0 (till date)Total	157038.0129770(c)& (d)The funds have been released by the Govt. of India on the basis of proposals& Annual Plan of Operations (APOs) submitted by the Government of Madhya Pradesh and approved in the National Executive Council (NEC) meeting.
