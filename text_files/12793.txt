                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1813






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Schemes Entrsuted to Non-Governmental Organisations




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether work of implementation of several schemes of the Government is entrusted to several non-Governmental organisations/agencies at regional level;(b)  	if so, details of these agencies working State-wise and especially in Jharkhand;(c)  	whether the required payment has been made to these organisations/agencies which are entrusted with the work of imparting training in the most under-developed areas through Tailoring/Embroidery centres or other socially useful programmes;  (d) 	if not, the details thereof and the reasons for not making the payment; and (e)  	whether the Government proposes to set up any monitoring mechanism so as to ensure the completion of entrusted work and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) Yes, Sir. The Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) scheme is being implemented through non- Governmental organisations/agencies in 28 states and 2 UT’s of the country. (b)  The details of such agencies state-wise including Jharkhand is given at Annexure-“A”(c) Yes, Sir. As per the scheme guidelines, the non-Governmental organisations/agencies implementing the JSS scheme are given recurring annual Grants-in-Aid of Rs. 50 Lakhs in two installments (d)    All operational JSSs are regularly being given Grants-in-Aid by the Ministry.(e) 	The monitoring of enrollment, training, assessment, and certification of JSS beneficiaries is     monitored electronically through web-portal by the Directorate of Jan Shikshan Sansthan. In addition, the performance of various Jan Shikshan Sansthans is reviewed by the said Directorate at regional levels twice a year.
