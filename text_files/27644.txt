                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3297






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Digitisation of Records Related to Freedom Movement




Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy













                                                    Manne Srinivas Reddy
                                                








                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                








                                                    Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the steps being taken for National Archives'' records related to freedom movement and freedom fighters to be digitized which houses over 18 crore pages,  64,000 volumes and 1.2 lakh maps as a part of the commemoration of 75 years of Independence next year;(b) if so, the details thereof and progress made, as Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav,  the yearlong series of activities leading up to Independence  Day 2022.;(c)  the steps being taken to review the digitization project in general and that of the freedom fighters in particular soon to speed up the work ensuring safety precautions of the records during the proposed demolition of the annexe building as part of the Central Vista redevelopment project, as precaution need to be taken for each and every page/record and if so, the details thereof and progress made, till date in Telangana?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT  OF  NORTH  EASTERN  REGION(SHRI  G. KISHAN  REDDY)(a) & (b)    National Archives of India has already digitized records of Home Political (1907-46) which contains information on Freedom Movement and Freedom Fighters. Also, private papers of prominent freedom fighters like Subhash Chandra Bose, Mahatma Gandhi and Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel have also been digitized. Records have been digitized series wise.(c)       All necessary precautions are being taken up during the digitisations of these records. No decision regarding demolishing the Annexe building of National Archives of India has been taken so far. National Archives of India has no branch office in Telangana. *****
