                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3185






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Traditional Sports




Gajendra Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the locations where Ministry is developing new stadiums in the State of Madhya Pradesh;(b) whether the development of new stadiums in the Parliamentary Constituency of Khargon-Bharwani is under consideration with the Government and if so, the action taken in this regard;(c) whether any new scheme is under consideration with the Government to promote the traditional sports like archery and mallakhamb which are played in tribal areas and if so, the details thereof; and(d) the details of the sports schemes running at present in the Parliamentary Constituency of Khargaon-Barwani?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) and (b) ‘Sports’ being a State subject, the responsibility of development of sports stadiums in the country, including the Parliamentary Constituency of Khargon-Bharwani, rests primarily with the respective State/Union Territory Governments. The Union Government supplements their efforts. However, under the “Utilisation and creation/upgradation of sports infrastructure” Component of the Khelo India Scheme, this Ministry has sanctioned 12 sports infrastructure projects of various categories in the State of Madhya Pradesh since the inception of the Khelo India Scheme in 2016-17. Details of the same are at Annexure. In addition, one Khelo India Centre (district level) for Hockey at Khel Yuva Klyan Vibhag Stadium Ground, Khargone has also been approved under the Khelo India Scheme.(c) This Ministry runs a Central Sector Scheme, namely, ‘Khelo India – National Programme for Development of Sports’ Scheme, wherein one of the verticals, namely, ‘Promotion of rural and indigenous/tribal games’, is specifically dedicated to the development and promotion of rural and indigenous/tribal games in the country. Indigenous games of Mallakhamb, Kalaripayattu, Gatka, Thang-Ta, Yogasana and Silambam have been identified for promotion under this vertical. Grants have been sanctioned for infrastructure development, equipment support, appointment of coaches, training of coaches and scholarships under this vertical. Further, scholarship to 283 medal winners of Mallakhamb, Kalaripayyattu, Gatka and Thang-Ta (? 10,000/- per month, per athlete) have also been provided by this Ministry.(d) The Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports implements the following schemes for development of sports in the country, including Khargaon-Barwani:(i) Khelo India Scheme; (ii) Assistance to National Sports Federations; (iii) Special Awards to Winners in International Sports Events and their Coaches; (iv) National Sports Awards; (v) Pension to Meritorious Sportspersons; (vi) Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Sports Welfare Fund; (vii) National Sports Development Fund; and (viii) Running Sports Training Centres through Sports Authority of India.Details of the above schemes are available in the public domain on the websites of this Ministry and the Sports Authority of India.As mentioned above, a Khelo India Centre for Hockey has been approved under the Khelo India Scheme at Khargone at Khel Yuva Klyan Vibhag Stadium Ground.****
