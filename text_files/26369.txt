                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Skill Development




Indra Hang Subba














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of institutions/Universities dedicated for skill development that have beenestablished in the country during the last 5 years if any along with the details thereof;(b) whether there is any University in the country dedicated for apprenticeship and vocationaltraining?(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the amount of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) that has been utilised by theGovernment for apprenticeship programme to skill literate youths?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (c) During last 05 years, following five (05) Skill Universities have been established:-S. No Name of university Date/Year of Notification/Establishment1. Shri Vishwakarma Skill University Gurugram,Haryana.20.09.20162. Symbiosis Skills and Professional University,Pune, Maharashtra05.05.20173. Bhartiya Skill Development University, Jaipur,Rajasthan.30.03.2017In the year 2020-21, 139 universities were approved by UGC to offer skill-based programmes.UGC has also issued Guidelines for Apprenticeship/Internship embedded Degree Progrmameand all the universities have been requested to introduce such degree program.Sector Skill Councils (SSCs) under Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship(MSDE) are implementing “Degree Apprenticeship” programme in Retail, Logistics and Media.So far 47 institutions are imparting Degree Apprenticeship.The year-wise number of institutions approved by UGC, to offer skill-based programmes are asunder :Year Number of Institutions2016-17 4252017-18 4242018-19 6242019-20 3542020-21 1208(d) The amount utilized under Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) by various companiesin “enhancing vocational skills” under the heading of “promotion of education” as enshrined inthe Schedule VII of the Companies Act 2013 in the last six years are given below :-DevelopmentSectorFY2014-15FY2015-16FY2016-17FY2017-18FY2018-19FY2019-20Total(Rs. inCr.)VocationalSkill (in Rs.Cr.)277.07344.40373.46391.76759.491,094.693240.88(Data upto 31.03.2021) (Source: National CSR Data Portal)****4.
