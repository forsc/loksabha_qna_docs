                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3177






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Institutes with Minority Status




Mohammad Azam Khan














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of educational institutes with minority status in the country, 	State/ 	UT-wise; (b) 	the details of the financial assistance provided to these institutes, 	State/District-wise; (c) 	whether there is any proposal of the Government to increase or reduce the 	State financial assistance; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a): There are 13,555 educational institutions which have been granted Minority status by the National Commission for Minority Educational Institutions (NCMEI) till 2nd July 2019. Details of the State-wise & year-wise minority status issued to institutes in the country is annexed.(b) to (d):   Department of School Education and Literacy is implementing an umbrella Scheme for Providing Education to Madarsas / Minorities (SPEMM) which comprises of 2 schemes namely Scheme for Providing Quality Education in Madarsas (SPQEM) and Infrastructure Development in Minority Institutions (IDMI). The scheme is being implemented at National level. 16 States i.e. Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Haryana, Jharkhand, Karnataka, Kerala, Manipur, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, Tripura, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, West Bengal, Sikkim, Mizoram, have availed benefits under the scheme from 2014-15 onwards. The physical and financial achievements under SPQEM and IDMI are at Annexure I and II.Further UGC has provided financial support of Rs.85 Lakhs to Jamia Millia Islamia and Rs.65 Lakhs to Maulana Azad National Urdu University for establishment of Residential Coaching Academies for Minorities, Women/SCs/STs during 2017-18. There are two central universities namely AMU & JMI, where most of the girl students are from minority communities.  During the last three years under Salary, Recurring & Capital Assets head grant of Rs.3,01,040.81 Lakhs has been released to AMU, Aligarh and Rs.1,00,206.29 Lakh   has been released to Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi.*****
