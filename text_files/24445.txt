                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

370






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Implementation of PMGSY




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of roads constructed under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) covering lengths of roads, State-wise;(b) 	the total funds sanctioned and released for each component of roads and the status of their completion; and(c) 	the details of roads being planned/approved/under consideration for the upcoming projects in the State?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)	  & (b) Under various interventions/verticals of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), 1,64,003 road works of 6,69,981 km road length have been constructed till 15.07.2021. The funds for implementation of the PMGSY are released by the Ministry to the states/UTs as a whole, considering the overall quantum of works remaining, unspent balance and the pace of expenditure. A sum of Rs. 2,13,714 crore has been released till 15.07.2021 as Central share to the States/UTs under PMGSY and expenditure of Rs. 2,54,281 crore has been done, including State share. The state-wise details of road length sanctioned, constructed, balance and value of projects under PMGSY as on 15.07.2021 is given at Annexure-I. Details of funds released and spent state-wise are given at Annexure-II.(c)  PMGSY is an ongoing scheme. All the works under PMGSY-I & II have been sanctioned, the details of which are given in Annexure-I. Under RCPLWEA, a total of 9,268 km has been sanctioned and 4,125 km completed. Proposals of 2,024 km have been received. Under PMGSY-III, the overall target is 1,25,000 km and 62,968 km has already been sanctioned. The timeline for completion of PMGSY-III is till March 2025. Details of State-wise sanction and progress is given in Annexure-I.*****
