                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

978






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Study on Diseases post COVID-19




Riti Pathak













                                                    Dileshwar Kamait
                                                








                                                    Geeta Kora
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government has conducted a study on various diseases observed/identified post COVID-19 b)	if so, the details thereof:c)	whether the Government has taken any concrete measure or established protocols to fight various diseases post COVID-19; and d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARTI PRAVIN KUMAR)(a) to (d): The Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) has established a National Clinical Registry on COVID that provides insights into clinical course of COVID-19 disease, its spectrum and outcome of patients. After extensive expert consultations, Ministry of Health & FW on 21st October 2021 has issued National Comprehensive Guidelines for Management of Post-Covid Sequelae (available at: https://www.mohfw.gov.in/pdf/NationalComprehensiveGuidelinesforManagementofPostCovidSequelae.pdf). The document contains detailed guidance for doctors on managing post-COVID complications affecting cardiovascular, gastrointestinal, nephrological, neurological and respiratory systems and rehabilitation from such complications.****
