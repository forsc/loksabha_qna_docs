                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3820






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






GI Tag to Handloom Industries




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any plan to issue Geographical Indication (GI) tag to the famous handloom sarees produced in the country;(b) 	if so, State-wise details thereof;(c) 	whether the Sambalpuri handloom saree of the State of Odisha will be considered for GI Tag in the current year; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b):   	Geographical Indication tags are granted on the basis of application made by the Association of persons or producers or any organization or authority established by or under any law representing the interest of the producers of the concerned goods. Any such organization which is desirous of registering a geographical indication in relation to such goods could apply in writing to the Registrar of Geographical Indications. However, the Geographical Indications Registry does not have any suo-moto power to register any product as Geographical Indications (GIs). Various handloom sarees across the country have been accorded GI tag, a list of such GIs is at Annexure-I.(c) & (d): 	Sambalpuri Bandha Saree & Fabrics is registered as a Geographical Indication through GI application No. 208 based on the application made on 13/07/2010 by Directorate of Textiles & Handloom, Government of Orissa, Satyanagar, Bhubaneswar-751007, Orissa under class 24 & 25 as per the classification of goods given under the Geographical Indications of Goods (Registration & Protection) Rules, 2002 *******ANNEXURE-IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) & (b) OF THE LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 3820 FOR ANSWER ON 11.12.2019.List of Sarees registered as Geographical Indications            Sl.No.	GI.
