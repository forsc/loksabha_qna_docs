                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1487






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






DTTI Initiative




Midhun Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the measures taken by the Government recently to operationalize the Defence Technology and Trade Initiative (DTTI) under the Indo-US Bilateral Defence initiative;(b) the details of the specific areas of cooperation being developed under the initiative;(c) whether these measures would counter the ‘Make in India’ initiative of the Government; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  During the 9th Defence Technology & Trade Initiative (DTTI) meeting held on 24th Oct, 2019 in New Delhi, the Co-chairs from Government of India and Government of US signed a ‘Statement of Intent’ identifying key deliverables in short, medium and long terms.  These deliverables conform to the objectives of DTTI i.e. acquiring of niche technology and create opportunities for co-production & co-development of defence equipment and intensifying cooperation in R&D.(b) Four functional Joint Working Groups (JWGs) are presently under DTTI namely Naval System, Land System, Air System and on Aircraft Carrier Technology Cooperation which deal with various defence projects for co-development & co-production.(c) No, Sir.  DTTI does not counter the ‘Make in India’ Initiative as it works towards developing capabilities and partnership in co-production and co-development in the country.(d)  Not Applicable.*****
