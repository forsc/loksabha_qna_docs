                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4231






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






International Yoga Day




Jyotirmay Singh Mahato














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)   be pleased to state: (a) the details of the programmes organised by the Government on International Yoga Day; (b) whether the Government has conducted any study regarding impact of Yoga consequent to the programmes organized on the International Yoga Day during the last year; (c) if so, the details thereof; (d) the names of State Governments who have submitted proposals regarding the location to celebrate the next International Yoga Day; and (e) the details of such proposals, State/UT-wise including West Bengal?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):     Ministry of AYUSH has the mandate of being the Nodal Ministry for the observation of International Day of Yoga (IDY) and it encourages other Ministries, the governments of the States & UTs and other Stake-holders to observe International Yoga Day in a befitting manner. The main event of Mass Yoga Demonstration for the observation of 5th IDY was held at Prabhat Tara Ground, Ranchi, Jharkhand on 21.06.2019.The Ministry of AYUSH has also organized events of Mass Yoga Demonstrations for IDY at following places in NCT of Delhi: - i.	Rajpath, New Delhi ii.	Lodhi Garden, New Delhiiii.	Talkatora Garden, New Delhiiv.	District Park, Sector-11, Dwarka, New Delhiv.	Swarna Jayanti Park, Sector-10, Rohini, Delhi.vi.	Nehru Park, New Delhi. vii.	Yamuna Sports Complex, Surajmal Vihar, Delhi.viii.	Lal Quila, Delhi(b) & (c):    No such study has been conducted by the Ministry specifically for the impact of International Day of Yoga.(d) & (e):    There is no such proposal received by the Ministry in this regard from any State Government including West Bengal.……………..
