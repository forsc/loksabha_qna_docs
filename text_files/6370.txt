                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

60






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Kendriya Vidyalayas in Rajasthan




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the criteria to set up Kendriya Vidyalayas in a State/UT;(b) the number of Kendriya Vidyalayas functioning in all the States/ UTs includingRajasthan under the said criteria; and(c) the funds allocated by the Government to run Kendriya Vidyalayas in Rajasthanduring the last three years?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Proposals for opening of new Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) are considered onlyif sponsored by Ministries or Departments of the Government of India/StateGovernments / Union Territories Administrations thereby committing resources forsetting up a new KV as well as the availability of necessary sanction of theGovernment. The proposals received from various sponsoring authorities foropening of new KVs also have to compete among other proposals under the“Challenge Method”.The details of criteria/norms to set up KVs in a State / UT are as under:<pre>(i) Providing free of cost land, on permanent transfer/99 years on lease at thenominal rent, i.e., @ Re.1/- per annum, measuring 2.50 to 5.00 acres inmetropolitan cities, Hyderabad & Bengaluru, LWE district/Hill areas/NER,contiguous plot fairly levelled, measuring 5.00 to 10.00 acres in other locationsas the case may be.(ii) A rent-free temporary accommodation consisting of 15 rooms of the size7m x 7m with adequate space till the permanent Vidyalaya building isconstructed by KVS on the land provided by the Sponsoring Authority.(iii) Residential accommodation to at least 50% of staff posted in the proposedVidyalaya.(iv) Concentration of at least 500 employees of Defence/Central Govt./PSUsindividually or jointly at the station (200 in case of LWE districts/Hillareas/NER).(v) Availability of minimum 200 students of the aforesaid priority categories forenrolment in the proposed K.V.Besides, Kendriya Vidyalayas are also set up under Project/IHL Sector onreceipt of a proposal from the Govt. of India PSUs/ Institutes of Higher LearningSector in which all recurring and non-recurring expenditure is borne by theSponsoring Authority concerned.</pre>(b) 1224 KVs are functioning in the country including 76 in the State of Rajasthan.The State/UT-wise details are annexed.(c) Funds are allocated to Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) under threedistinct object heads –‘Grants-in-Aid – Salaries’, ‘Grants-in-Aid – General’ and‘Grants-in- Aid’ - Creation of Capital Assets’. These grants are not allocated Statewise.However, the details of expenditure incurred by KVS during the last three yearsto run the KVs in Rajasthan are as under:-<pre>Year Rs. in Crore2016-17 286.302017-18 304.742018-19 329.53</pre>*****
