                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3084






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Upgradation of National Highway




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to extend/ upgrade various National Highways (NHs) in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof including the progress made in this regard so far; (c) whether the Government has constructed more than 100 NHs during the last five years; and (d) if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Telangana and Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d) Development and maintenance of NHs is a continuous process which is carried out depending on the requirement of connectivity, traffic density, inter-se priority and availability of funds. The Ministry had taken up detailed review of NHs network and has given investment approval for Bharatmala Pariyojana Phase I Scheme for development of about 35,000 km of NH network in the country. Details of National Highways (NHs) constructed, State-wise including Telangana and Maharashtra, during last five years are placed at Annexure-I.
