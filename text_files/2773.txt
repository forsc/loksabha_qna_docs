                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

232






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Micro and Mini Food Processing Units




Santosh Kumar













                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any proposal to provide grants to establish Micro Food Processing units in near future; (b)	if so, the details thereof specially in Bihar including Purnia, North-Eastern States and Uttar Pradesh and if not, the reasons therefor; (c)	whether the Government has any proposal to establish Mini Food Parks to benefit small farmers, specially in Bihar including Purnia, North East Region and Uttar Pradesh; (d)	 if so, the details including the plan layout thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(e)	whether there is any other scheme pertaining to Food Processing Industries to benefit small and medium farmers and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SMT. HARSIMRAT KAUR BADAL) (a) to (e):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (e) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *232 FOR ANSWERED ON 09TH JULY, 2019 REGARDING MICRO AND MINI FOOD PROCESSING UNITS(a) &(b): Under the proposed Gram Samridhi Yojana, micro- food processing units are proposed to be assisted towards modernization and enhancing their competitiveness.  (c) & (d): With a total plan outlay of Rs. 750 crore, Ministry launched “Creation of Infrastructure for Agro Processing Clusters” scheme in July, 2017 under the “Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana” to create modern infrastructure closer to the production areas. The details of projects approved (till 30th June, 2019), in the States of Bihar, North East Region and Uttar Pradesh are at Annexure. (e):	All major schemes under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana, namely Mega Food Parks, Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure, Creation/Expansion of Food Processing & Preservation Capacities, Creation of Infrastructure for Agro-Processing Clusters, Creation of Backward & Forward Linkages and Operation Greens directly and indirectly benefit farmers.  *****
