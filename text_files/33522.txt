                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

944






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Skill Development of PwDs




Brijbhushan Sharan Singh













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Mahendra Singh Solanky
                                                








                                                     Pratap Chandra Sarangi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has devised any scheme for imparting skill development to Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) for increasing employability, if so, the details thereof; (b) the number of individuals who have been trained and provided employment under the said scheme particularly in the States of Odisha, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh along with the details of their disabilities; (c) whether the same scheme covers intellectual disabilities; and (d) if not, whether the Government proposes to introduce similar schemes for those with intellectual disabilities? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a)     With objective to provide skill training to persons with disabilities (PwDs) for enabling them to become self-reliant and productive members of the society in order to achieve gainful wage/self-employment and integrate them with the mainstream of the society, Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD) implements a "National Action Plan (NAP) for Skill Development of Persons with Disabilities (PwDs)" which was launched in March, 2015. NAP is implemented under the umbrella scheme – “Scheme for Implementation of Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 (SIPDA)” as per Common Norms guidelines of M/o Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE). Under NAP, for imparting training to persons with disabilities (Not less than 15 years and not more than 59 years of age), fund is released to training partners (ETPs) empanelled from Government (GOs) and Non-Government Organizations (NGOs) as well as organizations of the Department such as National Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation (NHFDC), National Institutes (NIs) and Composite Regional Centres (CRCs) directly.(b)   The number of individuals who have been trained and employed under NAP in the States of Odisha, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh  is as under: State	No. of PwD trained	No. of PwD employed 	Categories of Disabilities Odisha	935	496	Locomotor Disability, Hearing Impairment, Visual Impairment, Intellectual DisabilityRajasthan	762	557	Locomotor Disability, Speech and Hearing Impairment, Visual Impairment and  Intellectual Disability Madhya Pradesh	3320	1122	Locomotor Disability ,Hearing Impairment, Visual Impairment, Intellectual Disability ,Multiple Disabilities, Leprosy CuredUttar Pradesh	4628	1971	Locomotor Disabilities, Hearing Impairment, Visual Impairment (c) & (d) Any person of any category of disability identified in the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 including person with intellectual disabilities, with not less than 40% disability and having a disability certificate to this effect issued by a competent medical authority may avail facilities of skill training under NAP.  *****
