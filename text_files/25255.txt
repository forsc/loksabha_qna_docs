                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

944






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Primary Education of Tribals in NER




Rebati Tripura














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of tribals who have access to education and details thereof along with enrolment in the primary education in the country especially in North East Region;(b) whether the Government proposes to establish more schools in tribal and remote areas of NER for the benefit of students of these areas, if so, the details thereof;(c) the present status of the scholarship schemes implemented by the Government for tribal students of NER; and(d) the amount of funds allocated/utilized along with the number of students benefited under the said schemes till date?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SMT. RENUKA SINGH SARUTA)  (a)	As per UDISE+ 2019-20 of the Ministry of Education, 2,47,93,474 ST students are enrolled in different schools (I to XII) including 1,28,01,880 students enrolled in primary classes (I-V). State wise figure is at Annexure-I.(b)	The Government, through the scheme of Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS) funded by the Ministry of Tribal Affairs, provides quality education to the tribal students in a residential environment from class VI to XII. Currently 119 EMRSs have been sanctioned in the NER and another 31 EMRSs are to be sanctioned during the current financial year.(c)	Ministry implements following ongoing schemes for providing scholarships to ST students in the country including NER: -i.	Pre-Matric Scholarship for ST students (Class IX and X)ii.	Post Matric Scholarship for ST students.(Class XI onwards)iii.	National Fellowship and Scholarship for Higher Education for ST students.iv.	National Overseas Scholarship for ST students. Out of the schemes mentioned above, funds under those at Sl. No. (i) & (ii) are released to the State Governments/ Union Territory Administrations. These are demand driven open ended Centrally Sponsored Schemes.  The release of funds to States/UTs is done based on the proposal and expenditure details received from the State along with the submission of other requisite documents. The scheme at Sl. No. (iii) above is Central Sector Scheme implemented by Ministry of Tribal Affairs where scholarship amount is released directly to Students/Institutes in DBT mode. The scheme at Sl. No. (iv) is also Central Sector Scheme implemented by the Ministry through Ministry of External Affairs.(d)	 The amount of fund released during last five years under Pre-Matric and Post-Matric Scholarship Scheme to NER States and utilization by NER States along with the number of students benefited is enclosed as Annexure-II.
