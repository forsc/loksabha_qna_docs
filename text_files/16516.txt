                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

243






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Promotion of Hindi language




Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah













                                                    Pushpendra Singh Chandel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken cognizance of the increasing use of Hinglish - a hybrid of English and Hindi in common parlance and for official purpose;(b) whether the Government is making any special efforts to further promote the proper and practical use of Hindi language;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the details of the funds spent on training and promotion of Hindi language in Government Institutions during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	 No, Sir.(b) & (c): 	Yes Sir. The Official Language Policy of the Union is based on incentives, persuasion and goodwill. The Department of Official Language encourages usage of simple, lucid and comprehensible Hindi in the official work. In compliance with the Official Language Policy of the Government of India, Central Hindi Training Institute imparts training of Hindi Language, Hindi word processing and Hindi stenography to the Central Government employees who do not possess  knowledge  of Hindi  language  keeping  in view  the correctness  of-2/…-2-LS.US.Q.NO.243 FOR 15.09.2020grammar, standard terminology and standard structure of Official Hindi in order to provide them with working knowledge/proficiency in Hindi to execute their official work in Hindi. As far as possible, standard terminology is used by the Central Translation Bureau during translation work. Similarly, during translation training, trainees are introduced to standard terminology also and it is made clear that they should use standard terminology while doing translation in official work. This ensures accuracy, simplicity and spontaneity of the language.d. The details of the expenditure incurred by the Department of Official Language for the training and promotion of Hindi during the last three years are as follows:Sr. No.	Year	Expenditure (in crore rupees)1.		2017-18	63.152.		2018-19	68.173.		2019-20	70.15The expenditure incurred for training and promotion of Hindi in other government departments/institutions is done by the concerned institutions as per their own discretion.********
