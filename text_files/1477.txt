                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1367






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Investments in Renewable Energy




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there has been any investments in renewable energy projects by companies as a part of their CSR; and(b)  if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF FINANCEAND CORPORATE AFFAIRS                                     (Smt. NIRMALA SITHARAMAN)???????? ???????? ????? ?????? 					      (??????????????????????)(a)& (b): Item no. (iv) of Schedule VII of the Act i.e., “ensuring environmental sustainability, ecological balance, protection of flora and fauna, animal welfare, agro forestry, conservation of natural resources and maintaining quality of soil, air and water including contribution to the ‘Clean Ganga Fund’ set-up by the Central Government for rejuvenation of river Ganga;” covers wide range of activities including ‘Renewable Energy Projects’.The details of the CSR spent under item no. (iv) of Schedule VII of the Actfor the Financial Year 2015-16, 2016-17, and 2017-18 are Rs. 1005.17 crores, Rs. 1335.53 crores and Rs. 1008.48 crores respectively.********
