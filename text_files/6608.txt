                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

242






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Reimbursement under Saving-Cum-Relief Scheme for Fishermen




Kumbakudi Sudhakaran














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has received any proposal from the State Government of Kerala to reimburse Rs. 19.4 crore as Central share for Saving-Cum- Relief Scheme to provide off-season relief to marine fishermen, if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Union Government has reimbursed its share of Rs.19.4 crore for Saving-Cum-Relief Scheme to marine fishermen in Kerala;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




ANSWER  THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRYAND DAIRYING (SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) to (d): 	Yes, sir.	The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Animal Fisheries Husbandry and Dairying under the component of National Scheme on Welfare of Fishermen of the  Centrally Sponsored Scheme(CSS) on “Blue Revolution : Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries” had in July, 2019 accorded approval to the proposal of Government of Kerala for Saving-cum-Relief and released the central share of  19.14 Crore to the State Government for providing relief to a total of 1,27,600 Nos fishers in the State.  *****
