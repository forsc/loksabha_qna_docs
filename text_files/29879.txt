                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1535






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






ODOFP Scheme




Rita Bahuguna Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has finalized a list of all the 728 districts across the country for “One District One Focus Product” (ODOFP);(b)	if so, the details thereof, district-wise;  (c)	the list of districts where Government has started delivering all the centrally sponsored schemes to the farmers under the said project; and(d)	whether Allahabad in Uttar Pradesh and its nearby areas are also covered in ODOFP; if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????	 (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b): 	Yes Sir. The list of 728 districts is at Annexure I.(c): 	The States/Union Territories (UTs) have been advised to converge resources towards One District One Product (ODOP) from ongoing centrally sponsored schemes such as Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH), National Food Security Mission (NFSM), Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY), Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) of Department of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare (DA&FW),Pradhan Mantri Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises Scheme (PM FME Scheme) of Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI), schemes of Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying.These schemes of Agriculture and allied sector are primarily implemented to achieve the objectives of the respective schemes. PM FME is a major scheme for the promotion of One District One Focus Product (ODOFP)and ODOP products. Under PM FME, during 2021-22 Rs. 134.6 cr have been released to the States for implementation of the scheme. The progress of PM FME is at Annexure II.(d):	 Allahabad is covered for Guava under ODOFP. As on date 64 applications have been received on the PMFME portal out of which 16 applications are of ODOP. The products of neighbouring districts are given at Annexure-I.
