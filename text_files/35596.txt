                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2975






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






National Institute of Open Schooling




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)wheather the Government is aware about the new affiliation pending with National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS) in India;(b)the details of the new application received during the last three years;(c)the details of the affiliation completed the new application during the last three years;  	and(d)the details of the new affiliation application pending with NIOS Gujarat and their completion?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI) (a):NIOS does not affiliate schools but give accreditation to formal schools/ recognised institutions as Accredited Institute (AI) for academic courses, Accredited Vocational Institute (AVI) for Vocational Courses and Accredited Agencies (AAs) for Open Basic Education.(b) and (c) : The details of new applications received from the NIOS Regional Centres across the country and accreditation completed during the last three years are as per Annexure -I’. (d): During the year 2021-22 total 57 applications were received in NIOS Regional Centre Gandhinagar (Gujarat). These are under scrutiny. Two applications are rejected for non fulfilling NIOS norms. *****
