                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2651






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Crude Oil Price




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the international crude oil prices have crossed above US $ 100 a barrel, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether it is also a fact that domestic fuel prices have not been revised for a record 110 days in a row whereas rates were to be revised on a daily basis; and(c) if so, the reasons for not revising the rates either upwardly or downwardly?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):	Yes Sir. The price of Indian basket of crude oil as on 10.03.2022 was 116.73 $/bbl.(b) & (c):	Prices of petrol and diesel have been market-determined with effect from 26.06.2010 and 19.10.2014 respectively and are linked to the price of respective products in the international market. Since then, the Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) take appropriate decisions on pricing of petrol and diesel. x-x-x-x-x
