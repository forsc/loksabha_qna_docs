                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3430






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Scholarship for SC Students




Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel













                                                    Shardaben Anilbhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3430 TO BE ANSWERED ON 16.03.2021SCHOLARSHIP FOR SC STUDENTS †3430. SHRI MITESH RAMESHBHAI PATEL (BAKABHAI): SHRIMATI SHARDABEN ANILBHAI PATEL: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) the number of schemes being run by the Government to provide scholarship for the students belonging to the category of Scheduled Castes (SCs) to study abroad; (b) the details of students belonging to SCs category sent abroad for study during the last five years; and (c) the details of the funds allocated for such schemes during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA )(a) to (c):  The Department of Social Justice & Empowerment is implementing the National Overseas Scholarship for Scheduled Caste etc. candidates to enable them to study abroad. Under the scheme financial assistance is provided to the selected students from Scheduled Castes, Denotified, Nomadic & Semi - Nomadic Tribes, landless agricultural labourers and traditional artisan categories for pursuing higher studies  in abroad. Details of the students sent abroad for studying under the scheme and funds allocated during the last five years is annexed.*****  Annexure Statement referred to reply in parts (a) to (c) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 3430 for 16.3.2021 regarding Scholarship for SC Students.    The details of the number of candidates who went abroad for studying under the scheme and funds allocated during the last five years is as under:    Year	Number of candidates who went abroad in the Financial Year	Funds Allocated(Budget Estimate)(Rs. in Crore)2015-16	19	6.122016-17	23	15.002017-18	32	15.002018-19	56	15.002019-20	63	20.00*****
