                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4131






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Reservoirs in Western Ghat




D. Ravikumar














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether reservoirs situated in the western Ghats are prone to tremors; (b) if so, whether the Government has any reliable data in this regard; and (c) if so, the measures taken by the Government to avoid the danger?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c)  Western Ghats region falls in Zone II,III & IV on the seismic zoning map of India given by Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS). Dams/reservoirs are designed taking into account the seismic coefficients as prescribed in the BIS code.  Dams/ reservoirs including those in the Western Ghats are designed taking into account site specific seismic design parameters and approved by National Committee on Seismic Design Parameters (NCSDP). Instrumental monitoring of local seismicity around major reservoirs including those along Western Ghats is being continuously done by respective dam authorities. *****
