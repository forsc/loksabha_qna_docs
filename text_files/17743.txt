                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1568






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Procurement of Goods and Services by CPSE




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















 (a)  the total procurement of goods and services by each Central Public Sector Enterprise (CPSE) during the last three years, year-wise; (b) the value of goods and services procured under the Public Procurement Policy, 2012 during the said period in each CPSE; (c) the status of procurement under this policy from MSEs owned by OBCs/SCs/ STs and non-SCs/STs during the said period by each CPSE; and (d) the reasons for non-compliance, if any, of the policy?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) to (d): The Public Procurement Policy for Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs) Order, 2012 is administered by M/o of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (M/o MSME). M/o MSME has launched ‘MSME SAMBANDH’ Portal to monitor the progress of procurement from MSEs including the enterprises owned by SCs/STs and women by the Central Ministries/ Departments and Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs). The details of procurement made by CPSEs furnished by M/o MSME as uploaded by the CPSEs on the portal during the last three years are as under:(Rs. In crore)Year	No. of CPSEs 	Total Procurement 	Procurement From MSEs 	Procurement from MSEs owned by SC/ST Entrepreneur  2017-18	169	114012.31	26348.38	544.452018-19	165	153476.93	40399.47	824.772019-20	136	124021.10	38386.55	684.99The CPSE wise yearly details are given in Annex.  Data on procurement made from MSEs owned by OBCs is not available under the Public Procurement Policy for Micro & Small Enterprises Order, 2012, as issued under the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises Development Act, 2006.The CPSEs are complying with the Public Procurement Policy for Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs) Order, 2012, as the overall procurement from MSEs during the last FY 2019-20 has been 30.94% which is more than the 25% prescribed under the said Policy.***
