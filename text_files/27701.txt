                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3411






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Production of Crude Oil and Natural Gas




Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the actual annual production of crude oil and natural gas by the Oil and Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC) during each of the last three years and the current year, State-wise; (b) whether the share of ONGC in country’s total crude oil output and natural gas has fallen during the last three years and the current year; (c) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor, year-wise; (d) whether the Government has chalked out a scheme to achieve targets of production of crude oil and natural gas by ONGC from their existing oil fields; and (e) if so, the details thereof and the amount of investment proposed to be made by ONGC under the scheme and the challenges faced by ONGC in achieving the target? 
                                                





ANSWER

Challenges faced in operation of E&P activities are generic and ONGC is taking appropriate steps to address these.
