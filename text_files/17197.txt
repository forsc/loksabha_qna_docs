                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

474






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Auction of Mines




Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy













                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Pocha Brahmananda Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has decided that all mines in future will be auctioned without any end- use restriction; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has also removed the right of first refusal available to existing captive mines; (d) if so, the details thereof; (e) whether there is any proposal to enhance the percentage of the quantity sold of total mineral excavated by captive mines; and (f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a) & (b) There has been no decision by the Government to this effect. However, there is a proposal in Ministry of Mines to bring legislative amendments in the Mines and Mineral (Development and Regulation) Act, 1957, inter-alia, for enhancing private investment in Mineral Sector. Proposal has been put up for consultation with stakeholders. (c)  & (d) As given in (a) & (b) above. (e) & (f) As given in (a) & (b) above. *****
