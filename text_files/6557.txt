                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

400






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Joint Venture




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has formulated any policy to promote joint venture fertilizer projects taken up with foreign collaboration to minimize dependence on imported urea and to overcome the shortage of gas and if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether any positive response has been received from any foreign country for setting up of such projects; (c) 	if so, the details thereof; (d) 	the details of the forex saved and the quantum of additional increase in the fertilizers produced annually due to the said measures in the country; (e) 	whether the availability of the said products has been ensured from these measures in the country; and (f) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS(SHRI  D.V. SADANADA GOWDA) (a):	 No Sir.   There is no policy formulated to promote joint venture fertilizer projects taken  up with foreign collaboration. (b) & (c):  Yes Sir.  A proposal has been received from Gabon for establishment of Urea-Ammonia Joint Venture plant along with Urea off-take to India. On the advice of Ministry of External Affairs, Department of Fertilizers sent a technical team to Gabon to assess the said project.  Meanwhile, Ministry of External Affairs informed about the   political instability in Gabon and  advised Department of Fertilizers to put on hold the proposal of investment in Gabon until the situation improves.  (d) to (f):  Question does not arise in view of the above.*********
