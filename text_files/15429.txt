                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4102






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Flexi Fare System




Pankaj Chowdhary














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   the details of the seats which remained vacant in the trains under Flexi Fare System during the last eight months, i.e., from July, 2019 to February, 2020;(b)  whether the Government has contemplated upon the reasons behind the seats remaining vacant in the trains under Flexi Fare System; (c)   if so, the measures being taken by the Government to fill such seats in the said trains; and(d)  whether the Government is likely to consider giving concessions in the fare to the elderly, the differently abled and patients travelling in trains under Flexi Fare System?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
