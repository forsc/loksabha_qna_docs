                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4524






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Export of Covaxin and Covidshield




Preneet Kaur














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state: (a) whether it is a fact that the Government has decided to export Covid-19 vaccines namely, Covaxin and Covishield to the neighbouring countries, free of cost; (b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons for the same; (c) the details of countries that will receive Covaxin and Covishield; (d) the details of the vaccines export plans, if any; and (e) whether the Government has factored in the availability of vaccines for domestic use after exports and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS                                   (SHRI D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a) & (b):   India, as most other countries, is implementing COVID-19 vaccination programme in a phased manner. Taking into account domestic production, requirements of the phased roll out of national vaccination programme and the demand for Made-in-India vaccines, the decision was taken to supply the surplus to certain countries as grants. Till date, India has given a total of 81.55 lakh doses to 38 countries as grants. (c):	List of countries which have got Covaxin and Covishield under MEA grant program are given in Annexure.(d) & (e): A sub-group of National Expert Group on Vaccine Administration for COVID-19 (NEGVAC) is coordinating the external supplies of vaccine with due regard to domestic production and adequate availability of Covid 19 vaccines for national vaccination program through regular interaction between relevant Departments of the Government of India and vaccine manufacturers.
