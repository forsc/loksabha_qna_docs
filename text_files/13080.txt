                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1952






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Beneficiaries under PDS




Bidyut Baran Mahato













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of beneficiaries under Public Distribution System (PDS) belonging to BPL and APL categories under various schemes of the Government; (b) 	whether the Government proposes to review the National Food Security Act (NFSA); (c) 	if so, the details along with the aims and objectives thereof; (d) 	whether the Government has prepared any roadmap for various Ministries in this regard and if so, the details thereof; (e) 	whether a report by the Secretaries has also been submitted to the Government in this regard and if so, the details thereof; and (f) 	the steps taken/being taken by the Government to make National Food Security Act applicable only for deserving sections of the country?






ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a): 	The National Food Security Act, 2013 (NFSA) covers about 80 crore persons to receive subsidized foodgrains from Public Distribution System in two categories namely – Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) and Priority Households (PHH) only. Further, no category of beneficiaries under APL/BPL exists under NFSA provisions..........2/--2-(b) to (e): 	There is no such proposal under consideration in the Department at present.(f): 		NFSA inter-alia provides coverage for about two-thirds of the total population of the country as per Census 2011, i.e. up to 75% of the rural population and up to 50% of the urban population are eligible for receiving subsidized foodgrains under the Act.  Further, identification of eligible beneficiaries under NFSA and issuance of ration cards to them pertains to respective State/UT Governments.****
