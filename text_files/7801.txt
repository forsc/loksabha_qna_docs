                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1602






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Chinese Submarine




Kapil Moreshwar Patil














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether a Chinese submarine run on atomic energy stationed at Colombo International Container Terminal is a security threat for India;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether India has raised its concern to Sri Lankan Government regarding presence of increasing Chinese forces in the area;(d) if so, the reaction of Sri Lankan Government in this regard; and(e) the corrective measures adopted/proposed by the Government immediately in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) to (e) 	There have been no recent reports of Chinese submarines docking at Sri Lankan ports.Government keeps a close watch on all developments having a bearing on India’s security and economic interests, and takes all necessary measures to safeguard it. India and Sri Lanka share a strong and multi-faceted relationship and cooperate closely on various issues including defence and security aspects. India is confident about the strength and enduring nature of its bilateral ties with Sri Lanka. ***
