                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2292






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Advance Agreements with Foreign Vaccine Makers




Sarmistha Sethi














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that lack of advance agreements with foreign vaccine makers had delayed the rollout of the COVID-19 inoculation drive in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government proposes to roll out oral anti-COVID-19 drugs in the country; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR) (a) & (b) Government of India has taken proactive steps to secure adequate vaccines for National Covid-19 Vaccination Programme. There has been no delay in placing orders with vaccine manufacturers rather advance orders of vaccines have been placed with M/s Serum Institute of India and M/s Bharat Biotech for supply of COVID-19 vaccines. (c) and (d) Central Drugs Standard Control Organization (CDSCO) has approved various oral drugs for restricted use under emergency situation for COVID 19 treatment, namely- Favipiravir tablets, Baricitinib Tablets & 2- Deoxy-D-Glucose (2-DG) sachets.
