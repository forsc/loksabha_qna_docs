                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1526






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Soil Health Card




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details and the total number of farmers who have been provided with Soil Health Cards under Soil Health Management Scheme for increase in the agricultural production across the country during the last three years, State-wise, including Jharkhand; and(b)	 the number of Soil Health Testing Laboratories set up for soil testing in Santhal Pargana in Jharkhand? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	The details on number of farmers provided soil health card during last three years, State-wise including Jharkhand is given at Annexure.(b):	Santhal Pargana have two State Government Soil testing Labs (STLs), one at Dumka and another at Sahibganj.	Two STLs were set up at Godda and Pakur. Apart from these 245 Minilabs have been set up under Soil Health Management (SHM) scheme during 2016-17.
