                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1061






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Wildlife Act




Krishna Pal Singh Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to amend Wild Life Act, 1972; and(b)	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) and (b)  Yes, Sir. The Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change had constituted a Committee for drafting the amendments to the Wild Life (Protection) Act, 1972,on 1stMay 2018, which has submitted its report. The drafting Committee has proposed a comprehensive review of various provisions of the Act. 	***
