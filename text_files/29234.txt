                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

708






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Collateral-free Loan Scheme




Krishna Pal Singh Yadav













                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Rajbahadur Singh
                                                








                                                     Pratap Chandra Sarangi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of funds allocated and amount sanctioned under the Government’s 3 lac crore   collateral-free loan scheme; (b) 	the details of beneficiaries, State and year-wise since implementation of the scheme; (c) 	whether the Government is planning to expand the limit or the validity of the scheme; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a), (c) & (d):	     Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS) is under the operational domain of Ministry of Finance, Department of Financial Services (DFS). The existing overall guarantee limit under ECLGS has been enhanced from Rs. 3 lakh crore to Rs 4.5 lakh  crore and the scheme has been extended upto 31.03.2022. The last date of disbursement under the scheme has been extended to 30.06.2022. As informed by DFS, as on 19.11.2021, Rs.2.82 lakh crore of loans have been sanctioned under the scheme. (b):		The details of numbers of beneficiaries, State and year-wise since implementation of the scheme is at Annexure-I
