                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1750






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Houses for Homeless Persons




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of houses targetted to be constructed for 	homeless persons in various States of the country and 	particularly in Buldhana district of Maharashtra during the year 	2019-20;(b) 	the funds sanctioned for this purpose by the Union 	Government and the amount pending to be released as on 	date, State-wise and particularly for Buldhana district of 	Maharashtra during the said period; (c) 	the number of houses constructed and the number of the 	houses remaining to be constructed during the said period as 	on date, State/district-wise;(d) 	whether the Union Government has received requests from 	public representatives for releasing outstanding amount for 	construction of such houses particularly for Buldhana district 	of Maharashtra; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof and the action taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                          (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)&(b)	Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana- Urban (PMAY-U), so far about 110 lakh houses have been sanctioned in various States/Union Territories (UTs). The details of funds sanctioned and released for these houses are annexed as Annexure-I. The details of houses sanctioned and funds released particularly for Buldhana district of Maharashtra during the year 2019-2020, under the PMAY-U,are annexed as Annexure -II.(c) 	The details of the number of houses sanctioned during Financial Year 2019-20 and progress thereof district wise in the State of Maharashtra are annexed as Annexure- III.(d) 	Yes, Sir. The Union Government, through Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA), had received representations from public representatives. (e)   The Ministry has taken up the matter with the State Government of Maharashtra to provide Utilisation Certificates (UCs) for funds released by the Union Government. For release of funds under the Beneficiary Linked Construction (BLC) component, the State Government has been requested to link Aadhaar seeding of beneficiaries in the Management Information System (MIS). Further, under Affordable Housing Projects (AHP) and ‘In-situ’ Slum Redevelopment (ISSR) components, in addition to above, the State Government has been requested to register projects under Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016 (RERA). The State Government of Maharashtra has also been requested to provide Action Taken Report (ATR) on the recommendations of Third-Party Quality Monitoring Agencies (TPQMA), Geo-tagging of various stages of construction of houses/ projectsso that subsequent installment for projects being implemented under PMAY-U in the State of Maharashtra could be released.                                                     *****
