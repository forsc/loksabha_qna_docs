                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3624






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Cities/Towns under AMRUT




S. Ramalingam














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of cities under developed towns covered/included under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) across the country as on date, State-wise including Tamil Nadu;(b) 	whether the Government has any proposal to include Mayiladuthurai in Tamil Nadu under AMRUT which is the oldest and under-developed town for the last thirty years; and(c) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                                           THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                 MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                     (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a): 	A total of 500 cities across all States/ Union Territories (UTs) have been covered under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT). 	Number of cities covered State/UT wise under AMRUT is given at ANNEXURE - I.(b) & (c): There is no proposal to cover Mayiladuthurai in Tamil Nadu in AMRUT scheme.  However, Government has launched AMRUT 2.0 on 01 October, 2021 for the period of 05 years i.e. from the financial year 2021-22 to the financial year 2025-26.  AMRUT 2.0 is designed to provide universal coverage of water supply through functional taps to all households in all the statutory towns in the country and coverage of sewerage/septage management in 500 cities covered in first phase of the AMRUT scheme. All the statutory towns in the country including Mayiladuthurai town are covered under AMRUT 2.0.                                          *****
