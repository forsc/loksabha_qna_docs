                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

134






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






School Textbooks




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Paika Bidroha of 1817 has been included in the school historytextbooks as the first war of independence against British Rule;(b) whether there are plans to popularise the rebellion in schools through othermethods like workshops, plays, competitions, or lectures; and(c) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) The ‘Paika Bidroha’ has been mentioned under the heading `The KhurdaUprising – A case Study` in the National Council of Educational Research andTraining (NCERT)’s Social Science textbook, namely, “Our Pasts-III” (Part-II) forClass VIII.(b) No such proposal is under consideration at present.(c) Does not arise.****
