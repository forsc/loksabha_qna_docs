                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2909






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Fingerprint Authentication




Ajay (Teni) Misra














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of Electronics & Information Technology be pleased to state:-(a)	whether the Government has given any suggestion to introduce digital fingerprint authentication in messaging platform of facebook keeping in mind the fact that investigation agencies are unable to collect correct information in case any user logs a complaint regarding objectionable messages sent through facebook in the country; and(b)	if so, the extent to which these type of crimes are likely to be checked by introducing this system?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	and (b):  No, Sir. However, Hon''ble High Court of Madras is hearing two writ petitions (Civil) No. 20774 of 2018 and (Civil)  20214 of 2018 where petitioners have prayed to declare that linking of Aadhaar or anyone of the Government authorized identity proof as mandatory for the purpose of authentication while obtaining any email or user  account (Social Media Accounts – Facebook, Instagram, Youtube and Twitter or Utility Accounts – Paytm, Uber, Ola, Gmail, Yahoo and Hotmail) thereof.The matter is presently sub-judice.*******
