                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

213






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Public Service Commission in Puducherry




 Vaithilingam Ve.














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that there is no separate Public Service Commission in the Union Territory of Puducherry;(b) whether the Government has any proposal for setting up of Puducherry Public Service Commission for Government jobs; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (c):	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.-2-POSITION NO.13STATEMENT IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NUMBER *213 FOR 03.08.2021(a):	There is no separate Public Service Commission in the Union Territory of Puducherry as it does not come under the definition of ‘State’ in so far as Article 315 (1) of the Constitution of India is concerned.(b) & (c):	There is no such proposal. ********
