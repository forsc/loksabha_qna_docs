                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

317






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Medical Device Park




Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS, be pleased to state: (a) whether the Department of Pharmaceuticals has any scheme regarding setting up of Medical Device Park in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the number of Medical Device Park set up in various States so far; and (d) the number of Medical Device Park propose to be set up in the country along with the target date for setting up of the same?






ANSWER




MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(SHRI D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a) to (b): Department of Pharmaceuticals has a sub-scheme viz. Assistance to Medical Device Industry for Common Facility Center under the umbrella scheme for Development of Pharmaceuticals Industry. Under this sub-scheme, financial assistance to the tune of Rs.25 Crore or 70% of the project cost, whichever is less may be provided for creation of common facilities under any upcoming Medical Device Park promoted by State Governments/State Corporations. However, only a token amount of Rs.2 lakh for the financial year 2019-20 has been allocated for the sub-scheme.(c) & (d): Department has received four proposals under this sub-scheme from (i) Andhra Pradesh Medtech Zone Ltd. (AMTZ), Andhra Pradesh, (ii) Telangana Medical Device Park, Telangana (iii) Kerala State Industrial Development Corporation (KSIDC), Kerala and (iv) HLL Medipark Ltd (HML), Tamil Nadu. The “in-principle” approval has already been given to the proposal received from AMTZ and the remaining three proposals are under consideration.xxxxx
