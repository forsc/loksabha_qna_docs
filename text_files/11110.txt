                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

213






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Development of New Tourist Spots in Himalayan Region




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware that almost all the tourists destinations in the Himalayan region/hilly States are hitting a saturation point; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the steps taken by the Government to develop new tourist destinations in order to ease the load off the existing ones?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b):	Ministry of Tourism, Government of India has not undertaken study on saturation of tourist destination in the Himalayan region/hilly States. However, data on Domestic Tourist visits (DTV) and Foreign Tourist Visits (FTV) for 2018 for some of the States in the Himalayan region are given in annexure.(c):	Development of tourism spots, is primarily the responsibility of the concerned State Government/Union Territory Administration. However, Ministry of Tourism, Government of India under its schemes of Swadesh Darshan, PRASHAD and Assistance to Central Agencies, provides central financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territories/Central Agencies for development of tourism related infrastructure and facilities to provide an enriching tourism experience to visitors. The projects under the schemes are identified for development in consultation with State Governments/UT Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier.*******
