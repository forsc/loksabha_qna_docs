                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

143






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Status of Sites under Adopt A Heritage Scheme in Tamil Nadu




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of TOURISM be pleased to state:(a)	the details of the status of sites covered under the ‘Adopt a Heritage’ scheme of the Union Government in the State of Tamil Nadu;(b)	the details of the budget allocated and utilized by the State of Tamil Nadu under this scheme since 2019; and(c)	whether the Government has taken any steps or assigned timelines for the development and promotion of heritage sites in the State of Tamil Nadu and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM			(SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a)&(b): Under the Adopt a Heritage project, Vision Bids have been received from prospective Monuments Mitras to develop, upgrade, and maintain amenities at Mahabalipuram, Tamil Nadu and Bhagavati Temple, Chitral, Tamil Nadu, the proposals for the same are under review by Ministry of Tourism.  Furthermore, Expression of Interests have been received from prospective Monuments Mitra to develop, upgrade, and maintain amenities at Dhanushkodi and Pichavaram, the proposals for the same are under review by Ministry of Tourism.Also, under Adopt a Heritage project, an MoU has been awarded for Development of Multi-Lingual Audio Guide to the Monument Mitra Resbird Technologies Private Limited (BIRD Heritage Foundation) for 8 Iconic sites (ASI Monuments) across India which includes the monument Mahabalipuram, Tamil Nadu. The Multilingual Audio Guide App under brand name “Audio Odigos” is available for download on both Android and iOS platforms for tourists. The application is available in Hindi, English and other international languages of Japanese and Korean and can be used in both online and offline mode.  The project aims to encourage companies from public sector, private sector, trusts, NGOs, individuals and other stakeholders to become ‘Monument Mitras’ and take up the responsibility of developing and upgrading the basic and advanced tourist amenities at these sites as per their interest and viability in terms of a sustainable investment model under Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR). They would also look after the Operation & Maintenance of the same. The project does not involve any provision of allocation of funds by the Ministry of Tourism under the MoUs in the Adopt a Heritage project. (c):	Ministry of Tourism, promotes India in a holistic manner.  As part of  its on-going activities, it releases global print, electronic and online media campaigns in  important and potential markets overseas, under the ‘Incredible India’ brand-line, to promote various tourism destinations  and  products  of the country. Promotions are also undertaken through the website and Social Media accounts of the Ministry.
