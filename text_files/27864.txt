                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3557






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






KVK in Gujarat




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has started the construction of a Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) in Morbi, Gujarat; (b)	if so, the details thereof along with the total estimated cost of the project, including land acquisition and pre-construction activities; (c)	if not, the reasons therefor; (d)	whether any employment potential is likely to be generated locally during the construction of this KVK; and (e)	the time-frame and steps being taken by the Government for making the KVK, Morbi functional?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICUTLRURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ?? ????? ?????? ??????			                  	   (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	Sir, Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) in Morbi, Gujarat was sanctioned to Junagadh Agricultural University, Junagadh on 18.10.2016. Construction of administrative building and farmers’ hostel of the KVK was completed during 2019-20 with cost of Rs. 143.00 lakh and 61.00 lakh, respectively. Land for the KVK has been provided by the University free of cost.(d)	The question does not arise as the buildings have already been constructed.(e)	The KVK, Morbi has been functional since March 2017. **********
