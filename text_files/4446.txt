                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3955






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






New PO in Navi Mumbai




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the concerned department has been ordered to open Passport Office in Navi Mumbai and if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether the above mentioned office has been opened in post office situated in Vashi and  if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the material and facilities have not been made available to set up passport office so far due to which citizens are facing difficulties; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the steps being taken by the Government for setting up the office well equipped with all the facilities till date?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) & (b) A Post Office Passport Seva Kendra (POPSK) was inaugurated at the Vashi Post Office in Navi Mumbai on 10 March, 2019. (c) & (d) The POPSK at Vashi has been made functional with appointments released to enable the citizens to apply for a passport.***
