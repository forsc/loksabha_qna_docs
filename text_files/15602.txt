                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4298






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






World Future Energy Summit




Kotagiri Sridhar














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of constituents of the World Future Energy Summit of Abu Dhabi;(b) the main pillars of the summit;(c) whether Climate Innovation Exchange (CLIX) was launched in the summit; and(d)  if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) & (b) As per the inputs received from the Embassy of India, Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates and information available in public domain, the annual World Future Energy Summit (WFES) in Abu Dhabi is a global industry platform connecting business and innovation in energy, clean technology and efficiency for a sustainable future.    The event is hosted by Masdar, which is wholly owned by the Abu Dhabi Government’s Mubadala Investment Company.The 2020 edition of WFES was held from 18- 21 January 2020 and included exhibition and forum programmes across five main pillars namely energy, solar, water, waste and smart cities.  (c) & (d) The Climate Innovations Exchange (CLIX) launched in 2018 is part of the WFES initiative and the Abu Dhabi Sustainability Week’s Youth for Sustainability hub. CLIX aims at enabling promotion and direct funding of the next generation of climate change solutions and technologies.*****
