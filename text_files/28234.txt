                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Ecological Sensitive Areas




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether  the  Government  has  any  plans  to release  the  final  notification  demarcating  Ecologically Sensitive Areas  based on the Dr.  Kasturirangan Panel Report;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the final status of Ecologically Sensitive Areas  with  respect  to  Kerala  has  been  finalised; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI. BHUPENDER YADAV)(a) to (d)  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. 	*** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARAS (A) to (D) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 3 DUE FOR REPLY ON 29.11.2021 REGARDING “ECOLOGICAL SENSITIVE AREAS” BY SHRI ADV. DEAN KURIAKOSE:The draft notification demarcating Ecologically Sensitive Areas of Western Ghats re-published by the Ministry of Environment, Forest & Climate Change on 3rd October, 2018, based on Kasturirangan Report, is valid up to 31st December, 2021. The suggestions and feedback from the States and other stakeholders are under consideration for finalisation of the notification. The Government has been consistently making all efforts to bring all the States including Kerala on board and for this purpose consultations have been held on 15th February, 2019, 21st May, 2020, 9-10th July, 2020 and 5th October 2021.  Efforts are being made to finalise the notification taking into consideration the environmental concerns of the region while simultaneously accommodating the recommendations of the State Governments including Government of Kerala. Till the finalisation of the notification, the directions issued by the Ministry on 13th November, 2013 and its amendment on 3rd December 2018 in regard to administration of activities in the area proposed, in the draft notification are valid.*****
