                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1731






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Growth of Medium and Small Entrepreneurs




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) has taken any steps to improve the growth and development of the medium and small entrepreneurs in the current financial year 2019-2020;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the schemes and its financial allocation in FY 2019-2020;(c)	whether there is any Maharashtra specific Central Government scheme available; and(d)	if so, the details thereof State-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	 The Ministry of Micro Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) implements various schemes and programmes for the growth and development of MSME Sector in the country. These schemes and programmes include Prime Minister’s Employment Generation programme (PMEGP), Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI), A Scheme for Promoting innovation, Rural Industry & Entrepreneurship (ASPIRE), Interest Subvention Scheme for Incremental Credit to MSMEs, Credit Guarantee Scheme for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE), Micro and Small Enterprises Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP), Credit Linked Capital Subsidy  and Technology Upgradation Scheme (CLCS-TUS).(b): 	The details of funds allocated under various schemes/programmes of the Ministry (during the current financial year 2019-20) are given below:<pre>S. No.	Scheme/ Programme	Allocation (in Rs. Crore)1.	A Scheme for Promoting innovation, Rural Industry & Entrepreneurship (ASPIRE)	50.002.	Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI)	125.003.	Prime Minister’s Employment Generation programme (PMEGP)	2327.104.	Credit Guarantee Scheme for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE)	597.005.	Credit Linked Capital Subsidy  and Technology Upgradation Scheme (CLCS-TUS)	755.786.	Interest Subvention Scheme for Incremental Credit to MSMEs	350.007.	Cluster Development Programme for MSEs	227.908.	Tool Rooms & Technology Institutions	118.10</pre>(c) :  	There are no State Specific Schemes implemented by the Ministry.(d):   	Does not arise in view of ( c ) above.
