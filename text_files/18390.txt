                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2252






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Scheme of Integrated Textile Park




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any proposal to set up more Scheme of Integrated Textile Park (SITP) in the country, particularly in the State of Haryana;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) whether there is any proposal to set up any SITP in Bhiwani, Dadri, Mahendragarh, Nuh districts of Haryana;(d) if so, the details thereof and the time by which these SITPs are likely to be set up; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        ?????? ?????? (??????? ?????? ?????? ?????)MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b):	The Government is implementing Scheme for Integrated Textile Park (SITP) which is demand driven and it provides support for creation of world-class infrastructure facilities for setting up of textile units, with a Government of India grant upto 40% of the project cost  and Government of India grant upto 90% of the project cost for first two projects (each) in the States of Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Tripura, Sikkim, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand and Union Territory of Jammu & Kashmir and Union Territory of Ladakh; with ceiling limit of Rs. 40.00 crores for each textile park. The Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) formed by the representatives of local industry, Financial Institutions, State Industrial and Infrastructural Corporations and other agencies of State and Central Governments  registered as a Corporate Body under Companies Act would submit their proposal directly to the Ministry for consideration. SITP is thus a demand driven scheme. No proposal is pending for setting up of Textile Park in the State of Haryana in the Ministry of Textiles, Government of India. However, 1 textile park has been sanctioned under SITP in State of Haryana the detail of which may be seen as per ANNEXURE.  (c) to (e):	No proposal is pending for setting up of Textile Park in  Bhiwani, Dadri, Mahendragarh  and Nuh districts of State of Haryana in the Ministry of Textiles, Government of India. However, 1 textile park has been sanctioned under SITP in district of Panipat in State of Haryana the detail of which may be seen as per ANNEXURE.   AnnexureS no.	Name of the park	District	GOI share(In crores)	Total govt grant released (In crores)	Status1	Alishan Integrated Textile Park, Haryana	Panipat	40.00	4.00	Under construction****
