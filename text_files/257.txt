                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

201






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Professional and Higher Education




Faizal P.P. Mohammed














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any new projects for Professional and Higher Education are lyingpending before the Government for the Lakshadweep;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the steps being taken by the Government to expedite completion/implementation of such projects?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI. RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c): Education being in the concurrent list, the creation of new institutions isthe responsibility of both Central and State governments. However, recognizing theneed for central support for achieving the aims of access, equity and excellence bystrategically funding the State higher education system, a centrally sponsoredscheme namely Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) has beenimplemented by the Ministry of Human Resource Development.Under RUSA, inter alia, central assistance is provided for setting up of NewCollege (Professional & Technical). Approval for central assistance under thescheme is dependent upon the submission of the State Higher Educational Plan(SHEP) by the States/Union Territories (UTs) and the subsequent appraisal by theProject Approval Board (PAB) of RUSA. However, the UT of Lakshadweep has notyet submitted its willingness to participate in the scheme of RUSA.***
