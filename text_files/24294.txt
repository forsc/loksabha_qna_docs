                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

319






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Schemes for Fisheries and Dairy Farming




Raju Bista













                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Thomas Chazhikadan
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                








                                                    Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel
                                                








                                                    Shardaben Anilbhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    be pleased to state: (a)	whether the Government has received any proposal from the State Government of West Bengal on reviving Himalayan Cooperative Milk Producers’ Union Ltd. (HIMUL), for which the Hon’ble minsiter had mentioned National Dairy Development Board (NDDB)’s offer to provide technical support for the revival of HIMUL to Hon’ble West Bengal Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee vide D.O. No. 4-01/2019-DP dated 19th September, 2019;(b)	the details of animal husbandry and integrated farming initiatives run by the Government in the districts of Darjeeling, Kalimpong, and North Dinajpur;(c)	the details of registered beneficiaries under schemes for fisheries and dairy farming; and(d)	the details of assistance extended to SC/ST communities under various schemes of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying in Darjeeling, Kalimpong and Chopra block under North Dinajpur district?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(DR.SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying, Government of India, has not received any proposal from State Government of West Bengal for reviving Himalayan Cooperative Milk Producers’ Union Ltd. (HIMUL), after National Dairy Development Board (NDDB)’s offer to provide technical support for the revival of HIMUL to Hon’ble West Bengal Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee vide D.O. No. 4-01/2019-DP dated 19th September, 2019.(b)	Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying has not received any proposal for dairy development of the districts of Darjeeling, Kalingpong and North Dinajpur. However, the Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing following animal husbandry & dairy development schemes across the country including the districts of Darjeeling, Kalingpong and North Dinajpur for the benefit of Animal Husbandry and Dairy development:-i.	Rashtriya Gokul Missionii.	National Programme for Dairy Developmentiii.	National Livestock Missioniv.	National Health and Disease Control Schemev.	National Animal Disease Control Programmevi.	Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fundvii.	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fundviii.	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organisations engaged in dairy activities(c)	& (d) Details of beneficiaries covered and assistance extended under Department’s beneficiary oriented activities/schemes are as under;i.	Under Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY), Department of Fisheries has informed that the concerned States/UTs identify the beneficiaries under this Scheme and Government of West Bengal has not availed the benefits of the PMMSY during the first year of its implementation i.e 2020-21.ii.	Under National Animal Disease Control Programme (NADCP), an amount of altogether, Rs.38.34 Crore has been released to West Bengal during 2019-20 and 2020-21.iii.	Under National Programme for Dairy Development scheme, since inception, 3532 farmer-members have been benefitted in West Bengal till 30.06.2021. No project has been submitted by State Government of West Bengal for Darjeling, Kalingpong and North Dinajpur districts under the scheme.iv.	Under the erstwhile Dairy Entrepreneurship Development scheme (DEDS), altogether 204 SC/ST dairy farmers have been benefited with a subsidy amount of Rs 83.38 Lakh in Darjeeling, Kalimpong and North Dinajpur districts of West Bengal from 01.04.2014 to 14.07.2021.v.	Under the erstwhile Entrepreneurship Development and Employment Generation (EDEG) component of National Livestock Mission, altogether 3873 beneficiaries have been benefited with an amount of Rs.19.31 Crore in West Bengal during 2014-15 to 2020-21. District-wise data for SC/ ST beneficiaries is not available.vi.	Under Innovative Poultry Productivity Project for broilers under National Livestock Mission, altogether, 71 SC/ST beneficiaries with an amount of Rs 47.55 lakh have been assisted in West Bengal during 2017-18 & 2019-20.****
