                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1131






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Extension to PMUY




Geetha Viswanath Vanga













                                                    Sanjay Kumar Bandi
                                                








                                                    Manne Srinivas Reddy
                                                








                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                








                                                    Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Pradhan Mantri Ujjawala Yojana(PMUY) scheme is applicable nationwide andState-wise also and requires extensive dissemination, and propose for a fresh target to add 10million more PMUY beneficiaries to the existing 80 million, if so, the details thereof and progressmade, State wise as on date and present status thereof;(b) whether the Government is committed to create smoke free environment in rural areas and ifso, the number of Gas connections sanctioned and the number of beneficiaries under PMUY inthe State of Telangana for the last three years, year-wise and district-wise;(c) whether the Scheme has reached its objectives particularly in tribal areas of Telangana and ifnot, the reasons therefor; and(d) the measures the Government is going to take to implement the scheme in tribal areas?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (d) In order to provide clean and environment friendly cooking fuel to poor households,especially in rural areas, the Government had launched Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY)in May 2016 across the country including the tribal areas of Telangana. The primary objective ofthe PMUY is to provide access to clean cooking fuel LPG to poor households thereby protectingtheir health by reducing the serious health hazards associated with use of conventional cookingfuel such as firewood, coal, cow dung etc. which causes severe indoor household air pollution.Use of LPG as cooking fuel frees women from the drudgery of collecting firewood, reduces timespent on cooking and prevents deforestation. The Government has achieved the target todistribute 8 crore connections under the Scheme in September, 2019. District-wise details of LPGconnections released under PMUY in the Sate of Telangana during the last three years are givenat Annexure.Further, Hon''ble Finance Minister, during her budget speech on 1st February, 2021, made anannouncement to provide 1 crore more LPG connections under PMUY in addition to 8 crore LPGconnections already released. The modalities for releasing 1 crore LPG connections underPMUY have already been finalised and have been communicated to the Oil MarketingCompanies.------------
