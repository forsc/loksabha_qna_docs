                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1784






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






NIRDESH Project




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the current position of National Institute for Research and Development in Defence Shipbuilding (NIRDESH) project at Chaliyam in Kozhikode;(b) the plan proposed by the Government for expansion of the project; and(c) the time by which the project is proposed/likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a)  National Institute for Research and Development in Defence Shipbuilding (NIRDESH) is a society under Ministry of Defence, registered on 20th November, 2010 under Societies Registration Act XXI of 1860 with representatives of Indian Navy, Indian Coast Guard, Defence Research & Development Organisation, Mazagon Docks Limited, Garden Reach Shipbuilders & Engineers Limited, Goa Shipyard Limited and Hindustan Shipyard Limited as members.  NIRDESH has undertaken training, projects, seminars and technology meets in addition to projects with academia, that have resulted in proposals of 4 IPRs.(b)  Sir, No plan for expansion.(c)  Not Applicable. *****
