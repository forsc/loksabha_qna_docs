                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3935






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






ROBs in Ranchi and Dhanbad




Sunil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Road Over Bridges (ROBs) have been approved for construction by the Railways with the State Government’s funding under Ranchi and Dhanbad Railway Division in last four years;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c): Yes, Sir. 6 No. of works for construction of Road over Bridges (ROBs) on cost sharing basis with State Government has been approved/ sanctioned in Ranchi Division. Out of these, 4 No. of ROBs has been commissioned. 1 ROB work is in progress and for another ROB, drawing is under finalization in consultation with State Government of Jharkhand.7 No. of works for construction of Road over Bridges (ROBs) on cost sharing basis with State Government has been approved/ sanctioned in Dhanbad Division. Out of these, consent for sharing of cost in 4 No. of ROBs has not been received from State Government of Jharkhand. Remaining 3 No. of ROBs are at various stages of planning, estimation and execution.*****
