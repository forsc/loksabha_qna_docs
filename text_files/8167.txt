                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1712






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Water Connections




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is providing water connections to every household in the country at an estimated capital expenditure of over Rs. 3 lakh crore and is expected to create huge business opportunities for corporate and if so, the details thereof and progress made there in so far; and(b)	whether the investment in water infrastructure will spur growth and if so, the details thereof and steps being taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b)To enable every rural household to have potable water supply on regular basis in adequate quantity through Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) by 2024, Government of India is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) with an outlay of Rs. 3.60 lakh Crore.Details of State/ UT-wise fund allocation under JJM in 2019-2020 isannexed.In the rural areas, for developing in-village water supply infrastructure, water resource management, source strengthening/ augmentation, distribution network, treatment plant, etc., unskilled, semi-skilled and skilled human resource will be required. Further, materials forwater transfer, water treatment, storage, distribution network, etc. is also to be utilized, whichwill generate employment and boost the economy.
