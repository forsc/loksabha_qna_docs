                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

717






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Assistance to Sportspersons




Subhash Ramrao Bhamre













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    Manickam Tagore B
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has constituted a single selection committee for selecting awardees for various sports awards for the year 2020 and if so, the details thereof alongwith its composition;   (b) the number of persons in various categories who will be shortlisted to be given sports awards for the year 2020;   (c) whether many of the sportspersons after retirement are forced to live in poverty as there is no support from the Government and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;   (d) whether the Government will provide assistance for livelihood to these sportspersons after their retirement as they are suffering a lot due to covid-19 pandemic and if so, the details thereof; and   (e) the other steps taken by the Government to encourage more persons to take up sports as their career and offering them good financial remuneration during their active participation in the sports and after their retirement?  
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)  FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS [KIREN RIJIJU]  (a): Yes Sir. Department of Sports has constituted Selection Committee for selecting awardees for National Sports Awards 2020. The composition of the committee is  given below: <pre>S.No. Name of the Members Position 1 Justice Mukundakam Sharma, Retired Justice, Supreme court Chairperson 2 Shri VirenderSehwag (Cricket) Member 3 Shri Sardar Singh (Hockey) Member 4 Ms MonalisaBaruah Mehta (Table Tennis) Member 5 Ms Deepa Malik (Para Athletic) Member 6 Shri VenkatesanDevarajan (Boxing) Member 7 Shri Manish Batavia, Sports Commentator Member 8 Ms Neeru Bhatia, Sports Journalist Member 9 Shri Alok Sinha, Sports Journalist Member 10 Shri Sandip Pradhan, Director General, SAI Member 11 Commander (Retd.) Rajesh Rajagopalan CEO, TOPS Member 12 Shri L.S. Singh, Joint Secretary (Development) Member Secretary </pre>(b): The National Sports Awards ceremony 2020 was held on 29th August, 2020 virtually and following numbers of sportspersons/entities were awarded different sports awards. The details are given below: - <pre>S. No. Name of Sports Award No. of Sports Persons given Awards/entities 1 Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award 05 2 Dronacharya  Award (Regular Category) 05 3 Dronacharya Award (Life Time category) 08 4 Arjuna Award 27 5 Dhyan Chand Award 15 6 Rashtriya Khel Protsahan Puruskar Award 05 7 MaulanaAbulKalam Azad (MAKA) Trophy 01 </pre>(c) to (e): This Ministry is implementing the Scheme of ‘Pension to Meritorious Sportspersons’ for providing lifelong monthly pension to medal winners of Olympic Games, Asian Games, Commonwealth Games, etc. after they retire from active sports and the Scheme of ‘Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons’ (PDUNWFS)’ for providing financial assistance to sportspersons of yesteryears now living in indigent circumstances. Further, Government has enhanced the prize money of National Sports Awards recently i.e. for Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award from Rs 7.5 lakh to Rs 25 lakh, for Dronacharya Award (Regular Category) from Rs 5 lakh to Rs 10 lakh, for Dronacharya Award (Lifetime Category) from Rs 5 lakh to Rs 15 lakh, for Arjuna Award from Rs 5 lakh to Rs 15 lakh and for Dhyan Chand Award from Rs 5 lakh to Rs 10 lakh. Further, Cash award is given to the sportspersons for winning medals in international sports events under ‘The Special Awards to Medal winners in International Sports events and their Coaches Scheme’. There is provision of upto 5% reservation in direct recruitment in Group ‘C’ posts for meritorious sportspersons in Central Government. On this, recently, the Government has also increased the number of sports discipline from 43 to 63 in the list of games. Further under the National Sports Development Fund(NSDF), Special assistance is also provided for various sports promotion activities.   ****
