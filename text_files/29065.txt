Crime Against Children




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	The number of cases of crime against children recorded across the country, State/UT-wise including Assam;(b) Whether review of children homes/orphanages is being done at regular interval in Assam, if so, the details thereof; and(c) Whether the Government has received reports of atrocities committed on the inmates in such homes/ orphanages in Assam and if so, the details thereof along with the action taken thereon, district-wise? 






ANSWER




(a) to (c) As per the information provided by National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB),  state/UT-wise, (including Assam) cases registered, cases charge-sheeted, cases convicted, persons arrested, persons charge-sheeted and persons convicted under crime against children during 2018-2020 is at Annexure-I.    As per the information provided by National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB), the cases registered under Sexual Harassment in Shelter Homes for Women & Children etc. in the districts of Assam during 2018-2020 is at Annexure-II. National Commission for Protection of Child Rights (NCPCR) has conducted social audit of all Child Care Institutions (CCIs) across the country. The social audit reports and deficiencies found during the social audit in each CCI were provided to respective District Magistrates for their necessary action.
