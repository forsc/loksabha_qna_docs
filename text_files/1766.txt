                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1714






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






m-Kisan Portal




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the details of the services offered and information available on the m-Kisan portal;(b)  	the  total  number  of  farmers registered on the portal, State/UT-wise;(c) 	the number of SMSs sent to farmers since the launch of the portal; and(d)  	the  details  of  the  choice  of languages available on the portal?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):    Information and services available on mkisan portal i.e. https://mkisan.gov.in are crop specific advisories  to farmers through SMS and Voice;  Pull SMS service for farmers  to register themselves and give feedback on the service; Interactive Voice Response System service and extreme weather alert for farmers. (b):	 Details of State/UT-wise farmers registered on mkisan portal are given at annexure.   (c):     24,62,37,10,138 SMSs have been sent to the farmers since launch of this portal  on 25.05.2013 till 28.06.2019. (d): 	Advisories to the farmers registered on this portal, can be sent in 12 languages viz.,  Bengali, Gujarati, Hindi, Kannada, Malayalam, Marathi, Oriya, Punjabi, Tamil, Telugu, Urdu and English.
