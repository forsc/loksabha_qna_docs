                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3135






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Housing for Homeless People




Khagen Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is assessing the availability of houses for the homeless people and taking steps to provide temporary shelter and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the number of card holder people living below poverty line in Malda district of West Bengal have been allotted permanent/ temporary shelter during the last three years; and(c)	the details of the different amenities provided to the BPL card holder beneficiaries by the States and Union Territories including West Bengal?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS				(SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)&(b)	Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) (PMAY(U)) provides central assistance to implementing agencies through States/ Union Territories (UTs) and Central Nodal Agencies (CNAs) for providing all weather pucca houses to all eligible families/ beneficiaries including beneficiaries of Economically Weaker Section (EWS) category in urban areas of the country with basic amenities like water, electricity and toilets etc. As per demand survey conducted under the scheme, State/UTs have reported a validated demand of 1.12 crore houses. The number of houses sanctioned and completed in West Bengal State and Malda District is as follows:-Particulars	                                     West Bengal             Malda DistrictOverall	Houses sanctioned (nos.)	               4,11,876	                 5,539	House completed (nos.)	                       1,89,579	                 2,842During last three years	Houses sanctioned (nos.)        2,72,573	         3,339	House completed (nos.)	                        1,27,465	         2,089	The EWS beneficiaries include BPL in view of income criteria for EWS, but no separate data is reported/ maintained specifically for BPL beneficiaries.	Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs is administering a scheme ‘Shelter for Urban Homeless’ (SUH) under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM), through respective States/UTs to provide permanent shelters to the urban homeless. During last three years, 13 shelters have been made functional in West Bengal. Out of which, one shelter having capacity of 50 persons has been made functional in Malda District.(c)	No such information is available with Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs.                                            *****
