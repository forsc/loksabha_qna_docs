                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2477






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






Stoppage of Trains at Latehar




Sunil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether train numbers 18635-18636, 18310-18311, 12874-12873 and 13025-13026 had stoppage at Latehar railway station but presently these trains are not stopping at this station; (b) if so, the reasons to discontinue stoppage of these trains at the said station; and (c) the time by which stoppage of these trains is likely to start at the said station?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (c)  Latehar station is presently being adequately served by 09 pairs of train services including 18310 Jammu Tawi-Sambalpur Express.  The average number of passengers and earnings, at Latehar, for 18635/18636 Ranchi-Sasaram Express, 18311/18312 Sambalpur-Varanasi Express, 12873/12874 Hatia-Anand Vihar Jharkhand Express and 13025/13026 Howrah-Bhopal Express is less than the norms prescribed in the extant policy guidelines relating to provision of stoppages. *****
