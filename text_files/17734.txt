                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1407






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Organic Farming




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has chalked out any plan to promote organic farming and to discourage use of chemical fertilizers in farming; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the details of the total area in which organic farming is being done in the country, State-wise including Haryana; and (d) 	the number of farmers undertaking organic farming and the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Government of India has been encouraging organic farming in the country   through the dedicated schemes namely Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)and Mission Organic Value Chain Development of North East Region (MOVCDNER). Both the schemes aim at promotion of cluster/ Farmers Producer Organization (FPO) based chemical free, low input cost, sustainable organic farming and support farmers from input procurement to market linkages. Organic Farming has also been supported under other schemes viz. Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) and Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH), Network Project on Organic Farming under Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR). Third party certification of organic farming is promoted by Agriculture Processed Food and Export Development Authority (APEDA), Ministry of Commerce. The pattern of Assistance is given at Annexure l.(c) & (d):	As on date, 29.17 lakh hectares is covered  under organic farming (both National programme of Organic Produce - NPOP) and Participatory guarantee System - PGS) . The  state-wise details of area covered under organic farming including Haryana is  given  at Annexure ll.   As of now,   29.1  lakh farmers  including 13.63 lakh  under NPOP (including 0.83 of MOVCDNER) and 15.47 lakh farmers under PKVY/PGS are undertaking organic farming.
