                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2572






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Number of IAS/IPS Officers




Pratima Bhoumik














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any data with regard to the IAS and IPS presently in service and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise;(b) 	whether the Government has any data with regard to the departmental complaint/cases registered against IAS/IPS during the last five years (including financial and criminal cases); (c) 	if so, the State and UT-wise details of last five years thereof along with the action taken by the Government against the guilty officials; and (d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a):   The  cadre-wise Total Authorized Strength of IAS and IPS (as on 01.01.2020) along with officers in service is given at Annexures  “A” and “B” respectively.(b):  In respect of IAS officers, the data relating to number of complaints State-wise is not maintained centrally.  However, the total number of complaints against IAS officers received by the Central Government during last five years is as under:  S.No.	Year	Number of complaints received against IAS officers1.	2015-16	3802.	2016-17	4843.	2017-18	6234.	2018-19	6435.	2019-20	7536.	2020-2021(01.04.2020 to 28.02.2021)	581         In respect of IPS, as per information received from Ministry of Home Affairs, consolidated data on the complaints received is not maintained centrally.  Contd.P.-2/--2-         Further, as per information received from Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI), the total number of cases registered against IAS and IPS officers during last five years is as under:Year	IAS	IPS2016	2	-2017	8	72018	11	12019	20	-2020	2	4                  2021               (till  date)	1	-(c) & (d):  In terms of AIS (D&A) Rules, 1969, if at the time of misconduct, the officer was serving under the State Government, State Government concerned is the authority competent to take departmental action against an IAS/IPS. If at  the time of misconduct the officer was serving under the Central Government, action against him is taken by the concerned cadre controlling authorities viz Ministry of Home Affairs for IPS and Department of Personnel & Training for IAS.         The total number of cases, for the last five years, where Central Government has taken various actions against the guilty officers is as under:Penalty	IAS	IPSProsecution Sanctions	26	3Dismissal from Service	2	2Compulsory Retirement	1	1Reduction to Lower Stage of Pay	-	1Cut in Pensions	2	10Withholding of  Pension	11	-Censure	1	-*****
