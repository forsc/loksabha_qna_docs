                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4716






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Production of Iron Ore




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether efforts are being made by the Government to enhance the production of iron ore in the country;(b)	if so, the steps taken by the Government in this regard during the last three years and the details and the outcome thereof; and(c)	the State-wise details of the opened and closed steel plants during the said period along with the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)&(b): The Government has notified Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 2015 to streamline grant of mining leases in order to maintain sufficient availability of iron ore for the steel sector. To ensure supply of raw material by timely auction of leases expiring in 2020 under section 8A (5) of the MMDR Act, the Central Government has amended the Mineral Conservation and Development Rules, 2017, making it mandatory for the lease holders to carry out G2 level exploration over the entire mineralized area by 1st April, 2019. The State Governments are empowered to carry out auction of mineral blocks under the extant act/rules. As on date, 20 blocks of iron ore have been successfully auctioned with a reserve of 583.057 million tonnes.(c)	State-wise details of opened and closed steel plants during last three years is at Annexure.********
