                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

199






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Vedic Board




S. Muniswamy













                                                    Manne Srinivas Reddy
                                                








                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                








                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) Whether the Government has any plan to introduce new Board for vedic studies;(b) If so, the necessary steps taken by the Government in this regard; and(c) by when the Government is planning to introduce it?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION( SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (c): The Central Government has only two School Boards namely Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and National Institute of Open Schooling (NIOS). However, Maharshi Sandipani Rashtriya Ved Vidya Pratisthan (MSRVVP), Ujjain, an autonomous body under the Ministry of Education, has given permission for a private Board for blended education including both vedic studies and modern subjects. Maharshi Rashtriya Ved Vidya Pratisthan (MSRVVP), Ujjain is also considering setting up another Board for oral vedic traditions with modern subjects.*****
