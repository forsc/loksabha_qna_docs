                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1770






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Funds Released under PMAY




Durga Prasad Rao Balli













                                                    Srinivas Kesineni
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of funds released to the State Government of Andhra Pradesh under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)during the last four years;(b)	the names of districts covered under the scheme in Andhra Pradesh; and(c)	the number of houses constructed/likely to be constructed in Krishna district of Andhra Pradesh under this scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The funds released to the State of Andhra Pradesh under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana – Gramin (PMAY-G) and Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY-U) during 2015-16, 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19 is as below:Year	              Central Assistance released 	      Central Assistance sanctioned                        under PMAY-G (Rs. Crore)           for release under PMAY-U (Rs. Crore)  2015-16	                   281.13*	                                   1,159.832016-17	                   217.13	                                       4.452017-18	                   351.93	                                   2,971.652018-19	                   186.05	                                   6,153.25        *: Released under Indira Awaas Yojna(b):	All districts  of Andhra Pradesh are covered under PMAY – G. Under PMAY-U, actual demand of housing is assessed after a demand survey is undertaken by the States / UTs.(c):	As on 28th June 2019, a total of 5540 houses have been constructed in the Krishna district of Andhra Pradesh under PMAY - G.*****
