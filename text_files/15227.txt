                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3910






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






PMGSY in Chhattisgarh




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of additional projects under consideration of the Government for approval under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in Chhattisgarh during the current year;(b) 	the details of the ongoing projects under PMGSY in Raj nandgaon and Kabirdham districts of Chhattisgarh during the last two years; and(c) 	the details of the total funds allocated and spent in the said districts during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a)        The State of Chhattisgarh has been allocated a road length of 5,612.50 Km under phase-III of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY). The first batch of proposals of 355 roads of 3,729.17 km and 10 Bridges of 580.58 m have already been sanctioned to the State. The State has now submitted batch-II of proposals of 207 roads of 2,214.14 Km and 8 Bridges of 726.10 m under PMGSY-III . The balance eligibility of the State vis-à-vis allocated length is only 1,883.33 Km.
