                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

210






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






KCC under Atma Nirbhar Bharat Package




Dushyant Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government plans to provide Kisan Credit Card (KCC) to 2.5 crore farmers under Atma Nirbhar Bharat package; (b) 	if so, the details of the number of farmers who have been given these credit cards under Atma Nirbhar Bharat package, State-wise; (c) 	the details of the amount of loan availed by farmers who have been given these cards under Atma Nirbhar Bharat package, State-wise; (d)	 the size of land-holding of the farmers to whom these cards have been distributed under Atma Nirbhar Bharat package; (e) 	the number of agriculture credit agencies available in districts in which more than 50 of these cards have been distributed under Atma Nirbhar Bharat package; and (f) 	whether the target of 2.5 crore cards include the 1.5 crore dairy farmers covered under the Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		 	  (SHRI NARENDRA  SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (f):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (f) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.  210    DUE FOR REPLY ON 9TH MARCH, 2021.(a) to (c): 	Yes Sir. Under the Atma Nirbhar Bharat (ANB) package, the target is to provide KCC to 2.5 crore new farmers. Around 1.96 crore KCC have been sanctioned to farmers under ANB package. The state-wise details of the same are at Annexure.  The details of the amount of loan availed by farmers who have been given these cards under Atma Nirbhar Bharat package, State-wise is not available. However, loans are availed by the farmers as per the KCC limit sanctioned to them based on scale of finance for the crop under cultivation, post harvest/household/consumption requirements, maintenance expenses of farm assets, as also working capital requirement for animal husbandry, dairy and fisheries. Total amount of Rs. 1.87 lakh crore credit limits has been sanctioned which works out to an average sanctioned limit of around 0.95 lakh per farmer.(d): 	All farmers irrespective of the size of land, practicing Crop Husbandry (including sharecroppers and tenant farmers) Animal Husbandry, Dairying and Fisheries are eligible for KCC. (e): 	All the Scheduled Commercial Banks (SCBs), Cooperative Banks and Regional Rural Banks can issue KCC to the eligible farmers. Farmers can also apply through Common Service Centers (CSCs) for KCC.(f): 	The target set under Atma Nirbhar Bharat package includes farmers practicing Crop Husbandry, Animal Husbandry, Dairying& Fisheries.
