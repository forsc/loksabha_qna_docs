                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

78






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Development  of  Inland  Waterways




Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the current status of the development of the 106 newly declared National Inland Waterways;(b)	the funds allocated for the waterways in the country including Andhra Pradesh and the amount that has already been released for the purpose so far; and (c)	the status of implementation of the action plan for inland waterways in Andhra Pradesh in 2020?






ANSWER




                                                           (a) to (c)	  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. Statement referred to in reply to parts (a) to (c) of Lok Sabha *Starred Question No.*78 for 06.02.2020 raised by Shri Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy, Hon’ble M.P. on Development of Inland Waterways  (a)	Based on the outcome of techno-economic feasibility and Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) for the 106 new National Waterways (NWs) undertaken by the Inland Waterways Authority of India (IWAI), 20 NWs have been found technically feasible for shipping and navigation.   Development activities have been initiated on 10 new NWs as detailed at Annex.-1.(b)	To enable IWAI to undertake development of NWs, grants are released to it by the Government. IWAI was also authorized to raise Extra Budgetary Resources (EBR) of Rs.1000 crore as fully serviced, Government of India Bonds.
