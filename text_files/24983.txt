UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

736






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Smart Phones/Tablets for Anganwadi Workers




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a).     whether any scheme is being implemented/likely to be implemented by the Government regarding provision of smart phones/tablets for Anganwadi workers; (b)      if so, the details thereof and the manner in which the smart phones/tablets are likely to be beneficial for Anganwadi workers/helpers; and (c)       the details of the smart phones/tablets provided to the Anganwadi workers under the scheme, State-wise including Chhattisgarh? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) & (b) Under POSHAN Abhiyaan, AWWs are empowered with the provision of Smartphones procured through Government e-Market (GeM) for efficient service delivery.  In addition, States/UTs are advised to introduce transparent processes for procurement of mobile phones as per GFR and vigilance guidelines.  A total of 8.66 lakhs Smartphones have been procured by 32 States/UTs.The mobile application of POSHAN Abhiyaan digitizes and automates physical registers used by Aanganwadi workers. This saves the time of AWWs and AWHs, and improves the quality of their work while simultaneously allowing them real-time monitoring.(c)        The details of the Smartphones procured by States/UTs including Chhattisgarh under Poshan Abhiyaan is  Annexed.
