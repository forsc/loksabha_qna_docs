                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

384






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Pending Payments of Procurements




Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that lot of payments to several States are pending against procurement of food grains; (b) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise ; (c) 	whether the Government has set any target for the procurement of food grains in the country; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise and items-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a):	Payments are being made to State Government on regular basis against procurement of foodgrains.
