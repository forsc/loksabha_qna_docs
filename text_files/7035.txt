                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

790






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Assessment of Scholarship  Schemes for Minority Students




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) the total number of students who received pre-matric, post-matric andMerit-cum-means scholarships during the last three years, year-wise;(b) the total amount spent for giving scholarships to the students; and(c) whether the Government has revised the amount of scholarship given to each student and if so, the detailed response thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                 MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS                   (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a) & (b):	As per the latest information available on the National Scholarship Portal, the details of number of students awarded Scholarships under Pre-Matric, Post-Matric and Merit-cum-Means Scholarship Schemes of Ministry of Minority Affairs during 2016-17 to 2018-19 and the expenditure incurred thereon are as below:                                            In Rs. Crore)Scheme	Pre-Matric	Post-Matric	Merit-cum-MeansYear	Scholarship awarded	Amount Sanctioned/released	Scholarship awarded	Amount Sanctioned/released 	Scholarship awarded	Amount Sanctioned/released2016-17	4153524	749.43	624990	361.43	121937	323.792017-18	5311257	1078.42	698069	413.84	119472	324.862018-19*	5691854	1264.29	684265	423.18	117771	315.93* Provisional data; disbursement continues during 2019-20.(c):	No, Sir. The amount of Scholarship has not been revised.	                                *****
