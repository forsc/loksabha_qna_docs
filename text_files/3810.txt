                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3564






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Shifting of NIT




Tirath Singh Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that the land for construction ofpermanent complex for the proposed National Institute of Technology(NIT) in Paudi district, Uttarakhand run in the ITI campus, Srinagar,temporarily has been selected in village Sumaadi;(b) whether the Government has any proposal to shift the Institute at someother place and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government is formulating any action plan to construct theproposed institute at village Sumaadi, Pauri Garhwal; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): Yes Sir, Government of Uttarakhand has allotted land in village Sumaadifor the establishment of permanent campus of NIT Uttarakhand.(b): No such proposal is under consideration in the Ministry of HumanResource Development.(c) & (d): Plan for construction of permanent campus at Sumaadi for housingNIT Uttarakhand is under process.*******
