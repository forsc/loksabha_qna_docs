                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1998






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Janani Suraksha Yojana




Mahabali Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be please to state:(a)	Whether Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY) is being implemented across the country including North Eastern States;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with incentive provided to the eligible beneficiaries in the rural and urban areas particularly in Bihar: and(c)	the amount allocated to the said scheme during the last three years along with the number of persons benefitted there from?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	& (b)Yes sir. JSY is being implemented across the country including North Eastern States. JSY incentive provided to the eligible beneficiaries is different in ‘Low Performing States’ and ‘High Performing States’. The State of Bihar falls under Low Performing State and the details of JSY incentive provided to the eligible beneficiaries in Bihar are as follows:Rural area (Rs.)	Urban area (Rs.)	EligibilityFinancial Assistance for Institutional Delivery1400	1000	Available to all women regardless of age and number of children for delivery in government /private accredited health facilities.Financial Assistance for Home Delivery500	500	Available only to BPL women who prefer to deliver at home regardless of age and number of children(c) Allocation of funds for JSY scheme is done by National Health Mission (NHM) under RCH Flexible Pool. There is no separate fund allocation for Janani Suraksha Yojana. Details of financial support in the form of funds provided under NHM and number of beneficiaries benefitted at the national level during the last three years is as follows:Financialyear	State Program Implementation Plan Approval(Rs in Crore)	JSY Beneficiaries(Number in Lakh)2018-19	1976.23	100.412019-20	2032.25	107.352020-21(provisional)	2113.31	99.92***
