Cases of Gold Smuggling




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of increased instances of gold smuggling in the country especially in Kerala;(b) if so; the details of gold seized between 2016 to 2020 in Kerala;(c) whether the Government has taken note of shortage of officers in the Customs department leading to failure in vigilance and efficacy; (d) if so, the details of staff and vacancies remaining unfulfilled in CBIC department till date, region-wise with specific reference to Kerala; and(e) whether the Government intends to call for a direct recruitment drive for filling vacancies in the Customs Department and if so, the details thereof?
