                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4195






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Institute Development Plan




Bidyut Baran Mahato













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has asked the IITs to develop Institute Development Plan  envisioned in the NEP 2020;(b)	if so, the details thereof(c)	whether the mobility of faculty members and industry experts between technical institute and industry will promote collaborations between industry and academia and if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether the Government proposes to constitute a task force to review use of technology at all IITs and also to accelerate deployment of digital tools in the country and if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the details of the contribution of the academics during the challenging times of COVID-19 and for their contribution in combating the COVID-19 by new scientific research?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a)	to (d): These issues were discussed as agenda items in the 54th meeting of Council of IITs held on 22.02.2021.(e) : IITs have contributed in combating COVID-19 by way of various scientific researches in the area of Personal Protective Care Equipment, Testing Kit, Sanitization, Medical Equipment/ Robots, Surveillance, Treatment and Data Analytics etc.  *****
