                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3862






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Recast of PMS




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has decided to recast the Post-Matric Scholarship (PMS) scheme for Scheduled Castes (SCs) and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government is going to change the fund sharing formula; (c) if so, the details thereof; (d) whether it is true that there are as many as Rs. 4,000 crore dues to the States; and (e) if so, the details of the arrears to be given to Andhra Pradesh and the time by which the Government is going to clear its dues?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c): Yes, Sir.  The Government has formulated a proposal for revision in funding pattern under the Post-Matric Scholarship Scheme for SC students from the concept of ‘Committed Liability’ to ‘a fixed sharing ratio between the Centre and the States/UTs’.(d): No, Sir. The bulk of arrears accumulated at the end of 12th Plan Period and due for 2017-18 have been released to the States/UTs already except three States i.e. Punjab, Gujarat and Goa due to non-receipt of enabling documents from these States.(e): Funds amounting to Rs. 143.98 crore, Rs.317.43 crore and Rs. 90 crore have been released to the State of Andhra Pradesh during 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19 respectively. Thus, all dues of the State under PMS-SC scheme up to 2017-18 have been released.The dues against the expenditure incurred by the State during 2018-19 under the Scheme could not be released due to non-receipt of requisite information from the State.****
