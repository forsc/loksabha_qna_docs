                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

634






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






GramNet Project




Sanjay Seth













                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Chandra Prakash Choudhary
                                                








                                                    Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is planning to provide Wi-Fi in all the villages across the country through GramNet; (b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; (c) 	the number of villages where the Government has already provided the aforementioned services, State/UT-wise; and (d) 	whether any private agencies are involved as intermediaries or implementing agencies in the process and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) to (c)	BharatNet project is being implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity to all the Gram Panchayats (approx. 2,50,000) in the country. As part of BharatNet project, the Last Mile connectivity, through Wi-Fi or any other suitable broadband access technology, is to be provided in all Gram Panchayats.The State/UT-wise details of Gram Panchayat where Wi-Fi Services have been provided are attached as Annex-I.(d)	In the 1.23 lakh GPs (approx.) of BharatNet Phase-I, the provisioning of Wi-Fi Services has been assigned to Government of Rajasthan, CSC e-Governance Services India Limited(a Special Purpose Vehicle under Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology), etc. In the 1407 GPs of BharatNet Phase-II, the provisioning of connectivity over satellite media, along with Wi-Fi services, has been assigned to BSNL.
