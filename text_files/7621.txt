                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1359






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Records of Hindu Temples




Gaurav Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has maintained records of the number of Hindu temples under control of State Governments in India;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has maintained records of the number of Hindu temples functioning autonomously in India;(d)	 if so, the details thereof;(e)	whether religious institutions of any other faith are controlled, managed, or administered by State Governments; and(f) if not, the reason for this inconsistency?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) No Sir. However, there are 651 numbers of Hindu Temples which are also Centrally Protected Monuments, and thereby under the jurisdiction of the ASI. The State-wise no of such Hindu Temples is as under:-Name of State	No. of ASI protected Hindu Temples	Name of State	No. of ASI protected Hindu TemplesTamil Nadu	111	Jharkhand	1Chattisgarh	5	Uttar Pradesh	24Karnataka	149	Bihar	8Maharashtra	41	Orrisa	15Andhra Pradesh	91	West Bengal	2Kerala	39	Goa	1Rajasthan	30	J&K	8Madhya Pradesh	96	Himachal Pradesh	6Gujarat	16	Haryana	2Uttarakhand	5	Delhi	1As regards the information relating to the list of the Hindu temples under the control of State Government, the issue doesn’t relate to ASI.(b)  to (f) Does not arise in view of (a) above.
