                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2051






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Maharatna Status to BHEL




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd. (BHEL) is included in the list of Maharatna Companies;(b) if so, the details and number of branches of BHEL in the country and abroad, location-wise;(c) the total number of employees working in BHEL; and (d) the annual turnover of BHEL?






ANSWER




                                                                               THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES                                       (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a): Yes, Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) has been granted ‘Maharatna’ status vide Department of Public Enterprises (DPE)’s Office Memorandum No. 22(1)/2009-GM dated 1st February, 2013.b): The details in respect of number of branches and locations of BHEL are given below as Annexure.c): The total number of regular employees working in BHEL as on 31-12-2019 was 34,136.(d): The financial turnover of BHEL during last two years is as follows :BHEL	2017-18 	2018-19Turnover (Rs. Crore)	27,850	29,349AnnexureMajor locations and number from where business activity is undertaken by BHELNational locations (in India)	i)	Registered & Corporate Office : New Delhi.ii)	Manufacturing Units/Divisions (16 nos.) : Bengaluru (3 Units), Bhopal, Goindwal, Haridwar (2 Units), Hyderabad, Jagdishpur, Jhansi, Ranipet, Rudrapur, Thirumayam, Tiruchirappalli (2 Units) and Visakhapatnam.iii)	Corporate R&D Division: Hyderabad.iv)	Repair Units/Centres (2 nos.) : Mumbai and Varanasi.v)	Regional Offices - Power Sector (4 nos.) : Noida, Kolkata, Nagpur and Chennai.vi)	Service Centres (8 nos.) : Chandigarh, Kolkata, Nagpur, Noida, Patna, Secunderabad, Vadodara and Varanasi.vii)	Regional Marketing Offices (15 nos.) : Bengaluru, Bhubaneswar, Chandigarh, Chennai, Guwahati, Jabalpur, Jaipur, Kolkata, Lucknow, Mumbai, New Delhi, Raipur, Ranchi, Secunderabad and Vadodara.viii)	Other business offices : Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, New Delhi, Noida.ix)	Customer project sites : spread across the country. International locations (abroad)	Dubai (UAE), Dhaka (Bangladesh), Damascus (Syria) ;Major site/local offices : Rampal (Bangladesh), Wangdue Phodrang (Bhutan), Trongsa (Bhutan).
