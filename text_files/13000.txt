                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1913






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Drug Free Campaign




Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 1913TO BE ANSWERED ON 03.03.2020DRUG FREE CAMPAIGN 1913. SHRI MANOJ KOTAK: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether it is a fact that the Government has started “drug-free” campaign; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has made a list of districts which are vulnerable from the perspective of drug trafficking; (d) if so, the details thereof; and (e) the details of funds allocated for “drug-free” campaign?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT( SHRI  RATTAN  LAL  KATARIA)(a)	& (b) :National Institute of Social Defence (NISD) under the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment has conducted Drug free campaign with various statkeholders and Government functionaries.
