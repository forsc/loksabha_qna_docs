                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

83






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






PMGSY in Bihar




Ajay Kumar Mandal













                                                    Chandeshwar Prasad
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of roads constructed in kilometers under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in Bihar, particularly in Jehanabad, Arwal and Gaya Districts;(b) 	the quantum of funds sanctioned and released for the projects along with the status of their completion;(c) 	whether roads are being planned/ approved/under consideration for the upcoming projects in the State; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)& (b)   So far under various interventions/verticals of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY),  a total of  53,269 km of road length has   been constructed in the state of Bihar.  Further the total value of projects (VoP) sanctioned to state  is Rs. 34,651 crore  against which a sum of      Rs 27,564 crore including State Share of Rs 4,698 crore has been released so far.  A sum of             Rs 27,018.20 crore has been spent so far by the State Government out of the fund released.  The details relating to value of projects sanctioned, road length constructed and expenditure made against different verticals of PMGSY in Jehanabad, Arwal and Gaya districts are as under:- (Road length in km and Exp. in Crore) # Road Connectivity Project for Left Wing Extremism Affected Areas Information in the above table as per programme Online Management Monitoring and Accounting System (OMMAS). (c) & (d) The Government approved PMGSY-III in  July, 2019 for consolidation of 1,25,000 Km Through Routes and Major Rural Links connecting habitations, inter-alia, to Gramin Agricultural Markets (GrAMs), Higher Secondary Schools and Hospitals. A target length of 6162.50 km has been allocated to Bihar under PMGSY-III. Further, the State has been allocated additional road length of 220 Km under RCPLWEA.  The State Government has been asked to prepare detailed project reports for their allocated target under PMGSY-III and RCPLWEA.  ******
