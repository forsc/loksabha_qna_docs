                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

587






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






China’s Stand on Sikkim




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Ministry is aware of recent reports in official media of China wherein China supports independent country for Sikkim;(b)	if so, the details of steps taken in this regard;(c) 	the extent to which the Gurkha land agitation is creating problems for Sikkim and the manner in which the Ministry is planning to address the same; and(d) 	the other problems that Sikkim is facing and the way in which Ministry is going to resolve them with the help of DoNER and other Ministries?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a)	We are not aware of any such recent reports of China. It is a well known fact that Sikkim is a state of India and is an integral and inalienable part of India. (b)	Does not arise.(c) & (d) 	As regards any problem faced by the Government of Sikkim, it coordinates with the relevant State Governments. The Ministry of Home Affairs also coordinates with the State Governments as required. As regards other issues that the State of Sikkim may face, coordination is undertaken with relevant Ministries of the Government of India, including Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region (DoNER) as appropriate.***
