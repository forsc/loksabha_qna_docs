                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2626






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Welfare of Weavers




Sumalatha Ambareesh













                                                    Pratima Mondal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has put any mechanism in place for timely review of the schemes being run for the welfare of the weavers in the country;(b) if so the time by which the review of the welfare schemes would conclude along with the highlights of review of the weavers schemes; (c) whether the Government has taken any measures for further improvement of the schemes for weavers based on the review highlights; (d) if so, the details thereof; and (e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (d):	In accordance with the instructions issued by Ministry of Finance (Department of Expenditure), Ministry of Textiles has conducted evaluation studies through independent agencies for smooth implementation of the welfare and developmental Schemes for handloom weavers across the country. The third party evaluation studies of the schemes have revealed increase in working days, productivity and wages of the handloom weavers. The suitable suggestions if any, made by evaluating agencies for further improvement of schemes are incorporated.  Further, the Government of India is also conducting the third  party evaluation studies through independent agencies to review the following schemes being implemented during the last two years and current financial year:-i)	Handloom Weavers’ Comprehensive Welfare Scheme (HWCWS)ii)	National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP)iii)	Comprehensive Handloom Cluster Development Scheme (CHCDS)iv)	Yarn Supply Scheme (YSS)(e):	Does not arise.****
