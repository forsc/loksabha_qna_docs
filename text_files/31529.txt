                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2693






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Remunerative Price for Agricultural Produce




Amar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether farmers received remunerative price for their produce under the agricultural price policy keeping in view the COVID-19 crises during each of the last two years and current year:(b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise, especially Punjab, and if not, the reason therefor;(c)	whether the Price Support Scheme (PSS) for procurement of oilseed, pulses and cotton has been implemented across the country; (d)	if so, the details of procurement of farmers’ produce under the said scheme when the prices of the products fall below the Minimum Support Price (MSP)during the said period; and  (e) 	the details of alternate corrective steps taken by the Government to provide adequate price to farmers for their produce?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ??????? ?????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b): 	During the Covid-19 pandemic, the farmers of the country have proved that they are the backbone of our society. During the lockdown, the agriculture sector functioned smoothly. The lockdown was announced on 24.03.2020 and within three days, farming and allied activities including procurement were exempted from the lock down. The details of procurement of pulses, oilseeds & copra, wheat, paddy, coarse grains & cotton  at Minimum Support Price (MSP) (including Punjab) since 2019-20 are at Annexure-I,II,III,IV& V respectively.(c) & (d):   Yes sir. Government of India implements Price Support Scheme (PSS) under the umbrella scheme of PM-AASHA for procurement of Fair Average Quality (FAQ) of pulses, oilseeds& copra at Minimum Support Price (MSP) through Central Nodal Agencies after proposals are received from the State/UT Government.The detail of procurement of pulses, oilseeds & copra at MSP under PSS since 2019-20 is at annexure-I.(e):	Procurement centres are opened by respective State Government Agencies and Central Nodal Agencies like NAFED, FCI etc after taking into account the production, marketable surplus, convenience of farmers and availability of other logistics/ infrastructure such as storage and transportation etc. large number of the purchase centres in addition to the existing Mandis and depots/godowns are also established at key points for the convenience of farmers.
