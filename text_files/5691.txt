                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5169






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






National Mineral List




Kapil Moreshwar Patil














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the different mineral reserves, mineral and State/UT-wise; (b)	whether any project has been begun/proposed to begin to prepare National Mineral list of different  minerals in the country; (c)	if so, the present status thereof; (d)	the time by which it is likely to be completed; and (e) 	the details of funds sanctioned/ utilised for this purpose during each of the 	last 	three years, State/UT-wise ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a) to (e): National Mineral Inventory (NMI) of mineral reserves/resources in the country is prepared by Indian Bureau of Mines (a subordinate office of Ministry of Mines) and it is available on their official website (https://ibm.gov.in/). NMI is updated every 5 years by IBM based on the information received from the State Governments, exploration agencies and other stakeholders. NMI contains information of mineral-wise and State-wise reserves/ resources in the country.  NMI was last updated on 01.04.2015 covering 71 minerals. *****
