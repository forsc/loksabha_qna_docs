                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1203






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Setting up of Heavy Industries




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any heavy industry has been set up in Jaunpur district of Uttar Pradesh;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government is considering to set up a heavy industry suitable to the area keeping in view the abundance of skilled and unskilled labour force in the said area; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) to (d):	Since Industry is a State subject, no central data regarding establishment of heavy industries in any part of the country is maintained by Ministry of Heavy Industries. Ministry of Heavy Industries has not established any heavy industry in Jaunpur district of Uttar Pradesh and also there is no proposal in this regard.-----------
