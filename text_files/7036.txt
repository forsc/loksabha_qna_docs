                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

803






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Welfare Schemes for Minorities




Nusrat Jahan Ruhi














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                       (a)   the details of schemes implemented and the funds allocated for the welfare 	   of minority 	communities during the last three years, year-wise; and    (b)   the ratio of the benefit obtained by the minority communities viz., Muslims, 	   Christians, 	Sikhs, Buddhists, Jains and Parsis?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	Ministry is implementing the following schemes/ Programmes in the countryfor the welfare of six centrally notified minorities, namely, Muslims, Christians, Sikhs, Buddhists, Jains and Parsis. (1)  Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme (2)  Post-Matric Scholarship Scheme (3)  Merit-cum-Means based Scholarship Scheme Since 2015, the scholarship schemes are being implemented through DBT mode to improve the efficiency and bring transparency by removing duplicity and stopping pilferages.(4)  Maulana Azad National Fellowship (5)  Free coaching and Allied Scheme (6)  “Padho Pardes” – Scheme of Interest Subsidy on Educational Loans for     Overseas Studies.(7)  Support for students clearing Prelims conducted by UPSC, SSC, State  Public Service Commission (PSC) etc. (Nai Udaan)  (8)   Jiyo Parsi (9)   Nai Roshni – Leadership development of minority women to instill           	       confidence. (10)  Seekho Aur Kamao – Skill Development Initiatives (11)  Nai Manzil - A World Bank assisted scheme for formal school education    & skilling of school dropouts.(12)  USTTAD (Upgrading the Skills and Training in Traditional Arts/Crafts for Development) by providing a platform to traditional Master Artisans and Craft persons for getting opportunities and market by organising of Hunar Haats. (13)  Hamari Dharohar - A scheme to preserve rich heritage of Indian Minority    Communities under the overall concept of Indian Culture. (14)   Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakaram (PMJVK) - implemented in Identified Minority Concentration Blocks, Minority Concentration Towns & Clusters of Contiguous Villages for creation of basic infrastructural assets. (15)   Schemes implemented by Maulana Azad Education Foundation (MAEF) 	(a) Begum Hazrat Mahal National Scholarship for Meritorious Girls belonging to the six centrally notified Minorities namely, Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Buddhist, Jain and Parsi.	(b) Gharib Nawaz Employment and Training Programme is currently being implemented effectively for providing short-term job oriented skill development courses to youths belonging to minority communities. (16) National Minorities Development and Finance Corporation (NMDFC) releases funds for concessional loans and income generating ventures through the State Channelizing Agencies. 	The funds allocated for the welfare of Minority communities during last three years, is annexed.(b) The ratio of benefit extended to Muslims, Christians, Sikhs, buddhists, Jains and Parsi communities under schemes is not normally maintained by this Ministry.
