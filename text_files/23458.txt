                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4390






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Socio Economic and Caste Census




Sumalatha Ambareesh













                                                    Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that Socio Economic and Caste Census (SECC) 2011 was conducted and the raw caste data has been handed over to the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment for classification and categorization of the same;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has completed the classification and categorization of the raw caste data; and(d)	if so, the details thereof along with the major findings of the data, State-wise;






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b) : 	Socio Economic and Caste Census (SECC) 2011 was conducted by the Ministry of Rural Development (MoRD) and  the  then  Ministry of Housing and Urban Poverty Alleviation (HUPA) in rural and urban areas respectively.  The SECC 2011 data excluding the caste data have been finalized and published by MoRD and HUPA. The Office of the Registrar General, India had provided logistics and  technical support in conducting the SECC-2011. The raw caste data has been provided to the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment (MoSJE) for classification and categorization of the data. -2-LS.USQ. NO.4390 FOR 23.03.2021(c) & (d) :	As informed by MoSJE, there is no proposal to classify and categorize the raw caste data.*****
