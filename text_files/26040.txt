                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1800






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Registered MSME Units




Bache Gowda B.N. 














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details and the number of MSME units registered in the country so far, State-wise; (b)	the number of MSME units working in the State of Karnataka; and (c)	the details of loan sanctioned to MSME units by the banks in Karnataka during the last three years, bank-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI  NARAYAN RANE)(a) & (b):	As per Udyog Aadhaar Portal (since inception October 2015 to June 2020), the number of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) registered in All India and Karnataka was 102,32,468 and 4.11,205 respectively. The State wise details are attached in annexure-I. 	On 1st July, 2020, after adoption of new definition of MSMEs, a new registration portal ‘Udyam Registration’ has been launched by M/o MSME and so far 36,70,538 and 194,008 classified MSMEs are registered on the portal in All India and Karnataka respectively (from 01.07.2020 to 15.07.2021). The State wise details are attached in annexure-II.(c):     	As per PSB 59 minute portal 2,32,406 numbers of loans, involving Rs. 77,221 crores have been sanctioned and 2,16,506 numbers of loans, involving Rs. 63,030 crores have been disbursed in All India as on 27.06.2021. These include the loans sanctioned/ disbursed in the state of Karnataka. The bank wise details are attached as annexure-III.
