                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4052






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Network of Skill Development Institutions




Ramcharan Bohra













                                                    Devendra (Alias) Bhole Singh Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number and details of the Skill Development Centres in Uttar Pradesh including Akbarpur Parliamentary Constituency;(b) 	whether the Government proposes to set up a network of the institutions of skill development in various States especially in the backward areas of Rajasthan;(c)	if so, the details thereof and the action plan being formulated for this purpose along with the training model designed in this regard;(d)	the amount spent for skill development entrepreneurship during the last three years, year-wise;(e)	the areas wherein the employment was provided to the skilled people during the said period; and(f)	whether the Government is providing any funds to the State Government for skill development; and if so, the State-wise details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c)	Under Skill India Mission, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is implementing various programmes/ scheme for the skill development of youth across the country including in the State of Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan  such as Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) and Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS) through Industrial Training Institute (ITIs).  The number of skill development centres that have imparted training in the State of Uttar Pradesh including Akbarpur Parliamentary Constituency (PC) and State of Rajasthan under the programmes of MSDE, are given below: Programme/Scheme	Uttar Pradesh	Akbarpur PC*	RajasthanTraining Centres (TCs) under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) (including Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendras (PMKKs))	4,132	238	2,389 Jan Shikshan Sansthans (JSSs)	50	2	8 Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) under Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS)	3,207	93	1,789 Total	7,389	333	4,186  *Kanpur Nagar and Kanpur Dehat districts (d)       Under PMKVY, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship has disbursed / spent Rs. 4,253.15 crore in last three years (i.e. 2018-21) to implementing agencies for the implementation of the scheme across the country. During FY 2018-19, FY 2019-20 and FY 2020-21, funds amounting to Rs. 1,296.41 crore, Rs. 1,612.22 crore and Rs. 1,344.52 crore, respectively, have been disbursed / spent. (e)       Under PMKVY, placement opportunities are being provided to candidates certified in short term training. Under PMKVY 1.0, tracking of placement was not mandatory. Since inception, i.e., 2015, as on 31.12.2021, 53.88 lakh candidates have been certified under STT; out of which, 23.70 lakh candidates have been provided with the placement in various sectors across the country. (f)        Under State component of PMKVY, funds and corresponding physical targets have been allocated to the States for the implementation of scheme through State Skill Development Missions (SSDMs). Since incorporation of State component, i.e., 2016, as on 31.12.2021, an amount of Rs. 1,224.82 crore has been disbursed to States for the implementation of the scheme.
