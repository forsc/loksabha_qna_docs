                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2251






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Construction Work of Tourist Facilty  Centre in Vrindavan




 Hema Malini














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the present status of construction work of the tourist facility centre in Vrindavan under Krishna Circuit selected for the development of Swadesh Darshan Yojna; (b) 	the time by which the construction work is likely to be completed according to the scheme; (c) 	the present status of development work of Vringovardhan under Krishna Circuit selected for the development of Swadesh Darshan Yojna; and (d) 	the time by which the work is likely to be completed according to the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):	The project viz. Construction of Tourist Facilitation Centre at Vrindavan, Distt. Mathura was approved at the cost of Rs.9.36 Crore under PRASHAD Scheme of the Ministry of Tourism in the year 2014-15.  The construction of the Tourist Facilitation Facility has been completed and inaugurated in September 2019. (c) & (d):	No project has been approved for the development of Vrindavan and Govardhan under Krishna Circuit of Swadesh Darshan Scheme of the Ministry of Tourism.*******
