                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

23






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Rail Services in Uttar Pradesh




Kaushal Kishore














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any new rail services have been started to connect Uttar Pradesh with the other States of the country during the last two years; (b) if so, the service-wise and location-wise details thereof; (c) whether any proposals regarding rail services in Mohanlalganj and other districts in Uttar Pradesh submitted by the State Government are pending with the Government; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)	(a) to (d) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. 	****
