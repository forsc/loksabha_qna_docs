                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

57






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Cotton Storage Warehouse




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government has any proposal to set up a Cotton storage warehouse and Cotton Spinning mill in Peruvalapur in Perambalur District of Tamil Nadu for the benefit of farmers;(b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) 	the total number of Cotton storage warehouse that have been set up by the Government during the last three years, State/UT-wise; and(d) 	the total number of cotton storage warehouses that are functioning/operational across the country as on date, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a): 	There is no such proposal to set up a Cotton storage warehouse and Cotton Spinning mill in Peruvalapur in Perambalur District of Tamil Nadu.  (b):	Does not arise.(c):	Government has not set up any cotton storage warehouse in the country.(d):	Does not arise.******
