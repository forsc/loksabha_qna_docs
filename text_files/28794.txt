                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

262






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Armed Conflicts in North East Region




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has the data of armed conflicts occuring in North East Region;(b) if so, the number of such conflicts since 2014 in NE between insurgents/ militants and armed forces or between various militant groups, year-wise;(c) whether the Government has the data of casualties among armed forces, militants and civilians;(d) if so, the details thereof during the last five years, State-wise;(e) whether the Government has the data of funds it spent on military operations in North East Region during the said period; and(f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b): The year wise number of insurgency related incidents occurred in North Eastern Region since 2014 onwards and current year upto 15.11.2021 are as follows:Years	2014	2015	2016	2017	2018	2019	2020	2021 (upto 15.11.2021)incidents	824	574	484	308	252	223	163	187(c) & (d): The state wise number of civilians, security force (SF) personnel and insurgents killed in insurgency related incidents in North Eastern States during last five years and current year upto 15.11.2021 are as follows:-2/...-2-Years	PersonsKilled 	Arunachal Pradesh	Assam	Manipur 	Meghalaya	Mizoram	Nagaland	Tripura2016	Civilians 	-	29	11	08	-	-	-	SFs 	02	04	11	-	-	-	-	Insurgents 	07	51	09	15	-	05	-2017	Civilians 	03	06	23	02	-	03	-	SFs 	-	03	08	-	-	01	-	Insurgents 	09	16	22	06	-	04	-2018	Civilians 	01	07	08	04	-	03	-	SFs 	02	01	07	01	-	03	-	Insurgents 	12	05	10	03	-	04	-2019	Civilians 	12	-	07	01	-	01	-	SFs 	02	-	-	-	-	02	-	Insurgents 	02	-	09	-	-	01	-2020	Civilians 	-	02	-	-	-	-	-	SFs 	02	-	03	-	-	-	-	Insurgents 	07	05	07	-	-	02	-2021(upto Nov. 15, 2021)	Civilians 	-	11	09	-	-	-	-	SFs 	01	-	05	-	-	-	02	Insurgents 	07	13	18	-	-	-	01(e) & (f) : No separate fund is allocated for military operations in North Eastern Region. However, Central Government is reimbursing the Security Related Expenditure (SRE) to the North Eastern States.
