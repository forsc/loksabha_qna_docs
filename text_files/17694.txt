                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1453






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Uniformity in Designating Castes




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1453TO BE ANSWERED ON. 20.09.2020UNIFORMITY IN DESIGNATING CASTES 1453. SHRI KRUPAL BALAJI TUMANE: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether it is a fact that various castes have been designated differently, such as Other Backward Classes/ Scheduled Castes/Scheduled Tribes and General category in different States; (b) if so, the details of such castes which have been included in General category in one State and in some other category in another State along with the details of such Scheduled Castes/Scheduled Tribes in other States, State-wise; (c) the corrective steps taken by the Government to bring about uniformity in the country, in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) & (b): Yes Sir. Since the socio-cultural and educational conditions of castes differ from State to State and in some cases from region to region within a State, there are variations in categorisation of a particular caste from State to State. The analysis of such cases is not done in this Ministry.(c): In view of the above does not arise.
