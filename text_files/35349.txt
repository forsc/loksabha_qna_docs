                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2457






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






Unmanned Level Crossings




Jayadev Galla













                                                    Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the year-wise data of total number of unmanned level crossings in the country from 2018 till 2021;(b)	the number of deaths that have been reported on these crossings State-wise; (c)	the State-wise details of the total number of unmanned level crossings currently active in the country; (d)	the year-wise and State-wise details of total number of railway under bridges and railway over bridges built since 2018 to eliminate unmanned level crossings;(e)	the details of total expenditure incurred on eliminating unmanned level crossings; and(f)	the plan chalked out by the Ministry to completely eliminate unmanned level crossings in a prescribed time limit?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a)	to (f): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
