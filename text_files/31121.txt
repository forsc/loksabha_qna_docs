                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2875






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Surrender of Phone Connections




Vijayakumar (alias) Vijay Vasanth














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether number of subscribers of Government owned BSNL and MTNL have surrendered their mobile and landline phone connections owing to their poor quality of service and stiff competition from private operators;(b) 	if so, the details and the number of subscribers who have surrendered their phone connections as also applied for new connections during the last three years, year/State/UT and company-wise;(c) 	whether BSNL and MTNL have taken any steps to restore their market share;(d) 	if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) & (b) 	The landline and mobile services provided by Bharat Sanchar Nigam limited (BSNL) and Mahanagar Telephone Nigam Limited (MTNL) are in general working satisfactory and are meeting most of the Quality of Service (QoS) parameters prescribed by Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) in their Licensed Service Areas (LSA). The details of surrendered landline and mobile connections is at Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively. The details of gross landline and mobile connections is at Annexure-III and Annexure-IV respectively. (c) to (e) 	BSNL and MTNL augment their networks based on techno-commercial considerations. These PSUs have rationalized their tariff plans and tied up with different channel partners to improve their services and market share.
