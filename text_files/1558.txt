                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1365






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Exploration of Oil Reserves




Chunni Lal Sahu














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the oil and Natural Gas Corporation Limited (ONGC) has explored crude oil reserves during the last three years and the current year;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the estimated quantum of various varieties of oil available in these reserves;(c) the number of oil reserves from where oil is being extracted by ONGC along with the quantum of oil available in these reserves;(d) the quantum of oil being produced by ONGC from these reserves annually; and(e) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. ONGC has informed that, on the basis of exploration carried out in the acreages available to it, ONGC has established 230.29 MMT (2P) of Oil Initial-In-Place during the last three years. Estimation of reserves explored during a particular year is made at the end of the year. Oil type is defined by its API gravity i.e. Light or Heavy Crude Oil and such reserve estimation is done on the basis of total Oil and Condensate.(c): ONGC is extracting oil from its 16 Assets. As on 01.04.2019, total oil available in these assets is 459.84 MMT (2P).(d) & (e): ONGC has produced 21.111 MMT oil in the year 2018-19, and 65.665 MMT oil in the last three years.****
