                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

943






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Encroachment of Land of Air India




Ganesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has approved proposal of Airports Authority of India (AAI) for surrendering encroached AAI''s land;(b) if so, the details thereof, State/UTwise;(c) whether several acres of land of AAI has been encroached by someone and if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Satna district of Madhya Pradesh;(d) whether in lieu of AAI''s 106.76 acres encroached land, the Government of Madhya Pradesh has agreed to provide land measuring 96.56 acres approx. and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons for not utilizing the said land by AAI for aviation purposes; and(e) whether the land offered by State Government in lieu of the said land can be judicially utilized by the AAI for aviation related activities and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. The Government has approved the proposal of the Airports Authority of India (AAI) for transfer of  AAI land measuring  106.76 acres under encroachment at Bhopal Airport to the State Government of Madhya Pradesh in exchange of land of equivalent value.;;(c): As on 31.05.2019, approximately 979.11 acres of AAI land, including 341.93 acres at Satna, are under encroachment. The State-wise details are at Annexure-I.;;(d) & (e): Government of Madhya Pradesh has already handed over the physical possession of land measuring 96.56 acres approximately to Airport on 14.06.2018 in lieu of 106.76 acres of AAI''s encroached land situated at Bhopal.  The land provided by the State Government is contiguous to the airport land and AAI is in the process of utilising the said land for aviation related development of Bhopal Airport.
