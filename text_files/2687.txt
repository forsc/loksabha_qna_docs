                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2323






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Subsidy on Gas Connection




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva













                                                    Y. S. Avinash Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to provide subsidised gas connection to around seven million rural poor in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof indicating the details of such connections that have been provided to the rural poor in the country;(c) whether the entire subsidy is to be funded by the Central Government and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government has set any target in this regard and if so, the details thereof; and(e) whether the Government is contemplating to allow private companies to sell subsidised LPG and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d) Government of India launched Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) under which a deposit free LPG connection is provided to an adult woman member of a poor family having no LPG connection subject to fulfilling other terms and conditions. The cash assistance upto ? 1600 per connection under PMUY is borne by the Government of India. As on 01.07.2019, more than 7.30 crore connections have been provided to poor families against the target of 8 crore connections.(e) There is no such proposal under consideration of the Government.---------
