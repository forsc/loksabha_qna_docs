                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

280






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Fire Safety Measures in Educational Institutes




Bhagwanth Khuba














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of that last month tragedy in Gujarat wherein 40 studentsburn alive in coaching center;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has any guidelines to open coaching centres across the country withsafety measures;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) the number of coaching centres identified without licence in the country, State- wiseincluding Karnataka during the last three years?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’)(a) to (e): The Government is aware of the last month tragedy in Gujarat wherein 40 studentswere burnt alive in coaching center and expresses anguish over the tragedy.The private coaching centers are not regulated by this Ministry as many of these operateat the School level and hence are under the direct purview of the State Governments.Accordingly, all State Governments have been asked to take steps for effective regulation ofsuch centers in the interest of the students.Further, there is no centrally maintained data regarding coaching centers running withoutlicense in the country including Karnataka.*******
