                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

119






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Release of Funds to Telangana




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    *119. SHRI KOMATI REDDY VENKAT REDDY:Will the Minister of FINANCE be pleased to state:(a) 	whether the Government is aware that the financial condition of Telangana State is in a precarious stage; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto along with the details of funds released so far under different heads/ways during the last six years;(c) 	whether it is true that the Union Government has not provided any extra funds to the State;(d) 	if so, the details thereof along with the ministry wise details of regular funds and additional funds released to Telangana during the said period; and(e) 	whether Telangana Government had submitted any utilisation certificates towards the funds  released so far, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF FINANCE(SMT. NIRMALA SITHARAMAN)(a) to (e): A statement is laid on the table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED IN PART (A) TO (E) OF THE REPLY TO THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *119 BY SHRI KOMATI REDDY VENKAT REDDY REGARDING “RELEASE OF FUNDS TO TELANGANA” DUE FOR ANSWER ON MONDAY FEBRUARY 10, 2020.(a) & (b): No, Sir. As per State Budget documents, the State of Telangana is a Revenue Surplus State since 2014-15. The Debt-GSDP Ratio (from 2014-15 to 2019-20 BE) though on a rising trend is within the envelope prescribed by the 14th Finance Commission for its award period and is also in line with the Medium Term Fiscal Policy Statements of the State Government of Telangana. (c) & (d):  No, Sir. Details of funds released to the State of Telangana from 2014-15 to 2019-20 by the Union Government is provided in Annexure. (e) : Grants-in-aid to the States are released based on the applicable guidelines by the Union Government. The monitoring of the Utilisation Certificates submitted by the State Government of Telangana against the Central Assistance released, wherever applicable is carried out by the concerned Ministries/Departments and/or NITI Aayog as per extant guidelines and GFRs.***** Annexure with respect to Lok Sabha Starred Question 119*Releases made to the State Government of Telangana(Rs. in
