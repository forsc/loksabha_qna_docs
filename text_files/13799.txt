                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

221






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






DD Channels in NE Region




C. Lalrosanga














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any plan to transform the existing Doordarshan Channels of DDK Aizawl, Kohima, Imphal, Gangtok, Agartala and Shillong into 24x7 DD Regional Channels; (b)	if so, the details thereof along with the time by which these channels are likely to be made operational; (c)	whether it is true that Prasar Bharati started the trial run of 24x7 DD Assam Channel from 01.01.2020; (d)	if so, the details thereof along with the time by which the said channel is likely to be launched formally; and (e)	whether any special budget allocation is proposed for DD Assam Channel similar to DD Arunprabha Channel and if so the details thereof along with the time frame fixed for the purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR) (a) to (e): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.*********************** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.*221 FOR ANSWER ON 06.03.2020(a) & (b): Prasar Bharati has placed limited hours DD channels originating from Aizawl, Kohima, Imphal, Agartala and Shillong on DD FreeDish, a free to air  DTH platform of Prasar Bharati w.e.f. March 2019.  Prasar Bharati is assessing the resources and manpower requirement for converting into 24x7 channel.(c) to (e): Prasar Bharati has undertaken test transmission of converting DD North-East Channel into DD Assam channel from 01.12.2019.  Expenses for the channel would be met out of the overall allocation relating to Prasar Bharati.*****
