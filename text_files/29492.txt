                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1282






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Special Cultural Fund




Dileshwar Kamait













                                                    Jyotirmay Singh Mahato
                                                








                                                    Geeta Kora
                                                








                                                    Jugal Kishore Sharma
                                                








                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any mechanism to set up any special cultural fund for promoting our invaluable culture, especially for organizing cultural programmes at district level for promoting cultural/tourist spots;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including Jammu & Kashmir, West Bengal, Jharkhand, Bihar and Madhya Pradesh;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the details of formal communication with the concerned officials and nodal agencies in regard to the said objective including the amount of funds allocated to Jammu & Kashmir, West Bengal, Jharkhand, Bihar and Madhya Pradesh and the formal process for organizing programmes to promote tourism these States?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTRY OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d) The Ministry of Culture administers various scheme components such as Cultural Function Production Grant, Repertory Grant, Cultural Organization with National Presence etc., under which grants are being given as financial assistance to the organizations to organise cultural events across the country including Jammu & Kashmir, West Bengal, Jharkhand, Bihar and Madhya Pradesh.  Seven Zonal Cultural Centres across the country play a key role in promotion of culture through the programmes decided in consultation with their member State Government. 	The concerned Zonal Cultural Centres (ZCCs) of above mentioned five member states are as follows:-Sl. No.	Member States	ZCCs to which attached1.	Jammu and Kashmir	NZCC, Patiala2.	West Bengal	EZCC, Kolkata3.	Jharkhand	EZCC, Kolkata4.	Bihar	EZCC, Kolkata and NCZCC, Prayagraj5.	Madhya Pradesh	SCZCC, Nagpur and NCZCC, PrayagrajA part of the grant provided to concerned Zonal Cultural Centres (ZCCs) is being utilized to organise cultural programmes in Jammu & Kashmir, West Bengal, Jharkhand, Bihar and Madhya Pradesh. However, the execution process is not administered through districts.****
