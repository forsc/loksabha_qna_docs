                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4785






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Target of Smart Cities Mission




Dharmendra Kumar Kashyap














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of Smart Cities Mission (SCM)  in  the  State   of   Uttar Pradesh and the aims and objectives of the same;(b)	the  impact  of  COVID-19  on  the  projects  under  SCM  in   Uttar Pradesh  and  the  efforts  being made by the Government to deal with the same; and(c) 	the details of the targets that have been achieved under the SCM in Uttar Pradesh and the time by which all the projects would be completed in the said cities, city-wise?






ANSWER




                                                                           THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                 MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                        (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)	 to (c): Government of India launched the Smart Cities Mission (SCM) on 25 June 2015. 100 Smart cities have been selected through 4 rounds of competition from January 2016 to June 2018. 10 Cities in the State of Uttar Pradesh have been selected for development as Smart Cities. City-wise status of projects of Smart Cities in Uttar Pradesh is given in Annexure-I. COVID-19 pandemic related lockdowns and other unprecedented situations led to temporary interruptions in execution of Smart City works across the country, including in Uttar Pradesh. However, Smart Cities commenced works after un-lockdown in right earnest. The period of implementation of SCM has been extended upto June 2023. All Smart Cities, including those in Uttar Pradesh, are expected to complete their projects within the stipulated time.                                 ******
