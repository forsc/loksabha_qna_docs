                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

200






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Textile Workers Hostel




Rita Bahuguna Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether all the Textiles Parks/Manufacturing units in the country is having the Textile Workers Hostel in India; (b) if so, the details of such Textile Parks along with the textile workers being accommodated in such hostels; (c) if not, whether the Government plans to fix time line to complete hostel project in all the eligible textile production houses; and (d) the details of the grants allocated by the Government to the completed Textile Workers Hostel since its inception till December 2021?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) to (d):	In order to provide a safe and secured accommodation for the workforce and ensure better retention of the work force by way of decent accommodation in/vicinity of the textile parks, thereby improving the productivity, Scheme Guidelines for Pilot Phase of Textile Industry Workers’ Hostel was launched in 2015 and the scheme was in force till October 2021.  The details of the grants allocated by the Government to the Textile Workers Hostel and the textile workers being accommodated in such hostels since its inception till October 2021 are as under: S.No.	Projects	Sanction Date	Project cost (Rs.
