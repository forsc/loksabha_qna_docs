                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2497






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Civic Amenities




Mansukhbhai Dhanjibhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the adequate civic amenities are not available in small cities and if so, the reasons therefor; (b) 	whether the Government proposes to formulate any new plan in this regard; and (c)	 if so, the time by which such plan is likely to be formulated?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                     (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a).	Civic amenities such as water and public health are State subjects. The Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs, through various program interventions, assist the States/Union Territories (UTs) in improving the amenities for cities including small cities.(b)& (c).	Various Centrally Sponsored Schemes/Missions viz. Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojna (Urban), Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) and Swachh Bharat Mission (Urban) have been launched to improve urban infrastructure and civic amenities in various Indian cities including small cities.                                          *****
