Niryat Bandhu Scheme

                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether progress has been made so far in achieving the objectives under Niryat Bandhu Scheme; (b)	the details of funds allocated, sanctioned and disbursed during the past five years and the current year for the purpose, State/Union Territory wise, specially for Maharashtra; (c)	the details of the entrepreneurs benefitted during the last five years, State/Union Territory wise; and (d)	the future action plan of the Government for further encouraging the entrepreneurs of India if so, the details thereof and if not, the reason therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY(SMT. ANUPRIYA PATEL)(a):	The Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT) is implementing the Niryat Bandhu Scheme for mentoring new and potential exporter on foreign trade through counseling, training and outreach programmes in partnership with various stakeholders. The Scheme has benefitted around 1.15 Lakh Entrepreneurs since its inception in 2013. (b) & (c):	Niryat Bandhu Scheme has been allocated Rs. 5.45 Crore in last 5 years (2016-17 to 2020-21). An amount of Rs. 4.04 Crore has been utilized during this period and 65,184 entrepreneurs have benefitted from the training and outreach activities undertaken under the Scheme. Fund allocation & utilisation for the last 5 years is as under.Financial Year 	Allocation (Rupees in Lakhs)	Expenditure (Rupees in Lakhs)2016-2017	100	98.522017-2018	100	96.972018-2019	200	992019-2020	95	73.052020-2021	50	36.92TOTAL	545	404.46For the current year 2021-22 Rs. 4.48 Crores have been allocated under the Scheme.The details of fund allocation, utilization and entrepreneurs benefitted State/Union Territory wise under the Niryat Bandhu Scheme is at Annexure I.(d):	Niryat Bandhu Scheme has been extended for period of five years from 2021-22 to 2025-26 with enhanced objectives and components.
