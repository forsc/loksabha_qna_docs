                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2877






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Projects Approved under Khelo India Programme




Pradan Baruah














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of sanctioned and approved projects under the Khelo India Programme along with name of those projects in Assam during the last three years, district-wise;(b) the number of youths enrolled under the programme in Assam;(c) the current status of the projects under Khelo India Programme for construction of Bihpuria Stadium Complex in district Lakhimpur, Assam; and(d) the current status of the projects under Khelo India Programme for construction of Multipurpose Sports Hall at Khowang H.S. School in Moran, district Dibrugarh, Assam?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) and (c) Sports being a State subject the primary responsibility of promotion and participation in Sports rests with concerned State/Union Territory Government. Central Government supplements efforts of the State/Union Territories. Information of infrastructure projects sanctioned for the State of Assam during the last three years and the current year under Khelo India Scheme is at Annexure I.(b) Under the “Talent Search and Development” vertical of Khelo India Scheme, young sporting talents are identified and provided training. Presently, 40 athletes (20 male and 20 female) are getting training in the State of Assam under this vertical.(d) The project of construction of Multipurpose Sports Hall at Khowang H.S. School in Moran, district Dibrugarh, Assam was not found feasible for financial assistance under Khelo India Scheme. Larger number of athletes could be provided training in the existing sports infrastructure facilities in Assam.
