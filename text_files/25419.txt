                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1276






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Food Stocks Stored by FCI




K. Shanmuga Sundaram














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total quantity of food stocks stored by Food Corporation of India (FCI) as on date in metric tons; (b) 	whether these food procurements were acquired as per the Minimum Support Price (MSP), if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the total quantity of food grains released under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana (PMGKAY); and (d) 	the steps taken by the Government to prevent illegal sale of food grains released under PDS scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	The total stock of foodgrains in the Central Pool as on 01.07.2021 was 900.45 lakh tons consisting of 603.56 lakh of wheat and 296.89 lakh tons of rice. (b): 	Yes Sir, The procurement policy of Government of India is open ended, under which paddy and wheat offered by farmers within the stipulated period (confirming to specifications prescribed in advance by GOI) are purchased for Central Pool at Minimum Support Price (MSP) by Food Corporation of India(FCI)/State Governments /State Government  Agencies to help farmers get remunerative price and prevent distress sale.(c): 		During the COVID-19 pandemic year 2020-21 and 2021-22 under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY) a total of nearly 600.814 lakh tons of foodgrains was allocated. Out of this allocation nearly 400.703 lakh tons has been lifted (released) as on 14th july, 2021.(d): 		For transparent distribution of foodgrains and rightful targeting under NFSA,  nearly 4.96 lakh e-PoS devices have been installed, out of a total of nearly 5.38 lakh FPS, all over the country, through Aadhar authentication. In this regard, transaction details are available in the public domain. Further, whenever e-PoS device is not available or not working at FPS, this Department has advised all States/UTs to distribute the subsidized/free foodgrains in the presence of Government official/Staff.***
