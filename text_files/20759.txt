                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Beti Bachao Beti Padhao Scheme




Sudheer Gupta













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik

                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















1861.  SHRI SUDHEER GUPTA:SHRI BIDYUT BARAN MAHATO:SHRI SHRIRANG APPA B ARNE:DR. BHARATIBEN DHIRUBHAI SITIYALSHRI SANJAY SADASHIVRAO MANDLIK:SHRIMATI QUEEN OJA:SHRI CHANDRA SEKHAR SAHU: Will the Minister of WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state: (a)	whether Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) scheme has not been implemented in all the districts across the country and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether BBBP scheme has been able to bring behavioural change in the society towards birth and rights of a girl child in the country and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether BBBP Scheme has resulted in an improvement of Sex Ratio at Birth; and(d)	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a)         While pan India Advocacy and Media campaign  is implemented under the BBBP scheme,  405 districts have been identified for Multi Sectoral interventions. (b)        As per the evaluation report of NITI Aayog, the scheme has been able to generate significant mass- mobilisation to eliminate gender discrimination and the scheme has also been able to develop many good practices and community-level initiatives.(c) & (d)         BBBP aims to address the issues of declining Child Sex Ratio (CSR) and enabling Girl Child Education. As per the latest available report of Health Management Information System (HMIS) of Ministry of Health and Family Welfare,  an improving trend of 16 points at National level from 918 (2014-15) to 934 (2019- 20) has been observed.
