                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3321






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Capacity of Warehouses in AP




Midhun Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of warehouses in Andhra Pradesh at present; (b) 	the number of farmers who are benefitted from the said warehouses; (c) 	whether the capacity of these warehouses is adequate for the food grains procured from farmers in Andhra Pradesh; (d) 	whether the Government plans to set up more warehouses for storage in Andhra Pradesh; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	As on 01.02.2022, 40 (owned & hired) warehouses are available with Food Corporation of India (FCI) for storage of central pool foodgrains stock in Andhra Pradesh (AP).Also, Central Warehousing Corporation (CWC) is operating 43 warehouses in the State of Andhra Pradesh.(b): 	The total number of farmers benefited in Andhra Pradesh State is 4,35,374 during Kharif Marketing Season (KMS) 2020-21. State Government delivers procured rice to FCI after meeting their requirement for Public Distribution System (PDS)/Various schemes.Total stock stored by farmers at CWC warehouses in the State of AP is 12,020 MT.(c): 	Yes, Sir.(d) & (e): 	CWC is considering for the capacity expansion in Andhra Pradesh.*****
