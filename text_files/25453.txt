                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1362






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Agricultural Land Damaged Due to Flood and Cyclone




Supriya Sadanand Sule













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Manickam Tagore B
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that due to flood and cyclone agricultural land gets damaged every year and if so, the extent of agricultural land damaged by floods and cyclone during the last three years and the current year; (b) the details of the estimated damage to agricultural output as a result of flooding and cyclone during the said period, State-wise and year-wise;(c) whether the Government plans to provide special aid to farmers losing revenue due to flooding and cyclone and if so, the details thereof; and (d) whether the Government is drawing up plans to address the impending damage to agricultural land and losses to farmers/workers as a result of the growing rate of flooding and cyclone each year and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????      (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d): State-wise details of crop area affected due to hydro-meteorological calamities/ hazards during 2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22, are given at Annexure. The State Governments undertake relief measures in the wake of natural disasters from the State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF) already placed at their disposal in accordance with Government of India approved items and norms. Additional assistance is extended from the National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF) as per established procedure, upon receipt of Memorandum from the State. Thereafter, an Inter-Ministerial Central Team (IMCT) is constituted and deputed for an on-the-spot assessment of damage and requirement of funds for relief operations, as per the extant items and norms.  The report of the IMCT is considered by the Sub Committee of National Executive Committee (SC-NEC). Thereafter, the High Level Committee (HLC) approves the final quantum of financial assistance.
