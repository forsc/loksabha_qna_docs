                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

20






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Mandis under e-NAM




Kapil Moreshwar Patil














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the total number of mandis in the country linked under the National Agriculture Market (e-NAM) so far along with the details thereof, State-wise including Bhiwandi (Maharashtra); (b) 	whether e-NAM, an electronic agriculture portal, facilitates integrated market for agricultural products; and (c) 	the total number of Agricultural Produce Market Committees (APMCs) integrated with e-NAM along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		 	  (SHRI NARENDRA  SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (c):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.  20   DUE FOR REPLY ON 2ND FEBRUARY, 2021.(a) to (c):	National Agriculture Market (e-NAM) is a virtual platform integrating physical wholesale mandis/ markets of different States/ Union Territories (UTs) to facilitate online trading of agriculture and horticulture commodities through transparent price discovery method to enable farmers to realize better remunerative prices for their produce.As on 24.01.2021, 1000 mandis of 18 States and 3 UTs have been integrated with e-NAM platform and so far more than 1.69 crore farmers have been registered on the platform. State-wise details of e-NAM mandis/ markets are at Annexure-I. The details of the mandis/ markets integrated with e-NAM are at Annexure-II.Bhiwandi (Maharashtra) mandi is not integrated with e-NAM.
