                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5416






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Construction of New Stadiums in Kerala




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has submitted any proposal for construction of new stadiums in Kerala and if so, thedetails thereof;(b) the towns and villages identified for the same and the present status of these constructions; and(c) the status of identifying students at younger age for sports activities?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a)& (b) Sir, no proposal for construction of new stadiums in Kerala State is under consideration of this Ministry. ‘Sports’ being a State subject the responsibility of promotion of sports including construction of new stadiums in Kerala, rests with the State Government. The Central Government supplements efforts of the State Government. Details of the sports infrastructure projects sanctioned in Kerala State under the Khelo India Scheme are given in Annexure – 1.(c) 2437 young sporting talents have been identified and they are being supported under the Khelo India Scheme for nurturing their sporting talent.*****
