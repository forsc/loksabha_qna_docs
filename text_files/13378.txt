                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2276






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Facilities in Railways




Keshari Devi Patel













                                                    Kanakmal Katara
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a):	whether the Railways has deployed helpers, security and other personnel through private agencies in the trains and on the railway stations; (b):	the companies with whom the contracts have been signed for this purpose along with the details thereof; (c):	the date since when this arrangement is going on and the basis on which this work has been allocated including the number of days for which it has been allocated along with the details thereof; (d):	the year-wise details of the payments made to such personnel ;(e):	the details of the names of the trains originating from Prayagraj in which such arrangements have been made and the names of the companies that have been assigned the work along with the period for which this work has been given to them;  (f):	whether the Government is aware that these helpers, security and other personnel are being paid less than the minimum wage;  (g):	if so, the number of such agencies against which complaints have been received; and  (h):	the steps being taken by the Government to ensure that all the contract labours are paid minimum wages, along with the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (h):	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
