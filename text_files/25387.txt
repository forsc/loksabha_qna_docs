                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1242






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Procurement at MSP




(Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has procured crops at Minimum Support Price (MSP) during Kharif marketing season 2020;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the details of the crops procured at MSP during the Kharif marketing season 2020-21 along with the quantity thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ??????? ?????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c) : Government has procured Fair Average Quality (FAQ) of notified crops at Minimum Support Price (MSP) from the farmers during Kharif Marketing Season 2020.
