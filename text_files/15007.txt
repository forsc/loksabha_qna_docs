                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3744






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Revamp of PM Kisan Scheme




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government is in the process of revamping PM Kisan scheme which is a flagship income support scheme of the Government with a move to enroll more farmers under this scheme and issue more Kisan Credit Cards to farmers; (b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the reasons for revamping the said scheme and the extent to which the revamp process will help the farmers?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	PM-KISAN Scheme aims to enrol all eligible farmers for providing income support of Rs. 6,000 per annum. The scheme is not being revamped. PM-KISAN and Kisan Credit Card are two different Schemes. The data of beneficiaries of PM-KISAN is being leveraged to provide them benefits under other Schemes. To achieve this, a joint campaign with Department of Financial Services and Banks has been taken up to provide Kisan Credit Cards to all PM-Kisan beneficiaries who do not have the facility as of now.(b) & (c):	Do not arise.*******
