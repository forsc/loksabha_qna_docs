                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

369






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Wage Payments under MGNREGS




Gaurav Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) is facing a negative net balance, if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the details of the pending liabilities till date as part of the MGNREGS, with separate lists of the wage and material components, State-wise;(c) 	the number of person-days of work demanded and number of person-days of work generated in 2019-20 and thus far in 2020-21;(d) 	whether the Government has instructed that wage payments under MGNREGS be split according to caste categories; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor along with the reasons for retracting such order?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b)    Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme is a demand driven scheme. There are enough funds with the scheme to fulfill all the liabilities of the scheme for the States which have met the entire conditions for release of second tranche of funds. The Ministry has already sought additional resources for the Scheme.State/UT-wise details of pending liabilities for wage component under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS as on 25.11.2021 is given at Annexure-I.  State/UT-wise details of pending liabilities for material component under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS as on 25.11.2021 are given at Annexure-II.(c)  Under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS, the employment demanded by persons, employment offered to persons and employment provided to persons in the financial year 2019-20 and FY 2020-21 is given below:                          2019-20 (in lakhs)	                    2020-21 (in Lakhs)Employment     Employment     Employment    Employment  Employment  Employmentdemanded       offered        availed by    demanded    offered      availed byby persons     to persons     persons	    by persons	to person    persons933.95	       930.50	      788.45	     1332.58	1329.8	     1118.78 In the financial year 2019-20, a total number of 265.35 crore persondays have been generated and in FY 2020-21, a total number of 389.17 crore persondays have been generated under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS. (d) & (e)  The category wise (SC, ST and Others) wage payment system, as made applicable from this current financial year, has been introduced to accurately reflect on ground flow of funds to various population groups. Its further streamlining is being undertaken. *****
