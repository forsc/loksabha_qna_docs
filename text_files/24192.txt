                                    FINANCE

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

7






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Share of Central Taxes




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government allocates share of central taxes to all the State Governments for union budget schemes;(b)	if so, the details of the share of central taxes allocated to the State Governments during the last three years, state-wise;(c)	the amount of funds allocated to the State of Rajasthan from the said funds under various heads during the current financial year; and(d)	the details of funds allocated, scheme -wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a) 41% of the net proceeds of taxes are allocated as devolution to the States in accordance with approved recommendations of the Finance Commission. Devolution of taxes are allocated tax-wise. (b) Details of the share of central taxes allocated to the State Governments for the last three years,state-wise is at Annexure-A. (c)  Funds allocated under share of taxes to Rajasthan under various heads during the current financial year are at Annexure-B(d) Funds allocated under devolution to the States are tax-wise and not scheme-wise.
