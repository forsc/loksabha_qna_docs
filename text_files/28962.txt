                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

417






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Oil Seed and Oil Palm Scheme




Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has launched Oil Seed and Oil Palm Scheme under the National Food Security Mission (NFSM), if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether approval has been given to implement mini-kit distribution of rapeseed and mustard seeds in the country, if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government has approved 8,20,600 seed mini-kits, consisting of seeds of high yielding varieties of productivity of more than 20 quintals per hectare, for distribution in 343 identified districts of 15 States including Odisha and Uttar Pradesh;(d)	 if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	the other steps being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? (SHRI NAREDNRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	The Government of India has been implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, National Food Security Mission- Oilseeds & Oil palm (NFSM-OS&OP) from 2018-19. Now the Government has launched a separate Mission for Oil Palm which is the National Mission for Edible Oils (Oil Palm)- NMEO (OP). Both NFSM- Oilseeds and NMEO (OP) are being implemented in the country with the objective of augmenting the availability of edible oils by increasing the production and productivity of oilseeds and oil palm and reducing the import burden. NFSM- Oilseeds has three broad interventions viz., seed components, production inputs and transfer of technology. NMEO (OP) has been launched by targeting an additional 6.5 lakh hectare area from 2021-22 to 2025-26. (b) to (d):     Yes Sir.  The Government has approved 8,20,600 seed mini-kits of Rapeseed and Mustard (R&M) for distribution in Rabi 2021-22. The mini kits are of 2 kg size, consisting of seeds of high yielding varieties having productivity of more than 20 quintals per hectare, for distribution in 343 identified districts of 15 States including Odisha and Uttar Pradesh.  Details of seed mini-kits of Rapeseed and Mustard (R&M) allocated State-wise during Rabi 2021-22 are at Annexure-I. (e): 	The Government has also approved a special programme on R&M for distribution of High Yielding Varieties seeds in the major R&M growing States during Rabi 2021-22, details of which are at Annexure-II.	Further, the Government has also approved a pilot project for seed mini-kit distribution of Hybrid Mustard through the National Seeds Corporation during Rabi-2021-22, details of which are at Annexure-III.	Moreover, certified seeds of R&M are also being distributed to the farmers in major R&M growing States through approved Annual Action Plans of the State Government.
