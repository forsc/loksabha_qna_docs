                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1864






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Treatment in CGHS Empanelled Hospitals




Upendra Singh Rawat













                                                    Kaushal Kishore
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to refer to the Unstarred Question No. 4122 dated 04 January 2019:– (a) whether the Government has taken note of the fact that a large number of private general hospitals refusing medical treatment to the CGHS beneficiaries on the grounds that the desired medical specialty is not covered under CGHS, however if the beneficiary is willing to pay general rates for treatment they are provided with medical treatment in those hospitals, if so, the details thereof along with the corrective steps taken in this regard;(b) whether the Government has taken note of the fact that various private hospitals empanelled under CGHS do not list all the medical specialties they cater to the patients but only those specialties for which they are willing to cater to the CGHS beneficiaries, if so, the details thereof and corrective steps taken in this regard; (c) whether the Government proposes to revise the relevant CGHS list of empanelled hospitals either by removing the column “empanelled for” describing the specialty treatment catered to the beneficiaries or by ensuring that the said list of empanelled hospitals provide all the medical specialty to which the hospitals is catering to; and (d) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken/proposed to be taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b): Private hospitals are empanelled under CGHS for all such facilities/ specialties as recommended by National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Health Care Providers (NABH) or Quality Council of India (QCI) (for non-NABH), as the case may be. Recommendation of NABH/QCI is mandatory for empanelment under CGHS as a measure of Quality Assurance. Therefore, CGHS empanels hospitals only for those facilities/ specialties which are recommended by NABH/QCI.(c) & (d):      Presently, there is no such proposal.………………
