                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3854






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Targets under PMAY




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the target set under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Gramin)-PMAY(G);(b)	the number of houses so far sanctioned, constructed and handed over under PMAY during the last five years, year and State-wise;(c)	whether it is true that there are still more than 40 lakh houses left to be constructed and handed over to beneficiaries before December, 2018, if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Andhra Pradesh; and(d)	the reasons for not achieving the target along with the present status of the same, State-wise including Andhra Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) aims to provide a pucca house with basic amenities to all houseless households and households living in kutcha and dilapidated house in rural areas by 2022. To achieve the objective of “Housing for All”, the target set is construction of 2.95 crore houses in phases by the year 2021-22. In the first phase 1.00 crore houses were taken up for construction in 3 years i.e., 2016-17 to 2018-19 and in the second phase 1.95 crore houses are targeted for construction in 3 years i.e., 2019-20 to 2021-22.(b) to (d):	Against the target of construction of 1.00 crore houses under phase – I of PMAY-G, a total of 82.15 lakh houses have been completed till 15th July, 2019. A statement indicating number of houses sanctioned and completed under PMAY-G since its inception i.e., 1st April, 2016, State/UT-wise including the State of Andhra Pradesh, is given at Annexure.*****
