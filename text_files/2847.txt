                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2660






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Measures to Check Illegal Immigrations




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre













                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the total number of detention centres established under the NRC and the total number of persons detained till date, area wise;(b) whether the pilot project of smart fencing to check illegal immigration have been implemented in the borders of the country, if so, the details thereof during the last five years, border-wise;(c) if not, the strategy prepared by the Government in this regard;(d) whether there is a plan to adopt technology based solutions for bordersecurity and checking the illegal immigration;(e) if so, the details thereof; and(f) if not, the outline prepared by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	In the state of Assam, there are six Detention Centres to hold “Declared Foreigners” as adjudicated by Foreigners Tribunals. As on 25 May, 2019 the declared foreigners detained in the Detention Centres were 1,133, with the following details:--2-L.S.US.Q.NO.2660 FOR 09.07.2019S.No.	Detention Centre 	Nos. 1	Goalpara	2672	Kokrajhar	1813	Silchar 	704	Dibrugarh	485	Jorhat	2156	Tezpur	352	Total 	1133 Nos. (b) & (c):	Two Pilot projects have been taken up to replace the existing “old design/composite fencing” with a new “anti-cut and anti-rust” modular fencing (New Design Fence). While the pilot project for construction of New Design Fence in 3 Km border length in Assam has been completed, another pilot project in 7.18 Km border length in Punjab has been approved and work has been awarded.(d) to (f):	It has been decided to deploy technological solutions in the form of Comprehensive Integrated Border Management System (CIBMS), in difficult terrain on Indo-Pakistan Border (IPB) and Indo-Bangladesh Border (IBB). CIBMS involves integration of manpower, sensors, networks, intelligence and command & control solutions and includes inter alia Electro-Optic Sensors (high resolution day & night cameras), Radars and other devices. To begin with two pilot projects covering about 71 Kms. on Indo-Pakistan Border (10 Kms.) and Indo-Bangladesh Border (61 Kms.) have been completed. ********
