                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4081






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Delay in Jolarpet-Krishnagiri Railway Project




A. Chellakumar














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the proposed Jolarpet-Krishnagiri railway project is getting delayed resulting in huge cost overturn;(b)	if so, the details of the project and the reasons for the delay in its execution;(c)	the details of funds spent so far on this project; and (d)	the steps taken by the railways to expedite the execution of this project to check any further cost escalation?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) and (b): Surveys for Jolarpettai-Hosur via Krishnagiri new line were conducted during 2000-01, 2005-06, 2008-09 & 2010-11 to assess feasibility of taking up the project. As per survey completed in 2010-11 the cost of 101 Km new line was ` 688 crore with Rate of Return               (-) 2.25%. Project being financially unremunerative could not be taken forward. However, a fresh survey has been sanctioned in 2018-19. Survey has been taken up.(c) and (d): Do not arise.*****
