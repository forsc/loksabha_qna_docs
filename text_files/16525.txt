                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

308






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






PM-AASHA Scheme




Devji Mansingram Patel














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a):	whether the Government has launched an inclusive scheme named Pradhan Mantri Annadata Aay Sanrakshan Abhiyan (PM-AASHA) under which a holistic arrangement has been made to ensure agricultural production and productivity along with remunerative and fixed price for the farmers; (b):	 if so, the details thereof alongwith its achievements since its inception; (c):	whether the Government has conducted any study to find out the reasons behind the farmers getting low price for various crops like onion, potato, tomato etc.; and (d):	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	With a view to ensure delivery of MSP to the farmers, in October, 2018, Government launched an umbrella scheme of ‘Pradhan Mantri Annadata Aay Sanrakshan Abhiyan’ (PM-AASHA) by incorporating the erstwhile Price Support Scheme (PSS) with certain modifications and rolling out of new schemes of Price Deficiency Payment Scheme (PDPS) and pilot of Private Procurement and Stockist Scheme (PPSS) has been launched. Under PM-AASHA, States/UTs are offered to choose either PSS and PDPS in a given procurement season with respect to particular oilseeds crop for the entire State. Pulses and Copra are procured under PSS. Only one scheme i.e. PSS or PDPS may be made operational in one State with respect to one commodity. /Further, states have the option to roll out PPSS on pilot basis in district/selected APMCs of district involving the participation of private stockist for oilseeds. Maximum 25% of the total production of stats is procured. Further, under PM-AASHA, Wheat Paddy and Coarse Grains are procured under the existing schemes of Department of Food and Public Distribution and Cotton and Jute are procured under the existing schemes of Ministry of Textiles. The details of procurement made under PM-AASHA since its inception for Pulses, Oilseeds and Copra are placed at Annexure-I. The procurement for Wheat ,Paddy and coarse grains is at Annexure II, III &  IV respectively.(c) & (d):	No study has been conducted by the Central Government.
