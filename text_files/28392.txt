                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

7






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Opening of Universities in Backward District




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds allocated and utilised for the development of state-level educational institutions under Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) during the last three years and the current year so far, State-wise/Union Territory-wise including Haryana; (b) whether the Government has any plan to open Universities in backward districts of the country under the RUSA;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the time by when these Universities are likely to be opened in these backward districts of the country including Haryana?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) : Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) being implemented by the Ministry of Education. RUSA seeks to improve the overall quality of existing States higher educational institutions by ensuring their conformity to prescribed norms and standards and adoption of accreditation as mandatory quality assurance framework; correcting regional imbalances by facilitating access to high quality institutions in rural and semi urban areas. State-wise /UnionTerritory-wise details including Haryana of the funds released under RUSA during the last three years and the current year so far are at Annexure -I.(b) to (d) : Under RUSA, Universities are created under two components of RUSA viz. Creation of Universities by way of upgradation of existing autonomous colleges and Creation of Universities by conversion of colleges in a cluster by the States/UTs. Two Universities in Educationally Backward Districts identified by UGC and One University in Aspirational District identified by NITI Aayog have been approved under RUSA. State-wise details of the Universities approved under RUSA are at Annexure -II.***
