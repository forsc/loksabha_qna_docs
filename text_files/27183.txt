                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2858






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Allocation of Funds to Kerala under JJM




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the financial assistance sanctioned/ disbursed to Kerala under Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) from financial year 2019-20 to 2021-22; (b) 	the details of Kerala’s contribution/ share to the projects implemented under this Mission; (c) 	whether the Government has the data on the number of new connections provided under the new scheme in the State of Kerala; (d) 	if so, the details thereof; (e) 	whether the proposed target have been achieved; and (f) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b)	Since August 2019, Government of India, in partnership with States, is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) – Har Ghar Jal to make provision of potable tap water supply to every rural household in the country by 2024. The estimated outlay of the mission is Rs.3.60 lakh Crore. For Kerala, the fund sharing pattern between Centre and State is 50:50. The details of central fund allocated, fund drawn by the State and reported utilization both under Central and State share in Kerala under JJM in 2019-20, 2020-21 & 2021-22 (as on 02.08.2021) is as under:(Amount in Rs. Crore)Year	Central	Expenditure under State share	Opening Balance	Allocation	Fund Drawn	Total fund available	Reported utilization	2019-20	2.58	248.76	101.29	103.87	62.69	57.232020-21	41.18	404.24	303.18	344.36	304.29	311.252021-22 *	40.07	1,804.59	451.15	491.22	139.93	155.58* As on 02.08.2021 							Source: JJM-IMIS 	 (c) to (f)	At the time of announcement of Jal Jeevan Mission on 15th August, 2019, out of total 67.15 lakh rural households in Kerala, only 16.64 (24.78%) lakh rural households had tap water connections. Since then, 7.16 lakh rural households have been provided tap water connections in the State. Thus, as on date, out of the 70.68 lakh rural household in Kerala, 23.80 lakh (33.67%) rural households have provision of tap water supply. In 2020-21, State had planned to provide tap water connection to 21.42 lakh rural households, however, State could provide tap water connection to only 4.04 lakh households. In 2021-22, State has planned to provide tap water connections to 29.38 lakh households. The State has planned to ensure tap water supply to every rural household by 2024.*****
