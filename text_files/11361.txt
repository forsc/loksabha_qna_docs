                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

261






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Compensation to Manual Scavengers




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 261TO BE ANSWERED ON 04.02.2020COMPENSATION TO MANUAL SCAVENGERS261. SHRI VISHNU DAYAL RAM:Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the reasons for low compensation rates to the families of manual scavengers in States such as   Kerala; (b) the details of the number of beneficiaries to whom compensation has been paid, State-wise; and (c) the details of the quantum of the compensation paid to the beneficiaries, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RAMDAS ATHAWALE)(a):	The compensation rates to the manual scavengers is fixed at the national level at Rs. 40,000 which is to support them in resettlement. In Kerala, 600 manual scavengers have been identified in the National Survey in Kerala, of whom onetime cash assistance of Rs.40,000/- each has been paid to 510 families as per the guidelines of Self Employment Scheme for Rehabilitation of Manual Scavengers.As regards compensation paid to the families of persons who have died while cleaning sewers or septic tanks compensation of Rs. 10 lakh each has been paid to all the 13 such cases identified in Kerala from 1993 onwards, in accordance with the Supreme Court’s directives in its verdict dated 27.03.2014 in Civil Writ Petition No. 583 of 2003 Safai Karamchari Andolan Vs. Union of India and Others.(b) & (c): State-wise details of manual scavengers identified and onetime cash assistance paid are at Annexure-I.
