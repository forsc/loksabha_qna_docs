                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3744






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Setting up of TV Transmitters in Backward Areas




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details and the number of TV transmitters set up and presently working in the country especially in the backward and rural areas as on date; (b) 	whether various projects are pending for want of financial and technical sanction in various States; (c) 	if so, the details thereof State/UT-wise including backward and rural areas in the country; (d) 	the time by which these projects are likely to be granted sanction; and (e) 	the time by which the construction work is likely to be started, location-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a)  	 Presently, there are 338 TV transmitters (315 analogue & 23 Digital) of varying capacity located in various parts of country, including backward & rural areas.(b)	No project is pending currently for want of financial and technical sanction. (c) to (e) Do not arise.
