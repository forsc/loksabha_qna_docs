                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2129






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Medicines derived from Cow Urine/Dung




Vijay Baghel













                                                    Arun Sao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has conducted any study/research for products/medicine made from Panchagavya including Cow urine/Cow dung;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has taken any steps for marketing and promoting product/medicines made from Cow urine/Cow dung; and(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a): Yes.(b)      The  Central Council of Research in Ayurvedic Sciences, an autonomous body under the aegis  of Ministry of AYUSH,  has conducted a scientific study (safety/toxicity and biological activity) on Panchgavya Ghrita,  an Ayurvedic formulation in which Cow urine is one of the ingredients.             Being an Ayurvedic formulation, “Panchgavya Ghrita” is mentioned in Ayurvedic classics and is also referred in Ayurvedic Formulary of India.  It is a “cow-ghee” based product containing 5 ingredients including cow’s urine.           Panchgavya Ghrita was prepared at Pharmacopial Laboratory of Indian Medicines (PLIM), Gazhiabad as per the Standard Operating Procedures of Ayurvedic Pharmacopia of India.  The quality analysis  of ingredients and the finished product was carried out at M/s ARBRO Pharmaceuticals Ltd., New Delhi.  The immunomodulatory  activity and toxicity studies of Panchgavya Ghrtia were carried out at Department of Pharmacology and Toxicology, College of Verinary Sciences  and Animal Husbandry, Jabalpur (MP).         The quality assured Panchgavya Ghrita was subjected to immunomodulatory activities on experimental albino rats.  The study revealed significant immunomodulatory activities in terms of increasing serum gammas globulin, neutrophil and adhesion count and absolute lymphocyte count.          The chronic toxicity studies on albino rats revealed the formulations Panchgavya Ghrita and found safe.     (c)     No.   (d)     Does not arise.
