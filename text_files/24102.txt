                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Schemes for Tribals Welfare in Jharkhand




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of quantum of funds allocated and utilised by the State Government of Jharkhand for schemes of tribal welfare during the last three years and the current year; (b)	if so, the details of the amount utilised along with the details of works undertaken using these funds; and (c)	if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SHRI ARJUN MUNDA) (a)	to (c):  A statement is laid on the table of the house. Statement referred to in reply to part (a) to (c) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No.*14 for answer on 19.07.2021 tabled by DR.
