                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

189






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Revision of Norms of Annapurna Yojana




Rajmohan Unnithan














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any plan to revise the norms of Annapurna Yojana to bring eligible people under its coverage with special reference to Kerala where social security pension covers almost all the sections of people in the community, thus, as per the existing norms, almost all eligible people are excluded from the Annapurna Yojana ; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a)& (b):	No Sir. At present there is no proposal to revise the norms under Annapurna scheme.****
