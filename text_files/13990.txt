                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2721






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Adoption of Children with Special Needs




Prasun Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether it is true that very few parents have adopted children with special needs and if so, the details thereof; and(b)	the status of adoption of children with special needs?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b) As per the Child Adoption Resource Information and Guidance System (CARINGS), an online portal of the Central Adoption Resource Authority (CARA), 1614 Special Need Children have been placed in-country and inter-country adoptions during the last five years. The year-wise figures of adoptions of special needs children are as under :- Year	In-country	Inter-country	Total2015-16	76	294	3702016-17	49	242	2912017-18	42	349	3912018-19	40	362	4022019-20 (upto 03/03/2020)	11	149	160Total	218	1396	1614 *****
