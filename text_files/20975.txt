                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2264






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Development of Historical Tourist Places in Nalanda




Kaushalendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes any action plan to develop all the historical tourist places and heritage sites in Nalanda, Bihar in view of the historical importance of these places;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the total funds utilized for the development of historical places in Bihar during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)&(b)	Conservation, preservation and annual maintenance of all mounds, structures and ancient buildings at Nalanda declared as of national importance under Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act, 1958 is taken up by Archaeological Survey of India as regular activity.  Besides environmental development, visitors facilities like drinking water, toilet, pathways, sitting benches, signages, free publication, etc. are also provided, at the site.  (c)	Total funds utilized for conservation preservation and development of facilities at centrally protected monuments in Bihar during the last five years are as under: Year	Amount(Rupees in lakhs)2015-16	374.992016-17	153.532017-18	195.002018-19	175.982019-20	145.45*****
