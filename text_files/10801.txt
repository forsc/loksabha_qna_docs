                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4323






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Hostels for Working Women




Dilip Ghosh














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the details of the guidelines for the scheme “Hostels for Working women”. (b) 	the details of funds provided to West Bengal for the Hostels of the working women along with the fund sharing pattern between Centre and States; and(c)  	the list of hostels for which funds was allotted in West Bengal and its present status?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF  WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPEMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) 	Working Women’s Hostel Scheme  is for  promoting availability of  safe  and  conveniently located  accommodation  for  working  women,  with  day  care  facility  for  their  children, wherever possible, in urban, semi urban, and even rural areas where employment opportunity for women exists. The scheme assists projects for construction of new hostel buildings, expansion of existing hostel buildings and also for running hostels in rented premises. Detailed guidelines of the Scheme are available on the Ministry’s website i.e.,https://wcd.nic.in (b) 	No funds have been provided to West Bengal for Working Women Hostel during the last three years and current year as no proposal has been received from the State Govt. of West Bengal. The funding pattern of the Scheme is 60:15:25 between Central Government, States/ UTs (other than NE and Himalayan States) and Implementing Agencies for construction of building of Working Women Hostel/running hostel in rented premises. For NE and Himalayan States, the ratio will be 65:10:25.(c) 	List of Working Women Hostels sanctioned in West Bengal is placed at Annexure I.
