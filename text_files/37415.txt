                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4492






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Data Network related Issues




M. P. Abdussamad Samadani














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether BSNL has taken steps to address the Data network related issues in Kerala; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) has fixed any time limit to fully implement 4G wireless services in Kerala; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof including the delay, if any?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) & (b) 	Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) has 4731 mobile sites with 3G data and 913 sites with 4G data in Kerala circle. In addition, BSNL augments its network based on techno-commercial considerations.(c) to (d) 	Under Atma-nirbhar initiative of the Government, BSNL invited the Expression of Interest (EoI) on 01.01.2021 for Proof of Concept (PoC) for Indian Companies interested in participating in BSNL’s upcoming 4G tender. BSNL would rollout 4G services in Kerala circle based on techno-commercial considerations.
