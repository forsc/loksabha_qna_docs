                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4486






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Compensation for Land Acquired for Border Fencing




Jasbir Singh Gill













                                                    Gopal Chinayya Shetty
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether compensation has been given to the farmers whose land falls ahead of Barbed wire on international borders with Pakistan as per directions of Supreme Court;(b) if so, the amount of compensation given to the farmers including in Tarn Taran district of Punjab;(c) the number of years since when the compensation has not been paid to the farmers whose land falls beyond Barbed wire, year-wise and              district-wise; and(d) the time by which the said compensation is likely to be paid?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (d):	On the Order of Hon’ble High Court of Punjab & Haryana, Rs. 44,53,62,800/-  has been deposited as share of compensation by Union of India with State Govt. up to the year 2017 for further distribution to farmers in Punjab. The compensation for remaining period will be paid after receiving demand for further release of Central Governments share from the State Government of Punjab.********
