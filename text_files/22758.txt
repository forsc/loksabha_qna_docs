                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3714






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Grant of NH Status




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to grant status of National Highways (NHs) to Bhawanipatna - Muniguda - Bissamcuttack – Rayagada – Parvatipuram -Vizianagaram stretches;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) whether the Government also proposes to grant NHs status to Gunupur -   Baliguda (Phulbani) via Padmapur - Gudari - Chandrapur stretches; (d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d)  Ministry receives proposals for declaration of various State Roads/ Highways including in-Principle approved National Highways (NHs) such as state road of Bhawanipatna- Muniguda- Bisamcuttack- Rayagada- Parvatipuram- Vizainagaram stretch, Gunupur-Baliguda (Phulbani) via Padmapur - Gudari - Chandrapur stretch etc., as new NHs from various States/Union Territories (UTs). Ministry considers declaration of some of these State Roads/ Highways including In-Principle approved NHs as NHs from time to time keeping in view the requirement of connectivity, inter-se priority and availability of funds". *****
