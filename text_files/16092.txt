                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4616






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Irregularities in ESI Hospitals




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has prepared any comprehensive programme for employees for their health and safety in the country;(b)if so, the details thereof;(c)whether it is a fact that there are mass irregularities in ESI Hospitals at Telangana and Andhra Pradesh; (d)if so, the details thereof; and (e)the action taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b):	For promoting health and safety, the Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) has launched Annual Preventive Health Check up of all Insured Persons (IPs) of age 40 years and above in ESIC/ESI Scheme (ESIS) hospitals/equipped dispensaries.The health check-up, besides routine General Physical/Clinical Examination, includes following investigations:1.	Haemoglobin (Hb), Total Leucocyte Count (TLC), Differential Leucocyte Count (DLC), Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate (ESR)2.	Random Blood Sugar3.	Kidney Function Test-Blood Urea, Serum CreatinineContd..2/-:: 2 ::4.	Liver Function Test- Bilirubin, Serum Glutamic Oxaloacetic Transaminase (SGOT), Serum Glutamic Pyruvic Transaminase (SGPT), Serum Alkaline Phosphatase.5.	Urine-Routine and Microscopy6.	X-ray chest- PA (posteroanterior)  view7.	Electrocardiogram (ECG)(c) to (e):	A news article on discrepancy in purchase of medicines for State Government run ESIS hospitals in Telangana was reported to ESIC Hqrs office. Since the matter pertains to State Government of Telangana, Principal Secretary, Labour Department, Govt. of Telangana has been asked to send a factual report in this regard. Similarly, for Andhra Pradesh, representations have been received from Trade Union regarding irregularities in ESI hospital, Andhra Pradesh for which State Govt. has been requested to send report to Hqrs. Office.Further, from time to time, ESIC issues guidelines to all State Governments for procurement of medicines through Director General, ESIC Rate Contract/State Rate Contract in compliance with prescribed Financial Rules.*******
