                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1543






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Proposal for New Railway Zone in Kerala




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government  has decided to sanction railway zone for Kerala State;(b)  if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons thereto;(c) whether the Government has received a new proposal for the railway zone in Kerala State;(d)  if so, the details thereof;(e) whether the Government is intending to bifurcate or merge Trivandrum and Madurai divisions in Southern Railway; and(f)  if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Railways in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)	[	(a) to (f): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.   ***** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) To (f) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1543 BY SHRI KODIKUNNIL SURESH TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 28.07.2021 REGARDING PROPOSAL FOR NEW RAILWAY ZONE IN KERALA(a): No, Sir.(b): New zones on Indian Railways are created keeping in view the factors like size, workload, accessibility, traffic patterns and other operational/administrative requirements consistent with the needs of economy and efficiency. The proposal for creation of new Railway Zone in Kerala, when examined from these aspects, has not been found feasible.(c) & (d): Requests for formation of a new railway zone in Kerala have been received from time to time from various fora including Government of Kerala and Members of Parliament.(e): No, Sir.(f): Does not arise.*****
