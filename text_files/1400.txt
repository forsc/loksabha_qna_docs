                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Funds to Government Hospitals




Sukanta Majumdar














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the allocation made/grant provided/ fund made available by the Government during the last three years to Government funded hospitals in Delhi and West Bengal, hospital-wise; and (b) the number of OPD patients registered and surgeries performed in Central Government funded hospitals in Delhi and West Bengal during the last three years, hospital, State-wise?
