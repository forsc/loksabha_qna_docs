                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

545






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni













                                                    Gaddam Ranjith Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) whether Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan (NYKS) is one of the largest youth organizations in the world and if so, the salient features thereof;(b) the details of NYKS clubs in the country with a particular reference to Andhra Pradesh;(c) whether it is true that out of nine posts of District Youth Coordinators in District Nehru Yuva Kendras in Telangana, five are lying vacant and five Multi Tasking Staff are also lying vacant;(d) the details of challenges that NYKS is facing including posts lying vacant;(e) the efforts being made to address these challenges and the steps taken to fill up the vacancies; and(f) whether there is no District Nehru Yuva Kendra in Ranga Reddy district of Telangana and if so, the steps taken to set up one there?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan(NYKS) is an autonomous organization under Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports having its presence in 623 districts with its network of 2.55 Lakhs village based Youth Clubs with an enrolment of about 50.80 Lakhs rural youth as members, Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan qualifies as one of the largest youth organizations in the world.  The salient features of NYKS is at Annexure-I.(b) The core strength of NYKS is a network of 2.55 Lakhs Youth Clubs across India with an enrolment of about 50.80 Lakhs youth volunteers. State wise details of number of NYKS Youth Clubs and their members are at Annexure-II. In Andhra Pradesh, there are 9978 Youth Clubs with a membership of 92,970 youth.(c) No Sir, all the posts of District Youth Officer(DYO) in Telangana are filled up, 9 posts by regular DYOs and one post on additional charge basis. All the district NYKs in Telangana has been filled either by regular or outsourced MTS. (d & e) NYKS are functioning efficiently. While every effort is made to fill up the existing vacancies expeditiously, vacancies in NYKS do keep on arising on account of retirement, resignation or promotion. The NYKS is committed to speedy filling up of vacancies as per laid down procedure.(f) NYKS has its district office in Ranga Reddy district of Telangana since June 1989.***
