                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2994






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Availability of Raw Material in MSMEs




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any assessment has been made by the National Small Industries Corporation and Khadi Village Industries Commission (KVIC) about the liquidity, labour, logistics and availability of raw materials in MSMEs units in the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the details about the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises functioning in Bihar particularly in district Gopalganj and financial assistance provided to them; and (d) 	the measures taken by the Government to protect the MSME industry in rural areas of Bihar particularly in district Gopalganj?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a) & (b): 	Yes Sir.  National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC) Ltd. has conducted three Online Surveys during the year 2020.  A number of 5774 MSMEs responded during the survey conducted on 2-6 Jun; 3923 MSMEs responded during 15-20 Jul; and 3183 MSMEs responded during 9-14 Aug. 2020. The analysis of data has been done using MS-Excel.  However, Khadi Village Industries Commission (KVIC) has not made any such assessment. (c):	Details of financial assistance through National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC) Ltd. under Raw Material Assistance (RMA) against Bank Guarantee (BG), performance under Raw Material Distribution (RMD) in the state of Bihar and total number of units setup, Margin Money disbursed and employment generated under Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) through KVIC in Gopalganj District, Bihar during the last three years is placed at Annexure.(d): 	Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) is implementing the Khadi Vikas Yojana (KVY), Gramodyog Vikas Yojana (GVY), Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI) and Prime Minister Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) for promotion of Khadi and Village Industries in the country including in the State of Bihar.
