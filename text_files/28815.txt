                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

301






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Anti-Human Trafficking Units




Kumbakudi Sudhakaran













                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Anti-Human Trafficking Units (AHTUs) established under the Advisory on preventing and combating human trafficking especially during the period of COVID-19 pandemic, State-wise;(b) the details of bi-annual crime reviews conducted by the Government to monitor the progress of AHTUs, State-wise and year-wise, from 2014 till date; and(c) whether it is a fact that bi-annual crime review meetings with State Nodal Officers have not been conducted since 2017, and if so, the reasonstherefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI AJAY KUMAR MISHRA)(a):	As per National Crime Records Bureau’s annual publication “Crime in India 2020”, a total of 696 AHTUs have been established in various States and UTs. State/UT-wise details are given in the Annexure.--2--LS.US.Q.NO. 301 FOR 30.11.2021The Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) has provided financial assistance of approximately Rs 100 crores to all States and Union Territories (UTs) in the financial year 2019-20 and 2020-21 for strengthening the existing Anti Human Trafficking Units (AHTUs) in the States and UTs and for establishing new AHTUs in all districts of the States and UTs. MHA has issued an Advisory to all the States and UTs on preventing and combating human trafficking, especially during the period of Covid-19 pandemic on 6.7.2020, inter-alia, advising them to make the AHTUs functional on urgent basis.(b) & (c):	Engagement with States and UTs is a continuous process. MHA has been convening meetings with State/UT Nodal Officers of Human Trafficking on a periodic basis. MHA had reviewed the implementation of various Women Safety projects with States and UTs in December 2020, including the scheme for setting up/strengthening Anti-Human Trafficking Units, through a virtual meeting with State/UT functionaries. MHA has also established an online monitoring portal in January, 2021 for the States and UTs to update the progress of AHTUs on this portal and for sharing their best practices and success stories.******
