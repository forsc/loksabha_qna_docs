                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1077






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Launching of Audio-Visual Guide App




Sanjeev Kumar Singari













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the National Gallery of Modern Art (NGMA), New Delhi has launched an Audio-Visual Guide App recently. (b)	whether through this APP, will it be possible for museum viewers to listen to anecdotes and stories related to the Indian modern art exhibited at the Gallery; (c)	if so, the details thereof; and (d)	whether this APP is limited only to Art displayed at NGMA or other similar galleries across India and the details thereof ?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM  AND DEVLOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION (SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c) Yes Sir, NGMA has launched an Audio Visual Guide App on 18th May, 2021.There are two ways of listening to Audio-Guide:i.	By downloading and using App.ii.	By scanning the QR Code by a smart phone.There are presently two tours on the App, each in English and Hindi i.	NGMA Gallery Tourii.	NGMA Sculpture Garden There are 89 Audio Points, presently available for user’s access (Annexure-I).(d)	Yes, this APP is limited only to Art displayed at NGMA, New Delhi.
