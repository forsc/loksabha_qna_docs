                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

81






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






FAME-II Scheme




Sanjeev Kumar Singari













                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details and the current status of progress achieved in Faster Adoption and Manufacturing of (Hybrid &) Electric Vehicles in India (FAME India) II Scheme with respect to construction of Electric Charging Stations in the country including Andhra Pradesh; and(b)	whether the infrastructure of charging stations is available across the country, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) & (b):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****STATEMENTSTATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) & (B) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 81 FOR 08.02.2022 ASKED BY DR. SANJEEV KUMAR SINGARI, SHRI MAGUNTA SREENIVASULU REDDY REGARDING “FAME-II SCHEME”.(a):	Sir, Phase-II of Faster Adoption and Manufacturing of (Hybrid &) Electric Vehicles in India (FAME India) is being implemented for a period of 5 years w.e.f. 01st April, 2019 with a total budgetary support of Rs. 10,000 crores.  Under Phase-II of FAME-India Scheme, Rs. 1000 Cr. is allocated for the development of charging infrastructure. This Ministry has sanctioned 2,877 Electric Vehicle Charging Stations in 68 cities across 25 States/UTs. Further, 1576 charging stations across 9 Expressways and 16 Highways under Phase II of FAME India Scheme has also been sanctioned.  The state-wise details of charging stations as sanctioned under phase-II of FAME-India Scheme including Andhra Pradesh are attached at ANNEXURE.(b):	The Ministry of Heavy Industries had also sanctioned 520 Charging Stations/ for developing charging Infrastructure under Phase-I of FAME-India Scheme.  Under FAME India Scheme I & II, a total of 475 charging stations have been installed as on 31stJan 2022 as per ANNEXURE.Further, as per information received from Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas, 1536 electric vehicle charging stations have been installed at Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) Retail Outlets (ROs) in the country as on 01.01.2022.***** ANNEXURE1.
