                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1391






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2022






Allotment of Petroleum Outlets




D. M. Kathir Anand













                                                    Annasaheb Shankar Jolle
                                                








                                                    Umesh G. Jadhav
                                                








                                                    B. Y. Raghavendra
                                                








                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                








                                                    Sanganna Amarappa Karadi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government oil PSUs and private oil companies have allotted new petrol, diesel and CNG gas outlets and domestic cooking gas outlets in the country during the last five years particularly in Tamil Nadu; (b) if so, the details thereof, State and year-wise;(c) the details of steps taken by the Government to provide level playing ground as well as win-win situation for these new outlets to sustain their business competing with the existing retail outlets; and (d) whether the Government proposes to provide piped gas connection to all households in the metropolitan cities of the country and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a)&(b)   Allotment of Retail Outlets by Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) and private oil companies is a continuous process to ensure adequate availability of motoring fuels such as petrol and diesel throughout the country. The appointment of LPG distributorships is also a continuous process and the locations for setting up of LPG distributorships are identified based on sale potential that makes them commercially viable. State/year-wise details of Petrol Pumps and LPG Distributorships allotted (Letter of Intent issued) by the Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) in the country including Tamil Nadu during the last five years are given at Annexure-1 and Annexure-2 respectively.   (c)   Once the retail outlet is commissioned, Public Sector OMCs provide training to the dealers on various facets to manage the business and provide necessary support and guidance from time to time to improve their business. (d)   Upto the 10th City Gas Distribution bidding round, PNGRB has authorized 228 Geographical Areas (GAs) in 27 States/ Union Territories (GAs) inter alia covering the metropolitan cities of Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai & Kolkata. The Piped Natural Gas (PNG) connections are provided according to the Minimum Work Plan approved by PNGRB, and on demand of consumers. As on 30.11.2022, the number of PNG connections provided in Delhi and Mumbai are as below :State 	No. of PNG connections Delhi	10,65,662Mumbai	9,38,747 Number of PNG connections in Chennai and Kolkata is nil, as the necessary infrastructure for PNG is under development.   State/UT/Year-wise details of CNG Stations in the country including Tamil Nadu during the last five years (April 2016 to March 2021) are given in Annexure-3. ****
