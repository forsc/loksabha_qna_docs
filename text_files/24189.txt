                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

225






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Cultural Mapping Data on Art and Literature




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken any steps for cultural mapping data on art and literature 	forms of the State of Karnataka;(b)	 if so, the details thereof;(c)	 whether the Government has taken any steps to promote Karnataka  cultural heritage 	including Sharana, Sufi and Dasa saahitya in the country;  and (d)	 if so, the details thereof and the response of the Government in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b) Ministry of Culture is working on the National Mission on Cultural Mapping (NMCM) for building a comprehensive data base of artists, art forms and other resources. Objectives stated are as under: 	i.	National Cultural Awareness Abhiyan : Hamari Sanskriti Hamari Pahchan Abhiyan 		or Our 	Culture  Our Identity.	ii.	Nationwide Artist Talent Hunt/Scouting Programme: Sanskritik Pratibha Khoj 			Abhiyan.	iii.	National Cultural Workplace: Centralized Transactional Web Portal with database 		and demography of cultural assets and resources including all art forms and artists.   (c) & (d) There are 7 Zones in which Cultural Centres have been established. Karnataka is a member State of South Zone Cultural Centre (SZCC), Thanjavur and South Central Zone Cultural Centre (SCZCC), Nagpur. These ZCCs engage artists from the State of Karnataka to showcase their art/talent at Pan-India level. Besides, Sahitya Akademi which is also an autonomous body under Ministry of Culture is involved in publishing books related to Sharana, Sufi and Dasa Sahitya which is a part of Cultural Heritage of Karnataka. A list of prominent works on the subject published by the Sahitya Akademi is Annexed.  ANNEXUREList of Prominent Publications of Sahitya Akademi on Sharana, Sufi and Dasa Sahitya. 1.	Sri Basavannanara Vachana Sangraha (Kannada) (Selected Vachanas of Basavanna)Compiled by S.S. Malwad 2.	Basavesvara (Monograph on Kannada Saint Poet in English) By H. Thipperudraswamy(The monograph is available in Hindi, Maithili, Malayalam, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu and Urdu translations) 3.	Purandaradasaru (Monograph in Kannada) By G. Varadaraja Rao  	(The monograph is available in Hindi, Punjabi, Tamil and Urdu translations) 4.	Songs of Purandaradasa (English) Comp. & Tr. by Mydur Raghunandana 5.	Sarvajana Vachana Sangrah (Kannada) (Selected Vachanas of Saint Poet Sarvajana)Edited by M. Mariappa Bhatta
