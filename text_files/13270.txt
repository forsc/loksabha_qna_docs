Coalfield Projects in Madhya Pradesh




Ganesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       the district-wise details of the ongoing coalfield projects in Madhya Pradesh along with land acquired by the Government against the said projects; (b)     the details of the mining works carried out in these projects so far and the comparative details of the revenue generated from these projects; (c )  the details of mining work yet to be completed in these projects and whether any new projects are to be started in areas other than these areas and if so, the details thereof; (d)   whether compensation against land acquisition is pending with the Government; and(e )   if so, the details thereof along  with details of land acquired by the Government against the ongoing coalfield projects in Madhya Pradesh- district-wise ?
