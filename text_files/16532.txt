                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

263






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Deaths of Paramilitary Forces Personnel




Pratima Bhoumik













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Faizal P.P. Mohammed
                                                








                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any data with regard to the deaths of Paramilitary forces personnel during their service period/on duty, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(b) the number and details of jobs given on compassionate ground to the kins/dependents of the  deceased personnel during the last three years, Statewiseand year wise;(c) the number and details of cases pending with the Government with regard to giving jobs on compassionate ground to the dependents of the deceased personnel who have died during their service period; and(d) the details of the action taken/being taken by the Government so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	The detail of deaths in respect of Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs) and Assam Rifles (AR) personnel during their service period/on duty during the last three years is at Annexure-‘A’.(b):	The details of jobs given on compassionate ground to the kins/ dependents of the deceased personnel during the last three years is at Annexure-‘B’.-2/…-2-LS.US.Q.NO.263 FOR 15.09.2020(c) & (d) : The force-wise details of pending cases as on date  is as under:-Force	No. of cases pendingCRPF	37BSF	24ITBP	155SSB	0CISF	75AR	0As per Department of Personnel and Training (DoP&T) guidelines, Compassionate Appointments are made up to a maximum of 5% of the vacancies falling under direct recruitment quota in Group-‘C’ posts. It is an ongoing continuous process.
