                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2870






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Upgrading Railway Stations in Assam




Kripanath Mallah














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of railway stations in Assam that have been identified for upgrading to airport standards;(b) the district-wise details thereof;(c) the details of funds sanctioned, allocated and expended in Assam so far for their upgradation, district-wise;(d) the details of the targets set and the achievements made so far; and(e) whether the Government is planning to include some more railway stations of Assam under this scheme and if so, the details thereof and the details of funds estimated in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	 to (e): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.***** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2870 BY SHRI KRIPANATH MALLAH TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 11.03.2020 REGARDING UPGRADING RAILWAY STATIONS IN ASSAM (a) to (e): It is Railway’s endeavour to redevelop Railway stations by leveraging real estate potential of sparable railway land and air space in and around the station, generally at no cost to Railways. Railway conducts techno-economic feasibility studies of stations across the country.  Based on the outcome of these feasibility studies, stations are taken up for redevelopment in phases. 		Kamakhya railway station in the State of Assam has been identified for redevelopment and the process of preparation of Detailed Project Report has been initiated. *****
