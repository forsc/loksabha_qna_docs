                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

462






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Employment Generation through FDI




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any assessment of employment generated by Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) in the country has been made; and(b) 	if so, the details of job opportunities created during each of the last three years? 






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b): 	Data is not centrally maintained for assessing the impact of FDI on the employment generation. Foreign Direct Investment inflows serve to augment domestic capital and help to promote industrial development and employment generation across sectors. *****
