                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1597






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Setting up of Procurement Centres




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to set up procurement centres at village level; (b)	 if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government proposes to set up procurement centres in villages of Vadodara district in Gujarat; (d) 	if so, the number thereof and the details of the villages where these procurement centres have been set up so far; and (e) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Procurement centres are opened by respective State Government agencies/ Central Government agencies after mutual consultations and taking into account the procurement potential areas, production, marketable surplus, convenience of farmers and availability of other logistics/infrastructure such as storage and transportation etc. Further, large number of temporary purchase centres in addition to the existing Mandis and depots/godowns are also established at key points for the convenience of the farmers.(c) to (e):	Seven procurement centres have been operated in Vadodara district in Gujarat for Minimum Support Price (MSP) operations during the year 2018-19 in the APMCs at Vadodara, Dabhoi, Karajan, Savali, Vaghodia, Padara and Shinor.*******
