                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2442






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Optimal Harvesting of Marine Fisheries




Rahul Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the measures being taken to promote optimal harvesting of marine fisheries, particularly high value species like tuna; (b)  the measures being taken to improve hygiene and sanitary conditions of fish landing centers in India;(c)  the details of fishing harbours and fish landing centers, State-wise; and (d)  the details of the financial assistance provided for setting up fishing harbours and fish landing centers in Kerala?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRYAND DAIRYING    	(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a):  	The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying, under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) on Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries provides financial assistance  to the traditional fishermen of Coastal States/UT Governments for (i) procurement of resource specific deep-sea fishing vessels like tuna long liners  (ii) conversion of trawlers into resource specific deep-sea fishing vessels, and (iii) Capacity building and training to traditional fishermen  as measures towards harvesting of high value species like tuna and allied resources . Under the CSS, central financial assistance of Rs.312.09 crore has been released to various coastal State Governments/UTs during last two years i.e. 2017-18 to 2018-19 and current year (2019-20) for introduction of a total 918 deep sea fishing vessels and 313 traditional fishermen have been trained. Besides, Central financial assistance of Rs. 4.3 crore has been released for conversion of trawlers into resource specific deep sea fishing vessels in Andhra Pradesh. (b) :     The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying under the CSS on Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries inter-alia provides financial assistance to the tune of  (a) 50% of the project cost to State Government/State agencies and (b) 100% to Union Territories(UTs) & Central Government Agencies/ Organization for (i) construction of new fishing harbours and fish landing centres and (ii) Up-gradation/ expansion/ repair/ renovation of the existing fishing harbours and fish landing centres. The up-gradation/expansion/repair/renovation of the existing fishing harbours and fish landing centres primarily aims for improving the hygiene and sanitary conditions of the existing fishing harbours fish landing centres.(c) :	The State–wise details of fishing harbours and fish landing centres constructed with the central financial assistance are furnished at Annexure-I.(d) :   The Central financial assistance of Rs.10094.25 lakh has been provided to the Government of Kerala for development of fishing harbours and fish landing centres during past four years including current year as per details below: Sl No.	Financial Year	Central fund released (Rs in lakh)1.	2015-16	2900.0002	2016-17	1126.6253	2017-18	31.1254	2018-19	4686.5005	2019-20 (till date)	1350.000	Total 	10094.25*****
