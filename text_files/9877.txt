                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3296






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Transfer of Spouses in Kendriya Vidyalaya




Kaushal Kishore













                                                    Upendra Singh Rawat
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details regarding pendency of cases of TGT/PGT primary teachers wheretheir spouse intends to be transferred to the same station with KendriyaVidyalaya Sangathan since the last three years till date;(b) the reasons for delay; and(c) the terms and conditions of the transfer policy wherein both the husband and wifeare KV teachers?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) The details of transfers effected in the last three years is as follows:-<pre>Year Number of TGTs/PGTs andPrimary Teachers applied forrequest transfer on spousegroundNumber ofSpouse caseseffectedNumber of spouse caseswhich could not be effecteddue to lack of vacancy2017 1386 633 7532018 1280 537 7432019 1266 503 763</pre>(b) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) has informed that there is an establishedprocedure of transfer of employees of KVS. Every year online transfer applications areinvited from all employees at the time of annual transfer. Request transfers of employeesare considered on the basis of transfer counts.Different transfer counts are provided fordifferent categories of spouses such as spouse of KVS, defence employees, employeesof paramilitary forces and other government employees and spouse of those who arenot working in Government sectors.Spouses having higher transfer counts areconsidered in decreasing order till the available vacancies are exhausted / filled. Thereare many spouse cases which could not be considered due to less transfer counts andnon-availability of vacancies at the desired place.(c) KVS has further informed that if both husband and wife are KVS employees, theyare provided with highest transfer counts i.e. 50 (On spouse ground).*****
