                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2342






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Celebration of Birth Anniversary of Guru Nanak Devji




Gurjeet Singh Aujla














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to celebrate the 550th birth anniversary of Guru Nanak Devji in the last quarter of 2019;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the steps taken and funds allocated in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM.(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)a) to (c): Union Cabinet in its meeting held on 22.11.2018 decided to celebrate 550th birth anniversary of Shri Guru Nanak Devji at national and international level for a period of one year from November, 2018 to November, 2019. Inaugural function for the commemoration was held on 23.11.2018 at Sultanpur Lodhi, Punjab. Decisions taken in the meeting are as under:(i)	Development of Kartarpur corridor from Dera Baba Nanak in Gurdaspur district, Punjab to the international border.  Foundation stone for the project was laid by Hon’ble Vice President on 26.11.2018.(ii)	Development of historic town of Sultanpur Lodhi as a heritage town on the Smart City principles.(iii)	Upgradation of Sultanpur Lodhi railway   station with all the modern amenities and run a train passing through various holy places associated with  Shri Guru Nanak Dev Ji, for pilgrims and tourists.(iv)	Release of Commemorative Coin and postage stamps.(v)	 Setting up of a Centre for Inter Faith Studies at Guru Nanak Dev University,              Amritsar and to organize an International Seminar on the life and teachings of Shri Guru Nanak Dev Ji.(vi)	 To set up Chairs on Shri Guru Nanak Dev Ji in one University each in UK and   Canada.(vii) States/UTs to celebrate 550th Birth Anniversary of Shri Guru Nanak Dev Ji in a befitting manner.  Religious activities like Kirtan, Katha, Prabhat Pheri, Langar etc. to be organised throughout the country, especially at  Gurdwaras and places associated with Shri Guru Nanak Dev Ji.(viii) 	Doordarshan to arrange live telecast of programmes on Shri Guru Nanak Dev Ji and the Gurbani. (ix)	National Book Trust to publish Gurbani in different Indian Languages. UNESCO to translate and publish anthology of Shri Guru Nanak Devji’s writings in world languages.(x) Indian Missions overseas to organise special events to commemorate the 550th anniversary of the birth of Shri Guru Nanak Dev Ji. Separate funds have not been allocated for the purpose. It was decided in the meeting of Union Cabinet that financial implications of the proposals will be met by the respective Ministries from their allocated budget.
