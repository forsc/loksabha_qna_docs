                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1855






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Supplementary Nutrition Programme




Pramila Bisoyi














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    1855. 	SHRIMATI PRAMILA BISOYI: Will the Minister of WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state:  (a) 	the total Budget sought and approved for Supplementary Nutrition Programme and ICDS-General during 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21, up to 31 December 2020- State-wise, including Odisha; and (b) 	the total Budget allocated / released (both Central and States Share) for Supplementary Nutrition Programme and ICDS-General during 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21, up to 31 December 2020- State-wise, including Odisha? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) & (b) : The Anganwadi Services under umbrella ICDS is a centrally sponsored scheme implemented through the State Governments/UT Administrations. Funds are allocated to States/UTs on the basis of Annual Programme Implementation Plan (APIP) proposals received from the respective States/UTs.  Details of funds sought, approved and released including Odisha are given in Annexure.  Annexure (Rs.
