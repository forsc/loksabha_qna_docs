                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3677






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Ghani Khan Choudhary Institute of Engineering and Technology




Khagen Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the guidelines of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), University Grants Commission (UGC) and other regulatory bodies for recruitment process, qualifications of teaching staffs and other have been followed in recruitment of teaching and non-teaching staffs in Ghani Khan Choudhary Institute of Engineering and Technology (GKCIET);(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) & (b) Yes Sir.   The recruitment of teaching and non-teaching staff in GKCIET, during the last five years have been carried out as per Recruitment Rules and relevant guidelines issued by regulatory bodies like All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), University Grants Commission (UGC) and Memorandum of Association (MoA) of the institute. The total number of teaching and non-teaching staff recruited during the last five years i.e. after the appointment of the regular Director, GKCIET is as under:<pre>Year	Teaching Staff	Non-Teaching Staff2019	12	02021	20	16</pre>(c) Does not arise. ****
