                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

121






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Recovery under IBA Code




Mala Roy














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the total amount recovered under Indian Banks Association (IBA) Code, 2016 upto 30 June, 2021; and(b) the details of the total amount due verses amount recovered under the said code during the said period, per centage-wise?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a): The Indian Banks’ Association has apprised that it has not formulated any such code. (b): Question does not arise.                                          *****
