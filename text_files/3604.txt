                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3327






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Declining Child Sex Ratio




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 		whether a declining trend has been registered in the Child Sex Ratio (CSR) in some States/Union Territories of the country;(b) 		if so, the details thereof; (c) 		whether the Government has conducted any survey of the areas/districts where a decline has been observed in the CSR and if so, the details thereof; (d) 		the impact of the ‘Beti Bachao Beti Padhao’ (BBBP) scheme on the CSR of the country; and (e) 		the effective steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)  	& (b): Yes, Sir.  As per the census 2011, the Child Sex Ratio (CSR) in comparison of census 2001 has declined in 21 States/UTs.A statement regarding CSR for years 2001 and 2011 is annexed at Annexure-I. (c): 	No, Sir.  The Census 2011 was itself a surveyof the Government of India conducted through the Registrar General of India in every ten years to ascertain the factual status and obtaining data related to CSR.(d): 	As per the latest reports of Health Management Information System (HMIS) of Ministry of Health & Family welfare (MoHFW) reveal that Sex Ratio at Birth (SRB) is showing improving trends and SRB has increased from 923 to 931 at National level for the time period between F.Y. 2015-16 & 2018-19.  While at State/UT level, SRB in 21 States/UTs is showing improving trends whereas in 03 States/UTs, SRB is stable in F.Y. 2018-19 in comparison to previous F.Y. 2017-18.  (e): 	The Multi-sectoral interventions and Media Campaign & Advocacy in 405 Districts and Media Campaign & Advocacy only in 235 Districts of BBBP have been initiated for greater coverage and reach in the country. The BBBP Scheme is showing collective consciousness in the Country.  Under the BBBP Scheme, to sensitize the masses and for changing their mindset a Nation-wide campaign is being implemented which includes radio spots/jingles in Hindi and regional languages, video spots, SMS campaigns, community engagement through mobile exhibition vans, and field publicity Mailers, Hand-outs, Brochures and other education communication material in English, Hindi and regional languages has been adopted which includes social media platforms.********Annexure - IStatement refer to in reply to Part (a) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 3327 for 12.07.2019 regarding Declining Child Sex Ratio raised by Shri Nihal Chand.
