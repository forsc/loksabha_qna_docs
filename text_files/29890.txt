                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1546






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Survey of Fishing Ports




Lallu Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has conducted any survey to identify such fishing ports which lack infrastructure and other facilities; (b) whether the Government has received any proposal from States for conducting survey of fishing ports in coastal States; and (c) if so, the details of the proposals received for setting up fishing ports during the last three years and current year, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRYAND DAIRYING  (SHRI PARSHOTTAM  RUPALA)(a)	to (c): Formulation of proposals, construction, post-construction management of the  fishing harbours are done by the concerned State Governments /Union Territories(UTs) and in some cases by the Port Trusts.  To seek the central financial assistance under the Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY), the concerned State Governments/UTs/ Port Trusts are required to complete all pre-implementation activities such as (i) identification and selection of suitable site, (ii) completion of necessary engineering and socio-economic investigations and surveys, (iii) planning and designing of fishing harbour facilities, (iv) hydraulic model studies wherever required, (v) environmental impact studies, (vi) acquisition of necessary land and (vii) formulate of the Detailed Project Reports (DPRs). The concerned State Government/Port Trust is required to submit the DPR along with confirmation of availability of land, environmental clearance and willingness to meet their financial share for construction of new fishing harbours and also expansion of the existing fishing harbours for central financial assistance under PMMSY.  The Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and  Dairying,  Department of Fisheries through its institute  namely Central Institute of Coastal Engineering for Fishery(CICEF), Bengaluru also assists the State Governments/UTs/ Port Trusts in conducting necessary surveys to assess the infrastructure gaps in the existing fishing harbours, conducting required engineering & economic investigation and formulation  of proposals for construction of new fishing harbour facilities. Details of the fishing harbour proposals submitted under Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund (FIDF) are furnished at Annexure-I. In addition, the Union Government in its Budget 2021-22 has announced development of 5 major fishing harbours namely Kochi, Chennai, Visakhapatnam, Paradip and  Petuaghat as hubs of economic activities.*****
