                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

612






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






PMAY Beneficiaries in Chattisgarh




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of beneficiaries of Rajnandgaon and Kabirdham districts of Chhattisgarh which got benefitted under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban; (b)	the number of such beneficiaries who got the fund of first installment but not received second or third installments despite fulfilling all formalities, district-wise; and (c) 	the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                               (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a):   A total of 20,452 houses in Rajnandgaon District and 7,681 in Kabirdham District have so far been sanctioned in the State of Chhattisgarh under different verticals of the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U).(b)& (c):   As per the information provided by the State Government of Chhattisgarh, there are no such beneficiaries who have got the fund of first installment but not received second or third installments despite fulfilling all formalities, as per the scheme guidelines.                                              *****
