                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1949






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Implementation of DDU-GKY




Locket Chatterjee














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has been successful in implementing the Deen Dayal Upadhyaya-Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY);(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the number and details of trades in which training is being imparted; and(d) whether the State of West Bengal is being covered under this scheme and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b) The Government is implementing Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY), a placement linked skill development program for rural poor youth under National Rural Livelihoods Mission (NRLM) successfully all over the country. The achievements under DDU-GKY during the last four years and current year(upto January, 2020) is as below:-Year wise DDU-GKY Progress FY	           Trained	Placed2015-16	           236471	1095122016-17	           162586	1478832017-18	           131527	757872018-19 	   241080	1382482019-20  	  193704	125668(till January 2020)	(c)	Training under DDU-GKY is being imparted across 53 sectors and 555 trades as on date. The details in this regard is given at Annexure.(d)	Yes, Sir. DDU-GKY is being implemented all over the country including the State of West Bengal.
