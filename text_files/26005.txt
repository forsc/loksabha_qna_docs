                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1767






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Maintenance of NHs in Bihar




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken note of the dilapidated condition of various National Highways (NHs) in the Bihar particularly in district Gopalganj; (b) if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government thereon; (c) whether the Government has received any proposals from Bihar to improve the condition of NHs particularly in district Gopalganj; (d) if so, the details thereof along with the funds allocated, released and utilised for the maintenance and repair of NHs during the last two years and the current year, district-wise; (e) the steps taken by the Government to improve the condition of NHs in Bihar particularly in district Gopalganj; and (f) the details of NHs passing through district Gopalganj?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)	to (e) National Highways in district Gopalganj are in traffic worthy condition. Details of development works sanctioned on various NHs passing through Gopalganj District and work sanctioned for development & maintenance of National Highway work in last 3 years in state of Bihar is enclosed at Annexure. The maintenance & development of National Highways is a continuous process and the same is carried out based on the traffic, availability of Land and other clearances, inter-se-priority and availability of funds. Ministry allocates maintenance & repairs funds at State level and not District-wise.  The allocation and expenditure of fund for maintenance & repairs of NHs in State of Bihar in last two years and current year are as follows:(Amount in Crore)Year	Allocation	Utilized2019-20	93.82	85.432020-21	303.47	254.392021-22 till 25.07.2021	94.25	21.32(f)	Four NHs namely, NH-531 (old NH No. 85), NH-27 (Old NH-28), NH-331 (Old NH-101) and NH-227A passes through Gopalganj District.
