                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

451






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Software Technology Park




Hasmukhbhai Somabhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has established Software Technology Parks (STPs) in the country in last three years; (b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; (c) whether the Government has planned to established STP in Gujarat in the next five years; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b): Yes, Sir. Three Software Technology Parks of India (STPI) centres have been established in the last three years i.e. in Goa (2018), Deoghar - Jharkhand (2019) and Bhopal - Madhya Pradesh (2020).(c) and (d): Yes, Sir. Government has approved one STPI centre in Bhavnagar, Gujarat. Currently, the State Government has identified the land and is in process of transferring the land in favour of STPI.
