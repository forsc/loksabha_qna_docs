                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3892






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Defence PSUs




Shrirang Appa Barne













                                                    Girish Bhalchandra Bapat
                                                








                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                








                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the issue of the Defence PSUs reportedly being given undue preference by the Ministry for large orders has been raised by different quarters and if so, the details thereof;(b)  whether there is an inherent conflict of interest that prevents full unlocking of the ‘Make in India’ initiative and if so, the facts thereof;(c)  whether Union Government is examining recommendations by top two advisory bodies to move public sector units out of the supervision of the Defence Ministry;(d)  if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto;(e) whether National Security Advisory Board (NSAB) has recommended restructuring of the Defence Ministry to promote ‘Make in India’ if so, the details thereof and the response of the Government thereto; and(f)  the other steps taken or proposed to be taken by the Union Government to keep Defence manufacturing on a competitive basis, in which both PSUs and private industry may participate?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) to (b): No, Sir.(c)   No such recommendations have been received by this Ministry.(d)   Does not arise.(e) National Security Advisory Board (NSAB) has not made any formal recommendation on restructuring the Ministry of Defence. (f)   As per Annexure.*****
