                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1912






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Revival of Textile Mills




Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of textile mills which are operational across the country, State/UT-wise; (b) whether several textile mills are lying inoperative/ closed in the country, if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (c) whether any scheme has been formulated to set up textile mills in the country and if so, the details thereof;(d) the details of operational and non-operational textile mills in Odisha;(e) whether the Government of Odisha has given any proposal to the Union Government for revival of closed sick textile and spinning mills in the State; and(f) if so, the details thereof and action taken by Central Government thereon? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b):	As on 31.10.2020, there are 1360 operational and 649 inoperative/closed cotton/man-made fibre textile mills in the country. The details of State-wise number of operational and closed mills are at Annexure- I.   Closure of most of the mills took place before 2015.(c):	There is no scheme for setting up of textile mills in the country at present. However, the Government is implementing several schemes to support the Textile Industry which include the Amended Technology Up gradation Fund Scheme (ATUFS); Scheme for Integrated Textile Parks (SITP); Integrated Skill Development Scheme (ISDS); Technology Mission on Technical Textiles (TMTT) etc. The industry could avail benefits under the above schemes for upgrading/ reviving textile units.(d):		There are 3 operational   and 13   non-operational Textile Mills in Odisha as per  Annexure-II. (e):		No Sir.(f):	      Does not arise. ***
