                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2967






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Recognition to Roller Skate Basketball Game




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government is aware that roller skate basketball game is being played in the country on date without any recognition;b)	if so, whether any representation has been received requesting for recognition of the same Basketball Federations of India (BFI); and c)	if so, the reaction of the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a)	to (c) The Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports has not received request for recognition from any organization for roller skate basketball sport. *****
