                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

372






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Prices of Essential Commodities




Dayakar Pasunoori














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government is aware of the increase in the prices of essential commodities including onion; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the Government has imported hybrid onions from Turkey and other countries, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and (d) 	the steps being taken to control the prices of all essential commodities including onion, tomato, vegetables and pulses in future?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI DANVE RAOSAHEB DADARAO)(a) & (b) : The details of all India monthly average retail prices of 22 essential food items including onion is at Annexure.  (c)  : Government directed MMTC to import onion to improve domestic availability and moderate its prices. MMTC floated global/country specific tender to import onions.(d)  : Government has taken various measures from time to time to stabilize prices of essential food items which, inter-alia, include appropriately utilizing trade and fiscal policy instruments like import duty, Minimum Export Price, export restrictions, etc. to regulate domestic availability and moderate prices; imposition of stock limits and advising States for effective action against hoarders & black marketers ; and, provision of higher Minimum Support Prices to incentivize farmers for increasing production. Government is also implementing Schemes which, inter alia, include Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH), National Food Security Mission (NFSM), National Mission on Oilseeds and Oil Palm (NMOOP), etc for increasing agricultural production and productivity through appropriate interventions.
