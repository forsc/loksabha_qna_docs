                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

811






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Bids for City Gas Distribution Network




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board has invited bids for City Gas Distribution Network in the 65 geographical areas in 11th CGD bidding round; (b) if so, the details thereof, and (d) the details/list of geographical areas covered in Maharashtra State?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c)	Yes Sir. PNGRB has launched 11th bidding round on 17.09.2021 and invited bids for 65 Geographical Areas covering 208 districts in 20 States/Union Territories including Maharashtra. Geographical Areas identified for the 11th CGD Bidding Round in the state of Maharashtra are as under:S.No.	Geographical Areas (GAs)	State/UT1.		Buldana, Nanded and Parbhani districts	Maharashtra 2.		Beed, Jalgaon and Jalna districts	Maharashtra 3.		Akola, Hingoli and Washim districts	Maharashtra 4.		Amravati and Yavatmal districts	Maharashtra 5.		Chandrapur and Wardha districts	Maharashtra 6.		Nagpur district	Maharashtra 7.		Bhandara, Gondiya and Garchiroli districts	Maharashtra 8.		Alirajpur, Nandurbar and Barwani districts	Maharashtra & MadhyaPradesh****
