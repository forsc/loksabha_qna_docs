                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

800






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Khelo India Scheme




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has made any separate scheme for sportspersons of rural areas under ‘Khelo India’ scheme;  (b) if so, the details thereof;  (c) the State-wise details of the number of sportspersons selected so far under this scheme; and  (d) the details of the facilities being provided by the Government to sportspersons selected under the said scheme? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)  FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS  { SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }  (a) and (b) No Sir, there is no separate scheme for sportspersons of rural areas, as the Khelo India Scheme covers all sportspersons, including sportspersons of rural India.  (c) The State-wise details of athletes selected so far under Khelo India Scheme are given at Annexure – I.   (d) The following facilities are being provided by the Government to sportspersons under the Khelo India Scheme:  i. Out of pocket allowance ii. Coaching iii. Lodging and boarding iv. Sports kits v. Domestic travel vi. Insurance and medical benefits vii. Sports science management viii. Educational tie-up   ANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (c) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. †800 TO BE ANSWERED ON 06.02.2020 REGARDING “KHELO INDIA SCHEME” ASKED BY SHRI RAJESH VERMA, HON’BLE MEMBER OF LOK SABHA *****
