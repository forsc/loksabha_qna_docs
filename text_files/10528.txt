                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4134






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Air Strip in Azamgarh




Sangeeta Azad














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Manduri air strip at Azamgarh, Uttar Pradesh is being developed for domestic flight operation and(b) if so, the time by which the flight operation is likely to be started?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) and (b):  Airports Authority of India, the Implementing Agency for Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS)-UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik) had awarded routes connecting Azamgarh to Lucknow to the Selected Airline Operator (SAO) under RCS - UDAN on 24.01.2018. The same was cancelled due to non-compliance of SAO Agreement by SAO. If any bid is received, in future round of bidding under RCS-UDAN, the same will be considered as per the provisions of the scheme document.
