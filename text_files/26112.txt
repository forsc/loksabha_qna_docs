                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

180






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Swadhar Greh Scheme




D. M. Kathir Anand














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   	the number of women who have been provided with legal aid and rehabilitated under Swadhar Greh scheme so far in Tamil Nadu, district-wise; (b)   	the number of Swadhar Greh in Tamil Nadu, district wise; (c)   	the funds allocated and utilized during the last three years under the said scheme; and (d)   	whether all the women in Swadhar Greh have been vaccinated and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER

(b) The number of Swadhar Greh in Tamil Nadu, district-wise is at Annexure II. (c) The funds allocated and utilized during the last three years under said scheme in Tamil Nadu is at Annexure III.
