                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2971






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Poisonous Ground-Water




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of districts of Uttar Pradesh where people are suffering from various ailments due to the use of poisonous ground water; (b) the details of arsenic and other poisonous substances found in water of such districts along with the details of the arrangements made to provide safe drinking water to these areas; (c) the details of the programmes implemented by the Government in Uttar Pradesh to deal with the potable water crisis; and(d) the number of districts benefited from such programmes along with the details of the target fixed for future






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (b): As per the information entered by the State Government in the Integrated Management Information System(IMIS) of this Department, the District wise number of various quality affected rural habitations in Uttar Pradesh is placed at Annexure I.Drinking water supply is a State subject. For improving the coverage of safe drinkingwater to rural population, this Ministry supplements the efforts of the States byproviding them with technical and financial assistance through the centrally sponsoredscheme National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP). It is the StateGovernments who plan, design, approve, execute, operate & maintain the schemes forproviding safe drinking water to rural population.During March 2016, with the recommendation of NITI Aayog, an amount of Rs. 1000Crores was released to various Arsenic & Fluoride affected States/UTs for installation ofCommunitywater purification plants.Out of which Rs. 13.39 Cr. was provided to Uttar Pradesh State.In addition to this, the Ministry had launchedNational Water Quality Sub-Mission (NWQSM) on 22ndMarch 2017 under NRDWPtoprovide safe drinking water to 27,544 arsenic / fluoride affected rural habitationsinthecountry. An amount Rs. 49.95 Cr. has been released to Uttar Pradesh.Apart from above State can utilize NRDWP coverage funds for providing safe drinking water in Arsenic/Fluoride affected habitations.(c)&(d) According to the Govt. Of Uttar Pradesh, following programmesare being implemented in the State  to deal with the potable water:i.	National Rural Drinking water supply Programme. ii.	State Rural Drinking water supply Programme. iii.	Bundelkhand Package iv.	Border Area Development programme.v.	PardhanMantri Jan VikasKaryakram. According to the State Government, all the 75 district of the state are benefitted from the above programme. ******
