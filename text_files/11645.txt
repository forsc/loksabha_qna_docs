                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

590






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






ROBs/RUBs in Jharkhand




Annpurna Devi













                                                    Pashupati Nath Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received proposals from the Government of Jharkhand for the construction of Railway Over Bridges (ROBs) and Road Under Bridges during the last three years along with the details thereof, proposal-wise;(b)	the reaction of the Government on the said proposals;(c)	the number of proposals from Jharkhand that are lying pending with the Railways at present along with the details of the said proposals, location-wise; and(d)	the details of the ROBs and RUBs being constructed in Jharkhand at present, location-wise along with the current status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) : Railway have received proposals for construction of Road over Bridges/ Road under Bridges (ROBs/RUBs)  in lieu of Level Crossings (LCs) on cost sharing in the State of Jharkhand. Accordingly, the details of sanctioned ROBs/RUBs in Jharkhand during the last three years is as under:   2016-17	  2017-18	   2018-19	   TotalROB	RUB	ROB	RUB	ROB	RUB	ROB	RUB12	0	5	0	0	0	17	0(b): All proposals have been sanctioned. (c): No such valid proposals are pending with Railway.(d): As per Pink Book 2019-20, 41 ROBs and 109 Road under Bridges (RUBs) have been sanctioned in Jharkhand. Out of these, 3 ROBs and 22 RUBs have been fully completed. Railway portion of 3 ROBs have been completed. Work is in progress in 35 ROBs and 87 RUBs.*****
