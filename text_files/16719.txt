                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

460






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Inclusion of Disabled Persons under NFSA




Pritam Gopinath Munde













                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Central Government have requested the States to ensure inclusion of all eligible disabled persons under the National Food Security Act 2013; (b) 	if so, the details in this regard; (c) 	whether the Central Government have any data to find out the number of eligible disabled persons in various States as on 31st December, 2019; (d) 	if so, the details in this regard; and (e) 	the number of eligible disabled persons so far covered under the National Food Security Act, 2013 in various States?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) & (b): 	Yes Sir. All States/UTs have been directed under Section 38 of the National Food Security Act (NFSA) that disability as separate criteria within the eligibility criteria may be identified by all States/UTs for inclusion of persons with disability, subject to exclusion criteria, for coverage under National Food Security Act, 2013.   States need to ensure that all the disabled persons, who are eligible as per identification criteria of beneficiaries under NFSA, are covered under the National Food Security Act, 2013. Those who are not already covered should be covered with fresh ration cards to be issued as per the existing/revised eligibility criteria that include disability as separate criteria. If a disabled candidate is at par with another general candidate, preference should be given to disabled person. ........2/--2-(c) & (d): 	The latest available data of the number of disabled persons in the country is of the 2011 census covering 2.68 crore persons and may be seen at Annexure. The number of Disability Certificates issued by State Governments/Union Territories upto May 2020 is 1.66 crore.(e): 	Benefits under NFSA are being made available to all eligible disabled persons under NFSA.
