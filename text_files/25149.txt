UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

941






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Amount Spent on Maintenance of Historical Buildings




Chandeshwar Prasad













                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                








                                                    Rekha Arun Verma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number and details of historical buildings in the country, State-wise  including Bihar, Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh;(b)	the details of amount allocated and spent for the maintenance of these buildings during the last three years;(c)	the number of dilapidated historical buildings in the country especially in Bihar,   Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh and the number of buildings repaired during the last three years, year-wise and State-wise along with the details of the funds spent on the repair and maintenance of such buildings during the last three  years;(d)	whether the Government has conducted any survey regarding the buildings of  historical importance and if so, the number of such buildings which are in need of repair, State-wise especially in Bihar, Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh; and(e)	the number of tourists who have visited these monuments and the amount of revenue generated from these historical buildings during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a) 	The details of centrally protected monuments/sites including Heritage Buildings in country (State/UT –wise) are at Annexure-I.(b)&(c)	The details of allocation and funds spent State-wise on repair and maintenance of such monuments/sites including Heritage Buildings are at Annexure-II. There is no dilapidated monument in the country especially in Bihar, Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh.  The conservation and maintenance of Centrally protected monuments/sites is a regular process and necessary steps are taken by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) to conserve these monuments/sites including monuments/sites in Bihar, Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh as per approved Conservation programme.(d) Inspection of centrally protected monuments/sites is undertaken by ASI from time to time and conservation measures are taken up as per the need and priority within available resources.  5244 works under Annual Conservation Programme have been approved for centrally protected monuments in the country during 2021-22.
