                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4223






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






HRIDAY in Rajasthan




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Heritage City Development and Augmentation Yojana (HRIDAY) is being implemented in Rajasthan;(b)	if so, the cities of Rajasthan included in this scheme;(c)	the details of the work done during the last three years under HRIDAY scheme, city-wise; and (d)	the funds allocated/released/utilised under the above scheme during the last three years, city and year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS(SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) & (b) The National Heritage City Development and Augmentation Yojana (HRIDAY), a central sector scheme was launched on 21st January, 2015.  Under the scheme, one city viz. Ajmer in Rajasthan was included. The Mission period of the scheme has ended in March, 2019. (c) 	Following projects were taken up in Ajmer city in  Rajasthan:- SI.No.	Name of the Project1	Development of Heritage Walk connecting Akbari Quila and Sonijiki Nasiya2	Development of Jaipur Road Precinct 3	Development of Pushkar Heritage Walk 4	Development of Subhash Udyan5	Annasagar Lakefront Up-gradation 6	Development of Vehicular Surface Parking at Longia Hospital, Ajmer7	Development of Rail Museum at Ajmer (d) 	Under the HRIDAY Scheme, funds were not allocated to the cities year-wise but for the entire Mission period.A total amount of Rs. 40.04 crore was allocated to Ajmer out of which an amount of Rs. 34.17 crore was released to the city. The details of the funds released and utilized in Ajmer under HRIDAY scheme during the last two years are given as under:- (Rs. in crore)Name of the city	Funds released during F.Y. 2017-18	Funds released during F.Y. 2018-19	Total funds released during F.Y. 2017-18 & 2018-19	Utilization Certificates received till dateAjmer(Rajasthan)	6.72	8.20	14.92	26.10Since the Scheme has ended on 31st March, 2019,                         no funds were released during 2019-20.                                                  *****
