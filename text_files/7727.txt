                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1052






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






AYUSH CGHS Wellness Centres




Upendra Singh Rawat













                                                    Kaushal Kishore
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE  be pleased to state: (a) the number of AYUSH CGHS wellness centres available in the Country, particulary in Uttar Pradesh, district-wise; (b) the number of AYUSH wellness centres under consideration in Uttar Pradesh, particularly in Lucknow, districtwise; (c) the details of medical and associated staff strength sanctioned including doctors in the AYUSH Wellness Centres in Uttar Pradesh and the actual deployment thereof;(d) the details of employment generated through AYUSH in Uttar Pradesh during the last three years; and (e) the amount spent through AYUSH in Uttar Pradesh for training of youth during each of the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH ANDFAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):     Details of AYUSH CGHS Wellness Centres in India and in Uttar Pradesh (District-wise) are at Annexure-I. (b):     Posts of Medical Officer (AYUSH) have been sanctioned by the Government for setting up of new Ayurvedic Units and Homeopathic Units under CGHS, including 1 Ayurvedic Unit and 1 Homeopathic Unit each at Noida, Greater Noida, Sahibabad, Ghaziabad and Indirapuram in Uttar Pradesh. The process of recruitment of Medical Officers (AYUSH), which is done by Ministry of AYUSH, has been initiated.(c):      Details are at Annexure-II.-2-(d):      Ministry of AYUSH is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission through States/ UTs including Uttar Pradesh and providing financial assistance for development and promotion of AYUSH systems as per the proposal submitted by States/ UTs in their State Annual Action Plans. During last three years, Rs. 266.01 crore have been released to the State Government of Uttar Pradesh which resulted in the development and promotion of AYUSH System including generation of employment. However, data regarding employment generation is not being maintained centrally, as it is a State subject.(e):       No amount has been spent through AYUSH in Uttar Pradesh for training of youthduring each of the last three years.…………………
