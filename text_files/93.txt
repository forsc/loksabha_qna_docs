                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

124






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Awareness Regarding AYUSH




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has taken any initiative to spread awareness regarding homoeopathy and ayurveda and encourage their use in the country; (b) if so, the details of the number of AYUSH dispensaries and hospitals along with the number of AYUSH educational institutions running in the country, State/ UT-wise; and(c) the steps taken by the Government to increase access to Ayurveda and homoeopathy systems and strengthen these systems?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):     Under Central Sector Scheme for Promotion of Information, Education and Communication in AYUSH, the Ministry takes up initiatives for propagation, promotion and awareness of AYUSH systems of medicine including Homoeopathy and Ayurveda by organizing Arogya Fairs/Melas, Ayurveda Parv, Conferences, Exhibitions, Seminars, Workshops, Symposium, celebration of Ayurveda Day & Homoeopathy Day and also undertaking publicity activities through electronic multimedia and print media amongst the citizens all over the country.(b):      The number of AYUSH hospitals and dispensaries along with the number of AYUSH educational institutions running in the country, State/UT-wise are annexed at Annexure- I and Annexure-II.(c):      The Ministry has setup Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS) and Central Council for Research in Homoeopathy (CCRH) for coordinating, formulating, developing and promoting research on scientific lines in Ayurveda and Homoeopathy respectively to increase access to Ayurveda and Homoeopathy systems of healthcare. To Strengthen these education institutions, the minimum standards are prescribed to be fulfilled according to the existing AYUSH Regulations.…………….
