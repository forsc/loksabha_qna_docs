                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1067






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Review of Pending Projects under MSMEs




Sushil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has decided to review ongoing/pending projects under Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in the country particularly in Bihar and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the total number of such projects sanctioned and pending in the sector as on date;(c)	whether the Government is aware that the various projects are pending due to non-sanction of bank loans; and (d)	if so, the details thereof and the steps being taken by the Government to review such projects with concerned States of the country and the concerned banks?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI  NITIN GADKARI)(a)to(d):  Ministry of MSME implements Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP), which is a credit linked subsidy programme. Under this programme, subsidy of 25% to 35%  of the project cost upto Rs. 25.00 lakh is provided as Margin Money subsidy. Details of project proposals received and sanctioned by banks, State/UT-wise under PMEGP, during the financial years 2018 - 19 and 2019 - 20 are given in Annexure – I.Details of number of beneficiaries, projects established and Margin Money subsidy released to the entrepreneurs, State/UT-wise during the financial years 2018 - 19 and 2019 - 20 are given in Annexure – II.Progress under the programme is monitored regularly at the District/State level and also by the Ministry of MSME, Government of India.Ministry of MSME regularly carries out review, monitoring, impact assessment and evaluation of Schemes/Programmes/Projects implemented by it.
