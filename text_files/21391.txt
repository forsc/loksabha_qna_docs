                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2569






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Army Air Ambulance




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the procedure to avail the services of Army air ambulance an by ordinary citizen of the country; (b) whether anyone has availed military Air ambulance in the state of Tamil Nadu from January, 2017 to August, 2018 and if so, the details thereof; (c) the details of the authority under which the military Air ambulance service in the State of Tamil Nadu has been utilised during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) Armed Forces do not have dedicated Air Ambulance. Transport aircraft and helicopters are occasionally provided for medical evacuation of critical patients on the request of State/Central Government/Union Territories.(b) & (c):  On request of Government of Tamil Nadu and approval of MoD, one AN-32 aircraft was provided to Government of Tamil Nadu on 1st July, 2018 from Madurai to Chennai for casualty evacuation (emergency medical treatment) on payment basis. *****
