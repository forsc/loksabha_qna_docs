                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3254






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Doordarshan and Akashvani Channels in Nepali Language




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of any dedicated 24-hour Nepali language service being run on national broadcast, if so, the details thereof; and(b)	whether the Government has received any proposals or plans to start a dedicated channel via Doordarshan or Akashvani for Nepali language, one of the 22 languages recognized under the 8th Schedule of the constitution, if so, the details thereof and the steps taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; AND MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) & (b):  Prasar Bharati has informed that it does not have dedicated 24-Hour Nepali language Radio or TV channel. However, it broadcasts programmes of various duration of Nepali Language from its AIR stations at Kurseong, Siliguri, Gangtok, Gorakhpur and Guwahati, and also by External Service Division of AIR Delhi.Further, programmes in Nepali Language are also being telecast on Doordarshan Channels namely, DD Bangla, DD North-East, DD Arunprabha, DD Gangtok and DD Dehradun from time to time.***********************
