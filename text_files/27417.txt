                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3182






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Ayurvedic Doctors




Sanjay Kumar Bandi














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state:(a) whether the ancient system of ayurveda medicine has great benefits in curing COVID-19 disease, if so, the details thereof along with the recommendations made to COVID-19 patients under this system;(b) whether the Government is aware that there are many ayurveda doctors, traditional hereditary family persons, whose forefathers were into ayurveda practice had come out with medicines claimed to be successful in containing virus in patients;(c)  if so, the details of such doctors/persons and their medicines, State/UT-wise;(d) the number of Ayurveda doctors involved in the treatment of COVID-19 patients across the country, State/ UT-wise; and(e)  whether the Government had considered them as frontline workers and given priority in vaccination, if so, details of vaccine administered on ayurveda doctors in the country, especially in the State of Telangana?   






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a): i. Ministry of Ayush recommended self-care guidelines for preventive health measures and boosting immunity. ii. Government of India has released “National Clinical Management Protocol based on Ayurveda and Yoga for management of COVID-19”.iii. Ministry of Ayush has also released Ayurveda, Unani and Siddha preventive measures for self-care during COVID-19 and Guidelines for AYUSH Practitioners for COVID-19 Patients in Home Isolation.      iv. Ministry of Ayush has also issued advisory for AYUSH practitioners on ethical practices during COVID-19 pandemic.v. Ministry of AYUSH has released Home care guidelines for children and Advisory for AYUSH Practitioners about prophylactic care in Children during the COVID-19 Pandemic.vi. AYUSH-64 and Kabasur Kudineer has been identified for COVID-19 treatment. The research outcomes of the clinical trials has demonstrated efficacy of AYUSH-64 in the treatment of asymptomatic & mild cases as standalone and for the management of mild and moderate COVID-19 as an adjunct to standard care. It was observed that AYUSH-64 as adjunct treatment to Standard Care resulted in significant improvement in clinical recovery. AYUSH-64 is also recommended in “National Clinical Management Protocol (NCMP) based on Ayurveda and Yoga for management of COVID-19”. Further, Kabasur Kudineer, a Siddha preparation was also subjected to clinical trials for studying the efficacy in COVID-19 patients by the Central Council for Research in Siddha (CCRS) under the Ministry of Ayush and is also found useful in the treatment of mild to moderate COVID-19 infection.vii. In order to provide maximum benefit of AYUSH systems of medicine, a nationwide campaign has been undertaken for distribution of AYUSH-64 and Kabasura Kudineer through the Research Councils and the National Institutes under the Ministry of Ayush across the country. (b) & (c): The Ministry of Ayush has set up an online channel on its website for receiving suggestions based on scientific explanations and proposals on therapies and procedures prepared based on standard scientific guidelines that could restrain the spread of the COVID -19 pandemic, or to manage the disease. The Ministry has accordingly invited inputs from AYUSH practitioners and AYUSH institutions. The inputs received were scrutinized by a Committee of Experts. These proposals recommended by the Scrutiny Committee were taken up for further studies. Further, Ministry of AYUSH has constituted an Interdisciplinary Technical Review Committee (ITRC) for COVID-19 for the examination of the applications/claims on patent & proprietary (P&P) ASU&H medicines/ classical ASU& H medicines with new indication or re-purposing of licensed P&P, ASU&H medicines for COVID-19, forwarded by the State Licensing Authorities/Individuals referred by the Drug Policy Section of Ministry of Ayush.(d) & (e): As informed by State/UTs including State of Telangana, Ayurvedic doctors are considered as frontline workers. Details are at Annexure.
