                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3153






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Crude Oil Price




Pratima Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of increase/decrease in the price of crude oil in international market during the last two years and the current year and the number of times the price of petroleum products came down/reduced in both domestic and the international market during the said period; (b) the measures adopted/being adopted by the Government to pass on the benefits of low crude oil prices to the consumers; and (c) the details of the mechanism adopted by the Government to examine the prices of petrol and diesel fixed by various petroleum companies including private companies in conformity with the variation in the international crude oil prices?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c):	Prices of petrol and diesel have been market-determined with effect from 26.06.2010 and 19.10.2014 respectively. Since then, the Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) take appropriate decisions on pricing of petrol and diesel in line with their international product prices, exchange rate, tax structure, inland freight and other cost elements. Prices of Indian Basket of crude oil and petroleum products are available at www.ppac.gov.in .The details of number of times the prices of petrol, diesel and domestic LPG cylinder at Delhi have increased/decreased during the last two years and current year are at the Annexure.
