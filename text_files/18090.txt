                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1837






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Unmanned Level Crossings in Karnataka




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of unmanned railway level crossings in Karnataka zone-wise; (b)	the number of railway level crossings without gates in Karnataka zone-wise;(c) 	the number of deaths that occurred at these railway level crossings along with the reasons therefor; and(d) 	the measures taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to prevent deaths at railway level crossings? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d): (i) All unmanned level crossings on Broad Gauge (BG) route on Indian Railway have been eliminated.  (ii) Level Crossings having high Rail/Road vehicular traffic are interlocked with signals for enhancing safety. (iii) Drives to ensure availability of basic infrastructure at level crossings such as whistle boards, road warning boards, speed breakers, etc. are also undertaken from time to time.(iv) Further, public awareness campaigns are undertaken to create safety consciousness amongst road users for safe passage at level crossings.    *****
