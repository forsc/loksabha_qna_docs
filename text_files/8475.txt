                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2000






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Facilities in AIIMS




Khagen Murmu













                                                    Ajay (Teni) Misra
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to formulate any scheme to provide worldclass educational facilities in All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) and also world-class medical facilities for the treatment of more number of patients in view of their increasing number in this institute;(b) if so, the extensive details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):	Government has accorded in – principle approval for implementation of Master Plan  to convert All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi into a world class Medical University. Master Plan envisages redevelopment of the main campus of AIIMS to cater to the present and future needs of patients care, education and research in the Institute.
