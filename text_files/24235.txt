                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

154






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Bank Loan Defaulters




Uday Pratap Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the companies which have taken loans to the tune of crores of rupees by defrauding the public and private sector banks of the country prior to year 2014 and subsequently these loan accounts were declared Non Performing Assets (NPAs) and the action taken thereon;(b) whether top 100 companies of the country especially Kingfisher Airline, Geetanjali Gems and Crest Steel and Power Private Limited etc., are included in them; (c) if so, whether major bank of the country i.e., State Bank of India, has informed the Enforcement Directorate or Central Bureau of Investigation against them; and (d) if so, the details thereof including the action taken against the said companies?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a) to (d):The Reserve Bank of India has informed that it does not maintain information based on date of sanction or disbursement or details of companies to whom loans were sanctioned/disbursed by public and private sector banks prior to 2014 and which later turned NPA.*****
