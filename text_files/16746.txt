                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

746






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Free Coaching Centres in Assam




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of existing Free Coaching and Allied Scheme in Assam including locations thereof;(b)	whether the Government proposes to open coaching centers at the earliest in the State at the locations where there is no such center;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	whether the Government proposes to open one such centre at Barpeta in Assam and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	    The Ministry of Minority Affairs under the Free Coaching and Allied Scheme, have empanelled w.e.f. 2017-18 to 2019-20, 2 Project Implementing Agencies (PIAs) in Kamrup District of Assam.  (b)	to (d)  The Ministry of Minority Affairs, invites proposals from coaching institutions/organizations  from time to time for empanelment, and those coaching institutions which meet the eligibility criteria are empanelled by the Ministry as per due process. *****
