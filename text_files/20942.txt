                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2101






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Development of Five Archaeological Sites




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the details of the share of the budgetary allocation of Rs 2500 crore to the sector nationally, in the budget of 2020-21;(b)	whether the Government announced development of five archaeological sites across the country to promote tourism along with the criteria of selection of the sites;(c)	whether any of the sites in Odisha qualify for the scheme; and(d)	whether the Government proposes to include the archaeological sites from Odisha like Sun Temple at Konark, Jagannath Temple in Puri and the Buddhist Peace Pagoda in Dhauli in such a scheme, considering their importance and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	Out of total Ministry''s Budget allocation of Rs.3149.86 Crore at BE   2020-21 , Rs. 1246.75 Crore are allocated to ASI for meeting the salary   expenses, developmental work , conservation and preservation work of ASI   sites and Museum etc.Out of ASI''s Budgetary allocation   in year 2020-21 , Rs. 20.00 crore    under revenue sub head ‘Archaeological Museum - Minor works’, Rs.   35.00 crore  under the Capital head (   Major Works) and Rs. 5.10 Crore were provisioned under revenue subhead   ‘Archaeological Exploration and Excavation - other charges’. Any development   work of 5 sites viz., Rakhigarhi, Hastinapur, Shivsagar, Dholavira and   Adichanallur, as iconic sites with on-site Museums may be made through these sub   heads, as applicable.(b)	Yes, Government of India , in the   Budget of 2020,  announced development of five (05) nos. of Iconic sites   which include – 1. Hastinapur (Uttar Pradesh), 2. Rakhigarhi   (Haryana), 3. Sibsagar (Assam), 4. Dholavira (Gujrat) and 5. Adichanallur   (Tamil Nadu).  The sites were selected because of their   Outstanding Archaeological/ Historical Value. Each of the sites represents unique   aspects of Indian history and cultural development. The sites have invaluable   archaeological deposits that can be showcased for public viewing.(c)&(d) At present only five (05) sites are selected.
