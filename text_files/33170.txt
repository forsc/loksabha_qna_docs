                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

504






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Haemophilia Patients




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken note that Haemophilia patients in the  country  are facing acute shortage of anti haemophilia factor, a life saving drug; (b)	If so, the details thereof;(c)	Whether the Haemophilia Federation of India has urged the Government to make urgent intervention to make the drug available immediately; and (d)	If so, the steps taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	to (d): As per information provided by Department of Pharmaceuticals, such complaint of shortage of anti Haemophilia factor, a life saving drug, has not been received in National Pharmaceutical Pricing Authority (NPPA) under the Department of Pharmaceuticals. _____________
