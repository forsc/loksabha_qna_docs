                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

742






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Credit Rating Work for Cities




Goddeti Madhavi













                                                     Chinta Anuradha
                                                








                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Sanjeev Kumar Singari
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                








                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has completed Credit rating work for cities in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof, as on date; and (c) the number of cities out of these have received Investible Grade Rating (IGR) of A- and above?






ANSWER




                                                                    THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. Credit rating work has been completed in 470 AMRUT cities in the country. The list of cities with their rating is at Annexure. (c): 36 cities have received Investible Grade Rating (IGR) of A- & above.                     *****
