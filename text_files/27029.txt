                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2697






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Bio-toilets by DRDO




Ganesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the main features of bio-toilets developed by the Defence Research and Development Organisaton(DRDO);(b) whether the DRDO is using cowdung in the said bio-toilets;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the number of bio-toilets constructed so far, State-wise including district Satna along with the number of people benefited so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) The major features of bio-toilet developed by Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) are as under:(i)	It makes use of an anaerobic (without oxygen) process through which the human waste is digested by a consortium of bacteria(ii)	Bio-toilet has been customized and different designs have been developed for use under different geo-climatic conditions.(b) & (c):  Yes, Sir.  The Cow dung is used for the enrichment bacterial consortium.  Following four types of bacteria are present in the consortium:(i)	Hydrolase(ii) 	Acidogenase(iii) 	Acitogenase(iv)	Methogens(d) State wise details of bio-toilets constructed are attached as Annexure. 	However, till date no bio-toilet has been constructed in District Satna. 	Total 16507 bio-digesters have been installed in different states.  Bio-digesters have many variants as per the number of users and considering the design for 5-7 users, atleast 80000-112000 people have been benefitted by these bio-toilets.*****
