                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

559






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Storage Facilities in Rural Areas




Ramapati Ram Tripathi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to  provide  storage  facilities  in  the  rural areas of the country under the rural storage yojana/Gramin Bhandaran Yojana;(b)	 if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and(c) 	the total number of rural godowns constructed during each of  the  last  three years and the number of farmers benefited under the scheme, the details thereof, State-wise including Uttar Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	 Yes Sir. The Government is promoting storage facilities in rural areas of the country through the scheme of  “Agricultural Marketing Infrastructure (AMI)”, which is a sub-scheme under the Integrated Scheme for Agricultural Marketing (ISAM).  AMI scheme is a back ended capital subsidy scheme in which rate of subsidy provided is 25% and 33.33% based on the category of eligible beneficiary and provided on capital cost of the project.  Assistance under the sub-scheme is available to individuals, groups of farmers/growers, registered Farmer Produce Organizations (FPOs) etc.  So far, a total of 38,964 storage infrastructure projects (Godowns), with storage capacity of 655.48 Lakh MT have been sanctioned across the country in rural areas. The State wise details are at Annexure-I.  (c):     During last three years, a total of 2743 storage infrastructure projects (Godowns) have been assisted with storage capacity of 74.16 Lakh MT under storage component of AMI sub-scheme directly benefiting number of agripreneurs including farmers. The State-wise details are at Annexure II. The scheme is demand driven and no scientific storage facility in Uttar Pradesh has been sanctioned during the last three years.
