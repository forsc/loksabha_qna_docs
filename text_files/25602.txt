                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1456






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Export of Indian Products




Krishna Pal Singh Yadav













                                                    Sandhya Ray
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the names of all Indian products regarding which scheme is being prepared by the Government to export them in the coming years along with the details thereof; (b)	whether the quantum of some of the imported products may be reduced or fully stopped by manufacturing them in India itself; (c)	if so, the details thereof; (d)	whether by increasing the production level and quality of some of the Indian products, their export may be increased or started; and (e)	if so, the details thereof along with the number of such products which are associated with Madhya Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY(SMT. ANUPRIYA PATEL)(a)	to (e) Products and services with export potential have been identified under the Districts as Export Hubs initiative in all districts of the country. The list of identified products and services in districts, including from the state of Madhya Pradesh, is at Annexure.District export action plans include identifying specific actions required to support local exporters/manufacturers in producing/manufacturing identified products in adequate quantity and with the requisite quality, and reaching potential buyers outside India. This includes identifying and addressing challenges for exports of the identified products/services, improving the supply chains, market accessibility and handholding for increasing exports. As the focus under Districts as Export Hubs initiative will be on export growth of identified products, manufacturing of some of these products will also get a boost, leading to less import dependence.
