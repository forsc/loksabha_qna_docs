                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

167






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Beneficiary of Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana




Ramesh Bidhuri














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of people benefited through Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana; (b) 	whether the Government has taken any steps for making food grains accessible at reasonable prices especially to weaker sections of the society; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	Under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojna (PMGKAY), Govt of India has made allocation of additional foodgrains @ 5 kg per person per month free of cost for around 80 crore beneficiaries of all the 36 States/UTs covered under National Food Security Act (NFSA) (Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) & Priority Households (PHH)) including those covered under Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) for a period of 8 months i.e April-November, 2020 and again similarly during the period May -November, 2021. This scheme has been extended for a further period of four months i.e. December, 2021 to March, 2022.(b) & (c):	The coverage limit under the NFSA is about 81.35 crore beneficiaries all over the country under AAY & PHH, who are amongst the poorest and eligible sections of the country. Allocations under the PMGKAY was done in addition to the normal allocations under the NFSA. The responsibility for identification of eligible beneficiaries under NFSA and distribution of foodgrains to them rests with the States/UTs *****
