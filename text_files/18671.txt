                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

230






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Rise in Bird Deaths in Rajasthan




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether keeping in view the rising number of bird deaths and cases of bird flu and large scale animal husbandry in Rajasthan, the Government has proposed to set up veterinary RTPCR lab in the State; and (b) if so, the time by which it is likely to be done?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY & DAIRYING (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	No Sir. However, keeping in view the recent outbreaks of Highly Pathogenic Avian Influenza (Bird flu) as well as to facilitate testing processes, States/UTs have been requested o carry out preliminary screening of samples in their laboratories which have minimum BSL-2 facilities and diagnostic capability. Further, in response to the request of Government of Rajasthan for strengthening its laboratory, Rs. 20.00 lakhs has been released, which is included in Rs. 1024.11 lakh already released to Rajasthan under Assistance to States for Control of Animal Diseases (ASCAD), a component of Livestock Health & Disease Control (LH & DC) scheme.(b)	Question does not arise****
