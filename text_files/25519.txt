                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1158






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Employment under MGNREGA




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the total number of individuals in Udaipur who have been employed under MGNREGA during each year since 2017-18 to 2020-2021;(b) 	the number of individuals in Udaipur who have till date registered under MGNREGA for 2021-2022;(c) 	the details of the number of indiviiduals offered employment under MGNREGA in each block of Udaipur for every month since March 2020;(d) 	whether the unemployment rate was low in Udaipur during the COVID-19 pandemic under MGNREGA; and(e) 	the details of the number of households that have been covered under MGNREGA?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):	The details of number of individuals provided employment under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) in Udaipur district of Rajasthan from financial year 2017-18 to 2020-2021 are given below:                                                                        (Figure in lakh)Number of individuals provided employment under Mahatma Gandhi NREG Scheme in Udaipur district of RajasthanFY 2017-18	FY 2018-19	  FY 2019-20	    FY 2020-212.83	           3.73 	     3.99	      5.37(b):	A total of 14.44 lakh individuals have been registered under Mahatma Gandhi NREG Scheme in Udaipur district of Rajasthan as on 24.07.2021.(c):	Block-wise number of individuals offered employment under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in Udaipur District of Rajasthan in the financial years 2020-21, and 2021-22 (as on 23.07.2021), is given at Annexure.(d):	The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA) is an Act to provide for the enhancement of livelihood security of the households in rural areas of the country by providing at least one hundred days of guaranteed wage employment in every financial year to every household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work.  It provides livelihood security i.e., fall back option for livelihood for the rural households when no better employment opportunity is available. The demand for work is influenced by various factors such as rain-fall, availability of alternative and remunerative employment opportunities outside Mahatma Gandhi NREGS. The details of number of persons provided employment and persondays generated during last two financial years 2019-20, 2020-21 and current financial year 2021-22 (as on 24.07.2021) under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in Udaipur District of Rajasthan are as under:Number of persons provided employment and persondays generated under Mahatma Gandhi NREGA- Udaipur District, RajasthanFinancial Year	    Number of persons provided employment          Persondays generated                           (in lakh)                                   (in lakh)2021-22 (as on 24.07.2021)	          2.65	                                 53.992020-21	                          5.37	                                214.682019-20	                          3.99	                                144.54(e):	In the current financial year 2021-22, (as on 24.07.2021), a total of 2.16 lakh households have been provided employment under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in Udaipur district of Rajasthan.******
