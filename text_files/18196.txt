                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1895






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Air Services from Bihar and Maharashtra




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut













                                                    Veena Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any new air services have been started from Bihar and Maharashtra to other cities of the country;and(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Details of the new air services (routes) started by the Scheduled domestic airlines from Bihar and Maharashtra to other cities of the Country, as per Summer Schedule, 2020 approved by Directorate General of Civil Aviation are attached as Annexure. Domestic flight operations which remain suspended from 25 March, 2020 to 24 May, 2020, recommenced with effect from 25.05.2020. Airlines are resuming the commenced routes in a calibrated manner.;
