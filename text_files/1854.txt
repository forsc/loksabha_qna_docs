                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1661






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Monitoring of ISI Marked Productions




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of system available to check the quality of ISI marked products under present monitoring system; (b)	the number of raids conducted on premises of licence holders under the said system and the number of samples collected during the last three years; (c) 	the number of cases registered on the basis of samples collected during the said raids and the number of persons against whom action has been taken in this regard; and (d) 	the reasons for not conducting the raids against manufacturers of such products/marketing premises and the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI DANVE RAOSAHEB DADARAO)(a) :   The Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) licence holder is  required  to operate  Scheme  of  Inspection and Testing  (SIT),  which  prescribes  the specific  tests  as per relevant Indian standard and  the  frequency  for  conducting  them. BIS carries out surveillance on the manufacturers holding BIS licence through visit to the factory premises at periodic intervals. During the surveillance, verification of the implementation of SIT and compliance of the product to relevant standard is verified. Product samples are also drawn and sent to BIS laboratory or BIS recognized laboratory for checking conformance to relevant standard.   In addition, samples of the product bearing BIS standard mark are also purchased from the market and got tested at laboratory for checking conformance to relevant standard. Based on the non-conformance if any, observed from the test report, actions is initiated against the manufacturer as per BIS (Conformity Assessment) Regulations, 2018.(b)  :      BIS has informed that raids are carried out only on non-licence holder’s premises for misuse of BIS Standard Mark and evidence collected for prosecution in the Court of Law.Hence, the number of samples lifted is not applicable. (c)  :	Cases are registered on the basis of evidence found during the raids at the premises of non-licensees.  All cases of raids where evidence of misuse of BIS Standard Mark is observed are filed in the Court of Law under Section 29 of BIS Act.   As on date, 1078 criminal cases are pending in various courts all over India.  However, the number of cases registered during the last 3 year is as under:Year	Number of case registered2016-17	1312017-18	792018-19	147(d)  :     Raids are conducted only on premises which do not have a licence and misusing BIS Standard mark.*****
