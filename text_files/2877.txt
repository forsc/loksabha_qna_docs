                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2683






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Watershed Development Projects




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal and is taking review for Qualitative and timely execution of large number of watershed development projects undertaken by State Department or Agencies; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether cases of irregularities, corruption and financial malfeasance have been reported in various States and if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and (d) the action taken report on such incidents?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a) Yes Sir, suitable steps are taken by the Department of Land Resources (DoLR) for qualitative and timely execution of watershed development projects.(b) Reviews are made through the Steering Committee Meeting, Regional Review Meeting, Progress Reportsetc. The State Level Nodal Agencies (SLNAs) are responsible for monitoring of implementation of projects at the State level. (c& d)Watershed Development projectsare being implemented by State Governments.States have been requested for taking appropriate action in case of irregularities and / or corruption or financial malfeasances are reported. Further, States have been advised from time to time to examine issues of corruptions / malfeasances and take necessary action preferably by setting up of an independent Task Force / Committee at State level to look into any such allegations and conduct suo moto investigations. *****
