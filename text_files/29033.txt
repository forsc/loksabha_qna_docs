                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

985






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Assistance for COVID-19 from Other Countries




Ramya  Haridas














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of countries to whom India has provided medical and other assistance to fight against COVID-19; and(b)  	whether India has received any such assistance from other countries to fight against COVID-19 and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a)	India supplied COVID-19 related medical and other assistance, to over 150 countries since the beginning of Covid-19 pandemic. (b)	In the fight against the Covid-19  pandemic during the second wave, support in the form of Covid-related equipments and medicines were received from 53 countries have been received. These included various items from foreign governments, private companies, Indian associations abroad etc. The details available with Ministry of External Affairs of foreign consignents received from various countries from April 2021 till date is placed at APPENDIX-1.***
