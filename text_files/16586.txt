                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

372






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Constitution of GoM on Sexual Harassment




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government had constituted a GoM in October, 2018, after MeToo movement to address the issues of sexual harassment at the workplace; (b) if so, whether the said GoM was required to submit a report in three months time on strengthening a legal framework; (c) whether the said GoM had been reconstituted in 2019; (d) whether it is a fact that even the reconstituted GoM has also failed to submit the report as required, as on date; and (e) if so, the reasons thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e):	The Government had set up a Group of Ministers (GoM) to examine and give recommendations for strengthening the legal and institutional framework to deal with and prevent sexual harassment at workplace vide order dated 24.10.2018. The GoM was reconstituted on 18.07.2019. The GoM has submitted its report to the Government on 10.02.2020.*********
