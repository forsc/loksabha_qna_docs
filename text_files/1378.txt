                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is unable to contain the incidence of malnutrition among women and children in the country, particularly in tribal, rural and backward/ hilly areas, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(b)	whether the Government has identified the districts with maximum number of malnourished women and children in the country, if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the World Health Organisation (WHO) and United Nations Children’s Fund (UNICEF) and have also drawn attention to high prevalence of malnutrition in the country and fixed criteria in this regard and if so, the details thereof;(d)	the details of the schemes implemented by the Government for eradication of malnutrition along with the funds allocated/released and utilised thereunder during each of the last three years and the current year, scheme and State/UTwise; (e)	the impact of schemes in eradication of malnutrition in the country;(f)	whether the Government plans to revisit its policies to ensure that the earmarked funds reach the targeted population and achieve the objectives of reduction in malnutrition and if so, the details thereof; and(g)	the further measures taken by the Government to make the country free of malnutrition?
