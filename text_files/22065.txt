                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3089






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Training in Agriculture under PMKVY




Sanjay Bhatia














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)      whether any training is being imparted regarding the use of technology in machines used in agriculture sector for making the work easier under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana formulated by the Government;(b)      if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether any technical training regarding use of machines in agriculture sector is being imparted so far in the Parliamentary Constituency of Karnal under the said scheme;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the details of the places where training centres are being set up in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (d)	Yes Sir. Under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), training is being imparted in various National Skill Qualification Framework (NSQF) aligned job roles, which include those involving use of technology in machines in agriculture sector, across the country including Karnal Parliamentary Constituency.   Under PMKVY2.0, some job roles comprising the use of technology in agriculture sector include the Green House Operator, Plant Tissue Culture Technician, Harvesting Machine Operator, Micro Irrigation Technician, Seed Processing Worker, Artificial Insemination Technician, Vermicompost Producer, Organic Grower, Quality Seed Grower, Agriculture Machinery Repair and Maintenance Service Provider, Service and Maintenance Technician-Farm Machinery, Soil & Water Testing Lab Analyst, Solar Pump Technician, Tractor Mechanic, etc. As on 19/01/2021, 7.85 lakh candidates have been trained / oriented under agriculture sector in PMKVY 2.0 across the country, including 1,693 candidates in Karnal Parliamentary Constituency. (e)        PMKVY does not mandate the establishment of training centres. Under the programme, short term training (STT) is being imparted through district-level empaneled  training centres (TCs) in various sectors including agriculture sector. Under PMKVY 2.0, as on 19.01.2021, 15,124 training centres have been empanelled across the country including 86 empanelled TCs in Karnal Parliamentary Constituency.
