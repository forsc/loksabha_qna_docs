                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2602






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Revenue Earned form Petroleum Products




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the total amount received by the Union Government in form of Central excise duty on petroleum products during the last five years and the current year, State and year-wise; (b) whether the Government has used the amount received from excise duty on petroleum products to fund free vaccination programme against Covid-19; and(c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):	The details of the amount collected by the Union Government by levying excise duty (including cesses) on petroleum products during the last five years and current year are given as under:Rs./CroreParticulars	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	Apr.-Sep. 2021-22Excise Duty	2,42,691	2,29,716	2,14,369	2,23,057	3,72,970	1,70,894	      Based on data from 16 major oil & gas companies (Source: PPAC)(b) & (c):	The excise duty rates on petrol and diesel have been calibrated to generate resources for infrastructure and other developmental items of expenditure keeping in view the present fiscal position. x-x-x-x-x
