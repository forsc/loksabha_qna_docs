                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2808






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Sub-Transmission and Distribution Network




Sanjay Bhatia














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has formulated any scheme to strengthen sub-transmission and distribution network in urban areas of the State of Haryana;(b) 	if so, the details thereof, district-wise including Karnal; and(c) 	the steps taken by the Government to strengthen the sub-transmission and distribution network system and distribution of transformer/feeder/consumer metering in the said Constituency?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF POWER AND NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY (SHRI  R.K. SINGH)(a) & (b) :	Government of India had launched “Integrated Power Development Scheme" (IPDS) in December, 2014 with the aim of providing quality and reliable power supply in the urban areas with the following components:I.	Strengthening of sub-transmission and Distribution network in urban areas.II.	Metering of feeders / distribution transformers/consumers in urban areas.III.	IT enablement of distribution sector and strengthening of distribution network being undertaken under R-APDRP (Restructured Accelerated Power Development and Reforms Programme) as subsumed component.The IPDS scheme has a total outlay of Rs. 32,612 crore. Projects worth Rs. 31,314 crore have been sanctioned in 547 circles of 33 States/UTs, against which States/UTs have reported an expenditure of Rs. 24,440 crore.   Against the sanction of Rs. 447 crore under IPDS for Haryana, Rs 180 crore has been disbursed. District-wise details of system strengthening projects under IPDS in Haryana are at Annexure-I.		Further, Government of India has approved a “Revamped Distribution Sector Scheme: A Reform-based and Results-linked scheme” on 30.6.2021 with an outlay of Rs. 3,03,758 Crore. The Scheme seeks to improve the operational efficiency and financial sustainability of all Discoms/Power Departments excluding private Discoms by providing conditional financial assistance to Discoms for strengthening of supply infrastructure and for installation of smart Meters in Pre-paid mode. (c) : 		Under IPDS, projects worth Rs. 42.92 crores have been sanctioned for urban areas of Karnal Constituency of Haryana.  Details of the same are at Annexure-II.  State Government of Haryana has also reported that the following works have been undertaken in Karnal Constituency by the State Government through its own resources: i.	Installation of 1.30 lac smart meters under Nigam’s own Capex Scheme.ii.	Work for creation/ augmentation of 47 no. 33 KV sub-stations under Nigam’s own Capex Scheme. *************
