                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

296






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Visakhapatnam Chennai Industrial Corridor




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the steps taken by the Government to speed up the development of Visakhapatnam Chennai Industrial Corridor; (b) 	the total funds allocated and released for the development of Visakhapatnam Chennai Industrial Corridor; (c) 	whether any timeframe has been fixed for completion of the development project to avoid any delay and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether the State Government of Andhra Pradesh has submitted a proposal in October, 2018 for consideration of National Industrial Corridor Development and Implementation Trust; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Union Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a)to(c):  Visakhapatnam Chennai Industrial Corridor (VCIC) is under implementation as Phase 1 of East Coast Economic Corridor (ECEC). Two nodes namely Chittoor and Visakhapatnam are identified under VCIC. Consultant for master planning and preliminary engineering for Chittoor Node has been appointed by National Industrial Corridor Development Corporation Ltd. (NICDCL). Further, for Visakhapatnam node, the master planning and preliminary engineering is initiated by the State Government. As the project is in initial stage of development, no funds are allocated/released and no timeframe could be fixed at this stage. (d)&(e): The State Government of Andhra Pradesh had submitted a proposal for inclusion of two nodes namely Chittoor and Visakhapatnam as part of VCIC under the National Industrial Corridor Development and Implementation Trust (NICDIT) mandate.  NICDIT in its meeting held on 30th August, 2019 has accorded its approval for development of Vishakhapatnam and Chittoor as priority nodes in Phase-1 of VCIC.   ********
