                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4829






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Development of Solar Cities




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao













                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                








                                                    Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has initiated development of at least one city in each of the States of the country as solar city;(b) if so, the details thereof and the time by which the said proposal is likely to be implemented;(c) the details of cities identified for implementation of the scheme in each State, district-wise;(d) the quantum of funds sanctioned, allocated and released for implementation of the same during the last three years and the current year;(e) the details of the action plan formulated to turn the present cities into solar cities along with the steps taken/being taken by the Government to achieve the said target in time bound manner; and(f) whether the State Governments have been asked to provide a detailed report for implementation of the scheme and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) to (f) The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) has requested each State /Union Territory to select at-least one city to be developed as solar city. So far 24 States /UTs have identified cities in this regard. The list of the identified cities is placed at Annexure.There is no separate scheme for solar cities and funds available under different schemes are to be utilised for development of solar cities.A Concept Note on developing solar city was circulated to the States/UTs with a request to finalise an action plan for development of the identified cities as solar cities and implement the plan in a time bound manner.*****
