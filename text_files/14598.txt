                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

296






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Revision of Fee Structure of Doordarshan




C. Lalrosanga














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  Whether the fee being paid by Doordarshan to performing artists/ casual assignees/resource persons has not been revised for the last several years and if so, the details thereof;(b) Whether a high level committee constituted for the purpose submitted its recommendations and proposals to Prasar Bharati a few years back;(c) if so, the details and the present status of those recommendations /proposals;(d) whether Prasar Bharati has any plan to revise the fee structure of artists/casual assignees/resource persons in Doordarshan; and(e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR) (a) to (e): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.*********************** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.*296 FOR ANSWER ON 13.03.2020a)   Prasar Bharati revised fee paid by Doordarshan to performing artists/ casual assignees/resource persons in years 2012 and 2014. (b) & (c) The fee revision was based on the recommendations made by a Committee constituted to rationalise fee structure. All payments to performing artists/ casual assignees / resource persons are made based on revised fee structure.(d) & (e) There is no proposal in Prasar Bharati at present to revise the fee structure of Artists/Casual assignees/Resource persons. They are engaged on need basis for specific assignments.*****
