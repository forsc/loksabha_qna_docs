Land Records Management

                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy

RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    LOK SABHA    UNSTARRED QUESTION No. 4221   TO BE ANSWERED ON 29.03.2022    LAND RECORDS MANAGEMENT4221.  SHRIMATI VANGA GEETHA VISWANATH:       SHRI KOTHA PRABHAKAR REDDY:Will the Minister of RURAL DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state:(a)	the steps being taken for Land Records Management in the country, State-wise including Andhra Pradesh and Telangana since 2015 onwards as efficient use of land resources is a strong imperative and States will be encouraged to adopt Unique Land Parcel Identification Number to facilitate IT-based management of records as the facility for transliteration of land records across any of the Schedule VIII languages will also be rolled out; and(b)	the details of progress made including the funds sanctioned/spent till now since 2015 onwards, State wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER                   MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT                                  (SHRI FAGGAN SINGH KULASTE) (a)    Digital India Land Records Modernization Programme (DILRMP) is being implemented as a Central Sector Scheme with effect from 1st April, 2016 with 100% funding by the Centre. The objective of DILRMP is to develop a modern, comprehensive and transparent land record management system with the aim to develop an Integrated Land Information Management System which will inter alia: (i) improve real-time information on land; (ii) optimize use of land resources; (iii) benefit both landowners and prospectors; (iv) assist in policy and planning; (v) reduce land disputes; (vi) check fraudulent/benami transactions ; (vii) obviate need of physical visits to Revenue/Registration offices  and (viii) enable sharing of information with various organization/agencies.
