                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1301






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Reduction in GST




S. Jagathrakshakan













                                                    A.K.P. Chinraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has received any request from the Industry sector to substantially reduce the GST for 6 months to boost demand in the aftermath of the COVID pandemic across the country; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the action being taken or proposed to be taken by the Government on this request?






ANSWER




                                                        The MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) and (b): Requests to reduce GST rate in the aftermath of COVID has been received from Industry and Industry associations like CII, FICCI, ASSOCHAM etc.(c): GST rates are prescribed on the basis of recommendations of the GST Council as made from time to time. At present, GST Council has not made any recommendation in this regard.
