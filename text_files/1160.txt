                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1022






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Construction on NHs in Kerala




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has commenced construction of new National Highways (NHs) in Kerala;(b)	if so, the details along with their present status thereof;(c)	whether the Government has given priority status to construction of new National Highways in Kerala, considering its developmental requirements concerning transportation infrastructure; and(d)	if so, the allocation of funds for the ongoing and new projects during the last three years in Kerala, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI  NITIN JAIRAM  GADKARI) (a) to (d)	Development and maintenance of National Highways is a continuous process and the same are carried out based on traffic requirement, availability of funds and inter se priority of works.  Allocation of funds for development of National Highways is made State-wise and not district-wise.  Funds allocated for development of National Highways in Kerala during the last three years is enclosed at Annexure-I.
