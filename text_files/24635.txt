                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

528






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Cities Selected under AMRUT Scheme




Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the names of the cities of Maharashtra selected under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT)Scheme;(b) 	the funds allocated, sanctioned and released under this Scheme to the State of Maharashtra during the last three years, year-wise;(c) 	the details of authority assigned with the responsibility to monitor the utilization of these funds; and(d) 	the details of the completion status of works under the schemes,city-wise?






ANSWER




                                                                         THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                 (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)	A total of 44 cities of the State of Maharashtra are covered Under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT), 	list of which is given at ANNEXURE – I.(b)	The total approved State Annual Action Plan (SAAP) size of State 	of Maharashtra under AMRUT, for the entire Mission period, 	amounts to ?7,759.32 crore, which includes Central Assistance 	(CA) of ?3,534 crore. So far, CA amounting to 2,776.25 crore 	have been released to the Government of Maharashtra for AMRUT 	projects. Under AMRUT, funds are released under the heads of Administrative and Office Expanses (A&OE), Reform Incentives,GIS based Master Plan and Local Area Plan – Town Planning Scheme (LAP-TPS). Funds released to Government of 	Maharashtra under AMRUT during the last 3 years, year-wise, is 	given below:                                                                    (in Rs. crore)Head	                    Year 2018-19	Year 2019-20	Year 2020-21AMRUT Projects	              825.71	            -	          1,244.21A&OE	                       23.52	           32.68	       -Reform Incentives	       77.36	           42.08	       -GIS based Master Plan	         -	              -	               -LAP-TPS	-	                                     0.4	       0.8Total:	                       926.59	            75.16	   1,245.01(c) 	Under AMRUT, States are empowered to plan, approve and 	implement the projects.  Respective State Governments monitor 	the implementation of the Mission and utilization of the funds 	under AMRUT.  A State level High Powered Steering Committee 	(SHPSC) chaired by the State Chief Secretary monitors the 	progress of the implementation of the Mission. In addition, 	Independent Review and Monitoring Agency (IRMA) have been appointed by each State/ Union Territory. (d) 	The city-wise information on project completion status is given at ANNEXURE-II.                                      *****
