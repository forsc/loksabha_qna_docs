                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3475






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Skill Development in Rajasthan




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of persons trained under skill development programme in Rajasthan during the last three years;(b)	the total number of trained skilled persons already recruited in public/private sector in Rajasthan along with the names of the employers, district-wise and person-wise details thereof; and(c)	the district-wise details of percentage of local people employed in various Central Government undertakings/private sector out of the total skilled unemployed persons in Rajasthan?






ANSWER




(a)	Under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (2016-20), 3.14 lakh candidates have been trained under short term training (STT) courses and 3.29 lakh candidates have been oriented under Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) component of the scheme.(b) and (c)	Placement opportunities under PMKVY (2016-20) are being given to candidates under short term training courses. As on 17.01.2020, 2.66 lakh candidates have been certified under STT; out of which, 1.33 lakh candidates have been provided with placement opportunities which is 50%. The top ten employers in State of Rajasthan under the PMKVY scheme are Neha Associates, Lucky Associates, Human Resource And Placement Services, Havells India Ltd., AGP Relocators, United Team HR, Reliable First Adcon Pvt. Ltd., M4 Protects Services, Allena Auto Industries Private Limited and Jai Shree biotech.The district-wise number of candidates trained / oriented, certified and placed in the State of Rajasthan under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) (2016-20), as on 17.01.2020, is given at Annexure.
