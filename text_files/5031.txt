                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4683






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Vacant Posts in Research Institutes




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of posts lying vacant till date in Research Institutes of the Government ofIndia;(b) the steps taken by the Government to fill these posts; and(c) the number of Research Centres set up in Bihar along with the number of ResearchCentres which are likely to be set up?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) : A total of 423 posts are lying vacant till date in Research Institutes of the Governmentof India.(b) : In the Department of Science and Technology, necessary steps are being taken byconcerned institutions to fill up the vacancies in accordance with the extant rules. In theMinistry of Human Resource Development, all Indian Institutes of Science Education andResearch (IISERs) are making continuous efforts to fill all vacancies and recruitmentprocess is going on continuously.(c) : There are no Research Centres set up in Bihar. There is no decision in the Government,presently to set up Research Centres in Bihar.*****
