                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients




Dushyant Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state:(a) the list of countries exporting Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) to India along with the quantities over the last five years; (b) the top 10 APIs imported the most along with the quantity and the exporting countries; (c) the steps taken by the Government to reduce import dependence on China; (d) the list of 53 APIs short-listed by the Government for incentives; (e) whether the industry is seeking regulatory easing to reduce import dependence on China for APIs; (f) if so, the list of regulatory changes sought by the pharmaceuticals industry; (g) whether the Government intends to undertake regulatory easing to support the incentives given to boost API production in the country; and (h) if so, the details thereof?
