                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5109






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Renting of Telecom Equipments




Rama Devi














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has rented out the Government-owned telecommunication equipments to the private Telecom Service Providers (TSPs); (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the rates at which the rent is taken from them; (d) 	whether the said private TSPs are not paying rent on time and the Government is not taking any timely and appropriate action in this regard, if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto; and (e) 	the corrective steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (c) 	Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) and Mahanagar Telephone Nigam Limited (MTNL) have informed that they have shared mobile tower sites with private Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) as per Master Service Agreements (MSA). Monthly rental charges claimed by BSNL varies from Rs.21,000/- to Rs.38,700/- and for MTNL from Rs.30,000 to Rs.1,50,000.  BSNL and MTNL have rented out 13051 and 392 mobile tower sites respectively to private TSPs, details of which are at Annexure. (d) & (e) 	Mostly private TSPs are paying rent on time.  However, in case of any delay in payment, BSNL and MTNL regularly follow up the matter and take actions for early payment and to effect recoveries.
