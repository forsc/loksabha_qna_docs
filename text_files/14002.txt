                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2871






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Coal Reserves




Gomati Sai














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the quantum of coal reserves in Raigarh district of Chhattisgarh along with the per year production capacity thereof; and(b)	the amount of revenue earned therefrom along with the revenue earned during the years 2017-18 and 2018-19? 






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a): The quantum of coal reserves of approved coal projects of South Eastern Coalfields Limited (SECL) in District Raigarh (including custodian mines) as on 01.04.2019 was estimated to 802.13 Million Tonne (MT). The production capacity of running coal projects of SECL Raigarh Area including custodian mines is 22.95 Million Tonne per year (MTY).(b): The amount of revenue earned therefrom during the year 2017-18 and 2018-19 are as under:Year	Production (MT)	Despatch (MT)	Sales realisation(Rs. Crore)	Profit(Rs. Crore)2017-18	14.201	16.370	2182.92	1159.98		2018-19	10.634	10.839	1552.81	866.16 *****
