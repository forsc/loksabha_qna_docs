                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3192






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Wellness Centres of Yoga and Naturopathy




Manoj Kumar Tiwari














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)   be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has any proposal to set up compulsory wellness centres of Yoga and Naturopathy in all the Government hospitals in near future in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof, State/UTswise and particularly in NCT of Delhi or NCR; (c) if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) the details of implementation procedure and plan outlay for any such proposal in the near future?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (d):    As public health is a State subject, setting up of wellness centres of Yoga and Naturopathy in all the Government hospitals in the country including NCT of Delhi comes under the purview of respective State/UT Governments. However, Central Government is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) in the country including NCT of Delhi under which financial assistance is provided to States/UTs for setting up of Yoga Wellness Centres and Naturopathy Wellness Centres. The status of financial assistance provided for Yoga Wellness Centres and Naturopathy Wellness Centres, State/UT-wise including NCT of Delhi as per proposal received from them, is furnished at Annexure.                   Since, NAM scheme is being implemented by State/UT Governments, therefore details of implementation procedure and plan outlay for Yoga Wellness Centres and Naturopathy Wellness Centres proposal are under the domain of respective State/UT Governments.
