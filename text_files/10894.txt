                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4244






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






National Organs and Tissues Transplant Organisation




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has set up National Organs and Tissues Transplant Organisation (NOTTO) in the country; (b) if so, the salient features including major responsibilities thereof;(c) whether regional/State level organisations are functioning under the said organisation;(d) if so, the details of such regional offices/State level offices functioning in the country, State/UT-wise including Kerala;(e) whether the Government has laid down any formal policy for distribution of organs and tissues and if so, the details thereof; and(f) the number of hospitals empanelled under the said organisation, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	 National Organ and Tissue Transplant Organisation (NOTTO) has been set up as an apex level organisation under Directorate General of Health Services, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare for carrying out functions of National Human Organs and Tissues Removal and Storage Network as specified under Transplantation of Human Organs and Tissues Act, 1994 and under Transplantation of Human Organs and Tissues Rules, 2014.(c) & (d):	 As per the provisions made under Transplantation of Human Organs and Tissues Act, 1994 and under Transplantation of Human Organs and Tissues Rules, 2014 five Regional Organ and Tissue Transplant Organisations (ROTTOs) have been set up and funds have been released to 11 States for establishment of State Organ and Tissue Transplant Organisations (SOTTOs) under National Organ Transplant Programme (NOTP). Detail of such organisations is at Annexure. (e):	    Yes. Transplantation of Human Organs and Tissues Rules, 2014 provides for broad principles of organ allocation and sharing.  NOTTO has defined allocation criteria for various organs viz, kidney, liver, heart, lung, heart-lung and cornea to assist the allocation of organs/tissues in the States which are available on www.notto.gov.in. However, as per aforesaid Rule, the allocation criteria may also be State specific. (f):   The detail regarding number of hospitals registered with NOTTO is available on website of NOTTO at: https://notto.gov.in/approvedhospitalslist.htm
