                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4421






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Funds Utilized under CSR Project




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao













                                                    Debasree Chaudhuri
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    whether the Public Sector Undertakings under the Ministry have failed to utilise hundred per cent funds allocated for Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) projects towards the local development during the last five years;(b)        if so, the quantum of funds allocated and utilised during the last five years;(c)        whether the Government has some special budget for infrastructural development in local areas to develop its property, schools, parks, etc. through Public Private Partnership (PPP) model; and(d)        if so, the details of such PPP models, area-wise including Maharashtra Raiganj districts in West Bengal and details of funds allocated/spent by subsidiary companies from 2017-18 till date?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF  PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)  (a):  Coal India Ltd.(CIL) as well as NLC India Ltd. (NLCIL) along with its subsidiaries have collectively  utilized entire Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) funds allocated in each of the last  five years i.e. FY 2016-17 to FY 2020-21 as per the norms prescribed in section 135 of Companies Act  2013. (b): The details of CSR funds allocated and spent during last five years  are given below: CIL and its subsidiariesFinancial Year	CSR Fund as per Companies Act, 2013(Rs. crores)	Actual CSR Expenditure(Rs. crores)2016-17	442.75	489.672017-18	383.05	483.782018-19	353.98	416.472019-20	396.20	587.842020-21	434.51	553.85NLCIL and its subsidiariesS.No	Financial year	CSR Fund as per Companies Act, 2013 (Rscrores)	Actual CSR Expenditure(Rs. crores)1	2016-17	43.46	37.66 *2	2017-18	43.59	45.033.	2018-19	45.73	51.234.	2019-20	51.98	79.815.	2020-21	52.55	49.88* Rs. 6.27 Crore carried forward from FY 2016-17 and spent in FY 2017-18.(c):  CIL and its subsidiaries as well as NLCIL do not have any special budget for infrastructural development in local areas to develop its property, schools, parks etc. through Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode. (d): Does not arise in view of reply to part (c) above.                                                                     ****
