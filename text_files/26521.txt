                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2155






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Funds Allocated to Promote Tourism in Backward and Rural Areas




Arun Kumar Sagar














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the State-wise schematic funds allocated to promote tourism especially in backward and rural areas during the current financial year so far; (b) 	whether there has been a sufficient progress in the tourism industry of some States during the last three years; (c) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise especially of backward and rural areas details thereof; and (d) 	the number of foreign tourists who visited tourist places in various States in the country during the last one year as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d):	Ministry of Tourism releases funds to the State Governments / Union Territory Administrations for projects pertaining to tourism infrastructure  development under its ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ schemes. In addition, funds are also allocated to the State Governments / Union Territory Administrations for organizing tourism related fairs and festivals under ‘Domestic Promotion and Publicity including Hospitality (DPPH)’ scheme. The funds under these schemes are allocated for holistic development and promotion of the country which includes backward and rural areas. State-wise details of projects sanctioned under the ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘PRASHAD’ Schemes  and the  allocation  made  to the  States/Union Territory Administrations for organizing tourism related  fairs &  festivals   is at  annexure-I.The tourism industry comprises of multiple stakeholders.  The  Ministry of Tourism and the State Governments/ UT Administrations  act  as  catalyst and facilitator  to  implement  various  tourism development programmes in  order  to  increase Domestic  and  Foreign Tourist  Visits.  The details of the Domestic Tourist Visits (DTVs) and Foreign Tourist Visits (FTVs) in various States / UTs is at Annexure -II. The  Ministry of  Tourism  does  not  maintain data  on tourist place wise  foreign tourist  visits  in  various  states.      *******
