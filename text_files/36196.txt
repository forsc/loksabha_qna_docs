                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3551






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






PM SVANidhi Scheme




 Gopal Jee Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether PM Street Vendor’s AtmaNirbhar Nidhi (PM SVANidh) Yojana has been launched by the Union Government to provide financial assistance to street vendors; andb)	if so, the details of beneficiaries in Darbhanga district?






ANSWER




                                                                                  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                        MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS			      (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)	(a)  Yes, Sir. Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) is implementing Prime Minister Street Vendor’s AtmaNirbhar Nidhi (PM SVANidhi) Scheme since June 01, 2020 to facilitate collateral free working capital loan to street vendors to restart their businesses, which were adversely impacted during Covid-19 pandemic.   (b)   The details of beneficiaries in Darbhanga District, as on March 16, 2022 is at Annexure.                                 *****
