                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2345






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






Introduction of Train Services in Southern Zone




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any proposal has been received from the General Manager South Zone Headquarters Chennai on the resumption and introduction of train services which are pending for more than seven years, viz, extention of Mettupalayam-Coimbatore train upto Nagercoil via Pollachi, introduction of new over night train service to Bengaluru for which proposal is pending with Railway Board and resumption of Coimbatore-Pollachi-Madurai daily passenger train services and Coimbatore-Pollachi daily passenger train services; and (b) if so, the details thereof and the action taken on these proposals?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND     ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW) (a) & (b) Indian Railways had discontinued all regular passenger carrying trains w.e.f  23.03.2020  to contain the spread of COVID-19 pandemic. To cater to the needs of the passengers of Coimbatore- Pollachi- Madurai sector, 06463/06462 Coimbatore-Palani Special and 06479/06480 Palani-Madurai Special are being operated, at present. Further, there is no immediate proposal for introduction of an overnight train between Coimbatore and Bengaluru and extension of Coimbatore-Mettupalayam train upto Nagercoil. However, subject to operational feasibility, traffic demand, availability of resources etc, introduction and extension of train services is an ongoing process on Indian Railways.*****
