                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3017






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






COVID Vaccine Sold by Private Hospital




Harsimrat Kaur Badal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a)	Whether the Government has received any complaints regarding COVID-19 vaccine meant for free distribution/inoculation are being sold by private hospitals in Punjab;(b)	If so, the details thereof;(c)	Whether the Government proposes to conduct an enquiry into the circumstances leading to such serious act of commission; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR) (a) to (d) Taking cognizance of media reports regarding procurement of COVID-19 vaccines by private hospitals from State Government and overcharging by Private Hospitals for administering the Covid-19 vaccines, the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare had formally asked the State Government for clarification. Ministry had also communicated to the States that it is in clear violation of the existing Covid vaccination strategy. As per the response from Government of Punjab, all such private hospitals were asked to return the vaccine doses forthwith by the State Government.Further, communication has been sent to all States/UTs to ensure transparency by private hospitals with respect to pricing charges for Covid vaccination.****
