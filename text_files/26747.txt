                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2374






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Farmers Training Centres




Vivek Narayan Shejwalkar














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government has taken any steps to train farmers for adopting state of the art agriculture equipments and advanced agriculture methods to increase agriculture producer by establishing Farmers Training Centres;(b)	If so, the number of farmers training centres in the country and their locations in Madhya Pradesh; and(c)	Whether the Government is considering establishing farmers training centres at those locations where this centre does not exist?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ?? ????? ?????? ??????  (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a),(b)&(c)  Department of Agriculture, and Farmers Welfare, has established following four Regional Farm Machinery training and Testing Institutes (FMTTIs), including one in Madhya Pradesh, for organizing  in campus & off campus  training programmes..1)	Central Farm Machinery Training and Testing Institute, Budni,M.P.2)	Northern Region Farm Machinery Training & Testing Institute, Hisar, Haryana 3)	Southern Region Farm Machinery Training  & Testing Institute,  Anantapur, A.P. 4)	North Eastern Region Farm Machinery Training & Testing Institute, Biswanath Chariali,  Assam  These institutes impart training on different aspects of Agricultural equipments & Machinery viz; operational techniques, Maintenance, repair and management of agricultural machinery to farmers. Beside this, Central Institute of Agricultural Engineering (CIAE)-ICAR, Bhopal, (M.P), its Regional Centre, Coimbatore, Centres of All India Coordinated Research Project(AICRPs) and Consortia Research Platforms on “Farm Mechanization and Precision Farming, (FMPF)” and “Energy in Agriculture (EA)”, are also organizing field demonstration cum training programme on improved farm implements and safety gadgets. *****
