                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4029






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Employment in MSME Sector




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) and number of persons employed therein in various states including Kerala during each of the last three years and the current year; (b)	the number of the proposals received and sanctioned during the said period and the financial assistance provided to them;(c)	the number of proposals pending for approval of the Government and the reasons  therefor; (d)	the steps taken by the Government to grant immediate approval to the pending projects; and (e)	the details and the number of posts lying vacant in each unit at present and the steps taken/being taken to fill those posts?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL & MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI) (a) to (d):	Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP), a major credit-linked subsidy programme is aimed at generating self-employment opportunities through establishment of micro-enterprises in the non-agriculture sector by helping traditional artisans and unemployed youth. Under the PMEGP, the estimated employment generated (number of persons) in micro enterprises during the years 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 (as on 31.10.2019) has been 4.08 lakh, 3.87 lakh, 5.87 lakh and 2.11 lakh respectively. Employment generated state-wise including Kerala under PMEGP for the last three year and current year (up to 31.10.2019) is given at Annexure. Details of margin money subsidy disbursed and number of projects assisted under PMEGP during 2016-17 to 2019-20 (as on 31.10.2019) are given below:<pre>S. No.	Year	Margin money subsidy utilized (Rs. in lakh)	Number of projects assisted1	2016-17	1,28,093.86 	52,9122	2017-18	1,31,240.07	48,3983	2018-19	2,07,000.54	73,4244	2019-20 (as on 31.10.2019)	82,436.58	26,480</pre>(e):       As per rules,  vacant posts are  filled on regular basis.
