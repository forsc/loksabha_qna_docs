                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3807






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Engineering Clusters under IIUS




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has set up Engineering Clusters under the Industrial Infrastructure Upgradation Scheme (IIUS) in the country;(b) 	if so, the State/UT-wise details thereof including the State of Maharashtra;(c) 	the details of the funds sanctioned/ allocated/released and utilised so far for the said clusters, State/UT-wise and cluster-wise; and(d) 	the details of the progress made in this regard so far?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):	         Yes, Sir.	(b) to (d):  The details of the Engineering Clusters projects set up in the country and funds sanctioned/allocated/released/utilized along with their progress, cluster-wise, State/UT-wise, so far, including Maharashtra, under Industrial Infrastructure Upgradation Scheme (IIUS) and Recast IIUS is at Annexure-I and that under Modified IIUS (MIIUS) is at Annexure-II. *****
