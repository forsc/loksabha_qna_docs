                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

272






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






MoU with Fiji




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Fiji for the development of the agricultural sector, recently in June 2021; and(b)	if so, the details of the benefits likely to accrue to both the countries as a result of the above said MoU?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE  ???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? 	  (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) : Yes, Sir.      (b) :  The benefits likely to accrue to both the countries from the MoU,  are in the nature of capacity building, knowledge exchange through visits of scientists and technicians, development of agricultural infrastructure, human resources and dairy industry, promotion of joint ventures between private sectors and investment in marketing &value addition/downstream processing of agricultural commodities, etc. Such cooperation aids in development of appropriate technologies and farm practices for enhancing agriculture productivity at farmer’s field and also facilitates creation of opportunities for trade in agricultural commodities through market access. India’s strategic interests are also served through partnerships with other countries.
