                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4871






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Financial Aid to States under UDAY




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of special financial aid or grant provided to States under Ujwal DISCOM Assurance Yojana (UDAY), State-wise;(b) 	whether the participating States under UDAY are expected to undergo certain improvement in areas like production, distribution, transmission of electricity etc.;(c) 	if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	the details of improvements carried out by the States in above mentioned areas?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR POWER, NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR SKILL DEVELOPMENT & ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI  R.K. SINGH)(a) : 		There is no provision for special financial aid (grants) to States under Ujwal Discom Assurance Yojana (UDAY) from the Government of India. (b) :      	Yes, Sir. UDAY is a scheme for financial turnaround of Distribution Companies (DISCOMs) with an objective to improve the operational & financial efficiency of the State DISCOMs. DISCOM for the purpose of this scheme may include combined Generation, Transmission and Distribution undertakings.(c) & (d) :	   	As a result of participation under UDAY, State Power Distribution Utilities have reported improvement which include i) Reduction of Aggregate Technical & Commercial Losses (AT&C) from 23.70% in FY16 to 23.50%, 22.38% and 22.03%  in FY17, FY18 and FY19, respectively and ii) Reduction of  Average Cost of Supply (ACS) – Average Revenue Realised (ARR) Gap from Re.0.48 per kWh in FY16 to Re.0.37 per kWh in FY17 and further to Re.0.30 per kWh in FY18. However, the ACS-ARR Gap has increased to 0.52 per kWh in FY19. The details of the performance of the States on AT&C loss and ACS-ARR gaps are enclosed as Annexure.**************
