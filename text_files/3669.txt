                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3398






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Fake Ayurvedic and Homoeopathic Drugs




S. Gnanathiraviam














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government is aware of the availability of fake Ayurvedic and Homoeopathic drugs; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government proposes to take any effective steps in this regard; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b):    Yes, Government is aware and concerned about the instances of contravention of the provisions of Drugs & Cosmetics Act, 1940 and Rules thereunder in respect of substandard quality of Ayurvedic and Homoeopathic medicines. State-wise statement of sub-standard or spurious medicines for the last four years is provided in the annexure.  (c) & (d):    Provisions for checking fake or substandard medicines are prescribed in the Drugs & Cosmetics Act and Rules and Central Government has taken following measures to ensure effective quality control of medicines in the country-i)	Quality standards of Ayurvedic and Homoeopathic medicines have been developed        and published in respective Pharmacopoeias and Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) notified under Drugs & Cosmetics Rules, 1945 for mandatory compliance by the drug manufacturers. ii)	Training programs for the State regulatory personnel and manufacturers have been organized to build up the capacity for effective quality control of medicines.Contd……-2-iii)	States/UTs are provided grant-in-aid through Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission for strengthening of enforcement framework and infrastructure of State pharmacies and drug testing laboratories to improve the capacity for quality control of medicines.iv)	Drugs & Cosmetics Rules, 1945 have been amended in respect of seeking compliance to various license requirements and conditions for manufacturing of Ayurvedic and Homoeopathic medicines. v)	Mechanism for monitoring and reporting of adverse drug reactions and misleading advertisements & exaggerated claims of AYUSH medicines has been established through Central Sector Scheme of Pharmacovigilance implemented by the Ministry of AYUSH.
