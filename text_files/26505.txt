                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2278






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Renovation of Dilapidated Building under ESI




N.K. Premachandran














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government proposes to rennovate the building under IMS department and if so, the details thereof;(b)whether the Union Government has received request from the Government of Kerala for the renovation of dilapidated building under ESI and if so the action taken thereon;(c)the details regarding the action taken for renovation, rebuild and maintenance of ESI dispensaries under IMS department in Kollam Lok Sabha constituency, Kerala;(d)whether the Union Government / ESI corporation proposes to renovate the old dilapidated ESI dispensaries in Kollam Lok Sabha constituency in a time bound manner if so, the action taken thereon;and(e)the present status of action taken for the renovation and reconstruction of ESI dispensaries building in Kollam Lok Sabha constituency?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):		There are 49 dispensaries and 9 hospitals of the Insurance Medical Services (IMS), Government of Kerala which are housed in buildings owned by the Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC). Repairs and renovation of these buildings are done as and when needed on a continuing basis by the ESIC. Contd..2/-:: 2 ::From the year 2019 onwards, following works have been done:i.	Major repairs and renovations have been carried out in ESI hospitals at Feroke, Olarikkara and ESI dispensaries at Ollur, Punalur and Thottada. ii.	Waterproofing works have also been completed at ESI hospitals at Mulamkunnathukavu, Ernakulam and Feroke and ESI dispensaries at Perumbavoor and Pathirapally.  iii.	Electrical works have been carried out in ESI hospitals at Olarikkara, Thottada, Peroorkada, Feroke and ESI dispensary at Alagappanagar.  iv.	Boundary wall reconstruction has been done in ESI hospitals at Palakkad, Thottada and ESI dispensaries at Mavoor, Eranhipalam, Keralapuram and Aluva. (b):		Yes, Sir.  Letters have been received from the State Government, on the basis of which following actions have been taken:-i.	Necessary work order for renovation of Staff Quarters at Punalur issued.  ii.	Tender for renovation work at ESI dispensary at Fort Kochi floatediii.	Repair work of Staff Quarters at Karamana dispensary completed.iv.	Dispensary at Chakkorathukulam has been shifted to another ESI dispensary building  and demolition of dilapidated building has been approved.(c) to (e):	There are 17 dispensaries in Kollam Lok Sabha constituency. The list of 17 dispensaries together with the work of renovation of these dispensary buildings is placed at Annexure. *                                             ******
