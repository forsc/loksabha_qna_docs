                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

125






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Development of Tourism in Bihar




Mahabali Singh













                                                    Rama Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to develop and promote tourism in Bihar; (b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the names of the schemes implemented or being implemented by the Central Government to promote tourism in Bihar; (c) 	the names of places identified for this purpose along with the total amount allocated/utilized for the development and upgradation of the identified tourist places during the last three years; (d) 	whether any proposal has been received from the State Government in this regard and if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	whether the Union Government is incurring the whole cost on the implementation of these tourism projects or the same is being done on sharing basis and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c):	The Ministry of Tourism promotes India as a holistic destination and as part of its on-going activities, releases domestic and international print, electronic and online media campaigns under the ‘Incredible India ’brand-line, to promote various tourism destinations and products of the Country including Bihar. Promotions are also undertaken through the Website and Social Media accounts of the Ministry. In addition, the India Tourism Offices in India and overseas undertake various promotional activities with the objective of showcasing the tourism potential of the different States.Ministry of Tourism, Government of India provides financial assistance under its schemes such as Swadesh Darshan , National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD), Central Finance (CFA) Assistance to State / Union Territory Administrations for overall promotion and development of infrastructure of tourism in the country. The projects under the schemes are identified in consultation with the State Governments/ Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier.(d):	The information pertaining to projects sanctioned for the state of Bihar is given in Annexure.  (e):	Swadesh Darshan Scheme of Ministry of Tourism is a central sector scheme. PRASHAD Scheme is 100% GOI funded Central Sector Scheme.Subject to the scheme guidelines, Ministry of Tourism extends Central Financial Assistance upto Rs.50 Lacs per financial year to State Governments for organizing Fairs & Festivals.*******
