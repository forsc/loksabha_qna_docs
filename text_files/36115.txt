                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

317






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Development of Economic Corridor




Bhartruhari Mahtab














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to construct a special economic 	corridor from Paradip of Jagatsinghpur district to Barabil of Kendujhar 	district in the State of Odisha considering the business potential of 	the area;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the time by which the 	construction of said corridor is likely to be completed:(c)	the details of the ongoing/pending/completed National Highway (NH) 	projects in the State along with the funds allocated, sanctioned and 	utilised for the purpose during the last three years and the current 	year;(d)	the steps taken/being taken by the Government to complete the 	pending projects within the stipulated period of time;(e)	the details of State Highways converted into NHs in the State during 	the said period; and(f)	the details of mechanism put in place by the Government to monitor 	and check the construction quality and maintenance work of NHs?






ANSWER




                                                         ¬THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (f)  A statement is laid on the Table of the House.
