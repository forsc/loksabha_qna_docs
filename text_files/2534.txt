                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2315






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Theft of Idols




Devusinh Jesingbhai Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of theft cases of rare idols/artifacts reported during each of the last three years and the current year and the action taken to recover them during the said period, State /UT –wise;   (b) 	whether the Government has taken steps to prevent such incidents and provide proper security at these temples to protect such artifacts/idols; and  (c)	if so, the details thereof?    






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE, (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM (PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)     Six cases of theft of antiquities have been reported from the centrally protected monuments / sites / site museums managed by the Archaeological Survey of India across the country during the last three years and the current year. Details are at Annexure-I.When any theft of antiquity is reported, First Information Report is lodged in concerned police station and look out notice is issued to law enforcing agencies including custom exit channels to keep vigil to trace stolen antiquity and prevent illegal export.(b) and (c)  Yes Sir. Archaeological Survey of India is committed towards the protection of monuments, sites and museums under its jurisdiction. Besides regular watch and ward staff of Archaeological Survey of India, Private Security Guards, State Police Armed Guards, Central Industrial Security Force have been deployed as per requirement.
