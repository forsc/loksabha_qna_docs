                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

262






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






MSP of Rabi Crops




DNV Senthilkumar. S.













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    Manickam Tagore B
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total quantum of wheat production in Rabi Crop during this year; (b)	 whether the wheat produced by the farmers as Rabi crops have been procured by the Government at the prescribed Minimum Support Price (MSP) in the country and if so, the total quantum of wheat procured; (c) 	whether the wheat harvested and taken by farmers to Mandi could not be procured due to delay in procurement by Government agency resulting in rotting of wheat in open and loss to farmers; (d)	 if so, the details thereof and the action taken against the responsible officers; (e) 	whether the farmers had also incurred losses due to non-availability of agricultural labourers and scarcity of machines due to covid-19 pandemic during harvesting of rabi crop; and (f) 	if so, whether the Government will compensate the losses incurred by farmers through some financial assistance?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The wheat production in the country is estimated at 107.59 million tonnes as per the Fourth Advance Estimates, 2019-20.(b):	Yes, the Wheat produced by the farmers as Rabi crops have been procured by the Government at the prescribed Minimum Support Price in the country. Total procurement of Wheat during RMS 2020-21 for Central Pool is 389.85 LMT. (c) & (d):	There has been no loss to the farmers due to delay in procurement by Government agency.  Extensions in procurement period and relaxations in Uniform Specifications have been granted by Government of India on the requests of State Governments.(e) & (f):	No, such losses have been incurred by the farmers and all time record high of 389.85 LMT wheat has been procured from farmers at MSP. *******
