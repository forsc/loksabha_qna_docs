                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3944






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Nai Udaan Scheme




Jagdambika Pal 














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of progress made with regard to the Nai Udaan scheme;(b)	the total number of candidates provided with financial support under the same; and(c)	the total number of successful candidates thereof, State-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	to (c):Ministry of Minority Affairs has been implementing NaiUdaan Scheme to support minority candidates who clear Preliminary Examinations conducted by Union Public Service Commission (UPSC), Staff Selection Commission (SSC)and State Public Service Commissions (SPSCs). The scheme was reviewed and revised in 2017, and the number of minority candidates for support was increased from 800 per annum to 2000 per annum. Family income limit was also enhanced from Rs. 4.50 Lakh per annum to 6.00 Lakh per annum. The scheme has been further revised w.e.f. 01.04.2019 and number of minority candidates for support under the NaiUdaan Scheme has now been increased to 5100 per annum and family income limit has also been enhanced uptoRs. 8.00 Lakh per annum.  The following exams namely Indian Engineering Services & Indian Forest Services conducted by UPSC, Central Armed Police Forces Exams (Sub-Inspector Level) conducted by SSC and Graduate Level Exams for Non-Gazetted Posts conducted by SPSCs have also been included in the Scheme w.e.f. 2019-20.Year-wise number of minority candidateswho cleared prelims of UPSC, SSC and SPSCs and provided financial support and amount released under the scheme is given below: Year	No. of candidates	Amount released (Rs. in crore)2013-14	483	1.942014-15	786	2.972015-16	1071	3.972016-17	858	4.002017-18	1427	6.212018-19	1187	6.722019-20 (upto 30.11.2019)	885	5.01Total	669730.82Details of the candidates to whom financial support has been provided w.e.f. 2013-14 to 2018-19 is available on the website of this Ministry i.e. www.minorityaffairs.gov.in. *******
