                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2242






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






PMKK in Maharashtra




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKK) operating in the country presently including State of Maharashtra;(b)	the details of the number of youth who got employment through PMKK during the current and last two years, State-wise;(c)	whether the Government proposes to increase the number of PMKK centres in the State of Maharashtra;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (e)	Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) through National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) promotes establishment of model and aspirational skill centres known as Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKK) in every district throughout the country for imparting skill training through its flagship scheme Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY 2016-20). As on 11.11.2019, 812 PMKKs have been allocated in 707 districts across the country. Out of allocated PMKKs, 681 PMKKs have been established. In State of Maharashtra, 50 PMKKs have been allocated and out of which, 41 PMKKs have been established.
