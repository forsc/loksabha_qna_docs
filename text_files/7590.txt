                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1201






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Conversion of Historical Buildings as Hotels




Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the archaeological buildings identified under Archaeological Surveyof India in the country so far;(b)	whether archaeological buildings have been destroyed and have been reconstructed as hotels, clubs, complexes and marriage halls;(c)	whether the Government has taken note of this matter; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	There are 3691 monuments/sites declared Centrally Protected under the Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act, 1958 and looked after by Archaeological Survey of India (ASI).  A statement indicating State/UT-wise number of monuments/sites is given at Annexure.(b)	No such incident has been reported at any centrally protected monument.(c)&(d)	Does not arise.
