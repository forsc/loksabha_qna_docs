                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3121






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Mining in Protected Forest Areas




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is a prohibition on mining activities in protected forest areas; (b)	if so, the details of circulars/orders issued by the Government in this regard; (c)	whether mining activities are being carried out in protected forest areas of many States across the country and if so, the details of such cases including those reported from Rajasthan; (d)	whether the Government has granted any permission for carrying out mining activities in protected forest areas; and (e)	if so, the legal basis of the same along with State-wise details of such cases during the last ten years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (e) As per the order of the Hon’ble Supreme Court dated 04.08.2006 in the Writ Petition (C) no. 202/1995 the permission for mining can not be granted inside any National Park and Wildlife Sanctuary.However, the mining activities can be permitted in Protected Forest (PF) Areas by the concerned State Government subject to prior approval of Central Government under the provisions of Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. The State/UT wise details of such cases approved during last 10 years under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 for mining in forest areas (including Protected Forest Areas) is annexed.***
