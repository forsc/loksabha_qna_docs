                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3903






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Terrorist Incidents after Repeal of Article 370




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of terrorist incidents in the country till date after revocation of Article 370 in Kashmir; and(b) the difference between the facilities provided to the dependents of martyrs of Armed Forces and Paramilitary forces?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a):	No major terror attack took place in the hinterland of the country during the period from 05.08.2019 to 10.03.2020.  However, 79 terrorist incidents have taken place in Jammu and Kashmir during the period from 05.08.2019 to 10.03.2020 in which 49 terrorists have been neutralized.(b):	The details of admissible benefits given to the next of kin of Army personnel and CAPFs personnel are at Annex-I and Annex-II respectively.*********
