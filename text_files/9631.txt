                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3150






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Compensatory Afforestation Management and Planning Authority




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Compensatory Afforestation Management and Planning Authority (CAMPA) has been constituted; (b)	if so, the details thereof along with its composition; (c)	the details of the funds disbursed during the last five years, State/UT-wise; (d)	the details of the funds received by the said authority during the last five years, along with the associated project for which the compensation was paid; and (e)	the details of the area of land brought under afforestation through CAMPA, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)& (b) Yes, Sir.Compensatory Afforestation Fund (CAF) Act, 2016was enacted in August, 2016 and Compensatory Afforestation Funds Rules, 2018 were notified in August, 2018. As per notification no. S.O. 3967 (E) dated 13.08.2018, the Central Government appointed the 30th day of September, 2018, as the date on which the provisions of the Act came into force. As per Section 8(1) of CAF Act, 2016, the National Authority called the National Compensatory Afforestation Management and Planning Authority(CAMPA) consists of a Governing Body and isto be assisted by an Executive Committee, Monitoring Groups and administrative support mechanism.  (c) Details of the funds disbursed by Ad-hoc CAMPA to the States/UTs during the last five years are annexed at Annexure-A. Funds to the tune of Rs.47436 crore havealso been disbursed from theNational Fund to the State Funds in August, 2019. The details are annexed at Annexure-B.(d)Details of the compensatory leviesreceived by Ad-hoc CAMPA during the last five years are annexed at Annexure-C.(e) State/UTs wise details of land brought under CA are annexed at Annexure-D.***
