                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

520






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Permission for opration of wide body aircraft




K. Muraleedharan














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CIVIL AVIATIONbe pleased to state:-(a) whether it is fact that the Directorate General of civil Aviation is yet to give permission to operate wide body aircrafts from Calicut Airport;(b) if so, the reasons therefor;(c) whether the expert committee submitted any report regarding the Calicut airport for the operation of wide body aircraft; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. The commercial operations of wide bodied aircraft (Code E) from Calicut airport are temporarily suspended due to Air India Express aircraft accident at the airport on 07.08.2020 and the prevailing adverse weather conditions.(c) & (d) A team from Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) had visited the Calicut airport to review the wide body aircraft operations at the airport. In the report of the team, eight observations were made which were forwarded to the airport operator. As per airport operator, permanent action has been taken on some observations. However, the operations of wide body aircraft from Calicut airport is subject to satisfactory outcome of the Aircraft Accident Investigation Bureau''s (AAIB) accident investigation report.
