                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

239






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Roads Construction under PMGSY




Pradeep Kumar Singh













                                                    Shantanu Thakur
                                                








                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                








                                                    Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of roads constructed under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) including the lengths of roads in Gujarat, West Bengal and Bihar;(b) 	the funds sanctioned and released for each component of roads and the status of their completion during the last four years in Gujarat, West Bengal and Bihar;(c) 	the details of roads being planned/approved/under consideration for the upcoming projects in the States including Gujarat, West Bengal and Bihar, district-wise; and(d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): The details of roads constructed under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) including the lengths of roads in Gujarat, West Bengal and Bihar is as per Annexure –I.(b): The funds sanctioned and released under PMGSY  and the status of their utilization during the last four years in Gujarat, West Bengal and Bihar is as per Annexure- II. The status of completion of roads is reflected in Annexure-I.(c) to (d): The allocated road length to Bihar, West Bengal and Gujarat under PMGSY III is as under.Sl No	State	         Length in Km1	Bihar	           6162.502	West Bengal	   6287.503	Gujarat	           3012.50No proposal has so far been received and pending for sanction at Ministry of Rural Development under PMGSY-III  in respect of West Bengal and Bihar States for the year 2019-20. Gujarat, has submitted aproposal under PMGSY-III for its processing/sanction in the Ministry of Rural Development..The district wise proposal submitted by Gujarat is attached as Annexure III.*****
