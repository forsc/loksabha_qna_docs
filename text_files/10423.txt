                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3829






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Performance of Production Units




Sunil Kumar Mondal













                                                    A. Ganeshamurthi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of production units of the Indian Railways, name-wise;(b) the details of the performance of production units of Indian Railways during the last five years and the current year;(c) the income earned through these production units in the last three years;(d) the total number of employees working in all these production units;(e) if so, whether the Government has any new futuristic plan for the progress of production units of Indian Railways; and(f) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (f) A 	Statement is laid on the Table of the House.******
