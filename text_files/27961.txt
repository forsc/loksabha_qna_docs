                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3676






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Setting up of Cold Storage




Horen Sing Bey














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the procedure for setting up of cold storage under Ministry of Food Processing Industries at State level;(b)	the number of cold storages being operated in the country, State-UT-wise till date, particularly in the State of Assam.(c)	Whether the Govt. has any proposal / plan for setting up cold storage in Karbi Anglong Autonomous District (ST) in the State of Assam Under Ministry of Food Processing;(d)	If so the details thereof;(e)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a):	Ministry of Food Processing does not support stand alone cold storage facilities. However, the Schemes for Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure, Agro Processing Cluster and Mega Food Park includes setting up cold storage facilities as one of its components. These schemes are demand driven schemes, and potential entrepreneurs from all over the country, including from Karbi Anglong autonomous district (ST) State of Assam can apply for the projects under the schemes against Expression of Interest (EoI) issued from time to time. The projects are considered for selection for grants- in- aid first by Technical Committee and then by Inter-Ministerial Approval Committee (IMAC), according to the eligibility criteria provided under the relevant scheme guidelines. Stand alone cold storage projects are supported by Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare under their Mission for Integrated  Development of Horticulture (MIDH).(b):	Details of cold storage facilities created from completed Cold Chain projects, and Mega Food Parks in the country including State of Assam are in the Annexures A and B.  Information received from Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare regarding stand alone cold storage projects is at Annexure C.                                                                                                                                                .(c) & (d): Ministry has approved one project namely M/s Karbi Anglong Autonomous Council at Karbi Anglong in Assam under Agro Processing Cluster Scheme under which cold storage of 1000 MT capacity has been provided as part of core processing infrastructure.(e):	does not arise.*****
