                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3831






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Testing of Seeds and Fertilizers




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of institutes for testing of seeds and fertilizers in the country along with the details thereof;(b)	the total amount spent on the said institutes during the last three years along with the total testing work carried out by them; (c)	whether several problems are being faced in testing of specimen in the said institutes due to shortage of sufficient staff and equipments; and(d)	if so, the corrective steps taken by the Government in this regard and the target fixed for specimen testing by the said institutes during last three years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS’ WELFARE???? ?? ??????????? ?????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a).   	The 167 notified seed testing laboratories are working across the Country. The National Seed Research & Training Centre (NSRTC) Varanasi (Uttar Pradesh) under the Department of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare is working as Central Seed Testing Laboratory which analyse 5% seeds samples received from various notified Seed Testing laboratories to make uniformity in seed testing. Besides, NSRTC is also working as referral seed testing laboratory for the court referred seed samples. The details of notified Seed Testing laboratories are at Annexure-I.	  	There are 83 notified fertilizer quality control laboratories working in the Country with analysing capacity of 190100 samples per annum. Out of 83 laboratories, 4 laboratories are under the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare and remaining 79 laboratories are notified in different States. The details of State wise number of fertilizer laboratories and number of fertilizer samples analysed are at Annexure-II. (b). 		The Department of Agriculture & Farmers’ Welfare provides financial assistance for strengthening of notified seed testing laboratories under Sub-Mission on Seeds & Planting Material. An amount of Rs. 17.59 Crore has been released to the States/ implementing agencies for strengthening of Seed Testing Laboratories during last three years.                 In addition to this, the details of amount spent and numbers of seed samples tested in the Central Seed Testing Laboratory, NSRTC, Varanasi (Uttar Pradesh) under the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare during last three years is as under:S. No.	Year	No. of seed samples tested	Expenditure (Rs. in Crore)1.	2018-19	19812	4.242.	2019-20	20334	2.353.	2020-21	20611	2.12	The total amount spent on 4 fertilizer quality control laboratories under the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare during last three years is as under: S. No.	Year	Expenditure (Rs. in Crore)1.	2018-19	7.252.	2019-20	5.973.	2020-21	4.94 (c) &(d). 	No specific problem has been reported by the States due to shortage of staff. There are no targets fixed by the Govt. of India.
