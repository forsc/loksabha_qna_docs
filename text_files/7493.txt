STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

101






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Hostels for ST Girls and Boys




Rahul Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of assistance released under the Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-Scheme, State-wise;(b) 	whether the Ministry has received any proposals from the Government of Kerala for the construction of new hostels for ST boys and girls;(c) 	if so, the details thereof including the list of operational hostels in Kerala; and(d) 	the details of the funds sanctioned under the erstwhile scheme of ‘Hostels for ST Girls and Boys’?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (d):	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.***** Statement referred to in reply to Lok Sabha Starred Question No.*101 for answer on 25.11.2019(a): The state-wise details of the funds released under Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-Scheme under the Umbrella Scheme for Development of STs during the year 2019-20 is at Annexure – I.(b) & (c): During 2019-20, an amount of Rs. 292.67 lakhs was approved for release for ST hostel projects in Kerala. This amount relates to balance amount for projects for hostels for (ST) Girls at Palakkad and Kunnur that were taken up as new projects during 2018-19 for a total cost of Rs 500.00 lakh. .Hostels are constructed under various schemes of the Central Government, State Government and Voluntary Organisation for various purposes. Details of all hostels is not maintained centrally.
