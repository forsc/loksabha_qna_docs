                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5275






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Construction Work on NH-19




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has approved the construction of a new four- lane Bridge (parallel to the existing MG Setu) across river Ganga on NH-19 in Bihar; (b) if so, the details and the present status thereof; (c) the present status of work on NH-19 project in Bihar; (d) whether the Government is facing any hurdles in completion of NH-19 project and if so, the remedial measures taken by the Government in this regard; (e) the funds allocated, released and utilised for the purpose; and (f) the time by which the said project is likely to be completed and the steps being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)  & (b) The project of ‘construction of a new 4 lane Bridge (parallel to the MG Setu) across river Ganga on NH-19’ in Bihar has been approved with the condition that invitation of bids shall be called within 10 days after the award is announced under section 3G and forest clearance. The State Government of Bihar has recently awarded 3G for land acquisition, however, forest clearance is awaited. (c) & (d) The physical progress of ‘4-laning work on Chhapra- Hazipur Section of NH- 19’ is about 80% at present. The Government is facing hurdles of unavailability of land due to various reasons& scarcity of resources with concessionaire. The Government has taken remedial measures by infusion of fund through NHAI and lenders. State Government of Bihar has been requested to complete land acquisition at the earliest. The lenders have also been requested for timely release of funds under One Time Fund Infusion Scheme (OTFIS). (e)& (f) The funds of Rs. 347.00 Crore, Rs. 234.56 Crore & Rs. 231.44 Crore have been allocated, released and utilized respectively. The said project is likely to be completed 	by March, 2020.*****
