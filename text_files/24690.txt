                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

491






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Green Power




Mahua Moitra














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Solar Energy Corporation of India (SECI) is issuing tenders for Round-the-Clock (RTC) green power of coal combined renewable energy rather than natural gas combined renewable energy;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(c)	whether the Government proposes any holistic green transition strategy to focus on green power; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) & (b) Solar Energy Corporation of India Limited (SECI) has issued a tender for supply of Round-The-Clock (RTC) power from grid-connected renewable power projects complemented with power from any other source or storage. Power from any other source refers to power from any Power Generating Systems other than Renewable Power. (c) & (d) Government had set up a target for installed renewable power generation capacity of 175 GW by the year 2022 and has also issued a year wise trajectory for Renewable Purchase Obligation (RPO) to facilitate transtition to Green Power. *****
