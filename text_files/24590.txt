                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

601






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Facilities at Calicut International Airport




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any steps to develop the facilities to ensure safety at the Calicut International Airport in Karipur in the aftermath of the recent plane crash(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether the Government proposes to restore the Haj embarkation point at Calicut International Airport for the upcoming Haj Pilgrimage to reduce the difficulties for the pilgrims from the northern districts?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) & (b) Adequate safety measures are in place for ensuring safe operation of aircraft at Calicut International Airport as per International Civil  Aviation  Organization  (ICAO)  standards  and  DGCA  Civil  Aviation  Requirements.  However, as a measure of abundant caution, a Committee was constituted to suggest additional safety measures at some airports including Calicut. The Committee has suggested various measures including:;   (i) exploring the feasibility of providing 280m runway strip instead of 150m at present, ;  (ii) construction of Runway End Safety Area at both ends of runway to reduce payload restrictions of aircraft, ;  (iii) exploring the feasibility of increasing length of runway and,;  (iv) filling/levelling and construction of embankments.;The Government has requested the State Government of Kerala to provide 152.25 acres of land free of cost and free from all encumbrances for executing the works as per recommendation made by the Committee.  ;(c) Calicut International Airport was not designated as an Embarkation Point for  Haj  2021.  The embarkation points are decided by the Ministry of Minority Affairs on the recommendation of Haj Committee of India.
