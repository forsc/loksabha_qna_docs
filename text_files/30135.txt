                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1665






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Phase 4 of PMGKY




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya













                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the percentage of foodgrains which has been offtaken so far across the country under Phase 4 of Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana (PMGKY); and (b) 	the total food grains which have been offtaken from Gujarat till now under this scheme? 






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	Out of total allocation of 198.79 LMT, around 191.23 LMT foodgrains (i.e. approx 96.20%) have been lifted by States/UTs upto 30.11.2021, under Phase 4 of Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY)(b):  	Out of total allocation of 8.54 LMT, around 8.41 LMT foodgrains have been lifted by Gujarat upto 30.11.2021, under Phase 4 of Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY)****
