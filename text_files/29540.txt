                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1333






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Proposal to Intensify Tourism for Re-employment




Kalyan Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether it is a fact that to revive tourism, there is a need to kickstart recovery and growth in the country;(b)	 whether it is a fact that tourism employs one in every ten people on earth and because of the pandemic over 30 million direct tourism jobs are lost in the country; and (c)	  the proposal of the Government to intensify the tourism sector and re-employment therein?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM                                   (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a):	Yes, Sir. (b):	As per “United Nations Policy brief: COVID-19 and Transforming Tourism”, according to 2019 data, tourism employed one in every ten people globally.Ministry of Tourism engaged National Council of Applied Economic Research (NCAER) to conduct study on “India and the Corona Virus Pandemic: Economic Losses for Households Engaged in Tourism and Policies for Recovery”. As per the study, a significant number of jobs were lost in the tourism sector once the lockdown was implemented. (c):	The details of steps taken by Government to intensify the tourism sector and re-employment in tourism sector are at Annexure.*********
