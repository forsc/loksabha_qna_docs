                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1089






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Health conditions of Miners at Hindustan Zinc Mines




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government is aware of the deteriorating health conditions of miners working at Hindustan Zinc mines due to access lead found in their lungs; and (b)if so, the precautionary measures taken and the healthcare facilities provided for the ill workers?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b):	No case of excess lead in blood amongst the workers employed in the mines of M/s. Hindustan Zinc Limited (HZL) has been reported.  The mine management has been taking following precautions to prevent the exposure of mine workers to hazardous lead level:i.)	Automatic water sprinkling at all crushers, ore transfer points are provided.ii.)	Lead & zinc concentrate are loaded mechanically (by air conditioned cabin loaders) and concentrate transported in trucks which are covered by tarpaulin to prevent the exposure of workmen due to concentrate dust.iii)	Quarterly medical checkup is carried out for detection of lead level in the blood as well as in urine.iv)	Adequate medical facilities are provided at all mines and are equipped with proper facilities for detection of lead level.********
