                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2687






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Exams in Hindi Medium




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to implement Hindi as a medium of writing exam for recruitment to various posts; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor along with the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) to (c):  Major Recruitment Agencies of the Central Government viz. Union Public Service Commission (UPSC), Staff Selection Commission (SSC), Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) etc conduct recruitment examinations as per the recruitment rules framed and notified by the Nodal Departments/ Ministries of the Government of India. The preliminary examinations conducted by these agencies comprise of multiple choice objective type questions, set in Hindi and English. In the Civil Services (Mains) Exam conducted by UPSC, a candidate has option to write his answers in Hindi, except in case of Languages Paper. Railway Recruitment Boards provide Hindi language as a medium to candidates for writing exams for recruitment to various posts.Hindi is already one of the mediums for all Departmental Examinations being conducted or monitored in Department of Posts.Regarding the further steps taken, the Government is seized of the matter. *****
