                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2383






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Seed Testing Laboratories




S. Gnanathiraviam













                                                    Vijay Kumar
                                                








                                                    Rajkumar Chahar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the names of places in the country where seed testing laboratories are located in the country, State wise and their functions thereof; (b)	whether any proposal is under consideration of the Government to set up more Seed Testing Laboratories in the country particularly in major towns and also at block level in rural areas; (c)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Tamil Nadu; (d)	the number of poor quality samples detected by these laboratories; and (e)	the manner in which new laboratories would further promote production of quality   seeds in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE       ???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? 	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): Seed Testing Laboratories analyse the seed quality standard viz. germination per cent, purity, moisture content and admixture of seeds of other distinguish varieties/crops, inert matter etc. thereby enabling the farming community to get quality seeds for better production and productivity. The list of state wise seed testing laboratories is at Annexure-I.(b) & (c): The Department provides financial support to States/UTs for strengthening of Seed Testing Laboratories for purchase of new equipment, DNA Finger Printing Facilities and specialized Seed Health Testing Unit, office automation/ communication facilities etc. under Sub-Mission on Seeds and Planting Material (SMSP).(d)&(e): The information in respect to poor quality /sub-standard seed samples as reported by various States/ UTs is at Annexure-II. New Seed Testing Laboratories will further strengthen the seed quality control regime in the states and to make sure the availability of quality seeds to the farmers.
