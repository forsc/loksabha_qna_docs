                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2674






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Progress of DAY-NULM




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the progress of the Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana-National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM) under its various components, State-wise;b)	the number of livelihoods created, persons skilled and trained, ID cards issued to street vendors, shelter space created for homeless and certificate of vending issued to the beneficiaries in Jharkhand, district-wise;c)	the number of beneficiaries and Self-Help Groups (SHGs) created under DAY-NULM in Jharkhand, district-wise; andd)	the number of beneficiaries and the loan disbursed under the PM SVANidhi Scheme, State-wise and district-wise in Jharkhand?






ANSWER




                                                                              THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                   MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                       (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)	The statement showing State-wise physical progress of the Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM) under various components is at Annexure-I.(b)	The statement showing district-wise number of livelihoods created, persons skilled and trained, ID cards issued to street vendors, shelter space created for homeless and certificate of vending issued to the beneficiaries in Jharkhand is at Annexure-II.(c)	The statement showing district-wise number of Self-Help Groups (SHGs) created under DAY-NULM and beneficiaries mobilised into these SHGs in Jharkhand is at Annexure-III.(d)	The statement showing the State-wise number of beneficiaries and loans disbursed under PM SVANidhi scheme is at Annexure-IV. Statement showing the district-wise number of beneficiaries and loans disbursed under PM SVANidhi scheme in Jharkhand is at Annexure-V.                             *****
