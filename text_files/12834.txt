                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1684






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Integrated B.Ed. Programme




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has launched a four year integrated B.Ed. programme to improveteacher education;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government is considering to choose 700 colleges as model teacher traininginstitutions and one such college in every district; and(d) whether it is true that the Government is considering to review the outdated curriculum ofteacher training?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d) : Yes Sir, National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE) has notified Norms andStandards for four year integrated B.Ed. programme in gazette dated 29th March, 2019. After that,NCTE has invited applications from the eligible institutions online from 3rd June 2019 to 31st July,2019. The model curriculum prepared for this course includes crucial aspects like Gender,Inclusive education, ICT, Yoga, Global Citizenship Education (GCED) and Health & Sanitation.The teaching specialization would primarily be for the primary levels and the secondary level.A proposal has been submitted by NCTE to select and mentor model Teacher EducationInstitutes (TEIs), in the country.*****
