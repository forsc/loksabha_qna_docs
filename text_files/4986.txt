                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4589






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Funds to Central Universities




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of funds allocated/ released to Central Universities during the last three years;(b) the criteria to determine the scale of fund to each university;(c) the steps taken by the Government to ensure proper utilisation of the funds by CentralUniversities;(d) whether the Government is proposing to reduce the fund allocation of Jamia Millia Islamia, aCentral University having minority status; and(e) if so, the details thereof and reasons therefor along with the details of funds allocated to thisuniversity?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): The details of the funds released by the University Grant Commission (UGC) to CentralUniversities during the last three years is as under:<pre>(Rs. In Crores)Year Amount Released2016-17 6330.932017-18 7239.652018-19 6425.10</pre>(b): The allocation of funds under Salary and Recurring Heads is made on the basis of therequirement projected by the University and expenditure incurred during the previous year aswell as availability of funds. Upto the year 2017-18, the funding for all components viz. salary,recurring and capital heads was being done through the UGC. Now, the funding for theinfrastructure development in the Central Universities based on their demand is done throughHigher Education Funding Agency (HEFA) from the year 2018-19.(c): The University Grants Commission (UGC) reviews the financial performance of eachCentral University on a periodical basis for ensuring proper utilization of funds and for providingfurther financial assistance to the Universities. Further, the accounts of Central Universities aremandatorily subjected to audit by the Comptroller & Auditor General (C&AG) of India everyyear and the Annual Accounts duly audited are required to be tabled in both Houses of theParliament.(d): No, Sir.(e): Does not arise.* * * * *
