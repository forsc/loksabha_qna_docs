                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4367






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






NH Projects in Rajasthan




Jaskaur Meena













                                                    Nihal Chand Chauhan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of ongoing/pending National Highway Projects in the country including Rajasthan during the last two years and the details thereof; (b) the funds allocated and utilised for these projects so far; (c) the target set in this regard along with the steps taken to achieve the same;(d) whether the Union Government has received proposals from the  Government of Rajasthan to     declare some roads as National Highways in the State ; and(e) if so , the details thereof along with the steps being taken in this regard ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) 1,440 sanctioned projects amounting to Rs. 5,85,582 crore having length 49,740 km are ongoing in the country, as of December 2019, which includes 63 projects amounting to Rs. 28,270 crore having length 3,820 km in the state of Rajasthan. The funds allocated & released during the last two years and current year are given at Annexure-I. Projects are given a time period of 2 to 3 years for completion.  In order to ensure timely completion of the projects and their subsequent maintenance, regular review meetings are held in headquarter by Ministry/NHAI with Regional Officers, concessionaires /contractors. Further, various steps such as streamlining of land acquisition & environment clearances, exit for equity investors, premium re-scheduling, close coordination with other Ministries, revamping of dispute resolution mechanism are also taken for timely completion of projects.(d) and (e) The Ministry keeps on receiving proposals from various State Governments/Union Territories (UTs), etc., including from the State Government of Rajasthan for declaration of State roads as new National Highways. Declaration of State roads as new National Highways can be taken up after finalization of the policy/guidelines which is under consideration by the Central Government depending on traffic, connectivity and techno-economic feasibility based on outcome of DPR.
