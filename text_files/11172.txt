                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

219






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Production of Crude Oil and Natural Gas




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of crude oil and natural gas production from the State of Andhra Pradesh during the last five years, yearwise, company-wise and product-wise;(b) whether the KG Basin has a lot of scope and potential to produce huge quantity of natural gas and crude oil; and(c) if so, the action plan prepared by the Ministry to explore and exploit the same?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) The details of crude oil and natural gas production from the State of Andhra Pradesh during the last five years i.e. from 2014-15 to 2018-19 are as under:Crude Oil Production (in Thousand Metric Tonnes)Company2014-152015-162016-172017-182018-19ONGC272322292332311RIL266.66207.78145.5992.7432.51Cairn Energy1102.021019.33807.90717.46599.45Natural Gas Production (in Million Metric Standard Cubic Meters)Company2014-152015-162016-172017-182018-19ONGC659831133815612186RIL4461.913939.972862.091922.401029.44Cairn Energy324.13260.08192.42214.06220.72Page 2 of 2(b) & (c) Under the Hydrocarbon Exploration and Licensing Policy (HELP)/Open Acreage Licensing Policy (OALP) regime, four bidding rounds have been finalized in which 8 exploration blocks have been awarded in KG Basin covering an area of approximately 14,135 sq. km. Under Discovered Small Field (DSF) Policy Bid Round I & II, 11 contract areas have been awarded in KG Basin. Under National Seismic Programme (NSP) surface coverage of 980.80 Line Kilometre has been achieved in KG Basin.********
