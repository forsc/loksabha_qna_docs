                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1512






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Renovation of ROB at Kumardhubi




Pashupati Nath Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the development work of Railway Over Bridge (ROB) in Kumardhubi district of Dhanbad under Asansol Railway Manager Office is going on at snail’s pace as a result of which this bridge is unlikely to be completed in its scheduled time;(b)	if so, the reaction of the Government thereto; and  (c)	the time by which the ROB is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): Road over Bridge (ROB) in lieu of existing two lane Road Under Bridge (RUB) No. 20 between Kumardhubi and Mugma station is in progress as per scope at site available. The progress of the said ROB at present is as under:Railway Portion (ROB Proper): Work completed in February 2019.Approach Portion: Progress is hampered due to encroachments and the pucca structures.(b):	State Government is being pursued for shifting of utilities and removal of encroachments.(c): Target date for completion of the ROB will depend upon removal of all encroachments and shifting of utilities by the State Government.*****
