                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Beautification of Heritage Sites, Temples and Monuments




Ajay Nishad














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the State-wise details of world heritage sites, temples and monuments being conserved by Central Government in the country including Bihar;(b)	the details of funds sanctioned, released and spent by State Government during each of the last three years and the current year till date;(c)	the total number of tourists who visited these places and the revenue earned from them during the said period; and(d)	the steps taken by the Government for beautification protection and conservation of these tourist places?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE  AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	The State-wise details of protected monuments comprising world heritage sites, temples and other monuments conserved by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) in the country including Bihar are given in Annexure-I.(b)	The details of funds allocated and expenditure incurred for conservation of protected monuments in the country by the ASI during the last three years and the current year are given in Annexure-II. The expenditure for ASI protected monuments in the country including Bihar  is met by Central Government.(c)	The details of  total number of tourists who visited the ticketed monuments of ASI and revenue earned during the last three years and current year are as under:S.
