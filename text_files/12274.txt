                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1156






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Appointments of SCs/STs in NRL and OIL




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) The details of posts of SCs/STs currently lying vacant in Oil India Limited (OIL) and the time by which they are likely to be filled; and(b) The details of the appointments made and the promotions in NRL, Oil India Limited during the last three years?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) Oil India Limited(OIL) has informed that currently 36 vacancies in SC category and 57 vacancies in ST category are lying vacant in OIL. Vacancies are notified/filled as per recruitment plans and special drives are also conducted for recruiting SC/ST candidates.(b) OIL has reported that it has made 77 appointments in SC category and 95 in ST category during last three years. OIL has further reported that it has given promotion to 96 SC category and 80 to ST category employees during the last three years.Numaligarh Refinery Limited(NRL) has reported that it has made 15 appointments in SC category and 9 in ST category during the last three years. NRL has also reported that it has made 49 promotions in SC category and 30 in ST category during last three years.*********
