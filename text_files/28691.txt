                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

384






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Setting up of FM Transmitters in SSB




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government has initiated the setting up of approved Frequency Modulation (FM) transmitters in Sashastra Seema Bal (SSB) premises at seven locations along the Indo-Nepal Border including West Champaran in the State of Bihar;(b)	 if so, the details thereof; (c)	 the details and the total quantum of funds sanctioned by the Government for the same; and (d)	 the time by which the setting up of the FM transmitters will be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (d) : Yes, Sir. Schemes for setting up of Frequency Modulation (FM) transmitters in Sashastra Seema Bal (SSB) premises at Seven locations along the Indo-Nepal Border have been approved including SSB Premises at Narkatiaganj in West Champaran District in the State of Bihar.  Details of the transmitters are given in Annexure.
