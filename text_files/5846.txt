                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5443






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






National Service Scheme




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande













                                                    Bharti Pravin Pawar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the prescribed number of students in Maharashtra under the National Service Scheme (NSS);(b) whether the Maharashtra Government has proposed to increase the said strength of the students;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the steps taken by the Government so far for approving the said proposal along with the outcome thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS (SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) At present, the total allocated strength of National Service Scheme (NSS) volunteers in the State of Maharashtra is 327700.(b) The Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports has not received any proposal from Government of Maharashtra to increase the number of volunteers.(c) & (d): Do not arise.******
