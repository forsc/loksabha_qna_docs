                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

319






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Imposition of Section 144 of CrPC




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there has been undue imposition of section 144 of CrPC for suppressing freedom of speech and expression of people during the last three years resulting in a sense of unease among people in expressing their sentiments;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details regarding imposition of section 144 of CrPC in Delhi during each of the last three years and the number of places where police had to resort to lathi-charge?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):	No, Sir.(c):	Information is being collected.*****
