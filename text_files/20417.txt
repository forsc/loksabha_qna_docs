                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

148






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Udaipur Smart City




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the funds allocated and sanctioned for the 	development of Udaipur, Rajasthan as a smart city, head/work-	wise; (b) 	the details of the funds utilised out of the sanctioned funds so far 	for the said purpose, particularly for sewerage works; (c) 	the percentage of sewerage works which have been completed 	and the time by which the remaining works are likely to be 	completed; (d) 	the details of the schemes proposed for the development of 	Udaipur smart city; and (e) 	whether the Government proposes to formulate any new scheme 	in this regard and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	 to (e) :  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.                                               *****                                STATEMENTSTATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY OF PARTS (a) to (e) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. +*148 (8TH POSITION) FOR ANSWER ON 11.2.2021 REGARDING “UDAIPUR SMART CITY”.(a)	to (c) The city of Udaipur was selected for development as a Smart City in Round-I of the Smart Cities Challenge in January, 2016. As on 22 January, 2021, Udaipur Smart City has tendered 127 projects worth ? 1,794.4 crores, out of which 109 projects worth ? 1,491.6 crores are under implementation/completed. 4 projects worth ? 99.6 crores are in the Detailed Project Report (DPR) stage.The financial performance of Udaipur Smart City is as under :(? in crores)Central share released to State Government	Central share released by State Government to Smart City	State share released to Smart City	Utilization of Central share by Smart City	Utilization of State share by Smart City343	294	220.43	294 (100%)	220.43(100%)Two projects of Udaipur Smart City relate to development of sewerage infrastructure, the details of which are given below :? in crores)S.
