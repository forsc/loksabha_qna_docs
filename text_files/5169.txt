                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4779






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Revival of Indian Folk Arts and Music




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether several popular Folk Arts and music of the country are on the verge of extinction and if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether any survey has been conducted to identify the country’s folk arts and music which are on the verge of extinction; (c)	whether any action has taken to revive these Indian folk arts and music in the country; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d)	No such formal study has been conducted so far by the Government. However, to protect, preserve and promote various forms of folk arts and culture throughout the country, the Government of India has set up seven Zonal Cultural Centres (ZCCs) with headquarters at Patiala, Nagpur, Udaipur, Prayagraj, Kolkata, Dimapur and Thanjavur.  	These ZCCs under Ministry of Culture are implementing a number of schemes viz. Award to Young Talented Artistes, Guru Shishya Parampara Scheme, Theatre Rejuvenation Scheme, Research & Documentation Scheme, Shilpgram Scheme, Octave and J&K Festivals and National Cultural Exchange Programme (NCEP) for protection and conservation of different folk & tribal art forms.
