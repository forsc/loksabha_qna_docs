                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3138






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Indian Labour Conference




K. Subbarayan














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether it is a fact that the Indian Labour Conference (ILC) has not met since last five years which is in violation of international commitments of tripartism; and(b)if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b):	The 46th Session of the Indian Labour Conference (ILC) was held in New Delhi from 20-21 July, 2015. The 47th Session of the Indian Labour Conference was scheduled to meet on 26th and 27th February, 2018, but, the same could not be held due to administrative reasons. The Government has been holding tripartite consultations on important labour policies/issues.******
