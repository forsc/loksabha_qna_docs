                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PLANNING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1433






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Selling of Public Sector Infrastructure Projects




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





PLANNING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to sell successfully running public sector infrastructure projects such as power and steel plants to power companies;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and(c)	whether the Government has made any detailed consultations regarding this proposal with all Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) and if so, the response of those CPSEs thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING AND MINISTER OF STATE(INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION(RAO INDERJIT SINGH)(a): No information is available in NITI Aayog about any such proposal. (b) & (c): Does not arise in view of reply given in part (a) above.*****
