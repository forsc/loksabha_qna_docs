                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

782






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






CSR Funds for Conservation of Environment




Gomati Sai














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government has fixed any Corporate Social Responsibility towards conservation of environment in Chhattisgarh;(b) 	if so, the details thereof, district-wise; and(c)	the steps being taken by the Government for effective implementation of relevant schemes pertaining to conservation of environment?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) to (c) The Government has not fixed any Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) towards conservation of environment. However, the Ministry vide OM dated the 1st May 2018 and OM dated 30th September, 2020 has issued guidelines to set aside funds towards Corporate Environment Responsibility (CER) for conservation of environment. These OMs inter-alia have given policy framework to the Expert Appraisal Committees at the Central level and the State to prescribe the commitments made by the project proponent during the public consultation and towards mitigation plans committed by the project proponent in the EMP and before the EAC/SEIAA as the case may be, for safe guarding the environment as Corporate Environment Responsibility. The proposed activities shall be restricted to the affected area around the project and the entire activities proposed under the CER shall be treated as project and shall be monitored. *****
