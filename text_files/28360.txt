                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

198






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Beautification of Ancient Heritage of Nalanda




Kaushalendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is contemplating to take any special steps for the beautification of the renowned ancient heritage in Nalanda;(b)	whether the Government is contemplating a scheme for the construction of a five-star hotel in Rajgir to provide facility to the pilgrims and foreign tourists visiting the said place;(c)	whether the Government is contemplating to connect Nalanda directly  to Patna and Gaya airports through express-way road; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)  	The excavated remains at Nalanda are maintained and conserved by the Archaeological Survey of India(ASI) as per the requirement of the site and availability of the resources. They are in a good state of the preservation. (b)     No Sir.  (c)   &   (d)	Yes Sir, construction of both the highways has started. ******
