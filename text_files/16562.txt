                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

375






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Damage to Agricultural Land due to Flood




Aparajita Sarangi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has taken stock of the damage caused to agricultural land and produce due to floods in various parts of Odisha, if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether the Government is planning on introducing any special packages to help rehabilitate and compensate the farmers in Odisha for their losses; and(c) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) to (c):	In the eventuality of natural calamities, the State Governments are empowered to initiate necessary relief measures from State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF), which is readily available with them. Additional financial assistance, over and above SDRF, is considered from National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF), on receipt of Memorandum from State Governments and in accordance with extant norms and procedures.	As per the information from Ministry of Home Affairs, the State Government of Odisha has reported crop loss of 454250 ha in 27 districts in the wake of flood during current monsoon season. However, the State Government has not submitted any memorandum seeking financial assistance from NDRF in the wake of flood, so far. *******
