                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3696






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Action Plan for Development of Animal Husbandry




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the action plan of the Government to promote animal husbandry and to develop advanced breed of dairy animals in the country;(b) whether the Government proposes to set up any veterinary institutes of national level in Rajasthan to prevent increasing incidents of diseases in animals and for effective treatment of diseases; and(c) if so, the details thereof along with the action plan prepared in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN) (a) In order to complement and supplement the efforts of the States/UTs  for promoting animal husbandry and to develop advanced breed of cattle, the Government is implementing “ Rashtriya Gokul Mission” scheme since December, 2014 with an outlay of Rs 2,025 crore for  development and conservation of indigenous breeds, enhancement of milk production and productivity thereby making milk production more remunerative  to the farmers. (b) & (c) There is no separate proposal to set up any veterinary institute of national level in Rajasthan at present. However, under Establishment & Strengthening of Veterinary Hospitals & Dispensaries (ESVHD), a component of the Centrally Sponsored Livestock Health and Disease Control (LH&DC) scheme, the Government provides financial assistance to supplement the efforts of the States/UTs including Rajasthan.***
