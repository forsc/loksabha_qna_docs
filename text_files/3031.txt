                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2875






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Osmanabad -Tuljapur-Solarpur Rail Line




Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there are a number of districts in the country where transport facility is still not available despite the fact that the railway was introduced before independence in the country;(b)	if so, the action taken by the Government to lay a new rail line between Osmanabad-Tuljapur-Solapur; (c)	whether the Government proposes to construct railway line to provide facility to people coming to the various places of pilgrimage in this area; (d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):	New Railway line projects are not sanctioned district-wise or State-wise. Railway projects are taken up on the basis of remunerativeness, last mile connectivity, missing links and alternate routes, augmentation of congested/saturated lines, socio-economic considerations etc. depending upon throwforward of ongoing projects and overall availability of funds.(b) to (e):   New line from Solapur to Osmanabad via Tuljapur (84.44 km) has been sanctioned in January, 2019 at a cost of `904.92 crore and work has been taken up. After completion of this line, railway travelling facility will be available to the public and pilgrims to Shri Tulja Bhawani Temple and other places of pilgrimage in this area.*****
