                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1695






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Central Package and NEIDS




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor













                                                    Shantanu Thakur
                                                








                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                








                                                    Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   whether the Government has set up a proper mechanism for the implementation of the projects identified under Central Package and North East Industrial Development Scheme (NEIDS), 2019;(b)   if so, the details thereof;(c)   the names of projects identified under each of the said scheme including the details of the financial outlay in this regard;(d)  whether the Government has fixed any targeted date for the completion of these projects so that the people engaged in this sector can get their benefits at the earliest; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Independent Charge) of the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region[Dr. Jitendra Singh](a) to (e) : North East Industrial Development Scheme (NEIDS) 2017, is beingimplemented by Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade(DPIIT), Ministry of Commerce and Industry for NE States. The main components of NEIDS are: (1) Central Capital Investment Incentive for access to credit (CCIIAC), (2) Central Interest Incentive (CII), (3) Central Comprehensive Insurance Incentive (CCII), (4) Goods and Services Tax (GST) reimbursement, (5) Income Tax (IT) Reimbursement, (6) Transport Incentive (TI), and (7) Employment Incentive (EI).The objective of the scheme is to promote industrialization and to boost employment and income generation.Manufacturing and Service Sector units willing to avail the benefits under NEIDS, 2017 have to register on the website of DPIIT.Registration/in-principle approval is decided by the Committee, which inter-alia, considers the prima-facie eligibility of the industrial unit, availability of budget and decides the eligibility for registration under the Scheme on the recommendations of State Government. Rs.100 crore has been allocated for the year 2020-21 under NEIDS, 2017 and claims filed by 6 industrial units have been recommended by their State Level Committee.Ministry of MSME is implementing a scheme called Promotion of MSMEs in NE Region and Sikkim which provides financial assistance to various components for setting up new and modernization of existing Mini Technological Centres, development of new and existing Industrial Estates and Capacity Building of Officers engaged in promotion and development of MSMEs.Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region (MDoNER) implements special development packages for Bodoland Territorial Council (BTC), DimaHasao Autonomous Territorial Council (DHATC) and KarbiAnglong Autonomous Territorial Council (KAATC). The status of which are as under:-Under BTC package of Rs.750.00 crore,  an amount of Rs.711.19 crore has been released. 51 projects costing Rs.570.19 crore have already been completed. Under KAATC package of Rs.350.00 crore, 32 projects costing Rs.349.97 crore have been identified against which an amount of Rs.95.71 crore has been released. 2 projects costing Rs.6.26 crore have already been completed. Under special development package of Rs.200.00 crore for DHATC, 15 projects costing Rs. 199.82 crore have been identified against which Rs.98.51 crore has been released.
