                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

943






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






National Horticulture Mission Scheme




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any steps to grant approval to National Horticulture Mission Scheme in all the development blocks falling under Misrikh Parliamentary Constituency keeping in view the balanced horticulture development, adaptability of agro-climate and development prospects in the State of Uttar Pradesh;(b) if so, the details thereof, and the current status of this proposal; and(c) the time by which the proposal is likely to be finalised? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????  (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	National Horticulture Mission (NHM) a sub-scheme of Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) is being implemented by Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare for the holistic development of horticulture in the country. All States/UTs are covered under MIDH including the development blocks falling under Kanpur and Sitapur districts of Misrikh Parliamentary Constituency of Uttar Pradesh. 		MIDH is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented through States based on their Annual Action Plan, which are prepared by States keeping in view of their specific requirement towards balanced horticulture development, adaptability of agro-climate and development prospects in the State. 		For the year 2021-22, an Annual Action Plan for Rs. 109.92 crore including GOI share of Rs. 65.95 crore has been approved under MIDH for Uttar Pradesh including the development blocks falling under Kanpur and Sitapur districts of Misriskh Parliamentary Constituency to cover various horticulture activities including Nurseries, Area Expansion, Mushroom, Protected Cultivation, Organic Farming, Beekeeping, Horticulture Mechanization, Post Harvest Management, Centre of Excellence, Markets etc. 		For blocks falling under Hardoi district, which is not covered under MIDH, of Misriskh Parliamentary Constituency, the horticulture activities are supported under RKVY scheme of the Ministry. ********
