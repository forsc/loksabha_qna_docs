                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

40






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Installing mobile towers




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is any proposal under consideration to install towers in areas without BSNL signals of Rajasthan, Gujarat, Jharkhand and Madhya Pradesh; (b) 	if so, the places where such towers are likely to be installed; and (c) 	if not, the reasons for not installing such towers in other places?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) to (c) 	Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) augments its mobile network based on techno-commercial considerations in its areas of operation including Rajasthan, Gujarat, Jharkhand and Madhya Pradesh.	The Government has formulated several schemes under Universal Services Obligation Fund (USOF) for mobile connectivity in the areas in Rajasthan, Gujarat, Jharkhand and Madhya Pradesh where mobile coverage of any operator including BSNL is not available. Number of villages covered under such schemes are 226 in Rajasthan, 70 in Gujarat, 229 in Madhya Pradesh and 1277 in Jharkhand.
