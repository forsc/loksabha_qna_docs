                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1769






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Crops at MSP




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the quantum of various agricultural crops procured at MSP in the last five years in the State of Telangana; (b) 	whether the Government has communicated to the Telangana Government not to procure paddy or rice, (c) 	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and (d) 	the reasons for the present delay in procurement of paddy crop of kharif 2021?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	The quantum of various agricultural crops procured at MSP in the last five years in the State of Telangana is at Annexure-I(b) and (c): 	The estimate for procurement of paddy (in terms of rice) and wheat are finalized by Government of India in consultation with State Governments and Food Corporation of India, before the commencement of each marketing season based upon estimated production, marketable surplus and agricultural crop pattern. Procurement in a State depends not only upon production but also upon other multiple factors like marketable surplus, MSP, prevailing market rate, demand & supply situation and participation of private traders etc. Telangana is a Decentralized Procurement (DCP) State, where procurement is undertaken by State Government through its agencies. Only the surplus FAQ rice as per the requirements of FCI, after meeting State’s requirement, is delivered to FCI in central pool account for movement to deficit/ consuming States.(d): 	There has been no delay on the part of Government of India in procurement of paddy crop of Kharif 2021-22. In the States/UTs Food Secretaries meeting held on 17.08.2021, the procurement  period of paddy for Telangana State was  fixed from 1st October 2021 to 31st January, 2022, by Government of India in consultation with Government of Telangana.
