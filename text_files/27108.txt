                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2810






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Khadi Gramodyog Training Centres




Ratansinh Magansinh Rathod













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    Manickam Tagore B
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Khadi Gramodyog training centres presently operational in the country including Gujarat, Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra; (b) 	whether these training centres lack latest technology affecting training and if so, the details thereof and whether the Government proposes to modernize these training centres; (c) 	whether the Government has assessed the performance of these training centres and if so, the details and the outcome thereof; (d) 	whether the Government has received any proposal from the said States for setting up of new such training centres; (e) 	if so, the details of the centres identified for the purpose along with criterion adopted for the selection of such centres; (f) 	the details of the funds allocated and utilized for the modernization of each centre during each of the last three years and the current year, State-wise; and (g) 	whether the Government also proposes to open Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) training centres for providing training to unemployed persons in the country and if so, the details and the locations thereof, State/UT-wise and the time by which the said training centres are likely to be made functional?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a):	A total of 34 Training Centres of Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) are presently operational in the country including in Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra. No training centre of KVIC is functioning in Gujarat State.  State/UT-wise number of training centres of KVIC are placed at Annexure-I.(b):	The latest technologies are being implemented in the training centres to impart training in various field related to Khadi and Village Industries. Further, ‘Institutional Capacity Building’ is being carried out on requirement basis.In recent past, an amount of Rs.13.18 crore has been utilized till 31.03.2021 for training, modernization of 15 existing Multi-Disciplinary Training Centres (MDTCs), and establishment of one new MDTC (Centre for Excellence) at Kolkata and to set-up computer lab at 3 MDTCs viz. Guwahati, Bangalore and Nasik.(c):	No specific study has been conducted to assess the performance of training centres. However, KVIC assesses the performance of training centres from time to time and take necessary measures for improvement of these training centres.(d):	No specific proposal for setting up of new training centres from the States of Gujarat, Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra has been received.(e):	Does not arise.(f):	State/UT-wise funds allocated and utilized by training centres under the component of ‘Institutional Capacity Building’ including Modernization under ‘Khadi Reform and Development Programme’ (KRDP) during the last three years and current year are placed at Annexure-II.(g):	Under Technology Centre Systems Programme (TCSP) Projects of the Ministry, 11 new Technology Centres are providing Technical Skill Development training to the youth at varying levels. State-wise list of Technology Centres is placed at Annexure- III. 	In addition to this, under the scheme “Establishment of new Technology Centres / Extension Centres”, 23 Extension Centres have been made functional till date. State wise list of 23 ECs is placed at Annexure-IV.
