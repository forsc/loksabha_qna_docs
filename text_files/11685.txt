                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

649






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Trains from Gorakhpur




Baidyanath Prasad Mahto














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is a proposal to launch a direct rail service from Gorakhpur to Pataliputra via Bagha Narkatiaganj-Muzaffarpur during the current financial year; and(b) if so, whether there is a plan to operate the trains as before on this route from Gorakhpur to Patna or Muzaffarpur?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)  (a) and (b) No, Sir.  Owing to operational constraints including terminal constraints at Gorakhpur, introduction of new train between Gorakhpur and Pataliputra is not feasible, at present. However, Gorakhpur and Pataliputra are connected to each other by 01 pair of Mail/Express trains and 01 pair of Passenger trains.*****
