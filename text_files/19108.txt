                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

642






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Clearances for Central Vista Redevelopment Project




Thalikkottai Rajuthevar Baalu














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether all the clearances for execution of the Central Vista Redevelopment Project have been obtained before starting the project;(b)	if so, the details thereof including the clearances from various agencies;(c)	whether Delhi’s Heritage Conservation Committee was not consulted while plans for the revamped enclave was made and if so, the reasons therefor;(d)	the total cost of the Central Vista Redevelopment  Project; and (e)	whether, it was so necessary to undertake the project during the COVID-19 pandemic and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                             (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	&(b) All clearances for construction of New Parliament Building such as Central Vista Committee, Delhi Urban Arts Commission, Delhi Fire Services, Environment Clearance, Tree Cutting/ Transplantation clearance from Forest Department GNCTD, Heritage Conservation Committee, No Objection Certificate from Delhi Metro Rail Corporation and approval by Urban Local Body have been obtained. Clearance for all other projects shall be taken in due course before taking up construction activities.  (c) 	Clearance of Heritage Conservation Committee has been obtained on 11.01.2021 for construction of New Parliament Building. Clearance of Heritage Conservation Committee for development of Central Vista Avenue has been obtained on 02.02.2021. For other projects, same will be obtained, if required after finalization of plans. (d) 	Work on New Parliament Building and Central Vista Avenue have been awarded at the cost of ?862 crore and ?477 crore respectively. Detailed Project Report (DPR) and cost estimates of other projects have not been finalized.  (e) 	In the current scenario, the project shall generate large number of direct and indirect employment which could be an important fulcrum for economic revival.                                       *****
