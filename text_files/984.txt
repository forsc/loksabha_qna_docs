                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

83






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Completion of National Highway Projects




Sudheer Gupta













                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number and details of national highway projects which are on the verge of completion, State/UT-wise;(b)	whether the Government proposes to complete these projects during the next three months and if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the present status of Delhi-Meerut greenfield expressway along with the time by which it is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) -  A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 83 FOR ANSWER ON 27.06.2019 ASKED BY SHRI SUDHEER GUPTA AND SHRI GAJANAN KIRTIKAR REGARDING COMPLETION OF NATIONAL HIGHWAY PROJECTS  .(a) to (b): The State-wise details of the major projects which are on the verge of completion and will be completing during the next three months are given at Annexure-I. (c) 	The present status of Delhi-Meerut greenfield expressway along with the time by which it is likely to be completed is given at Annexure-II.
