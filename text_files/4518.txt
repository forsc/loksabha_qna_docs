                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4238






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Financing of Highway Projects




Mahabali Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the development and construction works of various highways are facing financial crisis due to the reluctance of nationalised banks to finance the same;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the steps being taken by the Government to address the issue;(c)	whether the Government proposes to establish a specific/dedicated financial institution for financing highway projects in the country on the lines of PFC and other infrastructure financial institutions;(d)	 if so, the details thereof; and(e)	 if not, the other steps being taken by the Government for adequately financing such projects in the country? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) No Sir. However, Banking Sector has been raising various concerns. Ministry is in continuous interaction with the lenders of PPP projects to resolve the issues. Steps being taken by the Ministry to address the concerns being raised by Banking Sector:(i)      Ministry is planning to share the information through its PMIS (Project Monitoring Information System).(ii)     Substitution cases are being expedited at NHAI.(iii)   The Model Concession Agreement (MCA) is under consideration for amendment taking  into consideration most of the issues raised by Bankers.(c) to (e) Yes Sir. Recently a Memorandum of Understanding has been signed between NHAI and National Investment & Infrastructure Fund (NIIF) for financing Highway Projects.*****
