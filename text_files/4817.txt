                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4431






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Ayush Doctors in PHCs




Janardan Singh Sigriwal













                                                    Santokh Singh Chaudhary
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government proposes to appoint Ayush doctors in all the Primary Healthcare Centres (PHCs) in the country, keeping in view the promotion of the AYUSH system of medicine;(b) if so, the details thereof indicating the number of doctors appointed and proposed to be appointed in such PHCs across the country, State/UT-wise; (c) the detail of procedures adopted for the screening and selection of the doctors in such PHCs; and (d) whether the AYUSH doctors will diagnose and treat mental disorders also, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (c):    Since Public Health is a State subject, the appointment of AYUSH doctors comes under the purview of respective State/UT Governments. However, Government of India has adopted a strategy of Co-location of AYUSH facilities at Primary Health Centres (PHCs), Community Health Centres (CHCs) and District Hospitals (DHs), thus enabling choice to the patients for different systems of medicine. The engagement of AYUSH Doctors/paramedics and their training are supported by the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, while the support for AYUSH infrastructure, equipment/furniture and medicines are provided by Ministry of AYUSH under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM). The number of doctors appointed in PHCs across the country, State/UT-wise is furnished at Annexure.                Public Health and Hospitals’ being a State subject, the administrative and personnel matters including recruitment of doctors for Primary Health Centres fall under the purview of respective State/UT Governments. Accordingly, screening and selection procedures for doctors are to be conducted as per the prevailing policies adopted by the respective State/UT Government.(d):       AYUSH doctors at AYUSH facilities are supposed to diagnose and treat the cases including mental disorders as per their qualification and entitlements provided under relevant Acts & Rules.
