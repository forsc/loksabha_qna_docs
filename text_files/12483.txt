                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1336






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






CIBS




Jamyang Tsering Namgyal














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning for bringing Central Institute of Buddhist Studies(Deemed University) under the ambit of UGC;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) if not, the reasons therefor;(d) whether the Government has any proposal to convert the Ladakh Cluster Universitywhich was announced under RUSA into a Central University in the UT of Ladakh;(e) if so, the details thereof; and(f) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c) : Central Institute of Buddhist Studies, Leh, Ladakh has been declared as Deemedto be University on 15.01.2016. This Deemed to be University is presently under thecontrol of the Ministry of Culture and fully funded by it.(d) to (f): The Ladakh University has been established by the Government of Jammu andKashmir by enacting the Ladakh University Act, 2018 (Governor Act No. LVI of 2018) on16.12.2018. Infrastructure grants can be given to the University for its establishment andquality improvement under RUSA.*****
