                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1710






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Impact of COVID-19 on Housing for All




Prataprao Govindrao  Patil Chikhalikar














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the scheme of the Government to provide houses to 	all by 2022 has been affected due to COVID-19; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the number of cities in the country where work is going on 	under this scheme;(d) 	the status of the ongoing construction of houses in 	Maharashtra; and (e) 	the details of the under construction houses in Mumbai, Pune, 	Nanded and Aurangabad? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                             (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)&(b)	Due to COVID-19, there has been some delay in construction of houses under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U). The progress of construction has	picked up very soon.	The Union and State/ Union Territory (UT) Governments have strategized to accelerate the pace of construction and complete the projects within the stipulated timeframe. (c) 	At present under the PMAY-U the work is going on in  4,459 cities throughout the country.(d)&(e)The details of ongoing /under construction of houses under PMAY-U in Maharashtra, including Mumbai, Pune, Nanded and Aurangabad are as under:S.No.	Particulars	Maharashtra	Mumbai	Pune	Nanded	Aurangabad1.	No. of houses sanctioned	12,86,611	2,15,413	1,89,534	24,127	18,8512.	No. of houses Grounded for construction	6,78,072	70,503	1,30,023	16,822	12,4302.	No. of houses completed 	4,11,334	21,025	98,404	10,968	10,7383.	No. of houses  under construction 	2,66,738	49,478	31,619	5,854	1,692                                                  *****
