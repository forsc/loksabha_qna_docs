                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

68






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Kendriya Vidyalaya in Maharashtra




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande













                                                    Bharti Pravin Pawar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to increase the number of Kendriya Vidyalayas in Maharashtra; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the names of the places in Jharkhand and Maharashtra where Kendriya Vidyalayas are presently functional, location-wise; (d) 	if not, the reasons therefor; and (e) 	the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) Proposals for opening of new Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) are considered only if sponsored by Ministries or Departments of the Government of India/State Governments / Union Territories Administrations thereby committing resources for setting up a new KV as well as the availability of necessary sanction of the Government.  The proposals received from various sponsoring authorities for opening of new KVs also have to compete among other proposals under the “Challenge Method”. 50 new KVs had been sanctioned in March, 2019 in different parts of the country including one at Gadchiroli in Maharashtra.  (c)	Location-wise details of the 39 KVs functioning in the State of Jharkhand and 59 KVs functioning in Maharashtra are annexed.  (d) & (e) Do not arise.******
