                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the coking and non-coking coal reserves and their production in the country during the last five years, year and State-wise;(b)	the quantity of coking coal existing in the country at present and the quantity of demand thereof;(c)	whether there is huge difference in the demands and production of coking coal and if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether India is to remain heavily dependent on imported coking coal and if so, the present status thereof;(e)	whether the Government proposes to acquire coking coal reserves sites in foreign countries;(f)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and(g)	the time by which those are proposed to be acquired along with the amount of funds earmarked for the said purpose?
