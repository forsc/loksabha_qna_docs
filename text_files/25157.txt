                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1089






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Development of Five Heritage Sites




Riti Pathak














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the proposed plan for development of five heritage sites mentioned in the Union Budget of the country;(b)	whether the Government propose to develop more such sites in future;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the criteria of selection of such heritage sites by the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM  AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	The Government of India announced five (05) sites to be developed as ‘Iconic Sites’. The Archaeological Survey of India proposes to provide public amenities and enhancement of visual aesthetics of the site, exposition and excavation of buried remains, wherever required, and on-site museum/ interpretation center to understand the site and its features in an immersive manner, in consultation with the state governments,  (b), (c)  &  (d)	   At present, there is no proposal.
