                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

299






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






AYUSH Medicines for Geriatric Population




T.R.V.S. Ramesh














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the ministry has taken any steps to improve health of the country’s geriatric population following the pandemic, particularly in Tamil Nadu; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the number of people of 60 years and above age across the country to whom AYUSH prophylactic medicines have been distributed till date under ‘‘Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav’’ campaign, State-wise including districtwise in Tamil Nadu?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a) to (c)   A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO.299* FOR 17TH DECEMBER, 2021(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. The Ministry has taken various steps to improve the health of the country’s geriatric population following the pandemic, including Tamil Nadu.	Prophylactic medicines for prevention of COVID-19 from Ayush systems including Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani and Homoeopathy were distributed free of cost to general public including geriatric population. Initiatives/Actions taken by Ministry of Ayush for management of COVID-19 pandemic are at Annexure-I.(c) State/UT-wise number of people including 60 years and above age across the country to whom AYUSH prophylactic medicines have been distributed till 13th December 2021 under ‘‘Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav’’ campaign by Ministry of Ayush is attached as Annexure-II. District-wise details of Tamil Nadu is attached at Annexure-III.
