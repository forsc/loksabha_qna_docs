                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1832






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Women Colleges in Odisha




Ramesh Chandra Majhi














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plan to establish dedicated women Polytechnics, Engineering and PG colleges in each district of the country in near future, specially in Odisha;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c) whether any progress has been made so far in this regard and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT (SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a):  		No, Sir. (b) & (c):	Does not arise. ***
