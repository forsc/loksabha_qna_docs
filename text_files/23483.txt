                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4449






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Terrorist Activities




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether terrorist activities have increased continuously in the country from the year 2014;(b) if so, the details of the terrorist activities reported from the year 2014 to 31 December, 2020 and the total number of Jawans and police personnel martyred along with the number of people who lost their lives in these incidents, year-wise;(c) whether the Government has formulated any concrete policy to deal with terrorism sponsored by Pakistan; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY) (a) & (b):	The number of the terrorist incidents,  the number of security force personnel martyred and the number of civilians who lost their life in  terrorist incidents in the hinterland of the country and in Jammu and Kashmir during the years 2014 to 2020 is at Annexure.--2--L.S.US.Q.NO. 4449 FOR 23.03.2021(c) & (d):	In furtherance of its zero tolerance policy towards terrorism, Government has taken various steps which include, inter-alia, strengthening of legal framework, streamlining of intelligence mechanism, setting up of National Investigation Agency (NIA) for investigation and prosecution of terror related cases, having various hubs of National Security Guards (NSG), stepping up border and coastal security, modernization of police forces and capacity building of state police forces, robust international cooperation and designation of individuals and entities as terrorists etc*****
