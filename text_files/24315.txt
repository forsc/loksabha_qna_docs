                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

292






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Advanced Weaponry with Terrorist Elements




Rahul Kaswan













                                                    Jaskaur Meena
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that terrorists have more advanced weapons and ammunition and resources available with them;(b) if so, whether the Government has prepared to equip security forces with advanced systems to counter the terrorists; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (c):	Terrorists have been using modern weapons and equipments to commit terrorist acts across the world. Combat against terrorism is a continuous process. The Government has been strengthening its counter terrorism apparatus including equipping security forces and intelligence agencies with requisite resources, equipments, weaponry, modern technology, training, etc.  ******
