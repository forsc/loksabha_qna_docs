                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4836






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Internship for MBBS Students under AYUSH




Nayab Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the MBBS students can opt for any elective from AYUSH-Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, Homeopathy and Sowa-Rigpa for internship along with other postings;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise;(c)	whether the Government has taken any appropriate steps to include super speciality training under internship in hospitals; and (d)	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTR OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a) to (b): Yes Sir,The MBBS Students can opt for any elective from Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, Homeopathy, and Sowa-Rigpa(AYUSH) for one week posting during internship along with other postings.This was notified by the National Medical Commission vide notificationNo. UGMEB/NMC/Rules & Regulations/2021,NewDelhi, dated 18th November 2021.In this notification there is a provision in Schedule –III under Specialties for training.The Detail notificationis available at the following link: https://www.nmc.org.in/ActivitiWebClient/open/getDocument?path=/Documents/Public/Portal/NmcGazette/231280.pdf.(c) to (d):No sir, at presentthere is no such proposal under considerationfor Students to include Super-Specialty training under internship in Hospitals.*****
