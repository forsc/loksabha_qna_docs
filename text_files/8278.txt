                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1933






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Special Grants for Exporters




Manoj Kumar Tiwari














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to provide special grants for exporters of Tribal Textiles in near future; (b)	if so, the details thereof specially in marginal areas of Delhi/NCR;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the details of plan outlay and implementation procedure for any such proposal in near future?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)  to (d):	There is no specific proposal to provide special grants to exporters of Tribal Textiles.  However, Government provides financial assistance to exporters for promotion of handicrafts and textiles including tribal textiles.*******
