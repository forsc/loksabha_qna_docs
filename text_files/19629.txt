                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

916






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Mahila Shakti Kendras




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    916. 	SHRI SHYAM SINGH YADAV:     	Will the Minister of WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state: (a)    	the number of Mahila Shakti Kendras set up by the Government so far in Uttar           	Pradesh;(b)    	the number of districts in which these Kendras are functional; and(c)    	the current status of the Mahila Shakti Kendras of Jaunpur district and the          	number of beneficiaries registered under these Kendras? 






ANSWER




ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a)	State Resource Centre for Women (SRCW) at State level and District Level Centre for Women (DLCW) in 64 districts are functional in the State of Uttar Pradesh.(b)	DLCWs are functional in 307 districts of the country. (c)	During April, 2020 to December, 2020, DLCW Jaunpur reached out to 7090 women/girls for awareness generation  and to 192 women for linking with government Schemes.   ********
