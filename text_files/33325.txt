                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

754






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Efforts Made to Highlight Ethos




Anil Firojiya














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any scheme has been formulated to correct the serious misrepresentations of the history of our country;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether any effort has been made to highlight the ethos of our country; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d): Ministry of Culture works for preservation and conservation of cultural heritage and promotion of tangible and intangible art and culture through its attached, subordinate and autonomous organizations. Efforts have been made to highlight the ethos of our country through the following Central Schemes being administered by the Ministry:1.	Centenaries and Anniversaries Scheme 2.	Kala Sanskriti Vikas Yojana3.	Development of Museum4.	Development of Libraries5.	Global Engagement6.	National Mission on Manuscripts
