                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4681






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Bharatmala Pariyojana




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya













                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present implementation status of Bharatmala Pariyojana;(b)	the length of roads to be constructed in the State of Gujarat 	under the said pariyojana;(c)	whether the targets fixed in this regard have been achieved in 	the State and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	if not, the time by which the remaining targets are likely to be 	acheived in the State?






ANSWER




                                                         THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (d)   Total 34,800 km length has been envisaged for development under Bharatmala Pariyojana Phase-I. Out of this, NH projects comprising length of 20,411 km have been awarded. In the State of Gujarat, out of 1,661 km envisaged length, NH projects with length of 1,163 km and cost of Rs. 48,587 crore have been awarded. All the projects envisaged under the Bharatmala Pariyojana Phase-I, including in the State of Gujarat, are targeted to be completed by 2026-27.*****
