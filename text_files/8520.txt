                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2086






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Teacher Training Schools/Colleges




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    Shantanu Thakur
                                                








                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the State-wise total number of teacher training schools/colleges functioning in the country;(b) whether National Council for Teacher Education grants recognition to B.Ed. colleges in theStates;(c) if so, the details in this regard; and(d) whether focus is laid on the quality of training imparted by private training institutes andthat training is being given on regular basis?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) : Total number of teacher training institutions/colleges functioning in the country as on31.03.2019 are 16728. The state-wise detail is at Annexure.(b) & (c) : Yes, Sir. National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE) grants recognition toB.Ed colleges by inviting applications for recognition to Teacher Education Programmes in newTeacher Education Institutions (TEIs), and enhancement of intake and capacity addition inexisting TEIs offering B.Ed Programme. Applications received in the Regional Committees ofNCTE are processed as per Norms laid down in the NCTE Regulations, 2014.(c) : The NCTE Regulations, 2014 (as amended from time to time) prescribes norms andstandards for running Teacher Education Institutions (TEIs). These Regulations provide forconstant maintenance of quality. The NCTE Act 1993 provides for withdrawal of recognition incase any TEI is found to be not complying with any of the provisions contained in extantRegulations. Accordingly, the scheme of NCTE Act, 1993 and Regulations, 2014 both layemphasis on qualitative functioning of TEIs.*****
