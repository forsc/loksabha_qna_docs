                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4863






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Residential Coaching Programmes




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has implemented residential coaching programmes for children above ten years of age in Gujarat;(b)	if so, the names of the sports to which these coaching programmes are affiliated along with the procedure being adopted for the same; and(c)	the number of sportspersons selected from Gujarat for the past Olympic and Asian games?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) & (b) Sports is a State Subject and the primary responsibility of development of sports, including implementation of  residential coaching programmes for children, rests with the State Governments and the Sports Federations which are responsible for governance of individual sports disciplines. The Central Government, through the sports development schemes implemented by it and organizations functioning under its aegis, especially the Sports Authority of India (SAI), complements and supplements the efforts of the State Governments and NSFs in this regard. Details of training centres and academies currently being operated in the State of Gujarat through the schemes being implemented by SAI are given in Annexure – I. The National Centre of Excellence (NCOE) and Extension Centres of SAITraining Centres (STCs) are set up for various sports disciplines on the basis of availability of talents.Under the Khelo India scheme, support is providedthrough the accredited academies to selected athletes based on a pre – qualification examination, physical assessment and verification.	(c)	State-wise details of sportspersons, representing India in International events including Olympics and Asian Games, are not maintained in this Ministry.*****
