                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2997






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Houses for All under PMAY-U




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of houses targeted to be constructed under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY-U) under ‘Housing for all’ by the year 2022;(b)	the number of houses constructed so far and the number of houses that are to yet be constructed as perthe target, State-wise;(c) 	the details of financial assistance provided under PMAY-U at present; and(d) 	whether the Government proposes to increase the said amount and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                                       THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                  (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a): 	Based on the project proposals submitted by the States/ Union Territories (UTs) as per their assessed demand of houses under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U), around 114 lakh houses have been sanctioned in urban areas across the country.(b):	Against the total sanctioned houses, nearly 53 lakh have been completed/ delivered to beneficiaries and remaining are at various stages of construction/ grounding. State/ UT-wise details are at Annexure.(c):  Central Government provides different levels of financial assistance under following four verticals of the Scheme: S. No.	                      Vertical	             Central Assistance to each beneficiary1.	Beneficiary-led individual house construction/ enhancement (BLC)	Rs. 1.50 lakh2.	Affordable Housing in Partnership (AHP)	                                Rs. 1.50 lakh3.	“In-situ” Slum Redevelopment (ISSR)	                                Rs. 1.00 lakh4.	Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS)	Category(Annual household income)	 Loan Amount	Interest subsidyEconomically Weaker Section (up to Rs.. 3,00,000)	up to Rs. 6 lakhs	6.5 %Low Income Group (from Rs.. 3,00,001 to Rs.. 6,00,000)		Further, States/ UTs also provide different levels of financial assistance to the beneficiaries under ISSR, AHP and BLC verticals of the Scheme.(d): No, Sir. There is no such proposal.                                              *****
