                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3291






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Objectives of Krishonnati Yojana




Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel













                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	The main objective of the Green Revolution Krishonnati Yojana; (b) 	Whether area expansion, restoration of soil fertility and enhancement of productivity has been achieved in the identified districts of the country through this scheme; and (c) 	The districts identified in the country especially in Gujarat for this scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????  (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)  & (b):	Green Revolution– Krishonnati Yojana  is an Umbrella Scheme  of 12 Schemes/Missions, both Central Sector as well as Centrally Sponsored,  initially applicable for the period 2017-18 to 2019-20 and later extended to 2020-21.  These schemes aim to develop the agriculture and allied sectors in a holistic and scientific manner to increase the income of farmers by enhancing production, productivity and valuation on produce. The Schemes/Missions have led to better outcomes in production and productivity through various interventions including area expansion, restoration of soil fertility and enhancement of productivity.(c):	Only some of the Schemes/Missions of Krishonnati Yojana like Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) and National Food Security Mission (NFSM) are focused on district wise implementation. Identified districts in Gujarat for such Schemes/Missions are given in the Annexure.
