                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

307






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Construction of Roads




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of roads constructed with the assistance of National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in Kerala including Alappuzha, so far, since 2015;(b)	the number of roads proposed to be constructed under PMGSY during 2019-20 in Kerala; and(c)	whether the Government would change the concerned width of 6m of PMGSY road to 4m due to scarcity of land in coastal areas in Kerala and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	As reported by the State, no road has been constructed with the assistance of National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in Kerala including Alappuzha, so far, since 2015. (b)	The State of Kerala has reported that 79 number of roads having a length of 230 km are targeted for completion under PMGSY during 2019-20 out of which 55 km have been completed.(c)	At present, there is no such proposal under consideration.
