                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2388






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Central Forensic Science Laboratories




Shardaben Anilbhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has established new central forensic science laboratories in the country during the last three years;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details of funds allocated released and utilized separately to raise the status of the present central forensic science laboratories and to establish such new laboratories during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) and (b): During the last three years, the Ministry of Home Affairs has completed the construction of new buildings for Central Forensic Science Laboratories (CFSLs) at Bhopal and Guwahati. These CFSLs have become operational in their new Buildings. Further, substantial work has been completed for the construction of a new building for CFSL at Pune.(c): Details of capital funds allocated/ released and funds utilized to raise the status of the present CFSLs and establishment of new CFSLs in last three years are as below:(Rs. in crore)Year	Funds allocated/released	Funds Utilized2016-17	15.56	14.092017-18	31.16	29.612018-19	32.85	29.58-2/…-2-LS.US.Q.NO.2388 FOR 03.12.2019Further, an expenditure of Rs.8.39 crore has been incurred under the Nirbhaya Fund in 2018-19 for setting up a state-of-the-art DNA analysis laboratory at Chandigarh.*****
