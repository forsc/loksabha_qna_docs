                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3399






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Adverse Effects of Ethanol




Haji Fazlur Rehman














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has allowed petrol companies to mix 10 per centethanol in petrol across the country;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has received complaints of adverse effects on theengine of vehicles due to mixing of ethanol in petrol;(d) if so, the steps taken by the Government in this regard; and(e) whether the Government is contemplating to change its decision?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM &NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (e): Government of India through Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) isimplementing Ethanol Blended Petrol (EBP) Programme, wherein ethanol is beingblended in petrol to achieve the objectives of reducing import dependence,promote clean fuel and provide boost to agriculture. Studies were carried out byAutomotive Research Association of India (ARAI) and Indian Oil CorporationLimited (IOCL) to establish the vehicle performance of 10% ethanol blendedpetrol (E10). Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS), after consultation with concernedstakeholders, issued fuel specification for E10 blending in 2014. Ministry ofPetroleum & Natural Gas (MoP&NG) vide notification dated 5th February, 2019directed OMCs to sell 10% ethanol blended petrol as per BIS specifications.******
