                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2150






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Effects of Rising Prices of Petroleum Products




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar













                                                    Mimi Chakraborty
                                                








                                                    Anumula Revanth Reddy
                                                








                                                    Nama Nageswara Rao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the price of petroleum products in the country has risen over 30 per cent in phasedmanner during the last three months period and if so, whether the LPG cylinder price has risenover 300 per cent during the last seven years and the petroleum fuel over 350 per cent in thesame period;(b) the reasons behind the price hike and effect on the consumers in terms of GDP;(c) whether the prices of petroleum products are responsible for the current high inflation rate, ifso, the steps taken to bring down the prices of these products;(d) the reasons behind not to take all petroleum products under GST regime and whether there isany proposal to include them under GST, if so, the details of effect on the States in terms of cessand price controling subsequently;(e) the amount collected by excise duty during the last five years; and(f) the details of average yearly crude oil price per barrel including the details of average yearlyrange of petrol price in New Delhi, Mumbai and Hyderabad per litre from 2016 to 2021?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a): No Sir. The details of prices of major petroleum products in the country duringlast three months and seven years are given as Annexure-I(b) & (c): Prices of petrol and diesel have been made market-determined by the Governmentwith effect from 26.06.2010 and 19.10.2014 respectively. Since then, the Public Sector OilMarketing Companies (OMCs) take appropriate decision on pricing of petrol and diesel in linewith their international product prices, exchange rate, tax structure, inland freight and othercost elements. The prices of petroleum products in the country are linked to the price ofrespective products in the international market. However, the Government continues tomodulate the effective price to consumer for Subsidized Domestic LPG.The weightage of petrol and diesel in the WPI index is 1.60% and 3.10% respectively andin the CPI index is 2.19% and 0.15% respectively.(d): As per Article 279 A (5) of the Constitution, the Goods and Service Tax Council shallrecommend the date on which the goods and services tax be levied on petroleum crude, highspeed diesel, motor spirit (commonly known as petrol), natural gas and aviation turbine fuel(ATF). As per the section 9(2) of the CGST Act, inclusion of these products in GST willrequire recommendation of the GST Council. So far, the GST Council has not made anyrecommendation for inclusion of these five goods under GST.(e): The details of total amount collected by excise duty on petrol, diesel, ATF, Natural Gasand Crude petroleum in the last five years is as under:(in Rs.lakh Crore)Item 2016-17 2017-18 2018-19 2019-20 2020-21*Total 2.37 2.41 2.35 1.97 3.01*Apr-Jan(f): The details of average yearly price per barrel of Indian Basket of Crude oil during the lastfive years is given below:Year Indian Basket of Crude Oil ($/bbl)2016-17 47.562017-18 56.432018-19 69.882019-20 60.472020-21 (till 28th February 2021) 42.78The Indian basket of Crude Oil represents a derived basket comprising of Sour grade (Oman &Dubai average) and Sweet grade (Brent Dated) of Crude oil processed in Indian refineriesThe details of average yearly price of petrol in New Delhi (as per IOCL) during the last fiveyears are given below:Year Petrol (Rs./Litre)2016-17 64.612017-18 69.202018-19 75.372019-20 72.692020-21 (till 28th February 2021) 79.82x-x-x-x-x
