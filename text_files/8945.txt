                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2521






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Camps for Differently Abled Persons




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of camps organized by the Government to provide equipments to the differently abled people for assistance till the year 2011;(b) the number of such camps organized since the year 2014; and(c) the number of equipments distributed in these camps to the beneficiaries in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR) (a) :	Consolidated information regarding number of camps organized for distribution of aids and assistive devices to the differently abled people till the year 2011 is not available.(b) & (c) : 8465 camps have been organized since 2014 in which 1492708 aids and assistive devices were distributed to persons with disabilities in the country.*****
