                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

126






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Upgradation of Calicut Railway Station




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government plans to upgrade Calicut railway station with the help of Indian Railway Stations Development Corporation (IRSDC); (b) if so, the details regarding the plans proposed in this regard;(c) whether any progress has been made by IRSDC in the development of Calicut railway station to international standards; and(d) if so, the details regarding the works undertaken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW) (a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.***** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF STARRED QUESTION NO. 126 BY SHRI M.K. RAGHAVAN TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 28.07.2021 REGARDING UPGRADATION OF CALICUT RAILWAY STATION(a) to (d): Yes, Sir. Calicut (Kozhikode) railway station has been identified for redevelopment and entrusted to Rail Land Development Authority (RLDA) for redevelopment. RLDA has engaged consultant for carrying out  techno-economic studies i.e., for Feasibility Studies, Detailed Master Planning, Urban Planning, Engineering and preparation of Detailed Project Report for redevelopment of the station under Public Private Partnership model.*****
