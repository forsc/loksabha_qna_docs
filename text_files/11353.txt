                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

446






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Misuse and diversion of MGNREGS Funds




Arvind Dharmapuri













                                                    Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has taken note that the State Governments have been indulging in misuse and diversion of funds released under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the details of the funds released;(c) 	whether the Government has assessed the details of the amount of funds diverted and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether the High Court has also directed to submit the details of the diversion of funds and if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	the action being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (e): No specific matter of diversion of funds released under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) has come to the notice of the Ministry. Instances of misuse of Mahatma Gandhi NREGS funds reported from States are promptly inquired into by the Ministry through local inspections, internal audits, social audits etc. and necessary corrective measures are initiated.State/UT-wise details of central fund released under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS during the current financial year 2019-20 (as on 30.01.2020) are at Annexure.
