                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1770






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Upgradation of Airports




Harish Dwivedi














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of such airports in metro and non-metro cities in the country whose upgradation/modernization have been started;(b) the amount spent thereon during each of the last three years and the current year; and(c) the present status thereof along with the time by which the said work is likely to be completed, State/UT/Airport-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c): Upgradation/Modernisation of airports is a continuous process and is undertaken by Airports Authority of India(AAI) and concerned private operators from time to time depending on the availability of land, commercial viability, socio-economic considerations, traffic demand / willingness of airlines to operate to/from such airports. AAI has spent Rs. 2054 crore, Rs. 2504.38 crore, Rs. 4297.44 crore and Rs. 2241.48 crore during years 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 (till 21.11.2019) respectively on the Upgradation/Modernisation work and other associated works at its various airports across the country. The State/UT/Airport-wise details of the Upgradation/Modernisation work at various AAI airports along with the present status and Probable Date of Completion (PDC) are at Annexure-I. The works taken up at private/Joint Venture airports are at Annexure-II.
