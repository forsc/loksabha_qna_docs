                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Siddha Units/Wellness Centres




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has any proposal to set up more Siddha units/ wellness centres in CGHS dispensaries across the country for promoting traditional medicines/treatments; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the total number of Siddha units/ Siddha welfare centres which are functional across the country as on date, State/ UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN) (a) to (c):  A statement is laid on the Table of the House STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. 5* FOR 21ST JUNE, 2019(a) & (b): At present there is no proposal to open new Siddha Units /Wellness Centres under CGHS.(c): Three Siddha Wellness Centres, one in Delhi and two in Tamil Nadu are functional under CGHS.*****
