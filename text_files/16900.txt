                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

605






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Market Share of BSNL and MTNL




Krupal Balaji Tumane













                                                    Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether MTNL and BSNL are more indebted due to the loss of market share in landline and mobile services than private telecommunication companies; (b) 	if so, the details thereof including the debt reported by BSNL and MTNL during the last three years and the current year along with the reasons therefor; and  (c) 	the separate number of mobile and landline connections surrendered during the said period, company-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	Market share of Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) and Mahanagar Telephone Nigam Limited (MTNL) for the last three years are as under:<pre>Market share as on	Market share of BSNL(in %age)	Market share of MTNL*(in %age)	Wireline	Wireless	Total	Wireline	Wireless	Total31.03.2018	53.78	9.44	10.30	54.13	3.94	7.1631.03.2019	51.47	9.96	10.72	52.51	3.83	6.9531.03.2020	43.16	10.35	10.92	48.80	3.68	6.61</pre>* Market share of MTNL is calculated in its LSAs(Delhi and Mumbai) only.MTNL and BSNL are indebted due to stiff competition in telecom sector resulting in reduced tariffs, high staff cost and absence of 4G services (except on a limited basis in certain areas for BSNL).(b)	Debt reported by BSNL and MTNL during the last three years and the current year is as under:(in Rupees crore)<pre>Debt as on	BSNL	MTNL31.03.2018	9,452	16,97431.03.2019	15,988	19,69231.03.2020	21,902	22,96631.08.2020	21,902	23,950</pre>Loans are availed to meet the capital expenditure and shortfall in operational revenue and expenditure.(c)	Number of landline and mobile connections of BSNL and MTNL surrendered during the last three years are as under:<pre>Period	Connections surrendered in BSNL	Connections surrendered in MTNL	Landline 	Mobile	Landline	Mobile2017-20	41,34,029	3,45,000 	5,28,520	4,88,491</pre>************
