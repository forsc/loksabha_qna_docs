                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2250






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Scholarship Scheme under TSP in Madhya Pradesh




Uday Pratap Singh














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received any proposals from the Government of Madhya Pradesh regarding Post-Matric and Pre-Matric Scholarship Schemes under Tribal Sub-Plan;(b)	if so, the details along with the present status of thereof; and (c)	the reasons for the pendency of the said proposals?






ANSWER




                                                         (a) to (c):    Yes Sir. The proposal under the scheme of Post-Matric Scholarship for ST students was received vide letter dated 8th May 2020 and 18th Sept 2020 and the proposal under Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme for ST students was received from the Government of Madhya Pradesh vide letter 4th May 2020.The proposals received were examined and since, the proposal under Post-Matric Scheme dated 18th Sept 2020 was not as per norms/General Financial Rules, the proposals dated 8th May 2020 and 4th May 2020 were processed and admissible amount, as per the guidelines, was released to the Government of Madhya Pradesh for Post-Matric and Pre-Matric scheme vide sanction order dated 21st Oct 2020 and 22nd June 2020 respectively. No proposal is pending from the Government of Madhya Pradesh as on date.
