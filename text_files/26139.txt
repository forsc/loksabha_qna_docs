                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1900






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






NPCDCS Programme




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the details of the districts across the country where National Programme for Prevention and Control of Cancer, Diabetes, Cardiovascular Diseases and Stroke (NPCDCS) programme is being implemented and the number of personnel working under the said programme, district-wise; (b) whether the Government has reviewed the performance of the said programme and if so, the details thereof; and (c) whether the Government proposes to extend the work tenure of the personnel working under the said programme, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (SMT. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) National Programme for Prevention and Control of Cancer, Diabetes, Cardiovascular Diseases and Stroke (NPCDCS) is being implemented in 684 districts across the country. State-wise number of NCD cells is attached as Annexure-I. The Department of Health & Family Welfare provides technical and financial support including for human resource to the States/UTs under the National Programme for Prevention and Control of Cancer, Diabetes, Cardiovascular Diseases and Stroke (NPCDCS), as part of National Health Mission (NHM), based on the proposals received from the States/UTs. This human resource is supported in addition to the regular human resource of the State Government. However, the recruitment of the human resources is done at the State/UTs level. The number of personnel working under the said programme is not maintained centrally.(b) The NPCDCS programme division periodically reviews the performance of the States/UTs to ensure the implementation of the programme across the States/UTs and to provide timely guidance and support, to strengthen the program implementation. The last review meetings were held in the months of December, 2020 and January, February, March 2021 along with the National Programme Co-ordination Committee (NPCC) meetings under NHM. The performance of States/UTs is also reviewed based on the progress reports that the States/UTs are required to submit regularly to the NPCDCS Programme Division. (c) The human resource needs are assessed by the State/UTs’ Governments at their level as per their own policies and requirements under the NPCDCS. Department of Health and Family Welfare provides support to States/UTs based on the proposals received from the States/UTs in their Program Implementation Plan (PIPs) subject to their overall resource envelope.
