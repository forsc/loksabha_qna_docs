                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

99






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Multi-Purpose Cooperative Societies




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware that multi-purpose cooperative societies set up in various Statesof the country, including Rajasthan have mis-appropriated crores of rupees of investors;(b)	if so, the number of such cases which have cometo the notice of the Government;(c)	the amount of money mis-approriated by the said societies alongwith the number of investors who lost their money in the process, society-wise; and(d) 	the steps taken by the Government in this regard to safeguard the interest of the investors?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COOPERATION???????? ?????? 	(SHRI  AMIT SHAH)(a) to (d): A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 99 DUE FOR REPLY ON 8TH FEBRUARY, 2022(a) to (d) Yes sir. A large number of complaints have been received for non-repayment / misappropriation of deposits of investors against some of the multi-state cooperative societies in various states of the Country, including Rajasthan. In case of delinquent societies against whom complaints are received, action for winding up is taken under Section 86 of the Multi-State Cooperative Societies (MSCS) Act, 2002.  Presently winding up proceedings is underway in case of 77 societies, a list of which is at Annexure A. Complete details regarding the amount of money misappropriated by the societies, along with the numbers of members who lost their money is not available.
