                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2166






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Child Protection Policy




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Draft National Child Protection policy has been prepared or is being prepared by the Government; and(b)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




(a) & (b) :  In pursuance with the direction of the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India in the matter of Sampoorna Behura vs. UOI & Ors (M.A. No.2069/2018 in W.P.(C) No.473/2005 regarding the drafting of the National Child Protection Policy, the first draft of the National Child Protection Policy was prepared by the Ministry of Women and Child Development. The draft was widely circulated in the public domain to gather the comments/ suggestions of all the stakeholders. Numerous comments have been received, which are presently being reviewed.  ***
