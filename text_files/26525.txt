                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2201






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Development of Worship Places as Tourist Spots




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande













                                                    Chandeshwar Prasad
                                                








                                                    Abdul Majeed Ariff
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the list of places declared as National Pilgrim Centres in the country till date; (b) 	the number of places of worship developed as tourist spots in the various States of the country during the last three years including Bihar particularly Barabar Hills in Jehanabad along with the details of funds allocated for the purpose during the last three years, State-wise; (c) 	whether the Government has prescribed any criteria for declaring a place of worship as a National Pilgrim Centre (NPC) and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether the Government intends to declare Sabarimala as a NPC as requested by the State of Kerala and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (e) 	the names of places of worship which are proposed to be developed as tourist spots in various States including Bihar during the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a), (c) & (d): Ministry of Tourism neither declares any spot as National Pilgrim Tourism Centre nor does it maintain any list of such Centres.  However, Ministry of Tourism, under the schemes viz., Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD) and “Integrated Development of Theme Based Tourist Circuit in the Country, Swadesh Darshan (SD)" provides Central Financial Assistance for infrastructure development and beautification of tourist spots on receipt of DPRs submitted by State Governments / Union Territories in adherence to the guidelines of these schemes, subject to availability of funds, liquidation of pending utilisation certificate against the fund released earlier and position of physical execution of the projects approved to the respective state / UT earlier.(b) and (e):  Approval of new projects under PRASHAD and Swadesh Darshan Schemes is a continuous process subject to the conditions mentioned in above para. Details of the projects approved for development of pilgrimage / religious places in various States / Union Territories, including in the State of Bihar and also for Sabrimala in Kerala and the projects approved across the country during the last three years and the current year is annexed.*******
