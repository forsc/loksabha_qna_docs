                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4175






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Projects for Cleaning of Ganga River




Geeta Kora














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of projects decided to be sanctioned in various cities of the country under the efforts to keep Ganga clean; (b) the funds allocated for the implementation of various projects; (c) whether the cost of operation and maintenance for a suitable period is also included in the contracts being provided; and(d) if so, the details thereof, State and project-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b)  Under the flagship programme of Namami Gange, a total 310 projects at the cost of Rs.28,790.66 crore have been sanctioned for various activities such as sewerage infrastructure, ghats & crematoria, river front development, river surface cleaning, institutional development, biodiversity conservation, afforestation and rural sanitation in 8 states of Ganga basin – i.e  Uttarakhand, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Jharkhand, West Bengal, Delhi, Haryana, and Himachal Pradesh. The status of various projects taken up State-wise in different sectors and expenditure incurred under Namami Gange Programme is Annexed. The projects across various cities of Ganga Basin are available in public domain at link:  https://nmcg.nic.in/projectsearch.aspx(c)  Yes Sir.
