                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3916






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Physical Training Centres




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) the number of physical education centers in the country, particularly in Maharashtra;b) whether the Government proposes to open more such centers in the country, especially in Maharashtra; andc) if so, the locations thereof and the time by which the same are likely to be opened with special reference to Maharashtra?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) to (c) While this Ministry does not run any physical education center in the country, academic programs for physical education are conducted by the Lakshmibai National Institute of Physical Education (LNIPE) at Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh, Lakshmibai National College of Physical Education (LNCPE) at Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala and National Sports University at Imphal, Manipur. LNIPE also runs a campus at Guwahati. No new institution/campus is proposed to be set up as the existing can provide training to large number of candidates.***
