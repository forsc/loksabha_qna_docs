                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3701






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Petrochemical Investment Regions




Rita Bahuguna Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the current status of all the four Petroleum, Chemicals and Petrochemical Investment Regions (PCPIRs) to be developed in the State of Andhra Pradesh, Odisha, Gujarat and Tamil Nadu;(b) the details of investment made on each PCPIR till date and employment generated from these PCPIRs;(c) whether the Master Plan for all PCPIR has been finalized and each PCPIR is having their active management board members;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?****
