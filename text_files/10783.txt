                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4213






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Anganwadi Centres in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    the details of Anganwadi centres sanctioned in the last five years in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, year-wise; (b) 	the details of Anganwadi centres that became operational against the sanctioned ones in the last five years in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, year-wise; and (c) 	the steps taken by the Ministry to help Anganwadis to increase wages and ensure social security to them?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)  	During last five years, no Anganwadi Centre has been sanctioned to the state of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana under the Anganwadi Services {under Umbrella Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Schemes}. (b) 	The details of Anganwadi centres that became operational against the sanctioned centres during last five years and current year in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, are as under:Year 	Andhra Pradesh	Telangana	Sanctioned	Operational	Sanctioned	Operational2014-15	55607	55574	35700	353532015-16	55607	55599	35700	356212016-17	55607	55605	35700	356342017-18	55607	55606	35700	356342018-19	55607	55607	35700	356342019-20	55607	55607	35700	35634(c) 	The Government has, recently, enhanced honorarium of Anganwadi Workers (AWWs) from ? 3,000/- to ? 4,500/- per month; AWWs at mini-AWCs from ? 2,250/- to ? 3,500/- per month; Anganwadi Helpers (AWHs) from ? 1,500/- to ? 2,250/- per month. The Government has also introduced performance linked incentive of ? 250/- per month to AWHs effective from 1st October, 2018. AWWs are paid performance linked incentive of ? 500/- per month for using ICDS-CAS (Common Application Software) under POSHAN Abhiyaan. Most of the States/UTs are also giving monetary incentives to these workers out of their own resources as per details given in Annexure. AWWs/AWHs are provided Social Security benefits under the following insurance scheme:i.	Coverage under Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY) for AWWs/AWHs in the age group of 18-50 years for life cover of ? 2.00 Lakh;ii.	Coverage under Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY) for AWWs/AWHs in the age group of 18-59 years for accidental cover of ? 2.00 Lakh / ? 1.00 Lakh.iii.	Modified Anganwadi Karyakarti Bima Yojana (AKBY) for AWWs/AWHs in the age group of 51-59 years for life cover of ? 30,000/-.iv.	Female Critical Illness benefits of ? 20,000/- on diagnosis of identified illness.v.	Scholarships to their children studying in 9th to 12th Standard (including ITI courses).*********             AnnexureStatement referred to in reply to Part (c) of Lok Sabha Un-Starred Question No. 4213 by Shri Margani Bharat to be answered on 13.12.2019(Monetary incentives given by States/UTs to AWWs/AWHs from their own resources)S.
