                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1127






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Assistance to Cooperative Banks




Ramapati Ram Tripathi














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) has launched or proposes to launch any plan to provide financial assistance to cooperative banks being run in the rural areas of the country; (b) if so, the details thereof and the purpose for which this financial assistance is provided by NABARD; and (c) the details of assistance provided by NABARD during each of the last three years and the current year, State-wise






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a)	& (b) National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) had established a fund called ‘Cooperative Development Fund (CDF)’ to provide financial assistance to the Rural Credit Cooperative Institutions including Rural Cooperative Banks (RCBs). The objectives of CDF are: •	To supplement the efforts of the ground level cooperative credit institutions, viz., Primary Agricultural Credit Society (PACS) and weak District Central Cooperative Banks (DCCB) & State Cooperative Banks (StCBs), in mobilising resources for business development.•	Human Resource Development aimed at capacity building for efficient decision-making and effective implementation.•	Building up of better Management Information System (MIS) and analytical capability.•	Conduct of special studies for improving functional efficiency of rural cooperatives.The above financial assistance is provided to RCBs for the following purposes: •	To Cooperative Training Institutions (CTIs) of RCBs for training and capacity building of personnel as well as grass roots cooperatives.•	Establishment of PACS Development Cells at DCCBs.•	For conducting Organisational Development Intervention (ODI) programme for Cooperative Banks. •	Programmes on Corporate Governance and Exposure Visits for Board of Directors, Senior Managementand other key functionaries of RCBs.•	Support for organising Conference/ Seminar/ Workshops on topics of relevance; conduct of Studies and Surveys on issues of national and regional importance. •	Infrastructure support to PACS foroffice furniture, cash counter, safe, note counting machine, solar panel, etc.•	Further, in the current financial year, following new eligible activities are included under CDF:?	PACS Computerisation - Stimulus grant of a maximum of Rs.5.0 crore per state to be provided by NABARD for PACS computerisation subject to matching grant by State Government and/ or the Cooperative Bank.   ?	Establishment of Business Diversification and Product Innovation (BDPI) Cells at StCBs, to develop new and innovative products in the Cooperative Credit Structure.  ?	Support for various promotional measures (viz., sensitisation, mapping of potential PACS and business activities, DPR preparation) to transform PACS as Multi-Service Centres (MSCs).          ?	CTIs are allowed to conduct training programmes on-line.?	Publications brought out by Cooperative Organisations.    Cumulatively Rs.218.50 crore (till 31st January 2021) has been disbursed under CDF for various promotional programmes conducted by different tiers of short-term and long-term credit cooperative structure. (c) State-wise and year-wise details of the financial assistance provided by NABARD under CDF during the last three years and the current year are given in the Annexure.******
