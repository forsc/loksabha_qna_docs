                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1357






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






Indian Apparel Exports




Thirumaa Valavan Thol














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the Duty disadvantage faced by Indian apparel Exporters to European Union, vis-a-vis Exporters from Pakistan, Bangladesh, Cambodia, Turkey and Sri Lanka; (b) if so, the percentage of Duty disadvantage with above-mentioned countries; (c) whether the Government has taken any measures to remove the Duty disadvantage faced by the Indian Apparel Exporters to European Union; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) & (b):	Indian apparels have been facing an average import duty of 9.6% in European Union as against 0% for similar products exported by Bangladesh, Cambodia, Turkey and Sri Lanka. (c) & (d):	India is currently engaged with UK, European Union (bloc of 27 European Countries), UAE, Australia and European Free Trade Association (bloc of 4 European Countries) for Free Trade Agreements, wherein Government is aiming to conclude mutually beneficial agreements.***
