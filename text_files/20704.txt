                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1867






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Financial Assistance for Tree Plantation




Mahabali Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether any scheme for providing financial and technical assistance to various States for tree plantation is being implemented by the Union Government; (b) 	if so, the details thereof, scheme-wise and State/ UT-wise including Bihar; (c) 	the financial assistance provided to Bihar during the last three years, year-wise; and (d) 	the number of trees planted with the said financial assistance during the said period, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) The Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEFCC) implements    two major afforestation schemes for increasing forest resources in the country namely National Afforestation Programme (NAP) for tree plantation in degraded forests through people’s participation and National Mission for a Green India (GIM), for plantation on landscape basis. Besides, the funds are released under Compensatory Afforestation Fund Management and Planning Authority (CAMPA) to States/UTs for carrying out afforestation activities, which also increases green cover. (b)The scheme-wise and State-wise details of assistance provided for afforestation including Bihar for the last three years (2017-18 to 2019-20) is given in Annexure I.(c) and (d) The details of year-wise funds released under various schemes to Bihar and seedlings planted as reported by the State under Twenty Point Programme are given in Annexure II.  *****
