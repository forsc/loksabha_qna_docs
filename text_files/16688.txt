                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

434






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Welfare of Handicapped




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total works carried out in Bharatpur District of Rajasthan for the welfare of Handicapped persons during the last three years and the current year;(b) the total amount released for the said works; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR)(a) to (c) Relief to Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) is a State subject by virtue of Entry 9 of the State List of the Constitution of India. However, the Central Government supplements the efforts of the State/UT governments for empowerment of PwDs through various schemes and programmes which are applicable across the country. So far as the Bharatpur District of Rajasthan is concerned, the number of beneficiaries and the amount released under various schemes of Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities during the last three years and current year are as under:-  (i) Aids & assistive devices provided to 409 Divyangjan including 01 Cochlear Implant surgery under the Scheme of Assistance to Disabled Persons for Purchase/Fitting of Aids/Appliances(ADIP). An amount of Rs.52.97 lakh  has been utilized for providing the aids &  assistive devices to  divyangjan.(ii) Scholarship amount of Rs.37.64 lakh provided to 86 students with disabilities under the Umbrella Scheme - Scholarship for Students with Disabilities.(iii) Under the Deendayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme (DDRS), an amount of Rs. 26.41 lakh has been released as financial assistance to Non-Government Organisations for various projects for the welfare of Person with Disabilities (PwDs).(iv) 13039 Unique Disability ID (UDID) cards have been generated for the Bharatpur District of Rajasthan. Under this project, an amount of Rs. 1.00 lakh has been released to the Government of Rajasthan for Bharatpur District for procurement of IT infrastructure at the office of Chief Medical Officer (CMO) in 2018-19.(v) The National Trust, a statutory organization under the Department is implementing the Gharaunda (Group Home for Adults) Scheme at Mahila Bal Vikas Gramoudhyog Shiksha Samiti, Bharatpur since 19.04.2017. Under the scheme residential care facility is being provided to 9 female beneficiaries. An amount of Rs.16.15 lakh has been released under the scheme so far.*****
