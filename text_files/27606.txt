                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3293






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Vikramshila University




Ajay Kumar Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Vikramshila University is in a state of ruin;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details of steps taken to utilise the funds out of the corpus of Rupees Five Hundred Crore under the packaage as announced by the Prime Minster for the purpose; and(d) the other appropriate measures taken/proposed to be taken by the Government for the revival of Vikramshila University?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d) The Central University functions as a pace setter institution in the region. Keeping in view the requirements of the state, the Government has already established four Central Universities in Bihar. The PM Package for Bihar, 2015 inter-alia provides for establishment of a new Central university near Bhagalpur at the historic site of Vikramshila University. The Government of Bihar has offered the sites for the proposed University and the Site Selection Committee of this Ministry is to visit the site to finalize the suitability of one site. This University will be functional only after the legislation by the Parliament.*****
