                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1086






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






SoS Safety App for Women




Feroze Varun Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government is planning to bring an SoS safety app for women to ensure women safety by connecting victims/needy with police stations; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether there are any training sessions for male cops to sensitize them on the issues of women and whether any collaboration with NGOs have been made in this regard, if so, the details thereof; and (d)	the number of Nirbhaya homes established, State-wise?






ANSWER




ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (b) : 	The Government has launched the 112 Emergency Response Support System (ERSS), which provides for a single number (112) based assistance in emergencies to women in distress. ERSS can be accessed through 112 India Mobile App from any smart phone.  (c) : ‘Police’ and ‘Public order’ are State subjects under the Seventh schedule to the Constitution of India.  Maintaining law and order, protection of life and property of the citizens, including safety of women, is primarily the responsibility of the States/ UTs. The States/ UTs arrange training to police, including male personnel, to sensitize them in handling crime against women.  The Central Government also provides training to the police personnel from time to time that includes sensitisation courses on issues related to safety of women.     (d) : The Government has set up 684 One Stop Centres for violence affected women with the help of Nirbhaya Fund. The State-wise number of operationalised One Stop Centres is at Annexure-I.
