                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4111






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Check on Manufacturing and Sale of Sub-Standard Helmets




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware of increasing number of manufacturers and sellers of sub-standard helmets resulting in increase in the number of accidental deaths involving two-wheeler in the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the reaction of the Government thereto; (c) 	the steps being taken by the Government to check and prevent manufacturing and sale of such sub-standard helmets in the country; (d) 	whether the Government proposes to reduce prices of good quality helmets so as to encourage use of such helmets by a larger number of people; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)  &  (b)  As per information provided by Police Departments  of all States and UTs, total number of persons killed due to non-wearing of helmet was 10135 in 2016 and 35975 in 2017.  As per Section 129 of Motor Vehicle Act, 1988, every person driving or riding (otherwise than in a side car, on a motor vehicle of any class or description) shall, while in a public place, wear protective headgear confirming to the standards of Bureau of Indian Standards.(c) to (e)    The ministry has issued draft Helmets (Quality Control) order, 2018 for inclusion of protective Helmets for Motorcycle riders as per IS 4151 under the mandatory BIS certification.  *****
