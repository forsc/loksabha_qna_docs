                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

708






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Disposal of Waste Plastic in Cement Factories




 Imtiaz Jaleel Syed













                                                    Asaduddin Owaisi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether at present only 46 cement plants out of 238 facilities across the country use waste plastic as fuel; (b)    whether the Government proposes to increase the limit for use of plastic waste as fuel in some factories from current 10% to 25% to increase the disposal of waste plastic in cement factories and if so, the details thereof;(c)    whether cement manufacturers association has welcomed this step of the Government; and(d)    if so, the total waste likely to be used by cement factories after increasing limit to 25% and its pros and cons in using waste plastic?;






ANSWER




                                                        (a) :  As per information submitted by Cement Manufacturers Association (CMA), only 46 cement plants have facility to use Refuse Derived Fuel (RDF) of plastic and other waste as fuel in their plants. (b) : At present no proposal to amend the Solid Waste Management (SWM) Rules, 2016, for increasing the limit for use of plastic waste as fuel from current 5% to 25% in cement factories is under consideration.  (c) and (d) : Does not arise                             *****
