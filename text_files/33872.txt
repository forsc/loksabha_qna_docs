                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1564






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2022






Foreign Investment by Indian Petroleum Companies




S. Ramalingam














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether it is true that the three state run oil companies of the country i.e. ONGC-Videsh Ltd., Indian Oil Corporation Ltd. and Oil India Ltd., have decided to invest jointly in the massive Vostok project of Russia’s Rosneft and has completed the technical evaluation of the proposed project ;   (b)	 if so, the details thereof along with reasons to invest in Russia;   (c)	 the total amount to be invested by these companies, company- wise; and   (d)	 the time by which the proposed investment would be made by these companies?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS  (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a)to(d): To augment energy security of the country, Indian Oil and Gas Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) explore various opportunities for acquiring overseas’ oil and gas assets, subject to technical due diligence and commercial viability.   This assessment, inter alia, includes Russia’s Vostok project.*****
