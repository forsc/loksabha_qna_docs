                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

847






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Action on the Recommendations of the Committee on MSMEs




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the action taken by the Government on the recommendations of the Prabhat Kumar Committee on MSME; and(b)	whether there is any proposal to amend the MSME Act to have a uniform definition of MSMEs across goods and services sector based on annual turnover and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor ? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) & (b):      After considering the recommendations of the Prabhat Kumar Committee and other stakeholders’ views on the subject, a bill to seek amendment for the MSMED Act, 2006, for change in the definition of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises was introduced in the Parliament. The Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises Development (Amendment) Bill, 2018, is deemed to have lapsed on dissolution of the Sixteenth Lok Sabha on 25/05/2019.
