                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

766






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Fishermen in Pakistan’s Custody




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of fishermen in Pakistan’s custody;(b) 	the steps being taken by the Government to get these fishermen released; and(c) 	the number of fishermen released during each of the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a)	As per available information, currently 209 Indian and believed-to-be-Indian fishermen are in Pakistan’s custody.(b) & (c)	Government of India has consistently taken up the issue of early release and repatriation of Indian fishermen along with their boats from Pakistan’s custody. As a result of persistent efforts, release and repatriation of 1447 Indian fishermen has been secured since 2016. This includes 355 Indian fishermen who have returned this year. The year-wise details are as below:<pre>Year	2016	2017	2018	2019 (till 20 June 2019)Indian Fishermen released	410	508	174	355 </pre>***
