                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

71






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Review of Social Justice Policies




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 71TO BE ANSWERED ON 02.02.2021REVIEW OF SOCIAL JUSTICE POLICIES 71. SHRIMATI MEENAKASHI LEKHI:Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a)  whether the Government is considering a collective review of the Social Justice policies;(b)  if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government intends to seek help from the private sector to implement the  amended Social Justice policy, if any; and  (d)  if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a):    No Sir, there is no proposal for collective review of the Social Justice policies. (b) to (d):   Does not arise.****
