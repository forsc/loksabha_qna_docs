                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3486






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Pre-Matric Scholarships for OBC Students




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the details of pre-matric scholarships provided to students belonging to Other Backward Classes (OBCs) category during the last three years in Uttar Pradesh, district-wise; (b) whether the Government is considering to increase the amount of scholarship for these students; and (c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a): Under the Pre-Matric Scholarship scheme for OBC students, the  Ministry releases funds to State Government of Uttar Pradesh  not to the districts. The details of funds allocated, released and beneficiaries covered under the scheme in the State of Uttar Pradesh for the last three years is as under:(Amount and beneficiaries in lakh)Year	Funds  Allocation	Funds Released	No. of Beneficiaries2016-17	2180.00	2772.99	0.932017-18	2180.00	3112.00	1.032018-19	3605.00	3605.00	2.57(b) & (c): There is no proposal for increasing the amount of scholarship for these students;
