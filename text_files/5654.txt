                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5246






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Setting Up of Medical Colleges by CIL




Chandra Prakash Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Coal India Limited and its ancillary companies are running medical college, hospital, dispensary and educational institutions in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof, location and State/UT-wise?(c)	whether the Government has acquired land for setting up of medical colleges by Coal India Limited and its ancillary companies;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the time by which these colleges are likely to be made functional along with the amount likely to be spent in this regard?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a) & (b) : Coal India Limited and its subsidiaries are not running any medical college and educational institutions. However, hospitals and dispensaries are being run by Coal India Limited and its subsidiaries to meet the medical requirements of its employees and their families. Subsidiaries of CIL have entered into an agreement with institutions viz. Central School, DAV, Delhi Public School etc. for setting up of schools in their command areas and aids are provided for running the schools. The loacation of Hospitals, Dispensaries etc. State/UT wise are as under:Company	State	Hospital	Dispensaries	School of Nursing /Paramedical TechnologyCIL Coal India Limited	West Bengal	-	01	-Eastern Coalfields Limited (ECL)	West Bengal	10	92	01	Jharkhand	3	14	-Central Coalfields Limited (CCL)	Jharkhand	19	63	-Mahanadi Coalfields Limited(MCL)	Odisha	07	13	02Western Coalfields Limited(WCL)	Maharashtra	07	24	-	Madhya Pradesh	03	10	-Bharat Coking Coal Limited(BCCL)	Jharkhand	11	64	02Northern Coalfields Limited (NCL)	Uttar Pradesh	01	02	-	Madhya Pradesh	02	08	-South Eastern Coalfields Limited(SECL)	Madhya Pradesh	03	22	-	Chhattisgarh	08	33	- 	West Bengal	-	01	-North Eastern Coalfields (NEC)	Assam	01	04	-Central Mine Planning and Design Institute Limited(CMPDIL)	Jharkhand	-	01	-(c) to (e): Mahanadi Coalfields Ltd. (MCL), a subsidiary of Coal India Ltd. has acquired 8.50 ha (21 acres) land for construction of Mahanadi Institute of Medical Science and Research (MIMSR), Talcher, Angul district, Odisha from its CSR fund. The work is likely to be completed by the end of March 2020. The total project outlay is Rs. 492.62 crores.*****
