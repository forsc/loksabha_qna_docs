                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

573






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Fund Allocation under PMAY




Sanjay Kumar Bandi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the quantum of funds allocated under Pradhan Mantri Awas 	Yojana (PMAY) - Urban to the Government of Telangana during 	the last three years; (b) 	the number of houses sanctioned and built during the last	three years, year-wise; and (c) 	whether the Union Government proposes to take any action for not complying with the guidelines of the yojana in building the houses as per schedule and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                                    THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS				(SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)	 (a)&(b):The details of funds/central assistance approved/allocated, number of houses sanctioned and constructed in the State of Telangana during the last three years under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban [PMAY-U] are as under:F.Y.	Funds sanctioned   Houses sanctioned       Houses Constructed*          (? in Cr.)          (Nos)	              (Nos)2018-19	  341.98	       15,588	            55,0142019-20	  384.76	       16,895	            39,0962020-21	  987.01	       48,550	            87,037*Includes houses constructed out of the sanctioned houses of previous years. (c):	The houses sanctioned under PMAY-U are to be completed as per schedule mentioned in the Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) of respective projects. Ministry provides necessary guidance and hand-holding to the concerned Departments of State/Union Territory (UT) governments and also persuade them to comply with the conditions stipulated in the scheme guidelines of PMAY-U so that construction of houses in sanctioned projects are completed within the scheduled timelines.                         *****
