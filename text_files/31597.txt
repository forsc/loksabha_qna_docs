                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2995






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Fund Allocation under Swachh Bharat Abhiyan




Gurjeet Singh Aujla














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether any State specific fund allocation has been made under Swachh Bharat Abhiyan to relocate stray cattle/dogs from urban areas of the States and if so, the details thereof; and (b)	whether there is any other concrete plan to address issue of stray cattle and dogs in urban areas and if so, the details thereof?ANSWER






ANSWER




                                                                         THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE             MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                   (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) to (b) : Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U) 2.0 has been launched on October 1, 2021 with the following objectives :(i). To achieve Sustainable Solid Waste Management.(ii). To achieve Sustainable Sanitation and treatment of used water.(iii). To create awareness along with large scale citizen outreach for ‘jan andolan’ and to institutionalize ‘swachh’ behaviour.(iv). To create institutional capacity to effectively implement programmatic interventions to achieve mission objectives.Relocation of stray cattle and dogs from urban areas is not an objective of the Mission.                                            *****
