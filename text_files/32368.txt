                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3800






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Gram Swaraj Abhiyan




Sunita Duggal














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the progress of Gram Swaraj Abhiyan in Haryana; District Wise(b)	the number of village people in the State who have benefited under different segments, if so, the details thereof, district-wise along with the figures thereto; and(c)	the number of villages which are yet to benefited therefrom?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI KAPIL MORESHWAR PATIL)(a)	to (c) : “Gram Swaraj Abhiyan” was organized from 14th April to 5th May, 2018 under the aegis of Department of Rural Development. As a special endeavor during the Gram Swaraj Abhiyan, efforts were made to saturate the eligible households/ persons under flagship pro-poor programmes namely, Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana, PM Sahaj Bijli Har Ghar Yojana (Saubhagya), Ujala Scheme, PM Jan Dhan Yojana, PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana, PM Suraksha Bima Yojana and Mission Indradhanush. At present, there is no such Abhiyan being conducted by the Department of Rural Development.***
