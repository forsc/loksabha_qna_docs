                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5061






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Raids by Central Agencies




Mala Roy














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of raids conducted by the central agencies such as Enforcement Directorate against the opposition leaders in poll-bound States; and(b) the details of all such raids that happened after announcement of Assembly election in a State since 2014?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) and (b): Action under relevant acts (like Prevention of Money Laundering Act, Foreign Exchange Management Act, Income Tax Act etc.) are initiated against the offenders/violators on the basis of incriminating evidence irrespective of the position, status or political affiliation and irrespective of announcement of election or otherwise.*****
