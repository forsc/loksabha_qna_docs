                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3901






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Gramin Kaushalya Yojana




E. T. Mohammed Basheer














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has reviewed the progress of implementation of Gramin Kaushalya Yojana;(b) 	if so, whether any shortcoming has been reported; and(c) 	if so, the corrective steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): Ministry of Rural Development is successfully implementing Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY), a placement linked skill development program for rural poor youth under National Rural Livelihoods Mission (NRLM) since September, 2014. Under DDU-GKY, a total of 10.29 lakh candidates have been trained and 6.30 Lakh candidates have been placed so far. DDU-GKY is a standard led delivery of skill training program based on Standard Operating Procedures and Guidelines. The Scheme guidelines provide for concurrent monitoring through 3 tier mechanism for inspections carried out by Central Technical Support Agency, State Rural Livelihoods Mission/State Skill Development Mission and Q team of Project Implementation Agencies (PIAs). Further, implementation of DDU-GKY is reviewed through regular     National / Regional review meetings with State/UT Governments.  (b): No shortcoming has been reported so far for DDU-GKY.(c): Does not arise in view of reply given to part (b) above.********
