                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

202






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Gender Equality in Education Sector




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya













                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry is making appropriate/necessary efforts for womenempowerment and gender equality in the field of education particularly in Gujarat and ifso, the details thereof; and(b) the efforts being made by the Ministry for increasing women participation inhigher education institutes, particularly induction of more female teachers ineducational institutes as well as higher education institutes in Gujarat?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) and (b): A statement is laid on the table of the House.*****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) and (b) OF THE LOKSABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.202 FOR REPLY ON 13.12.2021 ASKED BYSHRI NARANBHAI KACHHADIYA AND SHRI PARBATBHAI SAVABHAI PATEL,HON’BLE MEMBERS OF PARLIAMENT REGARDING GENDER EQUALITY INEDUCATION SECTOR(a) and (b): Education is in the concurrent list of the Constitution. The Ministry ofEducation has taken a number of initiatives for women empowerment and genderequality in the field of education sector across the country including Gujarat. TheMinistry is implementing Samagra Shiksha Scheme under which there are provisionsof gender segregated toilets in all schools, Teachers’ sensitization programmes topromote girls’ participation, Rani Laxmi Bai Atmaraksha Prashikshan, stipend for GirlChildren With Special Needs, etc. In addition, Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalayas(KGBVs) have been opened in Educationally Backward Blocks for girls belonging todisadvantaged groups. Out of 5615 sanctioned KGBVs, 170 KGBVs have beensanctioned in the State of Gujarat.Similarly, University Grants Commission (UGC) and All India Council for TechnicalEducation (AICTE) are implementing various fellowship/scholarship schemes. 159Women Studies Centres, including 4 Centres in Gujarat, have been established invarious Universities and Colleges to undertake research and develop curricula in theareas of gender equity, economic self-reliance of women, girls’ education, etc. In orderto improve Gender Balance in Undergraduate Programmes in IITs, 5039supernumerary seats for female students have been created since 2018-19.Under Samagra Shiksha Scheme, assistance is provided to the States and UTsincluding Gujarat for deployment of additional teachers.*****
