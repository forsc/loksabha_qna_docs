                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3981






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Chennai-Bengaluru Industrial Corridor




Srinivas Kesineni













                                                    Narayana Swamy Abbaiah
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the progress on the development and upgradation of the Chennai-Bengaluru Industrial Corridor on all industrial nodes especially on the Krishnapatnam node;(b) 	the details of the land acquisition, funds allocated and released for the development and upgradation of the said node along with the developmental projects to be undertaken for the same; and(c) 	whether all the relevant approvals have been received from the State of Andhra Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        ??????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b): 	Perspective plan for the entire Chennai-Bengaluru Industrial Corridor (CBIC) has been completed. Three nodes namely (i) Krishnapatnam, Andhra Pradesh (ii) Tumakuru, Karnataka and (iii) Ponneri, Tamil Nadu have been identified for development in phase-1. Details of the node-wise progress are as under:(i)	Krishnapatnam (Andhra Pradesh)Shareholder’s Agreement (SHA) and State Support Agreement (SSA) have been executed and a SPV namely ‘NICDIT Krishnapatanam Industrial City Development limited’ has been incorporated between National Industrial Corridor Development and Implementation Trust (NICDIT) and the State Government. NICDIT has already released an amount of Rs. 2.50 crore to the SPV as its initial equity contribution in the project.  Detailed master planning and preliminary engineering have been completed for implementation of the project over an activation area of 2500 acres out of which approximately 2180 acres of land is under possession of the Government of Andhra Pradesh.  The project proposal amounting to Rs. 2139.44 crore (inclusive of cost of the land) has been approved by NICDIT in its meeting held on 30th August, 2019. Process for obtaining appraisal and approval by the Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs (CCEA) has been initiated. (ii)	Tumakuru (Karnataka)	SHA and SSA have been executed and a SPV namely ‘Tumakuru Industrial Township Limited’ has been incorporated between NICDIT and the Government of Karnataka. NICDIT has already released an amount of Rs. 2.50 crore to the SPV as its initial equity contribution in the project. Detailed master planning and preliminary engineering have been completed for implementation of the project over an activation area of 1736 acres out of which 1722 acres of land is under possession of the Government of Karanataka. The project proposal amounting to Rs. 1701.81 crore (inclusive of the cost of the land) has been approved by NICDIT in its meeting held on 30th August, 2019. Process for obtaining appraisal and approval by CCEA has been initiated. (iii)	Ponneri (Tamil Nadu_)SHA and SSA have been executed on 21st February, 2020 between NICDIT and the Government of Tamil Nadu.  Activities related to selection of consultant for preparation of detailed master planning and preliminary engineering have been initiated.(c):  	Following actions need to be taken expeditiously by the State of Andhra Pradesh:i.	Notification of the entire master plan for Krishnapatnam Industrial Area admeasuring 14000 acres;ii.	Approximately 2180 acres of the land is under possession of the State Government. Accordingly, acquisition of the balance 170 acres of land needs to be expedited;iii.	Transfer of entire 2500.4 acres of the land to the SPV immediately after approval of the project by CCEA;iv.	Following confirmation from the State Government for seeking final Environmental Clearance from Ministry of Environment Forest and Climate Change:a)	Site is outside boundary of ‘Eco Sensitive Zone’ (ESZ) of ‘Pulicat Bird Sanctuary’ and ‘Turtle Nesting Site’;b)	Exact area of the site falling within the Forest Area; andc)	Status of sand dunes falling within the proposed site.*********
