                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

346






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Unmanned Level Crossings in Uttar Pradesh




Ravindra Kushawaha













                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Subrata Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the number of unmanned railway crossings in the country, State-wise; (b) the number of unmanned railway crossings likely to be converted into manned level crossing in the next five years;(c) whether the Government is considering to construct under passes and overbridges on various railway crossings in Uttar Pradesh; and (d) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b):  There is no unmanned level crossing (UMLC) on Broad Gauge (BG) routes on Indian Railway. However, as on 01.04.2020, total 948 UMLCs on Meter Gauge (MG) and Narrow Gauge (NG) are existing, which will be eliminated alongwith gauge conversion works in future. State-wise list of UMLCs is appended.(c) & (d): As on 01.04.2020, Road over Bridges (ROBs) at 265 Level Crossings (LCs) & Road under Bridges (RUBs) at 995 LCs are sanctioned out of 3545 Level Crossings (LCs) in Uttar Pradesh. These are in different stages of implementation. However, the construction of ROB/RUBs depends upon the consent of State Government for closure of LCs & cost sharing, wherever applicable, availability of encroachment free land for approach roads, technical feasibility, shifting of utilities,  allocation of adequate funds by State agencies, public support etc.  Railways have taken pro-active measures in close coordination with various stakeholders t¬o improve the progress of construction of ROBs/RUBs/Subways as under:(i) Joint survey with State Government to finalize the alignment & General Arrangement Drawing (GAD).(ii)  Standardization of design & drawings for various spans. (iii) Regular coordination meeting with State Government officials to sort out various issues.(iv) Construction of ROB on single entity basis.(v) Monitoring of progress at different levels.*****
