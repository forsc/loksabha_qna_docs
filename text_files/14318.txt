                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3057






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Contribution by States on Costof Land Acquisition




Nalin Kumar Kateel














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has issued any guidelines prescribing the percentage of contribution by the State Governments on cost of land acquisition for National Highways; (b) 	if so, the ratio of State and Union Government in sharing the said cost; (c) 	the total amount spent on land acquisition for NHs in the State of Karnataka during the last three years, district and year-wise; and (d) 	the total number of persons who have received compensations along with the area of land acquired for the purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) and (b)	The National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) has issued a guideline dated 30.07.2019 regarding alternative mechanisms to fund Land Acquisition Cost.  The guidelines prescribes for sharing the cost of Land Acquisition for the unviable projects to make them viable and for all Projects in urban areas like Ring Roads/ Bypasses. If a State Government agrees, a Memorandum of understanding is signed between the NHAI and the State Government(s) to contribute towards the Land Acquisition for the identified projects.(c)	The total amount spent on land acquisition for NHs in the State of Karnataka during the last three years, are at Annexure.(d)	Total Number of persons who have received compensation are 33,297 and total area of land acquired is 6176.51 Ha.
