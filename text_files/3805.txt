                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3550






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Reserved Vacancies in IIT/NIT/IISER




Kaushalendra Kumar













                                                    Rajiv Ranjan (Lalan) Singh
                                                








                                                    Queen Oja
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) total number of regular recruitments done for the post of Assistant Professors, Associate Professors andProfessors in the Central Government Colleges and Universities, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT),National Institute of Technology (NIT), Indian Institute of Management (IIM), Indian Institute of Science,Education and Research (IISER) since 1995 till date, college-wise and University-wise;(b) the total regular recruitments done in Central Government Colleges/ Universities along with thecategory-wise details of (UR/OBC/SC/ST) recruitments done since 1995 till date for Assistant Professors,Associate Professors and Professors;(c) the number of employees working on contract basis as Assistant Professors, Associate Professors andProfessors and also details of those belonging to UR/OBC/ SC/ST, college-wise and University-wise; and(d) the duration of appointment of contractual employees and details of sanctioned strength, disciplinewiseand category-wise in Central Government Colleges and Universities?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): The vacancies at various levels are caused by various processes including retirements,resignations, deaths, deputations, expansion and opening of new institutions. Occurrence of vacancies andfilling them is a continuous process. Such data of recruitments made to the posts of Assistant Professor,Associate Professor and Professor since 1995, alongwith their categories, in various Higher EducationalInstitutions is not centrally maintained.(c) & (d): The details of Adhoc/Guest/Contract/Re-employed Teachers deployed in various CentralUniversities as on 01-04-2019 are as under:-<pre>Adhoc Guest Faculty Contract Faculty Re-employed Total551 1582 871 64 3068</pre>There are about 4000 teachers (adhoc/guest/contract) working in the 53 colleges affiliated to University ofDelhi which are getting maintenance grants from UGC/MHRD.However, the data regarding the categories of contractual faculty and their duration of appointment is notmaintained centrally.******
