                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2513






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Complaints Received by NCRB




Shivkumar Chanabasappa Udasi














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether as per the National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) and the National Commission for Women (NCW), Karnataka is one of the top 10 States with the highest number of complaints on violence against women including such complaints registered by women and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government proposes to set up women police stations in all the States; and






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a):	No, Sir. As per the National Commission for Women, Karnataka  is not one of the top 10 States with highest number of complaints of violence against women/ deprivation of their rights, in terms of total complaints received by them, in the last five years and current year.  Such data is not published by National Crime Records Bureau.  (b) & (c): ‘Police’ and ‘Public Order’ are State subjects under the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution of India. The responsibility of setting up of women police stations rests with the State Governments.*****
