                                    CULTURE

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1816






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Development of Forts




Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is  a fact that there are 350 forts in Maharashtra with beautiful landscape and of historical interest that can be used to promote tourism;(b)	if so, the details of the forts in Maharashtra;(c)	whether it is also a fact that due to lack of basic facilities at tourist sites visitors are facing lots of problems especially cave area in Maharashtra; and(d)	the plan of the Government to develop those sites as heritage tourism with basic facilities at these forts?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)&(b)	Forts in Maharashtra are themselves tourist attraction due to their architecture, grandeur, history associated and natural settings.  A list of forts under the jurisdiction of Archaeological Survey of India in Maharashtra is at Annexure.  (c)&(d)	In general forts and caves in Maharashtra are situated in   a difficult geographical terrain and as such provide challenging excursion. Though there is no specific plan by Archaeological Survey of India to develop forts as Heritage Tourism.  Facilities are provided at forts like pathways, benches, drinking water, toilets, signages, etc.  ANNEXURE ANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a)&(b) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 1816 FOR 2.3.2020LIST OF CENTRALLY PROTECTED FORTS IN MAHARASHTRASl.
