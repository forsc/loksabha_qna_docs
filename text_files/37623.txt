                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4933






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Munich Security Conference




Raksha Nikhil Khadse













                                                    Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the issues of the unsustainable infrastructure projects financing were raised during the recent exchange at the Munich Security Conference held at Munich, Germany; and(b) 	if so, the details and the outcome thereof along with the review of the said conference?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) & (b)	Yes. The Munich Security Conference (MSC) is a forum for high-level deliberations on international foreign and security policy issues. External Affairs Minister (EAM) Dr. S. Jaishankar attended MSC 2022 held in Munich, Germany from February 18-20, 2022, where he spoke at the panel discussion on the topic ''A Sea Change? Regional Order and Security in the Indo-Pacific''.While the discussions in the panel discussion mainly focused on the Indo-Pacific, the issue of unsustainable infrastructure project financing was addressed by EAM, wherein EAM emphasized the importance of commercially viable and sustainable infrastructure projects.  ***
