                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2087






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Incentives for ASHA Workers for Covid-19 Duties




Rahul Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether ASHA workers are being provided incentives in addition to their honorarium for the additional tasks being undertaken for COVID-19 duty;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the details of steps being taken to ensure timely payment of honorarium to ASHA workers;(d) whether a number of ASHA workers have died in the course of COVID-19 duty;(e) if so, State/UT-wise details thereof; and(f) the number of claims filed and processed under the insurance scheme for health workers fighting COVID- 19 under the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan package?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b) : Yes. The Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Govt. of India has provisioned  additional incentive of Rs. 1000 per month under the “India COVID 19 Emergency Response and Health Systems Preparedness Package” to ASHAs for undertaking COVID 19 related activities for the period of their engagement in this work. Further the benefit under “Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package : Insurance Scheme for Health Workers Fighting COVID 19”, has been launched  to provide an insurance cover of Rs. 50 lakh to public health providers including ASHAs, who may be at risk of being impacted by    COVID 19.(c):	Public Health and Hospitals is a State subject,  as such responsibility for timely payment of incentive to ASHAs lies with the respective States/UTs.  The Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India, requests the States/UTs  time and again, to ensure that  incentives  are paid to ASHAs without any delay.(d) & (e) : A statement showing, State/UT-wise, numbers ASHAs died during COVID 19 activities as reported by the States/UTs under “Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package: Insurance Scheme for Health Workers Fighting COVID 19”,  is given at Annexure-I.  (f):	A statement showing details of claim filed and processed for ASHA workers under “Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package : Insurance Scheme for Health workers fighting COVID 19”  is given at Annexure-II.
