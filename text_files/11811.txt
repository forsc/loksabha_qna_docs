                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

845






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Modernisation of Airways




Sunil Kumar Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has been successful in developing and modernizing the airways in India during the last six years and the upcoming programmes in the present and coming year;(b) if so, the details thereof along with futuristic steps in this regard, State/UT-wise; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. Developing and modernizing the airways is an ongoing process and based on the operational requirements, Airports Authority of India (AAI) has successfully established/modernized its air navigation services during the last six years. Also, AAI has prepared an action plan to further strengthen the airways by way of conversion of entire continental airspace into Performance Based Navigation airspace, implementation of Space Based Automatic Dependent Surveillance-Broadcast etc.;;(c): Does not arise in view of the above.
