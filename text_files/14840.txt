                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3673






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Increasing Number of Private Schools




Talari Rangaiah














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the number of private schools in the country are more than theGovernment schools and if so, the details thereof;(b) the details of new schools introduced by the Government during the last 3 yearsin the country; and(c) the details of recognition/affiliations granted to private schools during the last 3years?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): No Sir, As per Unified District Information System of Education (UDISE) 2017-18(Provisional), there are 1094537 government schools, 84422 aided, 322242private schools and 57740 other schools in the country.(b): 43 Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya (JNV) and 88 Kendriya Vidyalaya Schools(KVS) have been opened during the last 3 years.(c): The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has informed that 3961Private/ Independent School got CBSE affiliation in the last three years.*****
