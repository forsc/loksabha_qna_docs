                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5261






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Proposal to Include Hoysala Temple in UNESCO’s List




Prajwal Revanna














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the hoysala temples of Karnataka belur, halebidu in Hassan district and somanathapur in Mysore are proposed to be listed under UNESCO’s world heritage sites;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the detailed benefits and funds likely to be extended by the Centre and UNESCO to such sites for  better conservation?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPOMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	& (b)	Yes, Sir. Government of India has submitted the nomination dossier of the serial property ‘Sacred Ensembles of the Hoysala’ comprising  1) Chennakeshava Temple, Belur 2) Hoysaleshvara Temple, Halebidu and 3) Keshava Temple, Somnathpura; to the World Heritage Centre, Paris for consideration for the year 2022-23. (c)	All three are Centrally Protected Monuments and are conserved and maintained by Archaeological Survey of India (ASI). The inscription of the property as World Heritage Site enhances tourism and development of the adjoining areas and provides employment opportunity to the local population.*****
