                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4820






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Female Army Officers




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of female army officers that have left their positions in November, 2020 after the screening done by a Special Selection Board Number 5; (b) the number of female army officers who were released without a pension out of this number; (c) the details of the number of years that these Army officers had spent serving the Army;(d) the details of any Government schemes to offer alternative means of livelihood to officers who leave the army;(e) whether any of these schemes are directly aimed at employing women and if so, the details thereof;(f) whether any help is offered by the Army to these female officers, through alternative employment or any maintenance and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) to (c):  The Women Officers considered in Special Selection Board No.5 held in November, 2020 were commissioned from 1994 to 2010, thereby, after having completed a period ranging from 10 to 26 year’s service.  However, the result of this board is under observation of Hon’ble Supreme Court.(d) & (e):  Age relaxation has been provided to Ex-Servicemen, including Female Officers for the appointment to all Central Civil Services & Posts upto the level of Assistant Commandant in all Para-Military Forces under Government of India.  Adequate reservation has also been provided to Ex-Servicemen in various categories of posts.(f)  Women Officers, who have retired after 20 years of service, have been granted Pensionary Benefits.*****
