                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

527






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Registration of MSMEs




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of MSMEs registered in the country, State-wise;(b)	the details of proposals made to increase the ease of registration of MSMEs on the udyamregistration.gov.in portal;(c)	the number of rural enterprises registered as MSMEs in the Country, State-wise ;(d)	the details of any campaigns or initiatives undertaken to increase awareness of MSMEs and their registrations on a national or State level; (e)	the details of any policies considered along with the national logistics policy, to increase ease of doing business with MSMEs;(f)	the steps taken in this direction and measures to streamline  and make easy the process of registration of MSMEs on the Government e-Marketplace platform?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a): 	 The details are at annexure-I and II.(b):	 To increase the ease of registration on Udyam Registration portal, the Ministry of MSME has made the registration process hassle free by making it paperless, online and without any supporting documents and fee. The portal has linkages with Income Tax, GST portals for fetching the investment and turnover figures automatically. (c):  	The annexure-I and II include rural enterprises also.(d): 	The Ministry of MSME, vide notification no. S.O.2119(E) dated 26.06.2020, has created the Champions Control Rooms functioning in various institutions and offices of the Ministry of MSME including the Development Institutes (MSME-DI) that shall act as Single Window Systems for facilitating the registration process and further handholding the MSMEs  in all possible manner. The District Industries Centres (DICs) under State Governments/UT administrations  also act as Single Window facilitation Systems in their Districts. The Government is also running campaigns in social media to popularize the Udyam Registration and to spread awareness about the same.(e):	Online filing of Udyam Registration and Champions Portal for speedy online redressal of grievances are the steps taken by Ministry of MSME to increase Ease of Doing Business. The Udyam registration Portal has facility to link the registrant with the GeM platform to participate in Government’s mandatory procurement program. In addition, the portal has a provision to link MSMEs to the TReDs portal for factoring of bills. (f):	Ministry of MSME is making every attempt to encourage the MSMEs to get themselves onboarded on the GeM portal. As on 02.02.2021, 506186 MSEs (sellers and service providers) are registered on the GeM portal and around 57.84% (orders value MSE% ) has been placed with MSEs through GeM.  All the field offices attached with this Ministry have also been requested to encourage more number of MSEs for onboarding themselves on the GeM Portal for supply of goods or services.
