                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2399






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Export of Onion Seeds




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the State Government is contemplating not to export onion seeds;(b)	if so, whether the Government has taken steps in this direction; and(c)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER of AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	All matters relating to foreign trade and EXIM (Export Import) trade policy fall within the jurisdiction of the Central Government. The export of onion seeds has been prohibited vide DGFT Notification dated 29th October, 2020 to ensure its availability for domestic harvesting as  more than 80% seed requirement is met through farmers’ seeds.*********
