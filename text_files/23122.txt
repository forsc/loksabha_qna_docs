                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

362






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Renovation of Historical Buildings




Upendra Singh Rawat













                                                    Ramesh Bidhuri
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any survey of historically important buildings including   monuments/forts has been conducted and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise, particularly in Uttar Pradesh, Delhi and Bhagalpur district of Bihar;(b)	whether most of these buildings/forts/monuments are in  dilapidated condition and are in urgent need of repairs and further maintenance; (c)	if so, the details of such Building/ forts/ monuments in the country, State/UT-wise  and the list of buildings repaired in Uttar Pradesh, Delhi and Bihar during the last three years, year-wise, district-wise and location-wise along with the funds allocated and spent thereon;(d)	whether the Archaeological Survey of India proposes to take over these forts/buildings/heritage sites and ensure proper  upkeep and maintenance thereof; and(e)	if so, the details thereof along with the steps taken for the proper maintenance of ancient monuments, particularly in Delhi?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)to(e)	A statement is laid on the table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (e) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 362 FOR 22.03.2021(a)	Conducting survey/exploration to identify/located ancient sites/ monuments/ building or fort is a regular phenomenon of Archaeological Survey of India. Details of potential monuments/site identified for declaring them as Centrally protected Monuments/sites is at Annexure. (b)	No sir, the conservation work of centrally protected monuments in the country, including Uttar Pradesh, Delhi and Bihar, is attended regularly by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) according to the need and as per the availability of funds and resources, and they are in a good state of preservation.(c)to(e)	There are 743 centrally protected monuments/sites in Uttar Pradesh, 173 in Delhi and 70 in Bihar. The conservation work of protected monuments/sites is continuous process and it is attended regularly on need basis as per the availability of funds and resources.
