                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

224






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Implementation of NCLP




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                








                                                    Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the number of districts in which the National Child Labour Project (NCLP) is being implemented, Statewise specially in Maharashtra and Gujarat;(b)the extent to which the implementation of NCLP has been successful during last Ten years; and (c)the fresh steps taken by the Government to provide education to the children of the families engaged in various industries near the place of their residence?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):	The number of districts in which the National Child Labour Project (NCLP) Scheme has been sanctioned on the basis of proposals received from the State Government/District Administration and is currently operational is annexed. (b): 	6,80,528 number of children were rescued/withdrawn from work, rehabilitated and mainstreamed under NCLP Scheme during 2011-12 to 2020-21. Contd..2/-:: 2 ::(c):	Under Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA) Scheme, special training for age appropriate admission of out of school children and residential as well as non-residential training for older children, seasonal hostels/ residential camps, special training centres at worksites, transport/ escort facility are also supported to bring out of school children to the formal schooling system. Also, mid-day meal is provided to the students at the elementary level of education.*                                                      ********
