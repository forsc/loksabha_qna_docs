                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2562






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






PMKSY Projects




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the catchment projects over which the work is being carried out by the Union Government under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY)at present,  State/UT-wise;(b)	whether the  Union  Government  proposes  to sanction new projects under PMKSY, if so, the details thereof;  and(c) the plans of the Union Government to provide water to each farm in case of less availability of water for irrigation in rainfed agricultural areas?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? 	 (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b): 	Government of India is implementing Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) since 2015-16. Under PMKSY, Department of Land Resources (DoLR) is implementing watershed management projects under Watershed Development Component (WDC) for development of rainfed/ degraded areas.  	DoLR has sanctioned about 8214 watershed development projects covering an area of 39.07 million hectare across the country during the period 2009-10 to 2014-15 under Integrated Watershed Management Programme (IWMP). Subsequently on launch of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana in 2015-16, IWMP was amalgamated as Watershed Development Component of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (WDC-PMKSY).  Out of above sanction projects, 1832 projects (345 uninitiated and 1487 in preparatory stage) were transferred to various States in 2018. Therefore, DoLR has been implementing only 6382 projects. Out of these 6382 projects being funded by DoLR, 5151 projects have been reported as completed (as on 31.10.2021). State/UT-wise status of WDC-PMKSY projects sanctioned, projects transferred to States / UTs and projects supported by DoLR is given at Annexure. Expenditure Finance Committee (EFC), Government of India in its meeting held on 6.8.2021 recommended continuation of WDC-PMKSY for another five years from 2021-22 to 2025-26.(c): 	The Government is implementing Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) from 2015-16  with the motto of ‘Har Khet Ko Paani’ for providing end-to-end solutions in irrigation supply chain, viz. water sources, distribution network and farm level applications in the country. The details of components of PMKSY is as under: ?	Accelerated Irrigation Benefit Programme(AIBP): To focus on faster completion of ongoing Major and Medium Irrigation projects including National Projects.  From  2016-17 onwards, 99 prioritized Projects have been identified for expeditious completion adopting innovative funding through creation of ‘Long Term Irrigation Fund’ under NABARD for Mission Mode Completion of projects. This component is being implemented by Department of Water Resources, RD&GR.?	PMKSY - Har Khet Ko Pani: Source augmentation, distribution, ground water development, lift and minor irrigation, repair, restoration, renovation of traditional water bodies.  This component is being implemented by Department of Water Resources, RD&GR. ?	PMKSY- Watershed Development Component: Ridge area treatment, drainage line treatment, soil and moisture conservation, water harvesting structure, livelihood support activities and other watershed works. This component is being implemented by Department of Land Resources. ?	PMKSY- Per Drop More Crop (PDMC): Micro-irrigation systems viz. Drip & Sprinkler irrigation systems, Micro level water storage structures, secondary storage, efficient water conveyance, water lifting devices etc. This component is being implemented by Department of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare.
