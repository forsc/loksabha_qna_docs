                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1477






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






District Rural Development Agencies




A. Chellakumar














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the allocation and utilization of funds made to the District Rural Development Agencies (DRDAs) during the last three years, State-wise and district-wise including Tamil Nadu;(b) 	whether any suggestions/complaints have been received to strengthen the DRDAs for their effective functioning and implementation of schemes; and(c) 	if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)   The State-wise  details (including those of Tamil Nadu) of Central allocation and release of funds under the DRDA Administration Scheme by the Centre during last three years is enclosed at Annexure ‘A’ and District-wise details of Tamil Nadu  in enclosed at Annexure ‘B’.  (b) & (c)  No specific suggestions/ complaints have been received to strengthen the DRDAs for its effective functioning and implementation of Scheme. However, general complaints received against DRDAs were forwarded to the concerned State Governments for taking appropriate action as the District Rural Development Agencies are functioning under their jurisdiction.  *******
