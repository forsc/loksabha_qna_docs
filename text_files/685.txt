                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

533






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Sexual abuse of Women in Jails




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether cases of women inmates claiming of being forced into sex in jails by the authorities have been reported recently;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has decided to conduct the necessary investigation in view of such allegations; and(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b): No such incident has been reported to the Ministry of Home Affairs recently.(c) & (d) : Does not arise in view of above.*****
