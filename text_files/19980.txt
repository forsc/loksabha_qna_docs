                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1471






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Five Star Wards




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the postal department is considering to cover a minimum of 100 households with schemes offered by them in each ward declared by the department as five star ward;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the department has declared many wards in the State of Kerala as five star wards; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) & (b) 	Department of Posts has a Scheme of “Five Star Village” as a part of financial inclusion initiative, to promote various savings and insurance schemes of the Government. A village identified under this Scheme envisages to cover at least 100 households in respect of each of the following schemes:i.	Post Office Savings Account or Recurring Deposit Account or Time Deposit Account or Monthly Income Scheme Account or National Savings Certificate/Kisan Vikas Patra.ii.	Sukanya Samridhi Account/ Public Provident Fund Accountiii.	Funded India Post Payments Bank Account linked with Post Office Savngs Accountiv.	Postal Life Insurance Policy/Rural Postal Life Insurance Policyv.	Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana /Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima YojanaOne star is awarded to the identified village for 100 households covered in respect of each of the above schemes. A village that covers all the five schemes is awarded five stars.In respect of State of Kerala the “Five Star Village” Scheme is being run in respect of wards.(c)  	Yes Sir.(d) 	The details of ‘Five Star Ward’ declared in the State of Kerala is annexed.
