                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1574






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Defective Ammunition to Army




S. Ramalingam














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the fact that around Rs.62.10 crore has been reported as loss on replacement of defective ammunition to Army by Ordnance Factory, Badmal;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has any proposal to constitute a committee to inquire the reasons for such loss;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) & (b):  Yes, Sir.  Comptroller and Auditor General of India through its Report No.15 of 2019 [Audit Para 3.5] has reported loss of Rs.62.10 crore on replacement of defective ammunition to Army by Ordnance Factory Badmal.(c)  & (d):  Yes, Sir.  DGQA carried out preliminary defect investigation in August, 2016.  Further, a Task Force was constituted by DGQA on 07.10.2017.  The reason for loss was attributed to lack of homogeneity in TNT.(e)  Does not arise.*****
