                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5190






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






New Train Services




Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the criteria laid down to introduce new train services on any route;(b) the reasons for not introducing new train services on Bhusaval to CSTM/Pune despite the fact that waiting list remains 300 to 400 every day on all trains from Bhusaval; and(c) the corrective measures taken/proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c): Introduction of trains on Indian Railways,  is dependent  inter-alia on factors like operational feasibility, availability of resources,  traffic demand, etc. Presently, 74 pairs of trains and 28 pairs of trains are already available for the passengers of Bhusaval-Mumbai and Bhusaval-Pune sectors respectively. Introduction of additional trains from Bhusaval to Mumbai/Pune is not feasible, at present, due to operational and resource constraints including terminal constraints at Bhusaval, Pune and Mumbai and path constraints on sections enroute.******
