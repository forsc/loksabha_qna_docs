                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

265






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Works under PMEGP in Maharashtra




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any works are being carried out under the Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) in the country, particularly in naxal-affected tribal dominated Gadchiroli-Chimur parliamentary Constituency and other backward regions of Maharashtra; (b) 	if so, the details thereof as on date; and (c) 	the details of the central funds earmarked and allocated along with its utilisation during each of the last three years as well as the number of beneficiaries, State-wise, as on date in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a) & (b):	Ministry of MSME is implementing a major credit-linked subsidy programme named Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) since 2008-09 with Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) as nodal agency at the national level for generating employment opportunities in the country including naxal-affected tribal dominated Gadchiroli-Chimur Parliamentary Constituency and other backward regions of Maharashtra by setting up of micro-enterprises in the non-farm sector. The performance of PMEGP during the last three years and current year (as on 28.01.2022) in Maharashtra State including naxal-affected tribal dominated Gadchiroli-Chimur Parliamentary Constituency is as under:<pre>(Margin Money:- Rs. in lakh)Year	All Maharashtra	Gadchiroli-Chimur District	No. of Units assisted	Margin Money disbursed	Estimated Employment Generated	No. of Units assisted	Margin Money disbursed	Estimated Employment Generated2018-19	5642	15272.02	45136	56	133.82	4482019-20	4406	11219.47	35248	50	81.54	4002020-21	3104	8844.29	24832	89	120.26	7122021-22 (as on 28.01.2022)	2307	8069.36	18456	19	34.71	152</pre>(c):	State-wise details of the target for Margin Money subsidy disbursement and achievement made with respect to Margin Money disbursed and number of beneficiaries/units assisted during the last three years and current year is given at Annexure.
