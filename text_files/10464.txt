                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3754






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Strike on Launch Pads in POK




Kotagiri Sridhar













                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether it is a fact that Indian army has recently carried out fire assault on POK launch pads in Neelum valley and other three places in Pakistan;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the number of terrorists eliminated in this strike; and(d) the number of such launch pads that exist across the LoC?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) to (d): Based on threat assessment, intelligence inputs, ground situation and operational circumstances, the Indian Army takes appropriate proactive/retaliatory action as required from time to time.*****
