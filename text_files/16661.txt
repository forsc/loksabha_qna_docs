                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

317






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Monitoring Institutions for Domesticated Animals




Behanan Benny














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the number of institutes, organizations under the Ministry that are in existence for monitoring and tending domesticated animals in India; (b) whether the Ministry has undertaken any review of these institutions recently and whether any of these have been scheduled to close, if so, the details thereof; and (c) the details of budgetary allocation for these institutions thereto?






ANSWER




ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)  	The institutions or organisations in existence under the Ministry for development of livestock including poultry are given below along with their numbers. 1.	Central Poultry Development Organizations (4 Nos.),2.	Central Poultry Performance Training Centre (1 No.), 3.	Central Sheep Breeding Farm (1 No.), 4.	Regional Fodder Stations(5 Nos.). 5.	Central Cattle Breeding Farms (7 Nos.)6.	Central Herd Registration Scheme (4 units).7.	Chaudhary Charan Singh National Institute of Animal Health (CCSNIAH)(b) The Government is undertaking a periodical review of all the institutions under the Department and there is no proposal to close any institutes. (c) As per Budget Estimates for the year 2020-21, a total of Rs.101.27 Crores have been allocated for the above institutions/organisations.
