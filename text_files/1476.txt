                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1357






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






CSR Funds towards Social Venture Funds




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the companies have been contemplating to contribute their CSR Funds towards Social Venture Funds (Formed under SEBI guidelines)and if so, the details thereof; and (b) whether the Government permit CSR contribution to such funds and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF FINANCEAND CORPORATE AFFAIRS                                        (Smt. NIRMALA SITHARAMAN)???????? ???????? ????? ?????? 						(??????????????????????)(a) Schedule VII of the Companies Act, 2013, enumerate the CSR activities to be undertaken by the companies and ‘Social Venture Fund’ is not covered under it.(b) Does not arise.********
