                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3523






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Courses in Artificial Intelligence




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to introduce specialization courses in ArtificialIntelligence (AI) and Cyber Security in the National Universities;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has any proposal to promote international collaboration in AI andCyber Security; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’ )(a) & (b): The University Grants Commission has issued instructions to all the Universitieson 16.02.2013 that Cyber Security/Information Security is introduced as a subject in theuniversities/technical institutions at the under-graduate and post-graduate levels.(c) & (d): No, Sir. The Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) has not any suchproposal.                                  ******
