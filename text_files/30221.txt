                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1862






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Targets under PMAY-U




D. Ravikumar














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has achieved its target of providing 2.1 crore houses to poor under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) as announced in 2019 Budget and if so, the details thereof; (b)	the details of PMAY houses sanctioned during the last five years, State-wise; and (c)	the measures taken by the Government to increase the unit cost of PMAY house to meet the escalating cost of building materials?






ANSWER




                                                                   THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                 (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)	: Yes, Sir. Government of India has sanctioned 2.13 crore and 1.14 crore houses under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)- Gramin & Urban respectively. (b)	: The details of houses sanctioned under PMAY- Gramin & Urban during the last five years, State/UT-wise are at Annexure-I & II respectively.(c)	: Government of India is sanctioning the proposals based on the Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) along with project cost prepared by the implementing agencies of States/UTs. Government of India is providing its fixed share as Central Assistance from ?1.20 lakh to ?1.30 lakh under PMAY-G and ?1.0 lakh under In-Situ Slum Redevelopment (ISSR), ?1.5 lakh for Affordable Housing in Partnership (AHP) & Beneficiary Led Individual Construction or Enhancement (BLC) verticals of PMAY-U. Under Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS) vertical of PMAY-U, an interest subsidy at the rate of 6.5% which amounts upto ? 2.67 lakh per house is provided for beneficiaries of Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) and Lower Income Group (LIG) category. The remaining cost of the house as per DPR is shared by States/UTs/ULBs/Beneficiaries.                                 *****
