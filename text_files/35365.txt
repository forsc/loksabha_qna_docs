                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2497






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






New Board Gauge Line in Kevadia




Mansukhbhai Dhanjibhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to lay new broad gauge line from Rajpipla to Statue of Unity Kevadia, Gujarat; and (b) if so, the details thereof and the time by which this project is likely to be started?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) and (b):  At present, there is no such proposal to extend Ankleshwar-Rajpipla line upto Statue of unity, Kevadia. However, Broad Gauge rail connectivity to Statue of unity, Kevadia has already been provided by Chandod-Kevadia new line. *****
