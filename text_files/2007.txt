                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1854






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Investment under Make in India




 Dharambir Singh













                                                    Kanakmal Katara
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the collaboration of the Government with other countries in various sectors under ‘Make in India’ initiative from the year 2014 till date;(b) 	whether foreign companies are providing assistance in production/ information regarding technology/raw material/action plan under Make in India programme;(c) 	if so, the details of various services provided by the said foreign companies; (d) 	whether the Government has estimated the total amount of investment made under Make in India during the last five years i.e. from 2014 to 2019;(e) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(f) 	the media-wise details of the advertising expenditure on Make in India initiative till date?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c):	Activities under Make in India initiative are being undertaken by several Central and State Governments departments from time to time. Programme specific data of such activities and details of foreign companies are not centrally maintained. (d) & (e):	Total Foreign Direct Investment Equity inflows received since the launch of Make in India may be seen below <pre>  Sl No	Year	FDI (in US$ million)1	2014-15 Oct-Mar	15,045.892	2015-16	40,000.983	2016-17	43,478.274	2017-18	44,856.755	2018-19	44,366.03 	Total	187,747.93</pre> However, no data regarding total investment under Make in India is maintained centrally (f):	Promotion activities under Make in India initiative are being undertaken by several Central and State Governments departments from time to time. Details regarding these are not maintained centrally.  ******
