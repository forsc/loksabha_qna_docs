                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4187






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Employment under PMEGP




Sisir Kumar Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the target of generating employment under the Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) under Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) is still far behind since 2016 and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	the details of funds released to new entrepreneurs from Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE) since 2016; and (c) 	the targets and achievements made by Government to generate employment and funding to business since 2016 under the programme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a)&(c):	No, Sir.
