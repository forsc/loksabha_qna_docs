                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1378






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Information and Communication Technology in Education




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the initiatives taken by the Government to achieve inclusive education ;(b) the objectives set for each initiative including use of Information and CommunicationTechnology (ICT) in education;(c) Whether the Government has set any time frame to achieve the objectives set for eachinitiative; and(d) if so, the details thereof ?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): The focus of Samagra Shiksha is on providing inclusive education to children withdisability wherein, children regardless of their abilities/disabilities participate and learntogether in the same class, thus creating an enabling educational environment for all students.Under Samagra Shiksha, initiative is taken by the Government to achieve Inclusive Educationby providing support to CwSN under Inclusive Education component through:<pre>(i) Student oriented Component (SOC)(ii) Resource Support</pre>(b): Various initiatives have been taken with the objective of providing quality education toCwSN in the regular schools which includes activities under SOC such as :<pre>(i) Provision of identification & assessment camps to provide diagnostic support.(ii) Provision of aids and appliances for enhancing mobility of CwSN.(iii) Provision of Teaching Learning Materials (TLMs) for addressing education needs ofCwSN.(iv) Provision of Information Communication Technology (ICT) resources / solutionssuch as JAWS, SAFA, other technological solution in resource rooms, schools etc.(v) Provision of teaching aids, assistive devices, equipments etc for addressing diverseneeds of CwSN. In service training of special educators and general teachers toaddress scholastic and co-scholastic needs of CwSN.(vi) Provision of sports events, escorts allowances, Transportation allowances to ensureenrolment and retention of CwSN.(vii) As Samagra Shiksha has special focus on girls with special needs therefore stipend ofRs. 2000/- for 10 months is provided to girls with special needs through DBT (DirectBenefit Transfer).Various initiatives under Resource support include provision of special educator and theirtraining to address the scholastic and co-scholastic needs of CwSN through the support ofgeneral teachers, special educators and resource persons. Apart from the above, CwSN areprovided therapy support at the block level resource room by trained therapists. Further, CwSNwith severe impairment requiring individualized attention are provided home based educationthrough special educators. Additionally, there is a provision of barrier free access, ramps anddisabled friendly toilets in schools.</pre>(c): No time frame is feasible as Inclusive Education component is part of an ongoingscheme.(d): Question does not arise.
