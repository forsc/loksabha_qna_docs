                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state: (a) the total number of Aadhaar Seva Kendras (ASKs) currently in operation in the country, State/UTwise; (b) the kind of services provided by these ASKs; (c) the steps taken by the Government to expand the services offered by these centres; (d) whether the Government proposes to provide the services of updating the Aadhaar card for free; and (e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        a) to (c): As on date, more than 55,000 Aadhaar Enrolment/Update Centres are functional across the country, operated by Banks, Post Office, State Governments, Common Service Centres (CSCs) as well as ASKs operated by UIDAI (State/UT wise details are annexed). Presently, the services such as enrolment for Aadhaar, demographic update, biometric update, find Aadhaar, download & print out of e-Aadhaar services are available at the Aadhaar Enrolment/Update centres and the Aadhaar Seva Kendras. (d) and (e):  Mandatory Biometric Update on attaining the age of 5 years and 15 years is free for residents. For other updates, based on resident’s requirement, Registrars are permitted to collect a nominal fee of Rs.50/- for demographic update and Rs. 100/- for biometric update (with or without demographic update) respectively to cover the cost incurred by the Registrars, in providing such services. ********Annexure List of Active Enrolment CentresS.
