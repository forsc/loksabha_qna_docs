                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1534






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






GI Products




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle













                                                    Umesh G. Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the products under Geographical Indication (GI) in Karnataka and the steps taken by the Government to promote and increase the sales of GI products; and(b) 	whether it is a fact that authorized user registration for different GI products has been very less and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a):     	As on 26th July, 2021, 46 products have been registered under the provisions of the Geographical Indications of Goods (Registration & Protection) Act, 1999 from the State of Karnataka, details of which are given at Annexure-A1. The Government has undertaken several steps for promotion of Indian products registered as Geographical Indications (GIs). These, inter-alia, include  participation in trade fairs and other events to promote and create awareness on GIs; and increase the sales of products, promotion of GIs through social media, involving State Governments and Union Territory Administration and other relevant organisations for facilitation of GI producers.(b): 	As on 26th July, 2021, the GI Registry, Chennai has registered 4,885 GI Authorized Users. In order to spread awareness for registration of GI Authorized Users, awareness/ sensitization programmes are conducted for concerned stakeholders at various places in the country. These programmes are aimed at acquainting GI stakeholders about importance and benefit of GI registration and GI Authorised User registration. Further, in order to increase the filing of GI Authorised User registration the Statutory fee for filing GI Authorised User Application has been reduced from Rs. 500/- to Rs.10/-. *****
