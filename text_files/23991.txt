                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4875






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Green Highways Policy




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total length of highways covered under the Green Highways Policy in the country; (b) the details of the projects sanctioned along with those which have been completed under the said policy; (c) the progress of plantation under the said policy; and (d) the measures being taken by the Government to ensure plantation of indigenous, non-invasive and non-oranamental species of trees?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) The Green Highways (Plantation, Transplantation, Beautification & Maintenance) Policy, 2015 covers all the National Highways of the country with the aim to promote greening of corridors. Under the policy, 199.78 lakh plants have been planted across 728 NH projects having length of 42,969 kms.(d) The local indigenous, non-invasive shrubs and ornamental as well as non-ornamental shade bearing trees suitable for different agro climatic zones are planted as per the Indian Road Congress’ guideline (IRC SP:21-2009).*****
