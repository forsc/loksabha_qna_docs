                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2775






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






ROB in Haryana




Arvind Kumar Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether there is any plan for extension of railway line from Farukh Nagar to Jhajjar and from Jhajjar to Jhadli in Haryana;(b)  if so, whether there is any plan of constructing railway flyover at the double railway crossing in Jhadli;(c)   if so, the details thereof; and (d) if not, the steps proposed to be taken by the Ministry in this  regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) The survey for Farukh Nagar to Charkhi Dadri via Jhajjar  (73 km) new rail line was carried out in 2016.      As per survey report, the cost assessed was  1529.63 crore, with Rate of Return (ROR) of (-)5.01%.    Project being financially unremunerative, it could not be taken forward.  However, feasibility study of Farukh Nagar –Jhajjar –Charkhi Dadri has been taken up by Haryana Rail Infrastructure Development Corporation (HRIDC), Joint Venture (JV) Company of Government of Haryana.Survey for new line between Dadri and Jhadli via Jhajjar (82 km) was carried out in 2015.  As per survey report, the cost assessed was  1528.75 crore, with Rate of Return (ROR) of (-)4.37%.    Project being financially unremunerative, it could not be taken forward. (b) to (d)   There is plan of  Road Over Bridge (ROB) at the double railway crossing in Jhadli.   Road Over Bridge (ROB) at level crossing No. 27 near Jhadli was sanctioned vide Pink Book 2018-19.  This Road Over Bridge (ROB) is to be constructed on cost sharing basis.  Public Works Department /Haryana has not submitted its General Arrangement Drawing (GAD) to Railway so far.*****
