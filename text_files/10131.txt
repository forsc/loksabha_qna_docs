                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3675






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Additional Battalions of CAPFs




Ramesh Chandra Majhi













                                                    Manjulata Mandal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the Government of Odisha has requested the Union Government for induction of two additional Battalion of Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs) in the Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected areas of the State; and (b) if so, the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):	The Government of Odisha has requested for two additional battalions of CAPF for deployment in LWE affected districts. At present, 08 battalions of CRPF, 08 battalions of BSF and 01 battalion of CoBRA are deployed in Odisha for assisting the State Police in anti-LWE operations. The deployment of CAPFs in  LWE affected States is a dynamic process based on the situation, availability of forces and other ground realities.*******
