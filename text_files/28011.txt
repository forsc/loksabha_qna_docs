                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3658






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Warehouse and Godown in Jharkhand




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of warehouses and godowns constructed under Public Private Partnership during the last three years, State-wise, including Jharkhand; 	(b) whether the Government plans to develop any new warehouses/godowns in Jharkhand in the years to come; (c) 	if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	if not, the reason therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	State-wise details of Number of warehouses and godowns constructed by Food Corporation India (FCI) under Public Private Partnership during the last three years including Jharkhand are Annexed.(b):	Yes, Sir.(c): In Jharkhand currently three projects are planned by FCI under Central Sector Scheme (2017-22). The location and capacity of godown are as follows: -S.No.	Location	Storage Capacity ( in MT)1.	Itkhori/(Chatra)	10,0002.	Poreiyahat /(Godda)	10,0003.	Dumka Ph-II	15,000      	In addition, construction of godown under PEG scheme on the following locations under Jharkhand region has also been planned by FCI:Sl no.
