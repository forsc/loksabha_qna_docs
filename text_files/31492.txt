                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2656






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund (AHIDF)




Midhun Reddy













                                                    Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)     the details of funds sanctioned under Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund (AHIDF) to incentivise private investment; andb)    Whether the Government has set up plants for exporting ‘niche’ products under AHIDF?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER                       THE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a)  Details of funds sanctioned under Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund till 9th December, 2021 is as under:Category	No. of Projects	Total Project Cost( Rs.in cr.)	Term Loan Sanctioned(Rs. in cr)Dairy Processing and Value Addition	29	950.04	701.33Meat Processing and Value Addition	6	231.80	108.81Animal Feed Plant	36	588.26	404.97Total	71	1770.10	1215.11 (b)  The plants established under AHIDF can leverage benefit for export of their products to niche market.*****
