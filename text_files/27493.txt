                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3071






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Development Project near National Zoo Park




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Central Zoo Authority has received a proposal from National Capital Region Transport Corporation seeking a permission and issuance of No Objection Certificate for development of a Metro line under the area of the National Zoological Park connecting Meerut-Alwar stretch;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the steps the Government has initiated to save the wildlife in the zoo including mammals like Spotted and Swamp Deer, Black Buck, Rhino and Tigers and various species of Reptiles and Birds/Aviary and seasonal migrating species of birds to the National Zoological Park as these are very sensible and can sense the vibrations?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) and (b) The proposal has been received by the National Zoological Park. However, No Objection Certificate has not been issued by the Central Zoo Authority. (c) In view of (a) and (b), the question does not arise. ***
