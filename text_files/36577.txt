                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2764






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Tribal University




Guman Singh Damor














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the norms fixed for establishing Tribal University along with the details thereof; (b) the total number of such Tribal University in the country at present; (c) the number of tribal students studying in these Universities and the subjects which are being taught there, University-wise; (d) whether Ph.D course is available in these Tribal Universities; (e) if so, the details thereof along with the subjects in which Ph.D is offered, University-wise; and (f) the details of various facilities being made available by the University for such Ph.D students?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) to (f) As far as Central Government is concerned, it has established two Central Universities in predominantly tribal areas. Apart from these, there are two State Tribal Universities functioning in the country. The details of number of tribal students studying in these universities, subjects being taught, availability of Ph.D course along with subjects in which Ph.D is offered and various facilities being made available by the Tribal Universities are available at https://www.education.gov.in/en/parl_ques.***
