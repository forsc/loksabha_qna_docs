                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3520






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






New IIMs/NITs




S. Muniswamy













                                                    L.S. Tejasvi Surya
                                                








                                                    Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Krupal Balaji Tumane
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has identified the places to start new NITs, IIMsin the country including State of Karnataka along with the criteria forselecting these areas;(b) the funds allocated and utilized for this purpose;(c) whether the Government has any mechanism to check on the quality ofeducation in technical and non-technical Institutions and if so, the detailsthereof; and(d) whether the Government has received any requests from States forsetting up new NITs/IIMs and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): Apart from thirty one National Institutes of Technology (NITs) andtwenty Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs) already functioning in various partsof the country, including an NIT and IIM in Karnataka, there is no proposal forsetting up of any new NIT or IIM.(c): The Government has launched several initiatives viz. National InstitutionalRanking Framework (NIRF), Impacting Research Innovation & Technology(IMPRINT), Uchchtar Avishkar Yojna (UAY), Global Initiative of AcademicNetworks (GIAN) and Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) etc., whichhave in-built mechanisms to check and provide for qualitative development ofeducation in the country. All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) hasprescribed norms and standards for grant of approval to the new Institutions andexisting Institutions. The University Grants Commission undertakes maintenanceof standards in teaching, research and quality assurance in Universities, Deemedto be Universities and Colleges through framing and notifying regulations,schemes and disbursing grants to the eligible Institutions.(d): Requests have been received from various quarters for setting up of IIMsin States of Telangana, Puducherry and Karnataka.
