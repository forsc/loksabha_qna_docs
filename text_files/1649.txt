                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1547






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Hostels in Navodaya Vidyalayas




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the plan of Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan to provide hostel facility ineach Kendriya Vidyalaya is under consideration of the Government;(b) if so, the time by which it is likely to be implemented; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) No such proposals are under consideration of the Government at present.(b) Does not arise.(c) Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) are day schools which have been opened primarilyto cater to the educational needs of the wards of transferable Central GovernmentEmployees including Defence personnel on receipt of the proposal in the prescribedproforma from a Ministries / Departments of the Government of India / StateGovernments / Union Territories Administrations and committing thereby availabilityof requisite resources for setting up a new KV as well as on availability of necessarysanction of the Government. However, hostel facilities are available in 9 KVs withlimited number of seats.*****
