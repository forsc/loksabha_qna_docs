Rashtriya Vayoshri Yojana




Nalin Kumar Kateel














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.275 TO BE ANSWERED ON 20.07.2021RASHTRIYA VAYOSHRI YOJANA 275. SHRI NALIN KUMAR KATEEL: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) whether  Rashtriya  Vayoshri  Yojana (RVY) is funded from the Senior Citizens‘ Welfare Fund and if so, the details thereof; (b) the main objectives of the RVY; (c) the details of the implementing Agency and financial outlay for the Scheme during the last three years; (d) the total number of beneficiaries under RVY during the Financial Year 2020-21, State-wise; and (e) the number of camps organised across the country under the scheme since its inception, Statewise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a):  Yes sir. The Scheme of Rashtriya Vayoshri Yojana (RVY) is funded from the Senior Citizens’ Welfare Fund (SCWF), established vide Senior Citizens’ Welfare Fund Rules, 2016 notified on 18.03.2016.(b):  The objective of the scheme is to provide the Senior Citizen, suffering from any of the age related disability/infirmity, with assisted living devices which can restore near normalcy in their bodily functions, overcoming the disability/infirmity manifested such as low vision, hearing impairment, loss of teeth and loco-motor disabilities.(c):  The Scheme is implemented through the ‘Artificial Limbs Manufacturing Corporation (ALIMCO)’, (a Public Sector Undertaking under the Ministry) as the sole implementing Agency.  The details of the funds released to ALIMCO for implementing the Scheme of RVY, till date are as under:(i)  F.Y. 2016-17: Rs. 16 crore(ii) F.Y. 2017-18: Rs. 1.50 crore(iii) F.Y. 2018-19: Rs. 106.51 crore(iv) F.Y. 2019-20: NiL (due to to availability of sufficient funds with ALIMCO) (v) F.Y. 2020-21: Rs. 26.50 crore(d): List of the total number of beneficiaries under RVY during the F.Y. 2020-21, State-wise is enclosed at Annexure-I.
