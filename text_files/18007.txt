                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1732






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Delay in Recruitment Process




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the reason for delay in issuing appointment letters for the Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP) and Technician examination 2018;(b)	the steps proposed by the Government to address this delay;(c)	the date of release of notification of examination and vacancies released for the RRB Group D 2020 and RRB NTPC 2019;(d)	whether the examinations for RRB Group D 2020 and RRB NTPC 2019 have not been conducted as yet and if so, the reasons therefor;(e)	the reasons for not declaring the results of the SSC CGL 2018 examination; and(f)	the date by which the Government intends to fill the vacancies for RRB ALP and Technician 2018, RRB Group D 2020, RRB NTPC 2019,  SSC CGL 2018 ?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (f) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
