                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2774






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Production of Copper




Shivkumar Chanabasappa Udasi













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether India has become the net importer of copper during the last three years;(b)	if so, the reasons for reduction in the production of copper along with the total production of copper during the last five years; and(c)	the steps taken by the Government to re-start the closed copper factories at the earliest?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a): The annual import and export of refined copper (in quantity terms) and trade balance from FY 2017-18 onwards are given below:(Quantity in thousand tonne)Year	Export	Import	Trade Balance (Export minus Import)2017-18	380.655	47.215	(+) 333.4402018-19	49.650	95.713	(-) 46.0632019-20	47.181	154.901	(-) 107.7202020-21	125.825	156.724	(-) 30.899Source: Department of CommerceAs per the table above, though India has been a net importer of refined copper during the last three years, volume of net import has decreased in 2020-21 after it had increased in 2019-20. (b) & (c): The closure in May 2018 of the Smelter plant at Thoothukudi, Tamil Nadu of M/s Sterlite Copper (a Unit of Vedanta Ltd), which has an annual production capacity of four lakh tonne, has affected the domestic production of refined copper. The matter relating to re-start of the Smelter plant of M/s Sterlite Copper is sub judice. The total production of refined copper in the country during the last five years is as under:Year	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21Refined copper production (in thousand tonne)	798.712	842.961	457.245	408.398	363.638*****
