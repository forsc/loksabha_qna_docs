                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1309






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






Kishore Vaigyanic Protsahan Yojana




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana (KVPY), a national programme of fellowship in basic science is being conducted in regional languages on date;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, whether the Government is considering for conducting the same in all scheduled languages in the near future; and(d)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY AND EARTH SCIENCES(DR. JITENDRA SINGH)??????? ?? ???????????? ??? ?????? ??????? ???????? ??  ????? ?????? (???????? ??????)  (??. ???????? ????)(a) & (b):	No Sir. Presently, the Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana (KVPY), a national programme of fellowship in basic sciences is being conducted in Hindi and English languages.(c)  & (d):	KVPY Aptitude test, being a test for science stream students involving scientific terminologies, Government is exploring the possibilities to conduct it in as many scheduled languages as possible, including finding alternate arrangements which will have lesser stress on students and their parents. *****
