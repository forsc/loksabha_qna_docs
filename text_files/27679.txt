                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3285






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Criteria fixed for LPG Subsidy




C.N. Annadurai













                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                








                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                








                                                    Gautham Sigamani Pon
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the criteria fixed by the Government to avail LPG subsidy by consumers;(b) whether the Indian Oil Corporation (IOC)/OMCs have stopped giving LPG subsidy toconsumers and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(c) whether the Government has taken any action against IOC and other oil marketing companiesand if so, the details thereof;(d) the main reasons for irregularities in transfer of LPG subsidy along with the measurestaken by the Government to address the said issue; and(e) the corrective steps taken by the Government for release of the subsidy to LPG consumersin a time-bound manner?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (e): The prices of petroleum products in the country are linked to the price ofrespective products in the international market. However, the Government continues to modulatethe effective price to consumer for Subsidized Domestic LPG. The subsidy on the productincreases/ decreases with the increase/ decrease in the product price in international market anddecision of Government on subsidy. The whole cycle of settlement of subsidy in bank account ofcustomer normally takes around 2-3 working days. The subsidy is paid to consumers by OilMarketing Companies (OMCs) and the same is reimbursed by Government to OMCs on monthlybasis.x-x-x-x-x
