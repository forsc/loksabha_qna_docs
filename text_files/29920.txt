                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1576






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






PEMSR Offences




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of convictions under the Prohibition of Employment as Manual Scavengers and their Rehabilitation Act, 2013, year-wise;(b) the reasons for the non-inclusion of PEMSR offences as a separate category in National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) reports;(c) whether the Government plans to rectify this through publication of data regarding manual scavenging in the Annual Reports of NCRB; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI AJAY KUMAR MISHRA)(a) to (d):	The National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) collects and compiles crime statistics from the States/ Union Territories as per defined protocol and format, and publishes  it in its annual publication ‘Crime in India’. These pertain to the Indian Penal Code and the Special and Local Laws (SLL). Crime data on the Prohibition of Employment as Manual Scavengers and their Rehabilitation Act, 2013, is included in the sub-category “Other SLL crimes”. *****
