                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1217






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Pashu Seva App




Gautam Gambhir














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of number of downloads of the “Pashu Seva App” been done since its launch;(b)	whether the Pashu Seva app is being made available in regional languages; and(c)	If so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a) In order to provide digital platform to farmers and livestock keepers Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying has created e-Pashuhaat portal and as on date 91,354 Organizations and 87,021 Artificial Insemination Technicians registered with the portal. The e-Pashu Haat portal has also been linked with UMANG portal and e-Pashu Haat portal is now available with 4 crore users of Umang portal. Further, e- Gopala app a digital platform for farmers in the country for managing livestock including buying and selling of disease free germplasm has been launched by Hon’ble Prime Minister on 10th September 2020 and as on 5th February 2021, total users registered with e-Gopala App is 55,517.(b) and ( c) Yes, Sir. The e-GOPALA app has been made available in 6 different regional languages namely Hindi, Gujarati, Marathi, Odia, Telugu and Malayalam and also in English.
