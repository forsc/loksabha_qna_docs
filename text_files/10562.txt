                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4124






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






USTTAD Scheme




Midhun Reddy













                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has recently empanelled organizations to undertake training programmes for beneficiaries under the USTTAD scheme and if so, the details thereof;(b) the total amount of budget allocated and utilized for the USTTAD scheme till date for the last two financial years; and(c)  the amount allocated to Andhra Pradesh and the total number of organizations empanelled from Andhra Pradesh for providing such training programmes under the USTTAD scheme during the last two years? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)  Yes Sir. This Ministry has empanelled 84 organizations (Project Implementing Agencies) during 2018-19 to undertake training programmes for beneficiaries under the USTTAD Scheme.  The list of these 84 Project Implementing Agencies is available on the website of the Ministry, i.e., www.minorityaffairs.gov.in/.  (b)  The details of the total amount of budget allocated and utilized for the USTTAD scheme during the last two financial years is as follows:S.No.	Year	Financial 		BE	Achievement1.	2017-18	22.00	21.802.	2018-19	30.00	31.26 (c)  No State-wise funds and/or targets are allocated/fixed under the USTTAD scheme.  05 organizations from Andhra Pradesh have been empanelled for providing training under the USTTAD Scheme during last two years.*****
