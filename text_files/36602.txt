                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3625






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Bagaha-Madanpur-Paniyavaha National Highway




Sunil Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) Whether the Government is aware that the commuters have to face difficulties in movement due to non-construction of National Highway (NH)-727 from Bagaha to Paniyavaha via Madanpur;(b) If so, whether the Government has formulated any proposal for construction of this NH; and (c) If so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) (b) & (c) Yes, Sir. The widening of existing NH from Km 97 to Km 104.200 (Madanpur More) and from Km 104.200 (Madanpur More) to Km 110 (U.P. Border) were sanctioned in 2 packages. However, bituminous renewal work over existing surface has been done from Km 97 to Km 104.200, while bituminous renewal work from Km 104.200 (Madanpur More) to Km 110 (U.P. Border) could not be done as Forest Department did not allow work in Valmiki wildlife sanctuary & Tiger Reserve.This Ministry has taken up DPR work for developing an alternative alignment of above said stretch.*****
