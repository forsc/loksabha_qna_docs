                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2145






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Durgapur Steel Plant




Subhas Sarkar














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the quantum of steel produced by the Durgapur Steel Plant (DSP) during the last three years; and whether DSP is exporting its products to other countries;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the details of revenue being generated by the Government every year by this export; and(c)	the details of production by Alloy Steel Plant and quantum of sales during the last three years including the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)&(b): Details of crude steel production, export and revenue generated by export from Durgapur Steel Plant (DSP) during the last three years are as following:-(Production and export in ’000 Tonne)Financial Year	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20Crude steel production	2042	2220	2194Quantity exported	229.26	291.60	341.92Revenue from export(Rs. in Crore)	707.62	1033.56	1000.74(c)	The details of crude steel production and sales by Alloy Steel Plant (ASP) for the last three years including the current year (upto February, 2021) are as following:-(Unit:’000 Tonne)Financial Year	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21(Apr-Feb’21)Crude steel production	96	101	104	50Sales	78.89	115.93	97.73	64.86********
