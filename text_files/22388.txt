                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3581






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Registered Migrant Workers




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Indian migrant workers who registered to 	work in the Gulf countries;(b)	whether the Government has reduced the minimum referral wages for employment in the Gulf countries and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government will consider revising this reduction given the general economic distress caused due to Covid-19 and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the number of labour complaints received by embassies in Gulf countries in the last three years?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) 	The number of Indian migrant workers who registered on eMigrate to work in the Gulf countries during last five years is follows:-<pre>S.No.Name of Country201620172018201920201.Kuwait7238456380576134571281072.Qatar3061924759344713181089073.Oman6323653332360372839272064.Saudi Arabia1653557861172399161103443165.United Arab Emirates16371614996211205976112178916.Bahrain1196411516914299974175</pre>(b) & (c)	The revised Minimum Referral Wage (MRW) in Bahrain, Oman, UAE and Qatar has been fixed at USD 200. MRW in Saudi Arabia has been fixed at USD 324 and MRW in Kuwait has been fixed at USD 245 for work visa holders and USD 196 for Domestic Sector Workers.At present, there is no proposal for further revision of the MRW, which was reviewed recently, inter-alia, in view of economic conditions, due to Covid-19.(d)	The number of labour complaints received by embassies in Gulf countries in the last three years is as follows:-<pre>S.No.Name of Country2018201920201.Kuwait3287528637722.Qatar3244289619243.Oman3594298411504.Saudi Arabia8271797319455.United Arab Emirates2153528610086.Bahrain734836674</pre>***
