                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

244






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






New Railway Projects




Diya Kumari













                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering to launch any new railway projects to cater to the increasing passenger footfall and to connect the areas not covered so far under railway network;(b)  if so, the details of railway projects under consideration along with the ongoing projects in various States of the country including Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar and Maharashtra, State/UT and zone-wise and if not, the plans of the Government to take up such projects in the near future;(c) the State-wise status of the projects announced during the last three years along with the number of projects out of them on which work is going on and the number of projects completed so far, State/UT-wise;(d) the amount of funds spent so far in this regard along with the estimated amount likely to be spent on these projects, State/UT-wise; and(e) the details of the operation of new trains announced during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	 to (e):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.
