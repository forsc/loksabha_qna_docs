                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1080






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Mobile Science Exhibition Van




Bhagwanth Khuba














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether it is a fact that the Government is going to start a new institute which will offer the MA, PhD, diploma and certification courses in History of art, Conservation, Musicology etc; if so, the details thereof;(b)  whether it is a fact that the Government provides Mobile Science Exhibition van to the States;(c)   if so, the details thereof;(d)  the number of Mobile Science Exhibition vans that were allotted during the last 5 years, State and UT-wise details thereof; and(e)  whether the Government has any plan to give Mobile Science Exhibition Van to all the states in the coming days if so, the details thereof and if not, the reason thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) :  	It was announced in Union budget 2020-21 that an Indian Institute of Heritage (IIH) will be established under Ministry of Culture. The proposed Indian Institute of Heritage (IIH) would conduct various courses/programs in the fields of Archaeology, Conservation, Archives, Museology, Curation, Epigraphy, Manuscriptology and allied fields. As per the information received from Ministry of Culture, as on 1st February 2021, the administrative structure of the Institute is being finalised. (b) and (c) : 	Ministry of Culture has further informed that Government of India does not provide any Mobile Science Exhibition Van to the States. Rather National Council of Science Museums, working under the aegis of Ministry of Culture, Government of India operates 48 Mobile Science Exhibition (MSE) buses all over the country, from its units spread across India, which includes 25 MSE buses launched during 2019-2020 for aspirational districts. During 2019-2020, these buses have covered 2042 sites in various States of which 25 MSE buses have coved 1161 sites in 45 Aspirational districts before the programmes were temporarily suspended in March, 2020 due to the COVID-19, pandemic.(d) and (e) : As mentioned above National Council of Science Museums (NCSM) does not allot any Mobile Science Exhibition (MSE) bus to any State/UT. The MSE buses are directly operated by NCSM.
