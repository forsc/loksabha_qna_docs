                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4631






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Foreign Cooperation and Funding for Projects




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Government has conducted any study on various new and renewable energy projects started with foreign cooperation and funding during the last three years and the current year; (b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government proposes to introduce state-of-the-art technology with a view to exploit new and renewable energy sources in the country;(d) if so, the details thereof; (e)  the details of new and renewable energy projects implemented in the country during the said period, State/UT-wise; and(f) the quantum of funds provided for such projects, year and State-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) & (b) The Ministry of  New and Renewable Energy has not conducted any study on new and renewable projects started with foreign cooperation and funding during the last three years and the current year. However, under Technical Assistance programmes with various bilateral/ multilateral agencies, such projects are reviewed by Steering Committees/Joint Working Groups. (c) to (f): The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy supports a scheme named “Renewable Energy Research and Technology Development Programme” through various research institutions and industry to enable indigenous technology development and manufacturing for wide spread applications of new and renewable energy, including new battery technologies, in an efficient and cost effective manner across the country.The Ministry encourages research and technology development proposals in collaboration with the industry and provides up to 100% financial support to Government/non-profit research organizations and up to 50-70% to Industry, Start-ups, Private Institutes, Entrepreneurs and Manufacturing units for such proposals.The details of Research and Development projects executed during the last three years, State/UT-wise, along with quantum of funds sanctioned as well as released till date are attached as Annexure I.*****
