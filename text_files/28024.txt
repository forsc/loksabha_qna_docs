                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3596






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Data on Number of Man Days Provided under MGNREGS




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is having any data on the number of man days provided under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS);(b) 	if so, the details of man day provided and expenditure incurred during each year of the last four years including current year, year-wise/State-wise/category-wise; and(c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) to (c): 	Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven wage employment programme which provide for the enhancement of livelihood security of the households in rural areas of the country by providing at least one hundred days of guaranteed wage employment in every financial year to every household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work. State/UT-wise and category-wise detail of persondays generated and expenditure incurred by the States/UTs under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS during the last four financial years and current financial year 2021-22 (as on 06.08.2021) is given at Annexure.********
