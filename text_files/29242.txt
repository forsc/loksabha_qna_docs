                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

595






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






ROB in Assam




Kripanath Mallah














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that there is a strong public demand for construction of Rail Over Bridge (ROB) at Badarurghat and Kanisail near Karimganj town in Assam for uninterrupted Road communication and for safety of the road users; and  (b)	if so, whether the Government is going to take up the matter with the Union Surface Transport Ministry and Government of Assam for formulating a proposal for early materialization of the public demand?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) & (b): No, Sir.  There is no place known in this context as Badarurghat.  However, it may be Badarpurghat.  Level Crossing (LC) No. 1-BS at km. 2/9-3/0 near Badarpurghat and LC No. 1-KM at km. 189/7-8 at Kanisail near Karimganj Town, fall on National Highway (NH). As per the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) signed between Ministry of Railway & MoRTH/ NHAI dated 10.11.2014, all LCs on NH corridors are to be replaced by ROBs/RUBs by Ministry of Road Transport and Highways (MORTH)/ National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) at their cost on single entity basis.             During last coordination meeting with MoRTH/NHAI/NHIDCL, list of LCs on NH corridors (including these two LCs) has been given to them for planning ROBs. *****
