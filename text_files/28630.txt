                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

501






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






IPR Awareness




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether steps have been taken by the Ministry to encourage Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) awareness among tribals, cultural artists, tribal entrepreneurs in NER;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Ministry has taken any action on setting up of an IP Museum as per the National IPR Policy 2016 and if so, the details thereof;(d) 	whether the Ministry has released awareness campaigns, audio/visual/print material in tribal languages of NER; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) & (b):  	Focusing on the Northeast region, the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) through its professional body, Cell for IPR Promotion and Management has conducted various IPR awareness events. The list of events conducted is as below:<PRE>Schools/Colleges	 DATEVivekananda Kendra Vidyalaya, Roing, Arunachal Pradesh	January, 2020Department of Sociology, St Joseph’s college (Autonomous), Jakhama, Nagaland “Intellectual property rights for academic and research visibility”	22nd - 23rd October 2021RoadshowsGuwahati, Assam	17th June 2016Imphal, Manipur	21st June 2016DPIIT in collaboration with MSMEOverview of IPR, Assam	3rd Mar. 2020Introduction to IPR, MSME DI Guwahati	26th Mar. 2021Assam, Guwahati	26th Feb 2019 Assam, Jorhat	6th Mar 2019Guwahati , Assam	22nd Oct 2019Guwahati, Assam	22nd Nov 2019 </PRE>As an initiative to promote GIs of different regions of India, CIPAM-DPIIT has launched a video on Muga Silk of Assam, one of the registered GI of North East. CII in association with DPIIT organised a GI Festival of India, a mega virtual exhibition and conference from 9th Jan 2021 to 8th February 2021. Six GI Exhibitors from the various states of North East actively participated in this virtual exhibition and conference and promoted their GIs. These exhibitors included MUGA SILK (ASSAM), JOHA RICE (ASSAM), KAJI NEMU (Assam), CHOKUWA RICE (ASSAM), CHAKSHESANG SHAWL (Nagaland), IDU MISHMI TEXTILES (Arunachal Pradesh). This exhibition helped connect consumers and producers and show case & promote the registered GIs of different regions of the country with an opportunity to improve livelihood and economy of rural community.(c) to (e):  	No such steps have been taken at present******
