                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2598






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Survey of Socio-religious Bifurcation




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has conducted any survey/study to identify the number of people involved in the unorganised sector in India with Socio-religious Bifurcation;(b)  if so, the details thereof; (c) if not, whether there is any proposal to do so in view of the observations made by different Commissions/Committees on the issue; and (d) if so, the distinctive features of the unorganised sector that could be identified from such study/survey?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION; MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF CORPORATE AFFAIRS (RAO INDERJIT SINGH) (a) to (d): This Ministry has not conducted any survey/study to identify the number of people involved in the unorganized sector in India with socio-religious bifurcation. However, NSO, MoSPI has conducted survey on ‘Unincorporated Non-Agricultural Enterprises (Excluding Construction)’ during July, 2015 - June, 2016 (NSS 73rd round). In the survey, inter-alia information was collected on the social group of the owner or the major partner of the enterprise. The per 1000 distribution of enterprises by social group of owner / major partner for each State/UT (only for proprietary and partnership enterprises), is given in Annexure.                                                *****
