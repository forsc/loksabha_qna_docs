                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

767






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Broadband Service Ranking




Jagdambika Pal 














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Indian Telecom Companies ranks low in rolling out broadband services in the Asia-Pacific region;(b) 	 if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and(c)  	the corrective measures taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (c)	As per the TRAI report of March 2019, India has 544 million mobile broadband connections and 18 million fixed line broadband connections.  As per latest International Telecommunication Union (ITU) data, amongst all countries of the Asia Pacific region:i.	India has second highest number of mobile broadband connectionsii.	During 2017 India added third highest number of mobile broadband connections.
