                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2375






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Adoption of Villages under SAGY




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether more than half of the schemes have not been completed in the villages adopted by the Members of Parliament under Sansad Adarsh GramYojana (SAGY) during the period from 2014 to 2019 and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the interest of the Members of Parliament have got defeated by the advent of third phase of Sansad Adarsh Gram Yojana and even the various cabinet Ministers of the Union Government have not adopted any village in their respective Lok Sabha constituencies in the third phase of the Yojana; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): No Sir. So far, 1,327 Gram Panchayats have uploaded their Village Development Plans (VDP) containing 70,008 activities on the SAGY website (saanjhi.gov.in). Out of these, implementation of 42,483 (61%) activities have been completed as per the information available on SAGY website (saanjhi.gov.in) as on 27 November 2019.(b) & (c): No Sir. The details of Hon’ble Members of Parliament (MPs), including Cabinet Ministers who have identified Gram Panchayats under SAGY under Phase-III are available on saanjhi.gov.in and can be accessed (using their respective logins) by Hon’ble MPs.
