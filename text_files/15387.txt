                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4051






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Haryana Express




Sunita Duggal














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Haryana express train was not restored from Sirsa to New Delhi via  Bhiwani, Rewari, Gurugram after gauge conversion before 25 years and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Haryana express train is likely to be restored now to facilitate six districts of Haryana; and   (c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)  (a)	Prior to gauge conversion. Haryana Express used to operate on the Delhi-Rewari-Bhiwani-Hisar route. Presently, 14085/86 Haryana Express is operating between Delhi and Sirsa via Rohtak-Bhiwani-Hisar section.(b) and (c) Owing to operational constraints, at present, there is no proposal for operation of Haryana Express via Rewari-Bhiwani section. However, it has been decided to extend 74001/74004 Delhi-Rewari DEMU upto Hisar via Bhiwani.                                                        *****
