                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

147






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Projects under Sagarmala Programme




Nalin Kumar Kateel














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   	whether it is a fact that more than 60 projects are being implemented under Sagarmala Programme in Karnataka; (b)  	 if so, the details of each of the projects; (c)   	the status of implementation of each of the projects as on 31st March, 2021; (d)  	 whether there is any time and cost overrun of any of the aforesaid projects and   if so, the details thereof; and (e)  	the details of the impact of COVID-19 pandemic on projects under Sagarmala Programme?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	to (e)   A statement is laid on the Table of House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) of LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *147 TO BE ANSWERED ON 29TH JULY, 2021 RAISED BY SHRI NALIN KUMAR KATEEL REGARDING “PROJECTS UNDER SAGARMALA PROGRAMME”.(a)  to (c)  In Karnataka, currently 45 projects worth Rs. 11,701 cr. are included in Sagramala of which 11 projects worth Rs. 739 cr. have been completed and 34 projects worth Rs. 10,962 cr. are under various stages of development and implementation. The details and status of the projects in the State of Karnataka is annexed [ Annexure-I] .(d)    Out of 9 Sagarmala funded projects, 3 are already completed. The detailed status of all the Sagarmala funded projects is annexed [Annexure –II].(e)  Maritime sector being essential service, continued to operate amid Covid-19 pandemic. As far as Sagarmala Programme is concerned, under implementation projects suffered due to precautionary measures taken to control the spread of virus (i.e., implementing SOP of COVID-19, migration of labours, etc).
