                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

767






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Accounts opened under SSY




Veena Devi














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of accounts opened under the Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY) launched for girls in the country during the last three years, State-wise;(b)	the substantial steps being taken for successful implementation of this scheme for providing maximum benefits to the girls in the country; and(c)	the number of girls benefitted from the said scheme during the last three years, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a)	& (c):   The details of account opened (State-wise) under the Sukanya Samriddhi Account Scheme (SSA) during the last three years is attached as ‘Annexure-A’.(b)	The Sukanya Samriddhi Account (SSA) Scheme is being given due audio, video electronic and print media publicity. Campaigns to open accounts are also launched from time to time for information of the public by Department of Posts (DoP). The details of publicity / campaign run by NSI / DoP is given below:Sr. No.	Details of Publicity / Campaign	Duration	Publicity / Campaign run by1	Balika Shakti Campaign	09.12.2019 to 18.01.2020	By Department of Posts2	Mahila Divas Campaign	24.02.2020 to 07.03.2020	3	National Girl Child Day Campaign	24.01.2021	4	Radio Jingle on National News / various FM Stations	21.09.2019 to 30.09.2019	By National Savings Institute5		30.10.2019 to 09.11.2019	6		30.10.2019 to 09.11.2019	7		26.01.2022 to 24.02.2022	*****
