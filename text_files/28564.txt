                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

797






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Steps for Safety of Passengers




P. Velusamy














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Airport Authority of India (AAI) has installed the Airport Operations Control Centre (AOCC) in Chennai which allows airport officials, security agencies and other stakeholders to see the live feeds about ground and air operations throughout India except Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru and Hyderabad and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the above mentioned metros are unable to anticipate and prepare for flight delays and disruptions;(c) if so, the steps taken by the Government to ensure safety and convenience of the passengers traveling to and from Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru and Hyderabad;(d) whether the AOCC will be interlinked to these cities; and(e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) Yes, Sir. Airports Authority of India (AAI) has installed Airport Operations Control Center (AOCC) in Chennai. AOCC, which is airport specific, is integrated with Air Traffic Control (ATC) systems and enables monitoring of the live feed on ground and air operations at the airport.;  (b) & (c): No Sir. AOCC is in operation at Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru and Hyderabad airports as well which allows the airport to function safely and conveniently.;(d) & (e): It is specific to each airport. AOCC of different cities can not be interlinked.
