                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

800






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Repair Work on NH-26




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the repair work on dilapidated stretch from Borigumma to Jeypore on National Highway (NH)-26 has been completed;(b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	the details and the present status of land acquisition for Koraput bypass on NH-26 after recent intervention by the Railways; and(d)	the time by which the Koraput bypass project is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) & (b) Yes Sir. The work of widening to 2-lane with paved shoulder from km 319/280 to km 345/480 (Borigumma – Jeypore section) of NH-26 having length of 26.20 km is currently in progress and black topping with Dense Graded Bituminous Bacadam (DBM) has been completed in 22.75 km length out of total 26.20km length. The maintenance of the balance road length where black topping is yet to be completed has been carried out by the Contractor as per the provisions of the Contract Agreement and the road is in traffic worthy conditions.(c) & (d) The approved alignment of Koraput bypass on NH-26 passes through Railway land. Indian Railways had raised the issue of shifting of the approved alignment of the bypass. As per meeting among the officials of State PWD, District administration and Railway authorities, a revised alignment is under finalization by State PWD. The work of Koraput bypass shall be taken up after finalization of alignment and completion of land acquisition by the State Government. *****
