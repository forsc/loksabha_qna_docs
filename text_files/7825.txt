                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1582






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Electronics Hardware Park in West Bengal




Locket Chatterjee














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be please to state:(a)	whether the Government proposes to set up any electronics hardware park/manufacturing centre or units in West Bengal;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard;(c)	whether the Government has set up any Software Technology Parks of India (STPI) units in the state; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b):  Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), under the Electronics Manufacturing Cluster (EMC) scheme has approved two (02) Greenfield Electronics Manufacturing Clusters (EMCs) at Naihati, North 24 Parganas District and Falta, South 24 parganas district in the state of West Bengal. These Greenfield Electronics Manufacturing Clusters are being developed over an area of 128.04 acres with a project cost of Rs 117.17 crore including a Grant-in-aid of Rs. 51.34 crore from Government of India. Further, this Ministry has approved 5 applications under Modified Special Incentive Package Scheme (M-SIPS) in the state of West Bengal. Details of approved EMCs and applications approved under MSIPS in the state of West Bengal are enclosed at Annexure.(c) and (d): Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology has approved five (05) Software Technology Parks of India (STPI) centres at Kolkata, Siliguri, Durgapur, Haldia and Kharagpur in the state of West Bengal which are operational. ******* AnnexureDetails of Electronics Manufacturing Cluster in the state of West Bengal Rs. in Crore#	State	Project Location	Implementing Agency	Area (Acres)	Project outlay	Grant-in-aid	Grant-in-aid - released1	West Bengal 	Sector-IV & V, Falta Industrial Centre, P.S Ramnagar, District South 24 Parganas	West Bengal Electronics Industry Development Corporation Limited (WEBEL)	58.04	58.86	25.64	5.132		Naihati town,  North 24 Parganas district 		70	58.31	25.70	5.14                                                           Total	128.04	117.17	51.34	10.27Details of applications approved under M-SIPS:#	State	Applicant	No. of application	Investment(Rs. in Crore)1	West Bengal 	M/s Century  LED Limited	1	185.602		M/s Megatherm Induction Pvt.
