                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2158






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Restoration of Monuments in Rajasthan under Swadesh Darshan Scheme




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any initiatives have been taken under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme to restore monuments in Rajasthan, particularly those in Bansur and Virat Nagar, such as the Bhim ki dungari, beejak ki pahari and bansur fort; (b) 	the funding available through Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives of Central Public Sector Undertakings and Corporate Sector in Rajasthan since the implementation of the scheme; (c) 	the total amount sanctioned towards the State of Rajasthan along with the details of the relevant expenditure; and (d) 	the number of projects, out of the 75 sanctioned, that involve Rajasthan, and details of the circles in which the projects were approved?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	Development and Promotion of Tourism is primarily the responsibility of the State Government/Union Territory (UT) Administration. The Ministry of  Tourism  under  ‘Swadesh Darshan’ scheme provides financial assistance to State Governments/ Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies. The Ministry of  Tourism  has  sanctioned  a  project  ‘Development of Spiritual Circuit– Churu (Salasar Balaji)– Jaipur (Shri Samode Balaji, Ghatke Balaji, Bandheke Balaji)- Alwar (Pandupole Hanumanji, Bharathari)- Viratnagar (Bijak, Jainnasiya, Ambika Temple)- Bharatpur (Kaman Region)- Dholpur (Muchkund) – Mehandipur Balaji- Chittorgarh (Sanwaliyaji)’ in Rajasthan in  the  year  2016-17  for  an  amount of  Rs. 93.90 Crore and  some of the  major  components  sanctioned at different locations in Viratnagar  include  drinking  water  facility,  solar lights, signages, cultural cum interpretation centre, development of  tourist facility centre, CCTV  surveillance system, surface parking, landscaping etc. Swadesh  Darshan scheme  is  a  central  sector  scheme under which  funds  are  provided  by the Ministry of  Tourism  to the  State Government/ Implementing  Agency. The Ministry of  Tourism has  sanctioned  76 projects in the country under  different  identified  themes of Swadesh Darshan Scheme, out  of  which  4  projects  have  been  sanctioned  in Rajasthan  as per the  list  annexed.   ******
