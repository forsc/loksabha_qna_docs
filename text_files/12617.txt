                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1388






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Crisis in Automobile Industries




K. Subbarayan














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and number of auto industries that have declared lay off since April 2018 and the number of permanent, contract and casual workers who have lost their jobs;(b) whether these companies obtained permission before lay off, if not, the reasons therefor and the action taken against such companies; and(c) whether the companies complied with the law in making payment to the laid off workers and if not, the action taken against such companies?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) to (c): Due to cyclical slowdown across various sectors including automobile sector, retrenchment of temporary workers has been reported by the Industry. However, no confirmed data on job loss is available with the Government.
