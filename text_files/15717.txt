                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4217






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Funds for BSUP




Kani K. Navas














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the funds allocated to provide Basic Services to Urban Poor (BSUP) are not being utilised properly and if so, the reaction of the Government thereto;(b)	the number of States which have utilised less than 50 per cent of the allocated funds per year during each of the last three years and the current year, State-wise; and(c)	the reasons for such underutilization along with the corrective steps being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                    (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	to (c) 	Under the Basic Services to Urban Poor (BSUP) which was a component of Jawaharlal Nehru National Urban Renewal Mission (JnNURM ), Additional Central Assistance      ( ACA ) to the tune of Rs. 11,469.55 crore was released to the States/UTs. Utilization of Rs. 10,883.00 crore has been reported by the States/UTs. The scheme of BSUP was upto 31.03.2012 which was extended till 31.03.2017 only to complete the ongoing projects approved till 31.03.2012. State/UT-wise details of funds released and utilized under BSUP during the last three years are at Annexure.                                         *****
