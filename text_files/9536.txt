                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3067






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






SAMARTH Scheme




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the number of workers trained under the SAMARTH scheme, State-wise;(b)	 whether the trained skill workers under the scheme, if any, have been employed in the textile units; and (c)	 if so, the number of such employed skilled workers, in Uttar Pradesh specifically?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (c):	Ministry of Textiles has been implementing training and skill development in all segments of textile sector across all value chains, with a view for capacity building and employment generation in the sector. During the period 2010-2017, under Integrated Skill Development Scheme (ISDS) in the textiles sector, 11.14 lakh persons have been provided with skill development, out of which 8.43 lakh were given employment. In Uttar Pradesh, total 116671 persons were trained and 75202 persons were provided with wage employment.			In further continuation, Ministry of Textiles has expanded the skill development programme, called Samarth- Scheme for Capacity Building in Textile Sector, for the entire value chain of the textiles sector (except spinning and weaving in organized sector which are being trained under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana) with a target of 10 lakh persons at a total outlay of Rs.1300 crore. In this regard, Ministry of Textiles has allocated targets to State Governments including Uttar Pradesh for training of  3.66 Lakh persons in textiles sector under Samarth Scheme. The State-wise break-up is given in the Annexure. In addition, a target of 69,174 persons has also been allocated to Industry/Industry Associations for meeting the captive needs of textile Industry.
