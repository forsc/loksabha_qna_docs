                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

853






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Expansion of CNG Netwrok




Sukhbir Singh Jaunapuria














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has fixed target to expand CNG network in all the States and districts across the country; (b) if so, the details thereof, district and State-wise; (c) the details of the proposed number of CNG pumps in Tonk and Sawai Madhopur districts of Rajasthan; and (d) the time by which pipeline works in these districts are likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b) Establishment of CNG Stations is part of the development of City Gas Distribution (CGD) network and the same is carried out by the entities authorized by Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB). PNGRB is the authority to grant authorization to the entities for developing of CGD network in Geographical Areas (GAs) as per PNGRB Act, 2006.  As on date 232 GAs covering 407 districts spread over 27 States/UTs have been authorized for the development of CGD networks.  The details of the authorized GAs along with Minimum Work Program (MWP) targets for setting up of CNG stations are given in Annexure.(c) & (d) PNGRB has not authorized Tonk and Sawai Madhopur districts of Rajasthan to any entity yet. However, these districts have been included in the list of GAs identified for the 11th CGD bidding round, launched on 17.09.2021. The MWP targets are assigned based on the numbers quoted by the successful bidder. The completion schedule for achievement of MWP targets in the GAs is 8 years from the date of authorization of the GAs.****
