                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

349






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Coconut Oil from Lakshadweep




Faizal P.P. Mohammed














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is any plan to stop the import/supply of coconut oil from other parts of the country to Lakshadweep since Lakshadweep is self sufficient in coconut; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Coconut oil to the extent of 570 MT during 2017-18 and 470 MT in 2018-19 was imported against domestic production of 5,35,000 MT and 5,71,900 MT respectively, in the country. There is no plan under consideration to prohibit import of coconut oil. There is also no plan to stop supply of coconut oil from other parts of the country to Lakshadweep. ********
