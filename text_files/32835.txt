                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

364






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Housing Scheme for Beedi Workers




Shriniwas Dadasaheb Patil














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















the number of beedi workers who were provided houses under the Revised Integrated Housing Scheme (RIHS) and the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) during the last three years and the current year, State/year-wise?






ANSWER




                   THE MINISTER OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                               (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)Revised Integrated Housing Scheme (RIHS) is being implemented by Ministry of Labour and Employment. The Region/year-wise number of beedi workers who were provided houses under the RIHS during the last three years and the current year is at Annexure-I. Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) is implementing ‘Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana- Urban (PMAY-U) – Housing for All’ Mission since June 2015. So far, 1.14 crore houses have been sanctioned under PMAY-U. Year wise houses sanctioned in States/ Union Territories during the last three years and current year is at Annexure-II. However, the Ministry does not maintain occupation-wise beneficiaries’ data under PMAY-U including beedi workers.                                      *****
