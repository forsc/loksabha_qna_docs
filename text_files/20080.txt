                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1466






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






New Industrial Development Scheme for J&K




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has recently approved a New Industrial Development Scheme for the UT of Jammu and Kashmir; (b)	if so, the details along with the features thereof and whether there is any such proposal to extend such scheme to other States in the country particularly for Tamil Nadu; (c)	if so, the details thereof; and (d)	the extent to which the new proposal is likely to help in attracting more investments and create more employment in the region?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a):     	Yes Sir; a Central Sector Scheme for industrial development in the UT of  Jammu and Kashmir (J&K) has been approved by the Government of India.(b) to (d):   	The Scheme with a total Financial outlay of Rs 28,400 Crore (Upto the year 2037) provides for four types of incentives namely (i) Capital Investment Incentive, (ii) Capital Interest subvention, (iii) Goods & Service Tax Linked Incentive (GSTLI) and (iv) Working Capital Interest Subvention have been provided. The scheme is expected to bring significant investment and create additional employment in the UT.As the UT of J&K falls in the hilly regions in Himalayas with remoteness and other disadvantages that are unique to this region, and  falls under the category of the special package States/UTs, hence, the above scheme has been approved specifically for industrial development in the UT  of J&K. There is no proposal to extend such scheme to other States  including Tamil Nadu. ******
