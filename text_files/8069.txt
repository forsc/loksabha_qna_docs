                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1726






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Review of Various Road Projects




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to review the work in progress under the contracts awarded for extension or conversion of State Highways to National Highways, two to four lanes, four to six and eight lanes and for super express highway in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the details of National Highways on which work has been discontinued midway by the contractor in the country, State-wise including Maharashtra along with the reasons therefor; and(d) the remedial steps taken by the Government to curb the inconvenience caused to the road travellers and prevent heavy damages caused to the vehicles as a result of such discontinuation of road projects?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	& (b) Regular review meetings at various levels are held with Regional Officers, State Governments and concessionaires/contractors to make the construction of projects hassle free and completion within stipulated time.  To expedite completion of these projects various steps are also taken which include  streamlining of land acquisition & environment clearances, premium re-scheduling, close coordination with other Ministries, revamping of dispute resolution mechanism etc.(c) & (d)  Details of projects discontinued mid-way, State-wise including Maharashtra along with reasons therefore are at Annexure-I. Necessary steps are being taken to maintain and complete the balance work.
