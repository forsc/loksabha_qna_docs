                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2232






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Advanced Diploma Programme under NSTI




Rampreet Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of National Skill Training Institutes in the country which are running advanced diploma programmes with the assistance of IBM; (b) 	the details thereof, institution wise, gender-wise and year-wise; (c)	 the names of the institutes out of the said institutions which are running courses only for girls; and (d)	 the number of trainees provided regular employment after completion of courses from such training centres?






ANSWER




                                                        (a). 	Directorate General of Training (DGT), Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with M/s IBM India Private Limited in Feb. 2018 to offer Advanced Diploma (Vocational) in ‘IT, Networking and Cloud Computing’. There are 16 National Skill Training Institutes (NSTIs) including NSTIs exclusively for women (NSTI(W)) in the country that are offering the said course. (b) and (c) 	The institute-wise, gender-wise and year-wise details are given in Annexure-I which include 7 NSTIs(W) that are running course exclusively for women.(d). 	The duration of the course is 2 years and the first batch of Advanced Diploma will complete the course by March 2020.
