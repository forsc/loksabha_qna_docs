                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3452






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Street Vendors Act




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the progress of registration of street vendors under the Street Vendors Act, 2014 in Delhi as on 2021;b)	the details of progress made for carrying out vendor surveys, forming Town Vending Committees (TVC) and notifying vendors zone / market, State-wise;c)	the progress being made in amending Sections 283 and 431 of the Indian Penal Code, Section 34 of the Police Act to further streamline the provisions of the Street Vendors Act, 2014;d)	whether the Government has considered revisiting the ceiling on the number of street vendors at 2.5 percent of the population of the city, town, zone or ward and if so, the details thereof; ande)	if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                                                  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                        MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                               (SHRI  KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a):	As reported by NCT of Delhi, so far, a total of 72,457 street vendors have been identified. (b):	Statement is attached at Annexure-I. (c):	There is no such proposal. (d)&(e):	There is no proposal for revisiting the norm mentioned in the Act. The Act provides that the plan for street vending shall ensure that all existing street vendors identified in the survey, subject to a norm conforming to two and a half per cent of the population of the ward, zone, town or city are accommodated. Further, where the number of street vendors identified are more than the holding capacity of the vending zone, the TVC shall carry out a draw of lots for issuing the certificate of vending for that vending zone and the remaining persons shall be accommodated in any adjoining vending zone to avoid relocation.                                *****
