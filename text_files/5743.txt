                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

462






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Economic Development of Minority Community




Haji Fazlur Rehman














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the action plan of the Government for the economic development of the minority community in the country; and(b)	the number of students belonging to the said community who after clearing preliminary examinations conducted by the Union Public Service Commission, the Staff Selection Commission and the State Public Service Commission have been provided assistance under the Scheme NaiUdaan being implemented by the Government for the minority  community along with the nature of the said assistance?ANSWER






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS  (a)	&(b):  A statement is laid on the table of the House.                                ********
