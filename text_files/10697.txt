                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3942






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Projects under Bharatmala Pariyojana




Manne Srinivas Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the projects proposed under Bharatmala Pariyojana in Mahabubnagar district of Telangana; (b)	the present status of preparation of Detailed Project Report (DPR) for each of the projects approved till date;(c)	the estimated cost of these projects along with the allocation of funds made for the purpose, project-wise;(d)	the time by which these projects are likely to be completed; and(e)	the extent to which these projects are likely to generate employment potential?	
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)  to (e) In the Mahabubnagar district of Telangana state, the road from Jadcherla to Deosugur  with an aggregate length of 90 km  is included under Bharatmala Pariyojana. Detailed Project Report (DPR) of the project is under preparation.  The physical development of the stretch will be taken upon completion of DPR & approval of the project, based on inter-se priority and availability of fund. It is too early to indicate the date of completion. *****
