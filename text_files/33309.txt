                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

796






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Atmanirbhar Bharat Corner




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Sanjeev Kumar Singari
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the present status of setting up Atmanirbhar Bharat Corner in 100 Indian Missions/ Embassies across the world;(b) whether the Government has devised a plan in identification of which Geographical Indication tagged tribal art and craft products would be promoted there; and(c) if so, the details thereof??






ANSWER




                                                        (a) As per the information received from Tribal Co-Operative Marketing Development Federation of India (TRIFED), tribal products have been dispatched to 63 Indian Mission/Embassies abroad for Atmanirbhar Bharat Corners, out of which the products have already been displayed in 42 places as on 1.2.2022.(b) & (c) So far, 14 GI tagged tribal art and craft products have been sent for display in the Atmanirbhar Bharat Corners in the Indian Missions/Embassies abroad.*****
