                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5358






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






e-commerce Platform for MSME Sector




Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru













                                                    L.S. Tejasvi Surya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to bring out a  common e- commerce platform for the benefit of MSME sector and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the steps being initiated by the Government in this regard to make the market for MSMEs across the world;(c)	the deadline for the proposed e- commerce and payment platform for MSMEs to be launched by the Government along  with its salient features;(d)	whether the usage of the payment platform would be made compulsory for all the State Governments, private MSMEs and agencies and if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the number of days likely to be cut down after the usage of the payment portal in making payments?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) to (e):	Yes, the Ministry of Commerce and Industry has already launched e-commerce platform named as Govt. e-Marketplace (GeM).  MSME Vendors are being on boarded in this platform for the benefit of MSME Sector. Procurement from GeM is mandatory for Central Govt. offices/PSUs/Autonomous Bodies. The usage of Payment Platform on GeM is compulsory for Central Govt. offices/PSUs/Autonomous Bodies. This paves the way for reduction in the days taken otherwise in receipt of payments. Government has taken several steps for export promotion. These include support to MSMEs for participate in the international exhibitions/trade fairs/buyer-seller meet, etc. abroad, for holding International conferences/seminars/workshops in India and under Export Credit Guarantee Scheme.  The share of MSME related products in export during 2018-19 (provisional) is 48.10%.
