                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1982






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Construction of Kozhikode Bypass on NH-66




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of construction work of Kozhikode bypass on NH-66;(b)	the time frame set for completion of the said project; and(c)	the steps taken by the Government for timely completion of the said project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS[GEN. (DR.) V.K. SINGH (RETD.)] (a) to (c)   Concession Agreement for construction of Kozhikode bypass in the State of Kerala was executed on 18.04.2018 with M/s Calicut Expressway Pvt. Ltd. with completion period of 30 months. Appointed date for the project is delayed due to delay in achievement of financial close by concessionaire.*****
