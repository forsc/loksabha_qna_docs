                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2881






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






New Passenger Train in Telangana




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plans to start a passenger train on the railway line between Vishnupuram and Motumari in Telangana; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) the details of the operational constraints due to which this passenger train has not been started yet; (d) whether the Government has conducted the required surveys recently to determine the operational feasibility of starting such a passenger train; and (e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND     ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (e) No, Sir. Motumari-Vishnupuram section is fit only for freight train operation. The statutory authorization by Commissioner of Railway Safety for operation of passenger train services is not available. As such, introduction of passenger train services on the said section is not operationally feasible, at present.*****
