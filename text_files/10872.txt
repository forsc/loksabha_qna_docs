                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4326






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Task Force under National AYUSH Mission




Ganesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)   be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to extend the reach of Homoeopathy and other AYUSH system of medicines to the rural areas under the ‘Ayushman Bharat Yojana’; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government proposes to constitute a task force to make the said action plan successful under ‘National AYUSH Mission’; and (d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b):     Yes. Central Government has taken the decision to operationalize AYUSH Health & Wellness Centres (HWCs) by upgrading existing health care facilities. It is envisaged to transform existing Sub-health Centres/AYUSH dispensaries to Health & Wellness Centres to ensure universal access to an expanded range of Primary Health Care services as appropriate to people’s needs.Till date, 1032 AYUSH dispensaries have been approved for upgrading to AYUSH Health & Wellness Centresand budget of Rs. 89.92 crore have been released to States/UTs under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission.(c ) & (d):     There is no separate task force under National AYUSH Mission (NAM). However, there is an existing institutional mechanism under NAM to operationalize these AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres.……………….
