                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1554






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Companies with Access to Aarogya Setu App




Faizal P.P. Mohammed













                                                    Amar Singh
                                                








                                                    T.N. Prathapan
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY   be pleased to State:(a)	whether the National Informatics Centre (NIC) has refused to provide the list of companies that have access to the Aarogya Setu Open Application Programming Interface (API);(b)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefore;(c)	if not, the list of companies with access to Aarogya Setu Open API;(d)	the list of service providers integrated with the Aarogya Setu App; and(e)	whether the Government has entered into any agreement with companies using Aarogya Setu Open API service and if so, the details thereof? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ): Yes, Sir. National Informatics Centre had received an RTI request for providing the list of organizations who have registered for the Aarogya Setu Open API. The request was denied.(b): Sharing the details of the organizations is in violation of the Aarogya Setu Privacy Policy. (c): NA, as per point (a).(d): Aarogya Setu App interfaces with the following services :•	e-pass - For displaying e-pass issued to users for travel during lockdown•	Ayushman Bharat - For IVRS based telephonic health assessment •	ICMR - For data on covid-19 testing and statistics(e): Yes, Sir. The organizations registering for Open API service agreement to abide by the Open API Terms of Service. The Aarogya Setu Open API Terms of Service is available at : https://openapi.aarogyasetu.gov.in/tandc*******
