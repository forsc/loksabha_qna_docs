                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

131






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Foreign Aid




Kani K. Navas













                                                    Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the allocation of foreign aid received from April, 2021 till date, including the dates and destinations of such allocations;(b) 	whether any foreign aid consignments are pending for allocation and if so, the details of the same including reasons for delay in allocation;(c) 	the details of every foreign aid consignment received as COVID-19 relief from April, 2021 till date including the date of receipt, the quantum and nature of each consignment and the respective donor country; and(d) 	the details of the procedure/formula followed to determine the allocation of these received resources, including the resource type and quantities to be distributed?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS(DR. SUBRAHMANYAM JAISHANKAR)(a) to (d)	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) TO (D) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *131 REGARDING “FOREIGN AID” TO BE ANSWERED ON 28.07.2021(a), (b) & (d)	A dedicated Inter-Ministerial Coordination Cell in Ministry of Health and Family Welfare was set for coordination, allocation and distribution of foreign COVID relief material. The allocations were done keeping in view the need and urgency in tackling the Covid second wave crisis. As on date, the Inter-Ministerial Covid Cell has completed allocation of foreign consignments. (c) 	 In the fight against Covid pandemic during the second wave, materials from 52 countries have been received so far. These included various items from foreign governments, private companies, Indian associations abroad etc. The details available with Ministry of External Affairs of foreign consignments received from various countries from April 2021 till date is placed at APPENDIX 1.
