                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3781






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Status of Naga Talks




Manish Tewari














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the current status of the talks with the Naga Underground Groups that the Government initiated in 2014;(b) the details of the Framework agreement signed by the Government with the Naga Underground groups in August 2015 along with the complete text of the said agreement;(c) whether the NSCN (IM) and other underground Naga groups involved in the negotiations have dropped their demand of secession from the Indian Union; and(d) whether the Government is ready to consider a Naga flag for a       pan-Naga cultural body and to term the final agreement as the “Naga Constitution” as reported in a section of the press on March 5, 2019 ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d):	Naga Political Negotiations are at an advanced stage.  Any disclosure of the details of the negotiations will be premature and prejudicial to the final settlement.  ********
