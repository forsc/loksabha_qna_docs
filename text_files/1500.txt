                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1509






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Sanctioning of Research Project by Anthropological Survey of India




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Anthropological Survey of India has sanctioned/ commissioned any research projects during the last three years;(b) if so, the details thereof and outcomes achieved therefrom; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) Yes, Sir.(b) The details and outcome of the research projects are as under :S.No.	Name of project	Outcome(i)	Development and Sustainability; Year: 2017-18; Area/Village covered: 17	Critical assessment of the impacts of different developmental initiatives and its sustainability among different tribal and non-tribal communities of India.(ii)	Community  Health,  Disease  and Genetic  Structure  of Indian  Population;  Year : 2017 onwards; Community covered : 35	Health awareness, especially among the tribal communities by ‘Genetic Screening-cum-Awareness Camps''.(iii)	Anthropological Study of De-Notified, Nomadic and Semi Nomadic Communities; Year : 2018-19; Community covered: 50	Generation of detailed ethnographic information   and examination on the relation of these communities with the outside world with a view to help in de-stigmatizing them by bringing awareness among the people.(c) Does not arise.***
