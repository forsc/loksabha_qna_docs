                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1019






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Price Support Scheme




Amar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the farmers received a remunerative price for their produce under the agricultural price policy keeping in view the COVID-19 crisis during each of the last two years and the current year;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise, especially Punjab and if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	whether the Price Support Scheme (PSS) for procurement of oilseeds, pulses, and cotton has been implemented across the country; and(d)	if so, the details of procurement of farmers’ produce under the said scheme for whenever the prices of the products fall below the Minimum Support Price (MSP) during the said period and if so, the details of alternate corrective steps taken by the Government to provide an adequate price to farmers for their produce?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ??????? ?????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b) : The state-wise procurement details of agricultural commodities procured at MSP including the state of Punjab for the last two years is at Annexure-I. (c) & (d) : Government implements Price Support Scheme (PSS) for procurement of notified oilseeds, pulses and copra of Fair Average Quality (FAQ) at Minimum Support Price (MSP) through Central Nodal agency from registered farmers after receiving the proposals from the State Government as per scheme guidelines. Further, FAQ of Cotton is procured at MSP by Cotton Corporation of India (CCI) in consultation with the concerned State Governments. However, if farmer gets better price in comparison to MSP, they are free to sell their produce in open market. For making effective procurement by government agencies at MSP and providing maximum benefits of MSP to the farmers, procurement centres are opened by respective State Government Agencies and Central Nodal Agencies like NAFED, FCI, CCI etc. after taking into account the production, marketable surplus, convenience of farmers and availability of other logistics / infrastructure such as storage and transportation etc. large number of the purchase centres in addition to the existing Mandis and depots / godowns are also established at key points for the convenience of farmers. The details of oilseeds, pulses and cotton procured at MSP for the last two years is also at Annexure-I.
