                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1939






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Implementation of PMJAY in Jammu and Kashmir




Prasun Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of persons hospitalized in Kashmir under PMJAY since 5 August, 2019 onwards, week-wise and hospital wise figures; and(b) the necessary steps that have been taken to implement PMJAY efficiently in the State?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	Week-wise and hospital-wise details of hospital admissions under Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) in Kashmir since 5th August, 2019 onwards are at Annexure I and II respectively.   (b):	As per the guidelines of AB-PMJAY (available at www.pmjay.gov.in), for hospitals facing intermittent internet connectivity, records can be updated within 7 days time, provided the processes outlined in the guidelines are followed.    	 In the interest of beneficiaries of UT of Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh, on 27.08.2019 provision of timeline of 7 days and telephonic approval for packages requiring pre-authorisation after admission of beneficiary in hospital have been relaxed.
