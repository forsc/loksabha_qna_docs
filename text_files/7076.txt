                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

701






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






National Service Scheme




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor













                                                    Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the prescribed number of students of Gujarat and Telangana under the National Service Scheme at present;(b) whether the State Governments of Gujarat and Telangana have sent any proposals regarding increasing the said number;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the steps taken by the Union Government in this direction for approving the said proposals along with outcome thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI KIREN RIJIJU](a) Number of students under National Service (NSS) Scheme at present in Gujarat and Telangana are as under:-<pre>SI.No. Name of the State 	No. of Students under National Service (NSS) Scheme at present 1      Gujarat			1,87,900 (1879 NSS Units)2      Telangana		1,39,800 (1398 NSS Units)</pre>(b) No such proposal from the State Government of Gujarat and Telangana have been received.(c) & (d) Does not arise in view of point (b).****
