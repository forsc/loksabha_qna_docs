                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

82






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Sports University




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the status of the Sports University in Manipur as on date and the amount allocated and spent for the same till date;(b) whether every North Eastern State would be provided with a college or campus for sports and if so, the details thereof;(c) the number of students enrolled every year in the University and the courses offered therein; and(d) the number of seats allotted for every State as quota, if available, Statewise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(KIREN RIJIJU)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the table of the House.STATEMENT REFERED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) TO (D) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 82 TO BE ANSWERED ON 27-06-2019, ASKED BYSHRI VINCENT H. PALA, REGARDING SPORTS UNIVERSITY(a): The National Sports University Bill has been enacted into the National Sports University Act, 2018 on 17.08.2018 during Monsoon Session, 2018 of the Parliament. M/s Hindustan Steel Construction Works Ltd. has been engaged as Project Management Consultant and it has been decided to engage TATA Institute of Social Scienes (TISS) for academic and professional consultancy. Construction has started. One Post Graduate and two Under Graduate programmes have been started in the University. A sum of Rs. 50.00 crore has been sanctioned for pre investment activities for the University, out of which Rs.31.10 crore has been released so far.(b): At present no proposal is under consideration for providing college or campus for sports in each North Eastern State under the National Sports University(c): Information is as below:<pre>Program 			Number of students enrolled 				Academic Year 		Academic Year				2018-19			2017-18Bachelor of Physical		14 			35Education and Sports(B.P.E.S)B.Sc. (Sports Coaching) 	12 			36M.A. (Sports Psychology) 	-- 			04</pre>(d): State-wise quota / seat is not allotted.****
