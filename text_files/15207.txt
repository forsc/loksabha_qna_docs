                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3796






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Rurban Clusters




Shrirang Appa Barne













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of rurban clusters identified under the Shyama Prasad Mukherji Rurban Mission (SPMRM) across the country, State/UT-wise;(b) 	the present status of Rurban clusters developed in the country during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise;(c)	the details of the initiatives taken/approved by the Government for the development of clusters in Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh and Jharkhand so far;(d) 	whether the Government has recently decided to construct 1000 urbanised clusters in rural areas and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise;(e) 	whether the Government has launched a mobile app. Geo-Rurban to be used for Geo-tagging of the assets in rural areas; and(f) 	if so, the details thereof along with the aims and objectives thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a) 	Out of the mandated 300 clusters to be developed under Shyama Prasad MukherjiRurban Mission (SPMRM), 296 clusters have been identified and approved across 28 States and 8 Union Territories. The details of 296 Rurban clusters, State/UT-wise, is at Annexure-1. (b) 	Of the identified 296 Rurban clusters, 288 Integrated Cluster Action Plans (ICAPs), which are the investment plan for each cluster, have been approved till date. 248 Detiled Project Reports (DPRs) have been approved by respective State Level Empowered Committees across the country.The physical and financial progress status of Rurban clusters selected for development during the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise is Placed at Annexure- 2.    (c) 	Out of the total 20 clusters allocated to Maharashtra, Integrated Cluster Action Plan (ICAP) for 19 clusters have been approved by the Empowered Committee of the Ministry. Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) have been approved for 19 clusters by the State Level Empowered Committee (SLEC) . Out of the total 19 clusters allocated to Madhya Pradesh, Integrated Cluster Action Plan (ICAP) for all 19 clusters have been approved by the Empowered Committee of the Ministry. Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) have been approved for 18 clusters by the State Level Empowered Committee (SLEC) . Out of the total 15 clusters allocated to Jharkhand, Integrated Cluster Action Plans (ICAP) for all 15 clusters have been approved by the Empowered Committee of the Ministry. Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) have not yet been completely approved by the State Level Empowered Committee (SLEC) for any cluster in Jharkhand. Partial DPRs have been approved for some clusters. SPMRM clusters focus on interventions which can be broadly categorized as basic civic amenities , economic, social and digital amenities. Strengthening economic activities is one of the key priorities in cluster development. This is achieved through investment in skill development  linked to economic activities, employment generation through promotion of Self Help Group (SHGs), agri-processing and tourism activities. (d) 	There is no proposal under the current Mission period to take up more  clusters as on date. (e) 	Yes, Government has launched a mobile app Geo-Rurban, for Geo-tagging of the assets in rural areas created under SPMRM. (f) 	Geo-Rurban app is a digital platform for registering works carried out under the Mission and will help in monitoring progress of works. The users can  update the physical status of the works being executed on the ground in the clusters at regular intervals, with  geo-tagged  images.********
