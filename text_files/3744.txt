                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3424






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Funding of Infrastructure Projects




A. Chellakumar














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Public Sector Banks have funded infrastructure projects in various States; and(b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise during each of the last three years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a) & (b) Yes Sir. The gross advances for infrastructure as reported by Public Sector Banks to Reserve Bank of India at the end of Financial Years 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19 stood at Rs.7,71,242 crore, Rs.7,91,702 crore and Rs.8,66,686 crore (provisional) respectively. State wise data of gross advances is not centrally maintained.******
