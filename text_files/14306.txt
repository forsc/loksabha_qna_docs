                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3076






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Guidelines for Payment at Duty Free Zone




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the guidelines with respect to the payment of the items purchased from the duty free zones at international airports during the foreign visits made by the Indians; and(b) whether these passengers have to make payments in foreign currency for the items purchased from duty free zone and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): Central Board of Excise and Customs (CBEC) under Ministry of Finance has issued a Circular No.31/2016-Customs dated 06.07.2016 and has subsequently modified it vide Circular No.50/2017- Customs dated 18.12.2017 stipulating the guidelines for incoming and outgoing passengers in respect of payment for items purchased from duty free zones in international airports in India.;(b): As per the CBEC Circular 50/2017-Customs dated 18.12.2017, the prices of all goods on sale at duty free shops are to be mandatorily displayed in Indian Rupees only. The Circular also permits passengers for making payments in Indian Rupees through an INR Credit/Debit Card at duty free shops, without any need for conversion of foreign currency into Indian Rupees.  However, payments made through an INR Credit/Debit Card at duty free shops located at arrival hall is subject to a limit of Rs.25,000/- for an incoming passengers.  Payments through an INR Credit/Debit Card at duty free shops located at departure hall is without limit for an outgoing passengers.
