                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2538






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Mega Food Parks




S. Muniswamy














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to start Mega Food Parks in the country including Kolar region of Karnataka;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the areas identified for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a)	to (c): No, Sir. The proposals under the scheme for Mega Food Parks of Ministry are invited through Expression of Interest (EOI) from time to time. At present, Ministry has not floated any EoI for submission of proposals under the Scheme. Now, this scheme is a component of the new Central Sector Umbrella Scheme – PRADHAN MANTRI KISAN SAMPADA YOJANA[PMKSY] and as on date, there is no proposal under consideration to start Mega Food Parks in the country including Kolar Region of Karnataka.  However, Ministry has accorded final approval to two projects in the state of Karnataka for setting up of Mega Food Park project under the scheme. Ministry of Food Processing Industries does not establish Mega Food Parks on its own but assists Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) registered under the Companies Act and State Government/ State Government entities/Cooperatives to establish Mega Food Parks under the Mega Food Park Scheme.
