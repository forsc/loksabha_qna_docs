                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3961






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






NPYAD Scheme




 Chandra Sekhar Bellana













                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total funds allocated and utilized under the National Programme for Youth and Adolescent Development (NPYAD) during the last two financial years;(b) the total funds allocated to the State of Andhra Pradeshunder the scheme during the last two years; and(c) the success achieved under the NPYAD Scheme so far?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a): The total funds allocated and utilized under the National Programme for Youth and Adolescent Development Scheme during the last two financial years are as under:<pre>S.No.		Financial Year 		Funds Allocated 	Funds Utilized1. 		2017-18 		Rs. 18.00 Crore 	Rs. 17.78 Crore2. 		2018-19 		Rs. 25.00 Crore 	Rs. 21.90 Crore</pre>(b): Under the scheme, there is no provision for state-wise allocation of funds.(c): In the last two financial years, following achievements had been made under the scheme:(i) Tenzing Norgay National Adventure Awards were conferred upon four individuals by the Hon’ble President of India on 29th August, 2017.(ii) 5th North East Youth Festival was organized at Gangtok, Sikkim from 28th to 30th October, 2017.(iii) 22nd National Youth Festival was organized at Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh from 12th to 16th January, 2018.(iv) National Youth Award 2015-16 was conferred upon 23 individuals and 7 organizations during the opening ceremony of 22nd National Youth Festival.(v) Tenzing Norgay National Adventure Awards were conferred upon ten individuals by the Hon’ble President of India on 25th September, 2018.(vi) 6th North East Youth Festival was organized at Agartala, Tripura from 15th to 18th November, 2018.(vii) Grants had been released to various NGOs for carrying out youth related activities.****
