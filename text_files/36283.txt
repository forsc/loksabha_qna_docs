                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3270






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Expenditure under MPLAD Fund




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether funds spent on laying foundation stones and plaques, etc. by the public representatives comes under the head of MPLADS or Government expenditure and if so, the details thereof;(b) the quantum of funds spent on laying of foundation stone and inscripting/ engraving plaque etc. during each of the last three years, State-wise;(c) the head under which the said expenditure is covered;(d) whether the Government has taken/proposes to take any step for diversion of this fund for other developmental works;(e) if so, the details thereof; and (f)  if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION, MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF CORPORATE AFFAIRS (RAO INDERJIT SINGH)(a)     No, Sir. As per MPLADS Guidelines, funds are not allowed to be incurred on laying foundation stones and plaques etc. by the public representatives. (b), (c ),(d), (e) & (f):  Does not arise.                                       ***
