                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

220






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Development of Gramin Agricultural Markets




Uday Pratap Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to improve/ develop Gramin Agricultural Markets (GrAMs) according to the requirements/demands of the farmers so as to provide right price for their produce so that they could avoid unnecessary expenditure and save time; (b)	 if so, the manner and the time by which the same is proposed to be achieved; and (c)	 if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		 	  (SHRI NARENDRA  SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (c):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.  220    DUE FOR REPLY ON 3rd AUGUST, 2021. (a):	Yes Sir. The Government is developing and upgrading rural haats into Gramin Agricultural Markets (GrAMs) according to the requirements/demands of the farmers so as to provide right price for their produce so that they could avoid unnecessary expenditure and save time. (b):	The development and upgradation of physical infrastructure of rural haats under control of panchayats through States / Union Territories is undertaken through Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) as ongoing process for development of GrAMs. So far, physical infrastructure/facilities under MGNREGS have been developed in 1025 rural haats and is under development in 1420 number of rural haats. (c):	In view of the above, the question does not arise. ******
