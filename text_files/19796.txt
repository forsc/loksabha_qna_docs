                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1029






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Number of Foreign Visitors in Chhattisgarh




Gomati Sai














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any rise has been registered in the number of foreign tourists in Chhatisgarh and other States during the last three years; (b) 	if so, the estimated number of foreign tourists visited Chhatisgarh during the last three years; (c) 	whether any steps have been taken by the Government to provide adequate infrastructure facilities in every State to cater the needs of foreign as well as domestic tourists; and (d) 	if so, the details of the steps taken in Chhatisgarh as it has enormous tourism potential?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b):	Ministry of Tourism compiles annual data on Foreign Tourists Visits (FTVs) based on the information received from State/UTs. Number of FTVs in Chhattisgarh during 2017 to 2019 are as follows:<pre>Foreign Tourists Visits year 2017	year 2018	year 20196701	14399	6817</pre>Details for other State/UTs are given in Annexure I. (c)& (d):	Development of tourism is primarily the responsibility of the concerned State/UTs. However, Ministry of Tourism provides financial assistance under the schemes of ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ for development of tourism infrastructure in the country. Submission of project proposals by the State Government/UTs and its sanctioning is a continuous process. The projects are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. The details of the projects sanctioned under Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD scheme in Chhattisgarh is given at Annexure-II.*******
