                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1085






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Implementation of National Digital Health Blueprint




Behanan Benny














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has planned for implementing the National Digital Health Blueprint (NDHB);(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether NDHB could bring a common platform for integration of existing applications in health domain in private healthcare and public healthcare; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	A Committee under the chairpersonship of Shri. J. Satyanarayana, Ex Chairman, Aadhaar was formed along with stakeholders to create a vision for implementation of digital health in the country.  The Committee has submitted its report- National Digital Health Blueprint-after due stakeholder’s consultation. Further action to implement the NDHB is being initiated.(c) & (d):	The blueprint proposes a federal architecture and identifies building blocks for implementation of digital health solutions across health spectrum duly highlighting issues related with privacy, consent management, national portability and provisioning of health services across multiple access channels.It highlights need for creating various registries, adoption of health data standards to ensure interoperability of health data across facilities. It envisages a vision for creation of electronic health record for an individual across both government and private health facilities. It has accordingly proposed set of building blocks which can facilitate the same.
