                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

419






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Supply of Imported Onions




Kotagiri Sridhar













                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has offered to supply imported onions to State Governments at Rs. 49 to Rs. 58 per kg recently; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	the States which have purchased onions from these imported stocks and the details thereof; and (d) 	the total amount spent by the Government on these imports?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI DANVE RAOSAHEB DADARAO)(a)  & (b) : Government had earlier offered/ supplied imported onions at cost price which is now offered/supplied to States at prevailing market/mandi/online portal rates. (c)	   : The details of imported onion supplied to various States/UTs is at Annexure.(d)        : As on 31.01.2020, MMTC has placed a demand of Rs. 226 crore towards imports of onion.***** ANNEXURE ANNEXURE REFERRED IN REPLY TO PART (c) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.419 FOR 04.02.2020 REGARDING SUPPLY OF IMPORTED ONIONS.Dispatch of imported onion to States/UTs                                                                                                           (As on 30.01.2020)S.No.	States/UTs	Quantity (in MT)1	Andhra Pradesh	893.182	Kerala	49.893	Telangana	111.684	Uttar Pradesh	211.795	Uttarakhand	262.606	West Bengal	123.197	Himachal Pradesh	120.648	Assam	190.279	Goa	50.3110	Jammu & Kashmir	45.7111	Haryana	218.6012	Meghalaya	282.5913	Odisha	48.50	Total	2608.95Source : NAFED*****
