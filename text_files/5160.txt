                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Funds Allocated for Cultural Assistance and Projects




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the funds allocated and utilized by the Government for various cultural assistance and projects in Maharashtra during the last two years;(b)	the details of the ongoing cultural programmes and projects in Maharashtra; and(c)	the details of the projects likely to be completed on time and the projects which are lagging behind the schedule along with the reasons thereof?






ANSWER

There has been no delay in completion of projects.
