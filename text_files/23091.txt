                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4202






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






CNG/PNG in Gopalganj




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government proposes to promote the use of Compressed Natural Gas (CNG)/Piped Natural Gas (PNG) in districts of Bihar except Gopalganj; (b)if so, the reasons therefor; (c) whether the Gopalganj district has not yet been authorized for development of CGD network and PNGRB identifies GAs for authorizing the development of CGD network in synchronization with the development of natural gas pipeline connectivity, natural gas availability and techno-commercial viability and if so, reasons for not authorizing for development of CGD network; (d) whether Saran district is adjacent to Gopalganj district in the State of Bihar, which has been covered under CGD network of Muzaffarpur, Vaishali, Saran and Samastipur Districts GA and if so, the time framework for inclusion of Gopalganj district?






ANSWER




                                                        (????????????????????)MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) and (b)  Petroleum & Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB) is the authority to grant authorization to the entities for the development of City Gas Distribution (CGD) network in Geographical Areas (GAs) as per PNGRB Act, 2006.  PNGRB identifies GAs for authorizing the development of CGD network in synchronization with the development of natural gas pipeline connectivity, natural gas availability and techno-commercial viability and in the state of Bihar also. Establishment of CNG Stations is a part of development of City Gas Distribution (CGD) Network by an authorized entity. Following GAs/ Districts have been covered under City Gas Distribution (CGD) network in the state of Bihar:Sl. No.	State	Name of Geographical Area (GA)/District1	Bihar	Patna District2		Aurangabad, Kaimur & Rohtas Districts3		Begusarai District4		Gaya &Nalanda Districts5		Araria, Purnia, Katihar and Kishanganj Districts6		Arwal, Jehanabad, Bhojpur and Buxar Districts7		Khagaria, Saharsa and Madhepura Districts8		Lakhisarai, Munger and Bhagalpur Districts9		Muzaffarpur, Vaishali, Saran and Samastipur Districts 10	Bihar and Jharkhand	Nawada and koderma Districts11		Sheikhpur, Jamui and Deoghar Districts(c)and (d) Gopalganj district has not yet been authorized for development of CGD network. Saran district is adjacent to Gopalganj district in the state of Bihar has been covered under CGD network of Muzaffarpur, Vaishali, Saran and Samastipur Districts GA, and the development will take place as per the approved plan in the work plan period.*****
