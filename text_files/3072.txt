                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2921






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Policies Governing Coal Sector




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Coal sector in India is facing high stress from excessively strict laws and policies governing coal sector and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the steps being taken by the Government to boost competitiveness of coal producing entities in international market;(c)	whether the domestic production of Coal is sufficient for domestic consumption in India and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the details of amount and cost of total coal imports by the country during the last five years?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a)	       No, Sir.(b & c):  Demand/consumption of coal is higher than the current level of supply of coal in the country. Presently, Coal producing entities in the country are not competing in the international market. During the year 2018-19, as against a total actual demand /consumption of coal of 969.47 MT (Provisional), the domestic supply was 734.23 MT (Provisional) and coal import was 235.24 MT (Provisional). (d)	The details of value and import of coal during the last five years is given below:-Year	2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19 (Provisional)Total Import (MT)	217.78	203.95	190.95	208.27	235.24Value of Import (in Rs Crores)	104507	86034	100231	138477	170881* * * *
