                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

163






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Welfare Schemes for Weaker Section




Talari Rangaiah














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO.  *163TO BE ANSWERED ON 03.03.2020WELFARE SCHEMES FOR WEAKER SECTION *163. SHRI TALARI RANGAIAH:Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:  (a) whether the Government has welfare schemes for the weaker section of general category in the country; and (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF  SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI THAAWARCHAND GEHLOT)(a) & (b): A statement is laid on the table of the House.***** Statement for answer to part (a) to (b) of the Lok Sabha Starred Question No. *163 for answer on 07.02.2020 raised by Shri Talari Rangaiah regarding “Welfare Schemes for Weaker Section”. (a)	Yes Sir, following schemes are implemented for the welfare of weaker section of general category across the country by this Ministry. 1.	Dr. Ambedkar Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Post Matric Scholarships for the Economically Backward Class (EBC) Students.2.	Dr. Ambedkar Scheme of Interest Subsidy on Educational Loan for Overseas Studies for OBCs/EBCs3.	Skill development Programme for OBC/EBCs run by NBCFDC(b)	The details of the Schemes are enclosed in Annexure-I Annexure as referred to part (b) of Lok Sabha Starred Qn no. 163 to be abswered on 03.03.2020. 1.	Scheme of Dr. Ambedkar Post-Matric Scholarship for Economically Backward Classes (EBCs)•		This is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme being implemented through the State Government and Union Territories w.e.f. 2014-15. The objective of the scheme is to provide financial assistance to the EBC students studying at post-matriculation or post-secondary stage. The income ceiling of parents/guardians for eligibility is Rs.1.00 lakh per annum (including self-income, if employed).•		The Scheme is a “Funds-Limited” Scheme.  The funds will be released to the States on first-come first-served basis, subject to a maximum amount to be worked out every year based on total budget made available that year, under the scheme. The objective of the scheme is to award interest subsidy to meritorious OBC and EBC students so as to provide them better opportunities for higher education abroad and enhance their employability.   To be eligible under the scheme, a student should come under the income ceiling Rs 2.50 lakh per annum. 50 % of the outlay every year is earmarked for Girl students.  The students should have secured admission in the approved courses at Masters, M.Phil or Ph.D levels abroad. He/She should have availed loan from a scheduled bank under the Education Loan Scheme of the Indian Banks Association (IBA) for the purpose.•	Under the scheme, interest payable by the students availing the education loans of the IBA for the period of moratorium (i.e. course period, plus one year or six months after getting job, whichever is earlier) as prescribed under the Education Loan Scheme of the IBA, shall be borne by the Government of India. After the period of moratorium is over, the interest on the outstanding loan amount shall be paid by the student, in accordance with the existing Educational Loan Scheme of the Banks as may be amended from time to time. The candidate will bear the Principal instalments and interest beyond moratorium period.   The Canara Bank has been nominated as Nodal Bank under the Scheme.
