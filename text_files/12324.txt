                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1186






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Reduction in Steel Imports




Sajda Ahmed














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the inclusive policy adopted by the Government to reduce the import of steel in the country;(b)	whether the Government proposes deduction on custom duty on raw material for steel industries; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)	In order to minimize import of steels, the Government has taken following measures:(i)	Domestically Manufactured Iron and Steel Products Policy (DMI&SP) has been notified with an objective to encourage consumption of domestically produced steel by Government organisations. (ii)	To meet the domestic demand for high performance steel, the Government, is facilitating R&D in the sector through the establishment of Steel Research and Technology Mission of India (SRTMI).  (iii)	The Ministry is addressing the issue of quality of steel products that are produced as well as block import of sub-standard steel. It has notified Steel and Steel Products Quality Control (QC) Orders. (iv)	To prevent import due to unfair trade, the government has imposed Anti-Dumping Duty on a number of steel products imports.  (v)	To reduce dependence on imported steel scrap, the government has notified the Steel Scrap Recycling Policy. (vi)	The Government has recently introduced Steel Imports Monitoring System (SIMS) to monitor steel imports.(b)&(c): Import duties on basic raw materials used by the steel industry, such as coking coal, ferro scrap, iron ore, ferro-alloys etc. is at a lower level.********
