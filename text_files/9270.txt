                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2820






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Homeless Urban Population




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has conducted any survey regarding the homeless urban poor population across the country including Rajasthan;(b)	If so, the details thereof;(c)	If not, whether the Government is considering to have such a scheme in future;(d)	Whether the Government has received any proposals from States/UTs including Rajasthan to provide financial assistance for procuring required land for housing projects; and(e)	If so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	to (c): "In pursuance of the Government''s vision of "Housing for All" by 2022, Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMA Y(U)] since 25.06.2015, for providing assistance to States/Union Territories (UTs), including the State of Rajasthan, for  addressing  the  housing  requirement of the people belonging to Economically Weaker Section (EWS), Lower Income Group (LIG) and Middle Income Group (MIG) categories in urban areas. States/UTs have undertaken demand survey under the Scheme for assessing actual demand of housing. The validated demand reported by States/UTs so far is around 112 lakh.	Based on the project proposals received so far from States/UTs, as on 25.11.2019,total 93,18,613 houses have been sanctioned under the scheme; out of which 55,85,738 are at various stages of construction and 28,36,195 are completed/delivered.(d) and (e): No sir, the cost of land for projects is not admissible under the PMAY(U).                            *****
