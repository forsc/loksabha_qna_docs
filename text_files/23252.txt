                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4303






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Promotions and Postings in SBI




Rodmal Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the extant promotion and posting policies of State Bank of India (SBI); (b) whether a person can be retained in the same department on promotion from CGM to AMD in SBI; (c) if so, the details thereof including the details of the violators of the said policies; and (d) the details of the action taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a) to (d): The State Bank of India has informed that the bank has Board approved policies for promotion to various grades. In accordance with the policies, promotion is based on various parameters such as service in present grade, annual performance report, residual service, evaluation process, etc., for which criteria are laid on.A policy on placement is applicable up-to Middle Management Scale. Officers in Senior Management and above grades are part of all India pool and their postings/placements across departments/offices are undertaken inter-alia as per bank’s operational requirement, rotational policy, etc.Complaints regarding alleged non adherence to laid down policies in this regard are dealt with as per extant guidelines and procedures.***
