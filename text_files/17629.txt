                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1291






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Placement Agencies Exporting Money fromUnemployed




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether in the country, especially in metropolitan cities, various agencies are functioning which extort money from unemployed people in the name of registration or by promising them to provide employment;(b)if so, the details thereof as on date;(c)whether the Government proposes to take action against such agencies and to formulate some legislation for their monitoring, if so, details thereof; and(d)if not, reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (d):	States/UTs have been advised to regulate the conduct of private placement agencies. Complaints, if any are received by respective State Governments/UT administration and are dealt under the provisions of IPC or others prevailing Acts under which such establisments are registered. Central Government and many State Governments have launched online job-portals so that job-seekers can connect with the employer and vice-versa free of cost. Central Government is also assisting State Governments to modernize employment exchanges for providing employment services to job seekers.******
