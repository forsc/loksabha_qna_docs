                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3177






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Hospitals Denying Treatment under Ayushman Bharat Yojana




P. C. Mohan














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has taken note of the fact that many private hospitals in Karnataka are refusing treatment to Ayushman Bharat – Arogya Karnataka card-holders;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the number of beneficiaries in Karnataka benefited under the scheme so far, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	The beneficiary of Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) can avail the cashless healthcare benefits of the scheme in all the empanelled public and private hospitals. However, two grievances in this regard relating to Karnataka were received on grievance portal and the same have been forwarded to the State Health Agency (SHA) Karnataka for resolution.  (c):	As on 02.12.2019, the total number of hospitals admissions under AB-PMJAY in the State of Karnataka is 4.13 lakhs. District-wise details of hospital admissions are at Annexure.
