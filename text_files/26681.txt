                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2494






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Financial Assistance under PMAY in Gujarat




Ratansinh Magansinh Rathod














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of houses for which financial assistance for construction has been provided under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) during the last two years;(b) 	if so, the details thereof, including the State of Gujarat district-wise;(c) 	whether there is any plan to increase the financial assistance; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)& (b) To achieve the objective of “Housing for All”, the Government is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) w.e.f. 1st April, 2016 in rural areas which is being facilitated by Ministry of Rural Development and Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana Urban (PMAY-U) since 25th June, 2015 in urban areas which is being facilitated by Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs.	A total target of 1,02,96,341 houses have been allocated to all States/UTsincluding targets of Gujarat under PMAY-G during the last two years i.e. financial year 2019-20 and 2020-21. Out of this, 97,22,861 houses have been sanctioned and first instalment has already been released to 90,29,860 beneficiaries. The district-wise details of number of houses for which at least first instalments have been released in the State of Gujarat during the last two years are given in Annexure.(c)& (d) At present, there is no proposal   to increase the financial assistance for construction of houses under PMAY-G. *****
