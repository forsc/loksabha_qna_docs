                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2748






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Railway Line project in Uttar Pradesh




Shiromani Ram













                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of railway line project between Bahraich and Khalilabad via Bhinga, Shravasti, Balrampur, Utraula, Domariyaganj, Mehdawal, Bansi and Kushinagar in Uttar Pradesh; (b) the steps taken to complete the said project at the earliest;  (c) the status of the said projects along with the estimated cost likely to be incurred in this regard; and(d)  the time by which the said project is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY ANDCONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2748 BY SHRI RAMSHIROMANI VERMA  AND SHRI VIJAY KUMAR DUBEY TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 10.03.2021 REGARDING RAILWAY LINE PROJECT IN UTTAR PRADESH(a) to (c): Bahraich-Khalilabad via Bhinga, Shravasti, Balrampur (Tulsipur), Uttraula, Domariyaganj, Bansi & Mehdawal (240 kms) new line project was included in the Budget 2016-17 and  sanctioned on 31.10.2018 at a cost of Rs. 4939.78 crore.  	Total 1060 Hectare land is required for this project.  Land plans for 779.246 hectare have been submitted to the District Land Acquisition Officer of Bahraich, Balrampur, Siddharthnagar and Sant Kabir Nagar for verification.  (d):	Timely completion of any Railway project depends on various factors like quick land acquisition by State Government, forest clearance by officials of forest department, shifting of infringing utilities, statutory clearances from various authorities, geological and topographical conditions of area, law and order situation in the area of project site, number of working months in a year for particular project site due to climatic conditions, priority of projects, etc. and all these factors affect the completion of the project. Therefore, confirmed timeline for completion of the project cannot be given. *****
