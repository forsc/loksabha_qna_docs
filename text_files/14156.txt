                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2911






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Extension of Trains to Vizianagaram




 Chandra Sekhar Bellana














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to extend service of Godavari Express, Tirumala Express and Ratnachal Express from Visakhapatnam to Vizianagaram; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c) Owing to operational constraints, at present, there is no proposal to extend 12727/12728 Visakhapatnam-Hyderabad Godavari Express,17487/17488 Cuddapah -Visakhapatnam Tirumala Express and 12717/12718 Visakhapatnam-Vijayawada Ratnachal Express upto Vizianagaram. However, extension of trains is an ongoing process over Indian Railways subject to operational feasibility, traffic demand, availability of resources, etc.*****
