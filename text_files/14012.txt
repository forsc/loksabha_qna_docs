                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2901






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Gokul Mines




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the incident in Gokul mine that took place on 14th August, 2018 in Umred of Bhivapur tehsil;(b) if so, whether the Government has conducted any inquiry in this matter along with the details of persons found guilty in this incident and action taken against them;(c) whether the services of M/s Khan Transport was banned after this incident and the reasons due to which the ban has been lifted on 17.4.2019;(d) whether the compensation has been given to the affected individuals/women and their families by the Government and by the mining authority; and(e) whether the Government has issued any order to the mine to provide compensation to the affected men, women and their families and if so, the action taken on such order?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)  (a) to (e): Information is being collected and will be laid on the Table of the House.
