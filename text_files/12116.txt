                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1032






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Anganwadi Centres




Srinivas Kesineni














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the number of Anganwadi centres constructed in the country so far; State-wise including Andhra Pradesh(b)	the details of funds allocated under the scheme; State-wise including Andhra Pradesh;(c)	whether there is a mechanism in place to monitor the efficient running of the Anganwadi centres and to ensure that record keeping and documentation is done properly;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	if not, the reasons for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b) :	Keeping in view the acute shortage of AWC buildings, Joint Guidelines by Ministry of Rural Development, Panchayati Raj and Women & Child Development were issued for construction of 4 lakh Anganwadi buildings across the country in convergence with Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS). Under convergence with MNREGS, funds for construction of 29941 AWC buildings in 2015-16 and 81447 AWC buildings in 2016-17 were released to States/UTs. State-wise details of funds released and units approved for construction of AWC buildings in convergence with MNREGS is at Annexure-I. However, subsequently under the revised scope of Anganwadi Services implemented from 01.12.2017, the central assistance for construction of new Anganwadi Centre building is available in rural areas in convergence with MGNREGS on reimbursable basis after completion of construction. Details of units approved, funds allocated during 2018-19 and 2019-20 to all States/UTs under the scheme including Andhra Pradesh is at Annexure-II. Under the said scope, State Government of Andhra Pradesh have constructed 3960 AWCs during 2018-19. (c) to (e) :	To monitor the efficient functioning of Anganwadi Centres, Government has introduced 5-tier monitoring and review mechanism at different levels (National/State/District/Block and Anganwadi level) for monitoring the status of implementation of the scheme. Government has issued guidelines on 31.03.2011 for constitution of Monitoring and Review Committees at different levels to review progress in implementation of the Scheme.
