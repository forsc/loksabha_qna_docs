                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

357






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Blue Revolution Scheme




Faizal P.P. Mohammed













                                                    Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the status of implementation of the Blue Revolution scheme, State/UT-wise; and(b) whether there is delay in implementation of the said scheme, if so, the reasons thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING:(Dr. L. MURUGAN)(a) and (b) :  The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying has implemented the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS): Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries for a period of five years from 2015-16 to 2019-20 and this scheme ended on 31st March, 2020. Under the CSS, central financial assistance of Rs.2577.49 crore had been released to various State Governments/Union Territories and other implementing agencies for development of fisheries and aquaculture. State/UT-wise details of the central funds released during the period from 2015-16 to 2019-20 under the CSS is annexed.  *****
