                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1067






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Air Pollution




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government has conducted any recent survey to find out major contributors of air pollution in the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has prepared any policy to promote electric vehicles as an alternate to diesel, petrol run vehicles in metro cities of the country; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)& (b)As per several studies conducted across the country the major contributors of air pollution inter alia include road dust suspension, vehicular emissions, biomass/garbage burning, construction& demolition activities and industrial emissions. (c)& (d) The Central Government has approved the proposal for implementation of scheme titled ‘Faster Adoption and Manufacturing of Electric Vehicles in India Phase II (FAME India Phase II)’ for promotion of Electric Mobility in the country. ***
