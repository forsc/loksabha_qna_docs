                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

563






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Sainik School in Telangana




Manne Srinivas Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government has received any proposal from the State Government of Telangana to establish a Sainik School at Narayanpet district;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the time by which it is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) No, Sir.  However, some Peoples Representative have written to the Ministry, but no prescribed proposal from the State Government has been received.(b) & (c):  Does not arise.*****
