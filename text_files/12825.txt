                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1651






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Kendriya Vidyalayas in Uttarakhand




Ajay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to open 1-2 Kendriya Vidyalaya in eachDevelopment Block of Uttarakhand to improve the condition of education;(b) if so, whether the Government has started the process for opening theKendriya Vidyalaya in each Development Block in Uttarakhand; and(c) if not, the time by which, the process to open the Vidyalaya is likely to bestarted?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c) Opening of Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) is a continuous process. KVs areopened primarily to cater to the educational needs of the wards of transferableCentral Government Employees including Defence personnel by providing acommon programme of education. Proposals for opening of new KVs are consideredonly if sponsored by Ministries or Departments of the Government of India/StateGovernments / Union Territories Administrations thereby committing resources forsetting up a new KV as well as the availability of necessary sanction of theGovernment. The proposals received from various sponsoring authorities for openingof new KVs also have to compete among other proposals under the “ChallengeMethod”.******
