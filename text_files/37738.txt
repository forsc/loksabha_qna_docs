                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5014






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Sainik Schools in Telangana




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Sainik schools that have been opened since 2014, year-wise and State-wise; (b) the reasons for not opening any new Sainik School in Telangana;(c) whether any communication has been sent to the State Government of Telangana regarding opening of the Sainik school; and(d) if so, the response received in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a)  09 Sainik Schools have been opened since 2014. Year-wise and State wise details of Sainik Schools opened since 2014 is placed at Annexure.(b) to (d):  Sainik Schools under the existing pattern are established at the request of State Governments.  State Govt of Telangana had requested for establishment of Sainik School at Warangal district on the erstwhile pattern of Sainik Schools based on which an in-principle approval was accorded and Memorandum of Agreement (MoA) was signed between Government of Telangana and Sainik Schools Society.  Further to that, no communication with respect to completion of infrastructure facilities has been received from Government of Telangana, thereafter.	Meanwhile, the Government approved the initiative for setting up of 100 new Sainik Schools in partnership mode with NGOs/Private Schools/State Governments in a graded manner class-wise, starting from Class 6 onward.  Based on applications received in this regard, ‘Telangana Social Welfare Residential School’ (TSWRS) located in Karimnagar district of Telangana has been approved as New Sainik School subject to signing of the Memorandum of Agreement by the TSWRS with the Sainik Schools Society.*****
