                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

331






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






ROB in Alwarthirunagari




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is considering to remove the Level Crossing gate LC-29 situated in Alwarthirunagari, Thoothukkudi district;(b)	if so, whether the Government will construct a Road over bridge (ROB) or under bridge at the same place of the level crossing; and(c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c):  Yes, Sir.  Level Crossing (LC) No. 29 situated in Alwarthirunagari, Thoothukkudi district is proposed for closure by diverting traffic by construction of road between LC No. 29 and LC  No. 30 which is 600 m from existing level crossing.  The Train Vehicle Units at LC No. 29 is only 5866 as on March’2019, which does not qualify for construction of ROB/RUB on cost sharing basis.*****
