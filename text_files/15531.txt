                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4301






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Sports Infrastructure under Khelo India Scheme




Bhagwanth Khuba














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is developing/creating sports infrastructure under Khelo India scheme in the country including Tripura and if so, the infrastructure created in the last five years along with the amount spent on it, State-wise; and  (b) whether about 7 proposals of creating sports facilities in the State of Karnataka under Khelo India scheme is pending with the Government and if so, the details thereof and the reason of delay in sanctioning the proposal, if any? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)  FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS  { SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }  (a) Yes, Sir. Under the revamped Khelo India Scheme, which is in operation from 2017-18, this Ministry has sanctioned 179 sports infrastructure projects in various States/Union Territories of the country, including in Tripura. State/Union Territory-wise details of the same, including the amount spent on it are placed at Annexure - I.  (b) A total of 11 project proposals have been received in this Ministry from Karnataka for creation of sports infrastructure under the Khelo India Scheme as detailed in Annexure - II. In view of the fact that Sports is a State subject and available funds under Khelo India Scheme being limited to meet the needs of projects already sanctioned, it has not been possible to sanction these projects. ANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 4301 TO BE ANSWERED ON 19.03.2020 REGARDING “SPORTS INFRASTRUCTURE UNDER KHELO INDIA SCHEME” ASKED BY SHRI BHAGWANTH KHUBA, HON’BLE MEMBER OF LOK SABHA *****
