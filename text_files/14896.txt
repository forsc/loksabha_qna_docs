                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3617






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Para-Medical Cadres in ESIC




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a)whether it is a fact that Employee’s State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) has recently declared certain para-medical cadres/posts as dying cadre; (b)if so, the details thereof including the total number of posts likely to be abolished; and(c)whether it is also a fact that present incumbents would not get their promotion as a result of this decision and if so, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b):	Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) has reviewed recruitment regulations (RRs) of various Para-Medical cadres to keep apace with development of Medical Services and conform to organizational and professional requirements. On review, the following posts were identified as dying cadres: Contd..2/-:: 2 ::1.	Tailor,2.	Linen Keeper,3.	Laundry Operator, 4.	Head Laundry Operator,5.	Laundry Supervisor,6.	Laundry Manager,7.	Boiler Attendant,8.	Auxiliary Nurse Midwife,9.	Jr. Technician (Orthotic), 10.	Electrician,11.	Dark Room Assistant,12.	Nursing Orderly,  The Corporation in its 178th meeting held on 13-09-2019 has approved to declare the above para-medical cadres/posts as dying cadres.(c):		The promotional avenues for the present incumbents of aforesaid posts have not been closed.  The direct recruitment for these posts will not be made in future against the vacancies arising, and after retirement of the present incumbents, the posts/cadres will stand abolished. The promotional avenue for the said post will be available as per the RRs of the hierarchy posts and the existing instructions in the matter.*****
