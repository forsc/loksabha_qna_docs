                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3906






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






PM-KISAN Scheme in Maharashtra




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)      the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan SammanNidhi (PM-KISAN) Yojana in Maharashtra till date, district-wise;(b)      the number of beneficiaries covered under the said scheme in Maharashtra along with the details thereof;(c)     whether the Government has transferred any amount into the accounts of farmers in Maharashtra during the lockdown period;(d)         if so, the details thereof, district-wise and if not, the reasons therefor;(e)    whether the Government proposes to transfer a few instalments into their accounts in advance considering the COVID-19 crisis; and (f)          if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ????  ???  ?????   ??????   ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):		Under PM-KISAN Scheme, funds are not allocated State-wise and the financial benefits of the Scheme is given directly into the bank accounts of intended beneficiaries through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) mode. 	         The district-wise details of number of beneficiaries and amount paid to them under PM-KISAN Scheme in the State of Maharashtra are placed at Annexure-I.(c) & (d):	       Yes, Sir. The district-wise details of benefits transferred to eligible beneficiaries under PM-KISAN Scheme in the State of Maharashtra during the COVID-19 period are placed at Annexure-II. (e) & (f):	     At present, there is no such proposal to transfer advance installments under PM-KISAN Scheme.
