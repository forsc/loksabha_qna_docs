                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

626






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Wheat Storage Capacity




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) 	the increase registered in wheat storage capacity of Food Corporation of India (FCI) in silos in the country during the last three years along with the details thereof including Maharashtra and Rajasthan; (b) 	the target fixed to increase the silo capacity in Maharashtra and Rajasthan in the next two years; and (c) 	the details thereof, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI RAMVILAS PASWAN)(a) to (c): 	The increase in storage capacity of wheat in silos of Food Corporation of  India (FCI) during  the last three years upto current year is given below:-Year	Silo Completion (LMT)	Remarks2016-17	NIL	-2017-18	0.25	Kotkapura (Punjab)2018-19	0.50	Barnala (Punjab)2019-20(upto 20.06.2019)	0.50	Patiala (Punjab)Total	1.25		A target of 50,000 MT silo capacity has been fixed by FCI at Borivali in Maharashtra. However, there is no target fixed for Rajasthan. ****
