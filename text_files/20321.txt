                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1630






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Funds for Minority Welfare Programmes




Kani K. Navas














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds allocated and spent on the welfare programme for the minorities in Tamil Nadu during 2015 to 2020, year and district-wise; (b) the amount spent on the scholarship of the students of minority communities in Tamil Nadu, year-wise; (c) the number of students benefited from this scholarship during the said period in Tamil Nadu; and (d) the classification of scholarship beneficiaries among six religious minorities during 2015 to  2020 viz. Muslims, Christians, Sikhs, Buddhists, Jains and Parsis for Tamil Nadu State?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a): Scheme-wise details of the funds allocated and released/utilised under various schemes to meet the scheme objectives during the last five years i.e. 2015-16 to 2019-20 is  at Annexure I. No state-wise allocation is done in the Budget. During the last five-years about 20.41 lakh scholarships amounting to Rs.524.48 crore have been sanctioned to the Minority students in Tamil Nadu under various scholarship schemes of the Ministry. Further, under Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK) projects of Rs. 4268.79 lakh involving central share of Rs.2561.27 lakh have been approved. The projects approved include:- Library building-1, Anganwadi centre-28, Toilet blocks in schools-2, Health related projects-23, Children home-1, Common service centre-4, Fish drying yard-2. An amount of Rs.1280.63 lakh have been released to state as installment of central share for implementation of above projects. (b) to (d): The community-wise number of scholarships and amount sanctioned under Scholarship  Schemes in the State of Tamil Nadu during the last five years is as at Annexure-II.*******
