                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2162






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Conversion of Metre Gauge into Broad Gauge




Akshaibar Lal














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government proposes to convert the Metre Gauge line from Bahraich to Nepalganj via Nanpara of North-Eastern Railway into Broad Gauge;  (b)  if so, the time by which the gauge conversion work is supposed to start; and   (c)  whether the Government proposes to convert the metre gauge line into broad gauge for about 24 Kms. between Nanpara to Mihipurva station which is the Tahseel headquarter and also an industrial town?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c):  Gauge conversion of Behraich to Mailani including Nanpara to Nepal Ganj was included in Railway Budget 2016-17 subject to requisite approvals. Presently, Gauge conversion of Bahraich-Nanpara-Nepalganj is under consideration and section from Nanpara to Mailani via Mihipurua falling under Dudhwa National Park, will be retained as Metre Gauge (MG) section.    *****
