                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

224






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Polytechnic Colleges and Hostels




Rajan Baburao  Vichare













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                








                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government of Maharashtra has requested to open Polytechnic Collegesand Hostels in the State if so,the number of Government Polytechnic Colleges andhostels opened in Maharashtra during the last three years;(b) the district-wise and year-wise details of the financial assistance provided to openPolytechnic Colleges during the last three years; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI. DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) No, Sir.(b) & (c ) Under “Scheme of Polytechnics” in Ministry of Skill Development andEntrepreneurship(MSDE), no request was made by State Government of Maharashtra to openGovernment Polytechnic Colleges and Hostels during the last three years.****
