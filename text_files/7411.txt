                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1101






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Dialysis Services under PMNDP




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:	(a)whether the Government has any proposal to establish peritoneal dialysis services under Pradhan Mantri National Dialysis Programme (PMNDP);(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the number of kidney patients likely to get the benefit of such services;(d) whether the various State Governments are asked to establish the same under their health services; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (e): 	Guidelines for establishing the Peritoneal Dialysis services under Pradhan Mantri National Dialysis Programme (PMNDP) has  been released by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare in October, 2019 and the same is available at https://nhm.gov.in/New_Updates_2018/PMNDP/Guidelines for PMNDP.pdf . All the States/UTs have been requested to include the Peritoneal Dialysis proposal in their Programme Implementation Plans (PIPs) under National Health Mission(NHM). No data regarding the number of  Kidney patients likely to benefit is maintained centrally.
