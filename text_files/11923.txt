                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

886






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Cleaning of River Ganga by NBCC




Chandan Singh














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has assigned the work to clean river Ganga in Bihar to NBCC; (b) if so, the salient features of this project; (c) whether the NBCC has started its work and if so, the details of the work done so far; (d) the quantum of funds likely to be spent on this project and the funds released so far; (e) the current status of works of removing silt from the bed of river, construction of cremation grounds, public toilets, improving drainage system in the villages and other works under this project; and (f) the agency assigned with the monitoring works of this project along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b) Yes Sir. National Mission for Clean Ganga has approved 10 projects in Bihar involving 28 Ghats, 4 Crematoria and 1 Promenade to be executed by NBCC. (c) & (d)  Yes Sir. The sanctioned cost of the projects assigned to NBCC is Rs.210.75 crore, out of which Rs.47.75 crore has so far been released to NBCC for execution of projects in Bihar. The physical and financial progress of the projects as on 31st December 2019 is Annexed.(e)  The construction of cremation grounds, public toilets in ghats is in process at different stages of the projects. National Mission for Clean Ganga (NMCG) has not undertaken  works related to removing of silt from the bed of river or project for improving drainage system in the villages.(f)  Projects are monitored by NMCG through a designated Project Management Consultant (PMC).
