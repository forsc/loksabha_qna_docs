                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3242






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Environmental Clearance for Airports in Andhra Pradesh




Geetha Viswanath Vanga














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has sanctioned environmental clearances for proposed/ongoing airports in Andhra Pradesh; (b)	if so, the details and the present status thereof, airport-wise; and (c)	if not, the reasons there for?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO) (a) to (c)	In the state of Andhra Pradesh, the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEF &CC)  has accorded environmental clearances stipulating environmental safeguards following the provisions of the EIA Notification, 2006 under the Environment (Protection) Act, 1986, to the following five (5) airport projects in the last 3 years:- I.	Extension of Runway at Rajahmundry Airport at Village Madhurapudi, District East Godavari, Andhra Pradesh in favour of M/s Airports Authority of India on 6th March, 2017.II.	Development of Green Field Airport at Village Kannamadakala & Pudicherla, Mandal Oravakallu, Kurnool District, Andhra Pradesh in favour of M/s Bhogapuram International Airport Corporation Ltd on 7th June, 2017.III.	Development of Greenfield Airport at village Damavaram and KK Gunta, Mandal Dagadarthi, District Nellore, Andhra Pradesh in favour of M/s Bhogapuram International Airport Corporation Ltd on 7th June, 2017.IV.	Extension of Runway 26 at Vijayawada Airport, Tehsil Gannavaram, Dist Krishna, Andhra Pradesh in favour of M/s Airport Authority of India on 10th August, 2017.V.	Development of Greenfield International Airport at Tehsil Bhogapuram, District Vizianagaram, Andhra Pradesh in favour of M/s Bhogapuram International Airport Corporation Ltd on 14th August, 2017.*****
