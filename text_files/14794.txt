                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3560






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Approval for Proposals




Prataprao Govindrao  Patil Chikhalikar














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of proposals received by the Union Government from the StateGovernment of Maharashtra for approval thereon during the last three years;(b) the total number of proposals approved/lying pending out of them; and(c) the time by which the pending proposals are likely to be approved by theGovernment?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c): The details of proposals received from the State Government ofMaharashtra under various schemes of the Ministry during the last three years is asunder:-<pre>(i) Samagra ShikshaUnder the centrally sponsored schemes of Sarva Shikhsa Abhiyan (SSA), RashtriyaMadhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan (RMSA), and Teacher Education, separate proposalswere received during the year 2016-17 and 2017-18 under their Annual Work Plan &Budget proposal. These schemes have been subsumed in the Integrated Schemefor School Education- Samagra Shiksha from 2018-19. The interventions proposedby States under the said schemes including Maharashtra are appraised, evaluatedand approved as per the norms of the scheme, physical & financial progress of theState and availability of funds in consultation with the concerned States/UTs.(ii) Mid Day Meal (MDM) SchemeUnder MDM, 11 proposals have been received from State of Maharashtra during thelast three years. There is no proposal pending.(iii)Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) SchemeUnder RUSA, 70 eligible proposals have been received from State of Maharashtraand approved by Project Approval Board (PAB) of RUSA during the last three years.There is no proposal pending.At present, no proposal for Opening of new medical, dental or Engineeringuniversities, new IITs, IIITs, IIIMs and IISERs have been received from the State ofMaharashtra.<pre>*****
