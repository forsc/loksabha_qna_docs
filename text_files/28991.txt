                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

450






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Collaboration for Digital Transactions under eNAM




Sukanta Majumdar













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has collaborated with a private bank for National Agriculture Market (eNAM) to enable digital collections and settlement of funds to various eNAM beneficiaries in the country, if so, the details thereof;(b)	the number of registered farmers and traders on eNAM and who have benefitted from the digital marketplace;(c) 	the total value of agri-produce traded on eNAM so far since its inception;(d) 	the achievements made by the Small Farmers Agri-Business Consortium (SFAC) set up as the nodal implementing agency by the Ministry since its inception;(e) 	whether the SFAC is implementing Central Sector Scheme (CSS) on “Formation & Promotion of 10000 FPOs” across the country, if so, the details thereof; and(f) 	the other steps being taken by the Government for development of agriculture and welfare of farmers in Bolangir constituency of Odisha? 	






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): 	Small Farmers Agribusiness Consortium(SFAC) under aegis of Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, Government of India has empanelled banks viz. Axis Bank Limited, Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited, HDFC Bank, Indusind Bank Limited & ICICI Bank for provisioning of payment & settlement services for National Agriculture Market (eNAM) transactions. The empanelment of above mentioned banks enable collections and settlement of funds to various eNAM registered beneficiaries across the country. (b): 	As on 31st October 2021, a total of 1.7 crore farmers and 1.98 lakh traders have registered themselves and many of them have benefitted from National Agriculture Market(e-NAM). (c): 	As on 31st October, 2021, the total value of Agri-produce traded on e-NAM is worth Rs. Rs. 1,50,664 Crore.(d) & (e): 	SFAC is one of the Implementing Agencies under the Central Sector Scheme for formation and promotion of 10,000 FPOs. Under the Scheme, SFAC has been allotted target for formation and promotion of 566 FPOs during 2020-21, out of which 442 FPOs have been registered so far.        Further, SFAC is also implementing schemes of Venture Capital Assistance(VCA) and National Agriculture Market (e-NAM). (f):	 As informed by Govt. of Odisha, the major schemes are implemented in the Bolangir District  of Odisha as at Annexure-I.
