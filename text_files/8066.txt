                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1713






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Underpass and Bridge on NH-65




Nayab Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received any letter/request/representation for construction of an underpass and a bridge at Dhanirampura bypass, Urunai road (Pehowa, Kurukshetra) at National Highway-65; and(b)	if so, the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	& (b) Yes Sir. A representation has been received for construction of underpass on Arunai road on Dhanirampura bypass. The present traffic volume on the cross road does not warrant grade separated intersection at this location. However, intersection with Arunai village road and Dhanirampura bypass has been properly designed as at-grade four-legged junction with all safety measures. *****
