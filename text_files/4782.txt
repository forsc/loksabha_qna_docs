                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4338






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Treatment of Muscular Dystrophy




Devji Mansingram Patel













                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    (Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the method of examination used to find out the reasons for muscular dystrophy, cerebral palsy, autism in patients along with the provisions for treatment of these diseases in the country; (b) the number of people suffering from Muscular Dystrophy in the country, State/UT-wise particularly in UP and Rajasthan; (c) whether the Government has any scheme or is contemplating to formulate any special scheme for treatment and rehabilitation of the persons suffering from Muscular Dystrophy and to provide financial assistance to them; (d) if so, the details thereof; and (e) other arrangements being made by the Government for such patients?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):     The diagnosis process of muscular dystrophy, cerebral palsy, autism starts with a detailed medical history and  physical examination followed with clinical investigations, consisting of laboratory tests like Enzyme tests, Electromyography, Genetic testing, Muscle biopsy, Heart-monitoring tests and Lung-monitoring tests. (b) to(e):    Information is being collected and will be placed in the House.………………..
