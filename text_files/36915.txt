                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3945






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Convert ITIs into Technology Training Institute




Sanjay Bhatia














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a)    whether the Government aims to increase the youth workforce through ITIs (Industrial Training Institutes) and convert ITIs into technology training institutes and if so, the details thereof;(b)    	whether the Government has such proposal in the Parliamentary Constituency, Karnal; and(c)    	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) 	Under Skill India Mission, more than 20 central Ministries/Departments are running schemes/programs to impart skill training to the youth.  Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is delivering skill training to youth through skill development schemes viz. Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS), Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) and Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS) through Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs). The Governmentis also encouraging ITIs to participate in the Dual System of Training program and On the Job Training (OJT) so that the youth in the ITIs are industry-ready. The State wise ITI detail along with trainee count is given at Annexure –I.The ITIs are under the administrative control of State Government. Establishment and administration of ITIs comes under the jurisdiction of State Government, while the policies such as setting the criterion for affiliation, conducting the examination along with certification and designing curriculum are the responsibilities of Central Government.(b & c) There is no proposal from State Governments including the State of Haryana to convert ITIs into Technology Training Institutes.
