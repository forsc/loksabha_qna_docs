                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1277






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Financial Assistance to prevent Cancer and TB




Chunni Lal Sahu














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the details of international financial assistance sought and received to prevent cases of Cancer and TB in the country during each of the last three years and the current year;(b) whether funds have been allocated to certain Non-Government Organisations (NGOs) for the said purpose; and(c) if so, the details thereof across the country during the said period, State/UT wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	Financial assistance has been received from Global Fund for AIDS, TB & Malaria (GFATM) for Revised National Tuberculosis Control Programme (RNTCP), during each of the last three years and the current year as per details at Annexure-I. 	The International financial assistance covers the following TB control activities:   (i) Procurement of Second Line Drugs(ii) Strengthening of RNTCP Lab Network(iii) Strengthening of RNTCP Information Technology  systems.(iv) Drug Resistant TB Patient Direct Benefit Transfer(DBT) incentive support(v) Active Case Finding(vi) Engagement with Private SectorRegarding cancer, technical collaboration exists with international organizations.(b) & (c):	 The details of funds allocated to Civil Society Organizations for the said purpose is at Annexure-II.The fund allocated for during the said period has been done at the National level and there is no specific State/UT wise allocation.
