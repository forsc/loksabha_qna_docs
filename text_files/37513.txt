                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4888






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Special Integrated Infectious Diseases Hospitals




Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah













                                                    Pushpendra Singh Chandel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to set up Special Integrated Infectious Disease Hospitals for the prevention and treatment of infectious diseases; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the details of the funds allocated and utilized for the prevention of infectious diseases during the last two years, State/UT-wise including Uttrakhand and Bundelkhand in Uttar Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)  (a) to (c):	U¬¬nder PM-Ayushman Bharat Health Infrastructure Mission, Government envisages to establish 50/75/100 bedded Critical Care Hospital Blocks in 602 districts, to provide diagnosis and treatment services for infectious diseases and to respond adequately during the public health challenges posed by the pandemics and in periods of epidemic outbreaks.The financial total outlay for establishment of Critical Care Hospital Blocks under PM-ABHIM scheme during the period from FY 2021-22 to FY 2025-26 is Rs 19064.80 Crore. As  public health & hospitals is a State subject, the implementation of the scheme lies with respective State /UT Governments. However, Government of India, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, provides technical and financial support to the states/UTs including the states of Uttarakhand & Uttar Pradesh for the implementation of PM-ABHIM.  Allocation for establishment of 14 Critical Care Blocks in Uttarakhand (7 under PM ABHIM & 7 under ECRP-II) and 74 districts of Uttar Pradesh is provided under the scheme. ****
