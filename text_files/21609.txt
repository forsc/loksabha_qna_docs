                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2867






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Roll out of Second Dose of Vaccine




A. Ganeshamurthi













                                                    S. Ramalingam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Union Government has rolled out the second dose of vaccines against COVID-19 across the country;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the total number of beneficiaries who have received the second dose as on date,         State/UT-wise;(d) whether the roll out of the second dose vaccines against the COVID-19 across the country was off to a slow start and only a meager number of healthcare workers received the second dose; and(e) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c): The roll-out of COVID-19 vaccination drive has begun from 16th January 2021. All those beneficiaries who have completed 28 days since the receipt of 1st dose of COVID-19 vaccine are eligible for 2nd dose. Till 8th March 2021, a total of 37.61 lakh beneficiaries have received 2nd dose of COVID-19 vaccine. The State/UT-wise details are at Annexure.(d) & (e): The roll-out of the second dose of COVID-19 vaccine has been satisfactory. As on 8th March 2021, 66.67% of the eligible Health Care Workers have received 2nd dose of COVID-19 vaccine.
