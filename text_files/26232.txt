                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1961






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Ragging in Medical Colleges




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                                   Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government is aware of the inhuman behaviour and brutal deaths of the medical students in medical colleges and other medical institutions due to alleged ragging;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the complaints received in this regard during last five years, medical colleges/institution/year-wise as on date;(c) the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to check alleged ragging in various medical colleges and medical institutions in the country; and(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH ANDFAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) & (b): The details of the complaints pertaining to alleged ragging received by the erstwhile Medical Council of India (MCI) and the National Medical Commission (NMC) in the last five years is enclosed as Annexure. (c) & (d): The following steps have been taken by the Government / NMC to curb the menace of ragging in medical colleges:i.	The erstwhile MCI with the prior approval of Central Government, notified the Medical Council of India (Prevention and Prohibition of Ragging in Medical Colleges/Institutions) Regulations, 2009. These Regulations seek to provide for elimination of ragging in all its forms from medical colleges/institutions in the country by preventing its occurrence and punishing those who indulge in ragging through provisions of these Regulations and also of appropriate law in force.ii.	The erstwhile MCI and the NMC send a circular every year to all the medical colleges for furnishing information with regard to constitution of anti-ragging committee in the college, details of the cases of ragging reported and action taken thereon.iii.	The colleges are also asked to provide specific information on anti-ragging in their admission brochures / prospectus / booklets.iv.	Also, the colleges are advised regarding installation of CCTV Cameras in all the vulnerable places of colleges, hospital and hostels and placement of anti-ragging posters and hoarding in different parts of medical colleges / hostels.v.	The colleges are also advised to ensure submission of online undertaking by each student and every parent in an online portal of University Grant Commission. vi.	The NMC has also created a portal where students can register their complaints pertaining to ragging.
