                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3126






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Four Laning of NH in Odisha




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is a long delay in completion of four laning of 	National Highway (NH) that passes through Sambalpur 	Parliamentary Constituency in Odisha;(b)	if so, the reasons therefor;(c)	whether any time frame is fixed to complete this project with the 	prescribed quality and standard; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d)       Total 4 works for widening/upgradation to 4-lane have been taken up, through National Highways Authority of India (NHAI), in the Sambalpur Parliamentary Constituency in Odisha. Out of 4 works, Provisional Commercial Operation Date (PCOD) has been issued in 2 works i.e., Sambalpur Bargarh-Odisha/Chattisgarh Border of NH-6 and Binjabahal to Telebani of NH-6, on 21.06.2016 and 27.05.2021 respectively. two, works are in progress, their details such as length, progress and reasons for delay etc. are as under:S. No.	Name of Project	Length(in Km)	Physical Progress/ likely date of completion	Reasons for Delay1.	4-laning of Angul-Sambalpur of NH-42 from Km 112.00 to Km 265.00 on EPC Mode	153	70%/ 30.06.2022	Delay in land acquisition, forest diversion, financial issues of contractor and COVID pandemic etc.2.	4-laning of Telebani - Sambalpur of NH-6 from Km 493.30 to Km 521.30 & Km 545.176 to Km 567.40 on EPC Mode	50.75	75.5%/07.05.2022	Delay in land acquisition, forest diversion, NCLT issue of original contractor and COVID pandemic etc. *****
