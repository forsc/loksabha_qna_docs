                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

37






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Operational Guidelines for PCI Scheme




Pritam Gopinath Munde













                                                    Girish Bhalchandra Bapat
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                     Rahul Ramesh Shewale
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has issued the operational guidelines recently for the Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for the textiles sector; (b) if so, the details of the outlay approved therefor; (c) whether the Government has set up an Empowered Group of Secretaries (EGoS) to monitor the progress of the scheme and take appropriate action to ensure that the expenditure is within the prescribed outlay; and if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the EGoS is also empowered to make any changes in the modalities of the scheme and address any issue related to genuine hardship that may arise during the course of implementation; (e) if so, the details in this regard; and (f) the time period fixed for the implementation of the scheme?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) & (b):	Operational guidelines for the Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for Textiles were issued on 28.12.2021. The approved outlay of the scheme is Rs. 10,683 crore.(c) to (e):	Empowered Group of Secretaries (EGoS), as constituted and Notified vide gazette Notification No. P 36017/144/2020-Investment & Promotion dated 10.06.2020 issued by the DPIIT will monitor the implementation of the scheme. The composition of the EGoS for monitoring of PLI for Textiles will be as under:i.	Cabinet Secretary, Chairpersonii.	CEO, NITI Aayog, Memberiii.	Secretary, Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade, Member Convenoriv.	Secretary, Department of Commerce, Memberv.	Secretary, Department of Revenue, Membervi.	Secretary, Department of Economic Affairs, Membervii.	Secretary, Ministry of TextilesThe EGoS chaired by the Cabinet Secretary will monitor the progress of this PLI scheme; undertake periodic review of the outgo under the Scheme; ensure uniformity with other PLIs and take appropriate action to ensure that the expenditure is within the prescribed outlay. EGoS is also empowered to make any changes in the modalities of the scheme, and address any issue related to genuine hardship that may arise during the course of implementation, within the overall financial outlay of Rs 10,683 crore.(f):	The scheme shall be valid upto 2029-30. The gestation period of the scheme is two (2) years i.e. FY: 2022-23 to FY: 2023-2024.***
