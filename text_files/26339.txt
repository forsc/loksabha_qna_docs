                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

182






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Beneficiaries under PMUY in UP




Rekha Arun Verma














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the district-wise details of beneficiaries under the Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana(PMUY) in Uttar Pradesh;(b) the details of the number of LPG connections and the total consumption of LPG inUttar Pradesh during the last three years and the current year; and(c) the details of steps being taken by the Government to meet the demand for LPGconnections and consumption of LPG?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI HARDEEP SINGHPURI)(a) to (c) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) TO (C) OF THE LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. 182 BY SHRIMATI REKHA VERMA TO BE ANSWEREDON 2ND AUGUST, 2021 REGARDING ‘BENEFICIARIES UNDER PMUY IN UP’.(a) District-wise details of beneficiaries of Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana(PMUY) inUttar Pradesh are given at Annexure.(b) Details of number of LPG connections and LPG consumption of LPG in UttarPradesh during the last three years and the current year are as under :-Year Domestic LPG consumer as onend of the period (in crore)Total LPG sale by OMCs(in TMT)2018-19 3.76 31662019-20 4.04 35322020-21 4.23 3820April-June20214.25 933(c) Release of LPG connections is a continuous process. LPG distributors are underinstructions to immediately register any request for new LPG connection. As on01.07.2021, there are 29.11 crore domestic LPG customers in the country. Further,Hon''ble Finance Minister, during her budget speech on 1st February, 2021, hasannounced to provide 1 crore more LPG connections under PMUY in addition to 8 croreLPG connections already released. The modalities for releasing 1 crore LPGconnections under PMUY have already been finalised and have been communicated tothe Oil Marketing Companies(OMCs).The procedure for obtaining LPG connection has also been simplified. Further, to meetthe increased demand/consumption of LPG, OMCs continuously review thedistributorship network and bottling capacity and increase them, if required. From 2014,the Government has improved supply infrastructure in LPG sector to meet the increaseddemand of LPG. Details are as under :-Description As on 01.04.2014 As on 01.07.2021No. of LPG distributorships 13896 25116No. of bottling plant 186 199Bottling capacity 13235 TMTPA 21309 TMTPANo. of Import Terminals 11 14Import capacity 12.02 MMTPA 16.8 MMTPALength of LPG pipeline 2311 KM 4093 KMPipeline capacity 4.53 MMTPA 11.03 MMTPATotal LPG consumption 17.6 MMT (2014-15) 27.7 MMT (2020-21)-------
