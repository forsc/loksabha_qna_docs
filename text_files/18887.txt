                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

401






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Temptation of High Salary to Railway Employees




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit













                                                    Dileshwar Kamait
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that railway employees are being offered temptation of high salary by private companies as published in Hindustan newspaper on 20 October, 2020;(b) if so, the steps taken by the Government in this regard;(c) whether the Government is taking positive steps to protect Government Organisations like Railways or to protect its personnel from such temptation in the future; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY ANDCONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)  No such incidence has been reported.(b)  Does not arise.(c)   Does not arise.(d)   Does not arise.*****
