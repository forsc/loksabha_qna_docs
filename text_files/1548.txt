                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1540






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Vocational Training under Jan Shikshan Sansthans




Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government proposes to waive off fee for SC/ST candidates joining vocational training under Jan Shikshan Sansthans;(b)	If so, the details thereof;(c)	The details of the Jan Shikshan Sansthans which are currently running in the country; and(d)	Whether the government plan to open Jan Shikshan Sansthans in the coming years and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b) 	Yes, Sir. The fee for SC/ST candidates joining vocational training under Jan Shikshan Sansthan Scheme has been waived off with effect from 4th June, 2019(c)	Presently 247 Jan Shikshan Sansthans are operational in the country as per the list attached in Annexure-A.(d)	Yes, Sir. The Government is in process of opening 83 new Jan Shikshan Sansthans in the country during the current financial year as per the list attached in Annexure-B.
