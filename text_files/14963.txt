                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3548






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Tourism Infrastructural Projects under PIDDC




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the number of theme-based tourist circuits completed and their expenditure under the PRASAD scheme since 2014, especially of rural circuits; (b) 	the State-wise list of the amount of central financial assistance provided to States/UTs for tourism projects identified during the last three years; and (c) 	the State-wise list of the number of tourism infrastructure projects completed since 2014 under the Projects Infrastructure Development for Destination and Circuits (PIDDC)?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a):	Ministry of Tourism under its ongoing scheme viz. “Integrated Development of Theme Based Tourist Circuit in the Country, Swadesh Darshan (SD)” provides Financial Assistance to the State Governments/U.T. Administrations for tourism infrastructure development under its 15 theme-based tourism circuits. Details of the projects completed under this scheme is given below:“Development of Circuit at Kakinada - Hope Island - Coringa Wildlife Sanctuary - Passarlapudi - Aduru - S Yanam – Kotipally” approved in the year 2014-15 as Coastal Circuit at the cost of Rs.67.84 Crore. The amount Rs.64.44 Crore has been released so far.Details of the projects sanctioned under Rural Circuits of Swadesh Darshan scheme is as below: i.	“Development of Gandhi Circuit: Bhitiharwa- Chandrahia- Turkaulia” in Bihar sanctioned in 2017-18 at the cost of Rs. 44.65 Crore. The amount Rs.21.24 Crore has been released so far.ii.	“Development of Malanad Malabar Cruise Tourism Project” in Kerala sanctioned in 2018-19 at the cost of Rs.80.37 Crore. “Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)”of Ministry of Tourism is not a Circuit based scheme. (b):	The State-wise list of the amount of central financial assistance provided to States/UTs for tourism projects identified and sanctioned under schemes viz., Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD) and “Integrated Development of Theme Based Tourist Circuit in the Country, Swadesh Darshan (SD)"  during the last three years is annexed.(c):	The scheme “Product Infrastructure Development of Tourist Destination and Circuits (PIDDC)” was delinked from Union Support on 29.06.2015. In the year 2014-15 no project was sanctioned under the scheme.*******
