SHRI ADALA PRABHAKARA REDDY:           DR. SHRIKANT EKNATH SHINDE:  SHRI SRIDHAR KOTAGIRI:  SHRIMATI GODDETI MADHAVI:  SHRI N. REDDEPPA:  SHRI MAGUNTA SREENIVASULU REDDY:  SHRI UNMESH BHAIYYASAHEB PATIL:  DR. HEENA GAVIT:  SHRI M.V.V. SATYANARAYANA: DR. SUJAY RADHAKRISHNA VIKHE PATIL:  SHRI DHAIRYASHEEL SAMBHAJIRAO MANE:Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the details and the total number of distribution camps organized under the Rashtriya Vayoshri Yojana (RVY) in the country, State-wise particularly in Andhra Pradesh; (b) the number of assistive living devices distributed and the total number of beneficiaries under the scheme during the last three years and the current year, Statewise particularly in Andhra Pradesh; (c) the estimated expenditure for the scheme during the year 2021-2022; (d) whether the Government intends to further introduce such schemes; and  (e) if so, the details thereof?

(c): The estimated expenditure for the scheme during the Financial Year 2021-22 is Rs. 50 Crore.
