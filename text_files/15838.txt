                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4501






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Education and Research under AYUSH




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether AYUSH, the governmental body that is purposed with developing, education and research in the field of alternative medicine, is substantially funded by the Government; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) the scheme-wise plan, outlay and expenditure under department of AYUSH for the year 2019-20; (d) whether the Government has made recommendations under 15th Finance Commission for considerable grants to AYUSH departments in the country, State/UT-wise and if so, the details thereof; and (e) the details of the performance of Kerala in implementing the various schemes under AYUSH?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):    No. (b):The comparison of projected requirement and allocated funds for this Ministry for the last four years are as under:- Year	Projected Demand	Allocated Demand	% of allocation w.r.t. projection2016-17	2111.52	1326.20	62.802017-18	2478.78	1428.65	57.642018-19	2796.93	1626.37	58.732019-20	4469.59	1939.76	43.40Contd…..-2-(c): 	The scheme-wise Plan, Outlay and expenditure for year 2019-20 is at Annexure-A.(d):   Yes.   The recommendation made under 15th Finance Commission for the grant of additional fund is at Annexure-B.(e):     For the promotion and development of AYUSH System the Ministry of AYUSH is implementing through its  flagship Centrally Sponsored Scheme namely National AYUSH Mission. As per the proposal received from the Government of Kerala, an amount of Rs. 7113.72 lakh has been released for different activities of National AYUSH Mission. Furthre, as per expenditure reported by the State Government of Kerala, an amount of Rs. 5227.138 lakhs has been utilized till date.
