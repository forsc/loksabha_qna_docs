                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2148






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Guidelines to Visit Tourist Places




Rakesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the tourist places of the country are witnessing huge footfall of tourists; (b) 	whether the Covid-19 guidelines are being kept at bay at these places; (c) 	whether the Corona report is not being made mandatory for visiting these tourist places; (d) 	if so, whether the Government has taken action in this regard; and (e) 	whether the Government has issued any guidelines in this regard; and (f) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a):	Ministry of Tourism does not maintain data on tourist place wise footfall of tourists. However, State/UT wise details of Domestic Tourist Visits (DTVs) and Foreign Tourist Visits (FTVs) during last three years are at Annexure. (b) to (f):	Development and Promotion of tourist places is primarily the responsibility of the State Government/Union Territory Administration. However, Ministry of Tourism has formulated Operational Recommendations (SoPs) for the different segments of Travel and Hospitality Industries such as Hotels, Restaurant, B&B & Home Stays and Travel Agents, Tour Operators, Tourist Guides etc. based on the guidelines issued by Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Govt. of India for the Covid-19 pandemic. The same has been circulated to Tourism Secretaries of all States/UTs and Travel, Trade & Hospitality Associations.********
