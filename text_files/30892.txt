                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2469






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






National Green Corps Programme




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of eco-clubs in Maharashtra State, district wise operating under the National Green Corps programme;(b)	the details of the utilisation of the funds allocated under the National Green Corps programme since its inception;(c)	the details of the districts which have failed to utilize the allotted funds; and(d)	the total funds returned to the Ministry since the inception of the programme till date, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)  	District-wise number of Eco-clubs in Maharashtra, as provided by the State Nodal Agency for implementation of National Green Corps (NGC) programme in the State, is given at Annexure-1.(b) The National Green Corps (NGC) programme is supported under the Central Sector Scheme - Environment Education, Awareness and Training (EEAT). There is no separate budget allocation for the said programme. However, details of utilization of funds under EEAT scheme since the inception of NGC programme is given as Annexure-2.(c)& (d)District-wise information regarding utilization of funds is not maintained by the Ministry. However, Utilization Certificates are issued by the State Government [State Nodal Agencies] based on overall utilization of funds by the State. Till the year 2018-19, unutilized amount wasin general permitted to be carried forward to be utilized in the next financial year. Funds returned to the Ministry during the subsequent years 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 (as on 08.12.2021) are Rs. 0.97 crore, Rs. 3.88 crore and Rs. 1.61crore respectively. This returned amount includes unutilized grant-in-aid out of that released for various activities under EEAT scheme, including NGC programme, as well as bank interest earned on the released grant-in-aid.*****
