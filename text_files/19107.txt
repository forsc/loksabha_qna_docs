                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

638






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






House Tax Relaxation




Haji Fazlur Rehman














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government have any scheme to provide rebate in the house tax for the population residing in backward and slum habitations in selected smart cities under the municipal corporation areas of the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether funds allocated under Smart City Mission(SCM) for the development of smart wards in slums and backward haitations are being spent on developed wards in Saharanpur Municipal Corporation in Uttar Pradesh; and(d)    if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                     (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	& (b) :   No, Sir. House Tax is a State Government/Urban Local Body subject and hence, no such scheme is envisaged at the level of Central Government.(c) & (d) : Saharanpur Smart City Limited has informed that 21 wards have been selected for Area Based Development (ABD) under their Smart City Plan. Saharanpur Smart City is using available funds for retrofit developments of the area selected for ABD and certain pan city projects that impact the life of citizens across the city.                                 ……
