                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1302






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Construction of Anganwadi Centres under MGNREGS




Sanjay Seth














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether construction of Anganwadi Centres under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) is permissible;(b) 	if so, the number of Anganwadi Centres constructed under MGNREGS in the State of Jharkhand during the last five years and the current year, year-wise and district-wise; and(c) 	the number of Anganwadi centres that the Ministry is planning to construct during the current year and in 2021-22 in the State of Jharkhand, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b)   As per Para 4 (I) of the Schedule–I of the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA), construction of buildings for Anganwadi Centres is permissible under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS). Year-wise and district-wise details of Anganwadi Centers constructed under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in the State of Jharkhand during the last five years and the current financial year 2021-22 (as on 22.07.2021) is given at Annexure-I. (c)	Mahatma Gandhi NREGS is demand driven wage employment programme with bottom up planning. The Ministry does not plan or set any target for any specific work (including Anganwadi Centres) under the Scheme for any States/UTs. The Gram Panchayat is responsible for identification of works in the Gram Panchayat area to be taken under the scheme as per recommendations of the Gram Sabha, and for executing and supervising such works. Number of Anganwadi Centers ongoing districts-wise (as on 22.07.2021) is listed at Annexure-II.*****
