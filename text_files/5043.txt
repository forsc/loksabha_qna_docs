                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4731






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Guidelines of UGC




Rajkumar Chahar














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether private universities established by the Centre are governed by therules/directions/Regulations/notifications/Guidelines of University Grants Commission(UGC);(b) whether these Universities are given some kind of governance/rules mentionedabove for the technical education/degrees by their respective councils such as MedicalCouncil of India, Pharmacy Council of India or AICTE etc., if so, the details thereof;(c) whether private universities ask the students seeking admission to their courses todeposit their Migration Certificates/Class X and XII Original Certificates and Marksheetsand if so, the details thereof; and(d) whether the Government has taken any action against the institutions and if so, thedetails thereof along with the steps taken by the Government in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): No, Sir. No Private University has been established by the Central Government. Allthe existing Private Universities have been established by the Act of State Legislaturesconcerned. These Private Universities are regulated by University Grants Commission(UGC) (Establishment of and Maintenance of Standards in Private Universities)Regulations, 2003.(b): As per the UGC Regulations, 2003, every Private university is required to fulfil theminimum criteria in terms of programmes, faculty, infrastructural facilities, financialviability, etc., as laid down from time to time by the UGC and other concerned statutorybodies such as All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Bar Council of India(BCI), Dental Council of India (DCI), Indian Nursing Council (INC), Medical Council ofIndia (MCI), National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE), Pharmacy Council of India(PCI), etc.(c): The UGC has issued Notification on Refund of Fees and Non-Retention of OriginalCertificates in October, 2018. As per the UGC’s Notification, no Higher EducationalInstitutions including Private University can insist upon the students to submit theiroriginal academic and personal certificates, like, mark-sheets, school leaving certificatesand other such documents, at the time of submitting admission form, but thesubmission of self-attested copies thereof shall be mandatory.(d): On receipt of complaints regarding retention of original certificates by anyUniversity, UGC asks the University concerned to take necessary action in the matter, asper the UGC Notification.*****
