                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1303






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Rural Development Schemes




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether job card is provided to each person seeking employment under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) and if so, the details thereof along with the number of job card holders, State-wise;(b) 	whether social audit has been conducted thereto, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) 	whether the Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana- National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM) is being implemented in the State of Maharashtra and if so, the details thereof, district-wise; and(d) 	the details of works scrutinized and sanctioned by the National Rural Road Development Agency (NRRDA) during the last two years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven wage employment programme. A rural household, adult members of which agree to do unskilled manual work, is entitled for hundred days of wage employment. Mahatma Gandhi NREGA entitles adult members of every household residing in any rural area and willing to do unskilled manual work for issuance of a job card. States/UTs-wise details of number of job cards issued under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS so far are at Annexure-I.(b):     Social Audit Units have been established across 26 States and 1 Union Territory. In FY 2020-21, States/UTs are conducting Regular Social Audits and Concurrent Social Audits. During FY 2020-21, a total of 16 States/UTs have conducted Regular Social Audits. The details are given at Annexure-II. During FY 2020-21, a total of 17 States/UTs have conducted Concurrent Social Audits. The details are given at Annexure-III.(c):	The Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana - National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM) is implemented in the all 351 blocks of 34 districts of Maharashtra. As on 31st January, 2021, a total of 50.67 lakh households are mobilised into 4.91 lakh Self Help Groups (SHGs) and further federated into 21,224 Village Organisations (VOs) and 868 Cluster Level Federations (CLFs) in the State of Maharashtra. District wise details are at Annexure-IV.(d):	A total of 17,308 proposals of road works and Long Span Bridges (LSBs) of value Rs. 77397.78 crore were scrutinized by the National Rural Infrastructure Development Agency (NRIDA) and 17,319 proposals of road works and LSBs, including the proposals received during previous year(s) worth Rs.71,569.75 crore were cleared under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during the last two years and the current financial year.  ********
