                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3664






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Funds Allocated for Various Cultural Programmes and Projects




Bharti Pravin Pawar














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the funds allocated by the Government as assistance to various cultural programmes and projects in the country including Maharashtra and the amount utilized in this regard during the last two years;(b)	the details of the ongoing cultural programmes and projects in Maharashtra;(c)	the details of the projects completed in time and the projects lagging behind schedule along with the reasons for such delay;(d)	the amount of funds released to the cultural centres of Maharashtra during the last three years along with the details thereof; and(e)	the purposes for which the said funds were utilised by the said centres along with its details?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	:  The Ministry of Culture provides assistance for various cultural programmes and projects in the country  through various central sector schemes.  These schemes are also applicable for the State of Maharastra. Funds allocated/utilized under various central sector schemes during the last two years are as under:Year	Fund  allocated  Fund utilized        (Rs. in crores)	 (Rs. in crores)2017-18	61.00	43.042018-19	65.76	48.33(b) to (c): Details of the projects in Maharashtra are at annexure:(d)  to (e):  Fund released to South Central Zone cultural Centre, Nagpur  during the last three year are  as underYear	Amount (in lakh)2016-17	588.432017-18	726.382018-19	703.16ANNEXURE IN REPLY TO PART (b) to (c) OF THE LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 3664 FOR 16.03.2020	Name of the scheme	Projects of Maharashtra	Current status	Remarksa. Indian Numismatic, Historical and Cultural Research Foundation, Raheja Centre, Nariman Point, Mumbai	Project has been completed c.
