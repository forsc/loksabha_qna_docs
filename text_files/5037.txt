                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4707






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Mid Day Meal in Kerala




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the implementation of mid day meal project inpre and upper primary schools in Kerala;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has sanctioned any amount to provide mid day meal forschool children in Kerala;(d) if so, the details of funds released during the last three years and if not, the reasonstherefor; and(e) the steps taken by the Government in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): The National Programme of Mid-Day Meal in Schools, popularly known as theMid-Day Meal Scheme, is an on-going Centrally-Sponsored Scheme which covers allschool children studying in Classes I-VIII of Government, Government-AidedSchools, Special Training Centres including Madarsas and Maqtabs supported underSamagra Shiksha. In Kerala, mid-day meal is provided to all the eligible children inClasses I-VIII under the scheme. Mid-day meal is also provided to pre-primarychildren with the resources of State Government.(c) to (e): The details of funds released during the last three years to State Government ofKerala under the Scheme is as follows:<pre>StateCentral Assistance Released (Rs. In lakhs)2016-17 2017-18 2018-19Kerala 17781.46 32978.36* 19856.63</pre>*This includes Central Assistance of Rs. 12658.26 lakh for construction ofkitchen-cum-stores.*******
