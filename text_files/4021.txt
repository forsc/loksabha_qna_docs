                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3792






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Promotion of Technology in Agriculture




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government has any proposal  to  promote  technology  in agricultural  sector  and  if  so,  the  details thereof;(b)	 whether there is any proposal for training programmes for the awareness of e-agriculture;(c)	  whether  any  specific  institution has been set up to promote e-agriculture in various States of the country and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	 the details of the farmers who got e-agriculture training during the last one year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	 Government of India is already promoting technology in agriculture sector through various Schemes and Programs as per details indicated at Annexure 1.(b):	 To create awareness on e-agriculture, National Institute of Agricultural Extension Management (MANAGE) and Regional Extension Education Institutes (EEIs) have been conducting training programmes relating to Information Communication Technologies (ICTs) in Agriculture, e-governance, e-extension, knowledge management, social media for capacity building of extension officials across the country to enable them to deliver effective extension services to the farming community. (c) & (d):	 No specific institution has been established to promote e-agriculture. However, the details of programmes on e-agriculture conducted by MANAGE and EEIs for extension  functionaries and cluster resource persons,  who are from farming community along with   their numbers are given at Annexure II.
