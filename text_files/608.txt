                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

630






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Fishing Permit to Foreign Ships in Indian Waters




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government will reconsider the existing policy of permitting fishing by huge foreign ships off the Indian Coast to help local fishermen;b)	if so, the details thereof;c)	whether the Government assessed the serious impact of foreign ships fishing within Indian territory and consequent unemployment of Indian fishermen , if so, the details thereof;d)	whether various fishermen associations raised objections in this regard; ande)	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken to redress their grievances?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	& (b). There is no policy exists in the Department of Fisheries, Government of India for permitting fishing by huge foreign ships off the Indian Coast.(c)	, (d) & (e) Doesn’t arise.
