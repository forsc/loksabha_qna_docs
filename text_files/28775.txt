                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

96






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Expenditure on COVID-19 Vaccination




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the details of expenditure incurred by the Government on COVID-19 vaccination, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra; and (b) the details of expenditure on vaccine doses supplied by the Government free of cost in Wardha and Amaravati districts of Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a)	& (b) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. 96* FOR 3RD DECEMBER, 2021(a) & (b) In the current financial year i.e. 2021-22, INR 35,000 crore has been budgeted for implementation of COVID-19 Vaccination Programme. As on 27th November 2021, an expenditure of INR 19,675.46 crore has been incurred against this allocation which has been utilized for procurement of COVID-19 vaccine for free of cost supply to State/UTs. The State/UT-wise details of COVID-19 vaccine supply is at Annexure-1.The allocation and distribution of vaccines from State to Districts is managed by the State/UT Government. As per the information received from State Government of Maharashtra as on 25th November 2021, a total of 13,74,120 & 24,50,680 vaccine doses have been supplied to the districts of Wardha and Amravati respectively and total Expenditure for COVID-19 vaccination for State of Maharashtra till Oct 2021 is Rs. 818.74 Lakh out of which the budget allocated to Wardha district is 24.13 Lakh & Expenditure incurred is 11.91 Lakhs (49.36%) and budget allocated to Amravati district is 52.67 Lakhs & Expenditure incurred is 30.10 Lakh (57.15%).
