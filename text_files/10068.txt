                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3631






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Beneficiaries under National Food Security Scheme




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of beneficiaries covered under the National Food Security Scheme in the country, State/Union Territory-wise; (b) 	whether the Union Government has fixed any target under the National Food Security Scheme, if so, the present status of the target and if not, whether any target is being considered for future; and (c) 	the details of the amount allocated under the National Food Security Scheme in the country during the last two years, State/Union Territory-wise?






ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a):  	A statement indicating the State/ Union Territory (UT)-wise number of beneficiaries covered under the National Food Security Act, 2013 (NFSA) is at Annexure-I.(b):  The NFSA provides for coverage upto 75% of rural population and upto 50% of urban population for receiving highly subsidized foodgrains under Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS), thus covering about two-thirds of the total population.  Corresponding to the all India coverage of 75% and 50% in the rural and urban areas, State-wise coverage was determined by the then Planning Commission (now NITI Aayog) by using the NSSO Household Consumption Expenditure Survey data for 2011-12.         At present, against the intended coverage of 81.35 crore, about 80 crore beneficiaries are identified by the States/UTs under the Act.(c):	Food Subsidy under the NFSA is not allocated on the basis of States/UTs.
