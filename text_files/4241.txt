                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

354






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Passenger Amenities at Railway Stations




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total amount of funds allocated for passenger amenities at Kishangarh, Nasirabad, Vijaynagar and Beawar railway stations along with the railway stations of district headquarters, Ajmer in Ajmer Parliamentary Constituency during the last five years, year and station-wise; (b) whether display screens have been installed at the platforms of all the said railway stations for the convenience of passengers and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the provision of installing two new escalators and lifts at the new railway station premises of Kishangarh (Ajmer) has been made in the last Budget and if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the work of installation of the said escalators and lifts has not been started so far due to alleged careless and negligent attitude of senior officials of the Railway Department; and (e) if so, the details thereof along with the reasons for delay in the said work?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (e) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
