                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3795






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Issues of Para-Athletes




Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta













                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the sports quota in the Union and State Government jobs also extend to para-athletes and if so, details thereof ;(b) whether the sports quota in the Central and State universities also extend to para-athletes and if so, the details thereof and the admissions made thereunder; and(c) whether there is any officially recognized association or tournaments at the Government level to select such para-athletes and if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a) :  The Department of Personnel & Training has issued instructions on recruitment and promotion etc. of sports-persons on 03.10.2013. These instructions relate to appointment of meritorious sports-persons under direct recruitment quota in Group C posts in Ministries/Departments of Government of India, up to 5% of the total vacancies, subject to fulfillment of eligibility conditions. These instructions do not specifically prohibit para–athletes from appointment as meritorious sports-persons..(b):University Grants Commission has informed that it has not prescribed any sports quota. However, as per Section 32 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 reservation of not less than 5% of seats in Government/Government aided higher educational institution is available for persons with benchmark disabilities. Information relating to admission to various universities under sports quota, if any, is not compiled in the Ministry.(c) :  Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports has informed that the Paralympic Committee of India (PCI) is the recognised National Sports Federation (NSF) for para sports.*****
