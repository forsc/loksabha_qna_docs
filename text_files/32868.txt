                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

259






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Construction of Houses under PMAY-U




Amar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of housing units constructed and proposed to be constructed in FatehgarhSahib, Punjab under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana- Rural and Urban both, till date, location/year-wise; (b)	the details of housing units sanctioned and percentage of those completed in FatehgarhSahib parliamentary constituency till date, location, and year-wise;   (c)	the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated, and utilized under the Yojana for FatehgarhSahib, Punjab; and(d)	the detail of delays, if any, of the house construction along with their locations in FatehgarhSahib, Punjab?






ANSWER




                                                                    THE MINISTER OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                  (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	&(b): The year-wise details of housing targets, housing units sanctioned and percentage of housing units completed so far in Fatehgarh Sahib district and parliamentary constituency, Punjab under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana– Gramin (PMAY-G) is at Annexure-I and II.The city/location-wise and year-wise details of houses sanctioned, completed with percentage so far in Fatehgarh Sahib parliamentary constituency, Punjab under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana– Urban (PMAY-U) is at Annexure-III.(c)	& (d): Ministry of Rural Development has reported that the district level data related to fund sanctioned, allocated and utilized is not maintained by the Ministry. The city/location-wise details of fundssanctioned, released and utilised in Fatehgarh Sahib district, Punjab under PMAY-U is at Annexure-IV.The construction period of projects sanctioned under PMAY-U is between 12-36 months from the date of approval. The Ministry has advised States/ UTs to expedite the construction of sanctioned houses so that these are completed within the stipulated timeline.                                       *****
