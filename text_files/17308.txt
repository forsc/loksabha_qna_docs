                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

700






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Drinking Water Availability




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether drinking water has been made available in all the villages across the country especially in Maharashtra; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the percentage of villages which have been provided drinking water till date, State-wise especially in Maharashtra;(c) whether the Government has set a target under a scheme for providing drinking water facility in all the villages across the country especially in Maharashtra; and(d) if so, the time by which the said target is likely to be achieved?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)& (b) As reported by States/ UTs, including Maharashtra, as on 14.09.2020, 79.16% rural habitations having 77.69% population have provision of minimum 40 litre per capita per day (lpcd) of potable drinking water and 17.69% rural habitations having 19.34% population have service level of less than 40 lpcd potable water, whereas 3.15% rural habitations having 2.97% population with water sources having quality issues. State/ UT-wise details are annexed.(c)& (d)To enable every rural household in the country to have potable water through Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) by 2024,Government of India is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM)-Har Ghar Jal in partnership with States, at an estimated outlay of Rs. 3.60 lakh Crore.****
