                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3905






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Modernisation of Nasik Airport




Bharti Pravin Pawar














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the progress made so far in the work of modernisation of Nasik (OZAR) Airport(b) whether the Government is considering to give international status to Nasik (OZAR) Airport; and(c) if so, the details thereof and the time by which it is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): Nasik (Ozar) Airport  is maintained by Hindustan Aeronautics Ltd. (HAL). As informed by HAL, the Nasik airport was built in collaboration with State Government of Maharashtra with all the modern facilities and no immediate modernisation is envisaged.(b) & (c): Nasik airport has been declared as a Customs notified airport and it is being operated as international airport for cargo operations. Declaration of an airport as an International airport depends upon the traffic potential and the demand form airlines for operation of international flights besides provision of Ground Lighting Facilities, Instrument Landing System, adequate runway length, Immigration, Health and Animal & Plan Quarantine services.
