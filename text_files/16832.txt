                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

755






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Solar Projects




Ranjeeta Koli













                                                    Annpurna Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of ongoing solar projects in the district of Bharatpur in Rajasthan;(b) the expenditure incurred on the said project(s); and(c) the work done/being done in this regard in the said district?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) to (c)Solar Roof Top Programme :Rajasthan Renewable Energy Corporation Limited (RRECL) has taken up implementation of  45 MW Solar Roof Top Programme in the State, wherein every interested beneficiary can install Roof Top Solar system at their residence availing subsidy as per norms of MNRE, Gol on first come first priority. This programme has also been taken up in Bharatpur district. Three Roof Top Solar Systems of total 104.92 kW capacity have already been installed in Bharatpur district.KUSUM COMPONENT.A:Under KUSUM COMPONENT-A scheme, total 4 Nos. Solar Power Generators have been selected for an aggregate capacity of 4MW Solar Power Plants installation. Under this scheme, Rs. 3.14 per unit tariff shall be paid to Developers. Letter of awards have been issued on 8.7.2020. Off-grid SPV Home Light Programme : Under DDUGJY, total 41 Nos. Home Lighting Systems (HLS) of 100 wp have been installed. *****
