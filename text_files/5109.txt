                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Financial Assistance to Andhra Pradesh




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the State Government of Andhra Pradesh is facing financial crunch after it has been bifurcated;(b)	if so, the details of financial assistance extended to the State in the last five years and the current year, year- wise; (c)	the details of the demand of Andhra Pradesh towards financial assistance;(d)	whether the said demand of the State has been fulfilled; and(e) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) & (b): Y-o-Y growth of Gross State Domestic Product (GSDP) at Current Prices has increased from 13.1% in 2014-15 to 16.1% in 2017-18. As per State Budget-2019-20 documents, Revenue Deficit of the State has decreased from Rs.24315 crore in 2014-15 to Rs.16152 crore in 2017-18 and estimated to be Rs.11655 crore in 2018-19 (RE) and Rs.1779 crore in 2019-20 (BE). Capital Expenditure has risen from Rs. 11409 crore in 2014-15 to Rs.14172 crore in 2015-16, Rs.15181 crore in 2016-17, Rs.13491 crore in 2017-18 and estimated to be Rs.20398 crore in 2018-19 (RE) and Rs.32293 crore in 2019-20 (BE). The details of financial assistance extended to the successor State of Andhra Pradesh  in terms of the Andhra Pradesh Re-organisation Act, 2014 in the last five years is enclosed as Annexure-A.(c) to (e) A charter of  demands dated 26.05.2019 including fiscal challenges faced by the successor State of Andhra Pradesh and Special Category Status have been received from Chief Minister, Andhra Pradesh, which have been submitted to the 15th Finance Commission for their consideration.  ******Annexure-AAnnexure referred to in reply to Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 4712 to be answered on 22.07.2019 Releases made to the Successor State of Andhra Pradesh post bifurcation(Rs. in crore)Central Assistance provided under Andhra Pradesh Re-organization Act, 2014Provisions of APRA, 2014 	Items identified for providing ‘Special Assistance’ 	Releases	Total uptoFY 2018-19		2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	Section 46(2)	Resource gap  	2303.00	500.00	1176.50	-	-	3979.50Section 46(2) & (3) and 94 (2): special for backward areas.
