                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

412






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Skill Development Scheme in Uttar Pradesh




Sanghamitra Maurya














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether unemployed youths were to be given training and employment under the centrally sponsored skill development schemes;(b)	the total number of unemployed youths who have been given training and the number of youths who have been given employment in Uttar Pradesh, especially in Budaun during the last three years and the current year so far, scheme-wise; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c)	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.	   STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) TO (C) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 412 TO BE ANSWERED ON 23.03.2020 ASKED BY SHRI DR. SANGHMITRA MAURYA REGARDING SKILL DEVELOPMENT SCHEME IN UTTAR PRADESH(a)	Yes Sir. Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship is implementing its flagship demand driven centrally sponsored scheme, Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) (2016-20), for short duration skill development training (Short-Term Training (STT) and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)) to unemployed youth across the country including State of Uttar Pradesh.(b) and (c)	The number of candidates trained/oriented, certified and placed in State of Uttar Pradesh and district of Budaun of Uttar Pradesh under STT and RPL component of PMKVY (2016-20), as on 17.01.2020, are given below: Short Term Training (STT) - PMKVYState/district	Trained / Oriented	Certified	PlacementUttar Pradesh	6,33,081	5,10,085	2,46,846District of Budaun	6,323	4,933	3,090 Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) - PMKVYUttar Pradesh	4,32,377	3,53,421	Not applicable*District of Budaun	1,534	1,031	[*RPL does not mandate placement as it orients and certifies candidate with prior learning experience or skills.]
