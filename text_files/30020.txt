                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1628






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Autokast Limited




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that Autokast Limited under Government of Kerala is involved in production of grey iron, ductile iron and steel castings used in heavy industrial units and if so, whether the Government will explore the possibility of central public sector units like BEML, BHEL, Cochin Shipyard, etc. to purchase such castings from the Autokast limited and if so, the details thereof; and(b)	whether the Government will consider streamlining of the process of supply of raw metals for Steel and Industrial Forgings under the Government of Kerala from Steel Authority of India on a priority basis and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)&(b): Steel being a deregulated sector, all commercial decisions are taken by individual companies keeping in view commercial considerations and market dynamics. However, in its role as a facilitator, Government has announced Domestically Manufactured Iron & Steel Products Procurement (DMI&SP) Policy, which provides for preference to domestically manufactured steel products both from public and private sector in Government procurement, which includes BEML, BHEL, Cochin Shipyard etc. ********
