                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

768






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Database of Monuments




Debasree Chaudhuri













                                                    Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Government has prepared a database of monuments of national and international importance in the country and if so, details of monuments under protection of Government, State-wise;(b)	the details of budget allocated for the maintenance of these monuments;(c)	whether the Government has received any proposal seeking the inclusion of more  monuments to the list;  (d)	if so, the details thereof State-wise including Maharashtra along. with the reaction of Government thereto; and(e)	whether the Government is aware of the fact that many such monuments have been encroached upon and a few have been completely ruined and if so, the measures taken for their restoration?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) 	Yes, Sir. The database pertaining to details of monuments of national importance and  World Heritage Sites are available in public domain and can be accessed at https://asi.nic.in. (b)	Details of budget allocation and expenditure for conservation, preservation and maintenance of these monuments/sites are at Annexure-I. (c) & (d)  The details of monuments/sites identified to be declared as of National Importance are at Annexure-II. (e)	Action for removal of encroachment is taken up as per the provisions contained in the Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act, 1958 and Rules 1959 framed there under. The Superintending Archaeologist in charge of the Circles have also been vested with the powers of an Estate Officer to issue eviction notices/orders to the encroachers under Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1971. Assistance in containing and removing encroachments is also sought from the respective State Government/Police.
