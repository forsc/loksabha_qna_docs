                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1359






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Implementation of Schemes in Jharkhand




Sudarshan Bhagat














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the schemes run by the Ministry are not being implemented properly in Jharkhand; (b) 	if so, the district-wise details thereof; and (c) 	the details of the irregularities being reported in the implementation of the said schemes?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c)	Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE)has been  implementing following schemes/programmes namely; (i) Pradhan Mantri Kaushal VikasYojana (PMKVY),   (ii) Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS),(iii)Apprenticeship Scheme  and (iv) Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS) for skill development of the youth in different districts of Jharkhand. District wise information in respect of these programmes are given at Annexure-I, Annexure-II, Annexure-III and Annexure IV respectively. Further, the Ministry has not received any complaint about irregularities in the implementation of these programmes.
