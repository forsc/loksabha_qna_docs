                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2757






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






e-Commerce Consignment




E. T. Mohammed Basheer














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Indian Railways wish to start e-Commerce consignment in non-peak hours;(b)   if so, the details thereof; and(c)   the programme of action to extend it in different places?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c)  A pilot project was launched to study the feasibility of using Railways’ parcel service for e-tail players under which Amazon India has been provided 2.5 tonnes space in the SLR/Break Van under Guard’s charge for loading/unloading of consignments, in 12314/12313 New Delhi-Sealdah Rajdhani Express and 12952/12951 New Delhi-Mumbai Rajdhani Express trains.  Moreover, in Sealdah-Dankuni section over Sealdah Division of Eastern Railway, movement of 7 tonne consignment of Amazon India through suburban train (EMU) during non-peak hours, has been permitted as a pilot project for a period of three months initially.  Detailed guidelines will be formulated based on the outcome of the Pilot Project.  ******
