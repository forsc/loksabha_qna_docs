                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1033






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-Scheme




Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar













                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the amount of funds allocated and spent on Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-Schemes in Maharashtra and Rajasthan over the last five years;(b) the details of the amount of fund spent by the Government in Maharashtra and Rajasthan during the last five years, district-wise;(c)	whether the Government has observed any lack of adequate usage of the funds divulged for the welfare and development projects; and(d)	if so, the actions taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SMT. RENUKA SINGH SARUTA)  (a): 	The details of amount of funds allocated under the scheme of Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-Scheme SCA to TSS) during last five years is given below:(Rs. in Crore)Year	BE	RE	Actuals2016-17	1250.00	1200.00	1195.022017-18	1350.00	1350.00	1349.192018-19	1350.00	1350.00	1350.002019-20	1350.00	1350.00	1350.002020-21	1350.00	800.00	799.93The details of funds spent / released by Ministry of Tribal Affairs under the scheme of Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-Scheme (SCA to TSS) in Maharashtra and Rajasthan during the last five years is as under:(Rs. in crore)S.No.	Name of State	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-211.	 	Maharashtra	95.47	137.60	138.02	119.29.00	 -2.	 	Rajasthan	110.72	100.51	103.27	114.61	86.62 (b): The details of amount spent / utilized by the Government in Maharashtra and Rajasthan during the last five years out of the funds released to them under the scheme of SCA to TSS:(Rs. in crore)S.No.	Name of State	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-211. The Government of Maharashtra has responded in affirmative for utilization of released funds.  ******
