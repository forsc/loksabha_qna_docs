                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

108






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






ODOP Scheme




M V V Satyanarayana













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government has approved One District One Product (ODOP) Scheme for 707 districts of 35 States and UTs which also include 137 unique products as per the recommendations received by States/UTs and if so, the details thereof;b)	the number of products approved under the ODOP in the Pradhan Manatri Formalisation of Micro food processing Enterprises (PMFME) Scheme from the State of Andhra Pradesh;c)	whether the Government has introduced a GIS - ODOP digital map of India to provide details of ODOP products of all the States and UTs and it also has indicators for Tribal, SC, ST, and aspirational districts; andd)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PASHUPATI KUMAR PARAS)(a)	to (d)       A statement is laid on the Table of the House.***** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.  *108 ANSWERED ON 27.07.2021 REGARDING  “ODOP SCHEME”(a)	Yes, Sir. The Government has approved One District One Product (ODOP) for 707 districts from 35 States and UTs which includes 137 Unique products. Detailed list of 137 unique products along with the respective districts and States is at Annexure I. (b) 	11 unique products are approved for the 13 districts of Andhra Pradesh. The details of ODOP are at Annexure II.(c) & (d)  Yes Sir. In the GIS-ODOP digital map, ODOP of 707 districts from 35 States and UTs have been indicated. Along with it, districts covering 216 Integrated Tribal Development Areas, 112 Aspirational districts and 35 districts with more than 40% SC population are indicated in the map.The ODOP-GIS is available at https://odop.mofpi.gov.in/odop/*****
