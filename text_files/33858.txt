                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






Exports of Goods and Services




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether India is at an all-time high in export of goods and services during the current financial year;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the total number of goods and services that have been exported to other countries during this financial year and the total income earned through these exports, product-wise and country-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY(SMT.
