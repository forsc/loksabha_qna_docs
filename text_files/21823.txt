                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3009






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Condition of Historic Libraries




Vinod Kumar Sonkar













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Nisith Pramanik
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Rajveer (Raju Bhaiya) Singh
                                                








                                                    Sarmistha Sethi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the historic libraries of the country are suffering from and in a pathetic condition and if so, the Government’s reaction thereto;(b)	whether the Government is proposing to create an All India Library Services Cadre for common service conditions and pay scales in libraries across country and if so, details thereof;(c)	whether the most of the important and historic libraries of the country are now headed by the non library professionals and if so, the details thereof along with the reaction of the Government in this regard;(d)	whether the Government has any roadmap for development of Public Library System and allocate separate funds in this regard and if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the details of man power shortage in public libraries in the country, State-wise and the steps being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a)	Sir, as per Seventh Schedule of the Constitution of India, Library is a state subject and comes under the purview of the respective State/Union Territory authorities. However Raja Rammohun Roy Library Foundation (RRRLF), Kolkata, a nodal agency of Central Government for promoting and supporting Public Library movement, provides under its matching and non-matching schemes financial assistance for development of public libraries for various purposes eg. Books, furniture & equipments, modernization, construction of library buildings, mobile library services, establishment of children corner, facilities for specially-abled readers, seminars/workshops etc.(b)	No such proposal is under consideration in this Ministry.(c)	Library is a State subject and it comes under the purview of the State/UT Library Authorities.  There are six libraries under administrative control of Ministry of Culture namely:i.	National Libraryii.	Central Secretariat Libraryiii.	Central Reference Libraryiv.	Delhi Public Libraryv.	Khuda Bakhsh Oriental Public Libraryvi.	Rampur Raza LibraryAs per Recruitment Rules, all the aforesaid Libraries were earlier headed by the Library Professionals. Presently the Posts of Heads of National Library, Delhi Public Library, Rampur Raza Library, Central Secretariat Library are vacant. For running their day to day activities, additional charge was given to JS(Lib) in respect of National Library, Kolkata (upto 06.03.2021),  Delhi Public Library(upto 08.12.2020),  to DM, Rampur in respect of Rampur Raza Library  and  to Director (Lib)in respect of Central Secretariat Library till regular appointment is made.(d)	RRRLF, an autonomous organization under the Ministry of Culture with the active co-operation of the State Library Authorities is providing financial assistance for improvement of Public Library Services through implementation of various Matching and Non-Matching Schemes. National Mission on Libraries (NML) under its NML Model Library scheme provides financial assistance to one (1) State Central Library and one (1) District Library in each State/UT as recommended by the respective State/UT Library Authority. Six (6) libraries under the Ministry of Culture are also eligible for assistance under this scheme.(e)	Library is a State subject and man power shortage in public libraries in the country comes under the purview of the State/UT Library Authorities.  Accordingly, the steps taken to meet shortage of man power in public libraries in the country do not come under the purview of this Ministry.
