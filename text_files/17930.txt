                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1611






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






National Cadet Corps




Vishnu Dutt Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the steps being taken by the Government to provide training facilities to the cadets of National Cadet Corps (NCC) so as to connect them with nation building tasks;(b) whether the Government proposes to integrate the activities of NCC, Indian Scouts and Guides, Homeguard and Territorial Army with the aim of strengthening national security; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) In order to align the objective of NCC training with the task of nation building due focus is being laid towards training of NCC cadets in social service and community development activities for nation building and social upliftment.  The cadets are also being trained for skill development, soft skills, leadership, etc. Training programmes are also being undertaken in areas like Atmanirbhar Bharat, Fit India Movement, Ek Bharat Shrestha Bharat, Swachh Bharat, Jal Shakti Abhiyan, etc.  (b) & (c)   Government has set up a Coordination Group under the aegis of Department of Youth Affairs; with National Cadet Corps (NCC), National Service Scheme (NSS), Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangthan (NYKS), Bharat Scout and Guides (BSG) and Hindustan Scouts and Guides (HSG); to ensure better coordination and faster dissemination of youth related schemes and proper implementation.*****
