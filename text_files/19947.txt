                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1464






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Web Portal for cases Pending in Various Courts




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)













                                                    Upendra Singh Rawat
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has initiated any step to establish the web portal for each Ministry/ Department of the Government where they can upload the details of cases which are pending in various courts and the status of these cases so as to help in effective monitoring of court cases by the Department of Legal Affairs;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has started any structural legal framework of mediation; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The MINISTER OF LAW AND JUSTICE, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)	to (d) : A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) to (d) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO-1464 DUE FOR ANSWER ON 10.02.2019 IN THE LOK SABHA(a)  and (b)    Department of Legal Affairs(DoLA) vide Gazette Notification dated 08.02.2016 has implemented Legal Information Management & Briefing System (LIMBS), an IT enabled application for monitoring of court cases where Government of India is one of the party. It has been implemented in 72 Ministries/Departments of the Government where they can upload the details of cases which are pending in various courts and the status of these cases. Further the application has been revamped to a new robust secure platform equipped with dashboard view for quick analysis of cases at senior level so as to help in effective monitoring of court cases. As on 02.02.2021, Ministries/Departments have uploaded 6.32 lakh court cases in LIMBS application.	Parts (c) to (d)Mediation is one of the Alternative Dispute Resolution mechanisms available to the court under Section 80 of the Civil Procedure Code and has emerged as a popular and useful method for settlement of disputes. It involves negotiations also. The Legal Services Authorities have been actively involved to get the disputes settled through Mediation.
