                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3623






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Veterinary College at Chidambaram




Thirumaa Valavan Thol













                                                    T. R. Paarivendhar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government will take steps to start a college of Veterinary Sciences and Animal Husbandry at Chidambaram or Ariyalur in Tamil Nadu in view of the fact that most of the people in these areas rely on agriculture and cattle rearing in their secondary profession ; and (b) if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (Dr. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a) and (b):   Establishment of veterinary colleges is under the purview of State Governments as per their requirement of trained veterinary manpower. There is no decision to start a college of Veterinary Sciences and Animal husbandry at Chidambaram or Ariyalur in Tamil Nadu by the Central Government.
