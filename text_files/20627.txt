                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1808






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Housing for All in Assam




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of people who have been benefitted under “PM Housing For All” scheme during the last year;(b)	the details of the total number of houses sanctioned, allotted and still under construction in the State of Assam in the FY 2020-21; and(c)	whether there has been a delay in construction and allotment of houses under the scheme due to COVID-19 and if so the details thereof including the steps takenby the Government to remedy the same? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                         (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	  A total of 24.24 lakh houses have been sanctioned under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY-U) Mission during the last year. (b)	 So far, 1,23,268 houses have been sanctioned against the assessed demand of 2.19 lakh; out of this 94,590 have been grounded for construction and 22,888 have been completed and delivered to beneficiaries of Assam under PMAY-U Mission. A total of 5,558 houses have been sanctioned for the State of Assam in the Financial Year 2020-21. (c)	Due to COVID-19, there has been some delay in construction of houses. The progress of construction of houses has picked up. Govt. of India through the various Ministries/Departments has issued advisories/protocols to start the construction work after following COVID-19 guidelines.                                                        *****
