                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4137






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Housing Proposals under  PMAY




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of proposals received from the State of Tamil Nadu including Madurai district for sanctioning houses under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) during 2018-19 and 2019-20; and (b)	the action taken by the Government on each of the above proposal?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	& (b): During 2018-19 and 2019-20, 1186 project proposals under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY (U)] for construction of 2,70,359 houses in various districts of Tamil Nadu had been received which included 23 project proposals for 10,994 houses in Madurai district. These proposals were considered by Central Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee in the Ministry for central assistance of Rs. 4078.43 crore of which Rs 595.93 crore has so far been released to the State Government. Moreover, under Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme of PMAY (U), 29,507 beneficiaries have availed interest subsidy of Rs. 634.52 crore for acquiring / construction of houses in the various districts  of Tamil Nadu during 2018-19 and 2019-20.                    *****
