                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4006






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Coal Reserves




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a.	whether the coal reserves are so big now that they are producing 1/6th part of their annual generation;b.	if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor; andc.	whether the power companies have requested to cut down supply of coal for the last three years and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a & b): The total estimated coal resources in the country is 319.02 billion tonnes as per “The inventory of Geological Resources of Indian Coal” (as on 01.04.2018), prepared by the Geological Survey of India having proved reserve of 148.787 billion tonnes. Total coal   production of the country during 2017-18 was 0.68046 billion tonnes.(c): No such request has been received to the Ministry of Coal during last three years to cut down supply of coal.  *****
