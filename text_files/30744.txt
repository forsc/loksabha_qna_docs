                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

219






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Theme Based Tourist Circuits




Rebati Tripura














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has achieved the target under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government proposes to include more theme based tourist circuits in North Eastern Region (NER) and Uttar Pradesh specially in Jhansi and Lalitpur and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the amount of funds allocated and utilised under the said scheme during the last three years; and(d) 	the steps taken by the Government for proper implementation of the said scheme for development of tourism in the country particularly NER and Uttar Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			         (SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d):   A statement is laid on the table of the House.******* STATEMENT IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO *219 ANSWERED ON 13.12.2021 REGARDING THEME BASED TOURIST CIRCUITS.(a) to (d): 	The Ministry of Tourism has sanctioned 76 projects under 13 thematic circuits during  the period  2014-15 to 2018-19 in  the  country under  its  Swadesh Darshan scheme out of which 16 projects are physically complete whereas the remaining  projects are  under  different stages of implementation. As of now, there is no proposal to sanction new projects under the scheme including in North East Region (NER) and in Uttar Pradesh. Ministry of Tourism is monitoring the projects by undertaking regular review meetings with the States/ Union Territories/ Central Agencies at various levels. The Ministry of Tourism has also issued instructions fixing timelines for completion of all the projects sanctioned under Swadesh Darshan scheme.  In addition, the Ministry has developed a dashboard for monitoring implementation of the sanctioned projects under the scheme.The details of the funds sanctioned, released and utilized in respect of the projects sanctioned under Swadesh Darshan scheme including for last three years is annexed.  *********
