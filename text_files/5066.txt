                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4682






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Group ‘A’ Posts in ESI Hospitals




Kaushalendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the total number of regular Group ‘A’ posts created and recruitments done in the New ESI hospitals set up in the last 15 years, hospital-wise;(b)out of the total regular recruitments done in New ESI hospitals, the details of category-wise UR/OBC/SC/ST recruitments done till date for Group ‘A’ posts; (c)the number of employees working on contract basis against Group A, Institution-wise;(d)the number of contract basis employees belonging to UR/OBC/SC/ST categories, institution-wise;(e)the duration details (month and year) of Contract basis employees with break or without break, institution-wise; and(f)the percentage of contract basis employees compared to the total sanctioned posts, Speciality/Department-wise and category-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a):		The details of Group ‘A’ posts created in the new ESIC Hospitals set up in the last 15 years in respect of the administrative officers are at Annexure-I and the details in respect to Medical Officers for the last 15 years are in Annexure-IIContd..2/-:: 2 ::(b):		Category-wise Group ‘A’ recruitment of administrative officers done are as under:-UR	OBC	SC	ST	Total57	34	15	10	116Further, Group ‘A’ recruitment is done on All India basis, and not Institute-wise. Category-wise details of recruitments of medical officers done till date in respect of Gr. ‘A’ posts pertaining to ESIC Hospitals is as under:Post	UR	OBC	SC	ST	TotalSpecialist Gr.
