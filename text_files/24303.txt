                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

435






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Veterinary Hospitals and Dispensaries




Devji Mansingram Patel













                                                    Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the veterinary hospitals/dispensaries functioning in the rural areas in the country, State-wise as on date;(b)	the details of the funds provided to these veterinary hospitals/dispensaries during the last three years and the current financial year, State-wise;(c)	whether the Government proposes to open new veterinary hospitals/dispensaries specially in the rural areas of the country deprived of such facilities;(d)	if so, the details thereof, state-wise; and(e)	the time by which the said veterinary hospitals/dispensaries are likely to be opened in all the villages of the country? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING                                                  (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)a)	There 12129 Veterinary Hospitals, 25597 Veterinary Dispensaries and 28168 Veterinary Aid Centres / Stockman Centres / Mobile Dispensaries in the country. the state-wise  details of these Veterinary Institutions is at Annexure-1.b)	The Department has released Rs. 719.80 lakh, Rs. 247.05 lakh and Rs.2053.03 lakh during 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 respectively, under “Establishment & Strengthening of Veterinary Hospitals & Dispensaries (ESVHD)” a component under centrally sponsored Livestock Health & Disease Control (LH&DC) scheme. The details of funds released to the states is at Annexure-II.c)	to e)  Veterinary hospital and dispensaries are set up by States/ Union Territories. However, in order to supplement the efforts of the States/ Union Territory, Central Government provides financial assistance for establishment &  strengthening of veterinary hospitals & dispensaries as well as for running of mobile veterinary clinics under “Establishment & Strengthening of Veterinary Hospitals & Dispensaries (ESVHD)”,  a component under centrally sponsored scheme “Livestock Health & Disease Control” (LH&DC).*****
