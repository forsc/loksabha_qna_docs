                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2399






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Fellowship Scheme




T.N. Prathapan













                                                    S. Gnanathiraviam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of Senior /Junior Fellowship Scheme to provide basic financial support for advanced training on individual creative efforts for revival of our traditional forms of arts and if so, the details thereof during the last three years, State-Wise including Tamil Nadu;(b)	whether the award of Senior/Junior fellowships in Outstanding Persons in the field of Culture have a minimum number of female holders and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the categories in the Senior/Junior fellowships in ‘New Areas related to Culture and are expansive in nature ; and(d)	the measures taken or proposed to be taken by the Government to include more areas in culture for recognition?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)The Ministry of Culture is implementing the Scheme for Award of Senior / Junior Fellowships to Outstanding Persons for cultural research in different fields of Culture. Junior/Senior Fellowships are awarded to outstanding persons in the age group of 25 to 40 years (Jr.) and above 40 years (Sr.) in different cultural fields for the period of 2 years for cultural research in different cultural fields.  A Table indicating details of Sr./Jr.  Fellowships during last three years, State wise including Tamil Nadu is annexed.(b)	The Senior / Junior Fellowships are open to all and there is no specific quota for females.(c)&(d)Yes Sir, the categories mentioned in ‘New Areas’ in Scheme guidelines are expansive in nature and  diversely cover different subjects related to culture.  The research proposals that are innovative and application oriented, preferably inter-disciplinary in nature, are considered as per the Scheme guidelines. There is no proposal presently under the consideration of the Government to include more areas.
