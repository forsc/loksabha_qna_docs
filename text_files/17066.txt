                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1000






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Financial Assistance to Scribes




Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to increase financial assistance under welfare scheme for scribes and their families in times of extreme hardships and if so, the details thereof ;(b)	the criteria fixed for covering journalists and their families under the welfare scheme;(c)	whether the part-time journalists, freelance journalists, photographers, cameramen are  eligible for coverage under this scheme and if so, the details thereof;(d)	the details of the beneficiaries under this scheme during the last three years. State/UT-wise; and(e)	the details of the funds sanctioned and released under this scheme during the said period, State/UT wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE: MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES   {SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR}(a) to (c) : Financial Assistance to Journalists and their families in extreme hardship is extended in accordance with the Guidelines on Journalist Welfare Scheme dated 05.03.2019. Detailed criteria, coverage, eligibility and quantum of financial assistance have been laid down under the guidelines which are available on this Ministry’s website :  www.mib.nic.in.  (d) & (e): Details of financial assistance given in the last three years are as under:-Financial Year	Total number   of Beneficiary	Amount   of funds Sanctioned /Released    (in Lakhs)	States/UTs2017-18	11	39.37	Bihar, Delhi, Gujarat, Karnataka, Maharashtra, Punjab, Uttar Pradesh.2018-19	27	99.99	Bihar, Delhi, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Manipur, Odisha, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, West Bengal.2019-20	28	115.25	Bihar, Chattisgarh, Delhi, Haryana, Jharkhand, Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Manipur, Odisha, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh.******
