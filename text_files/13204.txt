                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1846






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Proposal to Enlist more Countries under Visa on Arrival Scheme




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to enlist more countries under visa on arrival scheme to increase foreign tourism in the near future;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (c):	Nationals of Japan, South Korea & UAE are entitled for visa-on-arrival presently.  Besides this, nationals of 170 countries are eligible for electronic Visa at the moment.  Expansion of these facilities is an ongoing process depending upon availability of infrastructure etc.******
