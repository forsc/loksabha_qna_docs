                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3027






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Upgradation/modernization of Medical Colleges in Karnataka




Sanganna Amarappa Karadi














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a)	whether the Government has received a number of proposals from State Government of Karnataka for setting  up of trauma care centres and up gradation/ modernization of medical colleges;(b)	if so, the details  thereof during each of the last three years and the current year;(c)	the progress made on such proposals by the Government along with the funds released for the purpose during the said period , proposal- wise ; and(d)	the number of said proposals pending for approval with the Government along with the necessary steps taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	to (c) : During 11th Five Year Plan (2007-2012) under “National Programme for Prevention and Management of Trauma and Burn Injuries” proposals were received from State Government of Karnataka and eight hospitals/medical colleges were approved for setting up of trauma care facilities. All the eight trauma care facilities are reported to be functional by State Government. Details of fund released facilities wise attached at Annexure-I.(d)	In view of above reply, does not arise.
