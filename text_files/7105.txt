                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

888






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Houses Built under PMAY




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva













                                                    Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    the number of houses built so far under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana(PMAY);(b)    the number of houses built in Gujarat, district-wise; and(c)    the quantum of funds sanctioned, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY(U)], against an assessed demand of around 1.12 Crore houses in urban areas of the States/UTs, 93.00 lakh houses have so far been sanctioned, of which 55.40 lakh houses have been grounded and 28.06 lakh houses have been completed.(b)	The district-wise details about the number of houses built in Gujarat is at Annexure – I.(c)	Details of State-wise funds sanctioned under PMAY(U) is at Annexure – II                                           ****
