                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2421






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Deendayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme




Annpurna Devi













                                                    Pashupati Nath Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has implemented Deendayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme (DDRS) in Jharkhand; (b) if so, the number of schools and vocational training centres given grants under this scheme during the last three years and the current year till date, year-wise; (c) the proposals received from Jharkhand for this scheme so far; (d) the number of proposals given administrative and financial sanction till date out of above along with the details thereof, proposal-wise; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor and the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR)(a) Yes, Sir. (b) The details of number of schools and vocational training centres given grants under Deendayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme (DDRS)  during the last three years and current year in the State of Jharkhand are is under:-(Rs. in lakhs)Year	Schools	Vocational Training Centres(*)	Number	Amount Released	Number	Amount Released2016-17	1	1.59	0	0.002017-18	1	6.85	0	0.002018-19	0	0	0	0.002019-20(as on 27.11.2019)	0	0	0	0.00(*) As per revised scheme of DDRS, the project of Vocational Training Centre has been discontinued with effect from 1st April, 2018.(c) to (e) The Government of Jharkhand forwarded one (01)  proposal for each of the years 2016-17 and 2017-18 for the project of Special School for Intellectual Disabilities. The details of financial sanctions released for both these proposals are given below:Name of the Organisation	2016-17	2017-18Srijak Samuh, Deoghar	1st installment- Rs.1.20 lakh2nd Installment Rs.0.39 lakh	1st installment- Rs.6.85 lakh For the year 2018-19 and 2019-20 no proposal has been received from Govt. of Jharkhand till date. ******
