                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1507






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Development of Villages under SAGY




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of development of Gram Panchayats (GPs) adopted by the Members of Parliament under Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY) in the country, State-wise;(b) 	whether dilapidated roads, poor health system and open defecation are still prevalent in the GPs adopted by the Members of Parliament under SAGY;(c) 	if so, the details thereof;(d) 	whether there is any plan or proposal to sanction special fund for SAGY villages for their developmental needs; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): The Gram Panchayats adopted under SAGY prepare Village Development Plans (VDP) for themselves through a participatory process under the guidance of Hon’ble Members of Parliament. The VDP includes prioritised time-bound activities to achieve holistic and integrated progress of the village. The State/UT-wise status of progress of implementation of VDP in SAGY Gram Panchayats, based on the information uploaded on the SAGY website by the respective States/UTs till 6 February 2020, is given in Annexure-I.(b) & (c): Inter-village connectivity, by way of a single all-weather road to the eligible unconnected habitations and upgradation of roads in the SAGY Gram Panchayats / villages has been provided through Pradhan Mantri Gram SadakYojana (PMGSY). Further, providing connectivity to identified rural production centres to existing pucca road network as well as construction of pucca internal roads or streets including side drains and culvert within a village are permissible activities under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme. These works are taken up at the Gram Panchayat level as per demand.Under the National Health Mission (NHM), support is provided to States/UTs for strengthening their healthcare system on the basis of proposals received. All Gram Panchayats identified under SansadAdarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY) have been declared Open Defecation Free (ODF).(d) & (e): Under the SAGY framework, the development of Gram Panchayats is envisaged through convergence and implementation of existing Government Schemes and Programmes under the administrative control of respective Ministries. There is no plan or proposal to sanction special funds for SAGY villages as of now.Annexure-IStatement referred to in reply to Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 1507 scheduled for 11-02-2020State/UT-wise progress of implementation of Village Development Plans in SAGY Gram Panchayats based on the information uploaded on the SAGY website by the respective States/UTs till 06 February 2020.
