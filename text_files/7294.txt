                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

983






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






AYUSH Medical Centres




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva













                                                    Shantanu Thakur
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                








                                                    Pradeep Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government plans to open AYUSH Medical Centres/Ayurvedic Colleges as well as Regional Research Centre in the country, State/UT-wise during the current year; and (b) if so, the details thereof along with the allocation of fund for each of them?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b):    As health is a state subject, opening of AYUSH Medical Centres/Ayurvedic Colleges in the country comes under the purview of respective State/UT Governments. However, under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), there is a provision of financial assistance for opening of upto 50-bedded integrated AYUSH Hospital and New AYUSH Educational Institutions including Ayurvedic Colleges in the States where it is not available in Government sector. As per the proposal received, six number of upto 50 bedded integrated AYUSH Hospital during the current year has been approved as per details enclosed in Annexure. However, under NAM no proposal has been received from the State/UT Government for opening of Ayurvedic Colleges during the current year.Further, as far as opening of Regional Research Centre is concerned, it has been decided to establish 02 post graduate institute of Yoga  & Naturopathy Education and Research Centre at Jhajjar (Haryana) and Nagamanagala (Karnataka)  under Central Council for Research in Yoga & Naturopathy, an autonomous  organization of Ministry  and released an amount of  Rs. 41.42 crores & 35.60 crores respectively. Similarly, under various streams viz Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani, Sowa Rigpa & Homoeopathy 83 number of Regional Research Centres are working in different parts of the country.
