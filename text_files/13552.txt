                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2382






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






NH Projects in NER




Rebati Tripura














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of National Highways (NHs) under construction in the North Eastern Region at present; (b)	whether the NH-44 in Tripura is the only land-link between Tripura and the rest of the country and if so, the details and the present status thereof;(c)	whether the condition of the said highway is very poor and if so, the reasons therefor;(d)	the funds allocated by the Government for the maintenance of the said NH; and(e)	the steps taken by the Government for proper maintenance of the said NH?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	156 Projects costing Rs. 46111 crore for development/improvement covering 3912 km length of National Highways are in progress in North Eastern Region (NER).(b)	No, Sir, Apart from NH-44(New NH-8), Two other NH’s viz., NH-108 (New NH 44A) and NH 208A connect Tripura with rest of the country.(c) to (e) Ministry allocates funds State-wise and not project-wise. NH-44 is maintained in traffic worthy condition for which an amount of Rs. 35.49 crore has been allocated to Tripura State during 2019-20. Further work of widening & strengthening covering 124 km length costing Rs. 565.57 crore has been sanctioned, out of which work in 25 km has been taken up at a cost of Rs. 171.90 crore.*****
