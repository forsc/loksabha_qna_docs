                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4225






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Cooperative Schemes in Chhattisgarh




Sunil Kumar Soni













                                                    Vijay Baghel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of schemes functional in the country in the field of cooperatives;(b) the schemes being implemented for development of this sector in Chhattisgarh and the total funds allocated by the Government for the said purpose; and(c) whether the Government has received any proposal from Chhattisgarh Government for work in this sector and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF COOPERATION ???????? ??????  (SHRI AMIT SHAH)(a)	Ministry of Cooperation is presently implementing Central Sector Integrated Scheme on Agriculture Cooperation (CSISAC) which provides financial assistance to NCDC Programme for Development of Cooperatives, Assistance to Cooperative Education and Training and Assistance to Multi State Cooperative Societies. For the current financial year, Rs. 373 Crores have been earmarked under this scheme. (b)	In the State of Chhattisgarh, NCDC has cumulatively disbursed over Rs. 69953.25 Crore to the cooperative sector for wide range of activities like Marketing & Inputs, Sugar, Storage, Processing and Consumer Cooperatives projects under its schemes. Since 2018-19, details of amount disbursed and subsidy released under CSISAC scheme is mentioned below:Year	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22Disbursement (Rs. in crore)	8000.31	5500.34	12000.07	12400.80Subsidy Released (Rs. in lakhs)	3.81	34.72	Nil	37.42(c)	No such proposal is available on record of Ministry of Cooperation.*****
