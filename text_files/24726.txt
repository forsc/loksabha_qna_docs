                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

652






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Construction of Flyovers




Mansukhbhai Dhanjibhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)     whether the construction work of flyovers on the busy   junctions    	in the country including Gujarat is progressing as per 	schedule; (b)     if so, the details and present status thereof along with the funds 	allocated and utilised thereon; and (c)     the time limit fixed for completion of these flyovers?   






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	      Yes Sir(b)	  62 Nos of flyovers are in progress a various stages of construction. The funds are allocated for development and maintenance of National Highways as State-wise instead of project wise. The state wise allocation and expenditure on National Highways during last three years and current Financial Year 202-21 (till 30.06.2021) are placed at Annexure.(c)	  All flyovers are being completed within their scheduled time of completion.
