                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2397






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Empowerment of Differently Abled




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the steps being taken by the Government to create a comprehensive database of physically disabled citizens of the country, and the details of any such steps that have been taken so far along with achievements; (b) whether there exists a database on the number of disabled people across the country and if so, the details thereof, State-wise, along with the details of measure being takne to create awareness about such disabilities; (c) the details of the funds allocated and spent under the schemes for the empowerment of persons with physical disabilities and the achievements there under so far; and(d) the details of the overall benefits that have accrued to such people, overall, owing to schemes/steps taken by the Government for their empowerment?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR)(a) and (b) : The Ministry relies on census data regarding number of Persons with Disabilities (PwDs). As per the last Census of 2011, there are 2.68 Crore PwDs in the country. State/UT-wise number of PwDs is at Annexure–I. The Central Government launched the Unique Disability ID project in 2016 for developing a national database of PwDs and also issuing unique identity card to each PwD. The database is dynamic and envisages capturing of data in respect of all categories of disabilities i.e. physical, intellectual and developmental disabilities. Details of e-UDID cards generated so far, State/UT-wise is at Annexure-II. The Government supports the State Governments to carry out publicity activities for implementation of the project so as to cover all PwDs in the database.  (c) and (d) : Details of the funds allocated, amount spent and the number of beneficiaries under major schemes of this Department for last 3 years and current year (i.e. 2019-20) are at Annexure-III.                                                *****
