                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1315






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Data for Census 2021




Aparajita Sarangi













                                                    Mala Roy
                                                








                                                    Anumula Revanth Reddy
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Mondal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has begun collecting data for the Census 2021 and if so, the details thereof including the criteria being following in this regard;(b)	whether the COVID-19 pandemic and the lockdowns have affected the reliability of the data so collected and if so, whether the Government proposes to restart the process;(c)	whether the Government proposes to conduct caste based census and also to make public the findings of the same, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(d)	whether the Government would be including data on external migration from a State in Census 2021, in the light of the migrant labour issues if not, the reasons therefor; and (e)	the time by which the Census is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (e):	The Government decided to conduct Census 2021 under the Census Act, 1948 in two phases, viz., (a) House listing  & Housing  Census during   April-September,  2020  and  (b)  Population   Enumeration   during 9th to 28th  February,  2021.	 However,  Census   related  field  activities  have  been    postponed      till     further     orders      due   to   Covid-19   pandemic. -2/...	-2-LS.US.Q.NO.1315 FOR 27.07.2021In Census, data is collected on demographic and various socio-economic parameters like education, SC/ST, religion, language, marriage, fertility, disability, occupation and migration of the individuals.  Data on migration characteristics along with the reasons of migration such as work/employment and business is collected.                                                     *****
