                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2072






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Zero Defect, Zero Effect Scheme




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the total number of MSMEs that have started to follow the manufacturing patterns as provided under the guidelines of the Zero Defect, Zero Effect (ZED) scheme; (b)	whether the National Monitoring and Implementation Unit (NMIU), i.e. the Quality Council of India (QCI) has been following the guidelines on a consistent basis, if so, the details thereof; and(c)	whether there is any mechanism for reporting the periodic effect of the scheme on the environment, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)  As per information received from the Ministry of Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSME), more than 23,000 nos. of Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) have registered themselves to take up Zero Defect, Zero Effect (ZED) manufacturing under ZED Scheme. (b) & (c) The Quality Council of India (QCI) have organized awareness programme and training, developed e- learning modules, have carried out assessment, and have awarded ZED ratings. A provision has been made for periodic reporting of the effect of the scheme on the environment in the revised ZED scheme guidelines.   *****
