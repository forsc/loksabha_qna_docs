                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5134






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Construction of ROBs




Harish Dwivedi














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of Rail Over Bridges (ROBs) constructed/approved for construction over National Highways during the last five years, place and State-wise;(b)	the details of funds released and spent in this regard; and(c)	the time limit fixed to complete the under construction bridges?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c):  As per Memorandum of Understanding(MoU) signed between Ministry of Railways and Ministry of Road Transport and Highways (MoRTH) on  10.11.2014, MoRTH/ National Highway Authority of India (NHAI) will replace all Level Crossings on NH corridors by Road over Bridges (ROBs)/ Road under Bridges (RUBs) at their own cost on single entity basis. Status for construction of Road over Bridges (ROBs) in lieu of Level Crossings on National Highway is appended.  *****
