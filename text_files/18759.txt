                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

122






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






DDU-GKY in Tamil Nadu




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of youths from Tamil Nadu including Thoothukkudi, trained under the DDU-GKY, since April,2019, district-wise;(b) 	the number of youths from Tamil Nadu who are employed after completion of training since April 2019, district-wise, along with the details of their employment; and(c) 	the number of youths from SC, ST, OBC background from Tamil Nadu who were trained under the scheme and got employment since April 2019, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)      The district-wise number of youths from Tamil Nadu including Thoothukkudi trained since April 2019 is given at Annexure-1 (b)      The district-wise number of youths of Tamil Nadu placed in jobs in different sectors after completion of training since 2019-20 is given at Annexure-2. (c)      The district-wise number of youths from SC, ST and OBC background trained and placed in jobs since April 2019 is given at Annexure-3.*****
