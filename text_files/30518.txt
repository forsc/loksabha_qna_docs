                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2113






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Army Regiments




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of regiments in the country including the budget allocated to them annually;(b) whether there is any proposal of the Government to form Cooch Narayani Regiment, if so, the details thereof; (c) whether a number of different communities have requested the formation of new regiments in the country and if so, the details thereof; (d) whether there is any proposal to form a tribal regiment and if so, the details thereof; (e) whether the inspiration lines of freedom fighter Bir Birsa Munda form the slogan of any regiment; and (f) if so, the details along with the date of constitution of different regiments and their slogans thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) There are 27 Infantry Regiments in the Indian Army. Details are attached as per Appendix. Budget allocation in the Army is not carried out Regiment wise. (b) As per Government policy on the subject, all citizens irrespective of their class, creed, region or religion are eligible for recruitment in the Indian Army. After Independence, it has been the policy of the Government not to raise any new Regiment for a particular class/community/religion or region. Adequate vacancies are being provided to all classes to ensure equitable opportunity for recruitment into the Army.(c) Yes, Sir. Many Petitions, VIP references, Parliament questions, Private Member Bills etc for raising of new regiments based on Class/Region/Names of Historical and National Heroes etc have been received from time to time. However, as per Government Policy quoted above, no new regiment has been raised. (d) No, Sir. (e) Yes, Sir. One of the War Cries of BIHAR Regiment is “BIRSA MUNDA KI JAI”. (f) Indian Army has numerous Regiments across various Arms & Services. They have been constituted in different years as indicated at Appendix and have different slogans.*****
