                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4738






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Negative Trend in Tourism Sector After Outspread of Coronavirus




K. Muraleedharan














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that the tourism sector in the country has been showing a very negative growth after the spread of Coronavirus across the globe; (b) 	whether it is also true that the arrivals of tourists have sharply declined and is nearly nil in tourists favoured spots like Kerala; and (c) 	if so, the steps taken by the Government to correct the situation in the coming months?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	Ministry of Tourism, Government of India has not conducted specific study on impact of spread of Coronavirus on tourism sector in the country. However, provisional data of Foreign Tourist Arrivals (FTA) in the country during January & February, 2020 and those of like period in previous year are given below:<pre>	January 2019	February 2019	January 2020	February 2020FTA	11,03,380	10,87,694	11,18,150	10,15,632</pre>Data on tourist visits on various tourist spots are not maintained in Ministry of Tourism, Government of India. Details of steps taken by Ministry of Tourism, Government of India to sensitize about the measures required to be taken as well as to facilitate tourists in the country is attached at Annexure.******
