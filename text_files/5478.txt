                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5060






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Kanker-Bhanupratappur Railway Line




Mohan Mandavi














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Kanker district of Bastar division is naxal affected scheduled tribes district of Chhattisgarh State; (b) if so, whether the long pending demand of this district to link the Kanker district headquarters with railway line has been connected upto Bhanupratappur only; (c) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (d) whether the Government proposes to extend railway line from Bhanupratappur to Kanker district headquarters; and (e) if so, the time by which it is likely to be completed? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) Yes, Sir. (b) to (e) Railway projects are not sanctioned district wise. These projects are sanctioned based on many factors including operational requirement, last mile connectivity, financial viability, willingness of State Govt. to share the cost of project etc. New line project from Dallirajahara to Jagdalpur via Rowghat (235 km) is already in progress. This includes Bhanupratapur which falls in Kanker district. Railway line from Dalli Rjahara to  Keoti via Bhanupratapur has already been completed. There is no proposal to connect Bhanupratapur to Kanker. *****
