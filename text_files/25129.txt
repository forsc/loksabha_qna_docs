                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

97






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






ESI Hospitals




 Dharambir Singh













                                                    Sanganna Amarappa Karadi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the details of Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) hospitals set up in the country, State-wise;(b)whether the Government plans to set up new ESI hospitals in the country and if so, the details thereof, State-wise particularly in Charkhi Dadri, Mahendragarh and Nuh districts of Haryana and Koppal district in Karnataka; (c)if so, the time by which the said new ESI hospitals are likely to be set up; and(d)the details of the criteria followed by the Government for setting up new ESI hospitals?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI BHUPENDER YADAV)(a) to (d):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.***                                         ******* STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 97 TO BE ANSWERED ON 26.07.2021 ASKED BY SHRI DHARAMBIR SINGH AND SHRI SANGANNA AMARAPPA REGARDING “ESI HOSPITALS”(a) to (d)   There are 160 hospitals under the Employees’ State Insurance (ESI) Scheme, of which 50 hospitals are run by the Employees’ State Insurance (ESI) Corporation and 110 by the ESI Directorate of respective State Governments.   The State/UT wise details are placed at Annexure-I.  		Setting up of new ESI hospitals is a continuous and ongoing process.  The ESI Corporation has given approval for setting up of 50 new ESI hospitals. The State/UT wise details of these hospitals are placed at Annexure-II. The existing norms for setting up of a new ESI hospital are as under:Sl. No.	Number of beds	Minimum number of Insured Persons (IP) 1.	100 bedded hospital	   50,000 2.	150 bedded hospital	1,00,0003.	200 bedded hospital	1,50,0004.	250 bedded hospital	2,00,0005.	300 bedded hospital	2,50,0006.	350 bedded hospital	3,00,0007.	400 bedded hospital	3,50,0008.	500 bedded hospital	4,00,0009.	600 bedded hospital	5,00,000The Insured Person (IP) population is taken in a radius of 25 kms. and there should not be any other ESI hospital within a radius of 50 kms. The ESI Corporation has also approved setting up of 30 bedded ESI hospital to provide certain medical care services in areas with minimum 20000 IPs in a plain area and 15000 IPs in North eastern region/ hilly area. The present IP population in Charkhi Dadri, Mahendragarh and Nuh districts of Haryana and Koppal district of Karnataka is 4084, 3555, 2314 and 16,144, respectively.
