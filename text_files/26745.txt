                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2361






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Formation of FPOs




Sushil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of the target of setting up of 1,000 Farmer Producer Organisations 	(FPOs) for which the Government had allocated Rs 6,865 crore;(b) 	whether during 2020-21, a total of 2,200 FPO produce clusters have been 	allocated for the formation of FPOs, which also include specialised FPO 	produce clusters such as 100 for organic and 100 for oilseeds; and(c) 	whether these 2,200 FPO clusters are still functional?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	 Government of India has launched the Central Sector Scheme for “Formation and Promotion of 10,000 Farmer Producer Organizations(FPOs)”to form and promote 10,000 new FPOs with a total budgetary outlay of Rs.6865 Crore till 2027-28. During 2020-21, a total of 2365 FPOs were allocated to all the Implementing Agencies for Formation and Promotion of FPOs which includes specialized FPO produce clusters of 130 Organic and 100 Oilseeds.  So far, a total of 4465 Farmer Producer Organizations (FPO) produce clusters have been allocated to Implementing Agencies for formation of FPOs, of which, a total of 694 no. of FPOs have been registered.*****
