                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1684






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Outstanding Payments




Pramila Bisoyi














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of amount of outstanding payments, State-wise, that need to be paid to the State Governments and their implementing agencies in connection with procurements to Central Pool as of date; (b) 	the amount of outstanding payments to be paid to Odisha State Civil Supplies Corporations and by when the due will be cleared by the Ministry; (c) 	the quantity of surplus rice received by FCI from Odisha during the last five years, year-wise; and (d) 	whether there is any restriction on the receipt of surplus rice from the State of Odisha during the current financial year?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) and (b): 	Payment to States for Central Pool Procurement of Food grains i.e Wheat, Rice and Coarse Grains is made by Food Corporation of India (FCI)/Department of Food and Public Distribution (DFPD) on the basis of admissible claims made by State Government and its agencies which is an ongoing process. As per available information, details of State wise outstanding payments (as on 02.12.2021) including Odisha is at Annexure-A.......2/--2-(c): 	Details of surplus rice received by FCI from Odisha are as follows:KMS	Quantity (in LMT)2016-17	16.092017-18	12.362018-19	17.832019-20	17.212020-21	18.75** Rice delivery under KMS 2020-21 is still going on.(d): 	The estimates for procurement of Paddy (in terms of Rice) are finalized by the Government of India in consultation with State Governments, Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare (MoA&FW) and Food Corporation of India, before the commencement of each marketing season based upon estimated production, marketable surplus and agricultural crop pattern– July/Aug for Kharif and Feb/March for Rabi crops etc. Procurement in a State depends not only upon production but also upon other multiple factors like marketable surplus, MSP, prevailing market rate, demand & supply situation and participation of private traders etc.Odisha is a DCP State, wherein procurement is undertaken by State Govt. through its agencies. Only the surplus rice, after meeting State’s requirement, is delivered to FCI in central pool account for movement to deficit/consuming States. State of Odisha has delivered 18.75 LMT rice to FCI during KMS 2020-21 (upto 01.12.2021). Procurement of paddy crop of Kharif 2021-22 has already commenced through State procuring agencies and 7600 MT paddy has been procured upto 01.12.2021.**** Annexure-AANNXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a)&(b) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1684 FOR ANSWER ON 08.12.2021 IN THE LOK SABHA.State wise outstanding payments to be made for Central Pool Procurements	Outstanding Amount (in Rs crore)	 State	FCI	State claim under DCP*	TotalJharkhand	236.04	-	236.04Odisha	1500.12	5001.11	6501.23West Bengal	82.72	2268.08	2350.8Assam	134.22	-	134.22Haryana	1406.19	-	1406.19Punjab	2910.5	-	2910.5Rajasthan	22.14	-	22.14Uttar Pradesh	987.14	-	987.14Uttarakhand	47.37	130.47	177.84Andhra Pradesh	316.67	194.86	511.53Telangana	1464.64	287.15	1751.79Maharashtra	37.79	3456.34	3494.13Madhya Pradesh	548.43	4694.63	5243.06Chhattisgarh	751.39	2814.7	3566.09Karnataka	-	1278.01	1278.01*Only Admissible amount shall be released.****
