                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4253






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






National Council for Teacher Education




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to review the functioning of National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE); (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	the objectives to review NCTE; and (d)	whether the review of NCTE will be helpful and useful to the students and teachers community?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a)	: Yes Sir. (b)	: As per the mandate of New Education Policy 2020, National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE) in its new role as a Performance and Standard Setting Body (PSSB) will continue to draw the curricula, lay down academic standards and coordinate between teaching, research and extension of their domain/discipline, as member of the General Education Council (GEC) along with other tasks as assigned by Government of India.(c)	and (d):	 The New Education Policy 2020, proposes the revision and revamping of all aspects of the education structure, including its regulation and governance, to create a new system that is aligned with the aspirational goals of 21st century education, including Sustainable Development Goal 4 (SDG4), built upon India’s traditions and value systems. Various policy decisions have been mandated with this in view, of which transformation of NCTE as a PSSB is one of them.*****
