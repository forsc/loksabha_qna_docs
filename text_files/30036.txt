                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1682






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Exploration activities in NE States




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Mineral exploration activities in North Eastern States specially in Assam are put on hold which is causing bad impact on development of the region;(b) if so, the reasons therefor;(c) whether is it true that many mineral blocks are non-operational due to non clearance from environment and forest department;(d) if so, whether any steps have been taken to simplify Clearance Process to expedite the operations at mine blocks and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the details of all Mineral mine blocks in NE States specially in Assam, state-wise and location-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a) & (b): No, Sir.  Geological Survey of India [GSI], an attached office of Ministry of Mines, has prioritized mineral exploration keeping in view of the thrust given by Government of India and accordingly GSI is conducting G2/G3/G4 stage mineral exploration for various mineral commodities with an aim to augment mineral resource in different parts of the country including North Eastern States (NER).During the ensuing Field Season 2021-22, 16 nos. of mineral exploration projects have been taken up by GSI in NER and particularly in the state of Assam, two exploration projects have been taken and all projects are being executed without any hindrance.(c) & (d): As per the information available with Directorate of Geology and Mining, Assam, Govt. of Assam, the State Mines and Minerals Department has continuously been sitting for discussions with the State Environment and Forest Department to speed up the environment clearances. Since the constitution of the State Environment Impact Assessment Authority as the competent authority for issuing Environment Clearance Certificate, the process for the same has been considerably simplified and now, the process has become faster. (e): In practice, the resource bearing G2/G3 reports are being handed over to the concerned State Governments for auctioning of mineral concession.During last five years, (2016-17 to 2020-21) GSI has carried out a total of 113 nos. of mineral exploration projects for various commodities under different stages (G2/G3/G4/RMT) in different parts of NER.
