                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

245






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Passport Seva Kendras




Bidyut Baran Mahato













                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the passport offices and Passport Seva Kendras (PSKs) currently operational in the country, State/UT-wise;(b)	the number of post offices where passport service centres have been opened in the country till date, State/UT-wise;(c)	whether the Government has prepared/is preparing a list of the areas where new passport offices and PSKs may be opened and existing ones be modernized in the country;(d)	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; and (e)	the steps taken/being taken by the Government for maximum coverage area under these centres?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS(DR. SUBRAHMANYAM JAISHANKAR)(a) to (e) 	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) to (e) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 245 REGARDING “PASSPORT SEVA KENDRAS” FOR ANSWER ON 10.07.2019(a)	There are 36 Passport Offices in the country as on 04 July, 2019. The list is at Annexure-I. There are 93 Passport Seva Kendras (PSK) in the country as on 04 July, 2019. The list is at Annexure-II.(b)	There are 412 Post Office Passport Seva Kendras (POPSK) in the country as on 04 July, 2019. The list is at Annexure-III.(c) to (e)	The opening of Passport Kendras in various States in the country is an on-going activity and depends on various factors, including distance from the existing Passport Kendras and volume of applications from a particular region. 	The Passport Offices in the country are fully modernised. After the nation-wide rollout of the Passport Seva Project, the Passport Offices and the Passport Seva Kendras are working with best amenities and state-of-the-art architecture for passport issuance.
