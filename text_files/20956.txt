                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2227






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Health Insurance for Domestic Workers




Rekha Arun Verma














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has the data of people working as domestic workers across the country;(b)if not, whether the Government is formulating any scheme to identify and nominate domestic workers and if so, the details thereof;(c)whether the Government proposes to make Health Insurance mandatory for the domestic workers and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a):	The number of domestic workers as per Census 2011 are at Annexure-I.(b):	Does not arise.Contd..2/-:: 2 ::(c):	The Central Government had enacted the Unorganized Workers’ Social Security Act, 2008, now subsumed in the Code on Social Security, 2020, for providing social security to all unorganized workers including domestic workers. The Act provides formulation of social security schemes viz life and disability cover, health and maternity benefits & old age protection by the central Government. The state Government are mandated under the Act to formulate suitable welfare schemes for unorganized sector workers including domestic workers relating to provident fund, employment injury benefits housing, education schemes for children, skill up gradation of workers, financial assistance & old age homes. Several Central Sector Schemes like PMJJBY, PMSBY, PM-SYM etc. already provide social security cover to all the unorganised workers including domestic workers regarding insurance, disability, pension etc. Domestic Workers Sector Skill Council (DWSSC) under Ministry of Skill Development Entrepreneurship is imparting various skill development training programmes to domestic workers. Ayushman Bharat PMJAY provides secondary and tertiary health benefits to all unorganized workers including domestic workers who are covered as eligible beneficiaries as per Socio Economic Caste census Data, 2011.*                                                     ********
