                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5194






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Preservation and Beautification of Ancient Caves in North Mumbai




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken or proposes to take any steps to develop, preserve and beautify the most ancients religious caves in the country, including the Mandapeshwar caves located in Borivali, Dahisarsuburb of North Mumbai Parliamentary Constituency;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has also received requests from public representatives in this regard in March, 2022;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	 the updated status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b) 	Yes, Sir. The Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) has taken up steps to develop, preserve and beautify all 3693 centrally protected monuments, including  Mandapeshwar caves located in Borivali of North Mumbai,  in the country.(c)  to (e)	Yes, Sir.  A request has been received from Hon’ble Member of Parliament Shri  Gopal Chinnaya Shetty regarding development of the  Mandapeshwar caves, Borivali, Dahisar suburb of North Mumbai.  The ASI has initiated steps for the beautification & development of the Mandapeshwar Caves for the financial year 2022-23.
