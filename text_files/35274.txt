                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2619






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Impact of Pandemic on Urban Poor




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether it is true that urban poor greatly impacted due to pandemic and if so, the details thereof;b)	whether there is no employment guarantee for urban poor like the one for rural poor and if so, the details thereof; andc)	whether Government has proposal of having a scheme like National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (NREGA) for urban poor National Urban Employment Guarantee Scheme (NUEGS) and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                                                  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                     MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                           (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)	The impact of Covid pandemic has been largely disruptive inter-alia, in terms of economic activity. It affected all sectors including livelihoods of urban poor.  (b)& (c)	There is no employment guarantee scheme for urban poor. However, in order to mitigate adverse impacts of COVID-19 pandemic on urban poor, including urban street vendors, Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs has implemented schemes like Affordable Rental Housing Complexes (ARHCs) and PM Street Vendor’s AtmaNirbhar Nidhi. Further, Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana-National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM) is being implemented in all statutory towns of the country to reduce poverty and vulnerability of urban poor households on a sustainable basis.                                        *****
