                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1236






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Pradhan Mantri Kisan Pension Yojana




Andimuthu Raja














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Pension Yojana (PMKPY);(b)	the number of beneficiaries who have joined PMKPY in the country so far, State-wise including Tamilnadu;(c)	the number of States implementing this scheme and the details thereof; and(d)	the efforts made to make the farmers aware of this scheme at large?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maan-Dhan Yojana (PM-KMY), a Pension Scheme for Small and Marginal Farmers (SMF) was launched by the Government in the month of September, 2019 to provide old age protection and social security. PM-KMY is a voluntary and contributory pension scheme in which eligible SMF falling in the age group of 18-40 years are required to make a monthly contribution between Rs. 55-200 and matching contribution are shared by Government of India to the pension fund manager, Life Insurance Corporation of India. From the accruals, a monthly pension of Rs. 3000 per month would be provided as and when the farmers attained age of 60 years.(b) & (c):	The scheme is implemented all across the country. The details of               state-wise number of farmers enrolled (including in Tamil Nadu) under PM-KMY as on 23.07.2021 is enclosed  at Annexure I.(d):	The Common Service Centre (CSC, e-governance services India Ltd.) under MeiTY, Government of India which is responsible to enroll the eligible farmers has taken campaign from time to time to aware the desirous farmers. Besides, the State Nodal officer for implementation of PM-Kisan / PM-KMY have been   assisted the CSC in the awareness campaign.
