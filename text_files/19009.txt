                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

250






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Irregularities in AINC Camp




Kotagiri Sridhar













                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Ramya  Haridas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has identified any irregularities while selecting cadets for All India Nau Sainik Camp(AINSC) 2018 in Andhra Pradesh;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the action taken by the Government against the officials responsible for such irregularities; and(d) the details of cadets selected and attended the camp?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) to (c):  In connection with the All India Nau Sainik Camp 2018 held at Karwar, Karnataka, a Court of  Inquiry is instituted by NCC Directorate of Andhra Pradesh & Telengana on 21.03.2020 to investigate the complaint regarding the selection process.(d)  In this event 590 cadets were selected for the camp.  Details are at Annexure.*****
