                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

264






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Welfare Schemes for Sportspersons




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to formulate any scheme for the welfare of Indian sportspersons of National and International level; (b) if so, the details thereof along with the welfare schemes formulated for the purpose; and (c) the total amount spent for the welfare schemes/programmes meant for sportspersons of National and International level during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (c) : A statement is laid on the table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 264 FOR ANSWER ON 22/03/2022 REGARDING ‘WELFARE SCHEMES FOR SPORTSPERSONS’ BY SHRI RAMDAS C. TADAS, HON’BLE MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT(a) and (b): Yes Sir. The Government is already running two schemes viz. scheme of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons (PDUNWFS) and Scheme of Pension to Meritorious Sportspersonsfor the welfare of Indian sportspersons of National and International level. (1)Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for SportspersonsThe Objectives of the scheme are:(i)	To provide suitable assistance to outstanding sportspersons now living in indigentcircumstances;(ii)	To provide suitable assistance to outstanding sportspersons injured during the period of their training for competitions and also during the competitions, depending on the nature of theinjury;(iii)	To provide suitable assistance to sportspersons for medical treatment;(iv)	To provide suitable assistance to promote welfare of sportspersons generally in order to alleviate distress among them and their dependents in indigentcircumstances;(v)	To provide assistance to sportspersons living in indigent circumstances for training, procurement for sports equipment, participation in national and international sports eventsetc.The Quantum of assistance given under the scheme for different purposes will be as under:<pre> S. No.	Purpose 	Amount of assistance a.	Assistance to sportspersons living in indigent circumstances	UptoRs. 5 lakhIn addition, monthly pension of Rs. 5,000/- per month may be considered for specific period taking into consideration merit of request of a sportsperson.   b.	Assistance to Families of deceased Sportspersons	Upto Rs. 5 lakh c.	Assistance for Medical Treatment to sportspersons or family members 	Upto Rs. 10 lakh d.	Assistance for injuries sustained  during training for and participation in Sports  competitions	Upto Rs. 10 lakh e.	Assistance for training, procurement of equipment and participation in national and international sports events	Upto Rs. 2.5 lakh f.	Assistance to coaches and support personnel 	Upto Rs. 2 lakh </pre>(2)Scheme of Pension to Meritorious SportspersonsThe objective ofScheme of Pension to Meritorious Sportspersons is to provide assured monthly income through annuity for outstanding sportspersons that has become imperative due to the fact that sportspersons spend prime of their youth in pursuit of intensive training, sacrificing other beneficial opportunities normally available. The assured monthly income will be a compensation for the opportunities spurned by them in pursuit of their high ambition and achievement in sports.The rate of monthly pension under the scheme is as under:<pre> S. No.	Category of meritorious sportspersons	Rate of Pension(Rs./per month)1	Medalists at the Olympic Games / Para Olympic Games	20,0002	Gold medalists at the World Cup/World Championship* in Olympic and Asian Games disciplines	16,0003	Silver and Bronze medalists at the World Cup* in Olympic and Asian Games disciplines	14,0004	Gold medalists of the Asian/Commonwealth Games/Para Asian Games	14,0005	Silver and Bronze medalists of the Asian/Commonwealth Games/ Para Asian Games	12,000 </pre>*  WorldCup/WorldChampionshipheldonceinfour year onlyshall be considered.(c) The total amount spent for the welfare schemes/ programmes meant for sportspersons of National and International level during the last three years under the two schemes viz. scheme of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons (PDUNWFS) and Scheme of Pension to Meritorious Sportsperson is given as under:<pre> S.No.	Name of the Scheme	2020-21(in Rs.)	2019-20(in Rs.)	2018-19(in Rs.)1.	Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons (PDUNWFS)	1,18,85,000	63,32,415	66,00,0002.	Pension to Meritorious Sportsperson	13,22,00,000	46,99,00,000	29,77,00,000 </pre>*****
