                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3656






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Vegetable Processing Units




Kirron  Kher














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken any special steps to set up vegetable based processing industries in Punjab, Haryana and Chandigarh;  (b)	if not, the reasons therefor; and  (c)	the reaction of the Government on the quality of vegetables being grown in the districts of the aforesaid States?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a) & (b): The Ministry does not establish any food processing unit of its own. Rather, the Ministry promotes setting-up of food processing units under the umbrella scheme of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) by means of financial assistance. (c): 	Processing quality of vegetables are also grown in some districts of the aforesaid States. *****
