                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3763






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Theft/Burglary in Government Residential Complexes




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of crimes like theft, burglary, etc. reported by the residents of Central Government Residential Complexes to Delhi Police during each of the last three years;(b) whether the concerned Residents Welfare Associations (RWAs) are empowered to control the access to the gates of these Government residential complexes and manage them as per instructions of the Department of Personnel and Training (DoPT);(c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the Government agency responsible to ensure safety and security of these complexes in New Delhi;(d) whether regular periodic meetings are being conducted by the Delhi Police with the concerned RWAs and Area Welfare Officers to address their security concerns; and(e) if so, the details of such meetings held during the said period, area-wise along with the steps taken so far for implementing the decisions taken in the meetings?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	: Details of theft and burglary cases reported by the residents of Central Government Residential Complexes to Delhi Police during the last three years and the current year (upto 15.02.2020) are as under:-2/...-2-LS.US.Q.NO.3763 FOR 17.03.2020Year	No. of cases registered	No. of persons arrested	Theft	Burglaries	Theft	Burglaries2017	477	125	144	512018	564	88	61	722019	449	83	86	1072020 (upto 15.02.2020)	61	02	05	03(b)	and (c): The Department of Personnel & Training  and Delhi Police have issued guidelines to all Residents Welfare Associations (RWAs) in Government colonies regarding access control to the premises.
