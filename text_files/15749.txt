                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4564






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Stone Craft Village




Rajashree Mallick














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering to establish craft village at Raghurajpur in Puri district to develop stone craft work of Odisha;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the funds allocated during the last three years; and (c) the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (c):	Yes Sir, the Government has already sanctioned craft village at Raghurajpur, Puri district to develop stone craft work of Odisha during the year 2014-15. The Government has so far released Rs. 6.00 crore for setting-up of craft village at Raghurajpur in Puri district of Odisha under the component of Infrastructure and technology development scheme. The major works executed are house-cum-work shed improvement (for 136 units), common facility renovation (includes renovation of existing society building etc.), new construction (includes guest house, cafeteria, toilets etc.), internal roads in the village/renovation of internal roads, infrastructure (includes external water supply system, external sewerage system, storm water, rain water harvesting & internal electrification for village), external development (includes pathway in the rear side on both sides of the craft village), signage landscape & interiors.****
