                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3088






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Beautification of Temples in Odisha




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the places of pilgrimages like temples and ancient monuments which are being developed in the State of Odisha;(b)	whether steps have also been taken for the beautification of such temples and monuments;(c)	if so, whether the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI), Odisha Unit has made any recommendation in this regard;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and (e)	the funds provided for the beautification and development?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)  	The conservation, preservation and environmental development work of protected monuments and temples in the country including in the State of Odisha is a continuous process and same is attended regularly by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) as per the availability of resources.(b)	Yes Sir. In addition to conservation of monuments the development works are attended keeping regard to the archaeological norms and principles and Conservation Policy of ASI, 2014, with a thrust on retaining originality, authenticity & integrity of protected monuments. (c)  &  (d) ASI Odisha Unit submits Annual Conservation Programme following conservation guidelines, archaeological norms & principles and Conservation Policy of ASI which is implemented after due scrutiny.(e)	The funds allocated for conservation, preservation & environmental development work of protected monuments of ASI in Odisha, for current financial year 2020-21, is Rs. 552 lakh.*****
