                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4505






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Ayush Drugs




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government proposes any specific course for technical personnel to ensure quality of raw materials in the production of Ayush drugs and if so, the details thereof; (b) the details of course structure along with the name of institutions identified for the said course; and (c) the details of financial and technical support being given by the Government to the institutions conducting the said course?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (c):   Modules of identification and quality assessment parameters for the raw materials used in the manufacturing of Ayurvedic, Siddha, Unani and Homeopathic (ASU&H) drugs are inbuilt in the syllabus and curriculum of respective courses of study conducted under the provisions of Indian Medicine Central Council Act, 1970 and Homoeopathy Central Council Act, 1973. Pharmacy Courses of AYUSH are also separately conducted by some institutes in which training is imparted for identification and quality parameters of raw materials. As per Drugs & Cosmetics Rules, 1945 such qualified technical personnel are required in the manufacturing units of ASU&H drugs.  State-wise details of institutions conducting diploma & degree courses of ASU&H pharmacy are provided in the annexure. National Medicinal Plants Board has published Good Agricultural Practices and Good Field Collection Practices of medicinal plants and Ministry of AYUSH published Ayurvedic, Siddha, Unani and Homeopathic pharmacopoeias containing standards of materials used in the manufacturing of ASU&H drugs. Financial support is provided to the eligible institutions through National AYUSH Mission and Central Schemes.
