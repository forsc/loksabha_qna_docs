                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

153






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.09.2020






Pradhan Mantri Urja Ganga Scheme




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the progress of the PradhanMantriUrja Ganga scheme in Odisha;(b) the number of kilometres of pipe line laid so far, district-wise;(c) the cities which will have the facility of piped natural gas for domestic use and thetime by which they will get; and(d) the number of households connected through piped natural gas so far?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) GAIL is executing Jagdishpur-Haldia-Bokaro-Dhamra Pipeline (JHBDPL) with extension ofBarauni-Guwahati Pipeline (BGPL) and spur line to Dhamra-Haldia (DHPL) as part ofPradhanMantriUrja Ganga Project (PMUG) to connect the states of Uttar Pradesh, Bihar,Jharkhand, West Bengal, Odisha and Assam. GAIL has informed that out of about 2,655 km ofpipeline, approximately 1,401 km has been lowered as of now, and 766 km pipeline crossesthrough the state of Odisha of which approximately 196 km has been lowered as on 8.09.2020.(b) District wise details are as under:Sl. No. District Lowered (in km)1 Dhenkanal 332 Cuttack 323 Puri 14 Jajpur 265 Jagatsinghpur 36 Khorda 107 Deogarh 88 Sundergarh 309 Jharsuguda 1910 Sambalpur 34(c) Further, PNGRB has authorised City Gas Distribution entities covering more than 70districts of which there are 19 districts in State of Odisha.(d) About 55,200 number of households getting piped natural gas, along the route of JHBDPL atpresent.**
