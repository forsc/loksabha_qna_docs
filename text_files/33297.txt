                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

666






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Availibility of Oxygen Equipments during Omicron Wave




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has conducted a high level meeting with all States to review the preparedness of the entire spectrum of oxygen equipment including ventilators, Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) plants, oxygen concentrators and oxygen cylinders for the timely and effective management of COVID-19 rise across the country fuelled by the virus’s Omicron variant;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the outcome of the meeting;(c) whether the Government proposes to provide any financial and technical assistance to various States subsequent to the said meeting; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) & (b): To review the preparedness of the entire spectrum of oxygen equipment including ventilators, Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) plants, oxygen concentrators and oxygen cylinders and for the timely and effective management of COVID-19, the Government has constituted various Empowered Groups (EG) including EG 4 on Oxygen related work. Apart  from the monitoring by Empowered Groups, weekly review meetings are being held by the Ministry with State Nodal Officers of States/ UTs to review Medical Oxygen infrastructure preparedness & for timely and effective management of COVID-19 rise across the country. The outcomes of these meetings are following:-(i)	Installation & commissioning of all PSA Plants under PM-CARES.(ii)	Monitoring of establishment of PSA Plants under PSUs, Government of India/ State funds.(iii)	Mock Drills of all PSA Plants conducted.(iv)	Capacity building for PSA Plant maintenance.(v)	Oxygen Audit by all States/ UTs.(vi)	Training of Oxygen Stewards for rationale use of Oxygen. (c) & (d) The Government of India has provided the necessary financial and technical assistance to all States/UTs in building up the oxygen infrastructure. Under Emergency Response Package (ECRP)-I & II, the Government has provided Rs. 15,000 Crore and Rs 23,123 Crore respectively to the states for augmentation of health infrastructure, oxygen supported beds, isolation beds, ICUs & Human Resources etc. ****
