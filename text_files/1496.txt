                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1441






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Cultural Exhibitions in Foreign Countries to Promote Indian Culture




Vijay Kumar Dubey













                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                








                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to organize cultural exhibitions in foreign countries to Promote Indian cultural worldwide including South East Asia;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the reason therefor; and(d)	 the steps taken/proposed to be taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM (I/C)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d)Government of India has been organizing Festival of India  abroad under the scheme “Promotion of International Cultural Relations”.    All the stakeholders are consulted and geographical representation is taken into consideration before selecting a country to organize Festival of India.     During Festival of India, a number of activities like dance&musical performances (classical, regional, folk dance and drama etc.), food festival, literary festival, film festival, yoga, etc. including cultural exhibitions are organized.*****
