                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1020






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Sex Ratio at Birth




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 		whether there has been improvement in the Sex Ratio at Birth (SRB) across the country, on account of the Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao Abhiyan; (b) 		if so, the details thereof along with the details of districts with the most marked improvement in SRB across the country; (c) 		whether the Government has noticed any improvement in the literacy rate of girls as a result of this campaign; and (d) 		if so, the details thereof along with the average female literacy rate before and after the implementation of the Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao Abhiyan, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (b)	Beti Bachao Beti Padhao Scheme was launched during F.Y. 2014-15 with overall goal to celebrate the girl child and enable her education. Sex Ratio at Birth (SRB) is showing improving trends and has increased from 918 to 931 at National level during the time period between F.Y. 2014-15& 2018-19.Out of 640 districts (as per census 2011),395 districts have shown improvement in SRB during this period, as per reports of Health Management Information System of Ministry of Health & Family Welfare(c) to (d)   The literacy data in the country is collected through decennial census conducted by Registrar General of India.  The female literacy rate in the age group of 7 and above as per last Census 2011 is 64.63 percent.  Gross Enrolment Ratio of girls at secondary education level was 80.51% in 2017-18 (provisional) as per Unified District Information System for Education (U-DISE).********
