                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

832






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Late Running of Trains




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether problem of trains running late has not been resolved despite sincere efforts of the Government;(b) if so, the details of the action plan of the Government thereon; (c) the number of trains that started/ arrived late from its point of origin or destination, during the last three months and the number of days on which trains arrived late at destination points and the reasons therefor; (d) the extent to which Government has been successful to run trains on time even in adverse conditions when there was mist and fog and steps taken to streamline the signalling system; and (e) the steps being taken by the Government in this regard to achieve complete success?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (e)  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
