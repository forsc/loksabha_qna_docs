                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2880






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Scheduled Caste Candidates




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number and details of candidates belonging to Scheduled caste who have passed the Indian Administrative Service Examination during the last three years but could not be imparted training, State/UT-wise; (b) 	the details of provisions on the basis of which the Government selects the candidates belonging to the Scheduled Caste; (c) 	whether the quota for the scheduled caste is not being filled through this selection process and general category candidates are being appointed in place of them; and (d) 	if so, the corrective measures taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a):   Recruitment to Indian Administrative Service (IAS) is made on the basis of Civil Services Examination (CSE) conducted by UPSC on annual basis. As per records available with Lal Bahadur Shastri National Academy of Administration, Mussoorie, the details of Scheduled Caste candidates who were selected to IAS but could not be imparted training, are as under:           2018 Batch: Nil           2019 Batch: Nil           2020 Batch: One candidate did not join the Service.(b):    As per the policy of the Government, the percentage of reservation provided for Scheduled Castes is 15% for recruitment through CSE.(c) & (d):   No, Sir. The vacancies for Scheduled Caste are being filled as per the prescribed norms.*****
