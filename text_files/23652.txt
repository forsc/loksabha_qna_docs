                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4795






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Stoppage at Dabra Station




Vivek Narayan Shejwalkar














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a large number of people from other States visit Dabra Vidhan Sabha of Gwalior as it is making progress in agricultural and industrial sector and a large number of pulse and rice mills are located here and the krishi mandi here is among the leading mandis of the State;(b) if so, whether it is proposed to provide stoppage of Shridham express, Ujjaini express, G. T. express or other express trains at the Dabra station of Gwalior; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): During financial year (F.Y.) 2019-20, approximately 37.33 lakh passengers (both inward and outward) were handled at Dabra railway station.  (b) & (c):  Provision of stoppage of 12191/92 Hazrat Nizamuddin-Jabalpur Shridham Express (Daily), 12615/16 MGR Chennai Central-New Delhi G.T. Express (Daily), 14309/10 Ujjain-Dehradun Ujjaini Express (2 days a week) and other trains at Dabra is neither operationally expedient nor commercially justified, at present. However, provision of stoppage of trains is an on-going process on Indian Railways.*****
