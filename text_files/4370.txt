                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3927






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Call Rates in Intra Network and Inter Network




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) has issued any directive to bridge the gap between the call rates in intra network and inter network so as to have a uniform rate in every network; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the separate details of the private telecom companies complying with and not complying with the said directions of the TRAI as on date; (d) 	whether the Government has taken any action against the private telecom companies not complying with the aforesaid directions of TRAI; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)  	TRAI has not issued any such direction. (b) to (e)	Do not arise in view of above.
