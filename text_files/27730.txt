                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3268






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Refurbishing of Famous Temples in Tamil Nadu




T.R.V.S. Ramesh














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to enhance the visual impact of the various temples in the Cuddalore district, from the point of view of tourist attraction; (b) 	the district-wise total number of temples in the State of Tamil Nadu; and (c) 	whether the Government proposes to strengthen and refurbish the famous temple structures in Cuddalore district being a tourist attraction place from the point of view of pilgrimage and worship?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (c):	The Ministry of Tourism under PRASHAD Scheme provides financial assistance to the State Governments / Union Territory Administrations to create pilgrimage tourism infrastructure at the selected temple destinations including enhancing the visual impact of the temples across the Country. The temples / destinations are selected based on the proposals received from the respective State Governments/ Union Territory Administrations.  As of now there is no proposal under consideration for a temple in Cuddalore district in the Ministry of Tourism. (b):	No such list is maintained by the Ministry of Tourism.*******
