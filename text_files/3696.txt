                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3278






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Textile Training Centres




Virendra  Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the total number of textile training centres functioning in the country at present, State-wise including Madhya Pradesh;(b) whether there is any proposal to open more such centres in the country;(c) if so, whether any proposal has been received from Madhya Pradesh in this regard; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (d): This Ministry is implementing various Textiles sector schemes/programmes and conducts textiles training activities in partnership with training institutes, industry partners, State Governments by providing them necessary support for operational technical requirements. The details of the various Textiles Training Centres under various Textile Sector Schemes is at Annexure. Opening of training centres under various schemes is need based and demand driven.
