                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1708






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Inauguration of Kendriya Vidyalayas




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a large number of Kendriya Vidyalayas across the country are waitingfor inauguration after completing all constructions works; and(b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons for the delay in opening them?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan has informed that a school building is madefunctional as soon as its construction gets completed so that better infrastructurefacility becomes available to the students at the earliest. At present, no completedbuildings are awaiting commencement of classes in the absence of inauguration.*****
