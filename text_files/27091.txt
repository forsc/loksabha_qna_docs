                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2853






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Renaming of Airport




Arvind Ganpat Sawant














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to rename Aurangabad Airport as a Sambhajinagar Airport and if so, the details thereof; and(b) the present status of renaming the airport and the time by which it is likely to be renamed?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a): The request for renaming of Aurangabad airport as Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj Airport has been received from the Government of Maharashtra along with the Resolution passed by the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly.; (b): The final decision in the matter is taken by the Union Cabinet after due consultations with the concerned Central Ministries/Departments. As decision on renaming of Aurangabad Airport involves consultation at various levels, the exact time to rename Aurangabad Airport cannot be ascertained at this stage.
