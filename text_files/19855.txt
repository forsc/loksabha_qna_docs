                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

950






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Shortage of Polytechnics ans Skill Centres in NE States




Ramesh Chander Kaushik













                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is a huge shortage of Polytechnics and Skill Centres in North-Eastern States including Assam;   (b)	 if so, the district-wise details thereof; (c)	 whether the Government proposes to increase the polytechnics and Skill Centres in future; and (d)	 if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	& (b)  	No Sir.  (c) & (d)    	All states of North-Eastern Region including Assam are covered under the component ‘Setting Up of New Polytechnics’. Sixty six (66) polytechnics are being set up in the North-Eastern states. In the State of Assam, Twenty one (21) districts are covered.  District-wise details are given in Annexure-I.  The component ‘Community Development Through Polytechnics (CDTP)’ provides non-formal, short-term skill training programmes through ten (10) AICTE approved polytechnics including six (06) in Assam.Further, in North-Eastern Region, a total of eighty three (83) number of ITIs are available. This includes thirty five (35) ITIs in Assam (Annexure-II).In addition, North-Eastern Region have a total of three hundred ninety one (391) short term training centres including one hundred and eighty five (185) in Assam and one hundred ninety (190) special project training centres including eighty eight (88) in Assam under PMKKs (Annexure-III).
