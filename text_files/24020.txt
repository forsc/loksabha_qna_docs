                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4996






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Deployment of Ambulance and Patrolling Vehicles on NHs




Ramapati Ram Tripathi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) Whether the Government has made it mandatory for every road developer to deploy an ambulance and a patrolling vehicle at a gap of every 45 kilometers on National Highways (NHs) to take the victims of road accidents to the nearest medical aid center; (b)  If so, the   details thereof; (c) Whether the Government has received complaints against road developers regarding deployment of ambulances and patrolling vehicles during the last three years and the current year; and (d) If so, the details thereof along with the action taken by the Government thereon? 






ANSWER




                                                         (a) to (b) As part of incident management under operation and maintenance of the NH stretches, Highway ambulances and Patrolling vehicles are deployed in their respective service area to extend assistance in case of any emergency on National Highways.(c) to (d)  Road users generally register all their highway related complaints through 1033 helpline number and the same are duly addressed by the corresponding Regional Offices and Project Implementation Unit of NHAI and are resolved at local level itself.*****
