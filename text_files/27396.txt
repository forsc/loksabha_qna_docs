                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3074






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Setting up of AIIMS at Indo-Nepal border




Sunil Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether AIIMS hospital is proposed to be set up in remotely situated Valmikinagar at Indo-Nepal border; and (b) if so, the details thereof and if not the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(Dr. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	& (b): Government of India has plans to establish AIIMS in all States of the Country in a phased manner under the Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY).  As far as Bihar is concerned, one AIIMS at Patna has already been established and is functional. Another AIIMS at Darbhanga has also been approved. There is no proposal under consideration of the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare to establish a new AIIMS in Valmikinagar.*********
