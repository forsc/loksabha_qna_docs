                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

938






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Progress of National Horticulture Mission




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the progress of the National Horticulture Mission (NHM), the funds disbursed and the number of beneficiaries under the scheme since 2015, State-wise, including Jharkhand; and (b) the funds allocated and utilized in Jharkhand for the scheme so far for 2021-22?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????  (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	 National Horticulture Mission (NHM) a sub-scheme of Centrally Sponsored scheme of Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) is being implemented by Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare for the holistic development of horticulture in the country. All States/UTs are covered under MIDH. 		State-wise details of the progress including Jharkhand under the major components of the NHM scheme since 2015 is placed at Annexure-I. The State-wise details of the funds disbursed to all the States including Jharkhand is placed at Annexure-II. 		Since the MIDH scheme is being implemented by State Governments, therefore, the beneficiary data is maintained by the States.(b):	During 2021-22, an amount of Rs. 35.00 crore (including State share) has been allocated to Jharkhand under NHM scheme and utilization of an amount of Rs. 39.90 Lakhs (including State share) has been reported by the State.
