                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3518






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Funds Allocated Under Capacity Building Component of PRASAD Scheme




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is implementing projects under the Capacity Building, Skill Development component of the PRASAD scheme;(b)	if so, the details of projects being implemented thereof, State-wise;(c)	the quantum of funds allocated and utilized under the Capacity Building component of the PRASAD scheme; and(d)	the details about the number of beneficiaries who were trained under the Capacity Building Component of the PRASAD scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM  				(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	 to (d): PRASHAD scheme of the Ministry of Tourism has primary focus on infrastructure development to achieve rejuvenated pilgrimage and spiritual experience. Capacity Building and skill development components are included among the admissible components under the scheme.  The project components under the scheme are sanctioned on the basis of requirements and requests received from the respective State Governments. The Ministry has not received any request from the respective state governments for such components in the sanctioned projects. *****
