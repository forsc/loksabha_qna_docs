                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3304






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






NARI Portal




Shantanu Thakur













                                                    Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor
                                                








                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                








                                                    Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                








                                                    Pradeep Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has launched ‘NARI portal’ and ‘e-Samvad portal’ to make citizen aware about various schemes and initiatives for women as the information in this regard is not easily accessible in one place;(b)	if so, the number of women registered on the portals;(c)	whether all the villages of the country have been covered under this and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the manner in which it is likely to help in formulation of policies for the welfare of women?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (d):  National Repository of Information For Women (NARI) Portal was launched on 02nd January 2018 to provide the information about the Government Schemes and legislations for Women at one platform.The NARI portal summarizes over 350 government schemes and other important information for the benefit of women. It provides links to the Ministries, Departments and autonomous bodies offering these schemes as well as easy access to online applications and grievance redressal.e-Samvad portal was launched to provide a platform for NGO and civil society  to provide their feedback, suggestions, put up grievances, share best practices etc. *****
