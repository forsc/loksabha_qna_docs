                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4325






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






National Food Mission




Sudarshan Bhagat














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether a large number of farmers are being benefitted by the National Food Mission;b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise; andc)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ????????????????????????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	to (c) Yes Sir. The Government is implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) of National Food Security Mission (NFSM) benefiting farmers in 28 States and 2 Union Territories viz; Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh. Under the scheme, assistance is provided to farmers for interventions like cluster demonstrations on improved package of practices, demonstrations on cropping system, seeds of High Yielding Varieties (HYVs)/hybrids, improved farm machineries/tools, water saving devices, micro nutrients & plant protection chemicals, processing & post-harvest equipments, trainings to the farmers etc. The expenditure on subsidy is shared between centre & state government on 60:40 for general states, 90:10 for North Eastern states & Hilly states and 100% Central share in case of Union Territories. The state-wise release of funds for under various components of NFSM during last 3 years are at Annexure.
