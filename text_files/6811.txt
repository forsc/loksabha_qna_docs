                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

505






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Quality of Telecom Services




Mahua Moitra














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the statistics on the quality of service provided by the Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) in the country during each of the last three years, State/UT-wise and TSP-wise; and(b) 	the statistics on call drop rate and calls with good voice quality rate in terms of percentage, State/UT-wise and TSP-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) & (b) Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) has been monitoring the performance of telecom service providers against the benchmarks for various Quality of Service parameters laid down by TRAI by way of Quality of Service regulations issued from time to time, through Quarterly Performance Monitoring Reports (PMRs). The performance is assessed for a Licence Service Area (LSA) as a whole.Prior to 01st October,2017 PMRs were assessed quarterly for 2G and 3G services separately. After issue of “The Standards of Quality of Service for Basic (Wireline) Service and Cellular Telephone Mobile services (Fifth) Amendment, Regulations (2017)” effective from 01st October 2017, quarterly PMRs for Cellular Mobile Services are being accessed together.The statistics of Quality of Service in the country, including statistics of call drops and good voice quality, during last three years, LSA and TSPs-wise are detailed in Annexure-I (for quarter ending September 2019), Annexure-II (for quarter ending September 2018), Annexure-III(a) (for quarter ending September 2017 for 2G Service) and Annexure- III(b) (for quarter ending September 2017 for 3G Service).The above statistics is also being published by TRAI on its website www.trai.gov.in under the Reports as Performance Indicator Report.
