                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4156






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Threat to Wildlife




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that a large number of species of wildlife in the country are facing threats to its habitat due to encroachments and other affiliated/associated reasons;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the number of wildlife sanctuaries in the country, State/UT-wise:(d)	whether the Government has any proposal to set up new wildlife sanctuaries in the country; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	and (b)	Encroachments and unplanned human actions do have an impact in the wildlife and its habitats. Species like Great Indian Bustard, elephant,  tigers, Sangai deer etc have been effected  due to  negative human interaction.(c)	A per the available information, there are total number of 870 Protected Areas in India i.e. 104 National Parks, 551 Wildlife Sanctuaries, 88 Conservation Reserve and 127 Community Reserve. State/ UT-wise detail of Wildlife Sanctuaries is given in the Annexure.(d)	Declaration of Wildlife Sanctuaries falls under the jurisdiction of concerned State Government. (e)	In view of (d) above, the question does not arise. ****
