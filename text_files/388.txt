                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

412






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Promotion of Spiritual-Cultural-Eco Tourism




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any statistics on the number of foreign tourists visited the country in the last financial year and forex earned through it; (b)	 if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the details of the actions taken by the Government to promote spiritual-cultural-eco tourism in the country; (d) 	whether the Government proposes to take any measure to promote cultural-spiritual-eco tourism putting a place like Thrissur as a centre, which is considered as the cultural capital of Kerala; and (e) 	whether the Government has identified any potential sites for developing spiritual circuit tourism in Kerala and if so, the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) and (b):		The number of Foreign Tourist Arrivals and estimated Foreign Exchange Earnings during the financial year 2018-19 were 1,06,06,049 (provisional) and US $ 27.776 billion respectively.(c) to (e):	Development and promotion of tourist places is primarily the responsibility of concerned State Government/UT Administration. However, the Ministry of Tourism, Government of India promotes India as a holistic destination covering its various tourism destinations and products across different States/Union Territories in the country. The Ministry as part of its on-going activities, annually releases print, electronic, online and outdoor media campaigns in the international and domestic markets, under the Incredible India brand-line, to promote various tourism destinations and products of various interests including those for spiritual, cultural and eco tourism.For creation of tourism infrastructure in the country, the Ministry has two flagship schemes viz. Swadesh Darshan - Integrated Development of Theme-Based Tourist Circuits and PRASHAD- Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive. The projects including those for Spiritual circuit sanctioned under these schemes for the State of Kerala are given in the Annexure.   ***********
