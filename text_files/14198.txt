                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2785






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Innovative Defence Projects




 Dr. T. Sumathy (a) Thamizhachi Thangapandian














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government has taken special measures to promote new and innovative defence production projects under Make in India programme in Tamil Nadu and other States;(b) if so, the details thereof and the list of projects given approval during the last three years;(c) whether the Government has taken note on certain defence production projects particularly manufacturing of small arms under the Make in India programme not been given approval by the Ordnance Factory Board (OFB);(d) if so, the details thereof and the list of projects pending for approval in the last three years, year-wise; and(e) the decision taken by Government and the Ordnance Factory Board in this regard and to expedite the process of approval?
