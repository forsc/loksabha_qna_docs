                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1612






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Works under Swachch Bharat Mission




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of works undertaken under Swachh Bharat Mission in the districts of Gujarat so far and the details of funds allocated/released/utilised thereunder during the last three years, district-wise;(b) the estimated number of people benefitted under this mission; and(c) the details of the works undertaken in Gujarat under the said mission?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) District-wise number of Individual household latrines (IHHLs) constructed under Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen) [SBM(G)] in Gujarat, as per the data reported by the State on the online Integrated Management Information System (IMIS) of SBM(G) are given at Annexure.  Under SBM(G), funds are released to States/UTs. Further distribution of the same to the Districts/Blocks/Gram Panchayats is done by the States/UTs.  Details of Centre share funds released to the State of Gujarat under SBM(G) during the last three years and the funds utilised as per the utilisation certificates received from the State are as under:Financial Year	Funds released (Rs. in crore)	Funds utilised (Rs. in crore)2018-19	192.92	191.692019-20	238.45	660.192020-21	312.56	383.13Utilisation of funds in 2019-20 and 2020-21 is more than the release amount.  This is on account of availability of unspent funds of previous years with the State and interest amounts accrued, etc. (b) All the villages in the country including Gujarat have declared themselves Open Defecation Free (ODF) as on 2.10.2019.  The benefits of ODF are accrued to all the people in a village.  However, with reference to no. of IHHLs constructed, estimated number of persons benefitted under SBM (G) in Gujarat (assuming 5 persons per household) is 2,10,17,030.(c) 	As per the data reported by Gujarat on the IMIS of SBM(G), 42,03,406 Individual household latrines (IHHLs) have been constructed in the State under SBM(G) from 2014-15 to 2021-22.  *****
