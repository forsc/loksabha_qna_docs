                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3339






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






New Virology Institutes




S. Muniswamy














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has identified the places to set up/start new virology institutes and testing laboratories in the country, State/UT-wise and location wise including Karnataka;(b) the criteria adopted for selecting these areas; and(c) the funds allocated and utilized so far for the purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)	Yes. However identification has been done only for setting up Viral Research and Diagnostic Laboratories (VRDLs).  (b)	Criteria adopted for selecting Regional Level VRDLs is based upon available infrastructure and epidemiological needs of the region. However, the proposals for setting up of State and Medical College Level Laboratories are given by the State Governments/Central Government Institutes.(c)   Allocation of funds during the current financial year is Rs. 80 crore, out of which Rs. 22.50 crore, have so far been utilized for the purpose.
