                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

140






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Remaining dues from Vodafone




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Vodafone has exercised option of converting its accrued interest dues into equity to the extent of 35.8 percent; if so, the details thereof; (b) 	the remaining dues from Vodafone in respect of Spectrum and AGR dues as on 15.01.2022; (c) 	the dues of other Telecom operators in respect of Spectrum and Adjusted Gross Revenue (AGR) dues as on 15.01.2022; and (d) 	whether any other Telecom company is being offered conversion of its dues into equity and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)  	In response to the government’s offer towards conversion of interest amount, arising out of protection of net present value on account of deferred spectrum and AGR dues, pertaining to the moratorium period, Vodafone vide its letter dated 10.01.2022 has exercised its option for converting its interest liabilities into equity.(b) & (c ) 	The remaining dues of Vodafone and other Telecom operators in respect of Spectrum and AGR dues as on 15.01.2022 are  enclosed as Annexure-I.(d) 	M/s Vodafone Idea Ltd. (VIL), M/s Bharti Airtel Ltd. and M/s Reliance Jio Infocomm Ltd, were given the option of moratorium for 4 years for Spectrum auction dues and converting interest amount into equity. M/s Vodafone Idea Ltd. (VIL), M/s Bharti Hexacom Ltd, M/s Bharti Airtel Ltd., M/s Reliance Jio Infocomm Ltd, M/s Quadrant Televentures Ltd., M/s Tata Teleservices Ltd. (TTSL) and M/s Tata Teleservices (Maharashtra) Ltd. (TTML) were given the option of moratorium for 4 years for AGR dues and converting interest amount into equity. Three (3) TSPs namely M/s Vodafone-Idea Limited, M/s Tata Tele Services Limited and M/s Tata Tele Maharashtra Limited have exercised the option for conversion of the interest liabilities into equity.
