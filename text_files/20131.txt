                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1315






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






PMAY-G in Uttar Pradesh




Keshari Devi Patel














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of beneficiaries allotted houses under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) in Prayagraj district of Uttar Pradesh;(b) 	the funds allocated for the scheme during the financial years 2019-20 and 2020-2021;(c) 	the number of beneficiaries allotted houses under the scheme during the said financial years; and(d) 	the number of remaining eligible beneficiaries who have not been provided funds for houses under the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	To achieve the objective of “Housing for All” by the year 2022 in rural areas, the Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) is being implemented w.e.f. from 1st April, 2016 in the country. A total target of 82,068 houses have been allocated to the Prayagraj District by the State. Out of the allocated target, 81,702 houses have been sanctioned and 52,129 houses have been completed in Prayagraj District of Uttar Pradesh since 2016-17 till 04.02.2021.
