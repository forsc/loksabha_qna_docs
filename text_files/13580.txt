                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2422






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Protocol for MPs




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any guidelines have been issued by the Government to the private airlines to provide new facilities to the Members of Parliament (MPs) and dignitaries, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether any facility is provided by the private airlines to the MPs/dignitaries for check-in help; and(c) if so, the details of the airlines providing such facility and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c): Ministry of Civil Aviation regularly issues guidelines relating to protocol/courtesy to be extended to Members of Parliament at airports from time to time. The guidelines have been consolidated and reiterated recently in January, 2020. The protocol/courtesy is to be extended by Air India, Airports Authority of India/Other airport operators and Bureau of Civil Aviation Security (BCAS). As per these guidelines, facilitation for check-in is extended by airport operator after proper coordination with airlines. Facilitation for security check is also extended by airport operator after coordination with airlines and Central Industrial Security Force (CISF).
