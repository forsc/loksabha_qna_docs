                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3881






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Social Welfare Board




 Vaithilingam Ve.














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the present status of Central/State Social Welfare Board in the country;   (b) 	the present status of Puducherry Social Welfare Board along with the future plan in the interest of existing staff? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)  (a) & (b) The Ministry of Women & Child Development has in-principle agreed to the recommendations of the Department of Expenditure, Ministry of Finance for dissolution of Central Social Welfare Board (CSWB). Further action for dissolution of CSWB taking into consideration its assets, financial liabilities, manpower etc. would be taken accordingly.  With regard to State Social Welfare Boards (SSWBs), including Puducherry Social Welfare Board, the respective State Government /UT Administrations have been asked to take their respective views and decisions in relation to continuation or otherwise of their respective Boards, personnel, field operations, assets and liabilities etc. On dissolution of CSWB, the Central Government will not bear any financial, administrative, service-related or any other kind of obligations in respect of SSWBs.  ******
