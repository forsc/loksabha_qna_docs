                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3985






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Promotion/Modernisation of Khadi Industry




Darshana Vikram Jardosh













                                                    Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the steps taken by the Ministry in association with the various State Governments to promote Khadi textile and modernise the Khadi industry as on date, State-wise; (b) 	the steps taken by the Ministry to promote the export and exporters of Khadi Textiles, State-wise; (c)	whether the sales, production and job opportunities have been declined in Khadi sector during the last three years; (d) 	if so, the details thereof for the last three years and the reasons therefor; and (e) 	the number of new institutes set up under Khadi and Village Industries Commission during the last three years in various States including in the State of Gujarat?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	Ministry of Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME), through Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) is implementing the following components/sub-components of ‘Khadi and Gramodyog Vikas Yojana’ (KGVY),to promote Khadi textile and modernize Khadi Industry in the country:(1)	Rozgar Yukt Gaon (RYG) – To give boost and generate employment opportunities in the villages through hand spun charkha, a new scheme ‘Rozgar Yukt Gaon’ has been launched with an objective to introduce enterprise-led model replacing subsidy-led model in 50 villages with a total cost of Rs. 41.00 crore for the years 2018-19 and 2019-20. As part of the scheme, the artisans will be provided with charkhas, looms, warping units, professional skills and administrative framework support to create 12500 direct and 5765 indirect totalling 18265 employment opportunities. Zone-wise distribution of Khadi Gaons is placed at Annexure-I.(2)	Khadi Reforms Development Programme (KRDP) was launched in 2009-2010 with an aim to revitalize the Khadi sector with enhanced sustainability of Khadi, increased incomes and employment for spinners and weavers, increased artisans’ welfare and to achieve synergy with village industries.  An amount of Rs.585.46 crore has been released by the Ministry to KVIC during the period from February 2010 to 31.3.2019.  State/UT-wise number of Khadi Institutions sanctioned Direct Reform Assistance (DRA) by KVIC under KRDP as on 30.11.2019 is placed at Annexure-II.In addition to the above, Ministry is also implementing the ‘Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries’ (SFURTI) for making Traditional Industries more productive, market driven and competitive by organizing the Traditional Industries and artisans into clusters. The Scheme envisages providing need-based assistance for setting up of Common Facility Centres (CFCs), product development, quality improvement, improved marketing, training and capacity building etc. Under this scheme, traditional industries clusters including Khadi related clusters are set up.  State/UT-wise number of SFURTI Khadi clusters along with project costs as on 30.11.2019 is placed at Annexure-III.(b):	The export of Khadi & Village Industries (KVI) products is done by the KVI Institutions and units directly or through merchant exporters. Initiatives taken by the Government to promote the export and exporters of Khadi Textiles and the details of the countries to which Khadi products are exported along with the quantum of Khadi products exported during each of the last three years is placed at Annexure-IV.  State-wise data is not maintained by the KVIC.(c)&(d):	No,  Sir.
