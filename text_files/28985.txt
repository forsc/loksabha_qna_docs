                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

295






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Funds Allocated to Telangana




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of amount of funds allocated, sanctioned, released and spent for rural local bodies in the State of Telangana under 14th and 15th Finance Commission; and (b)	the details of expenditure made and assets created with above funds in rural local bodies, with a particular reference to rural bodies in Vikarabad, Ranga Reddy districts of the State of Telangana?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI KAPIL MORESHWAR PATIL)(a)	The details of amounts of funds allocated, sanctioned & released by the Ministry of Finance for the rural local bodies in the State of Telangana and expenditure under 14th& 15th Finance Commission are given below:-Rs. in CroreGrant	                        Allocation             Sanctioned / Released                Expenditure14th Finance Commission (2015-16 to 2019-20)	          5375.29	                5060.09	                     5060.0915th Finance Commission (2020-21 & 2021-22)	          3212.00	                2529.50	                     2159.37(b)	The details of expenditure and assets created out of 14th and 15th Finance Commission funds to rural local bodies, including Vikarabad, Ranga Reddy districts, as provided by the State Government, are given at Annexure.***
