                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3245






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Integrated Scheme for Development of Silk Industry




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds sought under the detailed action plan by the States for the implementation of Integrated Scheme for Development of Silk Industry (ISDSI), State-wise including Gujarat for the last three years; and (b) the amount sanctioned by the Government with reference to the said proposals and details of the amount yet to be given to the States?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) & (b):	The State-wise details of proposals received including from Gujarat and funds released by Central Silk Board, Ministry of Textiles, Government of India under the Scheme “Silk Samagra”- an Integrated Scheme for Development of Silk Industry during last three years (2018-19 to 2020-21) are given in Annexure. There is no fixed allocation for each state under the scheme. The release of funds is made considering the Quantity of raw silk produced by the state, Utilization of funds released during previous years, requirement & viability of the components proposed by the state and budgetary allocation.
