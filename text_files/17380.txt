                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

910






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Flights from Jaipur International Airport




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of international flights to and from Jaipur International Airport operated in the last two years to various countries; and(b) whether the Government has any plans to increase the international flights to and from this airport and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): The total number of international flights to and from Jaipur Airport in the last two years to various countries are as follows:   Year	                    International Flights (in nos.)2019-20                                36122018-19                                4166(b): Airlines are free to select whatever markets and network they wish to service and operate within the ambit of bilateral agreements. It is, therefore, upon the airlines to provide air services to specific places depending upon the traffic demand and commercial viability. ;Indian carriers are free to operate services from any point in India including Jaipur to international destinations available under respective bilateral arrangements. Moreover, Jaipur is already included in the list of 18 destinations where the designated Indian carriers as well as designated carriers of SAARC countries (except Afghanistan and Pakistan) and ASEAN countries (Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Brunei, Vietnam, Indonesia, Myanmar, Cambodia, Philippines and Laos) can operate unlimited frequencies.
