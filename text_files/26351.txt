                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2091






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






New Research Centres in Universities




Hemant Patil














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number-wise and State-wise details of the Universities in which new Research Centreshave been established to encourage research work and to find affordable and local solutions tothe problems during the last three years; and(b) the details of the subjects on which research work has been encouraged in the ResearchCentres established in Maharashtra?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b): AICTE Doctoral Fellowship (ADF) scheme was started from 2018-19. The state wisedetails of Research Centres/ Universities during the last three years are attached at Annexure.The research work has been encouraged, under ADF scheme, in the subjects of Engineering andPharmacy in the state of Maharashtra.*****
