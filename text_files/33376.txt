                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

915






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 0.3




Ramesh Chandra Majhi













                                                    Margani Bharat
                                                








                                                    Kripanath Mallah
                                                








                                                    Jayant Sinha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the salient feature of the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana 0.3;(b) 	the number of districts in the country where the phase 0.3 of the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana launched in January, 2021 has been started;(c) 	the funds allocated, distributed and utilized by the Government for the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 0.3, State-wise;(d)	the number of persons benefited/ trained under phase 0.1 to 0.3 of the said scheme; State-wise including Assam, Andhra Pradesh, and Jharkhand details thereof; and(e)	the achievements of PMKVY 0.3 so far, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Under Skill India Mission, Ministry has launched the new and modified version of PMKVY i.e. PMKVY 3.0 (2020-21) on 15.01.2021 on the basis of the evaluation studies and learning from the implementation of PMKVY 1.0 and 2.0. Under the new version of PMKVY i.e. PMKVY 3.0, the informed choices / aspirations of the candidates have been given primacy and it promotes candidates to opt for local skills with local jobs. Demand driven with bottom-up approach has been adopted under PMKVY3.0. District Skill Committees (DSCs) are the focal point for implementation of PMKVY 3.0 at the district level. The DSCs are entrusted with district level skill gap and demand assessment, mobilization and counselling of candidates, formation of training batches, post training support and provide handholding support to the candidates after placements, besides addressing the grievances, if any. Some of the salient features of PMKVY 3.0 are given below:i.	The scheme is aligned with Common Cost Norms and National Skill Qualification Framework (NSQF).ii.	Payment to Training Providers has been broken into tranches namely 30% on commencement of training batches, 40% on successful certification and 30% on placement verification. Other incentives like boarding & lodging, post-placement support, conveyance and other support are as per Common Cost Norms.iii.	Private sector participation to be encouraged with greater focus on training in industries.iv.	Cross utilisation of available infrastructure with ITIs/School/Universities.v.	Phase-wise introduction of vocational courses in schools in coordination with Ministry of Education.(b)       Under PMKVY 3.0, as on 31.12.2021, training has been imparted in 711 districts across the country. (c)        PMKVY 3.0 has two components, namely, Central component being implemented through National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) and State component being implemented through State Skill Development Missions (SSDMs) of the State/UT. Under Central component of PMKVY, there is no provision of State-wise funds allocation. As on 31.12.2021, an amount of Rs. 644.27 crore has been disbursed to implementing agency, i.e., NSDC towards the implementation of the scheme. Under State component of PMKVY, funds and corresponding physical targets have been allocated to the States for the implementation of scheme through State Skill Development Missions (SSDMs). As on 31.12.2021, an amount of Rs. 40.60 crore has been disbursed to 18 States/UTs for the implementation of the scheme. The State-wise funds allocated and disbursed to the States/UTs under State component of PMKVY 3.0, is given at Annexure I. (d)       The State-wise and Phase-wise number of persons benefited under PMKVY, since inception i.e. 2015 till 31.12.2021, including State of Assam, Andhra Pradesh and Jharkhand is given at Annexure II. (e)       Despite disruption caused due to COVID-19 pandemic, 4.45 lakh candidates have been trained / oriented till 31.12.2021, under PMKVY 3.0 across the country.
