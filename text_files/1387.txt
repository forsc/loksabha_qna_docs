Nirbhaya Fund




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has set up Nirbhaya Fund for enhancing safety and security of women in the country and if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether the Government maintains any statistical data regarding funds allocated and utilized under the scheme; (c)	if so, the details of such allocation and utilization of funds during the last five years, year and State-wise; (d)	whether the Government has any plan to enhance the financial assistance provided under the said scheme; and(e)	if so, the details thereof along with the further steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	to (c): Yes, Sir. Government has set up a dedicated fund ‘Nirbhaya Fund’ which can be utilized for projects specifically designed to improve the safety and security of women. It is a non-lapsable corpus fund, being administered by Department of Economic Affairs, Ministry of Finance. Ministry of Women and Child Development (MWCD) is the nodal Ministry to appraise/ recommend proposals and schemes under Nirbhaya Fund. Further, various Ministries/ Departments are responsible for obtaining competent financial and administrative approvals as well as for implementation of their respective projects/ schemes out of their departmental Nirbhaya Fund budgets. The respective Ministries/ Departments are also responsible to maintain their respective data regarding allocation and utilization of Nirbhaya Fund. The details of fund allocation and utilization under Nirbhaya Fund received from concerned implementing agencies is at Annexure.(d):  	At present, the Ministry of Women and Child Development does not have any plan to enhance financial assistance provided under the scheme.(e):  	Does not arise in view of reply to part (d) above.******* AnnexureProject wise details of funds allocated (funds released) to States / UTs for projects being taken under Nirbhaya SchemeMinistry of Home Affairs1.
