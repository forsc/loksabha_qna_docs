                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3158






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






AMRIT Pharmacy




Vinod Kumar Sonkar














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the names of the places where AMRIT pharmacy has been set up as on date, State/UT-wise;(b) whether the Government has set up ‘AMRIT pharmacy’ at Kunda under Kaushambi Lok Sabha Constituency of the State of Uttar Pradesh;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the time by which the Government proposes to set up ‘AMRIT pharmacy’ at Kunda?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (d): 	AMRIT Pharmacies are being opened and operated by M/s HLL Lifecare Ltd., a Central Public Sector Undertaking (CPSU) of Ministry of Health & Family Welfare. The State/UT-wise list of operational AMRIT pharmacies is at Annexure.AMRIT Pharmacy outlets are generally set up in AIIMS, Government Medical College Hospitals and large District Hospitals which have about 300 beds. Proposal for setting up of AMRIT Pharmacy at Kunda has been examined by M/s HLL Lifecare Ltd. and found that it is not in a position to operate an AMRIT Pharmacy outlet at Kunda, in light of lack of logistics network to cover the area and fixed and working capital requirement vis-a-vis the expected footfall.
