                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3762






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Benefits under Credit Guarantee Fund Trust




Krishna Pal Singh Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) which have been given benefit of funds under Credit Guarantee Fund Trust in Madhya Pradesh by the Union Government;(b)	whether the Government has any proposed for setting up of Khadi Gram Udyog and cottage industries in Madhya Pradesh and if so, the details thereof;(c)	the names of schemes being run in the country for market promotion and the extent to which Madhya Pradesh has been benefitted from it; and(d)	the number of traditional industries benefitted under a Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional  Industries (SFURTI) and the details with special reference to Madhya Pradesh? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	There are 2,30,348 numbers of Micro and Small Enterprises benefited under the Credit Guarantee Scheme run by Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE) as on 28.02.2021 in the state of Madhya Pradesh.(b):	 Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) under Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprise (MSME) has taken a number of steps to promote khadi and village industries throughout India including Madhya Pradesh, which are as below: (i)	KVIC provides Modified Market Development Assistance (MMDA) to the registered Khadi institutions and 40% of total MMDA to be shared with the khadi artisans engaged in production activity. (ii)	Making available concessional credit (@ 4% interest) to khadi institutions under the Interest Subsidy Eligibility Certificate (ISEC) Scheme; (iii)	Providing better work environment to khadi spinners and weavers under the  Workshed Scheme for Khadi Artisans; (iv)	Financial assistance is provided under the scheme ‘Strengthening of Infrastructure of Existing Week Khadi Institutions and Assistance for Marketing Infrastructure’. (v)	Developing clusters of khadi and village industries under Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI) with improved equipment, business development services, training, capacity building and exposure visits, design and marketing support and common facility centres. (vi)	 Apart from the above, KVIC is also implementing a comprehensive reform package for the khadi sector namely Khadi Reform and Development Programme, which includes capacity building of khadi institutions and incentivizing production. (vii)	Ministry of MSME implements a major credit-linked subsidy programme named Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) through Banks since 2008-09 (with KVIC as nodal agency at the national level) for generating employment in the country including in Madhya Pradesh by setting up of micro-enterprises in the non-farm sector. (c):	Ministry of MSME runs Marketing Assistance Schemes which are applicable throughout the country including the state of Madhya Pradesh to enhance the market exposure of MSMEs to both domestic and international markets through organising / participating in Trade Fairs / Exhibitions / MSME Expo. / Vendor development programmes (VDPs) etc. Under the Procurement and Marketing Support Scheme 44 MSE units of Madhya Pradesh were benefitted in the last two years.(d):	 	Under the Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI) 30 Clusters are approved and out of which 5 are functional in the state of Madhya Pradesh.
