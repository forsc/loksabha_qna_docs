                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Funds for Digital Education




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the digital education programmes and schemes being run to impart employmentoriented and quality education to students and to provide training to the teachers along with theirteaching work in the remote areas and rural areas of the country, State-wise including Maharashtra;(b) the funds allocated and spent on digital education specially in the rural areas during the last threeyears and the details thereof, State-wise; and(c) if so, the digital schemes related to health and the main objectives thereof along with the detailsthereof, State-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) The key initiatives for the Ministry of Human Resource Development on Digital Educationprogrammes include:? SWAYAM: the online education platform covering all disciplines in higher and school education.? SWAYAM Prabha: 32 DTH TV high quality educational channels for higher and school education.? The Free and Open Source Software for Education (FOSSEE): to promote the use of open sourcesoftware in higher education.? ARPIT: An online Annual Refresher Programme in Teaching (ARPIT) for the in-service teachers inthe higher education.? Further to facilitate digital education in rural areas, full-fledged degree/diploma level onlineeducation have started from January, 2020 by IGNOU(Indira Gandhi National Open University) andAmity University.? DIKSHA: The DIKSHA (Digital Infrastructure for Knowledge Sharing) platform offers teachers,students and parents, engaging learning material relevant to the prescribed school curriculum.? Talking books for Visually Impaired Learners was developed in DAISY (Digital AccessibleInformation System) format for Senior Secondary and Secondary Course. DAISY is designed to be acomplete audio substitute for print material and is specifically designed for use by people with "printdisabilities," including blindness, impaired vision, and dyslexia.? Mukta Vidya Vani: Web Audio Streaming – Live Personal Contact Programmes of 3 hoursbroadcast every day from NIOS Audio Studio, then repeat 2 times and archived.? Open Educational Resources (OER): OERs available specifically for Vocational programmes to beoffered at Secondary (10th) and Sr. Secondary (+2) levels, including standalone programmes, inpartnership with state level institutions and organizations.? e-Pathshala : A web portal (http://epathshala.nic.in/, http://epathshala.gov.in/) and National Repositoryof Open Educational Resources (NROER): NROER portal (http://nroer.gov.in/welcome) were developedfor teachers and teacher-educators.? National Initiative for School Heads and Teachers Holistic Advancement (NISHTHA): A web portal andmobile app for management of NISHTHA training has been developed. The training for Heads & Teachershas been completed for 8 states and is in progress for 18 States.(b) All the above schemes of Higher Education & School Education are applicable pan India includingMaharashtra.
