                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2825






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Policy and Facilitation Bureau for AYUSH Sector




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether the Strategic Policy and Facilitation Bureau for promotion of AYUSH has been set up by the Government;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the amount of investment facilitated for the Strategic Policy and Facilitation Bureau in the AYUSH sector?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):	 Yes, (b):	M/o AYUSH has engaged Invest India for the establishment of Strategic Policy and Facilitation Bureau in respect of M/o  AYUSH initially from 01.11.2020  to 31.03.2021.  The Strategic Policy and Facilitation Bureau (SPFB) activities and deliverable are as under:- 	i)	Policy, Strategy and Investment Facilitation Unitii)	Project Monitoring for Inter-Ministerial Groupsiii)	Skill Development Initiativesiv)	States AYUSH Policy Benchmarking Analysisv)	Strategic Intelligence Research Unitvi)	Innovation Program (Startup India & AGNIi)  Ministry of AYUSH and Invest India has organized following 03 roundtable conference as a part of Strategic Policy and Facilitation Bureau (SPFB) activity-Date 	Theme21.01.2021	AYUSH Integrated Classification (HS Codes)22.02.2021	AYUSH Education & Training26.02.2021	Global Promotion of AYUSH products(c):	The amount of the investment facilitated for the FY 2020-21 is Rs.46.96  lakh only.
