                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2009






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Demand of Fertilizers




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the estimated demand of fertilizers in the country and the total domestic production of the same; (b)	whether the estimated demand of fertilizers in the country is more than the total domestic production and if so, the reasons therefor; (c)	whether the Government has any plan to increase the Government’s investment in the fertilizer sector; and (d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(SHRI D.V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a): The assessed requirement (demand) and the actual production of all fertilizers (Urea, DAP, MOP, Complex and SSP) during 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 is as follows:-                                                                                    <Figures in LMT>Year	                           Demand (Requirement)                     Actual Production2017-18	                                   598.95	                       413.612018-19	                                   615.92	                       414.852019-20	                                   643.13	                       (upto Jan’2020) 359.29(b):   The production capacity in case of Urea is limited as compared to the demand. This gap is fulfilled through the imports. In case of MOP, there is complete dependence on imports.
