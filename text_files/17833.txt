                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1547






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Convergence of Central & State Government Schemes




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Central and State Governments‘ schemes at the implementation level can be converged with the help of technology so that continuous monitoring can be done with the help of dash boards;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Ministry has considered working in collaboration with district Governments through capacity building of personnel at the local level and at the national level;(d) 	if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b): The Ministry of Rural Development (MoRD) launched District Development Coordination and Monitoring Committees (DISHA) Dashboard on 11th October 2017 as a single source of information in respect of schemes run by various Ministries which aims to create a data driven governance solution for planning, monitoring and evaluating multiple parameters of various schemes under DISHA monitoring system. As on date, 36 schemes of different Ministries have been integrated through Application Programming Interface (APIs) for real time data display, on the DISHA Dashboard. Status of Schemes integrated on DISHA Dashboard as on date is at Annexure-I The Ministry, with the support of the National Informatics Centre (NIC) has designed, developed and implemented various IT driven solutions for its flagship programmes. A snapshot of the IT solutions that support the implementation and monitoring of rural development programmes, promote transparency and accountability is detailed at Annexure-II.(c) & (d): With a view to attaining the the collaboration of the States/ UTs and Districts with respect to the various schemes of MoRD, the Ministry has been organizing periodic training/ workshops/ seminars with Officers/ Officials from States/ UTs including District/ Block/ GP level Officials for capacity building of personnel at the local and national level.(e): Does not arise.*******ANNEXURE-I REFERRED IN STATEMENT IN REPLY TO PART (a) & (b) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 1547 DUE FOR ANSWER ON 20.09.2020 REGARDING ‘CONVERGENCE OF CENTRAL AND STATE GOVERNMENT SCHEMES’  Status of Scheme integrated on Disha Dashboard as on date
