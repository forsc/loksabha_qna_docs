                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3181






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Expansion of Airport in Jamshedpur




Geeta Kora














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes expansion of airport at Jameshedpur in Jharkhand;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has fixed any time limit in this regard; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) to (d): Jamshedpur airport is maintained by Tata Steel Ltd. Ministry of Civil Aviation has launched Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik) on 21-10-2016 to stimulate regional air connectivity and making air travel affordable to the masses. The primary objective of RCS is to facilitate / stimulate regional air connectivity by connecting unserved/underserved airports/heliports/water aerodromes in the country. Under the scheme, the routes connecting Jamshedpur airport have been awarded and the airport has been included in the list of airports being developed / revived under "Revival of unserved and underserved airports" scheme for operation of RCS flights. The timeline for completion of the project depends upon factors such as land acquisition, availability of mandatory clearances, financial closure etc.
