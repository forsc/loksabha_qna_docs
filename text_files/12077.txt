                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1112






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Upgradation of AYUSH Facilities




Ganesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has upgraded existing health care facilities in AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres (HWCs) in the country, if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise particularly in District Satna; (b) whether the Government has transformed the existing Sub-health Centres/AYUSH dispensaries to Health & Wellness Centres to ensure universal access to an expanded range of Primary Healthcare services, if so, the details thereof along with its requirements in District Satna; (c) whether the Government has approved/allocated any budget for upgrading such AYUSH Health & Wellness Centres, if so, the details thereof, States/ UT-wise including District Satna; (d) whether any irregularity and complaint has been received for not functioning of AYUSH Health & Wellness Centres as per norms of Government; and (e) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including District Satna and the action taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):    Upgradation of 1037 AYUSH dispensaries to HWCs have been approved (in year 2019-20) under the existing provision of Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) and Rs. 90.20 Crore have been released to the States/UTs till date. State-wise status of approved Health and Wellness Centres is furnished at Annexure.These AYUSH Health & Wellness Centres envisage a holistic wellness model based on AYUSH principles and practices.Further, 25 AYUSH dispensaries have been approved for upgradation as HWCs as per the proposal received from Madhya Pradesh State including two AYUSH dispensaries in the district of Satna and total Rs. 1.99 Crore have been released to the State for this purpose.(d) and (e):  There is no complaint received so far regarding functioning of AYUSH Health & Wellness Centres.
