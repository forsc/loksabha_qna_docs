                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2674






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Indian Sign Language




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government intends to recognise Indian Sign Language as an official language;(b) whether the Government has held consultations with the representatives of the persons with disabilities community on this subject;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (d):	No,  Sir.  ********
