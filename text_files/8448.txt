                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2054






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Ayushman Bharat Yojana in Odisha




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether it is proposed to implement Ayushman Bharat Yojana in Odisha as it has been implemented by all the State Governments but the Government of Odisha is not implementing it;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the number of people likely to be benefitted under the said scheme in Odisha?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)& (b):	The State Government of Odisha has not signed Memorandum of Understanding for implementation of Ayushman Bharat – Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY).So far the scheme has not been implemented in the States of Odisha, Telangana and Union Territory of Delhi, whereas West Bengal has withdrawn from the scheme in January 2019. The Government has been requesting these States/UT including Odisha to join the scheme.(c):	The total number of eligible beneficiaries families under AB-PMJAY in Odisha is 61.93 Lakh as per Socio- Economic Caste Census data.
