                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4660






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Scheme for Integrated Development of Tourist Circuits in Uttar Pradesh




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has formulated any scheme for the integrated development of tourists circuits in Uttar Pradesh; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor; (d) 	whether any financial allocation has been made for the developments of tourist circuit in Uttar Pradesh; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (e):    The Ministry of Tourism, under  Swadesh Darshan Scheme is developing thematic tourist circuits in the country. The details of projects sanctioned by the Ministry for Uttar Pradesh under the scheme is given at the Annexure.*******
