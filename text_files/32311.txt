                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3743






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Insurance Companies under PMFBY




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government maintains data of insurance companies under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY), if so, the total number of insurance companies providing insurance service to the farmers, State-wise/UT-wise; and (b) the total premium paid by the Centre, State and farmer including the percentage of each party, in each year since the commencement of PMFBY till date, State/ UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) :   Government of India has empanelled 19 insurance companies including all the 5 Public Sector General Insurance Companies  and 14 Private Sector General Insurance Companies for implementation of Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)/Restructured Weather Based Crop Insurance Scheme (RWBCIS) in the country.  However, the implementing insurance companies in State/Union Territory (UT)/cluster of districts are selected by the concerned State/UT Government through competitive bidding process i.e. lowest premium quoting company is selected as L-1.  State/UT-wise details of insurance companies selected during Kharif 2020, Rabi 2020-21 and Khraif 2021 season  are at Annexure-I.(b) :  State/ UT-wise  details of total premium paid by the Centre, State and farmer including the percentage of each, in each year since commencement of PMFBY in 2016-17 till Kharif 2021 season are given in Annexure-II.
