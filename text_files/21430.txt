UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2688






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Release of Fishermen




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Indian fishermen who have been kept illegally by neighbouring countries and Iran were released recently; and(b) 	if so, the number of fishermen released by these countries and their details, State-wise?    
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS [SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) & (b)	From time to time, Indian fishermen are apprehended by neighbouring countries on charges of fishing in their territorial waters.As soon as cases of apprehension of Indian fishermen by neighboring countries are reported, immediate steps are taken by the Indian Missions there towards seeking consular access. All possible assistance, including legal assistance, is extended to the fishermen for their early release and repatriation. Government attaches the highest priority to the welfare, safety and security of Indian fishermen.
