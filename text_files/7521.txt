                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1331






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Inclusion of  More Countries in ‘Tourist Visa on Arrival’ Facility




Ritesh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there has been any increase in the foreign tourist arrivals owing to the e-tourist visas; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether there are any plans to include more countries in ‘Tourist Visa on Arrival’ facility; (d) 	if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	the projects or schemes currently being implemented by the Government for developing avenues and increasing tourism in Uttar Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) and (b):		Yes, Sir. As per the information received from Bureau of Immigration, there has been significant increase in the foreign tourist arrivals (FTAs) through e-tourist visas, details of which are given below: <pre>Year	2016	2017	2018FTA (in Millions)	1.18	1.74	2.09        </pre>    (c) & (d):	There is no proposal to extend visa on arrival facility to more countries.(e):	Development of tourism is primarily the responsibility of the concerned State Government/Union Territory Administration. However, Ministry of Tourism, Government of India under its schemes of Swadesh Darshan, PRASHAD and Assistance to Central Agencies, provide central financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territories/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure and facilities in the country. Details of tourism projects sanctioned by Government of India under Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD in Uttar Pradesh are at Annexure.*****
