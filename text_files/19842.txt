                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1098






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






GST on Marble and Granite




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Goods and Services Tax(GST) rates on marble and granite slabs have beenslashed from 28 per cent to 18 per cent in the country w.e.f 15.11.2017 and if so, the detailsthereof;(b) whether 5 per cent GST is being levied on raw and roughly cut pieces of marble andgranite and 12 per cent GST is being levied on their blocks at present and if so, the detailsthereof along with the time since when the same is being done;(c) whether the Government proposes to reduce the prevailing GST rates on marble andgranite slabs from 18 per cent to 12 per cent to provide relief to the marble and graniteindustries generative revenue in crores per month from global recession in view of coronapandemic in the country and to make 5 per cent GST on their raw products mandatory tomaintain the balance of GST input and output; and(d) if so, the time by which it is likely to be done and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a): Granite and marble slabs were initially placed in GST rate slab of 28% as per therecommendation of GST Council in its 14th meeting dated 18th and 19th May 2017. Aspart of the rationalization exercise, GST rate on marble and granite slabs was reducedfrom 28% to 18% with effect from 15.11.2017, on the recommendation of GST councilin its 23rd meeting dated 10th November, 2017.(b): The details of GST rates oncrudeor roughly trimmedmarble and granite and theirblocks is as under:S.No.Description GSTRateWith effect from1. Marble and travertine, crude orroughly trimmed5% 01.01.20192. Marble and travertine blocks 12% 01.07.20173. Granite, crude or roughlytrimmed5% 01.07.20174. Granite blocks 12% 01.07.2017(c) and (d): The request for further reduction of GST rates on Marble and Granite wasplaced before the GST Council in its 37th meeting held on 20th September, 2019.However, the said request was not agreed to by the Council.*****
