                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3545






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






SHG in Rajasthan




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Self Help Groups (SHGs) in Rajasthan, district-wise;(b)	the number of women who have found employment via SHGs in Rajasthan, district-wise;(c)	whether SHGs have been established in all districts of Rajasthan and if not, the reasons therefor;(d)	whether any additional layer of activities/responsibilities related to health, nutrition, etc. have been added to SHG’s in the country and Rajasthan in particular if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the estimated timeframe for the SHGs in the country and Rajasthan in particular, to become self-sustainable and self-reliant?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	There are a total of 150438 Self Help Groups formed in Rajasthan under Rajeevika till November, 2019. The details of numbers of SHGs district wise is provided in the Annexure -1. (b):	As per information provided by Government of Rajasthan, 333489 women have found direct/indirect employment through SHGs in Rajsathan. The district wise list of SHG members who found employement through SHGs is provided in Annexure -2. (c):	Yes Sir. SHGs have been promoted in all districts of Rajasthan under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM). (d):	Yes Sir. The State Rural Livelihood Missions (SRLMs) of all states have initiated integration of Food, Nutrition, Health and WASH (FNHW) within the programme activities with a focus on diet diversity, reducing anemia, menstrual hygiene, diarrhea and health & nutrition care of elderly & differently abled etc. Hand wash practices, usage of toilets and management of waste are also being promoted through SRLMs. In Rajasthan, during the “Poshan month” celebrated in September 2019, the SHGs in the State have participated very actively in the activities like hand washing demonstration, rallies, Jan Andolan, setting up of food stalls etc across all 33 districts of Rajasthan.(e):	The main strategy of DAY-NRLM is to build strong self-reliant institution of Women SHGs. The SHGs are provided financial support of Rs.10,000 to Rs.15,000 per SHG as Revolving Fund(RF) and upto Rs.2.50 lakh per SHG as Community Investment Fund (CIF).Providing continuous capacity building support to the SHGs is the integral part of the programme. Funds received by SHGs are managed by these organizations on their own.
