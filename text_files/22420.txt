                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3548






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Market Share of TSPs




Sanjeev Kumar Singari














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the market share of Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) in the country in terms of landline, mobile and FTH services, TSP-wise;(b) 	whether BSNL has taken any steps to increase its market share and if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	the staff strength of BSNL when compared with other private TSPs?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) The market share of Telecom Service Providers (TSP) in the country in terms of landline and mobile as on 31.01.2021 is given at Annexure-I. For FTTH (Fiber to The Home) services, TSP-wise market share as on 31.12.2020 is given at Annexure- II.(b) The steps taken by BSNL to increase its market share are as under:1.	Competitive new tariff plans to provide affordable services to customers.  2.	One time promotional sales incentive scheme for cluster partners for the sale of Landline/ Broadband connections on copper.3.	Addition of new channel partners and providing attractive incentive schemes to existing channel partners and monitoring their performance4.	Engaging channel partners for rolling out FTTH services in country5.	Bulk close use group (CUG) activation through channel partners.6.	Agreement with OTT (Over the Top) Platforms like Eros Now, Lokdhun, etc.7.	Introduction of Digital know your customers (D-KYC) process for new SIM activation.8.	Installation of Next Generation Packet Aggregation Network technology based nodes for better and faster aggregation of Data traffic.9.	Strengthening the broadband core network.10.	Installation of 100000 numbers of 4G nodes.(c) The staff strength of BSNL as compared with other private TSPs is given at Annexure-III.
