                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4746






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Reservation for Locals in Private Jobs




Sougata Ray














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government noticed Haryana Government’s move to reserve private jobs for locals;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the industrial forums approached the Government on the said issue;(d) 	if so, the details thereof;(e) 	whether the Government is concerned that the move is likely to provide shield to some firms indulging in unethical practices;(f) 	if so, the details thereof; and(g) 	the remedial measures taken/likely to be taken in this regard? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) & (b): 	Government of Haryana has informed that they have notified the Haryana State Employment of Local Candidates Act, 2020 (Haryana Act No. 3 of 2021) on 02.03.2021.  The Act provides for employment of 75% of the local candidates with respect to such posts where gross monthly salary or wages are not more than Rs.50,000/- or as notified by the Government from time to time.  (c) & (d): 	Government of Haryana has further informed that no industrial forum has approached the Government on this issue. (e) to (g): 	Government of Haryana has conveyed that no information is available about any firm indulging in unethical practices. *****
