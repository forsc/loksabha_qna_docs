                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

326






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Employment under DDU-GKY




Sushil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the employment under Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushal Yojana (DDU-GKY) has fallen following the onset of COVID;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the reasons for the fall in employment rate; and(d) 	the efforts being made by the Government to increase the employment under DDU-GKY?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI GIRIRAJ SINGH)(a) to (d): 	A Statement is laid on the table of the House.   Statement referred to in reply to Parts (a) to (d) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No. *326 due for answer on 21.12.2021 (a) to (c)  Due to surge in COVID-19 and subsequent lockdowns, all economic activities were impacted including training and placement under DDU-GKY. The number of candidates trained and placed under DDUGKY during last three years and the current year are given below:Financial Year  	Financial Year  	Financial Year 	          Financial Year  18-19                   19-20                   20-21                     21-22 till Nov''21                                                                        Trained	Placed	        Trained	Placed	         Trained Placed	         Trained	Placed241509	137251	        247177	150214	         38289	  49563	           23186	22067 (d)  Under DDU-GKY various awareness campaigns, job fairs and meetings of Chief Executive Officers of Companies/Employers have been undertaken by States and UTs for improvement in placement of trained candidates.  ******
