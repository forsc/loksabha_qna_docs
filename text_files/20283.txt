                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

140






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Facilities at Kishangarh Railway Station




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Kishangarh, Ajmer railway station started working in a new complex on 15th December, 2017; (b) if so, the month-wise income of the station during one year before 15th December, 2017 and one year thereafter including the month-wise decline therein, if any;(c) whether a provision for two new escalators and lifts was made in the Budget 2018-19 for this complex, if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the above funds were allegedly diverted to another railway station, if so, the facts and also the reasons therefor;(e) whether the Government propose to urgently set up the escalators sanctioned in the past in view of the problems being faced by women, divyangs, old age persons and children in climbing the steep stairs of the gigantic foot over bridge located at this railway station complex; and (f) if so, by when and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY ANDCONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	 to (f):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
