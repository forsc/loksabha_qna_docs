                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2446






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Development of Fishing Business




Kapil Moreshwar Patil














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government is taking any initiative to promote development of fishing business and its protection in the State of Maharashtra including Thane and Palghar districts; (b)   if so, the details thereof; and(c)  the details of development of the fishing business and the amount of funds released for its development so far? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRYAND DAIRYING 	(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) to (c): Yes sir. The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a flagship scheme namely “Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY)- to bring about Blue Revolution through sustainable and responsible development of fisheries sector in India” with highest ever investment of ?20050 crore in fisheries sector for a period of 5 years with effect from the financial year 2020-21 in all the States and UTs including Maharashtra. PMMSY addresses the critical gaps in fish production and productivity, quality, technology, post-harvest infrastructure and management, modernization and strengthening of value chain, traceability, establishing a robust fisheries management framework and fishers’ welfare. Under the PMMSY the fishers/fish farmers and other beneficiaries are provided with financial assistance for taking up fisheries developmental activities including fisheries business. The Department of Fisheries under the PMMSY during 2020-21 has approved the fisheries development proposal of Government of Maharashtra at a total cost of Rs. 153.19 crore with central share of Rs.50.64 crore. The State Government of Maharashtra has reported that Palghar and Thane district are also targeted districts for implementation of PMMSY approved activities and an amount of Rs.431.00 lakh and Rs.322.000 lakh has been allocated to these districts respectively for the Financial Year 2020-21. *****
