                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1233






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Spinning Mills in Uttar Pradesh




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















a)	the details of such spinning mills in Uttar Pradesh particularly in Sandia tehsil under the Mishrikh Parliamentary  Constituency in the state of  Uttar Pradesh  which have been closed down/ shut down;b)	whether the Union Government has taken or proposes to take any steps to restart such closed spinning mills particularly in the backward and scheduled caste dominated areas in the country;c)	if so, the details thereof; and    d) if not, the reasons thereof?	






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a):	 As per State Government record, there are 44 cotton and man-made spinning mills reported closed as on 31.03.2019 in the state of Uttar Pradesh(U.P.). Sandila Spinning Mills which is one of the unit of M/s Uttar Pradesh State Textile Corporation (UPSTC) has been officially closed on 14.03.2001. (b) to (d):	At present, there is no proposal to restart such Spinning Mills.*****
