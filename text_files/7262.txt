                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

982






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Reimbursement of Medical Expenses under AB-PMJAY




M V V Satyanarayana














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE  be pleased to state: (a) the number of persons benefited under the Ayushman Bharat Yojana in the country including the State of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana since its inception, State/UT-wise; (b) the details of Government, Private Hospitals empanelled under this scheme in the State of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana; (c) whether the Government is considering to increase the ceiling limit from Rs. 5.00 lakhs per person to Rs. 10.00 lakhs per person under this scheme, if so, the details thereof; (d) the details of diseases covered under the scheme; (e) whether the Government is considering to fully reimburse the amount spent by the patients, for the critical ailments like kidney transplant, heart transplant, liver transplant, and other major diseases under this scheme; and (f) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)	& (b):      From the inception of Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana                     (ABPMJAY) till 20.11.2019, total number of hospital admissions under the scheme is 61.53 Lakh. State/UT-wise details of hospital admissions are at Annexure.     As on 20.11.2019, the total number of hospital admissions in the State of Andhra Pradesh is 5.03 Lakh. 692 Hospitals, including 467 Private and 225 Public Hospitals, are empanelled under AB-PMJAY.      The State of Telangana has not signed the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for implementation of the scheme.(c ):      No.(d) to (f):    As on date, 1392 treatments (packages) across 24 specialities and 1 unspecified package are available under AB-PMJAY at the empanelled hospitals. Details of packages are available at www.pmjay.gov.in. Organ transplant is not included in the health benefit packages under AB-PMJAY.AB-PMJAY is a cashless scheme and the beneficiary is not required to pay for availing the benefits under the scheme. Therefore, the issue of reimbursement of cost of treatment to the beneficiary does not arise.………………..
