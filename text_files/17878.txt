                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1665






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Chinese Mobile Phones and Elecetronic Items




Rodmal Nagar













                                                    Keshari Devi Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of Electronics and Information Technology be pleased to state: - (a)	the total number and the names of Chinese Mobile Companies which have failed safety norms of the country and are selling their products in the Indian market; (b)	the steps taken/being taken by the Government to check the same; (c)	whether the Government has formulated any scheme to ban such Chinese mobile phones and electronic items which emit maximum radiations thereby causing many diseases and if so, the details thereof; (d)	the number of electronic items imported from China which were tested by the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) along with the details thereof; and (e)	the details of imported Chinese electronic items, samples of which failed the BIS tests along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        Will the Minister of Electronics and Information Technology be pleased to state: - (a)	the total number and the names of Chinese Mobile Companies which have failed safety norms of the country and are selling their products in the Indian market; (b)	the steps taken/being taken by the Government to check the same; (c)	whether the Government has formulated any scheme to ban such Chinese mobile phones and electronic items which emit maximum radiations thereby causing many diseases and if so, the details thereof; (d)	the number of electronic items imported from China which were tested by the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) along with the details thereof; and (e)	the details of imported Chinese electronic items, samples of which failed the BIS tests along with the details thereof?
