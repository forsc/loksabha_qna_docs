                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2792






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Accelerating the pace of Vaccination




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has allocated Rs.35000 crore for COVID-19 vaccination in the Budget 2021-22;(b) whether the Government has reviewed the status and progress of COVID vaccination, if so, the details thereof as on date, State/UT-wise;(c) whether the Government has identified the issues that need attention of the States/UTs to improve and accelerate the coverage, if so, the details thereof;(d) the steps taken by the Government to exponentially increase the pace of vaccination;(e) the names of the States/UTs that have achieved 60 percent or more vaccination coverage of healthcare workers; and(f) the time by which Co-WIN 2.0 version will be released?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):	The Union Budget 2021-22 provides for Rs 35,000 crore for COVID-19 vaccination.(b):	Yes, the Government of India regularly reviews the progress of COVID-19 vaccination across the States/UTs. The State/UT-wise details of vaccination are at    Annexure-1.(c) & (d):	The Government of India is monitoring the daily vaccination coverage in States/UTs based on which advisories are issued to States & UTs for improving the coverage.  The current rate of COVID-19 vaccination is satisfactory and is being further increased through increase in number of COVID-19 Vaccination Centres (CVCs) in both Government and Private health facilities.(e):	As on 8th March 2021, a total of 32 States/UTs have achieved 60% or more coverage of Health Care Workers. The names of the States/UTs are at Annexure-2.(f):	Co-WIN 2.0 has been released on 1st March 2021.
