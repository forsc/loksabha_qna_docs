                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3556






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Population of Slums




Kani K. Navas













                                                    Devendra (Alias) Bhole Singh Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether it has come to the notice of the Government that the total number of slums in the country and their population are increasing and if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether any data regarding the total number of slums in the country has been collected; and  (c)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Tamil Nadu?






ANSWER




                                                                          THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE             MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                     (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)	to (c): As per Census of India-2001 and 2011, the population of slum dwellers in the country has increased from 52,371,589 in 2001 to 65,494,604 in 2011. However, the number of slums has reduced from 51,688 to 33,510 as per 58th and 69th round Survey of National Sample Survey Office (NSSO) on urban slums in 2002 and 2012 respectively.Data regarding the total number of slums in the country is collected by Ministry of Statistics and Program Implementation on regular basis. The State/ Union Territory (UT) wise total population and number of slums including Tamil Nadu is at Annexure.                                 *****
