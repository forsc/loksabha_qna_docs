                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

783






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






National Talent Development Programme




Girish Bhalchandra Bapat














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the aims and objectives of the National Talent Development Programme;(b) the details of sports identified by the Ministry for providing assistance to talented sportspersons particularly in Maharashtra; and(c) whether the National Talent Development Programme is limited only to rural areas or has it also been extended to urban areas particularly in Maharashtra and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a)to (c) The Department of Sports is assisting 2,741 young athletes in 24 sports disciplines (including Para sports) under the Talent Search and Development vertical of Khelo India Scheme. These include 149 male and 199 female athletes from the State of Maharashtra. The programme covers rural and urban areas both.****
