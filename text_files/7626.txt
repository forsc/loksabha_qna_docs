                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1300






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Per Child Cost of Right to Education




Sanjay Jaiswal













                                                    Karti P. Chidambaram
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of Per Child Cost (PCC) under section 12.1.c of Right of Children to Freeand Compulsory Education (RTE);(b) whether all the States have State Advisory Councils and if so, the details thereof;(c) the frequency at which this said cost is revised, State-wise;(d) the action taken if this cost is not revised yearly; and(e) whether the costs notified are checked by any other authority and if so, the detailsthereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a): The State-wise Per Child Cost as notified by concerned States and UnionTerritories under section 12(1)(c) of RTE Act, 2009 is at Annexure.(b): All States and Union Territories except Tamil Nadu, Jammu & Kashmir, Ladakhand Telangana have State Advisory Councils under the RTE Act.(c) to (e): Education is in the concurrent list of the Constitution and majority of theschools under the jurisdiction of respective State and UT Governments. The States andUTs are required to notify per child cost norm to claim reimbursement of expenditureincurred by them towards implementation of the provisions of Section 12(1) (c) of theRTE Act. The notification and revision of per child cost is completely under thedomain of concerned State and UT Governments, which are the AppropriateGovernment under the Act.****
