                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2164






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Submission of Details of Data Collection




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the data collected as notified from the companies for whom payments are due and receivable exceeding forty five days from the date of supply of bill from Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) sector, State wise;(b)	whether the Government proposes to extend the dates for submission of these details in the returns as many companies have left out for these submission the date to ROC in filling; and(c)	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a)to(c):	 The state wise number of companies who received the supplies of goods or services from Micro and small enterprises and whose payments to micro and small enterprise suppliers exceeded forty five days from the date of acceptance or deemed acceptance of goods or services are at Annexure. The MSME Form can be filed even after the expiry of due date without any fee or additional fee.
