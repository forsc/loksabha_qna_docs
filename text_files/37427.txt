                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4589






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Regions deprived of Telecommunication Facilities




R.K. Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government have conducted any study to identify the regions deprived of telecommunication (phone/internet network) facilities in Uttar Pradesh; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; district-wise including Banda, Chitrakoot and Bundelkhand districts; and (c) 	the steps taken or proposed to be taken by the Government for providing telecommunications facilities across the country including Uttar Pradesh?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)	to (c) 	As per data provided by Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) and field units of the Department of Telecommunications as of December 2020, it is estimated that out of 5,97,618 inhabited census villages in the country (as per Census 2011), 5,72,551 villages are having mobile coverage. Further in the state of Uttar Pradesh, out of 97813 inhabited census village (census-2011) 97486 villages are having mobile connectivity. The remaining uncovered villages of the country, including the state of Uttar Pradesh, are being covered by the Government, under USOF scheme, and Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) in a phased manner. Government is implementing various schemes under Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF) to enhance telecom connectivity in the rural areas of the country including the regions deprived of Telecommunication facilities. The scope of BharatNet project has been recently extended up to all inhabited villages beyond Gram Panchayats (GPs) in the country. The mobile connectivity is extended in a phased manner to the uncovered villages through Comprehensive Telecom Development Plan for North Eastern Region (NER) and areas affected by Left Wing Extremism (LWE), Border areas, Aspirational Districts and Islands. District-wise details of mobile coverage in the state of Uttar Pradesh is attached as Annexure-I.
