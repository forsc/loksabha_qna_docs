                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1976






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Subsidy on Kerosene




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is any proposal with the Government to stop providing subsidy on kerosene which is being supplied through PDS shops across the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether there is any proposal with the Government to franchise the PDS shops to the private agencies; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) & (b): 		The prices of petroleum products are linked to the price of respective products in the international market. However, the Government continues to modulate the retail selling price of PDS kerosene and the consumers get the product at subsidized rate.(c) to (d): 		There is no such proposal under consideration in the Department at present.****
