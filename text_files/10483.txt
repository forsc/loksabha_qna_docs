                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3894






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Strategic Electronics Factory by HAL




Rajmohan Unnithan














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the purpose envisaged for the setting up of the Hindustan Aeronautics Ltd.’s (HAL) Strategic Electronics Factory at Kasaragod in Kerala, the foundation stone of which was laid on August 23, 2008;(b) the funds allocated and the expenditure incurred for the purpose; and(c) the details of defence logistics produced in the said Strategic Electronic Factory so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  HAL’s Strategic Electronics Factory (SEF) at Kasaragod, Kerala is established for assembly, testing, repair & manufacturing of various types of airborne computers.(b)  HAL has allocated Rs. 67 crores for setting up the facility of SEF at Kasaragod  in 2008, out of which Rs. 66.31 crores has been incurred so far.(c)  SEF, Kasaragod has so far produced 1210 and repaired 2237 airborne computers like Mission Computers, Radar Computers & Display processors for various types of fighter aircrafts.*****
