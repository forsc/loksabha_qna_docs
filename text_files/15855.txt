                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4403






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






e-Waste Clinics




Jaskaur Meena













                                                    Kripanath Mallah
                                                








                                                    Nihal Chand Chauhan
                                                








                                                    Andimuthu Raja
                                                








                                                    V. Kalanidhi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has set up e-Waste clinics in the country in the form of pilot project;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor;(c)	the details regarding the funds sanctioned and allocated along with the funds utilized for this purpose;(d)	the details of the target fixed under this project;(e)	whether the Government proposes to setup more e-Waste clinics in the country including Rajasthan;(f)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Rajasthan; and(g)	the time by which these clinics are likely to be set up by the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) to (g):  Government has not setup any E-Waste Clinics in the country in the form of pilot project. There is no proposal for setting up of E-Waste Clinicsunder consideration in the Ministry.*****
