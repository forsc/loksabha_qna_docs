                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3559






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Monetization of Assets




Sushil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of all bodies including autonomous and statutory bodies under the administrative control of the Ministry; (b)	the details, locations, total area of all land assets owned by the Railways;(c)	the monetary value of each such land asset;(d)	whether the Railways has undertaken any exercise for monetization of assets; (e)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (f)	the year-wise details of all assets and monetized by anybody under the administrative control of the Ministry during the last ten years? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (f):   A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. *****
