                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

413






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Recruitment Process in PSUs




Ganesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether discipline-wise recruitment process is being adopted in Public SectorUndertakings (PSUs);(b) if so, the details thereof along with the date when it was started and the details ofthe discipline-wise recruitment process since 1993;(c) whether the format of roster register of DoPT has been changed between 1993 to2019 and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether it is causing any adverse effect on OBC candidates; and(e) the details regarding the draft of the roster register and discipline roster issued byDoPT and DPE between 1993 and 2019?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a & b): Recruitments to posts below Board level in Central Public Sector Enterprises(CPSEs) are done by the management of CPSEs as per their human resource (H.R.)policies within broad guidelines issued by the Government. While formulating thesepolicies, statutory provisions regarding reservation are followed by CPSEs. Departmentof Public Enterprises (DPE) collects data in respect of the number of employees, breakupof executives and other categories of employees as well as the number of ScheduleCaste (SC), Schedule Tribe (ST) and Other Backward Class (OBC) employees.Information in respect of discipline-wise employees or their recruitment is not maintainedcentrally by DPE.(c to e): Department of Personnel and Training (DoPT) vide Office Memorandum (OM)No.36012/2/96-Estt(Res) dated July 2, 1997 had circulated general principles for makingand operating the reservation roster post wise. Through OM dated 25.02.2015, DPEstipulates that instructions as issued by Government in respect of reservations for SC/ST/ OBC/ Disability & Ex-servicemen are to be mutatis mutandis extended to all CPSEsunless specified otherwise by DPE. Presidential Directives / instructions are also issuedto CPSEs by their administrative Ministries / Departments to ensure reservation inemployment as applicable to Central Government Ministries / Departments.******
