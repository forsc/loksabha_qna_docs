                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

97






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Bharatmala Pariyojana in Kerala




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has commenced works under Bharatmala Pariyojana of 	NHAI on National Highway Nos. 744, 183 and 183A in Kerala; (b)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard along 	with 	the funds allocated during the last and the current year on the basis of work; 	and (c) 	the details of the status of the new National Highways in Alappuzha-Kodaikanal and 	Kayamkulam-Munnar in Kerala and the steps taken by the Government to augment 	the work in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c)	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 97 ON 27THJUNE, 2019 ASKED BY SHRI KODIKUNNIL SURESH REGARDING ‘BHARATMALA PARIYOJANA IN KERALA’-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------(a) & (b)	No civil work for upgradation has been commenced on NH-744, 183 and 183A.  However, the work for Detailed Project Report/Feasibility Study for widening of NH-183 and NH-183A has been initiated and study of alignment options for development of NH-744 has also been initiated.  (c)	The road stretches connecting Alappuzha (on NH 66) to Vazhoor (on NH 183) via Changanasseryand Kayamkulam (on NH 66) to Thiruvalla (on NH 183) (which is part of the road connecting Kayamkulam to Munnar) are not National Highways. However, these stretches have been identified as the potential stretches for declaration as new National Highways subject to outcome of DPR study as well as tying up of funding requirements. Study of alignment in the stretch connecting Alappuzha (on NH 66) to Vazhoor (on NH 183) has been initiated.*****
