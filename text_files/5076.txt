                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4748






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Employment through Employment Exchanges




Kapil Moreshwar Patil













                                                    Sukhbir Singh Jaunapuria
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of the Employment Exchanges in the country, State/UT-wise;(b)	the number of persons whom call letters have been issued by the Employment Exchanges in the country during the last five years and the current year, State/UT/District-wise; and(c)	whether the registration rate has reduced/increased in Employment Exchanges during the last five years, if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (c): As per the information received from the States/UTs, the number of employment exchanges functioning in the country are 997. The State/UT-wise detail of the number of job-seekers, all of whom may not necessarily be unemployed, who got registered themselves in employment exchanges and job-seekers placed through employment exchanges in the country  to the extent available is given at Annexure.
