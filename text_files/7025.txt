                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

855






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






E-Commerce Portal for MSME Sector




Sunil Dattatray Tatkare













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    A.K.P. Chinraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	whether  the  Government proposes to launch Bharat Craft, an e-commerce portal, on the lines of Alibaba and Amazon;(b) 	if so, the details thereof and the objective behind the move;(c) 	whether this e-commerce portal would provide a platform for MSMEs to market and sell their products and in turn boost the sector and if so, the details thereof;(d) 	the details regarding the potential of this portal in terms of revenue generation in the coming years;(e) 	the number of days likely to be cut down after the usage of the portal in making orders to be delivered; and(f) 	the other steps proposed by the Government to make this portal to compete with big players?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) to (c):   National Small Industries Corporation, a PSU under the Ministry of MSME already has a B2B MSME Global Mart Portal and efforts have been made to further strengthen activities under it. The salient features of the portal include online registration, web store management, multiple payment options, customer support through Call Centre and enhanced security features. (d) to (f):	The revenue generation from the portal will depend on membership of the portal.
