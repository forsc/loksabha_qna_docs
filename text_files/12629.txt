                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1408






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Centrally Funded Food Processing Schemes




Behanan Benny














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether many centrally funded food processing schemes are active in Kerala;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the details of the Ministry''s schemes to further push up food processing industry in the State, considering its variety of fauna and flora?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c): The Central Sector Umbrella Scheme – PRADHAN MANTRI KISAN SAMPADA YOJANA - (PMKSY) with an allocation of Rs. 6000 crore for the period 2016-20 and the scheme components viz: (i) Mega Food  Parks,(ii) Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure, (iii) Infrastructure for Agro-processing Clusters, (iv) Creation of Backward and Forward Linkages (v) Creation / Expansion of Food Processing & Preservation Capacities (CEFPPC), (vi) Food Safety and Quality Assurance Infrastructure, (vii) Human Resources and Institutions and (viii) Operation Greens are active in all States/UTs in the country including the State of Kerala for overall development of food processing sector. The scheme component ‘Operation Greens’ for integrated development of value chain exclusively of Tomato, Onion and Potato (TOP) crops is implemented on a pilot basis in selected States only. The details of projects sanctioned in the State of Kerala along with the grants-in-id approved and released under various component schemes of PMKSY is as under:(As on 31.01.2020)Name	of Scheme	Number	of projects Sanctioned	Amount	of Grants-in-aid Approved(Rs. in Crore)	Amount	of Grants-in-aid Released(Rs. in crore)Mega Food Parks	2	90.57	66.52Integrated	Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure	5	32.35	13.66Infrastructure	for Agro-ProcessingClusters	1	9.97	0.00CEFPPC	9	32.74	4.6Creation	of Backward &Forward Linkages	2	8.02	1.25Total	19	173.65	86.03*****
