                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

251






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Coastal Highways in Gujarat




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that the State of Gujarat has 	1600 Km of coastal highways;(b)	if so, the details of stretches of the coastal highways that have 	been upgraded into four lanes;(c)	whether the Government has any plan to upgrade the entire 	stretch of these coastal highways into four lanes;(d)	if so, the details thereof along with the funds earmarked for the 	purpose; and(e)	the time by which such work is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	Yes Sir.(b)	4-laning of projects comprising about 620 Km length of coastal 	highway with cost of Rs. 7,950 crore have been completed.(c) to (e) At present construction works of projects comprising length of 520 Km with cost of Rs. 8,036 crore are ongoing. The physical progress of these works is varying from 3.5% to 92.19%. These works are targeted for completion by 2022-23. However, at present, no Detailed Project report (DPR) is in progress.  *****
