                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

353






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Demands of States under PDS




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) 	the details of the total demand received so far from each of the State Governments for supply of wheat, rice, sugar, foodgrains, kerosene oil under Public Distribution System (PDS) during the current year along with the extent of allocation made per month to each of the States; (b) 	whether the demand of Maharashtra State has been met fully; and (c) 	the extent of quantity lifted by each of the State Governments per month during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) to (c): 	Allocation of foodgrains to the States/UTs for Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS) under National Food Security Act, 2013  is made on the basis of percentage coverage as determined for that State/UT by Govt. of India, number of beneficiaries identified by that State/UTs (including Maharashtra) and scale prescribed as per the provisions of NFSA.  The total allocation and offtake/lifting of rice and wheat under TPDS during the current financial year State-wise/month-wise (including Maharashtra) is at Annexure I and II respectively. Further, no allocation of sugar is done under Public Distribution System by the Central Government. However, The Central Government is reimbursing a fixed subsidy of Rs. 18.50 per kg @ 1 Kg per month per AAY family to participating States/UTs.Government of India (Ministry of petroleum and Natural gas) makes quarterly allocation of Public Distribution System (PDS) Kerosene to States/UTs. The PDS Kerosene allocation to the States/UTs made for 4th quarter 2019-20, 1st, 2nd and 3rd quarter of current Financial Year (i.e. 2020-21) and actual monthly upliftment is attached at Annexure-III.****
