                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1749






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Mono Rail Projects




Vijay Baghel














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received proposals regarding mono rail projects from various State Governments;(b)	if so, the details thereof State-wise; (c)	whether all the said projects have been sanctioned by the Government;(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor: and(e)	the current status of the said projects?






ANSWER




(a) to (e): “In-principle” approval has been accorded to the proposal received from Government of Tamil Nadu for implementing mono rail project from Poonamallee to Kathipara for 20.68 kms at an estimated cost of Rs. 3,267 crore, in November 2014, without any financial commitment on the part of Government of India. No proposal for implementation of mono rail has been received from any other state government for                                      *****
