                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

775






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






ICUs and Trauma Centres in Rural Areas




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to address the shortage of hospitals and modern healthcare facilities including ICU and Trauma Centre in the country;(b) if so, the steps/proposed to be taken for the purpose, State/UT-wise, particularly in the rural/remote areas in Jaunpur District;(b) whether the Government proposes to provide subsidy and loans at low interest rate to encourage people to set up Trauma Centers particularly in rural areas in Jaunpur District; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)& (b): Health & Hospitals being a State subject, the primary responsibility to address the shortage of hospitals and modern healthcare facilities including ICU and Trauma Centre lies with the respective State Government. The Central Government, however, provides technical and financial support to States/UTs under the National Health Mission (NHM) for improving their health systems through various schemes based on the proposals from States, subject to availability of resources.Under the Ayushman Bharat initiative, the Government is committed to setting up of 1.5 lakhs Health & Wellness Centers (HWCs), across the country through new construction and upgradation of existing Sub-Health Centers (SHCs) and Primary Health Centers (PHCs) in rural and remote parts of the countryby December, 2022. Sufficient resources have been provided to the States/UTs as per their proposals under National Health Mission (NHM) for the creation of the same. More than 57,000 HWCs have become functional, for making available primary healthcare services to the community. So far, more than 35 crores footfall has been recorded at the HWCs. Apart from this, States/UTs are also being supported under NHM for expansion in capacities, viz through provision of extra hospital beds, ICUs/HDUs etc., free diagnostics and free drugs/medicines wherever necessary.The particulars of Health & Wellness Centers being set up in States/UTs, is enclosed at Annexure-I. The details of health facilities in Jaunpur District (Uttar Pradesh), as per the information received from State Government of Uttar Pradesh, is enclosed at Annexure-II.(c) & (d):  There is no such provision presently.
