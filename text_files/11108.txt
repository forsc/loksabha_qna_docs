                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

155






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Development of Tourism in Tripura




Rebati Tripura














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there are many unexplored tourist places in Tripura which have huge potential to be developed as tourist destination; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard; (c) 	whether the Government has taken any steps to develop Chabimurra in Tripura; (d) 	if so, the quantum of funds allocated for this purpose; (e) 	whether the Government has also launched North East Circuit of Swadesh Darshan scheme for development of North East; and (f) 	if so, the details of funds allocated and the achievements made during each of the last three years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	Yes ,Sir. Tripura has huge potential to be developed as a tourist destination. For development of Tourism Infrastructure in the country including Tripura the Ministry of Tourism under its schemes of Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD provides financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory Administrations. The projects under the schemes are identified in consultation with the State Governments/UTAs and are sanctioned subject to submission of Detailed Project Reports, their adherence to scheme guidelines, availability of funds and liquidation of pending utilization certificates against the funds released earlier.Ministry under the North East Circuit has sanctioned funds of Rs.4.87 Crores for development of Chabimurra. The components taken up for development includes Construction of Approach Road to Chabimura Tourist Spot, Construction of one Foot Bridge at Chabimura, Solar Lighting System, River safari, speed boat, Floating Jetty for stone curving, Maharani to Chabimura Speed Boat, Jungle Walk Signage and Solid Waste Management.(e) and (f):		Yes ,Sir. North East Circuit is one of the 15 thematic circuits identified for development under Swadesh Darshan scheme for development of North East Region. The details of the projects sanctioned in North Eastern Region under the Swadesh Darshan scheme  during last three years and current year is at Annexure.******
