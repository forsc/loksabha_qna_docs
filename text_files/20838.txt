                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

200






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






CNG Pipeline in Odisha




Ramesh Chandra Majhi














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of the areas where CNG pipeline is being expanded in Odisha along with theareas where such expansion is proposed;(a) the names and details of the companies entrusted with the expansion of CNG pipeline inOdisha;(c) whether CNG pumps are likely to be operated by the companies themselves or there isany provision of dealership distribution in Odisha; and(d) if so, the details thereof including the norms/details in case of dealership distribution?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF THE LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. *200 ASKED BY SHRI RAMESH CHANDRA MAJHI, MPTO BE ANSWERED ON 8TH MARCH, 2021.(a) and (b) Petroleum & Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB) is the authority to grantauthorization to the entities for the development of City Gas Distribution (CGD) network inGeographical Areas (GAs) as per PNGRB Act, 2006. PNGRB identifies GAs for authorizing thedevelopment of CGD network in synchronization with the development of natural gas pipelineconnectivity, natural gas availability and techno commercial viability. Presently, there are 9 GAsauthorized by PNGRB for development of CGD networks in Odisha. Details of authorized GAsand the entities in the state of Odisha tabulated below.S.No. Name of GA Authorized Entity1. Khordha District GAIL2. Cuttack District GAIL3. Angul & Dhekanal Districts Bharat Gas Resources Limited4. Sundargarh & Jharsuguda Districts GAIL Gas Limited5. Balasore, Bhadrak and Mayurbhanj Districts Adani Gas Limited6. Bargarh, Debagarh and Sambalpur Districts Bharat Gas Resources Limited7. Ganjam, Nayagarh and Puri Districts GAIL Gas Limited8. Jagatsinghpur and Kendrapara Districts Bharat Gas Resources Limited9. Jajpur and Kendujhar Districts Bharat Gas Resources Limited(c) and (d) CGD entities authorized by PNGRB devise modalities to establish and operate CNGstations as per work plan within their respective GAs, based on techno-commercial viability.
