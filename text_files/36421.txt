                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3757






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Medicinal Plants Conservation and Development Areas




Jugal Kishore Sharma













                                                    Geeta Kora
                                                








                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Medicinal plants conservation and development areas established under the supervision of the National Medicinal Plants Board, State/UT-wise; and(b)	the amount sanctioned and spent for establishing Medicinal plants conservation and development area?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a)	: The National Medicinal Plants Board (NMPB), Ministry of Ayush, Government of India has provided support to State Forest Departments for establishing 105 Medicinal Plants Conservation and Development Areas (MPCDAs) in different states of the country. The State / UT-wise detail is at Annexure – I.(b)	: The NMPB, Ministry of Ayush has so far sanctioned Rs.6415.90 Lakhs out of which amount of Rs.4722.98 Lakhs has been released and spent / utilized Rs.3284.70 Lakhs for establishing the Medicinal Plants Conservation and Development Areas.
