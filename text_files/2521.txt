                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2353






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






National/State Testing Agencies




Ganesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the National/State Testing Agencies that conduct the JointEntrance Examination Main and National Entrance Eligibility Test and otherexamination for purpose of Government employment;(b) whether any corruption has been found against the National/State TestingAgencies during the conducting of online examination and if so, the detailsthereof along with action taken thereon;(c) whether the modalities regarding fee structure, syllabi, exam pattern, etc. forexams for OBC and EWS are the same or different;(d) if so, the details of such modalities for the two exams along with the modalitiesfor establishment of computer centres in rural areas; and(e) whether the Government has found any discrepancy to conduct AIIMS/JIPMERexam from 2015 onwards and if so, the details thereof along with action takenthereon?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (e): Ministry of Human Resource Development has established National TestingAgency (NTA) to conduct entrance examinations for admission to the Higher EducationInstitutions as well as grant of fellowship. These include Joint Entrance Examination- Main (JEE-Main), National Eligibility-cum-Entrance Test-Undergraduate (NEET-UG),University Grants Commission- National Eligibility Test (UGC-NET), GraduatePharmacy Aptitude Test (GPAT), Common Management Admission Test (CMAT),Jawaharlal Nehru University Entrance Examination (JNUEE) etc. The Ministry doesnot maintain centralized data of State Testing Agencies. No case of corruption hasbeen received against NTA. There is no difference in the fee structure, syllabi andexam patterns for OBC and EWS candidates in these examinations. In order to servethe requirements of the rural students, NTA has established a network of about 3,400Test Practice Centres (TPCs), which are located in educational institutions across 622Districts in 33 States/UTs. All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) andJawaharlal Institute of Post Graduate Medical Education and Research (JIPMER),which come under the purview of Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, have informedthat no discrepancies have been reported in conducting of their examinations from2015 onwards.*****
