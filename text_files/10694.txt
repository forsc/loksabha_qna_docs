                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3928






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Metrino Project in Chennai




K. Shanmuga Sundaram














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to introduce Metrino project in Chennai;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the manner in which the investment for the project is likely to be made by the Government and/or by any other agency;(d)	the cost of investment for this project along with the likely benefits to the general public; and(e)	the basis of price fixation and the estimated number of passengers to be accommodated after completion of the project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	This Ministry is primarily responsible for development and maintenance of the National Highways based on inter-se priority and availability of funds. The subject matter pertains to the mobility in metropolitan cities, which is a subject matter of Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs. As per the information received from the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, no such proposal is under consideration.(b) to (e) Dose not arise.*****
