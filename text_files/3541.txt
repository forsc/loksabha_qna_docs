                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3221






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Air Purifying Plant




Ajay (Teni) Misra














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether scientists have developed any plant with air purifying capabilities to clean polluted air in mineral, mining/ producing cities;(b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the time by which the use of such plants is likely to be made available to provide clean air to the people?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) to (c) 54 Air purifying units (WAYU) have been installed as pilot project for control of pollution at traffic intersection in Delhi by Central Pollution Control Board and efficiency WAYU is being assessed. Out of 54 devices, 10 nos. are installed at Anand Vihar, 14 nos. at ITO intersection, 06 nos. at Wazirpur Chowk, 11 Nos. at Shadipur and 13 Nos. at Bikaji Cama place. *****
