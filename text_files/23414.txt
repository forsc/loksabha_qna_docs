                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4444






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Reduction of Coverage under NFSA




Abdul Majeed Ariff













                                                    Adoor Prakash
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether NITI Aayog has recommended reducing the rural and urban coverage under the National Food Security Act, 2013, to 60 per cent and 40 per cent, respectively; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (c) 	whether the Government has considered the same and taken any decision; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) to (d): 		There is no formal proposal under consideration of the Government for reducing the rural and urban coverage under the National Food Security Act, 2013 to 60 percent and 40 percent respectively. The Act currently mandates a coverage of upto 75% of the rural population and upto 50% of the urban population as per Census 2011. Section 9 of the Act provides that the total number of persons to be covered in rural and urban areas of the State shall be calculated on the basis of the population estimates as per the Census of which the relevant figures have been published.*****
