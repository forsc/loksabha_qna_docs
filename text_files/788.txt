                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

689






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Ease of Doing Business




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has a proposal to improve India’s ranking in ease of doing business;(b) 	if so, whether the Government proposes to take any steps in this regard; and(c) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c): 	Yes, Sir. Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), in coordination with Central Ministries/Departments and Governments of States/UTs, has taken several reform measures with an aim to improve regulatory environment and facilitate doing business in India thereby increasing India’s rank in World Bank’s Doing Business Report. The details of steps taken in this regard are provided in Annexure. *****
