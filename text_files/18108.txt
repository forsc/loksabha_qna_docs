                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1917






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Archery Sport




Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik













                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that Archery sport is not getting due importance at National and International level;  (b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons for the same;  (c) whether the Government is also not considering Archery sport while conducting recruitment under sports quota in the country;  (d) if so, the reasons therefor along with the corrective measures taken/being taken by the Government in this regard; and  (e) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to promote Archery in the country? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS [SHRI KIREN RIJIJU]  (a) & (b): Government has been giving due importance to Archery Sports. Archery sport has been placed in the ‘High Priority’ category so that it gets maximum financial and other assistance from Government under Scheme of Assistance to National Sports Federations. Archery sport being part of the Olympics programme, Archery players are also included in the Target Olympic Podium Scheme and are being supported for their customized training.  (c) & (d): Archery sports is already included in the list of sports disciplines for recruitment of meritorious sportspersons to Group ‘C’ posts in Central Government offices against sports quota vacancies.  (e) The Ministry is promoting Archery through various Schemes of the Ministry and SAI. Archers are being provided training in SAI Centres across the country under SAI’s sports promotional schemes. Furthers, Archery sport is included in Khelo India Youth Games and Khelo India University Games. Under the Scheme of Assistance to National Sports Federations, financial and other support are provided for preparation and participation of Indian Archers in the international archery events.   ****
