                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

50






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






AYUSH Super Speciality Hospitals




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to set up AYUSH super speciality hospitals and wellness centres in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the names of the districts of Gujarat identified in this regard in the first phase; and (d) the amount of funds provided to Gujarat for conservation and production of herbs/medicines during the last three years and the current year, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a) to (d) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO.50 * FOR 4TH FEBRUARY, 2022(a)	& (b)(i)	Ministry of Ayush has established following hospitals attached to National Institutes of Ayurveda, Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha and Homoeopathy in different states to provide system specific health care.S.No.	Name of the Institute	No. of Beds1.		All India Institute of Ayurveda, New Delhi	2002.		National Institute of Ayurveda, Jaipur, Rajasthan	2803.		Institute of Teaching and Research in Ayurveda, Jamnagar, Gujarat 	3004.		National Institute of Naturopathy, Pune, Maharashtra	305.		National Institute of Unani Medicine, Bangalore, Karnataka	1806.		National Institute of Siddha, Chennai, Tamil Nadu	2007.		National Institute of Homoeopathy, Kolkatta, West Bengal	1008.		North –Eastern Institute of Ayurveda and Homoeopathy, Shillong, Meghalaya	i.	60 bedded Ayurveda Hospital ii.	40 bedded Homoeopathy Hospital (ii)	In addition to this, following satellite centers have also been established:-S.No.	Name of the Satellite Centre	No. of Beds1.		All India Institute of Ayurveda, Goa	2502.		National Institute of Ayurveda, Panchkula, Haryana	2503.		National Institute of Unani Medicine, Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh	2004.		National Institute of Homoeopathy, Narela, Delhi	100(iii)	Under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the National AYUSH Mission (NAM), there are provisions of financial assistance for establishment of new 10/30/50 bedded integrated Ayush Hospitals and establishment of AYUSH Health & Wellness Centres by upgrading AYUSH Dispensaries and existing Sub Health Centres in the States/ UTs. State/UT Governments may avail the financial assistance for establishment of new 10/30/50 bedded integrated Ayush Hospitals and establishment of AYUSH HWCs by submitting suitable proposal through State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs) as per the provision of NAM guidelines. So far 104 upto 50 bedded integrated Ayush Hospitals have been approved under NAM. State/ UT wise details are annexed at Annexure-I(iv)	Union Cabinet on 20.03.2020 has approved the proposal to operationalise 12,500 Health and Wellness Centres (HWCs) through National AYUSH Mission under Ayushman Bharat in a phased manner from year 2019-20 to 2023-24. Till date, a total of 4464 facilities are approved for up-gradation as AYUSH HWCs in 33 States/UTs during 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 and Central share of Rs. 321.75 Crore has been released to these States/UTs. Out of approved 4464 AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres, 2322 units are progressively functional. State/UT wise status of approved AYUSH HWCs under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the National Ayush Mission is annexed at Annexure- II(v)	Under Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS), total 96 Ayush units/ wellness centres are functional in the country. One Ayurvedic wellness center/ unit and one Homoeopathic wellness/ unit centre is functional in Ahmedabad and one Ayurveda wellness centre is functional at Gandhi Nagar under Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS).(vi)	In addition, Ministry also supports the private sector to encourage them to establish Ayush super specialty hospitals under Central Sector Scheme AYURSWASTHYA YOJANA and Medical Value Travel component of Central Sector Scheme for Champion Services Sector. (vii)	Under Centre of Excellence (COE) Component of Central Sector Scheme of AYURSWASTHYA YOJANA, financial support upto Rs. 10.0 cr. is provided to support establishment of advanced/ specialized AYUSH medical health unit in reputed AYUSH and Allopathy institutions.(viii)	Under Medical value Travel component of Central Sector Scheme for Champion Services Sector, Ministry of Ayush provides financial assistance to the private investors in the form of interest subsidy maximum upto Rs. 8.15 cr. to encourage them to establish super specialty hospitals/ day care centers of systems recognized under National Commission for Indian System of Medicines Act, 2020 and National Commission for Homoeopathy Act, 2020 under Central Sector Scheme for establishment of Ayush super specialty hospitals/ Day care centers.(c)	District wise status of approved AYUSH HWCs in the State of Gujarat is as follows:Name of District	No. of Ayush HWCAhmedabad 	10Amreli	5Anand	8Aravalli	4Banaskantha	12Bharuch	6Bhavnagar	17Botad	2Chhota Udaipur	9Dahod	11Dang	1Devbhumi Dwarka	3Gandhinagar	13Gir Somnath	5Jamnagar	3Junagadh	15Katchchh Bhuj	5Kheda	12Mahisagar	2Mehsana	12Morabi	9Narmada	9Navsari	10Panchmahal	7Patan	7Porbandar	4Rajkot	5Sabarkantha	10Surat	13Surendranagar	12Tapi	4Vadodara	13Valsad	7 Total	265 (d)	The year-wise amount of funds provided to Gujarat under “Central Sector Scheme for Conservation, Development and Sustainable Management of Medicinal Plants” during the last three years and Current Year for Conservation is as under:S.
