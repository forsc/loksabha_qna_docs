                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

705






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






E-Shram Portal




Kripanath Mallah













                                                    Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak
                                                








                                                    Anumula Revanth Reddy
                                                








                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                








                                                    Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu
                                                








                                                    Anil Firojiya
                                                








                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                








                                                    Raksha Nikhil Khadse
                                                








                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the sailent features of the E-Shram portal including the registration process, nodal authorities responsible for registration and the number of unorganised workers registered on the Portal, State-wise including Assam and Telangana;(b)whether it is true that the Government has developed e-Shram Portal for creating National Database of unorganised workers which is seeded with Aadhaar, if so, the details thereof along with eligibility criteria and the total number of workers registered so far in the country, State and district wise including Maharashtra;(c)the State-wise percentage of the targeted number of registration made and achieved till date and the challenges faced by the Government in getting the unorganised sector workers registered on the online portal along with benefits of the various social security schemes which can be accessed for workers, district-wise in Andhra Pradesh;(d)whether the Government has planned to deliver financial and other benefits to the workers registered under this portal and the manner in which it is to help the public at large in future and if so, the details thereof along with the details of statutory benefits/welfare measures extended to the workers under this plan and the reasons behind seeding of bank account of workers on E-Shram Portal; (e)the details of fund allocated and utilized under different welfare measures for workers registered on the E-Shram Portal; and(f)the number of male and female unorganised workers who got employment after registering themselves on e-Shram portal across the country including Gujarat?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)Contd..2/-:: 2 ::(a) to (f):	On 26.08.2021 Ministry of Labour & Employment has launched eSHRAM portal which is a National Database of the Unorganised Workers. One of the main objectives of this portal is to facilitate delivery of Social Security Schemes to the Unorganised workers. Any Unorganised worker aged between 16-59 years and not a member of ESIC/EPFO is eligible for registration on this portal. The workers can easily get themselves registered on the portal through following three modes:-(i) Self registration (ii) Through Common Service Centres (CSCs) (iii)  Through State Seva Kendras. As on 01.02.2022, 24.75 crore unorganised workers have already been registered on eSHRAM portal. The detail of State wise registration and targeted number of registration made of unorganised workers is annexed.  All eligible registered unorganised workers are entitled to get benefit of an accidental insurance cover of Rs. 2.0 Lakh for a year free of cost through Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY). The registered workers on eSHRAM are also nudged to take benefit of Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maandhan (PM-SYM) pension scheme & NPS-Traders pension scheme. The bank accounts of workers on eShram portal are seeded to provide benefit of welfare schemes, if any, directly to their bank account. *                                                           ******
