                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4085






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Diversion of Forest Land




Arvind Ganpat Sawant













                                                    Krupal Balaji Tumane
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has received proposal from the State Government of Maharashtra regarding diversion and acquisition of forest land of Karnataka for Tillari Hydro-Electric Project; (b)	if so, the details and the present status of the proposal;(c)	whether the said project has been accorded approval by the Government; (d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the time by which said proposal is likely to be approved by the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) to (d) No Sir, the Government of India has not received any proposal from the Government of Maharashtra regarding diversion and acquisition of forest land of Karnataka for Tillari Hydro-Electric Project. (e) As and when a proposal complete in all respect is received from a State/UT, appropriate decision on merit is taken by the Central Government expeditiously. ***
