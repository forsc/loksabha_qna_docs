                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1960






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Development of Rajkot Junction




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the time frame for making the Rajkot Junction capable of utilizing its resources for primary maintenance/ secondary maintenance/terminal attention, etc. for all sorts of trains;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c): Rajkot coaching depot has one pit line of 24 coach capacity on which Primary as well as Secondary maintenance can be carried out. Presently, Primary maintenance of train nos. 19571/72 (Rajkot-Porbandar Express), 19579/80 (Rajkot - Delhi S Rohilla Express), 22937/38 (Rajkot -Rewa Express), 19569/70 (Rajkot - Veraval Express), 59507/08 (Rajkot - Somnath passenger) and Secondary maintenance of train no. 16614/13 (Rajkot - Coimbatore Express) is being carried out on this pit line of Rajkot coaching depot.A work for construction of new pit line at a cost of ? 5.4 cr has commenced in June 2019.*****
