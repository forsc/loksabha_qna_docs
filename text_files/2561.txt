                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2418






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Sakshar Bharat Mission




Chandra Prakash Joshi













                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Sakshar Bharat Mission has not achieved the targets fixed during the lastthree years;(b) if so, the details of the target fixed and the success rate achieved during the last threeyears across the country, State/ UT-wise including Rajasthan, Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh;(c) the number of women declared literate during the last three years, State/ UT-wise; and(d) the details of the existing school buildings and community centres which are being usedas study centres for the night class, State-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) & (b) No Sir. Under the Saakshar Bharat Mission, against the overall target of making 7crore adult non-literates to be literates, around 7.64 crore learners successfully passed thebiannual Basic Literacy Assessment Tests conducted by National Institute of Open Schoolingbetween August, 2010 to March, 2018 and certified as literates.A statement indicating State/UT-wise learners declared successful in the biannualBasic Literacy Assessment Tests conducted by National Institute of Open Schooling betweenAugust, 2010 to March, 2018 under Saakshar Bharat Mission, including Rajasthan,Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh is at Annexure-I.(c) A statement indicating State/UT-wise number of women learners declared successful in thebiannual Basic Literacy Assessment Test conducted by NIOS under Saakshar Bharat Missionduring 2016-17 & 2017-18 is at Annexure-II. No assessment test has been conducted during2018-19 as the Saakshar Bharat Programme was implemented upto 31.03.2018.(d) No school buildings are being used as study centres for night class.***
