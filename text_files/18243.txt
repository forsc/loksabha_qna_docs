                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1935






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Impact of COVID-19 on Smart Cities




Sanjay Kumar Bandi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is planning to add any additional features to the Smart Cities in the country to meet any future exigencies like COVID-19 and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	the status of Karimnagar smart city of Telangana; and (c) 	whether the COVID-19 pandemic has impacted negatively on the development of Karimnagar Smart City and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):	Keeping in mind the need for Smart Cities to meet any future exigencies like COVID-19, an Advisory has been issued by Smart Cities Mission on 18th June, 2020 allowing flexibility in prioritization of projects to Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs) in Smart Cities. The Advisory contains a list of innovations / interventions that could be taken by them in such situations.        (b)  :	As on 18th September, 2020, in Karimnagar Smart City, out of a total of 54 projects worth Rs. 1760 crore, 13 projects worth Rs. 287.97 crore have been tendered out, all of which are under implementation/completed.(c) : The lockdown resulted in temporary delay for about a month. The implementation of works under the Mission has now gained momentum.                                         *****
