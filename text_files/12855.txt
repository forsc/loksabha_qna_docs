                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1752






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Allegations against Vice-Chancellor




Ranjan Singh Rajkumar














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Independent two Member Committee has submitted its report in respect ofthe Vice-Chancellor of Manipur University;(b) if so, the details of the action taken thereof;(c) if not, the reasons therefor;(d) whether the issue relating to allegations made against the Vice-Chancellor has beenresolved or settled by the Government; and(e) if so, the appropriate action taken by the Government under the provisions of the Act,2005 and relevant status to bring a full time regular Vice-Chancellor of ManipurUniversity to maintain the academic sanctity of the University?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): Yes, Sir.(b) to (e): On consideration of the recommendations of two Member Committee and reply tothe Show Cause Notice submitted by Prof. Adya Prasad Pandey, the then Vice-Chancellor(under suspension), Manipur University, the Hon’ble President of India, in his capacity as theVisitor of Manipur University, has been pleased to dismiss Prof. Adya Prasad Pandey fromthe post of Vice-Chancellor, Manipur University and hence, dismissal order has been issuedon 21.02.2020. The process of appointment of new regular Vice-Chancellor has been set inmotion.******
