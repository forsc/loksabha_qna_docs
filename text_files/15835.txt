                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4490






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Cashless Insurance Facility for AYUSH




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government is providing cashless insurance facility upto 5 lakhs to poor patients admitted in hospitals under PM-JAY on the basis of allopathic medicine and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government is emphasizing on including alternative system of medicines under the AYUSH scheme; (c) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (d) whether any AYUSH scheme have been proposed to facilitate AYUSH treatment for poor people; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):       Yes, Ayushman Bharat  Pradhan   Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB PM-JAY) aims to provide health cover upto Rs. 5 lakhs per family per annum for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization to 10.74   crore poor and vulnerable families belonging to Socio-Economic Caste Census- 2011. The benefit of the scheme can be availed   in empanelled hospitals at cashless basis for 1393 defined allopathic treatment packages including one unspecified surgical package. Relevant details and the list of treatment packages are  available on the AB PM-JAY website https://pmjay.gov.in .Contd…….-2-(b) & (c):  Ministry of AYUSH has constituted a Task Force involving two officers of the National Health Authority to look in to the inclusion of AYUSH packages in the scheme of AB PM-JAY. (d) & (e):    Health Insurance Regulations, 2013 notified by the Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI) have provision for insurance coverage to AYUSH treatment of the beneficiaries.  In December, 2019, IRDAI also notified modified guidelines of standardization of Health Insurance Business for empanelment of AYUSH treatment facilities in the insurance network.  Through Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission, grant-in-aid is provided to the States/UTs for establishment & up gradation of AYUSH health facilities and supply of AYUSH medicines for the benefit of poor people and general public. .............
