                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3325






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






LPG Gas Cylinder Blast




Alok Kumar Suman













                                                    Khagen Murmu
                                                








                                                    Ajay (Teni) Misra
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a number of cases of LPG gas cylinder blast have occurred in different parts of the country causing loss of life and property and if so, the details thereof and the measures taken in this regard;(b) whether the Government has given any compensation to people in Bihar particularly in district Gopalganj who were killed by gas cylinder blast and if so, the amount spent on them; and(c) whether the Government has formulated any scheme to ensure the safety of LPG cylinders to prevent/reduce the accidents caused due to it and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) have reported that no LPG accidents have occurred due to blowing up of cylinders on its own in the last 3 years of any OMCs customers. However, cases have been reported where LPG cylinder(s) got engulfed in fire caused by other sources/reasons and subsequently burst due to extreme external heat generated by fire. Details of accidents, fatalities and amount of compensation given to LPG customers of OMCs during three years are as under :-YearNo. of accidents where LPG is the primary cause of fireNos. of fatalitiesAmount of compensation (? in lakhs)2017-1811512922344.442018-199832541026.592019-20 (till September)435925.98(b) OMCs have reported that from 2017-18 to September, 2019, ? 1.80 crore have been paid as compensation for the fatalities in the Bihar including ? 6.00 lakh in the district of Gopalganj.(c) The steps taken by OMCs to ensure safety of LPG cylinders include manufacturing of LPG cylinders as per Indian Standard Specifications, testing of cylinders periodically as per norms of Petroleum and Explosives Safety Organisation, multiple quality control checks at bottling plants, pre-delivery check of the cylinders, mandatory checks of the LPG installation, mandatory use of Suraksha hose pipe, distribution of safety leaflets and organising safety and customer education clinics from time to time
