                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1878






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Reduction of Aviation Turbine Fuel




Sunil Kumar Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken/proposes to take any step for the reduction of Aviation Turbine Fuel (ATF) tax and if so, the details thereof; and(b) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) and (b): Yes Sir. Ministry of Civil Aviation has already taken up the issues of reduction in Central Excise Duty on Aviation Turbine Fuel (ATF) with Ministry of Finance and reduction of Value Added Tax (VAT) on ATF with State Governments/ Union Territories (UTs) on multiple occasions. Based on the request, VAT on ATF has recently been reduced in the range of 1-4% by Andaman & Nicobar Islands, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Ladakh, Madhya Pradesh, Tripura and Uttarakhand in their respective States/ UTs.
