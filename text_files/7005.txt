                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

820






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Air Service in Solapur




Jaisiddeshwar Shivacharya Mahaswamiji














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any proposal for introduction of aviation services from Solapur (Maharashtra) is under consideration of the Government;(b) if so, the details of the blue print to introduce air services from Solapur to other cities of the country for the benefit of people along with the time frame fixed in this regard;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the details of efforts made so far by the Government to deal with the above situation?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (d): Under Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik) version 2.0, routes from Solapur to Bengaluru & Hyderabad have been awarded to Selected Airline Operators. RCS flights could not be commenced due to obstacles i.e. Chimneys in the Flying Path. Implementing Agency has requested Government of Maharashtra for taking appropriate steps for removal of the Chimneys, so that the RCS flights could be commenced.
