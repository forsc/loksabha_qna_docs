                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2065






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Regional Air Cargo Hub Development Programme




Faizal P.P. Mohammed













                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of plans chalked out and initiatives taken by the Government to introduce Regional Air Cargo Hub Development Programmes; and(b) whether there is a specific proposal received from any State, including Kerala for the same and if so, the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): Ministry of Civil Aviation has released the National Air Cargo Policy Outline 2019 and the Mission Statement on India: Next-Gen Aviation Hub at the Global Aviation Summit at Mumbai on 15-16 January 2019. Therein development and growth of all three categories of air cargo transport are covered, namely,;(i) Domestic cargo: To ensure efficient flow of goods across India and facilitative measures for development of regional and State cargo hubs which would act as gateways for the region and States, connected by domestic trade corridors to production hubs and logistics hubs across the country.;(ii) International cargo: For facilitation of all indigenous export and import of goods, and ;(iii) Transit and transshipment international cargo: To make India the transit and transshipment cargo hub of choice to and from other parts of the globe.;;(b): So far, no proposal from any State, including Kerala, has been received by the Ministry of Civil Aviation for introduction of Regional Air Cargo Hub Development Programmes.
