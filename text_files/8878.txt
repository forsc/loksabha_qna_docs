                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2348






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Fisheries College




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the fisheries colleges running in the country, State and placewise; (b)	whether the only fisheries college in Chhattisgarh is located in district Kabirdham, Kawardha which is affiliated to Kamdhenu University, Durg; (c)	if so, whether there has been a demand to set up a fishery university in Bilaspur with a view to cherish the memory of ‘Bilasa Kevtin’ who laid the foundation of Bilaspur, the judicial center of Chhattisgarh and also to preserve her honour and glory for future generations; (d)	if so, the details thereof; and (e)	the decision taken/likely to be taken by the Government in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING:(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a)	List is enclosed at Annexure.(b)	Yes. One fisheries college is located at Kabirdham Kawardha which is a constituent unit of Chattisgarh Kamdhenu University, Durg, Chattisgarh.(c)	No.(d)	Does not arise.(e)	No.                                                            *****
