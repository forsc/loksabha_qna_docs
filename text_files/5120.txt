                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4737






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Funds earmarked for Tribal Welfare Schemes




Feroze Varun Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is true that seven central ministries and departments have not earmarked funds for tribal welfare schemes in their budget for 2019-20 and twelve have kept the provision below the level stipulated by the NITI Aayog;(b)	if so, the reasons therefor; and (c)	the measures which the Government is planning to take for receiving the funds?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE AND CORPORATE AFFAIRS(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (c):	Two (2) Ministries/Departments have not earmarked funds for tribal welfare schemes and fifteen (15) Ministries/Departments have kept the allocation below the level/percentage stipulated by the NITI Aayog.  Ministries/Departments have earmarked funds as per their assessment under the head ‘Tribal Area Sub Plan’ in BE 2019-20. However, it may be mentioned that overall allocation of funds for the welfare of Scheduled Tribes in BE 2019-20 has been increased by about Rs.17,791 crore over RE 2018-19, which shows substantive growth of about 29%. *****
