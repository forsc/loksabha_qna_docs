                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1588






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Procurement of Foodgrains by FCI




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of total procurement of foodgrains by Government agencies like FCI during the Rabi season and Kharif season in 2019, season-wise, foodgrain-wise and State-wise; and (b) 	the rate at which foodgrains have been procured under MSP, grain-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) and (b): 	Department of Food & Public Distribution (DFPD) extends  price support for procurement of paddy and wheat for central pool which are procured by the Food Corporation of India (FCI) and State Governments/State Government agencies.     	The details of procurement of paddy and wheat during Kharif/Rabi  Marketing season 2018-19 & 2019-20 by FCI/State Governments/ State Government  agencies is at Annexure-I. Coarsegrains are procured at MSP by State Governments as per the procurement plan prepared in consultation with FCI and approved by the Central Government, subject to  its distribution by the State Government  under National Food Security Act (NFSA)/ Other Welfare Schemes (OWS).
