                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

27






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Mid-Day Meal Scheme




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of children enrolled under Mid-Day Meal Scheme during 2018-19,State/UT-wise;(b) the number of children out of these, who had linked their Aadhaar numbers by March31, 2019; and(c) the number of children out of the rest, who have been listed as proxy/duplicate and yetto link their Aadhaar numbers?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): During 2018-19, the number of children enrolled under Mid-Day Meal Scheme are11,98,27,939. The State and UT-wise details of children enrolled under mid-day mealscheme is given at Annexure.(b) & (c): As per information reported by States and UTs (except J&K, Assam &Meghalaya which are exempted), out of 11,39,06,555 enrolled students in ElementaryLevel (Classes I -VIII) 10,10,84,233 (89%) students have Aadhaar number and1,28,22,322 students do not have Aadhaar number as on 31.03.2019. The States andUTs also reported that 6,70,818 proxy/duplicate names have been deleted afterAadhaar authentication. However, no eligible child has been deprived of mid-daymeal.*******
