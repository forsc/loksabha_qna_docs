                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4177






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Beneficiaries of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    the number of houses constructed under each component of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY), State/UT-wise;(b)    the number of houses allotted to beneficiaries under its each component, State/UT-wise;(c)    the number of houses constructed thereunder and lying vacant, State/UT-wise; and(d)    the details of the social-audit conducted as per the guidelines of the scheme, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                      (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) to (c):	The Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY(U)] since 25.06.2015 in urban areas.  State/Union Territory (UT)-wise details of houses sanctioned and houses completed/allotted under each component of PMAY(U) alongwith houses in process of occupation are at Annexure.Housing is a State subject and unoccupied houses completed under AHP and ISSR verticals are allocated by the Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) as per PMAY (U) Guidelines.  (d):	Under the PMAY(U), Social Audit is conducted by States/UTs for projects as selected by them.  This Ministry provides 100% financial assistance to States/UTs to conduct Social Audit with the help of an Independent Facilitating Agency (IFA), selected through a competitive bidding process.  1st instalment of funds (50%) for social audit has been released to States namely Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, Jharkhand, Kerala, Uttarakhand, Mizoram and Manipur.  Remaining States/UTs have been requested to submit their plans for Social Audit.                            *****
