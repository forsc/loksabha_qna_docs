                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1908






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Environmental Projects




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government allows non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) to undertake projects towards spreading environmental awareness among people,(b)	The details of the schemes / programmes  under which NGOs can collaborate which the Ministry towards environment conservations,(c)	The details of NGOs from Darjeeling and Kalimpong Districts of West Bengal engaged with the Ministry currently or in the past for the purpose, and (d)	The funds granted to NGOs working towards Environment Conservation during the last three years in the said Districts? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) Yes Sir,(b) There are various schemes in the Ministry under which NGOs can collaborate for the conservation of environment and spread awareness in the country.  The major details of different schemes are enclosed as Annexure-I.  (c) and (d) : The details of grants released, Scheme-wise, to Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) from Darjeeling and Kalimpong District of West Bengal during the last three years are given at Annexure-II.****
