                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2095






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Industrially Backward Areas in Bihar




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has identified industrial backward districts in Bihar;(b) 	if so, the details thereof, district-wise;(c) 	whether the Government is planning to formulate any scheme to ensure favourable investment in Bihar especially in the district of Gopalganj;(d) 	if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	whether the Government proposes to formulate any policy for setting up of industries in the backward areas of Bihar and if so, the steps being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):	No industrial backward districts have been identified in the State of Bihar.(b):	Not Applicable(c):	Department of Industries, Government of Bihar formulates suitable policies from time to time for promoting establishments of industries by investors/entrepreneurs. Presently, Bihar Industrial Investment Promotion Policy – 2016 is applicable by which Financial Incentives are granted to the unit after getting approval from the State Investment Promotion Board (SIPB). Government of India has de-licensed all industries other than in four sectors of Defence, Hazardous Chemicals, Industrial Explosives and Cigars and cigarettes. In case of licensed sectors also it does not indicate location of the projects. Private sector is free to choose location of their projects. (d):	3 Units in Gopalganj district have got financial incentive clearance under Bihar Industrial Investment Promotion Policy – 2016. All the three units are operational and getting financial incentive under the scheme. List of these three units is at Annexure.(e):	Presently, Bihar Industrial Investment Promotion Policy – 2016 is applicable across Bihar. No Backward Areas policy is being proposed as of now.********
