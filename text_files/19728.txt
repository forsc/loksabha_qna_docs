                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1361






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Babu Jagjivan Ram Chhatravas Yojana




Sanjeev Kumar Singari














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1361 TO BE ANSWERED ON 09.02.2021BABU JAGJIVAN RAM CHHATRAVAS YOJANA 1361.   DR. SANJEEV KUMAR SINGARI: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether Babu Jagjivan Ram Chhatravas Yojana (BJCY) scheme is being implemented in the State of Andhra Pradesh; (b) if so, the details of projects sanctioned under the scheme to Andhra Pradesh during the last three years and the current year; and (c) if not, the eligibility criteria for including any institution under this scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) and (b):    Yes, Sir. Babu Jagjivan Ram Chhatravas Yojana (BJRCY) is being implemented in the State of Andhra Pradesh and since 2008-09 to 2015-16, 16 hostels have been sanctioned under the scheme. However, no complete proposal has been received from the State Government of Andhra Pradesh during the last three years and the current year under the scheme.  During the year 2018-19, however, funds were sanctioned under the scheme to the Central Institute of Plastics Engineering and Technology, Vijaywada, Andhra Pradesh for construction of a girls'' hostel.(c):     Does not arise in view of reply given in part (a) above.*****
