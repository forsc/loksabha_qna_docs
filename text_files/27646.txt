                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3343






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Development of Protected Monuments in Odisha




Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of monuments which are declared as of national importance in Odisha;(b)	whether the Government has any plans for the conservation, restoration and overall development of the protected monuments in the State of Odishafor posterity and to make them as popular tourist destinations;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the details of funds allocated a5nd expenditure incurred on conservation, restoration, scientific preservation and environmental development, developmentof surrounding including beautification on each protected monument in Odisha during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a)	80 monuments/sites have been declared as of National importance in Odisha.(b)&(c)	Conservation, preservation and environmental development work of centrally protected monuments in Odisha is a continuous process taken up as per the Annual Conservation Plan (ACP) drawn by the field office, and same is attended by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) as per the availability of resources and priority. Further, providing basic facilities/amenities (e.g. drinking water, toilet, facilities for physically challenged, pathways, cultural notice boards/signage, parking, cloak rooms, etc.) are regular activities which the Archaeological Survey of India undertakes, as per need, priority and availability of resources.  (d)	The fund allocated and expenditure incurred on conservation, restoration,scientific preservation and environmental development on protected monuments in Odisha during the last five years is as under: Year	Amount (Rupees in lakhs)2016-17	865.002017-18	689.922018-19	590.002019-20	530.002020-21	363.00
