                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1073






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Central Assistance to Puducherry




 Vaithilingam Ve.














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	Whether the Union Government is aware that the annual central assistance to the UT of Puducherry remains static for a quite long time without  reasonable increase to match the revenue deficit;b)	If so, the details thereof;c)	Whether  the Union Government will come forward to adequately  increase the central assistance and if so, the details thereof;d)	Whether the Union Government has made any study on the minimum requirement  of central assistance  for UT Government based on a reasonable formula; ande)	If not, whether the Union Government will come forward to initiate steps in this direction and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE    (SH. PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) to (c):The annual growth in central assistance to the UT of Puducherry is about 4.5%, except in the year 2020-21 in which about 10% increase was given.      Details of central assistance received during the last 7 years are given below:Year	Rs. in crore Growth %2015-16	1300.00	      --2016-17	1353.13	      4.092017-18	1418.69	      4.852018-19	1470.99	      3.692019-20	1539.98	      4.692020-21	1698.00	     10.262021-22	1724.77	      1.58(d) and (e): The Annual allocation by the Ministry of Finance towards Central Assistance to the UTs are based on the projections/expenditure trends and availability of resources, which are reviewed and provided for through supplementary grants approved by the Parliament for respective Financial Year.
