                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3164






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Restoration of Sendamanglam Siva Temple




D. Ravikumar














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any plan to sanction funds to restore the outer prahara of 12th century Sendamangalam Siva Temple in Viluppuram Constituency  maintained by ASI; (b)	if so, the details thereof; and if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	whether the Government has sanctioned funds to restore the Temple tank and the huge Musical horses there; and(d)	the details of the amount sanctioned to Tamilnadu between 2015-2020 to take up excavations at various sites?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)  to (c) 	Yes Sir. Presently the conservation work of Vasanta Mandapa in the Sendamangalam Siva Temple complex is in progress. The work of its outer Prakara wall, Temple tank and sculptures of horses will be taken up in due course. (d)	The year wise details of amount sanctioned for excavation work at various sites in Tamilnadu are as follows:- Sl. No. Year	Amount sanctioned in Rs.                (Plan & Non Plan)1.	2015-16	51,00,000/-2.	2016-17	48,50,000/-3.	2017-18	50,00,000/-4.	2018-19	10,36,327/-*****
