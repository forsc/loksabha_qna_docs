                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3170






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Assistance to Farmers in Odisha




Rajashree Mallick














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Union Government provides assistance to farmers of Odisha during natural calamities;(b) if so, the names of the schemes under which assistance has been provided to Odisha during each of the last three years and the amount thereof; and(c) the reformative steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????      (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c): The State Government is primarily responsible for providing necessary relief measures in the wake of natural calamities. For undertaking relief measures, funds are available with the State Government in the form of State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF). Additional financial assistance, over and above SDRF, is considered from National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF) for natural calamities of severe nature and is approved on the basis of Memorandum received from the State Government, in accordance with established procedures.  Funds released to the State Government of Odisha under SDRF and NDRF during 2019-20 to 2021-22 are as under:(Rs. in crore)Year	Allocation under SDRF	Release of Central share under SDRF	Release from NDRF	Central Share	State Share		2019-20	681.75	227.25	552.00	3294.102020-21	1604.00	535.00	1604.00	500.002021-22	1283.20	428.00	1283.20	500.00******
