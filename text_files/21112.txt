                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2398






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Shutting Down Procurement Centres in Telangana




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government  is aware of the Telangana State Government announcement that procurement centres across the State will be shut down and if so, the Ministry’s response in this regard;(b)	whether this step by the State Government has been enabled by the passing of the new farm laws by the Centre and if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the Ministry’s response to the stand taken by the State Government’(d)	the estimated number of farmers who sell their produce to the procurement centres run by IKP Self Help Groups and Primary Agricultural  Cooperative Societies in Telangana  along with a breakdown according to size of landholding of farmers; and(e)	whether there is any legal safeguard to guarantee these farmers MSP on their produce if they do not sell to these procurement centres and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ??????? ??????   (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	  There is no such announcement made by the State Government. (b) & (c): Do not arise.(d):	The estimated number of farmers who sell their produce to the procurement centres run by IKP Self Help Groups and Primary Agricultural Cooperative Societies in Telangana along with a breakdown according to size of landholding of farmers as provided by the State Govt. is annexed. (e):	The procurement at MSP is being done by Central and State Agencies under various schemes of Government.  Besides, the overall market also responds to declaration of MSP and Government’s procurement operations which results in private procurement on or above the MSP for various notified crops.  However, farmers are free to sell their produce anywhere if they get better price.
