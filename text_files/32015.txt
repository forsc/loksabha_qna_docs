                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3393






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Adolescent Friendly Health Clinics (AFHC)




Faizal P.P. Mohammed














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	The details of  Adolescent Friendly Health Clinics (AFHC) set up under the Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram (RKSK) scheme from inception till date , State/UT wise;(b)	 whether these are currently functioning and counseling services are being offered; and (c)	the details of targets set and achievements made in aspirational districts under RKSK Scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFAREDR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	& (b) The details of Adolescent Friendly Health Clinics (AFHC) set up under the Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram (RKSK) scheme from inception in 2014 till date , State/UT wise are placed in the Annexure. These Clinics are providing counselling services through trained health service providers.(c) The achievements of RKSK in aspirational districts for FY 2021-22 till Nov 2021 are:•	5.36 lakh adolescent boys and girls  have received counselling and clinical services at  the clinics •	50.5 lakh adolescent boys and girls have been provided Weekly Iron Folic Acid Supplementation (WIFS) tablets every month in schools while 8.2 lakh out of school adolescents girls have been covered every month •	25.4 lakh Adolescent Girls were provided sanitary napkin packs under the Menstrual Hygiene Scheme
