                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2394






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Schemes of Animal Husbandry




Gajendra Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of schemes related to animal husbandry being run in the rural areas; and (b) whether a new scheme is going to be operated for animal husbandry in the rural areas in the future and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a) and (b) The Government is implementing various schemes for development of animal husbandry across the country including rural areas as under:i.	Rashtriya Gokul Mission ii.	National Programme for Dairy Developmentiii.	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund iv.	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activities v.	 National Livestock Mission vi.	Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fundvii.	Livestock Health and Disease Control viii.	National Animal Disease Control ProgrammeSo far, there is no plan to operate any new scheme for animal husbandry specific to rural areas. However, if any such plan approves by the Government in the future, it would be implemented across the country including rural areas.****
