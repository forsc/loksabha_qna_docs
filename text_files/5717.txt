                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5251






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Operations at Adhartal Railways Station




Rakesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to start operation of trains from Adhartal Railway Station, Jabalpur under the West Central Railways Zone; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has taken action on an access road to Adhartal besides providing facilities therein; and(d) if so, the details thereof?	






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b) Presently, Adhartal station lacks basic infrastructural facilities for originating/ terminating of train services.  As such, it is not feasible to operate originating trains from Adhartal. (c) & (d) Adhartal is a Non-Suburban Grade (NSG-6) category station dealing with 280 passengers on an average per day. All minimum essential amenities have been provided as per norms. However, upgradation/modernization of railway stations is a continuous and ongoing process and works in this regard are undertaken depending upon need, volume of the passenger traffic and inter-se priority subject to availability of funds.*****
