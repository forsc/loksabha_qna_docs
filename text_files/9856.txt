                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3392






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Exploration of Petroleum Resources




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the exploration and exploitation of petroleum resources within India’s borders is being undertaken by the Government either by itself or with some form of private partnership; and(b) if so, the details thereof in terms of geographical areas where exploration is taking place, the progress so far and the private entities involved, if any?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) Exploration and exploitation of petroleum resources within the country are being undertaken by Public Sector Undertakings and private companies including domestic Indian companies and foreign companies either standalone or through Indian subsidiary or unincorporated Joint Venture basis. At present, exploration activities by private E&P companies in onland areas under various contractual regimes is taking place across approximate 32,000 sq. km area. Upto 2018-19, approximate 21,000 line km. of 2D seismic Survey, 14,000 sq. km of 3D seismic survey and 400 exploratory wells in exploratory acreages have carried out by private E&P companies in onland areas.********
