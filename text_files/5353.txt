                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4962






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Agricultural Land




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the average land holding of agricultural land among farmers in various States of the country; (b) 	whether an increase or decrease has been registered in this regard as compared to the previous financial year; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) to (c): 	The data on number and area of operational holdings is collected as part of  Agriculture Census conducted in the country at five yearly intervals. State-wise details of average size of operational holdings as per results of latest Agriculture Census 2015-16 (provisional) and Agriculture Census 2010-11 are given at Annexure.              At national level, average size of land holdings has gone down from 1.15 hectare in 2010-11 to 1.08 hectare in 2015-16. States/UTs reporting significant decline in the average size of land holdings include Goa, Sikkim, Kerala, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Nagaland, Rajasthan and Telangana.
