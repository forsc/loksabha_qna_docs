                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

74






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Damage to Red Fort




Umesh G. Jadhav













                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that on January 26, 2021 some hooligans have made damage to Red fort and if so, the details of the damage done to Red Fort;(b)	whether Government is planning or going to amend the CHAPTER II, clause 9 to make it more-strict to protect the Monuments in the country; and(c)	whether Government is planning to ban  hooligans to enter ASI sites in the country and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reason thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a)    Yes Sir. During the incident, two brass finials of the dome at Lahori Gate were dislodged from their original positions.  Apart from that, the entrance plaza with turnstile gates, baggage scanners, Door Frame Metal Detectors, other security equipment and the booking counter at Lahori Gate were also damaged.(b) & (c) No such proposal is under consideration. Certain activities are not allowed at protected monuments and whoever destroy, remove, defaces or misuse a protected monument shall be punishable under Section 30 of the Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act, 1958.
