                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2562






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Central Vigilance Commission




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Central Vigilance Commission (CVC) has received any complaints especially from public representatives during the last one year till date; (b) 	if so, the details regarding number and nature of complaints received particularly from public representatives as on date; (c) 	whether any inquiry into the above complaints has been conducted or being conducted by the CVC; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) to (d):  As per information provided by the Central Vigilance Commission (CVC), data relating to complaints received in CVC from ‘representatives of people’ is not maintained separately. However, details of all complaints received in the Commission and action taken thereon during the last one year and current year (upto February 2021) are as follows:Year	No. of complaints received*	Action Taken	Pending		Sent for investigation and report toCVO/CBI	Sent for necessary action to CVOs concerned	Filed	2020	27590	140	15004	10688	1758Up to Feb, 2021	5922	14	2853	1940	1115*including those brought forward from previous year*****
