                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

748






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Target Capacity of PMAY-U




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to make provision of new fund to increase target capacity of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojna (PMAY) and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the details of funds proposed by the Union Government under PMAY, district-wise; and(c)	the details of houses build under PMAY-Urban and rural scheme which is audited by Central Government authority?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                  (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a):   No, Sir. (b): States/ Union Territories (UTs)-wise and district-wise details of houses sanctioned under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY-U) along with Central assistance approved thereof are given at Annexure.(c): Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY-U), participating States/ Union Territories (UTs) have undertaken demand survey for assessing actual demand of housing in urban areas and reported a demand of 112.24 lakh houses. Against this, a total of 114.06 lakh houses have been sanctioned so far involving Central assistance of  ? 1,84,808.00 crore; of this, ? 1,02,505.00 crore has been released. Of the sanctioned houses, 89.36 lakh houses have been grounded for construction; of which 52.55 lakh houses have been completed/ delivered. Further, as reported by Ministry of Rural Development, a total of 208.76 lakh houses have been completed under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G).                             *****
