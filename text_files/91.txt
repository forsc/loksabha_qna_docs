                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

167






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Shelter Homes in States




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether her Ministry has proposed to construct one huge shelter home with a capacity of more than 1,000 inmates in each State and transferring the same to State for maintenance;(b)	if so, the details of the proposal; and(c)	whether the Government is contemplating to use Nirbhaya Funds for such construction and create more than one shelter and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a)	No Sir.(b)	& (c)  Does not arise.******
