                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

976






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






PMGSY in Odisha and West Bengal




Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo













                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of roads constructed in Odisha and West Bengal under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY);(b) 	the funds approved, spent/unspent during the last three years under PMGSY in Odisha and West Bengal;(c) 	the details of roads proposed to be constructed in the States of Odisha and West Bengal, district-wise;(d) 	the status of work completed in Bolangir of Odisha and Jalpaiguri of West Bengal and the details of the amount sanctioned and released for this purpose;  and(e) 	the time-frame fixed to complete the work and progress made under PMGSY till now in this regard?
