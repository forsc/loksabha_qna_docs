                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3565






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Supply of Pulses and Seeds




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the amount sanctioned and released to the State Government of Odisha during the years 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19 for disbursal of pulses and seeds to the farmers under the National Food Security Mission (NFSM), year-wise; (b)	 the quantum of seeds disbursed by the State Government and funds yet to be released till date; and (c)	the steps taken to release the funds to that State under NFSM?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The Government of India (GoI) is implementing National Food Security Mission (NFSM) on Pulses in 638 districts of 28 States and 2 Union Territories including 30 districts of Odisha. The year wise amount allocated and released (GoI share) under NFSM–Pulses and other special programmes on additional area coverage of pulses, Targeting Rice Fallow Area (TRFA) and intercropping of pulses with sugarcane to the State Government of Odisha including disbursal of pulses seeds from the year 2016-17, 2017-18 & 2018-19 are as under:-                         									(GoI share, Rs. in crore)Components	Allocation & release	Years		2016-17	2017-18	2018-19NFSM-Pulses	Allocation 	35.21	26.64	26.64	Release	17.61	13.32	26.64Addl. Area coverage of pulses	Allocation 	5.11	7.19	8.25	Release	-	7.19	8.25Targeting Rice Fallow area (TRFA) for sowing of pulses	Allocation 	-	21.99	26.40	Release	-	21.99	26.40Intercropping of pulses with Sugarcane	Allocation 	-	-	0.02	Release	-	-	0.02Total	Allocation 	40.32	55.82	61.31	Release	17.61	42.50	61.31Contd….2/-- 2 -(b & c):	The allocated funds in respect of NFSM –Pulses during 2018-19 were released to the State Government of Odisha. As per the information provided by State Government of Odisha, the quantum of seeds targeted & disbursed and funds released by the State Government during 2016-17, 2017-18 & 2018-19 is as under:- Year	Physical /Financial 	Progress		Target	Achievement 2016-17	Physical (qtl)	39142.000	6278.600	Financial (Rs. in crore)	8.505	0.2902017-18	Physical (qtl)	39690.000	15414.600	Financial (Rs. in crore)	6.783	0.7142018-19	Physical (qtl)	24809.600	10614.600	Financial (Rs. in crore)	6.446	0.520    	 Funds are released to the State Government based on the approved action plan. The total funds released to the State Government are indicated in the part (a) of the answer.*****
