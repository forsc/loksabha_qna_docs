                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3033






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






National Service Scheme




Rama Devi













                                                    Giridhari Yadav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of students fixed to avail benefits under National Service Scheme from Bihar at present;(b) whether the Government of Bihar has send any proposal toincrease the said number;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the steps taken by the Union Government so far to approve the above proposal and the outcome thereof as on date?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) At present, the total allocated strength of National Service Scheme (NSS) volunteers in the State of Bihar is 66800.(b) The Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports has not received any proposal from Government of Bihar to increase the number of volunteers.(c) & (d): Do not arise.******
