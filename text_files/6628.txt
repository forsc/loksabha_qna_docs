                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

398






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Promotion of Animal Husbandry and Dairying




Uday Pratap Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the steps taken by the Government to promote animal husbandry and dairying along with encouraging the farmers during the last three years and the current year (b) the amount of funds allocated and utilized for animal husbandry and dairying in Madhya Pradesh during the abovementioned period; and(c) the details of income and employment created in animal husbandry and dairying industry in the country especially in Madhya Pradesh during the said period, State-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	The Government is implementing various schemes to complement and supplement the efforts of States/UTs for promotion of animal husbandry and dairying along with encouraging the farmers during the last three years and the current year such as;i.	Rashtriya Gokul Mission (RGM)ii.	National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD)iii.	National Dairy Plan-I (NDP-I)iv.	Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme (DEDS)v.	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund (DPIDF)vi.	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activities (SDCFPO)vii.	 National Livestock Mission (NLM) viii.	Livestock Health and Disease Control (LH & DC)ix.	Livestock Census (LC) and Integrated Sample Survey (ISS) (b) The details of funds allocated/ released and utilized under animal husbandry and dairying in Madhya Pradesh during last three years and current year is as under:(Rs. in lakh)	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20 (till Oct, 2019)RGM	Funds Released	850.00	1618.48	2852.32	2155.86	Funds Utilized	850.00	1618.48	989.32	0.00NPDD	Funds Released	155.21	358.83	2085.27	1503.33	Funds Utilized	155.21	358.83	1229.22	0.00NDP-I	Funds Released	357.00	422.00	188.00	9.00	Funds Utilized	387.00	180.00	367.00	58.00DEDS	Funds Allocated	525.00	816.43	1826.00	2939.02	Subsidy disbursed	494.17	819.71	1032.06	186.45NLM	Funds Released	1278.96	48.00	1247.04	0.00	Funds Utilized	1179.67	48.00	874.72	0.00LH & DC	Funds Released	419.25	1717.10	2944.55	180.00	Funds Utilized	399.00	1310.65	2842.31	0.00LC & ISS	Funds Released	121.24	117.96	402.17*	322.22	Funds Utilized	85.28	100.72	236.45	141.5Note: State-wise fund allocation is not done under NPDD and NDP-I.NDP-I phase –I is going to close in November, 2019. NDP-I scheme has been discontinued w.r.t. release of fund during 2019-20.*The amount includes the revalidated amount of Rs. 85.57 lakhs released during the year 2014-15(c)  For employment generation and promotion of entrepreneurship in dairy and small ruminant sector especially in rural areas, the Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing two schemes, namely, Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme, and Entrepreneurship Development and Employment Generation across the country including Madhya Pradesh.  The State-wise list of number of entrepreneurs benefitted and amount of subsidy disbursed under the scheme during last three years and current year is at Annexure.***
