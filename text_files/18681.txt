                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

157






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Action plan for Agricultural Productivity




Saikia Dilip














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has formulated any action plan to increase agricultural productivity in NorthEastern States including Assam; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has fixed any responsibility in this regard; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) to (d):  Agriculture being a State subject, the State Governments undertake implementation of programs/schemes for the development of the sector. Government of India supplements the efforts of the State Governments through various schemes/ programs. These schemes/ programs of the Government of India are meant for the welfare of farmers by increasing production, remunerative returns and income support to farmers. The  initiatives taken by the Government to increase agricultural productivity, income support and remunerative prices in the North Eastern Region (NER), including the State of Assam, are by way of  implementation of Oil Palm cultivation scheme, issuance of Soil Health Card, implementation of Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) and Restructured Weather Based Crop Insurance Scheme (RWBCIS) and  with a view to increase coverage in NER, revision of the sharing pattern from 50:50 between Central and State Government to 90:10 w.e.f. Kharif 2020 season, extending PM-Kisan benefits, establishment of new Centres of Excellence under IIAP for promotion of fruits and vegetables in Kamrup (Assam), East Khasi Hills (Meghalaya) and Amarpur (Tripura), more focus on quality planting material including import of planting material of high value crops like Apple, Walnut, Kiwi etc, cluster based development of important horticulture crops in NER like Pine apple, Turmeric, Kiwi etc, development of Horticulture Promotion Parks in each State, establishment of nurseries for production of quality planting material, creation of water harvesting structures, promotion of organic farming/ micro irrigation/ bamboo/ honey, creation of Agriculture Marketing Infrastructure (AMI) fund and   Micro Irrigation Fund (MIF), formation of FPOs, promotion of Start-ups, implementation of  Krishi Udaan Scheme, operationalization of  three Kisan Special Rails Connecting NER States etc.   **********
