                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

352






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Umang Mobile App




Pocha Brahmananda Reddy













                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                








                                                    T.R.V.S. Ramesh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:(a)	whether the Government has developed the UMANG Mobile App, as a unified platform aggregating major Government services;(b)	if so, the details and the major features thereof;(c)	the State Governments that are offering their services through this UMANG Mobile App; and(d)	whether this App is integrated with Aadhaar, DigiLocker and Payment Gateway and if so, details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b): Yes, Sir. UMANG is a unified platform and common mobile app to facilitate single point access to major government services through mobile. UMANG App is available on Android, iOS, Windows & Web (www.umang.gov.in). UMANG supports multiple languages - English and 12 regional languages.As on Jan’21, about 1007 Services from 205 Central/ State/ UT Government departments along with 19361 Bharat Bill Payment Services (BBPS) have been on-boarded on UMANG.Some of the features of UMANG Mobile app are as below:•	Aggregation of major Govt. services •	One App, URL, SMS Code and IVR (Toll Free) Number •	Unified, Robust , Scalable cloud based platform at the back-end •	Customized branding for departments/states •	Intuitive discovery and powerful search •	Built-in Analytics (c): As on Jan’21, 28 State Governments are offering 544 Services from 103 State Government Departments through the UMANG Mobile App. The list of 28 State/ UTs is enclosed at Annexure I.(d): Yes Sir. UMANG app is integrated with Aadhaar, DigiLocker, Payment Gateway, Feedback System, etc. The brief of these core integrations is as below:1.	Payment Gateway – if a department is not integrated with payment Gateway and needs to collect some fees, the payment gateway integrated with UMANG i.e. PayGov may be used.2.	DigiLocker – All documents in DigiLocker of a person can be seen by that person on Home page of his UMANG or while applying to a new service a user can use documents from his DigiLocker to upload/apply.3.	Feedback System – If a person takes any service from UMANG, a feedback request can be sent to him and his feedback can be collected by Rapid Assessment System (RAS) - the feedback system integrated with UMANG.4.	SMS – An SMS can be sent to a user of any department, if required5.	Email Gateway – an e mail can be sent to user of any department, if required.******* Annexure IList of the States/UTs offering their services on UMANGS.
