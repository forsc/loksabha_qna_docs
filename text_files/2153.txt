Common Service Centres in Panchayats




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya













                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                








                                                    Chandra Prakash Joshi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:-(a)	whether the Government has achieved the target for setting up of common service centres;(b)	if so, the  total number of gram Panchayats in which common service centres have been set up at present along with the details thereof, state wise;(c)	the details of services being provided by the common service centre in gram panchayat; and(d)	 whether any assistance/fund is being provided by the Government to Village Level Entrepreneur (VLE) known as the CSC operators and if so, the details thereof?
