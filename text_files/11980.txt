                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

823






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Khelo India Scheme




Subhas Sarkar














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to develop playfields in the villages of India under Khelo India Scheme;  (b) if so, the details thereof;  (c) the number of villages in India specially in West Bengal with a functional playfield, State-wise; and  (d) the number of playfields developed since December, 2017? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)  FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS  { SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }  (a) to (d) Sports being a State subject, the primary responsibility of development of playfields rests with the concerned State/Union Territory Government. The Central Government supplements their efforts by bridging the critical gaps. However, under the Khelo India Scheme, this Ministry has sanctioned 179 sports infrastructure projects including playfields in rural areas, for development of sports infrastructure facilities in the States / Union Territories (UTs) of the country, including West Bengal. State/UT wise details of the same are placed at Annexure – I. Further, this Ministry has introduced geo-tagging of all playfields in the country and sharing the information with the public, which is in progress. So far 3005 playfields have been geo-tagged across the country, which includes 94 in West Bengal.   ANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) to (d) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 823 TO BE ANSWERED ON 06.02.2020 REGARDING “KHELO INDIA SCHEME” ASKED BY DR. SUBHAS SARKAR, HON’BLE MEMBER OF LOK SABHA*****
