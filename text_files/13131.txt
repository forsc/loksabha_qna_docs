                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1873






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Organic Farming




Shrirang Appa Barne













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether India is one of the largest countries in organic agricultural land and the largest producer of the organic food segment in the world and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government has taken any steps for the growth of organic sector and awareness about the benefits of consuming organic produce and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the details of the quantum of certified organic products produced in India during each of the last three years and the current year, State and product-wise;(d) 	whether the Ministry has organized a National Organic Festival recently, if so, the details thereof along with the aims and objectives thereof; and (e) 	the steps taken/being taken by the Government for encouraging organic farming in the country particularly in Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh and Jharkhand?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	India is the 9th largest country in the world in   terms of organic agricultural land.  India is likely to be one amongst 10 largest organic producer in the world. (Source: FIBL-IFOAM, 2020) (b):	Government of India has been promoting organic agriculture   through dedicated schemes such as   Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) and Mission Organic Value Chain Development for North Eastern Region (MOVCDNER).  Government is providing necessary hand holding, training, awareness generation and market linkage to the farmers aggregated under the cluster and Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs) under these schemes.   The State-wise details of the area covered under PKVY, MOVCDNER, NPOP and others are given at Annexure-I. (c):	State-wise and product-wise data of the quantum of certified organic products produced in India is available for last two years i.e., 2017-18 and 2018-19. The detail is given at Annexure-II & IIA.(d):	Government of India has organized a National level Krishi Unnati Mela, at Mela Ground, IARI, Pusa, New Delhi on 16-18 March, 2018 with an aim to  encourage the farmer for adopting organic farming with emphasis on sustainable productivity, food security and soil health.  (e):	Government of India has been promoting organic farming under two dedicated schemes namely Mission Organic Value Chain Development North Eastern Region (MOVCDNER) and Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) since 2015 through State Governments. Under these schemes, support is provided for formation of farmers’ clusters/ Farmer Producer Organization; input procurement, value addition including post harvest infrastructure creation, packaging, branding, publicity, transportation, organic fairs etc. Under the PKVY scheme the physical and financial status of Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh and Jharkhand is as below:State	No. of Clusters	Amount release (Rs. in lakh)	Area (in ha)	Farmers benefittedMaharashtra	1258	7937.46	25,160	62,900Madhya Pradesh	3828	10080.60	76,560	1,91,400Jharkhand	250	845.56	5,000	12,500
