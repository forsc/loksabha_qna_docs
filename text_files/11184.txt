                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

119






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






New Medical Institutions




Pushpendra Singh Chandel














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Employees State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) is contemplating any scheme to establish new Medical Institutions in other States of the country including Mahoba of Bundelkhand;(b)if so, the details thereof;(c)the details of the expenditure estimated by the ESIC for this purpose; and(d)the extant establishment status of these medical institutions?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (d):	 No scheme is contemplated to establish new Medical Institutions under ESI Scheme, including at Mahoba.  All approvals for setting up of new ESI Hospitals are as per existing norms, details of expenditure sanctioned and status of which are given below: Sl. No.	State	Location	Status1.		Chhattisgarh	Bhilai	Work is at construction stage.Sanctioned amount-Rs. 103.00 crore2.		Chhattisgarh	Raigarh	Work is at construction stage.Sanctioned amount- Rs. 74.23 crore3.		Karnataka	Doddabalapur	Work is at construction stage.Sanctioned amount- Rs. 83.00 crore 4.		Odisha	Angul	Construction work completed.Sanctioned amount- Rs. 64.47 crore.5.		West Bengal	Siliguri	Work is at construction stage.Sanctioned cost- Rs. 59.47 crore.6.		West Bengal	Haldia	Work is at construction stage.Sanctioned cost- Rs. 57.15 crore. *****
