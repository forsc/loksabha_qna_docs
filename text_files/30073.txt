                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1747






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Investment in Maharashtra




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total foreign investment in the State of Maharashtra in the year 2020;(b) 	the number of projects that have been setup in the State under the ‘plug and play’ model in that year; and(c) 	the details of investments and ‘plug and play’ models in the State over the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a):	During the calendar year 2020, an amount of Rs. 1,32,992.79 Crore has been reported as Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) Equity inflow in the State of Maharashtra.(b) & (c):       Government of India has developed industrial corridors under ''plug and play'' model. As part of this, one project viz. Shendra Industrial Area (8.39 sq. Km) under Delhi Mumbai Industrial Corridor (DMIC) has been setup in 2019  in the State of Maharashtra. The total investment in Shendra Industrial Area in the last three years is Rs. 2,801 crore and the total Foreign Investment in the year 2020 is Rs.620 Crores.********
