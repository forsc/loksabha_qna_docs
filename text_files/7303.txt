                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

998






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Setting up of AYUSH Hospital




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao













                                                    Abdul Majeed Ariff
                                                








                                                    Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)  be pleased to state: (a) the total number of AYUSH hospitals running across the country at present, State/UT-wise; (b) whether the Government is working on the plan to set up AYUSH hospital in each district of the country, particularly in backward areas and if so, the details thereof, State/UT and location-wise; (c) the total fund proposed to be spent for each district for the purpose; (d) the estimated number of patients being treated each day at various AYUSH hospitals at present; and (e) the other measures being taken by the Government to promote AYUSH in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):   State/UT wise the total number of AYUSH hospitals running across the country is furnished at Annexure-I. (b) & (c):  As health is a State subject, setting up of AYUSH Hospital in each district of the country particularly in the backward areas come under purview of respective State/UT Governments. However, under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission(NAM) there is a provision for providing Grant-in-aid to set up upto 50-bedded integrated AYUSH Hospital. Under NAM, grant-in-aid is being provided to State/UT Governments as per their proposed State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs). Since, implementation of the programme comes under the purview of State/UT Governments, therefore Grant-in-aid has been provided to the States/UTs for setting up of 50 bedded AYUSH Hospitals as per the proposal submitted by them in their SAAP. The State/UT wise list of assisted 50 bedded integrated AYUSH Hospitals is furnished at Annexure-II(d):  As health is a State subject, the information regarding estimated number of patients being treated each day at various AYUSH hospitals is not maintained by the central Government. However, as per the information received from State/UT Government total number of 19.58 Lakhs IPD patients and 1773.75 lakhs OPD patients have reported State/UT Government health care facilities under AYUSH during 2017-18.(e):   The Central Government has launched Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) for overall promotion of AYUSH including Ayurvedic treatment in the country. The Mission envisages better access to AYUSH services including Ayurveda, strengthening of AYUSH educational institutions, enforcement of quality control of Ayurveda, Siddha and Unani & Homoeopathy (ASU &H) drugs and promotion of medicinal plants for sustainable availability of raw-materials for ASU & H drugs in the States/UTs. Further, the Ministry has taken up initiatives for propagation and promotion of AYUSH systems of medicine including Ayurveda by organizing Arogya Fairs/Melas, Conferences, Exhibitions, Seminars, Workshops, Symposium and also publicity through electronic and print media. The Ministry is also observing important days dedicated to AYUSH Systems of Medicine viz. International Day of Yoga, Ayurveda Day, Unani Day, Siddha Day, Naturopathy Day and Homoeopathy Day etc. to create awareness about the benefits of AYUSH systems of medicine.
