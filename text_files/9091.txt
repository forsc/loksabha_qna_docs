                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2632






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






State Cadre Officers on Deputation




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of officers of State cadre, i.e., Officers selected by the State Public Commission deputed in Government of India services; and (b) 	the rules for deputation of State level officers selected by the respective State Public Service Commission (non-IAS) for deputation at Government of India offices or Ministries or as personal staff of Ministers?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE(DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a):   No centralized data /details of officers of State cadre / officers selected by State Public Service Commission on deputation in Government of India is maintained.(b):    The appointments, including those of State Government officers, on deputation basis to the posts in Central Government and in the personal staff of Ministers are governed by the Service Rules/ Recruitment Rules of the respective posts and other relevant rules and instructions issued from time to time. The selection of individuals appointed on co-terminus basis in the personal staff of Ministers are made at the discretion and pleasure of the Minister concerned.*****
