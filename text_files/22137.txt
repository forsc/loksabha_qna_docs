                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3450






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Vacancies in ICAR




Parvatagouda Chandanagouda Gaddigoudar













                                                    Ravneet Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that over 1,000 posts of scientists and over 2,100 technical posts are lying vacant in Indian Council for Agricultural Research (ICAR); (b)	if so, the details thereof including the complete information of sanctioned posts and actual strength in various grade of scientists and other technical posts in ICAR; (c)	whether it is a fact that the shortfall of technical personnel in ICAR can have serious repercussions on the research activities of the Institute; (d)	the extent to which the shortfall has adversely affected the research activities of ICAR; (e)	whether any steps are being taken by the Government to enable the early filling up of the vacant posts in ICAR; and (f)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Out of the total sanctioned strength of 6586 positions in scientific cadre in ICAR, 1504 are vacant.  Likewise, out of the total sanctioned strength of 6756 in case of technical cadre 2311 are vacant.  Cadre and category wise details are as under:-Name of the Post	Sanctioned Posts	In position	VacancyCadre Wise details of vacancy position of ScientistsScientist	4451	3776	675Sr. Scientist	1295	889	406Principal Scientist (Heads, Project Coordinators)	665	323	342Research Management Positions	175	94	81Total	6586	5082	1504Category wise details of vacancy position of Technical staffCategory I	3699	2512	1187Category II	2555	1689	866Category III	502 	244	258Total	6756	4445	2311(c) & (d):	ICAR has taken efforts to ensure that the mandated and prioritized research activities of the Institutes do not suffer.  Effective steps including optimal deployment of human resources are taken in ICAR to prioritize the research activities to address the mandates of the institutions and the national priorities.	(e) & (f):	The recruitment of scientific and technical positions is a continuous process and the vacancies are filled up through an established procedure by the Agricultural Scientist Recruitment Board (ASRB) or the Institute subject to availability of qualified candidates.  The vacancy position is monitored regularly within ICAR and with ASRB towards timely selection and deployment of human resource.	*******
