                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

497






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






AYUSH Medicines for Geriatric Population




M V V Satyanarayana













                                                    Goddeti Madhavi
                                                








                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil
                                                








                                                    Maddila  Gurumoorthy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry has taken any steps to improve health of the geriatric population of the country following the pandemic;(b) if so, details thereof; and (c) the number of people of 60 years and above age across the country to whom AYUSH prophylactic medicines have been distributed till date under ‘‘Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav’’ campaign, State-wise including Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL) (DR. MAHENDRABHAI MUNJAPARA)(DR. MAHENDRABHAI MUNJAPARA)(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. The Ministry has taken various steps to improve health of the general public including geriatric population of the country following the pandemic.	Prophylactic medicines for prevention of COVID-19 from Ayush systems including Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani and Homoeopathy were distributed free of cost to general public including geriatric population. Initiatives/Actions taken by Ministry of Ayush for management of COVID-19 pandemic are at Annexure-I.( c ) State/UT-wise number of people including 60 years and above age across the country to whom AYUSH prophylactic medicines have been distributed till 01st February, 2022 under ‘‘Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav’’ campaign by Ministry of Ayush is attached as Annexure-II. ***********
