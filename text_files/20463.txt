                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1967






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






TV Transmitters




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the number of TV transmitters set up and operational in the country particularly in backward and rural areas as on date; (b) whether several projects are lying pending in the absence of financial and technical clearance; (c) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise particularly in backward and rural areas; (d) the time by which the said pending projects are likely to be sanctioned; and (e) the time by which construction work is likely to be started therein, State/UT-wise and location-wise






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) 			Presently, 589 TV transmitters of varying capacities (High power and low power transmitters) are functioning in various parts of country, including backward & rural areas of the country.  (b) to (e)	     No project is pending currently for want of financial and technical sanction.******
