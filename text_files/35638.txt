                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2913






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Visits of World Bank Team




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the names of the States in which the villages have been visited by the World Bank Team during the last three years and the current year;b)	the objective of the said visits of the World Bank Team;c)	the names of the World bank aided project sites visited by the said team; and d)	the reaction of the World Bank team given to implementation of the said project?






ANSWER




                                                                                 MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE                                   (SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) to (d): The World Bank does not maintain a centralized record of their teams’ visits to various project sites, including the objectives / purpose of their visit and findings regarding the implementation of project. However, at the end of each mission conducted by the World Bank virtually / physically, an aide memoire is issued by the World Bank Team which reflects findings, recommendations and agreements reached including details of site visit. Further, report of project implementation status is prepared by the World Bank team and posted on the relevant page of World Bank’s project website. This report is publicly available at World Bank’s project website at https://projects.worldbank.org. Since March 2020, all missions of World Bank Team including the sites visits were suspended due to COVID-19 pandemic.
