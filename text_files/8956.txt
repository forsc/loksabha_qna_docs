                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2357






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






PMAY-G in Telangana




Manne Srinivas Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of RURAL DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state the total number of beneficiaries under Pradhan Mantri AwasYojana – (Gramin) (PMAY-G) in the State of Telangana as on date and with special focus on Mahabubnagar district?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)The State of Telangana had been allocated total target of 70,674 houses for the financial years 2016-17 and 2017-18 under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G). An amount of Rs.19078.865 lakhs were also released as 1st instalment against the targets of 2016-17 for implementation of the scheme. However, the State of Telangana including Mahabubnagar district has not started implementation of PMAY-G in the State and shown “NIL” progress till date on AwaasSoft, the MIS of the Scheme.
