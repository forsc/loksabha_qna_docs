                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2268






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Beautification of Ancient Temples




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal for the repair, maintenance and beautification of the old and ancient temples in different States;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(c)     whether any central assistance has been sanctioned and spent for the purpose during the last three years; and(d)  if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)  & (b)	Conservation, preservation and environmental development work of protected monuments in different States of the country, including ancient temples, is a continuous process and the same is attended regularly as per the availability of resources and requirements of different sites.(c)  & (d)	Yes Sir.  The budget allocated and expenditure incurred for conservation, maintenance and development works at the protected monuments, during the last three years is given as under:                                                                                                                                     Rs. in LakhsS. No.	Year	Allocation	Expenditure Incurred1.	2019-20	43561.18	43539.262.	2018-19	40637.86	40502.013.	2017-18	41080.32	41076.79*******
