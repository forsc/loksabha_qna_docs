                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1699






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Construction of Roads under PMGSY




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of roads constructed under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in Assam;(b)	the names of villages covered and the places where roads have been constructed under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana in Kokrajhar Parliamentary Constituency;(c)	the number of roads identified under the said scheme during the last three years and the current year, district-wise; and(d)	the details of the projects identified under the said scheme, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Since inception of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), 8479 road works have been sanctioned  for Assam and the State has reported to complete5850 road works till date.(b):	PMGSY is a one-time special intervention to provide rural connectivity, by way of a single all-weather road to eligible unconnected habitations in the core network. Under PMGSY, the unit of programme is a habitation and not a village. The details of habitations covered and the places where roads have been constructed under PMGSY in Kokrajhar Parliamentary Constituency are available at www.omms.nic.in -> Progress Monitoring -> Physical progress of works.(c):	District-wise details of roads sanctioned under PMGSY during the last three years for the State of Assam are at Annexure-I (a to c). During the current financial year, no project has been sanctioned for the State of Assam. (d):	District-wise details of projects sanctioned under PMGSY since inception for the State of Assam are at Annexure-II.*****
