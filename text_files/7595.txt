                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1235






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Establishment of Cultural Centre in Ladakh




Jamyang Tsering Namgyal














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any plans or has taken any steps to establish a Cultural Centre in Ladakh as announced by Hon’ble Prime Minister during his visit in Ladakh in 2018; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	if not, the time by which the Government is likely to do so; and (d)	the steps taken/being taken for preservation and promotion of Ladakhi Culture for organizing cultural programmes in the newly declared Union Territory?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c)	To protect, promote and preserve the various forms of folk art and culture throughout the country, the Government has already set up seven Zonal Cultural Centres (ZCCs) with headquarters at Patiala, Nagpur, Udaipur, Prayagraj, Kolkata, Dimapur and Thanjavur. These ZCCs organize various cultural activities and programmes on regular basis all over India. The erstwhile State of Jammu & Kashmir (which includes the newly created Union Territory of Jammu & Kashmir and Union Territory of Ladakh) is a member State of North Zone Cultural Centre (NZCC), Patiala. If UT of Ladakh provides appropriate land, the proposal can be considered as per the policy.   (d)	Folk artists of Union Territory of Ladakh are invited by these ZCCs to showcase the rich cultural heritage of Ladakhi Culture in the cultural programmes/festivals organized by them all over India. 	Besides, these folk artists of Ladakh are also invited to perform during major festivals like Rashtriya Sanskriti Mahotsavs (RSMs) organized at national level.
