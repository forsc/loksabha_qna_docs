                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2957






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Regional Rapid Transit System




Raj Hans Hans














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has finalized any proposal for connecting Delhi-Sonepat-Panipat under Regional Rapid Transport System (RRTS);(b) if so, the details thereof together with the financial allocations made there under; and(c) the time by which the project is likely to be commenced and completed?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                        (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a):	No, Sir.(b) & (c):	Do not arise.                     *****
