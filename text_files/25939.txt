                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1763






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Water Supply in Coastal Areas




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the steps taken by the Union Government to ensure safe drinking water supply in coastal areas across the country; (b) whether there is any proposal to set up more desalination plants in the coastal areas to achieve the above target and if so, the details thereof; (c) the total number of desalination plants set up and functioning in the coastal areas across the country as on date, State-wise; and (d) the details of the funds utilised for setting up desalination plants during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) Government of India in partnership with States/ UTs, is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM)–Har Ghar Jal to make provision of potable tap water supply to every rural household in the country including in coastal areas, by 2024.(b) to (d) Drinking water supply is a State subject and powers to plan, approve, implement, operate and maintain rural water supply schemes are vested with States, inter alia which includes setting up of desalination plants. As such, details of desalination plants set up by States, are not maintained by this Ministry. *****
