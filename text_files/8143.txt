                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1827






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Implementation of various Schemes in Telangana




Nama Nageswara Rao














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the amount which has been released for implementation of various schemes under different municipalities of Telangana during the last five years;(b)	the details of developmental projects being taken up in the municipalities of 	Telangana;(c)	the allocations that have been made as per the recommendations of the 14th	Finance Commission;(d)	whether any review has been undertaken to know the functioning of urban local bodies in different metros, in general, and of Hyderabad, in particular, during the last two years; and(e)	if so, the details thereof and if not,the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	& (b): The Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs(MoHUA) facilitates and assists States/Union Territories (UTs) including Telangana, through its flagship Missions/Schemes  - Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban(SBM-U), Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation      (AMRUT),   Smart   Cities   Mission   (SCM),    and Pradhan      Mantri     Awas    Yojana    (Urban) (PMAY-U), Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana-National Urban Livelihoods Mission(DAY-NULM) and Heritage City Development and Augmentation Yojana(HRIDAY) to improve urban infrastructure and enhance  the quality of life in cities and towns covered under these Missions/Schemes. The funds released for implementation of these Schemes to Telangana and details of development projects taken up are given in the Annex.(c): The details of the allocations that have been made as per the recommendations of the 14th Central Finance Commission for the municipalities of Telangana are as under;                                   Rs. in croreYear	       Basic Grant	Performance Grant2015-16    	325.23	        02016-17	        450.33	        132.912017-18	        520.32	        150.412018-19	        601.92	        170.812019-20	        813.32	        223.66(d) & (e): The MoHUA reviews the progress of the implementation of the projects, regularly through progress reports, review meetings, video-conferencing with the officers of State Government of Telengana/ Urban Local Bodies to assess the progress and address any issues being faced in the implementation process.                                *****
