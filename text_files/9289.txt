                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2852






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Shelter Homes for Urban Homeless




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of schemes under which the Government supplements efforts of the States to provide shelter homes for urban homeless;(b)	the details of shelter homes that the Government has provided to the State of Telangana during the last five years and the current year, year-wise and district-wise;(c)	whether no allocation has been made to Telangana in the current year; and(d)	if so, the reasons therefor and the number of shelter homes that so far have been constructed and have become operational during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):	It is the primary responsibility of respective Governments of the States/Union Territories to provide shelters for the urban homeless. However, in order to complement and supplement their efforts, Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs is administering scheme of ‘Shelter for Urban Homeless (SUH)’under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM), through respective States/UTs. It focuses on providing permanent shelters equipped with basic facilities to the urban homeless.(b) to (d):	The State level Project Sanctioning Committee (PSC)is competent to approve proposals for establishment of shelters. As reported by State Government of Telangana, the details are as under:Year	             Shelters	  Sanctioned by PSC	Functional2014-15	      42	         172015-16	       2	          32016-17	       3	          12017-18	       0	          42018-19	       8	          12019-20	       -	          2	DAY-NULM is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under which, for Telangana, 60% of funds are provided by the central government in a consolidated manner for the Mission and remaining by the state government.In 2019-20, an amount of Rs. 31.60 crore has been released under DAY-NULM to Telangana.It is the responsibility of the state to apportion appropriate amount for the shelters based on the local requirement.	For the year, as reported by State Government, a target has been fixed for construction/refurbishment of 10 shelters.
