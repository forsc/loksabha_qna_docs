                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2981






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






ESI Hospitals in Jharkhand




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the total number of beds available in the Employees‘ State Insurance (ESI) hospitals situated in Jharkhand expecially Santhal Pargana of Jharkhand;(b)the sanctioned strength and also the actual strength of staff in the said hospitals;(c)the total amount provided for infrastructure development of the said ESI hospitals during the last three financial years and the current year; and (d)the total number of labourers who got treatment in the said hospitals during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b):	 There are 210 beds in the Employees’ State Insurance (ESI) hospitals at Adityapur, Ranchi and Maithan in the state of Jharkhand.  ESI has no hospital in Santhal Pargana of Jharkhand.The sanctioned and filled up strength of the staff in these hospitals are given below:-Contd..2/-:: 2 ::Name of Hospital of Jharkhand	Sanctioned Strength	Actual StrengthESI Corporation (ESIC) Hospital, Ranchi	142	141ESIC Hospital, Adityapur	141	110ESI Scheme (ESIS) Hospital, Maithan	118	13(c):	 	The total amount provided for infrastructure development of the ESI hospitals in the state of Jharkhand during financial years 2018-19, 2019-20 & 2020-21 were Rs.25.59 crore, Rs.39.71 crore and Rs.29.89 crore respectively and for the current year (upto January, 22) the amount is Rs.8.60 crore.  (d):	 	The number of ESI beneficiaries who got treatment in these hospitals during the last three years i.e. 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 are 146075, 139167 and 131771 respectively.********
