                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2220






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






ESIC Hospitals




Rajiv Ranjan (Lalan) Singh













                                                    Kaushalendra Kumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the number of hospitals of Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) set up in the country including State-wise locations thereof;(b)whether the treatment of patients is taking place there in a proper manner;(c)the State-wise number of hospitals of ESIC constructed in the country during the last five years including present status thereof;(d)the number of hospitals of ESIC set up in Bihar including present status thereof; (e)whether the Government is aware of the status of ESIC hospital set up in Bihar Sharif city of Nalanda district which lacks state-of-the-art facilities; and(f)if so, the details thereof and the efforts being made by the Government to equip Bihar Sharif located ESIC hospital with state-of-the art facilities?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b):	The Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) has set up 49 ESIC hospitals in the country. The state-wise details of these hospitals are placed at Annexure-I.Contd..2/-:: 2 ::ESIC hospitals provide primary and secondary care medical facilities viz. out patient care/ inpatient care, drugs, injections and other specialized care in most of the specialities, including facilities under AYUSH. Further, tertiary medical care/Super Specialty Treatment (SST) services are provided in select ESIC hospitals.(c):	The details of the hospitals constructed by the ESIC during the last five years including the present status are placed at Annexure-II.(d):	ESIC has set up two hospitals at Phulwarisharif (Patna) and Bihta in the State of Bihar. These hospitals provide primary and secondary care services in most of the specialties. (e):	No ESIC Hospital has been set up in Bihar Sharif in the Nalanda district of Bihar.(f):	Does not arise in view of (e) above.**                                         ******
