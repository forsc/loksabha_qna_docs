                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

424






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Cotton Cultivation




Andimuthu Raja














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that the production of cotton has increased compared to last year across the country, especially in Tamil Nadu; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has increased the rate of cotton in the present year, if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	the details of the steps taken by the Government to promote the cultivation of cotton?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) & (b):	Yes, Sir.  The production of cotton has increased in 2019-20 as compared to 2018-19 in the country. The production of cotton in the country including Tamil Nadu during last 2 years is as below:                           (Production: in lakh bales of 170 kgs each)State	2018-19	2019-20*Tamil Nadu	2.69	4.12All India	280.42	354.91    *4th Advance EstimatesContd….2/--2-(c):	 The Government of India has enhanced Minimum Support Price (MSP) of cotton for 2020-21 as under:                                   (Rs. Per Quintal)Variety	2019-20	2020-21Medium Staple	5255	5515Long Staple	5550	5825(d):	Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare is implementing Cotton Development Programme under National Food Security Mission (NFSM) in 15 major cotton growing states including Tamil Nadu from 2014-15 with the aim to increase production and productivity. The Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare has approved Annual Action Plan of the States/agencies for 2020-21. Besides, Government of Tamil Nadu is also implementing Tamil Nadu Cotton Cultivation Mission from 2014-15 to increase production & productivity of cotton in the state. *********
