                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Contingency Plan under Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Rules 19




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1012 TO BE ANSWERED ON 08.02.2022CONTINGENCY PLAN UNDER SCHEDULED CASTES AND THE SCHEDULED TRIBES (PREVENTION OF ATROCITIES) RULES 19951012. SHRI NABA KUMAR SARANIA: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the number and name of States that have prepared the Contingency Plan as per Rule 15(1) of the Scheduled Cases and the Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Rules 1995; (b) the name of the States implementing the contingency plan, State-wise along with the details of the package of relief and rehabilitation measures undertaken and plan of implementation including budget allocation in the year 2021-2022; and (c) the reasons for not preparing Contingency Plan by the rest of the States?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RAMDAS ATHAWALE)(a) to (c): Rule 15 (1) of the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Rules, 1995 specifies that the State Government shall frame and implement a plan to effectively implement the provisions of the Act and notify the same in the Official Gazette of the State Government.        State Govt. of Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Goa, Gujarat, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Odisha, Punjab, Tamil Nadu have prepared contingency plan.       The Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act 1989 and the Rules thereon are primarily implemented by the State Govts./UT Administrations.  For effective implementation of the provisions of the Protection of Civil Right Act, 1955 and the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act, 1989 & the Rules thereon, Central Assistance is released under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme to the State Governments/UT Administrations. The Central Government advises the State Governments/UT Administrations to implement the provisions of the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act, 1989 and Rules in letter and spirit.  The State-wise Central assistance released inclusive for the relief and rehabilitation of the atrocity victims under the scheme in the year 2021-22 is attached as Annexure. *****Annexure Lok  Sabha Unstarred Question No. 1012 for 08.02.2022 Central Assistance released under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme for implementation of the Protection of Civil Rights Act, 1955 and the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act, 1989 during the Financial Year 2021-22.
