                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






World Bank Assistance for Agricultural Sector




B. Y. Raghavendra














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the World Bank assistance provided for the agricultural sector during each of the last three years;(b)	whether the agricultural sector is not getting the entire benefit of the said assistance;(c)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(d)	whether the Government has conducted any inquiry into under-utilisation of the World Bank assistance in the agricultural sector;(e)	if so, the details and the outcome thereof; and (f)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                                                    MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE                                   (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (f): The World Bank assisted projects being implemented at the national and/or the state level in various sectors including agriculture sector, have twin objectives of reducing poverty and building shared prosperity. The loan assistance from the World Bank is provided to the concerned Central Ministry / Department or the State Government based on the demands posed by them. All sectors including agriculture sector get the intended benefits of the above loan assistance. The details of World Bank assisted projects implemented in agriculture sector for last three years i.e. from 2016-17 to 2018-19 (as on 31.03.2019) is at annexure. The assistance provided by World Bank for agriculture sector is utilised for development of agriculture and allied activities, which helps in increasing farm productivity and family incomes along with creating infrastructure, and improving physical access to market and social welfare services. The project monitoring and concurrent evaluation is carried out periodically, however, the final development/economic impacts of the each project are assessed after completion of the project in form of ‘Project Completion Report’.                                                *********                                                                                             Annexure Sl.
