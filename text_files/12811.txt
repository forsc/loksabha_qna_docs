                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1777






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Strategic Oil Storage Capacity




Darshana Vikram Jardosh














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the progress made till date in the construction of strategic oil storage capacity in Rajkot of Gujarat;(b) the time by which it is likely to be completed;(c) whether the Government of Gujarat has been or is being contacted in this regard;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor for not taking action in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (e): Under Phase II of Strategic Petroleum Reserve (SPR) programme, the Government has given “In Principle” approval in June 2018 for establishing 6.5 MMT Strategic Petroleum Reserves only at two locations namely Chandikhol in Odisha and Padur in Karnataka. The location of Rajkot has not been approved.*******
