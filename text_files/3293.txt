                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3011






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Mode of Payment at Toll Plazas




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the  steps  taken/  being  taken  by the  Government  to  promote  payment  of toll through 	cashless mode at all the toll plazas  at  National  Highways  across  the country;(b)  whether  the  Government  is satisfied with these steps or contemplating any other new scheme in 	this regard; and(c)   if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) In order to facilitate smooth movement of traffic and reduce congestion on fee plazas, the Government has rolled out a cashless payment mechanism i.e. FASTag by implementation of Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) based Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) system on National Highways. At present, it is operable at 457 fee plazas on National Highways.  To make availability widespread and maximizing existing vehicles to affix FASTags, as per Notification No. 1361(E) dated 02.11.2017, all vehicles under category M & N, sold after 1st of December 2017 are required to be fitted with FASTag by the manufacturer of the vehicle or its authorized dealer. As per Notification no. 1081(E) dated 02.11.2018, Government has made it mandatory to affix FASTag for issuance of the National Permit (N/P) from 1st Oct 2019. Further, bank neutral FASTag “NHAI FASTag” have been launched for easy availability at petrol-pump and on e-commerce site Amazon.*****
