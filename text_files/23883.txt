                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4888






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Availability of Houses for Poor




Ajay (Teni) Misra














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to make easy availability of 	houses to the poor and all the people belong to middle class in the 	country; and(b) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise/district-wise and if not, the 	reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                      (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	Government of India has launched Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) on 25thJune, 2015 for providing assistance to States/ Union Territories (UTs) for addressing the housing requirement of all eligible beneficiaries especially for urban poor and also for those belonging to Middle Income Group. The Mission comprises four components viz. In-Situ Slum Redevelopment (ISSR), Affordable Housing in Partnership (AHP), Beneficiary Led Construction (BLC) and Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS).ISSR, AHP and BLC components under PMAY (U) are Centrally Sponsored Schemes that are implemented through the States/UTs  and their agencies while the CLSS component of PMAY (U) is a Central Sector Scheme.Under ISSR component, the Central assistance is ?1 lakh per house while for the AHP and BLC components, the Central assistance is?1.5 lakh per house. Under CLSS component of the PMAY(U), there is an interest subsidy of 6.5% for Economically Weaker Section (EWS)/ Lower Income Group (LIG), 4% for Middle Income Group-I (MIG-I) and 3% for Middle Income Group-II (MIG-II), calculated on housing loan up to ?6 lakh, ?9 lakh and ?12 lakh respectively, over a tenure of 20 years.(b)	The State-wise/district-wise details of houses sanctioned under PMAY-U is at Annexure – I.                                 *****
