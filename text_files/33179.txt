                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

61






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Establishment of Central University at Anantapur




M V V Satyanarayana













                                                    Talari Rangaiah
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has approved in principal a proposal for setting up a Central 	University at Anantapur, Andhra Pradesh and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the details of Government approved budget and Detailed Project Report (DPR) for 	establishment of the said Central University;(c) 	whether the Government has set any target time and road map for sanctioning the 	approved budget to complete the establishment of the said University within the 	stipulated time; and(d)	 if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d) A statement is laid on the table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) TO (D) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 61 TO BE ANSWERED ON 07.02.2022 ASKED BY SHRI M.V.V. SATYANARAYANA, HON’BLE MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT AND SHRI TALARI RANGAIAH, HON’BLE MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT REGARDING ESTABLISHMENT OF CENTRAL UNIVERSITY AT ANANTAPUR(a)	The Thirteenth Schedule to the Andhra Pradesh Reorganisation Act, 2014, inter alia, provides that the Government of India shall take steps to establish Central University in the State of Andhra Pradesh.  Accordingly, Central Universities (Amendment) Act 2019, for establishment of Central University of Andhra Pradesh (CUAP) with territorial jurisdiction to the whole of the State has been passed by the Parliament and assented to by the Hon’ble President of India. The provisions of the said Act have come in force w.e.f. 05.08.2019. The University has started functioning from the academic year 2018-19. (b) to (d) On the basis of Detailed Project Report (DPR) for establishment of new campus, Government of India has sanctioned Rs.450.00 crore. Ministry releases funds as per the demand/requirement of the University. An amount of Rs.8.00 crore, Rs.4.80 crore and Rs.18.44 crore has been released to CUAP during the year 2018-19, 2020-21 and 2021-22 (As on 31.01.2021) respectively.*****
