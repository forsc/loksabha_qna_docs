                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2817






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Solar Cities




Y. S. Avinash Reddy













                                                    Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether  the Union Government has initiated development of solar cities in the country and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the details of the cities identified for implementation of the scheme in each State, district-wise;(c)	the quantum of funds sanctioned and released during this financial year;(d)	the details of the action plan formulated to turn the present cities into solar cities;(e)	whether the state  Governments have been asked to provide a detailed report for implementation of this scheme; and(f)	if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) & (b)  The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy under its scheme, ‘Development of Solar Cities’, has sanctioned 60 cities including 13 Pilot and 5 Model Solar Cities. The details are given in Annexure-I.(c)  During current financial year a sum of Rs.21,81,462/- has been released as committed liability.  (d)  to  (f) The scheme for ‘Development of Solar Cities’ was not continued beyond the 12th five year plan ( 2012-2017).*****
