                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

290






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Development of Jabalpur-Hyderabad Road as NH




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any plan to develop Jabalpur-Kabirdham-Rajanandgaon-Manpur-Chandrapur Hyderabad road as National Highway;(b) it so, the details and the present status thereof; and(c) the time by which the project is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (c ) Jabalpur -Kabirdham Section is part of  National Highway (NH)- 30 in the state of Madhya Pradesh  and Chhattisgarh and Manpur - Chandrapur section is part of NH-930 in the State of Maharashtra. The present status of ongoing NH projects alongwith their likely date of completion  of   Jabalpur –Kabirdham section of NH-30 in Madhya Pradesh & Chhattisgarh and Manpur –Chandrapur section of NH-930 in the State of Maharashtra are annexed.  The other sections of Jabalpur-Kabirdham-Rajanandgaon-Manpur-Chandrapur Hyderabad road are not part of NHs and  policy for declaration of NHs are under finalization within the Government.
