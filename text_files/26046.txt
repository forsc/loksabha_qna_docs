                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1701






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Setting up of Energy Plants




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether  the  Government  proposes  to  set  up some  energy  plants  through  the  new  and  renewable energy  sources  in  the  State  of  Odisha  and  if  so,  the details thereof;(b) whether the projects are pending for implementation and if so, the details thereof;(c)  whether  any  of  these  projects  have  been approved by the Government; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) to (d) A total of 549.57 MW of renewable energy capacity (excluding large Hydro) has been installed in the State of  Odisha till 30-6-2021 which includes 401.72 MW from solar power, 88.63 MW from small hydro power and 59.22 MW from bio-power. Several measures have been taken by the Ministry for the promotion of renewable energy projects  in the state of Odisha. The details are as follow:1.	Ministry has approved three parks under the solar parks scheme. The details of these parks are as follows:(i)	100 MW NHPC Solar Park-I(ii)	40 MW NHPC Solar Park-II(iii)	100 MW Floating Solar Park2.	Ministry has sanctioned a scheme for “Installation of grid connected solar project & various off-grid applications for 100% Solarization of Konark Sun Temple & Konark Town” on 19.05 2020. 3.	Under Off-grid & Decentralized Solar PV Applications Programme Phase-III, 1 MW capacity of off-grid solar power plants is under implementation in the State of Odisha, out of which 0.9 MW has been installed upto 30.06.2021. 4.	Under Component-A of PM-KUSUM Scheme for installation of grid-connected solar power projects, 500 MW capacity has been sanctioned to Odisha Renewable Energy Development Agency (OREDA). Further, under Component-B of the Scheme, cumulative quantity of 5700 nos. of standalone solar pumps has been sanctioned to OREDA, out of which 203 nos. of pumps have been installed upto 30.06.2021.5.	The Ministry, under phase II of the solar rooftop programme, has allocated CFA for aggregate 4 MW capacity to 4 DISCOMs of Odisha State for implementation of grid connected rooftop solar projects in the residential sector with the following breakup:Sr. no	Name of DISCOM	Allocated capacity1	TP Central Odisha Distribution Limited (TPCODL) formerly CESU	1MW2	TP Southern Odisha Distribution Limited (TPSODL) formerly  SOUTHCO	1MW3	TP Western Odisha Distribution Limited (TPWODL)  formerly WESCO	1MW4	TP Northern Odisha Distribution Limited (TPNODL) formerly NESCO)	1MWTotal	4 MW*****
