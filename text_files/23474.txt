                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4493






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Utilisation of Funds by Tamil Nadu




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of RURAL DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state the allocation and utilisation of funds under different schemes of the Ministry in Tamil Nadu, during the last three years, year-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)The scheme-wise allocation and utilization of funds under the various schemes of the Ministry of Rural Development during the last three years in the State of Tamil Nadu are given below:i.	Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS)  Mahatma Gandhi NREGS being a demand driven programme, no State/UT-wise (including of Tamil Nadu) financial allocation is made.
