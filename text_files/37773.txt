                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4981






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Orphanages




Devendra (Alias) Bhole Singh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the number of orphanages in the country and the details of funds released to the said orphanages during the last three years, State-wise including Uttar Pradesh;(b)	whether the Government has received any suggestions regarding the condition of the said orphanages and the persons residing in them; and(c)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken so far in this regard? 






ANSWER




(a) to (c) :  State-wise number of Child Care Institutions and funds released during the last three years including Uttar Pradesh under Child Protection Services Scheme – Mission Vatsalya are at Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively.  As informed by the National Commission for Protection of Child Rights (NCPCR), in compliance to the order of the Hon’ble Supreme Court in WP (Crl) 102 of 2007 in Re-Exploitation of children in orphanages in the State of Tamil Nadu, NCPCR has conducted social audit of 7163 Child Care Institutions including Uttar Pradesh and submitted the report to Hon’ble Supreme Court on 06.03.2020.
