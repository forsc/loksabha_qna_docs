                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3211






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Displacement due to Kaleshwaram Project




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the number of people displaced by the Kaleshwaram Irrigation project, district-wise; (b) the details of the steps taken by the Government for the rehabilitation of affected people; (c) whether the Government is aware of the forceful eviction of the affected people; and (d) if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU)(a) Kaleshwaram Irrigation Project (KIP) is being implemented by Government of Telangana by its own resources. As intimated by the Government of Telangana (GoT), details of displaced people district-wise, by the Kaleshwaram Irrigation project, are as under:  Name of the Reservoir	Name of the District	No of Persons displacedKonda Pochamma Sagar Reservoir	Siddipet	3,772Sri Komaravelli Mallanna Sagar Reservoir	Siddipet	14,359Ananthagiri Reservoir	Siddipet	368	Rajanna Sircilla	2,379Total	20,878 (b)  Details of Rehabilitation & Resettlement (R & R) centres created for rehabilitation of affected people in the Kaleshwaram Irrigation Project, are given below: Contd…P/2 Name of the Reservoir	Name of the Village	Name of R&R CentreAnanthagiri Reservoir	Ananthagiri (V), Ellanthakunta (M),Rajanna Sircilla (Dist)	Ananthagiri (V)Ellanthakunta (M), &Thagallapally (V&M) ofRajanna Siricilla District	Kochaguttapally (V) & Chelkalapally (V) of Chinnakodur (M),Siddipet (Dist)	Ranganayaka Puram R&R colony, Lingareddy Pally (V) of Siddipet Rural (M) Siddipet District	Allipur (V) of Chinnakodur (M),Siddipet (Dist)	Allipur (V) Chinnakodur (M) Siddipet DistrictSri Komaravelli Mallanna Sagar	Thoguta (Part), Etigaddakistapur (V), Vemulaghat (V), & Pallepahad (V) of Thoguta(M),Siddipet (Dist)	Integrated Township across 600 Acres with all urban infrastructure facilities in Muthrajpally and Sangapur villages of Gajwel Mandal of Siddipet District.	Singaram(V), Yerravally(V) of Kondapaka (M), Siddipet (Dist)	Konda Pochamma Sagar Reservoir	Mamidyala (V), Bahilampur (V), &Thanderpally (V) of Mulugu (M), Siddipet (Dist)	Thunikibollaram (V), Mulugu (M), Siddipet (Dist) Note- (V)- village; (M)- Mandal; (Dist)- District(c) & (d)  Government of Telangana has informed that there is no forceful eviction of people affected by the project.*****
