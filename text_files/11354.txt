                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

448






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Social Audit of PMAY-G




Manne Srinivas Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has brought out the Social Audit guidelines for National Social Assistance Programme & Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana–Gramin (PMAY-G);(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government has released a report on the status of Social Audits in India; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d): The Ministry of Rural Development has released the Social Audit Guidelines for Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) and National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP) on 13th November, 2019. The basic objective of Social Audit is to ensure public accountability in implementation of the schemes. The Social Audit Units (SAU), set up by the State/UT Governments under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA), are to be utilized to facilitate conduct of Social Audit of both the schemes. No report on status of Social Audit in respect of PMAY-G and NSAP has been released by the States/UTs. **********
