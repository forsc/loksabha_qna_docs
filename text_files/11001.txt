                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

165






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






CSR Spending




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of companies spending funds under the Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) head during last two years till date in the State of Jharkhand and the total number and names of those districts where the amount was spent under CSR head, year-wise; and (b)	the details of total amount spent by companies in CSR head for environment protection and sustainable development in the State of Jharkhand during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCEAND CORPORATE AFFAIRS                                         (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)????? ??? ???????? ?????  ???????? ??? ????? ??????                               (???? ?????? ???? ?????) (a) & (b):   CSR is a board driven process.  The Board of the company is free to decide the activities to be undertaken as per Schedule VII of the Companies Act, 2013 taking into consideration the recommendation of its CSR committee. Ministry issued a Circular no. 21/2014 dated 18th June 2014 which provided that items listed in Schedule VII are broad based and be interpreted liberally. Item no. (iv) of Schedule VII relates to environment sustainability. The entire CSR architecture is disclosure based and CSR mandated companies are required to file details of its CSR annually in the MCA 21 registry. All data reported in the MCA21 registry by companies on CSR is available on National CSR Data Portal at www.csr.gov.in.  The details of total amount spent by companies in CSR head for environment protection in the State of Jharkhand for financial years 2016-17 & 2017-18 are given below:Major Development Sector head	FY 2016-17	FY 2017-18 2017-18Environment, Animal Welfare,  Conservation of Resources  (in Rs. Crores)	4.62	2.83      (Data upto 30.06.2019) [Source: National CSR Data Portal]****
