                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1340






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Bhartiya Shiksha Board




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government aims to establish a Bhartiya Shiksha Board;(b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons for its creation; and(c) the timeline by which it is likely to start functioning?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c) : Bhartiya Shiksha Board is being established under MSRVVP which is an autonomous body.  The board is being established for standardization, management, affiliation, recognition, authentication of education in the Indian traditional knowledge such as Vedic education, Sanskrit education, Shastras & Darshanas education, Bhartiya Art education which can be provided with or without modern education in conformity with national curriculum framework upto Senior Secondary level.  ******
