                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1145






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Upcoming Census




K. Muraleedharan













                                                    Adoor Prakash
                                                








                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                








                                                    Rajmohan Unnithan
                                                








                                                    T.N. Prathapan
                                                








                                                    Kumbakudi Sudhakaran
                                                








                                                    Anto  Antony
                                                








                                                    A. Chellakumar
                                                








                                                    S. Jothimani
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has framed an alternate timeline for the upcoming census;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) the number of census officer posts that have been filled since requisition orders were sent out and the targeted number of posts to be filled, as on date;(d) whether the Government plans to supplement the National Sample Survey Office (NSSO) and its surveys without the framework provided by the census; and(e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b): 	Due to outbreak of COVID-19 pandemic, the Census 2021 and related field activities have been postponed till further orders.(c):	A total of 372 posts have been filled in 2020 and 2021.  Requisitions to fill 1736 posts have been sent to Staff Selection Commission and Union Public Service Commission.(d) & (e):	National Sample Survey (NSS) uses latest published Census data as frame for its surveys in rural areas.  But for urban areas NSS uses listing of urban blocks taken from Urban Frame Survey conducted by them. *****
