                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4529






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






CGHS Hospital in Coimbatore




K. Subbarayan














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government is planning to set up full fledged CGHS Hospital in Coimbatore;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the time by which the said hospital would be functional?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):	Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS) is a contributory health scheme of Central Government employees and pensioners. CGHS presently covers 72 cities including Delhi/NCR. The scheme provides OPD treatment and medicines to the subscribers/ beneficiaries through Wellness Centres (WCs). With a view to ensuring comprehensive health care facilities to CGHS beneficiaries, CGHS has been empaneling private hospitals, exclusive Eye hospitals / Centers, exclusive Dental Clinics, Cancer Hospitals/units, diagnostic laboratories and imaging centers periodically. The Government is not planning to set up full-fledged CGHS Hospital(s) anywhere in the country, including Coimbatore.
