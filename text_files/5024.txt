                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4667






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Kendriya Vidyalayas in Minority Areas




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to open 100 Kendriya Vidyalayas in minorityareas across the country;(b) if so, the names of the areas which have been identified to open these KendriyaVidyalayas under Public Private Partnership (PPPs) model in the country; and(c) the names of the minority areas in Bihar, particularly in Gaya district, for which theGovernment has issued guidelines for opening Kendriya Vidyalayas?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) No such proposal is under consideration at present. The Kendriya Vidyalayas(KVs) are opened primarily to cater to the educational needs of the children oftransferable Central Government employees including Defence and Para–Militarypersonnel by providing a common programme of education.(b) Does not arise.(c) No such guidelines have been issued as KVs are not opened on the criteria ofminority concentrated districts/areas.*****
