                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2468






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Deployment of CISF




Prasun Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of campuses in the country in which Central Industrial Security Force (CISF) personnel have been currently deployed;(b) the basis on which decision was taken to deploy CISF; and(c) the details of the circumstances under which CISF was deployed in Visva- Bharati University in West Bengal?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	CISF personnel have been deployed at the following 02 campuses under Department of Space:-i) Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST), Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala; andii) Indian Institute of Remote Sensing (IIRS), Dehradun, Uttarakhand.(b):	The decision for deployment of CISF is taken on the basis of threat perception and the commitment of the organization concerned to bear the cost of deployment.(c):	At present no CISF personnel have been deployed in Visva­Bharti University in West Bengal.  However, Government has approved, ‘In­principle’, induction of CISF at Visva­Bharti University, West Bengal based on security assessment and the commitment received from Ministry of Human Resource Development that the University will bear the cost of deployment of CISF.*******
