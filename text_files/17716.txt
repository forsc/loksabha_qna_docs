                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1544






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Establishing New NIFTEMs




Locket Chatterjee














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Ministry proposes to establish new campuses of the National Institute of Food Technology, Entrepreneurship and Management (NIFTEM);(b)	if so, the details thereof; state-wise(c)	whether inadequate and unskilled manpower is a major challenge for Food Processing Industries in the country; and(d)	whether the present NIFTEMs have in-campus incubation centers and if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b). There is no proposal as on date(c).	Yes(d).	Yes. NIFTEM, Kundli, Sonipat Haryana has recently incorporated Technology Business Incubator on 04.08.2020 as a Section 8 company under the Companies Act in the name of "NIFTEM Technology Innovation and Business Incubation Foundation".*****
