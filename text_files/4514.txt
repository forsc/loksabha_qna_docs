                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4108






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Remuneration to Workers under SBM




A.K.P. Chinraj













                                                    Su Thirunavukkarasar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the remuneration/wages paid to the workers/labourers working under Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (SBA) or Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM) is very less and at present only Rs. 85 per day are being paid and if so, the details thereof; and(b) whether the Government has any proposal to increase the said remuneration/ wages if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (b) 	Sanitation is a State subject.  Government of India provides technical and financial support to supplement their efforts.  Under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) [SBM(G)], no specific wages have been prescribed for its functionaries in the States/UTs.   The State Governments have to ensure proper wages to the workers/labourers, if any engaged by them.
