                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1420






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Promotion of Officers




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact the complaints/objections have been received by the Government of India regarding the recommendations sent by the Rajasthan Government for promoting the officers working in cadres other than Rajasthan Administrative Services to Indian Administrative Service; and (b) 	if so, the details of the investigation and action taken by the Government of India on the issue of concerned complaint/representations etc. presented before the Government of India for the years 2019, 2020 and 2021?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) & (b):  The Selection Committee Meeting for the year 2019, for preparing the Select List for Appointment by Selection from non-State Civil Services to the Indian Administrative Services for the year 2019, had been convened by Union Public Service Commission on 30th and 31st December, 2020 based on the inputs from the State Government of Rajasthan. The recommendations of the Selection Committee are yet to be received by the Central Government from the State Government.	Complaints/objections that were received regarding the recommendations sent by the Rajasthan Government for promoting the officers working in cadres other than Rajasthan Administrative Services to Indian Administrative Service have been forwarded for appropriate action to the State Government of Rajasthan, as it is the sole custodian of service records of the concerned Officers.*****
