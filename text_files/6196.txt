                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5519






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






National Research Foundation




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether his Ministry has initiated any discussions to collaborate with the upcoming National Research Foundation;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the manner in which National Innovation Foundation align its efforts with the National Research Foundation?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE; MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY; AND MINISTER OF EARTH SCIENCES(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)????????? ?? ?????? ?????? ??????; ??????? ?? ???????????? ??????; ?? ?????? ??????? ????????. ???? ?????(a)	Ministry has the knowledge of the upcoming National Research Foundation as was announced in the budget.(b)	Inter Ministry coordination and collaboration shall be done in due course of implementation of the budget announcement.(c)	The goal of National Innovation Foundation align with the agenda of National Research Foundation. National Innovation Foundation will work in close coordination with the NRF to achieve the overall goal of developing innovation led India.                                               ********
