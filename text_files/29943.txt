                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1596






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Gramin Agricultural Markets




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to improve/develop Gramin Agricultural Markets (GrAMs) according to the requirements/demands of the farmers so as to	provide right price for their produce so that they could avoid unnecessary expenditure and save time;(b) 	the number of ‘mandis‘ targeted to be set up under ‘Gramin Agricultural Markets‘ (GrAMs) along with the details thereof;(c) 	the number of total ‘mandis‘ set up in compliance with the said target, State-wise particularly in Maharashtra;(d) 	the number of the said ‘mandis‘ that organize weekly markets and the number of ‘mandis‘ which function on daily basis; and(e) 	the number of ‘mandis‘ out of the above which have basic facilities and those which lack it?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		       (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (e):	 Based on requirements of farmers, the Government is developing and upgrading rural haats into Gramin Agricultural Markets (GrAMs). The development and upgradation of physical infrastructure of rural haats under control of panchayats through States/ Union Territories is undertaken through Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) as ongoing process for development of GrAMs. So far, physical infrastructure/ facilities under MGNREGS have been developed in 1251 village haats and is under development in 1553 number of village haats. State-wise list is given at Annexure-I.As per the preliminary analysis of the rural haats surveyed, 73 % of Rural haats are weekly operated, while 11% are operating on daily basis. Further the details of per centage of the mandis/rural haats having the basic facilities is given in Annexure – II.  	As informed by Directorate of Marketing, Government of Maharashtra there are 3500 rural haats/ GrAMs in Maharashtra State. These GrAMs work under the control of the Rural Department of the State Government.
