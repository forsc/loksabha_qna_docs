                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3125






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Ischemic Heart Disease




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has taken serious note about steep increase in the prevalence of ischemic heart disease and stroke in the country and if so, the details thereof and the reasons for this rise in disease;(b) whether the Government is taking adequate steps to provide necessary facilities under Ayushman Bharat and the National Health Protection Mission and if so, the details thereof;(c) the total number of beneficiaries registered under Ayushman Bharat and the National Health Protection Mission till date with regard to the said diseases, State/UTwise and scheme-wise; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	 According to Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR)’s report titled ‘India: Health of Nation’s States’ report, there is a rise in the burden of Ischemic Heart Disease (IHD) and Stroke in the country as evident from their contribution to Disability Adjusted Life years (DALY), given below:-Contribution to DALYYear	Ischemic Heart Disease	Stroke1990	3.7%	2%2016	8.7%	3.5% The main causes of these diseases are rapid urbanization, unhealthy diet, sedentary life style, smoking, alcohol and lack of physical activity.(b) to (d):	 Under Ayushman Bharat – Health and Wellness Centers (AB-HWCs), 1.5 lakh health sub centers and primary health centers are to be transformed into Health and Wellness Centers for provisions of Comprehensive Primary Healthcare that includes preventive healthcare and health promotion at community level. The AB-HWCs would provide an expanded range of services to include care for Non-Communicable Diseases including Population Based Screening of common NCDs – Hypertension, Diabetes, Cancers of Oral Cavity, Breast & Cervix. Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) has a set of 1392 insurance packages across 24 specialties and one nonspecific surgical package. These packages also include General Medicine, Pediatric Medicine and Cardiology, which provide care for ischaemic heart disease and stroke. AB-PMJAY is an entitlement- based scheme and there is no requirement of registration of eligible beneficiaries for availing the benefits.
