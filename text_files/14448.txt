                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3098






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Projects under PMJVK




Lallu Singh













                                                    Anil Firojiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has implemented projects under Pradhan Mantri Jan VikasKaryakram (PMJVK) in Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh; (b) the details of schools/colleges, health care centres, hostels, drinking water facilities, polytechnics, ITIs, market sheds for artisans-farmers, dukhbharimandaps, job-oriented skill development centres, hunar hubs constructed/set up under PMJVK in above States; and (c)        the funds allocated and utilised for various projects in above States under PMJVK so far?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI) (a) to (c):         Yes, Sir.  In the State of Madhya Pradesh, the Ministry has sanctioned projects to the tune of Rs.399.41 crore having Central Share of Rs.252.26 crore.   In the State of Uttar Pradesh, projects to the tune of Rs.4882.58 crore having Central Share of Rs.3395.08 crore have been sanctioned.   The States/UTs are encouraged from time to time to send project proposals.  Till now the projects approved are as under: S.No.	Project	Number of Units sanctioned in Uttar Pradesh	Number of Units sanctioned in Madhya Pradesh1	Schools	615	32	Colleges	24	13	Health Centres	1079	14	Hostels	42	95	Drinking Water facilities	13179	 6.	Polytechnics	27	 7	ITIs	87	 8	Hospital/Medical College	1	19	Market Sheds	1	 10	SadbhavMandap	30	411	Skill Development Centres	2	12Further details are available on the website of the Ministry at www.minorityaffairs.gov.in*****
