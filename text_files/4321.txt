                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4014






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Nagpur-Nagbhir Railway Line




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken any decision to convert Nagpur-Nagbhir railway line into broad gauge line in the railway budget; and(b)	if so, the time by which the same is likely to be done along with the current status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)  (a) and (b) :  Yes, Sir. The work of “Nagpur-Nagbhir railway line into broad gauge line” has been included in the Budget 2013-14. This work is being taken up by Maharashtra Rail Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited (MRIDC), a Joint Venture Company of Government of Maharashtra.  “In Principle Approval” of Ministry of Railways for this work has been given to MRIDC on 09.07.2019.    Timeframe for implementation of the project will be decided after sanction of the project.*****
