                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4073






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






S-400 Missile Defence System




 Imtiaz Jaleel Syed













                                                    Margani Bharat
                                                








                                                    Asaduddin Owaisi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the details of S-400 deal with Russia and total cost involved therein;(b)  the time by which S-400 deliveries are likely to be made;(c)  whether USA has threatened India with its Countering America’s Adversaries Through Sanctions Act (CAATSA) to pressure on India to purchase its Terminal High Altitude Area defence; and(d)  if so, the response of the Indian Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  A contract has been signed on 05 October, 2018 for delivery of S-400 system from Russia.(b)  The deliveries are likely to be made by April, 2023.(c) & (d):  Government takes sovereign decisions based on threat perception, operational and technological aspects to keep the Armed Forces in a state of readiness to meet the entire spectrum of security challenges.*****
