                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

248






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Skill Development in Bihar




Ashok Kumar Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)        the total number of youth nominated from Bihar for Skill Development Training under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana during the last three years; gender-wise; (b)        the district-wise number of such youths in Bihar who got employment or jobs after the training during the last three years, gender-wise; (c)        the total amount of funds allocated and spent for the said programme during the last three years;  (d)        whether the Government proposes to set up Skill Development Training Centres in Madhubani, a backward district of Bihar; and (e)       if so, the details thereof and if not the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (e)         A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.         STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) TO (E) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 248 TO BE ANSWERED ON 21.03.2022 ASKED BY SHRI ASHOK KUMAR YADAV REGARDING SKILL DEVELOPMENT IN BIHAR. (a)       Under Skill India Mission, Ministry is implementing its flagship scheme Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) to impart skill development training to youth of the country, including the State of Bihar. Under PMKVY, in the last three years (i.e. 2018-21), 3,90,246 candidates have been enrolled for training/orientation in the State of Bihar, of which 2,51,696 are male and 1,38,543 are female. (b)       PMKVY has two training components, namely, Short Term Training (STT) and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL). Under PMKVY, placement opportunities are being provided to STT certified candidates, while RPL does not mandate placements as it recognizes the existing skills of candidate. Under PMKVY-STT, in the last three years (i.e. 2018-21), 81,285 candidates (Male: 50,659; Female: 30,626) have been placed in the State of Bihar. The district-wise and gender-wise number of candidates placed during the last three years (i.e. 2018-21) under PMKVY in State of Bihar are given at Annexure. (c)        Under Central component of PMKVY, there is no State-wise allocation; however, Rs. 226.83 crore was disbursed to training centres in the State of Bihar in the last three years (i.e. 2018-21) for the implementation of the scheme.             Under State component of PMKVY, there is provision for allocation of funds and corresponding physical targets to States/UT.  Under this component of PMKVY, Rs. 128.65 crore was allocated to the State of Bihar, out of which, Rs. 36.82 crore has been released till date. Out of total released funds (Rs. 36.82 crore), State of Bihar has spent Rs. 2.38 crore till date. (d)       Under Skill India Mission, Ministry is implementing various programmes/ schemes for the skill development of youth across the country including the State of Bihar such as Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) and Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS) through Industrial Training Institute (ITIs).
