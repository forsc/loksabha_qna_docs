                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1125






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






AYUSH Medicine




Sunil Dattatray Tatkare













                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Shivkumar Chanabasappa Udasi
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) the number of companies manufacturing Ayurvedic, Siddha, Unani and Homoeopathic medicines in the country, State/UT-wise; (b) the number of applications received for procurement of licenses to manufacture Ayurvedic, Siddha, Unani and Homoeopathic medicines along with the number of them approved in the country during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise; (c) whether the Government proposes to formulate uniform rules with regard to grant of licenses for manufacturing Ayurvedic, Siddha, Unani and Homoeopathic drugs and to ensure their safety, quality and standards across the country; (d) whether the licenses of any manufacturing company of Ayurvedic, Siddha, Unani who have been found to be indulged in unfair practices has been suspended during each of the last three years; and (e) if so, the details thereof and the other steps taken by the Government to provide quality Ayurvedic, Siddha, Unani and Homoeopathic drugs to the patients?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):  There are 8954 licensed Ayurvedic, Siddha, Unani & Homoeopathy (ASU&H) manufacturing units in the country.  State/UT-wise details are given in annexure-I.  (b):   Under the provisions of Drugs & Cosmetics Rules, 1945, licenses for manufacturing of Ayurvedic, Siddha, Unani and Homoeopathy medicines are granted and renewed by the State Licensing Authorities and their records are not maintained at the central level. However, on the basis of information received from some States, a tabulated statement of number of license applications to manufacture Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani and Homoeopathy medicines and number of licenses suspended and cancelled during the last three years and current year is provided in Annexure-II.  (c):    Exclusive provisions for grant of license to manufacture Ayurveda, Siddha & Unani and Homoeopathy drugs are prescribed in the Drugs and Cosmetics Act 1940 and Rules 1945, which are enforced by the State Licensing Authorities. Drugs & Cosmetics Rules, 1945 seek compliance to Good Manufacturing Practices and adherence to standards prescribed in the respective pharmacopoeias along with and proof of safety and effectiveness. (d) & (e):    Number of licenses cancelled or suspended by the State Authorities are mentioned in the Annexure-II.  Government has taken following steps to strengthen the quality of ASU&H medicines- a)	Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) notified, which is mandatory for all Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani & Homoeopathy drug manufacturers for grant of license. b)	Quality standards of ASU&H drugs are published in respective Pharmacopoieas and Government has set up Pharmacopoeia Commission of Indian Medicine and Homeopathy to develop, revise and publish standards of these medicines.   c)	Pharmacopoeial Laboratory for Indian Medicines & Homoeopathic Pharmacopoeia Laboratory, Ghaziabad conduct training programs of the State Regulators, In-charges of Drug Testing Laboratories, Pharmacies in every year.d)	Through Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission,  financial support is provided to the States/UTs for supply of essential medicines to dispensaries & hospitals and for strengthening of pharmacies, drug testing laboratories and regulatory capacity building & drug control framework.
