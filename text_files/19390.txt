                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

576






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






SAI Centres




Sumedhanand Saraswati














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that SAI centres have been established in various states to promote sports;(b)	if so, the essential criteria to set up a SAI centre; and(c)	the names of locations where SAI centres have been established including Parliamentary Constituency Sikar, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) to (c)	Yes Sir. Sports Authority of India (SAI) Centres have been established in various States to promote sports. These Centres are established based on various criteria including the potential and performance of athletes in the catchment area along with availability of infrastructure and funds.The names and locations of SAI Centres in the country are placed at Annexure-I.*****
