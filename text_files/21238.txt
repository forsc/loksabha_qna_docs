                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2485






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Free Assistive Aids and Devices to Divyangs




Annpurna Devi














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government provides  free assistive aids and devices to divyangs by organising special camps at district level; and(b) if so, the details thereof alongwith the names of the districts of Jharkhand where the said camps have been organised or proposed to be organised?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR)(a) Yes Madam. Under the ‘Assistance to Disabled Persons for Purchase/Fitting of Aids and Appliances’ (ADIP) Scheme, special camps are  organized at district level throughout the country on the requests of Members of Parliament and other dignitaries or as per requirement/demand from District/State authorities. (b) During the last six years and current year, 686 special camps have been  organized across the country wherein aids and assistive devices amounting to Rs.517.88 crore were distributed to 6.07 Lakh beneficiaries.    Holding of camps for distribution of aids and assistive devices under the Scheme is a continuous activity. Being a Central Sector Scheme, camps are held in the States/Uts across the country including Jharkhand, based on the requirement and demand in a particular district.                The details of camps  organized in the districts  of Jharkhand   under the Scheme during the last six years and the current year are at Annexure.*****
