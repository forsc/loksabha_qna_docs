                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1179






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






AISHE




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) whether according to the report of the All India Survey of Higher Education (AISHE)only 2.5 per cent colleges in the country run Ph.D programmes;b) if so, whether such dismal situation is due to extreme restrictive rules by theGovernment to run Ph.D courses;c) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government to increase thevolume of research studies;d) whether any incentive programme for colleges/ students have been formulated by theGovernment in this regard; ande) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (e): As per All India Survey on Higher Education (AISHE) 2018-19, 2.5 per cent ofcolleges in the country run Ph.D programmes. The procedure for admission toM.Phil./Ph.D. programmes is governed by University Grants Commission (MinimumStandards and Procedure for Award of M.Phil./Ph.D. Degrees) Regulations, 2016, whichalso gives Academic, administrative and infrastructure requirement to be filled byColleges for getting recognition for offering M.Phil./Ph.D. programmes.To increase the volume of research studies and provide incentives for the same,Government has introduced many schemes/programmes, including the following:<pre>(i) Prime Minister Research Fellowship (PMRF) Scheme for promotion of research in thefrontier areas of science & technology. It seeks to incentivize a maximum of 3,000most talented students from recognized Universities/Institutions who will be selectedas per PMRF guidelines to enrol in the Ph.D program in IITs/IISc/IISERs/CUs byproviding attractive rates of fellowship @ Rs.70,000/- per month for the first twoyears, Rs.75,000/- per month for the 3rd year, and Rs.80,000/- per month in the 4th and5th years. In addition, a research grant of Rs.2.00 lakh per year for a period of 5 yearswill be given to each fellow to meet the cost of presenting research papers.(ii) The University Grants Commission (UGC) is providing financial assistance to thePh.D scholars under the schemes of Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) and SeniorResearch Fellowship (SRF) in Science, Humanities and Social Sciences (NET), SwamiVivekanand Single Girl Child Fellowship for research in Social Science, MaulanaAzad National Fellowship for Minority Students, National Fellowship for ScheduledCastes (SCs) and National Fellowship for Other Backward Classes (OBCs), whereinfellowship @ Rs.31000/- per month is provided for first 2 years as JRF followed byRs.35000/- per month for remaining tenure as SRF. In addition to the fellowship,admissible HRA & contingency grants are also provided.(iii) Scheme for Trans-disciplinary Research for India’s Developing Economy (STRIDE)for Humanities and Human Sciences.(iv) Impacting Research Innovation and Technology (IMPRINT) for technical research.(v) Impactful Policy Research in Social Science (IMPRESS) for social sciences research.(vi) Scheme for Promotion of Academic and Research Collaboration (SPARC) for jointresearch with Foreign Universities; and(vii) National Doctoral Fellowship (NDF) scheme to promote research culture andcollaborative research between Institute and Industries leading to start-ups in AICTEinstitutions.</pre>******
