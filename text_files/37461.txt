                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4404






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Non-resumption of Senior Citizen concession




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the number of senior citizens who  availed  concession and the amount of revenue foregone as a result of concession during 2019-20;(b)  the number of senior  citizens  who  actually  travelled and the amount of concession saved during 20 March, 2020 to 31 March, 2021 and 1 April 2021 till date in the current Financial Year (FY)  after the withdrawal of the said concession;(c)   whether the amount of concession saved is meagre as compared to the earnings of Railways and most of the senior citizens have been fully vaccinated; and(d)   if so, the reasons for non-resumption of senior citizen concession?






ANSWER




                                                           MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY                              (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.******
