                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4758






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Funds Allocation for Bihar




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the date on which the funds for the year 2021-22 have been released against the annual  action plan submitted by the Government of Bihar to the Union Government under Jal Jeevan Mission;(b)	  whether more funds are likely to be allocated to Bihar under Jal Jeevan Mission; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a), (b) & (c): Government of India allocated Rs.6,608.25 crore to Government of Bihar under Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) for financial year 2021-22. As per records, Government of Bihar did not submit the Annual Action Plan (AAP) for 2021-22 and neither participated in scheduled meeting to finalize AAP. In October, 2021, Government of Bihar conveyed that there are no projects left to be proposed under JJM in the State and thus do not require any funds under JJM.*****
