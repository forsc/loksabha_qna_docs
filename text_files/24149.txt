                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

112






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Export of Finished Steel




Ramya  Haridas













                                                    Vishnu Dutt Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of finished steel exports as compared to imports ;(b)	whether it is a fact that the country‘s exports of finished steel are presently falling and if so, the reasons therefor; and(c)	whether any action has been initiated by the Government to reverse this trend with any new policies and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL		   (SHRI RAM CHANDRA PRASAD SINGH)(a)	Details of export of finished steel as compared to imports is given as follows: -							       (in Million Ton)Year	Total Finished Steel	Import	Export2020-21	4.75	10.78April-June, 2021*	1.16	3.56	    *Provisional, Source: Joint Plant Committee (JPC)(b)&(c): Steel is a de-regulated sector.  Commercial decisions such as exports are taken by the steel companies.
