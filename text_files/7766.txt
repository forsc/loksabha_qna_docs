                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PLANNING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1458






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






District Action Plan for Wayanad District




Rahul Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





PLANNING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the empowered Committee chaired by the CEO, NITI Aayog has initiated any measures to ensure the convergence of schemes and address issues brought up by the Central Prabhari officer in Wayanad district under the aspirational districts programme;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether one of the stated strategies is to work on the strength of each district and if so, the details thereof;(d)	the details of the key strengths identified in the District Action Plan for Wayanad district; and(e)	the measures taken to foster collaboration between citizens, Central and State Governments?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION(RAO INDERJIT SINGH)-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------(a)&(b) The Empowered Committee of Secretaries for the on-going Aspirational Districts Programme (ADP) has been constituted inter alia to review the performance of the Programme. This entails reviewing the progress in a district across 49 key performance indicators which are captured regularly on a web portal championsofchange.gov.in. These indicators are selected from the sectors which affect quality of life or economic productivity of citizenry viz. health & nutrition, school education, agriculture and water resources, financial inclusion and skill development and basic infrastructure. The list of these indicators catalogued in a primer is at http://championsofchange.gov.in/publications. The Central Prabhari Officer of Wayanad district had inter alia recommended that district should formulate an action plan, its data collection mechanism should improve and there should be convergence and team spirit in the district administration. These recommendations are in line with the directions of the Empowered Committee which reviews the Programme from time to time.In compliance of the above, the district team has formulated an action plan for development of Wayanad district which is being implemented through convergence of different schemes subject to availability of resources to the district.  As regards improvement in data collection mechanism, since the ADP is a data driven Programme under which districts compete on basis of their performance, data collection mechanism has been strengthened and progress are regularly captured on web portal championsofchange.gov.in (c) Yes. A key element of strategy is to identify strength in each district and to incorporate the same in the vision and plan for the district. (d) A brief collation of strengths identified in the district action plan is placed at Annexure-A.(e) Making development a mass movement is an important aspect of strategy of this initiative and Niti Aayog has requested the State Government to take measures to involve citizenry in this Programme. As stated above, the web portal capturing progress in key performance indicator namely championsofchange.gov.in is available in public domain and citizens concerned may see the progress made in the district under the Programme. In addition, Government of India has nominated Central Prabhari Officer for every Aspirational district and their functions inter alia include coordination between district, State and Central Government. Availability of sufficient rain enables the crops and farm land to become more fertile and productive”. (para 1.13)2.
