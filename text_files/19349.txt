                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

511






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Drainage System along National Highways




Shriniwas Dadasaheb Patil














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether some portion of the planned estimate for roads upgraded from State Highways and reassigned to National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) is earmarked for ensuring proper drainage of water to avoid water logging in the fields adjoining the newly widened constructed roads to avoid damage to the crops in the adjoining agricultural lands;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the percentage of the planned estimate allocated for the purpose;(c) whether the affected farmers are eligible for any compensation from NHAI for resultant crop damage due to blockage of the pre-existing natural drainage or inadequate drainage provided by NHAI; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) Adequate provisions are made in the estimates being prepared for upgradation of roads to take care of drainage aspects under the scope of relevant items and made part of scope of the contracts for all newly constructed/widened roads taken-up by NHAI duly following Indian Road Congress (IRC) Standards & Guidelines and specifications of Ministry of Road Transport and Highways. (b) Construction of roadside lined/unlined drains, Cross Drainage structures viz., Culverts, Minor & Major bridges across natural streams/drainage etc., are carried out as per scope of contract by the contractors/concessionaires as per standard specifications and IRC codes. No fixed percentage of planned estimate can be explicitly allocated for this purpose as it varies from project to project depending on the site requirements. (c) & (d) Does not arise.
