                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Telecom Infrastructure Programme




Arun Kumar Sagar














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether funds have been allocated under telecom infrastructure programme during the last three years; and(b) 	if so, the year-wise/State-wise details thereof along with its utilization?
