                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3194






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Infrastructure Built along LoC




Rakesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether infrastructure is being built by Pakistan along the Line of Control (LoC) after last year‘s ceasefire and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether launching pads and training camps have also been constructed and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether terrorists have tried to infiltrate from these launching pads and if so, the details thereof; and(d) the number of terrorists killed in various operations conducted during the last year?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS (SHRI NISITH PRAMANIK)(a) to (d):	Govt. of India regularly reviews the security situation along the Line of Control and takes precautionary measures for thwarting any move by threat elements including the terrorists.     However, information sought being a sensitive operational matter concerning  National security,  cannot be divulged in the interest of National Security. *****
