                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

311






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Toilets in Schools




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has achieved the construction of at least one toilet in eachschool in the country comprising primary, upper primary and secondary levels ofeducation;(b) if so, the details of total number of toilets constructed so far and number of schoolscovered, State-wise;(c) the district-wise number of schools with defunct toilets along with the total number oftoilets in both Government and private categories in Andhra Pradesh as on February,2020; and(d) the number of toilets constructed, repaired and functional in Andhra Pradesh during thelast three years, year-wise and district-wise?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARREDQUESTION NO. 311 TO BE ANSWERED ON 16TH MARCH 2020 ASKED BY HON’BLE MPSHRI N. REDDEPPA REGARDING TOILETS IN SCHOOLS(a) and (b) In response to the announcement made by the Hon’ble Prime Minister on 15August, 2014, the Department of School Education & Literacy, Ministry of Human ResourceDevelopment had launched Swachh Vidyalaya initiative to provide separate toilets for girls andboys in all Government schools, within one year upto 15th August, 2015. Under the initiative,construction/re-construction of 4,17,796 toilets was completed in 2,61,400 Governmentelementary and secondary schools as reported by the States and UTs. State/UT-wise details aregiven at Annexure-I.(c) As per UDISE 2017-18, district-wise number of schools, number of schools withfunctional toilets and dysfunctional toilets in Andhra Pradesh are given at Annexure-II.(d) The district-wise numbers of toilets constructed in schools in Andhra Pradesh during thelast three years are given at Annexure-III.***
