                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

120






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






New KV in Madhya Pradesh




Rodmal Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has fixed any new criteria to open new KendriyaVidyalayas;(b) if so, details of demand based Kendriya Vidyalayas set up in such districts as per thenew criteria, State-wise;(c) if not, the action plan of the Government in this regard; and(d) the details of newly opened Kendriya Vidyalayas in Madhya Pradesh?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) The Board of Governors of Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan has in its 109thmeeting held on 6.3.2018 amended the norms prescribed for opening of new KendriyaVidyalayas (KVs).Pursuant to this amendment, new KVs under the Civil Sector areopened after evaluation of the proposals as per the parameters and weightages underthe “Challenge Method”.(b) After introduction of the “Challenge Method”, 33 KVs have been opened underthe Civil Sector in the country so far. State/UT- wise details are annexed.(c) Does not arise.(d) The details of newly opened KVs in the State of Madhya Pradesh are as under:-<pre>(i) Agar-Malwa,District Agar-Malwa(ii)Anuppur,District Anuppur(iii) Shamgarh, District Mandsaur(iv) CRPF, AB Road NayagaonGwalior.</pre>*****
