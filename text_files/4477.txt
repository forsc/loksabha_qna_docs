                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4107






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Recalled Vehicles for Safety Defects




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any data regarding the number and class/type of vehicles 	recalled by automobile manufacturers for safety defects in the country;(b)	 if so, the details thereof for the last three years and the current year;(c) 	whether the Government has prepared a mandatory recall policy for vehicles which do 	not meet safety standards;(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(e)	whether a study of road accidents involving light four wheelers has been conducted and 	if so, the details and outcome thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) and (b) Sir, vehicle manufacturers provide the vehicle recall data to the Government on voluntary basis from time to time. Presently,it is not a mandatory requirement under the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988. Therefore, such information is not maintained.(c) and (d) Ministry under the proposed clause 40 of the proposed Motor Vehicles (Amendment) Bill, 2019 as introduced in Lok Sabha on 15th July 2019, has provided for  provision for recall of motor vehicles.(e) The Ministry analyses the road traffic accidents based on the data received from the Police department of all States/Union Territories. As per the report, light four wheelers (Car/Jeep/Van/Taxi) accounted for 1,13,737 road accidents during the calendar year 2017.*****
