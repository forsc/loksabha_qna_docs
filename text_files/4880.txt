                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4471






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Relaxation for Private Educational Institutions




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government offers any financial or institutional relaxation for private educational institutions in setting up new medical colleges, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (b) whether the Government plans to offer any such relaxation in the near future, if so, the details thereof; (c) whether any form of relaxation gives private medical colleges better advantage compared to All India Institute of Medical Sciences or other Government medical colleges; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (d):	No. The regulations of Medical Council of India prescribe minimum standard requirements and criteria for establishment of new medical college. The MCI regulations are statutory in nature and binding on both Government and Private medical colleges/ institutions. The said regulation do not provide for any relaxation for private institutions in setting up new medical colleges.
