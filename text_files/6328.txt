                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

31






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






KV and JNV in Bihar




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has asked the Kendriya and Navodaya Vidyalayas ofGopalganj district of Bihar to fill up the vacancies quickly;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether it is fact that the Kendriya and Navodaya Vidyalayas of Gopalganj district ofBihar are running with teachers on contract basis to meet the shortage of teachers;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the details about teachers strength in these schools?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. Both Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) and Navodaya VidyalayaSamiti (NVS) have been advised from time to time to review their vacancy position andtake action for filling up the vacancies as per the provisions of the relevant RecruitmentRules of the posts.(c) to (e) As informed by KVS and NVS, the vacancy position of teaching staff in theKendriya Vidyalaya (KV), Gopalganj and Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya (JNV),Gopalganj along with the details of teachers engaged on contract basis are as under:<pre>Vidyalaya Sanctioned strength ofteaching staffVacancy Teachers engaged oncontract basis.KV, Gopalganj243PGT (English) 1PGT (Economics) 1Primary Teacher 1JNV, Gopalganj213PGT (Physics) 1TGT (Mathematics) 1TGT (Assamese) 1</pre>*****
