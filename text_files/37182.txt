                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4266






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Hindustan Newsprint Limited




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Hindustan Newsprint Limited (HNL) has been handed over to the State Government of Kerala;(b)	if so, the details including terms and conditions for the same;(c)	whether the Union Government has any role in functioning of HNL;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	whether the Government has given any financial support for the revival of this unit and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) & (b):	National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), Kochi, in the hearing held on 29.01.2021 approved the resolution plan for Hindustan Newsprint Limited (HNL) submitted by the Kerala Industrial Infrastructure Development Corporation (KINFRA).  The approved resolution plan envisages the business plan to acquire HNL as a going concern entailing a proposed outlay of Rs. 145,60.17 lakhs.(c):	No, after the initiation of liquidation proceeding the Government of India has no role in the functioning of HNL.  (d):	Question does not arise.(e):	No, the Government has not given any financial support for revival of HNL.******
