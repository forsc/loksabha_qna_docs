                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1050






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Mega Food Parks




Kotagiri Sridhar













                                                    Devendra (Alias) Bhole Singh Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the list of Mega Food Parks and their functioning status, State-wise;(b)	the timeline envisaged for the completion of Mega Food Parks in Andhra Pradesh; (c)	whether the Government is facing any obstacle in the completion of the above Mega Food Parks, if so, the measures taken to overcome them; and (d)	whether there is any proposal of setting up a Mega Food Park in Kanpur Dehat district or city in Uttar Pradesh and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c): Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) has been implementing Mega Food Park (MFP) Scheme to provide modern infrastructure facilities for food processing industries along the value chain from farm to the market. As per the scheme guidelines, time schedule for completion of Mega Food Park projects is 30 months from the date of issue of final approval letter. Ministry has accorded final approval to 39 Mega Food Parks and in-principle approval to 2 Mega Food Parks in the country. Out of these, 22 projects are operational and remaining are under various stages of implementation. The status of 41 Mega Food Park Projects, state wise, is at Annexure.          The implementation of Mega Food Park projects has taken time due to delay in grant of statutory clearances by the State Government including Pollution Control consents, changes in approved project cost and components midway –Revision in DPR, Changes in shareholding and bank approval thereon, less focus on completion of PPCs & attracting units in the park etc.Ministry has taken various measures for expediting the implementation of sanctioned projects like- (i) Holding regular review meetings with the promoters to help remove bottlenecks in implementation; (ii) Creation of Food Processing Fund of Rs. 2000 crore with NABARD to make available affordable credit to the Mega Food Park promoters; (iii) Facilitating the promoters in obtaining the required statutory clearances from concerned authority; (iv) Modifying the provisions of the scheme guidelines to facilitate implementation of the project; (v) Providing financial assistance to promoters  who set up food processing units in Mega Food Parks  under scheme for Creation/Expansion of Food Processing and Preservation Capacities, a component scheme under Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADAYojana; (vi) Creating awareness of benefits available under the schemes for establishment of food processing units in the park. (d):	Presently, there is no proposal under consideration with Ministry for setting up a Mega Food Park project in Kanpur Dehat district or city in Uttar Pradesh. *****
