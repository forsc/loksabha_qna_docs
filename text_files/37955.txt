                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5232






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Monuments Declared as Monuments of National Importance in Ladakh




Jamyang Tsering Namgyal














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of monuments or sites declared as monument of national importance by the Archaeological Survey of India, District-Wise in Union Territory of Ladakh;(b)	the details of the funds spent on the said monuments or sites during the last three years;(c)	the revenue earned by the Government from these monuments during the last three financial years;(d)	whether the Government proposes to declare any other monument especially the famous rock carved Maitreya Buddha status-Kartse Chamba, Apati/Sodh Chamba, Mulbeck Chamba, Drass and Skidmartse in Sankoo as monument of national importance; and(e)	if so, the details thereof, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	There are 15 monuments/sites of national importance in the Union Territory of Ladakh. Three of them are in Kargil district and twelve in Leh district.(b)	The expenditure on these monuments/sites during the last three years is as under:Year	Expenditure           (Rupees in lakhs)2019-20	28.492020-21	20.002021-22	43.00(c)	There is only one ticketed monument in Ladakh i.e. Leh Palace. Revenue earned from entry fee at Leh Palace, Leh during the last three years is as under:Year	 Amount in Rupees2019-20	31573002020-21	    417152021-22	2661175(d)&(e)	Mulbekh Chamba and Sculpture at Drass are already declared as monuments of national importance.  The Kartse Chamba and Apati/Sodh Chamba are under Department of Archives, Archaeology and Museums, Union Territory of Ladakh. There is no such proposal for Skidmartse in Sankoo under consideration.  *****
