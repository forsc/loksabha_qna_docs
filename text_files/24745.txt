                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

624






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Works under Saubhagya




Rodmal Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the work undertaken in Rajgarh district of Madhya Pradesh under Pradhan Mantri Sahaj Bijli Har Ghar Yojana (Saubhagya), location-wise;(b) 	the number of Gram Sabhas/Majras identified in Rajgarh district under Saubhagya and the number of locations where work has been completed and the number of locations where work is yet to be completed;(c) 	the funds sanctioned and spent for the said purpose during the last three years and the current year along with the names of agencies which have been provided the said work in Rajgarh district, work and village-wise;(d) 	whether there is any possibility to cover the remaining Gram Sabhas/Majras under Saubhagya; and(e) 	if so, the time by which the said work is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF POWER AND NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY (SHRI  R.K. SINGH)(a) to (b) : Government of India had launched Pradhan Mantri Sahaj Bijli Har Ghar Yojana – Saubhagya in October, 2017 for electrification of rural and urban poor households in the country to achieve universal household electrification of all households in the country including Rajgarh district of Madhya Pradesh. The State of Madhya Pradesh has reported 100% electrification of all the willing un-electrified households in the State, including Rajgarh district, as on 31.03.2019. Under Saubhagya scheme electricity connections to 62,478 households of the Rajgarh district were provided. The State has also reported installation of 147.79 KM of 11 kV line and 298.34 KM of LT line and 219 Distribution Transformers covering 2,465 habitations of Rajgarh district.(c) : 		Under Saubhagya Scheme, projects were sanctioned on the basis of un-electrified household data submitted by the States; and as such no district-wise sanction/release of funds was made by the Government of India. An amount of Rs.872.65 Crore was sanctioned for the State of Madhya Pradesh including Rajgarh district for implementation of works under Saubhagya Scheme. Disbursement details during the years 2017-18 to 2021-22 (up to 30.06.2021) are as under:(Rs. In crore)Sanctioned amount	Central Share	State Share	Loan	Grant released upto 30.06.2021				2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	 2020-21	2021-22	Total872.65	523.59	87.27	261.80	260.37	147.07	-   	6.32	-   	413.76 The State Government has reported that 100% amount released has been utilized. Further, electrification works under Saubhagya scheme in Rajgarh district has been implemented by the Madhya Pradesh Madhya Kshetra Vidyut Vitaran Company Limited (MPMKVCL) and an expenditure of Rs. 38.494 crore has been reported towards electrification of works in the District under the scheme.  (d) & (e) : 	Saubhagya Scheme got closed on 31.3.2019. The State of Madhya Pradesh reported 100% electrification of all the willing un-electrified households in the State including Rajgarh district, as on 31.03.2019.**********
