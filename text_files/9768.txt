                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3314






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Cultural Schemes launched for Solapur




Jaisiddeshwar Shivacharya Mahaswamiji














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the details of the schemes launched by the Ministry in Solapur district of Maharashtra during the last two years;b) 	whether the Government has any scheme for Solapur district in the ensuing years; and c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c) Ministry of Culture operates a number of central sector schemes which are applicable to all States and UTs including Solapur district of Maharashtra. The names of schemes are as follows:-I.	Repertory Grant              II.	Financial Assistance to Cultural organizations with National Presence                     III.	Culture Function Production Grant           IV.	Preservation and Development of Cultural Heritage of the Himalayas   V.	Preservation and Development of Buddhist / Tibetan Organizations       VI.	Financial Assistance for Building Grant including Studio Theatres               VII.	Financial Assistance for allied Cultural Activities VIII.	Financial Assistance for Tagore Cultural Complexes                         IX.	Scheme of Scholarship and Fellowship for Promotion of Art and Culture                 X.	Scheme for Pension and Medical Aid to Artists                  XI.	Scheme of Financial Assistance under Sewa Bhoj YojanaXII.	Museum Grant Scheme                 XIII.	Scheme for Promotion of Culture of Science (SPoCS)     XIV.	Intangible Cultural Heritage Scheme      XV.	Festival of India                               XVI.	Grant in Aid to Indo Foreign Friendship Cultural Society
