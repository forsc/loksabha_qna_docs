                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2771






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Purchase for Buffer Stock




Gurjeet Singh Aujla














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Union Government has purchased produce from farmers at the same price during lockdown to maintain the buffer stock and if so, the details thereof; and (b) 	the alterations made in prices at which farm produce was bought from farmers to store in buffer at every stage of lockdown in 2020?






ANSWER




A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	Wheat and paddy has been procured at Minimum Support Price (MSP) for central pool smoothly in 2020 and 2021 including lockdown period. A quantity of 389.92 LMT and 433.44 LMT of wheat was procured in RMS 2020-21 and RMS 2021-22 respectively. The quantum of paddy procured under central pool during KMS 2019-20, KMS 2020-21 was 770.93 LMT and 894.18 LMT respectively.   During lockdown, the stock of wheat and rice was sufficient to maintain buffer stock, in-fact it was surplus. (b): 	No alternations made in price during lockdown period. Procurement of wheat and paddy for central pool were made at MSP announced by Government of India.  ****
