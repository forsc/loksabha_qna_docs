                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1419






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






National Dairy Development Programme




Jaskaur Meena













                                                    Nihal Chand Chauhan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the main features of National Dairy Development Programme; (b) the details of funds sanctioned, allocated and utilised under this scheme during the last three years and the current year, district-wise in Rajasthan; (c) the details of the target fixed under this scheme as well as the details of the achievements made in this regard so far; (d) whether the Government is planning to include the vaccination programme under the said scheme; and (e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the time by which it is likely to be included and the details of the funds to be earmarked in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (DR.SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD) focuses on creating/strengthening of infrastructure for Production of quality milk, Procurement, Processing and Marketing of Milk & Milk Products by the State Implementing Agency. A brief note on main features of NPDD scheme is at Annexure-I.(b)	The details of funds sanctioned and utilised under NPDD scheme during last three years and current year in Rajasthan is at Annexure-II.(c) 	The details of the total project target and achievements made under NPDD scheme as on 31.12.2019 is as follows:-S. No.	Parameters	Target(31.03.2020)	Achievement(31.12.2019)1	Average Daily Milk Procurement (TLPD) 	3821.15	2218.582	Dairy Plant Capacity (TLPD)	2667	1729.003	Chilling Capacity (Bulk Milk Cooler) TLPD	3921.4	1111.904	Automatic Milk Collection Unit /Data Processor & Milk Collection Unit (Number)	16691	91825	Electronic Adulteration Testing Machine (Number)	2562	1313	TLPD-Thousand Litre Per Day(d) No sir. Vaccination Programme is done under separate schemes.(e) Does not arise.
