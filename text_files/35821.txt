                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2750






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Allotment of Petrol Pumps




R.K. Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of categories/persons being provided reservation or special concession in the allotment of petrol pumps and gas agencies; and (b) the details of the application process for the allotment of petrol pump or gas agency to these categories/persons under special concession?






ANSWER




       MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a)&(b)  Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) have implemented new “Dealership Selection Guidelines” for Retail Outlets w.e.f. 24.11.2018 and “Unified Guidelines for Selection of LPG Distributorships” w.e.f. 23.6.2016. Both these guidelines inter alia, have a provision of 22.5% reservation for SCs/STs and 27% reservation for OBCs in allotment of retail outlet dealerships and LPG Distributorships. Within SC/ST, OBC & Open categories, there is reservation also for Defence personnel, Para Military Personnel/Central/State Govt. and Central/State PSU employees, Physically Handicapped Persons, Outstanding Sports Persons and Freedom Fighters.The allotment of RO and LPG Distributorship are done by following a transparent process which inter-alia include Advertisement in National/State news paper inviting application from eligible participants, online submission of applications, draw of lots to select the allottee etc.    Application Procedure / Norms / Criteria / Reservation Policy for allotment of Retail Outlet Dealerships are available on the respective websites of the Public Sector (OMCs) i.e. www.iocl.com, www.bharatpetroleum.com & www.hindustanpetroleum.com. The detailed guidelines namely “Unified Guidelines for Selection of LPG Distributorships” are available on the website www.lpgvitarakchayan.in.****
