                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2638






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Implementation of Har Ghar Nal Se Jal Scheme




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the status of implementation of Har Ghar Nal Se Jal Scheme in Dahod district of Gujarat;(b) the number of households benefitted from thisscheme so far;(c) the time by which Har Ghar Nal Se Jal Scheme is expected to be fully implemented in Dahod district of Gujarat State;(d) the action being taken by the Government in this regard; and(e) the details of the amount of funds allocated forthe said scheme so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JALSHAKTI(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d) Since August 2019, the Government of India in partnership with States is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM), to make provision of potable tap water supply to every rural household of the country by 2024. However, the State Government of Gujarat has planned to provide all its households, including those in Dahod, provision of tap water supply by 2022. In Dahod district, out of the total of 3.21 Lakhs rural households, provision of the tap water supply has been made in 1.79 Lakhs (55.93%) households. To review the implementation of JJM in States, regular meetings with the State Government are held which includes review of planning, implementation and progress made so far, inter alia. (e) Since the launch of JJM, details of funds allocated to the State of Gujarat, are as under:Financial year	Fund allocated (Rs. in Crores)2019-20	390.312020-21	                                883.082021-22	3,410.61*****
