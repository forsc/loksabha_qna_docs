                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

650






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Doubling of Railway Tracks




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the present status of railway track doubling of (i) Surendranagar-Daladi section and (ii) Daladi-Rajkot section;(b) whether the sections of this project are progressing as per the schedule, if not, the reasons thereof; and(c) by when the railway track doubling of both the sections are targeted to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): Surendranagar-Daladi-Rajkot doubling project was included in the Budget 2015-16. Latest anticipated cost of the project is `1002 crore and expenditure of `501 crore has been incurred upto March, 2020. An outlay of `205 crore has been provided for this project for the year 2020-21. The present status of the project is as under:(i) Surendranagar-Daladi section: Land acquisition is completed except in village Muli. Works have been taken up wherever  land is available. Work in Surendranagar-Disgar section (about 14 Km length) has been completed.  (ii) Daladi-Rajkot section: Land acquisition is completed except between Khorana to Rajkot. Works have been taken up wherever land is available.(b) & (c): Completion of project would depend upon land acquisition in the remaining section. Therefore, confirmed timeline for completion of the project is not feasible to be fixed.*****
