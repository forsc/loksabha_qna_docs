                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

230






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Series on Martyrs




Sumedhanand Saraswati














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) whether the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) has started aseries on martyrs;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) if not, the reasons therefor;(d) whether the Government has sought any suggestion from the State Governments in thisregard; and(e) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) No, Sir.(b) Does not arise.(c) The contents relating to struggles and sacrifices of various martyrs are already included inthe National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) History textbooks forClasses VIII, X and XII. Besides, NCERT has also brought out supplementary readers such as‘The Cellular Jail in our Freedom Struggle’, ‘The Indian Army - A Glorious Heritage’ and ‘VeerGaatha: Stories of ParamVir Chakra Awardees’, which highlight the sacrifices of the freedomfighters in the pre-independence era and the war heroes in the post-independence era, includingthose who attained martyrdom.(d) No, Sir.(e) Does not arise.*****
