                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1774






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Manganese Ore India Ltd.




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Manganese Ore India Limited (MOIL) company is engaged in mining and if so, the quantum of ore mined by MOIL since 2014 till date;(b)	the value of the ore mined by MOIL so far since 2014 and the total cost incurred thereon; and(c)	the comparative analysis of the mining cost of excavation of ore by non-Government and public sector companies and MOIL since 2014 till date?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)&(b): Yes, Sir.  Manganese Ore India Limited (MOIL) is engaged in mining.  The quantity, turnover and expenditure of ore mined since 2014 is given in Table -1.Table-1Year-wise Production, Turnover and Expenditure Sr. No.	Year	Production (Manganese ore)(Metric Tonne)	Turnover(Manganese ore)(Rs. in Crore)	Expenditure(Manganese ore)(Rs. in Crore)1	2014-15	1139156	749.55	442.482	2015-16	1032275	571.79	542.063	2016-17	1004845	905.34	676.684	2017-18	1201113	1210.78	788.095	2018-19	1301191	1320.86	826.91Source: MOIL Ltd.(c)	No such information is maintained in the Ministry.********
