                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4497






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Release of PM-KISAN Funds in Bihar




Veena Devi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether funds for the second and third installments under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Yojana have been released to farmers in Bihar ;(b)	If so, the details thereof; and (c)	If not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) and (b):	Yes, Sir. The funds for the second and third installments under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Yojana have been released to farmers in Bihar.  The details of all the seven installments given under PM-KISAN Scheme,  in the State of Bihar (district-wise)  have been given at Annexure-I.(c):	Does not arise in view of above
