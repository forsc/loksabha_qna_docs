                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2263






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Access to Broadband Services




H. Vasanthakumar














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government will provide broadband access to all villages across the country in the targeted time and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government has launched any mission to enable fast track growth of digital communication infrastructure in the country and if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the fund allocation by the Government in this regard during the last three years, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) & (b) 	National Broadband Mission (NBM) was launched on 17th December 2019 with a vision to fast track growth of digital communications infrastructure, bridge the digital divide, facilitate digital empowerment and inclusion, and provide affordable and universal access of broadband for all. The Mission inter-alia envisages to provide broadband access to all villages in the country by year 2022. Towards this end, BharatNet project is already being implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity to all the Gram Panchayats (GPs) (approx. 2.5 lakh) in the country. As on 21 February, 2020 1,36,341 GPs (including Block Headquarters) have been made service ready.(c)  State/UT-wise Subsidy disbursed from Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF) during last three years i.e 2016-17 to 2018-19 for provisioning of Broadband access in the country is at Annexure.
