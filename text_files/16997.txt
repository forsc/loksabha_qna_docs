                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

609






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Suicide by Defence Personnel




Dibyendu Adhikari













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether more than 110 Defence personnel died by suicide in the country during the last decade;(b) if so, the details thereof and whether Government has made any study with Defence Institute of Psychological Research (DIPR) on the issue;(c) if so, the details and the outcome thereof;(d) whether it is also a fact that mental stress among the Defence personnel is the only cause of suicide; and(e) if so, the year-wise details of suicides by Defence personnel and the steps taken to prevent such unnatural deaths?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) to (e):  The details of suicide cases in the armed forces from 2010 to 2019 are as under:Year	No. of suicides	Indian Air Forces	Indian Navy	Indian Army2010	14	01	1162011	23	04	1052012	15	01	952013	15	06	862014	24	04	842015	15	03	782016	19	06	1042017	21	05	772018	16	08	832019	20	02	73Total	182	40	901The DIPR after conducting a number of studies since 2006, has, inter-alia, listed Domestic and Personal problems, Marital Discord, Stress and Financial problems as the major causes of suicides by armed forces personnel.The Government has taken several steps towards stress amelioration amongst troops, which include, deployment of trained psychological counsellors, improvement in the quality of food and clothing, training in stress management, provision of recreational facilities, buddy system, leave concessions, approachability of seniors, facilities for movement of troops from border areas and establishing a grievance redressal mechanism at various levels, etc.*****
