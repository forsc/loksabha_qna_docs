                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1947






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






External Assistance in Health Projects




Sushil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether there has been a decrease in external assistance received by the Government of India in projects for public health and family welfare, if so, the details thereof;(b) the details of external assistance received by the Government of India in projects for public health and family welfare in multilateral, bilateral and NGOs categories since 2000;(c) whether such collaboration have any impact on technical expertise, transparency, accountability, capacity building and implementation in the projects;(d) the details thereof;(e) whether there have been any issues in financial sustainability and timely completion of such projects; and(f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	A statement of year-wise externally aided component of budget routed through Controller of Aid Accounts & Audit (CAAA) Division since 2000 is attached.(c) to (f):	External assistance for public health programmes provide focused support in key areas with enhancement of technical expertise and capacities as well as a structured monitoring and review mechanism complementing efforts of the Government of India. The projects with external funding are generally completed timely.  After successful completion, further continuation is supported from funds of Government of India, if warranted.
