                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1008






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Requirement of Dialysis Machines in Jharkhand




Annpurna Devi













                                                    Pashupati Nath Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether there is an acute shortage of dialysis machines vis-a-vis their requirement in Jharkhand;(b) the reaction of the Government thereto;(c) whether the Government is providing any assistance at the central level to increase the number of dialysis machines in Jharkhand;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (e):	Public Health and Hospitals is a State subject.  However, Central Government supplements the efforts of State Governments.  As reported by the Government of    Jharkhand, dialysis units have been established in 8 Districts in the State   in Public Private Partnership mode under the  Pradhan Mantri National Dialysis Program implemented through the National Health Mission, Remaining 16 Districts are provisioned for coverage in 2019.  Dialysis services are provided free of cost to BPL families under NHM for which support is provided to State Governments through their Programme Implementation Plans.  During 2018-19 and 2019-20, the budget allocated for Jharkhand for this purpose was Rs.189.00 lakh and Rs.151.20 lakh respectively.
