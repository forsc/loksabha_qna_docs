                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

8






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Statue of Unity Like Initiatives




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government assumes the Statue of Unity as a model initiative of the Government of Gujarat to boost tourism;(b) 	whether the Government proposes to direct the other State Governments to take similar initiatives;(c) 	if so, the details thereof;(d) 	whether the Government proposes to identify similar locations State-wise to develop such initiatives under the Central Financial Assistance to boost tourism;and(e) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e): 	A Statement is laid on the table of the House.*************** STATEMENT IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.*8 ANSWERED ON 29.11.2021 REGARDING STATUE OF UNITY LIKE INITIATIVES.(a):	Yes, Sir.  The  Statue  of  Unity  has  emerged  as  an important  tourist  destination  of the  country  both  for  domestic  as  well  as  international  tourists. (b):	No, Sir.  The  Ministry of  Tourism has  not  given  directions  to  the  State  Governments  in this  regard. (c):	Does not  arise.(d) & (e): The  Ministry of  Tourism  as part  of  its  ongoing  schemes of  ‘SwadeshDarshan’, ‘Pilgrimage Rejuvenation & Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ and  ‘Assistance to  Central Agencies for Tourism Infrastructure Development’ provides central financialassistancetovarious State Governments/ Union Territory Administrations and  Central Agenciesfor development of tourism  infrastructure  in  the country.  The details of the projects sanctioned under these schemes is as  under:- Sl.No.	Scheme	No. of  States / UTs	Total projects  sanctioned	Total sanctioned amount(Rs. in Cr.)1.	SwadeshDarshan	30	76	5524.822.	PRASHAD	24	37	1209.803.	Assistance to Central Agencies	17	31	553.03*****
