                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

989






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Financial Assistance to Andhra Pradesh for Development of Tourism




Y. S. Avinash Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has proposed any schemes and financial assistance to the State for development of tourism; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the number of projects being selected and considered for development; (d) 	the details of the financial assistance sanctioned/being sanctioned for each project; and (e) 	the details of the projects identified particularly in the district of Kadapa in Andhra Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (e):	The Ministry of Tourism under its schemes of ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ provides financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies including Andhra Pradesh for development of tourism infrastructure in the country.  The projects are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier etc.  Sanctioning of  projects  under  these  schemes  is  a  continuous  process  however  sanctioning of  projects  in  Swadesh  Darshan  scheme  is under  review. Amaravati, Srisailam and Simhachalam (Andhra Pradesh) are the sites identified for development under PRASHAD scheme.The details of projects sanctioned to various States/UT including Andhra Pradesh under ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘PRASHAD’ schemes are annexed.*******
