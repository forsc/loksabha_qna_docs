                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1252






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






New Schemes to Promote Tourism




Sumalatha Ambareesh














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has introduced new schemes to promote tourism in the country; (b) 	if so, the details for the last three years, year wise; (c) 	the total financial allocation made and the amount spent on these schemes during the said period along with its details, year-wise and scheme-wise; (d) 	whether the Government has set any target to create job opportunities under the tourism sector and if so, the details thereof; (e) 	the number of old tourist centres updated and developmental works done for new tourism centres along with its details, State-wise and place-wise; and (f) 	the number of new tourism centres planned to be developed by the Government in coming three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	Ministry of Tourism promotes India as a holistic destination including its various tourism products. Activities are undertaken for promoting varied tourism products and destinations of India in the Domestic and International markets under the Domestic Promotion and Publicity including Hospitality (DPPH) and Overseas Promotion and Publicity including Marketing Development Assistance (OPMD) schemes.  In addition to the existing schemes, Government of India has recently launched the Champion sectors in Services Scheme to give focused attention to 12 identified Champion Services Sectors for promoting their development, and realizing their potential including ‘Tourism and Hospitality Services’ sector.  The Tourism Sectoral Plan under the Champion Services Sector Scheme includes the following components: •	Creation of Tented Accommodation in Buddhist Circuit and Marketing & Promotion of these destinations and accommodations.•	Extending Incentives for improving air connectivity.•	Capacity Building Programmes in & around Iconic Sites (Language training of Tourist Facilitators)•	Incentives to Tour Operators in emerging markets.•	Promotion of MICE Tourism.EFC has approved a total budget of Rs.535.40 Crores for implementing the above component.  Ministry of Tourism has projected a budget outlay of Rs.123.00 Crore in the Budget Estimates of the Ministry of Tourism under the Champion Services Sector Scheme for the financial year 2020-21 for implementing the identified tourism components.As per estimation in accordance with 3rd Tourism Satellite Account (TSA) the share in employment in the tourism sector for the year 2018-19 is 12.75%. (Direct- 5.56% and Indirect- 7.19%).(e) & (f):	Ministry of Tourism launched two major schemes, Swadesh Darshan – Integrated development of theme-based Tourist Circuits and PRASHAD- Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation drive for development of tourism infrastructure in the Country including historical places and Heritage cities.  Sanction of projects under Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD scheme is a continuous process. The projects under the schemes are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier.The details of the project sanctioned and released under Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD Schemes are placed at Annexure-I and II.*******
