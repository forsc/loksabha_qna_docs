                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4439






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






PLI Scheme for Telecom Sector




Shivkumar Chanabasappa Udasi













                                                    Anurag Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the PLI Scheme in the Telecom Sector has been rolled out and if so, details thereof; (b) 	whether any special provision for the MSME sector has been made in the Scheme and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the details of the number of domestic and global eligible applicants for the Scheme; (d) 	the projected investments in the Telecom Sector due to the Scheme; (e) 	the financial outlay reserved for this scheme; (f) 	the companies that have been eligible and given approval under the PLI Scheme; and (g) 	the other steps taken/being taken by the Government to reduce dependence on other countries for importing telecom and networking products?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)	The Government has notified Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme on 24.02.2021 to promote Telecom and Networking Products manufacturing in India within overall financial outlay of Rs.12,195 Crore over 5 years.(b) 	The Scheme Guidelines has following provisions for MSME Category:i.	The Scheme stipulates a minimum investment threshold of Rs. 10 Crore for MSME and Rs. 100 Crore for non MSME applicants.ii.	For MSME category, there is financial allocation of Rs. 1,000 Crore out of   Rs. 12,195 Crores over a period of 5 years.iii.	Higher incentives are provided for MSMEs as compared to NON MSMEs in first 3 years.(c)	Total 31 applicants have been approved under the Scheme, out of which 16 are MSME, 8 Non-MSME (Domestic) and 7 Non-MSME (Global).(d)	The projected investment proposed in the telecom Sector under the PLI Telecom Scheme is Rs. 3,344.90 crores.(e)	The financial outlay reserved for this scheme over the scheme period is Rs. 12,195 crores.(f) 	31 Companies have been selected under the PLI Scheme for manufacturing of telecom and networking products in India are detailed at Annexure. (g) 	To reduce dependence on other countries for importing telecom and networking products, the Government has taken steps like imposition of basic custom duty (ranging from 10-20%) on certain identified telecom products and notified Public Procurement (Preference to Make in India) Order on 29.08.2018.
