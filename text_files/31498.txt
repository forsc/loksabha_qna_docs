                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2662






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Ensuring Benefit of MSP




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds allocated and expended along with State-wise details, in 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 for the Pradhan Mantri Annadata Aay Sanrakshan Abhiyan (PM-AASHA) scheme, which was explicitly instituted to ensure that all farmers get the benefit of Minimum Support Price (MSP);(b)	the funds allocated and expended along with State-wise details inn 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 for the Price Support Scheme which meant for MSP operations; and(c)	the number of centres in which the market price has been lower than MSP in the 23 crops under MSP, month-wise details?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ??????? ?????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Under Pradhan Mantri Annadata Aay Sanrakshan Abhiyan (PM-AASHA) scheme, funds are not allocated to States. Government has provided Government Guarantee amounting to around Rs. 40,000/- cr. to lender banks for availing cash credit facility by Central Nodal Agencies i.e. NAFED & FCI for procurement of pulses, oilseeds & copra at Minimum Support Price (MSP) under Price Support Scheme (PSS). However, the annual budget is allocated towards meeting reimbursement of losses incurred, in undertaking PSS operation by Central Nodal Agencies for onwards crediting the same into the concerned cash credit account of PSS.Further, funds are also allocated for reimbursement of losses incurred in the implementation of Prices Deficiency Payment Scheme (PDPS) and Pilot Private Procurement & Stockiest Scheme (PPSS) under PM-AASHA. The details of fund allocated under PM-AASHA since 2019-20 to 2021-22 are as under: Schemes	Revise Estimate(Rs. in cr.)	Revise Estimate(Rs. in cr.)	Budget Estimate(Rs. in cr.)	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22PSS/MIS	2531.80	1681.10	1500.50PDPS+PPSS	321.00	300.00	400.00 Further, the budget estimates can be revised as per the requirement.(c) : 	MSP price of an agriculture commodities is based Fair Average Quality (FAQ). The price information captured of various markets does not incorporate the quality Standard with price.*****
