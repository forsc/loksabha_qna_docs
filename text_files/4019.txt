                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3783






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Implementation of PMFBY




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	 the  details  of  the  success accomplished in the implementation of the Pradhan  Mantri  Fasal  Bima  Yojana (PMFBY) in the State of Odisha;(b)	  the  number  of  farmers  insured under this scheme in Odisha;(c)  	the  kind  of  crops  for  which insurance claim has been demanded under this  scheme  and  the  insurance  amount disbursed so far in this regard in the last two years; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d):	With a view to provide a simple and affordable crop insurance scheme to ensure comprehensive risk cover for crops against all non-preventable natural risks from pre-sowing to post-harvest, and to provide adequate claim amount and timely settlement of claims, a yield based scheme namely Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) has been launched from Kharif 2016 by the Government. These schemes are optional for the States and they have to select insurance company, notify the crops & areas and provide past yield data to fix threshold yield and actual yield of current season to settle the claims under the schemes.  Odisha is implementing PMFBY since inception of these schemes.  Details of coverage including faremrs insured and claims paid to eligible farmers since inception of the scheme in Odisha under PMFBY are Annexed. All food and oilseed crops and commercial/horticultural crops can be notified under PMFBY subject to availability of past yield data based on requisite number of Crop Cutting Experiments   and capacity   of the States to  conduct the requisite number of CCEs for each crop and insurance unit area.  Details of crops notified by Odisha since inception of the scheme in Kharif 2016 till Rabi  2018-19 under PMFBY are given below:Season	Crops notified by Odisha under PMFBYKharif 2016	Paddy, Groundnut, Cotton, Giger & TurmericRabi 2016-17	Paddy, Groundnut, Mustard, Potato, Black Gram and Green GramKharif 2017	Paddy, Groundnut, Maize, Cotton, Arhar, Giger & TurmericRabi 2017-18	Paddy, Groundnut, Mustard, Black Gram, Green Gram, Potato, Sugarcane, Onion and SunflowerKharif 18	Paddy, Maize, Groundnut, Red Gram, Cotton, Ginger and Turmeric  Rabi 2018-19	Paddy, Groundnut, Blackgram, Greengram, Potato, Mustard, Sunflower, Onion and Sugarcane
