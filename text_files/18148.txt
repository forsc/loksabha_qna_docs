                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Funds for Namami Gange Programme




Shardaben Anilbhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds sanctioned by the Government under Namami Gange Programme during the year 2014-15 to 2019-20; (b) the quantum of funds spent till date, year and State-wise; (c) the details of the projects initiated under the said Programme and the projects which have been completed till date, State-wise; and(d) the cost of the above projects, project-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI  & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA) (a) Government of India has launched the Namami Gange Programme in 2014-15 for conservation of river Ganga and its tributaries with the total budgetary outlay of Rs.20,000 crore for the period from 2014-15 till 2020-21. The final allocation for Namami Gange Programe from the financial year 2014-15 to financial year 2019-20 was Rs.12,324.86 crore.(b) The details of amount released to State Governments /State Programme Management Groups/CPSUSs/ Other Executing Agencies from the financial year 2014-15 to financial year 2020-21 (till 31 July 2020), Year-wise and State-wise are annexed as Annexure-I.(c) & (d) Under the Namami Gange Programme (NGP) for conservation and pollution abatement of the River Ganga and its tributaries, a total of 315 projects have been sanctioned at a cost of Rs.28,854 crore. The projects were sanctioned for various activities such as sewerage infrastructure, industrial pollution abatement, Ghats & Crematoria, River Front Development, River Surface Cleaning, Institutional Development, Biodiversity Conservation, Afforestation, Rural Sanitation etc. Out of 315 projects, 132 projects have been completed and rest of the projects are at various stages of implementation. The cost incurred as on 31.7.2020 on these projects under NGP is Rs.9066 crore.
