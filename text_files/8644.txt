                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2085






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Gas Supply to Power Plants in Bihar




Rama Devi













                                                    Giridhari Yadav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether IGL is supplying gas to power plants in Bihar;(b) if so, the details of said power plants;(c) the power plant-wise details of volume of gas supplied during the last three years;(d) whether less gas is being supplied to these plants against the requirement;(e) if so, the reaction of the Government thereto; and(f) the details of corrective measures taken by the Government in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) Ministry of Power has informed that no gas based power plant is located in the State of Bihar. Therefore, gas supply by Indraprastha Gas Limited (IGL) to power plants in Bihar does not arise.(c) to (f) Does not arise in view of answer at (a) & (b) above.
