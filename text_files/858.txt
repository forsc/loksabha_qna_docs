                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

879






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Performance of BSNL




Kaushalendra Kumar













                                                    Anto  Antony
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   whether public sector Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) is on the verge of closure and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor along with the steps taken by the Government to strengthen BSNL;(b)  whether the Government has any statistics regarding existing BSNL landline and mobile phone connections and if so, the details thereof;(c)  whether BSNL is providing new landline and mobile connections and if so, the details thereof including the number of such connections provided by BSNL during each of the last three years and the current year;(d) whether the Government communication systems are getting affected due to poor performance of BSNL and if so, the details thereof including the loss incurred by BSNL during each of the last three years;(e)  whether the Government has any record regarding number of employees working under BSNL and if so, the details thereof; and(f)   whether the employees of BSNL are not getting their salary regularly and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)	 No proposal of closure of Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) is under consideration.(b) 	Details of existing landline connections and mobile connections of BSNL as on 31.03.2019 are as under:<pre>Landline Connections 	1.12 croreMobile Connections 	11.57	crore</pre>(c) 	Number of new landline and mobile connections provided by BSNL during the last three years and upto 31.05.2019 during 2019-20 are as under:<pre>	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20(upto 31.05.2019)Landline connections	11,82,630	8,90,520	7,19,228	95,063Mobile connections	2,50,19,494	2,54,99,981	2,16,48,088	35,90,444</pre>(d) 	No such case is reported where Government communication systems are getting affected due to poor performance of BSNL.  However, losses incurred in the last 3 years are as follows:<pre>	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19(unaudited)Losses in Rs. crore	4793	7993	7714</pre>(e) 	As on 31.05.2019, a total of 1,63,902 employees including 46,597 Executive and 1,17,305 Non Executive employees are working in BSNL.(f) 	The salary to BSNL employees is being paid regularly.  However there was some delay in the salary paid for the month of February, 2019.
