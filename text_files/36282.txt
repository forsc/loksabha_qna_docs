                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3433






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Demand of Steel




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of STEEL be pleased to state: the details of the demand and production of steel in India since 2014, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL		   (SHRI RAM CHANDRA PRASAD SINGH)Details of Production of crude steel along with the production and consumption of total finished steel in India from FY2014 to FY2021 are given as follows:-Year	Crude steel	Total Finished Steel	Production(in MT)	Production(in MT)	Consumption(in MT)2013-14	81.69	75.16	74.092014-15	88.98	81.86	76.992015-16	89.79	81.90	81.522016-17	97.94	91.54	84.042017-18	103.13	95.01	90.712018-19	110.92	101.29	98.712019-20	109.14	102.62	100.172020-21	103.54	96.20	94.89Source: Joint Plant Committee (JPC)********
