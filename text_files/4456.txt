                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3991






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Sanctioned Posts of Judges




Jasbir Singh Gill














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the details of total number of sanctioned posts of judges and their total number of vacancies in Punjab and Haryana High Court; andb)	the details of total number of sanctioned posts of judges and their total number of vacancies in subordinate courts of Punjab? 






ANSWER




ANSWERMINISTER OF LAW AND JUSTICE, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a): As on 01.07.2019, the sanctioned strength of Punjab & Haryana High Court is 85 Judges with 35 posts vacant at present. (b): As on 30.06.2019, the number of sanctioned posts of Judicial Officers and vacant posts of Judicial Officers in Subordinate Courts of Punjab are 675 and 89 respectively.
