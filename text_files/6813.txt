                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

517






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






New Railway Lines




Bharti Pravin Pawar














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	   whether any announcement has been made in the last budget for new rail route between Manmad-Malegaon-Dhule-Nardana-Indore and Nashik-Pune;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the details of work undertaken so far regarding the said proposed rail route?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c) : No, Sir. There was no announcement in the last budget for new rail route between Manmad-Malegaon-Dhule-Nardana-Indore and Nashik-Pune.However, Indore-Manmad new line project (368 km) was included in the pink book 2016-2017 subject to necessary approvals and sanction. The proposal of Indian Port Railway Corporation (IPRCL), a Public Sector Undertaking (PSU) under Ministry of Shipping, to execute this work under joint venture model of Participative Policy of Ministry of Railways (MoR), was approved in 2018. A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) associating Jawaharlal Nehru Port (JNPT) and state Government of Madhya Pradesh & Maharashtra has been entered into.	Pune-Nashik route has been identified as a Semi-High Speed route by the State Joint Venture Company of State of Maharashtra and MoR for execution.*****
