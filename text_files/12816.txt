                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1614






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Suicide by Students




Nitesh Ganga Deb













                                                    Shivkumar Chanabasappa Udasi
                                                








                                                    S. Jothimani
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether as per the National Crime Record Bureau (NCRB) there is an increase of studentsuicide including SC/ST/OBC students in the country in recent years;(b) if so, the details during the last three years, State-wise including Karur district of TamilNadu;(c) whether the Government has prepared any road map to tackle this problem; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) & (b): Category wise data regarding number of suicides by students in the country is notmaintained by the National Crime Record Bureau (NCRB). The state-wise details of suicides bystudents from the year 2016 to 2018 is at Annexure. District-wise information is not maintainedseparately by NCRB.(c) & (d): Under Samagra Shiksha, there is a provision for supporting States and UTs oninterventions related to Guidance and Counselling in schools. Funds are provided to the States/UTs toencourage teachers to function as the first level counsellors in schools. Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti(NVS) has adopted best practices for emotional, psychological and physical well being of the students.It has appointed Counsellors in the JNV schools for emotional and physical wellbeing of the children.In line with the National Curriculum Framework (NCF), 2005, National Council ofEducational Research And Training (NCERT) has developed textbooks that cover the aspects ofstress, anxiety and other related problems in schools.*****
