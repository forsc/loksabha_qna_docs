                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3717






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Street Lighting in Villages




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government provides funds to Gram Panchayats for street lighting in villages, if so, the details thereof during the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra; (b) whether the Government has demanded a utilisation certificate of funds from the concerned authorities and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether any actual target for fund utilization has been fixed for the said purpose, if so, the details thereof and the achievements made thereon, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra;(d) whether any time-frame has been fixed for providing street lights in all the villages in the country; and (e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	       Under the Fourteenth Finance Commission (FFC) award for the period of 2015-2020, grants to the tune of Rs. 2,00,292.20 crore are allocated to Gram Panchayats (GPs) constituted under Part IX of the Constitution in 26 States including Maharashtra. The grant is provided to GPs for delivering basic services including street-lighting. State-wise status of allocation and release of FFC grants for the last three years and the current year is given at Annexure. Funds are not directly provided to Gram Panchayats in Union Territories for the purpose.(b)	     Release of subsequent installments of the grants under FFC award is subject to submission of utilization certificates for the previous installments drawn along with compliance of other stipulated conditions.(c)	 to (e)  The GPs incur expenditure on basic services as per their approved Gram Panchayat Development Plan (GPDP) based on local felt needs and priorities.******
