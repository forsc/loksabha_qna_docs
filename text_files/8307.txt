                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1883






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Nepali Language Radio or TV




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is a dedicated 24-hour Nepali language radio or TV channel operated by Prasar Bharati; (b)	if not, the reasons therefor; and (c)	the recent steps if any taken by the Government to launch 24-hours radio as well as TV channel in Nepali language?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) to (c):  Prasar Bharati has informed that it does not have dedicated 24-Hour Nepali language Radio or TV channel. However, it broadcasts programmes of various duration of Nepali Language from its AIR stations at Kurseong, Siliguri, Gangtok, Gorakhpur and Guwahati, and also by External Service Division of AIR Delhi.Further, programmes in Nepali Language are also being telecast on Doordarshan Channels namely, DD Bangla, DD North-East, DD Arunprabha, DD Gangtok and DD Dehradun from time to time.***********************
