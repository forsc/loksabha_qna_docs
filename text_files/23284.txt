Tax Collection on Petrol, Diesel and Gas




Thirumaa Valavan Thol














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the amount of taxes collected on petrol, diesel and gas (Fuel) for both the Centre and State from the financial year 2013- 14 to 2019-20 and for the ten months from April, 2020 to January, 2021; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the taxes collected on petrol, diesel and gas, as a percentage on the Budget of the relevant years, have increased or decreased; and (d) if so, details thereof for the years from 2014-15 to 2019-20 and for the ten months from April, 2020 to January, 2021?
