                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2441






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Kisan Call Centre




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of calls registered by Kisan Call Centre (KCC) in the last three years including the current year, State-wise;(b)	the funds allocated/released and utilized for the development, maintenance and capacity building of the centre during the afore-mentioned period;(c)	whether it is true that the KCC is available only  in 22 official languages; and (d)	if so, the steps taken/being taken by the Government to extend the KCC services to the target beneficiaries who are unable to access the services due to language barriers?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????   (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	In the last three years, including the current year, a total of 17,467,074 calls have been registered by Kisan Call Centre. State-wise and year wise details are given in Annexure-I. (b)	The funds allocated and amounts released under KCC during the last 3 years and current year upto 28.07.2021 is given below: S. No.	Year	Funds earmarked (Rs.  in lakh) 	Expenditure incurred(Rs. in lakh)1	2018-2019	2900.00	2730.462	2019-2020	3320.00	3320.003	2020-2021	3976.00	3958.654	2021-2022(upto 28.07.2021)	3900.00	581.92Total	14096.00	10591.03(c)	Replies to the farmers’ queries are given in 22 official languages. The details of languages in which replies to the farmers queries are given at different locations in the country are given at Annexure-II.(d)	The Government has already increased the locations of Kisan Call Centres from 14 to 21 centres since September, 2018. Replies to the queries of farmers are given in all the 22 major languages used by farmers across the country.
