                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1574






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Telecom Facilities in Dadar and Nagar Haveli




Mohanbhai Sanjibhai Delkar














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether tele communication facilities are available for people living in tribal and hilly regions of Dadra and Nagar Haveli; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) 	whether arrangements have been made for setting up of towers in hilly regions; (d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (e) 	the total number of telecom consumers in hilly regions during each of the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (d)	Yes, as per data collected from the Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) and Department of Telecommunications (DoT) field units in 2019, all the inhabited villages of Dadra and Nagar Haveli are covered with mobile services. There are total number of 802 Base Transceiver Stations (BTS) radiating and providing telecommunication services in Dadra and Nagar Haveli.(e) 	Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) has stated that it does not have data specific to tribal & hilly regions of Dadra and Nagar Haveli. However, the total number of Wireless and Wireline subscribers in Dadra and Nagar Haveli during each of the last three years is as below:<pre>Union Territory	As on 31st March 2017	As on 31st March 2018	As on 31st March 2019	As on 30thSeptember 2019DadraandNagar Haveli	Wireless	Wireline	Wireless	Wireline	Wireless	Wireline	Wireless	Wireline	608,434	3,880	646,149	3,990	582,616	4,076	442,786	1,995Total	612,314	650,139	586,692	444,781</pre>
