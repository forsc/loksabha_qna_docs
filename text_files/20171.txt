                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:-(a)	whether the Government has setup and strengthened common service centres to provide services to citizens more effectively:(b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	whether any such service centres have been set in Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh;(d)	if so, the details thereof, district-wise and if not, the reasons therefor;(e)	whether the number of service centres in Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh is less than other states; and (f)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b): Yes, Sir. This Ministry is implementing Common Services Centers (CSC-2.0: A Way Forward) project under pillar-III of Digital India. CSC e-Governance Services India Ltd. (CSC Special Purpose Vehicle- CSC –SPV) being the implementing agency of CSC 2.0 is implementing the scheme, in consultation with State Governments/Union Territory (UT) Administrations. The objective of this project is to set up at-least one self-sustainable CSC per Gram Panchayat across the country. As on 31st December, 2020, the overall (including urban & rural) number of functional CSCs are 3,73,827 out of which 2, 78,048 CSCs are functional at Gram Panchayat (GP) Level. These Common Services Centers (CSCs) are providing nearly 400 services electronically to the citizens, at their locality and promoting entrepreneurship by encouraging youths, rural population, marginalized people and encouraging women empowerment for their livelihood. The State/UT wise list of functional CSCs is placed as Annexure-I.(c) and (d): Yes, Sir.
