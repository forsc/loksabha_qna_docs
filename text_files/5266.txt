                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4905






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Expenditure on Border Fencing




Vincent H Pala













                                                    Abdul Khaleque
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amount sanctioned and spent for the International Border Fencing between India and Bangladesh during the last five years;(b) the total distance to be fenced on Indo-Bangladesh Border and other borders and the distance fenced so far along with the time by which all fencing work are likely to be completed, border-wise;(c) the number of infiltrators who have infiltrated through the fencing along the Indo-Bangladesh border during the said period; and(d) the status of the schemes for Indo-Bangla Border during the said period and the reasons for delay thereof, if any?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	The details of amount sanctioned and spent for the International Border Fencing between India and Bangladesh during the last five years are as under:Sno.	Year	Amount Sanctioned (Rs. in Crore)	Expenditure (Rs. in Crore)1.	2014-15	274.13	274.182.	2015-16	175.00	156.243.	2016-17	100.00	97.484.	2017-18	200.00	201.955.	2018-19	408.58	408.63Total	1157.71	1138.48(b):	The details of fencing along Indo Bangladesh & Indo Pakistan border are as under:(Length in Kms)Border	Length of International Border	Fence sanctioned	Fence Completed	Work in progress	Balance	Probable date of completionIndo-Bangladesh Border	4096.70	3326.14	2803.013	169.64	353.487	December, 2020Indo-Pakistan Border	2289.70	2069.046	2004.66	64.38	-	March, 2022(c):	Border guarding forces conduct regular patrolling, lay nakas and establish observation posts and carry out anti tunneling exercise to stop illegal infiltration. -2/…-2-LS.US.Q.NO.4905 FOR 23.07.2419However, some illegal migrants are able to enter in a clandestine and surreptitious manner, mainly due to difficult riverine terrain in parts of international border with Bangladesh which are not amenable to physical fencing. As such, the accurate number of infiltrators is not available. However the details of infiltrators who were apprehended along Indo-Bangladesh border during the last 5 years w.e.f. 01.01.2014 to 31.12.2018 and current year up to 30.06.2019 is as under:-  India-Bangladesh Border Border State	Year	2014	2015	2016	2017	2018	2019 (upto 30.06.2019)West Bengal	2260	3296	1875	992	900	379Assam	22	8	7	11	11	6Meghalaya	64	57	63	47	39	11Mizoram	8	0	0	3	3	7Tripura	101	65	130	122	165	94Total	24553426207511751118497 (d):	The schemes for creation of border infrastructure works like fence, road, floodlight and BOPs are in progress and their expected date for completion is December, 2020.*****
