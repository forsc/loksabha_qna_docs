                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4803






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Resumption of Train Operations




 Bhagirath Chaudhary













                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                








                                                    Devji Mansingram Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the number of various categories of passenger trains, express trains, mail, superfast and MEMU train services which were running in the country before lock down, zone and division-wise; (b)	the number of special trains that started running again after lockdown;  (c)     whether these trains contain unreserved general class coaches;(d)    if so, the time by when the remaining trains are likely to resume their operations as before the lockdown period;(e)    whether stoppages at several railway stations have been removed during the resumption of the operation of the trains, if so, the details and the reasons therefor; and(f)     the details of the railway stations/trains on which the stoppages have been removed after resumption of the operation of trains, division-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a)	to (f)  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.****
