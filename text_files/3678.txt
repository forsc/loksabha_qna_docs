                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3218






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Researches done in the Field of Science




Akshaibar Lal














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to include researches done in the field of science within the country and abroad in the newly launched portal of the Government named ‘sampurn’;(b)	if so, the details thereof and other science related information likely to be included in this portal;(c)	whether patent facility is likely to be provided for researches being undertaken on different science subjects are also likely to be included on this portal; and(d)	if so, the terms and conditions in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE; MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY; AND MINISTER OF EARTH SCIENCES(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)????????? ?? ?????? ?????? ??????; ??????? ?? ???????????? ??????; ?? ?????? ??????? ????????. ???? ?????(a)	No Sir. There is no Portal named Sampurn launched by Government. However, a comprehensive portal named India Science Technology and Innovation (ISTI) portal has been recently developed by Vigyan Prasar, an autonomous body  of Department of Science and Technology (DST). This is  a one-stop information portal about science, technology and innovation  in India. The information offered includes Research being carried out in India, Funding, Organisations, Programmes and Schemes, Scholarships and Fellowships and Innovation.The portal includes scientific research projects done in the country (projects that got completed  by 2017 and beyond and also the ongoing research). Research collaborations of Indian scientists with other countries are also included.  Content on the portal are continuously being updated.(b)	Details of the S&T research projects like project title, area of focus, funding and implementing agencies, Principal Investigator involved with the relevant contact details are being provided. Also project duration, budget etc, are being provided on the portal wherever available. The collection, categorization, processing, formatting and updating of information is a continuous ongoing process. Scholarships and Fellowships related to S&T available for various stages from school level to senior scientists and for various groups like women, SC &ST are also included in the portal.(c)	No Sir.(d)	Question does not arise.                                                  ********
