                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3279






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Closure of SAIL Ancillary Companies




 Jai Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Public Sector Undertaking, Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL), has closed some of its ancillary companies in Uttar Pradesh and Jharkhand;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and(c)	the financial statements of these closed ancillary companies?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)to(c): As ancillary companies are set up around Steel Plants for supply of materials or services to Steel Plants by private entrepreneurs, Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL) does not take any decision regarding it.
