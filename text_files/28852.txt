                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

282






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Agricultural Techniques




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any scheme is being run by the Government to provide information to the farmers regarding latest agriculture techniques; (b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the total number of farmers benifitted under said scheme in the State of Uttar Pradesh, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE	 	 (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b)	The Government has set up a network of Krishi Vigyan Kendras (KVKs) in the Country to provide information to the farmers about latest agriculture techniques. The activities of KVKs include On-farm testing to identify the location specificity of technology under various farming systems; Frontline demonstration to establish the production potential of improved agricultural technologies on the farmers; fields; Capacity development of farmers for knowledge and skill up gradation; providing farm advisories using ICT and other media means on varied subjects of interest to famers; and production of quality seeds and planting materials for availability to the farmers. In order to develop awareness of improved agricultural technology among the farmers, KVKs take up a large number of extension activities.(c)	District wise number of farmers benefitted under different activities of KVKs in the State of Uttar Pradesh during 2020-21 is given in Annexure-I.*******
