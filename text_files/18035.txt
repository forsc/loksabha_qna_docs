                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1765






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Coal Production




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether company office is established in the location of Mahanadi Coalfields Limited (MCL) which produced 20 million tonnes of coal and if so, the details thereof;(b)	if not, the reasons for not setting up any office in Talcher by MCL even after producing 70 million tonnes of coal; and (c)	whether the Government has directed the concerned authority to take necessary action in this regard and if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a):  Company Offices are established as per requirement and business needs of the Company for efficient operation. (b):  MCL has many offices in Talcher Coalfields for efficient operation.(c):   Not applicable in view of part (a) and (b) above. * * * *
