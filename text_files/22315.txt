                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3260






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Agro Processing Clusters




Lallu Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has approved a number of projects to create infrastructure for agro-processing cluster (APC) under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Kalyan Yojana; (b) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (c) 	the steps taken by the Government to reduce wastage of surplus produce and add value to the horticulture/agricultural produce? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a) to (c): Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) has not approved any project under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Kalyan yojana. However, MoFPI has been implementing the Scheme for Creation of Infrastructure for Agro Processing Clusters (APC) under the Umbrella Scheme Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) to incentivize setting up of agro processing clusters in the country. Till date, Ministry has approved 56 agro processing cluster projects under the scheme. The State/UT wise number of projects approved under the scheme are at Table A. Further, MoFPI has been implementing various schemes namely Mega Food Parks, Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure, Creation/ Expansion of Food Processing/ Preservation Capacities (Unit Scheme), Infrastructure for Agro-processing Clusters, Creation of Backward and Forward Linkages, Operation Greens under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) to reduce wastage of surplus produce and add value to the horticultural / agriculture produce.
