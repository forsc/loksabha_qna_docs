                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PLANNING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4011






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Services of Foreign Consultancy Firms




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





PLANNING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether NITI Aayog is hiring the services of foreign consultancy firms; (b)	if so, the details of the firms hired during the last three years along with the works/projects undertaken by each of them;(c)	the money spent by NITI Aayog on each of these firms, company-wise; and(d)	the manner in which NITI Aayog exercises oversight on the works undertaken by such firms?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION(RAO INDERJIT SINGH)(a) to (d): Hiring of services by NITI Aayog is primarily from organisations having distinct legal status in India. However, some of the firms hired have global lineage in terms of foreign shareholding or global headquarters being in a foreign country or brand name usage. The details of firms and amount sanctioned/spent in the last three years are given in the Annexure.
