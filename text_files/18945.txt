                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

409






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






PM Wani Project




Sukanta Majumdar













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Nisith Pramanik
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has proposed to launch Prime Minister Wi-Fi access network interfaces (PM Wani) and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Department of Telecommunication (DoT)has constituted five working groups with industry representation to minimise hiccups in rolling out PM Wani and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government is targeting a four-fold increase in public Wi-Fi access points to 2 million by the end of 2021 and if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	the other steps being taken by the Government in this regard so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)  Yes, Sir. The proposal of the Department for Proliferation of Broadband through Public Wi-Fi Networks named as Prime Minister Wireless Access Network Interface (PM-WANI) framework has been approved by the Cabinet in its meeting held on 9/12/2020. As per the  PM-WANI framework, it is envisaged that last mile broadband connectivity, will be through a network of public Wi-Fi access points. The backhaul requirement for these Wi-Fi access points to be met by internet bandwidth from the telecom service providers/internet service providers.  Under the distributed architecture and unbundling of functions, the WANI eco-system consists of following players: •	Public Data Office (PDO): To establish, maintain, and operate WANI compliant Wi-Fi Access Points and deliver broadband services to subscribers. •	Public Data Office Aggregator (PDOA): An aggregator of PDOs and perform the functions relating to Authorization and Accounting. •	App Provider:  To register users and discover WANI compliant Wi-Fi hotspots in the nearby area and display the same within the App for accessing the internet service.•	Central Registry: To maintain, in accordance with the WANI architecture and specifications, the details of App Providers, PDOAs, and PDOs. (b)    No, Sir. However, five industry led working groups are working on various aspects of the framework.(c)    At present about 5 lakh Wi-Fi access points are working in the country. Setting up of public Wi-Fi access points by entrepreneurs under the PM-WANI framework will further enhance the deployment of Wi-Fi access points. (d)    A detailed guidelines have been issued by the Department for operationalising the PM WANI framework. An online system for free registration of entities (PDOAs and App Providers) has been implemented by the Department. The registration of a PDOA will provide it permission for pan India operations. ************
