                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1514






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Overseas Employment to Youth




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is implementing special Skill Development Scheme to train the Youth for overseas employment and if so, the details thereof?(b)	whether these trained youth have gained employment abroad with these efforts of the Government;(c)	if so, the number of such trained youth who got employment during the last three years;(d)	the names of agencies which are providing assistance to the Government in Skill Development Programme; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c) 	 Yes sir, the Government of India is implementing various programmes for training people for overseas employment. This includes training people in domain skills, language skills as well as pre-departure orientation & soft skills. These programmes are currently targeting Gulf countries (under India International Skill Centre (IISC) and 1-day Pre-Departure Orientation Training (PDOT)) and Japan (under Technical Intern Training Program (TITP)) as destinations for overseas employment. The trained youth have been provided employment through the efforts of the government and its accredited partners.	In the pilot phase of IISC program, 63 candidates have been provided overseas employment. Under 1-day PDOT program, 48,000 prospective emigrants have been trained. Under TITP program, 44 candidates have been sent to Japan as technical intern. (d)&(e)	The 12 training organizations which have been participating in the IISC pilot phase are: (i) Empower Pragati (ii) IL&FS Skills Development Corporation (iii) Mahendra Skills (iv) Prayas Juvenile Aid Centre (JAC) Society (v) SynchroServe Global Solutions (vi) Force 10 Protection Services (vii) Orion Edutech (viii) Quivan Skills (ix) Labournet (x) Espoir Academy (xi) Gulf Enterprises (xii) Social Action For Welfare And Cultural Advancement (SWACA).
