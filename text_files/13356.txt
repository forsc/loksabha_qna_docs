                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2232






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Rail Connectivity to Hisar Airport




Brijendra Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                              Will the Minister of RAILWAYS be pleased to state the time by which the rail connectivity from proposed new airport at Hisar, Haryana to New Delhi is likely to be taken up?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) At present Rail connectivity from New Delhi to Hisar is available. There is no proposal/sanctioned work with Ministry of Railways to connect Hisar airport to New Delhi as on date. However,  feasibility study for the  work of rail connectivity to Hisar Airport from Raipur Railway Station (near Hisar Railway Station), has been taken up by Haryana Rail Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited, a Joint Venture Company of Ministry of Railways and Government of Haryana on the request of Civil Aviation Department, Government of Haryana.  Report of the same has been submitted to Government of Haryana for their acceptance. *****
