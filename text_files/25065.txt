                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

88






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Quality Educational Institutes




Rakesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the country lacks world-class educational institutes;(b) whether foreign students rarely come to India to study and the the Indian students prefer to study abroad;(c) whether the Universities and educational institutes in the country lack basic facilities, resources and teachers; and(d) if so, the corrective steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to provide quality higher education in the country?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) to (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *88 FOR 26.07.2021 ASKED BY HON’BLE MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT SHRI RAKESH SINGH REGARDING QUALITY EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTES(a) No, Sir. The Government is committed to improvement of quality education and institutions in the country and has established a large number of premier institutions and Institutions of National Importance which includes the Indian Institutes of Management, Indian Institutes of Technology, Indian Institutes of Science Education & Research, National Institutes of Technology, Indian Institutes of Information Technology and Central Universities. Further, twelve institutions have been approved as Institutions of Eminence (IoE) under the scheme ‘World Class Institutions’ launched in the year 2017 to have world class academic & research facilities. In the recently released QS World University Rankings-2022, 9 IoEs have secured rank in top 1000 of global educational institutions. Apart from this, 26 other institutions i.e. total (9+26) 35 have also featured in the QS World University Rankings-2022 as against 29 last year. (b) As per the All India Survey on Higher Education (AISHE), the number of foreign students enrolled in higher education has increased from 42,293 from 164 countries (2014-15) to 49,348 from 168 countries (2019-20). (c) & (d) No, Sir. University Grants Commission as a part of its mandate determines and maintains standards of teaching, examination and research in universities and issues rules, regulations and guidelines from time to time.*****
