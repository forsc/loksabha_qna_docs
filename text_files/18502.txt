                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2184






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Drug for Breast Cancer




Prathap Simha














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether AIIMS has successfully tested a coded ayurvedic drug for improving quality of life of breast cancer patients and if so, the details thereof; and(b) whether the said drug is effective in curing the disease and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a):	Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS) in collaboration with All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi has conducted a clinical research study for evaluating Quality of Life with a coded Ayurvedic formulation named as ‘AYUSH QOL-2C’ in patients of local non-metastatic Breast Cancer treated with Adjuvant Chemotherapy/Radiotherapy and correlating it with Molecular Marker.  It was a prospective double blind randomized placebo controlled pilot study with enrolment of 60 patients, which revealed a trend towards better local control and survival in the Ayush QOL- 2 C group and the drug was safe and well tolerated.(b):	No such effectiveness of AYUSH QOL-2C was observed in the study.
