                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

866






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Supplementary Nutrition Programme




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    	the funds allocated by the Government to the State Governments for implementing the Supplementary Nutrition Programme during the last ten years; (b) 	whether the State Government of Meghalaya submitted the utilisastion statement for the last ten years and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the amount of rice and wheat which have been released by the Union Government to Assam during the said period; (d)  	whether the Government has taken note that one lakh bags of rice ware seized from a private warehouse on Assam-Meghalaya border, if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	whether any enquiry has been conducted in this regard and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a): The details of funds allocated by the Government to the State Governments for implementing the Supplementary Nutrition Programme during the last ten years are at Annexure-I.(b): Yes. The State Government of Meghalaya had submitted the utilization certificate/statement of expenditure for the last ten years and details are placed at Annexure-II.(c):   Details of the amount of rice and wheat which have been released by the Union Government to Assam are available for last 7 years and details are placed at Annexure-III.(d) & (e) The Department of Food & Public Distribution has forwarded a News Article “Mysterious and non-mysterious silence” published in the English Daily Meghalaya Times on 12.06.2021.
