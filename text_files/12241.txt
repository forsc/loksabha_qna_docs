                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1086






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Mandakol Reserved Forest




Rajmohan Unnithan














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken any step to finalise the interstate boundary of Mandakol Reserved Forest between the states of Kerala and Karnataka based on Traverse Sheets as recommended by the Commission of Survey Settlement and Land Records, Bengaluru; and (b)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) to (b) As per the information provided by the Government of Karnataka,there is a Commission of Survey Settlement and Land Records, pertaining to finalisation of the survey of interstate boundary of Mandakol Reserve Forest between States of Karnataka and Kerala. However, there is no correspondence regarding the recommendation by the Commission of Survey Settlement and Land Records, Bangalore to finalise the interstate boundary of Mandakol Reserve Forest between State of Kerala and Karnataka based on Traverse Sheet. Government of Karnataka has reported that a meeting was held on 15.11.2010 by the Deputy Commissioner Mangaluru, wherein decision was taken for joint verification of Karnataka-Kerala interstate boundary involving Deputy Director Land Records (DDLR), Dakshina Kannada District and Deputy Director Survey and Land Records(DDSLR), Kasargod as Team Leader. Government of Kerala has reported that survey will be conducted after getting original sketch of the Mandakol Reserve Forest.	***
