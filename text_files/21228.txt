                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2391






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana




Sanjeev Kumar Singari














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2391TO BE ANSWERED ON 09.03.2021PRADHAN MANTRI ADARSH GRAM YOJANA 2391.  DR. SANJEEV KUMAR SINGARI: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government is implementing Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAGY) in Kurnool Parliamentary Constituency; (b) if so, the details of villages adopted under this programme; (c) the eligibility criteria for adoption of any village under PMAGY; and(d) the details of funds released for villages under PMAGY in Kurnool district of Andhra Pradesh during the last two years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. A total of 11 villages have been selected in Kurnool District of Andhra Pradesh under Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAGY) out of which the following two villages fall under the Kurnool Parliamentary Constituency:S No.	Name of village	Name of Mandal1	Chagi	Adoni2	Enugubala	Yemmiganur (c):  The villages having population of 500 or more with Scheduled Caste population more than 50% are eligible for selection under the PMAGY scheme (based on 2011 census data).(d): In the last two years i.e. 2018-19 and 2019-20, the Government of India has released an amount of Rs.112.91 lakh for the eleven villages selected in Kurnool district.*****
