                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2857






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Fratricide Cases in Armed Forces




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the number of fratricide cases reported in the armed forces during the last three years;(b) whether the Government has taken steps to prevent such incidents; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  Details of instances of fratricide in the three services of the Armed Forces in the last three years are given as under:-Year	Army	Navy	Air Force2017	01	Nil	Nil2018	01	Nil	Nil2019	02	Nil	01(b) & (c):  Yes, Sir.  On the basis of the findings of the studies conducted by Defence Institute of Psychological Research (DIPR), the Government has taken several steps towards stress amelioration amongst troops which include deployment of trained psychological counsellors, improvement in the quality of food and clothing, training in stress management, provision of recreational facilities, buddy system, leave concessions, approachability of seniors, facilities for movement of troops from border areas and establishing a grievance redressal mechanism at various levels, etc.*****
