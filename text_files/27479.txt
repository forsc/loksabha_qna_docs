                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3002






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Anganwadi Workers and Anganwadi Helpers




Rahul Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether Anganwadi Workers and Anganwadi Helpers are receiving additional incentives for the additional COVID related tasks carried out by them;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether her Ministry has received complaints regarding violence against Anganwadi workers during the pandemic;(d)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon;(e)	whether a significant number of vacancies for Child Development Project Officers (CDPO) and Anganwadi Lady Supervisors are lying vacant across the country; and(f)	if so, the details thereof and the measures initiated to fill the backlog of vacancies, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTRY OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) & (b)   As Anganwadi Services is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, which is implemented by States/UTs. Government has issued directions to all States/UTs to make all possible arrangements for providing safety equipment like masks and sanitizers etc. to Anganwadi Workers, Anganwadi Helpers and ASHAs in the COVID-19 pandemic situation.  Also,  Anganwadi Centres are closed due to Covid-19 pandemic so as to control the spread of virus. Further, the Government has enhanced the honorarium w.e.f. 01.10.2018 to Anganwadi Workers (AWWs) from ? 3,000/- to ? 4,500/- per month; AWWs at mini-AWCs from ? 2,250/- to ? 3,500/- per month; Anganwadi Helpers (AWHs) from ? 1,500/- to ? 2,250/- per month; and introduced performance linked incentive to AWHs @ ? 250/- per month.Apart from the honorarium and other incentives paid by the Government of India, respective State/UTs are also giving monetary incentives to these workers. Various State Governments are compensating these AWWs/ AWHs out of their own resources for COVID related activities.(c) & (d) No State has reported any such incident to this Ministry. However, it is the duty of the State administrations to ensure  safety and security of AWWs/ AWHs.(e) & (f) The State/UT-wise details of positions sanctioned, in-position and vacant with respect to CDPOs and Supervisors is placed at Annexure I.From time to time, States/UTs are advised to fill the sanctioned positions of staff which are lying vacant for effective implementation of the Scheme.
