                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1468






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Pending Maintenance Works




V. Kalanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   the details of long pending maintenance works including yard remodelling, repair and re-girdering of old bridges, doubling and electrification of railway lines and renewal of scissor crossovers within Tamil Nadu;(b) the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized for these works within Tamil Nadu during the last three years;(c) the details of the number of works that have been completed so far; and(d) the steps being taken by the Government for timely completion of the pending works?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY ANDCONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.******
