                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

484






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Funds for Cluster Development Programme




Hemant Patil













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government had earmarked Rs. 450 crores for the cluster development programme during 2017-18 to 2020-21, and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government intends to continue earmarking funds for the cluster development programme, and if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of employment generated at each cluster during the last three years, State-wise;(d)	whether the Government intends to prioritise cluster development in certain industries to create export hubs and if so, the details thereof; and(e)	whether there are proposals to operationalise developed clusters in Maharashtra and if so, the details thereof, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) earmarked Rs. 184.00 crores, Rs. 279.00 crores, Rs. 227.90 crores and Rs. 390.69 crores for the year 2017-18, 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 respectively under “Micro and Small Enterprises Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP)”.(b):	Budget Estimate for 2021-22 is Rs. 156.50 crores under MSE-CDP. (c):	Under MSE-CDP, Common Facility Centre is set up for enhancing the productivity and competitiveness as well as capacity building of Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs). Job creation in the cluster depends on various factors such as aggregate domestic & global demand, seasonality etc. in and around the clusters.(d):	MSE-CDP is a demand driven scheme and proposals are received through respective State Governments duly recommended by them.(e):	13 Common Facility Centres (CFCs) and 5 Infrastructure Development (ID) projects have been completed in Maharashtra under MSE-CDP. District-wise details are enclosed at Annexure.
