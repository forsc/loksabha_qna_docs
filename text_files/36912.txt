                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4133






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Beautification of Places under Bardhaman Purba Constituency




Sunil Kumar Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to beautify the four most beautiful tourism places under Bardhaman Purba Lok Sabha Constituency in West Bengal i.e. Kalna, Katwa, Raina and Purbosthali as new tourism spots in India; (b) 	if so, the details thereof, along with the future initiatives in this regard, State/UT-wise; and (c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM                 	        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a):	The Ministry of Tourism has not received any proposal in this regard. (b) and (c):		The Ministry of Tourism under Schemes of Swadesh Darshan and National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD) provides financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory Administrations for tourism infrastructure development with their consultation and subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier etc. The details of the projects sanctioned  under  Swadesh Darshan  and PRASHAD  schemes in the state of  West  Bengal  is  as under:-<pre>Scheme 	Name of project	Sanction year	Amount sanctioned (Rs. In Cr.Swadesh Darshan 	Development of Beach Circuit: Udaipur- Digha- Shankarpur- Tajpur- Mandarmani- Fraserganj-Bakkhlai- Henry Island	2015-16	67.99 PRASHAD	Development of Belur 	2016-17 	30.03 </pre>The Ministry of Tourism has now revamped the ‘Swadesh Darshan’ scheme for sanctioning of new projects in the country, following a destination and touristic centric approach.********
