Funds for Dispensation of Social Justice




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.  1213TO BE ANSWERED ON 09.02.2021FUNDS FOR DISPENSATION OF SOCIAL JUSTICE 1213.      DR. UMESH G. JADHAV: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) the funds allocated by the Ministry specifically for improving the dispensation of social justice in the country during the last three years; (b) the amount of funds utilized in this regard during the said period; (c) whether any review has been undertaken by the Government to know the utility of its schemes, plans and programmes during the said period; and(d) if so, the details and outcome thereof?






ANSWER
