                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

295






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






COVID-19 Cases among Healthcare Workers




Brijendra Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the estimated number of healthcare workers, including doctors, nurses and lab technicians in the country, State/UT-wise; and (b) the number and details of healthcare workers who contracted COVID-19 between March, 2021 and June, 2021?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a)	& (b) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. 295* FOR 17TH DECEMBER, 2021(a) & (b) As on 14th December 2021, a total of 1,04,09,651 Health Care Workers including doctors, nurses and lab technicians are registered on Co-WIN portal. The State/UT-wise details are at Annexure-1.    Data on Healthcare Workers who contracted COVID-19 is expected to be maintained by respective States/UTs and are not maintained Centrally. Government of India has given first priority to Health Care Workers for COVID-19 vaccination so that they are conferred full protection provided by vaccine against COVID-19. The Covid vaccination in India started on 16th January 2021 and the first group taken up for this vaccination is the Healthcare Workers.*****
