                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3568






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Supply of Steel to Railways




Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil













                                                    Kapil Moreshwar Patil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of production and supply of various products by the Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL) to the Railways during the last three years;(b)	whether SAIL has entered into any agreement with the Railways for production of special steel for rail tracks and coaches and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether SAIL plans to set up a coach manufacturing plant in a joint venture with the Indian Railways and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the details of progress made in this regard so far?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)	The details of production and supply of various products by Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL) to the Railways during last three years has been as under:-Total Production:(in ‘000 ton)FINANCIAL YEAR	RAILS (UTS 90)	WHEELS	AXLES	FLAT PRODUCTS	OTHER LONG PRODUCTS	STAINLESS STEEL	ALLOY STEEL2016-17	649.3	24.7	2.4	7394	5566	111	1092017-18	902.7	23.8	2.0	7480	5474	85	1062018-19	985.0	20.8	2.4	7903	5934	99	126Supply to Railways:(in ‘000 ton)FINANCIAL YEAR	RAILS(UTS 90)	WHEELS	AXLES	FLAT PRODUCTS	OTHER LONG PRODUCTS	STAINLESS STEEL	ALLOY STEEL2016-17	620	23.4	2.3	64.7	9.4	25.1	26.72017-18	874	24.3	2	64.5	11	10.6	22.42018-19	945	21.2	2.4	54.2	8.1	6.4	30.9(b)	   SAIL and Indian Railways signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) on 1st February, 2003 for supply of Rails. The main features of the MoU are mentioned below:-Contd……….2/- -: 2 :-?	Railways commit to buy from SAIL its total requirement of long rails/ long rail panels, as also the balance of its normal requirements in other lengths like 13m, 26m, etc. subject to annual review within the overall policies of Govt. of India.?	SAIL/BSP to meet the Railways’ requirement as per mutually agreed delivery schedule?	The joint pricing committee of Indian Railways and SAIL to recommend the price of rails.?	Railways to make all efforts to arrange for regular and unhindered transportation of the long rails/long welded panels from Bhilai Steel Plant to enable SAIL to meet its supply commitments.(c)	No Sir.(d)	Does not arise.********
