                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1945






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






NHs Projects in Bihar




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether funds for construction and maintenance of National 	Highways (NHs) in district Gopalganj of Bihar were already 	sanctioned but works have not been started yet;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(c)	the details and the present status of work on flyover in the 	said district and the time frame set for its completion;(d)	the details and the present status of works of NHs in the said 	district and the time frame set for completion thereof; and(e)	the reasons for delay in completion of these projects despite 	sanctioning of funds? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS[GEN. (DR.) V.K. SINGH (RETD.)](a) & (b) National Highway Nos. 227A, 85, 28 & 101 are passing through Gopalganj district in the State of Bihar. Bids have been invited for widening and strengthening of NH-227A, fund for maintenance has been sanctioned for NH-85 and maintenance work on NH-28 has already started.(c) Detailed Project Report (DPR) for construction of 4-lane elevated highway at Gopalganj Town on NH-28 has been completed. Bids are invited after finalization, appraisal and approval.(d) & (e) Work in the stretch from km 52 to km 65 of NH-101 has been started. A length of 24 km out of total length of 28 km of NH-85 in Gopalganj District has already been completed. Except 0.8 km Dumaria Ghat Bridge & 1.866 km Flyover in Gopalganj town, which are scheduled for completion within 2 years after award of work, rest portion of 4 laning work on NH-28 falling within Gopalganj district has already been completed. The maintenance works for Service road in Gopalganj and for main carriage way between Dewapur to Kotwa have already been started.*****
