                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3986






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Gender Differential Child Mortality




S. Jagathrakshakan














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government policies have been proven effective in countering the gender inequality that drives son preference bias and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the mortality rates among girls below the age of 5 years is 75 percent more than boys and if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the steps being taken by the Government to improve the sex ratio in the country?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (c)    The  Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) Scheme has been launched to address declining Child Sex Ratio (CSR) and related issues of empowerment of girls and women over a life cycle continuum.  Consistent efforts are made under BBBP at all levels to generate awareness about the rights of girl child through community engagement, stop sex selection at birth and encourage positive action to support their education, growth and development. Convergent efforts are made in collaboration with other Ministries and stakeholders. The implementation of the Scheme is monitored by the task force under BBBP at District, State and National level. The HMIS report of Ministry of Health and Family Welfare suggest encouraging trends in respect of Sex Ratio at Birth (SRB) which has been reported as 934 in 2019-20 against 918 in 2014-15.As per latest Sample Registration System Report 2018 of Registrar General of  India, the under 5 Mortality Rate for girls is 37 per 1000 live birth as compared to 36 per 1000 live birth for boys.    ****
