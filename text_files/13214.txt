                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1890






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






National Investigation Agency




Dhanush M Kumar













                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the main objective of setting up of the National Investigation Agency (NIA) and its achievement since its inception;(b) the details of branches/units of the NIA functioning at present/proposed to be set up in the country, location and State-wise;(c) the details of cases being investigated by the NIA since its inception along with the present status of the cases, State-wise;(d) whether the Government has reviewed the functioning of the NIA in the country, if so, the details and the outcome thereof; and(e) whether NIA has adequate staff strength for effective functioning and if not, the corrective steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a):     The National Investigation Agency (NIA) was created in pursuance to enactment of  NIA Act, 2008 with a mandate to investigate and prosecute terrorism related cases having national and international ramifications and other crimes affecting sovereignty, security and integrity of the country. The achievements of NIA since its inception are as under:---2--LS.US.Q.NO. 1890 FOR 03.03.2020Total cases registered by NIA	319  CasesCharge-sheet filed by NIA	237  CasesClosure report filed by NIA	  15  CasesCases transferred to State Police	  03  Cases  (b):   The details of branches/ Units of NIA functioning at present are as under:-S.No.	Hqrs/Branches	State/UT	Jurisdiction  1.	NIA Hqrs, New Delhi  	Delhi	All India including States which are not covered by any branch office.  2.	NIA Branch office, Hyderabad 	Talangana	Andhra Pradesh, Telangana & Karnataka  3.	NIA Branch office, Guwahati 	Assam	Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland & Tripura.  4.	NIA Branch office, Lucknow  	Uttar Pradesh	Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Madhya Pradesh & Bihar.  5.	NIA Branch office, Mumbai  	Maharashtra	Maharashtra, Gujarat, Goa, Daman & Diu and Dadar Nagar Haveli.  6.	NIA Branch office, Kochi    	Kerala	Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Puducherry and Lakshadweep.  7.	NIA Branch office, Kolkata  	West Bengal	West Bengal, Sikkim, Odisha and Andaman & Nicobar Islands.  8.	NIA Branch office,  Jammu  	Jammu & Kashmir	Union Territory of Jammu & Kashmir  and Ladhakh.  9.	NIA Branch office,  Raipur  	Chhatisgarh	Chhatisgarh & Jharkhand.10.	NIA Branch office, Chandigarh 	Chandigarh	Punjab, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh and Union Territory of Chandigarh.--3--LS.US.Q.NO. 1890 FOR 03.03.2020(c):    Since its inception and till date, the Central Government has entrusted 319 cases to the National Investigation Agency (NIA) for investigation. Out of total cases registered, in 237 cases charge-sheets have been filed. Judgement has been pronounced in 62 cases in which, 56 cases have resulted in conviction. Total 276 persons have been convicted in these cases. The conviction rate is 90.32%. The State-wise details of these cases is at annexure.(d) & (e):   The manpower, financial and infrastructure requirements of NIA are reviewed from time to time and steps are taken to bolster the effective functioning of NIA. * * * * *
