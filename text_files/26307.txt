                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1984






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Foster Care and Sponsorship




Srinivas Kesineni














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether there has been an increase in funding under schemes such as foster care and sponsorships to support vulnerable children impacted by COVID-19;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	the number of cases of child marriages reported and/or rescued under provisions of the Prohibition of Child Marriage Act, 2006 during the last three years, State-wise?






ANSWER




ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI  SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b): The Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 and the Model Rules framed thereunder provide for the rehabilitation and reintegration of children through sponsorship and foster-care, inter alia. The Ministry is implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme namely Child Protection Services (CPS) in partnership with the State Governments/UT Administrations for delivering the institutional and non-institutional care as stipulated under the JJ Act. As per the Scheme guidelines, an amount of Rs.2000/- per month per child is available for non institutional care (sponsorship, fostercare, aftercare) subject to the limit of Rs. 10 lakh per district.(c): As per the National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) report available at their website i.e. https://ncrb.gov.in, State-wise, case registered under the Prohibition of Child Marriage Act, 2006 during the last three year is Annexed.  ***  AnnexureAnnexure to part (c) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.1984 for 30.07.2021 by   Shri Kesineni Srinivas regarding “Foster Care and Sponsorship”.
