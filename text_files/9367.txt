                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2967






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Ocean Energy Policy




Brijbhushan Sharan Singh














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has framed any Ocean Energy Policy;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) to (c) the Government of India has not frame any Ocean Energy policy. However, the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy under its Research, Design, Development and Demonstration (RD&D) programme provides financial support to academic institutions, research and development organisations and industry for research and development in renewable energy, including, inter-alia, for developing Ocean Energy technologies. *****
