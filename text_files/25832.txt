                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

149






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Construction of New Houses under PMAY-U




Bidyut Baran Mahato













                                                    Subrata Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has approved construction of new houses under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) in the recent past and if so, the details thereof; (b)	 the details of the States selected for construction of such houses in the country; (c) 	the details of the criteria fixed for selection of States for the said purpose; (d)	 the details of funds sanctioned and released to various such States for the said purpose; and(e) 	the details of the PMAY-U houses under construction as on date in various States along with the details of funds sanctioned, released and utilized so far for construction of such houses, State/UT-wise and the time by which these houses are likely to be completed? 






ANSWER




                                                                           THE MINISTER OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                    (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) to (e): 	A statement is laid on the Table of the Sabha.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *149DUE FOR 29.07.2021 REGARDING ‘CONSTRUCTION OF NEW HOUSES UNDER PMAY-U’(a)& (b): 	Yes, Sir. A total of 23,81,293 houses have been sanctioned in 34 States/ Union Territories (UTs) under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U)during the period from 01.04.2020 to 19.07.2021. State/UT-wise details are at Annexure-I. (c):	All the States/UTs having statutory towns as per Census 2011 and towns notified subsequently [including Notified Planning/ Development Areas]are eligible for coverage in the Mission. All States/UTs except UT of Lakshadweep, which does not have a notified urban area, have signed Memorandum of Agreement (MoA) to participate in the mission by agreeing to mandatory conditions.(d)& (e):	Based on the project proposals submitted by the States/ UTs,112,95,047 houses have been sanctioned across the country. Against the total sanctioned houses, 50,08,573 have been completed and delivered to beneficiaries and remaining are at various stages of construction. The Scheme will be implemented up to 31.03.2022.States/ UTs have been advised to expedite the construction of sanctioned houses so that all houses are completed within the stipulated period.State/ UT-wise details of number of houses sanctioned, grounded, completed and amount of Central Assistance approved, released and utilised under the Scheme are at Annexure-II.                             *****
