                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

45






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Regional Language Film Festivals




Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar













                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether regional language film festivals are organized in the country; (b)	if so, the details of such festivals organized along with the total expenditure incurred for the said purpose during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise; (c) whether the Government is providing financial assistance to filmmakers whose films are selected in the competition section of international film festivals; (d) if so, the details of the financial assistance provided by the Government during each of the last three years and the current year, film-wise; and (e) the other steps taken/being taken by the Government to promote regional language films at national and international levels?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; AND MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a)	Yes, Sir. Regional language film festivals are organised in the country. (b)	The information on regional language film festivals organized till date and financial assistance provided by the Ministry of Information and Broadcasting is attached as Annexure ‘A’.(c & d)	Ministry of Information & Broadcasting is providing financial assistance to Indian Films which are selected in various International Film Festivals namely Oscars, Toronto, Bussan, Berlin etc. During the last three years financial assistance has been provided to two films in 2017-18, namely ‘Newton’ (Rs.6,68,051/-) and ‘Loktak Lairembee’ (Rs.8,70,396/-),  which were selected in the Berlin International Film Festival.e)	Every year, Ministry of Information & Broadcasting organizes National Film Awards wherein films from across the country in Hindi/Regional Languages are awarded under different categories.Also, every year, films are selected for Indian Panorama section of the International Film Festival of India (IFFI). From these entries, 47 Films, including 26 Feature Films and 21 Non-Feature films, are selected in various Indian languages. Ministry of Information and Broadcasting has started patriotic film festival to mark the 70 years of Indian Independence under the banner “70 Saal Azaadi, Yaad Karo Kurbani” as part of the Ministry of Defence programme related to Independence Day celebrations from August 12-18, 2016 at Sirifort Auditorium Complex, New Delhi.Under BRICS Film Festival world class film productions from the BRICS nations i.e. Brazil, Russia, India, China & South Africa are being screened to inspire more collaboration. The format of the festival consisted of screening two feature films from each country in the competition category and three films in the non-competition category.In addition to the Indian Panorama films, films selected in the National Film Awards are also sent for participation as well as special screening in the various Indian and International Film Festivals organized by Ministry of Information & Broadcasting through Directorate of Film Festival.Indian Panorama films are screened in IFFI as well as I various Indian and International Film Festivals and Indian Film weeks/festivals abroad.…………….
