                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4592






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Funds Allocated under BharatNet




Kaushalendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the funds allocated and spent under the BharatNet project across the country, Parliamentary Constituency-wise, particularly in the Nalanda Parliamentary Constituency; (b) 	whether high speed optical fiber network has been installed in all the panchayats of Nalanda Parliamentary Constituency under the said project; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) 	Under the BharatNet Project, funds are allocated / disbursed State/UT-Wise, and the details of the same are given in Annexure-I. (b) & (c) 	All the 249 Gram Panchayats of Nalanda Parliamentary Constituency are Service Ready under BharatNet Project.
