                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2665






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Fertilizer Vending Machine




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether fertilizers vending machines have been installed in a few State; (b) if so, the details of the States where fertilizer vending machines have been installed; (c )whether the Government proposes to install the said fertilizer vending machines in the State of Maharashtra; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a) & (b):	M/s Gujarat Narmada Valley Fertilizers & Chemicals Limited and M/s GSFC were allowed to conduct field trials on Fertilizer Dispensing Machine (FDM) in States namely Gujarat, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh & Maharashtra. M/s GNFC has informed that technical and operational feasibility of FDMs could not be established. The company has, therefore, not installed the FDMs on commercial basis. However, M/s GSFC has informed that the company has installed one machine at Vadodara and have experienced technical and operational difficulties in dispensing of fertilizer through this machine.(c) & (d):	No proposal has been received by the Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers or Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare from any company for installing FDMs in any other State.********
