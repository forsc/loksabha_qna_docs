                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2996






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Road and Bridges under Road Requirement Plan of MHA




Rita Bahuguna Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and the length of total roads constructed along with the number of bridges constructed in the country especially in Uttar Pradesh under the Road Requirement Plan (RRP) of MHA;(b) whether there are some roads and bridges planned to be constructed during the current year under the said RRP;(c) if so, the details thereof;(d) whether any timeline is fixed to complete such identified roads and bridges; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)  Out of total 240 projects sanctioned under RRP-I, 213 nos have been completed. The length completed is 5003.57 kms. In Uttar Pradesh, two nos of projects for a length of 75 km including  High Level Bridge across river Sone have been approved of which 67kms of road length has been completed and work of “Construction of High Level Bridge across River Sone at Chachicala including its approaches on Cone, Nakatwar, Ranideeh Road in Sonebhadra District of UP State”  is under implementation and targeted for completion in February 2022.(b) & (c) Yes Sir. Balance length is to be completed in States of Uttar Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Odisha and Jharkhand.(d) & (e) The target date of completion is December 2022. *****
