                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2271






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Bids for Hydrocarbon Extraction




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has considered cancelling the bids called forHydrocarbon extraction in the Protected Agricultural Zone in Tamil Nadu, as requestedby the State Government of Tamil Nadu; and(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor including the legal basisfor doing such extraction which is prohibited under the Tamil Nadu ProtectedAgricultural Zone Act, 2020?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b): The Government of India has launched Discovered Small Fields BidRound-III (DSF-III) on 10th June 2021, offering 32 Contract Areas comprising 75discoveries spread over approx. 13,204 sq. km acreage across the country forextraction of hydrocarbons. This comprises of one Contract Area with two PetroleumMining Lease (PML) of Vadatheru and Ramanathapuram covering Pudukkottai andRamanathapuram districts of Tamil Nadu. PML for these areas were granted inDec.2007 and Nov’2012 respectively. Petroleum Exploration Licence (PEL) and PMLare granted under ‘Oilfields (Regulation & Development) Act, 1948’ and the‘Petroleum & Natural Gas Rules, 1959’ by the State Government for on shore areas.No extraction is taking place now.**********
