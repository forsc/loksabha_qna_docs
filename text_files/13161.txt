                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1947






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Sugarcane Cultivation




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details regarding the total sugarcane area (million hectare), sugarcane production (million tonnes), average yield (tonnes/hectare) and total sugar production (million tonnes) from 2014 till date, State-wise; (b) 	the average sugar recovery rate from 2014 till date, State-wise; (c) 	the details regarding total molasses, bagasse and press mud and other by-products produced from sugarcane from 2014 till date, State-wise; and (d) 	the contribution of the by-products in the total revenue of integrated mills, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b): 	The State-wise details of area, production and yield of sugarcane during 2014-15 to 2019-20 (as per Second Advance Estimate) are placed at Annexure – I.The State-wise details of sugar production and sugar recovery rate from sugar season 2014-15 to 2019-20 are placed at Annexure – II and Annexure – III, respectively.(c)&(d):	Department of Food and Public Distribution (DFPD) under Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution is the nodal department under Sugar (Control) Order and Sugarcane (Control) Order, 1966 for regulating the sugar mills. As per DFPD, the details regarding quantity of by-products i.e. molasses, bagasse and presscake generated in production of sugar and the revenue realized on account of these by-products are not maintained. However, it is estimated that the molasses, bagasse and presscake are generated at the rate of about 4.5%, 30% and 3.5%, respectively on sugarcane crushed by sugar mills during each of the sugar season depending on the quality of sugarcane available for crushing by the sugar mills in each state. Further, as per Dr. Rangarajan Committee report, the revenue realization on account of all three by-products contributes to about 5% of the total revenue of individual sugar mills.
