                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4789






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






DGT and NASSCOM Entered into MoU




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that the Directorate General of Training (DGT) and NASSCOM have entered into an MoU for training youth in high tech fields;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether any time-frame work and the number of persons to be trained under the said MoU has been mentioned; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	& (b) 	Yes Sir, An MoU has been signed between Directorate General of Training (DGT), Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship and National Association of Software Services Companies (NASSCOM) on 27th April, 2018. NASSCOM is working with DGT for training in emerging technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Virtual Reality, Robotic Process Automation, Internet of Things, Big Data Analytics and 3D Printing, to address the futuristic need of the Industry (Industry 4.0).Under this MoU, following new age trades/courses have been introduced under Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS) from the session started August, 2018. Internet of Things (Smart City)   	One Year	Level-44.	 Smartphone Technician Cum App Tester	6 months	Level-3(c)	& (d)	Yes, Sir. Details of new age trades / courses, introduced under CTS in the ‘National Skill Training Institutes (NSTIs)’ are Annexed.
