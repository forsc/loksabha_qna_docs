                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3318






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Development of Dawki Integrated Check Post




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total funds sanctioned for the development of Dawki Integrated Check Post (ICP) and the funds released so far during the last four years, year-wise;(b) the funds budgeted for Dawki for the year 2021- 2022;(c) whether there is any delay in completion and operationalization of Dawki ICP and if so, the reasons for the same;(d) the volume of trade through the Dawki Land Customs Station(LCS) in terms of export and import during the last five years, year-wise; and(e) the total number of cargo vehicles and total number of passengers passing through Dawki during the said period, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	The approved project cost for construction of ICP and accommodation for staff meant for ICP, Dawki is Rs. 115.73 crore. Details of funds released during the last four years, year-wise, are as follows:                                                                         (Rs. in crore)2017-2018	--2018-2019	2.682019-2020	1.982020-2021	4.32-2-L.S.US.Q.NO. 3318 FOR 16.03.2021(b):	Funds are provided as “Grants-in-Aid” to the Land Ports Authority of India (LPAI), the body for establishment and operation of ICPs. Further, fund for a particular project is released by LPAI based on the progress of the project. In the Budget Estimates for financial year 2021-2022, “Grants-in-aid” of Rs. 200.00 crore has been provided to LPAI for creation of capital assets. No separate funds have been budgeted for ICP, Dawki in the financial year 2021-2022.(c):	Though the tender for construction works had been awarded on 30.09.2016, the project got delayed initially due to issues related to construction within 150 yards of international boundary and later due to delay in grant of statutory permission for tree cutting and hill excavation. Finally, as the contractor failed to execute the work, the contract was cancelled in January, 2019.          After observing all the codal formalities, a fresh tender has been awarded in November, 2020.  The construction activities have commenced.   (d) & (e):  As per information received from Department of Revenue, Ministry of Finance, details of volume of trade in terms of import and export; number of cargo vehicle and passengers passing through LCS, Dawki, Meghalaya, during the period 2015-16 to 2019-20 is given in Annexure.  *******
