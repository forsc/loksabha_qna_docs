                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

522






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Spares Contract for IAF AN-32




S. Ramalingam














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Ukraine has requested for investigation against a Dubai-based company regarding alleged kickbacks for spares contracts for the IAF AN-32 fleet of transport aircrafts;(b) if so, the details and the status thereof; and(c) whether the involvement of the company is also alleged in influencing the procurement of 100 pieces of self propelled 155 mm/52 Cal. K9 Vajra Gun Howitzer tracked system contract awarded to L&T and its role in other ongoing contracts under discussion with companies from RoK and if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)	& (b):  The request for legal assistance for criminal proceedings was asked by NAB (National Anti Corruption Bureau) Ukraine vide criminal proceeding No.5201000000000889 to Ministry of Defence.  As per the request, M/s STE has signed an agreement with M/s Global Marketing SP Ltd whereby latter was requested to provide service to former relating to the implementation of the contract Air HQ/82891/32/Eng D3(T) (AN-32 RE/64-2014-18)/STE-1-171-K/KE-14 dated 26th November, 2014.  Ministry of Home Affairs has approached MEA to seek details of contract between M/s Global Marketing SP Ltd & M/s STE so as to invoke contractual provisions as applicable.(c)  At present Department of Military Affairs has no such information.*****
