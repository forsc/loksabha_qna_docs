                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2174






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Loan from JICA




P.P. Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of FINANCE be pleased to state:(a) 	whether the Ministry is considering to facilitate loan from Japanese International Cooperation Agency (JICA) on the proposal sent by the State Government of Rajasthan for building a Parallel Carrier System to Rajiv Gandhi Lift Canal from Indira Gandhi Canal (RGLC Phase-III); (b)	if so, the details thereof along with the progress made so far in this regard; and (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (c) : A project proposal submitted by the Government of Rajasthan titled ‘Parallel Carrier System to Rajiv Gandhi Lift Canal from Indira Gandhi Main Canal (RGLC Phase-III)’ seeking external assistance of Rs. 1,163.20 crore from Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA)  has been  included in the JICA Rolling Plan based on the recommendations of Central Line Ministries and NITI Aayog.  During the process of the technical appraisal of the Detailed Project Report, the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs has, on 23rd February, 2021, submitted its comments / observations along with revised cost estimates for compliance by the State Government, which have been conveyed to the State Government.  ****
