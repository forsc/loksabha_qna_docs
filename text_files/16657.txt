                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

367






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






PMAY in Assam




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) has been meeting its stated construction targets in the State of Assam and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	if not, the reasons therefor;(c) 	the number of houses constructed in Assam, particularly in the districts falling under Nagaon Parliamentary Constituency, since the launch of the programme; and(d) 	the quantum of funds utilized in the said State, particularly in the districts falling under Nagaon Parliamentary Constituency, during the last three years, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b)   To achieve the objective of “Housing for All” by 2022, the Government is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) w.e.f. 1st April, 2016 in rural areas and Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) since 25th June, 2015 in urban areas of the country. Under PMAY-G, the State of Assam has a target of 5,15,857 houses for constructions, out of which 4,43,644 have been sanctioned and 3,17,076 houses have been completed as on 11.09.2020.             Under PMAY-U, a demand survey was conducted by the State of Assam. As per the survey,  the total housing demand under four verticals of PMAY-U Mission is 2.19 lakh, out of which, 1,22,293 have been sanctioned and 20,895 have been completed.
