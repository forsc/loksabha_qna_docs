                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

535






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Defence Capital Procurement




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the quantum of capital orders placed to Indian and Foreign vendors under Defence capital procurement in FY 2019-20 and FY 2020-21;(b) the quantum of orders placed on DPSUs and Prívate Industry respectively, amongst the orders placed on Indian Companies;(c) the break-up of funds used for committed liabilities and placement of new contracts under Capital procurement budget in the last five years, and whether the defence capital budget is sufficient to meet the requirement;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) whether the Government is undertaking initiatives to bring parity while evaluating competitive bids for defence contracts between DPSUs and private sector entities, given that the government has historically invested in DPSUs and Ordinance Factories (OFs) as seen in the past decades and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a)  During last financial year (2019-20), 38 contracts have been signed with Indian vendors and 32 contracts have been signed with Foreign vendors.  During current Financial Year 2020-21 (Upto July, 2020), 10 contracts have been signed with Indian vendors and 6 contracts have been signed with Foreign vendors for capital procurement of defence equipment for Armed Forces.(b)  Out of the total of 48 capital acquisition contracts signed with Indian vendors from   April 2019 to July 2020, 18 contracts have been signed with DPSUs/PSUs/OFB/DRDO/ISRO and 30 contracts have been signed with Indian Private vendors.(c) & (d):  All efforts are made to optimally use budgeted funds for meeting committed liabilities relating to already concluded capital acquisition contracts and for new schemes contracted during the year.	The details of expenditure incurred under Capital Acquisition (Modernisation) of Armed Forces, under Defence Service Estimates (DSE), for the last five years is as under:-Year	Actual Expenditure(Rs. in Crore)2015-16	62,235.542016-17	69,280.162017-18	72,732.202018-19	75,892.852019-20	91,128.74(e)  While evaluating competitive bids for Defence contracts between DPSUs and Private Sector entities, no advantage is given to DPSUs.  The competitive bids are evaluated based on the RFP terms and conditions.*****
