                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1469






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Procurement of Paddy




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has taken any steps to pay the balance of amount claimed by the State of Kerala in respect of the procurement of paddy from farmers, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (b) 	whether the Government intends to review the present criteria for the allocation of funds for procurement of paddy which is solely based on the quantity of rice distributed through the Public Distribution System (PDS) in the State; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a):	Release of Food Subsidy to Decentralized Procurement Scheme (DCP) States including Kerala is a continuous process. Funds to DCP States are released by Department of Food and Public Distribution (DFPD) as per admissible claim of State Government, Monthly Expenditure Plan (MEP)/ Quarterly Expenditure Plan (QEP) and allocated budget. The following admissible amount has been released to Government of Kerala during Financial Years 2019-20 & 2020-21 (upto August 2020):Financial Year	Amount released(in rupees crore)2019-20	469.302020-21 ( As on 31.08.2020)	696.61(b) & (c): 	Yes Sir, Department of Food and Public Distribution, in consultation with concerned departments and State Governments, is exploring other methods of providing food subsidy to DCP States for procurement of paddy and wheat.****
