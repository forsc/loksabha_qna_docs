                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1754






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Solar Charkha Mission




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the main objective of Solar Charkha Mission; and (b) 	the manner in which the people are being trained in the rural areas of Gujarat particularly in the tribal areas under the said mission?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a):	 The aims and objectives of Mission Solar Charkha are:(i)	To ensure inclusive growth by generation of employment, especially for women and youth and sustainable development through solar charkha cluster in rural areas.(ii)	To boost rural economy and help in arresting migration from rural to urban areas.(iii)	To leverage low-cost, innovative technologies and processes for substance.(b):	Under Capacity Building of Mission Solar Charkha, the scheme envisages training courses for the spinners, weavers, stitchers, skilled artisans and other involved in the garmenting unit as a total cost of Rs. 0.595 crore per cluster over a period of two years.In Gujarat State, one project has been sanctioned under Mission Solar Charkha. No training has been conducted so far.
