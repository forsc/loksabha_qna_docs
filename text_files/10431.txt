                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3724






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Project in Tezpur




Pallab Lochan Das














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the status of schemes sanctioned in the last 10 years in Tezpur Constituency;(b) 	the details of the funds sanctioned till date, year-wise for the last three years;(c) 	the present status of the schemes sanctioned and new schemes launched, if   any, along with the details thereof; and(d) 	the status of old and new projects in Tezpur Constituency?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Independent Charge) of the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region[Dr. Jitendra Singh] (a)	In the last 10 years, four (04) projects costing Rs. 3935.69 lakh were sanctioned under Non-Lapsable Central Pool of Resources (NLCPR) scheme and six (06) projects costing Rs. 3561.66 lakh were sanctioned under the schemes of North Eastern Council in Tezpur Constituency.(b)	The status of funds released during the last three years 2016-17 to 2018-19 is as under:- <pre>Year	Funds released (Rs. in lakh)2016-17	718.992017-18	777.752018-19	271.14Total	1767.88</pre>(c) and (d) Three (03) projects under NLCPR scheme and two (02) projects under the scheme of NEC have been physically completed.
