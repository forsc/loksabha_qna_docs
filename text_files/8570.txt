                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2148






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Diversion of Unutilised  Cess




Ramya  Haridas














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the amount of cess collected by Government during the last five years and the current year, year-wise;(b) the amount of cess, out of cess collected that remained unutilised during the said period, year-wise;(c) whether the unutilised cess so collected for specific schemes like Swachh Bharat were being diverted for other purposes and schemes;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the status of the unutilised amount of cess at present?






ANSWER




                                                        MINSTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (e): The amount of cess collected and utilized thereof, during the last five years and the current year is at Annexure. The accounts of the Union Government for 2018-19 are still under finalization. However, information on estimated collection of cess in RE 2018-19 and BE 2019-20 is indicated in the Annexure.  Further, the amount of cess released to specific schemes through dedicated reserve fund depends on the absorptive capacity of the Department to spend in a year.  ****
