                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

820






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Training to Women SHGs




Sarmistha Sethi














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the training is being provided to members of women Self Help Groups (SHGs) for implementation of various projects under his Ministry; (b) if so, the details thereof including the number of women already given training during the last three years, State-wise, including Odisha; and (c) the details regarding the financial assistance provided by the Government for imparting such training to members of women SHGs?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU)(a) & (b) Yes. The  North Eastern Regional Institute of Water and Land Management (NERIWALAM), Ministry of Jal Shakti provided training to members of women SHGs under the Human Resource Development and Capacity Buildig (HRD&CB) Scheme  from 2018-19 to 2020-21. The details of trainings are as follows:              Total Programmes conducted-10 Nos.            Total Participants-474 Nos            State-wise participants: Assam-393, Meghalaya-47, Sikkim-34             (Details enclosed as Annexure) (c) NERIWALAM is fully funded by the Department of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation as under:I.	2018-19         Rs. 1175.35 LakhII.	2019-20         Rs. 820.00 LakhIII.	2020-21         Rs. 545.28 Lakh The financial assistance for imparting training to women SHGs was a part of actual expenditure on overall training.*****     ANNEXUREAnnexure referred to in reply to part (a) & (b) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.820 to be answered on the 02.12.2021 regarding ‘TRAINING TO WOMEN SHGs’.
