                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1956






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






AYUSH Medicines under CGHS




Sunil Kumar Singh













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    Pratima Bhoumik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of  HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE  be pleased to state: (a) whether it is a fact that medicines prescribed by AYUSH doctors/ practitioners are not available to the CGHS beneficiaries, specially in the State of Maharashtra; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has any plan to fully cover the AYUSH medicines under CGHS, if so, the details thereof; and (d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):    Formulary AYUSH medicines are generally available at CGHS AYUSH Dispensaries /Units. If any particular medicine is not readily available in Ayurveda, Unani and Homeopathy, the same is indented through Authorized Local chemist (ALC) in Delhi. For Siddha Dispensaries, which are functional in Delhi and Chennai, there is no ALC. There is no ALC in other Cities, including the State of Maharashtra. The AYUSH medicines which are not available in CGHS Wellness Centres outside Delhi, the same may be purchased by the CGHS beneficiaries and reimbursement claimed thereof.(c) & (d):    CGHS normally provides AYUSH medicines as per the approved formularies. Any other specific medicine prescribed by the doctor can be claimed for reimbursement by the beneficiary. In Delhi, CGHS also procures them through ALCs.……………….
