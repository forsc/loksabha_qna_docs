                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

266






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Grant for Development of Horticulture




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any provision is likely to be made by the Government to increase the amount of grant for development of horticulture and increasing its area under the National Agricultural Development Scheme, if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether the Government proposes to make any kind of changes in the rules for this purpose, if so, the details of the same;(c) 	whether the Government proposes to provide grant under the National Agricultural Development Scheme to set-up horticulture clusters; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d): 	Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY)/ National Agricultural Development Scheme (NADP)  provides flexibility and autonomy to the States in planning and implementing State specific interventions across agriculture and allied sectors as per their needs and priorities under this scheme for the holistic development of agriculture and allied sectors. Therefore, State-specific interventions under the scheme are approved by the State Level Sanctioning Committee (SLSC) headed by the Chief Secretary of the concerned State Government which is the empowered body to approve projects for implementation under this scheme in their respective State. Activities related to development of horticulture and increasing its area are already eligible for funding under  Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY). At present, this Ministry has no proposal to provide grant under RKVY to set up horticulture clusters. ********
