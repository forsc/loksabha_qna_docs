                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4858






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Scholarship for SC Students




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of students belonging to Scheduled Castes and the amount of scholarship approved for them every year for higher studies and training in foreign countries during the last three years; (b) the rate and amount of scholarship given to the residential and non-residential students for college education every year; and (c) the number of hostels of boys and girls with residential capacity thereof and the number of hostels required, district-wise in proportion to number of such students?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) The Ministry implements several Scholarship Schemes for the welfare of the Scheduled Castes Students, including a National Overseas Scholarship Scheme. The Details of number of students provided Scholarships under these Schemes and the amount released for them during last three years is in Annexure-1.(b)The rates and amount of scholarship given to all students are mentioned in Annexure-2.(c) A Scheme, namely, Babu Jagajivan Ram Chhatrawas Yojna (BJRCY), is being implemented by the Ministry across the country. Under the scheme eligible implementing agencies are provided central assistance for fresh construction of hostel buildings, for expansion of the existing hostel facilities and for periodic repair and maintenance of the hostels constructed under this Scheme. 469 girls hostels and 316 boys hostels have been sanctioned across the country from 2007-08 to till date.Implementing agencies, including State Governments send proposals for construction of hostels under these schemes as per the requirement assessed by them. Central Government sanctions construction of hostels depending upon availability of funds. No separate data on the District-wise requirement of hostels is maintained in this Ministry.****
