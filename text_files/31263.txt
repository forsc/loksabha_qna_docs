                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3140






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Irregular Supply of LPG




Khan Saumitra














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received any complaints regarding irregular supply of LPG to the consumers by the LPG distributors in various parts of the country including in the State of West Bengal, if so, the details thereof, State and company-wise; (b) the details of the action taken against the LPG distributors found guilty during the last three years and the current year, State and company-wise; (c) whether any incident has come to light regarding black marketing by the distributors and if so, the details thereof; and (d) the details of the action taken by the Government against the distributors found involved in such kind of activities?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (d) Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) receive various complaints which include irregular supply of LPG to consumers and black marketing by the LPG distributors across the country including State of West Bengal. In all established cases of irregularities/malpractices, action is taken against the distributors as per the provisions of Marketing Discipline Guidelines (MDG)/Distributorship agreement. State/UT-wise and company-wise details of established cases of diversion, pilferages, over charging, delay in refill supply and non-home delivery during the last three years and current year (till September, 2021) are at Annexure.
