                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has implemented the Integrated Child Development Scheme (ICDS) across the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof during the last five years along with the present status of the implementation of the said schemes, year-wise, State-wise including Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu and Telangana;(c)	the details of women and children benefited under the scheme, district-wise, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu; and (d)	the details of funds allocated, sanctioned, approved, released and utilized under the said schemes during the last five years and the current year; State/UT-wise, district-wise, year-wise and scheme-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	Yes, Sir.  Presently, Anganwadi Services Scheme under Umbrella ICDS is implemented across the country through a network of 7075 projects and 13.79 lakh Anganwadi Centres.  (b)	State-wise (including Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu and Telangana) and year-wise number of sanctioned and operational AWCs during the last five years is at Annexure-I.(c)	The services are currently being provided to 795.87 lakh beneficiaries of which 640.22 lakh are children under six years of age and 155.65 lakh are pregnant women and lactating mothers. Pre-school education is provided to 284.94 lakh children of 3-6 years of age (146.60 lakh are boys and 138.34 lakh girls). The State-wise details of number of women and children benefited under the scheme is at Annexure-II. (d)	The State/UT-wise details of funds allocated, released and utilized under Anganwadi Services Scheme during the last five years and the current year is at Annexure-III.  The funds are released to States/UTs for implementation of the scheme.
