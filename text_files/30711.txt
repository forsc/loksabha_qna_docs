                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2256






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Bearing Pathology Investigation Costs under Ayushman Bharat Scheme




Vivek Narayan Shejwalkar














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the beneficiaries have to bear the cost of the tests done during the treatment of many critical diseases covered under the Ayushman Bharat scheme as these tests are not included in the package of treatment;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to include such tests under the scheme






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH ANDFAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (c): Treatment packages under Ayushman Bharat -Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) are designed in a manner that the beneficiaries do not have to make any payment from their pocket. These treatment packages are very comprehensive and inclusive of the cost of all the indicative tests that may be required as part of the patient’s diagnosis and treatment. For certain expensive high-end investigations, the following procedures may be booked as “Add-on” packages.•	High-end radiological diagnostics (CT, MRI, Imaging including nuclear imaging)•	High-end histopathology (biopsies) and advanced serology investigationsUnder Ayushman Bharat -Health and Wellness Centres (AB-HWCs), diagnostic services for non-communicable diseases viz. Hypertension, Diabetes, Oral cancer, Breast cancer and Cervical cancer are provided free of cost to all citizens of the country.
