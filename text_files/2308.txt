                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2137






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Drinking Water Schemes




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of drinking water schemes in the country being funded by the Union Government or those which are in partnership with the State Governments; (b) the number of proposals which are lying pending with the Union Government along with their present status; and (c) the nature of Chambal-Bhilwara water supply project and any plans to expand this project to nearby villages of Rajsamand district?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FORJAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) As reported by States on Integrated Management Information System (IMIS), as on 30.06.2019, 25,361 schemes are being implemented by the States with funds released under National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP).(b) Under NRDWP, the proposals for rural drinking water supply do not come to the Ministry for approval. As per the extant guidelines, State Level Scheme Sanctioning Committee approves the schemes.(c)As per information furnished by State Government of Rajasthan, the Chambal-Bhilwara Water Supply project is sanctioned for drinking water supply purpose of 9 towns, 1688 villages of District Bhilwara and 23villages of District Bundi. The water demand of Rajsamand District hasnot been included in the sanctioned Chambal-Bhilwara Water Supply Project.
