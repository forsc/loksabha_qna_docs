                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2216






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






NEET




Dayanidhi Maran














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the percentage of students from Tamil Nadu who managed to clear the National Eligibility-cum-Entrance Test (NEET) conducted for medical admissions across the country; (b) 	how many of these students have cleared the exam without attending private coaching centres; (c) 	the total number of students from Tamil Nadu who managed to clear the National Eligibility-cum-Entrance Test (NEET) & secured admission in colleges within Tamil Nadu; (d) 	the details of total number of students who took up the NEET examination this year, the total number who cleared and the split-ups of percentage of CBSE, State-Board etc. students thereof; and (e) 	the total number of students from SC, ST, OBC, MBC, BC, FC & Religious Minorities communities who took up the NEET examination this year and how many of them cleared the exam, itemized by community?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT (SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a)   : 	The percentage of students, out of those who appeared in the NEET (UG)-2019, from the State of Tamil Nadu and cleared this Examination, is 48.57.(b)   :	No such data is available with the Government.(c)   :	The total number of students in Tamil Nadu who managed to clear the NEET (UG)-2019 is 59758. (d)  :	The total number of students who appeared and cleared the NEET (UG)-2019 in the country is 1410755 and 797042 respectively.  The total number who cleared and the split-ups of percentage of CBSE and other State Boards are as follows:<pre>Board	Appeared	Cleared	% ClearedCBSE	329441	250242	75.9Others	1081314	546800	50.5Total	1410755	797042	56.4(e)   : Category-wise details of students who appeared and cleared NEET (UG)-2019 are as under:-Category	Appeared	ClearedFC/General/UR	499884	286245OBC	631473	375635SC	193188	99890ST	86210	35272MBC	Not available	Not availableBC	Not available	Not availableTotal	1410755	797042</pre> *****
