                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2396






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






NH Projects in Assam




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details and the present status of the expansion projects of National Highways (NHs) in Assam;(b)	the funding mechanism of these NH projects;(c)	the details of the State Highways that have been converted to NHs in the last ten years in the State; and(d)	whether the Government proposes to convert State Highway No. 2 from Barpeta to Bongaigaon via Lengtisinga and Abhayapuri to National Highway and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	Project involving widening covering 658 km length of National Highways at an estimated cost of Rs. 10380 crore are in progress in Assam.(b)	These Projects are funded through National Highway Development Project (NHDP), Special Accelerated Road Development Programme – North East (SARDP-NE) and NH (O) schemes of this Ministry.(c)	During the last ten years, 10 State roads have been converted to National Highways and numbered as NH-117 A, 127C, 127D, 127E, 315A, 329 427, 702, 702C and 715A.(d)	No such proposal has been received from the State Government.*****
