                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1014






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Survey Conducted by ASI in Rajasthan




Balak Nath













                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any survey has been conducted by Archaeological Survey of India in all the districts of Rajasthan including Alwar district;(b)	if so, the report presented in this regard;(c)	whether the Archaeological Survey of India will conduct survey in allthe districts of Rajasthan with new methodology; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM (PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b)	Yes, Sir. Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) regularly conducts archaeological survey in Rajasthan and in the Alwar district as well. During last three years 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20, Rajgarh and Malakhera tehsils of Alwar district were surveyed wherein 78 villages were found with archaeological remains. The details are at Annexure I. (c) & (d)	Yes, Sir. Archaeological survey is a continuous process. ASI carries out it through conventional methods as well as latest scientific instruments and technologies such as GPS, GPR, GIS, Satellite Imagery, Remote Sensing, etc. wherever necessary.
