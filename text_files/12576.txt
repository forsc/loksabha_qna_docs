                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1502






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






PMGSY in Andhra Pradesh




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the length of road constructed in Andhra Pradesh under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during the last five years, district and year-wise;(b) 	the list of villages in Srikakulam which are yet to be connected through roads under PMGSY;(c) 	the time by which such villages are likely to be connected; and(d) 	the steps taken by the Government to monitor the scheme effectively?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): The length of road constructed in Andhra Pradesh under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during the last five years, district and year wise is at Annexure.(b) to (c): The unit of implementation under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) is a habitation and not a revenue village. All eligible habitations of more than 250 populations as per 2001 Census in Srikakulam district have already been provided connectivity. (d): The progress of implementation of rural roads under PMGSY is regularly monitored by way of Regional Review Meetings (RRMs), Performance Review Committee (PRC) Meetings, Pre-Empowered/Empowered Committee Meetings, Video Conference with the State. At District level, the District Development Coordination and Monitoring Committee (Disha) headed by a Member of Parliament (LS) monitors the implementation of various schemes of Government of India including PMGSY. In addition to this, special review meetings are also held by Secretary/ Additional Secretary/Joint Secretary, Ministry of Rural Development with Chief Secretaries/Principal Secretaries of the States. ***** ANNEXUREAnnexure referred to in part (a) of Lok Sabha Un Starred Question No. 1502 to be answered on 11.02.2020Length of road constructed in Andhra Pradesh under PMGSY during the five years, district and year- wise
