                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

907






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Ayurveda Treatment




Rajmohan Unnithan














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has envisaged any scheme to promote Kalari Chikitsa an ancient treatment of Ayurveda pertaining to body massage based in Kerala;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether the Government has any scheme to promote Kalari Chikitsa, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) & (b):  No, Sir. However, Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences, an autonomous body under the Ministry of AYUSH has undertaken an Intra Mural Research Project entitled "Documentation of Effective Marma Practices" at National Ayurveda Research Institute for Panchakarma, Cheruthuruthy, Kerala for documenting various Marmachikitsa practices under which certain treatment practices relating to Kalari including massage techniques have been documented. The Documentation has been published by the Council as a book entitled "Marmacikitsa – Basic Tenets in Ayurveda and Therapeutic Approaches" which was released by Ministry of AYUSH on the 7th of January 2021 at Regional Ayurveda Research Institute, Jhansi.(c): No.
