                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1539






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






PMGSY in Bihar




Rama Devi














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of roads identified for development under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in different areas/districts of  Bihar;(b) 	the targets fixed for road construction in kilometers during the current and forth coming fiscal year; and(c) 	the details of funds allocated under the aforesaid scheme for the above mentioned districts?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)        So far under various interventions/verticals (PMGSY-I, II, III & RCPLWEA) of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY),  60,866 km of road length with a project cost of Rs. 36,067 crore has been sanctioned to the state of Bihar, out of which 55,093 km road length has been constructed at an expenditure of Rs. 28,469 crore (including state share). The details of works (Roads & Bridges) sanctioned and constructed since inception till 01.12.2021 in different districts of Bihar are given at Annexure. (b)        During the financial year 2021-22, road length of 3,200 km  has been targeted for completion, out of which 972 km road length has been constructed in the State of Bihar under PMGSY till 01.12.2021. The targets for financial year 2022-23 have not yet been fixed.(c)        Funds are not released by the Central Government district-wise but to the State as a whole. Rs. 100 crore has been released to the State of Bihar during the current financial year (as on 01.12.2021).  Further, as on 01.12.2021, the State of Bihar has unspent balances of Rs. 3,215.14 crore (including state share) of the previous releases.*****
