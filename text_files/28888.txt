                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

316






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Cruelty Against Animals




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any cases relating to cruelty to animals are pending in the courts; (b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise as on date;(c)	the total number of cases out of the total cases pending during the last five years; and (d)	the details of the steps taken by the Government for the speedy disposal of the said cases?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING  (SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) & (b) There are 338 cases pending in various courts across the country. The State wise details are as under: State 	No. of Cases	State 	No. of CasesSupreme Court	44	Karnataka	15NGT (Delhi) 	4	Kerala	35NGT (Chennai)	1	Madhya Pradesh	6Andaman & Nicobar	1	Maharashtra 	42Assam 	1	Odisha 	2Bihar	5	Rajasthan 	9Delhi 	43	Tamil Naidu 	66Gujarat	6	Telangana & AP	23Haryana & Punjab	12	Uttar Pradesh	10Himachal Pradesh	2	Uttarakhand	4Jharkhand	2	West Bengal	5Total	338(c) The total number of cases are 118 which are pending since the last five years. (d) The Animal Welfare Board and the Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying is taking necessary steps by away of timely filing the counter affidavits in the all such case regularly.  *****
