                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1287






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Students Forced into Employment Due to COVID-19




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that many children are at risk of not returning to school next year due to the economic fallout of COVID-19 and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether it is also true that the economic impact of COVID-19 could push more children into employment and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has any plan to prevent children drop out of school on account of the above reason; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’) (a)  to (d)  Under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Samagra Shiksha, financial assistance is provided to the States and UTs for various activities to reduce number of dropouts, which include opening/strengthening of new schools upto senior secondary level, construction of school buildings & additional classrooms, setting up, up-gradation and running of Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalayas, setting up of residential schools/hostels, free uniforms, free text books, transport allowance and undertaking enrolment drives, residential as well as non residential trainings, Seasonal Hostels/ residential camps, etc. Financial assistance is also provided for Inclusive Education of children with special needs. Also, Mid-Day-Meal is provided to students at the elementary level of education. ****
