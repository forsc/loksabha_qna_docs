                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1196






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Nepali Language




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the number of Central Universities offering Masters and PhD level courses in Nepali language; (b) 	whether the Government is aware that the Visva Bharati the only Central University in West Bengal, where majority Gorkhas live, does not have undergraduate and post-graduate course in Nepali language; (c) 	if so, the reasons therefor; and (d) 	the steps taken by the Government for the establishment of the Centre for Promotion of Nepali Language?           






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a):	Two Central Universities are offering courses in Nepali language. Banaras Hindu University offers M.A. & Ph.D. Courses and Sikkim University offers M.A., M.Phil. and Ph.D. level courses in Nepali Language.(b) & (c): Visva-Bharati does not have undergraduate and post-graduate courses in Nepali language. The course does not exist since the inception of the University as the university did not receive any proposal to start such courses.(d): 	Central Universities are autonomous institutions governed under the provisions of their Act, Statutes and Ordinances framed thereunder. The statutory bodies of the Central Universities are competent to start new courses in any language/subject.******
