                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3936






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Nirbhaya Fund




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)    	whether the Government has made adequate budgetary allocation for ‘Nirbhaya Fund‘ scheme during the last three years and if so, the details thereof;(b)    	whether the Government has made any assessment in respect of judicious utilisation of funds provided under ‘Nirbhaya Fund’ scheme; (c)    	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (d)   	whether the Government has any proposal to ensure better utilization of ‘Nirbhaya Fund’ to check child trafficking by improving safety and security of women and the girl child; and (e)    	if so, the response of the Government thereon? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT        (SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a):      The details of budgetary allocation during the last  three years under Nirbhaya Fund are as under: Financial Year	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20Allocation(Rs. in Crore)	550.00	550.00	550.00 (b) &  (c) :  An Empowered Committee (EC) of Officers constituted under Nirbhaya Fund appraises and recommends the proposals for funding under Nirbhaya Fund and also reviews the status of implementation from time to time in conjunction with the concerned Ministries/ Departments/ Implementing Agencies. After appraisal by the EC, the concerned Ministry/ Department get / obtain approval of the Competent Financial Authority (CFA) to release the funds out of their respective budget and implement the project /scheme directly or through States/UTs / Implementing Agencies. Further, State/ UT Governments/ Implementing Agencies  also monitor at their level in respect of their respective approved projects/ schemes. Under Nirbhaya Fund, schemes/ projects worth Rs. 9288.45 Cr. have been appraised till date. An amount of Rs.5712.85 crore has been allocated and an amount of Rs.3544.06 crore has been disbursed/ released by the concerned Ministries/ Departments. (d) &  (e) : The EC has appraised an amount of Rs.100.00 under Nirbhaya Fund for ‘setting-up and strengthening of Anti-Human Trafficking Units (AHTU) in all districts of the country for preventing and assisting the victims of trafficking including child trafficking. Further, Ministry of Home Affairs which implements the project has already released the funds to the States/ UTs.*****
