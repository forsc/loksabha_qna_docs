                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4842






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Cold Storage for Turmeric




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)      whether the State Government of Telangana has sought the Union Government’s help for establishment of cold storages for storage of turmeric, if so, the details thereof; (b)    whether the Government has taken any decision on such a request, if so, the details thereof; and (c)    the stand of the Government on protection of turmeric farmers and establishment of necessary cold storages for storage of turmeric?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	 State Government of Telangana has included one cold storage project for horticulture crops including turmeric in their Annual Action Plan (AAP) (2019-20) of Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH). During 2019-20, an amount of Rs. 46.66 crores has been allocated for various activities including establishment of cold storage in Telangana under MIDH. The Government of India is committed to support the projects of State Governments as per the scheme guidelines.*****
