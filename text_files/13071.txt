                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

176






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Assistance for Banana Farmers




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether banana export was highly affected this year due to temperatures going below 10°C and affecting the banana crop ready for harvesting in the area/land to the tune of 5000 hectares out of total 46000 hectares of land in Jalgaon District of Maharashtra State; (b) 	whether the Government proposes to provide assistance for these affected farmers; (c) 	whether the Government proposes to advise the insurance companies to relax the recently amended norms for getting insurance claim to help these distressed farmers; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		 	  (SHRI NARENDRA  SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (d):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 176    DUE FOR REPLY ON 3RD MARCH, 2020.(a):	 No, Sir. Banana exports for the current period (April, 2019 to Jan, 2020) have not been adversely affected.  The exports of Banana were to the tune of 1,27,230 MT amounting to Rs. 447 crore during 2019-20 (Apr-Jan) against export of 98,044 MT amounting to Rs. 301 crore during the corresponding period of the last year. Thus, there has been an increase in banana exports to the tune of 29.7% in quantity and 48.7% in value terms. No report has been received from State Govt. regarding crops loss on account of adverse temperature. (b) to (d):	 No such proposal is  under consideration.*******
