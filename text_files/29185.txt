                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1054






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Special Marriage Act and Foreign Marriage Act




Shivkumar Chanabasappa Udasi














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to make Special Marriage Act and 	Foreign Marriage Act applicable to all couples regardless of gender 	identity and sexual orientation;(b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c) 	the remedial steps taken by the Government to modify the definition 	of marriages under the customary law keeping in view the current  	circumstances? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF LAW AND JUSTICE, (SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) to (c)         : No Sir, presently there is no such proposal.
