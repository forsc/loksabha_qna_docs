                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1100






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Cancelled Houses under PMAY due to Kisan Credit Card




Subhash Chandra Baheria














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of houses sanctioned and constructed under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) in Bhilwara Parliamentary Constituency;(b) 	whether any houses under PMAY have been cancelled after sanction;(c) 	if so, the number of the houses cancelled and the reasons therefor;(d) 	whether the houses have been cancelled due to Kisan Credit Card; and(e) 	if so, the norms therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI FAGGAN SINGH KULASTE)(a)  	Under Pradhan MantriAwaasYojana-Gramin (PMAY-G), a total of 41,051 houses have been sanctioned and out of these, 32,650 houses have been constructed as on date in Bhilwara Parliamentary Constituency. (b)&(c) Yes Sir. A total of 3 houses have been cancelled after sanction in Bhilwara Parliamentary Constituency as per the details given below:- 	          Reason for cancellationBeneficiary 1	 Already having pucca house/availed under other schemesBeneficiary 2	 Unwilling to construct houseBeneficiary 3	 Beneficiary not-traceable(d) & (e) As per Framework for Implementation of the Scheme, households having Kisan Credit Card with credit limit of Rs.50,000 or above are already excluded. None of the houses have been cancelled due to this criterion.*******
