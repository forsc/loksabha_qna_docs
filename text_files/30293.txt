                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1917






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Ethanol Plants




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya













                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the type of response meted out to the first Expression of Interest (EOI) for signing long-term agreement with upcoming dedicated Ethanol plants for supply of Ethanol;(b)	the efforts being made by the Government for setting up ethanol plants in the States having lack of ethanol; and(c)	whether there is any proposal for setting up such plants in Gujarat and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS  (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c): Oil Marketing Companies floated the first Expression of Interest on 27th August, 2021 wherein 197 bids have been received, out of which 133 bidders have been shortlisted in 19 ethanol deficit states in the country including 4 (four) bidders from Gujarat.Apart from above, Department of Food & Public Distribution has notified Interest Subvention Scheme for setting up new distilleries and expansion of existing distilleries in the country.******
