                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

284






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Setting up of Plastic Parks




 Gopal Jee Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a whether Plastic Parks are set up by the Government;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government proposes to set up a plastic park in Darbhangadistrict of Bihar for innovative management of plastic waste/recycling and foremployment generation at the grassroot/local level;(d) if so, the time by which it is likely to be set up; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(DR. MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a) to (e): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement referred to in parts (a) to (e) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No.284for answer on 17.12.2021 regarding '' Setting up of Plastic Parks''.(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. The Department of Chemicals & Petrochemicals implementsthe scheme for Setting up of Plastic Parks with an aim to develop an ecosystem withstate-of-the-art infrastructure building and enabling common facilities through clusterdevelopment approach, to consolidate and synergize the capacities of the domesticdownstream plastic processing Industry. Under the scheme, Government of Indiaprovides grant funding up to 50% of the project cost, subject to a ceiling of Rs. 40.00crore per project. The remaining project cost is to be funded by the State Government,beneficiary industries and by loan from financial institutions.So far, eight Plastic Parks have been approved and two are in the process of gettingfinal approval. These Plastic parks are located in the States of Assam, MadhyaPradesh (two Plastic Parks), Odisha, Jharkhand, Tamil Nadu, Uttarakhand andChhattisgarh currently in various stages of completion. The details of these PlasticParks are as under:1. Madhya Pradesh (Tamot): The Plastic Park at Tamot has completedphysical infrastructure and one unit is functional in the Plastic Park.2. Madhya Pradesh (Bilaua): The Plastic Park at Bilaua is at various stagesof implementation.3. Odisha (Paradeep): The work related to development of physicalinfrastructure is almost completed.4. Jharkhand (Deoghar): The work related to development of physicalinfrastructure is in progress.5. Tamil Nadu (Thiruvallur): The work related to development of physicalinfrastructure is in progress.6. Assam (Tinsukia): The work related to development of physicalinfrastructure is in progress.7. Uttarakhand (Sitarganj): The Plastic Park at Sitarganj was approved on3rd December, 2020 and currently the work related to development ofphysical infrastructure is in progress.8. Chhattisgarh (Sarora): The Plastic Park in Raipur, Chhattisgarh has beenapproved recently on 13.04.2021 and the work is yet to start.9. Uttar Pradesh (Gorakhpur): In-principle approval accorded.10. Karnataka (Ganjimutt, Mangaluru): In-principle approval accorded.(c) to (e) No, Sir. Since, no application has been received from the State Governmentfor setting up a Plastic Park at Darbhanga, the Department could not take any actionw.r.t. setting up a Plastic Park at Darbhanga, Bihar.*******
