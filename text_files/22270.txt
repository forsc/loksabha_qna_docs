                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3253






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Custodial Deaths




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of custodial deaths in the country during the last three years, including in 2020, State/UT-wise;(b) the number of cases of custodial deaths where the National Human Rights Commission (NHRC) recommended paying compensation during the above mentioned period, State/UT-wise;(c) the number of cases of custodial deaths where the NHRC recommended initiating prosecution against police officers during the above mentioned period, State/UT-wise;(d) the status of implementation of these recommendations;(e) whether the Government is considering to monitor the implementation of the DK Basu Guidelines by the States, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(f) whether the Government is considering to ratify the UN Convention Against Torture, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY) (a) to (d):	As per information received from National Human Rights Commission (NHRC) details of cases (Stat/UT wise) registered in respect of Custodial deaths  are given at Annexure-A. Details of cases where monetary --2--L.S.US.Q.NO. 3253 FOR 16.03.2021relief, disciplinary action, prosecution was recommended are given at Annexure-B.(e)	“Police and Public Order” are state subjects as per the seventh schedule to the Constitution of India and as such D K Basu guidelines are implemented by State/UT Governments. The Ministry of Home Affairs and NHRC also issues advisories from time to time to ensure protection of human rights. (f)	Ratification of the United Nations Convention against Torture would require an enabling legislation framing of which is a dynamic process and is undertaken, taking into account the views of the various stakeholders.                                    *****
