                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1441






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Cooperatrive Structure in West Bengal




Jayanta Kumar Roy














                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) 	whether the Government has any plans to strengthen the cooperative structure in the State of West Bengal, if so, the details thereof; and (b) 	whether any amount has been allocated for strengthening the cooperative structure in above mentioned State, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF COOPERATION??????? ??????      (SHRI AMIT SHAH)(a) & (b):  	The Country already has a rich cooperative heritage and a robust cooperative sector.  There are two types of co-operative structures in the country i.e. State Cooperative Societies and Multi-State Cooperative Societies. The Multi-State Cooperative Societies come under Central Government and the State Cooperative Societies, including the cooperative societies active in the state of West Bengal, under the State Governments. Government, through its two premier institutions, namely, the National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) and the National Council for Cooperative Training (NCCT), has already been conducting many cooperative development and training programmes in the State of West Bengal. The NCDC has provided the following assistance to the cooperatives in West Bengal under the Central Sector Integrated Scheme on Agricultural Cooperation (CSISAC):Year	Assistance given (Rs. in lakhs)	Loan	Subsidy	Total2018-19	32302.00	632.13	32934.132019-20	11340.00	1243.00	12583.002020-21	2229.86	3682.68	5912.54Similarly, the NCCT, through its Regional Institute of Cooperative Management at Kalyani, has conducted the following training programmes in the State:Year	No. of programme	Participants 	Women participants 2018-19	80	2046	2632019-20	100	2346	2742020-21	54	1199	334********
