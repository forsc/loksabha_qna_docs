                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2525






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Cold Storage Management




Kirron  Kher














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)		Whether the Government has any redressal and complaints management mechanism for farmers and users to raise concerns against any agencies managing cold storage centres; and (b)		if so, the details of the complaints received against the agencies responsible for implementation of cold chain centres? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a):  Government of India has a Centralized Public Grievance Redress and Monitoring System (CPGRAMS) portal for redressal of the grievances / complaints received from general public, where they can raise concerns against any agencies managing cold storage centres. They may also approach the Ministry directly. (b): Ministry has not received any grievance from farmers against the agencies responsible for implementation of cold chain projects, through CPGRAMS portal or otherwise.****
