                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3464






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






EWS Reservation




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3464TO BE ANSWERED ON 10.08.2021EWS RESERVATION †3464. SHRI DEEPAK BAIJ: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) whether the reservation share of general category has been increased after the implementation of 10 percent reservations for the Economically Weaker Section (EWS) general category due to which opportunities for other groups are decreasing, if so, the details thereof; (b) the State which have implemented 10 percent reservation for EWS of general category; (c) the details of the number of candidates provided appointment under the said reservation policy for EWS of general category till date, State-wise; and(d) whether filling of backlog vacancies of Scheduled Castes, Scheduled and Other Backward Castes has been put on hold and priority is being given to the appointment of candidates belonging to the 10 percent EWS of general category, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a): No Sir. The benefits of EWS reservation is available to those persons not covered under the existing reservation scheme for SCs/STs/OBCs in Central Government and this has no bearing on the opportunities available to the other groups.(b): As per available information the following States have implemented ten per cent reservation to EWS:- (1) Uttrakhand (2) Gujarat  (3) Karnataka  (4) Jharkhand (5) Maharashtra (6) Mizoram (7) Delhi (8) J&K (9) Goa (10)Assam (11) Andhra Pradesh and (12) Telengana.No information regarding implementation of EWS reservation in remaining States/UTs is available.(c): As per information sent by the Department of personnel & Training, State wise/District wise data in respect of EWS is not maintained by them.(d): Department of Personnel and Training (DoPT) monitors the progress in filling up of backlog vacancies reserved for SCs, STs and OBCs with ten Ministries/Departments, having more than 90% of the employees of Central Government. These Ministries/Departments collect these data from their attached /subordinate offices, Central Public Sector Undertakings, Financial Institutions, Nationalized Banks, etc and thereafter, furnish consolidated data to DoPT.
