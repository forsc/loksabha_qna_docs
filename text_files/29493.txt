                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1296






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Funds to States to Organise Fairs and Festivals




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is providing funds to the States to organize fairs and festivals and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the assistance is extended to the States to promote tribal culture also; and (c) 	if so, the fund provided to different States during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	No, Sir. State/ UT-wise funds are not released by the Ministry of Culture, Government of India to organize fairs and festivals. 	The Ministry of Culture organizes National Level Festivals like Rashtriya Sanskriti Mahotsavs (RSMs) through its Zonal Cultural Centres (ZCCs) where a large number of artists from all over India are engaged to showcase their talents. Besides, RSMs, ZCCs also organize Regional Festivals alongwith other cultural activities/ programmes for promotion of culture every year as per their programme calendar. The grant-in-aid released to these ZCCs during the last five years for organizing their activities including Regional Festivals and RSMs is as under: (Rs. in lakh)Sl. Under this centrally sponsored scheme, the grants are given to States and Union Territories inhabited by such tribal groups to ensure that along with development interventions, the culture of such communities is also preserved, protected and promoted. The Ministry allocates annual budgetary provision to the States/UTs where the PVTGs inhabit.
