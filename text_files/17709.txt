                                    ANSWERED ON:

Allocation to FPSs




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the allocation to fair price shops (FPSs) in units of weight in each of the last six years, State-wise and grain-wise; (b) 	the release to PDS beneficiaries by all fair price shops in units of weight in each of the last six years, State-wise and grain-wise; (c) 	whether the Government has estimated the percentage loss or leakage of grains between the procurement stage and the release to beneficiaries as on March 2020; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a)&(b):  	Department of Food & Public Distribution makes allocation of foodgrains(wheat and rice) to the States/UTs under Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS) in terms of National Food Security Act(NFSA), 2013. Under the Act eligible households, which comprise of Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) households and priority households(PHH), are entitled to receive foodgrains @ Rs 1/2/3 per kg for nutri-cereals/wheat/rice respectively. NFSA is operated under the joint responsibility of the Central and the State/UT Govt. Central Govt is responsible for allocation and transportation of foodgrains upto the designated depots of the FCI. The operational responsibilities for allocation of foodgrains within the States/UTs, identification of eligible beneficiaries, issuance of ration cards to them, distribution of foodgrains to eligible beneficiaries rest with the concerned State/UT Govt.
