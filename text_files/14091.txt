                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2815






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Renovation of Railway Stations




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plans to renovate all the major railway stations of the country including Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan;(b) whether any foreign company is providing technical support for the renovation of any railway station;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the details of funds allocated for the said purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) Yes, Sir. Refurbishment/renovation/augmentation/upgradation of stations under various developmental schemes is a continuous and ongoing process. Presently, upgradation of stations is done under ‘Adarsh’ Station Scheme. So far 1253 stations have been identified for upgradation under the ‘Adarsh Station Scheme’. Against these, 1172 stations have been upgraded/developed in the country and work on remaining stations is in progress. Various passenger amenities works have already been done at Jaipur Railway station. Following works have been inter-alia carried out in different passenger amenities works including soft-upgradation, provision of joint development with Minsitry of Tourism and by IRCTC:-improvement of façade of station building, lifts and escalators, improvement of circulating area by streamlining traffic flow with separate entry/exit, improved LED based illumination to brighten up all areas, fans provided, renovation of concourse by painting, Jaipur theme mural painting & NTES video wall, relocation of baggage scanner to accommodate more passengers in concourse, relocation of UTS booking counter in PRS building to extend concourse area, provison of meeting point and selfie point in concourse hall, revival of old paintings & provision of smart city kiosk, improvement of existing waiting halls, separate waiting rooms for ladies & gents, provision of sensor based entry and exit toughened glass doors in main concourse hall, divyang facility, improvement of platform surface, improvement of Foot Over Bridge, executive lounge, water vending machine to promote environement awareness, modular water kiosk & water ATMs.(b) No, Sir.(c) and (d) Do not arise.*****
