                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3536






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Development of Industrial Corridors




Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel













                                                    Shardaben Anilbhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is developing industrial corridors across the country;(b) 	if so, the details thereof including the proposed developmental activities;(c) 	the roles of the Union Government and the State Governments in the development of the industrial corridors; and(d) 	the details of the funds allocated, released and utilized for the development of the industrial corridors as on date? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) & (b): 	Government of India has accorded approval for development of 11 corridors (32 projects) in four Phases as part of National Master Plan for providing multimodal connectivity to economic zones which are under various stages of conceptualization / development / implementation. The list of projects in the respective states along with status of implementation is attached at Annexure. (c) & (d): 	As per approved Financial and Institutional Framework of Industrial Corridors Programme, State Governments provide land while the Central Government through National Industrial Corridor Development and Implementation Trust (NICDIT) provides funding support as matching equity for creation of trunk infrastructure.
