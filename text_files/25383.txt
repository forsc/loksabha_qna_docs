                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1231






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Vacancies in ICAR




Vijay Kumar













                                                    S. Gnanathiraviam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that several posts of scientists and technical posts are lying vacant in Indian Council for Agricultural Research (ICAR);(b)	 if so, the details thereof;(c)	the extent to which the vacancies would adversely affect the research activities at ICAR;(d)	whether any steps are being taken to fill up the vacancies at the earliest;(e)	 if so, the details thereof; and(f)	 if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICUTLRURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ?? ????? ?????? ??????			                        (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Out of the total sanctioned strength of 6586 positions in scientific cadre in ICAR, 1394 are vacant. Likewise, out of the total sanctioned strength of 6756 in case of technical cadre 2311 are vacant. Cadre and category wise details are as under: Cadre Wise details of vacancy position of ScientistsName of post	Sanctioned Posts	In position	VacancyScientist	4451	3882	569Sr. Scientist	1295	924	371Principal Scientist (Heads, Project Coordinators)	665	285	380Research Management Positions	175	101	74Total 	6586	5192	1394Category wise details of vacancy position of Technical StaffCategory I	3699	2512	1187Category II	2555	1689	866Category III	502	244	258Total	6756	4445	2311(c) 	ICAR has taken efforts to ensure that the mandated and prioritized research activities of the Institutes do not suffer. Effective steps including optimal deployment of human resources are taken in ICAR to prioritize the research activities to address the mandates of the Institutions and the national priorities.(d) to (f):	The recruitment of scientific and technical positions is a continuous process and the vacancies are filled up through an established procedure by the Agricultural Scientists Recruitment Board (ASRB) or the Institute subject to availability of qualified candidates. The vacancy position is monitored regularly within ICAR and with ASRB towards timely selection and deployment of human resource. ***********
