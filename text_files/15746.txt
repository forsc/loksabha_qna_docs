                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4523






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Textile Units in Assam




Queen Oja













                                                    Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to set up new textile units in Assam and Gujarat under ‘Make in India’ Scheme; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (c) the total number of textile units set up across the country during the last three years, State-wise including Gujarat?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b):	The Government has not received any proposal for setting up of textile units in Assam and Gujarat under ‘Make in India Scheme’. However, the Government is implementing Scheme for Integrated Textile Park (SITP) which is demand driven and it provides support for creation of world-class infrastructure facilities for setting up of textile units, with a Government of India grant upto 40% of the project cost  and Government of India grant upto 90% of the project cost for first two projects (each) in the States of Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Tripura, Sikkim, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand and Union Territory of Jammu & Kashmir and Union Territory of Ladakh; with ceiling limit of Rs. 40.00 crores for each textile park. The Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) formed by the representatives of local industry, Financial Institutions, State Industrial and Infrastructural Corporations and other agencies of State and Central Governments  registered as a Corporate Body under Companies Act would submit their proposal directly to the Ministry for consideration. SITP is thus a demand driven scheme. 1 textile park has been sanctioned in State of Assam  and 14 textile parks have been sanctioned in the State of Gujarat under SITP by the Ministry of Textiles, the details of which is placed at Annexure.(c): 	As per the data furnished by the textile parks sanctioned under SITP, the State-wise details of textile units within SITP,  set up in the last 3 years is as under:S. No.	State/ U.T.	Units set up in last 3 year1	Gujarat	362	U.T. of Jammu & Kashmir	33	Karnataka	74	Maharashtra	365	Punjab	56	Rajasthan	117	Tamilnadu	228	Telangana	1	TOTAL	121
