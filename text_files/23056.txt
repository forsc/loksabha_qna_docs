                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4037






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Destitute Homes




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the measures taken up by the Government in destitute homes to combat COVID-19 pandemic across the country;(b)	the total number of destitute homes along with the total number of inmates residing in such homes, State/UT-wise; and(c)	whether the Government has any plan to vaccinate the inmates of destitute homes across the country. If so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including Odisha? 






ANSWER




ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)  (a)	Advisory was issued to all States/UTs to inform the implementing agencies for taking preventive measures to contain spread of Novel Coronavirus (Covid19). Implementing have been asked to ensure that there is adequate provision of food, sanitization items including masks, gloves, basic medicines, soaps and other essentials items. Awareness among the inmates regarding social distancing, avoiding mass gathering and hand sanitization as per Government of India guidelines are being done.(b)	The total number of destitute homes along with the total number of inmates residing in such homes, State/UT-wise is at Annexure I.(c)	This Ministry has no special plan to vaccinate the inmates of destitute homes across the country.*********  Annexure IAnnexure referred to in reply of parts (b) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.4037 for reply on 19.03.2021 regarding Destitute Homes by Shri Mahesh Sahoo.
