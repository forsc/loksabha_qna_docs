                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

941






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Welfare and Employment of Artisans/Crafts Men




Subhash Ramrao Bhamre













                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the scheme being implemented by the Government for welfare and employment of artisans/crafts men engaged in handicrafts sector has not adequately benefited them, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (b)	whether the Government has any plan to assess the schemes being implemented for the development of handicraft sector and handloom weavers in the country especially Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu, if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the funds released for the implementation of these schemes are not being fully utilized; and (d)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor along with the shortcomings found in each scheme and the corrective measures taken or proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ?????? ?????? (??????? ?????? ?????? ?????)MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	No Sir, welfare schemes under National Handicrafts Development Programme implemented by office of Development Commissioner (Handicrafts) for handicrafts artisans and craftsmen are  effective in extending the benefit to them in terms of employment and growth.  The number of artisans benefitted across the country  as on date  through following  components of welfare schemes are as under :-Sl. No.	Component	No. of artisans benefittedi)	Issuance of Photo identity card to handicrafts artisans under PAHCHAN initiatives	25.27 lakhs  artisans registered ii)	Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yoajan/Pradhan Mantri Surkasha Bima Yojana and modified Aam Admi Bima Yojana	10,634 artisans enrolled during 2018-19 (1st June, 2018 to 31st May 2019)iii)	Interest subvention to handicrafts artisans availing MUDRA loan.	7,622 artisans iv)	Margin money to the handicrafts artisans availing MUDRA loan	84 artisans v)	Financial support to mastercraftspersons in indigent circumstances.	246 artisansSource : 	Life Insurance Corporation, Punjab National Bank and  field offices of Development Commissioner (Handicrafts)In addition to above, the  artisans benefitted through other development schemes of the office of the Development Commissioner (Handicrafts)  during the years 2017-18 and 2018-19 are as under :-Sl.No.	Name of the Scheme	No. of artisans benefitted		2017-18	2018-191.	Ambedkar Hastshilp Vikas Yojana (AHVY) 	55,000	10,5002.	Marketing & Support Services	15,987	11,5133.	Design and Technical Upgradation	3,880	6,5504.	Human Resource Development	5,670	6,7005.	Research and Development	400	1,0506.	Welfare scheme (Financial support to artisans in indigent circumstances)	257	2737	Mega clusters	23,185	19,328(b) 	Yes Sir,  the 3rd Party evaluation to assess all the scheme for development of handicraft sector across the country including Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu has been initiated.(c) to (d)  No Sir, the details of funds utilized during last 3 years in Handicrafts sector schemes is given in Annexure-I,  Further, the State agencies dealing with handicrafts sector are advised from time to time for speedy implementation  of the schemes to fully utilize the sanctioned grant.  ****
