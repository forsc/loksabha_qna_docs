                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3016






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Operation of Wide Body Aircraft from Kozhikode




M. P. Abdussamad Samadani














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the difficulties being faced by the passengers, especially the expatriates, due to the delay in restarting the operation of wide body aircrafts from the Kozhikode airport and if so, the detail thereof; and(b) whether the Government proposes to restart the same at the earliest and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) & (b) The Government has received representations from associations and public representatives regarding commencement of wide body operations at Kozhikode airport.;A Committee has been constituted by the Ministry of Civil Aviation with aviation experts to oversee the implementation of the recommendations made in the report of the investigation conducted by AAIB on the accident at Kozhikode Airport and the execution of the steps required for implementation of the recommendations. One of the mandates of the Committee is to study the feasibility of operation of wide body aircraft at Kozhikode airport and to give its recommendations thereof.
