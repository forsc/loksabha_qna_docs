                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4645






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Minority Status




Ranjeeta Koli













                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a)  the details of the communities accorded minority status by the Government so far; (b) whether any demand has been received by the Government from any community for according minority status and if so, the details and the dates on which such demands were received; (c)  the details of the provisions made by the Government to accord minority status; (d) whether there is any provision/proposal under consideration to abolish this status of minority community and if so, the details thereof; (e)  whether growing minority population has been reviewed; and(f)  if so, the time by which it is likely to be done and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a) to (c)  Central Government notifies minority communities in consultation with various stakeholders under Section 2 (c) of National Commission for Minorities (NCM) Act, 1992.  The Central Government has so far notified six communities namely Christians, Sikhs, Muslims, Buddhists, Jains and Parsis as Minority communities. Proposals for grant of minority status, as and when received are considered by Ministry of Minority Affairs as per provisions of the NCM Act, 1992.(d) to (f)  At present, there is no such proposal under consideration with the Ministry of Minority Affairs. *****
