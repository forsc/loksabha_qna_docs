                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4229






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Cash Deposit Machines




Pratima Bhoumik














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any data with regard to Cash deposit machines in the country and if so, the details thereof, bank and State/UT-wise;(b)	whether it is a fact that there is a shortage of Cash deposit Machines in the country and if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government to increase the number of Cash deposit machines in the country; and(c)	whether the Government has any plan to increase the number of Cash deposit Machines in NER states including Tripura and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?	






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a)	The bank-wise position of cash deposit machines and cash recyclers as on 31.01.2021, as intimated by Reserve Bank of India (RBI) is at Annexure.  RBI has informed that State-wise data on cash deposit machines and cash recyclers is not available with them.(b)	and (c) In terms of RBI Circular dated 02.02.2015, Scheduled Commercial Banks (including Regional Rural Banks) are permitted to install Cash Deposit Machines / Bunch Note Acceptor Machines at centres / places identified by them without having the need to take permission from RBI in each case, subject to certain conditions.Deployment of cash deposit machines / cash recyclers by banks is a continuous process. Banks have informed that deployment of such machines is need based and as per their business requirements after checking the feasibility. *****
