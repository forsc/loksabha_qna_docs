                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

76






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






States' Share in Shareable Pool of Taxes




Nalin Kumar Kateel













                                                    Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether it is a fact that the Government proposes for monetary disbursement to States from the shareable pool of taxes;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of total dues pending for disbursement to various states from the shareable pool of taxes during the last three years, year wise and state wise with special reference to Karnataka; and(d)	the total amount disbursed by the Union Government to the States including Karnataka?






ANSWER




                                                        ( a to d)	Union Government devolves States’ share in Central Taxes and Duties in terms of approved recommendations of the Finance Commission. There are no pendencies in disbursement on this account to Karnataka or in tax devolutions being made to State Governments. Details are annexed.
