                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3519






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Implementation of SPMRM




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of Shyama Prasad Mukherji Rurban Mission (SPMRM) along with the details of budget allocation made, funds spent under each head and the physical progress made till date under the Mission in Rajasthan in the direction of the target fixed in this regard;(b) 	the names of districts and locations wherein works were carried out under the said Mission in the State of Rajasthan including Nagaur district of Rajasthan;(c) 	the body entrusted with the responsibility of monitoring progress of the said Mission in all districts of Rajasthan including Nagaur; and(d) 	whether the Government has any proposal to include additional new village panchayats under the said Mission in view of the recommendations made by the local MPs and if so, the time by which the same are likely to be included under the said Mission?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b) The Shyama Prasad Mukherji Rurban Mission (SPMRM) is being implemented by the Ministry of Rural Development to develop 300 Rurban clusters across the country, with a funding support of up to 30% (Central and State Share) of the estimated investment for each Rurban cluster, as Critical Gap Funding (CGF), while 70% or more of the funds are mobilized by the States through convergence with synergic State and Central programmes.  The Mission aims to develop these Rurban clusters by   provisioning   economic,   social   and   physical   infrastructure   facilities   in   an   integrated manner.   The detailed guidelines / Framework of Implementation (FOI) of the Mission may be seen by visiting the Rurban website, i.e., https://rurban.gov.in Under the Mission, there is no provision for States/UT-wise allocation of funds.  However, the funds are being released based on fulfillment of certain progress achieved as defined in the guidelines of the Mission.  The details of funds released to the State of Rajasthan, District-wise, including the District of Nagaur, and the details of physical progress made under the Mission in Rajasthan are annexed at Annexure-I. (c)  The body entrusted with the responsibility of monitoring progress of SPMRM in the State of Rajasthan at District level is the Zila Parishad of the respective District, with the Chief Executive Officer (CEO), Zila Parishad as the District level Nodal officer. As per the provisions of FOI, the institutional framework for implementation and monitoring of the Mission is set up at three levels viz. the State government level, district level and at the cluster level.  Also, the Ministry has a web-based program monitoring platform “Rurbansoft” for SPMRM which is linked to Public Financial Management System (PFMS) for monitoring the progress of the said Mission at all levels including at District level. (d) No Sir.  Government has no proposal to include additional / new Gram Panchayat with the existing Rurban cluster. ******
