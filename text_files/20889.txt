                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2281






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Suicide by Students




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it has come to the notice of the Government that a girl from Telangana studying in Lady Shri Ram College committed suicide in November, 2020;(b) whether the college directing her to vacate the hostel accommodation is the prime reason behind her committing suicide;(c) if so, the reasons and the action Government has taken on the management of the said college;(d) whether the non-release of money under INSPIRE scholarship that she was given to pursue her studies was also a reason for her to take the drastic step;(e) if so, reasons for not releasing money under INSPIRE scholarship to her; and(f) the steps taken by the Government to prevent recurrence of such incidents and the planning to address digital divide in the Country?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’)(a) to (c) University of Delhi has informed that the Lady Shri Ram College for Women received the news of the demise of  its student from Telangana on November 3, 2020. The unfortunate incident happened at her home town where she was living after the hostel of the college was closed in March 2020 due to Covid-19.(d) to (f) As per the college records, there was no intimation by the student that she was a recipient of INSPIRE scholarship sponsored by the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India and the role of the college in such cases is limited to certifying the bona-fides of the candidate only. The student joined the college in the academic session of 2019-20 and applied for free-ship amount which was paid on 24.04.2020 in her account. University Grants Commission has circulated guidelines/advisories to universities/colleges for counselling the students appropriately so that they can cope up with the situation. In order to address the digital divide UGC has directed to adopt various modes and blended methods of learning.*****
