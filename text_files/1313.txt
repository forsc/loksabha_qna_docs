                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1229






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Integrated Wool Development Programme




Ritesh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a):	whether the impact assessment of the Integrated Wool Development Programme is being undertaken by the Government;(b):	if so, the State/UT-wise details thereof along with the manner in which the said scheme has impacted marginalized groups such as SCs, STs, OBCs and low- income women;  (c):	if not, the time by which the Government plans to release the impact assessment report of this scheme; and(d):	whether the Government plans to include the details of benefits delivered to SCs, STs, OBCs and low-income women in this report, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (d):	The Integrated Wool Development Programme (IWDP) provides for conducting field survey through an independent evaluating agency to assess the impact of the programme before its completion in March, 2020. Coverage of benefit delivered to SCs, STs, OBCs and low income women are implicit in the field survey. *****
