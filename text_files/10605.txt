                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4062






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Construction of Ducts




Ramesh Chander Kaushik













                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government is considering to have a provision for developing a system by constructing ducts in every town so that a single cable may be passed through them whenever need be and the same will check digging of roads for such purposes and if so, the details thereof; and(b)	whether the Government has fixed any time limit for the same, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	 & (b): Department of Telecommunication (DoT) has informed that a   High-Powered   Committee  (HPC)   under   the   Chairmanship  of Secretary, Telecom with Secretaries of Road Transport & Highways, Power, Petroleum and Member (Engineering), Railway Board as  itsMembers was formed to examine inter-alia the possibility of ''Development of common/integrated infrastructure such as common ducts for utilities’.  The Recommendations of the HPC inter-aliaincluded formulation of Common Right of Way (RoW) framework by various land owing agencies and Establishment of a central Common Duct Agency (CDA).Further, DoT has informed that the National Digital Communications Policy, 2018, inter-alia envisages, ‘promotion of collaborative models involving state, local bodies and private sector as necessary for provision of shared duct infrastructure in municipalities, rural areas and national highways; and establishing Common Service Ducts and utility corridors in all new city and highway road projects, for implementation of a ''Fibre First Initiative'' by 2022.                         ******
