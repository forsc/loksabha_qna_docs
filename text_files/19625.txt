                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Welfare Schemes for Rural Women




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    871.   SHRI CHANDESHWAR PRASAD:Will the Minister of WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state :(a)	whether the Government has launched any new schemes during the last three years with special focus on holistic development of rural women  in the country particularly in Bihar;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the financial allocations made under these new schemes, State/UT-wise ? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b): Yes Sir, Mahila Shakti Kendra Scheme was approved in November, 2017 as a centrally sponsored scheme for implementation during FY 2017-18 to 2019-20, to empower rural women through community participation.  The scheme envisages community engagement through college student volunteers in 115 inspirational districts as part of block level initiatives. State of Bihar approved the implementation of MSK Scheme in February 2020  for 5 inspirational districts, namely Aurangabad, Jamui, Nawada, Katihar and Muzaffarpur.  POSHAN Abhiyan was launched to improve nutritional indicators of women and children by way of leveraging technology, encouraging Jan Bhaagidari and convergence with other Schemes and programmes with similar objectives.
