                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4740






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Opening of New Tourism Centre in Karnal




Sanjay Bhatia














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to open new tourist centre in Parliamentary Constituency, Karnal; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b):	Ministry of Tourism does not have any scheme for opening of new Tourist Centers in the country. However under its schemes of Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD Ministry provides financial assistance to State Governments/ UT Administrations for development of tourism infrastructure in the country.  Ministry has sanctioned projects - Development of Tourism Infrastructures at Brahmsarovar, Sanhit Sarovar, Narkatari, Jyotisar of Kurukshetra under Krishna Circuit of Swadesh Darshan Scheme in 2016-17 for Rs. 97.35 Crore and Development of Nadasaheb Gurudwara and Mata Mansha Devi Temple, Panchkula under PRASHAD Scheme in 2019-20 for Rs.49.72 Crore.   *******
