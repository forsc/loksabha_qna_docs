                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

353






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Agricultural Universities




Bhagwant Mann














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has allocated funds to encourage education in agricultural universities located in Punjab to further boost the agriculture sector; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government is contemplating setting up a new agricultural university in Punjab to give a further boost to agricultural education; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) 	Yes, Sir.(b)	Financial support of Rs. 110.30 crore has been provided by the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) to the Punjab Agricultural University (PAU), Ludhiana and Guru Angad Dev Veterinary and Animal Sciences University (GADVASU), Ludhiana, Punjab during the period from 2017-18 till date for strengthening and development of the Universities. The details are at Annexures I.(c)  	There is no such proposal for setting up of a new agricultural university in Punjab by the Government.(d)	In view of reply to part (c), the question does not arise.*******
