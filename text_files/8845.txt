                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2424






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






FCI Godowns in Tamil Nadu




C.N. Annadurai














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the existing and proposed godowns of FCI in Tamil Nadu; (b) 	whether the godowns of FCI are to be constructed for storage of rice produced in Tiruvannamalai area; (c) 	if so, the time by which it will be constructed; and (d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) to (d): The details of existing owned  godowns of Food Corporation of India (FCI) and hired by it from Central Warehousing Corporation (CWC), State Warehousing Corporation (SWC), Private Entrepreneurs Guarantee (PEG) Scheme and silos in Tamil Nadu are as under :-(Fig. in LMT)Owned	Hired	Total	CWC	SWC	PEG	Silo	Total Hired	5.80	2.86	1.87	1.20	0.50	6.43	12.23There is already a godown of 20,000 MT constructed under PEG Scheme at Tiruvannamalai.  In addition, a 6,000 MT godown of  Tamil Nadu State Warehousing Corporation (TNSWC) has also been hired.  Both the godowns are sufficient for local requirements.***
