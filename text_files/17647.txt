                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1480






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Mission Milk Scheme




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the Mission Milk Scheme;(b) the details of gains made under the National Dairy Plan I in the aspirational districts, especially Hazaribagh and Ramgarh aspiration districts in Jharkhand; and(c) the number of milk cooperatives and female workers there in Jharkhand, district-wise? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                                                                           ANSWER MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY & DAIRYING                           						(DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	The National Dairy Plan Phase I (NDP I), a Central Sector Scheme of the Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying,  Government of India was implemented by the National Dairy Development Board (NDDB) with a network of End Implementing Agencies (EIAs) during the period between 2011-12 and 2018-19 with a total outlay of Rs. 2,242 crore. NDP I had following objectives:i)	Increase productivity of milch animals and thereby increase milk production to meet the rapidly growing demand for milk.ii)	Provide rural milk producers with greater access to the organized milk processing sector.NDP I was implemented in 18 major milk producing States including Jharkhand, where in a total of 577 sub projects of 172 EIAs were approved with total approved grant assistance of Rs. 1,759.97 Crore. The implementation of NDP I ended on 29 November 2019 and the financial closure was completed on March, 2020.(b)     Under NDP I, approved sub projects were implemented through eligible EIAs in their operational areas which included some of the aspirational districts. Out of the total 115 aspirational districts, 34 aspirational districts across 14 States were covered by the EIAs under NDP I, implementing sub projects with grant assistance of about Rs. 96 Crore. Jharkhand Milk Federation implemented sub project of Village Based Milk Procurement system (VBMPS) and Ration Balancing Program (RBP) under NDP I. The operational area of the project included Hazaribagh and Ramgarh districts.
