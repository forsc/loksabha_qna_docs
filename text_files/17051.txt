                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

790






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Loan Repayment by Metro Rail Networks




Behanan Benny














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is any impact on loan repayment by various metro rail networks in different cities of India due to country-wide lockdown since March, 2020 and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the measures taken/to be taken by the Government to mitigate the impact of such a situation in these metro rail corporations which are substantially funded internationally; and(c)	whether such a situation would lead to increase in losses suffered by such corporations and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	No, Sir. (b)	Urban transport is an integral part of urban development, which is a State subject. As per Metro Rail Policy,2017, financial sustainability during operations of metro rail system which also includes debt servicing is responsibility of the State Government.  (c)	The details of losses incurred due to the halting of metro rail services during Covid-19 period, as informed by some of the operational 50:50 Joint Venture metro rail corporations of Central Government and respective State Government are as under:Sr. No.	Name of Metro   Loss incurred due to        Project	        Covid-19 (in Rs. crore)1	Delhi Metro	1,609.002	Kochi Metro	34.183	Bangalore Metro	170.004	Chennai Metro	80.005	Lucknow Metro	90.00                                             *****
