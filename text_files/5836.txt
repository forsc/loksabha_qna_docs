                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5418






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Programmes/Schemes for Promotion of Sports




Ramapati Ram Tripathi














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of various schemes/programmes being implemented in the country including the rural areas for the promotion of sports and youth activities, State/UT-wise;(b) the details of the targets fixed and achievements made under the various schemes/programmes during each of the last three years and the current year, State/scheme/programme-wise;(c) the funds allocated and spent for this purpose during the said period, State and Scheme/programme/project-wise;(d) the steps taken to ensure the timely achievement of fixed targets; and(e) the manner in which information in disseminated to the youth regarding the said scheme/programmes/projects run by the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a): Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports implements following schemes to promote the sports as well as youth activities across the country including rural areas:Sports Promotional Schemes:-(i) Khelo India; (ii) Assistance to National Sports Federations; (iii)Special Awards to Winners in International sports events and their Coaches; (iv) National Sports Awards, Pension to Meritorious Sports Persons; (v)Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Sports Welfare Fund; (vi) National Sports Development Fund; and (vii)running spots training centres through Sports Authority of India.Schemes promote youth activities:-(i) Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan (NYKS) (ii) National Youth Corps (NYC) (iii) National Programme for Youth & Adolescent Development (NPYAD) (iv) International Coorperation (IC) (v) Youth Hostels(YH) (vi) Assistance to Scouting & Guiding Organisations (vii)National Discipline Scheme (NDS) (viii) National Young Leaders Programme (NYLP) (ix) National Service Scheme (NSS)(x)Rajiv Gandhi National Institute of Youth Development (RGNIYD).Details of these schemes are in the public domain on the websites of this Ministry and Sports Authority of India.(b): Sports being State subject, the primarily responsibility rests with respective State Governments to promote sports and youth activities, the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports only supports the efforts of Governments of States/UTs through its various schemes. Hence, no targets are fixed under these schemes.(c): Funds are allocated scheme-wise not state-wise. Funds allocated and spend during the last three years and the current year upto 8th July, 2019 is at Annexure-I.(d): In view of reply to part (b), question does not arise.(e): Information relating to schemes of the Ministry is in the public domain on the website of the Ministry. The Ministry also takes help of the print as well as electronic medium to disseminate the information relating to schemes/programmes/projects of the Ministry from time to time.*****
