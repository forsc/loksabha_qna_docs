                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

968






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Increase the Population of Women




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has conducted/proposes to conduct any survey regarding increase in the population of women in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the population of women has come down in the country as compared to other countries of the world; and(d) if so, the efforts made/proposed to be made by the Government for increasing population of women in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b):	The Population figures of females are available through Census of India.  The last Census was conducted in 2011.  Due to outbreak of COVID-19 pandemic, the conduct of Census 2021 and the related field activities have been postponed until further orders. (c) & (d):	The population of females has increased by 18.34% from          49, 65,14,346 in Census 2001 to 58,75,84,719 in 2011. ******
