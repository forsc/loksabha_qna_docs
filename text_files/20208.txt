                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1399






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Catering Policy




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether certain suggestions/demands have been received by Railways for continuation and implementation of Catering Policy, 2010 as well as withdrawal of modifications made in 2017 in respect of licensees of static units of Indian Railways for their livelihood and if so, the details thereof;  (b) whether the Akhil Bhartiya Railway Khan-Pan Licensees Welfare Association (Regd.) has given memorandum/letters on certain demands/suggestions after the issuance of the Catering Policy, 2017 till date and if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether any action has been taken or proposed to be taken by the Government for implementation of the said demands/ suggestions and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c): Yes, Sir. In respect of livelihood of the licensees of minor static units of Indian Railways, Hon’ble Supreme Court vide its judgements dated 29.01.2016 and 30.10.2018 has given directions to Railways regarding renewal of the existing licenses of static units, which have been implemented.Akhil Bhartiya Railway Khan Pan Licensees Welfare Association (Regd.) has also requested for renewal of licenses of minor catering units, and scrapping of the tendering system envisaged in Catering Policy 2017. This matter has been dealt as per the aforesaid Judgements of Hon’ble Supreme Court. Other suggestions have also been received from the Association from time to time which include relief to deal with Covid related hardship, permission for sale of packed items on Fruit/Juice stalls, platform vending permission, use of cooking gas on platforms and supply of electricity on subsidized rates. The suggestions have been examined on merits and action taken accordingly	*****
