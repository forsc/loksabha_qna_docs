                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2619






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Crime in Trains




Dayakar Pasunoori













                                                    Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta
                                                








                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                








                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of thefts and crime particularly related to women passengers reported in trains; (b)  the number of cases solved so far, zone-wise during the last five years; and(c)   the steps being taken to prevent such instances in future along with the compensation awarded to such victims and parameters adopted so far?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.
