                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1804






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Chinese Proliferation




Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik













                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Indian authorities have seized a dual use autoclave/instrument with military applications from a Pakistan bound Chinese merchant vessel near Kandla port recently and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Ministry has conveyed its concern to the Chinese Government regarding possible proliferation due to misuse of these Chinese entities; and (c)	if so, the details and the response of the Chinese Government/Authorities in this regard including the steps taken by the Government on the issue?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) to (c)	Indian authorities seized an autoclave, which is a dual-use controlled item and was mis-declared by the vessel at the Kandla port. We conveyed our proliferation concerns to the Chinese side.***
