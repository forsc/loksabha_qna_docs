                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

500






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Median Cut on NH-27




Devji Mansingram Patel














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that there is no median cut on the main road leading to Abu road industrial area from National Highway (NH)-27 in Gujarat resulting in frequent cases of accidents in that area; (b) if so, the details thereof along with reaction of the Government thereto; (c) the total number of the accidents occurred on NH-27 expecially in Rajasthan during the last three years and the current year; (d) whether the Government proposes to construct median cut at that location to prevent road accidents; (e) if so, the details thereof along with budgetary allocation made and time frame set for the purpose; (f) if not, the other steps being taken by the Government to prevent accidents at the said location?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)     No. There are number of median openings along Abu road Industrial Area. RICCO industrial area starts from Chainage Km. 635+000 and ends at Chainage Km.644+000 of the project Highway.(b)     There are number of median openings on Abu road industrial area which are mentioned as below.1.   643+1002.   636+00 (VUP to RICCO ind. Area)3.   642+2004.   640+5805.   638+1206.   635+100 (c)     Details of accident on NH-27 in Rajasthan is at Annexure-A.(d to f)     Normally median opening on highway is provisioned for 2 Km length each however at the said location there are 6 median opening for a total length of 9.00 Km at a distance of 1.5 Km each.
