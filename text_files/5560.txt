                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5249






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Engineering Cluster in Nasik




Bharti Pravin Pawar














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has established Nasik Engineering Cluster under Industrial Infrastructure Upgradation Scheme (IIUS) in Nasik district of Maharashtra; (b)	 if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the funds sanctioned/allocated/ released and utilised for the said cluster till date; and (d) 	the details of the development work done by the said cluster?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):	Yes Sir, Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) under Ministry of Commerce and Industry has established ‘Nasik Engineering Cluster’ in Nashik district of Maharashtra under ‘Industrial Infrastructure Upgradation Scheme (IIUS)’. (b)&(c):  	Details of the project is given below:Name of project	Date of Approval	Project Cost	Central Grant	Released central grant and utilized	Date of completion		(Rs. in crore)	Nashik Engineering Cluster, Maharashtra	24.03.2008	67.26	42.88	42.45	12.04.2013(d):    	The project at Nasik Engineering Cluster (NEC) has been completed and NEC has created common industrial infrastructure like ‘Rapid Prototype Development Centre’, Training Centre along with well-equipped training studio and work stations and a Calibration & Testing facility which has been accredited by National Accreditation Board for Laboratories (NABL). *******
