                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1556






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






National Sports Development Bill




Sukhbir Singh Jaunapuria














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	  whether the Government proposes to introduce National Sports Development Bill which mandates bringing all Olympic and Non-Olympic Sports Federations under the ambit of Right to Information; andb)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a)& (b) No Sir, the  Government does not propose to introduce National Sports Development Bill. However, Govt. Has separately issued instructions whereby all the National Sports Federations (NSFs) receiving grant of Rs.10 lakh or more have been declared as public authorities under the Right to Information Act, 2005.*****
