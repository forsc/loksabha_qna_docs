                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3644






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Funds Allocated for Infrastructural Development and Geographical Isolation in NER States




Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that tourism has a great potential for generating income and employment opportunities in North Eastern States of India; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether it is also a fact that lack of infrastructure and Geographical isolation are coming in the way of its development; and (d) 	if so, the Government plan for development of infrastructure and to tackle geographical isolation in the North Eastern States along with the funds allocated for this purpose during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	Yes, Sir.  The natural beauty, the rich cultural heritage, historical sites, spectacular biodiversity, wildlife, exotic flora and fauna, spirit of universal brotherhood and social fabric, diverse religion in the region are the factors that make a great potential for tourism development and generating the income and employment opportunities in the region. As per the information provided by DoNER the vision document of Ministry of Development of North East Region envisage increasing tourist footfall in region from 0.6% to 5% of all India tourist footfall.With efforts of Governments and other Stakeholders the infrastructure are being improved to develop this region as tourism hub of the country.(d):	10% of the yearly gross Budget Allocation of all the Ministries are allocated for development of the Northeast Region including Infrastructure Development. The details of financial assistance provided to the States for tourism infrastructure under the Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD scheme of the Ministry in the Northeast Region during the last three year is placed at Annexure-I.Expenditure for infrastructure development in the Northeast Region by Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region is placed at Annexure-II.*********
