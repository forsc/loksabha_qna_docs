                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3891






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Mobile Connectivity in Kodarma District




Annpurna Devi














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether mobile connectivity and network facility is not available in Kodarma district of Jharkhand due to shortage of BSNL mobile towers; (b) 	the reaction of the Government thereto; and (c) 	the corrective steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (c) 	Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) has informed that as on 31.05.2019, 2154 Base Transceiver Stations (BTSs) for 2G and 1118 BTSs for 3G are working in Jharkhand State. These include 34 BTSs for 2G and 17 BTSs for 3G in Kodarma district.70 BTSs (2G/4G) are approved for installation in Kodarma district for providing mobile connectivity in Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected areas at locations identified by Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) under scheme funded by Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF).
