                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2103






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Gas Based Fertilizer Units




Sisir Kumar Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state:(a)	whether the Government proposes to revive fertilizer plants of the country as gas based units; (b)	if so, the present status of the Talcher fertilizer unit and the Sindri Fertilizer Unit thereof; (c)	whether the Government has any proposal for revival/re-start of Haldia and Durgapur Fertilizer plants and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICAL & FERTILZERS.(DR.  MANSUKH  MANDAVIYA)(a)	:   Yes Sir, Government of India has mandated revival of (i) Ramagundam fertilizer unit to Joint Venture Company (JVC) of National Fertilizers Limited (NFL), Engineers India Limited (EIL) and Fertilizers Corporation of India Limited (FCIL) named Ramagundam Fertilizers & Chemicals Limited (RFCL) incorporated on 17.02.2015, (ii) Talcher fertilizer unit to JVC of Rashtriya Chemicals & Fertilizers Limited (RCF), Gas Authority of India Limited (GAIL), Coal India Limited (CIL) and FCIL named Talcher Fertilizers Limited (TFL) incorporated on 27.10.2015 and (iii) Gorakhpur, Sindri and Barauni fertilizer units to JVC of CIL, National Thermal Power Corporation ( NTPC ), Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL), FCIL / Hindustan Fertilizers Corporation Limited (HFCL) named Hindustan Urvarak & Rasayan Limited (HURL) incorporated on 16.05.2016. The capacity of each of these plants is 12.7 LMTPA. All above plants are gas based except TFL which is based on Coal gasification Technology.(b)	:  The present status of the Talcher fertilizer unit and the Sindri Fertilizer units are as under:-Sl No.	Name of the unit	Present progress as on 30.11.2021 (in %)1	Talcher unit, Odisha	                  16.71%2	Sindri unit, Jharkhand	                  92.8%(c):   In view of revival of units of FCIL and HFCL already mandated, there is no proposal to revive any closed fertilizer unit at present.********
