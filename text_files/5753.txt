                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5365






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Status of Four-laning Projects in Assam




Gaurav Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the present status of four-laning projects on NH-37 (Jorhat-Golaghat) and NH-39 (Numaligarh-Dimapur) in Assam; and(b)  the time by which the work on the said projects is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)   The work of four-laning of Numaligarh-Jorhat Section (except Deragoan bypass) of NH-37 in Assam, having a length of 39.72 Km is under progress.   Present progress is 29% and the work is expected to be completed by May 2020.  The stretch from Numaligarh-Dimapur Section of NH-39 is proposed under Economic Corridor of Bharatmala Pariyojna and is at feasibility study stage. *****
