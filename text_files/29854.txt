                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1510






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Schemes for Information and Broadcasting Sector




Sunil Kumar Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has been succeeded through its various schemes in improving the status of information and broadcasting sector in the country during the last six years; and(b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING AND MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a)	Yes, Sir.(b)	During the last six years, the Government has been successful in improving the status of Information and Broadcasting sector in the country through implementation of various schemes.  During this period, underthescheme of ‘Broadcasting Infrastructure Network Development’ (BIND), infrastructure of Doordarshan and All India Radio have been strengthened in terms of upgradation and replacement of transmitters, broadcasting equipment and digitization, expansion of TV Channels, expansion of FM Stations with specific strengthening of coverage in Border and Left Wing Extremism(LWE) affected areas.With an objective of improvingand augmenting the infrastructure support, Media Units of the Ministry have been strengthened with machinery, equipment and infrastructure. During this period, complete digitization of TV channel distribution has been implemented though the scheme of ‘Mission Digitization’. Under the scheme of ‘Supporting Community Radio Movement in the country’, large number of Community Radio stations have been set up in various States. This has empowered the local communities in rural and remote areas with effective dissemination of information relating to agriculture, health, education, environment, culture and social welfare areas.
