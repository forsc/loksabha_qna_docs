                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

695






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






National Service Scheme




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla













                                                    Queen Oja
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the National Service Scheme is operational in Uttar Pradesh; and   (b) if so, the reasons for not releasing funds to Uttar Pradesh for the years 2018-19 and 2019-20? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS [SHRI KIREN RIJIJU]  (a) Yes, National Service Scheme (NSS) is operational in the State of Uttar Pradesh.  (b) The NSS grant of Rs. 10.96 Crore was released to the Government of Uttar Pradesh for the financial year 2019-20 in the month of December, 2019. The grant for the year 2018-19, an amount of Rs. 6,51,956/- was released in the month of March, 2019. The grant is released annually to every State/UT depending upon the number of volunteers, camps organized, staff employed in NSS Cell of concerned States/UTs, furnishing of Utilization Certificates, QEP ceiling, actual expenditure, office expenses etc.  ****
