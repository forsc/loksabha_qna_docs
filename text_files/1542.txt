                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1488






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Training to Men, Women and Persons with Disability




Sumedhanand Saraswati














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details regarding the role of State Governments and National Sector Skill Council (NSDC) in planning the training programmes under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendras; and(b)	the number of women, men and persons with disability trained under this scheme so far?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)	 Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship through National Skill Development and Corporation promotes establishment of model and aspirational skill centres known as Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKK) in every district for imparting skill training through Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 2016-20. State Governments are expected to facilitate the establishment of PMKKs, by provision of land etc. Targets under Centrally Sponsored State Managed – PMKVY 2016-20 may be allocated to PMKKs by State Governments.Under the skill ecosystem, the concerned Sector Skill Council (SSC) develop sector specific QPs/NOS for various job roles. Some courses/job roles developed by concerned SSCs which are in high demand are kept under PMKVY for imparting skill development training.As on 12.06.2019, the number of women, men and persons with disability trained in the PMKKs under the PMKVY are as under:S. No.	Particulars	Trained/Oriented	PwD candidates1	Women candidates 	3,06,121	1672	Men candidates 	4,33,848	430
