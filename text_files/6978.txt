                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

716






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Development of Airstrip in Vellore




S. Jagathrakshakan














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken steps to develop the airstrip located at Vellore in Tamil Nadu as full-fledged airport ;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether the Government has taken any step for night landing facilities for aircrafts in Tuticorin and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Airports Authority of India (AAI) has taken up the following works for development of Vellore airport under Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) UDAN-2 with project cost of Rs. 32.52 Crore:;  i.  Construction of runway, taxiway and apron; ;  ii. Construction of pre-fabricated terminal building, substation building and electric & associated works. (c): Night landing facility has been provided at Tuticorin Airport.  However, the runway length is not adequate to handle big aircraft.  AAI has initiated process for augmentation of runway length of the airport from the existing 1350 x 30 meters to 3115 x 45 meters to make it suitable for bigger aircraft.
