                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

284






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Drought Situation in Maharashtra




Krupal Balaji Tumane













                                                    Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a):	whether the Union Government has received any request from the State Government of Maharashtra with regard to drought situation in some districts of the State since 2014 till date and if so, the details thereof; (b):	whether the Government had sent any Central Team to visit drought-hit districts and for assessing loss to paddy, cotton and other crops due to drought; (c):	if so, the details of the same; (d):	whether the Government has announced any financial package for the farmers of drought-hit districts of Maharashtra; and (e):	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (e):	Yes, Sir. As per the existing procedure, on receipt of Memorandum from the State seeking financial assistance from National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF) in the wake of drought, Inter Ministerial Central Team (IMCT) is constituted. IMCT visits the State for assessment of the drought situation and requirement of financial assistance from NDRF. Taking into account the recommendations of the IMCT and the recommendations of the Sub Committee of National Executive Committee (SC-NEC) thereon, the Government of India approves assistance from NDRF. The details of financial assistance approved by the Central Government to the State Government of Maharashtra for drought since 2014-15 are as under:  Year	Calamity	Number of districts affected by drought	Amount approved by Central Government(Rs. in Crore)2014-15	Drought (Kharif)	26	1962.992015-16	Drought (Kharif)	21	3638.83	Drought (Rabi)	02	679.542018-19	Drought (Kharif)	26	4714.28****
