                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

255






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






DISHA Committee in Maharashtra




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the progress of District Development Coordination and Monitoring Committee (DISHA) in respect of Maharashtra State; and(b) 	the details of meetings of DISHA Committee under the chairmanship of Lok Sabha Members of Parliament in respect of Maharashtra State?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): District Development Coordination and Monitoring Committees (DISHA) have been formed in 36 districts of Maharashtra. (b): The details of meetings of DISHA Committees under the Chairmanship of Lok Sabha Members of Parliament in respect of Maharashtra State are at Annexure.
