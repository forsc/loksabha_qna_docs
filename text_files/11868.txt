                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

691






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Performance of PMKSY




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has made any assessment regarding the works and performance of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) during the last three years and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government has developed an electronic platform in line with India’s ‘Digital India’ program to carry out all the processes related to the submission of proposals under various schemes and to make Grants-in-aid simple, easy and transparent; and (c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)  The progress of the scheme is reviewed by the concerned Department/Ministry at various levels from time to time.  Further, field visits are also made.  Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) Council under the Chairmanship of CEO, NITI Aayog has also four times reviewed the progress of works under various components of PMKSY.(b) & (c)  The status of submission and movement of Central Assistance (CA) proposals in respect of prioritized projects under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) - Accelerated Irrigation Benefit Programme (AIBP) and Command Area Development & Water Management (CADWM) works are tracked online.  Further, MIS has been developed to monitor physical and financial progress of various components of scheme.*****
