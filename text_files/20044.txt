                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1232






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Assistance for Cold Storage




Devendrappa Y














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the government has provided any assistance during the last three years to establish cold storage in order to protect fruits and vegetables from decaying in Karnataka, particularly in southern districts of Karnataka; (b)	If so, the details thereof; and(c)	the details of the above said assistance, year-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a) & (b): The Government does not setup cold storage of its own. However, financial assistance has been provided for establishment of 21 cold storages in Karnataka, including its southern districts, under the Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) implemented by the Department of Agriculture, Cooperation and Farmers Welfare.    The details of these 21 cold storages are given in Annexure. (c):      The year-wise details are as under:   Year	Number of Cold Storage 	Financial Assistance (Rupees in Lakh)	Capacity (in MT)2017-18	6	539.32	193062018-19	6	718.47	249552019-20	9	819.59	25212Total	21	2077.38	69473 *****
