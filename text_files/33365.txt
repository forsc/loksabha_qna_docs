                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

739






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






TEJAS Training




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any scheme called TEJAS- Training in Emirates Job and Skills, if so, the details regarding the same including the selection and application process;(b)	whether any new skills training centres are being started in India for TEJAS program and the details of the aims to use existing infrastructure mechanisms for skill training;(c)	whether Government has any schemes for the skill development of lakhs of skilled and unskilled labourers in Gulf region, if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether Skill Development Ministry has initiate any steps to coordinate with CRCs under Ministry of Social Justice for Empowerment of differently abled; and (e)	whether the Government will enhance programmes and facilities at National Skill Training Institute Kozhikode?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	 National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) is presently executing a pilot project in tie up with employers in United Arab Emirates (UAE) guided by aggregation of demand, mobilization, training and certification within existing infrastructure and facilities. This shall be scaled up if there is market demand by aggregating all variety of employers, supply institutions and with new processes under new brand of Skill India International.(b)	The existing training infrastructure of the IISC Network Member Organizations and Sector Skill Councils (SSCs) are being used for the Global engagement by NSDC.(c)	There is no Government scheme by Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) at present. However, NSDC had conducted Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) training to unskilled/skilled workers during 2019 in Abu Dhabi in collaboration with the training partners and employers. A similar RPL model would be replicated as per market demand in consultation with the destination country and candidates as registered on the Skilled Workers Arrival Database for Employment Support (SWADES) portal.(d)	Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship has not initiated any steps to coordinate with CRCs under Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment for empowerment of differently abled.(e)	National Skill Training Institute Kozhikode facilitates short-term training programme and regular long-term training programme in various disciplines. Long term courses are conducted under the Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS), Craft Instructor Training Scheme (CITS) and recently started Advanced Diploma in Information Technology (IT), Networking and Cloud also. There is high demand of IT, Networking and Cloud courses which are essential in the current scenario due to a lot of demand in software industries where every industry is using cloud as base.The number of Trainees trained for Skill Training under Craft Instructor Training Scheme (CITS) and Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS) in National Skill Training Institute Kozhikode is given in the Annexure.
