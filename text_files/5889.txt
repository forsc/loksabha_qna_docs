                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5277






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






National Commission for Minorities




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of tours or study trips made by the National Commission for Minorities since 2014, year-wise;(b)	 the details of expenses incurred/ spent for such tours, year and trip-wise; (c)	 whether an official report was published in the public domain by the Commission after each tour since 2014; and (d)	if so, the details thereof along with the report numbers, date of publication and report titles?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	 As per information furnished  by the National  Commission for Minorities (NCM),  the year-wise  tours undertaken by the officials of the NCM from 2014-15 to 2018-19 is as under:-Year	No. of tours undertaken2014-15	372015-16	292016-17	132017-18	742018-19	155(b)	 Year-wise details of the expenditure incurred on official tours of NCM is as under:-Year	Total expenditure (Rupees  in Lac)2014-15	41.432015-16	18.492016-17	16.112017-18	30.782018-19	55.88(c)&(d)   The tour reports of the Commission are incorporated in its Annual Reports, which comes under public domain (website:http://ncm.nic.in/), after laying the same in both the Houses of Parliament.*********
