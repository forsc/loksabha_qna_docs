                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






National Food Security Act




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of beneficiaries covered in the entire nation under National Food Security Act (NFSA); (b) 	the State/Union Territory-wise details thereof; (c) 	whether target of any kind was fixed under national Food Security Act by the Central Government; (d) 	if so, the present status thereof; (e) 	if not, whether the Government proposes to set any target for future; (f) 	the amount allocated under National Food Security Act (NFSA) in the country during the last two years; and (g) 	the State/Union Territory-wise details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	The National Food Security Act, 2013 (NFSA) is being implemented in all the States/ Union Territories covering approximately 79.53 crore beneficiaries at all India level.(b): 	A statement on State/Union Territory wise number of persons covered under NFSA is at Annexure-I.(c): 	The NFSA provides for coverage upto 75% of rural population and upto 50% of urban population for receiving highly subsidized foodgrains under Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS), thus covering about two-thirds of the total population which at Census 2011 comes to 81.35 crore.  Corresponding to the all India coverage of 75% and 50% in the rural and urban areas, State-wise coverage was determined by the then Planning Commission (now NITI Aayog) by using the NSSO Household Consumption Expenditure Survey data for 2011-12.(d): 	Presently, approx 79.53 crore persons are covered under NFSA. (e): 	Question does not arise.(f) & (g): 	Details of year-wise fund allocated and State-wise fund released during last two years as Food Subsidy to Food Corporation of India (FCI), Food Subsidy to Decentralized Procurement (DCP) States and Central assistance for expenditure incurred towards intra-State transportation and handling & FPS dealers margin is at Annexure-II.
