                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3548






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Functioning of NGOs




Durga Prasad Rao Balli














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the concept of Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs) and the number of NGOs that are rendering services to the society; (b) the details of the list of NGOs in Andhra Pradesh, Telangana and Tamil Nadu; (c) the details of fund allocation to the NGOs of Andhra Pradesh, Telangana and Tamil Nadu during the last three years and the current year; and (d) the list of NGOs registered with NITI AAYOG from Andhra Pradesh, Telangana and Tamil Nadu?






ANSWER




                                                        (a): The mandate of this Department is to implement of the schemes of educational, economic and social empowerment of Scheduled Castes (SCs) and Other Backward Classes (OBCs), Senior Citizens, victims of Drug Abuse, transgenders, Denotified Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Tribes (DNTs).This Department provides financial assistance to Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs) who work for the welfare of Scheduled Castes, Senior Citizens and in the field of Drug Prevention.(b): The details of the list of NGOs in Andhra Pradesh, Telangana and Tamil Nadu is given at Annexure-I.(c): The details of fund allocation to the NGOs of Andhra Pradesh, Telangana and Tamil Nadu is given at Annexure-II.(d) :  The State-wise list of NGO registered with NIT AAYOG is available on DARPAN portal of NITI AAYOG under following link:*****Annexure –IRefer to part (b) of the reply to the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 3548 for 10.12.2019 (i) Name of NGOs receiving financial assistance under the Scheme of Grant in Aid to Voluntary and other organisations working for Scheduled Castes:(A) Andhra Pradesh:1.	Lakshmi Mahila Mandali2.	Maddirala Sivaiah Memorial Education Society3.	Sri Venkateshwara Mahila Mandali4.	Centre for Rural Education and Development Society5.	International Education Society(B)	Telangana:1.	Goutham Education Society2.	Loka Seva Kendram3.	Padmavathi Palleseema Abhivrudhi(C) Tamil Nadu1.	Grameeya Makkal Abhivruddhi2.	Grameeya Social Welfare Society3.	Harijan Sevak Sangha4.	Center for Development and communication Trust
