                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2755






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Moratorium on Payment of Dues by TSPs




D. Ravikumar














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) who have been ordered by the apex court to pay in long pending overdues of nearly 92,000 crore rupees have asked the Government for a moratorium of two years on the payment of the said dues; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) &  (b) 	No proposal for a moratorium of two years on AGR related dues consequent upon Hon’ble Supreme Court judgement dated 24.10.2019 has been received from Telecom Service Providers(TSPs) in Department of Telecommunication.************
