                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4033






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






PMAY Houses in West Bengal




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of urban and rural houses constructed under Pradhan Mantri Aawas Yojana (PMAY) in the Districts of Darjeeling, Kalimpong and North Dinajpur since 2014 till date; (b) the number of eligible people awaiting allotment of houses under PMAY in the above districts vis-a-vis the eligible people allotted houses under PMAY so far; and (c) the steps taken by the Government to ensure provision of houses to all the homeless people of these districts at the earliest? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	& (b) : Details of the number of urban and rural houses sanctioned and constructed as well as eligible people awaiting allotment of houses under Pradhan Mantri Aawas Yojana (PMAY)in the Districts of Darjeeling, Kalimpong and North Dinajpur of West Bengal since 2014 till date are at Annexure. (c). The Government is committed to fulfil the demand of houses to achieve the objective of Housing for All by 2022. While the Ministry of Rural Development has assigned the target to the State for construction of houses for the identified beneficiaries in the rural areas, the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs has requested the State to submit project proposals under PMAY (U) to saturate their housing requirement at the earliest in the urban areas.                               *****
