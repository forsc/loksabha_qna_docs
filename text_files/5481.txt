                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5069






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Safety of Passengers in Trains




P.P. Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the zone-wise details of the number of complaints received from the passengers especially from the women passengers with regard to the difficulties faced by them during the last five years; (b) the details of the new steps taken by the Government for the security of train passengers during the last five years;(c) the new steps proposed by the Government for passenger safety in the coming year;(d) whether the Government proposes to install CCTV cameras in each coach of the trains;(e) if so, the details of the roadmap thereof; and(f) if not, the measures contemplated by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY       (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (f): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
