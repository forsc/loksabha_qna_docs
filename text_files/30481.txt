                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

187






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






AIIMS Projects under PM Swasthya Suraksha Yojana




Dayanidhi Maran













                                                    Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) projects sanctioned under Phase V, VI and VII of Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana along with their present status and the timeline set for their completion, project-wise including AIIMS, Madurai and AIIMS, Telangana; (b) the funds allocated and spent on each of the said projects, itemised list-wise, year-wise and project-wise; (c) the quantum of funds allocated and spent or proposed to be spent on recruitment, academic grants, salaries or institutional operational costs for AIIMS, Madurai; (d) whether any alternative locations or institutes have been selected for operationalising AIIMS, Madurai on a temporary or interim basis and if so, the details thereof along with the criteria adopted for selection of such locations/institutions; and (e) the reasons for delay in completion of the AIIMS, Madurai and AIIMS, Telangana projects?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a)	to (e) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. 187* FOR 10TH DECEMBER, 2021(a) to (e) Status in respect of 11 AIIMS sanctioned under Phase V, VI and VII of Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY) with their present progress and the timeline for their completion, project wise including AIIMS Madurai and AIIMS, Telangana,  is at Annexure-I.     	The funds allocated and released on each of the said projects, component-wise, year-wise and project-wise is at Annexure-II.  For operational expenditure at the Institute level, an amount of Rs. 5 Crore has been released to AIIMS, Madurai during the financial year 2021-22, out of which the reported expenditure is Rs. 24.25 lakhs.Funds for setting up of all new All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) as a project are provided separately and released to the Executing Agency out of the allocation under Capital Head of the PMSSY budget.  In the absence of own campus of AIIMS, Madurai at present, this Ministry is in discussion with Government of Tamil Nadu, to identify a temporary campus with adequate facilities for teaching / learning including laboratory facilities and hostel accommodation for students for starting MBBS classes.   	AIIMS Madurai project is being funded through an Official Development Assistance(ODA) Loan from the Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA).  The initial preparatory work by JICA, including survey and fact finding missions and multiple rounds of consultation with officials of Ministry of Health & Family Welfare took time and the loan agreement with JICA could be signed on 26-3-2021 only. The Executing Agency for pre-investment work has been appointed and the pre-investment activities are completed substantially. The process of appointment of Project Management Consultant is in progress and the target date for completion of the project is October 2026. As regards AIIMS, Telangana, pre-investment activities have already been completed and the   Executing Agency (EA) has been appointed for the main construction work.  Target date of completion of the project is November 2023.  In the meanwhile, MBBS batch of AIIMS Bibinagar, Telangana has already started from the academic year 2019-20 and limited OPD services have started with effect from 2-6-2020 at the Institute.  *****
