                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1186






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Work Status at HURL




Pashupati Nath Singh













                                                    Asaduddin Owaisi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the status of work completed in Hindustan Urvarak and Rasayan Limited (HURL), Sindri in Jharkhand; (b) whether the said work is running as per the schedule fixed by the Government and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) whether the Government proposes to undertake development of local area especially in the field of education, health and road infrastructure through joint venture between the Government and Hindustan Urvarak and Rasayan Limited, Sindri and if so, the details thereof; (d) if not, the reasons therefor and the details of the schemes and steps being taken to resolve the problems related to development of the said infracture?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS(SHRI MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a):	Hindustan Urvarak and Rasayan Limited (HURL) Sindri project has achieved overall progress of 90 % as on 30th June 2021.(b):	 As per Detailed Feasibility Report (DFR), the commissioning of the project was scheduled in May, 2021. However, due to Covid-19 pandemic, the commissioning date has been rescheduled to February, 2022.(c):	No Sir, there is no such proposal in this Department at present.(d): 	The denvelopment of education, health and road infrastructure etc in the region is primarily looked after by the State.                                       ********
