                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2006






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of people enrolled in Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana (RSBY) in the State of Telangana, district-wise;(b) the number of people who have claimed free medical treatment under RSBY in the State of Telangana, district-wise; and(c) the total funds claimed for free medical treatment under RSBY in the State of Telangana, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):	Rashtriya Swasthya Bima Yojana was not being implemented in Telangana at the time of launch of Ayushman Bharat – Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY). With the launch of AB-PMJAY on 23.09.2018, RSBY has been subsumed in it.Further, the State of Telangana is not implementing AB-PMJAY.
