                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3103






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Sick Heavy Industries in Public Sector




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details and the number of heavy industries set up in the public sector in different States in the country;(b)	the details of the performance of those units;(c)	whether any of these units has fallen sick;(d)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and(e)	the steps taken by the Government to resume those units?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a):	Setting up of industries is a State subject and therefore, the Ministry of Heavy Industries (MHI) does not maintain any centralized data on setting up of public sector heavy industries in different States in the country.(b) to (e):	Does not arise.----------
