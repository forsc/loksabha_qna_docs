                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

757






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






IISER at Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh




 Chinta Anuradha













                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                








                                                    Maddila  Gurumoorthy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the budget sanctioned by the Government for constructing permanent campus of IISER at Tirupati Andhra Pradesh; (b) whether the construction of the campus has started; (c) if so, the details thereof, and if not, the reasons therefor; (d) whether the Government can fulfill its obligation towards IISER by the year 2024 in accordance with Section 93 and item 1 in Thirteenth Schedule of Andhra Pradesh Reorganization Act, 2014; and (e) if so, the details thereof, and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a): The Union Cabinet has approved a total of Rs. 1491.34 Crore (Rs. 1137.16 Crore for Capital and Rs. 354.18 Crores for Recurring) for construction of permanent campus of IISER Tirupati.(b) & (c):The construction of permanent campus started in May, 2018. The details are as follows:(i) Construction of two students’ double occupancy hostels commenced in May, 2018 and was completed in August, 2020.(ii) Construction of Undergraduate Laboratory commenced in June, 2019 and was completed in October, 2020.(iii) Construction of other major buildings commenced in October, 2020. (d) & (e): Yes, Sir. An IISER has been established in Tirupati in the year 2015.*****
