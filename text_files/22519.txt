                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3607






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Fog Pass Devices




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has introduced fog pass devices in trains passing through foggy routes in Northern and North Eastern Region of the country ; (b)	if so, the details of trains fitted with this device till date ;(c)	whether the Government has any plans to extend this device to other trains that runs in climatic conditions like excessive rains, wind,  etc.; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	Yes, Sir.(b) 	A GPS based portable hand-held Fog PASS device is being provided to loco pilots in fog affected areas which raises audio visual signals whenever landmarks like signals, level crossing gates etc. are approaching within the Geo-fence range. As on 01-03-2021, 12,742 Fog PASS Devices have been provided on Indian Railways.(c)	No, Sir.(d)	Does not arise.*****
