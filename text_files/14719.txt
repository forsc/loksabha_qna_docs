                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3398






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Environment and Forest Clearance




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether a large number of project proposals are pending with the Government from North Eastern Region States including Assam for want of environment and forest clearance; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has categorised the development projects which have been pending for want of forest clearance and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether any new “Open Cast Coal Mining” projects have been cleared in the Tinsukia district of Assam and Changlang district of Arunachal Pradesh; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) to (c) As per the available information on PARIVESH, there are 11 projects, located in North Eastern Region including Assam, which are at various stages of appraisal under Environment Impact Assessment Notification, 2006 and its amendment from time to time in the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change.There are no cases pending in the Ministry for grant of approval (Stage II Clearance) under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 for North Eastern Region. However, there are 2 projects under process in the Ministry and 5 projects in the Regional Office, Shillong for grant of Stage-I approval (In-Principle) under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. State wise details of projects under consideration for environmental clearance, Stage I (In-Principle) forest clearance and projects for which Stage-I/Stage-IIforest clearance is granted are annexed.(d) & (e) No environment clearance for Open Cast Coal Mining project has been granted in the Tinsukia district of Assam and Changlang district of Arunachal Pradesh during 2018-19 and 2019-20.  However, forest clearance has been accorded to 1 project in Tinsukia district of Assam during 2019-20. State-wise details are annexed.*******
