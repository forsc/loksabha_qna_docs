                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1660






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Import of Maize




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the maize import policy has permitted the import of higher quantities of maize corn at lower prices and reduced the prices of native variety of maize in the market, especially in Telangana; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether there have been lapses in enforcing the user condition that imported maize must be utilised for self-use and manufacturing purposes alone; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        	THE MINISTER OF  COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY  (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a)	to (d):  Import policy of Maize (corn) except seed is ''Free''. However, in compliance of Orders of the Hon''ble High Court, at present, import of maize (corn) is allowed through licence for actual users only and on payment of prevailing customs duty without any concessional duty. For compliance of Actual User condition, prior inspection of applicant firms and verification of documents submitted by them is done before issuance of licences.*****
