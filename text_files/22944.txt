                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4139






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Stone Crusher Plants in North-Eastern States




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of Stone Crusher mills/plants in North-eastern States, plant-wise; (b)	the number of Stone Crusher mills set up in various districts of Assam, including the Bodoland Territory, district-wise and plant-wise; and (c)	whether the stone Crusher mills have been permitted to dig the area along rivers and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)to (c) More than 800  Stone Crushing  mills  are established in North-eastern states.  Details of Stone Crusher in North-eastern States are being obtained.List of Stone Crushing mills established in various districts of Assam is at Annexure .Stand-alone stone crushers are established for sizing of stones, therefore no digging is required. No Environment Clearance is to be obtained for establishing stand-alone stone crushers.  However, permission needs to be obtained as per Stone Crushing policy from State Pollution Control Boards (SPCBs)/ Pollution Control Committees (PCCs). 	Wherever Stone Crushers are established along with mining activity Environment Clearance is required to be obtained for the mining activity and Consent to Establish / Consent to Operate are required to be taken from concern SPCBs/ PCCs under the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974. No mining activity is permitted within 500 meters along the river.****
