                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

391






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Development of Coconut Cultivation




Thomas Chazhikadan














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the detailed programmes/schemes for development of coconut cultivation/production in the country;(b) 	the annual production of coconut in the country as on 31st October, 2021, State-wise;(c) 	the initiatives of the Coconut Development Board (CDB) for promoting value added products from raw coconut across the country and the details thereof; and(d) 	the detailed programmes of the Government/CDB for providing better marketing facilities to the coconut farmers?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????  (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) :  For the Integrated development of coconut sector in the country, the Coconut Development Board (CDB) is implementing following schemes under the Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) of the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, Government of India :(i) 	Production and Distribution of Quality Planting Materials(ii)	Expansion of Area under Coconut(iii)	Integrated Farming for productivity improvement(iv)	Technology Demonstration/Quality Testing Lab(v)	Marketing, Market Intelligent Services, Statistics and Strengthening of Export Promotion Council(vi)	Information & Information Technology (vii)	Technical Service, Project Management including Infrastructure and Administration(viii)	Technology Mission on Coconut(ix)	Rejuvenation and Replanting(x)	Coconut Palm Insurance Scheme(xi)	Kera Suraksha Insurance Scheme(b): 	As per the latest all India 2nd advanced estimates for the year 2020-21, the production of coconut in the country is 21,206.74 million nuts. The State-wise details are at Annexure.(c):  The Board implements ‘Technology Mission on Coconut’ (TMOC) from the year 2001-02 for promoting value addition in coconut with components of development, demonstration and adoption of technologies. The Board also implements a scheme ‘Technology Demonstration & Quality Testing laboratory’ under which CDB Institute of Technology (CIT) involves in product development, quality testing and imparting trainings on various coconut based products. The Board has also developed several new products through the CIT and other reputed research Organizations. (d): 	The Board undertakes various market promotion activities for development of the coconut sector in the country. The Board undertakes market intelligence activities, market research studies and supports farmer collectives. The Board is the notified Export Promotion Council for coconut and its products other than coir and performs the responsibilities for the same.
