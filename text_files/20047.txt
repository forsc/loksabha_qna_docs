                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1159






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Deaths due to Cold Waves




Kunwar Danish Ali














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of people who died due to cold weather and cold waves across the country during the last five years including 2020-21, State-wise;(b) whether the Government has given any compensation to the families of such people;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to face such a calamity?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (d):   The primary responsibility to take necessary measures to prevent deaths from cold weather and cold wave in the country rests with the State Government concerned. The data on deaths due to cold weather and cold wave is not maintained centrally by the Ministry of Home Affairs.However, as per information received from National Crime Record Bureau (NCRB), the details of the death caused due to exposure to cold during 2015 to 2019 are at Annexure. -2-L.S.US.Q NO. 1159 FOR 09.02.2021The damages caused due to cold waves & frost stands included as one of the eligible natural calamity for providing assistance by State Governments from State Disaster Risk Management Fund (SDRMF)/National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF) as per the norms. A copy of items and norms is available athttps://www.ndmindia.nic.in/images/pdf/SDRF-NDRFNormsdated8april,2015.pdf .
