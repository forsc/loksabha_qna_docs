                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

325






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Slum Development Projects in Salem




S.R. Parthiban














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of slum development projects under Smart Cities Mission (SCM) which have been identified in Salem district of Tamil Nadu;(b)	if so, the details of the proposals in this regard;(c)	whether the Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) have been finalized; and(d)	if so, the contours of works that are going to be undertaken in the above district and the time by which work orders are likely to be issued, town/city-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                               (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	to (d) : A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.                                   STATEMENTSTATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY OF PARTS (a) to (d) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *325 (5TH POSITION) FOR ANSWER ON 18.3.2021 REGARDING “SLUM DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS IN SALEM”.(a)	to (d) Salem Smart City has reported that no slum development project has been undertaken by them under Smart Cities Mission (SCM).  The city of Salem was selected for development as a Smart City in Round-2 of the Smart Cities Challenge in September, 2016. Salem Smart City has undertaken 93 projects worth ? 2,077 crore, out of which 44 projects worth ? 1270.4 crore (61%) have already been completed.  Currently, 49 projects worth ? 806.8 crore, for which work orders have already been issued, are under implementation.  Salem Smart City is expected to complete its projects within five years from the date of its selection as Smart City. Details of projects of Salem Smart City are given in Annexure-I.                                         ******
