                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4016






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Scholarship to Young Artists




Dileshwar Kamait













                                                    Geeta Kora
                                                








                                                    Jugal Kishore Sharma
                                                








                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is providing scholarships to the young artists through the Centre for Cultural Resource and Training (CCRT);(b)	the number of young artists who are to be provided scholarships in Jammu & Kashmir, Jharkhand, Bihar and Madhya Pradesh including the amount allocated in this regard during the last three years; and(c)	the number of young artists who have been provided the said scholarship in Jammu & Kashmir, Jharkhand, Bihar and Madhya Pradesh during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM and DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(G. KISHAN REDDY) (a)	Yes sir, Government is providing scholarships to the young artists through the Centre for Cultural Resource and Training (CCRT). In this scheme, a scholarship of Rs. 5,000/- per month is provided to selected scholars for a period of 02 years in four equal six monthly installments.   (b)&(c)	The scholarships are decided and given as per the eligibility of the applicant only. No separate State-wise quota is fixed for scholarships. The number of young artists who have been selected for scholarships under the scheme from the States of Jammu and Kashmir, Jharkhand, Bihar and Madhya Pradesh during the last three years are given below:-State	Number of selected scholarsJammu & Kashmir	9Jharkhand	14Bihar	38Madhya Pradesh	89***
