                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

443






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Infiltration of Terrorists




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Government has initiated various arrangements to stop the infiltration of the terrorists along the border areas of the country during the last four years and the current year; and(b) if so, the details and the outcome thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (b):  The Government of India in tandem with the State Governments, has adopted a multi-pronged approach to contain cross border infiltration.  This includes strengthening of border management and increased deployment of Security Forces along the International Border/ Line of Control plugging the changing infiltration routes.  The measures include construction/ maintenance of border fencing, modern weapons and equipments for Security Forces, installation of border floodlights, improved intelligence and synergizing intelligence flow, operational coordination and pro-active action against terrorists. A technological solution in  the  form of Comprehensive  Integrated  Border -2-LS.US.Q.NO. 443 for 25.06.2019Management System (CIBMS) has been implemented in some vulnerable border areas. This includes smart fencing, state-of-the-art surveillance technologies such as thermal imagers, infra-red and laser-based intruder alarms, aerostats for aerial surveillance and ground sensors etc.  *******
