                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

328






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Medical Officers in Para-Military Forces




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any steps for recruiting to the posts of Specialist Medical Officers and Medical Officers in Central Para-Military Forces, namely, Central Reserve Police Force, Border Security Force, Assam Rifles, Sashastra Seema Bal and Indo-Tibetan Border Police.(b) if so, the details of the un-filled vacancies in the above category as on 15th February 2021 force-wise; and(c) whether the Government will take any steps to expedite the recruitment process by appointing candidates from reserve rank list of candidates before its expiry?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI) (a) & (b):	 Recruitment of Specialist and General Duty Medical Officers in Central Armed Police Forces & Assam Rifles is being made regularly through Medical Officers Selection Board (MOSB). The force-wise details of the un-filled vacancies of Specialist Medical Officers (Spl. MO) and General Duty Medical Officers (GDMOs) as on 15th February, 2021 is as under:--2-L.S.US.Q.NO.328 FOR 20.07.2021Force	Spl.MOs	GDMOsAR	04	31BSF	51	93CRPF	112	85ITBP*	08	70SSB	18	53*ITBP Cadre includes Medical Officers of CISF also. Further, in the wake of Covid-19 pandemic, CRPF has been allowed to recruit General Duty Medical Officers (GDMOs) on contract basis against vacant posts of Specialists Medical Officers for a period of one year.(c):	Offers have already been given to the candidates in the reserve list of last MOSB and many candidates have joined against the vacant posts.*****
