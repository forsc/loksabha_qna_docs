                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2034






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






AYUSH Treatment Packages




Amar Singh













                                                    M.K. Vishnu Prasad
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government proposes to include certain Ayurvedic, Yoga, Unani, Siddha and Homoeopathy (AYUSH) treatment packages in the Pradhan Mantri Jan Aarogya Yojana (PMJAY), if so, the guidelines finalised and issued in this regard; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c) the time by which this proposal will be implemented and the estimated number of beneficiaries to be covered thereunder?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (c) :  The Ministry of AYUSH has suggested   bringing Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy,  Unani, Siddha and Homoeopathy (AYUSH) systems of medicines under the AYUSHMAN Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) scheme along with various packages for day care and hospitalization expenses to National Health Authority (NHA).   However, at present, there is no proposal by National Health Authority to include any Ayurvedic, Yoga, Unani, Siddha and Homoeopathy (AYUSH) treatment packages in the current package list of Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB PM-JAY).………………….
