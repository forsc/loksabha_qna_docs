                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3008






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Roads under National Highway Projects




S. Muniswamy














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to include more roads under the national highway 	projects for development and doubling in the country including Kolar in Karnataka; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	the details of roads under consideration for the purpose; and (d)	the time frame set for completion of these works?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d) 	The Government of India had approved Bharatmala Pariyojana Phase-I in October, 2017 for development of economic corridors, inter corridors, feeder routes, national corridors, border roads, international connectivity roads, coastal roads, port connectivity roads and expressways with an aggregate length of about 34,800 km (including 10,000 km residual NHDP stretches) in the entire country including state of Karnataka,at an estimated outlay of Rs. 5,35,000.00 crores. The scheduled completion period for Bharatmala Pariyojana Phase-I is five years. The following projects stretches have been envisaged for development in Kolar district of Karnataka under Bharatmala Pariyojana:Sr. No.	Name of project stretches	Length in Kolar District (km)(i)	Bangalore – Chennai Greenfield Expressway passing through Kolar District starting Gangapura of Malur Taluk and ending at NG HulkurBangarpet Taluk	59.318(ii)	Greenfield project from Tamil Nadu/Karnataka Boarder – Hoskote section of NH-648 passing through Kolar District starting from Sampangere village and ending at Dommaluru village.	8.6	The work of detailed project report / feasibility study for aforesaid project stretches has been initiated.*****
