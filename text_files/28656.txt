                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

848






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Present Status of PMAY




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) at the national level;(b)	the number of families already covered under this project so far in Odisha, district-wise;(c)	the target of households in the districts of Dhenkanal and Angul; and(d)	the number of houses constructed so far in above districts and the time by which remaining targets are likely to be achieved?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                   (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a):  Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban [PMAY-U], against validated demand of 112.24 lakh houses, 114.06 lakh houses have been sanctioned as on 22.11.2021; of this, 89.36 lakh houses have been grounded and 52.55 lakh have been completed. (b)	to (d) : The district-wise details of houses sanctioned, grounded and completed under PMAY-U in the State of Odisha so far, including Dhenkanal and Angul districts, are annexed. All  States/Union Territories, including Odisha, have been advised to complete all the sanctioned projects/houses within the stipulated timeline.                                      *****
