                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1347






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Preservation of Cultural Heritage related to Shivaji Raje




Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken measures to preserve and uphold all forms of Forts of Shivaji Raje, folk art and cultural heritage of thecountry;(b)	 if so, the details thereof;(c)	the efforts being made to properly store the treasure of knowledge for posterity;(d)	 whether the Government has taken adequate steps to preserve the cultural heritage of Forts made by King Shivaji; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE  AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)    to   (e)	Conservation work of protected Forts in the country including those related to Shivaji Raje is attended regularly by the  Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) as per the requirements of different sites and availability of resources.  Conservation work is a continuous process and regular action by ASI ensures proper preservation of protected art forms for posterity.  Due to regular repair & maintenance work the protected monuments including forts are in a good state of preservation.
