                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3513






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Differntly Abled Persons




Arun Kumar Sagar














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the estimated population of differently abled persons in the country as on date especially in the backward and rural areas of the country;(b)	the total number of visually impaired persons in the country, State-wise;(c)	the important schemes being run by the Union Government for the welfare of these person in each State of the country;(d)	the schemes run by the Government during the last three years till date in the country, the details thereof, year-wise and State-wise especially in the State of Uttar Pradesh;(e)	the details of the funds allocated during the said period, year-wise and State-wise; and(f)	the total number of differently abled benefited from these schemes especially in the State of Uttar Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a)	According to Census 2011, there are 2.68 crore Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) in the country. Out of the total population of PwDs, 1.86 crore are from rural areas.(b)	The total number of persons with visual impairment (State/UT-wise), as per the Census 2011, is at Annexure I.(c)	to (f): The Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities implements the following major schemes for empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) including persons with visual impairment:(i)	Assistance to Disabled Persons for Purchase/Fitting of Aids and Appliances (ADIP),(ii)	Deendayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme (DDRS),(iii)	Scholarship schemes for Students with Disabilities,(iv)	National Action Plan for Skill Development of Persons with Disabilities,(v)	Accessible India Campaign (AIC), Details of these schemes, fund allocated/released and persons benefitted, States/UTs and year-wise during the last three years and till date (including in the State of Uttar Pradesh) are at Annexure II. Ministry of Rural Development has informed that under Indira Gandhi National Disability Pension Scheme (IGNDPS), disability pension of Rs. 300/- per month is given to the persons belonging to Below Poverty Line (BPL) households of age of 18-79 years with severe and multiple disabilities. The pension increases to Rs. 500/- per month upon attaining the age of 80 years. State/UT- wise details of beneficiaries covered and funds allocated for implementation of IGNDPS, during the last three years and current year are given in Annexure III.*****
