                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3782TO BE ANSWERED ON 21.12.2021SCHEMES FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EQUALITY 3782. SHRI SUNIL KUMAR MONDAL: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has been succesful in providing social justice and equality among people through its various schemes with regard to poverty, homelessness, gender inequality, racial discrimination and etc. during last five years specially during corona pandemic; (b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise and the outcomes thereof, if not, the reasons therefor; and (c) whether any developmental steps taken in this regard, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER

The Department is implementing various programmes/schemes for the welfare of these poor and marginalized sections.
