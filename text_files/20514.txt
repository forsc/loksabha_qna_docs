                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1926






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Pending AIIMS Projects




Dushyant Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) under construction in the country, State-wise;(b) the launch date of each project along with the status of loan raised and amount of money disbursed for each of the undergoing projects;(c) the amount of loan that is being raised from foreign entities to fund building of these projects along with details of interest rate and amount of money disbursed by foreign entities funding the project;(d) the time period within which these projects are proposed to be completed along with current status of each project;(e) total quantity of the land that is to be acquired for each project and the current area of land that has been acquired by the Government; and(f) whether Government is looking forward to build more such institutes and if so, details of location of such institutes?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (d): Setting up of 22 new AIIMS has been approved under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY), 6 of which are functional. List of remaining 16 is at Annexure. AIIMS projects are primarily funded through budgetary allocations under PMSSY. However for some AIIMS projects, funding through loan from Higher Education Financing Agency (HEFA) was also provided. Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) is supporting setting up of AIIMS at Madurai. Details of total approved cost, funds released, loan availed from HEFA, progress and expected date of completion in respect of each of the 16 AIIMS is  at Annexure. (e): On an average, the requirement of land for setting up of AIIMS is about 200 acres. Acquisition of land for this purpose and its transfer   to Government of India is primarily the responsibility of the State Governments.(f): AIIMS are set up in various parts of the country in a phased manner as per budget announcements keeping in view gaps in tertiary healthcare infrastructures in different regions of the country and availability of funds.
