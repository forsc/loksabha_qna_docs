                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

732






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Fund Enhancement for Toilets




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to enhance the funds provided for construction of Individual Household, Community and Public Toilets (PTs) and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (b) 	whether funds provided to set up Project Implementing Units at the Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) level with 1.5 per cent of the total budget earmarked for capacity building activities is likely to be increased; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c) : No Sir, there is no such proposal. The Mission is upto 31.3.2021.                                            *****
