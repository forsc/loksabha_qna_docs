                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2412






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Funds For Heritage Property Development in Maharashtra




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	The strategy and policy of the  Government to maintain and develop heritage properties in Maharashtra;(b)	Whether the Government has sanctioned any funds for heritage property development in Maharashtra; and(c)	If so, the details thereof;






ANSWER




MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) 	Conservation, preservation and environmental development work of protected monuments in the country including Maharashtra is a continuous process and same is attended regularly by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) as per availability of resources.  All conservation works are undertaken as per ASI’s National Policy for conservation of monuments, Archaeological Sites and Remains, 2014. (b) & (c)	Yes Sir, the funds allocated for conservation, preservation and environmental development work of protected monuments in Maharashtra, for current financial year 2021-22 is Rs. 24.50 Crore. **********
