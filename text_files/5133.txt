                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4631






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Conversion of Natural Gas into Methanol




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has requested the Oil India Limited to locate at least 5 or 6 oil fields in North East region for converting standard gas into Methanol to accelerate country’s shift towards its use as an alternative fuel; and(b) if so, the details thereof along with the action taken by the Oil India Limited on this proposal?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) No such request has been sent by this Ministry to Oil India Limited. Gas is allocated as per Gas Allocation Policy, and as per the policy priority is given to City Gas Distribution (CGD) and gas based Fertilizer Plants and any increased production will also be allocated as per Gas Allocation Policy.*****
