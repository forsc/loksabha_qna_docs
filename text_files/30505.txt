                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2171






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Rohingya Issue




Manoj Kumar Tiwari














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has faced any setbacks in Myanmar policy due to Rohingya issue;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	if not  the reasons therefor; and(d)	the corrective steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) to (d)	Government has called for the safe, speedy and sustainable repatriation of displaced persons from the Rakhine State of Myanmar. In this context, Government has taken up the issue of their repatriation with the Government of Myanmar. The two sides have also agreed that the only lasting solution to the problem lies in the overall development of the Rakhine State in Myanmar. In this regard, Government has signed an MoU on the Rakhine State Development Programme with Myanmar in December 2017. Under this programme, a project of 250 fabricated houses in the Rakhine State was completed and handed over to the authorities in July 2019.***
