                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2905






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Retirement Age in Army




Abdul Khaleque













                                                    Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt
                                                








                                                    Suresh Kodikunnil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government is considering to increase the retirement age of Defence Service Personnel particularly of those below the rank of officers;(b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(c) whether this is for both the combat and the non-combat personnel and if so, the details thereof and its likely impact on the fitness level of the Army personnel;(d) whether there has been an actual decrease in the number of applicants in the Indian Army especially at the officers level; and(e) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor along with the action intended to improve the recruitment in Armed Forces?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  to (c): Defence Services are examining this issue; however no formal proposal has been received by the Ministry of Defence.(d) & (e):  There has been no perceptible decrease in the number of applications received by the Indian Army. *****
