                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3857






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Low Cost Organic Farming




Hanuman Beniwal













                                                    Indra Hang Subba
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details regarding the total amount of organic products produced in the country annually during the last five years; (b) 	the details of the action plans of the Government to promote organic farming and encourage farmers to adopt low cost organic farming along with the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Union Government provides subsidy to the farmers engaged in organic farming; (d) 	if so, the criteria adopted by the Government in this regard along with the manner in which subsidy is provided to the farmers; and (e) 	the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	During the last five years, 7,894,757.82 MT of organic production has been recorded as per the TraceNet report of   Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority (APEDA).  Similarly, as per Participatory Guarantee System (PGS) portal data,   213067.72 MT of organic production is recorded during the same period. (b) to (e):	Government  of India has been  promoting organic farming  through the dedicated schemes of Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) and Mission Organic Value Chain Development for North Eastern Region (MOVCDNER) since 2015-16.  Under PKVY, assistance is provided to the farmers for on farm and off farm input production/ procurement, creation of post harvest infrastructure,   marketing etc. for encouraging organic farming.   Farmer within a group can avail the benefit to a maximum of 2 hectare and the limit of assistance is Rs.50, 000 per hectare/ 3 years, out of which Rs. 31,000 (61%) is given as incentive to the farmer directly through DBT. PKVY farmers adopt Participatory Guarantee System (PGS) of certification through peer reviews. Contd…2/--2-The main objective of MOVCDNER is to encourage farmers of   north eastern region to form a third party certified hub ready for export of niche organic crops. Assistance is provided to the farmers in a value chain mode starting from formation of Farmers Producer Organisations( FPOs) ,    on/off farm input production, supply of seeds/ planting materials, post harvest infrastructure including collection, sorting, grading facilities,   establishment of integrated processing unit, refrigerated transportation, pre-cooling/ cold stores chamber, branding, labelling and packaging, etc .        ******
