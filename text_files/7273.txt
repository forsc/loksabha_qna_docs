                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1024






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






MoUs between India and Foreign Broadcasters




Geetha Viswanath Vanga













                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has given ex-post facto approval for the MoUs between India and foreign broadcasters in the field of radio and television, if so, the details thereof; and (b)	 the details of Indian and foreign broadcasters selected under this MoU?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)a)	& (b): Yes Sir, 27 Memoranda of Understanding (MoUs) have been signed between Prasar Bharati and foreign broadcasters for cooperation in the field of radio and television. The MoUs signed by Prasar Bharati with foreign Broadcasters provide for the following:a)	 Exchange of programmes in the areas of culture, education, archaeology, history, science, sports, news etc .b)	Cooperation in the fields of co-production, news gathering, content exchange, distribution of radio signal, training, exchange of technical knowhow, digitalization etc.c)	Mutual exchange of personnel with the aim of exchanging expertise d)	Signing of these MoUs paved the way for greater cooperation and exchange of rich heritage and culture between Prasar Bharati and the participating countries.***********
