                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1173






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Power Train Components of EVs




Ramya  Haridas














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that India supply chain for critical power train components of Electric Vehicles (EVs) will come up only if there are enough such vehicles on the road, if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether it is also a fact that the industry body Society of Manufacturers of Electric Vehicles (SMEV) has said that Government should give more thrust on mobility under the smart city project which is currently missing; and(c)	if so, whether the Government is considering the proposal of SMEV?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a):	Yes, Sir. Development of local supply chain for critical Electric Vehicles (EVs) components and uptake of Electric Vehicles (EVs) are inter-linked to each other.(b) & (c):	No such proposal is under consideration in Ministry of Heavy Industries. -----------
