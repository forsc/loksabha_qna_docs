                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3688






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Legal Recognition to YouTube Channels




(Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of Electronics & Information Technology be pleased to state:-(a) 	whether the Government proposes to formulate a plan to give legal recognition to the YouTube channels being made by the people by giving license for the same and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether the Government has any report regarding the people, institutions and organisations running YouTube channels; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):  No, Sir. Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology has no such proposal.(b):  No, Sir.(c):  Does not arise.********
