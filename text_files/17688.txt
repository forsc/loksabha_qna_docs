                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1492






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Auction of Damaged Foodgrains




Sanjeev Kumar Singari














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government holds auction to dispose of the damaged rice and wheat as cattle feed; and (b) 	if so, the total quantity of damaged foodgrains auctioned and the revenue earned during the last two years?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a):	Yes Sir. Damaged/non-Issuable foodgrains are disposed off through tender/auction to the bonafide manufacturer/consumer of Feed.(b):	The quantity and value of damaged/non-issuable foodgrains tendered/auctioned by FCI during the last 2 years is as under: 	2018-19	2019-20*	Qty in MT	Money realised in Lakh Rs.	Qty in MT	Money realised in Lakh Rs.Wheat	1496	102	1078	100Rice	2363	149	2489	247 *Provisional/unaudited.****
