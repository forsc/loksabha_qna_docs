                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2289






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






High Quality Aptitude Test




Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar













                                                    Anurag Sharma
                                                








                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                








                                                    C.N. Annadurai
                                                








                                                    Gautham Sigamani Pon
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering high quality aptitude test for admission to undergraduate programmes of Central Universities from the 2021-22 academic session;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has consulted all the stake holders before arriving for conducting high quality aptitude test and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government has also set up a committee to recommend modalities for conducting the high quality aptitude test; and(e) if so, the details of the composition of the committee and the time by which the committee is likely to submit its report to the Government?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’)(a) to (e): New Education Policy, 2020 envisions for high quality common test for different subjects for universities through common entrance test for the admission to reduce burden on students, universities and colleges and entire education system.  Accordingly, this Ministry has requested University Grants Commission (UGC) to set up a Committee including Vice Chancellors of old and new Central Universities to go into the nitty-gritty of implementation of National Education Policy recommendations about common entrance test at Under-graduate level in Central Universities. The UGC has constituted a Committee which comprises Vice Chancellors of Central University of Punjab, Mizoram University, Central University of South Bihar, University of Delhi, BHU, Director General, National Testing Agency and Joint Secretary of UGC.*****
