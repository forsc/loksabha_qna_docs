                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

434






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






New Food Processing Centres




Agatha K. Sangma













                                                    Kirron  Kher
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of newly established domestic/indigenous food processing industries in the country;(b)	the total number of food processing industries in the country at present, State/UT-wise including Punjab and Chandigarh;(c)	whether the Government proposes to set up any new food processing units/centres in Punjab, Chandigarh and the North-Eastern States; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SMT. HARSIMRAT KAUR BADAL) (a) to (d):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.     STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *434 FOR ANSWERED ON 23RD JULY, 2019 REGARDING NEW FOOD PROCESSING CENTRES (a):	A total 2,651 number of additional registered food processing units/factories have been established during 2014-15 to 2016-17 over 2013-14 as per the latest information available under Annual Survey of Industries, 2016-17.  (b):      As per the latest Annual Survey of Industries, 2016-17 there are 39,748 registered food processing units/factories in the country. As per 73rd round of NSSO, 2015-16, there are 24,59,929 number of unincorporated enterprises in the unorganized segment of food processing sector in the country. The State / UT-wise number of such registered and un-incorporated food processing units/factories/enterprises is at Annexure. (c) & (d): The Ministry of Food Processing Industries does not set up any food processing industries/ units/projects on its own. Under various schemes of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) financial assistance is provided as capital subsidy in the form of grants-in-aid to the individuals, farmers, Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs), Entrepreneurs, Cooperatives, Societies, Self Help Groups (SHGs), Private Companies and Central/ State PSUs etc. for setting up of food processing industries/ units/ projects anywhere in the country including in Punjab, Chandigarh and North-eastern States. *****
