                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3792






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






PMGSY in Telangana




Arvind Dharmapuri














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilised under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) across the country during the last 5 years and the current year, State-wise, including the State of Telangana, district-wise;(b) 	the details of the rural road projects sanctioned in Telangana during the said period and the present status thereof;(c) 	whether the Government has given approval for implementation of phase-III of PMGSY in the country;(d) 	if so, the details thereof including the number of districts proposed to be included under the same along with the details of funds to be sanctioned in this regard in Telangana; and(e) 	the details of the targets fixed under the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)    The allocation/release of funds to the States for implementation of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) is done on the basis of the proposal received from the state and depends, inter-alia, on works in hand, execution capacity of the State and unspent funds available with the State. The funds for implementation of projects under PMGSY are released by the Ministry to the state as whole. Further release of funds to the programme implementation units (PIUs) at the district level is done by the respective state depending on the expenditure to be done.   The details of central share of programme funds released to the states and utilization thereof during the last five years and the current year, State-wise are given in Annexure-I.  A sum of Rs. 789.23 crore has been released to the State of Telangana during the last five years and current year as central share of programme funds for implementation of various interventions/verticals of PMGSY in the State.  As reported by the State Government, an expenditure of Rs. 1,505.44 crore, including state share has been incurred in the last five years and current year towards implementation of the PMGSY in the State. The details of expenditure incurred during the last five years and current year, district-wise, as reported by the State Government is given in Annexure-II. (b)   A total of 3,810 km road length has been sanctioned under various interventions/verticals of PMGSY to the State of Telangana during the last five years and current year, out of which 1,276 km road length have been completed and balance road length are under various stages of execution.   (c) to (e)      PMGSY-III was launched in the year 2019 for consolidation of 1,25,000 Km Through Routes and Major Rural Links connecting habitations, inter-alia, to Gramin Agricultural Markets (GrAMs), Higher Secondary Schools and Hospitals. All States and Union Territories are eligible to participate under PMGSY-III.  The State of Telangana has been allocated a target of 2,427.50 Km road length under PMGSY-III, against which 2395.85 Km road length 100 bridges has already been sanctioned at an estimated cost of Rs. 1,729.81 crore, including state share. As informed by the State Government, all rural districts of Telangana are covered under the PMGSY scheme.    *****
