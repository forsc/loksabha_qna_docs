                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2742






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Contribution of MSME in GDP, Employment and Exports




Pratima Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the contribution of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in the country‘s GDP, employment and value exports during each of the last three years and the current year;(b) 	whether exports of MSMEs have been affected due to Covid-19 pandemic;(c) 	if so, whether the Government has initiated a plan of action to extend requisite support to MSMEs to make their export more competitive and if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	the extent to which the Government has achieved success in this regard so far? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI BHANU PRATAP SINGH VERMA) (a):	As per the information received from Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation, the share of MSME Gross Value Added in All India GDP at current prices are as follows:<pre>Year	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21 Share of MSME in All India GDP (in %)	29.69%	30.50%	30.50%	26.83% </pre> Total number of persons employed, in MSMEs which are registered on Udyam Portal of the Ministry and incorporated during last 4 years are as follows:<pre>Year	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22 (up to 11.03.2022)All India	51,98,478	38,55,539	43,37,444	89,53,149	93,94,957</pre>As per information received from Directorate General of Commercial Intelligence & Statistics, the Share of Export of specified MSME related products in All India Exports are as follows:<pre>Description	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21 Share of Export of MSME related products in All India Export (in %)	48.56	48.10	49.77	49.35</pre>(b):	COVID-19 epidemic has temporarily affected various sectors including Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises in the country including their export. (c):	The Ministry of MSME is implementing the International Cooperation (IC) Scheme.  The scheme aims at capacity building of MSMEs for entering export market by facilitating their participation in international exhibitions/fairs/conferences/seminar/buyer-seller meets abroad as well as providing them with actionable market-intelligence and reimbursement of various costs involved in export of goods and services.  (d)	The impact of COVID 19 pandemic has temporarily affected the Indian export sector as a whole and also exports from MSME Sector. However, the share of export from MSME sector in total export remained almost same in 2020-21 (49.35%) as compared to 2019-20 (49.77%)
