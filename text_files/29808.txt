                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1464






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Fish Farmer Producer Organisations in Gujarat




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of the Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) in the State of Gujarat; (b) 	whether the Government is taking any steps to improve cold storage and export of fishery products especially in Jamnagar and DevbhumiDwarka districts of Gujarat; (c) 	if so, progress made in the last three years; and (d) 	whether the Government is planning to increase number of Fish Farmers Producer Organizations (FFPOs) in coastal area of Gujarat?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a)	to (d):The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a flagship scheme Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) with highest ever investment of Rs.20,050 crore  for a period of five years with effect from the financial year 2020-21 to 2024-25 in all States/Union Territories(UTs) including Gujarat.The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying during last financial year (2020-21) and current financial year (2021-22) has accorded approval to the fisheries development proposals of Government of Gujarat at a total cost of Rs.362.25 crore under the PMMSY for development of fisheries in the State. The approved activities include support for  a variety of activities like establishment of hatcheries,  rearing units, ornamental fish rearing units, cage culture in open sea & reservoirs, biofloc units &Re-circulatory Aquaculture Systems (RAS) , for establishment ice plants/cold storages, cold-chain transport infrastructure, feed mills and markets, procurement of vehicles for fish vending, for acquisition of deep sea fishing vessels &upgradation of existing fishingvessels and for referral labs and such other disease prevention and management systems. In addition, under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana(PMKSY)scheme of Ministry of Food Processing Industries(MoFPI), incentives are given to set up processing plants for exporting fish products. Government of Gujarat has informed that the projects sanctioned are at various stages of implementation.PMMSY inter-alia envisages setting up of Fish Farmers Producer Organizations/Companies (FFPOs/Cs) and 45 units of FFPOs are allocated for Gujarat. *****
