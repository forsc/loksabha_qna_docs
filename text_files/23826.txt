                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4858






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Collateral Free Loans to MSMEs




Sunil Kumar Singh













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    Virendra  Kumar
                                                








                                                    Bhagwanth Khuba
                                                








                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                








                                                    Pratima Bhoumik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is giving collateral free loan up-to a limit of Rs one crore for Individual MSME and if so, the details of disbursement made to various States including Jharkhand and Maharashtra during the last two year; and(b)	whether the Government has any data of funds spent so far under Interest Subsidy Eligibility Certificate Program and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	The government does not directly extend any loan to individual MSEs. However, it facilitates collateral free loans from Member Lending Institutions through the Credit Guarantee Scheme of Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME). The Scheme is implemented through Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE) which provides guarantee to Member Lending Institutions (MLIs) for credit facilities sanctioned by them to Micro and Small Entrepreneurs (MSEs) without collateral security and third party guarantee up to ?2 crore. The State wise details of guarantees covered under the scheme including Jharkhand and Maharashtra during Financial Years 2018-19 and 2019-20 are given in Annexure. (b):	Yes, Sir.
