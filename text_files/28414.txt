                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

89






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Sponsorship for Kendriya Vidyalayas




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received any proposal intimating the withdrawal of sponsorship of project sector Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) in the country as on date;(b) if so, the details of such KVs and the respective sponsoring institutions and the recurring expenditure borne by the institution for running the KVs during the FY 2020-21;(c) whether the Government proposes to consider converting such KVs to civil sector if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the reason therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        The MINISTER OF EDUCATION (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. Project Sectors Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) are opened on request of the project mainly to cater to the educational needs of the wards of the employees of project concerned. Presently, the sponsoring authorities of 14 project sector KVs have either requested to convert the KV concerned into civil sector or withdraw the financial support due to financial constrains or less number of wards of project employees. The details of 14 project sector KVs alongwith sponsoring institutions and the recurring expenditure borne by the institution for running the KVs during the financial year 2020-2021 are annexed.(c) & (d) As per the norms of Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan there is no provision for conversion of project sector KVs into civil sector.*****
