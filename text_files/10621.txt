                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4090






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Supply of Drinking Water through Taps




Queen Oja













                                                    Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has proposed to provide drinking water through taps to every household by 2022;(b) if so, the details of the current project in this regard; (c) whether the Government has conducted any survey to provide drinking water through taps to every household; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b) To enable every rural household in all States/ UTs to have Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC), Government of India has launched Jal Jeevan Mission, which aims at providing potable water to every rural household through Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) at the rate of 55 litre per capita per day (lpcd) by 2024, with an estimated outlay of ?3.60 lakh Crore. (c) & (d) Water supply being a State subject, States/ UTs provide information on Integrated Management Information System of this Department which inter alia includes details of number of household connections. The same is in public domain. As reported the information in respect ofnumber of household connections with piped water supply in all States/ UTs are available on the website: https://ejalshakti.gov.in?IMIS Reports? Nos of PWS House Connections. ***
