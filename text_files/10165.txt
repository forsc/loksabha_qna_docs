                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3651






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Swachh Bharat Mission




Nama Nageswara Rao














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there has been any progress in the Swachh Bharat Mission Flagship programme since its implementation in 2014, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(b)	the increase in coverage under the Mission as of now;(c)	the details regarding the success of Swachh Bharat Mission, State-wise; and(d)	the number of villages declared free of open defecation as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d):	As per information provided by Ministry of Jal Shakti, under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) [SBM(G)], more than 10.15 crore toilets have been constructed till 05.12.2019. As per the data reported by the States/UTs on the online Integrated Management Information System (IMIS) of SBM (G), sanitation coverage in the rural areas of the country was 38.7% as on 2.10.2014, which as of now has increased to 100%.  All the 5, 99,963 villages of the country have declared themselves Open Defecation Free (ODF).  States/UTs-wise number of individual households latrines (IHHLs) constructed and ODF declared villages are given at Annexure.
