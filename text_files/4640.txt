                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4310






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Concession in Delhi Metro




 Kunhalikutty P.K.













                                                    Kani K. Navas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government provides or proposes to provide any concession to students, handicapped persons and senior citizens etc. in Delhi Metro; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) 	the steps taken by the Government in the matter?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                        (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) to (d):	No, Sir.  As per Section 33 and 34 of the Metro Railway (Operation & Maintenance) Act, 2002, fare of Metro rail is fixed by Fare Fixation Committee (FFC) headed by either a sitting Judge or a retired Judge of a Hon’ble High Court. The fixation of fares is not done by the Government.  The fare of Delhi Metro has been revised on the basis of recommendations of fourth FFC.  The Committee has not recommended any concession to students, handicapped persons and senior citizens etc.  As per Section 37 of the Act, the recommendations of the FFC are binding                              *****
