                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3110






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Objective of Pradhan Mantri Crop Insurance Scheme




Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel













                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the main objective of Pradhan Mantri Crop Insurance Scheme; (b) whether this scheme is voluntary for farmers who receive loan or is it mandatory for them and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether this scheme covers the loss happened due to disasters as well; and (d) if so, the number of farmers of Gujarat, especially Amreli, Banaskantha who have been benefitted so far from this scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? 		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	to (d) : Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) aims at supporting sustainable income in crop production, especially in adverse climatic conditions by way of : •	Providing financial support to farmers suffering crop loss/damage arising out of unforeseen events. •	Stabilizing the income of farmers to ensure their continuance in farming. •	Encouraging farmers to adopt innovative and modern agricultural practices. •	Ensuring credit worthiness of the farmers, crop diversification and enhancing growth and competitiveness of agriculture sector besides protecting the farmers from production risks.Earlier the scheme was mandatory for farmers availing crop loans/Kisan Credit Card loans for the notified crops and notified areas and optional for others.  However, the Scheme has now been made optional for all farmers w.e.f. Kharif 2020 season. Details of number of farmer applications enrolled who got the benefit of premium subsidy under the scheme in Amreli and Banaskantha districts under PMFBY are as under :Year	No. of Farmer Applications Enrolled	Amreli District	Banaskantha District2016-17	2,40,234	1,06,1832017-18	1,57,882	1,57,9212018-19	24,260	50,5622019-20	21,605	52,553	Gujarat State Government is not implementing the scheme since Kharif 2020 season as the scheme is optional for the States/UTs.*****
