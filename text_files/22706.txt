                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3764






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Registered MSMEs




Kani K. Navas













                                                    Amar Singh
                                                








                                                    Mimi Chakraborty
                                                








                                                    Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy
                                                








                                                    Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar
                                                








                                                    Karti P. Chidambaram
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of total number of MSMEs registered in the country, State/UT-wise;(b)	the number of MSMEs registered in the country including in Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu during the financial years 2014-15 and 2019-20, district-wise;(c)	the number of MSMEs in the country including in Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu which have  been closed down during the financial years 2014-15 and 2019-20 and the reasons therefor;(d)	the number of MSMEs in the country including in Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu that have been shut down due to economic distress between March, 2020 and December, 2020, district-wise;(e)	whether there is consistently low registration of MSME projects in the country since 2018 and if so, the details thereof;(f)	whether the Government has made any  assessment of the impact of COVID-19 pandemic  on MSME sector so far and if so, the details thereof; and(g)	whether the Government has an assessment of the likely loss of jobs in MSME sector due to COVID-19 and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) & (b):	As per the provisions of the MSMED Act, 2006, the process of filing of Memorandum by Micro, Small and Medium enterprises (MSMEs) has been made online w.e.f. 18th September, 2015 through the Udyog Aadhaar Memorandum (UAM) Portal.  The number of MSMEs registered on UAM portal till 30.06.2020, is at annexure-I.  With the introduction of new classification of MSMEs w.e.f. 01.07.2020, based on composite criterion of investment and Turnover, a new system of online Udyam Registration has replaced the erstwhile UAM Registration. The number of MSMEs Registered under Udyam Registration w.e.f. 01.07.2020 is at annexure –II.  The details of UAM filed by MSMEs in Maharashtra is at annexure-III and in Tamil Nadu is at Annexure- IV.(c):	No such record is available.(d):	No such record is available.(e):	The MSME sector consists of private players and the investment in this sector is made by entrepreneurs. There is no data available regarding low registration of MSME projects in the country since 2018.(f) & (g):   No formal study has been conducted on the impact of COVID-19 on the MSME sector and on the total number of jobs lost in the MSME sector. However, the Ministry held a number of consultations with various MSMEs /Industry Associations of the country. During the deliberations, certain specific problems such as need for easy finance, more liquidity, moratorium on loans, etc. were highlighted by stakeholders.
