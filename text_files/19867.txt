                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1274






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Irrigation for Farmers




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of schemes being planned by the Government for providing irrigation water to farmers; (b)	the Central assistance provided to each State, especially Andhra Pradesh, under different irrigation schemes during the current year and the previous year; (c) 	the names of the irrigation projects of Andhra Pradesh pending and the time since when these are pending and reasons thereof; (d) 	the ratio of area under irrigation to the net sown area in the State of Andhra Pradesh; (e) 	the details of efforts being made to expand irrigated agricultural land and fund spent thereon for the said period, State-wise including Andhra Pradesh; (f) 	the total hectares of additional land brought under irrigation during the current year and the previous year State-wise including Andhra Pradesh; and (g) 	the efforts made by the Government to arrange irrigation facility through the rivers situated in remote and backward areas of Andhra Pradesh particularly Srikaulam district of Andhra Pradesh and the extent to which irrigation facilities have been provided in remote and tribal areas of this district through these efforts?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c): & (e) to (g):	The Government is implementing Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) from 2015-16  with the motto of ‘Har Khet Ko Paani’ for providing end-to-end solutions in irrigation supply chain, viz. water sources, distribution network and farm level applications in the country including Andhra Pradesh. Contd…..2/-- 2 -The components of PMKSY and the facilities/ activities being provided to States including Andhra Pradesh under each of the component is as under: Programme Components: ?	Accelerated Irrigation Benefit Programme (AIBP): This component focus on faster completion of ongoing Major and Medium Irrigation projects including National Projects.  This component is being implemented by Department of Water Resources, RD&GR From  2016-17 onwards, 99 prioritized Projects have been identified for expeditious completion adopting innovative funding through creation of ‘Long Term Irrigation Fund’ under NABARD for Mission Mode Completion of projects. Out of the 99 prioritized projects, eight projects are in State of Andhra Pradesh. The project wise details is at Annexure-I and the district-wise details is at Annexure-II. A medium irrigation project, namely, Thotapally Irrigation Project, is under implementation under PMKSY-AIBP and Command Area Development (CAD). The project, targeting ultimate irrigation potential of 48.56 thousand hectare, benefits Srikaulam and Vijayanagram Districts of Andhra Pradesh.Further, under National Projects scheme of DoWR, RD & GR, Min. of Jal Shakti, Polavaram Irrigation Project in Andhra Pradesh has targeted Irrigation Potential of 2.91 Lakh ha (7.2 lakh acre- 3.2 lakh acre in W Godavari district & 4.0 lakh acre in E Godavari & Vishakhapatnam Districts). Central Assistance released for Polavaram Project is Rs. 1850 crore in 2019-2020 and Rs. 2234.20 crore 2020-2021 (so far). Water being a State subject, the States plan and implement irrigation projects in the respective states as per their priorities.?	PMKSY -Har Khet Ko Pani: This component includes Source augmentation, distribution, ground water development, lift irrigation, diversion of water from water plenty to water scarce areas, repair, restoration, renovation of traditional water bodies. This component is being implemented by Department of Water Resources, RD&GR. Out of the 99 projects, in respect of 8 projects, the States have indicated that either the Command Area Development (CAD)  works are already completed or not envisaged under the present programme. Of the balance 91 projects, 88 projects have been included while for 3 projects DPRs are not submitted by States for inclusion. The targeted culturable command area (CCA) of included projects is 45.08 lakh hectares. The total estimated cost of works for these 88 projects is Rs.18799 crore and total estimated Central Assistance (CA) is Rs. 8271 crore. Out of 88 included projects, 7 projects are from the Andhra Pradesh at an estimated total cost of Rs. 970.98 Crore with targeted Central Assistance of Rs 349.37 Crore and culturable command Area of 1.78 lakh hectares. The details of funds provided for current and last year and physical achievements are at Annexure-III respectively.Contd…..3/-- 3 -Besides, under PMKSY-Har Khet Ko Pani, Renovation, Repair & Restoration (RRR) of Water Bodies scheme and Surface Minor Irrigation (SMI) Programme is also included. Under the Repair, Renovation and Restoration (RRR) of Water Bodies (WB) schemes, since 12th plan onwards,  total 100 schemes are ongoing with an estimated cost of Rs. 66.766 crore in the state of Andhra Pradesh which were included during 2018-19. Central Assistance (CA) of Rs. 2.70 crore has been released to state upto March, 2020. Further, 66 schemes have been reported to be completed upto March, 2020. Target irrigation potential restoration of these schemes is 5611 Ha. with districts benefitted by the scheme Prakasham. State has not submitted the CA release proposal after 2018-19 till date. No SMI scheme is ongoing in the State of Andhra Pradesh.Central Assistance (CA) provided to various States under SMI and RRR of WBs schemes and potential created/restored during previous and the current year is given as Annexure-IV.?	PMKSY- Watershed Development Component: Ridge area treatment, drainage line treatment, soil and moisture conservation, water harvesting structure, livelihood support activities and other watershed works. This component is being implemented by Department of Land Resources. Sanctioned watershed development projects in 28 States including Andhra Pradesh [now 27 States and UTs  of J & K and Ladakh], during the period 2009-10 to 2014-15 covering an area of about 39.07 million hectare under Integrated Watershed Management Programme (IWMP). IWMP was amalgamated as the Watershed Development component of the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (WDC-PMKSY) in 2015-16. Keeping focus on completion of the ongoing no new projects have been sanctioned w.e.f 2015-16 onwards. During 2019-20 and 2020-21 (as on 31.01.2021), Central assistance amounting to Rs.2243.174 crore has been released to the States, including Andhra Pradesh. The state wise (including Andhra Pradesh) details of Central assistance released are given in Annexure-V. Out of 6382 WDC-PMKSY projects being funded by DoLR, as on 31.01.2021, 4743 projects have been reported to be completed by the States, 409 projects are in consolidation phase and 1230 projects are in work phase of implementation. Under WDC-PMKSY, as per the information received from the States, additional area of 232473.48 ha have been brought under protective irrigation during 2019-20 and 2020-21 (upto 31.12.2020). The state wise (including Andhra Pradesh) details are given in Annexure-V.In respect of Andhra Pradesh including district of Srikaulam, out of 373 WDC-PMKSY projects being funded by DoLR, as on 31.01.2021, 260 projects have been reported to be completed, 36 projects are in consolidation phase and 77 projects are in work phase of implementation. Contd……4/-- 4 –?	PMKSY- Per Drop More Crop (PDMC): Micro-irrigation systems viz. Drip & Sprinkler irrigation systems, Micro level storage structures, secondary storage, efficient water conveyance, water lifting devices etc. This component is being implemented by Department of Agriculture, Cooperation and Farmers Welfare.  In Andhra Pradesh including district of Srikaulam, total 7.43 lakh ha have been covered under Micro-irrigation, under PMKSY-PDMC since 2015-16 to Feb, 2021. The area coverage and funds provided under programme for current and last year for all states including Andhra Pradesh is at Annexure-VI(A) and (B).(d):	As per the latest statistical information available, Net Area Sown and Net Irrigated Area for the state of Andhra Pradesh for year 2018-19 is 6077 thousand ha and 2719 thousand  ha respectively i.e. 44% is irrigated area out of the net sown area.
