                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

19






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Beautification/Modernisation of Railway Stations




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has sanctioned works related to the beautification and modernisation of various railway stations across the country; (b) if so, the list of stations selected from various States/UTs of the country including Rajasthan for this purpose; (c) the details of the current progress of the beautification and modernisation works at the said selected railway stations;(d) whether the said works have been stopped due to lack of funds; (e) if so, the details thereof;(f) whether the Government has received any complaints regarding the irregularities found in the said developmental works; and (g) if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a)	to (g):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.****** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (g) OF STARRED QUESTION NO.19 BY SHRI NIHAL CHAND TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 02.02.2022 REGARDING BEAUTIFICATION/MODERNISATION OF RAILWAY STATIONS(a) to (c) Upgradation/modernization/beautification of stations on Indian Railways is a continuous and on-going process. Presently, Railway stations are upgraded/modernized under Adarsh Station Scheme based on identified need of providing better enhanced passenger amenities at stations. Under Adarsh Station Scheme, 1253 railway stations over Indian Railways have been identified for development; out of which 1212 stations have been developed so far and the remaining 41 stations are planned for development in Financial Year 2022-23. The list of stations identified for modernization under Adarsh Station Scheme is appended.In all 40 Stations were identified for upgradation under Adarsh Station Scheme in the state of Rajasthan. Out of these, 39 stations have already been developed under the Scheme. The remaining 01 station is planned for development in Financial Year 2022-23.Various passenger amenities such as improvement to façade of station building, retiring room, waiting room (with bathing facilities), separate waiting room for ladies, computer based public address system, landscaping of circulating area, signages, Pay & Use toilets, water coolers, high level platforms, Foot Over Bridges, ramps at entry to station for Divyangjan and Senior Citizens etc. are provided at Railway stations identified for development under the Adarsh station scheme as per the respective category of the station.  (d) No, Sir. Beautification /Modernization of Railway Stations including amenities at stations, is an ongoing and continuous process and works in this regard are undertaken as per requirement, subject to inter-se priority and availability of funds. (e) Does not arise.(f) No, Sir. (g) Does not arise.*****
