                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1887






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






CSR Funds for Coal PSUs




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the major development works/activities undertaken from Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) funds by coal PSUs;(b)	the quantum of funds accrued and utilized by coal PSUs for the purpose during the last three years and the current year;(c)	the status of physical progress of such development works/activities; and(d)	whether any of the coal PSUs has defaulted in spending CSR funds and if so, the details thereof along with the corrective action taken by the Government thereon? 






ANSWER




MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a) to (c): Development work/activities are undertaken by Coal India Limited (CIL) and its subsidiaries and NLC India Limited (NLCIL) under Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) as per Scheduled VII of Companies Act 2013 for infrastructure development, skill development, social empowerment, water supply, health and sanitation, sports and culture and education facilities etc. In addition to above, Coal India Limited & its subsidiaries and NLCIL have undertaken construction of toilets in Government schools of various states under Swachh Bharat Programme. The details of funds allocated for CSR activities during last three years and current year along with progress of these activities are given below.(Rs. Crores)CIL and its SubsidiariesYear	Budget	Expenditure	Activities Undertaken	Activities completed	Activities Ongoing2016-17	442.75	489.67	1149	1149	02017-18	383.05	483.78	1162	1162	02018-19 (Provisional)	370.64	408.94	1210	780	4302019-20 (Upto May 2019)	403.41	12.22	692	65	627 NLC India Limited2016-17	43.93	37.91*	Completed2017-18	45.03^	45.03	Completed2018-19(Prov)	67.32	53.15	13 developmental works/activities are pending which will be carried out in 2019-20.2019-20	 The budget provision for CSR expenditure for 2019-20 in respect of NLCIL and its subsidiaries is yet to be finalised and approved by the Board. *Rs. 6.27 Cr has been carried forward to 2017-18.^ Rs.6.27 Cr (includes carried forward amount for 2016-17).  (d):   Coal India Limited and its subsidiaries collectively have achieved CSR fund utilization during last three years i.e.FY 16-17 to FY 18-19. NLCIL and its subsidiaries have spent its CSR funds every year. *****
