                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1344






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Emergency Response Centre




Darshana Vikram Jardosh














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the progress made so far in respect of setting up of Emergency Response Centre at Hazira in Gujarat;(b) the time by which it is likely to be completed;(c) whether any communication has been made/ proposed to be made with the Government of Gujarat State in this regard and if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROELUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) One of recommendations of the M.B.Lal Committee was to set up Emergency Response Centres (ERC) in the country. For setting up of pilot ERCs, five locations namely Jaipur, Vizag, Manmad, Hazira, Dibiyapur were identified. Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas/Industry decided Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited (BPCL) shall act as the coordinator. BPCL floated a Global Tender for appointment of Project Management Consultant (PMC). BPCL has further submitted a timeline of total 28 months for completion of the job including construction of ERC, testing, commissioning and training after finalization of the tender.(c) & (d) BPCL has informed that since PMC for Emergency Response Center is not finalized, no communication has been made with Government of Gujarat.
