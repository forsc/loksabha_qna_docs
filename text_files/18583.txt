                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

99






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Financial Assistance to Parents of PwDs




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of the plight of the parents of persons with mental disabilities, who are forced to stay home to look after their children and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government has taken any steps to provide monthly financial assistance to such parents, at least to partially compensate their income; if so, the details thereof;(c)	if so the details thereof?(d)	whether the Government proposes to increase the amount of monthly financial assistance given to such parents; and(e)	if not, whether the Government will consider the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) 	Yes, Sir.  The Government is aware of the plight of the parents of persons with mental disabilities who are forced to stay home to look after their children.  To address the issue, the Government has set up National Trust for the Welfare of Persons with Autism, Cerebral Palsy, Mental Retardation and Multiple Disabilities in 1999.   Under the National Trust Act, there is provision of appointment of Legal Guardians for persons with disabilities through Local Level Committee, constituted at district level under the Chairmanship of District Magistrate/District Collector.(b) 	No, Sir.(c), (d) and (e)  Does not arise, in view of (b) above.*****
