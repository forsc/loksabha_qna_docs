                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3055






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Grants to Andhra Pradesh under PMMVY




 Chandra Sekhar Bellana













                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the total grants sanctioned to Andhra Pradesh in the last two financial years under the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY);(b)	the total number of beneficiaries under the PMMVY in the State of Andhra Pradesh during the last two years; and(c)	whether any evaluation of the PMMVY has been undertaken in the last two years and if so, the details and the outcome of the evaluation study?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	The total grants-in-aid released to the Government of Andhra Pradesh under the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) during the years 2017-18 and 2018-19 are Rs.7022.36 lakhs and Rs.14101.75 lakhs respectively.(b)	The total number of beneficiaries covered under PMMVY in the State of Andhra Pradesh during the years 2017-18 and 2018-19 are 2,97,735 and 3,25,301 respectively.	(c)	PMMVY was approved on 17.05.2017. The scheme has not been evaluated in the last two years.******
