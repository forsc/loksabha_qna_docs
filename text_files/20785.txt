12.02.2021






Nutrition Schemes




Saikia Dilip













                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) 	the details of nutrition schemes being implemented by the Government in view of challenges prevailing in North-eastern States, Assam and Haryana, district-wise;(b) 	the amount allocated for these schemes during the last five years in these States; and(c) 	whether the amount allocated has been utilized within the specified period and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)  (a to c) Government implements Anganwadi Services, Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana, Scheme for Adolescent Girls and POSHAN Abhiyaan under the Umbrella Integrated Child Development Services Scheme (ICDS) as targeted interventions to address the problem of malnutrition in the country. All these schemes are universal in nature and implemented in all Sates/UTs.
