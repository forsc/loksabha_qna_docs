                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1261






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Nirbhaya Fund




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of Finance be pleased to state:(a)	the total amount of funds in Nirbhaya Fund and the percentage of amount utilized so far;(b)	the steps being taken by the Government to increase effective usage of Nirbhaya Fund;(c)	the details of proposals received from the State Governments/UTs under the said fund, State/UT-wise; and(d)	the number of proposals recommended under Nirbhaya Fund, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)	Since the inception of Nirbhaya Fund, an allocation Rs.6212.85 crore (including Rs. 500 crore allocated to Ministry of Women and Child Development in the Financial Year 2021-22), has been made under Nirbhaya Fund and Rs. 4138.51 crore has been disbursed/released by the concerned Ministries/ Departments implementing Schemes/projects under Nirbhaya Framework. Rs. 2921.85 crore has been reported as utilized so far.		An Empowered Committee (EC) of Officers appraises and recommends the proposals for funding under Nirbhaya Fund and also reviews the status of implementation from time to time in conjunction with the concerned Ministries/Departments/ Implementing Agencies (IAs). The Ministries/Departments/IAs also review and monitor the progress of their respective schemes/projects for effective implementation at their level. (c) and (d)	Since 2016-17 onwards, a total number of 89 proposals under Nirbhaya Fund were received from various States/UTs including Central Ministries/Departments. Out of these, 35 projects are presently favorably appraised by the EC.
