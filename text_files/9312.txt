                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2854






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Operation of Toll Plazas by Private Companies




Sandhya Ray














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the salient terms and conditions under which the concerned private company has been operating at Sainya, Muraina, Mehra toll plazas at National Highway No. 3 for 9 years; (b)	whether it has come to notice that these companies collect toll tax but do not comply with the rules while operating their services and if so, the reaction of the Government thereto; (c)	whether the officials of the National Highways Authority of India do not take proper and suitable action in this regard and if so, the reaction of the Government thereto; and (d)	the remedial measures being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	The toll plazas at Jajau (Sainya), Choudha (Morena) & Mehra are being operated by Concessionaire M/s Agra-Gwalior Pathways Pvt. Ltd. under Operate-Maintain and Transfer (OMT)  contract agreement dated 28.10.2014.(b) to (d) No Sir, the Concessionaire is collecting user fee as per the terms and conditions of the contract agreement. *****
