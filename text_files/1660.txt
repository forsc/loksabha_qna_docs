                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1361






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Funds to North Eastern States




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has allotted and released any funds out of the turn from the Ministry of Finance to the North Eastern States during the last five years;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of share of each State Government in North East India from the Centre and the details of calculation and formula using for allotment of funds to North Eastern States; and (d)	the details of amount allotted and released to North Eastern States for subsidies in industries for the last ten Years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF FINANCE (SMT. NIRMALA SITHARAMAN)(a & b) 	Year-wise details of funds released to North Eastern States under ‘Special Assistance’ during Financial Year 2015-16 to Financial Year 2018-19 are furnished below: -(` crore) <pre>State	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19Arunachal Pradesh 	19.56	-	33.99	309.73Assam	551.36	945.90	176.06	-Manipur	46.84	150.00	126.00	-Meghalaya	267.40	-	65.97	-Mizoram	20.83	-	-	-Nagaland	37.23	-	53.75	226.80Sikkim	187.00	500.00	36.00	-Tripura	296.15	150.00	300.00	1500.00</pre>(c )	The share, calculations and formula used for allocation of net tax proceeds from the Central Government to North-East States, are recommended by different Finance Commissions.  The details of the same and funds released to the North Eastern States from 2014-15 to 2018-19 by way of Finance Commission Devolution and Grants during the last five years are in Annexure-I.(d)	The details of amount released to North Eastern States for subsidies in industries under the Transport Subsidy Scheme (TSS), 1971/ Freight Subsidy Scheme (FSS), 2013 and North East Industrial and Investment Promotion Policy (NEIIPP), 2007 for the last ten years are at Annexure-II.******
