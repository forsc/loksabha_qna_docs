                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1020






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Budget for MDMS




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Mid Day Meal disbursed since 2018-19 State-wise, year-wise, urban/rural- wise ;(b) the total budget spent on Mid-Day Meal Scheme (MDMS) in each State for 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21;(c) the details of total (MDMS) budget, percentage of unutilized funds since 2018-19, State-wise, year-wise details, urban-rural wise;(d) the details of total funds spent and number of beneficiaries who received the amount out of Rs. 1200 crs allocated for DBT of Rs. 100 to 1.18 crore students under mid-day meal scheme, State- wise;(e) the details of the number of beneficiaries eligible, percentage of beneficiaries actually availing MDM scheme in Andhra Pradesh since 2018-19, district-wise, year-wise, urban and rural- wise?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a): Mid-Day Meal Scheme (MDMS), is an on-going Centrally-Sponsored Scheme which covers all school children studying in Classes I-VIII of Government, Government-Aided Schools, Special Training Centres including Madarsas and Maqtabs supported under Samagra Shiksha across the country covering all the urban and rural areas. The state-wise and year-wise number of children to whom mid day meal has been disbursed during the last three years (2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21) under the scheme are given at Annexure-I.(b) & (c): The details of state-wise funds released and percentage of unutilized funds during the last three years (2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21) under the scheme are given at Annexure-II.(d): The States and UTs have been advised to provide monetary assistance through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) of cooking cost component of Mid-Day Meal to all eligible 11.8 crore children as per MDM norms for summer vacation period of 2021 only as a one time special welfare measure. Provision has been made for an amount of Rs. 1200 crore from Central Government and minimum mandatory contribution of States and UTs amounting to Rs. 800 crore for this purpose.(e): The details of district-wise beneficiaries, percentage of beneficiaries actually availed mid day meal in Andhra Pradesh during the last three years (2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21) under the Scheme are given at Annexure-III.*****
