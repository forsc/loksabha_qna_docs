                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4176






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Introduction of RF Tag on Vehicles




Rebati Tripura













                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether  the  Government  has  any  proposal  to make installation of Radio Frequency (RF) tag on vehicles mandatory  in  near  future  and  if  so,  the  details  thereof; and(b) the details of implementation procedure and the time limit set  for the purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) &(b) As per the Gazette notification G.S.R. 1361(E) dated 02.11.2017, all new motor vehicles under category M & N, sold after 1st of December 2017 are required to be fitted with FASTag by the manufacturer of the vehicle or its authorized dealer. Further, as per notification G.S.R. 1081(E) dated 02.11.2018, Government has made it mandatory to affix FASTag for vehicles under National Permit (N/P) from 1st of October 2019. *****
