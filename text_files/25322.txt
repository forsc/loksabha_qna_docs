                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1160






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Functioning of Agricultural Universities




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the names of places where Centrally controlled/ administered Agricultural Universities/colleges are located in the country, State-wise;(b)	the details of various courses offered in those Universities/Colleges and the vacant faculty positions thereof;(c)	whether any steps are being taken by the Government to fill all the vacant faculty positions;(d)	if so, the details thereof and the time by which the vacant positions are likely to be filled up; and(e)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICUTLRURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ?? ????? ?????? ?????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	State-wise list of Centrally/controlled University/colleges is placed at Annexure-I.(b)	The detailed list of various courses offered in these University/Colleges and the vacant faculty positions is placed at Annexure-II.(c) to (e):	Vacancies in faculty positions arise from time to time due to creation of posts, retirement, resignation and deaths etc.  Filling up of vacant posts is a continuous/ongoing administrative process.  Department has put in place necessary mechanism to fill up the vacancies expeditiously. ***********
