                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1305






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






PM-KISAN Scheme in Jharkhand




Sanjay Seth














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) scheme within Jharkhand so far, district-wise; (b) 	the details of the number of beneficiaries covered under this scheme in Jharkhand so far; (c) 	whether the Government has transferred any amount into the farmers‘ accounts during the period of lockdown within Jharkhand; (d) 	if so, the details thereof, district-wise and if not, the reasons therefor;(e) 	whether the Government is planning to transfer some more installments in their accounts in advance due to COVID-19 crisis; and (f) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):   	There is no state wise allocation of funds under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) scheme. Funds are released based on the verified data of eligible beneficiaries received from the States. The district-wise data of amount paid to eligible beneficiaries in Jharkhand is placed at Annexure-I.(b):	A total of 25,69,646 beneficiaries have been covered under PM-KISAN Scheme in  Jharkhand as on 4.02.2021.(c):	Yes, Sir.(d):	 The district-wise data of amount transferred into eligible beneficiaries’ accounts during the period from 23rd March, 2020 to till now, in Jharkhand is placed at Annexure-II.(e):	 No, Sir.  (f):	Efforts have been made by the Government of India for timely disbursement of benefits of PM-KISAN, as per existing Scheme, to all the eligible beneficiaries whose verified data was received in the PM-KISAN portal from the respective States during the period of lockdown in view of the COVID-19 crisis.
