                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

37






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Deen Dayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme




Veena Devi














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has implemented Deen Dayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme (DDRS) in Bihar; (b) if so, the numbers of schools and vocational training centres provided with grants under this scheme during the last three years and the current year, year-wise; (c) the number of proposals sent by the State Government of Bihar for Deen Dayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme so far; (d) the details and the number of such proposals which got approval for administrative and financial aid, proposal-wise; and (e) whether some proposals have not been approved and if so, the reasons for non approval of the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR)(a) Yes, Sir. The Government is implementing Deendayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme (DDRS), under which financial assistance is provided to Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs) for promoting the welfare of Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) in the Country including the State of Bihar.(b) The details of number of schools and vocational training centres provided with grants under  Deendayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme (DDRS) during the last three years and the current year in the State of Bihar are as under:-Year	Schools Amount (Rs. Lakhs)	Vocational Training Centres	Number	Amount Released	Number	Amount Released2017-18	03	29.45	0	0.002018-19	01	23.67	0	0.002019-20	0	0.00	0	0.002020-21  (as on 25.01.2021)	0	0.00	0	0.00(c) The number of proposals sent by the State Govt. of  Bihar for Deendayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme (DDRS) during the last three years and the current year is as under:-Year	No. of Proposal Received2017-18	092018-19	022019-20	032020-21  (as on 25.01.2021)	04(d) & (e) A statement indicating proposals-wise position in the State of Bihar is at Annexure-I.******
