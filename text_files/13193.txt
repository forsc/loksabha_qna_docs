                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2067






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Cold Storage in Odisha




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is a proposal for establishing cold storage in the State of Odisha; (b) 	if so, whether this will be processed or not; and (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	 As reported by the State Government of Odisha, no proposal for establishment of cold storage in the State of Odisha is pending at the level of State Horticulture Mission for approval under Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH).(b) & (c):	Question does not arise. *******
