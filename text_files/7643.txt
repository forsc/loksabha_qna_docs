                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1335






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Sanskrit Mahavidyalayas




Kanakmal Katara













                                                    Ramcharan Bohra
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) The State-wise number of Sanskrit Mahavidyalayas in the country including Rajasthan;(b) The number of vacant posts of Sanskrit lecturers in the Sanskrit Mahavidyalayas affiliated withthe University Grants Commission (UGC);(c) The time by which these vacant posts are likely to be filled up;(d) The efforts being made by the Government to improve the quality of education in SanskritMahavidyalayas; and(e) The total amount paid under UGC grant by the Government to Rajasthan University, JaipurUniversity and various universities located in the State under this head from time to time?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT (SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL‘NISHANK’)(a) A state-wise list of Sanskrit Mahavidyalayas in the country including Rajasthan is placed atAnnexure-I.(b) to (e): A total number of 709 vacant posts of Sanskrit lecturers exist in the SanskritMahavidyalayas affiliated to Universities. The University Grants Commission (UGC) vide letterdated 4.6.2019 has asked all the Universities to fill up the vacant posts within a period of sixmonths. This was followed by reminder letters dated 31.7.2019, 7.8.2019, 5.9.2019 and22.10.2019.The Commission provides Development Assistance and Block Grants to the eligiblecolleges for infrastructure development and for improving the quality of education in Sanskrit.UGC has also instituted National Eligibility Test (NET) to ensure minimum standards forentrants in teaching and research. The detail of the grants paid to the Rajasthan University,Jaipur University and other Universities in Rajasthan is placed at Annexure-II.*****
