                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5032






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






FHTC




Rita Bahuguna Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has devised a holistic policy to provide Functional Household Tap Connections (FHTC) to all the rural households; (b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the intermediate milestones and timelines envisaged by the Government; and (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b) Government of India is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) in partnershipwith States to make provision of assured potable water supply in adequate quantity and of prescribed quality on regular and long-term basis through tap water connection to every rural household by 2024. While implementing the programme principle of ‘no one is left out’ is followed. At the time of announcement of Jal Jeevan Mission on 15th August, 2019, out of 18.93 Crore rural households, 3.23 Crore households (17%) were reported to have tap water supply. Since then, 3.92 Crore rural households have been provided tap water connections. Thus, as on 22.3.2021, out of 19.19 Crore rural households across the country, 7.16 Crore (37%) households have tap water supply.
