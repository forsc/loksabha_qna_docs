                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

476






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Umrah Visa




Asaduddin Owaisi













                                                     Imtiaz Jaleel Syed
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Umrah Visa have been resumed after ten months;(b)  if  so,  whether  there  are  no  regular  flights  to Saudi Arabia for these pilgrims;(c) if so, whether there is an urgent need to start direct flights to Saudi Arabia under Air Bubble till the regular operation of flights starts; and(d)  if  so,  the  steps  taken  or  being  taken  by  the Government on the demand of pilgrims in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a):  Saudi Arabia has suspended the entry of passengers directly from India since September 22, 2020 due to the covid-19 pandemic.  The Consulate General of India, Jeddah has informed that Saudi Ministry of Hajj and Umrah have given licenses to Indian Umrah tour operators for bringing Umrah pilgrims, however, Umrah visa is tied to various logistic arrangements including transportation, and as such, Umrah visa has not been issued to Indian Umrah tour operators.(b) to (d):   Presently, there are no scheduled international commercial passenger services between India and Saudi Arabia. An Air Bubble agreement proposal has already been sent by India to Saudi Arabia.;
