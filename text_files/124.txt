                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

173






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Declining Adoption Rate




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken note of the declining adoption rates in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof and reasons therefor; and(c) the steps being taken to make the adoption procedure more transparent and accessible?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b) :	As per the Child Adoption Resource Information and Guideline System (CARINGS), the adoptions of children under the Juvenile Justice (Care and Protection of Children) Act, 2015 shows an increasing trend. Details are as under: Year	No. of adoption 2016-2017	37882017-2018	39272018-2019	4027(c)  Adoption Regulations, 2017 have been notified by Central Adoption Resource Authority under the provision of the Juvenile Justice Care & Protection Act, 2015. The entire adoption procedure has been made on-line through the Child Adoption Resource Information and Guideline System (CARINGS) which has made the system fully transparent and accessible. Further, the Government also undertakes awareness initiatives for this purpose. *****
