                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4253






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Revenue Earning by Toll Plazas




Prabhubhai Nagarbhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the number  of  toll  plazas functioning in the country, State/UT-wise including Gujarat;(b) the details of the revenue earned by these toll plazas during the last three years and the current-       year, State/UT-wise including Gujarat;(c)  whether  the  Union  Government has  received  a  proposal  from  the Government of Gujarat for shifting the toll plaza located at Mandal under district Tapi to  25-30  km  away  from  its  present location;(d)  if so, the details and the present status thereof; and(e)  the time by which it is likely to be shifted  along with  the steps  taken/being taken  by  the  Union     Government  in  this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) As on 30.06.2019, the State/UT-wise details of the number of fee plazas operational on National Highways in the country are at Annexure-A. (b) The State-wise details of the revenue earned by Government from fee plazas during the last three years and the current year are at Annexure-B.(c) to (e) Reference has been received in National Highways Authority of India from the Hon’ble MP, Shri Parbhubhai Vasava as also the Hon’ble Minister, Tribal Development, Forest, Women and Child Welfare, Government of Gujarat, Shri Ganpatbhai Vasava to shift the toll plaza which is under examination with National Highways Authority of India.
