                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

888






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Silent Engine Aircraft




Kirron  Kher














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is contemplating to promote silent engine aircraft to reduce the noise level; and(b) if so, the details thereof including the action taken thereon so far?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) No such proposal is under consideration in this Ministry.;(b) Does not arise.
