288






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Tourism in Coal Mining Areas




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas













                                                    Chandra Prakash Joshi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plan to promote  tourism in coal mining areas;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c)  the  other  steps  taken  by  the  Government  to develop tourism in the coal mining area






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF COAL, MINES AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a) Yes Sir. (b) & (c) : For promoting  mine tourism, Coal and lignite companies under Ministry of Coal have  explored and conceptualized plans for beautification of existing parks in their command area and creation of new eco-parks and mine tourism sites in reclaimed mining areas, which include water bodies, artificial underground tunnels, avenues for  recreation, adventure, water sports,  bird watching etc.  In order to promote tourism in mining areas, coal and lignite companies are also roping in  Tourism Development Corporations of respective States to integrate the developed eco-parks/mine sites with the local tourism circuit.
