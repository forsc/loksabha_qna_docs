                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5539






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Ayushman Bharat Beneficiaries




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE  be pleased to state: (a) the total number of Ayushman Bharat beneficiaries in various districts of Bihar; (b) the list of empanelled hospitals in Bihar where Ayushman Bharat benefits can be availed, district-wise; and (c) the list of diagnosis and treatment facilities which are included in the Ayushman Bharat Yojana including Oral healthcare?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):     Facilities under Ayushman Bharat – Health and Wellness Centres are free and universalto all.        The total number of Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) beneficiary families in various districts of Bihar is 108.95 lakhs(b):    District-wise total number of hospitals empanelled under AB-PMJAY in Bihar are at Annexure-I. The list of empanelled hospitals is available at the link https://hospitals.pmjay.gov.in/Search/empnlWorkFlow.htm?actionFlag=ViewRegisteredHosptlsNew(c):     The AB-HWCs are envisioned to provide an expanded range of services including care for non-communicable diseases as well as prevention, and Health promotion and wellness activities like Yoga apart from services already being provided for Maternal and Child Health including immunization and communicable diseases. To provide comprehensive health coverage to the beneficiaries, free essential drugs and diagnostic services are also being provided through these AB-HWCs.           Under AB-PMJAY, 1393 benefit packages along with rates have been put in place for usage of hospitals empanelled under the scheme for providing the treatment to the beneficiaries. The Specialty wise detail of benefit packages are at Annexure-II.            The details of benefit packages under AB-PMJAY are available at  https://pmjay.gov.in/sites/default/files/2018-07/HBP.pdf
