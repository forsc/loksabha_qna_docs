                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2275






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Madarsa Teachers




S.T. Hasan













                                                    Mohammad Jawed
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is true that the Madarsa teachers under Centrally Sponsored Scheme for Providing Quality Education in Madarsas (SPQEM) Yojna have not received salaries for few years particularly in Uttarakhand;(b)	if so, the reasons therefor; and(c)	the time by which the Government proposes to pay the salary to the madarsa teachers?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION (SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c):	 The Ministry of Education has released a total of Rs. 325.35 crore to various State Governments including Uttarakhand during 2017-18, 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21 (till date) under Scheme for Providing Quality Education in Madrassa (SPQEM). Out of this amount, Rs.6.89 crore has been released to Uttarakhand. The State Government releases the funds to the concerned Madrassas which includes honorarium for teachers.*****
