                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1685






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Green Highways Policy-2015




Kirron  Kher














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    whether the Green Highways Policy-2015 is being implemented by the Government;(b)   if so, the details thereof including the plantation data of the last three years and the current year;(c)   whether the Government proposes to create green highways/expressways in the country; and(d)    if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (b) Yes, Sir. The details of the Green Highways Policy-2015 can be seen at website of Ministry of Road Transport & Highways (morth.nic.in/green-highways). The summary of plantation under Green Highways Policy-2015 is as under:	Cumulative Plantation of last 3 Years (2016, 2017 & 2018)(No of Plants)	Plantation in 2019-20 upto August 2019(No of Plants)	Total Plantation Progress (from 2016 upto August 2019)(No of Plants)		Total		1	2	3 (1+2)	107,52,441	36,20,057	143,72,498 (c) to (d)  In Bharatmala Pariyojana Phase –I, development of 800 Km Expressway has been envisaged.*****
