                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

168






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.09.2020






Per Capita Income




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the details of measures being taken to increase per capita income in the States particularly those of Jhansi-Lalitpur parliamentary constituency in the State of Uttar Pradesh; and(b)the ratio of income generated from the districts of Bundelkhand in comparison to that earned from remaining districts of Uttar Pradesh under various heads?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) & (b) The per capita Net State Domestic Product (NSDP) in Uttar Pradesh at current prices has increased from Rs. 66,512/- in FY 2018-19 to Rs. 70,419/- in FY 2019-20 as per data available from Ministry of Statistics and Programme implementation (MOSPI) . Several steps have been taken by the central government and state governments to increase the per capita income levels in the country. The government is implementing several developmental schemes like Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS),  Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM), Deen Dayal Upadhyay – Gramin Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY), Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY), Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), Shyama Prasad Mukherjee RuRBAN Mission, National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP) and Sansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY) to increase the per capita income level.  District wise NSDP data is not published by the MOSPI.*****
