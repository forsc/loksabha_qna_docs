                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.2350 TO BE ANSWERED ON 03.08.2021FUNDS FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE 2350. SHRI ANNASAHEB SHANKAR JOLLE:  DR. UMESH G. JADHAV:  SHRI B.Y. RAGHAVENDRA: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the total funds allocated by the Government specifically for improving the dispensation of social justice in the country during the last three years;(b) the amount of funds utilized in this regard during the said period; (c) whether any review has been undertaken during the last two years by the Government to know the utility of its schemes, plans and programmes; and (d) if so, the details and outcome thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI A. NARAYANASWAMY)(a) & (b):  The details of fund allocated and utilised during last three years for progress of the SC, OBCs, Senior citizens and other marginalised sections is given in the Annexure. (c) & (d): A detailed evaluation of the schemes was undertaken through third parties in 2020-21.
