                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1994






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Environment Protection Measures




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Industrial units like Central Coalfield Limited (CCL), Damodar Valley Corporation (DVC), Oil and Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC) and steel manufacturing companies in Giridih, Jharkhand have reviewed the compliance of the safety and environment protection measures being undertaken in their plants/units in the last three years;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether any cases of irregularities in the compliance of environment protection measures by these companies with the nexus of officials have come to light; and (d)	if so, the details thereof along with the action taken by the Government thereon? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	& (b) As per the available information, five Environmental Clearances have been granted to three steel manufacturing companies in Giridih District, Jharkhand. The details of these ECs are at Annexure.During the period of last three years, half yearly compliance report is available for following companies:i.	Integrated Steel Plant of M/s Shivam Iron & Steel Company Ltd- for the period of December 2017.ii.	Integrated steel Plant of M/s Atibir Industries Company Ltd - for the period of March 2016, March 2017, and December 2018.(c)& (d) The industries have been also monitored by the Ministry’s Regional Office (RO), Ranchi and Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) to review the status of environmental compliance. Non-compliances have been observed during these inspections. Accordingly, the details of action taken are as given below:i.	M/s Shivam Iron & Steel Company Ltd.-On the basis of RO report, the proposal has been considered under the Violation Category and State Pollution Control Board has been directed to initiate the credible action under Environment (Protection) Act, 1986. ii.	M/s Atibir Industries Company Ltd.- On the basis of RO report, the corrective action plan on non-compliances has been initiated.iii.	M/s Saluja Steel and Power Pvt. Ltd.- CPCB had issued the Closure Direction on 07.05.2018, which was revoked on 15.01.2019 after report of compliance.***
