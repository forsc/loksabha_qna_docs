                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

160






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Fraud by LPG Distribution Centres




Uday Pratap Singh














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the cases of fake advertisement and fraud in the name of Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas through the LPG distribution centres have come to the notice of the Government and if so, the details thereof;(b) the details of LPG distribution during the last three years including current year, State/UT-wise;(c) whether the Government has achieved the fixed targets, if not, the details of the steps proposed to be taken in future by the Government; and(d) whether the Government has made a partnership with the private companies for LPG distribution and if so, the details of the companies selected in Madhya Pradesh, if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) Some unscrupulous persons/agencies have fraudulently created fake websites under different domain names for allotment of LPG distributorships of the Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) by using the emblem of Government of India, monogram of Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB) logo and stamps of Petroleum & Explosive Safety Organization (PESO) and Oil Marketing Companies.(b) & (c) Appointment of LPG distributorships is a continuous process and locations for setting up of LPG distributorships are identified based on sale potential that makes them commercially viable. As on 01.01.2020, there are 24,382 LPG distributorship across the country. The State/UT-wise details of LPG distributorships commissioned during the last three years and the current year(upto 31.12.2019) are at Annexure.(d) To meet the growing demands of LPG, OMCs have initiated steps for establishment of Private Bottling Plants in various areas and have floated tenders for 60 locations including in the State of Madhya Pradesh.
