                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3463






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Vacant Posts in NE Region Universities




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of posts lying vacant in the Universities of North East, University-wise;(b) the time by which these vacant posts will be filled; and(c) whether many teachers/ employees are working on temporary basis in these Universities andif so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): The University Grants Commission (UGC) has informed that as per All India Survey ofHigher Education, the details of sanctioned strength, in position and vacant positions of teachersin Higher Educational Institutions of North East region are as under:<pre>Sanctioned Strength (SS) In position (IP) Vacant Position (SS-IP)8184 6479 1705</pre>The details of posts lying vacant in the Higher Educational Institutions of North East is availableat https://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/5082511_LSPQ-No-3463-Annexure-I.pdfUGC vide its D.O. letter no. F.1-14/2019 (CPP-II) dated 4th June, 2019, has prepared theGuidelines for Recruitment of Faculty in Universities, Colleges and Institutions Deemed to beUniversities outlining the selection procedure and the time frame for recruitment. Theseguidelines have been circulated to all Universities to adhere to them. The Universities have alsobeen requested to take steps to ensure that the vacancies in the University as well as in theColleges affiliated to University are filled up at the earliest. A copy of the Guidelines circulated toall Universities is available at https://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/9291222_LSPQ-No-3463-Annexure-II-Eng--Guidelines-for-recruitment-of-faculty-04-06-2019.pdf.This Ministry has also requested all Higher Educational Institutions (HEIs) to fill up faculty relateddata on National Higher Education Resource Centre (NHERC) portal at https://nherc.in in orderto fill up all vacancies in various higher educational institutions on an urgent basis.(c): UGC has informed that at present there are 191 teachers working on temporary basis inHigher Educational Institutions of North East region. The university-wise number of teachersworking on temporary basis in North East Universities is available athttps://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/1424363_LSPQ-No-3463-Annexure-III.pdf*****
