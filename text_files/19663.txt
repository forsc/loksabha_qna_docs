                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

951






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Seva Bhoj Yojana




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva













                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Sanjeev Kumar Singari
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                








                                                    T.R.V.S. Ramesh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the main objective of the Seva Bhoj Yojana;(b)	whether the Government provides any grant to the institutes under this scheme; (c)	if so, the funds utilized/spent/re-imbursed under the Seva Bhoj Yojana since the scheme was implemented; and(d)	the list of religious institutions empanelled and are being run under the said scheme, State-wise including Gujarat?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)     Under the Scheme of ‘Seva Bhoj Yojna’, Central Goods and Services Tax (CGST) and Central Government’s share of Integrated Goods and Services Tax (IGST) paid on purchase of specific raw food items by Charitable/Religious Institutions for distributing free food to public shall be reimbursed as Financial Assistance by the Government of India.(b) & (c)  Yes Sir, under the scheme of Seva Bhoj Yojana, grant is provided to Charitable/Religious Institutions. An amount of Rs. 4.82 crores (approx..) has been sanctioned under the scheme of Seva Bhoj Yojana since its implementation w.e.f 01.08.2018.(d)     The State-wise list of Charitable/Religious Institutions which have been provided grant under Seva Bhoj Yojana till date is Annexed.AnnexureState-wise list of Charitable/Religious Institutions1.  Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams, Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh2.  Sri Venkateswara Annaprasadam Trust, Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh3.  Sikh Gurudwara Parbandhak Committee (SGPC), Punjab4.  Dreams & Beauty Charitable Trust, Ludhiana, Punjab5.  Durgiana Temple, Amritsar, Punjab
