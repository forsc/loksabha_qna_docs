                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1622






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






PMAY-U Targets




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the target set for building the houses under Pradhan Mantri 	Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) by the year 2022; (b) 	the details of such targets achieved and yet to be achieved, 	State-wise; (c) 	the quantum of funds allocated for PMAY-U; and (d) 	whether the Government proposes to increase the allocation 	of funds under PMAY-U and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                           (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI) (a) & (b) Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban [PMAY-U], States/UTs have undertaken a demand survey and reported validated demand of around 112.2 lakh houses in urban areas. So far, 109,88,765 houses have been sanctioned, State/UT wise details of which are at Annexure. (c) & (d) Sanctioned houses under PMAY – U involves Central Assistance of Rs. 1,77,409.3 crore of which Rs. 84,606.7 crore have already been released. Government is committed to provide approved Central Assistance to fund the sanctioned houses under PMAY-U.                                                       *****
