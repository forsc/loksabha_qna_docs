                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5461






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Funds for Urban basic Amenities in Maharashtra




Bharti Pravin Pawar














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has allocated any funds to State of Maharashtra 	for implementing the schemes for providing urban basic amenities in the State;(b)	if so, the details thereof indicating the funds allocated/released and utilized for the 	said purpose during each of the last three years and the current year; and(c)	the details of works done from the said funds, in the concerned areas?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) to (c): Yes, Sir. The Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) facilitates and assists States/Union Territories (UTs) including Maharashtra, through its Missions/Schemes in development of cities and towns covered under these Missions/Schemes. These Schemes support providing urban basic amenities like – water supply, sewerage, parks, community toilets, etc. Funds released to Maharashtra and utilized by the State for these urban development Schemes during the last three years and current year and work done are in Annex.                      *****
