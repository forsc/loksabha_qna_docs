                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1025






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Unified Farmer Service Interface




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details regarding the pilot project of the Unified Farmer Service Interface and Agristack, in furtherance of which the Department of Agriculture, Cooperation and Farmers Welfare has entered into four MoUs;(b)	the details of the benefits of the Unified Farmers Service Interface and Agristack; and(c)	the details of the villages selected in the State of Rajasthan under the said pilot project and the progress of the pilot project in these villages?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????        (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)  to (c) : The Department has commenced the work for creating Agristack in the country. This will serve as a foundation to build innovative agri-focused solutions leveraging technologies to contribute effectively towards increasing the income of farmers and improve efficiency of the Agriculture sector in the country.Leading Technology/Agri-tech/StartUp companies were identified and invited to collaborate with the Government of India and develop Proof of Concepts (PoC) based on data of select Districts / Villages in the federated Farmers’ database. A public call through the Department’s website was issued inviting proposals for MoU. Companies were invited to sign MoU on purely pro bono basis for a period of one year and develop the PoCs. These PoCs will help in understanding the uses of Agristack and service & solutions that can be built using available data and some of them, if found beneficial to the farmers will be scaled up at National level. In this regard, the Government during the last year have signed Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with leading Technology/Agri-tech Players & Start-ups for working on Proof of Concepts (PoCs) in the select Districts/Villages. The details of the MoUs alongwith the selected states/District/Villages including the state of Rajasthan, under the said PoCs project are given at Annexure I & II.
