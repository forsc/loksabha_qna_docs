                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1328






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Grant of Special Assistance to Odisha




Ramesh Chandra Majhi














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to grant financial assistance to Odisha under the Special Assistance Scheme during the current financial year;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the funds allocated for the same;(c)	the time by which the said allocation is likely to be released to the State Government; and(d)	the details of Infrastructure projects identified for development by the State Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE (SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY) (a)	to (b): Yes Sir. The Union Government has approved special assistance (loan) amounting to Rs. 374.00 crore for the capital projects and released an amount of Rs. 143.12 crore as the 1st installment  to the State Government of Odisha under the scheme for Special Assistance to States for Capital Expenditure for the year 2021-22.(c): As per Para (12) of the guidelines on the scheme for Special Assistance to States for Capital Expenditure for the year 2021-22 issued on 29th April, 2021 the amount allocated to the State shall be spent on eligible expenditure before 31.03.2022.(d): The details of capital projects posed by the State Government and approved by the State under the scheme for Special Assistance to States for Capital Expenditure for the year 2021-22 are provided in Annexure-I.******** ANNEXURE-I REFERRED IN PART (D) OF THE ANSWER TO THE LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 1328 FOR 06.12.2021.ANNEXURE – I (Rs. in crore)Sl.
