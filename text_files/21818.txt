                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2971






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Poorna Shakti Kendra Project




Sukhbir Singh Jaunapuria














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to implement Poorna Shakti Kendra project in the country for empowering women;(b)	if so, the details and the salient features of the project;(c)	the number of Poorna Shakti Kendra presently functional in the country, State/UT-wise;(d)	whether the Government is contemplating to set up more such centres in coordination with Panchyats across the country, if so, the details thereof;(e)	the number of the female members deployed in these centres along with the number of the women who were given employment under this Project; and(f)	the average salary being paid to the women workers?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (f)       Poorna Shakti Kendras were approved in pilot mode in selected districts under erstwhile National Mission for Empowerment of Women (NMEW) scheme. A new scheme under the name Mahila Shakti Kendra (MSK), was launched in November, 2017, under which the earlier components of NMEW, namely Poorna Shakti Kendra (which was operational in 19 districts) and Village Convergence and Facilitation Service (which was operational is 53 districts) were replaced by District level Centres for Women (DLCWs) under the MSK Scheme.*********
