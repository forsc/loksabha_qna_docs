                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

916






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Four-Laning of Highways




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has taken any step towards four-laning of Kanpur-Bilhaur-Kannauj –Etah-Bewar-Aligarh-Bulandshahr-Ghaziabad –Delhi G.T. road;(b)	if so, the details and the present status thereof; and(c)	the funds spent on the maintenance and repair of the said stretch during each of the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) Yes Sir. The details of the project along with their present status are as below:Sr.No.	Project Name	Length	Progress (in %)	Remarks1	4/6 laning of Aligarh-Bhadwas Section of NH-91 (Pkg.-I)	45.83 Km	3.99	Under implementation2	4/6 laning of Aligarh-Bhadwas Section of NH-91 (Pkg.-II)	45.164 Km	23.5	-do-3	4/6 laning of Aligarh-Bhadwas Section of NH-91 (Pkg.-III)	61.211 Km	17.3	-do-4	4/6 laning of Naviganj – Mitrasen section of NH-91(Pkg.-IV)	67.00  Km	NA	At Tender Stage5	4/6 laning of Mitrasen – Kanpur section of NH-91 (Pkg.-V)	58.00 Km	NA	At Tender Stage6	4laning of Ghaziabad-Aligarh Section of NH-91	126.30 Km	99.00	Substantially completed,(c) 	Ghaziabad –Aligarh section of National Highway (NH) has been developed on Design, Build, Finance, Operate and Transfer (DBFOT) mode and maintenance of the stretch is the responsibility of Concessionaire at his own cost. However, funds spent on maintenance and repair of Aligarh –Kanpur Section of NH  during 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 is Rs. 1.73 crore, Rs. 8.28 crore and Rs. Nil respectively. *****
