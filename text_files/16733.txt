                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

526






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Startup India Seed Fund




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of Startup India Seed Fund proposed by the Ministry;(b) 	whether the Scheme has been taken before the Cabinet for its approval and, if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether Startup India Scheme has been given anything under Atmanirbhar Bharat Package;(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):   	An EFC note on Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS), with a total proposed corpus of Rs. 945 Crore, has been formulated by the Department. The EFC note was circulated for Inter Ministerial consultations to the concerned Departments/Ministries. The comments/inputs received from the concerned Departments/Ministries are incorporated in the EFC Note on Startup India Seed Fund Scheme and EFC Note has been finalized.  Further, the grant of exception for the proposal of Startup India Seed Fund Scheme under clause 2(v) of DoE OM No. 42(02)PFC-I/2014 dated 04.06.2020 and the budgetary support for FY 2021-22 onwards has been solicited from Department of Expenditure. (b): 	No, sir.  The proposed SISFS, with the original Cost estimates (OCE) of Rs.945 crore, shall be appraised by EFC chaired by expenditure Secretary and approved by the Commerce & Industry Minister and Finance Minister. The Schemes with OCE above Rs.1000 crore are only placed before the Cabinet/Committee of the Cabinet concerned with the Subject.(c) & (d): 	No, sir, Startups are not given any benefits separately under Atmanirbhar Bharat package.******
