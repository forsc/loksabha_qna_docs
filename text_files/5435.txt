                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4841






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Dairy Milk Cooperatives




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of milk dairy cooperatives running at present in Bihar along with the details of the production of milk during each of the last three years, district-wise; (b) whether any special efforts have been made by the Government to increase milk production per cattle in Bihar; (c) whether the Government has any plan to establish/open new Dairy Science/ Dairy Technical Colleges in the country particularly in Bihar; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (DR.SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a) Government of Bihar has informed that 16683 number of dairy cooperatives are functional in the network of Bihar State Milk Cooperative Federation Ltd. (COMFED). District wise annual milk production in Bihar during 2015-16, 2016-17 & 2017-18 is annexed.(b) In order to complement and supplement the efforts made by the State to increase milk production per cattle Government is implementing following schemes in the country including Bihar:(i)	Rashtriya Gokul Mission (RGM)(ii)	Breed Improvement Institutes: Seven Central cattle Breeding Farms (CCBF)(iii)	National Dairy Plan- Phase I(c)	As per the information furnished by ICAR, Government has no plan to establish/open new Dairy Science/ Dairy Technical Colleges in the country. (d)	Does not arise.****
