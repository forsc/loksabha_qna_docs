                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3242






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Agreement with Foreign Petroleum Companies




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether talks were held with several officials of the foreign petroleum companies during the visit of the Prime Minister to the USA in September 2019;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the terms of reference/ agreements signed during the talks/ meeting; and(d) the benefits likely to accrue to the petroleum products sector?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) and (b): Hon’ble Prime Minister during his visit to U.S. participated in an Energy Roundtable with CEOs of global oil and gas majors on 22nd September 2019 in Houston.(c) and (d): Petronet LNG Limited (PLL) of India and Tellurian of U.S. signed a non-binding MOU, wherein PLL and its affiliates intend to negotiate the purchase of up to 5 MMTPA of LNG from the Driftwood LNG project.*****
