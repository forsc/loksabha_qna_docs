                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1438






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Frequency of Rajdhani Trains




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Rajdhani Express or Shatabdi Express trains runs daily for all the State capitals of the country from New Delhi railway station;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the number of those State capitals for which there is no arrangement of such trains and the reasons therefor;(d) whether the Government proposes to run 12441/12442 Bilaspur Rajdhani Express only two days of the week now; and(e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c) No, Sir. At present, the Capital cities namely Chandigarh, Dehradun, Jaipur, Lucknow, Patna, Kolkata, Guwahati (serving Dispur), Bhubaneswar, Mumbai, Ahmedabad (serving Gandhinagar), Bengaluru, Secunderabad, Bhopal and Kal ka (serving Shimla) have been provided connectivity, to Delhi area, by daily Rajdhani/Shatabdi Express services. Based upon operational feasibility and traffic justification, the  Capital cities of Chennai, Thiruvananthapuram, Madgaon (serving Panji), Amravati (proposed capital), Bilaspur, Ranchi and Agartala are presently connected, to Delhi area, by non-daily Rajdhani Express services.(d) to (e) Owing to operational and resource constraints, at present, there is no proposal to increase frequency of the bi-weekly service of 12441/12442 Bilaspur - New Delhi Rajdhani Express.*****
