                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3705






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Widening of Rural Roads in A.P.




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is true that as a part of100 days plan the Ministry proposes to rollout Phase-III of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) for strengthening and widening rural roads of 1.25 lakh kms length;(b)	if so, the details and length of kms of rural roads in Andhra Pradesh to be taken up under Phase-III; and(c)	the details of the expected expenditure involved and the blueprint that the Ministry has prepared for this purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	The Government has approved phase-III of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) for consolidation of Through Routes and Major Rural Links connecting habitations, inter-alia, to Gramin Agricultural Markets (GrAMs), Higher Secondary Schools and Hospitals at  an estimated cost of Rs. 80,250 crore. The Government is taking steps for implementation of phase-III of PMGSY. *****
