                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2840






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Concessions for Medical and Health Workers




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any scheme for providing concessions for medical and health workers in Railways; (b)	if so, the details thereof;  (c)	whether only Allopathic doctors are considered for concessions in Railways;(d)	if so, the reasons therefor;(e)	whether the  Government proposes to  include  the doctors  from  AYUSH sector also; and(f)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                           MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY                               (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (d): Prior to 20.03.2020, Railways used to extend concession in passenger fare to the following categories of medical professionals:-(i) Allopathic Doctors with minimum Bachelor of Medicines and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) qualification.(ii) Trained Nurses/ Midwives working in any recognized hospital or nursing institution, trained nurses /midwives in private practice, student nurses/student midwives undergoing training in hospitals etc. Concession to Allopathic Doctor was granted to render medical assistance to the needy passenger during journey as well as in Railway accidents.         In view of Pandemic & Covid protocol, concession to all categories of passengers (except 4 categories of Divyangjan, 11 categories of Patients and Students) have been withdrawn from 20.03.2020. (e): At present, there is no such proposal.(f): Does not arise.*****
