                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2354






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Research Centre for Pomegranate




Jaisiddeshwar Shivacharya Mahaswamiji














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to appoint more scientists in the National Research Centre on Pomegranate at Solapur in Maharashtra as this centre does not have sufficient number of scientists; (b)	if so, the number of scientists likely to be appointed and the time by which the appointment is likely to be done along with the details thereof; and (c)	 the funds allocated to the said centre during the last three years and the current year, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Yes, Sir.  In revised cadre strength of Scientists, ICAR has planned to increase sanctioned strength by 12 more scientists for ICAR-National Research on Pomegranate, Solapur thereby raising its strength to 22 from existing 10 scientists. Recruitment/appointment of Scientists is a continuous process and vacancies are filled up on the basis of specialized qualifications prescribed for a particular scientific post, through an established procedure by the ASRB subject to availability of appropriately qualified candidates in the required areas of specialization.  (c)	The funds allocated to the said centre during the last three years and the current year are given below:Year	BE (Rupees in lakhs)	RE (Rupees in lakhs)2019-20	873.43	-2018-19	-	1008.252017-18	-	808.342016-17	-	940.81*******
