                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4469






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Shortage of Non-Faculty Staff in the newly Set up AIIMS




Hemant Patil














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether there is a shortage of nonfaculty staff in the newly set up six AIIMS in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof and the corrective measures taken by the Government in this regard; (c) whether the Government has conducted any assessment regarding shortage of doctors and other medical professionals in the country and particularly in Government medical Institutions during last four years, if so the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (d) the details of healthcare budget spent on augmenting human capital in Government medical institutions, year-wise since 2014; and (e) the percentage of GDP spent on medical education and in subsidizing medical education to potential doctors, year-wise since 2014?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b): 	A total number of 3776various Non-Faculty posts have been sanctioned for each of six functional AIIMS namely AIIMS at Bhopal, Bhubaneswar, Jodhpur, Patna, Raipur and Rishikesh. The incumbency position at various AIIMS is monitored regularly and vacancies are advertised from time to time. The various Non-faculty posts have been filled up from time to time on need basis keeping in view the range of healthcare services and the volume of services and patient load. The details of Institute-wise Non-Faculty position for six AIIMSareshown at Annexure.(c):  No assessment has been made in regard to shortage of doctors and other medical professionals in the country. Health being a state subject, the primary responsibility to ensure availability of doctors and other medical professionals in the country lies with the respective state government.(d) & (e):	Exhaustive data regarding percentage of healthcare budget spent on augmenting human capital in Government Medical Institutions in the country and percentage of GDP spent on medical education in subsidizing medical education is not available with the Ministry.
