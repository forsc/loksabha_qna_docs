                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1370






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Contract Teachers in Lakshadweep




Faizal P.P. Mohammed














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of contract teachers employed from 2014 till January 2020 underthe Department of education Union Territory of Lakshadweep.(b) the number of contract teachers presently working under the Department ofEducation Union Territory of Lakshadweep from January 2020 to till date;(c) the reason for retirement of contract teachers or not renewing their contract forthis academic year in Lakshadweep;(d) whether there is any protest from students on non availability of regularteachers; and(e) if so, the details, thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)UT Administration of Lakshadweep has informed as follows :(a) The breakup of number of contract teachers employed from 2014 tillJanuary 2020 under the Department of Education, Union Territory of Lakshadweepis as under:<pre>Year	No. of contract teachers employed2014-15	1082015-16	1932016-17	1942017-18	2662018-19	2942019-20	2152020-21	91</pre>(b)Presently 40 contract teachers are working under the Department of Education,Union Territory of Lakshadweep.c) Contract teachers are appointed afresh every year up to 31st March and there isno renewal. This year, the Department of Education, UT Administration ofLakshadweep has employed 40 teachers on contract basis.(d) Yes(e) Students served strike notice from Kavaratti, Androth, Kadmath, Amini, AgattiKiltan, Chetlat and Bitra islands on alleged shortage of teachers. There is noshortage of teachers in Lakshadweep Schools as Education department, UT ofLakshadweep appointed 40 teachers on contract basis.****
