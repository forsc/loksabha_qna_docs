                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2840






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Ramsar Sites




Jasbir Singh Gill














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of Ramsar sites in the country, State/UT-wise including Punjab;(b) 	whether there is any proposal to develop Harikewetland for eco-tourism; and(c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)Based on the Ramsar criteria for designating Ramsar sites, India has designated 42 Ramsar sites since 1981. The State/UT-wise list of Ramsar sites in the country including Punjab is annexed.(b)&(c). This Ministry has not received any proposal to develop Harike wetland for eco-tourism. However, as per information received from the State Government of Punjab, it has approved a scheme on Eco-tourism in Harike Wildlife Sanctuary amounting to Rs. 15.00 crores for the year 2020-21 for promoting Eco-friendly tourism in the area.  For this purpose, the State Government has also made a budgetary provision of Rs. 7.56 crore in the budget estimates of FY 2020-21.*****
