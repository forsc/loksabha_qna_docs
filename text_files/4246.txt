                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3884






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Survey for Coal Exploration




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a.	whether the Government has engaged any agency to conduct a survey with regard to coal exploration during the last year;b.	if so, the details thereof;c.	whether the Government proposes to promote coal industry through mining in Rajasthan; and d.	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a& b): Geological Surveys of India (GSI), Central Mine Planning and Design Institute (CMPDI) and Mineral Exploration Corporation of India Limited (MECL) are the agencies engaged by Government of India for exploration of coal through Central Sector Scheme of Exploration of Coal and Lignite.(c& d): As per the Reserves estimated by Geological Survey of India, coal has not been found in Rajasthan and as such there is no plan for coal mining in the state. *****
