                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4493






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Poshan on Wheels




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government is intending to implement “Poshan on Wheels”, Mobile Anganwadi Vans which will carry nutritious food for the infants, expectant and lactating mothers especially at the construction sites endeavoured by the Chandigarh Union Territory administration, across the country, extending the mandate to deliver nutritious food to women and children in remote areas; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the Government has proposed the public private partnership initiative with institutions like AkshayPatra that serves 1.8 million children in 16,856 schools across 12 States on every school day, by employing data analytics, and Internet of Things to streamline effective delivery of meals so that intended beneficiaries of Mobile Anganwadis are catered effectively and if so, the details thereof; and (d)	whether any such proposals are under perusal by the Government and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (d) At present there is no proposal to implement “Poshan on Wheels” by the Ministry of Women and Child Development.Two Mobile Anganwadi Vans – "POSHAN on Wheels" were launched by Union Territory of  Chandigarh on 28.11.2019 under the Innovation component of  POSHAN Abhiyaan to  cover the uncovered marginalized population on the construction sites. States/UTs are at their liberty to choose any activities under Innovation component of POSHAN Abhiyaan as per the guidelines. *****
