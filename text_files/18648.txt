                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

86






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Payment of MSP




Mahua Moitra














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the status of payment of Minimum Support Price (MSP) and the budget for the same for the coming year;(b)	whether the Government is going to delay the implementation given the huge ongoing farmer protests and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	During the current Marketing Season of 2020-21, upto 31.12.2020, procurement of 599.33 LMT of Paddy having MSP value of around Rs 1,13,155 crore, Pulses & oilseeds of 3.06 LMT having MSP value of Rs 1,643 crore, Copra of 5,089 MT having MSP value of Rs 52.40 crore and Cotton of 89.85 lakhs bales having MSP value of Rs 26,277 crore has been done under respective MSP Schemes by the concerned Ministry. 		The payment of Minimum Support Price (MSP) of such procurement to the concerned farmers is being made as per scheme’s guidelines. 	The Budget Estimates (BE) for 2021-22 for the implementation of Price Support Scheme (PSS) for pulses, oilseeds and copra and Market Intervention Scheme is Rs 1500.50 crore. Similarly, Rs 2,42,616 crore has been provided in BE 2021-22 for Food Subsidy which includes MSP operations by the Department of Food and Public Distribution and Rs 136 crore has been provided by Ministry of Textile for cotton. (b):	Government will continue to implement the MSP.  *******
