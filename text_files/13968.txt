                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2595






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Funds Allocated under One Stop Centres




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki













                                                    Chandra Prakash Choudhary
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the details of the ’One-Stop Centers’ currently operational in Gujarat along with the details of the districts not covered under the scheme;(b)	the reasons for non-coverage of some districts across the country under the said scheme;(c)	the details of funds allocated under the scheme during the last two years, year and State/UT-wise; and(d)	the details of unutilized funds under the scheme and the reasons thereof for non-utilisation of the allocated funds thereunder along with the response of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT (SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBEN IRANI)(a) to (d) :  728 OSCs have been approved by Government of India in 724 districts and 680 OSCs have been operationalised by the States/UTs across the country. In Gujarat 33 OSCs have been approved by Government of India in 33 districts which have been operationalised by the State.The total fund released under OSC Scheme during the year 2015-16, 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19 is Rs.229.61 crore, total utilisation of fund is Rs.61.34 crore and total unutilized fund is Rs.168.27 crore. The States/UTs and district authorities undertake the implementation of OSC Scheme. Government of India undertakes review meetings with the officers of State Government through video conferencing and meetings from time to time at various levels for proper implementation of the scheme.The State/UT wise details of funds released under the Scheme during the last two years are at Annexure-I.
