                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3570






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






FDI




Raja Amareshwara Naik













                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has allowed Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) in the EducationSector and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government proposes to put in place legislative framework to regulate theentry and operations of the foreign educational institutions in the country and if so, thedetails thereof;(c) whether the Government has proposed that external commercial borrowings and FDI beenabled for the Education Sector and if so, the details thereof; and(d) the steps being taken by the Government for promotion of FDI in Education Sector?
                                                





ANSWER




Minister of Human Resource Development(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ''Nishank'')(a to d): One of the Budget 2020 announcements of the Government is to enable sourcingof External Commercial Borrowings and FDI in Higher Education. Presently, no suchregulatory framework has been established to regulate the entry and operation of the foreigneducational institutions. However, FDI is permitted in the higher education sector under autoroute.*****
