                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

68






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Approval of Bilhaur Thermal Power Project




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether his Ministry has received any request to accord approval to the 2 x 660 MW Bilhaur thermal power project of NTPC in Uttar Pradesh;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the action taken or proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a)&(b):The Ministry has received the proposal of 2x600 MW Bilhaur Super Thermal Power Project at Village DorvaJamauli, Tehsil Bilhaur, District Kanpur Nagar, Uttar Pradesh by M/s NTPC Limited for grant of Environmental Clearance on 24th July, 2014.(c) The proposal has been appraised by the Expert Appraisal Committee (EAC-Thermal Power Projects) in its meeting during 28th-29th August, 2014. The EAC had sought certain information related to land use, drainage map, plan for not disturbing natural drainage pattern, ground water withdrawal permission, water and wastewater balance diagram and Flyash utilisation plan, for reconsideration. The proposal has been de-listed on 15th June, 2015 as per the Rules.***
