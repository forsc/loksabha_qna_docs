                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

129






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Programs Selected under PRASAD Scheme in Andhra Pradesh




Y. S. Avinash Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of TOURISM be pleased to state:(a)	the details of the programs selected under PRASAD Scheme in the State of Andhra Pradesh;(b)	the number of projects completed during the last three years along with the details of the funds released so far;(c)	whether any projects in the district of Kadapa have been taken up for development;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the number of projects that are in the pipeline to be taken up under PRASAD Scheme? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 				(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)      (a) & (b):	Details of the projects approved under PRASHAD Scheme for the State of Andhra Pradesh is given below:Name of the Project	Sanction Year	Approved Cost(Cr. INR)	StatusDevelopment of Amaravati Town, Guntur District as a Tourist Destination	2015-16	27.77	Completed during preceding two years. 100% funds released. Development of Srisailam Temple	2017-18	43.08	(c) : No Sir;(d)&(e):	Identification of new destinations/places and approval projects for development of such places under PRASHAD scheme is a continuous process, which is done on the basis of the suitable and complete project DPRs received from the respective State Government / Union Territory in adherence to the Scheme guidelines. ***********
