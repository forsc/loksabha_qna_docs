                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1254






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Ranking of Institutes




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is proposing or plans to introduce ranking of educationalinstitutes;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) if not, whether the Government shall consider the same to encourage the performingeducational institutes;(d) whether the Government accept/acknowledges the rankings of few reputed surveyagencies; and(e) if so, the details thereof; and(f) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




(a) to (c): Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Human Resource Development hasalready launched National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) in September, 2015.The first Ranking of educational institutions in the country was launched in April, 2016using this Framework. Under NIRF, the institutes are ranked on the basis of their scoreon a set five (5) parameters namely:i) Teaching, Learning & Resourcesii) Research and Professional Practiceiii) Graduation Outcomesiv) Outreach and Inclusivityv) Perception.Details are at https://www.nirfindia.org/Home .(d) to (f): The Government takes note of some of the reputed international rankings such asTimes Higher Education World University Rankings and QS World University Rankings.*****
