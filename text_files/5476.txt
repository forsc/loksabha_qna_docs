                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5051






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Re-cycle of Scrap




Rameshbhai Lavjibhai Dhaduk














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the scrap of trains, engines, tracks, old coaches, etc. which gets accumulated is disposed of timely;(b)	if not, the concrete steps taken/proposed by the Government for the timely disposal and re-cycling of the said scrap; and(c)	the details in this regard during the last three years, division-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	Yes, Sir.(b)	Does not arise.(c)	The details regarding arising and disposal of scrap for the last three years are appended.*******
