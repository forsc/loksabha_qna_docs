                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

860






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Financial Assistance to Tribal Students




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government provides any financial assistance to tribal students for acquiring higher education in engineering, medical technology and science spheres;(b)  if so, the number of the beneficiaries covered under it every year during the last three years and the current year along with the State/UT-wise number thereof; (c)  whether the Government provides proper guidance to the tribal students to seek admission in foreign recognized universities; (d)  if so, the details thereof; and (e)  if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a): Ministry of Tribal Affairs provides financial assistance to the Tribal students to pursue higher education inter-alia in Engineering, technology and science under the following schemes:(i)  Post Matric Scholarship for ST Students.(ii)  National Fellowship & Scholarship for Higher Education for ST Students.(iii)  National Overseas Scholarship for ST students for studying abroad.  (b): Under the scheme of National Overseas Scholarship for ST students for studying abroad and National Fellowship & Scholarship for Higher Education, there is no state-wise release of funds. Hence, year-wise number of beneficiaries covered under the two schemes along with State-wise beneficiaries covered during the last three years and current year under Post Matric Scholarship Scheme is furnished as per Annexure. (c) & (d): Under the scheme of “National Overseas Scholarships to STs for higher studies abroad”, this Ministry provides financial assistance to students for pursuing higher studies abroad in the Institutes and the courses of their choice. This Ministry provides assistance for applying and verification of documents. Students can also register their difficulties in the grievance portal provided for the purpose for resolution of such grievances/difficulties. (e): Does not arise in view of (c) & (d) above.
