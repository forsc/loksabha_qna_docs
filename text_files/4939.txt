                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4464






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






News Broadcast




Satyadev Pachauri














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of licensed Hindi television channels as on date;(b)	whether the cable operators can broadcast news without obtaining necessary permission from the Government; and(c)	if not, the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to check this practice.






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; AND MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) As on 31.5.2019, of Information & Broadcasting containing Hindi as one of the permitted languages.(b) and (c) As per Rule 6(6) of the there are 573 numbers of Private Satellite Television Channels registered with Ministry Cable TV  Network Rules, 1994, no cable operator shall carry or include in his cable services any Television broadcast or channel which has not been registered by the Central Government for being viewed within the territory of India. Necessary punitive action is taken by the Government on the violations of the said clause.…….
