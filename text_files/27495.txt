                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3073






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Construction of Road in Wildlife Sanctuary




Ramakant Bhargava














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the National Board for Wildlife has given clearance to the National Highway Authority of India(NHAI) for construction of four lane road at National Highway-46 in the 12.30 km area of Ratapani Wildlife Sanctuary;(b)	If so ,the details thereof; and(c)	if not, the time by which clearance is likely to begiven?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a), (b) and (c) A proposal for upgradation / four laning of a stretch from km 8.300 km to 20.740 km (total length 12.440 km) on Obedullaganj-Betul Section of NH- 69 through Ratapani Sanctuary in Madhya Pradesh was received by the Ministry. However, the proposalwas incomplete as some essential information had not been submitted in the proposal. The Ministry has therefore requested the State Government to furnish the required information so that the complete proposal may be placed before the Standing Committee of National Board for Wild Life for consideration. ***
