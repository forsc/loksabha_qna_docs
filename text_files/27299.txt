                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2796






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Beneficiaries under Housing Schemes




T.R.V.S. Ramesh














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)the number of backward castes beneficiaries who have 	been provided housing facilities by the Government in 	the Cuddalore district;(b)the number of such population that has been left homeless as a consequence of natural disasters/calamities in the Cuddalore district in the recent past;(c)the quantum of Central financial assistance provided to 	such displaced population for the construction of houses;(d) whether the Government proposes to provide durable 	houses for the disaster-affected backward caste 	population in the Cuddalore district; and (e)if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                                           THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE             MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                  (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a):	Number of backward castes/classes beneficiaries who have been provided houses under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana– Urban (PMAY-U)by the Government in the Cuddalore district of Tamil Nadu is at Annexure-I. As informed by Ministry of Rural Development, under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G) there is no provision for reservation for backward caste beneficiaries. The total number of houses sanctioned under PMAY-G in Cuddalore District is 34,319.  (b) to (e): Land and colonization are State subjects. Government of India through various programmatic interventions supplements the efforts of States/ Union Territories (UTs) including the beneficiaries affected by natural disasters/calamities. Houses are being sanctioned based on the proposals received from the State/UT Governments after the approval of State Level Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee (SLSMC) headed by Chief Secretary. Government of India has not received any proposal from the State Government of Tamil Nadu for such population that has been left homeless as a consequence of natural disasters/calamities in the Cuddalore district in the recent past.                                     *****
