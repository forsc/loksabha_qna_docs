                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1472






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Centrally Sponsored Schemes




Kunwar Danish Ali













                                                    Girish Chandra
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. †1472TO BE ANSWERED ON 07.12.2021CENTRALLY SPONSORED SCHEMES †1472.    KUNWAR DANISH ALI:                SHRI GIRISH CHANDRA: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the number of centrally sponsored schemes running in the country; (b) whether the Government is contemplating to reduce or discontinue the number of these schemes, if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government proposes to conduct a social audit of the social sector welfare schemes in terms of beneficiaries; (d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (e) the details of its impact on the smooth functioning of social sector schemes?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(DR. VIRENDRA KUMAR)(a) & (b) : The details of the number of centrally sponsored schemes being run by the Department of Social Justice and Empowerment are enclosed at Annexure. (c) to (e) : Starting from 2021-22, it is programmed to conduct social audit with the stakeholders about the outcomes of all the schemes, and to bring out ways to improve the implementation. ***** AnnexureAnnexure as referred to in reply to part (a) & (b) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 1472 due for answer on 7.12.2021S.  No.	Programme/Schemes1	Pre-Matric Scholarship for SC Students studying in class IX and X2	Post Matric Scholarship for SC Students3	Pre-matric Scholarships for the children of those engaged in unclean occupation involving cleaning and prone to health hazards4	Babu Jagjivan Ram Chhatrawas Yojana for Boys5	Pradhan  Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana6	Central Assistance to States/UTs for effective implementation of the Protection of Civil Right Act 1955 and Prevention of Atrocities Act, 19897	Assistance to Scheduled Castes Development Corporations (SCDCs)8	Pre-Matric Scholarship for OBCs students9	Post-Matric Scholarship for OBCs students10	Construction of Hostel for OBC Boys and Girls11	Dr. Ambedkar Scheme of Pre-Metric Scholarship for students belonging to Economically Backward Classes (EBCs)12	Dr. Ambedkar Scheme of Pre-Metric Scholarship  and Post Matric Scholarship for students belonging to Denotified  and Nomadic Tribes  (DNTs)13	Nanaji Deshmukh Scheme of Construction of Hostels for DNT Boys and Girls14	National Action Plan for Senior Citizens15	National Action Plan for Drug Demand Reduction*****
