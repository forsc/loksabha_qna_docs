                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2743






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Contribution of MSME Sector in Production




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the participation of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in the successful factory production of the country is about forty per cent;(b) 	if so, whether any steps are being taken by the Government to provide state-of-the art technology, necessary investment and technology to these MSMEs that have been proved useful in the local economy;(c) 	if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether MSMEs have a forty per cent contribution in the country exports; and(e) 	if so, whether any special efforts are being made by the Government to further augment their export share and if so, the details thereof? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI BHANU PRATAP SINGH VERMA) (a):	As per the information received from Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation, the share of MSME Manufacturing Output in All India Manufacturing Output at current prices during 2020-21 was around 36%. (b):	The Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) has established Technology Centres (TCs) which provides technological support to industries through design & manufacture of tools, precision components, moulds, dies etc. in sectors such as general engineering, forging & foundry, electronics, electrical, fragrance & flavor, glass, footwear and sports goods. The Ministry also implements various schemes and programmes aimed at providing investment for adoption of latest technologies in MSME sector. These schemes / programmes, inter alia, include financial support. Some of these schemes include MSME Champions Scheme (Erstwhile CLCS-TUS), Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE), Prime Minister''s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP), Micro and Small Enterprises - Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP) and ECLGS etc.(c):	As informed by the NCGTC, under ECLGS, an amount of Rs. 3.15 lakh crore have been sanctioned till 31.01.2022 out of which final guarantees worth Rs. 3.02 lakh crore have been issued so far to MLIs benefitting around 1.17 crore  borrowers. A brief performance of ECLGS as on 31.01.2022 is as under:<pre>Entity Type Wise Guarantee Issued 	Guarantee Nos. 	% Guarantee Nos. 	Guarantee Amount (in Rs. Cr) 	% Guarantee Amount Micro	1,03,65,517	89%	73,901.23	24.42%Small	5,20,344	4%	77,588.40	25.64%Medium	2,63,451	2%	51,156.93	16.91%MSME Total	1,11,49,312	95%	2,02,646.56	67.00%Other Business Enterprises	5,53,507	5%	99,934.54	33.03%Grand Total	1,17,02,819	100%	3,02,581.10	100%</pre>(d) & (e):	As per the information received from Directorate General of Commercial Intelligence & Statistics, the Share of Export of specified MSME related products in All India Exports during 2020-21 was 49.35%.  The Ministry of MSME is implementing the International Cooperation (IC) Scheme.  The scheme aims at capacity building of MSMEs for entering export market by facilitating their participation in international exhibitions/fairs/conferences/seminar/buyer-seller meets abroad as well as providing them with actionable market-intelligence and reimbursement of various costs involved in export of goods and services.
