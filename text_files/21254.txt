                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2501






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Fish Production in North-Eastern States




Saikia Dilip














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is a huge gap in fish production and its supply in North-Eastern States including the State of Assam;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the names of schemes being implemented by the Government for fish production; and(d) 	the funds released in this regard by the Government to North-Eastern States including Assam since 2014 and if so, the details thereof, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRYAND DAIRYING (SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) to (d):	The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a flagship scheme namely “Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY)”- a scheme to bring about Blue Revolution through sustainable and responsible development of fisheries sector in India” with a highest ever investment of           Rs.20050 crore in fisheries sector over a period of 5 years from financial year 2020-21 to financial year 2024-25 in all States/Union Territories.  To address the demand and supply gaps in fish production and its supply in North-Eastern States including the State of Assam, PMMSY inter-alia provides for region specific interventions for promotion and development of inland fisheries and aquaculture in the North-Eastern region, with special emphasis on fish production and productivity, quality, technology, post-harvest infrastructure and management, modernisation and strengthening of value chain and fishers’ welfare.  In addition, a dedicated fund namely, ‘Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund’ (FIDF) with a total fund size of Rs.7522.48 crore has been set up for providing concessional finance for creation and strengthening of fisheries infrastructure facilities.The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying during the period from 2014-15 to 2020-21 has sanctioned funds to the tune of Rs. 364.66 crore  to the North-Eastern States for development of fisheries and aquaculture and state-wise and scheme-wise details of the funds released are furnished below: (Rs. in crores) S.
