                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

330






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Small and Marginal Farmers




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  Whether it is fact that the Government has initiated any specific programme to help small and marginal farmers in the country including district Gopalganj of Bihar; (b)  If so, the number of such farmers covered under such schemes in the last two years, State and year-wise; and (c)  The details of measures taken to mitigate the sufferings of small and marginal farmers in the country including Gopalganj district of Bihar?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????   (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	Most Schemes ie Central Sector and Centrally Sponsored, of the Department of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare (DA&FW) are for the benefit of small and marginal farmers in the country, including district Gopalganj of Bihar. (b)	Beneficiaries of these schemes, specially Centrally Sponsored Schemes, are decided/ selected by the respective State Governments and therefore details are available with them. However, Data on central sector schemes like PM-KISAN, Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maandhan Yojana (PM-KMY) and Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) are given in the Annexure I.(c)	Allocation and release in respect of major Centrally Sponsored Schemes in the country including for the State of Bihar is attached as Annexure II
