                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2709






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Promotion of Startups




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut













                                                    Rekha Arun Verma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Union Government has taken any specific measures to promote startups in the country especially in the States of Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh; and(b) 	if so, the number of startups that have been benefitted from such specific measures in Uttar Pradesh and Maharashtra?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a): 	Startup India initiative is a flagship initiative of Government of India which aims at building a strong ecosystem for nurturing innovation and Startups in the country. A 19-point Startup India Action Plan was launched in January 2016 which paved the way for the introduction of a number of policy initiatives to build a strong, conducive, growth-oriented environment for Indian startups. Hon’ble Prime Minister unveiled ‘Startup India: The Way Ahead’ at 5 years celebration of Startup India on 16th January, 2021 which lays down actionable plans for promotion of ease of doing business for startups, greater role of technology in executing various reforms, building capacities of stakeholders and enabling a digital Aatmanirbhar Bharat. The key objectives and the action plans of ‘Startup India: The Way Ahead’ is available at the website of Startup India i.e  https://www.startupindia.gov.in/content/dam/invest-india/Templates/public/Startup_India-Way_Ahead.pdf (b): 	A total of 52732 startups have taken the benefit of DPIIT startup recognition, as on 21st July 2021.  The number of startups that have taken such benefits in Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh and jobs created by them in respective States are as follows:  <pre> 	Total Startups recognised by DPIIT	Jobs created as reported by recognised startupsMaharashtra	9864	110510Uttar Pradesh	4642	48642</pre>*******
