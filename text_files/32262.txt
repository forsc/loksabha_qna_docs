                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3694






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Bill on Manual Scavenging




Francisco Cosme Sardinha














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3694TO BE ANSWERED ON 21.12.2021BILL ON MANUAL SCAVENGING 3694. SHRI COSME FRANCISCO CAITANO SARDINHA: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has any poposal to introduce ‘The Prohibition of Employment as Manual Scavengers and their Rehabilitation (Amendment) Bill, 2020, if so, the details thereof, if not, the reasons therefore; and (b) the details of technology that would replace the manual scavengers in the cleaning task in wake of above proposal of the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RAMDAS ATHAWALE)(a): At present, there is no such proposal.(b): Manual Scavenging is a prohibited activity with effect from 06.12.2013.  The Government is trying to promote mechanical cleaning of sanitation related cleaning activity associated with cleaning of sewers, septic tanks. Manual scavenging associated with manual cleaning of insanitary latrines, which have since been demolished and replaced with sanitary latrines under Swachh Bharat Mission. Such sanitary latrines are either connected to underground or twin pits do not require any manual cleaning.***
