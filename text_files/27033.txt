                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2757






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Special Armed Forces Recruitment Campaign




 Sunil Kumar Pintu














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to run a special armed forces recruitment campaign in view of the national security and increase in requirement of armed forces capacity by recruiting youth in the army from rural areas;(b) if so, the areas including of Bihar where this campaign is likely to be held: and(c) if not, the reasons threfor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) to (c):  No, Sir. At present, no special recruitment drive is being planned by the armed forces.The location for recruitment rally is decided from amongst the districts under jurisdiction of the Army Recruiting Office (ARO) in consultation with the civil administration.Recruitment for the Navy has migrated to an application-based process wherein online applications are invited from the eligible youth through an advertisement twice a year for examinations to be held in February-March and August-September at designated centres across the country.  Air Force Common Admission Test (AFCAT) for selection of officers and Scheduled Test for Airmen Recruitment (STAR) for the airmen are conducted                bi-annually.  Induction is an ongoing as well as time bound process and is being carried out strictly as per the timelines.*****
