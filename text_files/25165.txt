                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

961






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Paperless Work in Schools




Prabhubhai Nagarbhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any scheme of the Government to make the teaching and training work in schools paperless; and(b) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) and (b) The effort to minimise papers in teaching and training is a continuous process. An Integrated Teacher Training Programme called NISHTHA was launched on 6th October, 2020, it is an online capacity building programme for "Improving Quality of School Education through Integrated Teacher Training". There are total 18 modules in this programme: 12 for teachers, 5 for school heads and 1 specialised module on teaching and learning during COVID-19 times. A total of 30 states and 8 autonomous organisations have started their courses on NISHTHA in 11 languages. Till date 5,17,25,114 (Five crore Seventeen Lakh Twenty five thousand One hundred and Fourteen) enrolments have been made on NISHTHA of which 4,61,75,725 (Four crore sixty one lakh seventy five thousand seven hundred twenty five) courses have been completed and certificates have been issued.NISHTHA can be accessed from the below link: https://itpd.ncert.gov.in//**********
