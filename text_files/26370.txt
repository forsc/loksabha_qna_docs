                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2129






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Disbursement of Funds




Aparupa  Poddar













                                                    Mohammad Jawed
                                                








                                                    Hibi Eden
                                                








                                                    T.N. Prathapan
                                                








                                                    Vincent H Pala
                                                








                                                    Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka
                                                








                                                    Kani K. Navas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether an audit has been conducted on the disbursement of funds under the various schemes introduced to facilitate online learning;(b) if so, the details thereof and the details of disbursement and utilisation of funds, from the date of implementation of said schemes till May, 2021, State-wise for the following schemes-i. SWAYAM (Study Webs of Active Learning for Young Aspiring Minds),ii. DIKSHA,iii. e-PG Pathshala,iv. National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning (NPTEL),v. Virtual Labsvi. E-ShodhSindhu,vii. National Digital Library of India (NDLI)viii. Swayam Prabha; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (c) Yes Sir, the funds are disbursed under these schemes based on progress, utilization certificates and after due diligence. The details of releases/ expenditure of funds, are as under:Scheme/ Project NameDetails of Funds released/expenditure (Rs. in Cr.)SWAYAM (Study Webs of Active Learning for Young Aspiring Minds)459.97DIKSHA10.61e-PG Pathshala60.00National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning (NPTEL)263.28*Virtual Labs154.76E-ShodhSindhu1129.96National Digital Library of India (NDLI)62.15Swayam Prabha175.86* This amount is included in total releases under SWAYAMUtilization of funds is ascertained from utilization certificate submitted by the concerned Project Coordinator/ National Coordinator.*****
