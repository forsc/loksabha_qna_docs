                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

134






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Recruitment through RRBs




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is true that the number of persons recruited through Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) have been declining in the recent years and if so, the details thereof during the last three years;(b)	whether the number of contractual appointments are steadily increasing in the Railways in the recent years and if so, the details thereof during the last three years;(c)	whether the Government has taken note that the general public has expressed concern over privatization of Railways which is causing loss of jobs in the direct recruitment of RRBs; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the response of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF STARRED QUESTION NO. 134 BY SHRI D. K. SURESH TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 28.07.2021 REGARDING RECRUITMENT THROUGH RRBS(a):	No, Sir.(b):	No, Sir.(c) & (d): At present, there is no proposal to privatize the Indian Railways. However, to bridge the gap in Capital Funding and bring in modern technology and efficiencies, it has been planned to use Public Private Partnership (PPP) model to attract investment in Railway sector. Ministry of Railways has been taking several initiatives through various schemes to attract investment in various areas, which include provision of rail connectivity, acquisition of rolling stock, manufacturing of locomotives, setting up of private freight terminals, station redevelopment etc.*****
