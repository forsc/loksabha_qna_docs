                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3587






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Profit from Tax on Petrol/Diesel




Prasun Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and quantum of tax collected by the Government from the sale of petrol and diesel during the last five years and the current year, year-wise; and(b) the details of profit earned by the Oil Companies from it?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b)Prices of petrol and diesel have been made market-determined by the Government with effect from 26.06.2010 and 19.10.2014 respectively. Since then, the Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) take appropriate decision on pricing of petrol and diesel in line with international product prices and other market conditions.The total contribution to Central and State Exchequer from petroleum sector during the last five years is given as under:-(` in crore)Particulars2014-152015-162016-172017-182018-19 (P)Contribution to Central Exchequer172,065254,297335,175345,249365,113Contribution to State Exchequer160,554160,209189,770209,155230,325Total Contribution of Petroleum Sector to Exchequer332,619414,506524,945554,404595,438The details of profit earned by PSU OMCs from all activities during the last five years are given as under:-(` in crore)Companies2014-152015-16*2016-172017-182018-19IOCL5,27311,24219,10621,34616,894HPCL2,7333,7256,2096,3576,029BPCL5,0857,0568,0397,9197,132* Figures are as per IND AS from financial year 2015-16 onwardsx-x-x-x-x
