                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1089






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Forest Cover




Aparajita Sarangi













                                                    Feroze Varun Gandhi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total percentage of forest cover and tree cover in the country, State/UT-wise during the last five years; (b)	 whether the forest land has been cleared and diverted for non-forest purposes during the said period; and (c)	if so, the details thereof indicating the area of land in hectares cleared along with the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)   Forest Survey of India (FSI), Dehradun, an organization under the Ministry carries out assessment of forest cover of the country biennially and findings are published in India State of Forest Report (ISFR).  As per the last three ISFR reports, the total forest cover and tree cover outside forest in the country is as follows:-ISFR	Forest Cover in square kilometre and percentage of total geographical area 	Tree cover outside forest in square kilometre and percentage of total geographical area 2017	7,08,273 (21.54%)	93,815 (2.85%)2015	7,01,673 (21.34%)	92,572 (2.82%)2013	6,97,898 (21.23%)	91,266 (2.78%)State/UT wise detail of forest cover and tree cover are given in Annexure I and II respectively. (b) & (c)    Total forest area approved under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 for use of non-forestry purpose in the last five year is 69141.32 hectare. The purpose/category wise details are given in Annexure III. In the last four years compensatory Afforestation has been taken-up in 31161.43 hectare of non-forest land and 72097.69 hectare of degraded forest land.  ***
