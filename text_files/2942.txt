                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2640






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Food Processing through Indigenous Method




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is giving emphasis on Food Processing through indigenous methods; (b)	if so, the details of the plan/ programme in this regard; and (c)	whether there is a prospective plan for carrying out food processing through indigenous method? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a)	to (c): Government is promoting overall development of food processing sector in the country, supporting various methods of processing (including indigenous method) with objectives to reduce wastage of food, improve value addition, promote crop diversification, ensure better return to the farmers, generate employment, earn foreign exchange, etc. Ministry is accordingly implementing Pradhan Mantri Kissan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) to support various facets of food processing in the country.  *****
