                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4010






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Stolen Statue




S. Jagathrakshakan













                                                    A. Ganeshamurthi
                                                








                                                    A.K.P. Chinraj
                                                








                                                    T. R. Paarivendhar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Indian High Commission in London has received a report from Tamil Nadu Police that 15th century bronze statue of Tamil poet ‘Tirumankai Aalvar’ which was reportedly stolen from a temple in Kumbakonam, Tamil Nadu and was replaced with a fake one and that the stolen idol is the same one that is with Ashmolean Museum in Oxford;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the action taken by the Indian High Commission, London on this report? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS                 [SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) & (b)	 Yes. High Commission of India, London has received the report about the stolen idol of Tamil poet ‘Tirumankai Aalvar’  from Tamil Nadu Police.(c)	The Indian High Commission has taken up the matter with Ashmolean Museum, Oxford.***
