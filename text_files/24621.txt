                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

55






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Work on NH Projects




Chandra Sekhar Sahu













                                                    Prataprao  Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has been providing financial assistance for improvement and maintenance of National Highways (NHs) in various States;(b) if so, the quantum of funds allocated, released and utilised during each of the last three years and the current year, State-wise;(c) the details of the criteria fixed for allocation of funds to various States;(d) whether the progress of work of several stretches of NHs in various States which have been entrusted to National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) for upgradation to four-lane under National Highways Development Programme (NHDP) Phase-III has been very slow and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor, State-wise;(e) the cost escalation due to such slow progress of work on the said stretches, State-wise; and(f) the details of corrective steps taken by the Government for timely completion of work?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (f) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.
