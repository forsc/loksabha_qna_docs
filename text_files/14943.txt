                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3649






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Apprentice Protsahan Yojana




Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government has envisioned a target for training 20 lakh apprentices under the Apprentice Protsahan Yojana and if so, the details thereof;(b)	The details of progress of the Apprentice Protsahan Yojana along with number of apprentices trained under this Scheme till date, State-wise, particularly in Andhra Pradesh; and(c)	the details of the industries that have registered to avail this scheme till date, State/UT-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Apprentice Protsahan Yojana (APY) which was started on 16th October, 2014 to share 50% of prescribed stipend to the apprentices by Government of India for the first two years of training for one lakh apprentices to be engaged by establishments by March 2017. APY has been replaced with a new scheme named ‘National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme’ (NAPS) notified on 19th August, 2016 to promote apprenticeship training. Currently APY scheme is not in operation.(b) & (c)	The details of progress of Apprentice Protsahan Yojana along with number of apprentices trained and industries registered under this scheme State/UT-wise is attached at Annex-I.
