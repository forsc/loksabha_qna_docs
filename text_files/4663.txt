                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4252






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Cargo Transportations on National Waterways




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod













                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                








                                                    Bharatsinhji Shankarji Dabhi
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of cargo transportation on National waterways in the country during the last   five years and the current year; and(b)	whether there is an increasing trend in the cargo transportation during the said period and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b)  	Cargo transportation on National waterways (NWs) in the country during the last five years and the current year has been maintaining an increasing trend as per details given below. The transportation of cargo increased on NWs from 2016-17 onwards after declaration of new NWs under the National Waterways Act, 2016.Year	2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20Cargo transported on NWs (In Million Tonnes)	6.59	8.14	55.47	55.03	72.31	14.59 (up to May, 2019)                                              *****
