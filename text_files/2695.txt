                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2426






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Steel Plants in North East




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of steel plants in the country, State-wise;(b)	whether any steel plant is established in North East, if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has any plans to establish new plants in the country particularly in Assam and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the present status of Dagaon Steel plant of undivided Kamrup district of Assam?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)&(b): The details of steel plants in the country, State-wise are given at Annexure-I and  the  state wise number of downstream  industries  operating in North East during 2018-19 (prov) is given below:-Number of downstream industries (Re-Rolling/GP/GC) operating in North Eastin 2018-19 (provisional)State	Number of downstream industriesARUNACHAL PRADESH	3ASSAM	8MANIPUR	1MEGHALAYA	6TRIPURA	1Source : JPC (c) 	No, Sir.(d)	Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL) had taken up a Project for installation of Hot Dip Galvanizing Line (HDGL) at Dagaon, Assam in 1997. A Joint Venture Company was incorporated in the year 2005. However, the project could not be set up due to lack of infrastructure, non allocation of land to SAIL by the State Government and non availability of fiscal incentives like concessional excise duty, income tax benefits, backward area concession, purchase & price preference, etc. ********
