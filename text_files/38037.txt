                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4719






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Funds allocated under JJM




Chandrani Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the funds allocated so far to Odisha under Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM);(b)	the percentage of population covered under the Mission in each district of the State;(c)	the details of the number of households in Keonjhar HPC which has got tap water JJM so far; and(d)	whether the Government has achieved the target so far under the Mission and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)  The details of fund allocated to Odisha, fund drawn and reported utilization by the Government of Odisha under Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) in FY 2019-20, FY 2020-21 and FY 2021-22 as on 27.03.2022, is as under:(amount in ? Crore)Financial Year	Central Share	State Share	Opening Balance	Allocation	Fund Drawn	Total available amount	Reported expenditure	Reported expenditure2019-20	0.78	364.74	364.74	365.52	275.02	255.022020-21	90.50	812.15	609.11	699.61	688.69	673.002021-22	10.93	3,323.42	2,492.56	2,503.49	1,064.51	1,054.64(b) The data related to percentage of population, district-wise, covered under JJM is not maintained by this Department. The number of rural households for which provision of potable tap water supply has been made, district-wise, as on 27.03.2022 is at Annex.(c) As informed by the State, no JJM funding is involved in the projects for providing tap water connection in Keonjhar District. These projects have been taken up with funding under Odisha Mineral Bearing Areas Development Corporation (OMBADC). Further, as informed by the State, out of 3,50,950 households in Kendujhar district, 2,48,313 households have the provision of tap water connection. (d)  The Government of Odisha has informed that it has taken necessary steps for making provision of functional household tap connection for all rural households in the State within the mission period. It has further informed that required projects have been sanctioned, and phase-wise tendering & issuance of work orders has been taken up. *****
