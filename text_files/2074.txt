                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Earnings of Department of Post




Srinivas Kesineni














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Department of Post (DoP) is increasingly earning revenues from non-traditional sectors like e-commerce and payments bank and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	percentage of revenue earned by DoP from value added services, service-wise; (c) 	the future expansion plans proposed by the Government in these sectors in the State of Andhra Pradesh; and (d) 	the number of Indian Post Payment Bank (IPPB) accounts in Andhra Pradesh, Post Office-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)	The details of Revenue earned from non-traditional sector like e-commerce and Payment Bank during last three years is as under:<pre>Year	Revenue Earned in Rs.
