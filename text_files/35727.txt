                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3041






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Vacant Posts in AIR/Doordarshan




C. Lalrosanga














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of posts lying vacant in Engineering Programme and Administrative Cadres in all All India Radio (AIR), Doordarshan and other Prasar Bharati establishments of North Eastern Region (NER) along with number of such posts reserved for Scheduled Castes (SCs) and Scheduled Tribes (STs) showing break-up of all AIR, Doordarshan and othert Prasar Bharati establishments in North East Region for each cadre;(b)	the manner in which the Prasar Bharati proposes to fill up these vacant posts; (c)	whether the Government proposes any recruitment drive for Prasar Bharati through Staff Selection Commission or Prasar Bharati Recruitment Board for North East Region as special case; and (d)	if so, details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS [SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR](a) to (d): The cadre-wise details of posts lying vacant in Engineering, Programme and Administrative Cadres of various establishments of Prasar Bharati in North-Eastern Region (NER) along with  number of such posts reserved for Scheduled Castes (SCs) and Scheduled Tribes (STs) in NER are at Annexure.
