                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2487






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Sale of Adultrated Fertilizers




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	The details of the total number of cases that came to light wherein adulterated pesticides/chemical fertilizers were sold during the years 2014 to 2020. State/UT-wise; (b) 	The details of the action taken against companies involved in manufacturing and sale of such adulterated pesticides/fertilizers; and(c)	The details of such companies and their dealers against whom action has been taken in Balaghat/ Seoni districts of Madhya Pradesh?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(SHRI D.V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a):  (i). PESTICIDES : State Governments of Punjab, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, Jharkhand, Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Odisha, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Andhra Pradesh, Tamilnadu, Kerala, Goa, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya; Union territories of Jammu & Kashmir, Chandigarh have reported that no cases of sale of adulterated pesticides have been reported in their States during the years 2014 to 2020. However, State Government of Haryana, Gujarat, and Karnataka have reported that 07, 50 and 255 cases of adulterated pesticides were reported in their States respectively during 2014 to 2020. Government of Telangana has reported that During 2020-21, a total of 55 No. cases of adulterated pesticides came to the light in the State.During 2014-2020, 13 cases of adulterated pesticides have been reported by Central Insecticide Inspectors in which 5 cases were reported in Karnataka, 2 each in Odisha &Rajasthan and 1 each in Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Goa and Uttar Pradesh.(ii). FERTILIZERS: The year-wise details relating to non standard samples during 2013-14 to 2017-18 are as follows:-S.No	Year	          No. of Non- Standard samples1.	2013-14	          67772.	2014-15	          35603.	2015-16	          53604.	2016-17	          72655.	2017-18	          4872Further, the State Govt. of Madhya Pradesh has reported  1 case of adulterated Chemical Fertilizer(SSP Granular) and FIR has been registered in the Guna District in the year 2020 against the manufacturer company (Agro Phos India Pvt. Ltd.). The State Government of Andhra Pradesh has reported one case of fake MOP adulterated fertiliser in Prakassam district during the 2018-19, Prosecution has been initiated against seven persons. A copy each of the communication received from the State Government of Madhya Pradesh and Andhra Pradesh are enclosed at Annexure- A.(b):   (i). PESTICIDES: State Government of Haryana has reported that in one case FIR has been filed, in 4 cases warnings have been issued by Districts Courts and two cases are under process.  State Government of Gujarat has reported that in all 50 cases, court cases have been registered against companies involved in manufacturing and sale of pesticides under the Insecticide Act, 1968 and the Insecticide Rules, 1971. State Government of Karnataka has reported that 59 sale licences of dealers have been suspended and 35 cases have been booked.State Government of Telangana has reported that in all 55 cases, cheating cases have been filed against dealers/manufacturers involved in sale/ manufacturing of adulterated pesticides. Central Insecticide Inspectors have initiated action under provisions of Insecticide Act, 1968 against the dealers/ manufacturers involved in adulteration of pesticides in all 13 cases. Out of these 13 cases, accused have been convicted by Hon’ble Court in 5 cases and penalty have been imposed; while rest 8 cases are sub-judice. (ii). FERTILIZERS: The yearwise details relating to prosecution launched for non standard samples during 2013-14 to 2017-18 are as follows:-S.No	Year	        No. of prosecution launched1.	2013-14	        3312.	2014-15	        1623.	2015-16	        2054.	2016-17	        525.	2017-18	        839The state wise details of follow up action on Non Standard Samples are at Annexure-B.(c):	State Government of Madhya Pradesh has reported that no such case has been noticed. *********
