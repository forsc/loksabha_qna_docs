                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1074






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Institutional Deliveries




Aparajita Sarangi














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether institutional deliveries have decreased in the country since the lockdown was imposed, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government maintains records of data indicating the number of antenatal    check-ups conducted since March, 2020 as a part of Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA);(c) if so, the details thereof, and if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the State/UT-wise number of designated healthcare facilities established and currently functional in rural districts under PMSMA, particularly in Odisha?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) There has been a drop of 23.9% in institutional delivery in the country from April to June’20 as compared to the same period last year as per the Health Management Information System (HMIS), in the light of COVID-19 pandemic.  (b) & (c) Yes, As per PMSMA Portal, a total of 10,96,048 antenatal checkups have been conducted since March’2020 to Aug’2020(d) the details of State/UT-wise number of designated healthcare facilities established and functional  including in  Odisha is given in “Annexure”
