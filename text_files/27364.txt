                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3033






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Health Mela Scheme under NHM




Amol Ramsing Kolhe













                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Ratansinh Magansinh Rathod
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will be Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a)  the main objective of the Government to launch Health Mela Scheme under National Health Mission (NHM);(b)  the names of the States/UTs where Health Melas have been organized under NHM during last three years including the number of persons benefited therefrom;(c)  whether the Government provides funds to the States for organizing such Health Mela;(d)  if so, the details of funds allocated/released/utilized during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise;(e)  whether the Government proposes to organize more such fairs in various States/UTs particularly in Maharashtra; and(f)  if so, the details thereof, district-wise and block level-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH ANDFAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (e):	Public Health and Hospitals is a State subject and the responsibility of providing health care services, including the organising of Health Mela, lies with the respective State Governments. However, under National Health Mission(NHM) financial and technical support is provided to the States/UTs for health system strengthening, including for  organizing Health Camps as well as for other community outreach programme. Such support is based on the proposals, received from the States under Programme Implementation Plan (PIPs) as per the norms and available resources.The main objectives of Health Mela scheme is at Annexure I.	During the Financial Year 2018-19,funds have been given to States for organizing Health Melas, based on their proposals.   The details of funds released in 2018-19 &utilized during  the last three years is at Annexure-II&III
