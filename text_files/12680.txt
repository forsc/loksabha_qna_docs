                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1518






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Maize Cultivation




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government is aware of the vast scope of areas under maize cultivation in the hilly areas of Odisha State; (b)	 if so, the steps taken to develop the maize cultivation in the State; (c)	 the ways in which the maize farmers are being assisted by the Union Government; (d)	 whether any Central scheme has been launched for the purpose; and (e)	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????   (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (e):     Government of India (GOI) is aware of the vast scope of area under maize cultivation in the hilly areas of Odisha State. In order to encourage the cultivation of maize, GOI is implementing National Food Security Mission (NFSM) – Coarse Cereals (including maize programme) in eight districts of the State. Under this programme, assistance is being provided to the farmers for cluster demonstrations on improved package of practices of maize & inter-cropping with other crops and distribution of certified seeds of High Yielding Varieties (HYVs) & Hybrid Seeds for increasing the production of maize through area expansion and productivity enhancement.	             Further, state government of Odisha is assisting maize farmers through central sector scheme, National Agriculture Market (eNAM) for facilitating maize trading at Regulated Market Committees (RMC). Besides, research efforts of Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) has led to the development and release of 26 high yielding and stress tolerant varieties/hybrids of maize for Odisha during the years 2014 to 2019.********
