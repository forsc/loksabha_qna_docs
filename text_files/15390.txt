                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4053






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Free Train Services for Unemployed Youth




Rajveer Diler














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry of Railways is providing free transport facilities for the unemployed youth to appear in various competitive examinations, interviews, etc.;(b) if so, whether this facility is also provided to the candidates appearing in competitive examinations and interviews conducted by the States;(c)  if not, whether the Government is taking any step in this direction; and(d)  if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) and (b): Full concession is granted in the basic fare of second class and 50% concession in the basic fare of  sleeper class to Unemployed Youth of age upto 35 years to attend interview for a job in Central/State Government Offices.             Besides, 50% concession in the basic fare of Sleeper/Second class is also granted to Unemployed Youth of age upto 35 years to attend interview for a job in other Public Sector Organization viz. a Statutory Body, a Municipal Corporation, a Government undertaking or a University or Public Sector Banks.  Other charges like reservation fee, Superfast surcharge etc. wherever applicable are levied separately.             Moreover, Free journey facility is extended to SC/ST candidates appearing in exam conducted by Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs)/Railway Recruitment Cells (RRCs).(c) and (d):  Do not arise. *****
