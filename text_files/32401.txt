                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3833






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Cold Storage for Processed Food Items




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of cold storages set up for storing perishable commodities, processed food items, poultry meat, sea food etc., in Maharashtra;(b)	whether there is shortage of cold storage facilities in some districts;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the steps taken by Government to establish more cold storages, including in the Public-Private Partnership mode and the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a) to (d): Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) does not support setting up of stand alone cold storages. However, it incentivizes creation of cold storage infrastructure as part of food processing projects under relevant component schemes of Central Sector Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana (PMKSY). Under its component schemes,  Ministry provides credit linked financial assistance (capital subsidy) in the form of grants-in-aid to entrepreneurs for setting up of food processing / preservation industries with cold storage infrastructure also.  As part of 3 Mega Food Parks in Maharashtra, Ministry has approved  setting up of cold storage and deep freeze capacity of 8200 MT and as part of 62 Integrated Cold Chain projects in Maharashtra, Ministry has approved creation of cold storage capacity of 1.9 LMT under corresponding component schemes of PMKSY. Out of these projects, 2 Mega Food Parks and 58 Cold chain projects have been made operational. District-wise details of these projects is placed at Annexure.Also, Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare is implementing Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) for development of Horticulture in the country including Maharashtra under which financial assistance is available, inter-alia, for setting up of Post Harvest Management (PHM) infrastructure including establishment of cold storages (Construction, Expansion and Modernization) etc. The component is demand/ entrepreneur-driven for which assistance at the rate 35% of the eligible project cost in general areas and 50% in hilly & scheduled areas, is available as credit linked back ended subsidy through respective State Horticulture Missions. *****
