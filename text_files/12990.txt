                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1823






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Import Tax




 Sunil Kumar Pintu














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering to increase import tax on more than 50 items of various sectors including electronics, electrical goods, chemicals and handicrafts; (b) if so, the details thereof along with the percentage likely to be increased as a result thereof; and(c) the details of its impact on the people of the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE                  (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a):    At present there is no such proposal.(b) and (c):   Does not arise in view of the (a) above.*******
