                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3496






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Launch of MCA21 Portal




Shrirang Appa Barne













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                








                                                    Prataprao  Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to launch Version 3 of MCA21 portal and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Testing of the said portal has been completed and if so, the details thereof; (c) the details of the key components of MCA21 Version 3 portal;(d) whether the Government has set up any MCA Lab as part of the Version 3 of MCA21 and if so, the details thereof; and(e) whether the Ministry has allocated or issued any funds for the said purpose and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION; MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF CORPORATE AFFAIRS                                                                   (RAO INDERJIT SINGH)(a to c): Yes, Sir. Version 3 of MCA21 is proposed to be launched and deployed in phases and will include Company and LLP Modules, e-Adjudication, e-Consultation, e-Book, Learning Management and Compliance Management System driven by data analytics, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. The process of development and testing of different modules is in progress. Modules like e-Consultation, e-Book have already been launched and deployed along with revamped website in May 2021. (d):	 MCA Lab is proposed to be set up as part of MCA21 Version 3 for improving data analytics.(e): 	An amount of Rs 639.36 crore is to be paid to the Service Provider for implementation, operation and maintenance of different modules of MCA21 for a period of 78 months.******
