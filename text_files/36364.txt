                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3375






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






New FOB at Madan Mahal Station




Rakesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a new Foot Over Bridge (FOB) has been constructed at Madan Mahal Station, Jabalpur under the West Central Railway zone;(b) if so, whether it takes extra time to go from one platform to another as the bridge is located far away; (c) if so, whether this is causing problems for women, children and senior citizens; (d) if so, the details thereof; and (e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (e)  Yes, sir. A new Foot Over Bridge (FOB) has been constructed at Madan Mahal Railway station. On technical grounds, this foot over bridge has been constructed towards Kachpura end of the station. However, for the convenience of the passengers, each platform is being equipped with ramps as well as lifts. Apart from this, escalator is also being provided at platform no.4.*****
