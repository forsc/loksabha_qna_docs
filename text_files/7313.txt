                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1013






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






New AYUSH Medical College




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has set up any new AYUSH medical colleges since 2014; (b) if so, the details thereof, State/UTwise indicating the number of seats and courses taught in such colleges; and (c) whether Government is planning to establish any new AYUSH medical college, if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including Uttar Pradesh; and (d) the steps being taken by the Government to provide teacher training to AYUSH academicians to teach in such colleges?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b):    The Govt. has set up 18 new Ayurvedic, Unani, Siddha, Homeopathic and Sowa Rigpa Colleges since 2014. A list of State/UT wise New AYUSH Colleges is at Annexure.(c):     At present one (01) proposal received from Jammu & Kashmir State Govt. and the same is under consideration in the Ministry.(d):      This Ministry runs a Central Sector Scheme namely Continuing Medical Education (CME) since 11th plan. The overall structure of the said Scheme is aimed to encourage AYUSH personnel including teachers to undergo need-based professional training and bridge the knowledge gaps. Under the components of this scheme, one of the main programme is to conduct a ‘6-day subject/ specialty-specific CME programme for AYUSH teachers.’
