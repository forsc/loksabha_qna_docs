                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2510






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Soil Testing Laboratories




Brijbhushan Sharan Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of soil testing laboratories in the country, State-wise; (b) 	the number of soil testing laboratories in Uttar Pradesh, district-wise; (c) 	whether the Government is planning to set up at least one soil testing laboratory in each district of the country; and (d) 	if so, the time by which the same will be set up and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	 As per the information received from the State Governments, there are 7949 soil testing labs including 1293 static Soil Testing Laboratories (STLs), 165 Mobile STLs, 6337 Mini Labs & 154 Village Level STLs in the country. State-wise details are given at  Annexure- I.  (b):	There are 254 soil testing labs (243 static labs & 11 mini labs) in Uttar Pradesh.  The district –wise information is given at Annexure- II. (c) & (d):	The vision document (15th Finance Commission period) of Department of Agriculture, Cooperation and Farmers Welfare (DAC&FW) envisages for establishment of at least one soil testing lab in each district of the country. However, Under Soil Health Management scheme, new soil testing laboratories are sanctioned as per requirement of the State Government after receiving   the proposals duly approved by executive committee.
