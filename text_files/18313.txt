                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2027






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Vendor Development Programmes




Ganesan Selvam













                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                








                                                    C.N. Annadurai
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the criteria fixed by the Government for participating in Vendor Development Programmes (VDPs);(b)	whether the Government has achieved the objectives for which VDPs has been organized, if so, the details thereof and if not, the corrective steps taken in this regard;(c)	the details of VDPs conducted by Government at National and State level along with the outcome thereof during each of the last three years;(d)	the details of the vendors registered by the Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) in acumulative way during the said period; (e)	the details of the initiative taken by the leading PSUs in the country under the VDPs; and(f)	the other steps taken by the Government to provide business opportunities to Micro and Small Enterprises to expand their market base?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OFMICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI  PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a):   Any registered Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs) can participate in Vendor Development Programmes. The Vendor Development Programs (VDPs) are aimed at building the capacity of MSEs and enhancing their participation in public procurement process. (b) :	Yes Sir, the procurement from Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs) by Central Public Sector Undertakings (CPSUs) increased from 23.11% in 2017-18,  26.32% in 2018-19 to 30.95% in 2019-20 and 32.97% in 2020-21 (till 17th  September,2020).(c) & (d): 	The details of Vendor Development Programmes organized by Ministry of MSME and the vendors registered with Central Public Sector Undertakings (CPSUs) as on 17th  September, 2020 are given below.<pre>Year	Vendor Development Programmes (VDPs) by the Government	Vendors registered by CPSUs (as on 17th September, 2020)	State Level VDPs	National Level VDPs	2017-18	384	58	347552018-19	132	04 	2019-20	125	03	 </pre>(e): 	Details of Vendor Development Programmes/ Buyer-Seller meets during the last three years are given below:<pre>Year	General VDPs	SC/ST VDPs2017-18	913	2152018-19	1985	5832019-20 	2045	4652020 -21(till 17th September 2020)	187	22</pre>(f):	Ministry of MSME  provides business opportunities to Micro and Small Enterprises to expand their market base through Procurement and Marketing Support Scheme, Coir Vikas Yojana (CVY), scheme for International Cooperation, Public Procurement Policy for Micro and Small Enterprises  (MSEs)  Order,  2012 and  MSME Global Mart Portal developed by National Small Industries Corporation, a PSU under the Ministry .
