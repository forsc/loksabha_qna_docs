                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

37






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Sahakar Mitra-NCDC Internship Scheme




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the Sahakar Mitra – National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) Internship Scheme;(b)	the list of all the interns who have benefitted from this scheme till date; and(c)	the details of the funds invested by the Government to run this scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COOPERATION???????? ??????  (SHRI AMIT SHAH)(a) to (c):       A statement is laid on the Table of the House.  STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 37 DUE FOR REPLY ON 30TH NOVEMBER, 2021 (a): 	The details of the “SAHAKAR MITRA Scheme on Internship Program” of National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) is enclosed at Annexure-A. (b):	the list of all the interns who have benefitted from this scheme till date is enclosed at Annexure-B.(c):	The said scheme is neither a Government of India scheme nor funded by the Government. It is funded from its own resources by the NCDC.
