                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1293






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Fertilizer Points




Rekha Arun Verma














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is any requirement to increase the number of fertilizer points operated in the State of Uttar Pradesh at present;(b)	whether the Government has conducted any soil testing which being help in formulation and increase of fertilizer production; and (c)	the time by which these fertilizer points are likely to be increased? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS(SHRI MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a):	No Sir. (b)	Soil Health Card Scheme has been introduced in year 2014-15 to assist State Governments to issue soil health cards to all farmers in the country. Soil Health Card provides information to farmers on nutrient status of their soil along with recommendation on appropriate dosage of nutrients to be applied for improving soil health and enhancing productivity & farmer’s income. (i)	10.74 Crores grid based Soil Health Cards (SHCs) distributed to farmers in Cycle-I (2015-2017).(ii)	11.94 Crores grid based Soil Health Cards (SHCs) distributed to farmers in Cycle-II (2017-2019).(iii)	In Model village Programme (2019-20), 18.74 lakh land holding based soil health cards have been distributed to farmers across the country. (c):	In view of (a) above, Question does not arise. Present number of fertilizer points are adequate to meet the requirement. **********
