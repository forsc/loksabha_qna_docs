                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1316






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Fee Hike by CBSE




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of students in secondary and higher secondary levels registeredunder the CBSE curriculum;(b) the rationale behind the CBSE fee hike by two times for students belonging to thegeneral category and twenty-four times for students from the SC/ST category;(c) the reason why students from the latter category have not been exempted fromthis fee hike; and(d) the amount of increase in the funds collected by virtue of this increase along withthe expenditure plan for these added funds?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): The class wise details of the number of students registered under the CentralBoard of Secondary Education (CBSE) curriculum are as under:-<pre>Class IX - 22,41,285Class X - 19,20,651Class XI - 14,13,924Class XII - 12,64,946</pre>(b): The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has increased theexamination fees of class X and XII Board examination 2020, on no profit no lossprinciple, from Rs. 750/- to Rs. 1500/- for all categories of students, including SC/STcandidates for all schools in whole of India, except for schools of Delhi Governments.For 1299 schools of Delhi Government, examination fee for all categories of Class Xstudents has been increased from Rs. 375/- to Rs. 1200/- , which is only 3.2 times andfor Class XII students from Rs. 600/- to Rs. 1200/-, which is only 2 times.(c): CBSE has informed that there has never been a distinction (except in case ofDelhi) between different categories of students in the matter of payment of examinationfees. All categories, including SC, ST, General, OBC, etc., have always paid the samefees.(d): The increased fee has been made applicable for 2020 examination, for whichregistration is still under process, hence, at this moment, exact amount being collectedby virtue of this increased fee cannot be worked out. The examination fee has beenincreased only to meet out the increased expenditure being incurred at present by theBoard.*****
