Revenue Earned by India Post




Bhanu Pratap Singh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Indian Post is progressively earning revenue from non-traditional sectors like e-commerce and payments bank;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the revenue earned by the postal department as a percentage from various value-added services/nontraditional sectors during each of the last three years and the current year;(d) 	the details of future expansion plans proposed by the Government in the above sector/services in the State of Uttar Pradesh; and(e) 	the post office-wise number of postal payment bank accounts in the said State?
