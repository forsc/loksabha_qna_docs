                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

866






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Budget Allocation for Education




E. T. Mohammed Basheer














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to take steps for budget allocation and non-budgetary income generation in the education system; and(b) if so, the details thereof?	






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) and (b) : The budget allocation is done on annual basis by Ministry of Finance depending upon the demand, absorption capacity, availability of resources and after due appropriation by Parliament. Ministry of Education further allocates the budget to various Schemes and Non-Schemes/Autonomous Bodies as per the allocation made by Ministry of Finance. In addition to Budgetary Allocation, institutions are also generating income by way of charging academic fee/tuition fee etc, which is spent by them from time to time as per their need. The proposed budget allocation in respect of Ministry of Education (Department of Higher Education & Department of School Education & Literacy) for the Financial Year 2022-23 is as under :-<pre>                       Rs. In CroreMinistry of Education	BE 2022-23 (proposed)Department of Higher Education	40828.35Department of School Education & Literacy	63449.37</pre>****
