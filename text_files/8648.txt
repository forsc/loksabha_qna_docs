                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2142






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Selection Scale for Kendriya Vidyalaya Employees




Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) grants senior/selectionscale to their employees;(b) if so, the details of eligible Librarians under KVS, Delhi Region during thecurrent year;(c) whether there has been cases of delay in granting of senior/selection scale toKVS employees;(d) if so, whether the Departmental Promotion Committee has been constitutedfor screening for grant of senior scale for post of Librarians in KVS DelhiRegion;(e) if so, the details thereof; and(f) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to ensure timely grant ofsenior/selection scale to KVS employees?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Yes, Sir. The senior scale is granted to the teaching staff of KendriyaVidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) after completion of 12 years of regular service in thegrade subject to fulfillment of other required conditions. Selection scale is alsogranted after completing 12 years of service in senior scale of respective cadre.However, for grant of the selection scale, a provision of 20% posts in the seniorscale of the respective cadre has been made.(b) KVS has informed that there is no Librarian in KVS, Delhi Region, whosesenior scale is due before 31.12.2019. However, the matter for granting of seniorscale to Ms. Kalpna Pal (KV, Sainik Vihar) & Shri Mukesh Sharma (KV, AshokVihar), Librarians was deferred for the next Departmental Promotion Committee(DPC) due to their not attending 21 days mandatory in-service course before the duedate.(c) No such case has been brought to the notice of the Government.(d) & (e) A DPC with the following composition has been constituted in the DelhiRegion, KVS to consider the cases of eligible teachers of all categories (includingLibrarians) for grant of senior scale:<pre>Deputy Commissioner ChairpersonAssistant Commissioner MemberAdministrative Officer MemberFinance Officer MemberSection Officer Member Secretary</pre>(f) To ensure timely grant of senior scale / selection scale to the employees asper their seniority and eligibility, KVS has informed that the DPC meetings are heldtwice a year. The last such meeting was held on 23.10.2019.*****
