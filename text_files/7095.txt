                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

863






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Utilising Land Banks of PSEs for Housing




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has taken a decision to use land parcels belonging to sick and closing Public Sector Enterprises (PSEs) for the construction of housing schemes including BSNL land bank;(b)	if so, the details of such land parcels identified so far and their value as on date with the details of names of PSUs and cities where these are available;(c)	the details of funding mode decided for investment in these housing schemes; and(d)	if not, the timeline by which is will be finalised?






ANSWER




(a) to (d)	At present, no such decision has been taken by the Government.                                *******
