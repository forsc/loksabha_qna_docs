                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2686






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Swami Vivekanand National Institute of Rehabilitation Training and Research




 Rahul Ramesh Shewale














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Post Graduation Master in Physiotherapy courses in Rehabilitation, Neuro, Pediatrics, Muscular Skeletal Rehabilitation are being taught in Swami Vivekanand National Institute of Rehabilitation Training and Research (SVNIRTAR), Cuttack;(b)  if so, the details thereof along with the number of students awarded Post Graduation Degree in the said courses by SVNIRTAR during each of the last three years and current year;(c) the specialized faculty in SVNIRTAR to teach the said PG courses/ to conduct Seminars along with the number of classes/seminars taken by such faculty of respective categories;(d) the name of thesis guide of the said PG Courses;(e) whether a specialized course of Master in Physiotherapy in Cardiovascular Rehabilitation can teach the said PG courses run by SVNIRTAR, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor, and (f) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to present ongoing irregularities in MPT Courses in SVNIRTAR?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR)(a)  Yes, Sir. (b) Number of students awarded Post Graduation Degree in Master of Physiotherapy (MPT).2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20MPT Final Year Students admitted during 2017 will appear University Examination in 201916	10	16	15(c) Associate Professor in Physiotherapy, Assistant Professor in Physiotherapy, Lecturer in Physiotherapy, Lecturer in Prosthetics and Orthotics, Senior Physiotherapist-cum-Junior Lecturer, Demonstrator in Physiotherapy in SVNIRTAR, faculty of Department of Neurology and Department of Pediatrics, SCB Medical College, Cuttack and Guest faculties are specialized faculties, who teach/ conduct Seminars for Master of Physiotherapy Course. Number of classes/ seminars taken by the faculty is about 15 hours in a week.(d) During the year 2016 to 2018, 14 thesis submitted out of which 09 thesis are guided by Mrs. Monalisha Patnaik and 05 by Dr. P.P. Mohanty.(e) Master of Physiotherapy in Cardiovascular Rehabilitation can teach common topics such as Practice Issues in Physiotherapy (Paper-1), Bio-mechanics (Paper-1), Research Methodology (Paper-1), Cadio Respiratory (Paper-2) except the specialized courses of Neurology, Pediatrics, Muscle Skeletal and Rehabilitation.(f) As on date, no irregularity has been reported.  ****
