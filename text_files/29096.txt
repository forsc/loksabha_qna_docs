                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1075






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Jan Aushadhi Stores in Rajasthan




Sukhbir Singh Jaunapuria














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state: (a) the number of Jan Aushadhi Generic Drug Stores in Rajasthan; (b) the details of such stores in backward and SC/ ST/OBC dominated areas in Rajasthan, location-wise; (c) whether any requests have been made for opening up more drug stores in the State; and (d) if so, the details and the current status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS                                     (DR. MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a)  As on 28.11.2021, there are 137 Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendras (PMBJKs) functional in the State of Rajasthan.(b) Out of these 137 PMBJKs, eight stores are functioning in aspirational districts identified by the NITI Aayog, as under: Sl. No.	Name of the District	Number of PMBJK functional1	Baran	12	Dholpur	13	Jaisalmer	14	Karauli	15	Sirohi	26	Barmer	2Total	8(c) & (d) Department of Pharmaceuticals as well as Pharmaceutical & Medical Devices Bureau (PMBI), the implementing agency for PMBJP scheme regularly writes to all the State Governments, including the State of Rajasthan, for opening of more stores under the scheme.  Recently, in July, 2021, PMBI has released advertisements for inviting applications for opening of stores is 265 districts in the country, including 19 districts in Rajasthan. *****
