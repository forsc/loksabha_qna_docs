                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

134






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.09.2020






2G Ethanol Plants




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has approved to set up new 2G ethanol plantsusing paddy straw especially in the State of Maharashtra and if so, the detailsand the status of the projects thereof; and(b) the number of companies that have applied till date to set up these plantsalong with such plants that have been granted clearances till date, State/UTwise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (b): Government has allowed procurement of ethanol produced fromother non feed stock besides molasses like cellulosic and lignocellulosicmaterial including petrochemical route. In furtherance of this decision,companies are setting up 2G ethanol plants based on various feed stocksincluding paddy straw in the country. The 2G ethanol plants under constructionwith paddy straw as feed-stock are at Panipat (Haryana) by Indian OilCorporation Limited (IOCL), at Bathinda (Punjab) by Hindustan PetroleumCorporation Limited (HPCL) and at Bargarh (Odisha) by Bharat PetroleumCorporation Limited (BPCL).****
