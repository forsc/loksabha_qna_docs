                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3404






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






International Yoga Day




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government proposes to organize mega events on the occasion of International Yoga Day; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government proposes to reserve parks in various cities and towns for practice of yoga to promote yoga across the country; and (d) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b):     Ministry of AYUSH is the nodal Ministry for the observation of International Day of Yoga (IDY). The Ministry coordinates with various stakeholding institutions for the organising of different events and activities on the occasion of IDY on 21st June every year. In addition, the Ministry also organises a few events and activities directly. This year the Ministry will organise the main national event of IDY which involves a harmonious mass Yoga demonstration at Leh.(c) & (d):    There is no such proposal currently with this Ministry.………………….
