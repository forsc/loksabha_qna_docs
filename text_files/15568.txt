                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4268






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Airports in Kuppam and Madanapalle




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government plans to approve proposals received to construct and develop airports in Kuppam and Madanapalle in Chittoor district;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) the present status of construction and operationalization of the Kuppam greenfield airstrip approved by the Union Government in 2018; and(d) the details of the funds allocated, released and utilized so far for the Kuppam airstrip, year and headwise?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Presently, Government of India, Ministry of Civil Aviation has not received any proposal from the State Government for development of airports at Kuppam and Madanapalle in Chittoor district as per Greenfield Airport Policy. However, Government of Andhra Pradesh (GoAP) had earlier submitted proposal for development of greenfield airport at Kuppam in Chittoor district but subsequently withdrew the proposal. ;(c) & (d): State Government has an Administrative Airstrip at Kuppam but have not taken up its construction work. However, GoAP had allocated an amount of Rs. 20 crore towards land acquisition out of which Rs.16.34 crore has been released and utilized. In addition, an amount of Rs. 60.00 lakhs was also allocated by  the State Government towards development works at Kuppam airstrip which was not utilized.
