                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3340






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Coking Coal




Sushil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total expenditure of Steel Authority of India Ltd. (SAIL) on imported coking coal, coking coal from the domestic sources and coal from the captive mines of SAIL separately, from the years 2015 to 2019, year-wise;(b)	the details of both the domestic contractors and the contractors from whom the coking coal was imported from the year 2015 till date including the amount quoted in L-1 bid, year-wise;(c)	the list of all the participating entities/contractors companies along with the amount quoted by these contractors;(d)	whether any of these contracts cancelled by SAIL; and(e)	if so, the details of all such cancelled contracts along with the reasons for cancellation?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)Year-wise expenditure on imported coal including PCI Coal, Domestic Coking Coal and Coal from SAIL Mines for the year  2015-16 to 2018-19 is given below:-(Rs. in Crores)Year	Imported Coal including PCI*	Domestic  Coal Expenditure ( Ex Washery/Mines)		Coking Coal from CIL	Raw Coking Coal from CIL	Coking Coal from Captive2015-16	10448	814.48	197.93	339.292016-17	13871	1276.08	306.05	360.332017-18	20543	1054.13	342.45	523.502018-19	23704	793.01	94.98	468.03*Landed Cost(b)&(c):	Since 2015-16, SAIL has procured Imported Coking Coal through Long Term Agreements / EOI.  Supplier of imported Coking Coal and Domestic Coal from Coal India Limited (CIL) for the year 2015-16 to 2019-20 are as below: -Year	Supplier for Domestic  Coal	Supplier for Imported  Coal2015-16	Coal India Limited(BCCL/CCL/WCL)	M/s BHP Australia, M/s South 32 Singapore, M/s Anglo American Australia, M/s Peabody Australia, M/s Alpha Coal USA, M/s L&K USA, M/s Solid Energy New Zealand, M/s ICVL Mauritius2016-17	Coal India Limited(BCCL/CCL/WCL)	M/s BHP Australia, M/s South 32 Singapore, M/s Anglo American Australia, M/s Peabody Australia, M/s Alpha Coal USA, M/s L&K USA, M/s Solid Energy New Zealand, M/s ICVL Mauritius, M/s Teck Coal Canada2017-18	Coal India Limited(BCCL/CCL)	M/s BHP Australia, M/s South 32 Singapore, M/s Anglo American Australia, M/s Peabody Australia, M/s Contura USA, M/s L&K USA, M/s Solid Energy New Zealand, M/s ICVL Mauritius, M/s Teck Coal Canada, M/s AKT Indonesia, M/s Vale Mozambique, M/s Kestrel Australia,2018-19	Coal India Limited(BCCL/CCL)	M/s BHP Australia, M/s South 32 Singapore, M/s Anglo American Australia, M/s Peabody Australia, M/s Contura USA, M/s L&K USA, M/s ICVL Mauritius, M/s Teck Coal Canada, M/s AKT Indonesia,  M/s Kestrel Australia,2019-20	Coal India Limited(BCCL/CCL)	M/s BHP Australia, M/s South 32 Singapore, M/s Anglo American Australia, M/s Peabody Australia, M/s Contura USA, M/s L&K USA, M/s ICVL Mauritius, M/s Teck Coal Canada, M/s AKT Indonesia,  M/s Kestrel Australia, (d)&(e):	One trial order for the supply of Coking Coal of US origin  has been cancelled due to non-conformity to the specified quality  parameters.
