                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1706






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd.




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that the Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd.(BHEL) is planning to expand its business in International market through securing its maiden export orders from the countries of Chile and Estonia;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the value of the maiden export orders from the countries of Chile and Estonia, product-wise;(d) whether any Memorandum of Understanding(MoU) has been signed with these countries; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI ARVIND GANPAT SAWANT)(a) to (c): Yes Sir. Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) is continuously exploring business opportunities in the international markets to promote its various products and services. 	The details of maiden export orders secured by BHEL from Chile and Estonia are as under:(i)	Transformer bushings order of value approx. Rs. 29 lakh from Niquel Electric Ltd., Chile in November, 2016.(ii)	Electronic cards order of value approx. Rs. 2 lakh from SCANFIL OY VanaSauga, Estonia in February, 2017.         The above orders have also been successfully executed by BHEL.(d) : No Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) has been signed by BHEL with Chile and Estonia.(e) : Not Applicable in view of (d) above.*****
