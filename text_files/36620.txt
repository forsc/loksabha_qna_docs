                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3732






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Exercise Vayu Sakti




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy













                                                    Manne Srinivas Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Indian Air Force has postponed the Exercise Vayu Shakti 2022;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor;(c) whether the Government has also postponed Defence Expo this year and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor; and(d) the details of such defence programmes cancelled/postponed since 2014 along with the reasons for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)      IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE(a) & (b):  Yes, Sir.  Exercise Vayu Shakti-22 has been re-scheduled for a later date due to the current developing situation.(c):  Yes, Sir.  Due to logistics problems being experienced by participants, the 12th edition of Defence Expo 2022 proposed to be held in Gandhinagar, Gujarat from March 10th till March 14th has been postponed.(d):  Defence Expo 2022 is the only Defence Exhibition which has been postponed since 2014.*****
