                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2339






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Private Participation in FPIs




Y. S. Avinash Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Food Processing Industries (FPIs) set up under private sector in the State of Andhra Pradesh during the last two years; and(b)	the details thereof, district-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a) & (b): Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) has been implementing a central sector umbrella scheme- Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) for overall growth and development of food processing sector of the country including of Andhra Pradesh. Under component schemes of PMKSY, MoFPI provides mostly credit linked financial assistance (capital subsidy) in the form of grants-inaid to entrepreneurs for setting up of food processing / preservation industries. PMKSY is not any region or state specific but demand driven and is implemented across the country including Andhra Pradesh. Since 2019-20, Ministry has approved a total of 20 projects under component schemes of PMKSY in Andhra Pradesh. The district wise details of projects are placed at Annexure. *****
