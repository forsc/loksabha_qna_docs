                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1672






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Contract Labourers working in ONGC




M. Selvaraj














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that a huge number of contract labourers are working in ONGC in various Cauvery basin project if so, the details thereof;(b) whether there is any formulation of plan to convert their employment of contract labour to permanent job; and(c) the benefits being proposed on retirement of these contract labourers?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (c): One thousand fifty one (1051) contract labourers are working in various Cauvery Basin projects of ONGC. Contract labourers have been employed under various contracts awarded by ONGC to contractor/vendor companies. Benefits are provided as per statutory provisions and policies/rules of respective contractor/vendor companies.****
