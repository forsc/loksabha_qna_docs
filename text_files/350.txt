                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

420






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






NCERT Books




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has made it mandatory for all CBSE schools in thecountry to use NCERT books;(b) if so, the details and the objectives thereof;(c) whether the Government has made arrangements for online orders of NCERTbooks;(d) if so, the details thereof and the modalities fixed therefor; and(e) the steps taken/to be taken by the Government to ensure easy availability ofNCERT books in the remote areas of the country?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) and (b): The Central Board of Secondary Education has informed that the affiliationbyelaws of CBSE stipulates that the School may prescribe NCERT text books in theclasses and subjects in which these have been published by the NCERT. It has beenalso highlighted that extreme care should be taken in the selection of books of privatepublishers so that there is no objectionable content that hurts the feeling of any class,community, gender or any religious group in society.(c) and (d): Yes Sir, the detailed information can be accessed on the web portal ofNCERT (https://ncertbooks.ncert.gov.in).(e): The following steps have been taken by NCERT:-? NCERT has empanelled 895 vendors across the country for distribution ofNCERT books? For timely printing of books, NCERT has empanelled 140 printers.? NCERT books are also being distributed through its sales counters located atNCERT headquarter, New Delhi, regional centres at Ahmedabad, Bangalore,Kolkata and Guwahati, also from the sales counters located at its RIE''s at Ajmer,Bhopal, Bhubaneswar, Mysore and NERIE (Shillong).? NCERT books are also being supplied through online postal services at the doorsteps of the customers? All NCERT books are made available on its website www.ncert.nic.in for freedownload across the country.? NCERT books are also available on e-pathshala mobile app.***
