                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4911






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






PMS to SC Students




Ramesh Chander Kaushik













                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether budget allocation is presently being provided by the Government to the States on the basis of committed liability formula under the Scheme of Post Matric Scholarships to the Students belonging to Scheduled Castes; (b) if so, the details in this regard; (c) whether the Government proposes to provide Post Matric Scholarships to the students belonging to Scheduled Castes at par with scholarships provided to the students belonging to Scheduled Tribes; and (d) if so, the time-frame set in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. Under the Post Matric Scholarship Scheme for Scheduled Caste Students, Central Assistance is released to States/UTs for the total expenditure under the scheme, over and above their Committed Liability. As per the existing guidelines, the highest annual demand during the previous Plan Period/Finance Commission Cycle of the State/UT has been considered as the Committed Liability of the State for the next Finance Commission Cycle.(c): The rates of Scholarship under Post Matric Scholarship Scheme to the students belonging to Scheduled Castes are at par with Scholarship rates under the Post Matric Scholarship for Scheduled Tribes students.(d): Does not arise in view of reply at (c) above.****
