                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2820






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Maintenance of NH-44




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government / NHAI is aware of the dilapidated condition of the Borkhedi- Jam- Wadner section on National Highway (NH)-44 in the State of Maharashtra; (b)	if so, the steps taken/being taken by the Government / NHAI to repair and maintain that section; and (c)	the funds allocated and released for the purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) Borkhedi – Jam – Wadner section of NH-44 is in traffic worthy condition and at present is in O&M stage. For periodic renewal of Borkhedi – Jam – Wadner section, work has been awarded on 27.07.2021 for which an amount of Rs. 64.85 crore has been allocated. *****
