                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

893






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Accidents on Delhi-Alwar NH-248




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government is aware that hundreds of accidents occur in a month on Delhi to Alwar via Sohna-Nuh (NH-248) from Nuh to Rajasthan border due to double lane and heavy local traffic; (b)   if so, whether there is any project to convert it to a four lane; and (c)    if so, the details thereof and the time by which it is likely to be done? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	The road stretch Nuh to Rajasthan border is part of Section Sohna-Rajasthan border of NH to 248 A which has been entrusted to National Highway Authority of India (NHAI) since May,2017. The Ministry collects data/information in the perscibed format from the Police Department of all States/UTs on total number of persons killed and injured in road accidents in the country and on National Highways. (b)	&  (c )  At present, there is  no  proposal to convert the Nuh to Rajasthan Section of Delhi-Alwar  Road (NH-248A) to four lane.*****
