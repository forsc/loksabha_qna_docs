                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

798






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Airport in Bihar




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to build an airport in Chhapra, Siwan or nearby place in Bihar;(b) if so, the details thereof and the time by which such airport is likely to be built;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the policy regarding building airports in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c): Government of India, Ministry of Civil Aviation has not received any proposal for development of airport in Chhapra, Siwan or nearby place in Bihar as per Greenfield Airport Policy. However, Chhapra airstrip in Saran district of Bihar belongs to State Government of Bihar and Chhapra airstrip is included in the list of Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) -UDAN, but no bid has been received in RCS-UDAN bidding round 1, 2, 3 and 3.1 for this airport.The airport is also included for bidding in RCS- UDAN round 4. (d): Government of India has notified a Greenfield Airport Policy in 2008 for development of greenfield airports across the country. The Policy permits a State Government, airport company or individual developer to establish a greenfield airport after obtaining ''site clearance'' and ''in principle'' approval from Ministry of Civil Aviation. For the purpose, the applicant proposing to set up an airport is required to make an application to the Steering Committee for the first stage clearance i.e. ''site clearance'' followed by second stage clearance i.e. ''in principle'' approval in the prescribed proforma.
