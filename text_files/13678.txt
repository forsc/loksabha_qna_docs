                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2381






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Metro Rail between Hosur and Bengaluru




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Union Government has any proposal to start a metro rail between Hosur (Tamil Nadu) to Bengaluru (Karnataka);(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if no, whether such proposal will be considered to start the metro rail which will be useful to both the border cities to reduce travel time and boost the economy of both the States; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                        (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	No, Sir.(b)	Does not arise. (c)	Urban Transport including Metro Rail is an integral part of urban development, which is a State Subject. Hence, Metro Rail Projects are initiated/proposed by the concerned State Governments. No such proposal has been received either from Government of Tamil Nadu or Government of Karnataka. The proposals for metro rail projects received from the State Governments are considered under the ambit of Metro Rail Policy, 2017, depending on the viability of the project and availability of resources.(d) 	Does not arise.                                  *****
