                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5266






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






KV in Telangana




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) in Telangana;(b) the total strength of staff and students in the Kendriya Vidyalayas in Telangana;(c) the total number and strength of staff and students in Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs) in Telangana; (d) whether there are any infrastructure shortcomings in the KVs and JNVs in Telangana; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) to (c) At present, 35 Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) and 9 Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs) are functioning in the State of Telangana. The total staff strength (sanctioned / in position) and students enrollment in KVs and JNVs in the State of Telangana are as under:- <pre>	KVs	JNVsStaff Sanctioned	1610	394Staff in position	843	318Students enrollment 	39418	4598</pre> (d) & (e) All the JNVs in the State of Telangana are functioning from its permanent building constructed by Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti. Out of 35 KVs in the State of Telangana 9 KVs are functioning in temporary accommodation provided by the sponsoring authority as per norms of Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS). Construction of permanent buildings for KVs is a continuous process, which depends upon identification of suitable land, completion of lease formalities in favour of KVS by the sponsoring authorities, submission of drawings / estimates by construction agency, availability of funds and requisite approvals etc. *****
