                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4396






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Automatic Weather Stations in North East




Kripanath Mallah














                        Will the Minister of





EARTH SCIENCES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any modern automatic weather stations have been set up in the North Eastern-States and Assam; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has taken any steps to link these weather stations with micro insurance programmes for farmers, if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether there is any Government programme to understand the nature of monsoon pattern in these areas; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER
