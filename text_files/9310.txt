                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2823






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Road Projects in Karnataka




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Union Government has received proposals from the Government of Karnataka for declaration of State Highways (SHs) as National Highways (NHs) during the last four years and if so, the details thereof, year-wise;(b) 	the details of new NHs declared during each of the last four years and the current year in Karnataka;(c) 	the present status of Bharatmala Pariyojana in Karnataka, project-wise;(d) 	the present status of NH 173 Chitradurga to Mangalore, NH 17 Goa border to Kundapur, NH 275 Bangalore to Mysore and NH 206 Tumkur to Shimoga ;and(e) 	the present status of conversion of SHs as new NHs namely, Sringeri to Nadipur and Kundapur to Sringeri Road, Karkala to BC Road and BC Road to Mulki Road?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)	26 Proposals for declaration of about 4092 Km of State Highways as new NHs have been received from the State Government of Karnataka during the last 4 years.  Year-wise details of NHs declared during each of the last 4 years & current year for the State of Karnataka are as follows:Year	Length of NHs  notified (in Km.)2015-16	702016-17	2592017-18	8892018-19	1322019-20 (till Nov-2019)	Nil(c)	Project-wise present status of Bharatmala Pariyojana in Karnataka are at Annexure – I.(d)	 The present status of NH 173Chitradurga to Mangalore, NH 17 Goa border to Kundapur, NH 275 Bangalore to Mysore and NH 206 Tumkur to Shimoga are at Annexure – II.(e)    All four roads i.e  Sringeri- Nadipur, Kundapur- Sringeri  Road, Karkala-BC Road and BC Road- Mulki Road, are at DPR stage and their declaration as new NHs depend upon final out come of DPR, inter-se priority and availability of funds.
