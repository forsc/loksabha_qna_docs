                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3082






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






PNG Supply in Andhra Pradesh




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that Geographical Area of Krishna district has been given under PNGRB Act to provide piped natural gas in Krishna district of Andhra Pradesh and if so, the details thereof; and(b) the status of providing piped natural gas to the households in the above district?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b) Establishment of CNG Stations and providing PNG connections is part of the development of City Gas Distribution (CGD) network and the same is carried out by the entities authorized by Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB). PNGRB has authorized Vijayawada Geographical Area (GA) in Krishna district to Bhagyanagar Gas Limited (BGL) and Krishna district (excluding area already authorized) to Megha Engineering & Infrastructure Limited (MEIL) for development of CGD networks. As on 31.10.2021, total 59,874 households have been provided piped natural gas connections in Krishna district in Andhra Pradesh.*****
