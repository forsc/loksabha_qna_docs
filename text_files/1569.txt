                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1518






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Expand Intake of Seats in Universities




Sudheer Gupta













                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the University of Delhi is set to increase its student intake by 10 per cent for the comingacademic session following the implementation of EWS quota and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether this additional intake of students will increase the already existing campusaccommodation crunch for the new students coming to University of Delhi;(c) if so, whether the colleges under the University of Delhi have acute shortage of hostels for thestudents coming from different parts of the country; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government to increase the number ofhostels/construct new hostels for the students of University of Delhi?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ''Nishank'')(a) to(d) : Yes Sir. University of Delhi has informed that it has decided to implement the reservationpolicy for Economically Weaker Sections (EWSs) and increase its student intake by 10 percent foradmission to various Under Graduate/Post Graduate courses of the University from the academicsession 2019-20.The University has also decided to increase the hostel accommodation and in this regard the BuildingCommittee of the University has recommended the following proposals:i) Construction of Girls hostel at Dhaka Complex,ii) Construction of 400 seated hostel building at South Delhi Campus,iii) Construction of Research Hostel building at South Delhi Campus,*****
