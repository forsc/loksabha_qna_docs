                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2218






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Violation of FRA in Mining Project




Anubhav Mohanty













                                                    Hibi Eden
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has observed any violation of Forest Rights Act, 2006 at the ongoing mining projects at Hasdeo Arand Forests; and(b) if so, the details thereof and measures initiated by the Government in this regard; and(c) whether the Government proposes to amend the Forest Rights Act; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)  As per information received from the State Government, who, as per Forest Rights Act, 2006, is responsible for implementation of various provisions of the Act, no violation of Forest Rights Act, 2006 at the ongoing mining projects at Hasdeo Arand Forests has been observed.(b)  Does not arise.(c)  There is no proposal to amend the Forest Right Act.(d)  Does not arise.
