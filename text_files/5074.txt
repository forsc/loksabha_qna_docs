                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4745






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Closure of Industries




Sunil Kumar Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the details of the number of labourers who reportedly lost their jobs due to closure of various industries in the country; and (b)the details thereof during the last three years and the current year, State/ UT-wise including West Bengal?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b):	Labour Bureau brings out Statistics on “Industrial Disputes, Closures, Retrenchments and Lay-offs in India” based on the voluntary returns received every month from the Labour Departments of the States and the Regional Labour Commissioner (Central). The State/UT-wise information on labourers affected due to closure for the years 2016 to 2019 based on the returns / clarifications received in the Labour Bureau till 1st July, 2019 is at Annexure. *                                                      ******
