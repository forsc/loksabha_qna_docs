                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1456






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






National Horticulture Mission




Diya Kumari













                                                    Dharmendra Kumar Kashyap
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of districts, State-wise across the country covered under National Horticulture Mission (NHM); (b) whether the Government proposes to expand the said NHM to rest of the districts too, if so, the details thereof, State-wise, including Rajasamand and any other districts of Rajasthan under the NHM in the next phase; (c) whether representations have been received from several States for inclusion of more districts under the said Mission; (d) if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon; (e) the amount sanctioned and utilized by each State under the NHM during the last three years; and (f) the details of accomplishments of NHM during the last three years, State-wise, including Rajasthan? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????  (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) State-wise details of the total number of districts covered under National Horticulture Mission (NHM) is at Annexure-I.(b) to (d) Since launch of MIDH scheme in 2014-15, several representations have been received from different States for inclusion of more districts under the said Mission. There already exists a provision under MIDH scheme for setting up of infrastructure units relating to post harvest management, marketing and other project based activities in non MIDH districts also, provided there is a clear linkage of the units with the horticulture produce. 		Apart from this, activities for development of horticulture in non-MIDH districts can be carried out under other Central Government schemes viz. Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY), Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA), Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) etc.(e) Details of the amount sanctioned and utilized by each State under the NHM during the last three years are placed at Annexure-II.(f) Details of accomplishments of NHM during the last three years, State-wise, including Rajasthan is at Annexure-III.
