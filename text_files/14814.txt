                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3601






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Tamanna Aptitude Test




Kuruva Gorantla Madhav













                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the steps taken by the Government to ensure the accessibility of recentlylaunched Tamanna Aptitude Test for Ninth and Tenth class students in everyschool in the country;(b) the amount of funds the Government has allocated for its implementation; and(c) the targets of the said initiative launched in various States including AndhraPradesh for the next three years?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and the National Council ofEducational Research & Training (NCERT) have developed TAMANNA (Try andMeasure Aptitude and Natural Abilities) - An aptitude test for Senior School Students toenable stakeholders to know the aptitude of students of Class IX & Class X. The detailsabout use of aptitude test, dimensions measured in the test, construction andstandardization of the test, administration and scoring and understanding the meaningof aptitude test scores are available in the test manual. The test booklet and manual hasbeen made available on the DIKSHA portal of Ministry of Human ResourceDevelopment (MHRD) as well as on the websites of CBSE and NCERT.(b): No funds have been allocated by the Central Government for this purpose.(c): Tamanna Aptitude Test is optional for use by schools and respective StateGovernments to benefit the students.*****
