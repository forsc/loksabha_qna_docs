                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3354






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Renovation of Temples/Namghars in Assam




Queen Oja














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has identified the temples/’Namghars’ coming under the Archaeological Department situated in Assam State which are to be renovated;(b)	if so, the details thereof for Assam State including Guwahati;(c)	whether the Government has earmarked funds for renovation of temples/Namghars; and(d)	if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)    &   (b) Conservation work of protected monuments including  temples/’Namghars’ in Assam State is a continuous process wherein the essential conservation work is attended as  per the requirements of different sites and availability of resources. The details of protected temples in Assam are given in annexure. (c)   &   (d) The funds allocated for conservation of protected monuments under Guwahati Circle of Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) including for temples in Assam for the current financial year 2019-20 are Rs. 2.90 Crores.ANNEXURE ANNEXURE  REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) & (b) OF THE  LOK  SABHA  UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 3354 FOR 09.12.2019List of the protected temples under Guwahati Circle of ASI in AssamSl.No.	Name of Monument	Locality	Distrct1	Shri Shri Hayagriva Madhava Temple	Hajo	Kamrup2	Shri Shri Kedar Temple	Hajo	Kamrup3	Shri Shri Ganesh Temple	Hajo	Kamrup4	Shri Shri Kameswar Temple	Hajo	Kamrup5	Bishnu Dole	Sivasagar	Sivasagar6	Devi Dole	Sivasagar	Sivasagar7	Siva Dole	Sivasagar	Sivasagar8	Bisnhudol	Gaurisagar	Sivasagar9	Sivadol	Gaurisagar	Sivasagar10	Devidol	Gaurisagar	Sivasagar11	Bishnudol	Jaysagar	Sivasagar12	Devidol	Jaysagar	Sivasagar13	Sivadol	Jaysagar	Sivasagar14	Ranganath Dole, Sivasagar	Sivasagar	Sivasagar15	Rock-Cut Temple	Maibong	Dima-Hasao16	Sivadol	Negheritting	Golaghat17	Bordol Temple	Biswanath	Biswanath18	Dhandi Temple	N.C.
