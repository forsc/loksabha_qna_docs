                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1128






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Anganwadi Centres in Tribal Sub-Plan




Ajay Nishad














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    the number of Anganwadi centres being run in Tribal Sub-Plan (TSP) areas in the country, State-wise;(b) 	the number of beneficiaries therein, State-wise;(c) 	the fund sanctioned, released to States and utilized by them during each of the last three years and the current year;(d) 	whether the Government proposes to set up Anganwadi centres under TSP areas; and(e)  	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	& (b) The total number of Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) in Tribal Sub-Plan areas reporting on ICDS-RRS portal is 1,34,806 with 69,14,204 registered beneficiaries. State-wise details of number of beneficiaries in these AWCs is Annexed.(c)	Year-wise funds sanctioned/released to States/UTs and utilized by them under Anganwadi Services Scheme including the Anganwadi Centres being run in Tribal Sub-Plan areas during the last three years and current year is as under.(Rupees in Lakh)Year	Amount Sanctioned/Released	Amount Utilized2016-17	1442970.00	1252709.832017-18	1509431.95	1213863.752018-19	1675018.08	749081.76*2019-20	1400306.32	-** *	UCs/SoEs are still awaited from some of Stats/UTs**     No UCs/SoEs are received for 2019-20(d)	& (e) The Government has sanctioned 14 lakhs AWCs across the country.
