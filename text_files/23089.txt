                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4196






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






NGT Guidelines for Setting up Petrol Pumps




Devusinh Jesingbhai Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether National Green Tribunal (NGT) has issued any guidelines for setting up of new Petrol Pumps in India, if so, the details thereof; (b)	 whether it is implemented throughout India; and (c) 	the course of action taken by the Ministry in the case of violation of the NGT guidelines by the Oil Marketing companies?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)	to (c)	CPCB in compliance of Hon’ble NGT order, issued guidelines for setting up of new petrol pumps vide their OM dated 07.01.2020 which is applicable pan India. However, these guidelines are not applicable for those new petrol pumps where prior clearance/ initial approval/construction were initiated/ started prior to 7th January, 2020. Oil Marketing companies (OMCs) commission any RO only after receipt of NOC from concerned DM and PESO.  *****
