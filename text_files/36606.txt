                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3638






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Targets for Construction of National Highways




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the targets fixed by the Government for construction of National Highways (NHs) in the country;(b) the pace at which these targets are being achieved every year;(c) the number of road projects which could not be completed within the stipulated period of time, State wise; and(d) the number of new proposals for NH projects received by the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) Development of National Highways (NHs) is a continuous process as per requirement of connectivity, inter-se priority, traffic density and available resources. Ministry envisaged to construct about 60,000 Km of National Highways during the five year period from 2019-20 to 2023-24, of which 31,609 Km has been constructed up to February-2022 and the balance length of about 28,391 Km is planned to be constructed in the remaining period from March-2022 to the end of the financial year 2023-24. (c) Time for completion of project is generally kept as 2-3 years from the date of award. Out of the sanctioned National Highway Projects in the country, the details of delayed projects as on Feb-2022, is at Annexure-I. (d) Ministry has received 582 new proposals for NH Projects from the States/UTs during the current financial year up to February-2022.
