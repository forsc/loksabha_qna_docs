                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4246






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Complaints of Workers and Staff ofIndian Airlines




Sunil Kumar Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether  the  Government  has  received  any complaints regarding salary, work schedule etc. on behalf of Indian Airlines workers and staff; and(b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT/Airport-wise along with the action taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) and (b):  Yes, Sir. Representations on various issues including salary, work schedule etc are received from time to time which are generally addressed by Air India for arriving at an amicable solution.  A joint representation has recently been received from Indian Commercial Pilots Association (ICPA), Indian Pilots Guild (IPG), Air Corporations Employees Union (ACEU), All India Cabin Crew Association (AICCA), Indian Aircraft Technicians Association(IATA) Air India Employees Union (AIEU) and All India Aircraft Engineers Association  (AIAEA) inter-alia, raising issues related to disinvestment/ privatisation of Air India and suggesting ways to improve/address operational/financial concerns of Air India.  The concerns/interests of the employees of Air India will be safeguarded in accordance with the guidelines of Department of Investment and Public Asset Management, Ministry of Finance on strategic disinvestment of CPSEs and same will be reflected in the Share Purchase Agreement to be signed with new owner.So far as the payment of salaries and allowances are concerned, there have been slight delays in payment. However, the salaries are being paid to all employees including pilots of Air India on a regular monthly basis.
