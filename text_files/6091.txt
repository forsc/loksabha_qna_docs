                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5568






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Land Covered under Afforestation




C.N. Annadurai














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total land in hectares covered under afforestation by the National Afforestation and Eco-Development Board under National Afforestation Programme since its inception till Financial Year 2018-19 along with the total expenditure incurred in this regard, State/UT-wise; (b) the status of activities undertaken by the Board for rejuvenation of degraded forest areas in Jawadhu Hill in Thiruvannamalai and Vellore Districts along with the expenditure incurred thereon; (c) whether any plan/proposal is under contemplation of the Government to plant trees/medicinal/herb-plants on more than 10,000 acres of degraded forest land at Melchangam in Thiruvannamalai constituency; and (d) if so, the details of the proposal and time-line fixed for the implementation of the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	National Afforestation Programme (NAP) is implemented for afforestation in degraded forest areas with participatory approach by National Afforestation and Eco-Development Board of MoEF&CC.State-wise details of the total land area sanctioned for new afforestation and total amount released under NAP since inception (2000-02) till 2018-19 is given in Annexure. (b)	The NAP scheme is implemented through the State Forest Development Agency (SFDA) at the state level, Forest Development Agency (FDA) at the forest division level and the Joint Forest Management Committees (JFMCs) at the village level. The NAP is demand driven and SFDAs submit their FDA wise consolidated proposal for approval under the scheme. During last three years no demand has been submitted for Vellore and Tiruvannamalai districts by SFDA, Tamil Nadu under NAP. However, as informed by the SFDA Tamil Nadu plantation activities,maintenance of plantation andsoil moisture conservation works like construction of check dams, percolation pond  etc. were undertaken from 2004 to 2010 in 15 villages located in Jawadhu Hills in Tiruvanamali Division at a cost of Rs.93.69 lakhs.(c)	There is no such proposal at present in the ministry under contemplation to plant trees/medicinal/herb-plants on more than 10,000 acres of degraded forest land at Melchangam in Thiruvannamalai constituency.(d)	Question does not arise.*****
