                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2429






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Legislation for Protection of Consumers




Gangasandra Siddappa Basavaraj














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is keen to enact the long-pending legislation relating to protection of consumers‘ interests as envisioned in the Draft Consumer Protection Bill; (b)	whether with the increasing onslaught of e-commerce platforms on retailing sector taking gullible consumers on a ride, it has become expedient to see through the enactment of Consumer Protection Bill; and (c) 	if so, the steps being taken in this direction?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c) :  Consumer Protection Bill, 2019 was passed in Lok Sabha on 30.07.2019 and in Rajya Sabha on 06.08.2019 and was published in the official gazette on 09.08.2019. The Consumer Protection Act, 2019 has been enacted w.e.f 20.07.2020.       Under the provisions of CP Act, 2019, the Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules, 2020 have also been notified on 23rd July, 2020 for prevention of unfair trade practices in e-commerce.*****
