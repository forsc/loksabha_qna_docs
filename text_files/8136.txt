                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1791






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Assessment of PMAY-U




Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel













                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the number of houses sanctioned and completed under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) during the last three years in Maharashtra, district wise;(b)	 the target set for constructing houses under the scheme in urban areas from 2015 to 2022;(c) 	the number of houses handed over to the beneficiaries in Maharashtra during the last three years, district-wise; and(d) 	whether the Government has reviewed the progress of houses constructed under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Urban (PMAY-U) and if so, the details thereof for the period from 2015 to 2018, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	& (c) The details regarding number of houses sanctioned, completed and occupied under PMAY (U) during last three years in Maharashtra, district-wise are annexed.(b) Under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY(U)], against an assessed demand of around 1.12 Crore houses in urban areas of the States/UTs, 93.00 lakh houses have so far been sanctioned, of which 55.40 lakh houses have been grounded and 28.06 lakh houses have been completed.(d) The Ministry reviews and monitors progress of the projects approved under PMAY (U) through Monthly and Quarterly Progress Reports, in the meetings of Central Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee (CSMC), through video conferencing and review meetings at the level of Secretary (HUA) and also through field visits by the officers of the Ministry. 7 CSMC meetings were organized during 2015-16, 13 CSMC meetings were organized during 2016-17 and 12 CSMC meetings were organized during 2017-18.                                  *****
