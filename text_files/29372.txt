                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

705






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Crude Oil Extraction in Nagaland




Tokheho Yepthomi














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the details of crude oil extracted from Nagaland State during the last five years, oil-field and year-wise; and(b)  the details of the amount due to pay oil royalty to Nagaland State?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESHWAR TELI)(a): There was no crude oil production from Nagaland during the last five years.(b):  No royalty has been paid to Nagaland State during last five years.**************
