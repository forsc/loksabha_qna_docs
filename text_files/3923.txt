                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3607






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Integrated Tribal Development Project in Bihar




Rama Devi













                                                    Giridhari Yadav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the work done under Integrated Tribal Development Project in Bihar during the last three years; (b) 	the fund allocated and utilized separately under Integrated Tribal Development Project during the last three years; (c) 	whether any review has been done in this regard; and (d) 	if so, the outcome of the said review along with the reaction of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):	As per extant Guidelines, funds are provided to the States including Bihar by Ministry of Tribal Affairs under the Scheme ‘Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-Scheme (SCA to TSS)’ and ‘Grants under Article 275(1) of the Constitution’ for tribal development. There is no Integrated Tribal Development Project in Bihar; However, funds were allocated to State Government as per their share of allocation based on the criteria stipulated under the extant guidelines. Details of works / activities approved by the Ministry during 2016-17 to 2018-19 under the above-mentioned Scheme / Grant for the State of Bihar is at Annexure.
