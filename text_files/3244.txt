                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2984






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Increase in Operational Charges of Air India




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Air India''s operational charges have increased due to closure of air space over Pakistan in the aftermath of the Balakot airstrike;(b) if so, the details thereof including the number of flights affected from US, Europe and Southeast Asia; and(c) whether the Government has any record regarding excess expenditure/loss incurred by Air India for carrying out its flight operations to US and Europe due to the above reason and if so, the details thereof, month and sector-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c): Yes, Sir. Cost of operations of Air India due to Pakistan airspace closure has increased. The sector wise details of extra cost due to Pakistan airspace closure in view of operation to US, Europe and Middle East by Air India  are attached as Annexure. There is no impact on operations of Air India to South East Asia due to Pakistan airspace closure .
