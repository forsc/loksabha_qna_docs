                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3563






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Collection of Toll




Jaisiddeshwar Shivacharya Mahaswamiji














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that road commuters have to pay more toll amount per kilometer of journey on National Highways (NHs) in Maharashtra than the journey on NHs in other States;(b) if so, the details thereof and the details of the toll levied per kilometer for traveling on NHs in the State in comparison to other major States in the country having NH length of more than 3000 Kms;(c) whether the Government has received any requests from the State to withdraw the increased rate of toll and to stop collection of toll at the toll gates which are more than ten years old; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)   (a)  No Sir. Toll rates are calculated based on base rates provided in National Highways Fee (Determination of Rates and Collection) Rule 2008 and its amendments issued from time to time which are uniform for all NHs throughout the Country.(b) Does not arise.(c) No Sir.(d) Does not arise.*****
