                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4016






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Luxury Trains




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that many luxury trains are running below 	capacity and are hardly generating the revenue to meet their 	own working expenses; and(b) 	if so, the details thereof and Government’s response thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) and (b): Luxury trains being operated on Indian Railways are managed by State Tourism Corporations such as Rajasthan Tourism Development Corporation, Karnataka Tourism Development Corporation, Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation and Indian Railway Catering & Tourism Corporation which are responsible for generating revenues and meeting their expenses.	From the data collected from these corporations, the percentage occupancy of the trains show an upward trend.****
