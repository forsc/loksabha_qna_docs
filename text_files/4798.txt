                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4356






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Migration of Doctors




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether a number of senior doctors have left All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), Safdarjung and Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia hospital recently to serve in private hospitals in Delhi; (b) if so, the number of such doctors who left during the last one year and the reasons therefor; and (c) the remedial measures being taken by the Government to check the migration of senior doctors from ‘AIIMS’ and other Central Government Hospitals in Delhi?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)	& (b):    During the last one year, 10 doctors from AIIMS, New Delhi, 15 doctors from Safdarjung Hospital & Vardhman Mahavir Medical College and 1 doctor from Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia hospital have resigned or taken voluntary retirement on personal ground.(c ):      The doctors at AIIMS and other Central Government hospitals in Delhi are being paid salary and allowances as per recommendations of 7th CPC and have been provided a conducive environment for growth of faculty members.  Many doctors have resigned for personal reasons. .......................
