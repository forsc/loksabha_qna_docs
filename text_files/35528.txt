                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2883






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Conversion of Forest Villages into Revenue Villages




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received proposals from various State Governments to 	convert forest villages into revenue villages; (b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Rajasthan; and (c)	the steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)&(b)	Yes, Sir. Central Government receives proposals from the States//UTsfor conversion of forest village into revenue village under the provisions of the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980. During the last three years, Central Government has accorded final approval in 17 such proposals received from the States/UTs. The information in details is given at Annexure-I. No proposal has been received from the Government of Rajasthan regrading conversion of forest village into revenue village.  (c)	As and when a proposal for use of forest land for non-forestry purpose including conversion of forest village into revenue village is received from any States/UTs under the Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980, prompt action is taken as per the FC Rules and Guidelines made under FCA, 1980.***
