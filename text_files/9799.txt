                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3412






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Funds to Integrated Tribal Development Project




 Sunil Kumar Pintu














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) total funds allocated under the Integrated Tribal Development Projects (ITDP) in the country particularly in Bihar as the State also has a large population of tribals during the last three years, State-wise/district-wise/area-wise; and(b) the details of funds utilised out of the said funds during the said years till date, State/district/area-wise and the manner in which it has benefited the tribals?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b): Under the schemes namely Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-Scheme (SCA to TSS), Article 275(1) of the Constitution of India and Development of Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs), funds are released to State Government to undertake various activities as per proposals submitted by the respective State Government and approval thereof by the Project Appraisal Committee (PAC) constituted in this Ministry for the purpose. Funds under these schemes are not released directly to any ITDP/ITDA. However, funds are released to State for implementation of approved projects either through Integrated Tribal Development Projects (ITDPs)/Integrated Tribal Development Agencies (ITDAs) or through appropriate agency.  There is no Integrated Tribal Development Project (ITDP) in Bihar.
