                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

747






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Damage to Mangrove Forests




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of the National Green Tribunal Report about damage to MADA (Mangrove) Forest in East Godavari due to construction activities by the State Government;(b)	if so, the details there of along with the reasons for such damage caused to mangrove forest; and(c)	the extent of damage caused to mangrove and the distruption of livelihood of fisherman families caused due to construction activities?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)(b) and (c)  Two application on OA No. 65 and 74 of 2020 (SZ) are pending for judgment of Hon’ble National Green Tribunal (South Zone) on the matter pertaining to impact/ damage to MADA (Mangrove Forest in East Godavari) due to construction activities by the State Government. Hon’ble NGT vide order dated 30.04.2022 in OA No. 65 of 2020 constituted a Joint Committee  comprising of Senior Officers from Regional Office of Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, Andhra Pradesh Coastal Zone Management Authority, Senior officers from State Government of Andhra Pradesh  to visit of the effected sites and submit report. The committee has prima facie found that there exist mangroves and there was destruction. Hon’ble NGT vide order dated 18.03.2021 has directed the Committee to assess the environmental compensation as well as liability. Further, vide NGT order dated 12.07.2021, the Committee is directed to submit further report assessing the environmental compensation for the loss that has been caused on account of alleged destruction of the green cover including the mangroves taking into account the amount required for restoration of the damage to environment and submit its report to the Hon’ble NGT on or before 26.04.2021. The final report of the committee is yet to be accepted by the Hon’ble NGT as per order dated 12.07.2021.******
