                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

450






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Benefits of PMJAY to e-Shram Beneficiaries




Pradeep Kumar Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the funds spent for the welfare of the registered beneficiaries at e-Shram Portal along with the State-wise details thereof including Uttar Pradesh; (b)whether the Government proposes to extend the benefit of the Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) to the said beneficiaries;(c)if so, the details thereof; and(d)if not, whether the Government proposes to extend the benefits of PMJAY to the said beneficiaries in future?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI BHUPENDER YADAV)(a) to (d):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.**                                                  ****** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (d) OF           LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 450 TO BE ANSWERED ON 04.04.2022 REGARDING “BENEFITS OF PMJAY TO E-SHRAM BENEFICIARIES”.(a):		Ministry of Labour & Employment  launched eSHRAM portal in August, 2021 to prepare National Database of the Unorganised Workers. The main objectives of the portal are to maintain a dynamic database of workers, provide Universal Account Number (UAN), facilitate delivery of social security and welfare benefits, ensure portability of benefits, opportunities for job search and skill development by linkages to NCS, ASEEM & UDYAM portals.As on 30.03.2022, more than 27 crore unorganised workers have been registered on eSHRAM portal. The State-wise registration of unorganised workers as on 30th March, 2022 is at Annexure.Rs. 45.49 crore was released/utilized during 2020-21 and Rs. 255.86 crore has been released/utilized during the year 2021-22 on eSHRAM/National Database for Unorganized Workers project.All registered workers are entitled to get benefit of an accidental death or disability insurance cover of Rs. 2.0 Lakh for a year, free of cost through Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY).(b) to (d):	Certain categories of unorganized workers, eligible as per the set criteria (occupation criteria in urban areas and deprivation criteria in rural areas) and identified from the Socio Economic Caste Census, 2011 are already covered under Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Aarogya Yojna (PM-JAY). As on 29.03.2022, a total of 17.9 crore individuals have been verified and provided with Ayushman cards.
