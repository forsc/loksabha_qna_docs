                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

584






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Mobile Tower Footprint of BSNL




M.K. Vishnu Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) has expanded its mobile tower footprint in the country and if so, the details thereof during each of the last three years and the current year State/UT-wise including Tamilnadu; (b) 	whether all the existing mobile towers are not functional and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and (c) 	the action taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to make all the towers functional and provide better services to its customers?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) has informed that it has expanded its mobile tower footprint in the country.  Details are at Annexure.(b)&(c) 	BSNL has informed that all the mobile towers are functional. However, all faults arising due to power outage, media and hardware issues are attended by BSNL on priority to provide uninterrupted services to its customers.  BSNL is meeting Quality of Service (QoS) benchmarks of mobile network prescribed by Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI).
