                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4849






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Financial Package to Women Entrepreneurs




 Bhavana Gawali (Patil)














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)        	the details of financial package given by the Government to COVID affected women entrepreneurs and self help groups, State-wise and year-wise and (b)       the total number of women benefitted from such scheme announced by Government during COVID-19 pandemic? 






ANSWER




(a) & (b):  The Government has taken several initiatives to support and protect the interest of women and other entrepreneurs through stimulus given under Aatma Nirbhar Bharat Packages to combat the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic in India. The packages comprise of schemes for various sectors of the economy and also schemes having impact across sectors. The specific initiatives taken to protect women entrepreneurs and MSMEs are as under:1.	Rs. 3 lakh crore Emergency Working Capital Facility for Businesses, including MSMEs2.	Rs. 20,000 crore Subordinate Debt for Stressed MSMEs3.	Rs. 50,000 crore equity infusion through MSME Fund of Funds4.	New Definition of MSME and other Measures for MSME5.	Rs. 10,000 crore scheme for formalisation of Micro Food Enterprises (MFE) 6.	Relief of Rs. 1500 crore to MUDRA loanees 7.	Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS) for MSMEs, businesses, MUDRA borrowers and individuals8.	Rs. 5000 crore Credit facility for Street Vendors.9.	Under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package, a total of 20.40 crores (approx) women account holders (Pradhan Mantri jan Dhan Yojana) were given an ex-gratia of Rs. 500 per month for three months.10.	For Self-Help groups (SHGs), limit of collateral free lending was increased from Rs.10 lakhs to Rs. 20 lakhs for women organized through 63 lakhs SHGs, who supported 6.85 crore households.Under the ECLGS Scheme as on 28.02.2022, 81.18 lakhs women beneficiaries have been provided guarantees for loan.
