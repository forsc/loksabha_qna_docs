                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1400






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Scholarships for SCs




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1400TO BE ANSWERED ON 07.12.2021CENTRALLY SPONSORED SCHEME OF SCHOLARSHIPS FOR SCs1400. DR. ALOK KUMAR SUMAN: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government is implementing various centrally sponsored scheme of scholarship for SCs students in Bihar including district Gopalganj; (b) if so, the details thereof and the total amount provided to the State of Bihar for scholarship of SCs students and the amount utilised; (c) the details of the fund contributed by the State Government and the Union Government for scholarship of SCs students in Bihar particularly district Gopalganj; and (d) whether the Union Government did not sanction the amount due for the State of Bihar due to specified reasons, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(DR. VIRENDRA KUMAR)(a & b): Yes. The Ministry deals with the following two Centrally sponsored schemes of  scholarship for SC students in various States/UTs including the State of Bihar:     (i) Post-Matric Scholarship for SC students      (ii) Pre-Matric Scholarship for SC StudentsUnder the Post Matric Scholarship Scheme for SC Students, an amount of Rs 47.83 crore was released to State Government of Bihar during the Financial Year 2020-21. State Government has not provided the Utilization Certificate so far. Under the Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme for SC Students, an amount of Rs 55.50 crore was released to the State Government  of Bihar during the Financial Year 2019-20. No funds were released during 2020-21. State Government has so far provided the Utilization certificate of Rs 41.97 crore.(c):  The Ministry, based on the funding pattern of the scheme, releases the central share to the State Government/directly to the beneficiary as per the scheme provisions. No funds are released directly to the districts by the Ministry under these schemes.(d):  During the Financial Year 2020-21, no amount was released under Pre-Matric Scholarship for SC students due to deficiencies in the proposal submitted by the State Government.*****
