                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

509






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Jan Aushadhi Kendras




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state:(a) the names of the locations in the country, especially in the backward and Scheduled Caste dominated areas where Jan AushadhiKendras have been set up; (b) whether the Government has taken any steps for setting up Jan AushadhiKendras especially in the Scheduled Caste dominated areas; (c) if so, the details thereof; (d) whetherMishrikh in Uttar Pradesh is included in the said locations; and (e) if so, the details in this regard?






ANSWER




MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(SHRI D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)                               (a)  	As on 10.06.2019, 5312 PradhanMantriBhartiyaJanaushadhiKendras (PMBJKs) are functional in the country. The list of such Kendrasalongwith their addresses is  available on the website of Bureau of Pharma PSUs of India (BPPI) i.e. www.janaushadhi.gov.in .(b)&(c) Individual applicants/ NGOs/Trusts/Societies/Institutions/ Hospitals/Medical Colleges may apply for PMBJKs fulfilling the mandatory requirements through online/offline mode. Opening of new PMBJK in a particular area  entirely depends upon  the number of applications received from such area and obtaining of drug license by the approved applicants in the name of ‘PradhanMantriBhartiyaJanaushadhi Kendra’. In July 2018, BPPI had invited Expression of Interest across the country from    NGOs/Trusts/Societies/Institutions/Individuals for opening up of PMBJKs.(d)&(e) As on 10.06.2019, we have one PMBJK in Mishrikh in Uttar Pradesh. The address and contact details of this PMBJK are given in the Annexure-I.   Annexure-I Statement referred to in  part (d & e) of LokSabhaUnstarred Q. No. 509 for 25.06.2019 raised by SHRI ASHOK KUMAR RAWAT regarding Jan AushadhiKendrasDetails of PMBJP Kendra in Mishrikh, Uttar PradeshName of owner	Address 	Contact numberShriAshwani Pal	Mohr.
