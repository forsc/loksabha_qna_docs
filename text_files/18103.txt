                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1841






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Jobs Under the Sports Quota




Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    Pocha Brahmananda Reddy
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Gautam Gambhir
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government has recently introduced 20 new disciplines for Central Government jobs under the sports quota;   (b)  if so, the details thereof;  (c)  whether the Union Government is working on any proposal to encourage various State Governments to add these disciplines for sports quota in State Government jobs;   (d)  whether the Government has introduced Mallakhamb, tug of war and roll ball as disciplines,  if so, the details thereof; and   (e)   whether there is any officially recognized association or tournaments at the Government level to select such sportspersons and  if so, the details thereof ?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS [SHRI KIREN RIJIJU]  (a) &(b)  Yes, Sir.  The details of the newly introduced/included 20 sports disciplines for Central Government jobs under sports quota are attached at Annexure-I.  (c)          The recruitment of meritorious sportspersons under sports quota is done as per the guidelines issued by the Department of Personnel & Training from time to time and it is applicable to only Central Government Ministries/Departments. “Sports” being State subject, these instructions are not binding for State Governments.  (d)  The Department of Personnel & Training in consultation with the Department of Sports, vide its OM dated 01st September, 2020 has included Mallakhamb, Tug of War and Roll Ball for appointment of meritorious sportspersons under sports quota in newly introduced 20 sports disciplines.  (e)          National Sports Federations are annually recognized by the Department of Sports based on their performance and compliance of instructions enumerated in the National Sports Development Code 2011. Various sports tournaments/competitions are organized by these Federations for selection of meritorious sportspersons.  *****
