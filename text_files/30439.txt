                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1901






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Road Projects in NE Region




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of road and highway projects sanctioned by the Union Government which are under construction in North Eastern States specially in Assam along with their length and routes;(b)	the details of projects which are currently lying pending in these States including Assam;(c)	the funds allocated for these projects, State and project-wise; and(d)	the time by which these projects are likely to be completed? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) and (d)   This Ministry is responsible for development and maintenance of National Highways in the country. The details of  Projects sanctioned and under construction along with their likely date of completion in North-Eastern States including Assam are given at Annexure-I.(b)    The details of pending Projects in North-Eastern States including Assam are given at Annexure-II.(c)   Funds are allocated scheme wise, based on the requirement of sanctioned works. The details of Funds allocated for these ongoing projects   scheme wise in the current year in North Eastern states are given at Annexure-III.
