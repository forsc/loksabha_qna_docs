                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3491






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






MTS Vacancies at National Monuments




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Pradeep Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of 1/30 status workers working at present at various National monuments/cultural heritage monuments across the country;(b)	whether the Government is considering to regularize the services of such 1/30 status workers and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	the total number of MTS vacancies at various National monuments/cultural heritage monuments across the country; and(d)	the steps taken by the Government to regularize the services of all the 1/30 status workers against the MTS vacancies at monuments across the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	Total number of 1/30 status workers working at present at various Archaeological Survey of India National monuments/cultural heritage monuments across the country is 703.(b)	No, Sir. As per extant DoPT guidelines, there is no provision to regularize the services of 1/30 status workers at present.(c) & (d)	There are 2,474 number of vacancies of MTS in Archaeological Survey of India. However, as per extant DoPT guidelines, there is no provision to regularize the services of 1/30 status workers against   MTS vacancies.
