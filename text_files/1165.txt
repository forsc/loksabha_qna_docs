                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1035






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Maintenance of Roads in Lakshadweep




Faizal P.P. Mohammed














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government proposes to repair the roads older than five years and also which are in deteriorated condition in Lakshadweep and if so, the details thereof; (b)	the details of roads which have been demarked by the Union Government for repair/maintenance in Lakshadweep; and (c)	the time period fixed for repair/maintenance of these roads?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)    	Ministry of Road Transport and Highways is primarily responsible for the development and maintenance of National Highways (NHs) in the country.  There are no National Highways in the UT of Lakshadweep.  This Ministry also allocates funds to State Government for development of State Roads (other than Rural Roads) under the Central Road Fund (CRF), now amended as Central Road & Infrastructure Fund (CRIF), as per provisions of erstwhile CRF Act, 2000.  However, no proposal of State Road has been received by this Ministry from UT of Lakshadweep for sanction. (b)   	As per the information furnished by Lakshadweep PWD, a list of roads in the UT under PWD control, demarked and sanctioned for major repair are at Annexure-I. (c)	The minor repairs of roads are carried out within one month and when it requires re-surfacing, same is carried out within three months.
