                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5044






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Allocation/Utilization of Funds under Minority Schemes




Haji Fazlur Rehman













                                                    Asaduddin Owaisi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Ministry was able to spent just 1291 crore or almost 27.5% against the budget estimate of Rs. 4700 crore for all schemes and programmes till October 31, 2019 and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the total funds allocated to the Ministry and spent during the last financial year 2020-21 and till date and the details of the unutilised fund including the reasons therefor; (c)	the total funds spent by the Ministry on publicity about Government schemes for minorities to make aware minority students to take the benefit of the schemes;(d)	whether the proposed allocation of funds for 2021-22 has been reduced as compared to previous financial year and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and(e)	the steps taken or being taken by the Ministry to utilise allocated amount fully within the stipulated time limit?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	 The actual expenditure for the F.Y 2019-20 upto 31st March, 2020 was Rs.4505.10 crore (which is 95.85%) against the Budget allocation of Rs.4700.00 crore in 2019-20. (b)	The budget allocated to the ministry for the year 2020-21 is Rs.4005.00 crore out of which 81% has been expended till date and by the end of the financial year the entire budget allocated will be spent by the ministry(c)	An amount of Rs. 9.18 crore has been spent in the current financial year by the Ministry on publicity, social media and awareness programmes about Government Schemes.(d)	No, Sir. An amount of Rs.4810.77 crore has been allocated for 2021-22 as against budget allocation of Rs.4005.00 crore for 2020-21. (e)	The steps taken include concerted efforts and constant monitoring on regular basis to utilize the allocated budget before the end of the financial year. ******
