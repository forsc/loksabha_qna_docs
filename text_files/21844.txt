                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3193






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Review of Historical Places in Tribal Areas




Sudarshan Bhagat














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the historical places located in the tribal areas of the country are being reviewed; (b)	if so, the State-wise details thereof; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM                                             (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)to(c)	There are 3693 centrally protected monuments /sites looked after by Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) including monuments /sites located in the tribal areas of the country. A statement indicating state-wise distribution of all centrally protected monument/sites is at Annexure. Their periodic review, inspection, conservation, preservation is taken up by ASI as per the need and requirement considering availability of funds and resources.
