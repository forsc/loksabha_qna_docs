                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1469






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Allocation of Funds




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of funds allocated to IITs, IISERs, IIMs, UGC and AICTE during the last three years,State/UT-wise; and(b) the corrective steps taken/being taken by the Government to adequate funds allocation to saidinstitutions?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) The funds allocated to University Grants Commission (UGC), All India Council for TechnicalEducation (AICTE) and to IITs, IISERs and IIMs situated in various locations of the country duringlast three years is as given below:(Rs. in crores)Name of the Institution B.E.2016-17B.E.2017-18B.E.2018-19University Grants Commission (UGC) 4491.94 4691.94 4722.75All India Council for Technical Education 481.00 485.00 485.00Indian Institutes of Technology(IITs) 4983.71 7856.00 6326.00Indian Institutes of Management(IIMs) 730.00 1030.00 1036.00Indian Institute s of Science Education &Research (IISERs) 720.00 650.00 689.00</pre>This comes under ‘Other Central Expenditure’. Hence, state/UT-wise allocations are not made.(b) In addition to the above, the Government has established Higher Education Financing Agency(HEFA) as a Non Banking Finance Company to leverage funds from market for financingimprovement in infrastructure in education Institutions of the country including IITs, IISERs and IIMs.****
