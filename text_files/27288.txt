                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2940






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Northern Bypass in Agra




Rajkumar Chahar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of the project of the proposed Northern Bypass in Agra;(b)	the estimated cost, alignment and length of this bypass along with the time frame set for the commencement and completion of the project; and(c)	the objectives of making the said bypass?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) The Detailed Project Report (DPR) work of about 14 km Northern Agra Bypass has been initiated, which starts at NH-2 and ends at Yamuna Expressway. It is targeted for award in the Year 2021-22 and completion by Year 2024-25 with tentative cost of Rs. 523 crore. The 3A Gazette Notification has also been published on 09.04.2021. This bypass has been conceptualised to decongest and improve road connectivity in and around Agra City.	*****
