                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2304






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Increase in Steel Import




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the imports of steel from Japan and South Korea have increased since 2014;(b)	if so, the details of percentage-wise increase in steel import since 2014, country-wise and year-wise;(c)	whether the import of steel by the Railways has increased since 2014 and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	whether it is a fact that the National Steel Policy requires Government projects to use steel manufactured in the country itself and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)	From FY 2013-14 till 2018-19, imports of steel from South Korea have increased by 122% while steel imports from Japan have declined by 4%. (b)	Data on percentage change in imports of total finished steel (alloy/stainless + non-alloy) by India from Japan and South Korea during last five years and April-May 2019 is given below:-Import of total finished steelPeriod	Japan	South Korea	Qty.(''000t)	%change	Qty.(''000t)	%change2013-14	1358	-14	1321	-212014-15	1583	17	1875	432015-16	2160	36	3007	602016-17	1136	-47	2103	-302017-18	1176	4	2473	182018-19*	1299	10	2936	19April-May 2019*	187	-18	457	-2Source: JPC; *provisional(c)	Yes, Sir. During FY 2015-2019, the imports of steel for railways have increased by 319%. (d)	Government has notified a specific Policy for providing preference to Domestically Manufactured Iron & Steel Products in Government procurements and tenders to promote the domestic manufacturing.  ********
