                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2279






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Private Investment in J&K




Mala Roy














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of private investment proposals in Jammu & Kashmir (J&K) that have been received between August 5, 2019 and December 31, 2019; and(b) 	the number of the said proposals that have been accepted ?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL) (a) & (b): 	The Government of Jammu and Kashmir has informed that 44 Expressions of Interest for investment amounting to Rs. 13120 Crore have been received during the period from 5th August, 2019 to 31st December, 2019. All proposals are currently under scrutiny by respective departments.******
