                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3942






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Development of Heritage Sites into Museum




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government  proposes to promote India''s heritage by identifying potential historical and archaeological marvels of the Country, particularly in Rajasthan;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	whether the Government proposes to protect these sites by developing them into a museum or historical tourist spots?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b)	Identification of historical monuments and archaeological sites, is a regular exercise taken up by Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) all over the country including State of Rajasthan. Details of monuments/sites identified for declaring them as of national importance is at Annexure. (c)	Once any identified site or monument is declared as of National importance, it is conserved and preserved as per the provisions of the Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act,1958.
