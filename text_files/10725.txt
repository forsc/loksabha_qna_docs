                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4011






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Road Projects in Andhra Pradesh




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari













                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is undertaking road projects in Andhra Pradesh under Public Private Partnership (PPP) model namely Kadapa-Pulivendula to Damajipalli (NH-43)-Anantapur Ring Road, Rajampet to Nellore district-Gudur Road (Corridor-1), Chagalamarri to Rayachoti (Corridor-3) in Anantapurdistrict, Somandepalli to Thumakunta road in Chittor district and Kalavagunta to Nedragunta road; and(b)	 if so, the details and the present status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	No Sir.(b)	Does not arise*****
