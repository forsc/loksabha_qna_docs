                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

549






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha  Yojana (PMSSY)




Amol Ramsing Kolhe













                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the salient features of the Scheme and the current status of implementation of Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY) in the country along with the total funds allocated under the Scheme during the last three financial years and current financial year, State/UT-wise;(b)	the details of works undertaken under PMSSY State/UT-wise including Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu and Andman& Nicobar Islands;(c)	whether the Government has decided to upgrade medical institutions in the country under various phases of PMSSY and if so, the details thereof and the current status of the upgradation of projects, State/UT-wise including Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra;(d)	the details of medical colleges/institutions identified for upgradation under phase-III of the PMSSY for development of super specialty services, State/UT-wise; and(e)	whether the Government has conducted any assessment regarding works undertaken and performance of the PMSSY during the last three years;(f)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (d):The Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY) aims at correcting regional imbalances in the availability of affordable tertiary healthcare services and to augment facilities for quality medical education in the country. The Scheme has two components, namely, (i) Setting up of All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS); and (ii) Upgradation of existing Government Medical Colleges/Institutions (GMCIs). So far setting up of 22 new AIIMS and 75 projects of upgradation of GMCIs have been approved under the Scheme in various phases. The status of implementation of the scheme in the country including Phase-wise works approved under the Scheme are at Annexure. Allocation of funds under PMSSY is combined for all the projects undertaken under the scheme and there is no State-UT/wise allocation. The details of fund allocated during the last three financial years and current financial year under the Scheme are as under;Financial Year	Fund allocated (RE)(Rs. in Crore)2018-19	3825.002019-20	4714.902020-21	7517.252021-22	7623.70*			*including Rs.223.70 Crore under ECRP-II(e) to (f):An independent evaluation study of the PMSSY has been conducted through Indian Institute of Public Administration.  The Study was awarded in November, 2017 and its final Report was received in December, 2020.  The study covered 6 operational AIIMS and a sample of 8 up-graded GMCI projects out of 19 GMCI upgradation projects of Phases – I and II of the scheme.
