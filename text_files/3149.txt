                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Common Service Centres in Villages




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of  ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:(a)	whether central Government is planning to expand Common Service Centres in villages to Gram Panchayat under Digital India Programme;(b)	the total number of Gram Panchayats of Rajasthan linked under this scheme, District-wise;(c)	whether any time limit has been fixed to link all Gram Panchayats of the country, including Rajasthan to this service and if so, the details thereof; and; and(d)	the total number of services available in these centres and the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        	Yes, Sir.(b):	11611 Common Services Centres (CSCs) are setup in Rajasthan, out of which 9691 Gram Panchayat (GP) has at-least one CSC. All these CSCs are linked by creating system generated CSC ID in Digital Seva Portal (DSP). The district-wise detail of CSCs in Gram Panchayats of Rajasthan is at Annexure-I.(c): 	Yes, Sir.
