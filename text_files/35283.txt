                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2663






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Survey under PMAY-U




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has undertaken any survey under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana- Urban(PMAY-U) to find out the demand for housing in the country;(b)	 if so, the details thereof including the year of the survey conducted and demand for housing in each State;(c) 	whether the Government has taken any measures to validate the demand and meet the targets set under PMAY-U;(d) 	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	the total allocation of funds made and the amount spent under the scheme during the last three years, State and year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                                              THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                       (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE) (a) to (d):In pursuance of the Government’s vision of “Housing for All”, Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) since 25.06.2015 for giving Central assistance to States/ Union Territories (UTs) for providing all-weather pucca houses to all eligible urban households.  States/ UTs have undertaken demand survey under PMAY-U for assessing actual demand of housing for eligible beneficiaries.  Total assessed demand of houses as validated by States/ UTs in the year 2017 under the scheme is 112.24 lakhs.State/ UT-wise details are at Annexure-I.However, demand of housing being dynamic in nature, additional housing demand of urban households who became eligible during implementation of the scheme were also covered under PMAY-U.States/UTs formulate project proposals as per their assessed demand of houses under the Scheme and get them approved by State Level Sanctioning & Monitoring Committee (SLSMC) constituted under the chairmanship of Chief Secretary of the State/ UT concerned. After approval from the concerned SLSMC, States/ UTs submit the proposals to this Ministry for approval of Central assistance by the Central Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee (CSMC).  Based on the project proposals received so far from the States/UTs, more than 115 lakh houses have been sanctioned across the country; out of which 55.45 lakh have been completed/ delivered to beneficiaries and remaining are at various stages of construction/ grounding.(e): State/ UT-wise details of Central assistance sanctioned and released under PMAY-U during the last three years are at Annexure-II.                          *****
