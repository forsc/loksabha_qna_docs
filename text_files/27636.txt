                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3425






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Primary Schools




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of primary schools established or proposed to be established in rural areas with the help of the World Bank till date, State/location-wise;(b) the details of funds allocated therefor during the last three years till date;(c) the names of the rural areas where primary schools are proposed to be established in near future with the help of World Bank; and(d) the time by which the proposal is likely to be finalised ?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) and (b). In the last three years, no primary School has been established with the funding of the World Bank and such infrastructure is supported either through Samagra Shiksha or through State Budget.(c) and (d). Do not arise.*****
