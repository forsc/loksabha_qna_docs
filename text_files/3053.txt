                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2946






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Construction of Railway Flyover




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is any proposal to construct railway flyover at all major railway crossings of the district Gopalganj of Bihar for smooth running of vehicles;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has taken any decision to construct railway flyover at NH-85 near Hathuwa railway station of Mirganj-Gopalganj road; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b):  No, Sir. There is no proposal to construct Road over Bridge (ROB) at all major Level Crossings (LCs) of the district Gopalganj on cost sharing as Train Vehicle Unit is less than 1 lakh. If State Government desires to construct one, it can be made on deposit terms.(c) & (d): LC no. 12 at Km 19/1-2 between Hathuwa-Thawe stations falls on National Highways.  As per Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) between Ministry of Railways and Ministry of Road Transport and Highways (MoRTH), all LCs on National Highway Corridors are to be eliminated by MoRTH by construction of Road over Bridges /Road under Bridges (ROBs/RUBs) at their own cost.*****
