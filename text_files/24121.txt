                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

114






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Problems in Online Exams




P.P. Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the problems faced by students in online exams;(b) if so, the steps taken/proposed to be taken to address these problems along with relevant data; and(c) whether the Government has considered conducting exams offline; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d): In order to address the problems faced by the students in online exams, as well as to ensure continuation of academic activities, the University Grants Commission (UGC) has issued Guidelines dated 29.04.2020 on Examinations and Academic Calendar for the Universities in view of COVID-19 Pandemic and subsequent Lockdown. This provides for online/offline/blended mode of examination.In addition, the University Grants Commission (Open and Distance Learning Programmes and Online Programmes) Regulations, 2020 also prescribes for Conduct of Examination and Minimum Standards for Examination Centres.*****
