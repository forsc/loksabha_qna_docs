                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1388






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Entrance Test for College Admission




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Delhi University has any proposal to introduce entrance test for all college admissions;(b) if so, the details and the reasons therefor along with the graduate and post graduate courses forwhich Delhi University conducts computer-based entrance tests;(c) whether class XII marks will also be taken into account while deciding college admission;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) the time by which the Delhi University plans to introduce entrance exam based admission for allits undergraduate courses?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ''Nishank'')(a) to (e): University of Delhi has informed that there is no proposal to introduce entrance test for allcollege admissions. The University has been conducting entrance test for the Undergraduate andPostgraduate courses for the past several years. However, the computer based entrance tests have beenintroduced since 2018. The University conducts entrance test for all PG courses and followingUndergraduate courses:<pre>Faculty of Applied Social Sciences andHumanities· Bachelor of Business Economics· Bachelor of Management Studies· Bachelor of Business Administration (FinancialInvestment Analysis)Cluster Innovation Centre · B.Tech. (Information Technology andMathematical Innovations)· B.A. (H) Humanities and Social SciencesFaculty of Education · Bachelors of Elementary EducationFaculty of Inter-disciplinary & AppliedSciences· Bachelor of Science in Physical Education,Health Education & SportsIndraprastha College for Women · B.A. (H) Multimedia and Mass CommunicationFaculty of Social Science · Five Year Integrated programme in Journalism inDelhi School of Journalism</pre>Class XII marks are the criterion for all undergraduate admissions, except the above mentionedcourses.*****
