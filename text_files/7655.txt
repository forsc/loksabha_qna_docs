                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1355






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Fake Universities




Sukhbir Singh Jaunapuria














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that a large number of fake universities arecheating the students;(b) if so, the number of such cases reported during the last three years along with theaction taken by the Government thereto; and(c) the remedial steps taken by the Government apart from issuing a notice to alert thepublic against such fake universities?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c): Yes, Sir. On the basis of complaints received from the students, parents,general public and also through electronic print media, University Grants Commission(UGC) has released the list of 24 fake Universities which are offering Degreeprogrammes without recognition under Section 2(f) and Section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956.Out of these 24 fake Universities, 5 cases have been included during the last three years.In addition, two more Institutions namely Indian Institute of Planning and Management(IIPM), New Delhi and Bhartiya Shiksha Parishad, Lucknow, UP are also not recognizedby the UGC. The details of these fake Universities are available on its websitewww.ugc.ac.in.Apart from issuing the public notices to warn the people against these fakeuniversities, the Government/UGC has taken the following remedial steps:<pre>i. Sent letters to State Chief Secretaries, Education Secretaries and PrincipalSecretaries to take action against fake Universities/Institutes located in theirjurisdiction.ii. Updated the list of fake Universities on its website.iii. Lodged FIRs in Police station against Indian Institute of Planning andManagement (IIPM), New Delhi.</pre>*****
