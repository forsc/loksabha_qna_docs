                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3572






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Tribal University




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to establish Tribal University in Telangana as assured in theAndhra Pradesh Reorganization Bill; and(b) if so, the details thereof ?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ''Nishank'')(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. The Andhra Pradesh Re-organisation Act 2014, inter-alia, envisages forestablishment of one Central Tribal University in the State of Telangana. The State Government hasrecently earmarked a land measuring 335.04 acres free from all encumbrances for the proposed TribalUniversity. The University will be functional after enactment by the Parliament.
