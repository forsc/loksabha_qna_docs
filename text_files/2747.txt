                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

230






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Organic Farming




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the State-wise details of the present status of organic farming in the country; and (b) 	the State/UT-wise details of the number of farmers participated in the training workshop organized on organic farming across the country during the last three years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		 	  (SHRI NARENDRA  SINGH TOMAR) (a) & (b):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) & (b) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 230  DUE FOR REPLY ON 9TH JULY, 2019.(a):	 As per the report received from States and Agriculture Processed Food and Export Development Authority (APEDA),at present organic farming is taken up  in an area of 27.70  lakh hectares in the country including 5.98 lakh hectares under Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) , 0.639 lakh hectares under  Mission Organic Value Chain Development for North Eastern Region (MOVCDNER) , 19.38 lakh hectares under National Program for Organic Production (NPOP)  of APEDA and 1.70 lakh hectares under other initiatives. The State-wise details of the area covered under PKVY, MOVCDNER, NPOP and others is given at Annexure-I (b):	  The total number of farmers participated in trainings / workshop organised on organic farming in the last three years and in current year is 15.14 lakh. The state/ UT wise details of farmers participated in trainings is at Annexure- ll.Awareness generation among farmers through trainings, field visits are integral part of organic farming schemes of PKVY, MOVCDNER. Similarly, National Centre for organic Farming (NCOF), the subordinate office of DAC&FW is also involved in extension activities and conducts   Farmers’ Training and Field Demonstration Programmes (FTFD) under  Sansad Aadarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY) and jaivik sammelan programmes. Indian Council of Agriculture Research (ICAR) under All India- National Project on Organic Farming (AI-NPOF) conducts training, exposure visit and field demonstrations of technologies besides exhibition of technologies in Kisan Mela.
