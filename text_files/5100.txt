                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4672






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Tax Rebate Scheme




Behanan Benny














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is planning for extension of the Rebate of State and Central Taxes and Levies to other sectors; and(b)	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a)	& (b): Rebate of State and Central Taxes and Levies is a scheme administered by Ministry of Textiles, which is restricted to the apparel and made-ups segments for the present. Government has constituted an Inter-Ministerial Committee to look into the issue of extending the scheme to other segments such as yarn, fabric, etc. *****
