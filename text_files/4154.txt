                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3759






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Proposals for DDRC and ADIP Scheme




Ramesh Chander Kaushik














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of proposals received from Assam under District Disability Rehabilitation Centres (DDRC) and Assistance to Disabled Persons for purchase/fitting of Aids and Appliances (ADIP) scheme during the year 2017-18;(b) the number of proposals for which approval has been accorded;(c) the number of proposals pending for approval; and(d) the time by which the pending proposals are likely to be approved?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT (SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR)(a) to (c) The number of proposals received from Assam under District Disability Rehabilitation Centres (DDRC) and Assistance to Disabled Persons for purchase/fitting of Aids and Appliances (ADIP) scheme, number of proposals approved and number of proposals pending during the year 2017-18 are as under:-Name of the Scheme	No. of Proposals Received	No. of Proposals Approved	No. of Proposals PendingDDRC	2	2	0ADIP	0	0	0(d) Not applicable, in view of reply to (a) to (c) above.*****
