                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4320






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Decentralised Energy Resources Options




Shankar Lalwani














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the efforts made by the Government to promote alternative energy options particularly Decentralized Renewable Energy Option (DREO) for the socio-economic development of large number of tribal/rural areas of Madhya Pradesh which are devoid of power supply grid;(b) whether the Government has any scheme for starting micro Hydro System in Madhya Pradesh; and(c)  if so, the details thereof;
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy is implementing various schemes and programmes for promoting use of decentralized renewable energy options such as solar street lights, solar study lamps, solar power packs, solar water pumps, biogas plants, solar thermal systems, etc., in the country including in the tribal/rural areas of Madhya Pradesh. (b) & (c) No Sir.*****
