                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3675






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Impact of ‘Movement Against Animal Slaughtering’ on Tourism Industry




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the ongoing movement against animal slaughtering has affected the Indian Tourism Industry; (b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the estimated revenue and other losses suffered by the tourism industry due to this movement along with the number of people in the tourism sector who became unemployed due to it in the country, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	Ministry of Tourism, Government of India has not undertaken study regarding effect on Indian tourism industry such as revenue loss, unemployment etc. due to movement against animal slaughtering. However, details of estimates of Foreign Exchange Earnings (FEEs) from tourism and International Tourist Arrivals (ITA) during last three years are as under:<pre>S. No.	Parameter	2017	2018	2019 (Provisional)1	ITA to India (million)	16.81	17.42	17.872	Estimated FEEs through Tourism (Rupees crore) 	177874	194881	210981</pre>The above figures show no decline in the recent years.*******
