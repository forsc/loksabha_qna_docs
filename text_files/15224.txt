                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3909






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Increasing the Old Age, Widow and Disability Pension




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to increase the pension of old age persons, widows and disabled by bringing out a massive change in social assistance programmes;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c): Government has initiated a comprehensive third party evaluation study of the Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme (IGNOAPS), Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme (IGNWPS) and Indira Gandhi National Disability Pension Scheme (IGNDPS) under the National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP). The broad terms of reference of the study, inter-alia, include documentation of best practices and to bring about uniformity in the implementation of the schemes in all States/ UTs and to suggest changes in assistance and criteria related to these schemes of NSAP.******
