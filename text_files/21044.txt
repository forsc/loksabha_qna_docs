                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2138






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Development of Deomali Hills




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the State Government has submitted any project for Development in Koraput and Rayagada Districts to the Ministry of Tourism; (b) 	if so, the details thereof on the current status and; (c) 	whether the Central Government has surveyed potential tourist sites like Deomali Hills in Koraput on its own and plans to develop the same; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	Development of Tourism is primarily  the  responsibility  of  the  State  Government. However, the Ministry of Tourism under schemes of Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD provides financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure in the country. Sanctioning of infrastructure projects is a continuous process.  The projects under Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD schemes are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/UT Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier.   There  is  no  proposal  under consideration   for  Development  of  Koraput  and  Rayagada  under the  above  mentioned  schemes.   *******
