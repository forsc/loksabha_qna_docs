                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4061






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Quality of Higher Education




Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the quality of higher education is deteriorating due to shortage of faculty in higher education;(b) the number of sanctioned faculty posts as on 31.12.2021 including permanent staff and contractual staff; and(c) the steps being taken by the Government to fill the vacant posts and improve the quality education in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) to (c) Occurring of vacancies and filling up is a continuous process. The vacancy arises due to retirement, resignation and additional requirements on account of enhancement of students’ strength. The details of sanctioned, in-position and vacant posts of faculty in Higher Educational Institutes of Ministry of Education are as under:<pre>S.No.	Name of Institute	Sanctioned 	In Position	Vacant1.		Central Universities (CUs) 	18922	12514	64082.		Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs)	10881	6511	43703.		Indian Institutes of Information Technology (IIITs)	1142	545	6114.		Indian Institutes of Management (IIMs)	1448	1026	4225.		 National Institutes of Technology (NITs)	7483	4907	25766.		Indian Institutes of Science Education and Research	735	671	647.		School of Planning and Architecture	227	169	588.		Language Institutes	519	457	629.		National Institutes of Technical Teachers'' Training & Research(NITTTRs)	203	128	7510.		Other Centrally funded Institutions	735	494	251</pre>In order to ensure that studies of students are not affected, the institutions are adopting various measures to address the shortage of faculty which inter-alia include engaging research scholars, hiring through contract, re-employment or through visiting faculty. Further the SWAYAM and SWAYAM PRABHA platforms are available which help students get world class experience from faculties of IITs and Central Universities (CUs). The Ministry of Education has requested all the Central Higher Educational Institutions to fill up the vacancies in a Mission Mode within a period of one year starting from 5th September, 2021. Since August 2021, 8589 (eight thousand five hundred eighty nine) posts have been advertised for which the selection processes are on.***
