                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

304






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Dedicated Freight Corridor




Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Nisith Pramanik
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has recently dedicated few sections of Dedicated Freight Corridor (DFC) to the nation;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the details of earnings from freight including loading during the last three years;(d) 	whether the Railways has provided any incentive to Kisan Rail Freight transportation; if so, the details thereof; (e)  	whether the Government has taken any initiatives to attract more freight business and increase income from it and if so, the details thereof; and(f) 	the steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. The following two (2) sections of DFC have been commissioned and dedicated to the nation by Hon’ble Prime Minister of India:-(i) Bhaupur to Khurja (351 km) of Eastern Dedicated Freight Corridor (EDFC) on 29.01.2020.(ii) Rewari to Madar (306 km) of Western Dedicated Freight Corridor (WDFC) on 07.01.2021.(c): Details of freight and freight revenue for the last three years are as under:Year	      Freight                 Freight Revenue          (in Million Tonnes) 	         (in Crores)  2017-18	       1159.55	               `1,17,055.402018-19	       1221.48	               `1,27,432.722019-20	       1208.41	               `1,13,487.89(d): 50% subsidy is being given in transportation of fruits and vegetable by Kisan Rail through a scheme administered by Ministry of Food Processing Industries.(e) & (f): In order to attract more freight business, Railways have taken a number of tariff and non-tariff measures. These, include:- (i)	Setting up of Business development units to inter alia actively pursue and attract freight business for rail.(ii)	Withdrawal of 15% Busy season surcharge on railway freight for all commodities except iron ore and coal.(iii)	Concession on long lead traffic of 15 to 20% on Coal, Iron-ore and iron & steel.(iv)	Concession on short lead traffic of 10 to 50% for commodities (except Coal and iron-ore)(v)	5% discount on loaded Containers & 25% on empty Containers and waiver of stabling charges on Container rakes during COVID till 31st Oct, 2020. (vi)	Specific discounts on commodities such as fly ash.(vii)	Rationalisation of classification of industrial salt.(viii)	Making existing schemes like traditional empty flow directions, wagon investment schemes, PFT, Siding Policy simplified and made more customer friendly.(ix)	Debit/Credit scheme for Demurrage introduced for sidings.(x)	Launching of Freight Business Development Portal. *****
