                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1576






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Jan Aushadi Shops




Y. S. Avinash Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS, be pleased to state: (a) whether it is a fact that the Government has directed all the State Governments to ensure opening of Jan Aushadi Shops in the premises of every Hospital in the State with a view to provide affordable medicines to the people; (b) whether the Government has made any study on the implementation of the said order in each of the State and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the State Governments have given any feedback adhering to the directions of the Government; (d) whether some States are still lagging behind in opening of these Jan Aushadi Shops in the Hospital areas; (e) the action being taken by the Government against the States where these shops have not been opened till now; and(f) the steps being taken by the Government to ensure compliance of its orders by the State Governments?






ANSWER




MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(SHRI D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)                               (a)  	 The Government of India has requested the State Governments to provide space in government hospitals for opening of Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendras (PMBJKs).(b) to (e)   In pursuance to the request made by Government of India, various State Governments have responded and have provided space in government hospitals for PMBJKs.  A state-wise list of PMBJKs functional in Government premises is enclosed as Annexure-I. (f)  The Bureau of Pharma Public Sector Undertakings of India (BPPI), the implementing agency for this scheme is continuously pursuing the matter with all the State Governments.
