                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

789






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






National Ayush Mission




V. Kalanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state:(a) the salient features of the National Ayush Mission along with the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized under this mission within Tamil Nadu during the last three years and the current year;(b) the details of the targets set and achievements made so far thereunder;(c) whether the Government has encouraged the cultivation of medicinal plants especially in rural areas in the country under this mission, if so, the details along with the results thereof; and(d) whether the Government is providing any subsidy in this regard and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(DR. MUNJPARA MAHENDRABHAI)(a):	The salient features of the National AYUSH Mission are furnished at Annexure-I. The funds sanctioned/allocated, released and expenditure/utilization reported by the Government of Tamil Nadu during the last three years and the current year is furnished at Annexure-II.(b):	Under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission, the Grant-in-aid has been provided to State/UT Governments as per the proposals reflected in their State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs) based on their priority, need and as per the schematic provisions. Therefore, State-specific year-wise targets are not set up by the Central Government. However, the approved activities under the National AYUSH Mission in Tamil Nadu are furnished at Annexure-III.(c) & (d):	Government of India has implemented Medicinal Plants Component under NAM from 2015-16 to 2020-21. Under NAM, Ministry of AYUSH has supported market driven cultivation of 140 prioritized Medicinal Plants in identified cluster/zones in mission mode throughout the country including rural areas. The scheme was implemented through state implementing agencies. Till date, Ministry of AYUSH has supported 56305 hectare area under cultivation of medicinal plants including in rural areas, 220 nurseries, 354 post-harvest management units. Under Medicinal Plants component of National AYUSH Mission, Ministry of AYUSH has provided subsidy @ 30%, 50% and 75% as plant specific cost for cultivation of 140 prioritized medicinal plants on farmer’s land throughout the country from the F.Y. 2015-16 to 2020-21.
