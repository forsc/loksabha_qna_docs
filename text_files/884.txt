                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

805






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Setting up New Regiments




Pushpendra Singh Chandel














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has received any proposals for raising new regiments in the Army;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government is considering to raise Bundelkhand Regiment;(d) if so, the details thereof along with the time by which the said Regiment is likely to be raised; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF DEFENCE                                    (SHRI RAJNATH SINGH)      j{kk ea=h    			           ¼Jh ?????? ???? )(a)  to (e) : Yes Sir, Private Member Bills have been mentioned in the Parliament on several occasions.	As per Government policy on the subject all citizens irrespective of their class, creed, region or religion are eligible for enrolment in the Indian Army.  After independence, it has been the policy of the Government of India not to raise any new Regiment for a particular class/community/religion or region.******
