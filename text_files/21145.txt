                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2470






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Installed Capacity of PSEs




E. T. Mohammed Basheer














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that the major Public Sector Enterprises (PSEs) are not fully utilizing their installed capacity; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to ensure the optimum utilisation of installed capacities of PSEs in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI ARJUN RAM MEGHWAL)(a) to (c) The Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) function under different sectors having varied working conditions and are governed by sectoral rules, regulations and statutes. CPSEs in sectors like Crude Oil, Petroleum (Refinery & Marketing), Fertilizers, Power, etc. have been functioning mostly by utilizing their full capacity. Further, DPE has taken initiatives to make CPSEs profitable by bringing changes in MoU system for evaluation and monitoring the performance of CPSEs, granting autonomy for financial and functional empowerment of CPSEs through Ratna scheme and Capacity Building Programmes for executives and senior management of CPSEs. *****
