                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3614






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






PMAY in Maharashtra




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has achieved its target in rural areas in Palgharin Maharashtra under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY);(b)	if so, the number of beneficiaries given houses under this scheme; and(c)	the details thereof, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G) is being implemented to provide assistance for construction of houses in rural areas of the country w. e. f. 1st April, 2016. The district-wise details of targets allocated, sanctions made and houses completed in State of Maharashtra including Palghar district under PMAY-G during FY 2016-17 to 2019-20 is given in Annexure. *********
