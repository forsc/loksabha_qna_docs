                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1846






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Amenities at Stations




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    the details of the construction works under Zonal Railways, regarding extension of platform shelter, mini shed, extension and raising of Platform Number 1, provision of Divyang friendly toilets and small shelter in Birur, Kadur, Chikmagalur, Udupi, Surathkal, Padubidri, Mulki, Kundapura, Murdeshwar, Mookambika Road, Honnavar, Kumta, Ankola and Karwar Stations and the passenger amenities that have been completed in these Stations (including cover shed works) since 2014? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.	***** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1846 BY KUMARI SHOBHA KARANDLAJE TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 03.07.2019 REGARDING AMENITIES AT STATIONSBirur, Kadur and Chikamagalur are Stations in Mysore division of South Western Railway where all Minimum Essential Amenities are provided as per norms.  At Birur, Kadur and Chikamagalur station, 2682, 1088 and 800 square meter of platform shelter respectively have been provided. High level platform are also available at these stations. Further, 11 stations which falls under Konkan Railways, platform sheds and high level platforms have been provided (List – Appended). Divyang Toilets are available at all these stations.*****
