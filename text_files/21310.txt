                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2521






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Shyama Prasad Mukherjee Rurban Mission




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the current status of implementation of the ‘Shyama Prasad Mukherjee Rurban Mission’ (National Rurban Mission) and the details of the funds sanctioned and released to Maharashtra;(b) 	the details of the number of projects and the villages selected under the scheme in Maharashtra, district-wise;(c) 	the number of people benefitted under the mission and the manner in which it is linked to increasing rural investment, improving rural development and employment, and addressing forced migration to urban areas; and(d) 	the level of participation of people in the selection and development of ‘Rurban Clusters’ and the extent to which the selection is based on needs, income, vulnerability and level of development?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b) Under the Shyama Prasad Mukherji Rurban Mission (SPMRM), 20 Rurban clusters have been allotted to the state of Maharashtra. Integrated Cluster Action Plans (ICAPs) for all 20 clusters have been approved. Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) for 19 clusters have also been approved and on-ground works are at various stages.The details of the number of projects and villages selected with district names in the State of Maharashtra, as per the MIS entries done by the State, is given at Annexure-1. Details of the works sanctioned and completed under SPMRM are given at Annexure-2 and status of funds released is given at Annexure-3.(c) Though the Mission is not beneficiary oriented, Gram Sabhas that form part of the clusters are involved in formulation of ICAPs and DPRs, which are further approved by the district administration and the State Level Empowered Committee (SLEC) under SPMRM. The mission focuses on developing such rural areas by provisioning of economic, social and physical infrastructure facilities. There are 21 components envisaged for each cluster which is             given at Annexure-4. Development of cluster hinges around development of spatial plans and infrastructure development. In addition works are taken up for augmentation of sustainable livelihood activities. (d) Gram Sabhas that form part of the clusters are involved in formulation of ICAPs and DPRs, which are further approved by the district administration and the State Level Empowered Committee (SLEC) under SPMRM. Cluster Selection is done finally by the State as per the guidelines based on the selection of sub-districts by the Ministry of Rural Development. Cluster selection is done based on methodology defined in the Framework of Implementation which is given at Annexure-5.*****
