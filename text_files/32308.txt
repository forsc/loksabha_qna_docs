                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3740






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Poultry Venture Capital Fund




Talari Rangaiah













                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a.	whether the Government has released funds to the States particularly to the State of Andhra Pradesh under the poultry venture capital fund;b.	if so, the details of amount of funds sanctioned, released and expenditure incurred, number of entrepreneurs benefitted so far, State-wise; andc.	whether the Government has undertaken additional steps to enhance the entrepreneurial skills of the beneficiaries, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) The Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying was implementing the Entrepreneurship Development and Employment Generation (EDEG), a component of National Livestock Mission (NLM). Poultry Venture Capital Fund was implemented till June, 2021 as an activity under the EDEG. However, the erstwhile NLM Scheme has been realigned and approved on 14.07.2021. (b) The Year-wise and State wise of subsidy distributed under EDEG including for Poultry Venture Capital Fund through NABARD is at Annexure-I. (c) Government has taken many initiatives to develop entrepreneurial scheme. The Department of Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing Rs. 15000 crore, Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund (AHIDF) Scheme which has been set up under Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan for incentivizing investments by individual entrepreneurs, private companies, MSME, Farmers Producers Organizations (FPOs) and section 8 companies to establish (i) the dairy processing and value addition infrastructure, (ii) meat processing and value addition infrastructure and (iii) Animal Feed Plant 4) Breed improvement technology and Breed multiplication farm. The Department of Animal Husbandry & Dairying, Government of India is implementing the scheme of National Livestock Mission from 2021-22. The main focus of the revised scheme of National Livestock Mission (NLM) aims towards employment generation, entrepreneurship development, increase in per animal productivity and thus targeting increased production of meat, goat milk, egg and wool under the umbrella scheme Development Programme. The central Poultry Development Organizations, Central Sheep Breeding Farm, Hissar are carrying out Training programmes to increase skill of entrepreneurs in the field of Poultry, Sheep. The Department is also assisting State Government to impart training on Poultry, Sheep, Goat, Pig and feed and fodder farming to enhance technical knowledge and entrepreneurship development.
