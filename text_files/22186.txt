                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3309






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Suvidha Sanitary Napkins




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state: (a) the measures taken by the Government to increase awareness among the public regarding the availability of Suvidha sanitary napkins at subsidized prices at Janaushadhi Kendras; (b) whether the Government has any plan to increase the production of Suvidha sanitary napkins and if so, the details thereof; (c) the details of safeguards being observed to maintain quality standards in respect of Suvidha sanitary napkins; (d) the details of units/centres established for availability of suvidha napkins, State-wise including in Andhra Pradesh; and (e) the details of units/centres established for availability of suvidha napkins in Srikakulam district of Andhra Pradesh? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(SHRI D.V. SADANANADA GOWDA)(a) Janaushadhi Suvidha Oxo-Biodegradable Sanitary Napkin Suvidha sanitary napkins were introduced under the Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP) @ Rs. 2.50 per pad in the year 2018. The price per pad has since been reduced to Rs.1/-. Till date, more than 11 crore pads have been sold at more than 7,500 Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendras (PMBJKs). The awareness about the Scheme, including Janaushadhi Suvidha Oxo-Biodegradable Sanitary Napkins, is spread through advertisements in Print, Radio, TV, Cinema halls as well as outdoor publicity like Hoardings, Bus Queue Shelter branding, Bus branding, Auto wrapping, etc. The social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube, etc. are also widely used for spreading awareness. (b) The government is geared up to meet the demand of sanitary napkins throughout the country. Bureau of Pharma PSUs of India (BPPI), the implementing agency of PMBJP has entered into rate contract with four vendors for procurement of approx. 15 crore pads per annum. This quantity is tentative and can be increased as per the demand of Suvidha Sanitary Napkins. (c)   Janaushadhi Suvidha Sanitary Napkins are environmentally friendly.  These pads are made with Oxo-biodegradable material complying with ASTM D-6954 (biodegradability test) standards and pH of absorbent material complying with IS:5405-1980 standards. (d)  Suvidha Sanitary Napkins are available in all Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendras (PMBJKs) functional across the country including Andhra Pradesh.   As on 10th March, 2021, 7507 PMBJKs are functional covering all the districts of the country.  State/UT- wise list of PMBJKs functional is enclosed as Annexure. (e)  As on 10th March, 2021, 4 PMBJKs are functional in Srikakulam district of Andhra Pradesh.  Suvidha Sanitary Napkins are available for sale in these Kendras.
