                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3130






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






AYUSH Institutes at National Level




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)   be pleased to state: (a) the details of the national level institutes under AYUSH; (b) the details of the status and functioning of each such national institute, institute-wise; (c) whether large number of posts are lying vacant in these institutes; (d) if so, the number of posts lying vacant along with the total number of posts and the time since when these posts are lying vacant; and (e) the steps taken by the Government to fill these vacant posts?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a): There are following 11 National Institutes under the Ministry of AYUSH: (i)  National Institute of Ayurveda (NIA)-Jaipur(ii) National Institute of Unani Medicine (NIUM)-Bangalore(iii)National Institute of Naturopathy (NIN)-Pune(iv)National Institute of Homoeopathy (NIH)-Kolkata(v)All India Institute of Ayurveda(AIIA)-New Delhi(vi)Rashtriya Ayurveda Vidyapeeth (RAV) -New Delhi(vii)Morarji Desai National Institute of Yoga(MDNIY)-New Delhi(viii)North Eastern Institute of Ayurveda & Homoeopathy(NEIAH)-Shillong(ix)North Eastern Institute of Folk Medicine(NEIFM)-Pasighat(x)National Institute of Siddha(NIS)-Chennai(xi)Institute of Post Graduate Teaching and Research in Ayurveda(IPGTRA)-Jamnagar(b):	These National Institutes are autonomous organizations under the Ministry of AYUSH   and are registered under the Societies Registration Act in the respective State. However,IPGTRA-Jamnagar is governed under  the Gujarat Ayurved University Act, 1965. Details on  functioning of these Institutes are given in Annexure.  (c) & (d): There are total 1472 sanctioned posts in these 11 National Institutes, against which 490 are vacant. (e):	Recruitment is done from time to time to fill up the vacant posts as per the method of recruitment laid in the Recruitment Rules. The process to fill up the vacant posts has already been initiated  by respective Institutes by giving advertisements, etc.………………..
