                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1778






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Minority Scholarships in Kerala




 Kunhalikutty P.K.














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) the details of applications received and the amount sanctioned for the scheme of Pre-Matric, Post-Matric and Merit-cum -means Scholarships implemented for minorities in Kerala for the last five years;(b) whether the State Government has fully utilized the amount and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c) whether any amount is in arrear to the State Government under this scheme and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a):	The details of scholarships awarded and the amount sanctioned for the scheme of Pre-Matric, Post-Matric and Merit-cum-Means Scholarships for minorities in Kerala during the last five years are as follows: Scheme	Pre-Matric	Post-Matric	Merit-cum-MeansYear	Scholarships awarded	Expenditure (In Rs. Crore) 	Scholarships awarded	Expenditure (In Rs. Crore) 	Scholarships awarded	Expenditure (In Rs. Crore) 2014-19	2996028345.50324735139.55110906307.33(b) & (c):	The Pre-Matric, Post-Matric and Merit-cum-Means based scholarships are disbursed directly in the account of beneficiaries through Direct Benefit Transfer mode. As such, there is no pending amount for utilization on this count. However, for implementation of these Scholarship Schemes, the States are given administrative expenses at the rate of 1% under Pre-Matric and 2% under Post Matric and Merit-cum-Means, of the total expenditure made under the Schemes. Under Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme, the Utilization Certificate for Rs. 32.00 Lakh is pending from Kerala under Administrative Expenses. *****
