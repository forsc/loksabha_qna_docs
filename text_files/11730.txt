                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

624






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Warship Manufacturing and Testing Centres




Feroze Varun Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government’s plan of building multiple warship manufacturing and testing centres along the coastline of Andhra Pradesh has begun and if so, the details thereof;(b) the number of centres proposed to be created and total allocation of funds for the centre;(c) the terms and conditions of Foreign Direct Investment (FDI ) for the private or foreign partnerships under the scheme along with the details of the entities that are in collaboration with the Government in this regard;(d) whether this will help in bringing down the imports and increasing the FDI inflow along with any formal assessment made of the said policy to find out its impact; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER
