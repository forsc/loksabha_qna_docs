                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4626






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Corporate NPAs




Annpurna Devi














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the percentage of corporate sector in the amount sanctioned for NPA and write off during the last three years;(b)	the percentage of the farmers in the said accounts during the said period;(c)	whether corporate sector gets more benefit from the NPA accounts and write off rules;(d)	if so, whether any enquiry has been got conducted regarding the procedure of sanctioning the amount for NPA and write off; and(e)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE (a) and (b): Reserve Bank of India has informed that required data are not maintained by it.(c): No, sir. As borrowers of loans declared non-performing asset (NPA) and written-off continue to be liable for repayment and the process of recovery of dues from the borrower continues, NPA declaration and write-off does not benefit the borrower.(d) and (e): Questions do not arise.*****
