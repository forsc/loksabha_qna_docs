                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Temples Conserved by ASI




Jual Oram













                                                    Nishikant  Dubey
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the  details  of  historical    monuments    and    temples  conserved   by Archaeological   Survey  of  India (ASI)  in  the   country,  State/UT-wise including Jharkhand and Odisha;(b)	the amount of funds spent for the purpose during each of the last three years, monument-wise, temple-wise including ancient temples of Lord Jagannath, Lingaraj Temple and Konark Temple;  and (c)	the other steps taken by the Government to protect and conserve these temples in the country including the State of Jharkhand and Odisha?






ANSWER
