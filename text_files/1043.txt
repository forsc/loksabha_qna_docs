                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1044






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Incentives to Sportspersons




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is an increase of Rs. 4.77 crores for the incentives to the sportspersons and if so, the details thereof;(b) the number of additional sportspersons who can be covered in the increased amount of Rs. 4.77 crores; and(c) whether incentives and the sports facilities available are enough to motivate the sportspersons to elevate their quality and standard to compete in the international arena and bring results and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[KIREN RIJIJU](a) No Sir. The increase is Rs. 26.00 crore. Budgetary allocation for incentives to sportspersons in the Interim budget this year is Rs. 87.00 crore compared to Rs. 61.00 crore in the previous year.(b) All eligible sportspersons are being provided cash awards, meritorious pension as per existing policy and guidelines. In view of inadequate provisions last year money for distributing cash awards to medal winners was borrowed from the National Sports Development Fund and the same would be reimbursed out of allocation of the current year.(c) While augmentation of sports infrastructure / sports facilities for high performance at international arena is a continuing process , the extant infrastructure, facilities and incentives being provided by the Central Government have been motivating sportspersons to excel in international sports competitions.***
