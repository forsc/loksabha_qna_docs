                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

995






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Agriculture Summit




Sanjay Bhatia














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	 whether  any Agriculture Summit has been organized at Central or State level and is so, the details thereof;(b)	   whether the government proposes to organize an Agriculture Summit in Karnal, Haryana so that the discussion on empowering the farmers as well as on various aspects of agriculture –price chain, global knowledge and advance technologies may be   held and an ideal platform may be provided to farmers, scientists, agriculture sector entrepreneurs and progressive farmers for knowledge sharing and networking; and(c)	 if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????I? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	In the F.Y. 2021-22, following National Conferences were held in Krishi Bhawan, New Delhi with the States joining through Video Conferencing, namely, i)	Kharif Campaign, 2021 on 30.04.2021, ii)	Rabi Campaign 2021 on 21.09.2021 and iii)	Summer/Zaid Campaign on 27.01.20222.  The Conference of Lieutenant Governors/Administrators of all UTs was conducted on 15.09.2021 at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi to review the Agriculture sector of Union Territories.3.  At regional level, during the financial year 2020-21, the following Regional Conferences were held:-i.	South Region - Regional Agriculture Fair (RAF) organised from 08-12 February, 2021  at ICAR-IIHR, Bengaluru.ii.	North East Region - Regional Agriculture Fair (RAF) organised from 08-10, March, 2021, at Central Agriculture University,  Imphal, Manipur. iii.	North Region - Regional Agriculture Fair (RAF) organised from 20-22 February, 2021 at Banda University of   Agriculture   and Technology,  Banda, U.P.iv.	East Region- RAF organized  from 05-07 March, 2021 at Birsa Agriculture University,   Ranchi 4.    Following Exhibition/Mela were also held:i)	Virtual Agro-Industrial Exhibition 2020 was held during 13-14 Oct.2020 at Chaudhary Charan Singh Haryana Agriculture University (CCSHAU) Hisar, Haryana.ii)	Pusa Krishi Vigyan Mela was held from 25 to 27 February, 2021 at IARI, Pusa, New Delhi.5.   Apart from the above conferences/fairs/seminars etc. are held at different levels in the country. 6.   At present there is no proposal to organize Agriculture Summit at Karnal in Haryana*****
