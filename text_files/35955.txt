                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3197






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Allocation of Houses under PMAY-G in Bihar




Ashok Kumar Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has proposed to start work of next phase under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana Gramin (PMAY-G);(b) 	if so, the details thereof and the time by when it is likely to be started in Bihar particularly in villages of Madhubani and Darbhanga districts;(c) 	if not, the reasons therefor;(d) 	whether the names of villages of Madhubani and Darbhanga have been finalized under the above mentioned yojana and if so, the details thereof and the time by which the houses are proposed to be allocated;(e) 	if not, the reasons therefor; and(f) 	the number of houses proposed to be allocated in Madhubani and Darbanga districts of Bihar in the next phase during the coming next three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) to (f): In order to achieve “Housing for All” in rural areas, the Ministry of Rural Development is implementing the Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) with effect from 1st April, 2016 to provide assistance to eligible rural households with an overall target to construct 2.95 crore pucca houses with basic amenities by March, 2024. As per the information available, the State Government of Bihar has finalized the names of the villages to be covered in the Madhubani and Darbanga districts of Bihar under PMAY-G. The total number of villages covered is 1338 in 330 Gram Panchayats (GPs) in 18 Blocks of Darbhanga district and 1307 villages in 399 GPs in 21 Blocks of Madhubani district. The targets allocated to these villages by the State can be seen on PMAY-G website on www.pmayg.nic.in -----> AwaasSoft----> Reports----->Annual target and allocation.The targets under PMAY-G are set annually on the basis of availability of eligible beneficiaries, achievements of targets allocated during the preceding years, and proposal of the State/UT. After allocation of annual targets, further distribution of targets among beneficiaries in various districts/blocks/villages is done by the States/UTs on the basis of the eligible number of beneficiaries in the Permanent Wait List (PWL). The Ministry has already allocated a full target of 38,83,611 houses to Bihar, including 11,49,947 from the Awaas+ list. Out of this, 36,07,361 houses have been sanctioned to the beneficiaries. Balance 2,76,250 houses remain to be sanctioned by the State to the beneficiaries.
