                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

883






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Shortage of Water for Toilets




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware about the shortage of water supply in the toilets made under Swachh Bharat Abhiyaan and if so, the reasons therefor; and(b) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to ensure/facilitate effective implementation of the said abhiyaan?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b) 	Under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin), the incentive for individual toilet has been increased from Rs.10,000 to Rs.12,000 to provide for water availability, including for storing water for hand-washing and cleaning.  Under this programme, Government promotes twin-leach pit toilets and rural pans.  Because of their special design, the rural pans require less water (1-2 liters) for flushing.
