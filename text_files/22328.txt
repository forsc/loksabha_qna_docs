                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3500






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Exploration of New Regional Coal Blocks




Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















a.	the total number of new regional coal blocks explored during the last two years, State-wise;b.	the total length of drilling to explore new coal blocks during the last one year;c.	the total quantity of new resources added during the last one year; State-wise; andd.	the total length of non-Coal India Limited (CIL) blocks explored and the total drilling of the same during the last two years? 






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a):  The State wise detail of regional coal blocks in which drilling carried out during 2018-19 & 2019-20 by Central Mine Planning and Design Institute (CMPDI) are given below:   State	Number of Blocks during 2018-19	Number of Blocks during 2019-20Andhrapradesh	1	1ASSAM	-	1Chattisgarh	9	3Madhya Pradesh	5	4Nagaland	1	1Odisha	1	2Jharkhand	1	-Total	18	12 (b):  The total length of 82,092 meter drilling was carried out in 2019-20 by CMPDI to explore the new coal blocks. (c):   The total new coal resources added by CMPDI through submitted Geological Reports, during the year 2019-20, state-wise is given below:  Sl. No	State	Block	Total Resources Estimated(Million tonnes)1	Chattisgarh	Kotmer	656.492	Chattisgarh	Rajadahi	2924.503	Chattisgarh	Pipraul	188.614	Chattisgarh	Taulipalli	4920.125	Odisha	Baratap	1013.796	Madhya Pradesh	Bandha Bara	41.61 	Total	 	9745.12  (d):   The total length drilling in Non-CIL coal blocks in 2018-19 & 2019-20 are given below: Year	Number of blocks	Drilling  (Meter)2018-19	41	4836092019-20	56	670153 -----
