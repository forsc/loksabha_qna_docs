                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5401






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Foreign Airlines Operating in India




Manish Tewari














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of foreign Airlines that are operating in India;(b) the number of passengers for which they are entitled to carry into India daily in terms of various Bilateral Air Service Agreements and also the number of out board passengers, Indian carriers are entitled to carry to various countries, country-wise;(c) the details of capacity utilisation as on 30th June 2019 by these foreign airlines and Indian airlines as per bilateral air service agreements, country/carrier-wise;(d) the details of each Bilateral Air Service Agreements signed during the last five years; and(e) the number of passengers carried out of India by the UAE based/registered/ owned/airlines to various destinations and the number of passengers carried out by Indian carriers into India during the past five years?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) The details of foreign Airlines that are operating in India is at Annexure-I.;(b) The details of number of passengers for which they are entitled to carry into India daily in terms of various Bilateral Air Service Agreements and also the number of out board passengers, Indian carriers are entitled to carry to various countries, country-wise is at Annexure-II.;(c) The details of capacity utilisation as on 30th June 2019 by these foreign airlines and Indian airlines as per bilateral air service agreements, country/carrier-wise is at Annexure-III.;(d) The details of each Bilateral Air Service Agreements signed during the last five years is at Annexure-IV.;(e) The details of number of passengers carried out of India by the UAE based/registered/ owned/airlines to various destinations and the number of passengers carried out by Indian carriers into India during the past five years is at Annexure-V.
