                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1959






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






PMGSY in West Bengal




Dilip Ghosh














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of roads constructed under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in West Bengal so far, since 2015;(b) 	the number of roads proposed to be constructed under PMGSY during 2019-20 in West Bengal; and(c) 	the details of funds provided to West Bengal under PMGSY since 2015 along with the pattern of fund sharing and the utilisation of these funds thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):The details of roads constructed under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY)  in West Bengal since 2015 is as under:-Year (s)	No. of roads constructed	Length constructed (km)2015-16	                517	                     2466.152016-17	                482	                     2825.532017-18	                298	                     3213.102018-19	                649	                     5111.222019-20 (as on 26.2.2020)	746	                     1704.21 (b):The State of West Bengal has been given a construction target of  2,600 km of road length under PMGSY during the financial year 2019-20. (c):The PMGSY was launched as 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme in the year 2000. However, since the year 2015-16, the fund sharing pattern of PMGSY has been 60:40 between the Centre and the States for all States except North Eastern and 3 Himalayan States/UTs (Jammu & Kashmir,Ladakh, Himachal Pradesh and Uttarakhand) for which it is 90:10.The details of Central funds provided to West Bengal under PMGSY since 2015  and  utilisation thereof is as under:-Year (s)	            Fund Released              Fund Utilized                            (Rs. in crore)               (Rs. in crore)@	 2015-16	                     1427.58	                  1243.332016-17	                      819.18	                  1055.282017-18	                     1000.00	                  1253.132018-19	                     1386.44	                  2058.282019-20                       237.83	                  1323.15(as on 26.02.2020)	                 @ Including Central Share, State Share and unspent balance of the previous years
