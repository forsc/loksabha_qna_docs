                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3047






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Homoeopathic College




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)  be pleased to state: (a) whether any study has been conducted regarding number of homoeopathic colleges required in the country to meet the demand/necessity, if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government proposes to consider establishing adequate number of homoeopathic colleges in the country including district Gopalganj of Bihar; and (c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):    No study has been conducted regarding number of homoeopathic colleges required in the country to meet the demand/necessity as public health is a State subject. The Central Government approves eligible proposals received and recommended from the State Government.  (b)  & (c):    No proposal has been received by the Ministry for establishment of homeopathic College at Gopalganj, Bihar.…………………..
