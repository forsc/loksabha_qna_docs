                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

279






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Surprise Inspection of NH Projects




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande













                                                    Annpurna Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of National Highway projects for which surprise inspections have been undertaken by the vigilance wing of the Ministry and the National Highways Authority of India during the last three years, project/ location-wise; (b)	the details of irregularities found along with the action taken by the Government in this regard; and(c)	the outcome of the aforesaid action taken by the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) :	A statement is laid on the table of the house.
