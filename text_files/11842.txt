                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

852






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






PMAY Houses in Tamil Nadu




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of houses constructed under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) in the State of Tamil Nadu particularly in Namakkal District during the last three years.(b)	the total number of constructed houses that were handed over to the beneficiaries in Namakkal District during the last three years; and(c)	the total number of houses under construction and the time by which these are likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFIARS                                 (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) & (b) Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY (U)], a total of 1,98,186 houses (including 5,760 houses in Namakkal district) have been constructed in the State of Tamil Nadu during last three years. Of the constructed houses in Namakkal district, 4,905 houses have been handed over to the beneficiaries during last three years. Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Gramin [PMAY-G], a total of 2,11,929 houses (including 3,237 houses in Namakkal district) have been constructed and handed over to the beneficiaries in the State of Tamil Nadu during last three years.(c) In Tamil Nadu, 2,87,568 houses are under construction (including 5,358 in Namakkal district) under PMAY (U). Construction of houses sanctioned under the scheme is to be completed within the period (ranging from 1 to 2 years) as mentioned in the respective Detailed Project Reports (DPRs).	Under PMAY-G, 1,69,442 houses are under construction in Tamil Nadu and timeline for completion of these houses is 31 March 2022.                                            *****
