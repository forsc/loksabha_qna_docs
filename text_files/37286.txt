                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4311






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






New Astro Turfs for Hockey Players




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Sports Authority of India (SAI) has granted approval for laying of new astro turfs to provide better facilities to the young hockey players in the country including Maharashtra;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR) (a) to (c): Sports being as a State Subject, the responsibility to promote sports, including creating of sports infrastructure, primarily rests with the respective State/UT Government. However, Central Government supplements their efforts through its schemes viz. sports promotional schemes run by Sports Authority of India (SAI) and Khelo India Scheme.  The details of new and existing Synthetic Hockey Turf in various Sports Authority of India (SAI) Centres across the country including State of Maharashtra are at Annexure.*****
