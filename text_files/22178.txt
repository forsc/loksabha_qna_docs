                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3306






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






PM-Kisan Scheme for Fishermen




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government plans to include fishermen in the PM-KISAN scheme;  (b) if so, the details of the fishermen who are eligible to avail the said scheme;  (c) the funds to be allocated, released and utilized for the purpose during each year, State-wise; and (d) the benefits likely to accrue as a result of implementation of this scheme/programmes?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI) (a) to (d):  The PM-KISAN scheme is implemented by Ministry of Agriculture and Farmer Welfare, Government of India to provide income support to land holding farmers’ families  in the country having cultivable land.  For supporting fishers, fish farmers and other stakeholders, the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Fisheries Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a flagship scheme “Pradhan Mantri Matsya SampadaYojana (PMMSY) with an highest ever investment of Rs 20050 crores under which, inter alia, livelihood support @ Rs. 4500/- per fisherman family (including beneficiary’s contribution of Rs. 1500/- and Rs3000/- as central and state government share) is provided during fish ban/lean period for socio-economically backward traditional fishermen families of both marine and inland. *****
