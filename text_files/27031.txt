                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2718






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Sainik School in memory of a PVC Awardee




Arun Kumar Sagar














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to set up a Sainik School in the memory of Paramvir Chakra award winner Naik Shri Jadunath Singh born at village Khajuri, Tehsil-Kalan of Shahjahanpur district in Uttar Pradesh;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether the Government has decided to set up Sainik Schools in various other districts of the country and if so, the details thereof, STate/UT-wise and District-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI AJAY BHATT)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) to (c): Sainik Schools under existing pattern, are established on receipt of a request from the State Government who also need to agree to provide land alongwith funds for the creation and maintenance of basic infrastructure, purchase of equipment and other facilities besides providing scholarships for the cadets belonging to the State Government.  Once land is found suitable and other requirements are agreed to, an         in-principle approval is accorded, followed by signing of a Memorandum of Agreement (MoA).	The Government has announced in Budget 2021-22 a scheme for setting up 100 new Sainik schools in the country in partnership with NGOs/Private Schools/States etc.  Once the scheme is approved, new Schools will be set up accordingly.*****
