                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3335






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






POSHAN Abhiyan




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has any plan to hold a Conference of Ministers of Women and Child Development of all State Governments to deliberate on solutions related to malnutrition among women and children and to assess the progress of POSHAN Abhiyan;(b)	if so, the details in this regard; and (c)	if not, the steps being taken to ensure that women and children are not malnourished in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	to (c) There is presently no plan to hold a Conference of Ministers of Women and Child Development of all State Governments to deliberate on solutions related to malnutrition among women and children and to assess the progress of POSHAN Abhiyaan. However, in the last ten monthsMinister of Women and Child Development has reviewed POSHAN Abhiyaan with 16 Chief Ministers; Secretary, Women and Child Development has similarly reviewed POSHAN Abhiyaan with 10 Chief Secretaries apart from review of status in States / UTs through video conference, conferences, meeting etc.The Government is implementing several schemes/programmes of different Ministries/Departments through States/UTs to address various aspects related to health and nutrition. This Ministry is implementing POSHAN Abhiyaan, Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana, Anganwadi Services and Scheme for Adolescent Girls under the Umbrella Integrated Child Development Services Scheme (ICDS) to address the problem of malnutrition in the country. *****
