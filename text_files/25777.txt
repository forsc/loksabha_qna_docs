                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1419






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Disinvestment in Coal Sector




Dibyendu Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) whether the Government has identified coal mine projects and/or collieries for disinvestment purposes; b)   if so, reports of the expert committee and the proposal details of the Government and the reaction from different PSUs and federations therein; and c)   the detail of reserves of coal in such areas and FDI on commercial mining licences with regulatory authority given to private companies and MoU signed thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF COAL, MINES AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a)               Government has not identified any coal mine projects and/or collieries for disinvestment purpose. (b& c )               Does not arise in view of (a) above. *****
