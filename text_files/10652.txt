                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3940






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Recycling of Ships Bill




Jayanta Kumar Roy














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has approved the proposal for enactment of Recycling of Ships Bill, 2019 ;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government has proposed accession to the Hong Kong International Convention for Safe and Environmentally Sound Recycling of Ships, 2009;(d) 	if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	the other steps being taken by the Government for development and regulation of ship breaking and recycling in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)    Yes Sir. The Recycling of Ships Bill, 2019 has been passed by Lok Sabha on 03.12. 2019 and by Rajya Sabha on 09.12.2019.(c)and (d)  Yes Sir. India has acceded to the Hong Kong International Convention for Safe and Environmentally Sound Recycling of Ships, 2009 on 28.11.2019.(e)   Government has issued the Shipbreaking Code (Revised), 2013 to regulate the ship breaking and recycling in the country.                                           ******
