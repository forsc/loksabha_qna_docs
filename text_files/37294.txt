                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4318






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






PMAY in Uttar Pradesh




Dharmendra Kumar Kashyap














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) approved by Government for Uttar Pradesh from 2018-19 to 2021-22 and the amount of funds provided to the State for this yojana, year-wise;(b) 	the number of beneficiaries whose first, second, third and fourth installments have not been paid till date during the said period; and(c) 	the number of houses completed and those remained incomplete during the said period?






ANSWER
