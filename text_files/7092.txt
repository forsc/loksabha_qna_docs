                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

859






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Status of SCM




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Smart Cities Mission (SCM) Phase-1 will conclude in 2019 in accordance with original schedule;(b)	if so, the details of targets set and completed so far, city-wise, in terms of funds and progress of work;(c)	whether the Government is considering expanding the Smart Cities   Mission further; and(d)	 if so, timeline and criteria for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Government of India launched Smart Cities Mission (SCM) on 25th June, 2015 for developing 100 cities as Smart Cities over a period of five years from their respective dates of selection. These Cities were selected in following rounds:Round	         No. of Cities selected	              Month, Year1st	             20	                              January, 2016Fast Track	     13	                              May, 20162nd	             27	                              September, 20163rd	             30	                              June, 20174th	             10	                              9 cities in January, 2018 &                                                      1 city in June, 2018(b)The implementation of the Smart Cities Mission is being done by a city level Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV). The SPVs plan, appraise, approve, implement, manage, operate, monitor and evaluate their smart City projects. The details of the tenders/work orders issued and completed by the Smart Cities is given in Annexure-I.(c) & (d) There is no proposal under consideration for expansion of the Smart Cities Mission.                                   ……
