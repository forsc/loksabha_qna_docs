                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2831






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Financial Assistance for Drinking Water Projects




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has allocated any funds for Tamil Nadu for carrying out drinking water supply projects across the State keeping in view of the backdrop of perennial water shortage problem and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government has received any request from Tamil Nadu in June, 2019 for the allocation of 5400 crore rupees for drinking water projects across the State; (c) if so, whether the Government has decided to disburse any funds and if so, the details thereof; and (d) the total amount of central funds given to the State of Tamil Nadu for enhancing the capacity of drinking water during each of the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a to c) Government of Tamil Nadu requested for financial assistance inter alia seeking ?5,398 Crore as Central assistance. Meanwhile, Government of India has launched Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) with an outlay of Rs. 3.60 lakh Crore with Central share of Rs. 2.08 lakh Crore, to provide Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) to every rural household by 2024. Under JJM, funds are allocated to the States as per approved criteria and ?302.89 Crore, out of which ?151.45 Crore has been released as first instalment during 2019-20 for carrying out drinking water supply projects across the state. Moreover, once the State utilizes the available funds, it can seek 2nd instalment and after its utilization and reporting performance, the State can also ask for additional fund to be provided based on functionality assessment of household tap connections.(d) The total amount of central funds given to the State of Tamil Nadu under erstwhile National Rural Drinking Water Programme (now JJM) for rural water supply during the last five years is as under:								(Amount? in Crores)Year	Central Release2014-15	382.462015-16	150.352016-17	174.682017-18	185.102018-19	168.31***
