                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4256






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Status of Road Projects




Rajdeep Roy














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of Silchar-Guwahati four-lane expressway via Haflong; (b)	the present status of Silchar bypass (Ram Nagar ISBT to Sonabarighat to Kashipur);(c)	the present status of road from Balachera to Englabari via Kathakhal; and (d)	the time frame set for completion of work on the said projects?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	Four laning of Silchar-Guwahati National Highway via Haflong  has been completed substantially. However,  Jatinga-Harangajao section and Narimbanglo-Jatinga Section were affected due to natural calamities for which Detailed Project Report has been started. Morever, the work for development to 4-lane of Balachera-Harangjao section of NH-54 has also been started. (b)	The work of construction of Silchar bypass (Ram Nagar ISBT to Sonabarighat to Kashipur) is envisaged for foreclosure due to poor performance of Contractor.(c)	Balachera to Englabari via Kathakhal stretch has been taken up for upgradation. The stretch from Balachera to Badarpur via Kathakhal and has already been completed and preparation of Detailed Project Report from Badarpur to Englabari has been taken up.(d)	The work of the entire stretch is likely to be completed by 2023.*****
