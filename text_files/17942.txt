                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1738






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Bullet Proof Vests




K. Shanmuga Sundaram














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken immediate steps for providing bullet-proof jacket/vests for the soldiers especially gear like modular bullet proof vests or ballistic helmets;(b) if so, the total number of bullet proof vests required for the entire armed forces;(c) whether the ballistic helmets shall have internal communication gear and if not, the reasons therefor;(d) whether these bullet proof vests shall have the norms prescribed by General Staff Qualitative requirements and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the total number of casualties of soldiers till date due to the ineffective bullet proof vests?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) to (c):  Bullet Proof Jackets (BPJs) and Ballistic Helmets are provided to soldiers as per authorization and operational requirements.  The Contract for procurement of 1,86,138 BPJs and 1,58,279 Ballistic Helmets was concluded in April, 2018 and December, 2016 respectively.  Ballistic Helmets equipped with internal communication gear are also part of this procurement.(d)  BPJs are procured as per General Staff Qualitative Requirements (GSQRs) which are reviewed from time to time based on the operational requirements of the Armed Forces.(e)  It is difficult to ascertain every time the clear cause for casualty of soldiers attributable to BPJs.*****
