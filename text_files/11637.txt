                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

521






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Infrastructure for Digital India Programme and Broadband Connectivity




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of expenditure incurred on the development of basic infrastructure under the Digital India Programme across the country in last financial year, State/ UT-wise; and (b) 	the State-wise details of the achievement made for providing broadband connectivity to approximately 2,50,000 Gram Panchayats across the country under the National Optical Fibre Network (NOFN) project?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	The details of expenditure incurred on the development of basic infrastructure related to Department of Telecommunications under Digital India Programme across the country in last financial year, State/UT-wise is at Annexure 1.(b)	A total of 1,34,248 Gram Panchayats have been made Service Ready as on 24.01.2020, State/UT-wise details are at Annexure 2.
