                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2646






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Works under PMEGP in Rajasthan




Jaskaur Meena














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the salient features of Prime Ministers Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP);(b)	the details of amount sanctioned, allotted and utilised under this programme in Rajasthan during the last three years and the current year;(c)  	whether the Government has fixed any target for the generation of employment in rural areas under the said programme;(d) 	if so, the details thereof and the achievements made in Rajasthan so far;(e)  	whether the Government has set any target to provide employment to women in rural areas under the said programme;(f)   	if so, the details thereof and if not, whether the Government proposes to do so; and(g)  	if so, the time by which it is likely to be implemented?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI BHANU PRATAP SINGH VERMA)(a): 	   Ministry of MSME is implementing Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) since 2008-09 through Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) as nodal agency at the national level for generating employment opportunities in the country by setting up micro-enterprises in non-farm sector. Under PMEGP, General Category beneficiaries can avail Margin Money (MM) subsidy of 25 % of the project cost in rural areas and 15% in urban areas. For Special Categories, the Margin Money subsidy is 35% in rural areas and 25% in urban areas.  Maximum project cost for manufacturing unit is Rs.25 lakh and for service sector is Rs.10 lakh. Eligibility conditions for beneficiaries under PMEGP:(i)       Minimum age: 18 years (ii)      Income limit: No income ceiling. (iii)     Assistance under the Scheme is available only for new projects.(iv)    Existing units and the units that have already availed Government subsidy under any other scheme of Government of India or State Government are not eligible.
