                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3313






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Third National Commission for STs




Raksha Nikhil Khadse













                                                    Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has proposed to establish the Third National Commission for Scheduled Areas & Scheduled Tribes in order to review and formulate future policy for the Scheduled Tribes and Scheduled Areas; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF STATE FOR TRIBAL AFFAIRS (SMT. RENUKA SINGH SARUTA)(a)	&(b): As  per  the  provision  of Article 339(1) of  the  Constitution  of  India“The President may at any time and shall, at the expiration of ten years from the commencement of this Constitution by order appoint a Commission to report on the administration of the Scheduled Areas and the welfare of this Scheduled Tribes in the States. The order may define the composition, powers and procedure of the Commission and may contain such incidental or ancillary provisions as the President may consider necessary or desirable”. Accordingly, the Commission gets appointed as and when needed.*****
