                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

422






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Setting -up of FPIs under PMKSY




Jual Oram













                                                    Girish Bhalchandra Bapat
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                    Pritam Gopinath Munde
                                                








                                                     Rahul Ramesh Shewale
                                                








                                                    Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has invited proposals from the States for setting up of food processing units/ industries under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) especially in Maharashtra and Odisha; (b)	if so, the details of proposals received so far from various State Governments and Entrepreneurs for setting up of food processing units; (c)	whether the Government had conducted any study in Maharashtra for setting up of FPIs, if so, the details thereof;  (d)	if not, the steps taken for setting up of FPIs in Maharashtra along with efforts made to facilitate loans and grants to encourage food processing units in Maharashtra? (e)	whether the Union Government has approved the proposals under thescheme for Creation of Infrastructure for Agro-Processing Clusters of PMKSY, if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and (f)	the details of the assistance provided to the Entrepreneurs for setting up ofthe food processing units during each of the last three years, particularly in Odisha, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a):	Government approved a Central Sector umbrella Scheme, ‘Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana (PMKSY)’ in 2017 to promote setting up, modernisation and capacity enhancement of food processing industries in the country. The PMKSY has component schemes viz; (i) Mega Food Parks, (ii) Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure, (iii) Infrastructure for Agro-Processing Clusters, (iv) Creation of Backward and Forward Linkages, (v) Creation/ Expansion of Food Processing & Preservation Capacities, (vi) Food Safety and Quality Assurance Infrastructure, (vii) Human Resources and Institutions, (viii) Operation Greens. The PMKSY is not any region specific and is implemented across the country including in the states of Maharashtra and Odisha. Ministry of Food Processing Industries has invited proposals against the Expression of Interest (EoI) from time to time for setting up of food processing units/ industries in the Country.  (b):	Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) had been implementing the Scheme for Creation/Expansion of Food Processing and Preservation Capacities (CEFPPC) under PMKSY to incentivize the setting up of Food Processing units throughout the country including Maharashtra and Odisha. Ministry have received total 1351 no. of proposal under the Scheme for Creation/Expansion of Food Processing and Preservation Capacities (CEFPPC) under PMKSY for setting up of food processing units from all over the country. The list of State-wise no. of proposals received under scheme is attached as Annexure-I. (c) & (d): MoFPI has not conducted any study for setting up of FPIs specific to Maharashtra. In order to meet the credit demand of the food processing sector, the food and agro based processing units and cold chain infrastructure have been brought under the ambit of Priority Sector Lending (PSL) by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) with effect from 23.04.2015. A special food processing fund of Rs. 2000 crore has been created with National bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) in 2014-15 for providing affordable credit to designated Food Parks and Agro Processing Units located in the designated Food parks. Further, under various schemes of the PMKSY, subsidy is provided to the investors in food processing sector to enhance their credit worthiness for bank loans to set up various food processing industries. (e):	Ministry has approved 68 agro processing clusters so far under scheme of Creation of Infrastructure for Agro-Processing clusters of PMKSY. The State-wise details of approved agro processing clusters is attached as Annexure-II. (f):	The State-wise (including Odisha) details of the assistance provided to the Entrepreneurs so far for setting up of the food processing units till date is attached at Annexure-III.*****
