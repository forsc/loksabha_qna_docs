                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3211






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Applications under Scholarship Schemes




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of applicants under scholarship schemes for Minority students of pre, post matric, higher studies, merit-cum-means and number of applications granted during the last three years in the country, scheme and year-wise;(b)	the amount of scholarships and aid granted to minorities under all schemes, year and State-wise including Uttar Pradesh during the last three years; and(c)	the number of students of minority communities who are studying in Premier institutes of the country, including total number of students studying in those institutes?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a):	The total number of applications received and number of applications/scholarships granted under Pre-Matric, Post-Matric and Merit-cum-Means based Scholarship Schemes during the last three years (2016-17 to 2018-19) is as below: Scheme	Pre-Matric, Post-Matric, & Merit-cum-MeansYear	No. of Applications 	Scholarships granted2016-17	89,80,975	49,00,4512017-18	1,01,58,909	56,11,9932018-19*	1,01,49,886	64,93,890* - Provisional Data. Disbursement of Scholarship continues for 2018-19.  (b):	The number and amount of Scholarship and aid granted to minorities under Pre-Matric, Post-Matric and Merit-cum-Means based Scholarship schemes, State-wise, including Uttar Pradesh, during the last three years (2016-17 to 2018-19) is annexed.(c):	The number of students of minority communities, studying in 85 premier institutes, as listed under Merit-cum-Means based Scholarship Scheme for Minorities, who have applied under Merit-cum-Means based Scholarship Scheme during the years 2018-19 and 2019-20 is 2,974 and 1,940 respectively.	*****
