                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

193






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Legal Auditing of Organisations




Ravneet Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that the Government has undertaken a comprehensive legal auditing of several organizations under the Ministry;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether any action is being taken by the Government based on the findings of this auditing exercise;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	if not, the reasons thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR CULTURE AND MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b): Sir, so far no Legal Auditing of any organization under this Ministry has been undertaken by this Ministry. (c) to (e): Question does not arise.*****
