                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1069






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Jobs Created in MSME Sector




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of jobs created under MSME sector, State/UT  wise, during this year and the last year, with specific details regarding the number of jobs created for women and persons from Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes; (b)	whether this Program includes any provisions for skill development and training and if so, the details thereof and;(c)	whether the Government has taken any corrective steps to address the issues of delay in sanction of loans, demand for collateral and the tendency of projects to become non-performing assets, as found by the evaluation study conducted by the Management Development Institute and the Ministry and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI  NITIN GADKARI)(a):	Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises implements Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP), which is a credit-linked subsidy programme aimed at generating self-employment opportunities. The State/UT-wise details of employment opportunities created under PMEGP during the last year and current year (up to 31st May, 2019) are given in the Annexure. (b):	Entrepreneurship Development Programme (EDP) is mandatory for every PMEGP beneficiary before the release of loan. The objective of EDP is to provide orientation and awareness pertaining to various managerial and operational functions like finance, production, marketing, enterprise management, banking etc. EDP is conducted through training centers of govt. agencies such as KVIC, KVIB, NSIC, NIMSME, NIESBUD and RSETIs. (c):	The implementation of the Scheme during 12th Five Year Plan has been assessed with the help of Management Development Institute (MDI), Gurugram. Based on the recommendations of the evaluation study, the scheme has been revised.
