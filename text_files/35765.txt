                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3079






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Integrated Check Posts




Sukanta Majumdar














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has accorded approval for preparation of Detailed Engineering Report (DER) for developing 10 Integrated Check Posts (ICPs) in the country including one at Hili in West Bengal;(b) if so, whether the Land Ports Authority of India (LPAI) has completed the acquisition process and submitted DER for Hili in West Bengal;(c) if so, the details thereof along with the timeline of the project;(d) the current status and details of the ICPs being constructed along the border areas, State-wise including at Hili in West Bengal;(e) the details of facilities provided or to be provided at each Integrated Check Post (ICP); and (f) the other steps being taken by the Government for strengthening of ICPs in the country?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS (SHRI NISITH PRAMANIK)(a):	Government of India has accorded “in-principle” approval for acquisition of land and preparation of Detailed Engineering Report (DER) for establishment of 10 Integrated Check  Posts (ICPs) including one at Hili in West Bengal.-2-LS.US.Q.NO.3079 FOR 22.03.2022(b) & (c):	Several requests have been made to State Government of West Bengal for early acquisition of land parcel at seven identified locations including Hili. However, land has not been acquired till date.  In absence of land acquisition by Land Ports Authority of India (LPAI) DER has not been prepared.(d):  The current status and details of the ICPs under different stages of construction, border-wise & State-wise, is placed at Annexure “A”.(e):	Land Ports Authority of India, as an aggregator & facilitator of services essential for bilateral/international trade & passengers movement, provide requisite facilities at ICP as follows: For Trade: cargo terminal building; immigration clearance counters for drivers; import and export warehouse; cold storage facility, customs clearance counters; rummaging shed; inspection shed; drivers rest room; cargo vehicle parking facilities; quarantine building etc.For passengers:  Passengers terminal building; immigration clearance counters; public utilities cafeteria; passenger dormitory; medical room foreign exchange counters, duty free shop etc.For security forces: Electronic surveillance equipments, security and surveillance gadgets, Border Guarding Force accommodation etc.  -3-LS.US.Q.NO.3079 FOR 22.03.2022(f):	For strengthening of ICPs, Government has approved for     installation of Full Body Truck Scanner (FBTS); Installation of Radiation Detection Equipments.     LPAI constantly reviews the existing facilities and plans for strengthening of facilities to further ease the trade and passengers’ movement as well as for strengthening the security of the ICPs.  Based on the reviews, decision is taken at the level of LPAI for upgradation of facilities as per need of the particular ICP.*****
