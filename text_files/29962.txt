                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

143






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Food Security Schemes




Sajda Ahmed














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has assessed the impact of food security schemes on malnutrition situation in the country; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY, CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION AND TEXTILES(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (b): 	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (b) OF THE STARRED QUESTION NO.143 DUE FOR ANSWER ON 08.12.2021 IN THE LOK SABHA.(a)&(b):	      Significant improvement in nutritional status has been made in nutritional   outcomes   including that at state level.   The Ministry of Health and Family Welfare is conducting one integrated survey namely National Family Health Survey (NFHS) to generate data on important demographic, health and nutritional indicators. However,  no direct assessment has been made to study the  impact of Food Security Schemes. Overall improvement in nutritional outcomes including at State level can be seen from http://rchiips.org/nfhs.***
