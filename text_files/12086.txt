                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EARTH SCIENCES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

956






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






UN Decade of Ocean Science




Pocha Brahmananda Reddy













                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EARTH SCIENCES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of existing  policy relating to sustainable development  of oceans in the country;(b) 	whether the Government has initiated any steps to formulate policies in line with the United Nations Decade of Ocean Science for Sustainable Development (2021-2030): and(c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER FOR MINISTRY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY AND MINISTRY OF EARTH SCIENCES(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a)	The United Nations Convention on the Law of the Sea ratified by India in June, 1995, the Territorial Waters and Maritime Zones Act, 1976 and Ocean Policy Statement, 1981 are the primary instruments for sustainable development of oceans. Further, various domestic legislations are in place for the management and protection of the coastal and marine environment. (b)	& (c) 	Yes, Sir. India is a member of Executive Planning Group (EPG) of Intergovernmental Oceanographic Commission (IOC) for devising the action plan for UN Decade of Ocean Science for Sustainability (2021-2030). The Regional Planning workshop of the United Nations Decade of Ocean Science for Sustainable Development (2021-2030) for the Northern and Central Indian Ocean countries as well as Regional Organization for Protection of Marine Environment (ROPME) Sea area, was hosted by Ministry of Earth Sciences at Chennai, from 8-10 January, 2020.
