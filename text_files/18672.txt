                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

143






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Ineligible PM-KISAN Payments




Dulal Chandra Goswami













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Dushyant Singh
                                                








                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) 	the amount of funds disbursed under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Scheme along with the number of beneficiaries of the Scheme, State-wise;(b) 	whether undeserving beneficiaries have received payment from PM-KISAN transfers and these categories are ‘ineligible farmers’ and ‘income-tax payee farmers’, while eligible farmers have been left out; (c) 	if so, the reasons for such disbursal of funds along with the details of the amount disbursed to such beneficiaries; (d)	 the States to which such ineligible beneficiaries belong along with the number of details of the number of beneficiaries in such a state; (e) 	the steps taken by the Government to rescind such a transfer and the amount of money that has been recovered by the Government; and (f) 	the number of farmers of Katihar Parliamentary constituency in Bihar, Pratapgarh Parliamentary constituency of Uttar Pradesh and Wardha, Amravati and Nagpur of Maharashtra benefited under the Scheme and the number of people still deprived of the benefits of this scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	The amount of funds disbursed under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Scheme along with the number of beneficiaries of the Scheme, State-wise as on 22.01.2021 is placed at Annexure-I.Contd…2/--2-(b) to (e):	During the verification process, if it was found that an amount of Rs.2326.8846 crore was transferred to 40,48,049 ineligible beneficiaries including income tax payees.The state-wise list of such beneficiaries along with the amount transferred to them and the amount recovered is enclosed at Annexure-2. The list was also sent to the respective State Governments for rectification. The Union Government has issued a Standard Operation Guideline to identify such underserving beneficiary and recover amount from them.(f):	The number of beneficiaries under the Scheme from the Districts of Katihar, Pratapgarh, Warda, Amravati and Nagpur are given below:Katihar District , Bihar-                      1,84,505Pratapgarh District, Uttar Pradesh – 5,28,447Wardha District, Maharashtra -         1,55,227Amravati District, Maharashtra –      3,23,080Nagpur District, Maharashtra –        2,00,507   The eligible beneficiaries are not deprived of the benefits of PM-KISAN as it is a continuous and ongoing Scheme, and as and when data of the eligible beneficiaries are received from the States, the requisite amount is transferred to the account of beneficiaries through Direct Benefit Transfer.
