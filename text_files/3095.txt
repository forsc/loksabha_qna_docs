                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2772






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Right-Sizing of Army




Devji Mansingram Patel













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has prepared a blue-print for right-sizing the Army;(b) if so, the details and the salient features thereof; and(c) the fresh steps taken by the Government to transform the armed forces into lean, mean, rapidly deployable and integrated forces?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)    (a) to (c):  A Committee of Experts was set up by the Government in 2016 to recommend measures to enhance the Combat Capability and Rebalance Defence Expenditure of Armed Forces.  A total of 65 recommendations pertaining to Indian Army were approved for implementation.  The recommendations of Committee of Experts entails rightsizing of certain units of Indian Army, thereby optimising the resources.  It involves redeployment and restructuring of various posts like Officers/Junior Commissioned Officers/Other Ranks and Civilians, better utilization of supply and transport units, enhancement in standards of recruitment and improvement in the efficiency of National Cadet Corps.*******
