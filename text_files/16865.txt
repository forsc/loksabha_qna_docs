                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

575






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Internet Access in Rural Areas of Bihar




Mohammad Jawed














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the present status of the scheme of the Government to connect the rural areas of Bihar, particularly Kishanganj Parliamentary Constituency with internet; (b) 	whether the Government has achieved the targets fixed in this regard and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the number of villages in the State of Bihar connected with internet so far especially in the Kishanganj Parliamentary Constituency; and (d) 	whether there has reportedly been any delay in the completion of the work related to the said scheme in many areas and if so, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) & (b) 	BharatNet project implemented in a phased manner to provide internet connectivity to all the Gram Panchayats (GPs) in the country includes Bihar. In Bihar, as on 04.09.2020, out of 8963 GPs including BHQs, 8790 GPs is service ready. In Kishanganj Parliamentary Constituency all 133 GPs including BHQs is service ready.(c)	In Bihar, 38821 villages are connected with internet including 3G & 4G mobile services. This includes 1845 villages in Kishanganj Parliamentary Constituency.(d) 	Yes, Sir. Delay was due to Right of Way issues and natural calamity like heavy rains/floods etc.************
