                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

259






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Tribal Sub-Plan for Bihar




Rama Devi













                                                    Giridhari Yadav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the funds allocated by the Union Government to Bihar under the Tribal Sub-Plan; (b)	 the details of works proposed to be undertaken under the said Tribal Sub-Plan; (c)	 the amount of funds remained unutilized out of the allocation to Bihar under the Tribal Sub-Plan till date; (d) 	the reasons for non-utilisation of the said funds; (e) 	whether the Government has conducted any investigation regarding the construction works undertaken under the Tribal Sub-Plan in Bihar by an independent agency; (f) 	if so, the details thereof; and(g)	 if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (d):	Release of Tribal Sub-Plan (TSP) funds by Central Ministries / Departments to the States including Government of Bihar is a continuous process. Monitoring of Tribal Sub-Plan (TSP) has been mandated to MoTA since 2017. Funds released by Central Ministries / Departments during 2017-18 and 2018-19 to the Government of Bihar is at Annexure 1. Statement showing details of funds released to Government of Bihar and utilization reported by the State Government under the schemes of Ministry of Tribal Affairs (MoTA) during 2015-16 to 2018-19 is at Annexure 2. The State Government has not communicated the reasons for non-utilization of funds released by MoTA.
