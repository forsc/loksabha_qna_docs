                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1914






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






National Health Commission/National Medical Commission




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has formed the National Health Commission (NHC) or National Medical Commission (NMC) and whether it has taken over the Medical Council of India, if so, the details thereof along with the operational date and the details of appointment of members into the NHC or NMC;(b) whether medical colleges have to follow a different registration process since the formation of the NHC or NMC, if so, the details thereof stating variations in the old and new formats;(c) the mechanism under the NHC or NMC to monitor the functioning of medical colleges under its ambit;(d) the number of inspections conducted by NHC or NMC so far State/UT-wise and the action taken in each of the cases where the medical colleges were found flouting the norms; and(e) the details of steps taken by the NHC or NMC to ensure that interests of medical students are protected?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a): The Central Government has constituted the National Medical Commission (NMC) vide notification dated 24th September, 2020 and the Medical Council of India (MCI) has been dissolved. The NMC has started functioning with effect from 25th September, 2020 and taken over the functions, previously performed by the MCI.  The composition of the NMC is at Annexure. (b): The NMC does not register medical colleges. However, as per the NMC Act, 2019, NMC grants recognition to medical colleges for undergraduate, postgraduate and super-specialty courses. Under the NMC Act, 2019, the NMC has been mandated to frame policy and Regulations to carry out the purposes of the Act. However, the educational standards, requirements and other provision of the erstwhile Indian Medical Council Act, 1956 and regulations made thereunder would continue to be in force and operate till new standards and requirements are specified under the NMC Act, 2019. The NMC has framed the Minimum Requirement Regulations, 2020 to streamline the standards specified for the medical colleges. (c):   The NMC monitors the functioning of the medical colleges through regular assessments.(d) & (e):  The NMC carries out assessments as and when required as per Regulatory requirements; and if any deficiencies are found in a medical college, the medical college concerned is given opportunity to rectify the deficiencies. In the event of non-compliance, appropriate action as per Regulation is taken.
