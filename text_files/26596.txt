                                    CULTURE

UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2180






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Child Care Centres at ASI Protected Monuments




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a)	whether the Government has set up child care centres at various ASI protected monuments in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the names of monuments which were identified for setting up of the same and the estimated cost of the project, State-wise including Tamil Nadu; and(c)	the time by which the above facility is likely to be provided in all the ASI protected monuments in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION (SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a)	Yes, Sir.  Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) has provided child care room at various monuments. (b)&(c)	Details of child care rooms provided are at Annexure –I. Monuments identified for providing child care room during the current financial year are at Annexure-II.  Providing facilities and upgrading thereof is regular phenomenon. The expenditure for which is met from the budget of ASI.
