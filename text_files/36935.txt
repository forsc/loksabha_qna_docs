                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4019






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Underwater Archaeology Wing




D. M. Kathir Anand














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Underwater Archaeology Wing of the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) has not conducted and under water exploration during the last ten years and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether archaeological remnants belonging to Sangam period in ancient Tamil Nadu have been recovered from Tamil Nadu near Poompuhar and Korkai;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	whether ASI is willing to associate with the State Department of Archaeology, Tamil Nadu in the marine exploration works and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d)	Excavations at Poompuhar were conducted by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) between 1962 to 1968. Information about excavations carried-out at the site during different seasons has been published in “Indian Archaeology - A Review”, during corresponding years. Later, Tamil Nadu State Archaeology Department also conducted excavation at this site. Excavation at Korkai was undertaken by the Tamil Nadu State Archaeology Department and findings were also published.ASI works in collaboration with institutions/governments in the field of excavation, on the basis of mutually agreed terms and conditions.
