                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

369






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Enhancing Coal Supply




Ajay Kumar Mandal













                                                    Chandeshwar Prasad
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has received any request from the State Government of Bihar to enhance coal supply for its thermal power stations; (b) if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon; and(c) the details of coal blocks allocated to Bihar during the last year?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a): No request has been received from the State Government of Bihar to enhance coal supply for its thermal power stations. However, the Thermal Power stations of Bihar are having following Coal stock.(As on Date 27.01.21)Sl No	Name of Thermal Power Station	Capacity   (MW)	Actual Stock (in  ''000 Tonnes'')	Actual Stock (in days)1	BARAUNI TPS	710	148.75	242	BARH II	1320	308.2	163	KAHALGAON TPS	2340	731.93	264	MUZAFFARPUR TPS	610	122.57	215	NABINAGAR STPP	660	168.54	176	NABI NAGAR TPP	750	397.31	37 (b): Does not arise in view of (a) above. (c): No Coal blocks have been allocated to State Government Companies of Bihar during the last year.  *****
