                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

806






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Portable Solar Powered Cold Storage Units




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of portable solar powered cold storage units that have been installed and functioning under new and renewable energy schemes, State/UTwise;(b) whether enough steps have been undertaken by the Government to ensure that farmers have awareness and access to the said devices;(c) if so, the details thereof;(d) whether any impact study of the same have been undertaken by the Government; and(e) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY,  POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R. K. SINGH)(a) to (c) The Ministry of New & Renewable Energy (MNRE) does not have any specific scheme/programme for installation of portable solar powered cold storage units. However, under Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) of the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmer Welfare (MoAFW), few pilot projects have been done in the State of Tripura with technical support from National Institute of Solar Energy (NISE), Gurugram an Autonomous Institution of MNRE. Details of pilot projects are given in Annexure.The Ministry of Agriculture and Farmer Welfare has been requested for inclusion for solar cold storage developed by NISE under their Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH).(d) & (e) No Sir, all installations are at pilot scale and commissioned during the current year.                                                 *****
