                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4083






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Post Office Passport Seva Kendra




Krishna Pal Singh Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the amount of space provided to the Passport Department in the Head Post Office of the district to open a Post Office Passport Seva Kendra (POPSK) in each Parliamentary Constituency of the country; (b) 	the measures taken by the Government to provide space to Passport Department in the event of the unavailability of space in the post office; and (c) 	the number of districts in Madhya Pradesh where space has been provided by the post offices to open a POPSK so far? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) & (b)	As per MoU signed between Department of Posts (DoP) and Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), DoP shall provide 300 Sq. ft. to 1000 Sq. ft. space in Post Office at mutually agreed locations. Accordingly, space between 300 Sq. ft. to 1000 Sq. ft. has been provided to the Passport Department to open Post Office Passport Seva Kendra in the designated Post Offices as per availability and feasibility. As per MoU no further measures are required to be taken by the Department of Posts to provide space to the Passport Department in the event of the unavailability of the same in the concerned Post Offices. (c) 	17 districts of Madhya Pradesh have Post Office Passport Seva Kendras functioning at Post Offices**********
