                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3306






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Van Bandhu Kalyan Yojana in Maharashtra




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has received any proposal from Maharashtra Government relating to Van Bandhu Kalyan Yojana and conservation cum development plan for particularly vulnerable groups;(b)	if so, the details thereof as on date;(c) 	the present status of the said proposal; and(d) 	the time by which the said proposal is likely to be finalized along with the reasons for delay therein?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	To (c): “Van Bandhu Kalyan Yojana (VKY)” is a strategy for development of Scheduled Tribes in the country. Funds for development of Scheduled Tribes are provided under Scheduled Tribe Component of various schemes of the Government. Ministry of Tribal Affairs (MoTA) has received proposals amounting to Rs. 1520.80 lakh from the Government of Maharashtra for funding under the scheme of Development of Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs) during the year 2019-20. It has been decided to provide funds of Rs. 1510.00 lakh for approved activities 2019-20. The entire amount of Rs. 1510.00 lakh has been released to State of Maharashtra. The details of projects approved under the scheme of ‘Development of Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs) is given at Annexure.(d): Decision on proposal from State of Maharashtra has already been taken as stated in (a) to (c) above. Decision on additional proposals amounting to Rs. 2200.00 lakh sent by State of Maharashtra has also been taken subject to availability of funds from savings, if any, under the scheme.
