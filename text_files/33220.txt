                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

749






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






National Apprenticeship Training Scheme




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and the annual number of students enrolled and their employment percentage under the National Apprenticeship Training Scheme (NATS) since 2017, State-wise (b) the details of the budgetary outlay and phases of implementation of the continued version of NATS; (c) whether the Government plans to expand the fields for skill development under the NATS; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) The State-wise details of the annual number of students enrolled and their employment percentage under the National Apprenticeship Training Scheme (NATS) since 2017 i.e. during the year 2017-18, 2018-19, 2019-20 & 2020-21 are enclosed as Annexure-I.(b) The Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs has approved continuation of  NATS for  a further period of 05 years beyond 31.03.2021 i.e. from 01.04.2021 to 31.03.2026 with an outlay of  ` 3,054.00 crore. The year wise outlay for the scheme is given in the table below:<pre>                           Years                                            (in crore)2021-22	       2022-23	        2023-24	        2024-25	        2025-26	          Total500.00	     ` 550.00	`       605.00	`        666.00	`        733.00	`        3,054.00</pre>(c) & (d) Yes Sir. The Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs has also expanded the scope of the NATS to include students from Humanities, Science and Commerce besides students from engineering stream. *****
