                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4697






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Development of Tourism in Siwan and Chapra Districts




Kavita Singh














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has formulated any special action plan for the development of tourism in the Siwan and Chapra districts of Bihar; (b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) 	the developmental activities being carried out all historical places of tourism in Siwan district of Bihar; (d) 	if so, the details thereof; (e) 	the details of expenditure incurred on all special and important tourist places; and (f) 	the district-wise details of the expenditure incurred incurred at States-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (f):	The Ministry of Tourism, under the Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD Schemes provides Central Financial Assistance to State Governments/Union Territory (UT) Administrations for development of thematic tourist circuits and for holistic development of identified pilgrimage and heritage destinations in the country. The projects for development are identified in consultation with the  State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to submission of project proposals, their adherence to relevant scheme guidelines, submission of suitable detailed project reports, availability of funds and utilization of funds released earlier.The Ministry has not received any project proposal for development of tourism infrastructure in the Siwan and Chapra districts of Bihar.The details of projects sanctioned by the Ministry for all States/UTs under the above schemes are given at the Annexure.*******
