                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3148






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Condition of Anganwadi Centres in Rajasthan




Jaskaur Meena














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the efforts being undertaken to improve the condition of Anganwadi Centres in Rajasthan;(b)	whether the Government is aware that nutritious food is provided to only two girls in the said Anganwadi Centres; and(c)	if so, the reasons therefor along with the proposals for ensuring total food coverage of girls at such Anganwadi Centres?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) 	A number of steps have been undertaken to improve the condition of Anganwadi Centres in Rajasthan, some of which are as under:i.	Rs. 780.00 lakhs had been released to State of Rajasthan for the Up-gradation of Government owned buildings @ Rs. 2,00,000/- per AWCs during 2017-18.ii.	Rs. 333.46 lakhs and Rs. 261.46 lakhs had been released to State of Rajasthan for construction of toilets and providing drinking water facilities in AWCs during 2017-18 and 2018-19 respectively.iii.	Rs. 1662.00 lakhs for construction of 1385 AWCs Buildings and Rs. 2400.00 lakhs for construction of 2000 AWCs buildings were released to Rajasthan under MGNREGS during 2017-18 and 2018-19 respectively.iv.	As on 2nd December, 2019, 20,869 Anganwadi Workers (AWWs) have been provided with Smart Phones for IT-enabled monitoring under ICDS-Common Application Software (CAS).  v.	Under POSHAN Abhiyaan, 21430 weighing machines and 31730Stadiometer have been provided for growth monitoring.(b) & (c):Anganwadi Services is universal and available to all the eligible beneficiaries without any condition. It is a self selecting scheme and services are provided to those beneficiaries who visit the AWCs.  *****
