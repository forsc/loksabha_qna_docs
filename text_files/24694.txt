                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

484






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Road Projects in Haryana




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether NHAI has started construction of city bypass at Bhiwani city in Haryana at NH-709E and Dadri at NH-334B and conversion of Mandaula to Narnaul NH-148B to four-lane road;(b) 	if so, the details and the present status thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) 	whether the Government proposes to provide a cut between village Marora-Khanpur of Nuh district at Delhi-Mumbai expressway and if so, the time by which it is likely to be done and if not, the reasons therefor;(d) 	whether the Government proposes to convert NH-334B from Jhajjar to Dadri into four-lane; and(e) 	if so, the time by which it is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) The work of construction of Bhiwani bypass from Bhiwani – Loharu road (NH-709E) to Bhiwani – Hansi road (NH-148B) has been included in the Annual Plan 2021-22. The physical progress of construction work of bypass from Bhiwani-Loharu (NH-709E) to Bhiwani-Charkhi Dadari road and Bhiwani – Charkha Dadari road to Bhiwani Rohtak road is 93.90% and 20% respectively. Moreover, Bhiwani-Hansi stretch has been included in Bharatmala Pariyojana. Detailed Project Report (DPR) for improvement of the stretch has been commenced. 	At present, there is no plan for 4-laning of Mandaula to Narnaul stretch. Moreover, section Narnaul – Mahendergarh-Charkhi Dadri is excluded from NH 148B.(c)            No Sir, as per provisions of Contract Agreement, Interchange has been provided at km 34+610 & km 66+587 falling on either side of Maroara-Khanpur keeping in view need to accommodate traffic movement surrounding that vicinity. A light vehicular underpass (LVUP) of size 18 m x 5.5 m has also been proposed for Marora-Khanpur village at km. 49+002 to facilitate cross-movement of traffic. (d) & (e) No Sir, at present, there is no such proposal.  *****
