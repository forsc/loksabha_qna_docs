                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1484






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






BTS in Gujarat




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to install new Base Transceiver Station (BTS) in Gujarat to improve the mobile connectivity; (b)	 if so, the details thereof, location-wise; (c) 	the number of operational BTS in Gujarat, location-wise and district-wise; and (d) 	whether the number of present BTS in Gujarat are enough for present requirement and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) & (b)  Yes, Sir. Under Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF) projects, the Government has planned new mobile towers at 70 locations in the State of Gujarat for installation of Base Transceiver Stations(BTSs) for mobile service connectivity of uncovered villages. In this regard, the details are given at Annexure-I.(c)	The number of operational BTS in State of Gujarat as on 20th November, 2019 is 1,34,852 (2G - 29199 Nos., 3G - 17920 Nos.  and 4G-LTEs 87733 Nos.)  at 34,627 mobile tower locations. District-wise number of locations where BTSs are installed by Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) in the State of Gujarat is given at Annexure – II.(d)	The number of BTSs are by and large sufficient to cater to the population of Gujarat. However, TSPs install additional BTS to further improve coverage and/or capacity of their networks based on their techno-commercial requirements on continuous basis.
