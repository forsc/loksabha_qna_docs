                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4327






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Production of Gas




Ajay Nishad














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total quantum of gas production in thecountry during the last three years including the sourceof production, State-wise; (b) whether there is any gap between demand andsupply of natural gas and if so, the details thereof;(c) the steps taken by the Government to fill thedemand-supply gap including the import of LiquifiedNatural Gas;(d) the details of quantum of natural gas generallyimported to meet the said demand including the namesof the countries from which the said imports have takenplace, country-wise; and(e) whether the Government has fixed any target tomake the country self-reliant in the production of naturalgas and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)	     The total quantum of gas production in the country state-wise/source-wise during last three years is as under:( in MMSCM)OnshoreState/Source	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20Assam and Arunachal Pradesh	3249	3317	3187Gujarat	1607	1402	1342Tamil Nadu	1207	1208	1097Andhra Pradesh	959	1082	912Tripura	1440	1554	1473West Bengal (CBM)	531	350	306Jharkhand (CBM)	4	4	5Madhya Pradesh (CBM)	200	357	345Rajasthan 	1442	1483	1883Total Onshore	10639	10756	10549OffshoreMumbai High & Eastern Offshore	17791	19044	18576Pvt/JVs	4219	3075	2059Grand Total	32649	32875	31184(b) and (c)	No Sir. Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) is being imported under Open General Licensing (OGL) on mutually agreed terms betweenbuyers and sellers on techno-commercial basis. (d)	Currently major portion of LNG is being imported from Qatar, USA, Russia, Australia and Nigeria. Data regarding import of LNG during last three years is as under:(in MMSCM)	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20LNG import	27439	28740	33887Total	59170	60798	64144(e)	To boost the production of Natural Gas to make country self-reliant in the production of natural gas, Government is taking steps i.e. (i) introduction of Hydrocarbon Exploration and Licensing Policy (HELP)/ Open Acreage Licensing Policy (OALP) replacing NELP/PSC., (ii) introduction of Discovered Small Field (DSF) Policy for early monetization of un-monetized discoveries of National Oil Companies (NoCs) and (iii) National Data Repository (NDR) and National Seismic Programme for conducting 2D seismic survey for data Acquisition, Processing and Interpretation (API).******
