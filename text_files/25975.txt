                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1783






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Expansion of Indore airport




Shankar Lalwani














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CIVIL AVIATIONbe pleased to state:-(a) whether the Government has any plan for expansion of Indore airport;(b) if so, the time frame of the Government and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) whether the Government plans to construct a separate international terminal at Indore airport or to convert it into a terminal for international air travel; and(d) if so, the time by which it will be constructed?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a): The Airports Authority of India (AAI) has projected a land requirement of 2314.52 acre for expansion/development of Indore airport to make it suitable for Code-E type of aircraft operations. The expansion/development project includes extension of existing runway, parallel runway and other allied work.;(b): The development of the airport is subject to the provision of land by the State Govt. of Madhya Pradesh free of cost and free from all encumbrances to AAI. ;(c) & (d): The existing terminal building at the airport is an Integrated Terminal Building suitable for both Domestic and International operations. At present, there is no plan to construct new terminal building by AAI.
