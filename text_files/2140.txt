                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1949






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Projects and Schemes under NLCPR




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total amount released under NLCPR (Non-Lapsable Central Pool of Resources) scheme in North Eastern Region in health sector and the total number of projects going on in this regard; (b) 	the district-wise details thereof;(c) 	the places where the said projects are going on;(d) 	the number of projects completed so far and the number of projects yet to be completed along with the details thereof; and(e) 	the details of the facilities available in north eastern region in health sector?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Independent Charge) of the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region[Dr. Jitendra Singh] (a)	Under NLCPR (Non Lapsable Central Pool of Resources) scheme, Rs.616.39 crore have been released for projects in the Health sector in the North Eastern region. A total of 16 such projects are ongoing in this regard.(b) and (c) A list showing the districts and places where the projects are going on is given in Annexure- I.(d)	A total of 39 projects have been completed in this regard and a total amount of Rs.415.37 crore has been released for these projects. A total of 16 projects are ongoing in this regard and a total amount of Rs.201.02 crore has been released for these projects. (e)	As per information given by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare (the details of the heath facilities available in North East Region as per Rural Health Statistics 2017-18) is given in Annexure- II.
