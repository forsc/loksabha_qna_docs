                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1916






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Survey for establishing Heavy Industries




Devendrappa Y














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a):  whether the Government has made any survey in the areas/States where raw materials to establish heavy industries are available in abundance;(b):  if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and(c):  the reasons for not establishing any Central Public Sector heavy industries in the States where no Central PSUs have been set up so far?






ANSWER




                                                                              THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES                                       (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) to (c): No Sir, Department of Heavy Industry has not made any survey in the areas / States where raw materials to establish heavy industries are available in abundance. Further, setting up of industry is a state subject and role of this Department is confined to administration of the CPSEs under its administrative control.                                               *******
