                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

100






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






CNG/PNG Facility in Bihar




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to promote the use of CNG/PNG in order to controlpollution in Bihar and if so, the names of the districts in Bihar provided with such facility ofCNG/PNG till date including the names of districts proposed to be provided with CNG/PNGfacility;(b) whether the Government proposes to open CNG/ PNG facility/centre in the Gopalganjdistrict of Bihar and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c) whether there is any CNG/PNG project launched or proposed to be launched for Gopalganjdistrict of Bihar including any prospective project to be implemented in adjacent districts, if so,the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (c) : A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF THE LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. *100 ASKED BY DR. ALOK KUMAR SUMAN, MP TO BEANSWERED ON 8thFEBRUARY, 2021.(a)Yes. Government proposes to promote the use of Compressed Natural Gas (CNG)/PipedNatural Gas (PNG) in Bihar. Following GAs/ Districts have been covered under City GasDistribution (CGD) network in the state of Bihar:Sl. No. State Name of Geographical Area (GA)/District1 Bihar Patna District2 Aurangabad, Kaimur&Rohtas Districts3 Begusarai District4 Gaya &Nalanda Districts5 Araria, Purnia, Katihar and Kishanganj Districts6 Arwal, Jehanabad, Bhojpur and Buxar Districts7 Khagaria, Saharsa and Madhepura Districts8 Lakhisarai, Munger and Bhagalpur Districts9 Muzaffarpur, Vaishali, Saran and Samastipur Districts10 Bihar andJharkhandNawada and koderma Districts11 Sheikhpur, Jamui and Deoghar Districts(b) Gopalganj district has not yet been authorized for development of CGD network. PNGRBidentifies GAs for authorizing the development of CGD network in synchronization with thedevelopment of natural gas pipeline connectivity, natural gas availability and techno-commercialviability.(c) Saran district is adjacent to Gopalganj district in the state of Bihar, which has been coveredunder CGD network of Muzaffarpur, Vaishali, Saran and Samastipur Districts GA.*****
