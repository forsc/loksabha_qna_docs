                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2994






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Ports in Odisha




Chandrani Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number and status of functional ports in Odisha; and (b) whether the Government has any plan or proposal for     constructing new Ports in Odisha, if so, the details     thereof?  






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	At present 03 (Three) Ports are in operation in the State of Odisha, out of which Paradip Port is the only Major Port under Ministry of Shipping, Government of India and other 02 (Two) ports i.e., Dhamara and Gopalpur are non-major Ports under the control of Government of Odisha.(b)	No, Sir.                                     *****
