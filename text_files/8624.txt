                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2110






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Funds to Rural and Urban Areas under SDC




H. Vasanthakumar














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has provided funds to rural and urban areas population under Skill Development Schemes;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise;(c)	the criteria adopted by the Government to grant funds to rural and urban areas under the said programmes;(d)	if so, the details thereof along with the number of people who were benefited across the country, State/UT/ Scheme-wise; and(e)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c)	Under Skill India Mission, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship is implementing a flagship scheme called Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 2016-20 with an objective to provide skilling to 1 crore prospective youth under Short Term Training (STT) courses and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) across the country covering all areas (i.e. rural and urban areas) for four years i.e. 2016-2020 with an outlay of Rs. 12,000 Cr. PMKVY has two components known as Centrally Sponsored Centrally Managed (CSCM) being implemented by National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) and Centrally Sponsored State Managed (CSSM) being implemented by State Skill Development Missions of the States/ UTs popularly known as State- Engagement Component of PMKVY (2016-20).Under CSCM component of PMKVY 2016-20, there is no provision of State-wise funds allocation. However, under CSSM component of PMKVY 2016-20, 25% of the funds and corresponding physical targets of PMKVY 2016-20 have been allocated to the States/UTs for the implementation of the scheme through State Skill Development Missions. The allocation of skilling targets and funds amongst the States under PMKVY 2016-20 has been done on the basis of the following parameters namely; State Population in the age group (15 to 35 years), Human Development Index (HDI) of the State, Unemployment Percentage of the State and State wise Skill Gaps identified through NSDC studies.
