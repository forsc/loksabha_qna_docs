Rural Roads under PMGSY




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of rural roads built in Santhal Pargana of Jharkhand under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during the last three years, year and district-wise;(b)	whether the Government proposes for construction of more new roads under PMGSY in the current financial year; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?
