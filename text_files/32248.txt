                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

340






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Setting Up of Cold Storage in FPIs




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)     	the initiatives taken by the Government in Food Processing Industries (FPI) sector regarding renewal of the contemporary technology and protection against climate change, price fluctuations, demand shocks and also sustained investment; (b)     	the details of the procedure for setting up of cold storages under this Ministry at the State level; and (c)      the number of cold storages being operated in the country till data, State/UT-wise particularly in the State of Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PASHUPATI KUMAR PARAS) (a)	to (c): A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.340 FOR ANSWER ON 21ST DECEMBER 2021 REGARDING “SETTING UP OF COLD STORAGE IN FPIs” (a):	The Government is implementing National Action Plan on Climate Change (NAPCC) which is the overarching policy framework for climate action in the country. In line with the NAPCC, many schemes and programs have been launched to scale up India’s action on both, the adaptation and mitigation. Financial assistance is also provided by the Ministry to various institutions/research organization to develop green/low carbon foot print technologies for food processing sector. Besides supporting setting up of modern food processing infrastructure in the country through its schemes, the Ministry is undertaking price stabilization measures for perishable fruits and vegetables through financial support at the rate of 50% of expenses on transportation and storage for evacuation of surplus production crops from the producing area to the consumption centres during glut situations, under the Operation Greens scheme.   Further, regulatory and policy reforms have been undertaken to ensure conducive environment to the food processing investors, including those engaged in exports. These measures include shifting from product approval regime to an ingredient based approval system, putting in place an Empowered Group of Secretaries(EGoS) under the Cabinet Secretary and Project Development Cells in the Ministries; streamlining proposal approval and Grants disbursal process through on-line portal, etc.(b):	MoFPI invites proposals for setting up of food processing infrastructure (associated with compatible cold storages of varied nature, like Controlled Atmosphere, Modified Atmosphere, Frozen, etc.) by floating Expression of Interest (EOI)” from time to time under component schemes of Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana (PMKSY). The applications are received through online portal and examined based on eligibility and assessment criteria prescribed under the scheme guidelines.  The promoters of the selected proposals thereafter initiate action for setting up the relevant units as per sanctioned project.(c):	The State/UT-wise details of number of cold storages (CA/MA/Frozen, etc) associated with projects sanctioned under various schemes of the Ministry, including for the State of Maharashtra, is given as under: Sr.No	State/UT	Number1	Andaman & Nicobar	22	Andhra Pradesh	453	Arunachal Pradesh	74	Assam	135	Bihar	86	Chhattisgarh	97	D & N Haveli & Daman & Diu	18	Gujarat	399	Haryana	2910	Himachal Pradesh	2711	Jammu & Kashmir	2112	Karnataka	3713	Kerala	1114	Madhya Pradesh	1515	Maharashtra	11216	Manipur	1517	Meghalaya	1018	Mizoram	919	Nagaland	820	Odisha	1021	Punjab	7322	Rajasthan	3123	Tamil Nadu	5924	Telangana	1825	Tripura	126	Uttar Pradesh	4127	Uttarakhand	5728	West Bengal	23 	Total	731 *****
