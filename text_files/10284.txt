                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3880






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Enhancing Mineral Exploration and Mining Activities




Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy













                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has enhanced the mineral exploration and mining activities in the country recently and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has identified eleven Research and Development projects under its Science and Technology activity from academic institutions/PSUs under the programme and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the outcomes of these projects will pave the way in newer mineral discovery in the country; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a)	Yes, Sir. The Government has taken various steps to enhance the mineral exploration and mining activities in the country. Exploration agencies have been notified under section 4 of the MMDR Act. Further, National Mineral Exploration Trust (NMET) has been constituted under section 9 (C) of the MMDR Act for the purpose of regional and detailed explorations. The Central Government also increases the area limit on the request of State Governments for development of mineral and industries. (b) to (d) Yes, Sir. During FY 2019-20 till date, 11 research and development projects have been identified by the Government under the Science and Technology Programme Scheme of Ministry of Mines with the vision to promote research in applied geosciences, mineral exploration, mining and allied areas, mineral processing, optimum utilization and conservation of the mineral resources of the country, for the benefit of the nation and its people. The details may be seen on the website of Ministry of Mines (www.mines.gov.in). ******
