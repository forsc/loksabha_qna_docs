                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4899






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited




Karti P. Chidambaram














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any plan for expansion of Tiruchirapalli BHEL (Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited) in Tamil Nadu;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the number of people likely to be employed in the expansion plan; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES                    (SHRI ARVIND GANPAT SAWANT)(a): There is no investment proposal under consideration at present in Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) in respect of expansion at its manufacturing plant/unit located at Tiruchirapalli in Tamil Nadu.(b) & (c): Not Applicable in view of (a) above.(d): The manpower and other infrastructure facilities currently available at the manufacturing plant/unit of BHEL located at Tiruchirapalli, Tamil Nadu, are adequate with its scale of operations and product profile.                               **********
