                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1511






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Schemes for NER




Queen Oja













                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                








                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has announced any special package and schemes for Assam in North Eastern Region.(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of funds allocated for the said package/schemes during the last three years;(d)	whether the above said State has fully utilized the said package; and (e)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Independent Charge) of the Ministry ofDevelopment of North Eastern Region [Dr. Jitendra Singh](a) & (b):  Yes Sir.  Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region is providing funds to Government of Assam under the Special Packages of Rs 750.00 crore for Bodoland Territorial Council (BTC), Rs 350.00 crore for Karbi Anglong Autonomous Territorial Council (KAATC) and Rs 200.00 crore for Dima Hasao Autonomous Territorial Council (DHATC) ,  (c):Year-wise release of funds during last three years under these packages is as under:-<pre>Year	Fund released under BTC package  (Rs in crore)	Fund released under KAATC package  (Rs in crore)	Fund released under DHATC package  (Rs in crore)2016-17	50.00	3.43	0.082017-18	23.47	1.50	23.372018-19	0.00	39.63	12.00Total	73.47	44.56	35.45</pre>(d) & (e):  Project identification under all the packages has been completed and in principle approval has been granted for the balance of the sanctioned amount based on which the respective Councils will implement the projects. Further releases to the State is on the basis of progress of implementation of these projects.
