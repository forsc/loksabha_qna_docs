                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3142






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Project Planning and Implementation




 Rahul Ramesh Shewale













                                                    Girish Bhalchandra Bapat
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                    Pritam Gopinath Munde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether it is a fact that urban areas are expected to house forty percent of population of the country and contribute seventy five per cent to GDP by 2030;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c)   whether most of the cities in the country are lacking urban city planning;(d)    if so, the details thereof;(e)  whether there is a need to collaborate all relevant sectors for effective project planning and implementation; and(f)     if so, the steps taken by the Union Government in this direction?






ANSWER




                                                                    THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                               (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)	& (b) As per Report of the Technical Group on ‘Population Projections for India and States(2011-2036)’, published by the National Commission on Population, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, in July, 2020, the projected urban population for India would be 37.25% of total country’s population on 1st March, 2030. Further, the Mid-Term Appraisal of the 11th Five Year Plan projects the urban share in total GDP at 75 per cent in 2030. (c)	& (d) Urban planning falls under the domain of respective State / Union Territory Governments. According to the Report on ‘Reforms in Urban Planning Capacity in India’ brought out by National Institution for Transforming India (NITI) Aayog in September, 2021, about 52% of the statutory towns and 76% of census towns do not have Master Plans to guide their spatial growth and infrastructural investments. (e)	 (& (f) Urban development being a State subject, project planning and implementation is the responsibility of State / UT Governments.                                            *****
