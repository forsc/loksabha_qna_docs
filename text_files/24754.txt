                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

487






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Modernisation of Haldia Port




Sisir Kumar Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plan to allocate additional funds for ensuring world class facilities at Haldia port;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) whether the Government proposes for foreign trade with Chattogram port, Bangladesh through Haldia port; and (d)if so, the details of the necessary restructuring projects and modernisation of Haldia port thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)&(b)     Haldia Dock Complex has undertaken major projects amounting to Rs.658.86 Crores for modernization of its cargo handling facility. Details of major projects are annexed. In addition, Government has allocated a fund amounting to Rs.998.85 crores upto 2023-24 for undertaking maintenance dredging in the shipping channel, which will help accommodate deep drafted vessels at HDC.(c)&(d)	Haldia is one of the Ports of call in the Indo Bangladesh Coastal Shipping Agreement. Under the agreement there are four agreed routes for the movement of vessels as follows:-	i)	Chennai-Krishnapatnam-Kakinada-Vishakhapatnam-Paradip-Haldia-Kolkata-Mongla-Paira Chittagong;ii)	Chennai-Krishnapatnam-Kakinada-Vishakhapatnam-Paradip-Haldia-Kolkata-Mongla-Khulna;iii)	Chennai-Krishnapatnam-Kakinada-Vishakhapatnam-Paradip-Haldia-Kolkata-Paira;iv)	Chennai-Krishnapatnam-Kakinada-Vishakhapatnam-Paradip-Haldia-Kolkata-Pangaon Narayanganj-Ashuganj.In July, 21, 2020 a container vessel on a Trial run reached Chattagram from Haldia with cargo destined to Tripura.AnnexureList of major projects undertaken by the HDC for modernization of its cargo handling facility.i)	Construction of Rail Over bridge at Ranichak – Rs.127.8 crores (completed)ii)	2nd Railway Line from Durgachak take off point to “A” cabin at Durgachak at HDC. Route KM: 11,5 Km – Rs.117 crore(completed)iii)	Development of Outer Terminal-II – Rs.81.24 crore.iv)	Installation and commissioning of 3rd RMQC at Berth Nos.11 & 12 – Rs.52.82 crorev) 	Setting up of Liquid Cargo Handling, Jetty along with associated facilities at Shalukkhali, Haldia Dock-II, Haldia Dock Complex, SMPK, Kolkata on DBFOT basis for a period of 30 years – Rs.172.52 crore.vi)	Mechanisation of the berths (Berth Nos.5 & 10) through PPP.vii)	Development of Fire Fighting Facilities at 5 Berths/jetties – Rs.107.48 crore.                                        *****
