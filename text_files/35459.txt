                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2811






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Steps to Attract Domestic Tourists at Heritage Sites




Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the steps being taken by the Government to attract domestic touriststo the heritage sites in the country; (b)	the number of domestic tourists and the revenue generated at the national level during the last five years, State-wise; (c) 	whether any concrete steps have been taken by the Government to improve the tourist inflow; (d) 	whether the Government has any proposal to promote tourism in Maharashtra and if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	the steps taken to increase the tourist footfall so as to ensure the stability of the tourism and hospitality industry?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM     					   (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e):	The Ministry of Tourism has taken following steps to attract and increase the tourist footfall to various tourism destinations including the heritage sites and Maharashtra in the country:•	Ministry of Tourism has developed thematic tourist circuits under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme.   Heritage circuit has been identified as one of the thematic circuit for development under the scheme. •	Development of integrated tourism infrastructure at religious and heritage sites has been undertaken under the National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD) Scheme.•	Development of tourist amenities at tourist sites, heritage sites and monuments to make them tourist friendly by enhancing overall tourist experience under Adopt a Heritage programme.•	Promoting India as a holistic destination including various tourism destinations and products under the Incredible India brand line.•	 Launched Dekho Apna Desh initiative in January 2020 with the objective of promoting various tourism destinations including the lesser known destinations and heritage sites of India among the citizens.•	Domestic and Overseas Indiatourism offices promote tourism destinations and heritage sites thorough various promotional and marketing activities/ events. The Ministry does not maintain state wise data of the revenue generated by the domestic tourists.
