                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3735






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Panchayati Schemes in Assam




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the funds allocated by the Ministry to the State Government of Assam during the last three years including the purposes of the said funds; (b) 	whether the Government has taken proper steps to eliminate the irregularities in Government schemes at Panchayat level and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the details of funds allocated by the Union Government for BTC sector including the areas where the said funds have been utilized; and (d) 	the details of the schemes by which BTC sector is being benefited?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d) The Ministry of Panchayati Raj (MoPR) had implemented Capacity Building-Panchayat Sashaktikaran Abhiyan (CB-PSA) during 2016-17 to 2017-18 for strengthening of Panchayats and to address the critical gaps that constrain the functioning of Panchayats. Further w.e.f. from 2018-19, Ministry has been implementing Centrally   Sponsored   Scheme   of   Rashtriya   Gram   Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) for strengthening of    governance   capabilities   of   Panchayati   Raj Institutions in part IX area of the country as well institutions of rural local government in non- part IX areas where Panchayats do not exist including those in State of Assam to   deliver   on   Sustainable   Development   Goals. The funds released during the last three years under CB-PSA/RGSA to the State of Assam are as under:Year	                                     2016-17	2017-18	   2018-19Funds Released (Rs. in Cr.)                  49.08	27.59	    39.21 	Under the scheme of RGSA, funds are released to States/UTs as a whole for implementation of activities approved in the respective Annual Action Plans (AAPs). For monitoring the utilization of funds released to States/UTs under the Scheme, an online monitoring and reporting system (MIS) has been developed and operationlised. Moreover, transaction based Public Financial Management System (PFMS) has been introduced for release and tracking of funds under RGSA. Further, the MoPR is implementing e-Panchayat Mission Mode Project (MMP) wherein a suite of core software applications has been developed to address various aspects of Panchayats’ functioning including planning, budgeting, implementation, accounting, monitoring, social audit and delivery of citizen centric services like issue of certificates, licences, tax collection etc. ****
