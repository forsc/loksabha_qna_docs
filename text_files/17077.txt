                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

950






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






National Digital Health Mission (NDHM)




Midhun Reddy













                                                    L.S. Tejasvi Surya
                                                








                                                    Pocha Brahmananda Reddy
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                








                                                    K. Subbarayan
                                                








                                                    Pinaki Misra
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has announced National Digital Health Mission (NDHM) and Health Data Management Policy (HDMP), if so, the details thereof along with the manner and time by which it will be implemented;(b) whether the Government has prepared an opensource interface like UPI for the National Health ID proposed under the National Digital Health Mission (NDHM), and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has taken note of the concerns raised with regard to data privacy of the individuals, if so, the details thereof and necessary steps taken in this regard;(d) the details of the competent authorities who will be able to gain access to personal medical data under the NDHM and the criteria for gaining such access;(e) whether the creation of digital Health IDs will be voluntary and if so, whether it will be implemented through an opt-in or an opt-out model, if so, the details thereof; and(f) whether the Government would consider delaying the implementation of NDHM and Health Data Management Policy (HDMP) until such a time as a national data protection law has been enacted, if so the details thereof and if not the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	Yes, National Digital Health Mission (NDHM) has been announced by Hon’ble Prime Minister on 15th August 2020.The NDHM is being implemented in phase wise manner. •	Currently, Phase 1 is under implementation in 6 UTs on pilot basis.•	Phase 2 will cover additional States with expansion of the services.•	Phase 3 will target nation-wide roll-out, operationalizing and converging with all health schemes across India along with promotion, on-boarding, and acceptance of NDHM across the country.The draft Health Data Management Policy for the National Digital Health Mission has been uploaded on the portal on 20th August 2020 seeking public feedback.Public comments are awaited, after which the necessary action to finalize the policy will be taken.(b):	The NDHM will adopt the policy on Open Standards and Open Source Software, notified by MeitY, GoI, in designing of the building blocks and in all procurements.(c):	‘Privacy by Design’ is the one of the principles, on which NDHM is being implemented. The draft Health Data Management Policy has appropriately included measures for data privacy based on GoI guidelines, acts and laws. (d):	The NDHM will have a robust consent framework for facilitating sharing of data in the NDHM ecosystem.The patient is the owner of the data and has complete control over sharing his/her data in NDHM ecosystem. The personal medical data can only be shared by the patient by giving proper consent following consent framework of NDHM.(e):	Yes, the creation of Health ID is voluntary. It is an ‘opt-in’ model wherein an individual will be required to generate the Health ID if he/she wishes to be a part of NDHM. After opting in, at any stage, he/she can opt out.(f):	No, there is no proposal to delay the implementation of NDHM and Health Data Management Policy (HDMP).The privacy & security proposed in the Personal Data Protection (PDP) bill have been adopted in the NDHM ecosystem and Health Data Management Policy (HDMP).
