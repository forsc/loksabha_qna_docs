                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

307






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Growth of Rural Wages




Sisir Kumar Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has made any report on growth in rural wages, month-wise on the inflation rate (BASE rate 2010) and adequate demand for a four members family in the country;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government is of the opinion that the current per head rural income is not adequate to maintain a rural household;(d) 	if so, the reasons therefor alongwith the details of programmes to overcome post covid-19 crisis thereto; and(e) 	the measures being taken or proposed to be taken by the Government to increase the per head rural income?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):  As per the information shared by Ministry of Labour& Employment, Labour Bureau compiles and maintains Consumer Price Index Numbers for Agricultural and Rural Labourers (Base 1986-87=100) (CPI-AL & RL) and average daily wage rate data in respect of selected agricultural and non-agricultural occupations based on the data collected by the Field Operation Division (FOD) of National Sample Survey Office (NSSO). Month-wise average daily wage rate in respect of Non-Agriculture Occupation along with CPL-RL with effect from January 2010 onwards is at Annexure-I. As per section 6(1) of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA), 2005, the Ministry of Rural Development notifies Mahatma Gandhi NREGA wage rates every year for States/UTs based on Consumer Price Index for Agricultural Labour (CPI-AL) published by Labour Bureau, Shimla, to compensate MGNREGA workers against inflation. The wage rates are made applicable from 1st April of each Financial Year.  State/UT-wise wage rate under Mahatma Gandhi NREGA during the financial year 2014-15 to 2019-20are given at Annexure-II.  (d) & (e):  Initiatives taken with respect to the Ministry of Rural Development in response to COVID-19Initiatives taken by the Government in response to COVID-19Annexure-I referred in reply to part (a) & (b) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.307 dated 15.09.2020 Monthly average daily wage rate in respect of Non Agriculture Occupation along with CPI-RL with effect from January 2010 onwards: Annexure-II referred in reply to parts (c) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.307 dated 5.09.2020Notified wage rate under MGNREGA from 2014-15 to 2019-20(in Rs.)
