                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4259






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Agricultural Schemes in Dadra and Nagar Haveli




Kalaben Mohanbhai Delkar














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the details of various schemes being run for the farmers in Dadra and Nagar Haveli; (b) whether any subsidy is provided to the farmers for seeds, fertilizers and cultivation equipments in the Union Territory; (c) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) whether compensation is provided for the crops damaged due to bad weather and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE????????????????????????(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	to (c) schemes such as theRashtriya Krishi VikasYojana (RKVY-RAFTAAR),Pradhan MantriFasalBimaYojana (PMFBY), Pradhan MantriKisanSammanNidhiYojana (PM-KISAN), Soil Health Card (SHC), Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH), Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY), and Sub Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM),etc., are applicable to the UT of Dadra and Nagar Haveli, schemesfor which funds have been allocated/released by Department of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare to Dadra and Nagar Haveli during last 3 years are at Annexure. Assistance including subsidy to farmers is provided as per the guidelines of respective schemes.(d)	The State Government is primarily responsible for providing necessary relief measures in the wake of natural calamities. For undertaking relief measures, funds are available with the State Government in the form of State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF). Additional financial assistance, over and above SDRF, is considered from National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF) for natural calamities of severe nature and is approved on the basis of Memorandum received from the State Government, in accordance with established procedures. The financial assistance under SDRF/NDRF in the wake of notified natural disasters is given by way of relief and not for compensation of loss as suffered/claimed.	Relief assistance to the farmers for the crop damage due to unseasonal rains in 2019 in Dadra and Nagar Haveli was provided from SDRF, the details of which are given below.Year	No. of farmers	Amount (Rs)2020-21	8928	320023822021-22	1502	5060571
