                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Flood Management Projects




Rekha Arun Verma














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of Flood Management Projects pending for approval, State-wise; (b) the reasons for delay in this regard; and (c) the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government for granting approval to the said projects?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c) The schemes for flood management are formulated and implemented by concerned States as per their priority. The Union Government supplements the efforts of the States by providing technical guidance and also promotional financial assistance for management of floods in critical areas. The Ministry provides technical guidance to state Governments through Central Water Commission (CWC) and Ganga Flood Control Commission (GFCC) in formulation of flood management Projects. At Present there  are 27 project of flood management of various States under techno-economic appraisal in CWC and GFCC as per Annexure I.
