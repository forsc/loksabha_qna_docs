                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

25






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Digital Facility and Optical Fibre Connectivity




Chunni Lal Sahu













                                                    Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to provide more digital facilities by connecting all the regions of the country through optical fibre network under the Digital India Scheme; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the number of villages connected by optical fibre cable so far, State/UT-wise; and (d) 	whether the mobile network connectivity and related facilities have started in all the villages of strategic importance and border areas and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (d)	 A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT TO BE LAID ON THE TABLE OF LOK SABHA IN RESPECT OF PARTS (a) to (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 25  FOR 3RD FEBRUARY, 2021  REGARDING “DIGITAL FACILITY AND OPTICAL FIBRE CONNECTIVITY”.(a)	to (c)	Yes Sir, Government of India is implementing BharatNet project to provide digital facilities through broadband connectivity to all the Gram Panchayats (GPs) (approx. 2,50,000) in the country mainly though Optical Fibre Cable (OFC).As on 15.01.2021, a total number of 1,44,678 Villages (Gram Panchayats) have been connected through OFC. State/UT-wise list is given at Annexure-I.(d) State/UT-wise list of total inhabited villages as per Census 2011 and the number of villages covered with mobile connectivity in the country is given at Annexure-II.
