                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4049






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Opening of Off-Campus Medical Colleges




Brijendra Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether University Grant commission (UGC) or other relevant competent authorities have taken steps for starting-up of new off-campus medical colleges in the country; and(b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b): As informed by the University Grants Commission (UGC), State Universities are established by the Act(s) enacted by the State Legislatures. UGC is maintaining a list of Universities and name of a University is included in the UGC list on receipt of the Act as passed by the Parliament/State Legislature. The matters for establishment of any off-campuses and / or institutions therein is administered by the respective State Governments. Besides, UGC has not granted approval to any Private University to start off-campus. Further, Institutions deemed to be Universities are regulated by the UGC (Institutions Deemed to be Universities) Regulations, 2019.  These Regulations provides that those Institutions deemed to be Universities which are accredited by (National Assessment and Accreditation Council) NAAC with (Cumulative Grade Point Average) CGPA of 3.26 & above and ranked 1-50 in the ‘Universities’ category under National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) rankings, can start off-campus centers with the approval of the Government of India. The UGC has amended the said Regulations which allows the Institutions deemed to be Universities to have partnership with private players for establishment of Medical Colleges as off-campus centers. Currently, there are eight off-campus Medical Colleges under the Institutions deemed to be Universities. The list is placed at Annexure.
