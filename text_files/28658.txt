                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

861






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Programme for Waste Management and Recycling




Prataprao Govindrao  Patil Chikhalikar














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware that more than 25000 tonnes of waste have entered the oceans during Covid pandemic leading to threat to human life; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the measures being taken by the Government for its prevention; and (d) 	the scheme formulated by the Government with regard to waste management and recycle by private entities/companies along with the estimated annual expenses for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                       (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) & (b) : No such matter has come to the knowledge of the Government.(c) : Does not arise.(d) : An amount of ?7,365.82 Crore has been allocated to the States/ Union Territories (UTs) under Solid Waste Management (SWM) component of Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U), out of which ?6,373.54 Crore have been released till date.Under Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U) 2.0, launched on 1st October, 2021, ?10,168 Crore have been allocated to the States/ Union Territories (UTs) under Solid Waste Management (SWM) component.                                          *****
