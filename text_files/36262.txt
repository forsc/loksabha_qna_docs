                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3277






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Expenditure by SAIL from CSR Account




Mala Roy














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the amount spent by the Steel Authority of India Ltd. (SAIL) of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) account during the last five years;(b)	whether any amount spent in the State of West Bengal during the last five years; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL		   (SHRI RAM CHANDRA PRASAD SINGH)(a)	The details of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Expenditures of SAIL during last 5 years are as following:-S.No.	Financial Year	SAIL CSR Expenditures (Rs. in Crore)1	2016-17	29.052	2017-18	25.703	2018-19	31.184	2019-20	27.565	2020-21	47.18 (b)&(c): Yes, Sir. The details of CSR expenditures in the State of West Bengal during the last five years are as following:- S.No.	Financial Year	CSR Expenditures (Rs. in Crore)1	2016-17	5.662	2017-18	5.083	2018-19	7.444	2019-20	3.435	2020-21	2.96********
