                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

568






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Violence Against Women




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)   	whether the Government has taken note of the reports of violence against women and children in pandemic period;(b)   	if so, the number of instances reported as per official records in the years 2019-20, 2020- 21 and 2021 - 22 so far; and(c)   	the details of allocations made, disbursed and spent under “Nirbhaya fund” in the FY 2021-22 and so far?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (c):  National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) compiles and publishes information on crimes against women in its publication “Crime in India” which is available on the website of NCRB, viz. https://ncrb.gov.in/en/crime-india. The said report is available  upto the year 2020. As per the data published by the NCRB, the total number cases of  crime against women registered during the years 2019 and 2020 are 4,05,326 and 3,71,503 respectively. Further, total number of cases of crimes against children during the years 2019 and 2020 are 1,48,090 and 1,28,531 respectively. The data shows decline in the crimes against women and children during the year 2020 as compared to 2019.The allocation made, disbursed and spent under "Nirbhaya Fund” since inception are Rs. 6612.85 crore, Rs. 4241.91 crore and Rs. 2989.57 crore respectively .********
