                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4995






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan




Nihal Chand Chauhan













                                                     Bhavana Gawali (Patil)
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the district/block-wise number of centres empanelled under Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA), State/UT-wise including Maharashtra;(b)	the number of women benefitted under PMSMA in the country so far, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra;(c)	the number of doctors registered and those volunteered under PMSMA in Maharashtra, district/block-wise;(d)	the steps taken by the Government to encourage the professional healthcare practitioners to extend their services voluntarily;(e)	the total number of IT enabled registration done under PMSMA in the country during last three years; State/UT-wise including Maharashtra along with the steps taken to create awareness and to promote IT based registration thereunder;(f)	whether the said scheme is being implemented through private hospitals as well and if not, whether any such initiative is likely to be taken in future; and(g)	the guidelines issued by the Government to States for proper implementation of the said scheme in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) the district-wise number of centres empaneled under Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA), State/UT-wise including Maharashtra is given at Annexure – I(b) & (e) the number of women benefitted under PMSMA in the country so far and the total number of IT enabled registration of volunteers done under PMSMA in the country during last three years; State/UT-wise including Maharashtra are given at Annexure-II.(c) & (d) the number of doctors registered and those volunteered under PMSMA in Maharashtra, district-wise is given at Annexure-III. The following steps have been taken by the Government to encourage the professional healthcare practitioners to extend their services voluntarily and for creating awareness towards promoting IT based registration.•	Appeal to private medical practitioners to volunteer and join hands for PMSMA by Hon''ble Prime Minister on the occasion of "MANN KI BAAT" episode on 31st July'' 2019. •	PMSMA ‘I PledgeFor9’ Achievers Awards have been instituted to celebrate individual and team achievements and acknowledge voluntary contributions by private doctors for PMSMA. •	Centralised PMSMA Portal has been created for registering private practitioners. •	MoHFW in collaboration with professional organisations viz. FOGSI (Federation of Obstetric and Gynecological Societies of India), IMA (Indian Medical Association) and others, promote PMSMA through platforms like video conferences with member doctors, and Lok Sabha TV etc.       (f) & (g) The scheme is implemented in 19,174 designated Public Health Facilities. States/UTs have initiated the implementation of the PMSMA programme as per the Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan Guideline issued in 2016 which outlines the critical elements of this initiative. Additionally, Pradhan Mantri Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan-Operational Framework have been circulated to the States/UTs in 2016 to provide clarity on the operational aspects of the programme.
