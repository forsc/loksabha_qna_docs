                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4216






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Child Labour in Jharkhand




Sanjay Seth














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether child labourers are reportedly still being employed in various parts of the country especially in Jharkhand;(b)if so, the details thereof along with the total number of Child labourers in the country, State/UT-wise including Jharkhand;(c)whether the Government has collected any data of children of Jharkhand being transported to other parts of the country; and(d)if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b):	Child Labour is an outcome of various socio-economic problems such as poverty, economic backwardness and illiteracy. The Government stands committed to the eradication of child labour from the country.  The Government has enacted Child & Adolescent Labour (Prohibition & Regulation) Act, 1986 which inter-alia stipulates the complete prohibition on employment or work  of children below 14 years of age in any occupation or  process including domestic work and   also prohibits employment of  adolescents (14-18 years)  in hazardous occupation and processes.  As per Census 2011, the  number of main workers in the age group of 5-14 years in the country is 43,53,247  and in the State of  Jharkhand is 90,996.  State wise details are at Annexure.     (c) & (d):  As per the Human Trafficking data published by the National Crime Record Bureau (NCRB), based on annual data provided by States/UTs from their Anti-Human Trafficking Units, during 2019, a total of 172 children (below 18 years) were trafficked under Human Trafficking in the State of Jharkhand.*                                           *****
