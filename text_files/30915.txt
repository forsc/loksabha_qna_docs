                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2485






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Revenue Generated from ASI Protected Monuments




Rajkumar Chahar














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of year-wise and monument-wise collection of amount for last five years from the sale of tickets by Archeological Survey of India (ASI) from the protected monuments of Agra along with the corresponding amount spent on their conservation;(b)	whether the Government is aware that the surroundings of protected monuments of Agra are ordinarily dirty;(c)	whether the Government or ASI are making any efforts to ensure cleanliness in the surroundings of the monuments of Agra circle of ASI; and(d)	whether the Government is aware that most of the protected monuments of Agra have no parking facility and whether Government has made any effort to provide parking facility for every monument?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) 	The details of year-wise and monument-wise amount collected from entry fee at centrally protected ticketed monuments in Agra and amount spent on conservation and preservation of these monuments during the last five years is at Annexure. (b)  & (c)  	The area around the monument which is owned by the ASI is cleaned regularly with the help of outsourced staff.   (d) 	Parking facility is available for tourists at the ticketed monuments of Agra namely (i) Taj Mahal, (ii) Agra Fort, (iii) Akbar’s Tomb, (iv) Mariam’s Tomb, (v) Ram Bagh, (vi) Fatehpur Sikri (vii) Mehtab Bagh and (viii) Jhun Jhun Katora.ANNEXUREANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2485 FOR 13.12.2021  REVENUE COLLECTED FROM ENTRY FEE AT CENETRALLY PROTECTED TICKETED MONUMENTS IN AGRA AND AMOUNT SPENT ON CONSERVATION AND PRESERVATION OF THEM DURING THE LAST FIVE YEARS.Sl.
