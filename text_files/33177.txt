                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

530






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Development of Health Infrastructure in Rural Areas




C. R. Patil














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the funds allocated for the development of healthcare infrastructure in rural areas of the country during the last three years, State/UT-wise; and(b)	the details of various programs, projects, schemes being implemented by this Ministry and financial support provided to the State of Gujarat under each of these during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	and (b): The details of funds allocated for the development of healthcare infrastructure in rural areas of the country under National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) during the last three years, State/UT-wise is at Annexure. Financial Supportprovided to the State of Gujarat under the following Centrally Sponsored Schemes of Department of Health & Family Welfare is as under: (i)	National Health Mission (NHM): It is one of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme under which the Government of India provides financial and technical support to States/UTs to strengthen their health care systems. It comprises of two sub-missions viz. National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) and National Urban Health Mission (NUHM).During the last three years i.e. from F.Y. 2018-19 to F.Y. 2020-21 funds to the tune of Rs.1038.69crore, Rs.1110.80crore and Rs.1005.66crore have been released respectively to the State of Gujarat.  (ii)	ECRP-I:The ‘India COVID-19 Emergency Response and Health System Preparedness Package-I’ (ECRP-I)of Rs.15,000 crore has been approved by the Cabinet on 22ndApril 2020 with an objective to prevent, detect and respond to the threat posed by COVID-19. Funds to the tune of Rs. 304.16 crore have been released to the State of Gujarat during the F.Y.2020-21 under this Package through Agency-NHM. It is 100% centrally funded. (iii)	ECRP-II: The Cabinet has also approved the scheme, “India Covid-19 Emergency Response and Health Systems Preparedness Package-II” (ECRP-II) on 08th July 2021 for an amount of Rs.23,123 crorefor the F.Y. 2021-22. The scheme aims to accelerate health system preparedness for immediate responsiveness for early prevention, detection and management of COVID-19, with the focus on health infrastructure development including for Pediatric Care and with measurable outcomes.During the F.Y. 2021-22, funds to the tune of Rs.239.61 crore has been released to the State of Gujarat through Agency-NHM and is as per approved Centre-State funding under NHM.
