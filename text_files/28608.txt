                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

562






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Export target for Textile Sector




Chandra Sekhar Sahu













                                                    Girish Bhalchandra Bapat
                                                








                                                    Pritam Gopinath Munde
                                                








                                                     Rahul Ramesh Shewale
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has fixed any export target for textiles sector in the Budget 2021-22; (b) if so, the details in this regard; (c) whether the Ministry has prepared any action plan to achieve the target; (d) if so, the details thereof; (e) the names of the countries with whom the Government has been interacting to expedite Free Trade Agreement and Preferential Trade Agreement (FTAs or PTAs); (f) whether the Ministry of Textiles is working with the Ministry of Finance on interest equalisation scheme and enhancement of insurance cover; and (g) if so, the details along with the status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) & (b):	Details of commodity-wise export target for textiles sector is as follows:(USD mn)S. No.	Commodity	Target Fixed1	Cotton Yarn/Fabs./Madeups, Handloom Products Etc.           	136122	Man-Made Yarn/Fabs./Madeups Etc.                            	61393	Rmg Of All Textiles                                         	200294	Jute Mfg. Including Floor Covering                          	5075	Carpet                                                      	18176	Handicrafts Excl. Hand Made Carpet                          	2244	Total	44348                    Source: D/o Commerce(c) & (d):	Regular meetings are held by Department of Commerce with Ministry of Textiles to analyze the export trend of the Textile sector and discuss the strategy to achieve the textiles exports targets.  Regular interactions are also held with Export Promotion Councils.(e):	India is currently in the process of negotiating FTAs with UAE, EU (European Union), Australia, UK, Canada, Israel, Eurasian Economic Union and other countries/region.(f) & (g):	The representation received with reference to interest equalisation scheme has been forwarded to Ministry of Finance for appropriate action.***
