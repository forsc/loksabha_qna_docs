                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

62






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Battery Operated Vehicles to Divyangjans




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government is running any scheme to provide battery operated vehicles to the Divyangjans to facilitate their lives;(b)  if so, the details thereof;(c)  whether any eligibility has been prescribed for the Divyangjans in this regard; and (d)  the role of the State Governments in making available such battery operated vehicles?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) & (b) Yes Sir. Under the ''Assistance to Disabled Persons for Purchase/Fitting of Aids and Appliances (ADIP)'' Scheme, there is a provision for providing battery operated motorized tricycles and motorized wheelchairs for severely disabled and for persons suffering from Quadriplegia, Muscular Dystrophy, Stroke, Cerebral Palsy, Hemipeligia and any other person with similar conditions, where either three/four limbs or one half of the body are severely impaired. Extent of subsidy provided under the scheme is Rs.25,000/-. (c) The eligibility conditions  for providing such vehicles are as under:-i.	An Indian citizen.ii.	Holds 80 % or more  disablement certificate.iii.	Has monthly income from all sources not exceeding  Rs.15,000/- per month for getting full subsidy. Income from all sources ranging from Rs.15,001/- to Rs.20,000/- for getting 50%  subsidy.iv.	 To be provided   once in ten years.v.	To be provided to persons aged 16 years and above. (d)  The price of  battery operated Motorized Tricycle is Rs. 37,000/- while the price of battery operated Wheelchair is Rs. 65,000/-. The extent of subsidy provided by the department under the ADIP Scheme is Rs.25,000/-  while the difference  is met by  various sources including the State Government.Besides, the State Governments may also distribute Motorized Tricycles/Motorized Wheelchairs on their own by paying the full amount.*****
