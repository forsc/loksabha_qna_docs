                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3296






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Trials of Freight Trains




Kotagiri Sridhar














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   	whether the Ministry has conducted any trials of freight trains from India to other countries and if so, the details thereof;(b)  	whether the Ministry is planning to increase such freight trains from different States including Andhra Pradesh and if so, the details thereof; (c)  	whether the Ministry has set any targets for increasing the share of trade through rail freight with other countries over the years; and(d)   	if so, the details in this regard?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (d) :  Freight trains are already running from India to Bangladesh and Nepal.  Running of such trains depends upon demand from trade and capacity in these countries to receive and handle the freight trains.  Although, there is no set target for increasing share of trade through rail, various steps have been taken for increasing freight trains to Bangladesh and Nepal like increase in interchange points for running freight trains; diversification of cargo and use of different types of wagons; assistance in development of rail infrastructure in these countries; amendments to Rail Service Agreements for running freight trains; etc. The details of such trains are as under:-Year	Number of freight trains for	              Bangladesh	Nepal2019-20	                 660	        11282020-21	                 1335	        10152021-22 (till February, 2022)	 1395	         980*****
