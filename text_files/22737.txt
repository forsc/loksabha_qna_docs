                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3862






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Works under Jal Jeevan Mission




Sunil Kumar Soni














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amount of funds allocated to the State of Chhattisgarh under Jal Jeevan Mission (Har Ghar Jal) including the names of works for which the amount has been allocated along with the time by which the work is likely to be completed with the said amount; (b) the total number of villages of the said State including the number of houses targeted to get safe drinking water under the said mission along with the time limit for the same; (c) the dates on which the tender for the said works have been invited and whether the work has been started; (d) if not, the reasons therefor; and (e) if so, the details of the works completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c) & (e) Government of India, in partnership with States including Chhattisgarh, is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) to provide tap water connection to every rural household in the country by 2024.In 2019-20, out of Central fund allocation of Rs. 208.04 Crore to Chhattisgarh, the State could draw only Rs. 65.82 Crore. In 2020-21, out of Rs. 445.52 Crore allocated, as on 15.03.2021, the State has been able to draw Rs. 334.15 Crore.Under JJM, powers to plan, tender, approve and implement water supply schemes are vested with States, and State Government or its agencies carries out the tendering process. Details of individual tenders being executed at State, District and village level are not maintained at the Govt. of India level. However, State, district and village-wise status of physical achievements across the country, including Chhattisgarh, is available on JJM dashboard at:https://ejalshakti.gov.in/jjmreport/JJMIndia.aspx.(d) Doesn’t arise.****
