                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

123






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Aspirational Districts




Kripanath Mallah














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the aspirational districts selected so far within the  state of Assam;(b)	the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized under this  scheme in Assam during the last three years and the current year so far;(c)	the details of the target set and achievements made so far along with its response in this regard;(d)	whether the Government has planned to select some more districts under the said scheme in Assam; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region[Shri G. Kishan Reddy] (a)	Sir, 07 Districts have been selected as Aspirational Districts in the state of Assam namely Baksa, Barpeta, Darrang, Dhubri ,Goalpara, Hailakandi and Udalguri.(b)	The main aim of the Scheme is based on the convergence of Central & State Schemes which have their own funding arrangements, Collaboration of Central, State level ‘Prabhari’ Officers & District Collectors, and Competition among districts. Every month these Districts are assessed and ranked based on their performance in five sectors, i.e. Health & Nutrition, Education, Agriculture & Water resources, Basic Infrastructure, Financial inclusion & Skill Development. The best performing districts are given additional allocations as incentives. The details of the funds allocated and  released under this scheme in Assam since inception is as below:(Rs.in crores)S. No. 	District	Funds Allocated/ eligible	Funds released1.	Baksa	        11.00	3.992.	Barpeta	21.00	14.003.	Goalpara	2.49	1.004.	Udalguri	21.00	6.005.	Dhubri	7.00	3.996.	Darrang	12.00	11.007.	Hailakandi	22.00	10.65An empowered Committee of Secretaries approves plans of action submitted by the Districts.  Currently, 30 projects in Aspirational Districts of Assam after the approval under this arrangement, are under implementation.(c)	No specific targets are allotted to these districts. However, the performance of Aspirational Districts is assessed on the basis of achievements in five sectors every month on competitive basis among the Aspirational Districts..
