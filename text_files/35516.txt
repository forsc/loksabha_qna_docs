                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2804






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Scheme-wise Budget Allocations




S. Gnanathiraviam














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of Schemes running under the Ministry along with the details of budget allocations for them. Scheme-wise and State-wise;(b)	whether the budget allocations are made keeping in view the illegal forest areas in different States; and(c)	If so, the Scheme-wise percentage of allocation made to Tamil Nadu, including Panchayats, during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) There is no such provision of allocating budget State-wise and funds are released to States/UTs Government as per their Annual Plan of Operation (APOs) submitted as per the guidelines of the scheme.
