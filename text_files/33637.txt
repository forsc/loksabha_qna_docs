                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1059






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






PM Kisan Scheme




Sisir Kumar Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of Pradhan Mantri Kisan SammanNidhi (PM-KISAN) Scheme transactions that have taken place since its launch till December, 2021, Month-wise and State-wise; (b) the total number of PM-KISAN Scheme transactions which have failed, since its launch till December, 2021, Month-wise and State-wise; (c) whether the Government has identified the reasons for failed transactions therein;(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (e) the steps taken or being taken by the Government to ensure that beneficiaries receive their instalments in situations where transactions have failed thereto and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b)	The total number of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Scheme transactions that have taken place since its launch till December, 2021, is 91,22,94,503 of which the number of failed transactions is around 1%.  The cumulative number of PM-KISAN transactions which has failed since 1st February 2019 till December, 2021 is 91,57,756 out of which 32,05,764 transactions have been successfully re-processed. The month-wise and state-wise details of beneficiaries count, total transactions and failed transactions are attached at Annexure-I.(c) & (d)	Various reasons have been identified by the Union Government for the transactions failure under PM-KISAN Scheme such as Account closed/Transferred, Invalid IFSC, Account Inactive, Account Dormant, Amount exceeds limit set on Account by Bank for Credit/Debit per transaction, Account Holder Expired, Account Blocked or Frozen, Inactive Aadhar, Network failure, technical glitches etc.(e)	To ensure that beneficiaries receive their instalments in situations where transactions have failed, a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) has been developed and issued to all States/UTs for necessary action at their end.  In cases where the corrective measures are to be taken by States/UTs, such transaction failure records are opened to respective State/UT for correction under the “Correction Module” tab of PM-KISAN portal. Thereafter, all the transaction failure records are reprocessed for the payment of the respective installment under PM-KISAN Scheme. The Department through weekly video conferencing take up the matter with State/UT Governments to expedite the resolution of the issue and reprocess the failed transactions for the payments of the respective installments under PM-KISAN Scheme.
