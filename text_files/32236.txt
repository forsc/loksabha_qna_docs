                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

328






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Promotion of Sports




Girish Chandra














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the Centrally Sponsored Schemes being implemented by the Government for the promotion of sports in the country; (b)	the funds allocated under these schemes during each of the last three years and the current year, sports-wise; and(c)	the details of the expenditure incurred on these schemes during the said period, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR](a) to (c)	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *328 FOR 21/12/2021 REGARDING PROMOTION OF SPORTS ASKED BY SHRI GIRISH CHANDRA.(a): Presently, no Centrally Sponsored Scheme is being implemented by the Department of Sports. However, the Department of Sports, through the following Central Sector Schemes, supplements efforts of the State/UT Governments for development and promotion of sports: (i)  Khelo India; (ii) Assistance to National Sports Federations;  (iii) Special Awards to Winners in International sports events and their Coaches; (iv) National Sports Awards, (v) Pension to Meritorious Sports Persons; (vi) Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons; (vii) National Sports Development Fund; and (viii) Sports training centres through Sports Authority of India (SAI). Details of these schemes are in the public domain on the websites of this Ministry and SAI. (b) and (c) Details of funds allocated and expenditure incurred under the schemes mentioned in part (a) during each of the last three years and the current year are given at Annexure. The Department of Sports does not maintain sports-wise allocation of funds.*****
