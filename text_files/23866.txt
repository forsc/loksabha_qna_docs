                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5016






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Merger of NPCC with WAPCOS




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that National Projects Construction Corporation Limited (NPCC) has been merged with WAPCOS Limited; (b) if so, whether all the works awarded to NPCC are likely to be completed by WAPCOS; (c) whether NPCC has been given work of Grand Katende Hydro-Electric Project in Democratic Republic of Congo; (d) if so, the details thereof; and (e) whether the work of Grand Katende Hydro-Electric project has started and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)	& (b)  No, Sir. The shares of NPCC have been acquired by WAPCOS. Both are separate entities.(c)	 No, Sir.(d) & (e)  Do not arise.*****
