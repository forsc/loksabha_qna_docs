                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

24






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Mission Indradhanush




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the number of additional children that have been covered under the Indradhanush vaccination programme; (b) whether the Government is aware of the anti-vaccination campaign going on in certain regions of Andhra Pradesh that is trying to discourage religiously conservative people from getting their children vaccinated; and (c) if so, the steps being taken to discredit such campaigns?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):     Additional 3.39 crore children and 87.18 lakh pregnant women have been covered under various phases of Mission Indradhanush.(b):  Yes.(c):     For contradicting anti-vaccination messages in the country, Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India has initiated the following:(i)	To overcome vaccine hesitancy and increase acceptance for vaccination, new package of communication materials containing audio-visual and print material has been developed which includes poster, banner, leaflet, TV spot and radio spot.(ii)	Multi-stakeholder consultation by involving Community Based Organisations (CBOs)/Self Help Groups (SHGs) for increasing demand generation for vaccines(iii)	WhatsApp’s messages are being sent during Measles Rubella campaign about vaccines safety.(iv)	Media sensitization workshop has been conducted on risk communication............................
