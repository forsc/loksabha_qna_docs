                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4264






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Illegal Practice




Rama Devi













                                                    Ranjeeta Koli
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether several small businessmen have got identity card for artisans issued in collusion with the corrupt officers of handicraft commissioner office to get benefit of craft related schemes;(b)	if so, whether the Government is likely to get such illegal practices inquired into;(c)	if so, the details thereof;(d)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(e)	the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (e):   No. Aadhaar based Identity Cards called “Pahchan Cards” are issued to genuine artisans after proper verification and in coordination with representative of State Governments.*******
