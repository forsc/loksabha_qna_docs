                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4271






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Sustainable Development Goal for Education




Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy













                                                    M.K. Vishnu Prasad
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has approved/implemented the new National Education Policy (NEP) with the Sustainable Development Goal of ensuring inclusive and equitable quality education for all in the next 20 years; and(b)	if so, the details thereof and targets set/achieved so far, State-wise?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) The Government of India has approved the National Education Policy 2020 (NEP 2020) which aims to address the many growing developmental imperatives of our country. This Policy proposes the revision and revamping of all aspects of the education structure, including its regulation and governance, to create a new system that is aligned with the aspirational goals of 21st century education, including SDG4, while building upon India’s traditions and value systems.(b) Education is in the concurrent list of the Constitution. The National Education Policy   2020 (NEP 2020) itself provides the different timelines as well as principles and methodology for its implementation by the Centre and all States/UTs towards achievement of SDG Goals. *****
