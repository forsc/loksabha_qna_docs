                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

419






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Grievances Registered in MSMEs




Sunil Kumar Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the grievances registered through complaint cell of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) and the action taken in this regard for its quick resolution during the last five years and the current year; (b) 	the details of future initiatives proposed in this regard, State/UT-wise; and (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a) to (c): 	A statement is laid on the table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (c) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION  No.* 419  FOR ANSWER ON 31.03.2022(a)  & (b):	The Ministry of MSME attended grievances related to Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) through internet grievance monitoring system (IGMS) to track and monitor grievances upto 31.05.2020. After that Ministry of MSME launched an online Portal “CHAMPIONS” on 01.06.2020 to facilitate MSMEs for quick resolution of their grievances and help for encouraging, supporting, and handholding. This Information & Communication Technology (ICT) based platform facilitates a single window solution for the needs of MSMEs. State/UT-wise number of grievances received & replied on MSME internet grievance monitoring system (IGMS) during 2016-17 to 2020-21 ( upto 08.05.2020) is given at Annexure-I.  State/UT-wise number of grievances received & replied on ‘CHAMPIONS’ portal during 2020-21 (from 09.05.2020) to 2021-22(upto 24.03.2022) is given at Annexure-II.Since inception of the ‘CHAMPIONS’ portal to 24.03.2022, 44,637 grievances have been received, out of which 44,399 grievances have been replied. The portal, through its network, offers not just replies but also resolution and handholding of MSME.Ministry of MSME’s key initiatives under CHAMPIONS Portal are as under:(i)    	A network of control rooms is functioning in a Hub & Spoke Model.(ii)   	Dedicated Central Control along with 69 State Control Rooms of the Ministry have been set up for real time on-line monitoring and redressal of grievances and handholding. The offline grievance redressal and handholding is also offered. (iii) The Ministry has already onboarded nodal officers from banks, Central Govt. Ministries/Deptts, State Govts, CPSEs to deal with various issues related to finance, delayed payments, public procurement policy, Udyam registration and different schemes of Central and State Government.(iv)   Improvement in Champions Portal is a continuous  process and the recent initiatives are as under:(i)	Localization of ‘Champions’ portal in 11 regional languages(ii)	Initiating  Chat Bot(iii)	Improved MIS(iv)	Integration of CPGRAM with CHAMPIONS Portal  (c):	 Does not arise.
