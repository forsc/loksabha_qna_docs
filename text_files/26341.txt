                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

188






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






City Gas Distribution




Mahua Moitra














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is an increasing demand for natural gas all over the country, if so, theGeographical Area (GA) and Entity-wise details of the City Gas Distribution-Bid-5, 6, 7, 8 and 9of PNG home connections and CNG stations promised versus delivered of all entities;(b) the details of penalty regime for any shortfall;(c) the details of penalty accrued/charged to these entities till date and end date of marketing andinfrastructure exclusivity for each GA;(d) the expected date of implementation of unified tariff in the country; and(e) the expected date of implementation of domestic gas marketing and pricing freedom i.e.removal of cap on price?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI HARDEEP S PURI)(a) to (e) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF THE LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. *188 ASKED BY SHRIMATI MAHUA MOITRA, MP TOBE ANSWERED ON 2ND AUGUST, 2021.(a) Yes, The Geographical Area (GA) and entity wise details of City Gas Distribution (CGD)network authorized under 5th to 9th bidding round is Annexure-1.(b) and (c) Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB) may initiate penal action asper Authorizing Entities to Lay, Build, Operate or Expand City or Local Natural Gas DistributionNetworks Regulations, 2008. In this regard, details of the penalties imposed on CGD entities aregiven in Annexure-2.The Board may decide on the period of exclusivity to lay, build, operate or expand a city or localnatural gas distribution network for such number of years as it may be order, determine inaccordance with the principles laid down by the regulations made by it, in a transparent mannerwhile fully protecting the consumer interests.(d) PNGRB has notified Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board (Determination ofNatural Gas Pipeline Tariff) Second Amendment Regulations, 2020 (Unified Tariff Regulations)on 23.11.2020 coming into force on the date appointed by the Board in the official gazette.(e) Presently, there is no proposal to remove ceiling on the price.****
