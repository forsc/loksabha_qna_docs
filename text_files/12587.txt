                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1571






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






MGNREGS Funds for Panchayats




Geetha Viswanath Vanga














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to give funds under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) for taking up developmental works of Panchayats and its construction of buildings and also for construction of additional buildings in Andhra Pradesh;(b) 	if so, the details thereof and the steps being taken in this regard for the future needs of the State; and(c) 	the funds sanctioned/spent for each project during the last five years and the current year as on date, post bifurcation of the State?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c): As per Schedule-I of Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act, 2005 (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA) rural infrastructure, construction of buildings for Gram Panchayat, women self-help groups’ federation, cyclone shelters, Anganwadi centers, village haats and crematoria at the village or block level are permissible activities.Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven wage employment programme having bottom up approach and priorities of work are set at Gram Sabha level. Hence, no work-wise allocation of funds is made. As per the information shared by the State Government of Andhra Pradesh, the status of Gram Panchayat buildings constructed and expenditure incurred on them is given at Annexure.
