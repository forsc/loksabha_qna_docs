                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

145






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Pre-School Education




Bidyut Baran Mahato













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















a) whether the Government/ National Council for Educational Research and Training(NCERT) has prepared a draft to instil the values/value education like gender equality,equal respect, etc. at the preschool level shun the rote learning as it impairs the growthof the child;(b) if so, the details thereof and the salient features of the said draft;(c) whether the Government/NCERT has issued any directives to the schools not to thrustEnglish on the children and if so, the details thereof and the response of theGovernment in this regard;(d) whether the NCERT has also asked the schools to be sensitive to the diversive needs ofvarious students and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the details of other steps taken by the Government/NCERT to create amicable learning atmosphere for students at pre-school level?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) : Yes Sir, the two documents titled ‘Preschool Curriculum’ and ‘Guidelines for Pre-School Education’ developed by NCERT emphasise upon integrated early learningwherein issues like gender equality, value education, etc. are interwoven through playfulpedagogical practices.(b) : The documents titled ‘Guidelines for Pre-School Education’ and ‘PreschoolCurriculum’ aim at helping the teachers, administrators, policy planners and otherstakeholders to provide good quality preschool education to children. The guidelinespresent the parameters for infrastructure, monitoring and supervision mechanism andimportance of coordination and convergence with community and parents. The curriculumhas been designed for three years of preschool before class-I, which highlights goals, keyconcepts/ skills, pedagogical processes and early learning outcomes for Preschool I, II andIII. It also suggests ways of planning a preschool programme, classroom organizations andmanagement, assessment and building partnership with parents and community.(c) : NCERT or Ministry of Human Resource Development have not issued anydirectives to schools to not to thrust English on the Children.(d) : NCERT has always been giving emphasis on promoting sensitivity towards diverseneeds of students and also addressing diverse needs of children. The syllabi, textbooksand training material include these concerns in the content, illustration and assessmentexercises.(e) : The Integrated Scheme for School Education- Samagra Shiksha was launched in2018-19 by Ministry of Human Resource Development. Under Samagra Shiksha, thepreschool programme gives emphasis to create amicable learning atmosphere for studentsat pre-school level, MHRD has provided suggestive action points to the States and UTs inthe draft framework of Samagra Shiksha to ensure the implementation of Early ChildhoodEducation (ECE) with developmentally appropriate curriculum and to focus on todevelopment of school readiness among children, by providing them safe, secure, healthyand child friendly atmosphere in the pre-school.******
