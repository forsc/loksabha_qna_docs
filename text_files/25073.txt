                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

98






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






NCC Training in KVs and JNVs




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to make a provision regarding providing NCC training in Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) and Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs);(b) if so, the details thereof along with the time by which the said provision is likely to be implemented;(c) whether the number of admissions has increased due to growing interest of students in CBSE course;(d) if so, the details of the comparative analysis in this regard, State-wise; and(e) whether the Government proposes to open new KVs at each development block level keeping in view the growing interest of students in studies/education and also to provide CBSE course facility to the students in rural areas and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (e) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.******STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 98 RAISED BY DR. DHAL SINGH BISEN, HON’BLE MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT TO BE ANSWERED ON 26th JULY 2021 REGARDING NCC TRAINING IN KVs AND JNVs.(a) & (b) The provisions of NCC training have been implemented in 308 Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) and 319 Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs). NCC training is implemented in the KVs and JNVs as per the provisions and policy issued by Directorate General NCC, Ministry of Defence. Implementation of NCC training in KVs and JNVs is a continuous process which depends upon the vacancies and necessary approvals of the Directorate General, NCC.(c) The number of students in Classes X & XII in CBSE schools has increased. The overall position of the past 3 years is given below:-YearClass XClass XII201917,63,56911,89,389202018,77,85611,73,422202121,14,30413,71,482(d) The States / UTs-wise number of students appearing in classes X and XII from the CBSE affiliated schools in the last three years is annexed.(e) Opening of new Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) is a continuous process. KVs are not opened Block-wise or District-wise. KVS are opened primarily to cater to the educational needs of the wards of transferable Central Government Employees including Defence & Para-Military personnel, Central Autonomous Bodies, Central Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) and Central Institute of Higher Learning (IHL) by providing a common programme of education.Proposals for opening of new KVs are considered only if sponsored by Ministries or Departments of the Government of India / State Governments / Union Territories (UTs) Administrations and resources committed by them for setting up a new KV. The proposals received from various sponsoring authorities found fulfilling the mandatory pre-requisite for opening of new KVs have to compete with other such proposals under the “Challenge Method” and also subject to approval of the competent authority.Regarding provision of CBSE course facility to students in rural areas, students are already being provided the same. Out of 25606 CBSE affiliated schools, 13633 schools are in rural area.******
