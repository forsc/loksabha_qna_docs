                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1682






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






MEMU Car Shed




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry has under its consideration, a proposal for the setting up of a new MEMU car shed to cater to North Bihar in Sonpur, Saran District;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the current status, budgetary allocation and timeline for the project; and(c) the details of existing and proposed MEMU car sheds in the country, along with the railway divisions they cater to?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY                                     (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
