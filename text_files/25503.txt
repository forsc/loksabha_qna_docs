                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1275






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Centrally Sponsored Components of PMMSY




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampadana Yojana (PMMSY) contains any components aimed at relief or financial assistance to provide immediate help to the fisheries sector hit due to COVID; (b) 	whether any new employment has been generated within the fisheries sector through PMMSY, if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (c) 	the number of Sagar Mitras created in coastal fisher villages to encourage formation of Fish Farmers Producer Organizations (FFPOs) to help achieve the goals of PMMSY, State-wise; (d) 	the State-wise allocation, percentage utilization of activities carried out under the Centrally Sponsored components of PMMSY, with district-wise details for the State of Andhra Pradesh; (e) 	whether there is any delay in works in the State of Andhra Pradesh under this component of PMMSY, if so, the reasons thereof; and (f) 	the details of activities carried out, funds utilised in Srikakulam district under the Central Sector components of PMMSY?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRYAND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) and (b):  The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a flagship scheme namely Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY) as a part of Atma Nirbhar Bharat with an estimated investment of Rs. 20,050 crore in all States/Union Territories(UTs) for a period of 5 years from 2020-21 to 2024-25 for  development of fisheries sector including welfare of fishermen.  PMMSY provides income support component namely “Livelihood and nutritional support for socio-economically backward active traditional fishers families for conservation of fish resources during fishing ban/lean period”. Under this component, Governmental financial  assistance of Rs. 3000/- is provided annually to each enrolled fishers and this Governmental assistance is shared in the ratio of 50:50 between the Central and general State, 80:20 between the Central and North Eastern States and Himalayan States while 100% for UTs.  The Governmental financial assistance of Rs. 3000/- per enrolled beneficiary and beneficiary annual contribution of Rs.1500/- is provided to each enrolled beneficiary annually during the fishing ban/lean period.  PMMSY inter-alia aims to create about 55 lakhs both the direct and indirect employment opportunities in fisheries and its allied activities. (c): The State/UT-wise numbers of Sagar Mitras approved during the year of 2020-21 under the PMMSY  are as under;Name of the State/UT	Number of Sagar Mitra Andhra Pradesh	350Daman & Diu and Dadra Nagar Haveli	6Goa	20Karnataka	120Kerala	222Maharashtra	173Odisha	600Puducherry	32Tamil Nadu	600Total	2123(d)  to  (f):   State-wise details of the proposals approved and funds released during 2020-21 under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme Component of  Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana along with the central funds utilized so far as reported by State Governments/ UTs is furnished at Annexure-I. The district-wise details of percentage of activities carried out in the State of Andhra Pradesh including Srikakulam District as reported by the Government of Andhra Pradesh under the  Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana  is furnished at Annexure-II. The Government of Andhra Pradesh has reported that the  State Department of Fisheries is taking timely action for approval of beneficiaries and physically grounding of projects, as and when proposals are received from applicants through online process.  It is further reported that during the financial year 2020-21, under the PMMSY, the State Government has allocated an amount Rs. 743.12 lakh to Srikakulam District and so far, 128 units/beneficiaries have been approved by the District Level Committee (DLC) of Srikakulam District for grounding of the scheme. *****
