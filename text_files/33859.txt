                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1178






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






Discount on Automotives and Tractors on GeM Portal




Jayanta Kumar Roy













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                








                                                    Rajveer (Raju Bhaiya) Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has imposed mandatory discount clause for manufacturers of automotives and tractors on GeM portal;(b)	if so, whether these discounts are to the extent of 20% on tractors, 12% on utility vehicles and 15% on commercial vehicles whereas in DGS&D regime, mandatory discount was only 5 per cent;(c)	if so, the grounds (study conducted, recommendations of committee, etc) on which such a huge discount are determined;(d)	whether many latest models are not available on GeM portal on account of high discounts, which are preventing various Ministries/Departments from acquiring latest vehicles;(e)	if so, whether huge discounts on these products are affecting the competitiveness of Indian companies to offer their modern/upgraded products on the GeM portal; and(f)	if so, other steps being taken by the Government to rationalise mandatory discount regime on automobiles on GeM Portal to make it more competitive for domestic manufacturers?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY(SMT. ANUPRIYA PATEL)(a), (b) & (c):	 No, Sir. Government has not fixed any minimum discount for manufacturers of automotives and tractors on GeM portal. GeM takes decision regarding discounts based on consultation/interactions with various stakeholders including automobile manufacturers (OEM).Minimum discount for tractors and selective categories of automotive in GeM portal is as per table below: Sl. No.	Vehicle category	Minimum Discount (Percentage)1	Tractors	20 %2	Ambulances	12%3	Buses	20%4	Automotive Chassis Fitted With Engine	15% 5	Built Up Trucks	15%6	Troop Carrier Police Vehicles	15%7	Water Truck Cabin Truck With Water Tank	15%8	Cars	12%9	Utility Vehicles	12%10	Two Wheeler- Motor Cycles and Scooters and Mopeds	12% There was no mandatory discount clause for manufacturers of Automotives and Tractors in DGS&D.(d):	At present, large numbers of vehicle models including latest models are available on GeM as may be seen from the table below:Sub Category	Make	ModelTractors	15	267Utility Vehicles	10	99Ambulances 	14	80Cars	3	36Buses	6	120Automotive Chassis Fitted With Engine	6	115Built Up Trucks	11	111Two Wheeler-Motor Cycles And Scooters And Mopeds	6	95Water Truck  Cabin Truck With  Water Tank	8	22Troop Carrier Police Vehicles	4	6 Further, the above mentioned vehicle categories are the most popular and high gross merchandise value categories as may be seen from the transaction value as given in table belowSub Category	Transaction Value (in Cr.)Utility Vehicles	4916.60Ambulances - R	1482.21Buses	1129.57Cars	920.66Troop Carrier Police Vehicles	655.80Two Wheeler-Motor Cycles And Scooters And Mopeds	624.73Built Up Trucks	614.22Automotive Chassis Fitted With Engine	500.37Tractors	306.56Water Truck  Cabin Truck With  Water Tank	199.80 (e): Not such incident reported yet.(f): GeM is doing regular consultations with various stakeholders including buyers, manufactures of automobiles and any issues raised during the consultations is examined and resolved by GeM.*****
