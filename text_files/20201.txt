                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Setting up of DMF




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether District Mineral Foundation (DMF) has been set up in Karnataka; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the details of the works carried out so far under Pradhan Mantri Khanij Kshetra Kalyan Yojana (PMKKKY) for the welfare of the affected people in the said region particularly in Kalaburagi district during the last three years?

District Mineral Foundations (DMFs) has been constituted in all 30 districts of Karnataka States. Information is given in annexure-I.(c).
