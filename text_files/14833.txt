                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3662






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Mental Health of Students




Geetha Viswanath Vanga














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is measuring mental illness through IQ levels of studentsand if so, the details thereof; and(b) the progress made for Tenth and Twelfth class students in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) & (b): Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, vide Gazette Notification dated05.01.2018 has issued guidelines for evaluation procedure for certification of variousspecified disabilities, including Mental Illness in a person under The Rights of Persons withDisabilities Act, 2016. As per the notification, Mental Illness is measured in the followingfour levels:<pre>(i) Mild Mental Disabilities : The IQ is in the range of 50 to 69.(ii) Moderate Mental Disability: The IQ is in the range of 35 to 49.(iii) Severe Mental Disability : The IQ is in the range of 20 to 34.(iv) Profound Mental Disability : The IQ in this category estimated to be under 20.</pre>Based on these guidelines, Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has issueda circular dated 14.02.2020 for the purpose of facilitation in examinations for its PwD( Persons with Disabilities) candidates.*****
