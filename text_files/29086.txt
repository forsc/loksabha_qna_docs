                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1015






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Conversion of Feedstock into Fertilizers




S.R. Parthiban














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state:(a)	whether any existing fertilizer plants in the public sector and cooperative sector has converted feedstock plants into fertilizer and dual fuel;(b)	If so, the details thereof during each of the last three years, State/UT-wise; and(c)	the details of total cost incurred on conversion of feedstock plants into fertilizers by each plant?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS(SHRI MANSUKH LAL MANDAVIYA)(a) & (b): No existing fertilizer plants in the public sector and cooperative sector has converted feedstock plants into dual fuel. However, during last three years, Madras Fertilizers Limited (MFL)-Manali, a Public Sector Undertaking in Tamil Nadu has converted Feedstock from Naphtha to Natural Gas (RLNG) which was fully commissioned on 28th July, 2019.(c):	Total cost incurred by MFL on conversion of feedstock was Rs.603 Crore.*****
