                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1266






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Promotion of Adventure Tourism in Western Ghat




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government has made any policy topromote adventure tourism in Western Ghats, Konkan sea line and hill stations to increase tourism in Maharashtra;(b)	the procedure to apply for new adventure sports tourism hub development by State Government, Private limited firms or individuals; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c): Development and promotion of tourism, including Adventure Tourism, is primarily the responsibility of the State Governments/UTs Administrations.  However, Ministry of Tourism provides Central Financial Assistance to State Governments/UT Administrations in the country including Maharashtra in different thematic circuits under Swadesh Darshan Scheme, which also include Adventure Tourism components.The following project was sanctioned in the State of Maharashtra in Coastal Circuit under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme, which includes Adventure Tourism components:- (in Rs. Crores)<pre>Project	Amount SanctionedDevelopment of Sindhudurg Coastal Circuit [Shiroda Beach, Sagareshwar, Tarkarli, Vijaydurg (Beach & Creek), Devgad (Fort & Beach), Mitbhav, Tondavali, Mocehmad and Nivati Fort]	19.06</pre>The projects under the Scheme are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/UT Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier.*****
