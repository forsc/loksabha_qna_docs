                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4167






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Repair Work of NH-31




Annpurna Devi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of the dilapidated condition of National Highway (NH) No. 31 between Barhi and Rajauli; and (b) 	if so, the details of the work done to repair the said stretch during the last two years and 	the current year along with the amount spent thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) Yes Sir. An expenditure of Rs. 63.46 lakhs in the year 2017-18 and Rs. 108.92 lakhs in the year 2018-19 has been incurred to repair the Barhi-Rajauli stretch. Moreover, the work of 4-laning of Barhi-Koderma section of NH-31 has been awarded for an amount of Rs. 276.17 Crores with scheduled completion period as two years from appointed date. Further, the work of Detailed Project Report for 4-laning of Koderma-Rajauli section of NH-31 has also been initiated. *****
