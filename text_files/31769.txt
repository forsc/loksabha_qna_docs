                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3448






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Privately Run Ports




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any data of privately run ports in the country including those with facilities for capsize vessels, if so, the details thereof along with the name of companies running these ports;(b) the total revenue earned by each port along with the amount and value of cargo traded at each of these ports during the last five years; and(c) the details of revenue share of the Government as agreed at the time of handing out concessions for each of these ports?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c) Private sector participation has been allowed in  Major Ports on Public Private Partnership(PPP) basis, for specific projects/berths/terminals, through concession agreement for a specific period by way of bidding process and on revenue sharing/royalty between the Concessionaire and the Major Port Authority for specific terminals/assets within the port premises.Non- Major Ports are under the control of their respective State Maritime Board / State Government. Private Ports in the States are managed by State Governments through State Maritime Boards. The State Maritime Boards enter into a concession agreement with the private party to develop and operate the port in Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode. The concession agreement under the concessionaire’s obligation includes confidentiality rights of the concessionaire.
