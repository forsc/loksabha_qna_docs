                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1619






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram




Kotagiri Sridhar













                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu
                                                








                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of projects approved under Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram       (PMJVK) in the State of Andhra Pradesh since 2019-20; and (b) the details of breakup of the funds sanctioned there-under and the kind of projects sanctioned, district-wise? 
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)  (a) : The Ministry of Minority Affairs implements the Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK) in 1300 identified Minority Concentration Areas (MCAs) of the Country, with the objective to develop socio-economic assets and basic amenities. In the State of Andhra Pradesh, Ministry of Minority Affairs implements PMJVK in 19 MCAs which includes 07-Blocks and 12-Towns. Since 2019-20, a total number of 67 projects with a total cost of Rs. 290.76 crore having central share of Rs. 174.45 crore has been sanctioned for the State of Andhra Pradesh under PMJVK. The projects include 06-Residential Schools, 01-Degree College, 03-Polytechnics, 04-ITIs, 04 Hunar Hubs, 04 School Buildings, 02-Working Women Hostels, 17-Hostels, 09-Common Service Centers, 05 Additional Classrooms and 12-Toilets. (b)  The details of break-up of the funds sanctioned there-under and the kind of projects sanctioned, district-wise since 2019-20 is given at Annexure. ***
