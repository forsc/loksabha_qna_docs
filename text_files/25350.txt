                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1211






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






PITNDPS Act




A. Ganeshamurthi














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state: (a) whether it is true that Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB) is on the process of preparing list of the 100 top drug mafia kingpins in the country and initiate action against them under the prevention of illicit traffic narcotics drugs and psychopropic substance (PITNDPS) Act; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the time line by when acttion will be taken against them?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c): Yes, Sir. Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB), Ministry of Home Affairs, has already identified top 100 drug traffickers/kingpins and has initiated action against them under Prevention of Illicit Traffic in Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances (PITNDPS) Act, 1988. As part of the ongoing process, NCB has initiated total 25 PITNDPS proposals since December, 2019 after identification process. In 21 proposals, Department of Revenue (DoR) has issued detention orders, out of which 13 proposals are confirmed by Advisory Board. xxxxx
