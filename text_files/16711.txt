                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

387






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Installation of E-PoS Devices in Ration Shops




M V V Satyanarayana













                                                    Talari Rangaiah
                                                








                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu
                                                








                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has given directions to State Governments to install E-PoS devices in ration shops across the country; (b) if so, the details thereof and the States that have partially or fully implemented the program; (c) 	whether the Central Government plans to reimburse Rs. 130 crores for already purchased E-Pos devices by Andhra Pradesh which is pending with Government of India; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) & (b): Yes sir. This Department in November 2014 had issued the implementation guidelines to all States/UTs for automation of Fair Price Shops (FPS) by installing electronic Point of Sale (ePoS) devices to bring transparency and efficiency in the distribution of subsidised foodgrains to beneficiaries covered under the National Food Security Act, 2013 (NFSA). So far, about 90% of total 5.4 Lakh FPSs in the country at present are having ePoS devices. A State/UT wise statement showing status of implementation is annexed.............2/--2-(c) & (d): 	Proposal/claim amounting to Rs.131.72 crore was received from State Government of Andhra Pradesh for release of Central assistance towards intra-State movement & handling of foodgrains fair price dealers’ margin (Basic + Additional) for the financial year 2015-16 to 2018-19 under National food Security Act, 2013.
