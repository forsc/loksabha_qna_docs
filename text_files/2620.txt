                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2459






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Tax Base




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Goods and Services Tax (GST) has helped in widening the tax base of the Government;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details of the licenses of companies which have been cancelled since the implementation of GST?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) and (b) Yes Sir. GST has helped in widening the tax base of the Government. As on 2nd July, 2019, the total number of active registrations under GST is 1,22,98,368 which includes 64,42,325 taxpayers who had migrated into GST from the previous indirect tax regime.(c) As on 2nd July, 2019, a total of 15,85,408 registration under GST has been cancelled. Such cancellation has been done either on an application filed by the registered person or suo-motu by the concerned tax officer in appropriate cases.
