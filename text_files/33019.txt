                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

519






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Vacant posts of AYUSH Doctors




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a ) the number of doctors working and the number of their posts lying vacant under National AYUSH Mission, State/UT-wise including district-wise in Maharashtra; and(b) the time by when the vacant posts of doctors are likely to be filled up?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a) & (b): The State/UT wise number of doctors approved for deployment under Ayush Health & Wellness Centers and the number of Ayush doctors approved for deployment in 50 bedded integrated Ayush hospitals, upgradation of Ayush hospitals under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National Ayush Mission (NAM) are furnished at Annexure-I& II. Further, as Public Health is a state subject, the primary responsibility to fill up the posts of Ayush doctors and maintaining their status comes under the purview of respective State/UT Government. However, under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the National Ayush Mission (NAM), the components for setting up of upto 50 bedded integrated Ayush hospitals and upgradation of exclusive standalone State Government Ayush Hospitals inter-alia are having provision for contractual deployment of Ayush doctors. In this regard, State/UT Governments are required to submit their proposals through State Annual Action Plan (SAAP) as per the guidelines of the scheme.*****
