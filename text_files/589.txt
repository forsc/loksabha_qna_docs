                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

457






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Persons engaged in Fisheries and Aquaculture




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of people engaged in fisheries, aquaculture and its allied activities, State-wise; (b)	the details of production, export and domestic consumption of aqua products in the last five years and the current year, year-wise and State-wise; and(c)	whether the Government is trying to consolidate India’s second position in the production of aqua products and planning to become the highest producer of aqua products in the world through various policy interventions?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	As per the 17th Livestock Census, 2003,  the state wise details of total number of people engaged in fisheries, aquaculture and its allied activities are furnished at Annexure –I.(b)	The details of production and export, State-wise & year-wise, during last five years are given at Annexure-II & III. Further, the domestic consumption of aqua products as per details of the information received from Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation is at Annexure-IV.  (c)	The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying has been implementing the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) on Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries for integrated and holistic development and management of fisheries in the country, with an outlay of Rs.3000 crore.  To address the Infrastructure requirements in Fisheries sector, Department of Fisheries is implementing Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund with corpus fund of Rs.7522 crore. These schemes are primarily aimed at enhancement of fish production in the country.
