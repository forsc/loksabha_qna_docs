                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

140






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Eklavya Model Residential School (EMRS) in Kerala




Rahul Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government proposes to establish new Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS) in Kerala;(b) if so, the details thereof including EMRSs sanctioned in Wayanad parliamentary constituency;(c) whether suitable land has been made available by the State Government for establishment of EMRSs in all eligible blocks in Kerala;(d) if not, the details of blocks where suitable land is not available;(e) whether the Government has received proposals for the establishment of Eklavya Model Residential Schools and Centre of Excellence in Sports in Kerala; and(f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)	Government has decided that every block with more than 50% ST population and at least 20,000 tribal persons, as per census 2011, is scheduled to have an Eklavya Model Residential School (EMRS) subject to availability of suitable land by the State Government.In Kerala 04 EMRSs have already been sanctioned under Article 275(1) till 2018-19, out of which 2 are reported to be functional. No new block of Kerala have been identified fulfilling the above mentioned criteria for establishment of EMRSs. The details of 4 EMRSs sanctioned for Kerala including Wayanad parliamentary constituency is as under: - S.No. 	District	Block/ Taluka	Year of sanction	Whether Functional / Non Functional	Construction Status1.	Wayanad	Kalpetta (Vythri)	1997-98	Functional	Completed2.	Idukki	Idukki 	1998-99	Functional	Completed3.	Palakkad	Attapady (Manarkad)	2017-18	Non Functional	Not Started4.	Kasargod	Parappa	2018-19 (Old)	Non Functional	In Progress (c)	Yes Sir. As reported, State Government has identified land for remaining 02 non-functional EMRSs.(d)	Does not arise.(e)	Besides the EMRSs as mentioned above, no other proposal for the establishment of Eklavya Model Residential Schools and Centre of Excellence in Sports in Kerala has been received.  (f)	Does not arise.*****
