                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2771






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Swachh Bharat Abhiyan




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds allocated to Tamil Nadu under the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Rural),construction of community toilets and toilets for individual household during eachof the last five years;(b) whether the Government has any plans to check, verify and audit the physical condition of toilets constructed under the said Abhiyan; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) 	Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) [SBM(G)] is a demand driven scheme, hence fixed State-wise allocation of funds is not made.  However, details of funds released by the Government of India to Tamil Nadu under SBM(G) during each of the last five years are as under:Financial Year	Funds released (Rs. in crore)2014-15	205.122015-16	78.942016-17	537.022017-18	865.932018-19	760.99Total	2,448.00And, the number of Community Sanitary Complexes (CSCs) and individual household latrines (IHHLs) constructed under SBM(G) in Tamil Nadu during each of the last five years, as per the data reported by the State on the online Integrated Management Information System (IMIS), are as under:Financial Year	No. of IHHLs constructed	No. of CSCs constructed2014-15	3,80,047	2342015-16	9,46,386	702016-17	11,39,671	302017-18	22,06,083	212018-19	6,20,766	30Total	52,92,953385(b) and (c)   Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation have conducted National Annual Rural Sanitation Survey (NARSS) through an independent agency in 2017-18 and 2018-19.  Under these sample surveys, detailed information have been collected on access to toilets, usage of toilets, open defecation free (ODF) status of villages, etc.  Advisory has also been issued to the States to conduct similar kind of survey at State level.
