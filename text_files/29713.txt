                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1380






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Beneficiaries of NPS




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of beneficiaries above 60 years of the monthly contribution scheme under the National Pension Scheme (NPS);(b)	the details thereof, State-wise; and(c)	the district-wise details of Uttar Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a) & (b) As informed by Pension Fund Regulatory and Development Authority (PFRDA), the number of beneficiaries above 60 years under the National Pension System (NPS) is 4,04,566. This includes beneficiaries who are contributing under NPS after 60 years and those who have exited NPS after 60 years and are receiving annuity. The State-wise details are given at Annexure A.
