                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

381






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Implementation of Schemes




Saikia Dilip














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of various educational schemes being implemented by the Government acrossthe country, State/UT-wise including Assam;(b) the total fund allocated and utilised for these schemes across the country, State/ UT-wise,particularly in Assam;(c) whether the Government proposes to introduce certain special benefit schemes for North-Eastern States; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) Ministry of Human Resource Development is implementing following majoreducational schemes across the country including Assam:<pre>(i) Samagra Shiksha(ii) Saakshar Bharat(iii) National Programme of Mid Day Meals in Schools(MDM)(iv) Scheme for providing education to Madrasas/ Minorities(v) National Means-cum Merit Scholarship Scheme (NMMSS)(vi) National Scheme of Incentive to Girls for Secondary Education (NSIGSE)(vii)Research and Innovation(viii) Pandit Madan Mohan Malaviya National Mission on Teachers and Teaching(ix) Global Initiative for Academic Network (GIAN)(x) Technical Education – Quality Improvement Programme2(xi) Programme for Apprenticeship Training- Scholarships & Stipends(xii) National Education Mission : Rashtriya Uchhatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA)(xiii) Student Financial Aid - Interest Subsidy and contribution for Guarantee Fund,Scholarship for College and University Students, PM Research Fellowship(ix) Digital India e-learning - National Mission in Education through ICT, Setting ofVirtual Classrooms and Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs), e-ShodhSindhu, National Digital Library, National Academic Depository<\pre>The total fund allocated for these schemes in the financial year 2018-19 (R.E.) wasRs 50222.15 crore , out of which Rs 47112.61 crore has been utilized.(c) & (d) The North Eastern States including Assam are enjoying Special Category Status dueto their distinct characteristics. Central government sanctions 90 percent in the formof grants in plan assistance to the States in special category.
