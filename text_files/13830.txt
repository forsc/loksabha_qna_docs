                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2569






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Protection of Coastline




Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has taken steps to protect fragile coastlines of Maharashtra and create sustainable management of coastal and marine resources in Sindhudurg and Ratnagiri; (b)	 if so, the details thereof; (c)	the details of stretches of the coast of Maharashtra where the Coastal Zone Management Project is being implemented;(d)	whether the Government has taken or proposes to take steps for mangrove plantation in Maharashtra specially in Mumbai, Sindhudurg and Ratnagiri; and (e)	if so, the details of the projects undertaken in Maharashtra? 	






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) to (e) The Coastal Regulation Zone (CRZ) has been notified with an objective of conserving and protection of ecology, resources and facilitating development and livelihood security in the coastal regions of the country.  The Integrated Coastal Zone Management Project was launched to further the objectives of the CRZ Notification.  The Project, besides the national component, had State level components only in identified coastal stretches of 3 pilot States viz. Gujarat, Odisha and West Bengal.However, under the National component, a detailed study has been undertaken for shoreline changes, sediment movement and coastal erosion pattern.  Further, the project also undertook delineation and mapping of the high tide line, the hazard line and of Ecologically Sensitive Areas, as noted in the CRZ Notification, along the coast line of the main land, including the State of Maharashtra. The Government has taken steps to sustain and enhance mangrove in the country through promotional as well as regulatory measures. The promotional measures are being implemented through a Central Sector Scheme of the government.Further, Government of Maharashtra has initiated measures which includesetting up of a dedicated Mangrove Cell for the conservation of mangroves,intensifiedpatrolling to prevent degradation of mangroves, new mangrove plantations etc.****
