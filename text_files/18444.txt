                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2182






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Eradication of Blindness and Leprosy




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the amount of central aid provided to the States/UTs for the eradication of blindness and leprosy during the current year;(b) the funds released for this purpose during the current year so far, State/UT-wise;(c) whether the Government has issued any guidelines for the utilisation of central assistance or grant; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a & b) The detail of funds released  to the States/UTs under National Health Mission (NHM) during financial year 2020-21 for the eradication of blindness and leprosy  are given below:-                                                          (Rs. in lakh)Non Communicable Disease (NCD) Flexi pool including National Programme for Control of Blindness and Visual Impairment (NPCB&VI)	5069.00National Leprosy Eradication Programme (NLEP)	567.00State/UT-wise details of funds released are at Annexure. (c & d) The Government has issued Programme Implementation Plan (PIP) guidelines for use of the Central grant-in-aid for implementing various programmes including these programmes.
