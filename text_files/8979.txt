                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2524






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Progress in Rural Development Schemes




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the rural development schemes implemented/under implementation across the country, State-wise including Maharashtra;(b)	the funds allocated to the States during the last three years and the current year, State-wise;(c)	the performance of the States during the said period along with the present status, State-wise;(d)	whether the Government has fixed any time limit for completion of pending projects under the said schemes; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	& (b):	For development of rural areas through employment generation, strengthening of livelihood opportunities, promoting self-employment, skilling of rural youths, provision of social assistance, infrastructure development etc., the Ministry of Rural Development is implementing Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM), Deen Dayal Upadhyay – Gramin Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY),  Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana – Gramin (PMAY-G), Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), Shyama Prasad Mukherjee RuRBAN Mission, National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP) and Sansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY) in the country including Mahashtra. State-wise funds allocated under various rural development scheme during last three year and current year is at Annexure-I (a) to I (h).(c)	to (e):	The programmes/schemes are implemented as per their guidelines/ framework of Implementation (FFI). The pace of implementation is reviewed regularly during Performance Review Committee meetings, visits by officials of the Ministry to the States/UTs etc. State-wise performance during last three years and the current year is at Annexure-II (a) to (g).
