                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

94






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Garib Kalyan Rozgar Yojana




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the district-wise details of Schemes being run in Uttar Pradesh under Garib Kalyan Rojgar Abhiyaan (GKRA);(b) 	the district-wise details of total number of persons benefitted from this scheme in Uttar Pradesh;(c)	whether the Government has any plan to provide employment to the persons benefitting from this scheme; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




(a)	Garib Kalyan Rojgar Abhiyaan (GKRA) was focused on 25 works across the 116 selected districts of 6 States including districts of State of Uttar Pradesh.
