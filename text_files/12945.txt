                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1635






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






PSBs in Foreign Countries




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of branches of Public Sector Banks (PSBs) functioning in foreign countries as on date, country-wise;(b) the number of these branches running in loss; and(c) the amount of money paid by these banks to the Government of those countries where these branches are located?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a) to (c): As per data reported by Reserve Bank of India (RBI), as on 31.12.2019, 103 branches of PSBs are functioning in foreign countries, out of which, for the period from 1.4.2019 to 31.12.2019, 77 branches are in profit and 26 branches are functioning with negative profit after tax. Country-wise number of branches is at Annex.With regard to the amount of money paid by these banks to the Government of those countries where these branches are located, RBI has apprised that the data are not maintained.***** Annex   Lok Sabha unstarred question no. 1635, regarding PSBs in Foreign Countries Number of branches of Public Sector Banks in foreign countriesCountry	Number of branches  (as on 31.12.2019)	Country 	Number of branches (as on 31.12.2019)Australia	3	Korea (South)	1Bahama Islands	2	Maldives	1Bahrain	3	Mauritius	2Bangladesh	1	Myanmar	1Belgium	4	Nepal	1Bhutan	1	New Zealand	2Botswana	3	Russia	1Cambodia	1	Saudi Arabia	1Canada	1	Seychelles	1Cayman Islands	1	Singapore	6China (Mainland) People’s Republic	4	South Africa	4Fiji Islands	1	South Korea	1France	2	Sri Lanka	3Germany	1	Sultanate of Oman	2Ghana	1	Tanzania	3Guyana	1	Thailand	1Hong Kong	9	Trinidad and Tobago	1Indonesia	2	United States of America	5Israel	1	Uganda	2Japan	2	United Arab Emirates	6Jersey	1	United Kingdom	8Kazakstan	1	Vietnam	1Kenya	2	Zambia	1 Source: RBI*****
