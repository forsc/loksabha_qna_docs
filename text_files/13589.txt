                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2527






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Upgradation of Airports in Madhya Pradesh




Shankar Lalwani














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of airports under Airport authority of India in Madhya Pradesh;(b) the time by which Indore Airport is likely to be upgraded to international level alongwith the action taken in this regard;(c) the number of airports proposed to be upgraded to international level in Madhya Pradesh;(d) the time by which international flights are likely to be introduced after upgradation of Indore Airport; and(e) the time by which visa facility is likely to be extended to Indore Airport?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): Airports Authority of India (AAI) owns and maintains 8 airports in Madhya Pradesh.;(b) to (d): Indore airport has been notified as Customs Airport to handle International flight operations.  AAI has upgraded Bhopal, Indore and Khajuraho Airports in Madhya Pradesh for simultaneous operation of Domestic and International passengers by constructing new Integrated terminal buildings and other requisite facilities for international operations. International flights are already operating at Indore Airport. ;;(e) At present, no such proposal is pending for consideration of the Government.
