                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4361






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Construction of Stadiums




Chandeshwar Prasad













                                                    Ashok Kumar Yadav
                                                








                                                     Sunil Kumar Pintu
                                                








                                                    S. Venkatesan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and the number of sports stadiums in the country, State/UT-wise including Bihar and Tamil Nadu, district-wise;(b) the total number of sports stadium under construction in the country, State/UT-wise including Bihar and Tamil Nadu, district-wise;(c) the total funds utilized for construction of new sports stadium in the country, State/UT-wise including Bihar and Tamil Nadu, district-wise; and(d) whether the Government has any proposal to construct modern stadium in the country and if so, the details thereof, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) to (d) ‘Sports’ being a State subject, the responsibility to construct stadiums rests primarily with the respective State/Union Territory Governments. The Union Government supplements their efforts. The stadiums in various parts of the country are under the jurisdiction of the respective bodies/departments of the State/UT Governments. As such, the information about them is not maintained in this Ministry. However, under the “Utilisation and creation/upgradation of sports infrastructure” component of the Khelo India Scheme, this Ministry has sanctioned 289 sports infrastructure projects of various categories amounting to ? 2396.49 crore across the country, including Bihar (11) and Tamil Nadu (5), on the basis of proposals received from them from time to time. State/UT-wise details of the same are at Annexure. The complete details of the projects are available on the official website of this Ministry at https://yas.nic.in/khelo-india-infrastrucutre.
