                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1240






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






PDS During COVID-19 Pandemic




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has State-wise details of subsidised rates at which food grains were supplied during the COVID-19 pandemic and the expense incurred thereon; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) and (b): 	The Central Issue Prices (CIP) of wheat and rice, fixed by Govt of India for issuing foodgrains under NFSA and OWS (Other Welfare Schemes) are as under:                                                                                    (Rate: Rs./Quintal)Name of the Scheme	Rice	WheatNFSA (AAY/PHH)	300	200Mid-Day Meal	300	200Wheat Based Nutrition Programme	300	200Welfare Institution & Hostels for ST/SC/OBC	565	415Annapurna	565	415Nutritional Programme for Adolescent Girls	565	415NFSA (Tide over)	830*	610* Rate @795/qtl for common rice under APL is applicable only for J&K, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Sikkim and NE States. .......2/--2-Apart from issuance of foodgrains under regular schemes of NFSA and OWS, due to disruption caused by COVID-19 pandemic, DFPD, MoCAF&PD, GoI during 2020-21 & 2021-22 also issued additional allocation of foodgrains under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojna (PMGKAY I to IV), Atma Nirbhar Bharat Scheme for migrant labourers, Scheme for Non-NFSA card holders and Supply to Charitable/ Non-Governmental Organizations at following rates:                                                                                             (Rate: Rs./Kg)Name of the Scheme	Rice	WheatPradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojna (PMGKAY I to IV)	Free of costAtma Nirbhar Bharat Scheme	Free of costScheme for Non-NFSA card holders	22/-	21/-Supply to Charitable/ Non-Governmental Organizations	22/-	21/-Details of State-wise expenses incurred by FCI on account of foodgrain issued in addition to regular expenses due to Covid pandemic during Financial Year 2020-21 and 2021-22 are attached at annexure-I and that of food subsidy released to DCP States during FY 2020-21 and 2021-22 at annexure-II.*** Annexure-IIANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) AND (b) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1240 FOR ANSWER ON 27.07.2021 IN THE LOK SABHA.State-wise Food Subsidy released under PMGKAY (as on 21.07.2021) to DCP States is as below-(Rs.in crore)S.
