22.07.2019






Funds Provided for Maintenance of Unprotected Monuments




Ramapati Ram Tripathi














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the State/Union territory-wise details of the unprotected heritage monuments places in different parts of the country;(b)	Whether the Government allocates funds for the maintenance of unprotected heritage places/monuments;(c)	if so, the State/Union Territory-wise details thereof during the current year and each year of the last three years including Uttar Pradesh and if not, the reasons therefor;(d)	whether the Government has received any proposal from Uttar Pradesh regarding fund allocation for heritage places; and(e)	   if so, the details thereof along with the action taken on it?
