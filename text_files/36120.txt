                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3540






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Tax Incentives to Promote Air Sports




S. Jagathrakshakan














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has proposed tax incentives and dedicated airspace at select locations to promote air sports such as aerobatics, hand gliding, paragliding, and sky-diving;(b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) to (c)  Currently, there is no proposal in the Government for providing tax incentives to promote air sports. The proposed National Air Sports Policy (NASP 2022) is in draft stage. It was released in January 2022 for public comments. The draft proposes that dedicated airspaces may be considered in areas of frequent or seasonal air sports operations. Such dedicated airspaces for air sports would be established after due consideration of various safety and security aspects including the presence of military airspaces or civil/military aerodromes in the proximity.;;The Government has received several comments from the public on the draft NASP 2022 and the same are currently being considered.******
