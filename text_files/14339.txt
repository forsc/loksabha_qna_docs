                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3179






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Helicopter Service from Bandipur to Quazigund




Mohammad Akbar Lone














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any proposal under consideration of the Government to provide helicopter service from Bandipur to Quazigund and Dawar; and (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) and (b): Union Territory (UT) of Jammu & Kashmir (J&K) has nominated Pawan Hans Limited (PHL), a Public Sector Enterprise under the administrative control of Ministry of Civil Aviation, for providing helicopter services since April, 2017 in J&K (now UT of J&K), under subsidy scheme of Ministry of Home Affairs. UT of J&K decides the routes on which PHL would fly. Bandipura to Dawar is one of the routes earmarked by administration of J&K for regular services and PHL is operating helicopter services from Bandipura to Dawar (Gurez), UT of J&K.
