                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1731






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






MoU between India and Brazil in LPG Projects




V. Kalanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to sign an MoU with Brazil in Liquified Natural Gas (LNG) projects, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(b) the details of the funds estimated under this MoU;(c) the details of the target likely to be set;(d) the details of the number of research and development work likely to be done through this MoU within the country including Tamil Nadu;(e) whether the Government has included the expansion of the regional energy infrastructure networks under this MoU; and(f) if so, the details thereof along with the number of work likely to be done in Tamil Nadu along with the time by which it is likely to be started?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)( a) to (f): Government of India has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Government of Brazil on 25th January, 2020 for cooperation in the field of oil and natural gas, which also has a provision for cooperation in Liquefied Natural Gas projects. The MoU is non-binding and has no financial implications.*******
