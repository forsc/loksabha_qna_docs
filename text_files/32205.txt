                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3647






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Skill Development Centre in Bihar




Ajay Kumar Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       whether Skill Development Centres are working in the State of Bihar and how many youths and girls have benefited in their lives on the basis of training and if so, the details thereof, district-wise; (b)       whether the Skill Developments Centres are operational in district Bhagalpur, Bihar and are also planned to be opened in future so that youth and girls can be trained; and (c)        if so, the details thereof and name of the institute where centers are running at present?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)       Yes, Sir. Under Skill India Mission, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is implementing various programmes/ scheme for the skill development of youth across the country, including the State of Bihar. In State of Bihar, presently 1,486 Skill Development Centres are operational. Also, 9.81 lakh candidates including 2.72 lakh female candidates have benefitted in the State of Bihar. The number of operational skill development centres in the State of Bihar under the programmes of MSDE is given below: Programme/Scheme in Bihar	No. of centres operationalOperational Training Centres (TCs) under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) (including PMKKs)	134Jan ShikshanSansthan (JSS)	21Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs)	1,331Total	1,486 The district-wise details of operational centres and female candidates trained are given in Annexure. (b)       Yes, Sir. 40 Skill Development Centres are currently operational in Bhagalpur district of State of Bihar under PMKVY and ITIs. Further, Ministry promotes establishment of model and aspirational skill centres known as Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKK) in every district for imparting skill training in Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode throughout the country, including the State of Bihar. Currently, 49 PMKKs have been allocated to the State of Bihar, of which 48 have been established. Further, in   Bhagalpur district, 1 PMKK has been established by AISECT Limited (AISECT SKILLS MISSION). The district-wise details of operational centres in State of Bihar are given in Annexure.
