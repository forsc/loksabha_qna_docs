                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4518






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Dissemination of Information on Schemes/Programmes




 Sunil Kumar Pintu














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any concrete plan for the dissemination of information of Government/Non-Governmental Schemes related to education and employment;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	whether more effective measures are likely to be taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES[SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR](a) to (c) Ministry of Information and Broadcasting publicizes and generates awareness to masses on various schemes/programmes of Government through different media platforms, viz. electronic, print and social media as well as through interpersonal communication including those related to education and employment. In order to ensure effective outreach to citizens in a concerted manner, the Ministry through its Media Units employs 360 degree approach for information dissemination of Government’s flagship schemes and programmes.******
