                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3056






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Identification of Talent for 2020 and 2024 Olympics




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has recently constituted a committee under Sh. Abhinav Bindra towards identifying and supporting talent for 2020 and 2024 Olympics;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the action taken by the Government on the recommendations made bythe committee?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[KIREN RIJIJU](a) to (c): The Government has not constituted recently any committee under Shri Abhinav Bindra. Shri Abhinav Bindra was Chairman of the TOPS (Target Olympic Podium Scheme) Elite Athletes Identification Committee from 30.01.2017 to 24.12.2017. The Committee recommended 220 athletes for support under TOPS, gave recommendations regarding norms and criteria for identification/ selection of athletes for support under TOPS, protocols for monitoring and review of identified athletes and funding tosportspersons indicated for violation of anti doping rules. The recommendations of the Committee were largely accepted and the said 220 athletes were included for support at that time under TOPS.****
