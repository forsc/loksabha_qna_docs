                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3021






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Beti Bachao Beti Padhao




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama













                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                








                                                    Vijay Baghel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    	the total number of districts where the BBBP scheme has been implemented till date, State-wise; (b) 		whether the Government proposes to include more districts under the scheme; (c) 		if so, the details thereof indicating the requests received and the districts proposed to be included thereunder, State-wise including Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat and Mungeli district of Chhattisgarh; and (d) 		whether the Government has partnered with any NGO for implementation of this scheme in these States and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a to d) The Ministry of Women and Child Development has received the requests from the States of Uttar Pradesh¸ Himachal Pradesh and Mungeli district of Chhatisgarh for inclusion under the Scheme.  However, the Beti Bachao Beti Padhao Scheme (BBBP) is being implemented across the country in 640 districts (as per census 2011) since 8th March, 2018.   Out of these, 405 districts are covered through multi - sectoral intervention and advocacy& media, while remaining 235 districts are covered under advocacy and media only.  State/UT-wise list of number of districts under the Scheme is attached as Annexure –I. The multi - sectoral intervention under BBBP scheme is implemented by the District Magistrates in their respective districts. Under Advocacy and Media campaign, a Nation-wide media campaign is implemented to sensitize the people through various Government media agencies.********  Annexure – IStatement refer to in reply to Part (a to d) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 3021 for  06.12.2019 regarding Beti Bachao Beti Padhao raised by Shri Rajeshbhai Chudasama, Shri Vijay Baghel, Shri Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy, Shri P.V.
