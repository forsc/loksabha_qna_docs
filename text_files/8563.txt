                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2150






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Reasons for not Releasing Funds for Sree Narayan Guru Tourism Circuit




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the reasons for not releasing fund for Sree Narayana Guru Spiritual Tourism Circuit Project linking Sivagiri and other places in Kerala under Swadeshi Darshan Scheme; (b) 	whether the Government will consider releasing fund for completion of this project without delay; and (c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	The Ministry of Tourism had sanctioned the project Development Sivagiri Shree Narayana Guru Ashram- Arruvipuram- Kunnumpara Sree Subrahmania-   Chembazhanthi Sree Narayana Gurukulam in Kerala under Spiritual Circuit theme of Swadesh Darshan Scheme in 2018-19 for Rs.69.47 crore.As per the scheme guidelines, the first installment of funds is released after completion of tendering process and submission of work orders, good for construction Detailed Project Report, statuary approvals from respective agencies for construction initiation and detailed Bill of Quantities. The implementing agency has not submitted these documents to the Ministry. ********
