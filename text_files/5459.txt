                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4967






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Rehabilitation of Beggars




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has started any scheme to eradicate the social evil like begging in the country and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government proposes to initiate people education programme to ensure the rehabilitation of beggars with the Non-Governmental Organisations and through Non-Government financing; and (c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a): The States are responsible for taking necessary preventive and rehabilitative steps for beggars. As per available information, 20 States and 2 Union Territories have either enacted their own Anti-Beggary legislation or adopted legislations enacted by other States/UTs. Presently, there is no Scheme being implemented by this Ministry for beggars.(b) & (c): The National Backward Class Finance Development Corporation, a Government of India Undertaking under this Ministry was entrusted with the task of Skill Development of Beggars community during 2018-19. The Corporation has successfully completed skill development training for 400 members of beggars community.****
