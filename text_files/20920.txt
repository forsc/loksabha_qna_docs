                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2117






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Skill Development Scheme




Sunil Kumar Soni













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                








                                                    Arun Sao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)      the details of the schemes being implemented by the Government for skill development of the youth during the last three years including Maharashtra;(b)      the details of funds allocated and released by the Government to the states including the States of Chhattisgarh and Maharashtra during each of the last three years and the current year, State-wise and scheme-wise;(c)        whether any proposal has been sent to the Union Government by the State Government of Chhattisgarh during the last three years; and(d)       if so, the details thereof along with action taken by the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)      Under Skill India Mission, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is implementing Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), Scheme of Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) for Skill Development and National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS) across the country including State of Maharashtra.Under PMKVY 2.0, since inception, 1.07 Cr. candidates have been trained /oriented across the country. Funds amounting to Rs. 7,279 Cr. (approx.) has been released, till Feb''2021, to implementing agencies. There is a provision of State-wise funds allocation under Centrally Sponsored State Managed ( CSSM ) component of the PMKVY 2.0. Further, Under PMKVY 3.0, launched on 15th January, 2021, the demand driven short duration skill development training is being imparted at district level across the country. Till now, approx. 19.79 Cr has been released under  CSSM-PMKVY 3.0.    The scheme of Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) is being implemented through its institutional framework in the country with the objective to improve the occupational skills and technical knowledge of the non/neo literate. This scheme is central sector scheme and funds allocation is done directly to JSSs and no funds are released to State Government.Further, government launched the National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS) which makes special efforts to reach maximum number of establishments including MSMEs with an objective of the scheme is to promote apprenticeship training and to increase the engagement of apprentices. The State-wise details of funds disbursed under CSSM-PMKVY 2.0 and NAPS is given at Annexure.(c) and (d) Under CSSM component of PMKVY 2.0, a proposal had been received from Chhattisgarh State Skill Development Authority (CSSDA), Government of Chhattisgarh during FY 2016-17. After evaluation of proposal, Ministry has given in-principle approval of a total target of 48,532 candidates for FY 2016-20 with financial allocation of Rs. 71.16 Cr. which was further rationalized to Rs. 53.37 Cr. based on the performance of the State. Out of total allocation, funds amounting to Rs. 35.57 Cr. have been released so far to State of Chhattisgarh. Under CSSM-PMKVY 2.0, as on 19.01.202, 13,752 candidates have been trained in the State of Chhattisgarh.
