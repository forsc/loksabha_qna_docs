                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

142






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Violence and Assaults




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the large scale violence and assault against students ofJawaharlal Nehru University;(b) if so, has the Government taken cognizance of the matter;(c) whether the Government has constituted a high level enquiry committee to investigate thematters pertaining to attacks against students in JNU and ascertain the veracity of allegationsagainst the present Vice Chancellor about making preferential appointments made in variousteaching posts in the university;(d) whether the government is aware of any reports of students being unable to register for thewinter semester in JNU due to disruption of registration services for the upcoming academicsession; and(e) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ''Nishank'')(a) to (e): An incident of violence in the campus of Jawaharlal Nehru University has beenreported. University has informed that the Crime Branch of Delhi Police is investigating the matter.The registration process for Winter Semester 2020 without fine is going on smoothly.*****
