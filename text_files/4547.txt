                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4188






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Beautification of River Ghats




Shankar Lalwani














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any scheme for cleaning and beautification of river ghats; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether applications submitted by the State Governments are pending in this regard; and (d) if so, the time by which funds are likely to be sanctioned and the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (d)   Ghat cleaning works are primary responsibility of State Governments and Urban Local Bodies / Gram Panchayats. However, a project for cleaning of 84 ghats at Varanasi was sanctioned at a cost of Rs.15 crore for 3 years by National Mission for Clean  Ganga  of  Ministry of Jal Shakti . Subsequently, ghat cleaning projects have been taken up at Haridwar (72 ghats), Bithoor (19 ghats), Kanpur (20 ghats), Allahabad (21 ghats) and Mathura- Vrindavan (27 ghats). No application from the State Governments for Ghat cleaning  is pending with National Mission for Clean  Ganga under Namami Gange programme. River Front Development and construction/renovation/modernization of ghats have also been taken up on river Ganga.   *****
