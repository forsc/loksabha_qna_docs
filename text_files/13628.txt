                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2490






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Widening of Nellore-Thada NH




Adala Prabhakara Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to widen the Nellore-Thada National Highway from 	four lane to six lane; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	the steps taken by the Government in this regard during the last two years; and (d)	the time frame set for completion of the said project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d)  There is no such proposal for 6 laning of entire section at present. However, in a length of about 3.82 km which was  damaged due to flash flood in 2015,  the work of rehabilitation and upgradation to 6 lane has been awarded on EPC mode. The work has commenced  on 13.12.2019 and the stipulated date of completion of this work is 05.06.2021.*****
