                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3058






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Requisition under NPOP




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details about the total area required for organic certification process registered under the National Programme for Organic Production(NPOP), State-wise;(b)	whether the Government has any data on the installed production capacity and the under-utilisation capacity figures of the bio-fertilizers in the country and if so, the details thereof;(c)       whether the farmers in Delhi and adjoining areas have been encouraged to use  bio-fertilizers instead of chemical fertilizers; and(d)        if so, the details of land under organic farming in Delhi/NCR?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????      (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d):	The State-wise detail on the total area under organic certification process under the National Programme for Organic Production (NPOP) is given at Annexure I.		Government is promoting organic farming through dedicated organic farming schemes of Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) and Mission Organic Value Chain Development in North East Region (MOVCDNER) since 2015-16 in States including Delhi/ NCR. Under these schemes, farmers are primarily encouraged to adopt organic cultivation using various organic inputs including bio- fertilizers and provided end to end from production to marketing of organic produce. Hands-on training to farmers about on-farm production of organic fertilisers and its use are integral part of these schemes. Farmers are provided subsidy of Rs 31000/ ha / 3 years under PKVY and Rs 32500/ ha/ 3 years under MOVCDNER for various organic inputs including bio- fertilizers. The State-wise consumption of bio-fertilizers during last three years is given at Annexure Il and the land area under organic farming in Delhi as registered on PGS- India certification portal is 8259.74 ha.
