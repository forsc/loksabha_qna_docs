                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2440






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Skill Development Camps for SC/ ST, OBC, Girls and Women




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to organize training camps for skill development for SC/ST, OBC, Girls and Women in Bihar; and(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b)	Under Pradhan mantra Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), a flagship scheme of Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) in the Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) component there is a provision of orientation of trainees in the camp mode. These camps provides a mandatory orientation of 12 hours and wherever necessary provide bridge course training for up to 68 hours to candidates. The details of Females, Other Backward Class (OBC), Scheduled Caste (SC) and Scheduled Tribe (SC) enrolled, oriented, assessed and certified at such RPL Camps in Bihar is at ANNEXURE I.	The National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) has organized 54 Kaushal Melas in Bihar for building awareness, counselling and mobilization of candidates during April, 2018 to May, 2019.
