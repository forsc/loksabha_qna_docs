                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4160






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Traffic Jam on Service Road of NH-6




Rameshbhai Lavjibhai Dhaduk














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government is aware that trucks are parked on the service road in front of NTPC, HAEP and KRIBHCO townships on the section of Surat-Hazira National Highway-6 (NH-6) resulting in traffic jam;(b) 	if so, the details thereof along with the reaction of the Government thereto;(c) 	whether the Government/NHAI proposes to take steps in coordination with the local police administration to address the issues; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d) Yes sir, unauthorized parking on Surat-Hazira stretch at service roads and main carriage way has been reported. National Highways Authority of India has approached local police to remove unauthorized parking from time to time.*****
