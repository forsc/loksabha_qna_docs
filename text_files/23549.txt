                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4555






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Surveillance to Check Cross Border Smuggling




Gurjeet Singh Aujla














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Narcotics Control Bureau has strengthened the Surveillance Department at the Wagah Border to trap the illicit trade of narcotics hidden in spices and other goods which made drugs untraceable;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether new equipment have been provided to the surveillance forces at the border to identify the illicit trade of narcotics; and(d)	if so, the details of such equipment and the number of cases that have been detected using this equipment?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d) The Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB) has a robust mechanism of surveillance and works in close coordination with intelligence agencies, border guarding forces  and state police to curb drug trafficking.  For better coordination and information sharing among various Central and State Agencies and the border guarding forces, the Narco Coordination Centre (NCORD) mechanism and the Joint Coordination Committee (JCC) with DG, NCB  as  its  Chairman  serve  as institutional  mechanisms.  In  addition, the -2-L.S.US.Q NO. 4555 FOR 23.03.2021country’s international borders, including Wagah border, have been strengthened with the provisioning of Surveillance Devices, Night Vision Devices, Jammers, Mine Detectors, Trail Cameras etc.  The border guarding forces have an effective mechanism for surveillance at the borders and are equipped with latest gadgets and equipments. The details of State/UT-wise cases registered and drugs seized during 2017-2019 under Narcotics Drugs & Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985 are annexed. *****
