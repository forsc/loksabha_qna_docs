                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3485






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Monitoring Mechanism for Maintenance of Historical Sites




Chunni Lal Sahu














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any plan to institutionalise the monitoring mechanism of maintenance of historical and tourist sites of the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)     the district-wise names of all the historical and tourist sites in the State of Chhattisgarh;(d)	the details regarding the maintenance as well as revenue and expenditure in case of the said sites during the last two years and the current year; and (e)	the details of steps taken by the Government in this regard so far?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE  AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b)	The conservation, maintenance and monitoring of protected monuments in the country including historical and tourist sites is attended regularly by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) as per the availability of the resources. (c)	The details of protected monuments under the ASI in the State of Chhattisgarh are given in the Annexure.(d)  & (e)	The routine maintenance work of all the protected monuments in the  Chhattisgarh is attended regularly by the ASI  as per the availability of the resources.
