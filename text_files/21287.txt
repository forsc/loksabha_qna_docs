                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2469






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Smart Police Station Scheme




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to include all the States of the country under Smart Police Station Scheme; and(b) if so, the details thereof and the action being taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b) ''Public Order'' and ''Police'' being State subjects as per the Constitution of India, the principal responsibility of modernisation of Police Stations lies with the State Governments. There is no scheme named ‘Smart Police Station Scheme’, however, under the scheme of ‘Assistance to States for Modernisation of Police’, the Government of India provides assistance for acquiring modern gadgets and weaponry for police stations as per the proposal of State Governments.*****
