                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

54






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






National Level Sports Stadiums




Kanakmal Katara













                                                    Keshari Devi Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is a plan to construct national level sports stadiums to encourage international level sports in the tribal dominated areas of Rajasthan including Banswara and Dungarpur districts and in Prayagraj district in Uttar Pradesh which is known for education;(b) if so, the details thereof and the time by which the said plan is likely to be started and completed;(c) the amount of funds earmarked and released to promote sports in both Banswara and Dungarpur districts during the last five years including the details thereof; and(d) the provision of funds made during the years 2019-20 and 2020-21 for the local centre of Sports Authority of India (SAI) situated in Prayagraj?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) to (d) A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) to (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. †*54 ANSWERED ON 22.07.2021 REGARDING “NATIONAL LEVEL SPORTS STADIUMS” ASKED BY SHRI KANAKMAL KATARA AND SHRIMATI KESHARI DEVI PATEL, HON’BLE MEMBERS OF LOK SABHA(a) and (b) ‘Sports’ being a State subject, the responsibility of creating sports infrastructure including national level sports stadiums rests with the State / Union Territory Governments. Central Government supplements their efforts by bridging the critical gaps in sports infrastructure. No proposals have been received in this Ministry to construct sports stadiums in Banswara and Dungarpur districts of Rajasthan or Prayagraj district in Uttar Pradesh.(c) Funds are allocated by the Union Government Scheme-wise, not State/Union Territory or District-wise. However, funds released for development of sports in Rajasthan under Khelo India Scheme during the last five years are as under: -(Amount in ? crore)<pre>S. No.	Financial Year	Funds Released1	2016-17	38.902	2017-18	62.053	2018-19	14.874	2019-20	69.265	2020-21	36.05   </pre>(d) Sports Authority of India (SAI) releases funds to its Regional Centres instead of States/Districts. Funds allocated/released to the SAI Regional Centre, Lucknow (Uttar Pradesh) are as under:<pre>Financial Year	Amount (? in crore)2019-20	40.732020-21	36.42  </pre>Madan Mohan Malviya Stadium, Prayagraj for Lawn Tennis Discipline has been notified as Khelo India Centre (KIC) and ? 7 lakhs have been released.****
