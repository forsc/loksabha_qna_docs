                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3439






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Additional Funds to AIIMS




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has made available additional funds to new AIIMS-like institutions in the country to enhance facilities/infrastructure; (b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including Jharkhand; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	to (c) Funds for the new AIIMS sanctioned under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY) are made available out of combined budgetary allocation for the scheme. For setting up of new AIIMS as a project, funds are released to the Executing Agencies out of allocation under Capital Head of PMSSY Budget. AIIMS project-wise details of funds released during 2019 – 20 and 2020 – 21 is at Annexure – I.	For operational expenditure at institute level, funds are released to functional / partially functional AIIMS as Grants-in-Aid out of Revenue Head of PMSSY Budget, details of which are at Annexure – II. ************
