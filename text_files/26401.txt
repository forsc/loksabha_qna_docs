                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2157






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Chairman Post of LIC




A. Ganeshamurthi














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken a decision to abolish the Chairman post of Life Insurance Corporation (LIC) of India;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the reasons and purpose for abolishing the Chairman post?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a)	to (c): No, sir.  The Government has not taken a decision to abolish the office of Chairman in the Life Insurance Corporation of India. However, under the amended provisions of the Life Insurance Corporation Act, 1956, which have been brought into force from 30.6.2021, ‘Chairman’ in the Corporation stands re-designated as ‘Chairperson’.**
