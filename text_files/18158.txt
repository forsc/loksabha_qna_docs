                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1974






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Construction of NHs by NHAI




Parvatagouda Chandanagouda Gaddigoudar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the length of National Highways (NHs) presently under construction by National Highways Authority of India (NHAI), State-wise particularly in Karnataka;(b)	the length of NHs presently covered under tolling and the number of toll plazas are in operation, State-wise;(c)	whether the Government has taken any steps to increase the daily pace of NH construction in the coming months; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS[GEN. (DR.) V.K. SINGH (RETD.)] (a) & (b) Development of NHs is a continuous process and works are taken up in stretches depending upon preparedness, available resources and inter-se-priority.  In August, 2020, under implementation length of National Highways by NHAI is about 25,362 kms, details for the same are at Annexure-I.  Total length put for tolling by NHAI at present is about 31,092 Kms, the details of which are at Annexure-II.   Details of number of toll plazas are at Annexure-III.(c) & (d)    Frequent review meetings with the all stakeholders including State Governments, for resolving various issues (which are hindrances to the execution of projects) such as land acquisition, tree cutting, shifting of utilities, forest clearances, GAD approval, dispute resolution etc. pertaining to projects  are conducted at various levels periodically.
