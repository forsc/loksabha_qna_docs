                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2305






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






OCI Card Holders Travelling India




Manne Srinivas Reddy













                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                








                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                








                                                    Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the people of Indian origin and the Indian diaspora having Overseas Citizens of India (OCI) cards will not have to carry their old, expired passports for travel to India including in post-COVID-19 period, as was required earlier; and (b) if so, the details thereof/implementation status thereof/response received/violations noticed/action taken till date, country-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b):	OCI Cardholders are not required to carry their old passports while travelling to/from India. They are allowed to travel on the strength of their existing OCI Cards bearing old passport number along with the new passport.*******
