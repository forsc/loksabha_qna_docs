                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

997






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Green Belt




Gomati Sai














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the name of such industries out of total industries in Raigarh and Janjgir which developed green belt;(b)  	whether the green belt has been developed as per the guidelines, if so, the details thereof; and(c)  	the details of the action taken by the Government against those industries for not developing the green belt? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) to (c)-Development of green belt is generally one of the stipulated conditions of Environment Clearances accorded to various projects under the EIA Notification, 2006. As per the information available the details of the industries which have developed green belt in Raigarh and Janjgir is given in the Annexure. Non- development of green belt against the stipulated EC condition is considered as non-compliance for which issuance of Show Cause notices is considered by the Ministry on case to case basis.*****
