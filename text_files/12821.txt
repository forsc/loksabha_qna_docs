                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1632






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Technical Education




Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva













                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                








                                                    Pradeep Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the achievements of the third phase of the technical education quality improvementprogrammes in North Eastern States and hilly States including Bihar;(b) the type of assistance being provided to the students of the said States;(c) the number of students benefited under the said programmes; and(d) the provisions for financial assistance under the said programmes?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a), (c) & (d): TEQIP-III aims at improving quality and equity in engineering institutions in 19focus States in which 7 are Low Income States (Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh,Odisha, Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh), 3 are hilly States (Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmirand Uttarakhand), 8 are North-Eastern States (Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya,Mizoram, Nagaland, Sikkim and Tripura) and Union Territory of Andaman and Nicobar Islands.Achievements of the third phase of Technical Education Quality Improvement Programme(TEQIP-III) in North Eastern States and hilly States including Bihar are given as under:<pre>S.No StateTEQIPfacultyworkingAccreditation(programaccredited /availableprogram)Training forEXITExamination(trainedstudents/total)CollaborativeResearchProjects (No.of projects/grants in Cr.)DigitalBoard(Displayed)Financialassistanceprovided(Rs. in Cr.)No. ofstudentBeneficiaries1 NorthEast 95 14/26 (53%) 4030/7506(54%)26/Rs.3.44 Cr. 26 246 Cr. 411112 HillyArea 169 19/66 (29%) 7564/12364(61%)45/Rs.5.39 Cr. 72 234.25 Cr. 451643 Bihar 213 24/31 (77%) 3397/4636(73%)48/Rs.5.45 Cr. 74 96.20 Cr. 52486</pre>(b) TEQIP III provides financial assistance to students of selected AICTE approvedengineering colleges for:<pre>i. Induction programme for the first year studentsii. Student internships at IITs, NITs, IIM and other reputed institutionsiii. Industry visitsiv. Exit exam trainingv.
