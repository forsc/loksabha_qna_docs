                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1177






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Induction of Two Additional Battalions of CAPF




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Odisha Government has requested for induction of two additional battalions of Central Armed Police Force (CAPF) in Left Wing Extremist affected areas of the State;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the request under consideration; and(c) if not, the reason thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (c):	The Government of Odisha had requested for two additional battalions of Central Armed Police Force (CAPF) for deployment in LWE affected districts.  A large number of CAPF battalions are already deployed in Odisha to combat Left Wing Extremism.  The deployment of CAPFs in LWE affected States is a dynamic process based on the situation, availability of forces and other ground realities.*******
