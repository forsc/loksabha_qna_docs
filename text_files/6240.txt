                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

21






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Revenue Earned from Taj Mahal




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of persons who visited Taj Mahal and the revenue earned from it during each of the last five years;(b)	the amount spent on its maintenance during the period;(c)	whether pollution is affecting the colour of the monument; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the measures taken/being taken by the Government to protect its colour?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) The details of the number of visitors and the revenue earned during the last five years is as follows:Year	Total Number of Visitors	Revenue Generated (In Rs.)2014-15	6089901	212355330.002015-16	5070573	179227050.002016-17	6177196	550929860.002017-18	6565627	587604981.002018-19	7090207	864893100.00(b) The amount spent on the conservation and maintenance of Taj Mahal is as below:Year	Total (In Rs.)2014-15	39020190.002015-16	32837534.002016-17	45045819.002017-18	33842927.002018-19	54834582.00(c) & (d) The effect of pollution is there but the monument is cleaned periodically by appropriate methods, following archaeological norms and principles and is kept in presentable condition.********
