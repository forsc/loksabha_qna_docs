                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1908






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Disposal of Biomedical Waste




Dileshwar Kamait













                                                    Geeta Kora
                                                








                                                    Jugal Kishore Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of institutions identified by the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) which are not ensuring proper disposal of bio-medical waste; and(b) 	the details of hospitals against which action has been taken during the last three years for lacking adequate facilities for proper disposal of biomedical waste, State/ UT-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	After the re-notification of Biomedical Waste Management Rules, 2016 (BMWM Rules, 2016), the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) has assessed the compliance status of 16 Health Care Facilities (HCFs), 44 Common Biomedical Waste Treatment Facilities (CBWTFs), 20 Educational Institutions (EI) and other establishments. Based on the discrepancies observed in compliance, CPCB issued directions under section 5 of Environment (Protection) Act, 1986 against 12 HCFs and 28 CBWTFs. The CPCB had also imposed Environmental Compensation against 20 CBWTFs and 2 HCFs. Further, the non-compliances observed in respect of 92 Veterinary institutions were communicated to the respective State Governments and Union Territories. Additionally, for proper management of COVID-19 bio-medical waste, CPCB issued directions to 33 CBWTFs for not feeding data on a COVID-19 BMW Tracking App, which was developed for reporting the quantity of COVID-19 related biomedical waste collection and disposal.(b)	CPCB has initiated action against HCFs under Section 5 of Environment (Protection) Act, 1986 for violation of BMWM Rules, 2016, as mentioned below:State/ UT	No. of HCFs	Name of HCFAssam	1	Sub-divisional Railways Hospital Badarpur, Assam 788806Madhya Pradesh	1	Jaya Arogya Hospital (J.A.
