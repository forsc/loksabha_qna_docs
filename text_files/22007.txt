                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3152






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Womens Universities or colleges in Bareilly




Dharmendra Kumar Kashyap














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Universities dedicated exclusively for Girls and Women in Bareilly district;(b) 	whether the Government has any plan to open some new women’s University or College in Bareilly; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION (SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a):	There are six women’s colleges in Bareilly District.  There is no University dedicated exclusively for Girls and Women in Bareilly district.(b) & (c): 	As per the report received from the State Government, at present there is no such proposal under consideration to open some new women’s University or College in Bareilly.*****
