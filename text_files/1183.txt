                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

936






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Competition Faced by Toy Industry




Shivkumar Chanabasappa Udasi














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether domestic toys industry is facing competition from foreign manufactures and if so, the extent to which the domestic toys industry is affected;(b)	whether the Government has any proposal/proposes to encourage the manufactures to enable them to compete with global markets; and(c)	if so, the details thereof and the further steps proposed to be taken to save and promote domestic toys industry.                      






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a)to(c):  Government has taken several initiatives to support Indian manufacturing enterprises including toy industry through various schemes of this Ministry such as Micro & Small enterprises- Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP), Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme (CLCSS) , National Manufacturing Competitiveness Programme(NMCP) schemes namely Lean Manufacturing, Design Clinic and Incubation schemes to improve the productivity & competitiveness of the industry.             Ministry of Commerce and Industry has put in place quality & safety standards in respect of import of toys in the country.
