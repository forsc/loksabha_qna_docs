                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4826






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Funds for Flood Relief




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total funds allocated and utilised for rehabilitation due to flood and erosion in the North Eastern States including Assam during each of the last five years and the current year, year-wise;(b) whether the Government has any data on the losses of lives and properties due to flood and erosion during the last three years in Assam;(c) if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government would consider the request/demand for declaration of flood and erosion of Assam as National Calamity in the light of the fact that it causes disaster every year in the State; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a): The primary responsibility of disaster management rests with the State Government. To supplement the efforts of the State, the Government of India extends logistics and financial support to them to assist the rescue and relief efforts in the event of a natural disaster. Government of India also provides financial assistance to the affected States from State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF) and National Disaster    Response   Fund (NDRF) as   per laid down procedure for “immediate relief”. With regard to rehabilitation, this has to be undertaken by the concerned State Governments from their own resources/ State Schemes.--2--L.S.US.Q.No. 4826 FOR 23.07.2019 	Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region(MDoNER) has allocated central assistance of Rs.200.00 crores for the damaged infrastructure in the flood affected 5 States, namely, Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Nagaland, Manipur and Mizoram during 2019-20. 	North Eastern Council has funded projects for anti-erosion and flood control to the North Eastern States. The detail of ongoing projects funded in the last five years (2014-15 to 2018-19) is at Annexure.(b) and (c): Based on the information furnished  by the State Government of Assam, details of loss of lives and property during the last three years due to flood is :Year	Lives lost(No.)	Houses(Nos.)	Crop area(in lakh ha.)2016-17	64	66,887	2.352017-18	160	1,11,070	3.982018-19	45	1,03,967	0.31(d) and (e): Under the exiting guidelines, there is no provision for declaration of any natural calamity as National Calamity. *****
