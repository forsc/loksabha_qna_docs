                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4024






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Computerized Registration in Post Offices




Vijay Baghel














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the post offices where computers have not yet been provided for computerized registrations in Chhattisgarh; (b) 	the reasons therefor; and (c) 	the measures taken/proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to  (c)	Nil Sir, Computers have been provided to all the 351 Departmental Post Offices to carry out computerized operations in Chhattisgarh Postal Circle.
