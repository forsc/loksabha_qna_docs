                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1962






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Extraction of Methane Hydrate




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal













                                                    Arvind Kumar Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is making efforts for commercial extraction of methane hydrate deposits in Krishna-Godavari basin and Mahanadi- Andaman basin which has potential to make India energy independent; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b): Yes Sir. Gas hydrate exploration in India is steered by the Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas under National Gas Hydrate Program (NGHP). Till date, two  expeditions, the first in 2006 (NGHP-01) and the second in 2015 (NGHP-02) have been undertaken under National Gas Hydrate Program. Under NGHP-01, 39 holes were drilled to explore the presence of gas hydrate at 21 sites in Krishna-Godavari (KG), Mahanadi, Andaman, and Kerala-Konkan Basin. Under NGHP-02, 42 holes were drilled at 25 sites in KG and Mahanadi Basin. (c):  Does not arise in view of above.*********
