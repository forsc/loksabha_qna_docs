                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2324






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Strengthening of Cold Storage and Warehousing




 Bhavana Gawali (Patil)













                                                    Annasaheb Shankar Jolle
                                                








                                                    Umesh G. Jadhav
                                                








                                                    B. Y. Raghavendra
                                                








                                                    Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav
                                                








                                                    Sanganna Amarappa Karadi
                                                








                                                    Shardaben Anilbhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received any representation from various States to provide financial assistance the construction of cold storage and warehouses in Maharashtra and Karnataka especially Kalyana Karnataka side; and (b)	if so, the details thereof, district-wise including the number of cold storage and warehouses constructed in Maharashtra and Karnataka during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTRY OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? 	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): 	 No such representation has been received by the Government from any State to provide financial assistance for construction of cold storages and warehouses in Maharashtra and Karnataka including Kalyana, Karnataka.(b):	As per the information received from the States of Maharashtra and Karnataka and Central Warehousing Corporation (CWC), district-wise number of cold storages and warehouses constructed in Maharashtra and Karnataka during the last three years are at Annexure – I and Annexure – II respectively.
