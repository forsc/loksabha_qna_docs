                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

40






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Organisational Structure of Agriculture Ministry




P.P. Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is considering to change the organizational structure of the Ministry of Agriculture; (b) 	if so, the details thereof;. (c) 	whether the organizational structural change will affect the budget of the Ministry and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether any study was undertaken or any other process undertaken to bring the change; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof including process, results and people involved?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The change in organizational structure of Department of Agriculture, Cooperation and Farmers Welfare, Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare has not yet been finalized.(b) to (e):	Question does not arise.********
