                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1249






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Hike in School Fee




Kaushal Kishore














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that parents associations have submittedmemorandum to Central Government and the Government of Uttar Pradeshregarding increasing of school fees every year by Delhi Public Schools (DPS)and other private schools due to which these schools particularly DPS,Ghaziabad harassed their student in unconstitutional way;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether it is a fact that DPS and other private schools have refused to follow theguidelines issued by the Government of Uttar Pradesh regarding an increase inschool fees;(d) the reaction of the Government thereto and the details thereof; and(e) the details of guidelines regarding refund of increased fees by private schoolswith interest?
                                                





ANSWER




(a) to (e): Regulation of Fee and matters related thereto are the subject matter of theconcerned State Government.*****
