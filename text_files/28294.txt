                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

44






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Container Manufacturing Facility




Kuruva Gorantla Madhav













                                                    Talari Rangaiah
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of the fact that Andhra Pradesh has 170 million tonnes cargo turnover which is second only to Gujarat in the country;(b) if so, whether the Government has any plans of setting up a container manufacturing facility in Andhra Pradesh; and(c)  if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a)	to (c) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF STARRED QUESTION NO.44 BY SHRI KURUVA  GORANTLA  MADHAV  AND      SHRI TALARI RANGAIAH TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 01.12.2021 REGARDING CONTAINER MANUFACTURING FACILITY(a) Ministry of Railways does not have any specific information about Cargo Turnover in a specific State. Indian Railway mobilizes the resources as per transport requirements of Zonal Railways.  (b) & (c) As of now containers are procured from industry. There are no plans for setting any container manufacturing facility by Indian Railways.*****
