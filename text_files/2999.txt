                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2829






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Frequency of Trains in Kashmir




Farooq Abdullah














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that Indian Railways has made a huge difference in making distances irrelevant mainly in Kashmir valley and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether it is also true that lack of bogies create a lot of chaos to commuters who travel from South Kashmir to Central Kashmir on way to North Kashmir; and (c) if so, whether the Government would increase the frequency of trains, increase the number of bogies and also spread the train stations in other areas of the valley and if so, the details of steps taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c)  To cater to the needs of passengers, Indian Railways is operating  15 pairs of DEMU train services on the Banihal-Anantnag-Badgam-Baramula section, which are being operated with 8 coaches composition.  Augmentation & increase in frequency of these trains is operationally not feasible, at present.  *****
