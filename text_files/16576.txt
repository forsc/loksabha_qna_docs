                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

366






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Illegal Activities at Indo Nepal Border




Pankaj Chowdhary














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of incidents related to destruction of pillars, smuggling, infiltration etc. at the Indo-Nepal Border;(b) if so, the reaction of the Government thereto;(c) whether the Government is formulating any firm plan to stop smuggling, infiltration etc. to make Indian Border safe and secure; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (d):  Sashastra Seema Bal (SSB) is deployed along Indo Nepal border as Border Guarding Force. Damage of border pillars and smuggling activities along Indo-Nepal border are reported from time to time. Prompt action is taken to repair the damaged border pillars. SSB carries out various operational activities like patrolling, nakabandi, checking of persons  crossing  the border  both randomly and  on the-2-LSUSQ No.366 for 15.09.2020 basis of inputs received from own sources/ sister agencies to check illegal activities and prevent cross border infiltration. Regular coordination is maintained by SSB with civil administration and security agencies engaged in this regard.******
