                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3243






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






ICAR Courses




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) is planning to launch academic courses on the lines of Academy of Scientific & Innovative Research (ACSIR) of Council of Scientific & Industrial Research (CSIR); and(b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	The Academy of Scientific & Innovative Research (ACSIR) offers academic courses in several research institutes of CSIR by following a “Hub-and-Spoke Model”.  Since the National Education Policy-2020 recommends to eliminate the system of Affiliation and Deemed University, the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) has constituted a national level committee in September 2020 to develop a roadmap to move forward with the recommendations of National Education Policy-2020 (NEP-2020).  Through this ICAR has initiated the implementation of NEP-2020.*******
