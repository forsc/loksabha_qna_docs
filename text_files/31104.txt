                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2939






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Rubber Farmers




Thomas Chazhikadan














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that rubber farmers in Kerala are not getting adequate price for their product vis-a-vis expenditure; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the Government has any plans to provide subsidy to rubber farmers so that the farmers can get the price of rupees 250 per kg; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY(SMT. ANUPRIYA PATEL)(a)	and (b) :  The rubber farmers in Kerala are getting adequate price for their product  vis-a-vis  expenditure. Domestic natural rubber prices have increased in the country in the recent past and the monthly average price for RSS 4 grade natural rubber in Kerala during November 2021 was Rs. 182.79 per kg. against the tentative cost of production i.e. Rs. 99.46 per kg, including all paid out costs, interest on working capital and depreciation of fixed assets. (c)	and (d):    No such proposal is under consideration of the  Government.*****
