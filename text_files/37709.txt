                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4659






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Earning from Petroleum Products




Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta













                                                    Kavitha Malothu
                                                








                                                    Dayakar Pasunoori
                                                








                                                    Gaddam Ranjith Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that the Government has earned Rs. 4.55 lakh crores on petrol, diesel and other petroleum products during 2020-21 compared to Rs. 3.34 lakh crores during 2019-20; (b) if so, the detailed break-up of the earnings from petrol, diesel and other products; (c) the details of the estimated contribution and earnings of petroleum sector to Central exchequer during the year 2021-22; and(d) the earnings of the Government from States through collection of taxes during the last five years and the current year, year and State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (d):	 The details of contribution to central and state exchequer from petroleum sector during last five years are as under:Rs./CroresYear	Total Contribution to Central Exchequer	Total Contribution to State Exchequer	Total Contribution of Petroleum Sector to Exchequer2016-17	3,35,175	1,89,770	5,24,9452017-18	3,36,163	2,06,863	5,43,0262018-19	3,48,041	2,27,591	5,75,6322019-20	3,34,315	2,21,056	5,55,3712020-21	4,55,069	2,17,650	6,72,7192021-22 (April to December,)	3,54,264	2,07,658	5,61,922Based on data provided to Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC) by 16 major oil & gas companiesx-x-x-x-x
