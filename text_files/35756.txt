                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3070






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Loans to Women Self Help Groups




A. Ganeshamurthi














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the steps being taken to provide loans to women under Women Self Help Groups (SHGs) in the States to provide impetus to women entrepreneurs and help them gain self-confidence ;(b) 	the details of funds sanctioned/utilized, Statewise during the last five years and the current year; and(c) 	the details of loans waived for these SHGs during the said period, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)	Since 2013-14 so far, the SHGs have been assisted to access bank credit of more than Rs. 4.71 lakh crore under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM). Interest subvention is also being provided to all such bank loans since 2013-14, thus reducing cost of capital. DAY-NRLM provides training/orientation to the bankers in the States/UTs on SHG Bank Linkages as per the requirements received from the States/UTs.  The guidelines for providing credit to women SHGs are issued by the Reserve Bank of India every year.In addition, the Ministry is implementing the Start-up Village Entrepreneurship Programme (SVEP), as a sub-scheme under DAY-NRLM. The block is the unit of implementation of SVEP. The objective of the scheme is to help the rural poor (SHGs) to set-up enterprises at the village level in non-agricultural sectors. Besides providing start-up capital, a cadre of Community Resource Persons-Enterprise Promotion (CRP-EP) is set up to provide business support services to enterprises. SHG members are supported under the project.(b) 	The total amount of funds allocated and released to SHGs State-wise and the total loan disbursed by Banks to SHGs along with the target of loan to be disbursed State/UT-wise during the last five years and current year is attached at ANNEXURE I and ANNEXURE II respectively.(c) 	In Tamil Nadu, loan to the tune of Rs 2674.64 crore (the Principal and interest amount) outstanding in Co-operative Banks as on 31.03.2021 was waived off.*****
