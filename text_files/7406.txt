                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1084






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Family Planning Programme




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the detailed break-up of allocations and expenditures for family planning activities under Reproductive and Child Health (RCH) flexi pool from 2015 to 2019, State/UT-wise;(b) the detailed break-up of allocations and expenditures for each activity under family planning such as permanent methods, spacing methods, training, IEC/BCC, procurement of equipment, drugs and consumables, ASHA incentives and other activities under the RCH-flexi pool of NHM, State-wise from 2015-2016 to 2018-2019;(c) the proportion of the family welfare central sector scheme in the Union health budget spent for procuring, promoting and distributing spacing methods;(d) whether a programme/scheme exists to supply appropriate contraceptives to unmarried girls and boys under the family planning programme (including Mission Parivar Vikas districts), if so, the details thereof and the number of unmarried adolescents covered; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a): Under the flagship programme of National Health Mission (NHM) the allocations are made in flexible pools where as approvals are given activity wise to provide greater flexibility to States to utilize the funds as per their felt need. The detailed break up of approvals and expenditures for family planning activities under NRHM-Reproductive and Child Health (RCH) flexi pool from 2015-16 to 2018-19, State /UT-wise are at Annexure-1.   (b): the detailed break-up of approvals and expenditures for each activity under Family Planning such as permanent methods, spacing methods, trainings, IEC/BCC, procurement of equipment, drugs and consumables, ASHA incentives and other activities under the RCH flexi pool of NHM, State-wise from 2015-16 to 2018-19  are at Annexure-2 and 3. (c):	 In addition, under Central Sector budget of Department of Health & Family Welfare there is a budgetary provision of Rs.140 crore towards Free Distribution of Contraceptives and Rs.100 crore towards Social Marketing of Contraceptives for the F.Y. 2019-20. (d) & (e):	 All spacing methods viz. Condoms, Combined oral contraceptive pills, Centchroman (Chhaya), Intra Uterine Contraceptive Device (IUCD) and Injectable contraceptive under Antara program are offered to all beneficiaries irrespective of marital status in all districts including Mission Parivar Vikas districts;As per NFHS 4, current use of contraceptives in sexually active unmarried adolescents in India is 16.4 percent.
