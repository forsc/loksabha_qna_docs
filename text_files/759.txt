                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

660






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






New Railway Museum




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to build new railway museums in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof, Statewise;(c) whether any proposal is pending with the Government for the development or initiating any kind of activities at the Ernakulam Terminus Railway Station which is in an abandoned State now; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the action taken/proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b) The current policy of the Government is to develop Digital Museums at important Railway stations of the country subject to justification/feasibility. The work has been entrusted to M/s Railtel Corporation of India Ltd. (RCIL), a Public Sector Undertaking under the Ministry of Railways. Further, museums, as per the requirement, may also be developed at various places including Railway stations.(c) & (d) A proposal has been received from the Kerala Rail Development Corporation Ltd. (KRDCL), a joint venture between Railway and Government of Kerala for developing the erstwhile Ernakulam Goods Station under Public Private Partnership mode. *******
