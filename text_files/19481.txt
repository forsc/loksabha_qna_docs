                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

748






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Mega Integrated Textile Region and Apparel Park in Andhra Pradesh




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Pocha Brahmananda Reddy
                                                








                                                    Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu
                                                








                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering to sanction Mega Integrated Textile Region and Apparel Park (MITRA) in Andhra Pradesh during the current financial year; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the details of the funds sanctioned for various Integrated Textile Parks in Andhra Pradesh during the previous two years; and(d) the present status of the Integrated Textile Park at Guntur, Andhra Pradesh?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) & (b): The proposed scheme of Mega Integrated Textile Region and Apparel  (MITRA) Parks has been  under consideration. Recent Budget 2021-22 announcements by Hon’ble Finance Minister mentions about this scheme. (c):	A sum of Rs. 12 crore has been released to two parks during the previous two years, details of which are as follows: (In Rs. Cr)Name of the Park	Total GoI share	Fund released during last two year (2018-19 and 2019-20)Tarkeshwar Textile Park	40.00	6Guntur Textile Park	40	6(d):	The project of Guntur Textile Park Ltd, Andhra Pradesh was approved on 11.9.2014 with a project cost of Rs. 105.12 crore, out of which GoI share is Rs. 40.00 crore. Out of total GoI share, Rs. 10 crore has been released so far. The Park is under progress and an extension has been granted upto June, 2021. ****
