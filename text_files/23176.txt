                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Allocation of Central Taxes for Schemes




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government allocates the share of central taxes for Union Budget Schemes to all the State Government;(b)	if so, the details of allocated central taxes to the State Governments during the last three years, State-wise;(c)	the amount of funds allocated to Rajasthan from the said funds during this financial year, head-wise; and (d)     the details of allocated funds, Scheme-wise ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) Yes.
