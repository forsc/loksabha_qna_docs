                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2405






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Functioning of FASTag System




Devji Mansingram Patel













                                                    Pratima Bhoumik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether  the  Government  has  any  data  with regard to the  number  of FASTag issued in  the last one year  and  if  so,  the  details  thereof,  State/UT-wise;(b) whether the Government has received complaints against the toll operators due to non-working of FASTag scanner/system  and  if  so,  the  details  thereof  and  the action  taken  by  the  Government  in  this  regard;(c) whether the Government is aware of an increase in  the  number  of complaints  against  the  toll  operators’ behaviour  with  the  passengers;  and(d) if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the  Government  against  the  toll  operators?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) The State/UT-wise details of the FASTag issued on the basis of Vehicle registration number for the period of 1st March 2019 to 27th Feb 2020 are at Annexure. (b) to (d) FASTag related issues and complaints at fee plazas of National Highways (NH)  are addressed through a central NH Helpline Number 1033. Most of the complaints are resolved instantly by the customer executive. Other complaints are forwarded to concerned regional office of National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) and banks for necessary resolution. Apart from 1033, 24 participating banks of FASTag programme also have their own dedicated customer care helpline to cater to complaints of respective customer base. Complaints against toll operators have been received in some of the fee plazas. The issues are forwarded to concerned Project Implementation Unit (PIU) for proper investigation and necessary action. In case, there is any fault on part of toll operating agencies, action is taken as per the provisions of Contract Agreement.
