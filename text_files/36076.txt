                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3300






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Problems in Implementing PMGKAY




Rajendra Agrawal













                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of assistance provided so far under the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana; (b) 	the problems faced by the Government in the implementation of the scheme so far; (c) 	whether the Government proposes to further extend the scheme; (d) 	if so, the steps taken by the Government to reach individuals not covered under the scheme; (e) 	whether the scheme has contributed to reduction in malnutrition; and (f) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):  	Under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojna (PMGKAY), Govt of India has made allocation of foodgrains @ 5 kg per person per month free of cost for around 80 crore beneficiaries of all the 36 States/UTs covered under National Food Security Act (NFSA) (Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) & Priority Households(PHH)) including those covered under Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) for a total period of 19 months during the years 2020-21 and 2021-22 (Phase-I to V). .....2/--2-A total of approx 759 LMT foodgrains have been allocated to all the States/UTs during the above period with financial implication of about Rs. 2.60 lakh Crores.(b): 	Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS) is operated under the joint responsibility of the Central and the State/UT Governments. The operational responsibility under TPDS (and PMGKAY also, as targeted beneficiaries are same) including identification of beneficiaries and distributing ration to them, lies with respective State/UT.  No problems have been reported by States/UTs in the implementation of the scheme so far. So far nearly 94% successful distribution of foodgrains under PMGKAY (Phase-I-IV) (April 2020- November, 2021) has been reported.(c): 	PMGKAY is already operating under the extended period since December, 2021 to March, 2022. (d):  All NFSA beneficiaries have been allocated free of cost foodgrains under PMGKAY.(e) & (f): 	As per concurrent evaluation by monitoring institutions, there is nearly 98% satisfaction among beneficiaries in PMGKAY.*****
