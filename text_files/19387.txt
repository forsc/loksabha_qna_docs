                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

532






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Training and high performance centres




Sanjay Bhatia














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to set up training and high performance Centres of international / Standard to promote Boxing in Bhiwani and Kaithal, Hockey in Shahabad, Volleyball in Kurukshetra and Wrestling in Sonipat areas with a view of outstanding performance of athletes particularly of Haryana in specific Sports disciplines to make India a power in sports; (b) if so, whether the Government also proposes to set up any high level training centres/sports academies in Panipat line to promote more other local talents like athlete Neeraj Chopra and Gagan Narang of Panipat district; (c) if so, whether the Government proposes to set up any National Sports University in Haryana in view of the achievement made by sportspersons of Haryana State during the last so many decades; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) & (b)	 Sports is a State Subject and the primary responsibility of development of sports, including setting up training and high performance Centres of International Standard in the country to promote specific sports disciplines, like boxing, rests with the State/ Union Territory (UT) Governments and the National Sports Federations (NSFs), which are responsible for governance of individual sports disciplines. The Central Government, through the sports development schemes implemented by it and organizations functioning under its aegis, complements and supplements the efforts of the State Governments and NSFs in this regard.Sports Authority of India (SAI), an autonomous body under the aegis of the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports is implementing the following Sports Promotional Schemes across the country to identify talented sportspersons in the age group of 8-25 years and nurture them to excel at National and International competitions:-•	National Centres of Excellence (NCOE)•	SAI Training Centre (STC)•	Extension Centre of STC•	National Sports Talent Contest (NSTC)The above schemes are implemented to promote various sports including Boxing, Hockey, Volleyball & Wrestling in various SAI Centres including in the State of Haryana. Details of the above schemes operational in Haryana are given in Annexure I.In addition, under the Khelo India – National Programme for Development of Sports, various initiatives have been undertaken to setup training facilities across India including the State of Haryana. Academies have been accredited for support under the vertical of Support for National/Regional/State Academies in Haryana.  List of such Academies in the State of Haryana is given at Annexure-II.Apart from this, the following Khelo India Centres (KICs) are operational in the State of Haryana:i.	Kharkhoda for Boxing, Judo, Kabaddi, Wrestling & Wushu.ii.	MorMajra, Karnal for Kabaddi exclusively for girls.iii.	Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra for Hockey exclusively for girls iv.	Convent Gurukul, Kurukshetra for Hockey only.v.	Gurunanak Khalsa College, Kurukshetra for Weightlifting only.(c) & (d)	At present, there is no proposal in the Ministry to set up any National Sports University in Haryana.*****
