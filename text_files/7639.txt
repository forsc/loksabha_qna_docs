                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1326






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Vacancies in Delhi University




Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of permanent posts lying vacant in Delhi University as on 31st October 2019including all the Delhi University affiliated colleges from 2015 onwards, year-wise;(b) the total number of guest lecturers employed as on 31st October 2019;(c) the total number of Ad-hoc Assistant Professors including leave vacancy as on 31st October2019;(d) the measures have been taken by the Government to fill up the permanent positions and thereasons for delay; and(e) whether the Government proposes to have a permanent mechanism to appoint faculty on aregular basis like other Government departments by drawing a panel annually based on selectioncriteria in order to avoid delay in appointments?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ''Nishank'')(a) University of Delhi has informed that as on 31.10.2019 the vacancy positions of faculties inthe case of University are as follows: Professor- 167, Associate Professor- 431, Assistant Professor-267. In all, there are 865 vacancies. In case of affiliated colleges with University of Delhi, thepermanent faculties post lying vacant as on 31.10.2019 are 5043(Approx.) including 2nd trancheapproved by the UGC.(b)&(c) The total number of guest lecturers employed as on 31st October, 2019 is 89 for theUniversity and 1075(Approx.) for colleges affiliated with the University. The total number ofAd-hoc Assistant Professors including leave vacancy as on 31st October, 2019 is 69 for theUniversity and 4175 for Colleges affiliated with the University.(d) &(e) The University has already started the filling of the vacancies and recently advertised166 posts of Professor, 428 posts of Associate Professor and 263 posts of Assistant Professor. Asregards Colleges, the process for filling up permanent positions is being supervised by theUniversity pursuant to which the advertisement for teaching positions for 26 colleges have alreadybeen published. The appointment in Colleges is made by the Governing Bodies of the Colleges interms of the laid down procedure.The vacancies arise due to retirements, resignations, deputations, deaths, expansion andopening of new institutions. The filling up the vacancies is an ongoing and continuous process. Theonus of filling up of the posts lies on the University, being an autonomous body created under anAct of Parliament. As per UGC regulations, all the sanctioned / approved posts in the universitysystem shall be filled up on an urgent basis. University Grants Commission as well as the Ministryis continuously monitoring the process.*****
