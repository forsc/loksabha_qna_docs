                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1529






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Expenditure on CSR by PSEs




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the expenditure incurred under Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) of Public Sector Enterprises (PSEs) in the country during the last five financial years, year-wise;(b)	the total fund spent under CSR in Odisha during the same period, year-wise;(c)	the details of the total fund spent under CSR in Angul and Talcher districts in the State during the same period, year-wise; (d)	whether any major project has been taken up by any of the PSEs in both the districts under CSR activity and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a)	: As per information available, the details of CSR Expenditure incurred by CPSEs in the country for undertaking CSR activities/ projects during the last five financial years are given in the table:Year	No. of CPSEs	CSR Expenditure (? crore)2014-15	120	2450.312015-16	106	4028.042016-17	126	3336.502017-18	152	3442.382018-19	150	3873.32(b) & (c):  As per information furnished by Ministry of Corporate Affairs on the basis of filings made by the companies in MCA 21 registry, the CSR amount spent by all companies, including CPSEs in the State of Odisha and Angul district during the last five years is given below: Year	CSR Expenditure in State of Odisha(? crore)	CSR Expenditure in Angul District of Odisha(? crore)2014-15	252.18	25.842015-16	624.04	0.842016-17	316.72	3.412017-18	469.47	3.602018-19	682.60	0.85(d): The details of specific activities/projects undertaken by CPSEs in not maintained by the Department of Public Enterprises.*******
