                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2449






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Implementation of NFSM




Pritam Gopinath Munde














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Union Government has implemented National Food Security Mission (NFSM) in the identified districts of twenty-eight States and two Union Territories (UTs) of Ladakh and Jammu and Kashmir to increase the production and productivity of rice, wheat, pulses, coarse cereals and nutri cereals (millet) through area through area expansion and productivity enchancement;(b)	if so, the extent to which the productivity has increased since its implementation; (c)	whether the Government has trained farmers in different fields under the scheme; (d)	if so, the number of farmers in different fields who have been trained so far; and (e)	the target fixed for the next two years in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE?????????????????????????(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	Yes Sir.(b)	Since the inception of the National Food Security Mission (NFSM) in 2007-08, there has been increase in productivity to an extent of 23.61% in rice, 22.77% in wheat, 31.68% in pulses, 28.13% in coarse cum nutri- cereals. The overall increase in productivity of foodgrains during the period 2007-08 to 2019-20 is 25.97%.(c)&(d)  As per information available from the states, more than 43 lakh farmers have been trained through 1.43 lakh trainings under NFSM, so far.(e)	The target for training of farmers is fixed every year after receiving annual action plans from the States/UTs.*****
