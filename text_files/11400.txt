                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

25






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana




Jaskaur Meena













                                                    Nihal Chand Chauhan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the main features of the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY);(b) 	the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilised under the scheme across the country during the last three years and the current year, State-wise, including Rajasthan;(c) 	the details of the rural road projects sanctioned in Rajasthan during the said period and the present status thereof;(d) 	whether the Government has given approval for implementation of phase-III of PMGSY in the country;(e) 	if so, the details thereof including the number of districts proposed to be included under the same along with the details of funds to be sanctioned in this regard especially for the districts of Dausa and Ganganagar of Rajasthan; and(f) 	the details of the targets fixed under the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (f):   A Statement is laid on the table of the House. Statement referred to in reply to Parts (a) to (f) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No.25 due for answer on 04.02.2020(a): The Government launched Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in December 2000, as a one-time special intervention to provide rural connectivity, by way of a single all-weather road, to the eligible unconnected habitations of designated population size (500+ in plain areas and 250+ in North-Eastern States, Himalayan States, Deserts and Tribal Areas as per 2001 census) in the core network for uplifting the socio-economic condition of the rural population. In the critical Left Wing Extremism affected blocks (as identified by Ministry of Home Affairs), additional relaxation has been given to connect habitations with population 100 persons and above (2001 census). The Government subsequently launched new interventions/verticals namely PMGSY-II for upgradation of 50,000 Km of road length and Road Connectivity Project for Left Wing Extremism Affected Areas (RCPLWEA) for construction/upgradation of strategically important roads. (b): The details of funds sanctioned/allocated and utilised under the scheme across the country during the last three years and the current year, State-wise, including Rajasthan is placed at Annexure-I. (c): The State of Rajasthan has been sanctioned its full entitlement under PMGSY-I and PMGSY-II. The details of rural road projects sanctioned in Rajasthan during the last three years and the current year and the present status thereof are as indicated below:-Financial Year	       Proposals Sanctioned	     Works Completed 	                No.	  Length (Km)	       No.	Length (Km)2016-17	               1449	   4251.34	      1439	4213.642017-18	                401	   3464.263	       384	3336.3132018-19	                 -	       -	        -	-2019-20	                 -	       -	        -	- (d) to (f): The Government has on 10th July, 2019 approved phase-III of PMGSY with an aim to consolidate 1,25,000 km Through Routes and Major Rural Links connecting habitations, inter-alia to Gramin Agricultural Markets (GrAMs), Higher Secondary School and Hospitals at an estimated cost of Rs 80,250 crore.  The road length allocated to the States under PMGSY-III is placed at Annexure-II. The State of Rajasthan has been allocated 8,662.5 km under PMGSY-III. As reported by the State Government, all 33 districts of the State, including Dausa and Ganganagar are proposed to be included under PMGSY-III scheme. The State has recently submitted first batch of proposals of road length 2,839.13 km for consideration by the Ministry of Rural Development. **********
