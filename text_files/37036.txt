                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4155






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Onsite Treatment of Stray Animals




Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy













                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the steps being taken for the onsite treatment of strays in distress by the 	Government;(b) 	whether the Government has launched any mobile animal ambulances with facilities 	on wheels for strays and is fully equipped and customised for their treatments and in 	several instances, injured animals on the streets can be treated onsite and if so, the 	details thereof;(c) 	whether timely treatment of animals to prevent instances of being displaced from the 	areas where they are familiar with having the animal care ambulances by having a 	treatment table provisions to administer intravenous fluids to pets, oxygen cylinders, 	removable cages for holding dogs and dog-catching equipment and will have a doctor 	as well; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the time by which such steps  will be 	taken in 	future along with the funds sanctioned/spent during the last five years and 	the 	current year, State-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY & DAIRYING(PARSHOTTAM  RUPALA)(a) to (c) Animal Husbandry is a State subject. Government, under Livestock Health & Disease Control (LH&DC) scheme, supplements the efforts of States / UTs towards animal health by providing them financial assistance. In order to bring veterinary services at farmers’ doorsteps, Central Government, from 2021-22, is providing 100% central funds towards procurement & customization of mobile veterinary units (MVUs). However, recurring operational expenditure would be shared with States in the ratio of 90:10 for North Eastern & Himalayan States; 60% for other States, and 100% for UTs.These MVUs are customized with required basic facilities for onsite emergency treatment, minor surgeries, and sample collection for diagnostic purposes in field conditions for animals. Each MVU has one Veterinarian, one Para-veterinarian and one Driver-cum attendant.(d)	The Government has released a sum of Rs.681.57 Crore to 33 States/UTs towards procurement & customization of 4332 no. of Mobile Veterinary Units (MVUs) during 2021-22. The detail of funds released and no. of MVUs for all States/UTs is annexed as Annexure-I.
