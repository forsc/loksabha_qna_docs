                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

771






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Non-Lapsable Central Pool of Resources Scheme




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    †771. 	SHRI ASHOK MAHADEORAO NETE:Will the Minister of DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION be pleased to state:(a) 	whether the work is being carried out under the Non Lapsable Central Pool of Resources (NLCPR) scheme in the North-Eastern States of the country;(b) 	if so, the details thereof as on date, State-wise; and(c) 	the funds allocated and utilised during each ofthe last three years, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region[Shri G. Kishan Reddy] (a) to (c) Projects were sanctioned State-wise under the Non-Lapsable Central Pool of Resources-State (NLCPR-State) Scheme till it was replaced by the North East Special Infrastructure Development Scheme (NESIDS) in 2017-18. In total, 1635 projects worth Rs.16233.78 crore were sanctioned for the North Eastern States, under NLCPR-State scheme, out of which 1219 projects with sanctioned amount of Rs.9726.59 crore have been completed by 31.1.2022, while for the remaining 416 ongoing projects, the implementation is at various stages.The State-wise details of projects sanctioned under NLCPR-State scheme are as under:(Amount in Rs. crore)S.No.	State	Sanctioned projects	Completed projects	Ongoing projects		Number of projects	Amount	Number of projects	Amount	Number of projects1	Arunachal Pradesh	207	2484.01	141	1544.75	662	Assam	446	3875.97	260	1616.49	1863	Manipur	177	1857.13	148	1078.44	294	Meghalaya	107	1533.96	69	709.34	385	Mizoram	159	1393.1	141	1126.49	186	Nagaland	170	1805.4	137	1322.1	337	Sikkim	247	1409.69	226	1123.50	218	Tripura	122	1874.52	97	1205.48	25 	Total	1635	16233.78	12199726.59416Further, the expenditure under the NLCPR-State scheme is now being made for ongoing projects only to meet committed liabilities as per reported actual progress, without specific budgetary allocation of funds State-wise.
