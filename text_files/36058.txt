                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3316






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Optical Fibre Cable Network




Geetha Viswanath Vanga













                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the completion/pending details of Optical Fibre Cable (OFC) network in the country since 2014 onwards till date; and (b) 	the funds sanctioned/spent therefor till now, State-wise including Andhra Pradesh and Telangana?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)	BharatNet project is implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity to all the Gram Panchayats (GPs) in the country through an optimal mix of media (OFC/Radio/Satellite). Since 2014, out of about 7,40,706 km planned OFC, 5,67,941 km OFC  has been laid, under the project as on 28.02.2022.(b)	The State-wise total funds allocated/sanctioned/disbursed under BharatNet Project, including Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, are given in Annexure-I.
