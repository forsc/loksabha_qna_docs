                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5387






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Metro Rail Project in Coimbatore City




K. Shanmuga Sundaram














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to implement metro rail project in Coimbatore city and and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government has conducted any feasibility study to implement the said project and if so, the details thereof; and(c)	whether the Government has invited any foreign agencies to participate in construction of said metro project and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                   (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)& (b): Urban Transport, including metro rail, is an integral part of urban development, which is a state subject. Therefore, proposals for metro rail projects are initiated by the concerned State Governments, who also conduct feasibility study, prepare comprehensive mobility plan, alternative analysis report, detailed project report etc. No proposal to implement metro rail in Coimbatore city has been submitted by Government of Tamil Nadu. (c): 	Does not arise, in view of (a) & (b) above.                   *****
