                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4070






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Micro ITIs in Chhattisgarh and Rajasthan




Devji Mansingram Patel













                                                    Arun Sao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is making any efforts to set up Micro Industrial Training Institutes and promote technical education in various parts of the country particularly in the tribal dominated areas of the country including Chhattisgarh and Rajasthan;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the State-wise details of ITIs established in the country as on date; and(c)	the expenditure incurred on setting up of ITIs in the country during the last three years and the current year; and(d) 	the details of funding made under various heads for setting up of ITIs in the country including Chhattisgarh and Pindwada district of Rajasthan during the said period, State-wise and fund-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) 	There is no scheme of micro Industrial Training Institutes under Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE). However, there is an extensive network of more than 14000 Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) across India. The government is also relaxing guidelines to setup of Multi Skill Training Institutes (MSTIs) in unserved blocks with minimum one trade and with no restriction on Minimum land requirement.(b)	The detail of ITIs functioning across the country including Chhattisgarh and Rajasthan is given in Annexure – I.(c) & (d) Setting up of Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) and its operation are in the domain of respective State Governments. However, Central Government is providing financial assistance for establishment of ITIs in tribal dominated areas under two schemes namely “Skill Development in 47 Districts affected by LWE” and “Enhancing Skill Development Infrastructure in NE States”.The details of coverage and fund released under the above two schemes are given at Annexure-II. Pindwada district of Rajasthan is not covered under these two schemes.
