                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4401






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Anganwadi Kendras in Bihar




Giridhari Yadav













                                                    Rama Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the number of Anganwadi Kendras working in Bihar at present and the number of Kendras closed down among them, district-wise;(b)	the steps being taken by the Government to reopen the closed Anganwadi Kendras in Bihar;(c)	whether the Anganwadi workers are getting paid on time in Bihar and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor along with the honorarium fixed by the Government at present;(d)	whether Anganwadi centres in Bihar are functioning from rented buildings and if so, the details thereof along with the funds allocated and expenditure incurred thereunder, district-wise;(e)	the details of provisions prescribed for renting buildings to run Anganwadi centres; and(f)	the corrective measures taken by the Government in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILDDEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b)   As on 16.03.2020, against 1,14,718 sanctioned Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) in Bihar 1,08,260 Anganwadi Centres are operational. As informed by the Government of Bihar, no Anganwadi Kendra has been closed down in the State. District-wise number of sanctioned and operational AWCs in State of Bihar is at Annexure-I.(c)	As informed by the State Government of Bihar, honorarium to Anganwadi Workers is being paid on time through Direct Benefit Transfer. Per month honorarium paid to AWWs in the State of Bihar is as under:                                                                                             (Amount in Rupees)Functionary	Honorarium	Additional Honorarium by State 	TotalAWW	4500	1150	5650AWW at Mini-AWC	3500	900	4400 (d) to (f)     As on 16.03.2020, 61,502 Anganwadi Centres are functioning from rented premise in the State of Bihar. District-wise details of AWCs functioning from rented premises and funds allocated for rent to these buildings is at Annexure-II.
