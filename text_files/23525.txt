                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4527






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Hostels for SC and OBC Students




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. †4527 TO BE ANSWERED ON 23.03.2021HOSTELS FOR SC AND OBC STUDENTS†4527. SHRI ASHOK MAHADEORAO NETE: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has taken/proposes to take any steps to increase the capacity of hostels for the students belonging to Scheduled Castes (SCs), Other Backward Classes (OBCs) and poor children in the backward and tribal areas of the country including the Parliamentary Constituency of Gadchiroli-Chimur in Maharashtra; and(b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b):  The Department of Social Justice & Empowerment implements throughout the country,  Centrally Sponsored Schemes for construction/expansion of hostels for Scheduled Caste (SC) students, namely ''Babu Jagjivan Ram Chhatrawas Yojana (BJRCY)'', and Other Backward Class (OBC) students, namely the ''Scheme for Construction of Hostels for OBC Boys and Girls'' especially those hailing from rural and remote areas and from poor families, to facilitate the continuation of their education. The Schemes are implemented through the State Govts./UTs and Central Institutions.The schemes are demand driven, and on receipt of complete proposals from the implementing agencies, they are provided eligible central assistance as per provisions of the scheme.This Department has not received any  complete proposal for construction/expansion of  hostels for SC/OBC students in the Parliamentary Constituency of Gadchiroli-Chimur in Maharashtra. *****
