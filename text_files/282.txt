                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

225






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Increasing Seats in Kendriya Vidyalayas




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has increased seats of Kendriya Vidyalayas in thecountry including Delhi during the last five years and if so, the details thereof;(b) the average student-teacher ratio in Kendriya Vidyalayas in Delhi as of June2019;(c) the steps taken by the Government to improve infrastructure and technology inKendriya Vidyalayas in the country including Delhi during said period?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) has informed that the enrolment ofstudents in Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) in the country including Delhi is graduallyincreased during the last five years, as per the details given below : -<pre>Session Enrolment2014-15 11,76,6822015-16 12,05,7602016-17 12,27,9512017-18 12,54,9222018-19 12,75,795</pre>(b) The average student-teacher ratio in the KVs in Delhi, as of June 2019, is 34:1.(c) Budgetary support is provided every years as Grant-in-Aid – Creation of CapitalAssets for meeting the requirement of KVS for development of infrastructure,computerization etc. The budgetary support provided to KVS during the last five yearsas capital grant is as under:-<pre>(Rs. in Crore)Financial Years Budgetary support2014-15 322.872015-16 380.002016-17 476.732017-18 674.242018-19 231.35</pre>*****
