                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

10






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Beedi Workers’ Hospital




S. Gnanathiraviam














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the current status of the Beedi Workers’ Hospital in Mukkudal Town, Tirunelveli; (b)the amount of funds sanctioned for the renovation and upgradation of the said hospital; (c)whether the Government has any plans to transfer the administration of the said hospital to the Employees’ State Insurance Corporation; and (d)if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (d):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.*                                            ******** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 10 FOR 18.11.2019 RAISED BY SHRI GNANATHIRAVIAM S. REGARDING BEEDI WORKERS’ HOSPITAL.(a)	 Currently, the 30 Bedded Central Hospital for Beedi Workers is functioning at Mukkudal, Tirunelveli district, Tamil Nadu to attend to / treat out-patients only through static and mobile clinics between 09:30 A.M. and 04:30 P.M. from Monday to Friday and between 09:30 A.M. and 01:30 P.M. on all Saturdays.(b)	Ministry of Labour & Employment (MoLE) sanctions funds towards the Labour Welfare Health Scheme to the Regional Labour Welfare Organisations. No specific fund for renovation and upgradation of Central Hospital, Mukkudal, Tirunelveli has been sanctioned by the O/o DGLW, Ministry of Labour Employment. (c)	& (d) Yes, the matter related to merging of hospitals run by Office of Directorate General of Labour Welfare (DGLW), Ministry of Labour & Employment with the Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) is under process.******
