                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3429






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






e-Invoice System




Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is contemplating to introduce e-invoice system to plug the tax evasion by the traders and industries;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details of the norms and conditions of the e-invoice system?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a), (b) & (c)	The e-Invoice system would provide a platform to the GST registered taxpayers to report B2B Invoice(s) online. This would enable automation of tax processes and facilitate tax compliance. The e-Invoice system would also help tax authorities in improving the monitoring of tax compliance.The taxpayers would be provided the facility of uploading the Invoice(s) through various modes namely, online, offline tool, and ERP/Accounting software, as is being done for e-way bill.GST Council, in its 35th meeting decided that Phase 1 of the roll out of this system would be voluntary, and is expected to be rolled out from January 2020.*****
