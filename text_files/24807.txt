                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

521






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Maulana Azad National Fellowship




Talari Rangaiah













                                                    T.R.V.S. Ramesh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of fellowships sanctioned during the last two years for the Maulana Azad National Fellowship (MANF), State-wise; and(b) the total funds sanctioned during the last two years for the State of Andhra Pradesh and Tamil Nadu for the purpose? 	
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a):  The Ministry of Minority Affairs implements the Maulana Azad National Fellowship (MANF) Scheme for educational empowerment of students belonging to six notified minority communities i.e. Buddhist, Christian, Jain, Muslim, Sikh, Zoroastrian (Parsi) who pursue regular and full time M. Phil and Ph.D courses. During the last seven years i.e. from 2014-15 to 2020-21, a total of 5,750 fellowships have been sanctioned while during the last two years i.e. 2019-20 to 2020-21, a total of 1,726 fellowships have been sanctioned under the scheme (both data provisional). The details of State/UT-wise fellowships sanctioned during the last two years i.e. 2019-20 and 2020-21 are annexed.(b):  Funds are not sanctioned State/UT-wise under the Scheme. However, the fellowships amounting to Rs. 14,70,28,978/- and Rs. 8,14,90,253/- were disbursed  to the beneficiaries from the States of Andhra Pradesh and Tamil Nadu during  the last two years i.e. 2019-20 and 2020-21.*****
