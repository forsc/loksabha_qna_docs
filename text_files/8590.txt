                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2189






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Education Loans




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the educational loans provided by the nationalized banks during the last five years;(b)	whether it is a fact that the numbers of educational loans have reduced drastically;(c)  	if so, whether the Government is contemplating any plan of action to increase the provision of educational loans; and(d)  	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a)	to (b):  Details of educational loans disbursed by Public Sector Banks during the last five years are given below:During the Financial Year	No. of Accounts	Education Loan Disbursed(Amt in Rs Crore)2014-15	681685	9190.452015-16	684836	12595.392016-17	599729	11175.912017-18	786312	13470.472018-19	696656	15030.13Source: RBIThere has been an increase in amount disbursed under education loan for the past three years registering a growth of 20.53% Y-o-Y in 2017-18 and 11.57% Y-o-Y in 2018-19.(c)to (d):  To facilitate easy processing and disbursal of loans, Government has launched a web-based portal namely, Vidya Lakshmi Portal. Students can view, apply and track the education loan applications online by accessing the portal.*******
