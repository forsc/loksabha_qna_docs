                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2178






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Housekeeping Facility in Trains




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla













                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of trains running with in-house keeping facilities at present; (b)	whether the Government proposes to provide such facility in all long distance trains;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and (d)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d) On Board Housekeeping Service (OBHS) has been provided in nearly 1100 pairs of trains including Rajdhani, Shatabdi and other important long distance Mail/Express trains (except overnight trains) for cleaning of coach toilets, doorways, aisles and passenger compartments during the run of the trains. This includes nearly 575 pairs of trains added since 2015.  Railway will continue to provide this service in such important long distance Mail/Express trains in future also.*****
