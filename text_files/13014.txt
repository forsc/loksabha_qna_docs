                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2065






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Babu Jagjivan Ram Chhatrawas Yojana




Shyam Yadav Singh













                                                    Girish Chandra
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2065TO BE ANSWERED ON 03.03.2020BABU JAGJIVAN RAM CHHATRAWAS YOJANA 2065. SHRI SHYAM SINGH YADAV: SHRI GIRISH CHANDRA: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the number of hostels constructed under Babu Jagjivan Ram Chhatravas Yojana (BJRCY) in Uttar Pradesh; and (b) the number of hostels under construction under this scheme, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a): A total number of 25 SC boys/girls hostels have been sanctioned for construction in the State of Uttar Pradesh from 2007-08 till date under Babu Jagjivan Ram Chhatravas Yojna (BJRCY). Of these, construction work of 21 hostels has been completed as reported by the State Government. (b): As per the scheme guidelines of BJRCY, hostels are to be completed within a period of 2 years from the date of sanction of the project.
