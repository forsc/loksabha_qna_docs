                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

320






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






AICTE Regulations, 2020




A. Ganeshamurthi













                                                    A.K.P. Chinraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether as per the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) Regulations,2020, institutes/colleges are not permitted to conduct PGDM (Post Graduate Diploma inManagement) and MBA courses in the same campus;(b) if so, the details thereof along with reasons;(c) the total number of colleges/institutes which are presently conducting PGDM and MBAcourses in the same campus; and(d) the validity, safety and future of those students who are presently pursuing their PGDMand MBA and passed out students in the same colleges/premises?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (A) TO (D) OF THE LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. *320 FOR 16.03.2020 ASKED BY SHRI A.GANESHAMURTHI, SHRI A.K.P. CHINRAJ REGARDING AICTE REGULATIONS,2020.(a) : No, Sir. The All India Council for Technical Council (AICTE) has decided to permitconduct of Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) and Master in BusinessAdministration (MBA) in the same campus as per para 2.19.1(b) of Approval Process Handbook2020-21.(b): Does not arise.(c): As per data maintained by AICTE, the number of colleges/ institutes conducting PGDMand MBA courses in the same campus was 183 in academic year 2019-20.(d): The Degree/Certificates of existing students presently pursuing PGDM and MBA andthose of already passed out shall be valid for all proposes.*****
