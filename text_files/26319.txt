                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2061






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Vaccination for People Without Access to COWIN Platform




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka













                                                    Mohammad Jawed
                                                








                                                    Mimi Chakraborty
                                                








                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a)	the proportion of individuals vaccinated in the country without having to register in the CoWIN portal out of total vaccinated individuals;(b)	the proportion of persons vaccinated who do not possess any photo ID documents out of total persons vaccinated; and (c)	measures taken by the Government to enable those without access to digital technology platforms like CoWIN and those without photo ID to get vaccinated? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (c): All beneficiaries who have received vaccines in India are registered on CoWIN portal. As on 26th July 2021, a total of 3.83 lakh persons without any photo ID documents have been vaccinated through CoWIN. The Center has issued a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) to facilitate vaccination of eligible beneficiaries who do not possess the prescribed photo ID documents.    Registration and vaccination of people without access to digital technology can be done through:•	Walk-in registration and vaccination of either single individual or groups of individuals at COVID-19 Vaccination Centre (CVC).•	Registration at Common Service Centres•	Registration of up to 4 persons using a single mobile number to facilitate registration of people without mobile phones•	Use of assisted registrations through National Covid-19 Helpline (1075)/State integrated Helplines.     In addition, Persons Without Identity (PWI) can be vaccinated under special sessions, the SOP of which is available on MoHFW website.
