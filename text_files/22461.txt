                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3503






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Identification of Bogus Voters




Pratima Bhoumik













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                     Bhagirath Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Devji Mansingram Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Govenment proposes to implement any concrete plan to identify bogus voters and also check bogus voting in General Elections held in the country; and (b)	if so, the time by which it is likely to be implemented and if not, the reasons therefor? ANSWER






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF LAW AND JUSTICE, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)	 and (b): The Election Commission of India has informed that it has taken several measures to remove the duplicate/multiple/repeated entries from the electoral rolls, some of them are as under: -I.	Running of ‘De-duplication software’ at the polling booth and Assembly Constituency level to identify lists of possible duplicate/repeated entries before revision of electoral rolls.II.	House to house verification and collection of information regarding dead/shifted voters by Booth Level Officer (BLO).III.	 The Commission has launched Voter Helpline mobile App & ‘Voter Portal’ in which a person can check whether he/she/any other person is enrolled at two or more places and report to the Electoral Registration Officer (ERO) concerned to take appropriate action for deletion of duplicates/multiple registration.IV.	Uploading of electoral rolls at Chief Electoral Officer’s website and displaying of the same at ERO’s office and at designated locations for public scrutiny.V.	The Commission has ordered several electoral roll purification drives from time to time in the States/Uts to remove duplicate entries in electoral roll, in which EROs concerned find out probable duplicates and after getting the same verified from the field BLOs take necessary action to remove such duplicates following due procedure of law. Apart from the drive, EROs also remove such duplicates/entries by continuous updation of electoral roll.*******
