                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3123






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Yoga Parks




Devendra (Alias) Bhole Singh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state:(a)	whether the Government proposes to set up yoga parks across the country; (b)	if so, the details thereof State/UT and district wise including Uttar Pradesh along with the time by which the said yoga parks are likely to be set up; and (c)	the purpose behind setting up these yoga parks including the details of activities to take place under the same?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a)	: No. There is no such proposal presently with this Ministry to set up Yoga parks across the country.(b)	& (c): Does not arise in view of the above.  *****
