                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1566






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Disabilty Pension




Kaushalendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government provides uniform pension to the disabled in the country;(b) 	if so, the details thereof including the eligibility criterian;(c) 	whether the persons with less than 50 percent disability do not get the benefit of pension; if so, the details thereof;(d) 	whether the Government proposes to increase the pension of persons with disabilities; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) to (c): Disability pension at the rate of Rs.300/- per month is uniformly being disbursed to persons belonging to Below Poverty Line (BPL) households, having severe and multiple disabilities (disability level of 80%) and aged between 18-79 years under the Indira Gandhi National Disability Pension Scheme (IGNDPS). The amount of pension to IGNDPS beneficiaries increases to Rs.500/- per month on attaining the age of 80 years.   (d) & (e): No, Sir. There is no proposal to increase the amount of pension to persons with disabilities under IGNDPS. ******
