                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

323






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Business Incubators




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of business incubators set up under MSME scheme;(b)  	the details of funds allocated for this scheme from the date of initiation of the scheme;(c)  	the details of businesses set up through this scheme, State-wise; and(d)  	the details of the innovative business ideas that received funding through the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a): 	124 Business Incubators {Livelihood Business Incubator (LBI) -102; Technology Business Incubator (TBI)-22} have been sanctioned under A Scheme for Promoting Innovation, Rural Industry and Entrepreneurship (ASPIRE) under Ministry of MSME. The details of Business Incubators under ASPIRE scheme in Annexure- I. (b): 	A total of Rs. 725 crore of fund is allocated from the initiation of the ASPIRE scheme. Details are given below:<pre>Year	Budget Outlay  (Rs. in Cr.)2014-15	210.002015-16	2016-17	2017-18	470.002018-19	2019-20	2020-21	30.002021-22	15.00TOTAL	725.00</pre>(c) & (d):   A total of 47 registered Startups were incubated by the TBIs under ASPIRE scheme. The details of business set up under ASPIRE scheme in Annexure- II.
