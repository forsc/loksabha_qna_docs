                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1855






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Cooperation in the field of Health and Medicine




DNV Senthilkumar. S.













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    Manickam Tagore B
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) Whether the Government has signed bilateral Memorandum of Understanding (MoUs) with different countries for cooperation covering various areas in the field of health and medicine, including communicable and non-communicable diseases;(b) if so, the details thereof and the details of the Memoranda of Understandings signed by the Government, country-wise during each of the last three years and the current year;(c) the extent to which the aforesaid international cooperation is likely to address the challenges faced by the country in handling communicable and non- communicable diseases especially during COVID-19 and the benefits likely to accrue in this regard; and(d) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to exploit the benefits of the latest research/treatment undertaken worldwide for life-threatening communicable and non-communicable diseases and extend the same to patients?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	to (d): Memorandum of Understanding (MoUs) signed by Ministry of Health & Family Welfare for cooperation in the field of health and medicine during the last three years and in the current year are at Annexure-I.The important areas of cooperation under these MoUs are Public Health, Digital Health, Pandemic Management, Maternal and Child Health, Drug Regulation & Pharmaceutical Cooperation including communicable and non-communicable diseases.These agreements facilitate capacity building, training, exchange of information & experts, health manpower development, technical support in disease management including drugs, diagnostics and vaccines and research in mutually identified areas on the basis of equality, reciprocity and mutual benefit. It provides opportunities for sharing and emulating best practices and learning from each other to address public health challenges.
