                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

553






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Tap Water Connections




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the current status of implementation of the Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) across the country including Karnataka; and (b) the total number of the households that have been provided tap water connections under JJM during the last two years, State-wise including Andhra Pradesh along with the funds allocated and utilized for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)	& (b)  At the time of announcement of Jal Jeevan Mission – Har Ghar Jal on 15th August, 2019, out of 18.93 Crore rural households, 3.23 Crore (17%) were reported to have tap water connections. Since then, 3.33 Crore rural households have been provided tap water connections. Thus, as on date, out of 19.18 Crore rural households across the country, 6.56 Crore (34.20%) have tap water supply.As on date, in Karnataka, out of 89.61 lakh rural households, 26.11 lakh (28.63%) are reported to have tap water connections.State/ UT-wise details of households with tap water connections, including in Andhra Pradesh, is in public domain and available on the JJM dashboard @ https://ejalshakti.gov.in/jjmreport/JJMIndia.aspx.The State/ UT-wise details of Central fund allocated, released and utilized by States/ UTs in 2019-20 and 2020-21, is annexed. The amount released also include fund provided under National Drinking Water Quality Sub-Mission for providing potable water in Arsenic and Fluoride affected habitations, performance incentive grants and funds for Japanese Encephalitis –Acute Encephalitis Syndrome affected districts. ***
