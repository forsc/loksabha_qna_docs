                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3858






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Complaints against IRCON




Keshari Devi Patel














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of projects on which IRCON International Limited has worked during the last five years along with the names and cost of these projects;(b)	whether all the said projects have been completed under stipulated time limit;(c)	if so, the names of the said projects in respect of which estimates have been received along with the details thereof, project-wise;(d)	whether the Government has received complaints regarding the negligence/financial irregularities shown by the IRCON Chairman in these works and if so, the details thereof;(e)	if so, whether any enquiry is going on in this regard and if so, the details thereof;(f)	the number of complaints received by the Government along with the details of the action taken thereon; and(g)	whether the Government is aware that the IRCON Chairman does not reply the letters and phone calls of Hon’ble Members of Parliament and if so, the action taken in this regard and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)to(g): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (g) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 3858 BY SHRIMATI KESHARI DEVI PATEL TO BE ANSWERRED IN LOK SABHA ON 11.12.2019 REGARDING COMPLAINTS AGAINST IRCON(a) to (c):  As per information collected from IRCON International Limited, the company has worked on 64 Projects during the last five Years. Name, cost of project and project-wise details of the estimated cost is appended. Projects are executed in accordance with the contract agreements signed with projects sponsors and extension of time, as and when warranted, is granted as per contractual provisions. 24 out of 64 projects completed during the last five years have been executed within the contractual provisions.(d)to(f): As per the information that could be compiled from the records of the Ministry of Railways, 11 complaints were received during the last five years against Chairman & Managing Director, IRCON (position held by different individuals at different times). In terms of extant instructions of the Government, five of these complaints have been filed, being anonymous /pseudonymous in nature. Other complaints have been dealt with as per laid down procedure.(g) All offices in PSUs under Ministry of Railways have been instructed from time to time to follow laid down procedure for dealing with Hon’ble Members of Parliament. As and when complaints are received, the same are taken up under the extant procedure. *****
