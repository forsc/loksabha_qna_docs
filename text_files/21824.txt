                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3011






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Opening of Monuments After Sunset




Nayab Singh













                                                    (Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has decided to keep ten ancient monuments open even past sunset and if so, the details thereof;(b)	 the monuments in Haryana and Uttar Pradesh which will be kept open even sunset past; and (c)	whether there is any plan to extend the said decision to more monuments besides these ten monuments and if so, the time by which it is likely to be done?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	Thirty centrally protected monuments / sites remain open even past sun set, details of which are at Annexure. (b)	Shekh Chillis Tomb at Thanesar in Haryana is identified to be kept open even past sun set. This will be open to public after the illumination is done. Residency Building at Lucknow and Man Mahal at Varanasi,  in Uttar Pradesh are open for public even past sun set.(c)	No such proposal is under consideration at  present.ANNEXURE ANNEXUR REFERRED TO IN
