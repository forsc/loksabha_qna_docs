                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2220






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Infrastructure for Government Schools




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the proposals of the works such as the construction of new class rooms, building and other works in different Government schools of Rajasthan under Samagra Siksha Abhiyan which are lying pending for financial approval of the Government district-wise including district Nagore;(b) the time by which the Union Government is likely to accord approval to the said proposals; and(c) the details of the progress made in Rajasthan as per the targets set under Samagra Siksha Abhiyan that was launched by Government, District-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) and (b): The Annual Work Plan and Budget (AWP&B) for implementation of Samagra Shiksha in States/UTs is appraised and approved/estimated in the meeting of the Project Approval Board (PAB) at the commencement of the financial year in consultation with the concerned State and UT as per the programmatic and financial norms of the scheme, earlier progress of the State/UT and availability of budgetary resources. The meeting to consider the proposals of the works such as the construction of new class rooms, building etc. in different Government schools submitted by the Government of Rajasthan was held on 06.05.2021 and the feasible proposals have been approved.(c): Cumulative target and achievement of major components of infrastructure development works for Elementary, Secondary and Higher Secondary level for Rajasthan is enclosed as Annexure I.District wise cumulative target and achievement of major components of infrastructure development works for Elementary Level approved under Samagra Shiksha (from 2018-19 to 2020-21) is enclosed as Annexure II.District wise cumulative target and achievement of major components of infrastructure development works for Secondary and Higher Secondary level are enclosed as Annexure III and Annexure IV respectively.*****
