                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2267






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






RT-PCR Kits




Mala Roy














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether Indian companies are currently manufacturing RTPCR kits; (b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the percentage of kits that are indigenously manufactured and the percentage of kits being imported; and(d) the number of tests per day that can be done using the said kits?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b) The Central Drugs Standard Control Organisation (CDSCO) has informed that it has granted manufacturing licenses to manufacture COVID RTPCR kits to various indigenous companies under Medical Device Rules 2017. A statement containing the details of the indigenous manufacturer for COVID RTPCR kits is laid on the Table of the House is annexed.(c) CDSCO has informed that it has granted manufacturing licenses for COVID RTPCR kits to 30 companies while Import licenses have been granted to 119 companies.As per the online survey conducted by ICMR to know the manufacturing capacity of the Indian manufacturers for RT-PCR combo kits, it is learnt that Indian manufacturers have capacity to manufacture around 348.1 lakh test per month of RT-PCR tests based on combo kit in the country. During the month of March to June 2020 around 60% RT-PCR kits procured were from foreign manufacturers and remaining 40% were from Indian manufacturers. However, currently, 100% procurement of RT-PCR combo kits is from Indian manufacturers whereas, the COBAS, CBNAAT etc. kits are still being imported.(d) ICMR has informed that based on the current manufacturing capacity of the Indian manufacturer around 11.35 Lakh RTPCR tests based on combo kit can be conducted per day in the country.
