                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

151






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Community Radio in Rural and Urban Areas




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government proposes to simplify the process and promote the setting up of the community radio in rural and urban areas, if so, the details thereof; andb)	the details of the number of community radio centres established during the last three years along with the details of grants disbursed to them, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; AND MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING[SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR](a)	The M/o Information and Broadcasting has simplified the processes for setting up of more Community Radio (CR) Stations in 2017 and again in 2018. The Policy Guidelines for setting up of Community Radio Stations (CRSs) and its amendment are available on Ministry’s website www.mib.nic.in. In addition, the Ministry has launched an online portal i.e. www.broadcastseva.gov.in, which enables potential applicants to apply for CR permission online.(b)	During the last three years, 52 CR Stations have been established. A State-wise list of these CR Stations along with details of grant-in-aid disbursed is annexed.
