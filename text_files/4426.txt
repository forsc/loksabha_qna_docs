                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3800






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Annapurna Scheme




Ramesh Chandra Majhi














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has released the Central allocation of rice and funds under Annapurna Scheme to Odisha for the Financial year - 2017-18 which have already been distributed to the beneficiaries by the State Government out of the State pool and if so, the details thereof; and(b)	if not, whether the Government will release the Central allocation of rice and funds to the State of Odisha under Annapurna Scheme for the Financial Year-2017-18?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Government has allocated 7243.285 tons of rice to the State of Odisha under Annapurna Scheme for the financial year 2017-18. Further, an amount of Rs. 552.61 lakh  has been approved for  release to the State of Odisha for financial year 2017-18 under Annapurna Scheme. *****
