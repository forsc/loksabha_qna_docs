Anganwadi Kendras in Areas under Tribal Sub Plan




Ajay Nishad














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)  	the number of Anganwadi Kendras being run  in  areas  under  Tribal Sub-Plan  in  the  country, State/UT-wise and the number of beneficiaries thereunder;(b)  	the  funds  sanctioned,  released  to  the  State Government along with the funds utilised by them during each of the last three years and the current year;(c)  	whether  the  Government  proposes  to  set  up more Anganwadi Kendras in areas under tribal Sub-Plan; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?
