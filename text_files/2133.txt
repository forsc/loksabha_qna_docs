                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2028






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Air Crash




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government has any statistics regarding number of Indian Air Force’s (IAF) planes/helicopters/fighter jets crashed in the country;(b)  if so, the details thereof including number of IAF personnel who died in such incidents during the last five years, year-wise;(c)  whether the Government has conducted any enquiry in each incidents; and(d)  if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) & (b):  The details of aircraft crashes during last five years are as follows:	Type of aircraft/helicopterFinancial Year Fleet	Fighters	Helicopters 	Trainer	Transport2014-15	07	01	02	012015-16	04	01	01	-2016-17	06	02	01	012017-18	02	-	03	-2018-19	07	02	02	-2019-20	-	-	-	01	The number of IAF personnel who died in these accidents is as follows:Financial Year/Fleet	Pilots	Aircrew	Service Personnel2014-15	03	01	042015-16	-	-	-2016-17	02	02	162017-18	02	-	-2018-19	03	-	-2019-20	02	04	07(c) & (d):  Every aircraft accident in the IAF is investigated by Court of Inquiry (CoI) to ascertain the cause of accident and the recommendations of the completed Courts of Inquiry are implemented.*******
