                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3547






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






National Apprenticeship Training Scheme




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla













                                                    Harish Dwivedi
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                








                                                    (Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of National Apprenticeship Training Scheme (NATS);(b) whether the Government proposes to implement the said scheme to impart employment related training to the students of Arts, Science and Commerce streams;(c) if so, the details thereof and the duration of course in terms of years; and(d) the number of students imparted training under the said Scheme in Uttar Pradesh?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI) (a)National Apprenticeship Training Scheme (NATS) is a central sector scheme implemented by Ministry of Education through four regional Boards of Apprentices/Practical Training (BoAT/BoPT) located at Mumbai, Kanpur, Chennai and Kolkata in the country. The objective of the scheme is to provide a course of training in any industry/establishment to freshly pass out engineering graduates, diploma holders, graduate in General Streams like B.A, B.Sc & B.Com and students studying degree and diploma level courses in sandwitch programme of engineering. The scheme is operated as per “The Apprentices Act, 1961” and “The Apprenticeship Rules, 1992”. (b) & (c) Yes Sir. The duration of apprenticeship under this scheme ranges from a minimum period of six months to three years.(d)The number of students imparted training under the NATS scheme in Uttar Pradesh during last three years are as follows:<pre>2018-19	43262019-20	42842020-21	3330</pre>
