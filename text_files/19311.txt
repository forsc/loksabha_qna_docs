                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

676






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram




John Barla













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Dulal Chandra Goswami
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                








                                                    Chandra Prakash Joshi
                                                








                                                    (Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria
                                                








                                                    Nayab Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any step has been taken to promote education among minority communities under the Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK); (b) if so, the details of the works done in education sector in the country including Uttar Pradesh during the last five years; and (c) the names of the locations selected for development of resources for education along with the details of the works carried out in the said locations, State-wise particularly Haryana?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTRY OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a) to (c):    The Ministry is implementing Pradhan Mantri Jan Vikas Karyakram (PMJVK) in  identified Minority Concentration Areas (MCAs) of the country with the objective of developing socio-economic assets and basic amenities in these areas for improving quality of life of the people in these areas. The coverage of the scheme has been extended to 1300 MCAs of the country including 109 Minority Concentration District Hqrs, 321 Minority Concentration Towns and 870 Minority Concentration Blocks of 308 districts, in the year 2018. Under PMJVK, education is one of the priority sectors besides health, skill development and women centric projects.  During the last five years (2015-16 to 2019-20), projects of Rs. 8084.40 crore having Central Share of Rs. 5786.17 crore have been approved in the education sector. The projects include  157 - Residential Schools, 26 - Degree Colleges, 543 – Student Hostels, 2 - Navodyas Vidyalayas,  58 - ITIs, 12 - Polytechnics, 15 - Skill Centres, 2846 - Additional Classrooms (ACR),  354- ACR blocks, 3276 - Sanitation Projects in Schools, 8520 - Smart Classes and Teaching Aids, etc.   State-wise details of the projects approved under the programme, including in Uttar Pradesh and Haryana, are available at http://minorityaffairs.gov.in/en/pradhan-mantri-jan-vikas-karyakram-pmjvk-0.*****
