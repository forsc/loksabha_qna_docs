                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3996






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Survey for Laying New Railway Lines




Deepak (Dev) Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of surveys conducted for laying new railway lines in the country during the last three years, State-wise; and(b)	the steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) and (b): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.***** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) AND (b) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3996 BY SHRI ADHIKARI DEEPAK (DEV) TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 18.03.2020 REGARDING SURVEY FOR LAYING NEW RAILWAY LINES(a) and (b): Since Railway projects generally straddle over more than one State, these are neither surveyed nor sanctioned State-wise. Survey for new lines is a continuous and ongoing process of Indian Railways and are done based on demands raised by State Governments, Central Ministries, Members of Parliament, other public representatives and Railways’ own requirement. In the last three years and current financial year, surveys for 206 New Lines have been completed throughout the country. Zonal Railway wise details of these surveys are appended.*****
