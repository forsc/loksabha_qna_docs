                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3903






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






International Airport at Jewar




K. Shanmuga Sundaram














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has finalized the deal for second international airport at Jewar in Uttar Pradesh;(b) if so, the details of the bidder and the expected date of completion as per their bidding;(c) whether the main bidder has given sub-contract or any other authority to implement the project;(d) if so, the name of the sub-contractor or any Special Purpose Vehicle who will be engaged to carry out the project;(e) the estimated cost and the number of passengers expected to utilise the new airport on completion; and(f) the status of the existing Delhi International Airport and its utilization after the completion of the said airport?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (e): Government of India granted ''in principle'' approval to Government of Uttar Pradesh (GoUP) on 08.05.2018  for setting up of a new greenfield international airport at Jewar, Noida. GoUP appointed Yamuna Expressway Industrial Development Authority (YEIDA) as the implementing agency for the project under Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode. YEIDA has completed the bidding process wherein M/s Zurich Airport International AG has emerged as the successful bidder. State Government has awarded the project to M/s Zurich Airports International for development of the airport under PPP. M/s. Zurich Airport International AG has formed a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) -Yamuna International Airport Private Limited (YIAPL). YIAPL is now the concessionaire of the project.  The Concession Agreement has been signed for this on 7th October, 2020 between YIAPL and Noida International Airport Limited (NIAL), a State Government Company.As per the master plan submitted by YIAPL, the forecast for initial year traffic is 4 Million Passengers Per Annum (MPPA) for the airport against the initial capacity of 12 MPPA for Phase I . Further, Noida (Jewar) International airport is a project of State Government of Uttar Pradesh with original project cost Rs. 8638 Crore in Phase-I with Probable Date of Completion (PDC) in 2023-24. However, timeline for completion of the airport projects depend upon many factors such as land acquisition, availability of mandatory clearances, financial closure, etc.;(f): The IGI Airport, Delhi is an operational airport. Government of India has granted ''in principle'' approval for setting up of a new greenfield international airport at Jewar, Noida as a second airport to serve the need of Delhi National Capital Region. IGI Airport, Delhi will continue to remain operational.
