                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1020






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Homeopathic Medical Colleges




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has conducted any study regarding the number of Homoeopathic medical colleges in the country; (b) if so, whether their number is sufficient to meet the needs in the country; and (c) whether the Government proposes to consider establishing adequate number of Homoeopathic colleges in the country especially in Odisha in order to promote Indian systems of medicine in view of the priority being accorded to provide the affordable healthcare services to the increasing population: and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a)  & (b) No study has been conducted regarding the number of Homoeopathic Medical Colleges in the country. The National Commission for Homoeopathy (NCH) is Maintaining the list of approved/recognized Homoeopathic Colleges. There are total 250 Homoeopathic Medical Colleges (including 03 new colleges opened in academic year 2021-22 as on 29.11.2021) across the country. The State/Union Territory-wise list along with the number of UG and PG seats is attached at Annexure I.(c) & (d) Establishing Homoeopathic Medical College comes under the purview of concerned State government. The State Government/ Private bodies can apply to Medical Assessment and Rating Board for Homoeopathy, NCH under the Establishment of New Medical College (Opening of New or Higher Course of Study or Training and Increase of Admission Capacity by a Medical College) Regulations, 2011 (Amendments Regulations, 2018).Further, in Odisha at present there are four (04) functioning Government Homoeopathic Medical colleges.The Details of proposal received during year 2020 for the Academic year 2021-22 under (then) section 12 A of the HCC Act, 1973, Now Section 29 of the NCH Act 2020 is attached at Annexure- II***
