                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

144






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Criteria to Set up Tourist Spots under Swadesh Darshan Scheme




Vinod Kumar Sonkar














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the criteria set up to select tourist spots under ‘Swadesh Darshan’ scheme to develop and promote tourism; (b) 	the location-wise number of tourist spots identified in States including Uttar Pradesh; (c) 	whether development of Buddhist Circuit by the Government under Swadesh Darshan Yojana is likely to take more time; (d) 	if so, whether the famous Buddhist site located in district Kaushambi, Uttar Pradesh has been included in Buddhist circuit; and (e) 	if not, whether the Government is considering to include Buddhist site in Kaushambi, Uttar Pradesh under Buddhist Circuit in order to promote tourism?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a):	As per scheme guidelines of the Swadesh Darshan Scheme, the sanctioning of projects is based on following criteria: •	Potential to be showcased as world class destination with diverse tourism experiences.•	Proximity to existing destinations with connectivity and other infrastructure.•	Job creation and livelihood enhancement in the region, while conserving and promoting the local arts, cultural, handicrafts, cuisine etc. at the destination.•	Ability to attract investment in private sector/PPP with specific provisions.•	Follow a comprehensive area development approach for ensuring all the facilities required by the tourists in the identified circuits.•	Robust Operation and Management plan (O&M) as per pre-agreed service standards evaluated periodically by an independent agency.(b):	The Ministry has sanctioned 77 projects for Rs.6035.70 Crores under the scheme. The details of same are at annexure. (c) to (e):	Ministry of Tourism has identified Buddhist Circuit as one of the fifteen thematic circuits for development under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme. All sites in the country related to Buddhism including the ones in Uttar Pradesh are covered under this Circuit. Sanctioning of projects under the scheme is a continuous process. Ministry has sanctioned five projects for Rs. 355.26 Crore under the Buddhist Circuit theme covering the states of Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Madhya Pradesh, Gujarat and Andhra Pradesh. The work on all the projects is under implementation.*****
