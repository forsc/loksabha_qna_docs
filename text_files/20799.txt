                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1918






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Yoga Villages/Centers/Parks




Upendra Singh Rawat













                                                     Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)
                                                








                                                    Umesh G. Jadhav
                                                








                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) Whether the Government has been working to set up yoga villages/centers/parks in the country, State/ UT-wise, district-wise, block level-wise including Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan and Kalaburagi of Karnataka; and(b) if so, the details thereof alongwith approved yoga centers in Rajasthan;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the amount of funds released for these yoga villages/centers/parks during the last three years?






ANSWER




ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a): As public health is a State subject, setting up yoga villages/centers/parks in the country State/UT-wise, district-wise and at block level comes under the purview of respective State/UT Governments. The Ministry of AYUSH does not maintain data on such activities of State/UT governments.However, Under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) of Ministry of AYUSH there is a provision of financial assistance to States/UTs for establishing Yoga Wellness Centres including the States of Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan and Karnataka.  (b): 	As per the proposals received from the State / UT Governments in their State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs), total 1067 units of Yoga Wellness Centres has been approved under NAM during the last three years. An amount of Rs. 5900.36 lakhs has been sanctioned to different States / UTs for these Yoga Wellness Centres during last three years. (c): 	However, no Yoga Wellness Centre is approved in Rajasthan in last three years. (d): 	Details of the amount sanctioned is for the Yoga Wellness Centres under NAM during last three years are given at annexure.
