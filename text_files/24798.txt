                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

715






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Financial Assistance for the Victims of COVID-19




S. Muniswamy













                                                    A. Ganeshamurthi
                                                








                                                    Sougata Ray
                                                








                                                    T. R. Paarivendhar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of deaths reported in the country due to COVID-19, State/UT-wise;(b) whether the Government proposes to disburse/ have disbursed financial assistance for the family of the victims who have died due to COVID-19, such as exgratia payment/compensation etc.(c) if so, the details of ex-gratia payments/ compensation being awarded to the families of the victims of COVID-19;(d) whether the Government has any policy or scheme that provide for National Insurance Coverage for deaths due to COVID-19 and include the pandemic for risk insurance coverage against national disasters in the country, if so, the details thereof and if not, thereasons therefor;(e) the details of Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) to finalise COVID-19 deaths; and(f) the details of death cases reported after vaccination in the country along with the medicines/ vaccinations approved by Indian Government against COVID-19?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a):	Number of deaths reported in the country due to COVID-19, State/UT wise is at Annexure.(b):	Under the existing guidelines governing State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF) there is no provision for grant of ex-gratia assistance to the families of COVID-19 victims.(c) & (d):	Life insurance benefits are being provided under “Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package (PMGKP): Insurance Scheme for Health Workers Fighting COVID-19”. The scheme provides a comprehensive personal accident cover of Rs. 50 lakhs to healthcare providers, including community health workers and private health workers drafted by the Government for COVID19 related responsibilities, who may have to be in direct contact and care of COVID-19 patients and who may be at risk of being impacted by it. (e):	MoHFW has conveyed to States/UTs, WHO and ICMR guidelines on correct recording of COVID-19 related deaths in accordance with globally accepted ICD-10 classification. In addition, the issue of correct and timely reporting of deaths has been stressed to all States/UTs through video conferences and formal communications.(f):	Till 19th July 2021, causality assessment for 179 serious and severe AEFI cases has been approved by the National AEFI Committee of which only one death case was found to be associated with COVID 19 vaccine. This was a case of anaphylaxis which can occur with any medicinal product including vaccines and can be fatal.
