                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

63






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Advisory to TV Channel




Subhash Ramrao Bhamre













                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of advisories issued by the Government to television channels during the last three years; (b)	whether policy guidelines have been issued for uplinking of television channels from India, if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether his Ministry has issued any advisory regarding carrying of news and current affairs content on TV channels recently, if so, the details thereof and the objective behind the move;(d)	whether all the TV channels are complying with the said advisories, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor, and(e)	the steps taken by the Government to ensure that all the TV channels comply with the Government advisories?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE;AND MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a):     Issuing advisories as per law is a continuous process. This Ministry issues advisories to private satellite TV channels from time to time for ensuring adherence to the Programme and Advertising Code as prescribed under the Cable Television Networks (Regulation) Act, 1995 and the Rules framed thereunder. The Government has issued 32 advisories in the past three years and the current year. Further, 34 advisories had been issued between 2011 and 2014.(b):     Policy Guidelines for Uplinking Television Channels from India, notified in July, 2000 were amended in December, 2011 and are available on Ministry’s website i.e. www.mib.gov.in. (c) to (e): In accordance with the Uplinking Guidelines, 2011, an advisory dated 22.05.2019 was issued by this Ministry advising all TV channels to carry programmes as mandated under the category of their permission viz. News and Current Affairs and Non-News & Current Affairs. 	All private satellite TV channels are required to adhere to the Guidelines and advisories issued thereunder. Appropriate action is taken whenever any violation/contravention takes place.******
