                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2309






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Situation after Abrogation of Art. 370




Nitesh Ganga Deb













                                                    Kaushal Kishore
                                                








                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is an increase in terrorist incidents and the number of terrorists killed in Jammu & Kashmir after abrogation of Article 370;(b) if so, the number and incidents reported and terrorists killed so far in the said Union Territory in the year 2019-20 and 2020-21;(c) whether civilians, police personnel and para military forces have also been killed during the operations;(d) if so, the details thereof;(e) the details of scheme to provide assistance to family members of the martyred security personnel;(f) the number of terrorist incidents in the country reported and the number of terrorists killed during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UTwise and(g) the measures taken to check terrorist activities?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b) After abrogation of Article 370, the number of terrorist incidents in Jammu  and  Kashmir  have  reduced  significantly.  The  details  of  number  of --2--L.S.US.Q.NO. 2309 FOR 09.03.2021incidents and the terrorists killed in J&K in the year in the year 2019, 2020 and 2021 are as under:-	2019	2020	2021 (upto Feb)No. of terrorist incidents	594	244	15Terrorists killed 	157	221	8(c) & (d) The details of civilians, security force personnel killed during the operations in last 2 years are as under :-	2019	2020	2021 (upto Feb.)Civilian Killed	5	6	0Security Force personnel Martyred	27	33	1(e) Ex-gratia relief is given to the Next of kin (NoK) as per the extant rules of the security forces concerned .The details of the admissible benefits given to the next of kin of Army, CAPFs  and JK Police personnel are at Annexure-I, Annexure II and Annexure III  respectively.--3--L.S.US.Q.NO. 2309 FOR 09.03.2021(f) Details are at Annexure-IV.(g) The Government has adopted a policy of zero tolerance towards terrorism and has taken various measures, such as strengthening of security apparatus, strict enforcement of law against anti-national elements, intensified cordon and search operations to effectively deal with the challenges posed by the terrorist organisations. Security Forces also keep a close watch on persons who attempt to provide support to terrorists and initiate action against them.*****
