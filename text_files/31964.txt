                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3538






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






KAPILA Campaign




Vishnu Dutt Sharma













                                                    Arvind Kumar Sharma
                                                








                                                    Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal
                                                








                                                    Shankar Lalwani
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has launched a campaign namely Kalam Program for Intellectual Property Literacy and Awareness Campaign (KAPILA) for raising awareness on Intellectual Property Literacy in Higher Education Institutions;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has achieved the desired progress and results out of this campaign;(d) if so, the details thereof;and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a)to(e): The government has launched a campaign namely Kalam Program for Intellectual Property Literacy and Awareness Campaign (KAPILA) on 15th October, 2020 to increase awareness regarding protection and exploitation of Intellectual Property (IP) and also to provide funding support in order to promote filing of the Intellectual Property (IP) in Higher Education Institutions. A KAPILA portal has also been launched for this purpose.Under the initiative, activities like IP Clinic, Case Studies/ Articles regarding Innovation and Intellectual Property and online awareness programs and National Intellectual Property Literacy Week (NIPLW) have been conducted. More than 46,000 users have registered for KAPILA awareness programs.*******
