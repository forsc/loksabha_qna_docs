                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

952






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






AMRUT Scheme




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                








                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether urban development of small cities is being undertaken by the Government under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) scheme;(b)	if so, the number of cities benefited from this scheme so far, State/UT-wise; (c)	the funds allocated/released along with its utilization so far thereunder, State/ UT-wise;(d)	whether the Government proposes to start the next phase of the said scheme; and(e)	if so, the details of the cities selected for coverage under the said scheme and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                   (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a).	Government of India launched Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) in 500 selected Mission cities on 25th June, 2015. All cities/ towns with a population of one lakh or more as per Census 2011, all other capital cities of States/Union Territories (UTs), all heritage cities/towns under Heritage City Development and Augmentation Yojana (HRIDAY), 13 cities/towns on the stem of the main rivers with the population between 75,000 to 1,00,000 and 10 cities/ towns  from hill states, islands and tourist destinations (not more than one from each State)have been included under the Mission.(b).	The State/UT-wise number of cities selected under the Mission is placed as Annexure-I. (c).	The State/UT-wise status of funds allocated/released and Utilization Certificates (UCs) received so far under the Mission is placed as Annexure-II.(d) & (e).	 No, Sir. There is no such proposal under consideration.                               *****
