                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

337






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Jan Aushadhi Centres




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state: (a) whether Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP) Centres are functioning in Rajasthan; (b) if so, the details thereof, district-wise; (c) the places where new PMBJP centres are likely to be set up by the Government, location-wise; and (d) the efforts being made to set up the said centres at the earliest?






ANSWER




(a)  	Yes Sir.(b) 	District wise list of PMBJP Kendras functioning in the State of Rajasthan is enclosed as Annexure.(c) & (d)There is no location wise specific plan for the opening ofPMBJP Kendras in the country. As on date 696 districts out of 728 districts of the country are covered by this scheme. The plan is to cover the remaining districts by end of this financial year. Department of Pharmaceuticals is continuously writing and pursuing this matter with the concerned state government.   Annexure The statement referred to in  part ( b) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Q.
