                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

95






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Online Education in Telangana




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of Kendriya Vidyalayas in the Telangana State;(b) whether the students enrolled in Kendriya Vidyalayas have been availingonline classes and if so; the percentage of schools taking online classes;(c) if so, the percentage of students attending such classes and whether notesare being made available to those who are unable to attend such classes; and(d) if not, the plans in place to recover the academic time lost to students enrolledin Kendriya Vidyalayas in Telangana?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        The MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) Presently, 35 Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) are functioning in the State ofTelangana.(b) & (c) Blended mode of teaching learning process i.e. online / offline is going on inall 35 Kendriya Vidyalayas of Telangana. Approximately 50% students are attendingonline classes and the rest of the students are attending physical classes withstaggered timing.(d) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan has provided all the possible support to thestudents on academic front. Besides, online as well as offline tools were provided tothe students to bridge the learning gaps.*****
