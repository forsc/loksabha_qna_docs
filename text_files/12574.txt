                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1489






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Skill Development Training under DDU-GKY




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to impart any skill development training to rural youth in naxal and terrorism affected areas under Deen Dayal Upadhyaya-Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY);(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the total number of persons proposed to be imparted skill development training under DDU-GKY, State/Union Territory-wise;(d) 	whether there is any proposal to impart training in Information Technology for skill development of rural youths; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b): The Government is implementing Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY), a placement linked skill development program for rural poor youth under National Rural Livelihoods Mission (NRLM) all over the country. Under DDU-GKY a special initiative called Roshni has been launched that takes into account the peculiar situations in left wing extremist affected 27 districts. Under this initiative skill development shall be mandatory residential course with 40% coverage to women candidates. Roshni is oriented towards addressing the infrastructure, education, and health deficiencies in these areas, and leveraging the availability of natural resources and traditional skills and knowledge. A special scheme Himayat has been launched for Jammu and Kashmir under DDU-GKY. This is a 100% centrally funded scheme.  In addition to areas covered under Roshni and Himayat, trainings under DDU-GKY are provided in other affected areas.  (c): The Empowered Committee in MoRD has approved the Action Plans of the States/UTs consisting of number of candidates targeted to be trained for implementing DDU-GKY during the year 2019-22.
