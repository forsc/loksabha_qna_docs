                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1134






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Disability Certificates




P.P. Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of disability certificates issued in the State of Rajasthan, district-wise and State-wise data;(b)	 whether the Government is planning to formulate guidelines for hospitals and e-mitra centres for issuance of disability certificates;(c)	 if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	  the details of the steps taken by the Government to increase the efficiency of the software through which certificates are generated?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a)	Certificates of disability are issued to Persons with Disabilities (PwDs) by the competent medical authorities as notified by the States/Union Territories. As per information available from the office of Chief Commissioner of Persons with Disabilities (CCPD), the details of the number of certificates of disability issued by various States/Union Territories including Rajasthan as on 02.12.2021 are at Annexure I. As per information received from Government of Rajasthan district-wise status of issuance of certificate of disability only through online mode as on 02.02.2022 is at Annexure-II(b)	& (c)  The Ministry notified the guidelines for assessment of specified disabilities included under Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 (except Autism Spectrum Disorder) on as notified 04.01.2018. The guidelines for assessment of Autism Spectrum Disorder notified on 25.04.2016, is applicable. These guidelines are required to be followed by all certifying medical authorities while assessing disability. Further, the Ministry has also issued a notification on 5th May 2021, which mandates issuance of certificate of disability through Unique Disability Identity Card portal i.e.www.swavlambancard.gov.in with effect from 01.06.2021 by the competent medical authorities  by the States and Union Territories. Further, the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities has signed an Memorandum of Understanding with CSC e-Governance Services India Limited under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology with a view to enable registration of persons with disabilities for applying for certificate of disability under the Unique Disability ID (UDID) portal. Government of Rajasthan has informed that the facility of free online registration of PwDs and certification of disability through hospitals is available on the "Specially Abled Registration" online portal through e-Mitra.(d)	Up-gradation of the application software of the UDID portal through which certificates of disability are issued is a continuous process. The Ministry undertakes security audit of the UDID application software periodically and enhances the capacity of servers and addresses other technical parameters as and when required to increase the efficiency. *****
