                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

998






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Total Land Assets owned by All Bodies




Sushil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the list of all bodies (not restricted to just the autonomous and statutory bodies) under the administrative control of the Ministry;(b) 	the list, details, locations, total area of all land assets owned by all the bodies under the administrative control of the Ministry along with the monetary value of each land asset;(c) 	whether the Government has undertaken any exercise of monetization of assets;(d) 	if so, the details, thereof;(e 	if not, the reasons therefor;  and(f) 	the list of assets (all) monetized by any of the bodies under the administrative control of the Ministry in last 10 years, year-wise?
