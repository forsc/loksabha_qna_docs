                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3824






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Ventilators Supplied during the COVID-19 Pandemic




Arvind Ganpat Sawant














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has provided ventilators to hospitals across the country from special fund other than the budgeted Government funds as on February 23, 2022; and (b) if so the number of such ventilators disbursed utilizing the special funds, State/UT/hospital wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)& (b) The Government has provided ventilators to States/UTs for COVID-19 management. The number of ventilators provided to States/UTs and Central Institutions from out of funds other than budgeted Government funds is as below: Andaman and Nicobar Islands (34), 	Andhra Pradesh (5701), Arunachal Pradesh (63), Assam(1000), Bihar (500), Chandigarh (65), Chattisgarh (515), Dadra & Nagar Haveli  and Daman & Diu (20), Delhi (976), Goa (200), Gujarat (5181), Haryana (483), Himachal Pradesh (500), Jammu & Kashmir (908), Jharkhand (1210), Karnataka (2871), Kerala (480), Ladakh (130), Madhya Pradesh (1205), Maharashtra (4342), Manipur (247), Meghalaya (86), Mizoram (115), Nagaland (320), Odisha (917), Puducherry (107), Punjab (709), Rajasthan (1900), Sikkim (25), Tamil Nadu (2242), Telangana (2346), Tripura (92), Uttarakhand (800), Uttar Pradesh (4466), West Bengal (1545), Lakshwadeep (57), Central Institution (3317)                                                                   *****
