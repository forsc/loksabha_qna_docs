                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ATOMIC ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2933






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Protection from Nuclear Materials




 Rahul Ramesh Shewale














                        Will the Minister of





ATOMIC ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the details of the guidelines issued by the Government for physical protection  	from nuclear materials and facilities; (b)	whether the cases of violation of the said guidelines have come to the notice 	of the Government during each of the last three years and the current year;(c)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise and the reasons therefor along with the 	action taken by the Government in such cases so far;(d)	whether the Canadian Nuclear Safety Commission has suggested 	improvements to Indian Atomic Energy Regulatory Board towards 	strengthening the atomic regulatory framework in India;(e)	if so, the details of such improvements along with the compliance of such 	suggestions by the Government so far; and (f)	the other steps taken by the Government  in this direction?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES & PENSIONS AND PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (Dr. JITENDRA SINGH):    (a)	Atomic Energy Regulatory Board (AERB) has issued guidance documents 	regarding detailed requirements on physical protection of nuclear materials 	and facilities to be complied by all nuclear facilities in the country. These 	aspects are reviewed by AERB before giving clearance for various stages 	of 	nuclear facilities and have a well established process for monitoring 	compliance to these requirements through multi-tier reviews and periodic 	regulatory inspections. (b)	No, Sir. (c)	Not applicable in view of (b) above. (d)	No, Sir. (e)&(f) Not applicable, in view of (d) above*********
