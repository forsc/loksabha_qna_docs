                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2417






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






ONORC in Assam




Kripanath Mallah














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the salient features of the One Nation One Ration Card Scheme (ONORC); (b) 	whether the Government has started this scheme within Assam; (c) 	if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	if not, the reasons therefor and the time by which it is likely to be implemented in Assam?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	Through technology-driven One Nation One Ration Card (ONORC) system, all National Food Security Act, 2013 (NFSA) beneficiaries, particularly migrant beneficiaries, can lift their monthly entitled foodgrains, either in parts or full, from any electronic Point of Sale (ePoS) enabled Fair Price Shop (FPS) in the country – by using their same existing ration card or Aadhaar number with biometric authentication. Their family members back home, if any, may also lift the part/balance foodgrains on the same ration card. (b) & (c): 	No Sir. The ONORC plan is not yet enabled in the State of Assam. (d): 	The Department has regularly followed up with the State Government of Assam for installation of requisite ePoS devices in all FPSs of the State and to enable the ONORC plan for the benefit of migrant NFSA beneficiaries at the earliest.***
