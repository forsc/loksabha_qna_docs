                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1454






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Harassment of Women Police




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that women in the police and paramilitary forces are facing harassment in many forms;(b) if so, the number of such harassment cases brought to the notice of the Government during the last five years;(c) whether it is true that women Indian Police Service officers were also not spared from the harassment and in recent times, similar cases were reported and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government has taken note of any such cases and taken action thereon; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e) : “Police” is a State subject falling in List-II (State List) of the Seventh Schedule of the Constitution of India. It is primarily the responsibility of the State Governments/UT Administrations to implement suitable measures for women. The Centre also issues advisories to the States to strengthen welfare measures for women police personnel and ensure their safety and a conducive work environment.   -2/...-2-LS.US.Q.NO. 1454 FOR 11.02.2020The Government is deeply sensitive to the needs and requirement of women in the police. Police Forces of the States work within the existing legal & institutional framework in the matter. Data pertaining to such cases is not maintained centrally. *****
