                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

303






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Setting up of Storage Facilities




P. Ravindhranath














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received any representations from social organizations regarding financial assistance for setting up of chain of storage facilities including cold storage and associated logistics facilities to overcome the damages caused in final agro products; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof and action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Various social organizations such as Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) and Self Help Groups (SHGs) are eligible for assistance for setting up chain of cold storage facilities including cold storages and associated logistics facilities under various schemes of Government of India such as Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) / Agricultural Marketing Infrastructure (AMI) Schemes of Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare and under Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana of Ministry of Food Processing Industries, for developing post-harvest infrastructure to overcome post-harvest losses of agriculture and horticulture produce. Presently, no representation from any social organization is reported to be pending with Government of India regarding financial assistance for setting up of chain of storage facilities including cold storage and associated logistics facilities. ********
