                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4145






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Coastal Economic Zones




M. Selvaraj













                                                    K. Subbarayan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that the Government has identified 14 Coastal Economic Zones (CEZs) to be targeted for labour intensive and export oriented industries; and (b)	 if so, the details thereof along with the action taken by the Government to    develop these zones?






ANSWER




                                                        (a & b) Yes, Sir. In the National Perspective Plan(NPP) of Sagarmala Programme, 14 Coastal Economic Zones (CEZs) have been identified, details of which are placed at Annexure-I. CEZs proposed in the NPP of Sagarmala programme (2016-2035) are expected to reduce logistic cost and to generate new jobs. Proposal for developing one prototype CEZ has been taken up by the Government.
