                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1449






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






National Programme for Dairy Development




Manoj Kumar Tiwari













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Mahendra Singh Solanky
                                                








                                                    Krishna Pal Singh Yadav
                                                








                                                    Rajbahadur Singh
                                                








                                                     Pratap Chandra Sarangi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the release of funds under the National Programme for Dairy Development by Government of India during the last two years; (b) 	the amount of funds allocated and utilized under this scheme, State-wise; and (c) 	the number of projects and proposals received and sanctioned under this scheme in NCT of Delhi in the States of Madhya Pradesh, Odisha and Uttar Pradesh? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a)	& (b) An amount of Rs. 266.17 crore and Rs.285.86 crore has been released to States during 2019-20 & 2020-21 respectively under National Programme for Dairy Development scheme. The State-wise details of funds released and utilized is at Annexure.(c) 	The details of number of projects received and sanctioned under NPDD scheme in NCT of Delhi, Madhya Pradesh, Odisha and Uttar Pradesh is as under:-S. No.	Name of State	Number of Projects Received	Number of Projects Sanctioned1.	NCT of Delhi	0	02.	Madhya Pradesh	13	113.	Odisha	6	64.	Uttar Pradesh	7	6****
