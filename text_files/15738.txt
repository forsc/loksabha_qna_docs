                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4327






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Housing for All in Kerala




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of the beneficiaries identified under Housing for All by 2022 in Kerala, district-wise;(b) 	whether the Government has considered the loss of houses during the flash floods of 2018 in Kerala which destroyed large number of houses leaving the people houseless; and(c) 	whether the Government is also considering for increasing of the financial limits for EWS, LIG and MIG categories from the prevailing yardstick to avail the PMAY(U) benefits and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	District wise details of number of beneficiaries identified by the State Government of Kerala through demand survey under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY(U)] are at Annexure.(b)	 Considering the stress situation due to heavy floods of 2018 in Kerala, central assistance of Rs. 492.95 crore was released to the State Government as first and second installments together for construction of houses sanctioned under PMAY (U), by relaxing the conditions for release as a special case. Simultaneously, the State Government of Kerala was also advised to send project proposals 	for consideration under the scheme including for those who were affected by the flood.  (c) 	No Sir; there is no such proposal under consideration of the Government.                                            *****
