                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1638






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Allocation/Utilization of Funds under Swachh Bharat Mission




P. C. Mohan













                                                     Dr. T. Sumathy (a) Thamizhachi Thangapandian
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized under Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U) across the country including Karnataka; (b)	 the details of the target set and achievements made so far; (c)	 whether the Government has not been achieving its original objectives and targets within the set timeframe and if so, the details thereof including Karnataka and the reasons therefor and the steps being taken by the Government in this regard; (d)	 whether the Government has taken a decision to increase the amount of Central assistance towards SBM-U and if so, the details thereof; (e)	 the quantum of funds released to each State, particularly to Tamil Nadu during the last five years, year-wise; and (f)	 the details of the projects being executed during the same period and the quantum of funds proposed to be allotted to Tamil Nadu for the next three years?






ANSWER




                                                                   THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                           (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) & (e) : Under Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U), out of the total Mission allocation of `14,013.46 crore, the fund allocated,released and utilised by the States/ Union Territory Administrations including Karnataka and Tamil Nadu during last five years is at Annexure-I. (b) & (c) : No Sir. The objective of SBM-U is to make entire urban India Open Defecation Free (ODF) and 100% scientific management of Municipal Solid Waste (MSW) generated. Till date, all the Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) in the country have been declared ODF except 03 ULBs of West Bengal. Till date, the processing of MSW has increased to 68% from 18% in 2014. Details of progress made in the State of Karnataka during the mission period is given in Annexure-II.(d) : During the year 2016, the Ministry had enhanced the Viability Gap Funding (VGF) for the solid waste management component under SBM-U from 20% to 35% as Central Assistance. Further, during the year 2017, ceiling for construction of Individual House Hold Latrines (IHHLs) was raised from ?4,000/- per unit to `10,800/- per unit for the North Eastern States and Hilly Regions of the country.(f) : Details of progress made in the State of Tamil Nadu during the mission period is given in Annexure III. The SBM-U is nearing completion and quantum of fund to be allocated to Tamil Nadu in the next three years cannot be anticipated at this stage.                                *****
