                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2995






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Green Field Airport at Jewar




Kirron  Kher














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has accorded in-principle clearance for a Greenfield airport at Jewar and if so, the details thereof; and(b) the cost outlay for this new airport as well as the time-frame by which it is likely to become operational?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Government of India has granted ''in-principle'' approval to Noida International Airport Limited (NIAL) for construction of new airport at Jewar, Uttar Pradesh. The project is being implemented by Government of Uttar Pradesh through NIAL. As informed by NIAL, the bid documents including concession agreement have been finalized and approved by Government of Uttar Pradesh. NIAL has issued e-tender on 30.05.2019 for selection of the concessionaire with last date of receiving the bid as 30.10.2019. Indicative cost for Phase-I  of the project, as per the Techno Economic  Feasibility Report of NIAL  is Rs.4588 crore with the  projected year of operationalisation of the airport as 2023-2024.
