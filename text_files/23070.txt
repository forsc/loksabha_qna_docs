                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4123






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Swadhar Greh




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the total number of Swadhar Greh currently operational/setup in the country, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra:(b)	whether the Government has reviewed the implementation of Swadhar Greh Scheme and if so, the details thereof:(c)	the details of the monitoring mechanism in place to ensure the effective implementation of the scheme and the assessment made during the last three years thereunder;(d)	the steps taken to popularise the address and contact details of these Swadhar Greh;(e)	the current occupancy levels in all such already established and operational Swadhar Greh;(f)	the funds allocated/released and utilised under the Swadhar Greh scheme during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a)	The total number of Swadhar Greh currently operational in the country, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra is at Annexure I.(b)	The Government reviews the implementation of Swadhar Greh Scheme on a continuous basis through the State Governments and necessary actions are taken for proper implementation of the scheme.(c)	The Swadhar Greh scheme envisages monitoring of the implementing agencies at District, State and Central level. Swadhar Grehs are monitored by a District Monitoring Committee where at least two members are women. The district committee meets at least once in a quarter. Monitoring Committee at State level meets at least twice a year and monitors the projects. Continuation of grant to any agency depends on the satisfactory performance as assessed by the State/UT level Committee. Funds have been released on the basis of the documents provided and recommendations made by the State/UT Governments during the last three years. The implementing agency is required to display in capital letters, the name, telephone numbers and address of the Swadhar Greh along with the name and telephone numbers of District level officers concerned, in the local language, at a prominent place in the Swadhar Greh. Further, steps are taken to popularize the address and contact details of the Swadhar Grehs through contact programmes, print/electronic networking with NGOs, Police and other Stakeholders. (d)	The current occupancy level in all such already established and operational Swadhar Greh is 7719 as on 31.12.2020.
