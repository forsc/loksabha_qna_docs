                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1121






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Integrated Medical Laboratories




Sudheer Gupta













                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government proposes to establish/upgrade integrated lab/centre to strengthening of laboratory network in different parts of the country; (b) if so, the details thereof and along with the place where it is proposed to be set up; (c) the expenditure to be incurred thereon and the sources from where the funds are proposed to be generated; and (d) whether the Government proposes to set up more such centres/labs in the country and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)	& (b):     Under Integrated Disease Surveillance Programme (IDSP) integrated laboratories at District hospitals are being setup and strengthened to develop lab capacity for conducting surveillance of epidemic prone diseases in a decentralized manner.The details of The District Public Health Laboratory (DPHL), State Referral lab network (SRL), IDSP influenza Surveillance Network, National Programme on AMR(Antimicrobial Resistance) Containment Labs, are placed at Annexures I to IV.Further, 95 laboratories have been established in different parts of the country at Government Medical Colleges/ ICMR & Central Institutes. (c ):      Under IDSP for setting up of DPHL, 17.9 lacs (non- recurring) and annual grant of 4.00 lakhs (Recurring) is provided. For SRL Annual grant of 2 lacs for maintaining performance standards & subsequent reimbursements up to Rs 3 lacs per annum per state referral laboratory is provided. Rs 11.20 Lacs are given on turnkey basis per lab per year to Influenza lab. The funding is provided by IDSP through National Health Mission.National Programme on AMR Containment Funds of upto Rs 14 lakh is provided to each lab for manpower, equipment, consumables.  The AMR programme is funded under central sector scheme.The scheme of “Setting up of Nation-wide network of laboratories for managing epidemics and National Calamities” was approved by Standing Finance Committee held on 18th September, 2017 beyond 12th five year plan leading to 14th Finance Commission Period with an estimated cost of Rs 488.45 crore.(d):      Setting up of DPHL under IDSP is subject to demand and proposals raised by the respective States / UTs in PIP.  The lab network for AMR surveillance is being expanded in a phased manner to include additional state medical colleges.Further,106 Viral Research and Diagnostic Laboratories have been approved.
