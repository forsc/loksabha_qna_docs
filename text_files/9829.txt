                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3320






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






National Skills Qualifications Framework to Minorities




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government has taken any initiative for Skill 	Development for minorities under the National Skills Qualifications 	Framework (NSQF);	(b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; 	(c) 	whether any step has been taken to rope in the industry to provide 	skills to minorities; and 	(d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (d)	The National Skills Qualifications Framework (NSQF) is a competency based framework which organizes qualification according to a series of levels of knowledge, skill and aptitude. The NSQF is a quality assurance framework wherein levels are defined in terms of learning outcomes which the learner must possess acquired through formal, non-formal or information learning.	National Skill Qualification Framework (NSQF) caters to all categories of people including Minorities within the skilling ecosystem. And as per the Standard Operating Procedure of NSQF alignment process, the Qualification are validated by Industry prior to approval. The total number of NSQF aligned Qualifications is 3627.
