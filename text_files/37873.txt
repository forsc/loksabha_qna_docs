                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

454






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Funds for Green India Mission




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the cost-wise details of the works carried out in Balaghat and Seoni district coming under Balaghat Parliamentary Constituency under the Green IndiaMission during the last three years;(b) the details of the agencies or Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs) through which the said workshave been carried out;(c) whether training equipments are provided to theunemployed youths under the said Mission; and(d) if so, the details of the unemployed people in Balaghat and Seoni district under Balaghat Parliamentary Constituency who have been given training equipments under the said mission along with the cost of the training equipments?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BHUPENDER YADAV)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.***STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARAS (a) TO (d) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 454 DUE FOR REPLY ON 04.04.2022 REGARDING ‘FUNDS FOR GREEN INDIA MISSION’ RAISED BY DR. DHAL SINGH BISEN, HON’BLE MEMBERS OF PARLIAMENT(a) & (b) The Madhya Pradesh State Forest Development Agency has carried out the GIM activities which include advance&creation works, maintenance,distribution of alternate fuel energy devices and training programs with the financial implication of Rs 651.00 Lakh during the last three years in Balaghat and Seoni Districts. The details of work carried out during last three years is placed in Annexure-1.The NGOs were involved only in imparting trainings and their details are attached at Annexure-II.(c) &(d) The 12 training programs have been organized in the Balaghat and Seoni district under GIM. The total 302 individuals have been trained on various livelihood activities with the financial implication of Rs. 18.10 Lakh. The details of the training programs are placed in Annexure-III.List of the trained unemployed youth are compiled by the respective division of the State.***
