                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

450






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Fertilizer Subsidy




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the subsidy given on urea based and non-urea fertilizers by the Government as on date; (b)	whether the Government proposes to decrease the subsidy given on Non-urea fertilizers by twenty per cent and if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether any assessment has been done regarding the likely impact on the economic condition of the farmers by decreasing the subsidy given on non-urea fertilizers; and (d)	if so, the details thereof and the proposed corrective measures likely to be taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a) The details of the subsidy given to Urea & Non Urea fertilizer during the current year may be seen at Annexure I.(b) No, Madam. No proposal to decrease the subsidy on Non-Urea fertilizers has been given by the Government.(c)& (d) Do not arise in view of (b) above.*******
