                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2316






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Selling of  Essential Commodities




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that the number of online companies which are selling essential commodities have increased recently; (b) 	if so, whether the Government has given licence to those companies to sell essential commodities including provisions, medicines etc.; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof and the total number of licenses issued by the Government for the last five years and the essential commodities which are allowed to be sold online?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI DANVE RAOSAHEB DADARAO)(a)  : 	No such information is available with the Government;(b)  : 	The question does not arise. However, it is stated that the licence to companies to sell a commodity including essential commodities like medicines etc is given by the State Governments; (c)   :	The question does not arise. *****
