                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3506






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






National Programme for Dairy Development




Sunil Kumar Singh













                                                    Raksha Nikhil Khadse
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes sanctioning grants for establishing enhanced milk chilling facility, milk processing & marketing, bench mark survey, planning and monitoring cost under the National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD) scheme; (b)	if so, the number of project proposals received and sanctioned so far, State-wise; (c)	the progress achieved in this regard till date; (d)	the amount of funds released under the National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD) to Jharkhand, district-wise; (e)	whether there is any pending amount that has not been released yet; and (f)	if so, the details thereof, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (DR.SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	Yes Sir.(b) & (c) Department of Animal Husbandry & Dairying has received 156 proposals from various State Governments under National Programme for Diary Development (NPDD). As on 03.12.2019, this Department has approved 129 new projects in 28 States and 2 UTs with total outlay of Rs.1535.17 crore (Central Assistance Rs.1249.36 crore) during 2014-15 to 2019-20. A statement of indicating details of project received and sanctioned under NPDD scheme during 2014-15 to 2019-20 is Annexed.(d)	Two projects have been approved under NPDD scheme in Jharkhand. The details are as under:-(Rs. in crore)Project No.	Districts	Year of approval	Total Outlay	Central Share	Funds released	Funds Utilised	Unspent Balance1.	Latehar, Lohardaga, Palamu	2016-17	12.72	9.44	3.08	1.61	1.472.	Deoghar, Koderma and Latehar	2019-20	8.22	8.22	4.11	0.00	4.11	Total		20.94	17.66	7.19	1.61	5.58District-wise releases are not made under the scheme.(e)	No Sir. The implementing agency viz. Jharkhand State Cooperative Milk Producers'' Federation Ltd. has an unspent balance of Rs.5.58 crore available with them.(f)	Does not arise****
