                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1702






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Construction of Stadiums in Rural Areas




Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                








                                                    Devji Mansingram Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes any schemes and projects to consturct stadiums and promote sports activities in the rural areas of Maharashtra, Gujarat and Rajasthan including Latur, Jalore-Sirohi and Amreli; and(b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) to (b) ‘Sports’ being a State subject, the responsibility of constructing stadiums rests with the State / Union Territory Governments. The Union Government supplements their efforts. The ‘Khelo India – National Programme for Development of Sports’ was launched in 2016 with the aim to strengthen the entire sports ecosystem to promote the twin objectives of mass participation and promotion of excellence in sports across the country. It focuses on broad-basing of sports amongst the youth of the country and promotion of sports activities in various parts of the country, including rural areas of Maharashtra, Gujarat and Rajasthan. One of the components of the Scheme, “Promotion of rural and indigenous/tribal games” is specifically dedicated to rural sector of the country. The number of sports infrastructure projects sanctioned in the above three States under Khelo India Scheme so far is as under: -<pre>S. No.	Name of State	Number of sports infrastructure projects sanctioned under Khelo India Scheme1	Maharashtra	112	Gujarat	043	Rajasthan	40   </pre>****
