                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1154






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Payment of GST Compensation




Y. S. Avinash Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the attention of the Government is drawn to the matter of comments made by the Attorney General stating that it is not mandatory for the Government to pay shortfall in Goods and Services Tax (GST) compensation of the States;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has any plan of stopping the payment of GST compensation to the states taking the above statement; and(d) if so, the stand of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF FINANCE(Shri Anurag Singh Thakur)(a) & (b):- AG’s opinion has been obtained about payment of GST compensation to states in the event of shortage of compensation cess collection. Brief details of the AG’s opinion are as per Annexure.(c) & (d):- No Sir, GST compensation is being continued to be released as per the provisions of GST (Compensation to States) Act, 2017 out of the balance in the Compensation Fund.
