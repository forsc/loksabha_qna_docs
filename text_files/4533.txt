                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4157






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Drinking Water Problem in Bundelkhand




Vishnu Dutt Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the effective measures taken by the Government in Bundelkhand under the National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP); (b) whether the Government proposes to provide special assistance to rural areas of Bundelkhand with a view to tackle the drinking water problem;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) the financial assistance provided for different areas in Bundelkhand including Madhya Pradesh under the NRDWP along with the effective steps taken to address the problem of drinking water during the last one year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FORJAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c) Water is a State subject. Under National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP), this Ministry provides technical & financial assistance to the states to improve the coverage of safe drinking water in rural areas. No special assistance is provided under the programme.
