                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

615






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Gender Gap in Administration of COVID-19 Vaccination




Dibyendu Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government is aware that as on December, 2021, only 42 percent women are fully vaccinated and 46 per cent are partially vaccinated in the country; and(b)	If so the steps being taken by the Government to address the gender gap in administration of Covid-19 vaccines?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR) (a) and (b) As on 31st January 2022, around 48.8% of the total vaccine doses have been administered to women which approximates to the estimated proportion of women in the country’s population which is 48.5% as per Census 2011. Hence, there is no gender gap in administration of COVID-19 vaccines.****
