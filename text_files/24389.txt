                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

232






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Procurement of Paddy and other crops




Jayanta Kumar Roy













                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the quantum of paddy and other crops procured by the various procurement agencies including the number of procurement centres set up by these agencies in the country during the last two years and the current year, year-wise, agency-wise and district-wise including Odisha; (b) 	whether complaints have been received regarding the exploitation of farmers by the middleman and selling produce below MSP during the procurement of wheat and paddy in various States including Odisha; (c) 	if so, the details thereof and the corrective steps taken in this regard; (d) 	whether there is need for change in procurement policy to avoid distress selling by farmers; and(e) 	if so, the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	The quantum and procurement centre for last two years and the current year are as under: I. Paddy at Annexure-I  II. Wheat at Annexure-II  III. Coarse grain at Annexure-IIIIV- Pulses, Oils Seeds and Copra at Annexure-IVV. Jute & Cotton at Annexure-V. (b) and (c): The details of complaints received and action taken is at Annexure-VI.    (d) and (e): 	Government of India has formulated procurement policy to ensure that farmers get remunerative prices for their produce and do not have to resort to distress sale. However, if any producer/ farmers get better price in comparison to MSP, he/she is free to sell his/her produce in open market.   ***
