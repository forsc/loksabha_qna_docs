                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1365






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






Revival of Spinning Mills




Keshari Devi Patel














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering to revive the Monaima and Meja spinning mills in Praygraj that Mau Aima have remained closed since many years; (b) if so, the time by which there are likely to be revived; (c) if not, the reasons therefor; (d) whether such mills are likely to be included in the schemes that are being run for the welfare of weavers in Uttar Pradesh; (e) if so, the time by which these are likely to be included; and (f) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) to (f):	As per the information of Office of Textile Commissioner, Ministry of Textiles, Mumbai, Mau-Aima Sahkari Katai Mills Ltd., Mau-Aima, Prayagraj is closed with effect from 10.08.2010 and U.P. State Yarn Company Ltd., Meza, Prayagraj is closed with effect from 01.11.1998. ***
