                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

195






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.09.2020






Free Gas Connection under PMGKY




Nihal Chand Chauhan













                                                    Jaskaur Meena
                                                








                                                    V. Kalanidhi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the salient features of the Pradhan Mantri Ujjawala Scheme;(b) the details of the number of beneficiaries of this scheme in Tamil Nadu andRajasthan especially in Dausa and Ganganagar district;(c) the details of free gas connection provided in Tamil Nadu and Rajasthan especiallyin rural areas under the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana (PMGKY);(d) the details of number of gas connection made available free of cost to the personsbelonging to the Scheduled Tribe community in Dausa district and Scheduled Castecategory in Ganganagar district of Rajasthan under PMGKY;(e) whether the Government has transferred direct cash in the accounts of thebeneficiaries under the said scheme during the last few months, if so, the detailsthereof;(f) whether the Government proposes to extend the scheme for some more months inview of the COVID 19 crisis within the country, if so, the details thereof and if not, thereasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) was started on 01.05.2016 and the targetof the Scheme has been achieved in September, 2019. The Salient features of thePMUY Scheme were as under :-(i) LPG connection was released in the name of adult woman of the poor family,subject to the condition that no LPG connection exists in the name of any familymember of the household and fulfilling other terms and conditions.(ii) Initially, the beneficiaries under PMUY were identified either from SECC list orfrom seven other identified categories. Subsequently, Government extended the benefitto all left out poor families under PMUY subject to fulfilment of terms & conditions.(iii) Under the Scheme, cash assistance upto Rs 1600/- for providing new LPGconnection is provided by the Central Government.(iv) The beneficiary bore the cost of Hot Plate and purchase of first refill. Thebeneficiary was given the option to take Hot Plate or purchase first refill or the both onloan basis from OMCs at zero interest.(b) PMUY beneficiaries in the State of Tamil Nadu are 32.42 lakh and PMUYbeneficiaries in the districts of Dausa and Ganganagar District of Rajasthan are 2.16lakh and 1.7 lakh respectively.(c) & (d) No fresh LPG connections were mandated to be released under PradhanMantri Garib Kalyan Yojana (PMGKY)(e) Yes. OMCs have transferred an amount of Rs. 9670.41 crore to PMUYbeneficiaries under PMGKY for buying LPG refills.(f) Under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package, a scheme for providing free of costLPG cylinders for three months to PMUY beneficiaries was initiated w.e.f. 01.04.2020.The scheme has since been extended till 30th September for those beneficiaries whohave been credited with the advance for buying refills, but have not been able to buy thefree cylinders till 30th June 2020.
