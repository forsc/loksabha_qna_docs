                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1280






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Museums and Memorials on Veer Savarkar




Vishnu Dutt Sharma













                                                    Arvind Kumar Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether is it true that museums or memorials on Veer Savarkar and other forgotten heroes of our freedom fight, national movement and nationhood are either very few or non-existent; and(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the steps taken by the Government to address this issue?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c):	No Sir, There are several museums or memorials that are there in the country on the  forgotten heroes of our freedom national movement and nationhood. Some of the museums/memorials set up by the Government are:1.	1857 - India’s First War of Independence2.	Yaad-e-Jallian  3.	Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose and Indian National Army4.	Azaadi-ke-Diwane5.	1857 Residency Museum, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh6.	Nirbhik Subhash7.	Museum on Birsa Munda, Ranchi, Jharkhand8.	Jallianwala Bagh National Memorial, Amritsar, PunjabMinistry of Culture operates a Museum Grant Scheme under which financial assistance is provided to State Governments, Voluntary Institutions, Societies and Trusts registered as a society under the Indian Societies Act of 1860 (XXI) or a similar legislation of the state governments for setting up of new museums and development of existing museums. A list of museums/memorials setup through grant-in-aid under Museum Grant Scheme by the Ministry is as follows:-1.	INA Museum, Imphal, Manipur2.	Netaji Birth Place, Cuttack, Orissa3.	Pandit G.B. Pant Folk Art Museum, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh4.	Museum by Netaji Research Bureau, West Bengal5.	Freedom Fighters Museum by Office of Cantonment Board, Shahjahanpur, Uttar Pradesh The Biplobi Bharat gallery being set up at Victoria Memorial Hall, Kolkata includes a section on Veer Savarkar. Ministry of Culture has also constructed a cultural center in the memory of Rani Gaidinliu, famous freedom fighter from Nagaland at District Peren in Nagaland under her Centenary Commemoration celebrated during 2015-16.  *****
