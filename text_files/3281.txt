                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3030






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Sale of Art Collection




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Air India is considering to sell its massive art collections of more than five decades and if so, the details and the objectives thereof; and(b) whether these priceless art collections were dumped in a store room for the last two years causing damages to them and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a):  No, Sir. ;;(b): No Sir. The  art collection in Air India Building, Nariman Point has been properly inventorised and stored in the storage areas created in Air India Building. These storage areas are under CCTV surveillance round the clock by Air India security.
