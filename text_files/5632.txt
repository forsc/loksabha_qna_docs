                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5126






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Inducting Pharma D Degree Holders in Defence




Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government plans to induct the person having Degree of Doctor of Pharmacy in Defence Services including in DRDO laboratories and armed forces hospitals which may become part of Army Medical Corps along with the detailed plan to utilize their educational competence for defence services;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Armed Forces Medical College (AFMC) which is a tri-service organisation intend to create job opportunities in terms of faculty for Pharma D degree holders in the field of imparting education in pharmacy; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the action taken so far in this regard ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) & (b):  There is no proposal at present to induct persons having degree of Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharma D) in Armed Forces Hospitals/Army Medical Corps/DRDO.(c) & (d):  The following posts have been created for Pharma D degree holders in Armed Forces Medical College (AFMC), Pune:	i)  Professor			- 01 Posts	ii) Associate Professor	- 01 Posts 	iii) Assistant Professor	- 06 Posts*****
