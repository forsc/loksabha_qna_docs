                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

372






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Distance-based Criteria for Toll Plazas on NHs




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande













                                                    Mahabali Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether  the  National  Highways  Fee (Determination  of  Rates  and  Collection)  Rules,  2008 have been amended  for enabling/allowing  setting up of another toll plaza within a distance of forty kilometres on National Highways (NHs);(b)  whether  the  Government  has  allowed concessionaire to collect more money as a result thereof;(c)  if  so,  the  reaction  of  the  Government  in  this regard;(d) the corrective steps taken by the Government in this  regard;  and(e)  the  details  of  additional  toll  plazas  currently operating  across  the  country  within  a  distance  of  forty kilometres of a toll plaza, location-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (e)  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.
