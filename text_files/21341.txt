                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2653






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Development and Upgradation of the Industrial Corridors




Girish Bhalchandra Bapat














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the development and upgradation of the Industrial Corridors in Maharashtra and neighboring States;(b) 	the details of investment, land acquisition, funds allocated and released for the development and up gradation of the said Corridors;(c) 	whether all the relevant approvals have been received from the neighboring States of Maharashtra; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) to (d):  	Two Industrial Corridor projects, Delhi Mumbai Industrial Corridor  (DMIC) and Bengaluru Mumbai Industrial Corridor (BMIC) are being implemented  in the state of Maharashtra and neighbouring states i.e Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh and Karnataka. (i) Delhi Mumbai Industrial Corridor ProjectProject development work for Shendra-Bidkin Industrial area (40 sq. kms) in Maharashtra is initiated. The project development work in Bidkin (Phase -1 comprising 10 sq. kms) is at final stage of completion. State Govt. has transferred 32.55 sq. kms land and Rs. 3,000 crore has been released by National Industrial Corridor Development and Implementation Trust (NICDIT) as matching equity for development of trunk infrastructure. Shendra Industrial Area (8.45 sq. km) was dedicated to the nation by Hon’ble Prime Minister in September, 2019.  Till date 78 plots admeasuring 236 acres have been allotted to private companies in Shendra industrial region/node and the likely Investment is around Rs. 8,000 crore.  Project development activities have been initiated for Dighi Port Industrial Area (2,402 Ha), the second node/industrial region in the State of Maharashtra.  Project development activities initiated for two industrial regions/ nodes namely  Dholera Special Investment Region (DSIR) in Gujarat and Integrated Industrial Township-Vikram Udyogpuri (IITVU) at Ujjain in Madhya Pradesh.  For DSIR, State Government of Gujarat has transferred 44.26 sq.kms to the Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) and matching equity amounting to Rs.2551.94 crore has been released by NICDIT.  For IITVU, State Government of Madhya Pradesh has transferred 1100 acres land to the SPV and matching equity amounting to Rs. 55.93 crore and debt of Rs. 260.54 crore has also been released by NICDIT.(ii)	Bengaluru Mumbai Industrial Corridor (BMIC)Satara in Maharashtra and Dharwad in Karnataka has been identified for undertaking project development activities under BMIC.*********
