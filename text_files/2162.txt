                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1944






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Linking of SIM Cards with Aadhaar Number




M. Selvaraj













                                                    Su Thirunavukkarasar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:(a) whether the Union Government has issued directions and made it mandatory for all mobile users in the country to link Aadhaar number with mobile SIM cards; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the reason and purpose to link Aadhaar number with mobile SIM cards; and (d) the total number of mobile SIMs are linked with Aadhaar number as on date, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ) to (d):  No, Sir.   No directions were issued by Department of Telecommunications for making mandatory for all mobile users in the Country to link Aadhaar number with mobile SIM cards. However, the Aadhaar based E-KYC (Electronic-Know Your Customer) process was adopted as an alternative mechanism of KYC for issuance of new mobile connections on 16.08.2016 and a method of re-verification of existing mobile subscribers on 23.03.2017.Subsequently, in pursuance of Hon’ble Supreme Court’s judgment & order dated 26.09.2018 in W.P. (C) No. 494 of 2012 in the matter of Justice K.S. Puttaswamy (Rtd.) & Anr. Vs UoI & Ors., the Department of Telecommunications, vide its order dated 26.10.2018 (copy available at the link:  http://dot.gov.in/sites/default/files/Discontinuation%20of%20E-KYC%20-%20Order%20dated%2026.10.2018.PDF), had discontinued the use of Aadhaar based E-KYC service of UIDAI for issuing new mobile connections to subscribers and re-verification of existing mobile subscribers.  However, subsequently vide  an  ordinance, it has been allowed  on a voluntary basis to be used.*******
