                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1468






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Vande Bharat Express




Arvind Dharmapuri














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to expand the network of Vande Bharat Express; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the plan of the Government to operate new routes with respect to this train and other semi-high speed trains in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (c) To prevent the spread of COVID-19 pandemic, Indian Railways (IR) have discontinued all regular passengers carrying trains w.e.f. 23rd March,2020. Presently, only special trains, with limited stoppages, keeping in view the concerns and suggestions of State Governments are being operated. Prior to outbreak of the pandemic, 02 pairs of Vande Bharat Express were in operation as detailed below:-i.   22435/22436 New Delhi- Varanasi Vande Bharat Expressii. 22439/22440 New Delhi-Shri Mata Vaishno Devi Katra Vande Bharat Express Introduction of  new trains services, over IR,  is an on-going process and all available technologies are used for new trains. *****
