                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

544






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Doubling of YeshwantpurChannasandra Railway Line Project




P. C. Mohan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of doubling of Yeshwantpur-Channasandra railway line project in Bengaluru and the time by which the project is likely to be completed; and(b)	the details and present status of the 48 kilometre Baiyappanahalli-Hosur track work?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY   (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a)	The doubling work of Yeshwantpur-Channasandra railway line project with electrification for a length of 21.7 Km has been sanctioned at a cost of Rs ?330 Crores. The work is assigned to Rail Infrastructure Company (Karnataka) Limited (K-RIDE), a Joint Venture Company of Government of Karnataka and Ministry of Railways for execution on 09.09.2019. K-RIDE is gearing up to take up this project. The estimated time line for completion of this project is 2-3 years from now.(b)	The doubling work of Baiyappanahalli-Hosur railway line project with electrification for a length of 48 Km has been sanctioned at a cost of ? 449 Crores. The work is assigned to Rail Infrastructure Company (Karnataka) Limited (K-RIDE), a Joint Venture Company of Government of Karnataka and Ministry of Railways for execution on 09.09.2019. K-RIDE is gearing up to take up this project.  *****
