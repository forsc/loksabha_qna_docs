                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

236






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Livelihoods in Full Employment in MGNREGA




Thomas Chazhikadan














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of implementation of the “Livelihoods in Full Employment” project under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA);(b) 	the number of individuals who are undergoing/have completed skill development training under LIFE-MGNREGA, the details thereof, State-wise; and(c) 	the reasons for the slow progress of LIFE-MGNREGA project?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): The Project Livelihoods in Full Employment under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (LIFE-Mahatma Gandhi NREGS), aimed at promoting self-reliance and improving the skill-base of the Mahatma Gandhi NREGS workers, thereby improving their livelihoods so that they can move from partial employment to full employment status. The project, targets Mahatma Gandhi NREGS worker who had completed 100 days of work under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS. There are three broad categories of skilling identified under the project:(i)	Skilling for wages [This pertains to Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY)](ii)	Livelihood Upgradation(iii)	Skilling for self-employment [This pertains to Rural Self Employment Training Institutes (RSETIs)]Rs. 7000.00 Lakh has been released for facilitating conduct of capacity building under Skilling for Wages component of Project LIFE-Mahatma Gandhi NREGA for 12 States to be met out of Annual plan for Capacity building (Capacity building, PRI and Planning) under Mahatma Gandhi NREGA for the FY 2015-16.   Rs. 11983.74 lakh has been released to 6 States under skilling for wages component under project LIFE- MGNREGA for FY 2016-17. Details in this regard is at Annexure -I(b): State/UT-wise details of Project Livelihoods in Full Employment (Pro-Life) under DDU-GKY and RSETIs are given at Annexure-II.(c):	The reasons for slow progress of LIFE-Mahatma Gandhi NREGS in DDU-GKY & RSETI are given below:-(i)	There was no provision for compensation of wage loss for Mahatma Gandhi NREGA workers undergoing training.(ii)	There was no formal inbuilt mechanism for monitoring of implementation of the project in the guidelines.(iii)	Lack of proper mobilisation of eligible candidates for skilling training programme.(iv)	The project was providing training cost reimbursement for only placed candidates.
