                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

164






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Housing for CRPF




Kirit Premjibhai Solanki














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plans to increase the housing satisfaction of Central Reserve Police Forces (CRPF) personnel;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether new accommodations are being constructed for CRPF; and(d) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a)  to (d)   Yes Sir.  Upon completion of 7774 family quarters presently under construction at various locations, the housing satisfaction level of Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) personnel, would further enhance.  State/UT-wise details are given in Annexure.*****
