                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

49






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Free Coaching Facility to Minority Students




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is providing free coaching facilities to the students belonging to minority communities across the country including Bihar;(b) 	if so, the number of coaching centres where coaching is provided to such students; and(c) 	the amount allocated by the Government to the coaching centres for this purpose during each of the last three years and the current year ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)  to (c):  A statement is laid on the table of the House.
