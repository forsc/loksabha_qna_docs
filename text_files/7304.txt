                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

988






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Integrated Skill Development Scheme in the Textile Sector




Prathap Simha














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any workshop on Integrated Skill Development Scheme in the textile sector was organised recently; (b)	if so, the objectives thereof along with the details of participating industries and institutions in the aforesaid workshop; (c)	whether the participation of the various State Governments has been encouraging in the promotion of various schemes meant for the development of skills in the sector; (d)	if so, the details thereof, district and State-wise; and (e)	the other steps being taken for the participation of the State Governments in the overall growth of the sector?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b): A stakeholder meeting was organised under Samarth-Scheme for Capacity Building in Textiles sector (SCBTS) on 14.05.2018 to familiarize the stakeholders about the scheme and its guidelines. In addition regular interactive meetings are taking place at regular intervals with stakeholders in the Ministry on Samarth Scheme Implementation.(c) & (d): Under Samarth-Scheme for Capacity Building in Textiles sector(SCBTS),so far a total of 21 State Government agencies involving 18 States have agreed to partner with the Ministry under the scheme. The Ministry has accorded in-principle approval for allocation of over 3.5 lakh targets to the agencies nominated by State Governments to undertake training programme under the scheme. Details of State government agencies and allocated targets are given in Annexure.(e):  In addition to the Samarth Scheme, State Governments are also associated in the implementation of various other schemes/projects of Ministry of Textiles viz. Scheme for Integrated Scheme for Textile Park (SITP), Integrated Processing Development Scheme(IPDS), Scheme for Promoting usage of Geotechnical Textiles in North Eastern Region, In-situ Upgradation of Plain Powerlooms, National Handloom Development Programme, Comprehensive Handloom Cluster Development Scheme, Base line Survey and Mobilisation of Artisans (AHVY), Comprehensive Handicraft Cluster Development Scheme, Integrated Development and Promotion of Handicraft Projects (Special Projects), North East Region Textiles Promotion Scheme (NERTPS), Scheme for Promoting usage of Agro Textiles in North-eastern Region, Silk Samagra etc.
