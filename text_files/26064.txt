                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1745






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Khelo India State Centre of Excellence




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of funds allocated and utilised under Khelo India Scheme since 2019-20, State-wise; (b) the details of allocation and utilization of fund along with the progress of projects started in Andhra Pradesh under Khelo India scheme since 2019-20;(c) the details of delay in progress of projects started under Khelo India scheme in the said State along with the reasons therefor;(d) the State-wise details of applications received, selected under the component ‘Talent Search and Development’ of Khelo India Scheme since 2019-20 and the district-wise details for Andhra Pradesh;(e) whether any sports facility in Srikakulam was proposed by Andhra Pradesh for Khelo India State Centre of Excellence (KISCE); and(f) the details of three facilities proposed by Andhra Pradesh for KISCE?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS { SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR }(a) Funds are allocated by the Union Government Scheme-wise, not State/UT-wise. However, State-wise details of funds released under Khelo India Scheme since 2019-20 are at Annexure – I.(b) Since 2019-20, three sports infrastructure projects have been sanctioned in the State of Andhra Pradesh under the Khelo India Scheme. The details of the same are at Annexure – II.(c) A total of 07 sports infrastructure projects have been sanctioned under the Khelo India Scheme in the State of Andhra Pradesh since its inception in 2016. Out of these, 04 projects were sanctioned in 2017 and 03 projects were sanctioned recently in 2021. Out of the 04 projects sanctioned in 2017, 03 are more than 75% complete and 01 could not be started due to land issues for which the grantee is refunding the amount. Out of 03 projects sanctioned in 2021, the work could not be started in any of these projects due to lockdown because of Covid-19 pandemic.(d) State-wise details of applications received and those selected under the ‘Talent Search and Development’ and ‘Support to National/Regional/State Academies’ verticals of Khelo India Scheme are at Annexure – III. 02 proposals were received from Krishna District in Judo and Table Tennis during the year 2019-20.(e) No, Sir.(f) The following are the three facilities proposed by Andhra Pradesh for KISCE: -•	Dr. YSR Sports School, Kadapa.•	District Sports Authority, Kakinada, East Godavari.•	A.C. Subba Reddy Sports Complex, SPSR Nellore.******
