                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

536






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Emergency Care and Recovery Centres in Government Medical Colleges




Manne Srinivas Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has taken any steps for expansion of facilities in Emergency Care and Recovery Centres in some Government Medical Colleges including 10 exclusive beds in each facility along with a team of psychiatrists and nurses to treat the mentally ill patients;(b)	if so, the details of such steps taken thereof State/UT-wise during the last five years; and(c)	the details of budget sanctioned/spent for this purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (c): Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS), the Government of India provides assistance for setting up of Government medical colleges. In addition, under the National Health Mission, financial assistance upto Rs. 83.20 lakhs is provided to districts out of flexible pool for Non-communicable diseases for early detection, management and treatment of mental disorders/ illnesses. The State/UT-wise details of funds approved under the flexible pool for Non-communicable diseases from 2016-17 to 2020-21 is placed at Annexure.
