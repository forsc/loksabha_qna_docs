Issues Faced by Anganwadi Kendras in Urban Areas




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether Anganwadi Kendras are permitted to be established in urban areas; if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether Anganwadi Kendras are being run in rental buildings in several urban areas leading to unnecessary excessive expenditure thereon, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(c)	whether effective implementation of Anganwadi services is being affected due to scarcity of space, if so, the details thereof; and (d)	the steps taken to improve the situation so that enough space may be made available and unnecessary expenditure incurred on rent may be avoided?






ANSWER
