                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3671






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






ESIC Hospitals




Sunil Kumar Soni














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the current status of under construction Employee State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) hospitals at Raipur and Korba in Chhattisgarh and the time by which the said hospitals are likely to be functional;(b)whether medical officers and para medical staff have been posted in both the said hospitals and if so, the number of staff posted;(c)the number of proposed beds in Raipur and Korba hospitals and the number of beds to be functional in initial stage; and(d)whether Dispensary Cum Branch Office has been implemented in Chhattisgarh and if so, the number of districts where it has been implemented including the number of offices at present?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a):	Construction of both the hospitals at Raipur & Korba has been completed.(b):	Two medical officers each are posted in these hospitals. Besides, 17 Medical Officers have been newly recruited for posting in these two hospitals. Further, Nursing and Paramedical Staff have been posted to Chhattisgarh region for these two hospitals, of which four have joined at ESIC Hospital, Raipur and two at ESIC Hospital, Korba. (c):	100 beds each have been sanctioned for ESIC Hospitals at Raipur and Korba. Further, at initial stage, each hospital is to be made functional with 50 beds.(d):	 Dispensary cum Branch Office (DCBO) has been approved in Chhattisgarh in nine districts viz. Raipur, Rajnandgaon, Korba, Durg, Raigarh, Dhamtari, Baloda Bazar, Janjgir-Champa and Bilaspur. At present one DCBO at Rajnandgaon district is functional.*******
