                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4443






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Rashtriya Vayoshri Yojana




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.4443 TO BE ANSWERED ON 23.03.2021RASHTRIYA VAYOSHRI YOJANA†4443. SHRI JASWANT SINGH BHABHOR:Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the main objectives of the Rashtriya Vayoshri Yojana;(b) whether senior citizens of the country are provided assistance through this yojana so that they can lead a dignified life; (c) if so, whether the senior citizens from the tribal districts of Gujarat are also being provided benefits under this scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b):  The objective of the scheme is to provide the Senior Citizens, suffering from any of the age related disability/infirmity, with assisted living devices which can restore near normalcy in their bodily functions, overcoming the disability/infirmity manifested such as low vision, hearing impairment, loss of teeth and loco-motor disabilities. The devices are provided to Senior Citizens belonging to BPL Category and Senior Citizens with the monthly income having not more than 15,000/- per Month.(c): The Scheme is being implemented nationwide.*****
