                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

293






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Export of COVID Vaccine




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the increase in the export of COVID vaccines to Omicron affected countries is proposed to be met from the unused stock of vaccines with private hospitals and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government is planning to ramp up the production of vaccines in the country to meet the export demand and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the increase in exports is likely to impact the domestic supply in the country; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a)	to (d) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. 293* FOR 17TH DECEMBER, 2021(a) to (d) Export of COVID-19 vaccines to other countries, including for Omicron affected countries, takes into account and prioritizes domestic production and requirements of National COVID-19 vaccination program. Indian companies have significantly enhanced their vaccine production capacity and are in a position to meet domestic requirements of COVID-19 vaccines as also contribute their surplus production to global Covid-19 vaccination requirements.*******
