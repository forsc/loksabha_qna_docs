                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3045






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Women Self Help Groups in Rajasthan




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Women Self Help Groups (SHGs) which are registered with the Government, State-wise data, down to district level for Rajasthan and village level for Jaipur;(b) 	whether the Government has conducted any educational or awareness drives for such women led self help groups to increase their business and operations knowledge and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the total disbursement of funds to schemes that make special provisions for women entrepreneurs from self help groups, State-wise, down to district level for Rajasthan; and(d) 	whether the Government has any proposal to initiate mentorship programmes for such SHGs in collaboration with Institutes of National Eminence and other Government universities?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI) (a) This Ministry is implementing Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM) across the country since 2011 in a mission mode which aims at organising rural poor women into Self Help Groups (SHGs) and facilitating them to access credit from Banks for taking up various livelihoods activities for enhancing their incomes. Women Self Help Groups promoted by Rajasthan Grameen Aajeevika Vikas Parishhd (Rajeevika) are not registered under any Act. There are a total of 2,51,603 Women Self Help Groups in Rajasthan under DAY-NRLM. The district wise number of women SHGs are given at Annexure-I. There are 7102 Women Self  Help Groups in Jaipur and village wise number of Women SHGs are given at Annexure-II.(b)  The Government of Rajasthan through Rajasthan Grameen Aajeevika Vikas Parishad has conducted various kinds of awareness programs for women led self-help groups to increase their business and operations.
