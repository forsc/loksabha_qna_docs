                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4007






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






National Data Sharing and Accessibility Policy




Srinivas Kesineni














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)     whether the National Data Sharing and Accessibility Policy is still in use and if so, the details thereof;(b)     whether any amendment to the said policy is proposed to be released, if so, the details thereof;(c)     whether any Ministry/Department is yet to release its data, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(d)     whether timely update of the data by the Ministry/ Department takes place; and(e)     if so, the details thereof including the mechanisms in place to keep a check on it?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE; MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY; AND MINISTER OF EARTH SCIENCES(DR. HARSH VARDHAN) ????????? ?? ?????? ?????? ??????, ??????? ?? ???????????? ?????? ?? ?????? ??????? ????????. ???? ????? (a)     Yes, the National Data Sharing and Accessibility Policy (NDSAP) notified on 17th March 2012 is still in use and is under implementation since October 2012. (b)     There is no such proposal to amend the National Data Sharing and Accessibility Policy (NDSAP) at this stage. (c) A total of 86 Ministries/Departments/Organizations of Government of India are contributing datasets to the Open Government Data Platform established under the Policy. (d)     Datasets are being updated on a daily/weekly/monthly/yearly basis as per the frequency decided and set by the respective Chief Data Officers (CDOs) of the concerned Ministries/ Departments/ Organizations.(e)    In line with the provisions of the implementations guidelines for the Policy, the CDOs from the respective Ministries/ Departments/ Organisations are responsible for identifying, cleaning, publishing and updating datasets on a pro-active and voluntary basis.*****
