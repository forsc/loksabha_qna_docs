                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

718






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Education Budget




Pushpendra Singh Chandel














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to enhance the budget for education;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government proposes to spend more funds on conducting research on various subjects including science in the Universities; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) to (d) The Ministry of Education gives Grant in aid to various Higher Educational Institutions involved in teaching and research activities. The budget allocation of the Ministry for the financial year 2022-23 has been enhanced from the previous year 2021-22. The details are as under:(Rs. In Crore)<pre>S.No.	Department	             BE  (2021-22)        BE (2022-23)  Enhanced Allocation1. Department of Higher Education	        38350.65	40828.35	2477.702. Department of School Education and Literacy	54873.66	63449.37	8575.71    Total	                                 93224.31	104277.72	11053.41</pre>***
