                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1082






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Smart City Project in Karnataka




P. C. Mohan














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the funds released for all smart cities in Karnataka especially Bengaluru so far; (b)	whether the Government has taken note that only 1% of funds are utilised for implementing smart city projects in Karnataka;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and (d)	whether the Government has issued/proposes to issue any timeline for implementation of the project and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                   (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a): Funds released by Government of India (GOI) for Smart Cities in Karnataka, including Bengaluru, are given below :-Smart City	    Round of Selection by      Release by GoI                    Government of India       (Rs. in Crore)Belagavi	    1  (January, 2016)	        196Davanegere	    1  (January, 2016)	        196Hubballi- Dharwad   2  (September, 2016)	196Mangaluru	    2  (September, 2016)	117Shivamogga	    2  (September, 2016)	196Tumakuru	    2  (September, 2016)	196Bengaluru	    3  (June, 2017) 	         58                                   Total       1155(b & c):  A total utilization of Rs.190.21 crore (16.46%) against total release by GoI i.e. Rs.1,155 crore has been reported by Smart Cities from Karnataka, till now.(d): The timeline for completion of projects by Smart Cities is five years from the date of their selection as Smart Cities.                               *******
