Revenue from Central Excise Duty




Rahul Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of revenue from central excise duty collected on petrol, high speed diesel oil and aviation turbine fuel for the last three years; (b) the details of the estimated revenue gain to the Central Government on account of withdrawal of subsidy on domestic LPG cylinders for the current financial year; (c) the details of crude oil imported by PSUs and the average imported crude oil price for the last three years; and (d) the details of the increase in central excise duty and cess on petrol and high speed diesel oil from January 1, 2020 to July 31, 2020?
