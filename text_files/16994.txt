                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

607






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Covid-19 Cases in Defence Services




Anumula Revanth Reddy













                                                    Jugal Kishore Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the number of COVID-19 cases separately in Army, Navy and Air Force;(b) the details of the number of deceased due to COVID- 19 in all the three services, service-wise;(c) whether any special compensation has been given to the family of those deceased due to COVID-19;(d) if so, the details thereof; service-wise; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a)  The number of COVID-19 cases in Army, Navy and Air Force are 16758, 1365 and 1716 respectively.(b)  The number of deceased due to COVID-19 in Army and Air Force are 32 and 03 respectively and ‘NIL’ in case of Navy.(c) to (e):  As per the extant Rules, there is no special compensation for deaths occurring due to infectious diseases while in service.  However, all deaths while in service are awarded terminal benefits.*****
