                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1864






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Ayurvedic Medicine to Boost Immunity




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan













                                                    Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh
                                                








                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government is aware that the studies conducted on certain Ayurvedic medicines as immunity boosters in Kerala have drawn international attention;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether it is a fact that many nations have included these findings in their respective health programmes; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a)  & (b):	Ministry of AYUSH has conducted studies on Ayurvedic medicines as immunity boosters all over the country including Kerala.  Total 109 COVID -19 related studies have been conducted at various locations all over the country, out of which 60 studies have been conducted on Ayurvedic medicines with promising results.  As a result, as per the available data, there has been 23.6 %   growth in the export of Ayurvedic products world over during the period of April – December 2020- 2021 in comparison to period of April – December 2019 –20. (c)  & (d):	There is no such information available.
