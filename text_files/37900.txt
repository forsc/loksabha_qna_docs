                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4606






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Pre and Post Matric Scholarships




Mohammad Jawed













                                                    D. Ravikumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of minority students who have applied for Pre and Post Matric Scholarships in 2019-20 and 2020-21 and the number of girls out of them;(b) the number of girls who applied and received the scholarship; and(c) the details of total expenditure on scholarships for Muslims during the last three years, year-wise?	
                                                





ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a) & (b): In 2019-20 and 2020-21, 1,54,08,111 States/UTs verified applications from the notified minority community were received out of which 81,90,978 were from girls under Pre-Matric and Post Matric scholarship schemes and 64,66,444 girls were sanctioned scholarship.(c): The details of year-wise total expenditure on Scholarships sanctioned to the beneficiaries from Muslim community under the 3 Scholarship Schemes namely, Pre-Matric, Post Matric and Merit-cum-Means based Scholarship Scheme during 2018-19 to 2020-21 is as follows:Financial Year	Amount (in Rs. Crore)2018-19	1575.562019-20	1747.132020-21	1473.95Total	4796.64*****
