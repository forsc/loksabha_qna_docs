                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

304






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Skill Development Initiative Scheme




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of Skill Development Initiative Scheme (Seekho Aur Kamao) initiative in the State of Assam;(b)	the number and names of Project Implementing Agencies engaged with the aforesaid scheme in Assam, district-wise; and(c)	the details of the number of youths trained so far under the scheme; and(d)	whether there is any mechanism to know the number of trained youth who have been absorbed in employment or entrepreneurial venture and if so, the details thereof especially in Barpeta and Bongaigaon districts?
                                                





ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	to (d)  A statement is laid on the table of the House.
