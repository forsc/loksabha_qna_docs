                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3452






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Industrial Hemp




T.R.V.S. Ramesh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether in view of the fact that industrial hemp is not-toxic plant but belong to “Hemp Plants Family”, the Ministry is taking any steps to remove myth from minds of State Governments so that coordination in the production of the plant can start; (b)	the number of States that have so far brought out a policy for coordination in the production of industrial hemp; and(c) 	the quantum of industrial hemp plants and products exported from the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	As per Section 8 of Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances (NDPS) Act, 1985 prohibits cultivation of cannabis plant (hemp) except for medical or scientific purposes.  Section 10 of the NDPS Act, subject to the provisions of Section 8 empowers the State Governments to permit and regulate the cultivation of any cannabis plant, production, manufacture, possession, transport, import inter-state, export inter-state, sale, purchase consumption or use of cannabis (excluding Charas).  Special provision has also been laid down relating to cannabis in Section 14 of the NDPS Act wherein the act empowers the Government to, by general or special order, permit cultivation of cannabis exclusively for horticultural and industrial purposes.  Besides, National Policy on Narcotic Drugs framed in consultation with State Governments also provides provision for cultivation of Cannabis for industrial and horticulture purposes.   As per available information Government of Uttarakhand has regulated hemp cultivation in the State.(c):	Export of Hemp during the last two years and current year (upto September 2019) is as under:-ITCHS Code	Description	Value in US$		2017-18	2018-19	2019-20530210	True Hemp Raw or Retted	1213	407	0530820	True Hemp Yarn	71	2808	40Total---?	1284	3215	40Source:- Directorate General of Commercial Intelligence and Statistics (DGCIS),      Kolkata.********
