                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3962






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Stoppage at Sandila Railway Station




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received any representation from public representatives to provide stoppage of Punjab Mail (up-down) and Kisan Express (up-down) at Sandila railway station, a very important railway station, in Hardoi district of Uttar Pradesh; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the action taken or being taken by the Government thereon?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a) to (c)  Representations have been received for provision of stoppage of trains at Sandila. Stoppage of additional trains at Sandila station, which is already catered to by 10 pairs of Mail/Express trains and 4 pairs of Passenger trains, has not been found operationally feasible and commercially justified, at present.                                                *****
