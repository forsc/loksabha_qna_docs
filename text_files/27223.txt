                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2899






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Maritime Heritage Museum




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to develop world class museums dedicated to the legacy of maritime heritage of India; and(b)	if so, the details along with the locations thereof?	 






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b)  Yes, Sir. National Maritime Heritage Complex (NMHC) is proposed to be developed as a world class museum, dedicated to the legacy of maritime heritage of India, at Lothal, which is located at about 80km away from Ahmedabad, Gujarat. NMHC is to be a world class international tourist destination, the first of its kind in the country, comprising of maritime museum, light house museum, maritime theme parks, amusement parks centres, etc. It would showcase maritime heritage of the country from ancient to modern times by adopting an edutainment approach using the latest technology to spread awareness about India’s maritime heritage.                                       *******
