                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1849






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Swachh Bharat Mission in Rajasthan




Balak Nath













                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of works done under Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM) in all the districts of Rajasthan;(b) the amount of funds released and used under this campaign during the last three years;(c) the estimated number of people benefitted under SBM in Rajasthan, district-wise; and(d) the district-wise names of agencies through which amount released under the SBM has been spent in Rajasthan including Sikar district? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) District-wise number of Individual household latrines (IHHLs) constructed under Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen) [SBM(G)] in Rajasthan from 2.10.2014 to 18.9.2020, as per the data reported by the State on the online integrated management information system, are given at Annexure-1.(b) Details of funds released and utilised in the State of Rajasthan under SBM(G) during the last three years are as under:Financial Year	Funds released (Rs. in crore)	Funds utilised (Rs. in crore)2017-18	981.51	848.212018-19	865.88	350.192019-20	390.00	187.98(c) District-wise estimated number of people benefitted under SBM(G) in Rajasthan from 2.10.2014 to 18.9.2020 are given at Annexure-2. (d) Under SBM(G), funds are released to the State Government.  Further distribution/release of the same to the districts is done by the State.
