                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1370






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Updating NCERT Books




Ritesh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) allowed agap of 10 years between successive updates of textbooks (2007 and 2017);(b) if so, the reasons therefor;(c) whether the Government plans to reduce the time period between the successive updates ofNCERT textbooks;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) No, Sir. The National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) hasinformed that they have continuously been updating their textbooks and reprinting the updatedtextbooks every year by incorporating new priorities and initiatives occurred at the National level.The textbooks were also comprehensively reviewed in 2017-18. Further, NCERT has undertakenreview of its textbooks, under the initiative of rationalisation of curriculum in the year 2018-19.NCERT invited suggestions from different stakeholders for this purpose. On the basis of theanalysis of the suggestions received, NCERT has again reviewed, rationalized and published itsupdated textbooks in 2018-19.(c) to (e) NCERT has been updating its textbooks and reprinting the updated textbooks everyyear before the beginning of a new academic year. Therefore, there is no scope for furtherreduction of time period with regard to updation of NCERT textbooks.***
