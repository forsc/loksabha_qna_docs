                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

157






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Inter-Cultural Initiatives




Upendra Singh Rawat













                                                    Kaushal Kishore
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the  details of the Inter-Cultural initiatives taken by the Government with different countries of the world during the last three years;(b)	whether any tangible impact or positive outcome has been achieved by these interactions including help in promotion of Indian culture; and(c)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor ?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH  PATEL)(a) to (c):  A statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement referred to in reply to parts (a) to (c) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No.157 for 02.03.2020 regarding Inter-Cultural Initiatives.Ministry of Culture  signs bilateral Cultural Agreements (CAs) /Cultural Exchange Programme(CEPs)/Memorandum of Understanding(MoUs) with different countries having inter alia provisions  for  cooperation in the field of music, dance, theatre, archives, archaeology, libraries, museums, literature, exhibition, capacity building  etc.  The provisions of aforesaid agreements are implemented through different organizations/institutions. The list of CEPs signed during the last three years is enclosed at Annexure-I.To enhance India’s cultural outreach abroad, Ministry of Culture also implements a scheme for Promotion of International Cultural Relations.  This scheme has two components (i) Festival of India(FOI) (ii)  Grant in Aid  to Indo Foreign Friendship Societies.  The activities undertaken under the scheme include music, dance, theatre, food festivals, literary festivals, folk arts, yoga, exhibitions, lecture demonstration and workshops etc. The details  of FOIs and Grant in aid to Indo Foreign Friendship Cultural Societies  are enclosed at Annexure II.Indian Council for Cultural Relations(ICCR), an autonomous organization under Ministry of External Affairs also foster and strengthen cultural relations between India and other countries through its inter cultural initiatives. Details of major activities undertaken by ICCR are enclosed at Annexure III. Positive feedbacks/reports have been received from Indian Missions, Posts and diasporas on these cultural events organized abroad.  These events enhance awareness about Indian culture and India’s soft power diplomacy.  All these  inter cultural initiatives have been able to popularize and create interest about Indian art & culture and  foster closer friendship between India and other countries.   *****Annexure – IAnnexure  referred to in reply to parts (a) to (c) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No.*157 for 02.03.2020 regarding Inter-Cultural Initiatives.CEPs Signed during the last three years (2016-19)Sl.No	Name of the countries  	Signed on1.
