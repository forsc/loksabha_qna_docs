                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2315






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






National Bamboo Mission




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total land area brought under bamboo plantation in Odisha during the FY 2020-21 under the National Bamboo Mission; and(b)     the total land area to be covered by the government under the project in the state?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	The Restructured National Bamboo Mission (NBM) is operational from the Year 2018-19 for holistic development of the Bamboo value chain. As informed by the State Government, an area of 406 ha. has been brought  under bamboo plantation during the financial year 2020-21.(b)      The total area covered till now & to be covered under National Bamboo Mission is as under:Financial Year	Achievement (in ha.)2019-20	  53.002020-21	406.002021-22	500.00 (As per approved Action Plan for FY 2021-22)*****
