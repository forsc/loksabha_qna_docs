                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

336






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Loss of Lives in Avalanches




Sudheer Gupta













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether a number of soldiers and civilians have lost their lives in a massive avalanche in Siachen recently and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has provided assistance to the family members of the deceased and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether any precautionary steps have been taken to safeguard against such eventualities and if so, the details thereof;(d) the number of soldiers who lost their lives due to avalanches in the hilly States during each of the last three years and the current year; and(e) whether more than a decade long field testing and trials by Defence Research Development Organisation (DRDO) on Wearable Physiological Monitoring System (WPMS) to predict avalanches and protect the soldiers from the avalanches is not proving fruitful and if so, the present status of WPMS and the time by which it is likely to be developed and used for the benefit of soldiers of the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF DEFENCE                                    (SHRI RAJNATH SINGH) (a) to (e):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*******
