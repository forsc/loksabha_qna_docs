                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2742






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






IT Hub in Hyderabad




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state:(a)    whether the Government is considering to set up new Hubs for Information Technology (IT) in Hyderabad, Karnataka Region, which is accorded with special status under Article 371 of the Constitution, for the development of this region;(b)     if so, the details thereof; and (c)     the number of IT Hubs already existing in the State of Karnataka?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	and (b): A new Software Technology Parks of India (STPI) Centre is set-up based on a specific demand from respective State Government. Currently, no proposal has been received from the State Governments for establishment of Software Technology Park in Hyderabad, Karnataka Region. (c):  5 STPI centres at Bengaluru, Mysuru, Hubballi, Mangaluru & Manipal and 21 IT/ ITeS SEZ’s are operational in the State of Karnataka.*******
