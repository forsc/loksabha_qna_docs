                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2430






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Flights from Gondia Airport




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any steps to acquire 8.06 hectare land for construction of Khatia-Kamath road and Paraswada-Jilmil road so as to start early operation of flights from Gondia airport;(b) whether the Government has taken any steps to provide compensation to affected people at the market rate along with their rehabilitation besides construction of operational boundary wall of approach area on both sides of runway 22; and(c) if so, the details and the updated status thereof as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): The Airports Authority of India has projected a land requirement of 8.06 hectare to State Government of Maharashtra for diversion of road and construction of boundary wall. Compensation and rehabilitation grant  as per provision of Land Acquisition Act 2013 to  the affected families is to be disbursed by Land Acquisition Officer (LAO), Gondia. ;(c)  The process for selection of agency for conducting Social Impact Assessment, as per the provisions of Land Acquisition, Rehabilitation and Resettlement Act, 2013, has been initiated.
