                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2958






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Production of Hand Made Fabrics




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details and the quantity of hand-spun and hand-woven woolen, silk and Khadi fabrics produced in the country so far during the last three years, State/UT-wise; (b)	 the quality of minerals, forest crops, agro and foodgrains products, chemicals and polymer, hand-made paper produced and manufactured along with the details of production made in rural engineering sector during the said period, State/UT-wise;(c) 	the quantum of assistance provided to the Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) during the said period; and (d) 	the names of said units of KVIC along with the details of production made by those units and the quantum of assistance provided to them during the above period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	The production of Khadi fabric is made by the Khadi institutions promoted and assisted by the Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC). The State/UT-wise variety-wise Khadi production made by Khadi Institutions during the years 2016-17, 2017-18& 2018-19 is placed at Annexure-I.  (b):	KVIC promotes setting up of Village Industries under following major groups of industries by the entrepreneurs:1.	Agro Based & Food Processing Industry (ABFPI) includes industries viz. Pulses & Cereals Processing Industry, Gur & Khandsari Industry, Palmgur Industry, Fruit & Vegetable Processing Industry, Village Oil Industry, etc.2.	Forest Based Industry (FBI) includes industries viz. Medicinal Plants Industry, Beekeeping Industry, Minor Forest Based Industries, etc.3.	Handmade Paper and Fibre Industry (HMPFI) includes industries viz. Handmade Paper Industry, Fibre Industry, etc.4.	Mineral Based Industry (MBI) includes industries viz. Pottery, Lime, Plaster of Paris products, Stone Crushing & Carving etc.5.	Polymer and Chemical Based Industry (PCBI) includes industries viz. Leather Industry, Non-edible Oil & Soap Industry, Detergent Powder, Candle making, Cottage Match Industry, Plastic Industry, etc.6.	Rural Engineering and Bio-Technology Industry (REBTI) includes industries viz. Non-Conventional Energy, Bio-gas plants, Carpentry & Black smithy, Electronics, etc.7.	Service & Textile Industry (STI) includes services under Electronic and Electrical devise repairing, Machine repairing, Plumbing and other servicing works, Sweater Hosiery, Band aid cloth and other textile products etcThe quality of products is maintained by the manufacturers. The entrepreneur’s units promoted under Khadi and Village Industries sell their products directly in the market and also through the Bhavan, Bhandars run by the Khadi Institutions and also through State Khadi and Village Industries Board (KVIBs) owned sales outlets. Every year thousands of beneficiaries are being assisted to set up their micro units under Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP). Group-wise production of the Village Industries under the purview of KVIC during the last three years 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19is placed at Annexure-II. (c):	The quantum of assistance provided to the Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) during the years 2016-17, 2017-18& 2018-19 is as under:                                                   (Rs. in crore)<pre>Year	Fund released to KVIC2016-17	1849.13*2017-18	2125.512018-19	3181.70</pre>*including Establishment expenses.  Separate allocation for Plan & Non-Plan has been ceased from the FY 2017-18.(d):	KVIC do not undertake any direct production. The production activities are undertaken by the units promoted and assisted by the KVIC which has been given at Part (a) and Part (b) above.
