                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2893






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Reduce/Recycle of E-Waste




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal













                                                    Shankar Lalwani
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Delhi generates maximum plastic waste as compared to other metropolitan cities; (b)	if so, the steps being taken proposed to be taken by the Government to reduce and recycle the plastic waste; and (c)	the quantum of plastic waste generated in the four metropolitan cities every day?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c): As per the Annual Report of Central Pollution Board for the year 2019-20, the plastic waste generation of Delhi is 2,30,525 Tonnes Per Annum. The per capita waste generation is amongst the highest in the country.The Ministry has notified the Plastic Waste Management Amendment Rules, 2021, on 12th August 2021, prohibiting manufacture, import, stocking, distribution, sale and use of the following identified single use plastic items, which have low utility and high littering potential with effect from the 1st July, 2022. The prohibition of items under Plastic Waste Management Rules, 2016, will lead to reduction in waste plastic generation.In order to reduce the use of virgin plastic material for plastic packaging, the Guidelines for Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) notified on 16th February, 2022, set mandatory target for use of recycled plastic content in plastic packaging. The guidelines also prescribe mandatory target for reuse of rigid plastic packaging. Further in order to promote recycling, mandatory target has been prescribed for recycling of plastic waste collected under EPR by Producers, Importers and Brand owners. As per Annual Report of CPCB for the year 2019-20, the quantum of plastic waste generated in the remaining 3 metropolitan cities are. Maharashtra including Mumbai	443,724 TPATamil Nadu including Chennai	431,472 TPAWest Bengal including Kolkata	300236 TPA****
