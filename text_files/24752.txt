                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

596






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Funds under JJM




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the amount of funds allocated to the State of Maharashtra under Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) including the names of works for which the amount has been allocated along with the time by which the work is likely to be completed;(b) 	the total number of villages of the said State including the number of houses targeted to get safe drinking water under the mission along with the time limit for the same;(c) 	the dates on which the tender for the said works have been invited; and(d) 	whether  the said work has been started and if so, the details of the works completed and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE  FOR  JAL SHAKTI(SHRI  PRAHLAD  SINGH  PATEL)(a)& (d) In 2021-22 under Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM), Rs. 7,064.41 Crore Central grant has been allocated to Maharashtra. As reported by the State Government, out of total 2,255 proposed water supply schemes worth Rs. 2,412.28 Crore, work order has been issued for 2,043 schemes and 339 schemes have been completed.(b)   Out of total 40,596 villages having 142.36 lakh households in Maharashtra, as on date in 9,018 villages, every household  is  reported to have provision of tap water supply. Remaining 50.39 lakhs rural households in 31,578 villages are planned to be provided with tap water connection by 2024.(c) Under Jal Jeevan Mission, powers to plan, approve and implement schemes are vested with State Government and therefore details of dates of tenders to be issued by the State Government is not maintained by this Department.*****
