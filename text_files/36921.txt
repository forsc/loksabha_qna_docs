                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4057






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Target and Achievement of 1.0 and 2.0 PMKVY




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy













                                                    Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta
                                                








                                                    Dayakar Pasunoori
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of physical targets set and achieved under the first two phases of Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) in the country, State-wise;(b) 	the details of employment opportunities provided to skilled youth under the first two phases of the PMKVY, with a particular reference to Telangana; and(c)	the details of approved outlay, sanctioned, released and utilized in the first two phases of PMKVY and details of the same in the proposed third phase? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	 Under Skill India Mission, Ministry is delivering skills through Short Term Training (STT) and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) under its flagship scheme Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY). The targets and candidates benefited under PMKVY 1.0 and 2.0 are given below:                                                                                                         (No. in lakh)Phase	Target	Candidates Trained / OrientedPMKVY 1.0 (Phase I)  2015-16	24.00	19.86PMKVY 2.0 (Phase II) 2016-20	100.00	109.98 The State-wise number of candidates benefitted under PMKVY 1.0 and 2.0, as on 31.12.2021, are given at Annexure. (b)        Under PMKVY, placement opportunities are being provided to STT certified candidates, while RPL is not linked with placements as it recognizes the existing skills of candidate.             Under PMKVY 1.0, tracking of placement was not mandatory. As per data reported by training providers, out of 13.32 lakh STT certified candidates, 2.54 lakh candidates were placed across the country including around 21,000 in State of Telangana. Further, under PMKVY 2.0, out of 39.8 lakh STT certified candidates, 21.01 lakh candidates have been placed across the country including around 90,000 in State of Telangana.
