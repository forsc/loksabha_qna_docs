                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

929






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Draft Healthcare Bill




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















a)	whether the Government has entrusted preparation of the draft ‘Right to Health and Healthcare Bill’ with a private university, if so, the details thereof;b)	the process followed in selecting the said private university and details thereof;c)	whether his Ministry has been involved in the process of preparation of the Bill, if so, the details thereof;d)	whether the Government intends to publish the prepared draft Bill for public consultation and if so, the timeline thereof; and e)	whether there is any precedent of private organization drafting Bills for the Government of India, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	to (e): There is no “Right to Health and Healthcare Bill” with the Department of Health & Family Welfare. Neither is the Department preparing any such Bill nor has it entrusted preparation of any such draft Bill to any private entity.
