                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

913






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Construction of Stadium in Jhansi




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any proposal to construct a stadium in Jhansi to promote sports activities;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) No Sir.(b) Does not arise.(c) ‘Sports’ being a State subject the responsibility for providing sports infrastructure and training rests with the State Government. The Central Government supplements the efforts of the State Government. Sports Authority of India (SAI) Regional Centre at Lucknow and Training Centres at Lucknow, Rae-Bareli, Allahabad, Safai, Bareli and Varanasi are catering to the needs of the State of Uttar Pradesh. In addition, this Ministry has sanctioned grant-in-aid for 23 sports infrastructure projects in the State of Uttar Pradesh under Khelo India Scheme.*****
