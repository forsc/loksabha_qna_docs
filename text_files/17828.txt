                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1537






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Allocation under MGNREGS




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the highest ever allocation of Rs. 101500 crore has been made under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Gurantee Scheme (MGNREGS) during the current financial year;(b) 	the amount allocated and released so far, State-wise; and(c) 	the total works completed so far during the current financial year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):     During the Financial Year 2020-21, the Budget Estimates is of Rs. 61,500 crore for Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS). In order to meet additional demand for work due to return of workers from urban areas to rural areas, Government has made an additional provision of Rs. 40,000 crore under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS under the Atma Nirbhar Bharat package as announced by Hon’ble Union Finance Minister during the current FY 2020-21. The Ministry seeks additional fund for Mahatma Gandhi NREGS as and when required for meeting the demand for work on the ground.(b):     Mahatma Gandhi NREGS, is a demand driven wage employment programme. Hence no State/UT-wise allocation of funds is made.  States/UTs-wise details of central funds released during the current FY 2020-21, as on 17.09.2020 under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS is given at Annexure-I. (c):     Number of completed works under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS during the current FY 2020-21 upto 16.09.2020 is 37.71 lakh.****** Annexure referred in reply to part (b) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.1537 dated 20.09.2020Central funds released to States/UTs under Mahatma Gandhi NREGA during 2020-21 (as on 17.09.2020)
