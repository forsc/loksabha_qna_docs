                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3086






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Declaration of Road as NH




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government has any proposal to declare the existing Highway from Hyderabad Junction Gowrelly to Kothagudem Junction on NH-30, via Valigonda-Thossor -Nellikudur- Mahabubabad-Yellandu in Telangana State as National Highway;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the time frame set for approval of the said proposals;(c)  whether the delay in approval of the said proposals is causing hurdles in smooth movement of traffic/vehicles on this road; and(d)  if so, the remedial steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	The Government has identified this State road ‘in principle’ to be declared as National Highway in Telangana with a view to provide funds for preparation of Detailed Project Report (DPR) for ascertaining the feasibility of upgradation of State roads as National Highways based on outcome of DPR . (b) to (d) Guidelines for declaring such State roads as NHs are under finalization by Government. Since the above road is still a State road, responsibility for its development and maintenance vests with the Government of Telangana as this Ministry is primarily responsible for development and maintenance of National Highways in the country.*****
