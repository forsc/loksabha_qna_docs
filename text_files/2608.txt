UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2436






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Hostels for ST Students




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of girls’ and boys’ hostels for scheduled Tribe students, in Gujarat, area-wise;(b) 	whether the Government has implemented any centrally sponsored scheme for the construction of hostels for such students and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the amount allocated to Gujarat during the last three years and the current year under the said scheme and the amount spent by Government, out of the amount allocated; and(d) 	whether the Government has received any proposal from that State for construction of new hostels and if so, the steps taken by Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                         (a) to (d):	Ministry of Tribal Affairs (MoTA) was earlier running a separate scheme ‘“Hostels for ST Girls and Boys” under which funds were provided to State Governments for construction of hostels as per following norms:Funding PatternCentral Share	State Share(i) 100% for girls hostel	--(ii) 100% for boys hostels in LWE areas	--(iii) 100% for UTs	--(iv) 50% for Boys hostel in States.	50%(v) 90% for Central Universities and remaining 10% by the Universities.	--(vi) 45% for Universities other than Central Universities and 10% by the concerned universities.	45% (In case State does not contribute, the share is to be borne by the concerned universities.Statement showing details of hostels sanctioned to the State of Gujarat under the Scheme is furnished vide Annexure.    As part of rationalization of schemes of the Ministry, it has been decided to subsume the intervention of construction of hostels under the schemes of ‘Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-Scheme (SCA to TSS) and Grants under Article 275(1) of the Constitution from 2018-19 onwards. Under the scheme SCA to TSS and Article 275(1) Grants, assistance is provided as 100% grant in the form of an additive to State for tribal development including construction of hostels based on the proposal received from them after appraisal and approval by the Project Appraisal Committee (PAC) in the Ministry. No fund has been sanctioned to Government of Gujarat during the last three years under erstwhile Scheme.There is no proposal pending with the Ministry for construction of new hostels in Gujarat State under these schemes. ***** AnnexureAnnexure referred to in reply to part (a) to (d) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 2436 for answer on 8.7.2019.Location wise details of hostels sanctioned to Gujarat State under the Scheme ‘Construction of Hostels for Girls and Boys’ (Rs. in lakh)S.No. 	Location 	Distt.
