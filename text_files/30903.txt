                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2338






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Designing of Mobile App for Information of Monuments




P. Ravindhranath














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is any proposal with the Archaeological Survey of India to design mobile apps containing information about the monuments and other protected historical structures;(b)	if so, the details thereof and whether it would also assist in the development of tourism across the country; and(c)	if so, the details thereof and the follow-up action taken by the Government till now in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVLOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)  & (b) 	Yes, sir. The Archaeological Survey in India (ASI) has decided to develop a mobile application ‘Indian Heritage’ and place it in public domain. The application covers information about Centrally Protected Monuments along with provisions to upload information about State Protected Monuments. Besides the information in text form, maps and attractive visuals, the application provides other facilities like e-ticketing, film permissions, etc. (c)	ASI has collaborated with Bhaskaracharya National Institute for Space Applications and Geo-Informatics (BISAG), a part of Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology, Govt. of India, and entered into a Memorandum of Understanding to develop the mobile application.
