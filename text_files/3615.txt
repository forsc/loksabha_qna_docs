                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3309






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Upgradation of District Hospitals and Maternity Centres




Ajay Nishad














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the parameters for upgradation of district hospitals and maternity centres;(b) whether the Government accords preference to the upgradation of hospitals located at minorities and dalit (SC/ST) dominated areas;(c) if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government has received any proposal for upgradation of district and maternity centres; and(e) if so, the details thereof along with the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)  Public health and hospitals is a State Subject. However, under the National Health Mission (NHM), financial and technical support is provided to States for strengthening their healthcare systems including for strengthening districts hospitals for multi-specialty care and as sites for skill based training and teaching, particularly for clinical learning for different levels of health care workers. 	Similarly, District Hospitals having more than 70% bed occupancy can be upgraded with an additional Maternal & Child Health wing or maternity center.(b)&(c) As stated above, Public health and hospitals being a State subject, the states are encouraged to prioritize the upgradation of hospitals in High Priority Districts (HPDs) for which financial and technical assistance is provided under NHM based on requirements projected by the States/UTs in their Program Implementation Plans. Districts at the bottom 25% level based on composite health index, all Left Wing Extremism (LWE) affected districts and districts with majority tribal population, whose composite health index is below 50% are categorized as HPDs. There are 184 High Priority Districts (HPDs).(d) & (e) Under National Health Mission, technical and financial support is provided to States/UTs for strengthening their healthcare system including upgradation of district and maternity centres. More than 22,000 delivery points have been identified and additional funds for strengthening these facilities have been provided. The approvals conveyed through Record of Proceeding(RoPs) for the States/UTs for the last three years are available in the public domain and accessible at  https://nhm.gov.in/index4.php?lang=1&level=0&linkid=449&lid=53.              Funds approved for the last three years from 2016-17 to 2018-19 for District Hospital strengthening is placed at Annexure.
