                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2327






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Protectees Under Z+ Security




A. Ganeshamurthi














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received any request from any individual to provide Z+ security;(b) if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government on these requests;(c) the criteria and procedure be followed by the Government to extend Z+ security to an/any individual; and(d) the total number of individuals provided Z+ security by the Government during the last two years along with the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d) Yes Sir.  ‘Z+’ security is provided by the Government on the basis of threat assessment by Central Security Agency and is subject to periodic review.  Based on such review, the security cover is continued, withdrawn or modified.  The details of requests received for ‘Z+’ security cannot be disclosed due to security reasons.  However, presently there are around 40 ‘Z+’ protectees in the Central List.*****
