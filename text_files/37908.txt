                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4737






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Scholarships to Minority Students




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of Post-Matric Scholarships given to minority students, fund allocation, actual expenses and the number of beneficiaries during the financial years 2015 to 2021, religion-wise; and(b) the details of Merit-cum-Means Scholarships given to minority students, fund allocation, actual expenses and the number of beneficiaries during the financial years 2015 to 2021, religion-wise?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a) & (b): After 2014-15, 5.20 crore scholarships have been disbursed throughout the country compared to 3.03crore scholarships before 2014-15. The religion-wise details of Scholarships sanctioned to minority students under the Post-Matric and Merit-cum-Means based Scholarship Schemes during the financial years 2014-2015 to 2021-2022 along with the total amount sanctioned and disbursed under these Schemes directly to the beneficiary accounts under DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) mode during the said period is given in the table below:Religion-wise details under Post Matric and Merit-cum-Means based Scholarship Schemes from FY 2014-15 to 2021-22*Scheme	Buddhist	Christian	Jain	Muslim	Sikh	Parsi	Amount sanctioned (In Rs. Crore)Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme	12,98,637	53,13,905	4,58,665	3,36,11,677	35,90,880	6,015	9057.08Post-Matric Scholarship Scheme	33,450	6,82,623	1,21,926	43,10,991	5,38,345	1,051	3426.81Merit-cum-Means based Scholarship Scheme	3,376	1,63,188	31,206	7,51,778	49,963	97	2670.81Total	13,35,463	61,59,716	6,11,797	3,86,74,446	41,79,188	7,163	15,154.70* Disbursement of Scholarships continues in 2021-22. *****
