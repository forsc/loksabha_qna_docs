                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2442






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Industrial Training Institutes




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the details of the Industrial Training Institute thathave been brought Craftsman Training under the scheme, whether any private institutes have been collaborated with, details of funds disbursed, state wise distribution down to district level for Rajasthan (b)	 details of number of industrial clusters, businesses and SMEs under the Scheme,State wise down to district level forRajasthan, whether the Government is planning to introduce provisions to incentivize the private sector to participate in the Scheme;(c)	 total number of candidates and women candidates registered in Industrial Training Institute under the Scheme,State wise distribution down to district level for Rajasthan;(d)	the steps taken to increase participation from rural and remote areas, whether any special provisions were made to encourage women’s participation?(e)	the review mechanism for institutions participating in the scheme along with details of review visits or the reports submitted by institutions,State wise down to district level for Rajasthan?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Directorate General of Training, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship has formulated the following Centrally Sponsored schemes:i.	World bank assisted Skill Strengthening for Industrial Value Enhancement (STRIVE): The scheme has selected 426 Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) including 29 Private ITIs. Details of ITIs selected under STRIVE scheme and state-wise fund disbursed including details of Rajasthan are attached in Annexure - I (A&B).ii.	Upgradation of existing Government ITIs into Model ITIs:Existing Govt. ITIs in states have been taken up for Upgradation into Model ITI which is supposed to evolve as an institution showcasing best practices, efficient and high-quality training delivery and sustainable and effective industry relationship. Details of ITIs covered under the scheme along with state-wise fund disbursed including Rajasthan are given at Annexure – II.iii.	Skill Development in 47 Districts Affected by Left Wing Extremism: The scheme envisages establishment of 47 new ITIs (1 ITI per district in 47 districts of 10 States). The details of coverage along with fund released under the scheme state-wise is given at Annexure – III. iv.	Enhancing Skill Development Infrastructure in NE States: The scheme envisages establishment of 34 new ITIs along with upgradation of 22 existing Government ITIs by introducing three new trades per ITI and supplementing infrastructure deficiencies in 28 ITIs by constructing new hostel, boundary wall and replacing old and obsolete tools and equipment. The details of coverage and fund released under the scheme is given at Annexure – IV.(b)	Details of State-wise Industry Clusters selected under STRIVE scheme and its distribution isgiven at Annexure-V. Funds have been released to Industry Clusters to incentivise the private sector to participate in STRIVE scheme.(c)	Details of total number of candidates and women candidates registered in ITIs under centrally sponsored schemes with state wise distribution including details of Rajasthan is given at Annexure-VI (A&B). (d)	Under the STRIVE scheme,ITIs are incentivized for increasing both the total enrolment and women participation.Fund disbursement relates to Increase in Women enrolment as one of the Key Indicator.(e)	The schemesare reviewed by the state governments as well as Ministry of Skills Development & Entrepreneurship (MSDE) through online and offline modes including field visits. The last review visit for STRIVE scheme for Rajasthan was conducted on 10-11 November, 2021 where ITIs selected under STRIVE participated.
