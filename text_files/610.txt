                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

635






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Increasing Employment in Animal Husbandry




Sushil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any effective steps to increase the opportunities of employment in the field of animal husbandry, dairying and fisheries in the country including Bihar; (b) if so, the details of the amount allocated and expenditure incurred by the Government to promote fisheries, dairying and animal husbandry in the country during the last three years and the current year, State-wise including Bihar; (c) whether many unemployed youth and farmers want to establish their own employment in the field of fisheries, dairying and animal husbandry but are unable to access the facilities meant for them; and (d) if so, whether the Government proposes to provide them technical, economic and other assistance, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (Dr. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	Yes, Sir. The Government is implementing various schemes to supplement the efforts of States/UTs by providing financial support across the country including Bihar which helps to generate direct as well as indirect employment: I.	Rashtriya Gokul Mission II.	National Programme for Dairy Development III.	National Dairy Plan-IIV.	Dairy Entrepreneurship Development SchemeV.	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development FundVI.	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activitiesVII.	National Livestock Mission(NLM)VIII.	Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries	The National Livestock Mission, which commenced from 2014-15, under its Sub-mission on Livestock Development a component “Entrepreneurship Development and Employment Generation (EDEG)”, which supports the activities related to poultry, small ruminants, rabbit and pig through its various sub-components is implemented. The Component- EDEG is designed mainly for entrepreneurship development and technology infusion wherein a basket of activities which are bankable are placed from which farmers, individual entrepreneurs, NGOs, companies, cooperatives, groups of unorganized and organized sector which include Self Help Groups (SHGs), Joint Liability Groups (JLGs) etc. can choose from. Also, under Dairy Entrepreneurship Development (DEDS) scheme since inception, 3,69,438 entrepreneurs have been benefitted till 30.04.2019. (b) to (d)        The details of the amount allocated and expenditure incurred by the Government during the last three years and the current year, State-wise including Bihar under DEDS is placed at ANNEXURE-I and the details of the amount of subsidy provided to various beneficiaries to start small business/ units of NLM for last three years and current year including Bihar are placed at ANNEXURE-II, ANNEXURE-III, ANNEXURE-IV and  ANNEXURE-V.***
