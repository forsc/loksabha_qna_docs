                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1481






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Construction of Warehouses




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a):	whether the Ministry is giving assistance to construct warehouses in States and has allocated Rs. 1,000 crore for this purpose and if so, the details thereof; (b):	 whether 45 warehousing projects have been sanctioned for Andhra Pradesh; (c):	if so, the status of these projects and districts where the warehouses have been constructed; (d):	whether there are any plans to construct additional warehouses; and (e):	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Government of India is providing assistance for development of marketing infrastructure including warehouses and cold storage through various schemes. The Government is implementing Agricultural Marketing Infrastructure (AMI), a sub-scheme of Integrated Scheme for Agricultural Marketing (ISAM) under which assistance is provided for construction/ renovation of godowns/ warehouses in the rural areas of various States to enhance the storage capacity for agriculture produce. AMI scheme is a demand driven, back ended credit linked subsidy scheme in which rate of subsidy provided is 25% and 33.33% based on the category of eligible beneficiary and provided on capital cost of the project. No State-wise allocation is made in this scheme. Further, Warehouse Infrastructure Fund (WIF) was created in National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) during 2013-14 & 2014- 15 with corpus of Rs. 5000 crore each (from priority sector shortfall) to finance warehouses and cold storages.Contd…..2/-- 2 -In addition to above, the Government has approved a new central sector scheme for Financial Facility under Agriculture Infrastructure Fund of Rs. 1,00,000 Crore to provide a medium-long term debt financing facility for investment in viable projects for post-harvest market infrastructure including warehousing facility and community farming assets through interest subvention and financial support. The scheme has been launched by Hon’ble Prime Minister of India on 09.08.2020. The Government is implementing Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) under which financial assistance is provided for various horticulture activities including setting up of cold storages. The Scheme component is demand/entrepreneur driven for which Government assistance in the form of credit linked back ended subsidy is available at the rate of 35% (for general areas) and 50% (for hilly and scheduled areas) of eligible capital cost of the project for both public and private sector enterprises.(b) & (c):	Yes Sir. 45 warehouse projects had been sanctioned for Andhra Pradesh under Warehouse Infrastructure Fund (WIF). The status of these projects and districts where the warehouses have been constructed is at Annexure-I. (d) & (e):	In addition to schemes mentioned in reply (a) above, Department of Food and Public Distribution, Government of India, deals with creation of storage facilities for foodgrains procured under the Public Distribution System. Depending on requirement in specific areas and for modernization of the storage facilities, the Government implements Private Entrepreneurs Guarantee (PEG) scheme, Central Sector Scheme (erstwhile Plan Scheme), Construction of Steel Silos and Cover and Plinth (CAP) hiring scheme for construction of facilities for storage of Central Pool Stocks in the country. Central Railside Warehouse Company (CRWC) Ltd. plans construction of additional warehouses of total capacity of 37,700 MT.
