                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

113






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Course on Contract Farming




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any degree or diploma course is being offered on contract farming in Government-run agricultural colleges, universities, etc. in the country;(b)	if so, the names of the institutes and the syllabus thereof; and (c)	the number of degrees/diplomas awarded in this field so far?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICUTLRURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ?? ????? ?????? ??????		                           (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	to (c): 	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.  ************* STATEMENT IN RESPECT OF PARTS (a) to (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION No. 113 TO BE ANSWERED ON 27/07/2021 REGARDING                               “COURSE ON CONTRACT FARMING”(a)	 No, Sir. However, the Contract Farming is being offered in Under Graduate (UG) and Post-Graduate (PG) Programmes of Agriculture and Allied Sciences as a part of the following courses: (i)	Entrepreneurship Development and Industrial Consultancy (UG)(ii)	Fundamentals of Extension and Rural Development (UG)(iii)	Agricultural Marketing & Price Analysis (PG) (iv)	Market Led Extension Management (PG)Under Post Graduate Diploma in Management (Agri Business Management) PGDM-ABM, a course titled “Contract Farming & Farmer Producer Organizations (PGDM 717)” has been offered since 2009 to students as an elective course in the second semester. (b)	As there is no degree or diploma course offered in Contract Farming, the question of syllabi of such course does not arise. (c)	Question does not arise.**************
