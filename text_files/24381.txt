                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

353






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Fertilizer Demand




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the quantity of chemical fertilizers demanded by the Chhattisgarh Government in terms of metric tons for farmers for the Kharif crops this year.(b)	the quantity of chemicals fertilizers supplied against the demand made by the w(c)	Whether the Government has made available less quantity of chemical fertilizers to Chhattisgarh State which has created shortage of fertilizers to farmers, if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the details of chemicals fertilizers made available against the demands of the States in the country, state-wise in terms of metric tons?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS (SHRI MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a) & (b): The data regarding the requirement, availability and sales for the state of Chhattisgarh during the ongoing Kharif , 2021 is attached at Annexure-A.(c):	No Sir. There has been no shortage of fertilizers. (d):	The state-wise requirement and availability of fertilizers during Kharif 2021 (1st April, 2021 to 30th June, 2021) is attached at Annexure-B.******
