                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3081






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






National Highway Projects




Upendra Singh Rawat













                                                    Kaushal Kishore
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	  the total number of National Highway (NH) projects taken up in the country, State/UT-wise particularly in Uttar Pradesh;(b)	  the details thereof including the status of plantation on both side of these NHs; and (c)  the time since when these projects are under construction along with the time by which these are likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) 1440 projects on National Highways are under construction in the country including the 65 National Highway projects in Uttar Pradesh.  Details of these National Highway Projects are placed at Annexure-I. Projects are given a time period of 2 to 3 years for completion.  The plantation on both sides of Highways in the country is carried out as per relevant provisions of Manual published by Indian Roads Congress (IRC), Green Highways Policy, 2015 and other guidelines of Government of India.  Plantations are generally undertaken after substantial completion of Civil works.  The status of plantation activities depend on the stage of the project.
