                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5173






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Wildlife and Forest Tourism




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is a huge potential for wildlife and forest tourism in the country; and(b)	if so, the steps taken to tap the potential of tourism in the various States of the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 					(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):	Yes, Sir. There is a huge potential for wildlife and forest tourism in the country.Ministry of Tourism under its scheme of Swadesh Darshan Integrated Development of Theme-Based Tourist Circuits has identified Wildlife and Eco Circuits among fifteen thematic circuits for development. The details of the projects sanctioned under the Eco and Wildlife Circuits of Swadesh Darshan Scheme in various States/UTs is at Annexure. The projects under the above scheme are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier.Ministry of Tourism has also formulated Draft National Strategies and Roadmaps for (i) Sustainable Tourism and (ii) Eco Tourism & Adventure Tourism.Apart from above, Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEFCC) has informed that they have issued guidelines to all State Governments/Union Territory Administrations for sustainable ecotourism in forest and wildlife areas – 2021.*******
