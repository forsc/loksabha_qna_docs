                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2979






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Structural Audit of Canals and Dams




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government is carrying out structural audit of all canals and dams in Gujarat; if so, the details thereof indicating the intervals at which the structural audit of canals and dams is carried out; and (b) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (b)  Safety of Dams and Canals primarily rests with their owners which are mostly State Governments and Central/ State power generating PSUs. Central Dam Safety Organization of the Central Water Commission (CWC) encourages and assists the advancement of dam safety practices that will help ensure operation of dams to their full capacities and intended purposes and also to reduce the risk to lives and property from the consequences of both structural and operational dam incidents and failures. In the Guidelines for Safety Inspection of Dams, 2018 of CWC, the frequency of inspections by dam owners is defined and depends on the hazard classification of the dam, the condition of the dam and regulation of Central Dam Safety Organisation, CWC. 		As reported by Government of Gujarat, regular Pre-Monsoon Inspections and Post monsoon Inspections of the Dams are carried out as per department vernacular G.R. FWA/Flood Control/Inspection/2009/J-1 dated 9/4/2009 by various level of officers in respective time period as given in the Annexure. Inspections of canals are being carried out by concerned field officers in their jurisdictions during regular irrigation management activity and pre-monsoon inspection.*****ANNEXUREAnnexure referred to in reply to parts (a) & (b) of Unstarred Question No. 2979 to be answered in Lok Sabha on 27.06.2019 regarding “STRUCTURAL AUDIT OF CANALS AND DAMS”(vide Department vernacular G.R. FWA/Flood Control/Inspection/2009/J-1 Dt:9/4/2009 ).Sr No.	Officer Designation	Inspection Period	Type Of Dams 		Pre-monsoon	Post-monsoon	(1)	The Chief Engineer and Additional Secretary.	1st May to 30th May	1st November to 30th November	Major and Important Medium Dams under jurisdiction.(2)	The Superintending Engineer 	1st May to 15th May	15th  November to 30th November	Major and Medium Dams under jurisdiction.(3)	The Executive Engineer	Start by 15th April.	15th November to 30th November	Major and Medium Dams under jurisdiction.(4)	The Deputy Executive Engineer	Between 1th April to 30th April	Between 1st October to 30th October	All Dams under jurisdiction.*****
