                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Fertilizer Import




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the expenditure incurred on import of Fertilizer during each of the last five years;(b )the names of top ten countries from where fertilizers have been imported into the country, during the said period;(c)the names of Government PSUs involved in importing the same along with the quantity of fertilizers imported, fertilizers-wise;(d)the quantity of Fertilizers being produced in the country annually during the said period; and (e)the amount spent on subsidy every year?
