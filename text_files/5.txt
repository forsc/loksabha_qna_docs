                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

10






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






National Health Mission




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the status of implementation of National Health Mission (NHM) in the country during the last five years and the current year, year-wise and State/UT-wise; (b) the details of funds released under National Health Mission during the last five years and the current year, year and State-wise; (c) the details of components under which above said funds has been released component, State and year-wise; and (d) the details of each of the proposals received from Government of Andhra Pradesh under NHM since its implementation and action taken by the Government on each of the above proposal?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):    The National Health Mission (NHM) envisages achievement of universal access to equitable, affordable & quality health care services that are accountable and responsive to people’s needs. NHM encompasses its two Sub-Missions, the National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) and the National Urban Health Mission (NUHM). The main programmatic components include Health System Strengthening in rural and urban areas, Reproductive-Maternal- Neonatal-Child and Adolescent Health (RMNCH+A), and Communicable and Non-Communicable Diseases.Under the NHM, technical and financial support is provided to State / UTs for strengthening of their healthcare systems based on the proposals submitted by them in their Programme Implementation Plans (PIPs). The State/UT wise status of implementation of National Health Mission (NHM) in the country during last five year and current year is placed at Annexure (I-VI). (b):      The State/UT wise details of the funds released under National Health Mission during last five year and current year is placed at Annexure (VII).  (c):      The State/UT details of components under which above said funds has been released component and year-wise is placed at Annexure (VIII-XIII)).    (d):      Under National Health Mission technical and financial support is provided to States/UTs, on the proposals made by the States/UTs in their Programme Implementation Plans (PIPs). During the National Programme Coordination Committee (NPCC) meetings held every year, the proposals of the States/UTs are discussed and finalized in consultation with the State/UTs within their available resource envelope. Based upon recommendation of NPCC approvals are awarded in the form of Record of Proceedings (RoP) & issued to respective States/UTs Governments.    RoPs of Andhra Pradesh under NHM   is available  at https://nhm.gov.in/index4.php?lang=1&level=0&linkid=52&lid=65
