                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1767






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Promotion of Bamboo Products




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is taking any steps to promote bamboo products in other States as well which are currently being made in the north-east region;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government is planning to market such products through e-commerce platforms; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Independent Charge)of the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region[Dr. Jitendra Singh](a)&(b): Yes sir, Restructured National Bamboo Mission(NBM) is being implemented in 23 States including North East States. Under this Mission, for promotion of bamboo products, 172 processing units for value addition of Bamboo, 97 primary processing units for Management of Bamboo waste and 746 Micro/Medium processing units  have been approved from 2018-19 to 31.08.2020. Cane and  Bamboo Technology Centre(CBTC) has formed 16 clusters in NE States, During the last four years, Ministry of DoNER and NEC have sanctioned 17 projects costing Rs.134 crore for development of bamboo in NE States, including a Bamboo Industrial Park at DimaHasao in Assam. Various agencies ofGovernment of Indialike Cane and Bamboo Technology Centre, North Eastern Handicrafts & Handlooms Development Corporation Ltd. and National Bamboo Mission under Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfareorganize seminars, workshops and eventsfor promotion of bamboo products.(c) & (d): Restructured National Bamboo Mission launched in 2018-19 has inbuilt provision for intervention on marketing which includes e-trading. Various stakeholders are advised to use enam.gov.in and gem.gov.in portals for marketing and procurement or develop other effective online systems. 21 Bamboo Mandis (bamboo market place) have been approved for promotion and e-trading of bamboo from 2018-2019 to 31.08.2020 under National Bamboo Mission. Similarly, North Eastern Handicrafts and Handlooms Development Corporation Ltd has also created its online shopping portal for promotion of Bamboo.
