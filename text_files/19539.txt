                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

863






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Growth in Print Media




Devusinh Jesingbhai Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Indian print media including regional newspapers have shown healthy growth in recent years;(b)	if so, the details of the number of newspapers published, State-wise including in Gujarat;(c)	whether cases of alleged malpractices by certain regional newspapers have been reported; and(d)	if so, the details of such cases reported and action taken thereon, State-wise including Gujarat during each of the last three years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES {SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR}(a) and (b): As per the register of publications maintained by the Registrar of Newspapers for India (RNI), the total number of registered publications has consistently increased over the years.The State/UT-wise details of total number of registered publications, including newspapers, as on 31.03.2020 is given at Annexure.(c) and (d): Complaints regarding violation of the provisions of the Press and Registration of Books Act, 1867 and the Press Council Act, 1978, as and when noticed, are handled as per provisions of the Statutes.*******
