UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1087






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Protection of Heritage Places of India’s Freedom Movement




Suresh Pujari














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is any proposal of the Central Government to protect, preserve, maintain and improve the heritage places of India''s freedom movement on the eve of 75th year of her independence in 2022;(b)	if so, whether the Ministry proposes to take up ''Bara Pahar'' & other places of Barharh, Jharsuguda and Sambalpur where Veer Surendra Sai and his companions fought against the British Raj, 28 years before the First War of Independence of 1857;(c)	whether the Ministry proposes to give special importance in protecting, improving & beautifying ''GHENS'' of Bargarh where two generation of the then Zamindar family, namely Madho Singh, Khate Singh, Airi Singh, Bairi Singh, Kunjel Singh & others were either hanged or killed or were deported, again 28 years before 1857;(d)	whether there is any proposal from the state government in this regard; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) 	Yes Sir, Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) protects, preserves and maintains several monuments related to India’s freedom movement. List of these monuments is placed at Annexure-I.  All these monuments are in a good state of preservation.(b) & (c)	The Government of India has initiated several programmes for Odisha under Azadi Ka Mahotsava in collaboration with the State Government of Odisha.  Proposal for Bara Pahar and other places of Barharh, Jharsuguda &  Sambalpur will be taken up once these are received from the State Government of Odisha. (d) & (e)	No such proposal has been received from the State Government.
