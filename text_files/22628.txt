                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3876






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Expansion of Air Strips




Kishan Kapoor














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the scheme for the expansion of air strips in the hilly areas of the country; and(b) the number of tourist places in North India where the expansion of air strips are proposed?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Ministry of Civil Aviation launched Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik) on 21-10-2016 to stimulate regional air connectivity and making air travel affordable to the masses. States of Jammu and Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh & Uttarakhand, North Eastern Region of India, Andaman and Nicobar Islands and Lakshadweep Islands have been included in the Scheme Document as Priority Area(s). Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs  has approved the proposal for revival of existing unserved / underserved airports / airstrips of the State Governments, Airports Authority of India, Public Sector Undertakings and Civil Enclaves to promote the RCS-UDAN. An airport which is included in the awarded routes of RCS-UDAN  and requires upgradation/development for commencement of RCS operations is developed under "Revival of unserved and underserved airports" scheme. Details of the Tourism RCS Routes awarded under RCS-UDAN are attached as Annexure. The expansion of air strips in Tourism RCS (TRCS) routes  is undertaken by the airport operators {States/Airports Authority of India (AAI) etc.} as per requirements.
