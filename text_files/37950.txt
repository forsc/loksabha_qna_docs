                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5187






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Cultural Fund for Organising Cultural Programmes




Dileshwar Kamait













                                                    Geeta Kora
                                                








                                                    Navneet Ravi Rana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any plan/mechanism to establish/allocate a special cultural fund for organizing cultural programmes at the district level to promote our precious culture, especially the cultural/tourist places in the State of Jharkhand, Bihar and Maharashtra;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the details of funds allocated to the States of Jharkhand, Bihar and Maharashtra for the said purpose and the formal procedure for co-ordination between the concerned officers and the nodal agencies for organizing such an event to promote tourism and business in these States?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d)  Ministry of Culture, Govt. of India administers various scheme components such as Cultural Function Production Grant, Repertory Grant, Cultural Organization with National Presence etc., under which grants are being given as financial assistance to the organizations to organise cultural events across the country including the States of Jharkhand, Bihar and Maharashtra.  Seven Zonal Cultural Centres across the country play a key role in promotion of culture through the programmes decided in consultation with their member State Government.	The concerned Zonal Cultural Centres (ZCCs) of above mentioned three member states are as follows:-Sl. No.	Member States	ZCCs to which attached1.	Jharkhand	EZCC, Kolkata2.	Bihar	EZCC, Kolkata and NCZCC, Prayagraj3.	Maharashtra	SCZCC, Nagpur and WZCC, UdaipurA part of the grant provided to concerned Zonal Cultural Centres (ZCCs) is being utilized to organise cultural programmes in Jharkhand, Bihar and Maharashtra.
