                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3067






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Accessibilty of Water in Tamil Nadu




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the percentage of the population that does not have access to water supply facility in the State of Tamil Nadu, district-wise; and (b) 	whether the Government has reduced the budgetary allocation for drinking water schemes and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FORJAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) 	As reported by State Government of Tamil Nadu on Integrated Management Information System (IMIS), 97.22% of total  population are fully covered (i.e. getting more than 40 litres per capita per day safe drinking water) and 2.78% of the  population are partially covered (i.e. getting less than 40 litres per capita per day safe drinking water) as on 04.07.2019. 	District-wise details of the percentage of rural population having drinking water supply as on 04.07.2019 in the State of Tamil Nadu is at Annexure.(b) No Sir. The budgetary allocation for National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP) during 2018-19 was Rs. 7000 crore which has been increased to Rs. 10000.66 crore for financial year 2019-20.
