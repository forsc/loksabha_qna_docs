                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2693






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Allotment of Stalls




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the reservation system has been implemented for scheduled tribes and scheduled castes in the allotment of stalls at railway stations of all levels in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor with the Government’s response in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) and (b): Yes, Sir. As per Catering Policy 2017 and Multi Purpose Stall Policy 2017, there is a provision of 6% reservation for Scheduled Castes and 4% for Scheduled Tribes in allotment of minor catering and vending units such as stalls, trolleys etc. on A1, A, B & C categories of stations and 12% reservation for Scheduled Castes and 8% for Scheduled Tribes for allotment on D, E & F categories of stations.                    Presently, the licenses of 554 units are held by Scheduled Castes and licenses of 192 units are held by Scheduled Tribes on stations over Indian Railways.(c): Does not arise.*****
