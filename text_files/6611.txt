                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

401






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Funds for SCs and OBCs




Kaushalendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether data regarding the population of Scheduled Castes and Backward Classes in the country is available with the Government; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government is considering to allocate funds to be spent on development schemes on the basis of the available data; (d) whether it is a fact that the Government has failed to eradicate poverty and minimise starvation deaths despite poverty alleviation campaigns and schemes being implemented in the country; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b): The data regarding the population of Scheduled Castes is available with Government.  The details can be seen on the following links:(i) http://www.censusindia.gov.in/2011census/SC-ST/pca_state_distt_sc.xls	(ii) http://www.censusindia.gov.in/2011census/pca_sc/pca- sc.htmlThe data on backward classes are not available.(c): Fund allocation under some of the Schemes for Scheduled Caste Welfare i.e. Pre- Matric Scholarship for SC Students, Special Central Assistance for Scheduled Caste Sub Plan and Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana Pre-Matric and Post Matric Scholarship for OBC Students is based , inter alia, on the population data.(d) & (e): The mandate of this Department is implementation of the schemes of educational, economic and social empowerment of Scheduled Castes/Other Backward Classes, Senior Citizens, victims of Drug Abuse, transgenders, Denotified Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Tribes (DNTs). This Department does not maintain information related to poverty and death starvation.******
