                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

498






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Vocational Training under NULM




Sanjeev Kumar Singari














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is huge absentism in vocational training courses taken up under the National Urban Livelihood Mission(NULM); (b)  	if so, the details thereof including the details of number of beneficiaries trained, number of dropouts, number of job-placements in Andhra Pradesh, district-wise; and (c) 	the quantum of funds disbursed to various vocational training programmes of Andhra Pradesh since inception of NULM?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OFTHE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                   (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI) (a)	No, Sir. Under the “Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana- National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM)”, candidates undergoing skill training are forwarded for assessment & certification based on their benchmark attendance of minimum 70%. (b)	As reported by the State, since FY 2014-15, 1,52,319 candidates have been skill trained in Andhra Pradesh. Out of these 1,00,751 candidates have been placed. District-wise details annexed.(c)	Under DAY-NULM, funds are released as a whole in terms of Ministry of Finance, General Financial Rules (GFR) 2017 and Mission guidelines, to States/ UTs, including Andhra Pradesh. Inter-se allocation of fund for Employment through Skill Training & Placement (EST&P) component is made by States/UTs, as per their requirements. Since FY 2014-15, ? 311.80 Cr has been released to Andhra Pradesh.                                               *****
