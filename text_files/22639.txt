                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3707






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Jal Jeevan Mission (Urban)




Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Nisith Pramanik
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                








                                                    Rajveer (Raju Bhaiya) Singh
                                                








                                                    Sarmistha Sethi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has proposed to launch the Jal 	Jeevan Mission (Urban) for providing universal water supply in 	all 4,378 Urban Local Bodies with 2.86 crores household tap 	connections;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the liquid waste management in 500 AMRUT cities is 	also proposed by the Government; and(d)	if so, the details thereof including the steps being taken by the 	Government in this regard so far?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                            (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a).	Yes, Sir.(b).	Jal Jeevan Mission (Urban) has been proposed by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs as a Centrally Sponsored Scheme to provide universal coverage of water supply through functional taps to all households in all 4,372 statutory towns/cities of the country. The Mission proposes to promote circular economy of water through development of city water balance plan for each city focusing on recycle/reuse of treated sewage, rejuvenation of water bodies and water conservation.(c) & (d).	Universal coverage of household-level sewerage/septage management in 500 AMRUT(Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation) citieshas also been proposed under the Jal Jeevan Mission (Urban).                                                *****
