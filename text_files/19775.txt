                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

949






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Banking Frauds




Shardaben Anilbhai Patel













                                                    Shyam Yadav Singh
                                                








                                                    Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar
                                                








                                                     Rahul Ramesh Shewale
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of banking frauds unearthed and fund involved therein during each of the last three years till March 2020, bank-wise;(b)	the details of FIRs registered and chargesheets filed against the persons and bank employees involved therein and the number of persons convicted during the said period;(c)	whether there has been unprecedented rise in banking frauds during the period of lockdown due to COVID-19 pandemic and thereafter;(d)	if so, the details thereof including the loss of amount to the customers and banks due to such frauds, bank-wise; and(e)	the corrective steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to keep a check on banking frauds and the achievements made in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE (a) to (e): The information is being collected and will be laid on the Table of the House.***
