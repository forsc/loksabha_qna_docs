                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

910






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Beneficiaries under PMAY-U




Ramapati Ram Tripathi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of beneficiaries under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – 	Urban 	(PMAY-U) in Uttar Pradesh and the number of houses 	allotted to them 	under the yojana;(b)	the funds allocated under PMAY-U in Uttar Pradesh during the 	financial years 2019-20 and 2021-22; and(c)	the number of houses allotted to beneficiaries under PMAY-U 	during 	the said period? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS              (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a):	A total of 17,33,672 houses have been sanctioned, of which 14,23,139 houses are grounded and 9,71,143 houses are completed/delivered to the beneficiaries in the state of Uttar Pradesh under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U).(b):	The funds allocated under PMAY-U in Uttar Pradesh during the financial years 2019-20,2020-21 and 2021-22 are ?4,046.36 crore, ?20594,936.00 crore and ?1,673.73 crore respectively.(c):	The number of houses allotted/sanctioned to beneficiaries under PMAY-U during 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 are 3,80,046, 2,61,998 and 6,617 respectively.                            *****
