                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2120






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Promotion of Eco-tourism in Odisha




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka













                                                    Y. S. Avinash Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to promote Eco-tourism in various States of the country including Odisha and Andhra Pradesh; (b) 	if so, the details of the Schemes formulated for this purpose and the measures taken by the Government to promote eco-tourism along with the revenue generated during the last four years; (c) 	the places identified by the Government for promotion of eco-tourism; (d) 	whether some State Governments have requested for additional funds to carry out various tourism projects in the State and if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Odisha and Andhra Pradesh; and (e) 	whether Somasila Reservoir and Siddavattam projects have also been included in the list and the details of the funds earmarked for this purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (e):	The Ministry of Tourism promotes India as a holistic destination under the Incredible India brand-line. As part of its on-going activities, the Ministry releases print, electronic, online and outdoor media campaigns in the international and domestic markets, to promote various tourism destinations and products including Eco-tourism sites in the country.  In addition, the Indiatourism Offices in India and overseas disseminate information and undertake various promotional activities with the objective of showcasing the varied tourist destinations and products of the country.Eco circuit has also been identified as one of the fifteen thematic circuits for development in the country under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme. Ministry does not maintain information on revenue generated through tourism in India. However, the Foreign Exchange Earnings through Tourism in India during 2015, 2016, 2017 and 2018 were Rs.1,34,844 Crore, Rs.1,54,146 Crore, Rs.1,77,874 Crore and Rs. 1,94,881 Crore (Provisional), respectively.The projects under the Swadesh Darshan scheme are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments / Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. Submission of project proposals by the State Governments and its sanctioning is a continuous process. Ministry has sanctioned the project Development of Eco Tourism Circuit in Mahaboobnagar District in Telangana in 2016-17 for Rs.91.62 Crores.  Under the project Somasila Reservoir is one of the site taken up for development. The development works at Somasila includes Eco Log Huts, Floating Jetty, Pathways, Landscaping, Parking, signage’s, Boating Facility, Solid Waste Management, Tourist Amenities Building, Solar Lighting, Tourist Facilitation Centre, Open Plaza, Water Sports etc.  for Rs. 28.71 Crores. No project has been sanctioned for development of Siddavattam in Andhra PradeshThe details of projects sanctioned under the Eco Circuit theme and for Odisha under the scheme are annexed.*******
