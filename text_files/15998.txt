                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4433






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Funds Allocated for TB Eradication




T. R. Paarivendhar













                                                    Vincent H Pala
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the total number of TB patients presently registered/enrolled for medical treatment in the hospitals across the country as on date, State/UT-wise; (b) the amount allocated and utilised by the Government to eradicate TB in the country, State/ UT-wise; (c) the allocations by the Government towards the India TB Research Consortium (ITRC) on a yearly basis from financial years 2016-17 to 2019-20; and (d) the number of grants and the nature and value of grants given by ITRC so far?






ANSWER




                                                         ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):     Total number of TB patients notified across the country as on date-state/UT-wise is placed at Annexure – I.(b):   The amount allocated and utilized by the Government – State/UT wise for the year 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20 – placed at Annexure – II.(c):    The Standing Finance Committee was approved in December 2017’. And the budget for FY 17-18 was allocated in Feb. 2018. The budget allocated for ITRC for different FY is as below:FY 2016- 2017 -   NAFY 2017-2018 -    Rs. 8.5 CrsFY 2018-2019 -     Rs. 30 Cr.FY 2019-2020 -     Rs. 30 Cr. (d):    Total number of grant/projects was 33. The nature of the grant: The grants pertain to research work on TB in different thematic areas of diagnostics, therapeutics, vaccines and implementation and operational research.The total value of the grant/projects is Rs. 72.06 Cr.The total expenditure (budget released) till date is: Rs.49.94 Cr.The Committed expenditure for FY 19-20 is 8.67 Cr………………..
