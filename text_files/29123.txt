                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

65






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Targets under PMKSY




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the extent to which the targets fixed under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) launched in 2015-16 to enhance physical access of water on the farm and expand cultivable area under assured irrigation have been achieved; (b) the details of total amount of funds spent under the said yojana during the last five years, State-wise; and (c) the details of the efforts made under the said yojana for prevention of drought?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF JAL SHAKTI  (SHRI GAJENDRA SINGH SHEKHAWAT)(a) to (c)  A statement is laid on the Table of the House. ******     STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF STARRED QUESTION NO. †*65 TO BE ANSWERED ON 02.12.2021 IN LOK SABHA REGARDING “TARGETS UNDER PMKSY” ASKED BY DR. MANOJ RAJORIA“.(a)  Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) is an umbrella scheme, consisting of two major components, namely, Accelerated Irrigation Benefits Programme (AIBP), and Har Khet Ko Pani (HKKP) being implemented by Ministry of Jal Shakti. HKKP, in turn, consists of four sub-components, namely, Command Area Development and Water Management (CAD&WM), Surface Minor Irrigation (SMI), Repair, Renovation and Restoration (RRR) of Water Bodies, and Ground Water (GW) Development component.In addition, PMKSY also consists of two components being implemented by other Ministries. Per Drop More Crop (PDMC) component is being implemented by Department of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, while Watershed Development component (WDC) of PMKSY is being implemented by Department of Land Resources.The achievements of various components of PMKSY since 2015-16, are given below.PMKSY-AIBP INCLUDING CAD&WMAfter launch of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana - Accelerated Irrigation Benefits Programme (PMKSY-AIBP) in 2016, ninety-nine (99) on-going major/ medium irrigation projects were prioritized for assured financial assistance, along with their command area development and water management works. Out of these, 46 projects have been completed by the States Governments concerned, while another 21 projects have progress more than 90%. Against a target of 34.64 lakh hectare of additional irrigation, during 2016-2021 an additional potential of 22.74 lakh hectare has been created through these projects. Further, under CAD&WM, against a target of cultivable command area of 50 lakh hectare, 20.68 lakh hectare has been developed during 2015-21.PMKSY-HKKP (SMI & RRR of water bodies)Under PMKSY, SMI has achieved irrigation potential of 3.44 lakh hectare against a target of 2 lakh hectare. Under RRR of water bodies, achievement during the period is 1.19 lakh hectare, against a target of 1.5 lakh hectare. PMKSY-HKKP (GW)The ground water component of the scheme was taken up for implementation in May, 2019 after revision of the operational guidelines. An additional area of 35,438 hectare has been brought under irrigation through ground water upto March, 2021.PMKSY- PDMCAn area 57.30 lakh hectare has been covered under micro irrigation through PDMC scheme from 2015-16 to 2020-21. Contd…P/2PMKSY- WDAgainst the target of 11.5 lakh hectare, since 2015-16 to 2020-21 about 14.01 lakh hectare of additional area have been brought under protective irrigation. (b) The details of expenditure made on projects under components of PMKSY are placed at Annexure.(c) PMKSY is targeted towards drought proofing by augmenting irrigation potential and water harvesting. Further, under PMKSY-AIBP and CAD&WM, 59 number of projects specifically benefit drought prone areas, with additional irrigation potential of 20.47 lakh hectare. Out of these, 24 number of projects have been completed, and 13.27 lakh hectare irrigation potential has been achieved.******      ANNEXUREAnnexure referred to in reply to part (b) of Starred Question No. †*65 to be answered in Lok Sabha on 02.12.2021 regarding “TARGETS UNDER PMKSY”.S No	State	Expenditure incurred during 2016-2021 (Rs. in crore)	  1	Andhra Pradesh	3,269.604	 2	Arunachal Pradesh	340.833	 3	Assam	1,980.680	 4	Bihar	768.643	 5	Chhattisgarh	871.816	 8	Goa	182.970	 9	Gujarat	14,588.190	 6	Haryana	279.280	 7	Himachal Pradesh	622.590	 8	Jharkhand	2,540.311	 9	Karnataka	4,904.263	 10	Kerala	174.377	 11	Madhya Pradesh	4,905.728	 12	Maharashtra	16,463.302	 13	Manipur	939.359	 14	Meghalaya	229.281	 15	Mizorum	242.697	 16	Nagaland	535.347	 17	Odisha	6,239.329	 18	Punjab	168.430	 19	Rajasthan	3,657.740	 20	Sikkim	197.756	 21	Tamilnadu	2,363.271	 22	Telangana	7,211.946	 23	Tripura	160.280	 24	Uttar Pradesh	9,079.502	 25	Uttarakhand	329.925	 26	West Bengal	478.000	 27	UT - Jammu & Kashmir	629.879	 28	UT - Ladakh	29.471	 29	UT- Andaman & Nicobar Islands	0.500	  	TOTAL	84,385.299	 Note : Expenditure under Per Drop More Crop and Ground Water components are taken as central assistance released.***
