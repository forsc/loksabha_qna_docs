                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

344






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Incidents of Stone Pelting




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there has been a decline in the incidents of stone pelting since the scrapping of Article 370 in Jammu and Kashmir;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the number of stone pelting incidents reported during the period and the number of people arrested for involvement in such incidents and sent to jail;(d) whether the Government has identified the forces working behind it; and(e) if so, the action taken against such persons/organisations?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) Yes Sir.(b) & (c) Since 5th August, 2019 to 15th November, 2019, 765 persons have been arrested in 190 cases registered relating to stone pelting/ law & order. From 1st January, 2019 to 4th August, 2019, 361 number of such cases were registered. (d) & (e) The Government has initiated multipronged policies to check the stone  pelting  menace and  has succeeded in  curbing it to the  extent that a -2/...-2-LS.US.Q.NO.344 FOR 19.11.2019large number of trouble makers, instigators, mob mobilizes have been identified and various preventive measures have been taken against them which include detention under PSA and preventive arrests. Investigation has revealed that various separatist organisations and activists which are part of Hurriyat have been behind the incidents of stone pelting in Kashmir valley. NIA has chargesheeted 18 persons in the terror funding cases so far. *****
