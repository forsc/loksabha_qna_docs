                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1239






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Technical Education




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received any proposal from the State Government of Gujaratregarding the World Bank assisted Technical Education Quality Improvement Programme;(b) if so, the details of the proposals received during the last three years, college-wise; and(c) the details of the decisions taken so far on the above said proposals and the place-wise andcollege-wise details of the programmes sanctioned?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) to (c): Yes Madam. The Technical Education Quality Improvement Programme (TEQIP-III)project funded by the World Bank, for improving the quality of technical education is beingimplemented in 19 educationally backward States with effect from April 2017 for 3 years. Inaddition, well performing TEQIP-I & II institutes from States other than 19 focus states, includingfrom Gujarat, are also participating for mentoring purposes. Under this, the engineering collegesprepare Institution Development Proposals (IDPs), which are approved after due evaluation. So far,(10) IDPs have been received from Gujarat. Details of proposal are at Annexure.As per the proposals submitted by 10 institutes (listed at Annexure), only Birla VishvakarmaMahavidyalaya (BVM), Vallabh Vidynagar met the eligibility requirements and got selected throughchallenge method for participation in TEQIP-III.*****
