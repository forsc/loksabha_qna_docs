                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3632






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Training under PMKVY in Gujarat




Hasmukhbhai Somabhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)        whether unemployed youths were to be given training and employment under the centrally sponsored skill development scheme Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) (2016-20);(b)        the total number of unemployed youths who have been given training and the number of youths who have been given employment in the country, during the last three years and the current year so far;(c)        the details of the amount spent by Government under PMKVY in the country during each of the last three year till the date, in this regard; and(d)        the total amount spent by the Government under PMKVY during the said period in the State of Gujarat?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)        Yes, Sir.  Under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) (2016-2020), skill development training has been imparted to youth for becoming employable to earn their livelihood across the country.(b)        PMKVY has two training components, namely, Short Term Training (STT) and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL). Under PMKVY, placement opportunities are being provided to STT certified candidates, while RPL is not linked with placements as it recognizes the existing skills of candidate. Since FY2018-19 till 21.11.2021, 89.24 lakh candidates have been trained / oriented (STT: 33.04 lakh and RPL: 56.20 lakh). Further, under STT, 16.52 lakh candidates have been provided with the placement across the country during FY2018-19 till 21.11.2021.(c)        Under PMKVY, Rs. 4,483.55 Cr. (Rs. 3,808.35 Cr. under Central Component; Rs. 675.20 Cr. under State Component) has been spent by implementing agencies across the country in the last three years (FY 2018-21).
