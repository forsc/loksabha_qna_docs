                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

497






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.04.2022






Extension of Trains to Rajkot




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether trains which terminate at Ahmedabad are parked idle in the yard before the next journey; (b) if so, the names of those trains terminating at Ahmedabad along with the time these are parked idle in the yard, separately; (c) whether any public representative has given representation to extend a few trains, which are parked idle in the yard, upto Rajkot; (d) if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon; and (e) if not, the time likely to be taken for extension of the said trains upto   Rajkot?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a)	to (e)  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
