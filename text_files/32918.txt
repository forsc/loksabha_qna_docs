                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

266






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Construction of Roads under Bharatmala Pariyojana




Maddila  Gurumoorthy













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Goddeti Madhavi
                                                








                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                








                                                    Pocha Brahmananda Reddy
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                








                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there has been some percentage increase in road 	construction under Bharatmala Pariyojana in the State of Andhra 	Pradesh since the inception of the scheme and if so, the details 	thereof;(b)		the details of funds sanctioned, allocated and utilised for the 	purpose during the said period;(c)	the present status of the implementation of Raipur - Vizag 	Economic Corridor project being developed under Bharatmala 	Pariyojana Phase-I; and(d)		the funds sanctioned for the section of the corridor falling under 	Andhra Pradesh till date?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)   The Government of India had approved Bharatmala Pariyojana Phase-I in October, 2017 with an aggregate length of about 34,800 km (including 10,000 km residual National Highways Development Programme stretches) at an estimated outlay of Rs. 5,35,000/- crores. Length of 2,241 km has been envisaged for development under this scheme in the State of Andhra Pradesh. Out of which, works comprising length of 857 km having total cost of Rs. 30,551 crore have been awarded and length of about 311 km has been completed. (c) & (d)   Total length of Raipur- Vizag Economic Corridor is 464 km. Out of this, 356.268 km has been awarded. The corridor length in Andhra Pradesh is 99.629 km which is divided into 4 packages for construction. All 4 packages have been awarded with sanctioned cost of Rs. 3183.09 Cr.*****
