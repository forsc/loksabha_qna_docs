                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2729






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Washim-Badnera Rail Route




Navneet Ravi Rana














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to improve the means of connectivity and provide facilities to the farmers in the extremely backward Washim district, which is notoriously known as the ‘Cremation Ground’ of the farmers in terms of cultivation, in Wanhad (Berar) province in Central India; (b)	if so, the time by which the Government is likely to undertake the works of Washim-Badnera rail route; (c)	whether the survey for the same was approved in the year 2015 but it has not been completed till 2020; (d)	if so, the reasons for the delay in completion of the said work; and (e)	the time by which the said work is likely to be started and Washim district and erstwhile Marathwada district will be connected?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY ANDCONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a):   Washim is already connected on Railway network.  Washim Station is situated on Akola-Purna Railway Line.(b) and (c):   Survey for Narkhed-Washim (Badnera-Washim) was sanctioned in the year 2016-17.  Field Survey work has been completed.  (d) and (e):    Do not arise, as the project is not sanctioned. *****
