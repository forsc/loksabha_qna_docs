                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

205






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Inflationary Trend




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy













                                                    Y. S. Avinash Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the country is facing acute inflationary trend in the recent days;(b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and(c) the remedial measures taken or being taken by the Government to curtail the inflation?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (c)  No Sir. The Consumer Price Index – Combined (CPI – C) Inflation of the last three years and of the current year up to October, 2019 may be seen in Table – 1.Table 1: Consumer Price Index-Combined (CPI-C) Inflation	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	Apr-19	May-19	Jun-19	Jul-19	Aug-19	Sep-19	Oct-19CPI-C Inflation (per cent)	4.52	3.59	3.41	2.99	3.05	3.18	3.15	3.28	3.99	4.62Source: Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation*****
