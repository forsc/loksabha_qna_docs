                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3128






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






National Educational Alliance for Technology




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the main objective of National Educational Alliance for Technology (NEAT);(b)	whether the problems of the economically backward students are being solved independently through this scheme; and(c)	if so, the measures being taken to provide the best technical education to the economically backward students of Gujarat?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) to (c) Ministry of Education has announced National Educational Alliance for Technology (NEAT) as a Public-Private partnership model to bring the best technological Products in pedagogy on a single platform for the convenience of learners. NEAT is a national initiative and any student of economically and socially weaker sections across the country can benefit from NEAT courses/scheme. The Scheme has provisions for distribution of 25% free coupons to the students of higher education from weaker sections (Economically and Socially weaker) of society. *****
