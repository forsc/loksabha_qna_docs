                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4300






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Survey on Homeless Urban Poor




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has conducted any survey regarding the homeless urban poor people in the country including Rajasthan and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government is working on any scheme for upliftment of the urban poor and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has received any proposals to provide financial assistance for acquiring requisite land for housing projects from the States/UTs including Rajasthan; and (d) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise along with the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                     (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a):	Land and colonization are State subjects.  Identification of eligible beneficiaries including homeless is done by States/Union Territories (UTs).  Central Government provides different levels of financial assistance under four verticals of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY(U)].  States/UTs have undertaken demand survey under the Scheme for assessing actual demand of housing in urban areas.  The validated demand reported so far by States/UTs is around 112 lakhs.  As per the survey conducted so far by 19 States/UTs, including the State of Rajasthan, under Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NULM), the number of urban homeless persons is 1,71,949.  As per Census–2011, total houseless urban population is 9,38,348.(b):	Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs is implementing PMAY(U) since 25.06.2015 for providing assistance to States/UTs in addressing the housing requirement of the people belonging to Economically Weaker Sections (EWS), Lower Income Group (LIG) and Middle Income Group (MIG) categories in urban areas.  A total 83,68,861 houses have so far been sanctioned under the Scheme, out of this, 48,37,466 are at various stages of construction and 26,13,799 are completed. (c) & (d):	No, Sir.  Financial assistance is not provided for the land required for housing projects under the PMAY(U).  ******
