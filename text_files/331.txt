                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

376






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Vacancies in Ministry




Pushpendra Singh Chandel














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the posts lying vacant in the Ministry of HumanResource Development;(b) whether the Government is taking any special steps to fill upthe said vacancies;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’(MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT)(a) The details of vacant posts in the Main Secretariat of Ministry ofHuman Resource Development is attached as Annexure-I.(b)to (d) The posts belonging to various cadres such as Indian StatisticalServices (ISS), Sub-ordinate Statistical Services (SSS), Indian EconomicServices (IES), Central Secretariat Services (CSS), Central SecretariatStenographers Services (CSSS), Central Secretariat Clerical Services (CSCS),Central Secretariat Official Language Services (CSOLS) etc. and postsunder Central Staffing Scheme are filled up by respective CadreControlling Authorities as mentioned at Annexure I (A). In respect ofvacancies mentioned at Annexure I (B), it is stated that filling up ofvacancies is an ongoing and continuous process. These vacancies are filledthrough promotion/ direct recruitment/ deputation as prescribed in theirrecruitment rules issued by this Ministry.***
