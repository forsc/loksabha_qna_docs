                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

883






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Projects to Boost Eco-Tourism




Shardaben Anilbhai Patel













                                                    Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any proposal/ sanctioned projects to boost Eco-Tourism in the country and if so, the details thereof, State/UT wise;(b)	the State/UT wise details of the steps taken by the Government to boost Eco-Tourism projects in different areas including Gujarat; (c)	the details of the Central Financial assistance released to different States/Union Territories during the last three years along with the funds spent in this regard, year-wise; (d)	whether the Government has received proposals from different State Governments for the development and promotion of Eco-Tourism; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof along with its present status?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 				      (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e):	 Development and Promotion of tourism, including eco-tourism, is primarily the responsibility of the respective State Government/UT Administration. Recognising the potential of eco-tourism in the country, the Ministry of Tourism has identified Eco Circuit as one of the fifteen thematic circuits for development under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme to promote eco-tourism in the country. The  Ministry  of  Tourism  has  sanctioned  06 projects under  Eco Theme  in  Swadesh Darshan scheme, the state-wise details of which  are  at  Annexure. The Ministry has  not  sanctioned  any  projects  in  State of Gujarat  under  Eco  Theme  however  3 projects  under the  themes of  Buddhist and  Heritage  are  sanctioned  in  Gujarat  for Rs 178.95 Cr. Presently no new projects are being considered under the Swadesh Darshan scheme. ********
