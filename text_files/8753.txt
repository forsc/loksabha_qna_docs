                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2321






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






e-NAM




Mahabali Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the e-National Agriculture Market (e-NAM) has not been working properly due to lesser connectivity of internet;(b)	 if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	the steps being taken by the Government to tide over the said problem and to encourage online trade?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	State Governments have already been advised to explore the possibilities of upgrading the internet connectivity with internet service providers. As per the response received from the States, National Agriculture Market (e-NAM) platform is working properly and sufficient speed of internet connectivity has been provided to e-NAM mandis. Close monitoring of the progress through meetings at various levels and visits of the officrs to e-NAM mandis is carried out. Various  steps  taken  by  the  Government has led to inter-mandi and inter-State trade on e-NAM platform.********
