                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

937






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Floriculture Regional Station




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that there are demands since long from floriculture farmers for setting up of full-fledged Floriculture Regional Station with required scientists and other technical staff to guide Kadiyam nursery;(b) if so, the steps taken so far in this regard; and(c) the time by which the above station is going to be set up?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE 		  	 	   	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c): The Government is aware of the demands for setting up full-fledged Floriculture Regional Station with required scientists and other technical staff to guide Kadiyam nursery. 	Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) has set up a Regional Station of ICAR-Directorate of Floricultural Research (ICAR-DFR), Pune at Vemagiri (near Rajamahendravaram), Kadiyam Mandal, East Godavari District, Andhra Pradesh during 2017 in view of the requirement of nursery industry and farmers of floriculture of the region. Five scientist positions have been sanctioned for the Regional Station.*****
