                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

611






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Court Practice




Kripanath Mallah














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has notified Section 30 of the Advocates Act, 1961 recently, enabling the lawyers to practice in courts across the country irrespective of their enrolment in any bar council without the need to transfer licence to their desired States;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the date from which this section will come into force?	






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER  OF  LAW AND JUSTICE, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS                & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASASD)(a) to (c) : Yes Sir. Section 30 of the Advocates Act, 1961 has been notified on June, 9, 2011 and has come into force on June 15, 2011.
