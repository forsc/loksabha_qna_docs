                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4018






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Jobs on Compassionate Ground




Bhagwanth Khuba













                                                    Pratima Bhoumik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government has any data with regard to the deaths of  Railway staff during their service period/on duty;(b) if so, the zone-wise details thereof and if not, the reasons  therefor; (c) the number of jobs that have been given on compassionate ground to the kin/dependents of the deceased staff in the last three years, zone-wise; and(d)  the number of cases pending with the Government with regard to compassionate appointments, zone-wise and the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d):  	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.***STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 4018 BY SHRI BHAGWANTH KHUBA AND MS. PRATIMA BHOUMIK TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 18.03.2020 REGARDING JOBS ON COMPASSIONATE GROUND.(a) to (d):	Yes, Sir.  The data of deaths of Government Servants during service/on duty and the details of jobs given on Compassionate Ground to the kin/dependents for the last three years and the current year zone-wise and year-wise are given in Appendix-I.As  regards cases in which Compassionate Ground appointment (CGA) has not been given, these include cases wherein request for CGA has been rejected by the competent authority after due consideration.  Further, in some cases,  CGA has not been given either because there had been no request for the same or the applicant was minor, thus not eligible.  In other cases, either some legal issue of heir is involved or the matter is sub judice or the complete documents have not been submitted by the applicants.***
