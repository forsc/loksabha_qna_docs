                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2116






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Measures to check Child Marriages




Karti P. Chidambaram














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the number of cases of child marriage reported during the last one year, State-wise;(b)	the steps taken by the Government towards attaining the Sustainable Development Goal of eliminating early and forced marriage of children;(c)	whether a spike in the number of cases of child marriage due to the pandemic  crisis has been reported?(d)	if so, the details thereof and the measure taken by the Government to check the situation;(e)	whether the increase is in child marriages is due to domestic challenges and obligations faced by young girls; and(f)	if so, the details thereof and its potential impact on education of children?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)  (a) to (f) :  National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) compiles and publishes information on crimes in its publication “Crime in India”. The published reports are available till the year 2018. State-wise and year-wise total number of cases of child marriage is available on the website of NCRB at https://ncrb.gov.in. However, the National Commission for Protection of Child Rights (NCPCR) has reported registering of 111 cases / complaints of Child Marriage during the year 2019-20. The State/UT-wise details of cases/ complaints of Child Marriage received in NCPCR in the year 2019-20 are given in Annexure.           The Government has enacted ‘The Prohibition of Child Marriage Act, 2006’ in order to prohibit child marriages. Advisories are issued to States/ UTs, from time to time, to ensure effective implementation of the Act.  The Ministry of Women and Child Development implements the ‘Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP)’ scheme in which the creation of awareness on matters pertaining to gender equality and discouraging of child marriage is an important focus area. The National Commission for Protection of Child Rights (NCPCR) also undertakes awareness programmes and consultations with stakeholders from time to time in this regard. ****ANNEXURE ANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF THE LOK SABHA UN-STARRED QUESTION NO.2116 FOR 23.09.2020 ASKED BY SHRI KARTI P.
