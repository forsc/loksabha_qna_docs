                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

151






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Fish Farmer Producer Organisation




Riti Pathak














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether  the Government has any scheme to promote the Fish Farmer Producer  Organisation(FFPO);b)	if so, the details thereof;c)	whether the Government is working any scheme to promote the Animal Husbandry; andd)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRYAND DAIRYING(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) to (b):  Yes sir.  The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Fisheries Husbandry and Dairying under flagship scheme namely “Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY)”- a scheme to bring about Blue Revolution through sustainable and responsible development of fisheries sector in India as a part of the Atma Nirbhar Bharat relief package is extending support to promote the Fish Farmers Producer Organisation (FFPO).  Further, 100% central assistance is admissible for setting up of FFPOs in the States/UTs to economically empower the fishers and fish farmers and enhance their bargaining power. (c) to (d): 	The Department of Animal Husbandry & Dairying , Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry & Dairying is implementing Rashtriya Gokul Mission, National Programme for dairy development, National Live Stock Mission, Livestock health and disease control, Animal Husbandry infrastructure development fund, National Animal disease control programme to promote Animal Husbandry sector.*****
