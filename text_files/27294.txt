                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2976






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Bhavnagar-Somnath Highway Project




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of work on Bhavenagar-Somnath National Highway (NH) project in Gujarat;(b)	the year in which the said project was sanctioned along with the target fixed for completion of the project;(c)	whether the work on this NH has not completed even after so many years and if so, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the steps being taken by the Government for early completion of this project?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) The work of Bhavenagar- Somnath National Highway (NH) project was sanctioned in 2016 with completion period of 30 months. The stretch was split into 6 construction packages. The physical progress of these packages are as under: Sr. No.	Package	Length (in km)	Physical Progress(i)	Bhavnagar – Talaja (Package - I)	48.045	89.31%(ii)	Talaja – Mahuva (Package - II)	45.460	20.34%(iii)	Mahuva – Kagvadar (Package - III)	40.020	32%(iv)	Kagvadar – Una (Package - IV)	40.980	90%(v)	Una-Kodinar (Package-V)	40.95	64.81%(vi)	Kodinar-Veraval (Package-VI)	41	32.7%(c)	The work has been delayed due to various reasons such as land acquisition, financial issues with concessionaires and COVID pandemic etc. (d)	The works of packages-II & III were terminated and re-awarded on EPC mode to complete balance work.*****
