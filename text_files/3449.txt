                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3135






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Check Dams




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the number of operative check dams at present in the country, State/UT wise; (b) whether the said dams are regularly cleaned and if so, the details thereof; (c) the operative monitoring system for regular cleaning of these dams; (d) whether the operative monitoring system can trace for non-cleaning of these dams and if so, the details thereof for the last three years; and (e) the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (e)	Water being a State subject, construction, operation and maintenance of water resources projects including check dams is primarily States’ responsibility.  The information related to number of check dams, their functionality and their upkeep etc. is kept at the respective State level. As per information received from M/o Rural Development, 6,25,539 check dams have been built under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS).  State-wise details of number of check dams built under MGNREGS are given at Annexure.***** ANNEXUREAnnexure referred to in reply to parts (a) to (e) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.†3135 to be answered in Lok Sabha on 11.07.2019 on “Check Dams” State-wise details of number of check dams built under MGNREGS as on 09.07.2019S No.	State	Completed Work Since Inception1	Andaman and Nicobar	02	Andhra Pradesh	241863	Arunachal Pradesh	384	Assam	4475	Bihar	60476	Dadra & Nagar Haveli	17	Daman & Diu	08	Goa	09	Gujarat	2354610	Haryana	86711	Himachal Pradesh	639212	Jammu and Kashmir	333913	Karnataka	7237814	Kerala	653715	Madhya Pradesh	27948616	Maharashtra	4470917	Lakshadweep	118	Manipur	22719	Meghalaya	301120	Mizoram	12521	Nagaland	41222	Odisha	1394323	Puducherry	024	Punjab	9125	Rajasthan	3019126	Sikkim	1927	Tamil Nadu	1029328	Tripura	1630529	Uttar Pradesh	2875530	West Bengal	921331	Chhattisgarh	1629032	Jharkhand	743033	Uttarakhand	714034	Telangana	14120 	Total	625539*****
