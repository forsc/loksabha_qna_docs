                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2463






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Separate Insolvency and Companies Act




Ravneet Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to bring in a separate insolvency and companies act for small and medium sized businesses and if so, the details thereof; and(b)	whether the Government plans to offer lower corporate taxes for smaller companies and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCEAND CORPORATE AFFAIRS                                               (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR) ????? ?????????????????????????????????????                                              (???? ?????? ???? ?????)(a)	No Sir. The Government has no proposal as of now to bring in a separate insolvency and companies act for small and medium sized businesses.(b)	The Union Budget 2019-20 has proposed to extend the lower rate of 25% corporate tax to all companies with annual turnover up to Rs. 400 crore from Rs. 250 crore at present, thereby covering 99.3% of the companies.*******
