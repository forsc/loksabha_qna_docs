                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1477






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Formation of Five Special Tourism Zones




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to set up Special Tourism Zones in partnership with State Governments; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the locations identified for this purpose, State/UT-wise; (c) 	whether these special zones will be anchored as Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs) that will be set up in partnership with the States as announced in Union Budget for 2017-18 and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	the funds allocated and expenditure incurred in this regard; and (e) 	the percentage of utilization of the funds released for the creation of the Five Special Tourism Zones?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (e):	Pursuant to Budget announcements of 2017-18, the Ministry of Tourism has initiated action on formulation of scheme guidelines for development of five Special Tourism Zones in the country anchored on SPV (Special Purpose Vehicle) in partnership with the states. The Ministry envisages to implement the scheme on challenge model.The scheme is not approved yet and no locations has been identified for development.  *******
