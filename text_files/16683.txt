                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

343






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Digital Devices for Differently Abled Students




E. T. Mohammed Basheer














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government has evolved any programme of action on the development of digital devices for the differently abled students; (b) If so, the details thereof;(c)whether the Government has entered into any MoU with other countries for the skill development and technology development of differently abled students; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR)(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. The Department implements a Research and Development programme under the Scheme for Implementation of Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 (SIPDA) which inter-alia supports research on development/innovation in rehabilitation sector, which includes digital devices, for the benefit of Persons with Disabilities. Further, under Make in India and ATMANIRBHAR BHARAT initiative, Artificial Limbs Manufacturing Corporation of India (ALIMCO), a Central Public Sector Enterprise (CPSE) under the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities has recently developed its own indigenous prototype Smart Cane, a digital device, which is under extensive trial following which its in- house production will start.(c) No, Sir. The Government has not entered into any MoU with other countries for the skill development and technology development of students with disabilities.(d)  Does not arise in view of answer to (c) above.*****
