                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3477






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Training in Haryana




Sunita Duggal














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of students of Haryana being imparted skill development training by various ministries and the Skill Development Ministry;(b)	the number of trainees out of those who have got employment;(c)	the percentage of female students out of the said number;(d)	whether the Government is imparting training to the students of Haryana in the field of agriculture as per the need of State; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	 Skill India Mission (National Skill Development Mission) launched in July 2015. The Government of India is implementing more than 40 skill development schemes/ programmes across 20 Central Ministries/ Departments, for providing skill development training on a pan India basis, including the State of Haryana. In Haryana, under different schemes, 13,61,863 persons have been skilled. Details are in Table I below. (b)	Placement is one of the requisite outcomes in PMKVY and Fee based programme of NSDC, but not in other schemes; 34.09% and 54.88% of trained (skilled) persons, respectively, under the two schemes as mentioned, have got placement. Details are in Table I below.(c)	Out of the trainees those placed, 50.06% and 22.7% are women in PMKVY and Fee based programmes of NSDC, respectively. Details are in Table I below.Details and the Table for parts (a), (b) & (c) aboveThe Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship (MSDE) is implementing the following Schemes for skill development of youth including women in Haryana viz. Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) Scheme, National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS), and Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS) through the Industrial Training Institutes. The details of persons trained under these schemes since 2016-17 in Haryana are given below:Table ISl.No.	Scheme	Trained (in number)	Percentage of trained femalestudents	Reported Placed / Self- employed	Percentage of female students reported placed1.	PMKVY 2.0(CSCM) + PMKVY 3.0 (CSCM)	511664	34.09%	1,43,023	50.06%2. Under the Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Urban Livelihoods Mission (DAU-NULM) of the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs, 20,667 candidates have been trained in the State of Haryana since its launch in September 2013. 4,016 beneficiaries have been assisted for setting up individual/ group micro enterprises under this scheme, till November, 2021. (d)	Yes, Sir.(e):	16,609 candidates in the State of Haryana have been given training in the field of agriculture under PMKVY 2.0 (2016-2020) as on 16.12.2021. Besides, 8,162 candidates have been trained in the field of agriculture under the fee-based programmes of NSDC. Further, Agriculture Skill Council of India (ASCI), under the aegis of MSDE, facilitates skill training programmes in Haryana under various Central and State funded schemes like PMKVY, Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana, Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture, and schemes being implemented through the Haryana Skill Development Mission. Skill training in Haryana has been imparted in 37 Job Roles/ Qualification Packs developed by ASCI across different programmes.
