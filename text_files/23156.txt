                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4286






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Construction of Engineering College




Horen Sing Bey














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the status of Construction of Engineering College at Chakso in West Karbi Anglong and up-gradation of Assam University at Diphu Campus to full-fledged Central University;(b) whether the Government has any specific deadline to establis Engineering College at Chakso in West Karib Anglong and up- gradation of Assam University at Diphu Campus to full-fledged Central University under Clause no. 5.1 & 5.4 respectively under Memorandum of Settlement (MoS) signed with Govt. of India, Govt. of Assam and UPDS;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d):	As per record, Government of Assam has decided to establish engineering college at Karbi Anglong, Diphu and the land has already been allotted in favour of establishment of engineering College in Karbi Anglong, Diphu.Further, as approved by the National Development Council during the 11th Five Year Plan, the Government was required to establish one Central University in each of the States that did not have Central University. Assam already has two Central Universities, namely Tezpur University & Assam University (and one satellite campus at Diphu in Karbi Anglong).*****
