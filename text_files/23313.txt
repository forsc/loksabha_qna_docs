                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4373






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Purchase and Export of Cereals




Sumedhanand Saraswati














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether export of major cereals like wheat and rice is on the rise during the last three years;(b)       if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether it is also a fact that their purchase from the farmers by Government agencies is also on the rise; and(d)	If so, the details of such purchase from farmers during each of the three years including the current year, State-wise and agri-commodity-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER of AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????             (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)&(b) :     The details of  export of major cereals like Wheat and rice (basmati and non -basmati) during last three years indicate an increase in export of basmati rice during 2018-19  as against export in 2017-18 but a minor short fall in 2019-20. However, during current year 2020-21 both Wheat and non- basmati rice are showing significant increase in spite of pandemic.The requisite details are annexed at Annexure- I.(c)&d):     The purchase of wheat and rice from farmers by Government agencies has increased from 2017-18 to 2019-20. The details of state wise procurement of Paddy/rice, and Wheat during last three years and current year by FCI and NAFED are respectively at Annexure–II&III.
