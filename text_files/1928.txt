                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1689






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Food Processing Industries




Chandra Prakash Joshi













                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of food processing industries in the country at present along with the details thereof, State/UT-wise especially in regard to Gujarat, Maharashtra and Rajasthan; (b) 	whether the Government is formulating any scheme or proposes to set up any new food processing unit in other States of the country including Gujarat, Maharashtra and Rajasthan; and (c)	 if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a):	As per the latest Annual Survey of Industries, 2016-17 there are 39,748 registered food processing units/factories in the country. As per NSSO Survey, 2005-06, there are 24,59,929 number of unincorporated enterprises in the unorganized segment of food processing sector in the country. The State / UT-wise list of such units in the country including the States of Gujarat, Maharashtra and Rajasthan is at Annexure-I. (b) & (c): The Government is already implementing a Central Sector Umbrella scheme- PRADHAN MANTRI KISAN SAMPADA YOJANA (PMKSY) with a total outlay of Rs.6000 crore for the period 2016-20 coterminous with the 14th Finance Commission cycle with the following scheme components:i.	Mega Food Parks ii.	Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure iii.	Creation / Expansion of Food Processing & Preservation Capacitiesiv.	Infrastructure for Agro-processing Clusters v.	Creation of Backward and Forward Linkagesvi.	Food Safety and Quality Assurance Infrastructure vii.	Human Resources and Institutions The Ministry is also implementing the scheme "Operation Greens" as a vertical of PMKSY since November, 2018 for integrated development of value chain of tomato, onion and potato (TOP) crops in selected States on pilot basis that includes the States of Gujarat and Maharashtra.The schemes are demand driven. Financial assistance to the eligible applicant is provided as per the scheme guidelines against the Expression of Interest issued by the Ministry from time to time. Under these schemes, credit-linked capital subsidy in the form of grants-in-aid is provided to the individuals, farmers, Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs), Entrepreneurs, Cooperatives, Societies, Self Help Groups (SHGs), Private Companies and Central/ State PSUs etc. for setting up of food processing industries/ units/ projects. A Statement indicating State/UT wise number of food processing projects approved under the above schemes including in the States of Gujarat, Maharashtra and Rajasthan is at Annexure-II. *****
