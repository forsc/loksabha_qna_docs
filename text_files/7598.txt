UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1244






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Relaxation in Norms for Construction around Historical Monuments




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has relaxed the norms for the identifications of No Construction Zones in the vicinity of archaeological monuments in the suburban areas of Greater Chennai, Pallavaram, Mambakkam, Sembakkam,Selaiyur and Tambaram under the Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains (Amendment) Bill, 2017;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard and to relax the restricted areas for the construction and renovation of houses under the erstwhile Ancient Monuments Preservation Act, 1972;(c)	whether the Union Government has taken any decision in this regard;(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(e)	whether the Central Monuments Authority has provided any clearance or NoC to housing and construction activities after 2017 and if so, thedetails thereof, State-wise and month-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	No, Sir. (b)	There is no Ancient Monuments Preservation Act, 1972 as such no action could be taken. (c)&(d)	Question does not arise.  (e)	The details clearances or NoC given by National Monuments Authority are at Annexure.
