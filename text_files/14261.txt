                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3080






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Sports Infrastructure




Raju Bista













                                                    Pratima Bhoumik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government will share the number of sporting infrastructure established and upgraded under the Khelo India Campaign in the country, if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Tripura and West Bengal of the infrastructure created during the last three years along with the amount spent on it;(b) the details of participation from West Bengal in the Khelo India Campaign particularly in the districts of Darjeeling, Kalimpong and North Dinajpur;(c) whether the Government proposes to consider the demand for a state-of-the-art stadium in Darjeeling; and(d) whether about 7 proposals of creating sports facilities in the State of Tripura under Khelo India Campaign is pending with the Government and if so, the details thereof and the reasons for delay in sanctioning the proposals?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) Details of State / Union Territory-wise number of projects sanctioned and funds released during the last three years and the current year under Khelo India Scheme, including in Tripura and West Bengal, are given at Annexure – I.(b) The details of participation from West Bengal in the Khelo India Games are given at Annexure – II.(c) Sports being a State subject, the responsibility to construct stadium rests with the respective State/Union Territory Governments. This Ministry does not have any scheme to build a state-of-the-art stadium in Darjeeling.(d) A total of 23 project proposals have been received in this Ministry from Tripura for creation of sports infrastructure under the Khelo India Scheme as detailed in Annexure - III. In view of the fact that Sports is a State subject and available funds under Khelo India Scheme being limited to meet the needs of projects already sanctioned, it has not been possible to sanction these projects.****
