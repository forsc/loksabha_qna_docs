                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1888






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Proposal of Secunderabad-HyderabadVijayawada Rail Line




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government has any proposal to build a railway line from Secunderabad/Hyderabad to Vijayawada via Suryapet and Kodad; and(b)	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	 No, Sir. However, rail connectivity from Secunderabad/Hyderabad is available for Vijaywada via Kazipet. Further railway station Miryalaguda and Jaggayyapeta Town are at the distance of 32.53 km & 19.30 km from Suryapet and Kodad respectively and they also serve passengers from these places. (b)	 Does not arise.*****
