                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1831






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






DGCIS




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is planning to make available the entire data of Director General of Commercial Intelligence and Statistics(DGCIS) to the public; (b)	if so, the details thereof along with the current status of the proposal;(c)	whether DGCIS is charging for the data and has generated revenue from the sale of such data during the last ten years and if so, the details thereof, year-wise;(d)	the details of funds allocated and spent for the data collection by DGCIS in the last ten years and the current year; and (e)	whether the Government is making service export data from States and Union Territories public or is it notifying databases in line with DGCIS for commodities exports and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF  COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY  (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a) & (b):	 As per Data Dissemination Policy of DGCIS, only country-wise, commodity-wise and port-wise information along with State-wise export are disseminated to public. At present, there is no proposal to change this policy. (c):	Private users of trade data are charged @ Re 1.00 for each record/row of data and subscription charges for annual bulk data for Indian users are Rs. 350,000/- per year and US$ 7500.00 per year for foreign users.
