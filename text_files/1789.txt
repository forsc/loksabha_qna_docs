                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1681






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Elections of Panchayats




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that elections could not be held due to various reasons in many Panchayats;(b) if so, the details thereof along with a list of Panchayats where elections could not be held during the last three years; and(c) the time by which the Ministry proposes to complete the election process in all those Panchayats where elections are long overdue?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c)  ‘Panchayat’ being ‘Local Bodies’ is a State subject under the Constitution of India.   Under Part IX of the Constitution of India the Panchayats’ creation and holding election through their respective Panchayati Raj Acts is incumbent on the States. Article 243K of the Constitution of India empowers the State Legislatures to make laws regarding Panchayati Raj  elections. Accordingly, Panchayat Raj elections are to be conducted every five years, under the overall superintendence, direction and control of the State Election Commission.. Normally the Panchayati Raj elections are held as per schedule in all Part IX States except in the cases of pendency of court cases, delimitation etc. The details of States/Union Territory where Panchayat Elections are due are given at Annexure.**********
