                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

253






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






NH Projects




Riti Pathak













                                                    Shankar Lalwani
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of NH projects in Madhya Pradesh which are lagging behind for one year and more than two years, separately, along with the reasons therefor;(b) the steps being taken by the Government to expedite the works in order to complete the projects without any further delay;(c) the funds allocated for the National Highways (NHs) in Madhya Pradesh during each of the last three years;(d) the time by which the construction of National Highway from Indore to Ichhapur is likely to be completed; and(e) the time by which the construction of bypass road from Indore to Netaji Nagar is likely to be started?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (e)- A statement is laid on the Table of the House.
