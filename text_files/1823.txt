                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1585






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Inter-connectivity of Villages




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to construct short distance roads as link roads on the lines of Pradhan Mantri SadakYojana to provide inter-connectivity in villages and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether there is any proposal to conduct a survey for constructing short distance roads to inter-connect the link roads; and (c)	if so, the details thereof and the time limit for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	No, Sir. There is no such proposal under consideration as of now under the Pradhan  Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) to construct short distance roads as link roads to provide inter-connectivity in villages.(c):	Question does not arise in view of above reply.*****
