                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1836






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Land Acquired for Coal Production




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)        the name of the companies under the control of Coal India Limited (CIL) that have acquired agriculture and forest land for coal production during the last three years along with the area acquired by each of these companies;(b)        the company-wise area of the land lying unused out of the total acquired land at present;(c)        the reasons for this unnecessary acquisition; and(d)       the company-wise area of the coal mines being fully utilized and the land adjacent to them along with the details of the use thereof''?






ANSWER




  MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a):       There are seven coal producing subsidiaries namely, ECL, BCCL, CCL, NCL, WCL, SECL and MCL under the control of Coal India Limited that have acquired agriculture and forest land for coal production.  The subsidiary-wise details of land acquisition in the last three years are as under:-                                                                                                        (Area in Ha.)Subsidiary	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20ECL	126.73	134.34	179.75BCCL	0.00	0.00	0.00CCL	7590.47	306.67	74.30NCL	0.00	0.00	0.00WCL	165.40	30.25	72.20SECL	3484.714	1366.66	209.777MCL	0.00	0.00	0.00 (b):       At present, the company-wise area of the land lying unused out of the total acquired land are given below:- Subsidiary	Area of unused landECL	73.33 Ha.BCCL	NilCCL	NilNCL	945 Ha.WCL	556.09 Ha.SECL	NilMCL	Nil (c):  No land was acquired unnecessarily. Land will be utilised as per mine development plan.   (d):      Information is being collected. ****
