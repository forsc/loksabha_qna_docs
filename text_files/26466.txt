                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2299






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






North Eastern Cultue and Heritage in CBSE and NCERT




Pallab Lochan Das














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is fact that the Government have taken various initiatives for the promotion of culture and heritage of North Eastern States in CBSE and NCERTbooks;(b) if so, Whether the Government is planning to introduce chapters about North Eastern States in NCERT textbook.(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the details therefor ?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d) The National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) has informed that the contents about various aspects of the North Eastern Region have been included in the current NCERT textbooks for different disciplines of Social Sciences such as History, Geography, Political Science and Sociology across the stages. Besides, supplementary reading material, namely, “Women of North-East India: Making a Difference” and “North East India: People, History and Culture” have also been developed by NCERT. The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) follows the textbooks developed by the NCERT.****
