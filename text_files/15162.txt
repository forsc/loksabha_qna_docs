                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3786






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Assistance under NDRF




Ravneet Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the funds demanded and the funds disbursed to various States/UTs in the country under the National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF) during the last three years, year and State/UT-wise;(b) the details of the States/UTs which have not fully utilised these NDRF funds during the said period, year-wise;(c) whether the Government intends to develop any mechanism for proper and quick assessment and timely delivery of the NDRF funds to the States to ensure its correct utilisation and distribution; and(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b):	 Financial assistance is provided from National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF), as per laid down procedure.  State-wise details of assistance sought and releases from NDRF during the year 2017-18 to 2019-20 (as on 29.02.2020) are given in Annexure.  	As per the scheme of NDRF each State Government is responsible to ensure that the expenditure incurred out of the funds received under the NDRF is in accordance with the norms approved by the Central Government.  The State Accountant General is required to monitor the expenditure as per the items and norms of assistance. The Comptroller and Auditor  General of India conducts audit-2/...-2-LS.US.Q.NO. 3786 FOR 17.03.2020of NDRF.  All the States have reported full utilization of NDRF except partial utilization by the State Government of Andhra Pradesh (for cyclone ‘titli’ of 2018 & drought of 2018-19) and Kerala (for flood/ landslides of 2018-19).  (c) & (d):  The Government of India has taken a significant decision on 19th August, 2019 that an Inter-Ministerial Central Team (IMCT) would be constituted immediately in the aftermath of any natural calamity of severe nature, even before receipt of Memorandum from the State, which would visit the affected areas of State so as to have the first hand assessment of the damages caused and relief work carried out by the State Administration.  For timely delivery of NDRF, States have to ensure timely submission of detailed information/memorandum as per SDRF/NDRF guidelines, which are available on Ministry of Home Affairs’ website: www.ndmindia.nic.in.*******
