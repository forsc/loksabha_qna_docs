                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2356






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






Net Zero Carbon Emitter




Asaduddin Owaisi














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Indian Railways has plans to become ‘Net-Zero Carbon Emitter’ by 2030;(b) if so, whether the Railways plans to use its 20% parking capacity at railway stations or office complex for setting up electronic vehicle charging infrastructure; and(c)  if so, the detailed plan chalked out by Railways for using its infrastructure for electronic charging facilities especially at railway stations where such vehicles are likely to increase in the near future?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) Yes, Sir. Indian Railways (IR) has set a target of becoming net zero carbon emitter by 2030 by utilizing renewable energy to meet its power requirement and reducing its energy consumption thereby reducing its carbon emission.(b) and (c) Directions have been issued to Zonal Railways and Production Units to examine and take necessary steps for setting up of Electric Vehicle (EV) charging facility at railway stations and office complexes.*****
