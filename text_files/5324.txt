                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4916






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Agricultural Universities and Institutions




Vishnu Dutt Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of agricultural universities/institutions in India under the Union Government along with their functioning and allocation of funds during the last 5 years;(b)	whether the agricultural universities/institutes could fruitfully use the allocated funds, if so, the details thereof and if not, the action taken by the Government in this regard; (c)	the details of the total number of private agricultural universities in the country along with their locations; (d)	the admission process in these universities; (e)	whether there is any control of the Government in the functioning of such universities; and (f)	if so, the details thereof and if not, whether the Government would consider setting up any council/committee for monitoring of the functioning of these universities?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	There are three Central Agricultural Universities (CAU) namely Central Agricultural University (CAU), Imphal, Rani Laxmi Bai Central Agriculture University (RLBCAU), Jhansi,  Dr. Rajendra Prasad Central Agriculture University (Dr.RPCAU), Pusa, Bihar and four ICAR-Deemed Universities, viz. National Dairy Research Institute (NDRI), Karnal, Indian Veterinary Research Institute (IVRI), Izatnagar, Indian Agriculture Research Institute (IARI), New Delhi, Central Institute of Fisheries Education (CIFE), Mumbai under the Union Government.  Further, there are 101 ICAR- Research Institutes/ Research Centres/ Project Directorate/ Bureaux and 11 ICAR-Agricultural Technology Application Research Institutes (ATARI). Main function of the above Agricultural Universities/Institutions are to plan, undertake, aid, promote and coordinate education, research and its application in Agriculture, Agro-Forestry, Animal Husbandry, Fisheries, Home Science & allied sciences. The admission in CAUs is done through entrance exam conducted by ICAR/National Testing Agency (NTA). In case of CAU, Imphal there is a provision of admission of students from North Eastern States up to 85% of total seats. In four Deemed Universities (DUs) (NDRI, IVRI, CIFE and IARI) admission is done through ICAR/NTA/DU concerned.(e)	Yes, Sir.(f)	The Central Agricultural Universities (CAUs) are under the administrative control of Union Government. Private Agricultural Universities are under the control of the concerned State Governments.  Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR), an autonomous body under the Union Government does accreditation through National Agricultural Education Accreditation Board (NAEAB) to ensure the quality of Agricultural Education in the Universities.*******
