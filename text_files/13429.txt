                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2090






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Acquisition of Defence Land




Shrikant Eknath Shinde













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Ministry has assured to give the working permission for Defence land required for flyover project in Ahmednagar and if so, the details and the present status of the working permission to be given to the projects;(b) whether it is true that the proposal from both the NHAI and the Commissioner, Ahmednagar Municipal Corporation has been received in the Ministry and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether Ahmednagar Municipal Corporation has given undertaking regarding payment of the cost of Defence land required for Ahmednagar Flyover and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether his ministry proposes to give necessary permission for transfer of Defence land for the projects immediately so that the project is not delayed; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) to (e): The working permission is processed in consultation with other departments/DGDE/other stakeholders after the receipt of the proposal and finalization of the mode of transfer with other terms and conditions.	A proposal from NHAI and Commissioner, Ahmednagar Municipal Corporation alongwith the undertaking regarding payment of the cost of defence land has been received in the Ministry on 21.12.2018 and 13.11.2019 respectively.*****
