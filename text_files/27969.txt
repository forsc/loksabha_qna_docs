                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3514






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Demand of Fertilizers




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the demand for fertilizers has increased during the last five year and the current year,if so, the details thereof, area-wise and State-wise along with the reason therefor;(b)	whether the fertilizer producing companies of public and private sector have achieved the targets set for production of fertilizer in the country;(c)	if so, the details of production targets set/achieved during the said period,   company-wise and fertilizer-wise; and(d)	the details of the number of complaints received from some States regarding non-availability of fertilizers and the action taken/steps on these complaints during the said period, States/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS(SHRI MANSUKH  MANDAVIYA)(a):   The demand of urea has been increasing in the last five financial years. However, the demand of P&K fertilizers has remained uneven during the said period. The requirement of fertilizers from 2016-17 to 2021-22 (upto July, 2021) is as under:                                                        Requirement (Figures in LMT)Year-Wise	Urea	DAP	MOP	NPKS2016-17	        289.09	100.57	33.36	102.582017-18	       298.00	98.77	33.90	98.192018-19	       300.04	98.40	36.81	97.682019-20	       335.26	103.30	38.12	104.822020-21	       350.64	107.76	35.51	108.002021-22 (upto July, 2021)           116.75	43.93	13.17	39.44*ProvisionalSource:- Fertilizer Statistics and dbtfert.nic.in 2020-21(upto July)The state-wise requirement of fertilizers of 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19, 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 (upto July, 2021) is attached at Annexure I, II, III, IV, V and VI respectively.(b) & (c):   The details of the target production and actual production from 2016-17 to 2021-22 (upto July 2021) for Urea, DAP and NPK fertilizers is attached at Annexure VII, VIII and IX respectively.(d):    The availability of fertilizers has remained comfortable throughout the country and no shortage of fertilizers has been reported by the states. Further, the availability is being continuously monitored by the Department of Fertilizers through dashboard.*****
