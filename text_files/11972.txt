                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

741






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Accountability of Sports Federations




Hibi Eden














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken steps to improve the genuinity and the accountability of various  sports federations in India; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) whether there is any data which shows frauds/ misappropriations/ cheatings/deceptions committed by associations and federations and if so, the details thereof; and (d) the total funds allocated by the Government to recognized sports federations since 2016-17, federation and year-wise? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS [SHRI KIREN RIJIJU]  (a) & (b) The National Sports Development Code of India (NSDCI) 2011 provides for transparency and accountability in the functioning of National Sports Federations (NSFs). NSDCI requires NSFs to follow healthy management practices, accountability, transparency, impartial and transparent selection procedures, age and tenure restrictions of office bearers, basic universal principles of good governance in sports, proper accounting procedures, prevention of age fraud & sexual harassment of women, and holding of elections as per model guidelines. NSFs are required to submit Utilization Certificates for the grants received from the Government. Accounts of the NSFs receiving grant of more than Rs. 1.00 crore in a year are subject to audit by the Comptroller & Auditor General of India.  (c) No such data is maintained by the Ministry which shows frauds/misappropriations/cheatings/deceptions committed by associations and federations.  (d)    Budget allocation is made for different sports disciplines in consultation with the concerned National Sports Federation.  The information is at Annexure.  *****
