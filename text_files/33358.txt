                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

882






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Promotion of Tourism in Parasnath




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received any suggestions/ representations from the public representative to improve, develop and promote tourism in Parasnath of Giridih district in Jharkhand; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard; (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor; (d) 	whether the Government proposes to provide financial assistance to Jharkhand for development and promotion of tourism in Parasnath of Giridih district in Jharkhand; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM					(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e): Ministry of Tourism provides financial assistance to the concerned State Governments/Union Territory Administrations under its PRASHAD Scheme to create pilgrimage tourism infrastructure on receipt of suitable proposals from them in line with the Scheme Guidelines.  As per the request received from the State Government of Jharkhand, Parasnath, Giridih has been included under PRASHAD Scheme.*****
