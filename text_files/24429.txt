                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

412






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Projects under PM Kisan Sampada Yojana




Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government recently sanctioned 32 projects in the food processing sector with investment of about Rs 406 Cr under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana which is having a total outlay of Rs 6000 Cr for the implementing period 2016-20;(b)	 if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government proposes and has identified more projects to fast track them in near future; and(d) 	if so, the details of the projects under consideration thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a) to (d): The Ministry of Food Processing industries (MoFPI) has been implementing Central Sector Umbrella Scheme - Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana (PMKSY) for overall growth and development of food processing sector across the country including rural areas of Maharashtra. Under component schemes of PMKSY, MoFPI provides mostly credit linked financial assistance (capital subsidy) in the form of grants-in-aid to entrepreneurs for setting up of food processing / preservation industries. Ministry had approved 32 projects under Creation /Expansion of Food Processing & Preservation Capacities scheme of PMKSY in Feb 2020, with total project cost of Rs 406 crore. The details of projects are placed at Annexure-I. So far, a total of 818 projects have been approved under various component schemes of PMKSY for setting up food processing Industries. The details are placed at Annexure-II.Ministry makes consistent efforts for removing bottlenecks in implementation of the approved projects under component schemes of PMKSY in coordination with relevant state agencies, project promoters etc.*****
