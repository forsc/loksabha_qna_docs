                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4317






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Beneficiaries of PM-KISAN Yojana in Jharkhand




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of beneficiaries identified in Jharkhand, especially SanthalPargana, under the Pradhan MantriKisanSammanNidhi (PM-KISAN) Yojana and the number of beneficiaries being benefitted at present; and(b) the details of the States where the PM-KISAN yojana is not being implemented properly and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ????????????????????????	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) Release of benefits under PM-KISAN is a continuous and ongoing process. After the registration of farmers under the Scheme, the data of the farmers is verified/validated through various agencies like UIDAI, PFMS, Income Tax. Consequently, after the receipt of correct and verified data of farmers, the benefit of the Scheme are transferred directly into the bank account of beneficiaries through DBT mode. The district-wise details of number of eligible beneficiaries registered in Jharkhand, including in the 6 districts ofSanthalPargana namely Jamtara, Deoghar, Dumka, Pakur, Godda, Sahebganj, under the Pradhan MantriKisanSammanNidhi (PM-KISAN) Yojana and the number of eligible beneficiaries benefitted so far is attached at Annexure-1.(b) The Pradhan MantriKisanSammanNidhiYojana (PM-KISAN) is being successfully implemented in all States/UTs of the country. The benefits under the Scheme are transferred directly into the bank accounts of the beneficiaries via Direct Benefit Transfer Mode on receipt of their correct and verified data from the respective States/UTs after and through Aadhaar/PFMS/Income Tax database etc.
