                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3263






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Heritage Recognition to Ramappa Temple




Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy













                                                    Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta
                                                








                                                    Dayakar Pasunoori
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government of Telangana has requested for heritage recognition of some temples in the State including Ramappa temple; and(b)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken thereon including release of funds and funds spent for its development?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)& (b) Yes Sir, Government of Telangana has submitted proposal for recognition of Ramappa Temple as World Heritage Site for the nomination cycle 2019-20. ICOMOS expert has evaluated the site in the month of Sep 2019. Recently a small delegation team on behalf of Govt. of Telangana has also attended the ICOMOS World Heritage Panel Meeting in Paris on 22.11.2019 on the invitation of Director, ICOMOS evaluation unit to discuss the issues identified during the course of Evaluation process. The funds spent by ASI and State Govt. of Telangana are annexed at Annexure-‘A’.   ANNEXURE-‘A’ANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (b) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO 3263 FOR DATED 09.12.2019Details of funds spent on development works etc at Ramappa Temple, Mulugu District, TelanganaSr. No	Particular	Amount Spent1.	By Archaeological Survey of India	Rs 37,72101/-2.	By Govt. of Telangana	Rs. 22,40,000/-
