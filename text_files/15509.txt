                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

365






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Review of Motor Vehicles Act




Faizal P.P. Mohammed














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there has been an increase in fatalities due to road accidents in various States/UTs including Kerala and Lakshadweep during the last three years and the current year; (b) 	if so, the details thereof indicating the total number of deaths in road accidents during the said period, State/UT-wise; (c) 	whether the Government has decided to launch an awareness programme for repeat offenders and if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	whether, in view of the increasing road fatalities, the Government is contemplating to review the provisions contained in the Motor Vehicles Act in regard to the punishment prescribed for such offenders/guilty persons and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS         (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	 to   (d)      A statement is laid on the Table of the House.
