                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3857






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Unique Disability Identification Cards




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the date of launch of the Unique Disability Identification (UDID) cards for disabled persons;(a)	(b) the number of persons who have been issued such cards till date, the details thereof of the State of Maharashtra, district-wise; and(b)	(c)	(c) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK) (a)	The UDID application software for  Unique Disability Identity Card (UDID) database was made live on 18.05.2016 and hosted on NIC cloud. The first UDID card was generated on 27.01.2017.(b)	& (c) As of 15.12.2021, 65.37 lakh UDID cards have been generated in the country. The details of the UDID cards generated in the State of Maharashtra, district-wise are at Annexure.*****
