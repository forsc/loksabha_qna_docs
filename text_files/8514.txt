                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2248






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






12th Five Year Plan for TSP




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the funds sanctioned and allocated under the tribal sub-plans during the last three years and the current year; (b)	whether the entire funds for TSP under the 12th Five Year Plan have been sanctioned, if not, the time by which the remaining funds are likely to be sanctioned and allocated to the States; and (c)	whether the sizable portion of this scheme was rendered unutilised which resulted in less allocation of funds in the 12th Five Year Plan for tribal sub-plans?






ANSWER




                                                        (a): 	Details of funds allocated by the Central Government for the welfare of Scheduled Tribes / Tribal Sub-Plan during last three years and current year, as reflected in the Expenditure Profile of relevant Union Budgets, are given as under:(Rs. in Crore)Year	Budget Estimate (BE)	Revised Estimate (RE)	Actual2016-17	24005.39	25602.08	21810.562017-18	31919.51	32508.42	31913.722018-19	39134.73	41093.33	-2019-20	52884.82	-	-(b) & (c):  	Details of allocation of funds for the welfare of Scheduled Tribe / Tribal Sub-Plan by the Central Government during the 12th Five Year Plan Period i.e. 2012-13 to 2016-17 at Budget Estimate, Revised Estimate stages alongwith Actuals, as reflected in the Expenditure Profile of Union Budget, are given as under:(Rs. in crore)Year	BE	RE	Actual2012-13	21710.11	18721.33	20184.102013-14	24598.39	22029.97	22039.042014-15	32386.84	20535.52	19920.722015-16	19979.77	20963.17	21216.542016-17	24005.39	25602.08	21810.56Total	122680.5107852.07105170.96Evidently, during 12th Five Year Plan Period, Actuals stands at 97.51 % of the allocation of funds at RE stage.  *****
