                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

470






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Production of Tejas Fighter Planes




S. Muniswamy














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government is planning to build more Tejas fighter planes for the air force;(b) if so, the number of planes that are under production; and(c) the details of new features in the Tejas Fighter Flights and the time by which the final flight test is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????) (a) & (b): Yes, Sir. In addition to 40 Light Combat Aircraft (LCA) Tejas aircraft           [20 in Initial Operation Clearance (IOC) and 20 in Final Operation Clearance (FOC) version with 16 fighters & 4 trainers in each], Government has planned to manufacture improved 83 LCA Mk-1A aircraft with enhanced capabilities for Indian Air Force (IAF).	The production of 16 fighters in IOC has been completed in March, 2019 and the production of 16 fighters in FOC and 8 trainers has been undertaken by HAL.(c)  Several new features have been included in LCA Mk1A aircraft to enhance the combat capabilities in terms of avionics, radar, weapons, air to air refuelling etc.  Flight testing of Tejas Mk1A fighter aircraft has been planned to be completed by 2022.*****
