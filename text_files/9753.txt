                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

299






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Vocational Education




Ajay Tamta














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the steps taken by the Government to spread higher education and employment oriented vocational education in backward and border areas of the country;(b)	whether the Government has issued any guidelines to the State Governments in this regard and if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the Government proposes to open a new Central University in Pithoragarh, a backward and border area of Uttarakhand so that students of this area do not have to migrate to other places for higher education;(d)	If so, the time by which the said University is likely to be set up in Pithoragarh; and(e)	If not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a)to (e):	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (e) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 299 FOR REPLY ON 09.12.2019 ASKED BY SHRI AJAY TAMTA, HON’BLE M.P. REGARDING VOCATIONAL EDUCATION(a):	There are various steps being taken by the Government to spread higher education in the country including backward and border areas. The details ofMinistry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) funded higher education institutions in border states is given in annexure-I. Also, MHRD in 2013 launched a Centrally Sponsored Scheme named RashtriyaUchchatarShikshaAbhiyan (RUSA) with an objective of correcting the regional imbalances in terms of access to higher education by setting up institutions in un-served and underserved areas of the country. The detail of state-wise/UTs funds approved under RUSA from the year 2013 to till date is placed at annexure-II. Further, to provide greater access to employment oriented vocational education throughout the country, including backward regions and border areas, the steps being taken in this regard is placed *****
