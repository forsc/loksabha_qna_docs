                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3749






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






National Policy for Heavy Industries




Jugal Kishore Sharma













                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has formulated any National Policy for the development of heavy industries in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the number of heavy industries functioning in various parts of the country including Madhya Pradesh and Jammu and Kashmir;(d) whether there is any scheme to set up new heavy industries in the country including Madhya Pradesh and Jammu and Kashmir so as to check the migration of unemployed youth in the country; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES              (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) & (b): The Department of Heavy Industry (DHI) launched a scheme for the ‘Enhancement of Competitiveness in the Indian Capital Goods Sector’ in the year 2014 to strengthen technology development infrastructure in the Capital Goods Sector. This is a Pan-India scheme.     DHI has also launched a Mission Plan relating to Heavy Electrical Industry Sector, i.e., Indian Electrical Equipment Mission Plan 2012-2022.(c): This Department does not maintain data regarding number of heavy industries functioning in various parts of the country.(d) & (e):  This Department does not have any such scheme.                                *********
