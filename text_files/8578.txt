                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2164






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Population Living Below Poverty Line




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether inflation is rising day by day and the Government’s price control mechanism has failed completely; (b) if so, whether the number of poor people is increasing due to inflation; and(c) if so, the criteria for determining the poverty line along with the percentage of our population that comes under the category of poor people?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (c): No, Sir. The Consumer Price Index-Combined (CPI-C) Inflation for the last three years and of the current year up to October, 2019 may be seen in Table-1.Table 1: Consumer Price Index-Combined (CPI-C) Inflation-------------------------------------------------------------------	          2016-17   2017-18   2018-19   April-October, 2019-------------------------------------------------------------------CPI-C Inflation(per cent)	   4.5	       3.6	3.4	        3.5--------------------------------------------------------------------Source: Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation*****
