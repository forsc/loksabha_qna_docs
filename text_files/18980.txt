                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

379






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Coal reserves




Kapil Moreshwar Patil














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government has assessed the coal reserves in the country;(b)  if so, the quality-wise and State-wise details thereof;(c)  whether the Government has allocated coal blocks during the last one year and the current year; and(d)  if so, the details thereof along with the number of coal blocks to be allocated to the states and the Union Territories?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a): Yes Sir.  (b): The quality-wise and State-wise details of coal reserves in the country is given at Annexure. (c): Yes Sir.
