                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1833






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Review of Functioning of Petroleum Resources




Hasmukhbhai Somabhai Patel














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has reviewed the functioning of petroleum resources within our country or outside India, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether any private partner is involved within the country and outside India;(c) whether any new resources of petroleum have been found in our country during the last three years, if so, the details thereof;(d) whether there is any possibility of new resources within Gujarat and Arabian Sea, if so, the details thereof;(e) whether the Government has reviewed the production of petroleum and natural gas in the State of Gujarat during the last three years, if so, the details thereof; and(f) whether any private partnership is involved in Gujarat State?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM& NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) : Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas (MoP&NG) had approved constitution of a Multi Organization Team comprising of representatives of Oil & Natural Gas Corporation Limited (ONGC), Oil India Limited (OIL) and Directorate General Hydrocarbons (DGH) to carry out re-assessment of Hydrocarbon Resources of India in all its 26 sedimentary basins in 2014. The reassessment study of conventional hydrocarbon resources under technical supervision of International domain experts and Indian basin specialists was completed in November 2017 and the same has been approved by MoP&NG. c) The prognosticated conventional hydrocarbon resources in 26 sedimentary basins of the country are of the order of 41.87 billion tones (oil and oil equivalent of gas), which is about 49% increase as compared to earlier estimates of 28.08 billion tones based on earlier study. d) As per new re-assessment of Hydrocarbon Resources of India, new resources have been found in all three Sedimentary Basins in the state of Gujarat.Page 2 of 2The details of Prognosticated Hydrocarbon Resources in the 3 basins in the state of Gujarat is as under:-CategoryBasinPrognosticated Resource Present Study Total (MMTOE)Onland + Shallow Water+ Deep water(2017)Category ICambay2585.6Category IIKutch898.4Category IISaurashtra1325.2e) During the last three years, Crude Oil Production Trends (Thousand Metric Tonnes) in the State of Gujarat is as follows:-( PSC+Nomination Regime)State/Source2016-172017-182018-192019-20 (P) (Upto Dec''19)Gujarat4,6054,5914,6263,527During the last three years, Natural Gas Production Trends (MMSCM) in the state of Gujarat is as follows:-( PSC+Nomination Regime)State/Source2016-172017-182018-192019-20 (P) (upto Dec''19)Gujarat15801606.661402.221011.78 (f) Private partnership is involved in ownership of 31 Exploration block and fields (operational) under Production Sharing Contract regime in the state of Gujarat. In addition, 8 contract areas have been awarded to Consortium of private companies only under Discovered Small Field Bid Rounds and 15 exploration blocks have been awarded to private companies only under Open Acreage Licensing Policy Bid rounds in the state of Gujarat.
