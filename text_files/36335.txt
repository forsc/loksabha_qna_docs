                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3248






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Railway Network Project




Sarmistha Sethi














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to transform India’s railway network with new track and high speed trains;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the railway network in Odisha is included in the above project; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the timeline for completion of such projects? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY  (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (d): On expiry of codal life of assets, the renewal/up-gradation/ replacement of tracks and other Infrastructure on all over the Indian Railway are undertaken by the Ministry of Railways on regular basis under Annual Works, Machinery & Rolling Stock programme, which is a continuous process. With regard to High Speed Rail (HSR) network, as of now, the Government has sanctioned the Mumbai Ahmedabad High Speed Rail (MAHSR) Project with technical and financial assistance from Government of Japan.  Currently, no project of High Speed Rail is sanctioned in the state of Odisha.*****
