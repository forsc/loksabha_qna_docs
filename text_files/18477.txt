                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2105






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Swadhar Greh




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the total number of Swadhar Greh currently operational/setup in Uttar Pradesh, district-wise;(b)	whether any steps have been taken to curb violence in these Grehs, if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether any scheme has been formulated for regular inspection and monitoring of these homes; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (d) 	Currently,13 Swadhar Grehs are operational in the State of Uttar Pradesh. The district-wise details are at Annexure.As per the scheme guidelines, a committee under the chairpersonship of District Magistrate conducts the inspection of all Swadhar Grehs operational in the State. In order to prevent the occurrence of such incidents, the Government of Uttar Pradesh has issued necessary guidelines. AnnexureAnnexure referred to in reply of parts (a) to (d) to of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 2105 for reply on 23.09.2020 regarding Swadhar Greh by Shri Rajesh Verma. The district-wise number of Swadhar Grehs in Uttar Pradesh.
