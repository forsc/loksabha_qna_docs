                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1224






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






Subsidy to Small Textile Units




Hemant Patil














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plan to provide subsidy to the small textile manufacturing units particularly in Maharashtra; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) the details of the textile units approved by the Government in Maharashtra so far?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) to (c):	Ministry of Textiles has been providing subsidy support to textile industry, including Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) and small textile manufacturing units across the country including Maharashtra through schemes such as Amended Technology Upgradation Fund Scheme (ATUFS) and Powertex India. Details of support provided under the schemes are given in Annexure-I.(d):	Details of textile units in Maharashtra provided support under ATUFS and Powertex India Scheme are given in Annexure-II.
