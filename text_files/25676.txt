                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1561






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Transfer Policy




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is a transfer policy for all categories of Railway employees including Group B, C and D employees and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether there exists a policy for inter-zonal transfer accepting bottom seniority and if so, details thereof;(c)	the number of applications in each division that are pending for transfer at various levels of seniority for inter-zonal transfers accepting bottom seniority; and(d)	whether any exceptions have been made to the policy and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) and (b) Yes, Sir. There is a comprehensive transfer policy for Gr. C & erstwhile Gr. D staff, broad features of which are as follows:-(i) An employee can be transferred to carry out promotion or on administrative ground in SPE/Vigilance cases.(ii) Employees holding sensitive posts, who frequently come into contact with public or/and contractors/ suppliers, are required to be transferred after every four years.(iii) Transfer on request is permitted to employees on acceptance of bottom seniority in initial recruitment grades; or intermediate grades which have an element of direct recruitment. Such transfers are permitted only upon completion of 5 years regular service and subject to possessing of the educational qualification prescribed for the post to which the transfer is sought. (iv) Transfer is permitted in case spouse of an employee belongs to All India Service/ Central Service/ Railway/ State Service/ Central PSUs/ State PSUs/ Autonomous Body/ Private Sector, and is subject to administrative convenience.(v) Transfer is allowed to employees on mutual exchange basis from one cadre of a division/office/railway to the corresponding cadre in another division/office/railway. (vi) As regards Gr. ‘B’ employees, transfers can be considered subject to acceptance of bottom seniority and consent of the railway to which transfer is sought. 	(c) A statement is appended.(d) Yes, Sir. Exemption from minimum five years’ service condition is allowed to following categories of Non-Gazetted railway employees seeking transfer on request:-(i)	     Transfers sought on mutual exchange basis;(ii)	Transfers sought on spouse ground;(iii)	Railway servants who are care-givers to a disabled child; (iv)	Physically handicapped Railway Servants;(v)	Widow appointed on Compassionate ground.******
