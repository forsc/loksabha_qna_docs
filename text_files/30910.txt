                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2441






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Amount Spent by ASI on Conservation of Monuments




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) in partnership with trusts has undertaken the task of conserving large number of monuments in the country including lesser known monuments;(b)	if so, the details thereof during the last three years, State/year - wise; (c)	the amount spent so far on conservation of these monuments;(d)	whether it is proposed to generate revenue for the upkeep and maintenance of these monuments through various sources; and(e)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c)	Yes, Sir. Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) has undertaken conservation of monuments in partnership with various trusts in the last three years. Details are at Annexure.(d) & (e)	There is no such proposals under Archaeological Survey of India (ASI), for generating the revenue in such partnership.
