                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3831






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Cutting of Trees along NHs




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of trees cut down during the construction of Bilaspur-Raipur and Bilaspur-Mungeli-Podi National Highways (NHs) alongwith the number of trees planted in lieu thereof till now; (b) whether complaints have been received against cutting of trees without permission on both the said NHs; (c) if so, the details thereof alongwith the action taken agaisnt those found guilty; (d) whether complaints have been received regarding the poor quality in the construction of both the NHs; and (e) if so, the details thereof alongwith the action taken against those found guilty?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) The details of trees cut down and trees planted in Raipur-Bilaspur Section are as under:S. No.	Project Name	No. of tree cut down	No. of trees  planted (Avenue)1	Raipur-Simga	3367	312552	Simga-Sargaon	4028	400003	Sargaon-Bilaspur	1090	10880        In respect of Bilaspur-Mungeli-Podi Road (NH-130A), 2077 number of trees have been cut down. The tree plantation in lieu thereof has been taken up by forest department of State Government.(b) No, Sir.(c) Question does not arise. (d) to (e) The construction work is done as per the Manual & Codes of the Indian Road Congress. However, some complaints were received for Raipur-Bilaspur section of NH-200 (New NH-30 and 130) regarding cracks and quality of roads. Defects have been rectified by the contractor at his own cost as the projects are either in construction stage or under Defect Liability Period (DLP).  Moreover, cure period notices have also been issued to the contractors of Simga-Sargaon and Sargaon-Bilaspur sections.*****
