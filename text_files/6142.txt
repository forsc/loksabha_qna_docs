                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5521






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Nirbhaya Fund




Jugal Kishore Sharma













                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                








                                                    Sanjay Jaiswal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has any monitoring mechanism in place to ensure the proper implementation of projects/ proposals under the Nirbhaya Fund; (b)	if so, the details thereof along with the progress made thereunder during the past five years, State/UT-wise; and (c)	if not, the reasons therefor along with the details of projects/proposals/ schemes under the said fund during the said period, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT                                                  (SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	& (b): Yes Sir. In accordance with the Framework for Nirbhaya Fund, an Empowered Committee (EC) of officers chaired by the Secretary, Ministry of Women & Child Development (MWCD) appraises and recommends proposals to be funded under this framework. It also provides for the concerned Ministry/ Department to seek approval of the designated competent financial authority, as well as of the Department of Economic Affairs (DEA) for funding of such proposals under the Nirbhaya Framework. As per this framework, the MWCD is responsible to review and monitor the progress of sanctioned projects/ schemes in conjunction with the concerned Central Ministries/ Departments. Further, as per this framework, monitoring and reporting mechanisms are mandated at the State/ UT level as well as at the level of the concerned Central Government Ministries/ Departments. All the projects appraised by the EC are at various stages of implementation having staggered gestation periods for completion of the projects.
