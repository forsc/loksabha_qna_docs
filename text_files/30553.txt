                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Beneficiaries of DBT Scheme




Rodmal Nagar













                                                    Chandra Prakash Joshi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state:(a) 	the funds released for fertilizers under Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) programme during the last three years;(b) 	whether this amount was transferred to the fertilizer companies or to the bank accounts of the farmers; (c) 	the numbers of farmers benefitted under DBT during the last three years, State/UT-wise; and (d) 	the types of fertilizers for which provision of the funds is made under Direct Benefit Transfer Programme?

transferred to the fertilizer companies.(c):		The number of buyers benefitted under DBT during the last three years, State/UT-wise is enclosed in Annexure-II.(d):	    Under Direct Benefit Transfer
