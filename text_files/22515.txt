                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3596






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






ROBs/RUBs in Jharkhand




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of Railway Over Bridges (ROBs) Railway Under Bridges (RUBs) that are under construction in Jharkhand;(b)	the details of the proposals sent by the State Government of Jharkhand for construction of Over Bridges/ Under Bridges at railway crossings; and (c)	The steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): Railways have sanctioned 41 Railway Over Bridges (ROBs) and 186 Railway Under Bridges (RUBs) in lieu of Level Crossings (LCs) in the State of Jharkhand.  Present status of ROBs is as under: Fully completed ROBs	                                        04Railway portion completed	                                07Work is in progress	                                        08Consent for cost sharing not received from State Government	05Works at planning in consultation with State Government	        17Total	                                                        41Present status of RUBs is as under:RUBs completed	                           25Work in progress	                   06Work in planning stage	                  154Work not feasible	                   01Total	                                  186 (b) & (c): 13 eligible proposal have been received for construction of Road Over Bridge (ROB) in lieu of level crossings in the State of Jharkhand during last 3 years.  6  ROBs are already sanctioned.  Out of  balance 7 proposals, 5 proposals falling on Golden Quadrilateral – Golden Diagonal (GQ-GD) have been considered as per priority assigned to elimination of Level Crossings (LCs) on route while 2 proposals on other than GQ-GD routes could not be considered.*****
