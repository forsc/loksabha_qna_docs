                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Development of Protected Monuments in Delhi




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of monuments which are declared to be of national importance in Delhi;(b)	whether the Government has drawn any short-term, mid-term and long-term plans for the conservation, restoration and overall development of the protectedmonuments in Delhi for posterity and to make them as popular tourist destinations and if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the details of funds allocated and expenditure incurred on conservation, restoration, scientific preservation and environmental development on each protected monument in Delhi during the last three years, including thecurrent financial year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	173 monuments are declared as of National   importance in NCT of Delhi.(b)	The conservation, restoration, preservation,   annual maintenance and overall development of the protected monuments in   Delhi is continuous phenomenon taken up by Archaeological Survey of India as per the requirement, considering  availability of funds and resources.(c)	The funds   allocated and expenditure incurred for conservation,   preservation,    maintenance, scientific preservation  and environmental development of  centrally protected   monuments in Delhi during the last three years   and the current financial year is at Annexure. *****ANNEXURE ANNEXURE REFERRED IN REPLY TO PART (c) OF THE LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 1246 FOR 19.09.2020EXPENDITURE INCURRED DURING THE LAST THREE YEARS AND CURRENT YEAR FOR CONSERVATION, PRESERVATION AND ENVIRONMENTAL DEVELOPMENT  OF CENETRALLY RPTOECTED MONUMENTS/SITESAmount in RupeesSl.
