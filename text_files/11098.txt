                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

69






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Promotion of Tourism in Coastal and Hilly Areas




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is working on a new scheme to promote tourism in coastal and hilly areas; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	Ministry of Tourism thorough its existing schemes of Domestic Promotion and Publicity including Hospitality (DPPH) and Restructured Scheme of Overseas Promotion and Publicity including Marketing Development Assistance (OPMD) promotes India as a holistic destination under the Incredible India brand-line. As part of its on-going activities, the Ministry annually releases print, electronic, online and outdoor media campaigns in the international and domestic markets, to promote various tourism destinations and products of the country, including tourist attractions in the coastal and hilly areas.  In addition, the Indiatourism Offices in India and overseas disseminate information and undertake various promotional activities with the objective of showcasing the varied tourist destinations and products of the country.Ministry under the Swadesh Darshan scheme is developing thematic circuits in the country in a planned and prioritized manner. Coastal Circuit and Himalayan Circuit have been identified as thematic circuits for development under the scheme. The details of projects sanctioned under Coastal Circuit and Himalayan Circuit theme of this scheme are given at Annexure.*******
