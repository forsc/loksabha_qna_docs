                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3174






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Use of PNG by Industries




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam













                                                    Bharti Pravin Pawar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) has made it mandatory for several industries to use Piped Natural Gas (PNG) in order to check pollution;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether any industry has violated the said instructions and if so, the details thereof along with the monitoring mechanisms put in place in this regard; and(d) 	the number of such industries which have violated the instructions of CPCB along with the action taken thereto for non-compliance?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) to (d) Central Pollution Control Board(CPCB) has directed Delhi and NCR States for the switching over of industrial units to Piped Natural Gas (PNG) in Delhi and NCR, where PNG supply is available. About 2600 industrial units have switched over to PNG in Delhi & NCR. Further, the Task Force on Graded Response Action Plan (GRAP) on 24th October 2019 recommended that industries in Delhi, which had not yet shifted to PNG to remain closed during 26th to 30th October 2019. The closure was further extended till 15th November, 2019. ******
