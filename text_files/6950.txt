                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

536






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Visa Processing Offices




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Visa Processing Offices in Guwahati; and (b) Whether the Government will take necessary action to open such offices of neighboring countries; and(c)  If so, the details in this regard?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a)    Guwahati hosts the Assistant High Commission of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh and Consulate General of the Kingdom of Bhutan, which process visas.(b) & (c) 	As per established diplomatic practice, proposal to set up a Consulate in any part of India has to be mooted by the concerned foreign government to the Government of India.
