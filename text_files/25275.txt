                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1091






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






National Overseas Scholarship for ST Students




D. Ravikumar














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the annual allocation of funds to the National Overseas Scholarship (NOS) for Scheduled Tribe (ST) Students in the last three years;(b)	the number of ST students who have been annually awarded the NOS in the last three years;(c)	the total annual strength of ST applicants for NOS in the last three years;(d)	the reason to fix only 20 slots for Scheduled Tribes students;(e)	the rationale behind fixing the income limit to Rs.6 lakhs per annum and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SMT. RENUKA SINGH SARUTA)   (a)      The details of annual allocation and expenditure of funds under the scheme of National Overseas Scholarship (NOS) for ST students in the last three years is as below:-                                                                                                               (Rs. in Lakhs)2018-19	2019-20	2020-21BE	RE	Utilization	BE	RE	Utilization	B.E.	RE	Utilization200.00	200.00	200.00	200.00	200.00	200.00	200.00	476.00	476.00(b)     During the last three years 20 ST students annually have been awarded provisional award under the NOS Scheme. (c)      The year-wise number of applications received under the NOS Scheme during the last three years is as below:-Year	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21Total Application Received	138	162	215Eligible candidates called for interview	124	127	203 (d)   The current guidelines provide for 20 slots under the scheme. The number of eligible applicants who participated in the selection process were fewer than the number of eligible applicants.  Suitable candidates were selected for the 20 slots.  However, it was found that even the selected students were not able to avail of the scholarships for various reasons including their inability to obtain admission in Universities abroad.(e)    The rationale behind fixing the income limit to Rs.6.00 lakhs per annum is to cover majority of poorer scheduled tribepopulation for pursuing higher studies abroad.*****
