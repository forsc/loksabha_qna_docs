                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2002






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Training to Sportspersons




Keshari Devi Patel














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is imparting training to new and young sportspersons of Uttar Pradesh after selecting them to compete at international level;  (b) if so, the details along with the list of selected sportspersons thereof;  (c) the details and the names of sports which the Government of Uttar Pradesh is promoting;  (d) the number of new young sportspersons selected for training and preparation including the funds allocated/ released for the purpose during the last five years, District-wise; and  (e) whether adequate number of trainers and resources are available for training of sports persons in Uttar Pradesh and if so, the details and the names of sports and district where training is being imparted? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS (SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)  (a)  to (d): Yes Sir. ‘Sports’ being a State subject, the responsibility of promotion of sports, including identification of young talent and its nurturing, rests with State Governments. Government of India supplements the efforts of State Governments and National Sports Federations (NSFs) under its various schemes such as Khelo India, Assistance to National Sports Federations and by providing training facilities in Sports Authority of India (SAI) Centres.  In order to tap the sporting talent, under the component ‘Talent Search and Development’ of Khelo India Scheme of Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports (MYAS), selection of talented sports persons is carried out across the Country, as per competitive performance and assessments made through scientifically designed battery of tests. The selection is not done State wise or District wise.  A total of 2932 athletes under Khelo India scheme and 10248 athletes under various SAI sports promotional schemes, including 576 athletes from Uttar Pradesh in 15 sports disciplines  have been selected for further training to excel in various National/ International competitions.   Also, under the schemes of the Department of Sports, the identified promising sportspersons and teams are provided support of modern sports science, exposure abroad under experts of respective sports discipline, participation in International events/ competitions.   The funds are not allocated or released State-wise or District-wise but are released to the respective sportsperson, grantees, recipients based on the specific objectives of the respective schemes.  A statement of budgetary provisions for training in sports under the schemes of Department of Sports is at Annexure-I.  (e) Yes Sir. The details of SAI training facilities in the State of Uttar Pradesh, discipline-wise are at Annexure-II.  *****
