                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

599






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Solar Plants




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the quantum of electricity in megawatt currently being generated by the solar plants in the country and the targetted production thereof by the year 2024;(b) the name of the places in Chhattisgarh where solar energy plants are likely to be set up; and(c) the places at which new solar plants are likely to be set up during the next three years under the Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a): As on 30.6.2021, solar power projects of 42,335.45 MW capacity have been installed for production of electricity from solar energy in the country. The Government has set a target of achieving 100 GW of installed solar power capacity by December, 2022.(b): The Government of Chhattisgarh has reported that 26 solar plants of 649.21 MWp have been planned. The details are given at Annexure.(c): Government have allowed 100% Foreign Direct Investment under the automatic route in Renewable Energy (RE) projects including solar energy projects. As such, any developer can set up RE projects using foreign investment.*****
