                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1175






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Special Cultural Fund




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any mechanism to establish special cultural fund for organising cultural events at the district level to promote our incredible culture, specially in Karnataka;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the details about the concerned officials and nodal agencies regarding the aforementioned purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR CULTURE ANDTOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)There is no such proposal under consideration in Ministry of Culture. However, the Ministry of Culture administers various scheme components such as Cultural Function Production Grant, Repertory Grant etc. under which grants are being given to organise cultural events across the country including Karnataka.(b)	Does not arise.(c)       Does not arise.(d)	Does not arise.****
