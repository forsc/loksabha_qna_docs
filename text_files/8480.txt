                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2072






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Swayam Platform




Kirron  Kher














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the salient features of Swayam 2.0;(b) the number of students enrolled under various certificate programme in Swayam, yearwise;and(c) the steps taken by the Government to accord access, equity and quality education tostudents across India?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) The Key Features of SWAYAM 2.0 are - Higher Scalability and Performance , Enhancedfeatures for faculty and students , Improved assessment and evaluation, Internationalization,Translation to Indian languages, Local chapters and mentors and Offering of On-line Degrees(b) Year wise Breakup of Students Enrollment for SWAYAM Certificate Courses:<pre>Year No. Of Courses Enrollment2016 247 6,67,7632017 584 38,44,4422018 862 36,83,9782019 1172 42,88,886Total Cumulative 2865 1,24,85,069</pre>(c) Accesses to all SWAYAM Courses are free. 3800 Local chapters have been established inEducational institutions. Local Chapters provide a platform for discussion amongst studentsand faculty about enrolling of students, facilitating credit transfer, suggesting new courses,exam registration and helping students during the delivery of courses.*****
