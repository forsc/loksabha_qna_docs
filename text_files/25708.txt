                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PLANNING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1532






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Assessment of Schemes/Programmes




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





PLANNING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has made/proposes to make any assessment regarding important big schemes/ programmes in the country including in Rajasthan; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	the funds allocated for various schemes/ programmes during the last three years;(d)	whether the allocated fund is being utilized properly;(e)	if so, the details thereof; and (f)	if not, the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS & PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION; MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF CORPORATE AFFAIRS (RAO INDERJIT SINGH)(a)	& (b).The Ministries/Departments of the Government of India conduct evaluation of the Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSS)/Central Sector (CS) schemes being implemented by them. The NITI Aayog also conducts concurrent evaluation of the selected programmes/schemes.  Besides this, in States/UTs, the State Evaluation Offices (SEOs) conduct evaluations of their prominent schemes/programmes. Continuation of Central Sector (CS)/Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSS) from one Finance Commission cycle to another entails a third party impact evaluation. For the continuation of Central Sector (CS) / Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSS) in 15th Finance Commission Cycle, the Department of Expenditure in March 2019 entrusted Development Monitoring & Evaluation Office (DMEO), NITI Aayog to conduct evaluation of 28 Umbrella Centrally Sponsored Schemes comprising 125 CSS in selected sample States/UTs including Rajasthan. The list of Evaluation Studies of 28 Umbrella Centrally Sponsored Schemes (UCSS) is enclosed in Annexure-I.  The evaluation Reports have been shared with respective Ministries/Departments and Department of Expenditure, Ministry of Finance. The evaluation of CS Schemes was entrusted to respective Ministries/Departments.  (c) to (f).  The respective Ministries/Departments implement the schemes in co-ordination with States/UTs. Further, they release funds to States/Implementing agencies from time to time and also monitor the schemes. Based on the findings and insights from evaluation studies, respective Ministries/Departments make mid-course corrections in the schemes.
