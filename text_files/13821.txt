                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2640






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Coronavirus Medicine




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has taken any steps to provide medical or any other assistance to China, which is very badly affected due to coronavirus, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has identified any suitable medicine to prevent coronavirus;(c) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard;(d) whether the Government has issued any guidelines/notification/ban on import from Coronavirus affected countries; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a):	Government of India mounted a relief assistance flight to Wuhan, China on 26.02.2020 which brought over 15 tons of medical assistance comprising masks, gloves and other emergency medical equipment for Chinese healthcare professionals.(b) & (c):	There is no specific treatment or vaccine for Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19). Treatment is supportive. Guidelines on Clinical Management of severe respiratory illness (SARI) in suspect / confirmed COVID-19 cases, Surveillance for human infection with COVID-19, Infection Prevention and Control in healthcare facilities, etc. have been placed in public domain on the website of the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare at weblink https://mohfw.gov.in/node/4904.(d) & (e):	No such proposal is under consideration in the Ministry.
