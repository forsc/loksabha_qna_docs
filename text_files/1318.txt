                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1249






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Hathkargha Samvardhan Sahayata (HSS) Yojana




Andimuthu Raja














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government had launched Hathkargha Samvardhan Sahayata (HSS) Yojana for handloom weavers;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the aims and objectives thereof; (c)	the ratio of sharing financial assistance between the Government and beneficiary;(d)	the total financial assistance provided by the Government under the HSS Yojana, till date, State/UT-wise; (e)	the number of handloom weavers benefited from the HSS, till date, State/UT-wise; and(f)	the monitoring mechanism developed by the Government in this regard ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b):	The Government of India, Ministry of Textiles introduced the technology up-gradation scheme under National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP) and Comprehensive Handloom Cluster Development Scheme (CHCDS) in 2015-16. The technology up-gradation scheme with some modifications was named as “Hathkargha Samvardhan Sahayata (HSS) Yojana” in December 2016, for providing upgraded looms/accessories to handloom weavers to improve quality of fabric and enhance productivity.(c):	Under the Yojana, the Government of India bears 90% of the cost of looms/accessories & remaining 10% is borne by the beneficiary.(d) & (e): 	A statement showing State-wise total financial assistance provided and the number of handloom weavers benefited till date is at Annexure-I.(f):	The scheme is monitored at different levels by Government of India and State Government.
