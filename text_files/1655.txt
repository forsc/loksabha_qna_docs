                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1562






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Western Campus in Delhi University




Kapil Moreshwar Patil














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to set up western campus of Delhi University inNorth West Parliamentary Constituency;(b) if so, the location-wise details thereof;(c) the present status of the said proposal;(d) the time by which said campus is likely to be established; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ''Nishank'')(a)to(e) The University of Delhi has informed that it has decided to construct a college on a piece ofland measuring 83 Bighas and 19 Biswas (16.79 acres) at Village Roshanpura, Najafgarh. Further ithas initiated the process to appoint CPWD as PMC to construct the college at the proposed land andaccording to the proposal, the duration of construction activity is approximately 4 years.*****
