                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1544






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Corporate Offices in NE




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of regional offices and branches of the Ministry in the NorthEastern Region;(b)	whether the Government plans to shift its Regional Offices from Shillong, Meghalaya to Assam and if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the numbers of Companies registered in the NorthEastern Region for the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER FOR FINANCE AND CORPORATE AFFAIRS		                                                                   (SMT. NIRMALA SITHARAMAN)????? ???? ???????? ????? ?????? 		                               (??????? ??????????????)(a):	There are total 03 field offices of the Ministry in the North Eastern Region. These are offices of Regional Director (NorthEastern Region), Registrar of Companies(NorthEastern Region) and Official Liquidator. Besides this, National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT) under Ministry of Corporate Affairs has 01 bench office in the Region. (b):	The offices of Regional Director (North Eastern Region), Registrar of Companies (North Eastern Region) and Official Liquidator have been shifted from Shillong to Guwahati w.e.f. 22.05.2019 and a Liaison Office under administrative control of the Regional Director (North Eastern Region) has been set up at Shillong.(c):	The number of Companies registered in the NorthEastern Region for the last three years are as under:Financial Year	Numbers of Companies registered	2016-17		568	2017-18	794	794	2018-19		1086*******
