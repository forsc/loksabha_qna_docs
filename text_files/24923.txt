                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

763






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Sub branch of Cotton Corporation of India




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the time by which sanctioned sub-branch of Cotton Corporation of India will start functioning in Nagaur, Rajasthan; (b) whether the Government is contemplating any scheme for starting textile industry, in Nagaur, Rajasthan in view of cotton production there; and (c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a):	The Cotton Corporation of India (CCI) is already operating a sub-branch in Nagaur District, Rajasthan under its branch office, Bhilwara for procurement of cotton During Cotton Season 2019-20, (1.10.2019 to 30.09.2020), CCI procured one lakh quintal kapas (equivalent to 0.22 lakh bales) in Nagaur District and disbursed an amount of around Rs. 55 crores directly into the bank account of 3000 farmers. During current cotton season 2020-21, (1.10.2020 to 30.09.2021), CCI has procured 2.67 lakh quintal kapas (equivalent to 0.51 lakh bales) in this district and disbursed an amount of around Rs. 142 crores to 8000 farmers.(b) & (c):   Government is not contemplating any scheme to start textile industry in Nagaur. However, Government has been assisting textile entrepreneurs under various programmes and schemes such as Amended Technology Up gradation Fund Scheme, Scheme for Integrated Textile Parks, PowerTex India, a Comprehensive Scheme for Powerloom Sector etc. The benefits of these schemes are available across the states/regions of the country.***
