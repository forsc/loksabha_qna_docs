                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

135






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Preservation of Traditional Culture of Haryana




Ramesh Chander Kaushik














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the traditional culture of Haryana is getting extinct; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	if not, the details of the schemes of the Government to preserve the traditional culture of Haryana; and (d)	the details of the accountability of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)   No such formal study has been conducted in this regard. (b)   Does not arise. (c) & (d)   To protect, preserve & promote various forms of folk art and traditional culture throughout the country including Haryana, the Government of India has set up seven Zonal Cultural Centres (ZCCs) with headquarters at Patiala, Nagpur, Udaipur, Prayagraj, Kolkata, Dimapur and Thanjavur. These ZCCs engage artists to organize various cultural activities/ programmes all over the country on regular basis for which annual grant-in-aid is provided to them. A number of schemes viz. Award to Young Talented Artistes, Guru Shishya Parampara, Theatre Rejuvenation, Research & Documentation, Shilpgram, OCTAVE and National Cultural Exchange Programme (NCEP) are being implemented by these ZCCs. Apart from this, various schemes are being implemented by Ministry of Culture to preserve the traditional art and culture of the country.
