                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

77






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Funds for Development of States




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government provides funds to the State Government under various heads for the development of States;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether any mechanism has been put in place to monitor the expenditure being incurred from the said funds; and(d)	if so, the details thereof along with the manner in which the said mechanism functions and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir.  The Union Government provides funds to the State Governments under the Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSS), Central Sector Schemes (CS), Finance Commission grants (FC grants) and Other Grants /Loans/Transfers as per the guidelines of the respective Scheme within the availability of Gross Budgetary Support with the Union Government. The details of central transfers to States as per the Union Budget 2020-21(RE) and 2021-22 (BE) are as under:-(Rs in crore)Sr. No.	Nature of Central Transfers to States	2020-21  (RE)	2021-22 (BE)1	Total Transfers excluding Tax Devolution	713014.40	674253.42	Of which2.	Centrally Sponsored Schemes		315238.00	318857.203.	Central Sector Schemes	42374.08	43016.212.	Finance Commission Transfers	182352.43	220843.00  (c) & (d): The concerned State Governments and Central Ministries/Departments review and monitor implementation of the schemes as per the scheme/programme specific guidelines to ensure effective utilization of funds including expenditure incurred. *********
