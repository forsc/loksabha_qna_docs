                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3460






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Cultivation of Medicinal Plants in Uttarakhand




Ajay Tamta














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to start any program/scheme to promote cultivation of medicinal plants in Himalayan States particularly in Uttarakhand;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise;(c) 	whether the Government has taken any steps to promote farming of medicinal plants in mountainous States which can help create employment and mitigate the migration of people from this region;(d)	if so, the details thereof;(e)	whether any program is likely to be formulated to impart training to youths/ farmers in this regard; and(f)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d):	Subject related to Cultivation of Medicinal plant comes under National Medicinal Plant Board, Ministry of AYUSH.As per the reply received from National Medicinal Plant Board; presently, the Ministry of AYUSH is implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) since 2015-16 under which there is a component on ‘Medicinal Plants’ which primarily aimed at promoting cultivation of medicinal plants in identified cluster/zones with in the States/ UTs including Mountain states/ Himalayan states including Uttarakhand. The scheme implemented in a mission mode through growers, farmers, cultivators, Growers Associations, Federations, Self Help Groups, Corporates and Growers Co-operatives through synergistic linkage with production and supply of quality planting material, processing, quality testing, certification, warehousing and marketing to fulfil the demands of the Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani & Homoeopathy (ASU& H) industries. Subsidy is provided for cultivation of medicinal plants out of prioritised list of 140 species @30%, 50% and 75% of cost of its cultivation, depending upon the status of availability and market demand as per operational guidelines of the Scheme. Under NAM scheme Ministry of AYUSH has supported cultivation of medicinal plants in the Himalayan states. The details are at Annexure – I.(e) & (f):	Under NAM scheme financial support is also provided for flexi component. Under flexi component, Ministry of AYUSH has supported Market promotion, market intervention, along with training of farmers to make the farmers aware about cultivation of medicinal plants. Under the above said scheme, so far a total 45 training programmes have been supported in different States.
