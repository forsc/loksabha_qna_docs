                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

232






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Tribal Representation in NGT




Mahabali Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is no representative from the tribal community among the expert members of National Green Tribunal despite this community having expertise in conservation of forests:(b)	if so, the reasons therefor; and(c)	the steps taken by the Government to ensure participation/inclusion of tribal representative(s) in National Green Tribunal?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a)to (c): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.******* Statement referred to in reply to parts (a) to (c) of the LokSabha Starred Question No. 232 regarding ‘Tribal Representation in NGT’ by ShriMahabali Singh due for reply on 06.03.2020.(a) and (b): There are presently four Expert Members in position in the National Green Tribunal. One of these namely - Dr.Satyawan Singh Garbyal, Expert Member belongs to Scheduled Tribes. (c) The Expert Members of National Green Tribunal are selected as per the provisions of the National Green Tribunal Act, 2010 and the Rules framed thereunder. The posts of Expert Members are open to all eligible persons and based on their suitability. Accordingly, selections are made irrespective of the category. *****
