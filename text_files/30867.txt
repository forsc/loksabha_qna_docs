                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2508






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Schemes for Tribal Students




Mansukhbhai Dhanjibhai Vasava














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of extant ongoing schemes of the Government for tribal students of the Country; (b) the allocation of funds made for these schemes State-wise; (c) whether any time limit has been fixed for the completion of these schemes; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                                  (a)  :   This Ministry implements the following ongoing Centrally Sponsored/ Central Sector schemes for Scheduled Tribe students in the country:  (i)     Pre-Matric Scholarships for ST students.(ii)    Post-Matric Scholarships for ST students  (iii)    Grants under Article 275(1) of the Constitution of India (iv)    Special Assistance to Tribal Sub-Scheme 	  (v)     Development of Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups 	  (vi)    National Fellowship & Scholarship for Higher Education for ST students.  (vii)   National Overseas Scholarships for ST students for studying abroad  (viii)   Grants in aid to Voluntary Organisations Working for the Welfare of   STs(ix)     Strengthening Education among ST Girls in Low Literacy Districts(x)      Eklavya Model Residential Schools 	      (b)  :   In respect of Centrally Sponsored Schemes (at Sl.No i to v), the    States/UTs  wise funds released during the last three years is at Annexure. From Sl.No vi to x these are the Central Sector schemes and funds are not allocated State-wise.         (c)   :  All the above schemes are ongoing.  (d)   :   Does not arise in view of (c) above.
