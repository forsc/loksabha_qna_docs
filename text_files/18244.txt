                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1938






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Solid Waste Management in Chandigarh




Kirron  Kher














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   whether the Government has taken cognizance of the decline in Chandigarh’s solid waste management and ascertained the reasons behind it and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether any directions have been issued to the Chandigarh administration to achieve cent percent door-to-door waste collection and cent percent waste segregation at source for solid waste management in the city and if so, the details thereof; and (c)    the steps taken by the Government to further improve solid waste management in Chandigarh? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a) : Chandigarh has been performing consistently well during the last 5 years in the Swachh Survekshans (SSs), which includes Solid Waste Management (SWM) as one of the parameters. Chandigarh is also a Three Star certified city in Garbage Free City (GFC) rating protocol for cities.(b) and (c) : To further improve the performance in SWM, various steps have been taken by Government, which inter alia includes, funding of SWM projects; issuance of directions, advisories and guidelines on SWM (door-to-door collection, source segregation and processing); organising Capacity Building (CB) workshops; reviewing performance periodically, etc.                                     *****
