                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1734






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Setting up of Trauma Centres along NHs




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is provision of opening trauma centres along the National Highways (NHs) by the Road Transport department; (b) 	if so, the details of the trauma centres opened along the National Highways in the State of Madhya Pradesh along with the dates on which the construction of these trauma centres were completed, location-wise; (c) 	whether these trauma centres have become functional and if so, the details thereof indicating the date; (d)     if not, the reasons therefor and the time by which these are likely to be started; (e)     the details of sanctioned and posted strength in these trauma centres; (f)     whether these posts are filled up by the State Governments and if so, the details thereof along  with the cost sharing pattern between the State and the Union Government; and (g) 	whether the Union Government would operate the trauma centres by recruiting the doctors in case the State Government is not able to operate these centres and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                                         THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS               (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	   No Sir,  Ministry of Road Transport and Highways is mandated with the task of constructing and maintaining National Highways in the country.(b)	 to  (g)    Do not arise.
