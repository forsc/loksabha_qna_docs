                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1488






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






ROBs/RUBs in Gujarat




Devusinh Jesingbhai Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of Railway Over Bridges (ROBs) Railway Under Bridges (RUBs) that are under construction in Gujarat;(b)	the details of the proposals sent by the Government of Gujarat for construction of Over Bridges/ Under Bridges at railway crossings; and(c)	the steps taken by the Government in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): 77 Road Over Bridges (ROBs) and 94 Road Under Bridges (RUBs) are under construction in the State of Gujarat.(b) & (c): Consent for 113 proposals for construction of ROB/RUBs at level crossings (LCs) have been received by Western Railway from Government of Gujarat.  Out of these, 28 proposals (23 ROBs and 5 RUBs) have been sanctioned.  19 more proposals for ROBs have been processed after joint feasibility survey. At balance 66 locations, the feasibility is being examined jointly with State Govt.*****
