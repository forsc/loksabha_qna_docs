                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4744






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Global Solar Grid




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is in talks with  other countries towards developing a global solar grid; (b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) whether the Government is conducting scientific research and looking into the possibilities of taking up solar energy directly from the atmosphere of the earth and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government has constituted any international inter governmental and scientific panels to look into the feasibility and possibility of a global solar grid and if so, the details thereof; (e) whether the Indian Space Research Organization is collaborating with experts in the renewable energy field from India and abroad to conduct studies and innovate in the area of a global solar grid; and(f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH) (a)&(b) The Government is conducting a study for developing the long-term vision, implementation plan, road map and institutional framework for implementing a global grid named as One Sun One World One Grid (OSWOWOG).  At Glasgow on 02-11-2021, during COP26, a One Sun Declaration was issued by India and 88 other countries. To help deliver the vision of OSOWOG, it was resolved to combine efforts and create an inter-connected global grid. This will, inter-alia, include building long distance cross-border transmission lines to connect renewable energy generators and demand centres across continents.(c) The Ministry of New & Renewable Energy has not initiated any research on taking up solar energy directly from the atmosphere of the earth. (d) India and United Kingdom  have jointly written to USA, Australia, African Union, France, Germany and Oman to participate in the Ministerial Steering Group for the global “Green Grids Initiative – One Sun One World One Grid”.(e) & (f): No, Sir.*****
