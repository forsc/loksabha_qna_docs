                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3252






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Merger of Banks




 Rahul Ramesh Shewale













                                                    Girish Bhalchandra Bapat
                                                








                                                    Pritam Gopinath Munde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether proposal for merger of Rupee Cooperative Bank with Maharashtra State Co-Operative Bank is pending with the RBI;(b) if so, the present status of the aforesaid proposal; and(c) the time by which the final decision is likely to be taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a) to (c): Reserve Bank of India (RBI) had received proposal for merger of Rupee Co-operative Bank Ltd. with Maharashtra State Co-operative Bank Ltd. On examination, the proposal was found to be without any merit and was not pursued further by RBI. RBI has advised Registrar of Cooperative Societies (RCS), Maharashtra accordingly.*****
