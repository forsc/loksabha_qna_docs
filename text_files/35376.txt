                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2530






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






Construction of Railway Lines in Mizoram




C. Lalrosanga














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Railways has conducted a reconnaissance/ preliminary survey for proposed construction of a railway line from Sairang to Hmawngbuchhuah in South Mizoram under the Northeast Frontier Railway; (b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether there is any plan to pursue the report of the preliminary survey; and(d)	if so, whether the Government plans to extend the railway line from Sairang to Hmawngbuchhuah once the Bairabi - Sairang railway line is completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (d):  Reconnaissance Engineering-cum-Traffic Survey for new Broad Gauge railway line from Sairang to Hbichhuah (Hmawngbuchhuah) (223 km) has been carried out. As per survey report, cost of the project has been assessed as `15,007 crore with Rate of Return (RoR) (-) 10.45%. Project could not be taken forward being financially unviable.*****
