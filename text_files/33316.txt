                                    QUESTION NO:

                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Training to Tribal Women




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the scheme-wise details of the proposals received and approved under various loan schemes for the development of tribal women of Maharashtra during the last three years and the current year;(b) the number of beneficiaries under the said loan scheme, year-wise;(c) the details of the amount of loan granted by Government for setting up industries after providing training to the tribal women; and(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) Ministry of Tribal Affairs extends concessional loans to the eligible Scheduled Tribe persons through National Scheduled Tribes Finance and Development Corporation (NSTFDC), for undertaking income generation activities / self-employment. Under the loan scheme of NSTFDC, the details of proposals received from the State of Maharashtra for women beneficiaries during the last three years and current year upto (31.01.2022) is given below:Financial Year	Term Loan Scheme	Adivasi MahilaSashaktikaran Yojana (AMSY)	No. of proposal received	No. of proposal approved	No. of proposal received	No. of proposal approved 2018-19	1	1	0	02019-20	0	0	2	22020-21	3	3	0	02021-22 (Upto 31.01.2022)	1	1	0	0 (b) Number of women beneficiaries assisted from Maharashtra State during last three years and current year (upto 31.01.2022) under the schemes of NSTFDC is given below:Financial Year	Term Loan Scheme	Adivasi MahilaSashaktikaran Yojana (AMSY)	No. of Women Beneficiaries	No. of Women Beneficiaries2018-19	5	02019-20	0	4802020-21	610	02021-22 (Upto 31.01.2022)	442	0 (c) & (d): As per the information received from NSTFDC, no proposal for training of tribal women and setting up of industries thereafter have been received from Maharashtra during last three year and current year upto 31.01.2022.
