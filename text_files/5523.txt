                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5025






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Merchant Credit Card for Traders




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to introduce Merchant credit card for traders; (b)	 if so, the steps taken so far by the Government in this regard; and (c)	 if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL) (a)to(c):	Credit Cards are already being issued to traders/merchants, individually, by banks based on eligibility and as per extant policies of individual banks. Consultations with banks have been initiated on a traders/merchant credit card with standardised terms and conditions.*******
