                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

317






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Premium under PMFBY




Dushyant Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amount of premium paid by farmers for kharif and rabi crop insurance under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) since its launch till 01.01.2020, State-wise and district-wise for every district of Rajasthan; (b) the amount disbursed to the farmers for kharif and rabi crop insurance claims under the scheme since its launch till 01.01.2020, State-wise and district-wise for every district of Rajasthan; and (c) the details of the insurance company in charge for each district in Rajasthan? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? 		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b) : State-wise/season-wise  details of premium paid by farmers and claims disbursed to farmers since inception of the scheme in 2016-17 to 2019-20 under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) are given at Annexure-I.District-wise/season-wise details of premium paid by farmers and claims disbursed to farmers since inception of the scheme in 2016-17 to 2019-20 in Rajasthan under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) are given at Annexure-II.(c) : As per revamped Operational Guidelines of PMFBY, insurance companies have been selected for 3 years w.e.f. Kharif 2020 season in Rajasthan.  District-wise in charge insurance companies for implementation of crop insurance schemes viz. PMFBY and Restructured Weather Based Crop Insurance Scheme (RWBCIS) in Rajasthan is given at Annexure-III.
