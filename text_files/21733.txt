                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2874






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Impact of Cyclone on Wildlife in Odisha




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has conducted any study on the frequent cyclones and its impact on wildlife on coastal belts of Odisha;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the measures taken by the Union Governmentand State Government of Odisha to offset the effects ofthe cyclones on the wildlife in Odisha?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	and (b)	As per the information received from the State Government of Odisha, no study on the frequent cyclones and their impact on wildlife has been conducted along the coast of Odisha.(c)	The Government of India provides technical and financial assistance to the State Governments/ UT Administrations in management of wildlife and their habitats.The following measures were taken to offset the effects of cyclones on wildlife along the coast of Odisha:i.	Strayed wild animals were rescued by the staff and released in their habitats.ii.	Creation of afforestation and meadow development works alongthe coastal divisions of the State to reduce the impact of cyclones.iii.	Eco Development Committees of the coastal divisions have been sensitized for protection of wildlife during cyclones.iv.	Squads were developed for protection of wildlife during the time of cyclones.*****
