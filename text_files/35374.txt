                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2521






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






Gauge Conversion




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has completed the conversion from meter gauge to broad gauge railway lines from Ahmedabad to Udaipur via Himmat Nagar;(b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has any plans to start trains between Ahmedabad to Delhi via Himmat Nagar and from Mumbai to Delhi via Nanded, Modasa and Shamlaji; and(d) if so, the time by which these trains are likely to become operational?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2521 BY SHRI DIPSINH SHANKARSINH RATHOD TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 16.03.2022 REGARDING GAUGE CONVERSION(a) to (d): Ahmedabad-Himmatnagar-Udaipur gauge conversion including Modasa-Shamlaji Road new line project (317.73 Km) was sanctioned in 2008-09. The anticipated cost of the project is `2,165 crore. An expenditure of `1,831 crore has been incurred upto March, 2021. An outlay of `240 crore has been provided during 2021-22. 	Gauge Conversion of Ahmedabad-Himmatnagar – Dungarpur- Jay Samand (234 km) and Kharwachanda - Udaipur (24 km) sections has been completed and work taken up in the balance section.  	Further, land acquisition has been taken up in Modasa-Shamlaji Road new line project also. 	Train services between Ahmedabad to Delhi via Himmat Nagar and from Mumbai to Delhi via Nanded, Modasa and Shamlaji can be considered on completion of Ahmedabad-Himmatnagar-Udaipur gauge conversion including Modasa-Shamlaji Road new line project.*****
