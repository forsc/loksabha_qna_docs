                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1977






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Construction of Dam across Nagavali River




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government of Odisha has raised any objection for the construction of dam across Nagavali river in Kotu, Andhra Pradesh; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Union Government proposes to constitute a tribunal to resolve this issue and advise the State of Andhra Pradesh to stop all the ongoing works on Nagavali river; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU)(a) & (b)  No, Sir. Government of Odisha has informed that it has no information on such a proposal, hence it has not raised any objection regarding construction of dam across Nagavali river in Kotu, Andhra Pradesh. Further, Government of Andhra Pradesh has informed that its Water Resources Department has not taken up any works across the river Nagavali near Kottu village of Komarada mandal. (c) & (d) In view of the above, question does not arise.*****
