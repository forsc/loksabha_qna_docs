                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2976






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Converting Sea Water into Potable Water




Kani K. Navas














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any proposal toconvert sea water into potable water bysetting up of plants in Rameswaram tosolve water crisis in Tamil Nadu; and(b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)& (b)Drinking water supply is a State subject. For improving the coverage of safe drinkingwater to rural population, this Ministry supplements the efforts of the States byproviding them with technical and financial assistance through the centrally sponsoredscheme National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP). It is the StateGovernments who plan, design, approve, execute, operate & maintain the schemes forproviding safe drinking water to rural population. States can utilize NRDWP coverage funds for providing safe drinking water to needy rural habitations. The Ministry is not in receipt of any proposal for converting sea water into potable water by establishing desalination plant in Rameswaram from the Government of Tamil Nadu. *******
