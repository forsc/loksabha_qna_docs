                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2689






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Increasing Income of Farmers




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether dairy and animal husbandry sectors are likely to be promoted to double the income of farmers; (b) if so, the details of the schemes in this regard; and (c) the amount proposed to be allocated for the said scheme during the current financial year?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR  FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING                                                             (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN) (a) & (b) In order to complement and supplement the efforts of the States/UTs  for promotion of dairy and animal husbandry sectors thereby doubling the income of farmers, the Government is implementing various schemes to enhance production and productivity of bovines & small ruminants (sheep & goat), Piggery & Poultry namely:I.	Rashtriya Gokul Mission, II.	National Programme for Dairy Development, III.	National Dairy Plan-I,IV.	Dairy Entrepreneurship Development SchemeV.	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund, VI.	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activitiesVII.	National Livestock Mission, VIII.	Livestock Health and Disease Control(c) The details of funds allocated for the said schemes during the current financial year are as under:(Rs. In crore)S. No.	SCHEMES	2019-201.	Rashtriya Gokul Mission	      302.002.	National Dairy Plan-I*	0.003.	National Programme for Dairy Development	325.004.	Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme	325.005.	Dairy Processing Infrastructure Development Fund	58.006.	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activities	100.007.	National Livestock Mission 	480.008.	Livestock Health and Disease Control	474.98*2018-19 is the terminating year of NDP-I.***
