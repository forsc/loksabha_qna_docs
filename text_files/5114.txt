                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4595






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Development of Tourism Circuits in Madhya Pradesh




Riti Pathak














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has received proposals from the State Government of Madhya Pradesh regarding the scheme for the development of tourism circuits and tourist places; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the current status of the said proposals; (c) 	the progress and achievement made in this regard, so far; and (d) 	the details of the funds allocated and spent in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	Ministry of Tourism under the schemes of Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD provides financial assistance to State Governments/UT Administrations/ Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure and facilities in the country. The projects under the above schemes are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. Submission of project proposal by the State Governments/ Union Territory Administrations and its sanctioning is a continuous process.
