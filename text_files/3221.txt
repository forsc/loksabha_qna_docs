                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

271






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Rerouting of Flights




Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar













                                                    Sougata Ray
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government/ Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) has issued any advisory to all Indian airline companies to avoid Iranian airspace and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor;(b) whether rerouting of flights will substantially affect the airline companies and if so, the details thereof along with the number of flights affected due to this reason;(c) whether rerouting of Air India flights will increase the operating cost of the already burdened airlines due to closure of Pakistani airspace and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether this will put additional burden on the passengers, if so, the details thereof and the corrective measures taken/being taken by the Government in this regard to avoid extra burden on passengers;(e) whether some airlines have increased the fare due to route diversion/ increase in operating costs and if so, the details thereof; and(f) whether India has restricted any foreign airlines through Indian airspace and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (f): A statement is laid on the table of the House. ;Statement referred in reply to Part (a) to (f) of Lok Sabha Starred Ques. No. 271 regarding "Rerouting of Flights" to be answered on 11.07.2019.; ;  (a): No Sir. However, in view of the advisory issued by the United States Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) on 20.06.2019, all airlines in consultation with the Directorate General of Civil Aviation(DGCA) have decided to avoid overflying areas of Persian Gulf, Gulf of Oman and Strait of Hormuz within Iranian Airspace to ensure safety.; ; (b): Yes Sir. The rerouting of flights has affected the operations of Indian carriers. The details of rerouted flights are as under:; ; (i)    M/s Air India Charters Ltd.(Air India Express)                   -           9 flights per day; (ii)   M/s Interglobe Aviation Ltd.(IndiGo)                                  -           2 flights per day;; (iii)  M/s Air India Ltd.                                                                -           9 flights per day ; The said rerouting has resulted in increase in the operating cost of Air India Express by Rs. 22 lacs (approx) per day  and that of Indigo by Rs. 2 lacs (approx.) per day. ; ; (c): This rerouting of Air India Flights has resulted in an increase in the operating cost by Rs. 13  lacs (approx) per day as the flying time has increased by approximately 15 minutes. ;  (d) & (e): No Sir. No additional burden has been put on the passengers and no fare has been increased by airlines due to route diversion/increase in operating costs.; ; (f): No Sir, the Government has not placed any restrictions on the operations of foreign airlines over Indian airspace.
