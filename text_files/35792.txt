                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2626






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Oil and Gas Projects in North East




Pallab Lochan Das














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the Government have recently approved oil and gas projects in Northeast worth Rs. One Lakh Crore and if so, the details thereof along with the estimated date of completion of the said projects; (b) whether it is a fact that huge employment opportunities would generate from the said projects; and (c) if so, the details thereof?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE  IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI  RAMESWAR TELI) (a): The projects with investment of about Rs. one lakh crore are envisaged for implementation in North East Region. The details of oil and gas projects which have been planned to be completed by 2025 are as under: - Implementing Agency	Project Worth (in Crore Rs.)Oil & Natural Gas Company Ltd(ONGC)	14,300Oil India Ltd (OIL)	8,150Other Exploration and Production Companies	4,550Numaligarh Refineries Limited (NRL)	30,000Indradhanush Gas Grid Limited (IGGL)	10,000City Gas Distribution (CGD)* & Others 	33,000TOTAL	1,00,000* The investments in Geographical Areas are made by public/private entities in terms of business plan and authorisation of Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board.(b) & (c): As per previous experience & track record, such projects generate significant employment opportunities during various phases i.e. construction, operation and maintenance.  *********
