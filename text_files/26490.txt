                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2085






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Skill Development Scheme in Rajasthan




Gomati Sai














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the number of educated unemployed is on the rise at present and the names of schemes being run in this sector for Skill development; (b) 	the number of applicants who have been imparted training during the year of 2018-19 by institute of Skill Development and up gradation and livelihood college, Jashpur and Raigarh and the names of trades in which training has been imparted; and (c) 	the number of youths who have been given employment by placement agencies during 2018-19 and the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Total number of active job seekers registered on National Career Service Portal as of July 2021 is 99.64 lakhs while the corresponding figure for September 2020 was 103 lakhs. 	The Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship is implementing the following schemes across the country:S.No.	Schemes	Objective1	Pradhan Mantri Kaushal VikasYojana (PMKVY)	This Scheme is for imparting short-term skill training to  youth and for Recognition of Prior Learning of informally trained labour in National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF) alignedqualifications.2	Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) Scheme	It is meant for imparting skill training to the socio-economically backward and educationally disadvantaged groups of urban / rural population such as neo-literates, semi-literates, Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, women and girls, slum dwellers, migrant workers, etc. 3	National ApprenticeshipPromotion Scheme (NAPS)	This Scheme is for promoting apprenticeship training and increasing the engagement of apprentices by providing financial support to industrial establishments undertaking apprenticeship program under The Apprentices Act, 1961. 4	Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS)	This scheme is for providing long-term training in 137 trades through 14,604 Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) across the country.	Further, the list of the skilling schemes implemented by other Central Ministries/ Departments is given at Annexure I.(b)	Currently, two Skill Development schemes viz Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (Centrally Sponsored State Managed) and MukhyaMantri Kaushal VikasYojna are being run by Chhattisgarh State Skill Development Authority.1604 candidates have been imparted training during the year of 2018-19 by Livelihood College, Jashpur and Raigarh. The details of trades in which the training has been imparted in the Livelihood College are placed at Annexure II.(c)	7.04 lakh candidates have been placed under the PMKVY Scheme during the Financial Year 2018-19.
