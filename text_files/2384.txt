                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2188






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Guidelines to Coir Board




Ajay (Teni) Misra














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has issued guidelines to Coir Board for not selling their products through their shopping centres and the products are not being made available to them for sale;(b)	if so, the benefits likely to accrue to the Government as a result thereof;(c)	whether the Government is considering to privatise showrooms/ shopping centres with large turnover; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a)&(b):    The Government has not issued any guidelines to Coir Board for not selling coir products through its Showrooms and for products not being made available to them for sale.  Coir Board was in the process of revamping its showroom sales strategy as part of which the piled up damaged and dead stock has been liquidated.  The face-lifting of the showrooms is being carried out on a priority basis.  The showrooms of Coir Board are conducting sales. The Showrooms have been divided into 5 Zones as East, West, North, South & Central Zones.  Each zone is being lead by one Zonal Director assisted by one Assistant Zonal Director. Suitable efficient Managers have been designated as Zonal Directors and Assistant Zonal Directors. The showrooms are pursuing vigorous sales of coir products.  Coir Board is in the process to set up a franchisee network throughout India through an online marketing portal and connecting the franchisees with the suppliers through the portal for increasing the reach of the products. (c)&(d):     The Government is not considering any proposal to privatize Showrooms/ Shopping Centres with large turnover.
