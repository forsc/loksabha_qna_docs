                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1008






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Identification of Tourism Parks and Circuits for Development




Gautham Sigamani Pon













                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                








                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                








                                                    C.N. Annadurai
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the list of Tourism Circuits, Tourism Parks and Rural Tourism Clusters identified by the Government in each State/Union Territory for integrated development in association with the States/UTs; (b) 	whether the Government has received requests/ proposals from Tamil Nadu for inclusion of more such Tourism Circuits/Parks and Clusters, if so, the details thereof and the response thereof; (c) 	whether the Government proposes to include Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra as part of the international tourism circuit in the country to promote the culture of these States and if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	the other steps taken/being taken by the Government to popularize tourist circuits to people of the country and from abroad so that it can be beneficial for the economy of the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	Development and Promotion of Tourism is primarily the responsibility of the State Government/Union Territory (UT) Administrations. However, the Ministry of Tourism, under its ‘Swadesh Darshan’ scheme provides financial assistance to State Governments/ Union Territory (UT) Administrations/Central Agencies including the State of Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra for development of tourism infrastructure. The projects under these schemes are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier etc. Sanctioning of projects under Swadesh Darshan scheme is a continuous process however sanctioning of new projects in this scheme is under review. The Ministry of  Tourism as  part of  its  regular  promotional  exercises  promotes  various  tourism  destinations  and products of the  country  in  domestic  and  international  potential  markets in  a  holistic  manner. These  promotions  are  undertaken through  various  communication  platforms  such  as  media  campaigns,  events,  webinars,  road shows,  participation in  fairs  &  festivals, social media  platforms  etc. The list of the projects sanctioned under Swadesh Darshan scheme is annexed.*******
