                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1044






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Proposal from States for FPIs




Ramesh Chander Kaushik













                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any proposal regarding (FPI) projects have been sent by States including Haryana and North Eastern States particularly Asam to Central Government at present;(b)	if so, the details of action taken on that proposals;(c)	whether any proposal is also pending with the Government in this regard;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	whether the Government has fixed accountability of anyone in this regard and ifso, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a)	to (e): No Sir. Ministry of Food Processing Industries is implementing a Central Sector Umbrella Scheme – Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samapada Yojana (PMKSY) for the overall growth and development of food processing sector across the country.  The project proposals are invited through online submission based on Expression of Interest (EoI) issued from time to time under the various component schemes of PMKSY. Ministry is also implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme - PM Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises Scheme (PMFME) for providing financial, technical and business support for setting up/upgradation of 2 lakh micro food processing enterprises across the country.          Interested applicants may apply under PMKSY and PMFME in accordance with the respective guidelines about eligibility and assessment criteria. *****
