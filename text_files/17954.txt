                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1768






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






SCO Defence Ministers Meet in Moscow




S. Jagathrakshakan













                                                     Imtiaz Jaleel Syed
                                                








                                                    Asaduddin Owaisi
                                                








                                                    A.K.P. Chinraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Defence Minister has attended the Victory Day Parade recently conducted in Russia and also Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) Defence Ministers meeting held in Moscow;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether any meeting with Chinese counterpart was held on the sidelines of the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation;(d) if so, the outcome thereof;(e) whether the Union Government has signed any bilateral agreement with Russian Government during the visit and if so, the details thereof; and(f) whether he also met the Russian Defence Minister and apprised him of the border situation and if so, the reaction of Russian Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) & (b):  Yes, Sir.  Hon’ble Defence Minister (Raksha Mantri) represented India in the Victory Day parade in Moscow on 24th June, 2020 and also participated in the joint meeting of Defence  Ministers from the  SCO, CSTO, CIS member countries in Moscow on 4th September, 2020.  During the visit for the Victory Day parade, Raksha Mantri had a meeting with the Russain Deputy Prime Minister Mr. Yury Borisov and discussed bilateral defence and defence industrial cooperation as well as regional issues.(c) & (d): Yes, Sir.  Details have been put out in the Government of India press release by Ministry of External Affairs on 5th September, 2020.(e) & (f):  There was no separate bilateral agreement signed with Russian Government during the recent two visits of Raksha Mantri.  During bilateral meeting with the Russian Defence Minister, both bilateral and regional issues were discussed with Russia.*****
