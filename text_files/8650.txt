                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2145






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Ranking of Education Institutions




S. Muniswamy













                                                    Raksha Nikhil Khadse
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plan to setting up a specialized body IRS (Indian RankingSociety) to rank Indian education institutions and if so, the details thereof;(b) the details of the composition of the body;(c) the procedure for the ranking system and its benefit for the Indian educational system;(d) whether the Government proposes to give graded autonomy to selected higher educationalinstitutions to promote research & innovation in their campuses so that to enter in the globalinstitutional ranking and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the list of such higher educational institutions selected to leap forward in transforming educationalsector?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) Executive Committee in its 127thmeeting held on 21.08.2019 accorded approval for the creation of independent society in the name andstyle of “India Rankings Society (IRS)” with an objective to develop and evolve methodologies andsystems for ranking of higher educational institutions and based on such methodologies, rank and ratethem under various categories, domain and sub-domains.(c): Presently, National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) is being used for ranking of institutes onfive broad generic groups of parameters namely:<pre>i) Teaching, Learning and Resourcesii) Research and Professional Practiceiii) Graduation Outcomesiv) Outreach and Inclusivityv) Perception</pre>The India Rankings, 2019 ranked Indian Higher Educational Institutions into nine categories namelyUniversity, Engineering, College, Management, Pharmacy, Law, Architecture, Medical and Overall.Rankings help create an environment of excellence.(d) & (e): There already exists a mechanism for grant of graded autonomy vide University GrantsCommission [categorisation of universities (only) for grant of graded autonomy] Regulation, 2018 dated12.02.2018 which is available on UGC website at https://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/1435338_182728.pdf.*****
