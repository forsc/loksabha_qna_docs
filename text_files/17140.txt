                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

979






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Corona Affected Health Workers in Tamil Nadu




V. Kalanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of frontline warriors like doctors, nurses and para-medical staff etc. who have been found positive with COVID-19 during their duty in Tamil Nadu;(b) the number of such persons recovered and deaths occurred in Tamil Nadu so far;(c) whether the Government has announced any compensation for such persons;(d) if so, the details thereof along with the number of families of deceased workers who got compensation in Tamil Nadu so far; and(e) whether the Government has issued guidelines to the State Government in this regard and, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	Health is a State subject. Information sought for is not maintained at Central level by Ministry of Health and Family Welfare. (c):	No scheme for compensation for frontline warriors like doctors, nurses and para-medical staff etc. who have been found positive with COVID-19 during their duty has been announced by the Government except for the ‘Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package” for health workers fighting COVID-19 and contracting the disease resulting in loss of life. This scheme provides an insurance cover of Rs. 50 lakhs to the insured person. (d):	For Tamil Nadu, so far, a total claim of Rs. 3 crores have been disbursed covering six cases. (e):	The government has issued detailed advisory including FAQs to all the states about the insurance scheme which has also been made available at the MoHFW website.
