                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3526






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Krishna Godavari Basin




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Oil India Limited (OIL) has an onland discovery in the Krishna Godavari Basin in Andhra Pradesh with over 3 billion cubic metres of recoverable reserves;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether the Government is contemplating to provide higher price to bring it to production, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor along with the time by when it would be done?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM& NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b): Oil India Limited (OIL) had made gas discovery in 2014 in the onland Block KG-ONN-2004/1, located in Krishna Godavari Basin of Andhra Pradesh. After appraisal of gas discovery, recoverable gas reserves of around 3 billion cubic meters have been estimated in the Declaration of Commerciality submitted by OIL to Directorate General of Hydrocarbon (DGH).(c) Government vide Notification dated 28th February, 2019 brought several policy reforms in the upstream sector, including providing marketing and pricing freedom for natural gas. As per the Notification, marketing and pricing freedom to sell on arm’s length basis through competitive bidding is permitted to those new gas discoveries whose Field Development Plan (FDP) will be approved for the first time after the date of issuance of the policy. In the present case, the FDP was submitted in April 2019, that is after the issuance of Government Notification.*****************
