                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

613






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






MPLAD Scheme




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the commissioning of studies to developmental issues of constituencies can be considered as part of list of works permissible under the MPLAD Scheme and if so, details thereof; (b) whether the Government is considering an expansion of the scope of MPLAD works and funds to be allocated in the near future, particularly for providing architectural consultancy for infrastructural projects in the MPs’ constituencies and improve quality of works provided;  (c) if so, the details of the funds allocated and utilized by MPs in their respective constituencies in the 16th and 17th Lok Sabha so far State and year-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING (RAO INDERJIT SINGH) (a)  No, Sir. The objective of the Member of Parliament Local Area Development Scheme (MPLADS) is to enable Members of Parliament to recommend works of developmental nature with emphasis on  creation of durable community assets based on the locally felt need to be taken up in their constituency. (b) At present there is no such proposal under examination of the Government regarding an expansion of the scope of MPLADS works. (c) In view of reply to part (b) above, question does not arise.                                         *****
