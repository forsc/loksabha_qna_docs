                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

141






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Foreign Investment




Anubhav Mohanty














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there has been foreign investment in the State of Odisha under the scheme of infrastructure for Agro-processing clusters, Mega Food Parks;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the number of jobs created for farmers till date, district-wise in Odisha?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) No Sir.(b) Does not arise.(c) Two Mega Food Parks have been sanctioned in the state of Odisha and these are funded through domestic resources. The details on employment created and farmers benefitted are as follows: Name of Mega Food Park	Name of District	Direct &Indirect employment	Farmers benefitedMITS Mega Food Park	Rayagada, Odisha	771	1600Odisha Industrial Infrastructure Development Corporation (IDCO)	Khurda, Odisha	7480	70There are no agro processing clusters sanctioned in the state of Odisha.*****
