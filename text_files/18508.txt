                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2231






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Diversion of Funds under BBBP




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the funds released for Beti Bachao- Beti Padhao (BBBP) scheme have been diverted into wasteful activities such as inaugurations and promotion during the last five years and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the steps taken by Government to increase the productivity of this scheme; and(c)	whether the Government has taken steps to ensure that the benefits are accruing to the beneficiaries and if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                         ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	 No, Sir.(b)	 Beti Bachao- Beti Padhao (BBBP) scheme aims to sensitize the people  and encourage them to properly value the girl child. For this, nation-wide media campaign is implemented. That in turn includes radio spots/jingles in Hindi and regional languages, video spots, SMS campaigns, community engagement through song & drama, e-mailers, handouts, brochures etc. Other modes of information, education and communication including social media have also been adopted.(c)	The BBBP scheme has no provision for individual cash incentive/cash transfer. ********
