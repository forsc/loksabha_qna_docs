                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

468






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Vacancies in Legislative Department




Maddila  Gurumoorthy













                                                    Geetha Viswanath Vanga
                                                








                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there exist around 50 percent vacancies in the Legislative Department and its two wings, if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government has assessed the impact of such a large number of vacancies on its performance, if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the steps undertaken by the Government to fill up these vacancies?






ANSWER




                                                        MINSTER OF LAW & JUSTICE(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) to (c) : 	No, Sir. Presently around 171 posts out of 461 posts are vacant in the Legislative Department, which works out to 37.09% of the total posts. However, every effort is made to fill up the vacancies in the Department.   **********
