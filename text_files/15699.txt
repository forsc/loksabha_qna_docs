                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4276






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Removal of Traffic Snarls at Toll Gates




Sushil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether  the  Government  is  aware  that  a  lot  of lanes  at  the  toll  gates  are  often  closed  causing  traffic snarls especially  on NH-19;(b)  if  so,  the  steps  being  taken  to  address  such issues  and  to  penalize  the  toll-operators  for  such inefficiency;(c) whether the Government has initiated a process to compensate for the loss of time of public/commuters due  to  traffic  snarls  at  toll  gates;(d)  whether  the  Government  has  received  any complaints  against  toll  operators  and  if  so,  the  details thereof  along  with  the  action  taken  thereon;(e)  whether  the  Government  has  taken  any  action against any toll operator  for not operating the  tollgates properly  during  the  last  three  years;  and(f)  if  so,  the details  thereof  and  if  not,  the  reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to(c) Toll plaza operators (Concessionaire in case of BOT (Toll) plazas and user fee collecting agencies in case of Public Funded plazas) are mandated to operate all lanes at all times except for maintenance and repair requirements. All the lanes of the fee plaza on NH-19 (NH2 old) are open for tollable traffic, however, during maintenance some lanes are closed for short period. (d) to(f) Complaints are received in field offices of National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) against toll operators regarding misbehaviour, charging excess fee, etc. and action is taken by concerned officer as per the Contract Agreement.*****
