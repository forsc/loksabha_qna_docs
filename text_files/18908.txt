                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

447






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Budget Estimate for Odisha




Sarmistha Sethi














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the amount sanctioned to Odisha for Railway projects under Budget Estimate and Out of Budget Resources during the period of financial year 2010-11 and 2019-20;(b)  the amount actually released to Odisha under Budget Head and Out  of Budget Resources during the aforesaid period; and(c)   the year-wise and head-wise details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c):  Funds to the Railway Projects are allocated project-wise, not State-wise. However, during Financial Year, 2010-11 budget outlay of `900 Cr and during Financial Year, 2019-20 `4,568 Cr have been provided for infrastructure projects falling fully/partly in the State of Odisha. Odisha is covered by East Coast Railway, South Eastern Railway and South East Central Railway Zones of Indian Railways. The details of allotment of funds and expenditure project-wise and Zonal Railway wise are made available in public domain on Indian Railways website i.e. www.indianrailways.gov.in> Ministry of Railways >Railway Board >About Indian Railways >Railway Board Directorates >Finance (Budget) >Railway wise Works Machinery & Rolling Stock Programme >Regular Budget (year).*****
