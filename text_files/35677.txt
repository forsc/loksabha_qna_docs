                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2991






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Meetings of DISHA




Ajay Kumar Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Department of Rural Development has conducted any meeting of DISHA, if so, the details thereof and the decisions taken therein;(b) 	whether the Department of Rural Development has received information about the meeting of DISHA held in Bhagalpur district; and(d) 	if so, the action taken on the decisions taken in the said meeting and if not, the reasonstherefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)    	No Sir. The meetings of District Development Coordination and Monitoring Committee (DISHA) are conducted at the District level by respective District Administrations under the Chairpersonship of concerned Hon’ble Members of Parliament. The meetings of State level DISHA committees are chaired by respective Chief Ministers of States. (b)    	As per information uploaded by the District Administration of Bhagalpur, District level DISHA Committee meetings have been held on 12.07.2021 and 28.12.2021 during current financial year (2021-22). (d)    	Action on decisions taken in one DISHA Committee meeting is discussed in the next meeting of the Committee. The status of action on decisions taken in Bhagalpur District DISHA Committee meeting held on 12.07.2021 is enclosed in Annexure. *******
