                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3213






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Houses Built under PMAY-U




Locket Chatterjee














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    The number of houses built under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) in West Bengal and the funds sanctioned during the period of April, 2019 to March, 2021?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                                                               ANSWER                            THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                       MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                              (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U), a total of 1,22,570 houses have been constructed/ built in State of West Bengal during the period from April, 2019 to March, 2021. This Ministry has sanctioned Central Assistance of B 3,345.29 crore during aforesaid period to the State under the scheme.                                          *****
