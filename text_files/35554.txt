                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2820






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Entrance Tests for PG Courses




Goddeti Madhavi













                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether many Universities and Indian Institute of Technology (IITs)/ Indian Institute of Management (IIMs) are conducting entrance tests for Post Graduate (PG) course admissions in all States all over the country and at least in a city in each and every State.(b) if so, whether Delhi University and Ambedkar University of Delhi are not conducting their entrance examinations for PG courses in Andhra Pradesh inspite of CBT (Computer Based Test) Centers Infrastructure available in Visakhapatnam in AP.(c) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and(d) the measures taken/being taken by the Government to mitigate the hardships of students who have to travel all the way either to Hyderabad in Telangana or Bhubaneswar in Odisha, to take the said test?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) to (d) The admission to various masters programme in Indian Institute of Technology (IITs) is based on GATE  and JAM examinations, which are conducted in various parts of the country including in Andhra Pradesh. Likewise, the admission in Post graduate management courses in Indian Institute of Management (IIMs) is conducted through Common Admission Test (CAT) across the country. Dr B.R. Ambedkar University Delhi is under the Govt. of NCT of Delhi. The University of Delhi admits students against 50% of sanctioned Postgraduate seats through all India entrance examination which is conducted by National Testing Agency (NTA). In pursuance of National Education Policy, 2020, the admission in Central Universities for Undergraduate and Post graduate programmes is to be done through Common University Entrance Test (CUET) in minimum 13 languages and over hundreds of examination centres across the country.****
