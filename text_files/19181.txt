                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

654






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Airport in Darbhanga




 Gopal Jee Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Darbhanga airport in Bihar is the most successful airport under the UDAN scheme and if so, the details thereof; and(b) whether the Government proposes to develop Darbhanga airport as an international airport and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Darbhanga was one of the significant unserved airports which have now been covered under the Regional Connectivity Scheme-UDAN. The Implementing Agency, Airports Authority of India (AAI), awarded routes from Darbhanga connecting to Delhi, Bangalore and Mumbai in 2nd round of bidding under UDAN to the Selected Airline Operator (SAO). UDAN flights have been commenced by the SAO on 8.11.2020. ;;Under UDAN, as many as 64,238 passengers have travelled on 369 flights operated in two months after the commencement of flights from Darbhanga airport. At present, there is no proposal to develop Darbhanga as an international airport.
