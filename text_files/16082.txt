                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4553






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Anganwadi Centres in Backward Areas




Virendra  Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the total number of Anganwadi Centres approved in the country, State-wise;(b)	the number of such centres approved in most backward areas of the country; and(c)  	the steps taken by the Government to expedite the approval process of Anganwadi Centres in such areas?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILDDEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	State/UT-wise number of sanctioned and operational Anganwadi Centres across the country which includes Rural, Tribal, Riverine, Desert, Hilly and other difficult areas is Annexed.(b)&(c) Anganwadi Services under Umbrella Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Scheme is a universal, self-selecting Scheme. The Scheme is open to all eligible beneficiaries. All the 14 lakh AWCs/Mini-AWCs/ Anganwadi-on-Demand (AoDs) approved by the Government of India under 3rd phase of expansion of ICDS Scheme have already been allocated to the States/UTs. This Ministry had issued guidelines on 1st December, 2014 authorizing all the States/UTs to relocate the AWCs as per requirement and subject to fulfilment of prescribed conditions.********* AnnexureANNEXURE REFERRED IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.4553 FOR 20.03.2020 REGARDING ANGANWADI CENTRES IN BACKWARD AREAS ASKED BY DR.
