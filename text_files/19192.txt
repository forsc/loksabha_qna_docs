                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

475






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Collateral Free Loans to MSMEs




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    whether, Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) are given collateral free loans;(b)   if so, the total amount of loan sanctioned and disbursed for MSMEs during the last three years State/Year-wise;(c)    whether the Government has received any complaints from MSMEs about denial of collateral free loans from banks; and(d)   if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) & (b):	Yes, Sir.  The Credit Guarantee Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises  (CGTMSE) provides guarantees to various eligible Micro and Small units financed by the banks and financial institutions to facilitate collateral free loans. The upper limit for such loans is Rs. 2 crore. As a part of Atmanirbhar Bharat package, Government also announced the Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS) which is a Rs. 3 lakh crore collateral-free lending program with 100 percent credit guarantee for businesses including MSMEs. The State-wise/year-wise details of the loans under CGTMSE and State-wise details of the credit under ECLGS are given at Annexure I and Annexure II respectively. (c) & (d):	 All proposals that are eligible under the relevant guidelines for a collateral free loan are generally accepted. However, Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) sometimes receives complaints regarding denial of collateral free loans by the Banks.  As there is a prescribed procedure for obtaining a collateral free loans, such complaints received in the Ministry of MSME, are accordingly forwarded to concerned Bank for consideration as per the rule/procedure.
