                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2973






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Merger of Steel Plants




Kalyan Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Steel Executives Federation of India (SEFI) has been seeking strategic merger of the steel public sector units in the country;(b)	if so, the details of the proposal thereon;(c)	the present status of strategic disinvestment of Sail PSUs in the country therefor; and(d)	the details of the proposal for merger of alloy steel plant with Durgapur steel plants in West Bengal?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL		   (SHRI RAM CHANDRA PRASAD SINGH)(a)to(d): Government has taken ‘in-principle’ decision for strategic disinvestment of 3 (three) Units of Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL) [viz. Salem Steel Plant (SSP), Salem; Visvesvaraya Iron and Steel Plant (VISP), Bhadravathi and Alloy Steel Plant (ASP), Durgapur] on 27.10.2016 to a strategic buyer.  Representations are received from time to time with regard to strategic disinvestment/merger of Steel CPSEs.  The Government is guided by the New PSE policy approved by the Cabinet on 27.01.2021 which was notified on 04.02.2021.********
