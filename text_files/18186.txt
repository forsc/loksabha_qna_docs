                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2013






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Complaints Against Toll Plazas




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the fact that a  large  number  of  bouncers  are  being  employed  at various toll plazas on the National Highways across the country;(b)  if  so,  the  details  thereof;(c)  whether  the  Government  has  received  any complaints  against  toll  plazas  for  allegedly indulging in violence and fight by the bouncers with the car  drivers/owners  while  crossing  the toll  plazas  with  regard  to  payment  issues; and(d)  if  so,  the  details  thereof  along  with  the  action taken  thereon  by  the  Government?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS[GEN. (DR.) V.K. SINGH (RETD.)](a) & (b) In respect of Public Funded fee plazas, Clause 12 and 13 of the user fee collection contract defined the deployment of toll plazas personnel and the requirement of toll plaza personnel is listed as Schedule III of the Contract Agreement. In case of Concessionaire fee plazas, the toll personnel are employed as per the provisions of respective Concession Agreement.  Contract does not cater for deployment of bouncers at any fee plaza.(c) & (d) No such complaints have been received at NHAI Helpline No. 1033.*****
