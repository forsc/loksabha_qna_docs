                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

761






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Construction of National Highways




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)     whether the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) has achieved highest target for construction of National Highways (NHs) in the country in Financial Year 2019-20 and if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(b)    the initiatives taken to revive the stalled projects and expedite completion of new projects in order to accelerate the pace of highway construction across the country;(c)	whether the Government has surpassed the target for construction of NHs in the country for the period from April to August, 2020 and if so, the details thereof including the target fixed and achieved during the said period;(d)	whether it is a fact that 3300 km of NH works have been awarded in the current year till August, which is more than double of the 1367 kms during the same period last year and if so, the details thereof; and(e)	whether sanctions for construction of 2983 kilometers of NHs were issued during the said period and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)       The construction of National Highways achieved by National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) during last 5 years is 2015-16: 1,988 km; 2016-17: 2,628 km; 2017-18: 3,070 km; 2018-19: 3,380 km and 2019-20: 3,979 km. The State wise details of construction of NHs undertaken by NHAI in 2019-20 is at Annexure.(b)       Regular Meetings at various levels with Project Developers, State Governments and Contractors are held to speed up the stalled Highway Projects.(c)       3,322 km NH length has been constructed from April to August, 2020 against the target of 2998 km.(d)       About 3,500 km of NH works have been awarded till August, 2020. Whereas about 1367 km of NH works were awarded during the same period in last year.(e)       1,265 km, 1,183 km & 535 km of NH works were sanctioned for State PWDs, NHAI and NHIDCL respectively.
