                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2283






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Changing History Books




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plans to change history text books;(b) if so, the reasons therefor;(c) whether the Government had invited Bharatiya Shikshan Mandal to contribute their suggestions and recommendations on changing of history text books; and(d) if so, the reasons for inviting a particular organization for the purpose?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) and (b)	The National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT)’s present set of textbooks, including history textbooks, have been prepared based on the National Curriculum  Framework (NCF), 2005.  Consequent upon announcement of the National Education Policy (NEP), 2020, a new National Curriculum Framework (NCF) will be  prepared followed by development of  textbooks in all subjects, including history.  (c)	No, Sir.(d)	Does not arise.****
