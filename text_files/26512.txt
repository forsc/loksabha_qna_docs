                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2247






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Industrial Training Institute (ITIs)




Rebati Tripura














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to establish new Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) in North Eastern Region especially in Tripura, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (b) 	the total number of both Government and private ITIs presently running in Tripura State; (c) 	the amount of funds sanctioned by the Government for the establishment/ improvement of ITIs during the last three years and the current year; and (d) 	the further steps taken by the Government to improve the quality of ITIs in the country especially in NER?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	The Industrial training institutes (ITIs) are under the administrative control of State Government. Establishment and administration of ITI comes under the jurisdiction of State Government, while the policies such as setting the criterion for affiliation, conducting the examination along with certification and designing curriculum are the responsibilities of Central Government.		However, Directorate General of Training, an attached office under the Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship is implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme titled “Enhancing Skill Development Infrastructure in NE States (ESDI)”, in partnership with the State under which Government of India is funding for establishment of 34 new it is in North Eastern States including 3 in Tripura State, as given in Annexure – I. (b)	A total of 21 Industrial Training Institutes (19 Government and 2 Private) are running in Tripura State under Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS). (c)  The amount of fund sanctioned for establishment / improvement of ITIs in the country including North Eastern States is given at Annexure- II.(d)  The Central Government is emphasizing on outcome based monitoring to improve quality of ITIs. The release of funds to ITIs under “Skill Strengthening for Industrial Value Enhancement (STRIVE)” for improvement is based on achievement of key performance indicator such as following:-1.	Percentage increase in number of graduates at the respective ITI.2.	Increase in female enrolment rate across trades at the respective ITI3.	Percentage increase in total enrolments at the respective ITIs4.	Increase in percentage of total trainees at the respective ITI who have undergone OJT as per the prescribed curriculum.Under ESDI scheme, one of the objectives of the scheme is to strengthen infrastructure of ITI in N.E Region either through upgradation of 22 ITI, supplementing deficient infrastructure of 28 ITI and support state and build capacities of their stake holders through training and innovation.Also under Upgradation of Government ITI into Model ITI scheme an existing ITI in a State is being upgraded as Model ITI which will be evolved as an institution showcasing the best practices, efficient and high quality training delivery and sustainable and effective industry relationship.
