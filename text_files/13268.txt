                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2267






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Coal Extracted by WCL




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Western Coalfields Limited (WCL) carryout the excavation work in the coal mines;(b)	if so, the amount and value of coal extracted by WCL from the year 2014 till date and the details of total cost involved therein; and (c)	the comparative analysis of the mining cost of extraction of coal by public sector companies vis-a-vis private sector from 2014 onwards?  






ANSWER




MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a)&(b): Yes, Sir. The amount and value of coal extracted by WCL from the year 2014 to April-December 2019 and the total cost involved therein is:Particulars	2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20 (April to Dec.19)Coal Production (Lakh Tonnes)	411.47	448.16	456.32	462.20	531.80	330.50Total Expenditure(Rs. Crores)	7432.90	7838.05	8340.29	11579.90	10994.18	7417.90Total Expenditure of coal Extracted(Rs per Tonnes)	1806.43	1748.93	1827.74	2505.38	2067.36	2244.45(c): Data on mining cost of private sector is not maintained in the Ministry.****
