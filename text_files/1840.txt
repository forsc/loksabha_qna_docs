                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1757






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Pending Fertilizer Subsidy




Darshana Vikram Jardosh














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the action taken by the Government for release of the pending subsidy to PSU fertilizers/Co-operative Societies as fertilizer industries are facing great difficulties due to non-release of subsidy; (b)	the total amount of subsidy released during the last three years; and (c)	the details of fertilizer companies which received the said subsidies, Statewise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a):	The Government releases subsidy to the fertilizer companies on a regular basis subject to availability of budget, receipt of quality certificates (B2) from the State Governments etc. Whenever there is shortage of funds, the Government liquidates the pending subsidy by arranging loans under a Special Banking Arrangement (SBA).(b) & (c):	The details of fertilizer-wise subsidy made available by the Government on various fertilizers during the last three years is enclosed in Annexure-I to III. The State/UT-wise subsidy figures are not available as the subsidy is paid to fertilizers companies.*******
