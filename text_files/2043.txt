                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ATOMIC ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1971






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Site for Nuclear Plant in Andhra Pradesh




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





ATOMIC ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Department  of  Atomic Energy (DAE) and Nuclear Power Corporation of India Limited (NPCIL) have discussed with the Government of Andhra Pradesh the feasibility of identifying a second coastal  site  for setting up a nuclear power plant;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether DAE  is  keen  to identify  a site and  build  two Russian designed Water- Water Energetic Reactor (VVER) of 1000 MW; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES & PENSIONS AND PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR.JITENDRA SINGH):    (a)	Yes, Sir. 	(b)	Various scientific studies, such as geotechnical investigations, seismological studies etc. are required to be carried out by expert agencies as a part of determining the suitability of a potential site for setting up nuclear power plants. (c)&(d)Considering the future nuclear power programme expansion planned and the advantage of coastal sites in view of availability of abundant cooling water and ease of transport of large, heavy equipment by sea, the possibility of a coastal site in the state of Andhra Pradesh is also being examined to locate nuclear power plants with Russian technical cooperation.*****
