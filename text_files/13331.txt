                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2248






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






National Retail Policy




Nama Nageswara Rao














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to have a National Retail Policy to streamline retail trade in the country and promote Ease of Doing Business in the sector;(b) 	if so, the details thereof including timelines for implementing the same; and(c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c):	Yes, Sir. The Government proposes to have a National Retail Trade Policy to create conducive environment for retail trade including by simplifying rules and regulations hindering the growth of retail sector. As the  National Retail Trade Policy is under formulation after which implementation will  happen, it is not possible to give timeline for implementation.********
