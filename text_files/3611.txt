                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3380






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






NGOs Implementing Ujjawala Scheme




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs) implementing the Ujjawala Scheme for rehabilitating victims of trafficking during the last three years, year  and State-wise;(b) 	the number of NGOs that have withdrawn their approval from the scheme during the same period, year and State-wise;(c) 	the quantum of funds  sanctioned, released and utilized  under the scheme during the last three years, year  and State-wise; and(d) 	the number of beneficiaries covered under the scheme during the said period, year and State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	The state-wise and year-wise details of Non-Government Organisations (NGOs) implementing the Ujjawala Scheme for rehabilitating victims of trafficking during the last three years  is at Annexure-I. (b)	As per information received from Government of Uttar Pradesh, 9 NGOs have withdrawn from Ujjawala Scheme during the last 3 years.(c)	The details of funds sanctioned, released and utilized under the scheme during the last three years, year and State-wise is at Annexure-II.(d)	The number of beneficiaries covered under the scheme during the said period, year-wise and State-wise is at Annexure-III.********Annexure-IStatement referred in reply of part (a) of Lok Sabha Un-Starred Question No. 3380 to be answered on 12.07.2019 regarding NGOs Implementing Ujjawala Scheme by  Shri Rajeshbhai Chudasama during the last 3 years State-wise.Sl.
