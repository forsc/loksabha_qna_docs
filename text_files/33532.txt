                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

954






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Status of PMFME Scheme




Shankar Lalwani














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the current status of PM Formalization of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) Scheme;  (b)	the list of beneficiaries under the said scheme in Madhya Pradesh, district-wise; (c)	the criteria fixed by the Government for availing benefits of PMFME Scheme; and  (d)	the manner in which one can apply in this scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a):	The current status of PM Formalization of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PMFME) Scheme is as under:  (i). 35 States/ UTs have nominated State Nodal Agencies (SNAs) and also constituted State Level Approval Committees (SLACs) and District Level Committees (DLCs). State of West Bengal is yet to decide on participation in the scheme.(ii). One District One Products (ODOPs) for 710 Districts have been approved in 35 States / UTs.  (iii). Seed Capital amounting to Rs.127.46 crore released for 43,118 Self Help Group (SHG) Members. (iv). 75 Incubation Centers have been approved with an outlay of Rs. 200.03 crore.(v). Under Marketing & Branding component of the scheme, 7 One District One Products (ODOPs) brands have been launched in the States of Bihar, Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan, Maharashtra, Jammu & Kashmir and Haryana and 2 proposals from Punjab and Maharashtra have been approved. (vi). For capacity building, 2 National Level Technical Institutes (NLTIs) and 42 State Level Technical Institutes (SLTIs) in 35 States/UTs have been nominated. (vii). 760 Product Training Modules developed containing Presentations, Videos, Detailed Project Reports (DPRs) and Course content/ Handbook for the benefit of beneficiaries. (viii). 426 Master Trainers of 34 States/UTs, 580 District Level Trainers of 23 States/UTs and 1114 beneficiaries from 13 States/UTs have been trained. 932 District Resource Persons (DRPs) have been appointed to provide handholding support to beneficiaries. (ix). Grants-in-aid to 1793 beneficiaries have been sanctioned out of 14,565 applications submitted. (b):	A list indicating the district-wise number of beneficiaries under the PMFME scheme in Madhya Pradesh is at Annexure.   (c). The scheme guidelines indicating criteria fixed for availing benefits under PMFME Scheme are available on PMFME’s website https://pmfme.mofpi.gov.in/pmfme/#/Home-Page for the information of all stakeholders and public at large. (d): The interested Individuals and Groups can submit applications through online portal of PMFME scheme.  *****
