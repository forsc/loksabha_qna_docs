                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

942






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Mahatma Gandhi Bunkar Bima Yojana




Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the salient features of Mahatma Gandhi Bunkar Bima Yojana alongwith the details of its objectives and eligibility criteria;(b) the total number of people benefited under Mahatma Gandhi Bunkar Bima Yojana during the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise, particularly in Maharashtra; and(c) the details of efforts being made by the Government to provide coverage to maximum number of people under the Yojana? 






ANSWER




                                                        ?????? ?????? (??????? ?????? ?????? ?????)MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a):	The Converged Mahatma Gandhi Bunkar Bima Yojana (MGBBY), a component of Handloom Weavers Comprehensive Welfare Scheme, is an insurance scheme offering life insurance cover and accidental insurance cover for death or disability for a closed group of handloom weavers/workers in the age group of 51-59 years who have already enrolled under the scheme on 31.05.2017. LIC of India has conveyed to the Ministry of Textiles that the beneficiaries of the converged scheme have to be transitioned to full premium payment regime with effect from 1st April, 2020. The annual premium of Rs.470/- is shared as under:- Government of India	Rs.290/-Weaver/worker	Rs.80/-LIC of India	Rs.100/-Total Premium	Rs.470/-Benefits: Natural Death	Rs.60,000/-Accidental Death	Rs.1,50,000/-Total Disability	Rs.1,50,000/-Partial Disability	Rs.75,000/-(b):	The State-wise number of claims settled during the last three years and current year including in Maharashtra is at Annexure-I.  (c):	State Governments are advised to enroll maximum number of weavers/workers under Handloom Weavers Comprehensive Welfare Scheme. To provide the benefits to all handloom weavers/workers in an effective manner, the Government of India had organized Hasthkala Sahyog Shivirs in handloom clusters across the country in association with State Governments and LIC for creating awareness among the weavers for enrollment under the Scheme.
