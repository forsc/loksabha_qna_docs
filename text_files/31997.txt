                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3607






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Lalit Narayan Mithila University




 Gopal Jee Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has proposed to restart enrolment in various courses of Lalit Naryan Mithila University, Directorate of Distance Education in interest of students despite the fact that it has been stopped from 2020-21 session; and(b) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a)&(b): University Grants Commission (Open and Distance Learning Programmes and Online Programmes) Regulations, 2020 were notified on 4th September, 2020. Lalit Narayan Mithila University, Darbhanga, Bihar was not eligible as per the provisions of the said regulations and accordingly has not been accorded recognition for academic year 2020-21 onwards. Also, the University did not apply for one year extension (2020-21) within the due date i.e. 29.02.2020 under section 3(A) of the said regulations.***
