                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1173






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Covid-19 Vaccinated Students




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether as per the University Grants Commission (UGC) guidelines only partially orfully Covid-19 vaccinated students are/will be allowed to attend colleges physically;and(b) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) & (b): The University Grants Commission (UGC) has informed that UGC has issuedletters to Vice-Chancellors of all Universities and Principals of all colleges requestingthem for coordinated and collective efforts on spreading awareness about COVID-19appropriate behavior and vaccination. These letters are available athttps://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/2644226_Letter-reg-Covid-vaccine.pdf andhttps://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/Hoarding/UGC_covid_letter.pdf.UGC has further informed that Higher Educational Institutions have been advisedthrough guidelines issued from time to time, to carry out academic activities followingnecessary protocols / guidelines / directions /advisories issued by Central / StateGovernment or competent authorities, in view of COVID-19 Pandemic.*****
