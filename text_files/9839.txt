                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3253






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Exploitation of Hydrocarbons




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the list of sites in Tamil Nadu choosen for exploitation of Hyrdrocarbons;(b) the current status of the projects;(c) the steps taken by the Government to stop exploitation of Hydrocarbon wells in Tamil Nadu; and(d) the Government’s stand in this regard after the protests from Tamil Nadu Government and Farmers’ Organisations?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) Under Hydrocarbon Exploration and Licensing (HELP) policy, three bidding rounds have been finalized in which six exploration blocks have been awarded in Tamil Nadu. Under Discovered Small Field (DSF) policy, two bidding rounds have been finalized in which two contract areas have been awarded in Tamil Nadu. The contractor can commence exploration related activities after obtaining Petroleum Exploration License from State Government and other statutory clearances. Under Production Sharing Contracts (PSCs) regime, only two exploration blocks are operational in Tamil Nadu. Three hydrocarbon discoveries have been notified in these two blocks. Major portion of all these blocks falls in districts of Nagapattinam, Karaikal, Cuddalore, Thiruvarur, Sivaganga, Ramanathapuram and Ariyalur.(c) & (d) Some local people/organizations have represented/expressed apprehension regarding possible environmental impact. Tamil Nadu Government has intimated that it has constituted a High Level Expert Technical Committee to study the impact of hydrocarbon blocks in Cauvery delta region. The issues related to operational activities of the fields are dealt with in consultation with authorities concerned and as per applicable laws, rules/regulations, guidelines etc.********
