                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4249






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Housing Projects in Odisha




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the housing projects sponsored by the Union Government in Odisha during the last three years;b)	the allocation of funds made and amount actually spent in those projects during the said period, project-wise; andc)	the number of beneficiaries actually benefitted thereby?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                              (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	 to (c)Union Government through its scheme of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana ( Urban) [PMAY(U)], has sanctioned 616 projects for construction of 1,50,384houses for identified beneficiaries in the State of Odisha. The sanctioned houses involve Central Assistance of Rs. 2340.90 crore. Project wise details of number of beneficiaries, Central Assistance released to the State Government for these projects and amount utilized during the last three years are at Annexure.                                   *****
