                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

463






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Metro Rail Services




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is considering to introduce metro rail services in various cities of the country as per the requirement and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government is also considering to introduce metro rail services in various cities of Gujarat;(c)     if so, the details thereof including the names of the cities proposed in this regard along with the time by which the said service is likely to be introduced in the said cities; and(d)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                         (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	Urban transport, which is an integral part of urban development, is a State subject. States/Union Territories (UTs) are responsible for development of urban transport infrastructure including metro rail projects in their cities. The Central Government considers financial assistance for metro rail projects in the cities or urban agglomerates subject to feasibility of the project and availability of resources, as and when posed by the concerned State/UTs.(b to d)	Metro rail projects have been approved for cities of Gujarat as detailed below:S.No	Name of the Project	Name of the cities	Length (in Kms)	Sanctioned/Expected Date of                                                                          Completion of Project1	Ahmedabad Metro Rail       Ahmedabad & Gandhinagar   40.23  The stretch of 6.5 kms from          Project (Phase-I)                                          Vastral Gam to Apparel Park is                                                                    operational since March 2019.
