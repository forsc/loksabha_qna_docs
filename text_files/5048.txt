                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4758






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






NEET Exemption




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of Tamil Nadu State seeking exemptionfrom the ambit of National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) foradmission to MBBS/BDS courses;(b) if so, the present status and the action taken/being taken by the Government inthis regard; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c): Medical Education comes under the purview of the Ministry of Health& Family Welfare, which has informed that request was received from the Stateof Tamil Nadu seeking exemption from the ambit of National Eligibility cumEntrance Test (NEET) for undergraduate and postgraduate medical admissions.However, Section 10D of the Indian Medical Council Act, 1956 prescribesconducting of a uniform entrance examination, namely NEET for medicaladmissions. Since the provisions of the above Act apply across the countrywithout exemption, no relaxation was given to the State.*****
