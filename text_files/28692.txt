                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

525






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






PLI Scheme for Telecom Sector




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has launched a Production Linked Incentive Scheme (PLI Scheme) for Telecom and Networking Products and if so, the details thereof and the aims and objectives behind the move; (b) 	the estimated boost to domestic manufacturing of telecom and networking products as a result of this scheme; (c) 	the financial outlay reserved for this scheme; (d) 	the companies that have been eligible and given approval under the PLI Scheme; and (e) 	the other steps taken by the Government to reduce dependence on other countries for importing telecom and networking products?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)	to (e) 	Yes sir, the Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for Telecom and Networking Products has been notified on dated 24.02.2021to promote Telecom and Networking Products manufacturing in India and attract investments in telecom sector within overall financial outlay of Rs.12,195 Crore over 5 years. The Scheme is targeted to boost domestic manufacturing of telecom and networking products and also to reduce dependence on other countries for importing telecom and networking products. The details of companies found eligible and given approval under the Scheme are enclosed as ANNEXURE.
