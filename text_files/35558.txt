                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2827






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






IIITDM, Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh




Talari Rangaiah














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has sanctioned Indian Institute of Information Technology, Design and Manufacturing (IIITDM) Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh as per Andhra Pradesh re-organization Act, 2014; (b) if so, the details thereof indicating the progress and the performance of IIITDM, Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh; and (c) the funds allocated, released and utilised and the timeline for completion of the IIITDM, Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) : Yes Sir.  (b) :	Indian Institute of Information Technology Design and Manufacturing (IIITDM), Kurnool started its academic session from the campus of its Mentor Institute i.e. IIITDM Kancheepuram from 2015-16 with initial student intake of 50. The Institute is operating from its permanent campus at Jagannathagattu Hill, Kurnool from July 2018.  The current students'' strength is 616.  Institute offers UG, PG and Ph.D programmes in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Mechanical Engineering (ME), etc. Apart from existing buildings i.e. Administrative cum Academic building, hostels and Dining halls, construction of 12 buildings in permanent campus is in an advance stage. (c) : An amount of Rs.297.00 crore was approved for establishment of permanent campus of IIITDM Kurnool, out of which an amount of Rs.151.35 crore has been released.  The likely date of completion of permanent campus is December, 2022.  *****
