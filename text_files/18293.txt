                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1954






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Promotion of Hand Tool Industry




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)        whether the Government proposes to formulate any scheme to promote Hand Tool Industry of local districts through MSME centre located in Nagaur district of Rajasthan and to provide employment in this sector; (b)        if so, the time by which the said scheme is likely to be formulated by the Government; and  (c)        the funds allocated to the said centre located in Nagaur during the last two years along with the funds utilised, item-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OFMICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI  PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a)&(b):    Government of India, Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises has already set up an Extension Centre of MSME Technology Development Centre  (Process and Product Development Centre), Agra at Nagaur district of Rajasthan to support and handhold Hand tool manufacturing MSMEs of Nagaur and nearby areas. (c) :          During the last two years, the total funds released for the center is Rs.5.82 crores (Rupees Five Crore Eighty Two lakhs only) for procurement of machinery and equipment and civil works, which have been utilized.
