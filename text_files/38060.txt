                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4638






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Development under CRIF in Odisha




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds allocated and released to Odisha under Central Road and Infrastructure Fund (CRIF) during the last three years and the current year for development of State roads and various sector along with the utilisation status thereof; and (b) the details of road projects being developed in the State under CRIF?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS (Shri NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) The funds allocated & released to the State of Odisha under Central Road and Infrastructure Fund (CRIF) during the last three years and the current year are as under:- Sl. No.	          Year	Allocation / Accrual of funds to Odisha under CRIF (Rs. in Cr.)	Funds released (Rs in Cr)1	2018-19	332.23	111.932	2019-20	308.94	286.963	2020-21	286.97	534.194	2021-22	298.67	535.07  (b)     The details of road projects being developed in the State of Odisha under CRIF are at Annexure.
