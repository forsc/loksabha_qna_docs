                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2319






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






e-Governance for Agriculture




Amol Ramsing Kolhe













                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Krishna Pal Singh Yadav
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has launched the National e-Governance Plan in Agriculture (NeGPA);(b)	if so, the details thereof including the names of the States where it is being implemented along with the aims and objectives;(c)	the challenges faced by the Government while implementing the plan and achievements made since its inception;(d)	whether the Government has also launched Phase II of the scheme, if so, the quantum of funds allocated/released to the States/UTs;(e)	whether the Government has also amended NeGPA guidelines, if so, the details thereof;(f)	whether the Government has also launched Unified Farmers Service Platform, if so, the details thereof along with the aims and objectives behind the move; and(g)	the names of the programmes currently being run under the National e-Governance Plan in Agriculture and the role played by the public and private sectors in these programmes?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	to (c): Yes, Sir. National e-Governance Plan in Agriculture (NeGP-A) was initially launched in 2010-11 in 7 pilot states with aims to achieve rapid development in India through use of Information & Communication Technology (ICT) for timely access to agriculture related information for the farmers. The key objectives of the Project include: •	Bringing farmer centricity & service orientation to the programs •	Enhancing reach & impact of extension services •	Improving access of farmers to information & services throughout crop- 	cycle •	Building upon, enhancing & integrating the existing ICT initiatives of 	Centre, and States •	Enhancing efficiency & effectiveness of programs through process			 redesign •	More effective management of schemes of DAC •	Promoting a common framework across states Under the scheme funds were released to States for procurement of hardware, site preparation, training, State Project Monitoring Units, data Digitization, connectivity upto Block Level, consumables, dissemination of agriculture related information through ICT means, customization of applications as per State/UT specific requirement etc.(d)  Phase-II of the scheme commenced from 2014-15 which was extended for all the remaining States and 2 UTs. The quantum of funds allocated/released to States/UTs during last three years is as under:	In Rs. LakhsYear	Allocation	Release2017-18	1750.06	1748.002018-19	3100.00	2278.092019-20	2600.00	2192.07(e)    In 2020-21, following the recommendations made by Doubling of Farmers Income committee, NeGPA guidelines were revised envisaging sanctioning of funds to States/UTsfor projects involving use of modern Information Technologies mentioned above such as Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Block Chain Technology, Internet of Things, Robotics etc., and for customization / shifting of web & mobile applications already developed by the States, to the platform to be developed using digital/emerging technologies.(f)  Yes, Sir. The Department of Agriculture, Cooperation and Farmers Welfare is developing a Unified Farmer Service Platform (UFSP) consisting a Federated Farmer Database comprising of a nationwide land linked Federated Database of “Verified” Farmers and various services related to farmer identification and authentication which shall be built on top of it.(g)  Under the revised guidelines funds are only sanctioned to States/UTs undertaking/implementing projects involving new & emerging technologies described in (e) above. *******
