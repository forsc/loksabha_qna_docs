                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2194






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Preservation of Less Number of Monuments in Bihar




Veena Devi














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the reasons for preservation of less number of monuments in Bihar in comparison to other States of India;(b)	whether there is any proposal to increase the number of monuments of Bihar to be declared as Centrally protected monuments; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	Archaeological Survey of India protects monuments and sites that are found to be of National importance under the provision of Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act, 1958.  As of now, 70 monuments/sites in Bihar have been notified for protection by the Central Government.  There are several States which have lesser number of protected monuments compared to Bihar. (b)	At present there is no proposal. (c)	Does not arise. *****
