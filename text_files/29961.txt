                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

151






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






National Broadband Mission in Bihar




Dulal Chandra Goswami














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the amount of funds allocated so far by the Union Government to the State of Bihar under the National Broadband Mission (NBM);(b) 	the progress made under this mission at the State level so far; and(c) 	the time by which the access to Broadband is likely to be provided to all the villages of the country under this mission?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS & ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (c)	 A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT TO BE LAID ON THE TABLE OF THE LOK SABHA IN RESPECT OF PARTS (a) to (c) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 151 FOR 8TH DECEMBER, 2021 REGARDING “NATIONAL BROADBAND MISSION IN BIHAR”(a) 	For realizing the vision of National Broadband Mission (NBM), broadband facility is provided by the Government and the Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) through wireless mobile and fixed wireless connections in phased manner. Fund utilized by Government of India under BharatNet project through Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF) in Bihar,  as on 30.09.2021, is Rs.1300.29 crores. (b) 	As per data provided by Telecom Service Providers and field units of Department of Telecommunications, out of 39,703 inhabited villages (as per Census 2011) in Bihar, mobile wireless broadband coverage has been provided in 38,733 villages. Under BharatNet project,  8298 GPs have been made Service Ready in Bihar.  (c) 	On 30.06.2021, the scope of BharatNet was extended up to all inhabited villages beyond GPs in the country for complete coverage under the project by August, 2023.
