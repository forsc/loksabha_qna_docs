                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3132






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Homoeopathy College in Odisha




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether any study/assessment has been made to ascertain adequacy of homoeopathy medical colleges in the country, if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government proposes to consider establishing adequate number of homoeopathic colleges to promote Indian Systems of Medicine in the country; (c) if so, the details thereof, State/UTwise, particularly in Odisha; and (d) the steps taken by the Government to establish homoeopathy medical colleges in Odisha to provide affordable healthcare services to the increasing population?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to  (c):     No study has been made to ascertain adequacy of homoeopathy medical colleges in the country as public health is a State subject. The Central Government approves eligible proposals received and recommended from the State Government.(d):   Ministry of AYUSH has received no proposal for establishment of homeopathic medical college in Odisha.………………..
