                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1536






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Refund for Tatkal Cancellations




Vivek Narayan Shejwalkar














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether tatkal ticketing system does not have any provision for refund of any amount to railway passengers on cancellation of tickets whereas cancellation of reserved tickets of other category provide for refund of some percentage of the ticket cost;(b) whether the Ministry of Railways proposes to consider to refund amount by deducting some amount on cancellation of tickets by changing the tatkal ticketing system to protect the financial interest of railway passengers;  and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                           MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY                               (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW) (a) to (c): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
