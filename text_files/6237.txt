                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Database of Artists




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is likely to bring any provision to create State-wise list/database of the artists who create, preserve and promote art and literature of all kinds;(b)	if so, the details of artists likely to be included in the database and the rules/ guidelines/criteria to be followed for their inclusion; (c)	whether the Government proposes to make a list of all the artists across the country;(d)	If so, whether any direction to the states is likely to be issued in this regard, and(e)	If so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR TOURISM  CULTURE( SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	 to (e): A statement is laid on the Table of the House STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) TO (E) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 2(H)(a)	No, Sir.(b)	Does not arise.(c)	Yes, Sir(d)	No, Sir.(e)	Does not arise.***
