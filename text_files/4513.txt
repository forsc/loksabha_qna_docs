                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4217






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Construction of Bypass on NH-15




Saikia Dilip














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether 30 to 37 km stretch (Senglipara to Lengripara) under National Highway No. 15 is passing through congested city and causing several road accidents regularly; (b)	if so, the details thereof along with the reaction of the Government thereto;(c)	whether the Government proposes to construct bypass on the said NH and if so, the details thereof;(d)	whether the Government has prepared DPR in this regard and if so, the details thereof; and (e)	the time by which construction of said bypass is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (e) 	The existing road from km. 30 to km. 37 (Senglipara to Lengripara) on NH-15 passing through Mangaldoi Town is facing traffic congestion due to growth in traffic volume. To avoid traffic congestion, consultancy service for preparation of DPR for construction of bypass (Mangaldoi Bypass) from km 30.00 to km 42.00 of NH-15 (Old NH 52) is taken up. Alignment of bypass has already been approved in-principle by Ministry subject to vetting of Local Administration and Town & Country Planning Department. Construction depends on State Government vetting of alignment, acquisition of land and availability of funds.*****
