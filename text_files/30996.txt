                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

259






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Stoppage at Jaunpur




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any criteria to decide stoppage of trains at a particular railway station; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the details of all the trains having a stoppage at Jaunpur City and Jaunpur Junction railway stations; (d) whether the Government intends to increase stoppage of trains at the said railway stations; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW) (a)  to (e)  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF STARRED QUESTION NO. 259 BY SHRI SHYAM SINGH YADAV TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 15.12.2021 REGARDING STOPPAGE AT JAUNPUR(a) and (b) The criteria relating to provision of stoppages of Mail/Express trains, inter alia, stipulates to consider the following factors: (1) 	Total sale of tickets at the station for both UP and DN direction for all the trains already stopping at the station, distance wise, class-wise and cost-wise (2) 	trains already stopping/availability of alternative services, convenience and occupancy of the existing services(3) 	effect on line capacity of the section and punctuality of trains(4)	 population and significance of towns/cities and new developments taking place (5) 	nature of the train and occupancy of the train for which stoppage is demanded (6) 	operational constraints etc. (7) 	pattern of traffic at the station(8) 	passing time of the train at the station.(c) to (e)  At present, Jaunpur City is being served by 15 pairs of trains. Similarly, Jaunpur Junction is being served by 24 pairs of train services. These services are considered adequate to cater to the present level of traffic.  Provision of stoppage of additional trains at Jaunpur City and/or Jaunpur Junction is operationally not expedient, at present. However, provision of stoppage is an ongoing process on Indian Railways subject to operational feasibility and traffic justification.*****
