                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3199






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Irregularities/Corruption in Steel Plants




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the cases of corruption, irregularity and violation of rules have come into limelight on a large scale due to the nexus of officers and contractors in different steel plants in the country;(b)	if so, the details of total number of cases where the requisite action has been taken and the details of number of cases still pending; and(c)	the action taken/proposed to be taken by the Government against the defaulters?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)to(c): The registration and disposal of complaints in Steel CPSEs is an ongoing process. During the year, 2020 and 2021 (till 28th February, 2021), disciplinary proceedings [including those carried over from previous year(s)] have been completed against 42 employees with imposition of suitable penalty. ********
