                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2731






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Criteria for setting up of CGHS Wellness Centres




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the details of criteria fixed for setting up of dispensaries/hospitals under the Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS) in the country;(b) the dispensaries/hospitals functioning under the CGHS in the country at present, State/UT-wise;(c) the number of beneficiaries who had got treatment in Rajasthan under the CGHS during the last five years, district-wise;(d) whether the Government proposes to set up new dispensaries/hospitals in the districts having more than 1000 beneficiaries under the CGHS; and(e) if so, the details thereof and the time by which it is likely to be done?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a):	The criteria fixed for setting up a Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS) Wellness Centre in a particular area are as under:-I.	In an existing CGHS City:- For opening of a new Allopathic CGHS Wellness Centre in an existing CGHS city, there has to be a minimum of  2000 Card holders (serving employees of Central Government and Central Civil pensioners).II.	Extension of CGHS to a new City:- For extension of CGHS to a new city, there has to be a minimum of 6,000 Card holders.Proposals for setting up of new Wellness Centres are considered as per extant norms and subject to availability of resources.(b):	A statement showing number of CGHS Allopathic Dispensaries/Wellness Centres functioning in the country, state-wise is at Annexure.(c):	CGHS is in operation in Jaipur. Apart from this, the scheme is also operational in Ajmer, Jodhpur and Kota since 01.04.2019 after takeover of Postal dispensaries in these cities/districts.No. of beneficiaries availing CGHS facilities in Rajasthan during last five years and the current year are given as under:	01.04.2014-31.03.2019	01.04.2019-02.03.2020Jaipur	20,65,777	4,42,117Ajmer	Not applicable	13,462Jodhpur	Not applicable	4,815Kota	Not applicable	7,984(d) & (e):	No.
