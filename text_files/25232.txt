                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

956






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Petrol Pumps Operating on Rent




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of petrol pumps of Hindustan Petroleum, Indian Oil and BharatPetroleum currently in operation in Chhattisgarh;(b) the number of petrol pumps operating on rental land along with the number of petrolpumps which are being operated on the land of the dealers themselves;(c) the number of petrol pumps being operated on the rental land having valid rentagreement;(d) the number of petrol pumps still operational even after the lapse of their rentalagreement of the leased land;(e) the names of the companies which are running illegal petrol pumps; and(f) the number of the said petrol pumps in Chhattisgarh which are being operated as perprovision of Rule 152(1) of Petroleum Act 2002 of Section 118(1) (1) of 31.12.2008 ofGovernment of India‘s Gazette No.686 (Extraordinary Part II - Clause 3 - Sub-clause I)?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (d) As on 30.6.2021, Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) viz. IndianOil Corporation Limited, Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited and HindustanPetroleum Corporation Limited have 1776 operational petrol pumps in Chhattisgarh outof which 695 are operational on sites taken on lease from the landlords and 1056 are ondealers owned sites. Further, there are 25 sites where the lease period has expired and670 retail outlets are operating on the rental land having valid rent-agreement.(e)&(f) Public Sector OMCs have informed that all petrol pumps in the State ofChhattisgarh are operating with the required statutory approval/licences.****
