                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2966






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Implementation of JJM in West Bengal




Locket Chatterjee














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the State Government is cooperating with the Union Government for the implementation of Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM); (b) 	the details of the funds allocated to West Bengal under this mission since its inception; (c) 	whether West Bengal is at the last position in the country in providing piped drinking water to all households; (d) 	if so, the reasons therefor; and (e) 	whether the Government of West Bengal has changed the name of Jal Jeevan Mission to Jal Swapno in West Bengal?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI  PRAHLAD  SINGH  PATEL)(a) 	Since August, 2019, Government of India in partnership with States, including West Bengal, is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) to make provision of tap water connection to every rural household by 2024. (b) 	The details of fund allocated, released and reported utilization in West Bengal under JJM in 2019-20, 2020-21 & 2021-22, is as under:(Amount in Rs. Crore)Year	Central share	Expenditure under State share	Opening Balance	 Allocation	Fund Released/ drawn	Total fund available	Reported utilization	2019-20	760.82	995.33	994.75	1,755.57	609.00	445.032020-21	1,146.58	1,614.18	807.08	1,953.66	1,196.07	641.172021-22*	757.58	6,998.97	ND	757.58	77.82	74.37*as on 03.08.2021 			ND: Not Drawn 			Source: JJM, IMIS(c) & (d)	At the time of announcement of Jal Jeevan Mission in August, 2019, out of total 163.26 lakh rural households in West Bengal, only 2.14 lakh (1.21%) households were reported to have tap water connections. Since then, another 16.55 lakh [9.34%] households have been provided tap water connections. Thus, as on date, out of 177.23 Lakh rural households in the State, 18.70 lakh (10.55%) households have tap water supply in their homes. West Bengal has planned to make provision of tap water supply to every rural home by 2024. State/ UT–wise details of rural households having provision of tap water supply and progress made in providing tap water connections to households since launch of JJM, is annexed. The same is in public domain and available on the JJM dashboard at:https://ejalshakti.gov.in/jjmreport/JJMIndia.aspx(e) 	No such information is available with this Department. However, comment was sought from the State Government in the matter which has not been received at the time of the reply.*****
