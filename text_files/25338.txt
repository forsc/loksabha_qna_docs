                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1212






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Farmer Producer Organisations




Margani Bharat













                                                    Prabhubhai Nagarbhai Vasava
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of Farmer Producer Organiation (FPOs) set up in the country in 	2020, State-wise;(b) 	whether the target to set up 10,000 FPOs has been achieved;(c) 	if so, the details of target set for each State and the status of achieving the 	same, State-wise;(d) 	the manner in which FPOs have proven beneficial to small and medium 	farmers;(e) 	the details of number of FPOs in the State of Andhra Pradesh and whether it 	is not true that the Government provide Rs.18 lakhs to each FPO and, if so, 	details of financial assistance given to FPOs in Andhra Pradesh in 2020; and(f) 	the details of equity grant given to farmers registered under FPOs in Andhra 	Pradesh in 2020?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	 As informed by Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Govt. of India, the number of Producer Companies registered in India in 2020, State-wise, is at Annexure-I. Farmer Producer Companies have not been separately defined or categorized under the Companies Act. (b) to (c):	 The Government of India has approved and launched the Central Sector Scheme of “Formation and Promotion of 10,000 Farmer Producer Organizations(FPOs)” to form and promote 10,000 new FPOs till 2027-28 with a total budgetary outlay of Rs.6865 Cr.	Under the scheme, the formation and promotion of FPO is based on Produce Cluster Area approach and specialized commodity based approach. While adopting cluster based approach, formation of FPOs will be focussed on “One District One Product” for development of product specialization. 	Initially one FPO is allocated per block. So far, a total of 4465 new FPOs produce clusters have been allocated to Implementing Agencies for formation of FPOs, of which a total of 632 no. of FPOs have been registered. (d):	 As per the Evaluation Report of PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC) on FPO component titled “Impact Study 7- Enhanced realization of agriculture produce marketed through PCs/FCSCs  under Maharashtra Agricultural Competitiveness Project (MACP)” brings out the following major points : (i) sale through Farmer Producers Companies (FPCs) has resulted in increased price realization by members by 22%, (ii) Incidence of cost of marketing is 31% lower than other channels (iii) 28 percent of members have purchased inputs from PCs and it has resulted into net savings of Rs.1384 per acre.(e) & (f)	 As informed by the Government of Andhra Pradesh, a total of 852 FPOs have been formed till 2020 in Andhra Pradesh. 	Under the Central Sector Scheme for formation and promotion of FPOs, the Farmer Producer Organizations(FPOs), provision has been made for financial assistance upto Rs 18.00 lakh per FPO for a period of 03 years for meeting the FPO management cost. Under the new FPO scheme, the Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare(DA&FW) makes advance release to the Implementing Agencies for formation and promotion of FPOs. So far, Rs.249.08 Cr. has been released to all IAs. 	As informed by Small Farmers'' Agri-Business Consortium (SFAC), Equity Grant of Rs.31.22 lakh was extended to 4 FPCs in Andhra Pradesh during 2020-21.
