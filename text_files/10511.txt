                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3982






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Income from International Airliners




 Dr. T. Sumathy (a) Thamizhachi Thangapandian














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the income generated from International airliners, their international flights passing through Indian Air Zones and also for the use of airport and air strip in landing and take-off, airport-wise;(b) the income earned during the last five years, year-wise along with the list of foreign airliners of both passenger and cargo planes using Indian air strips and air navigation system;(c) whether the Government has taken any measures to increase the state-of-the-art technology at Chennai air traffic centre for providing the navigation signals; and(d) if so, the details thereof and total expenditure incurred during the last five years to till date for installation and commissioning of such gadgets?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Year-wise and airport-wise details of the income generated from International airliners passing through Indian Air Zones during the last five years and the list of foreign airliners of both passenger and cargo planes using Indian air strips and air navigation system are at Annexure-A and Annexure-B respectively.;(c) & (d): Yes, Sir. Airports Authority of India has incurred an expenditure of Rs. 20.95 crore during last five years on commissioning of Distance Measuring Equipment, Instrument Landing System, Approach Radar and installation of other navigation equipment at Chennai Airport.
