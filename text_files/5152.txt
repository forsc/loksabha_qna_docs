                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4678






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Preservation of Tribal Culture




Locket Chatterjee














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes any action plan for the preservation and promotion of languages, folk dances, art and culture of the tribals in the country and if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and (b)	the details of the financial assistance given to various agencies/Non Governmental Organisations for the said purpose during each of the last three years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	To protect, preserve & promote various forms of  folk dances, art and culture of the  tribals in the country, the Government of India has set up seven Zonal Cultural Centres (ZCCs) with headquarters at Patiala, Nagpur, Udaipur, Prayagraj, Kolkata, Dimapur and Thanjavur. These ZCCs organize various cultural activities and programmes on regular basis throughout the country. 	Sahitya Akademi, an autonomous organization under Ministry of Culture, encourages the preservation and promotion of languages, especially the unrecognized and tribal languages. The Akademi periodically organizes language conventions throughout the country in this regard. (b)	Annual grant-in-aid is provided only to the above seven ZCCs by Government of India for carrying out various cultural activities.
