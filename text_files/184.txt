                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

159






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Bannerghatta National Park




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware that the final Eco-Sensitive Zone (ESZ) notification of Bannerghatta National Park is pending for the last three years;(b) 	if so, the reasons therefor;(c) 	whether the Government is aware that all developmental activities including that of road infrastructure and industrial development is stalled in this area since the final notification has not yet been issued;(d) 	whether the pendency of final notification has invited the protest of the people as Information Technology/ Biotechnology Park areas are also a part of this zone; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government to resolve the said issue?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) & (b) The Draft Notification for declaring  Eco-Sensitive Zone (ESZ) around Bannerghatta National Park in the state of Karnataka was published on 15.06.2016. The proposal was considered in the ESZ Expert Committee meeting held on 28th February, 2017. The State Government representative proposed to reduce the ESZ area.  Thereafter, additional comments were received from stakeholders which were forwarded to the state Government for its response. In absence of any response from the State, the validity of the draft Notification expired.(c) to (e)  Based on the comments received from various stakeholders and considering the urgency of the ESZ Notification of the Banerghata National Park, the matter was expeditiously followed with the state. The State Government has submitted a fresh proposal for re-notification.  The fresh draft Notification was re-published on 30.10.2018. The same was considered in the Meeting of the Expert Committee held on 28.02.2019. Eleven views/comments received from public/stake holders were sent to the state Government for their consideration and these comments were further considered by the Experts in the meeting.  After detailed deliberation, the Expert Committee has recommended for finalization of the Notification. ***
