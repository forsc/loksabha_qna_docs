                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2445






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






PMGSY in Wardha and Amaravti




Girish Bhalchandra Bapat














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has approved the construction of roads in Wardha and Amravati districts of Vidharbha in the 3rd phase (2020-2021) under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY); and(b) 	if so, the details thereof alongwith the time by which the said work is likely to be started?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b)  The State of Maharashtra has been allocated 6,550 km length of road under Pradhan Mantri Gram SadakYojana-III (PMGSY-III). As on date no sanctions have been issued by this Ministry to the State of Maharashtra, including Wardha and Amravati districts.******
