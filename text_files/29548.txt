06.12.2021






Van Dhan Vikas Kendras in Gujarat




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the district-wise number of Van Dhan VikasKendras (VDVKs) constituted in Gujarat and the number of members affiliated to Self-Help Groups under the scheme; and(b) whether Government is planning to identify the top performing kendras, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)     The district-wise number of Van Dhan VikasKendras Cluster (VDVKCs) set up in Gujarat and the number of members affiliated to Self-Help Groups  are at Annexure.(b)    The TRIFED is the nodal agency to monitor the functioning of these Van Dhan kendras clusters. The first priority of the TRIFED is to make these VandhanKendras functional, which requires formation of self Help group, Identification of Minor Forest produce selected for value addition, Identification of Kendras, training of the members and making a plan for processing, packaging, marketing and branding for these kendras.  As reported by TRIFED,out of 3110 Vandhan Kendra clusters,703 VDVKs have become functional and there is sale of Rs.17.89 cr by these clusters.  In Gujrat,out of 116 VDVKs sanctioned, efforts are being made to make these VDVKs operational.  The best practices and success stories of the functional VDVKs are being shared by TRIFED to be emulated by other States.
