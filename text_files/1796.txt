                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1775






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Production of Mushrooms in the Country




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the quantum of mushroom produced in various States in the country along with the details thereof, State-wise; (b) 	whether any scheme has been prepared to produce specific species of Kumsi mushroom in hilly areas of Himalayan States; (c) 	if so, the number of people benefited so far by the said scheme; (d) 	whether the Government proposes to set up food processing units associated with mushroom; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	State-wise production of Mushroom (2017-18) is given in Annexure.(b)  & (c):	 As per Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR)-Directorate of Mushroom Research(DMR), Solan, Himachal Pradesh, the  following Mushrooms are cultivated in the Himalayan States:-              1.	White Button Mushroom – in Jammu & Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh2.	Shiitake/Black Forest Mushroom – in Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand3.	Kabuli Dhingri -  in Jammu & Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh and UttarakhandHowever, no data is available on production of specific species of  Kumsi Mushroom.Contd…2/--2-As reported by ICAR-DMR, Solan (Himachal Pradesh), State-wise number of  farmers of Himalayan states growing Mushrooms are as under:States	Number of  farmers (2017-18)Jammu & Kashmir	1368Himachal Pradesh	700Uttarakhand	427Total	2495Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture(MIDH), a Centrally Sponsored Scheme is implemented w.e.f. 2014-15, for holistic growth of the horticulture sector covering Fruits, Vegetables, Root and Tuber Crops, Mushroom, Spices, Flowers, Aromatic Plants, Coconut, Cashew and Cocoa. All States and UTs are covered under MIDH. Assistance is provided for promotion of Mushroom cultivation, under the area expansion component of MIDH.(d) & (e):	 Ministry of Food Processing Industries is implementing Scheme for Creation/Expansion of Food Processing and Preservation Capacities under the Pradhan Mantri  Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) for promotion of processing/preservation of agro-food products including Mushroom processing units.            The Scheme envisages grants-in-aid @ 35% of eligible project cost in general areas and @ 50% of eligible project cost in the North-Eastern States including Sikkim and difficult areas namely Himalayan States (i.e. Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir and Uttarakhand), State notified Integrated Tribal Development Project (ITDP) areas & Islands, subject to maximum of Rs. 5.00 crore per project.
