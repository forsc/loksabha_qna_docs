                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5615






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Afforestation




Jaisiddeshwar Shivacharya Mahaswamiji














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the afforestation scheme of the Ministry implemented in the State of Maharashtra during the last three years; and (b) the number of trees planted in Solapur district of Maharashtra during the last three years? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) The Ministry is implementing two major Tree Plantation/afforestation schemes in the country including Maharashtrai.e. National Afforestation Programme (NAP) scheme and National Mission for a Green India (GIM) under which financial grant is provided to states for taking up plantation. While NAP is being implemented for afforestation of degraded forest lands, GIM aims at improving the quality of forest/increase in forest cover besides cross- sectoral activities on landscape basis.  An amount of Rs. 26.82 crores  and Rs.10.30 crores has been released to Maharashtra state under NAP and GIM respectively during last three years (2016-17 to 2018-19).(b) The district-wise details of seedlings planted are not maintained in the ministry and therefore the information relating to Solapur district of Maharashtra state is not available.*****
