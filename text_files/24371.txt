                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






MGNREGS in Andhra Pradesh




Midhun Reddy













                                                    Talari Rangaiah
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there has been a significant increase in the number of people employed under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) in the State of Andhra Pradesh, if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the percentage of increase compared to the previous years; and(c) 	the total number of assets and number of person days generated thereby in the State of Andhra Pradesh?
