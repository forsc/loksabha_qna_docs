                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

241






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Residential Schools for SCs




Vijay Baghel













                                                    Arun Sao
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Soni
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.241† TO BE ANSWERED ON 20.07.2021RESIDENTIAL SCHOOLS FOR SCs†241. SHRI VIJAY BAGHEL: SHRI SUNIL KUMAR SONI: SHRI ARUN SAO: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has formulated any scheme to set up residential schools in the Scheduled Caste dominated development blocks of the country; (b) if so, the details thereof, State/district/ development block-wise including Chhattisgarh; (c) whether one residential school each in Masturi development block of Bilaspur district and Mungeli development block of Mungeli district in scheduled caste dominted area of Chhattisgarh is proposed to be set up; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the extant status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI A.NARAYANASWAMY)(a) : No Sir. Department of School Education and Literacy, Ministry of Education establishes residential schools, viz. Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya (KGBVs) and Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalyas (JNVs) in Scheduled Castes (SCs) majority/concentrated Districts. Further, KGBVs are set up in Educationally Backward Blocks.(b) to (d):  Do not arise.*****
