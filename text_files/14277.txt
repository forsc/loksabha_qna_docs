                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

275






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Vehicle Scrappage Policy




Nama Nageswara Rao














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to introduce a comprehensive vehicle scrappage policy;(b)	if so, the details thereof including time frame of its implementation;(c)	whether such policy is likely to include cars and two wheelers apart from commercial vehicles;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	the details of the benefits likely to accrue to the economy after implementation of this policy?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)  to (e):  A statement is laid on the Table of the House.
