                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1214






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Education Commission of India Bill




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the status of Higher Education Commission of India Bill;(b) whether the Government has recently circulated an Inter-Ministerial ConsultationPaper by inserting a clause in the Bill to permit highly reputed foreign universities toset up their campuses in India;(c) whether it is proposed to permit foreign universities into the country which isbeing opposed by many;(d) if so, the reasons for pushing it through;(e) whether permitting foreign universities in the country will increase the tuition fee,poaching of faculty from our premium institutions thereby resulting in making highereducation inaccessible to poor; and(f) if so, the steps proposed by the Government to address this issue?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) to (f): Till now no final decision has been taken in this regard.******
