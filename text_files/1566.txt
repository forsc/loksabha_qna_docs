                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1436






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Construction of CNG Gas Station




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has completed the construction work of CNG gas station at Palghar, Vasai and Virar in Mumbai;(b) if so, the number of gas stations opened, so far, State/UT/location-wise; and(c) the total number of gas stations that are fully operational?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB) has authorized Gujarat Gas Limited (GGL) for development of CGD Network in Palghar, Vasai and Virar as a part of CGD Network of Palghar District and Thane rural Geographical Area. PNGRB has informed that GGL has set up 6 CNG stations as on 31.05.2019.(b) & (c) PNGRB has intimated that there are total of 1742 operational Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) stations in the country as on 31.03.2019. State/Union Territory/Geographical Area wise details of operational CNG stations across the country are at Annexure.*****
