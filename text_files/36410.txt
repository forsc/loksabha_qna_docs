                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

339






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Arrest of Fishermen




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of Sri Lankan fishermen arrested and their boats seized by the Indian Navy with regard to border violation and other cases from 2014 to till date; and(b) the total number of Indian Fishermen arrested and their boats seized by the Sri Lankan Navy with regard to border violation and other cases during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                              MINISTER OF DEFENCE					(SHRI RAJNATH SINGH) (a) & (b):  A statement is laid on the Table of the House.***** STATE MENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) & (b) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION No. 339 FOR ANSWER ON 25.03.2022 REGARDING ‘ARREST OF FISHERMEN’:(a): Indian Navy has neither arrested any Sri Lankan fishermen nor seized any of their boats with regard to border violation or any other cases. However, 460 Sri Lankan fishermen have been arrested and 87 of their boats have been seized with regard to border violation and other cases by Indian Coast Guard from 2014 till date.(b)	Sri Lankan Navy has arrested 2601 Indian fishermen and seized 473 boats with regard to border violation and other cases from 2014 till date.*****
