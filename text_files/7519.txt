                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1328






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Promotion of Tourism in Khajuraho




Vishnu Dutt Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Khajuraho is a world heritage in view of tourism and whether the Government has prepared any draft plan for its beautification; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has provided any special package to promote tourism in Khajuraho; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and the funds allocated and expenditure incurred in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	Ministry of Tourism has identified Khajuraho world heritage site as one of the 17 sites for development in the country under the scheme – Development of Iconic Tourist sites. The Ministry shall be developing the Iconic Tourist Sites in collaboration with the Central Ministries, State Governments and Local Bodies, in a holistic manner. The draft masterplan for Khajuraho is being formulated by the ministry and has components of connectivity, better facilities/experience for the tourists at the site, skill development, involvement of local community, promotion & branding and for bringing private investment.Ministry of Tourism promotes India as a holistic destination including its various tourism sites/ destinations in different states and Union Territories under the Incredible India brand-line. Ministry as part of its on-going activities, releases print, electronic, online and outdoor media campaigns in the international and domestic markets. Ministry has produced a short film, developed print and digital creatives on Khajuraho which have been promoted on various media platforms including Social Media.  In addition, Ministry has sanctioned following projects/ events for development and promotion of Khajuraho under its other schemes:(Rs. in Lakhs)<pre>Scheme & year of sanction	Name of the project	Amount sanctioned	Funds releasedSwadesh Darshan (2016-17)	Development of Heritage circuit: Gwalior – Orchha – Khajuraho – Chanderi – Bhimbetka – Mandu in Madhya pradesh	9977.09	7437.66Domestic Promotion and Publicity including Hospitality scheme (2017-18)	Khajuraho Dance Festival	15.00	15.00Domestic Promotion and Publicity including Hospitality scheme (2018-19)	Khajuraho Dance Festival	15.00	15.00</pre>********
