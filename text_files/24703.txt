                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

535






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Repairing Work on Borigumma-Jeypore NH




Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the repair work on dilapidated stretch from Borigumma to Jeypore on NH-26 has been completed;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) the details and the present status of land acquisition for Koraput bypass on NH-26;(d) whether the Government proposes to realign the said project in case of problem in land acquisition and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the time by which the Koraput bypass project is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)&(b)  The work of widening to 2-lane with paved shoulders from Borigumma to Jeypore from km 319/280 to km 345/480 of  NH-26 in a length of 26.20 km is in progress with present physical progress of 48%.  Repairs have been done in this stretch under the contract of the widening work and the same is in traffic worthy condition.  (c) to (e)  Indian Railways raised the issue of shifting of approved alignment of the bypass as the proposed ROW is falling in Railway land. The issue raised by Railways is being verified at site jointly by the Railways and local district administration of Koraput. At present there is no proposal to change the approved alignment. The work of Koraput bypass will be taken up after completion of the land acquisition depending on inter-se priority and availability of resources.    *****
