                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2823






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Use of Honey in place of Refined Sugar




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to provide honey pouch and cube in place of refined sugar with tea/coffee in airlines and hostels in an effort to strengthen the rural economy in the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	the details of the target fixed/being fixed for the next two years under the said scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a):	Presently, there is no proposal/initiative by the Government to provide honey pouch and cube in place of refined sugar with tea/coffee in airlines and hostels. Airlines serve on board meals as per their policies.  Moreover, this is commercial matter of airlines and the Government does not interfere into commercial aspects of airlines.(b)&(c):	Do not arise.
