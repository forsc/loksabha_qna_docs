                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2257






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Skilled forest workers




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of skilled forest workers employed with all forest divisions on permanent and contract basis, State-wise; (b) the details of fire-fighting equipment available with forest divisions, State-wise and head-wise; and (c) the number of skilled forest workers and fire-fighting equipment available with the forest divisions in Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh, division-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	As per information received from the State Governments and UT Administrations, the number of skilled forest workers employed with all forest divisions on permanent and contract basis is enclosed at Annexure-I.(b)	As per information received from the State Governments and UT Administrations, the details of fire-fighting equipment available with forest divisions are enclosed at Annexure-II.(c)	As reported by the Government of Andhra Pradesh, skilled forest workers are not engaged in Chittoor District. The list of fire-fighting equipment available in the forest divisions in Chittoor district is enclosed  at Annexure-III .***
