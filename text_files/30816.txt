                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2428






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Beneficiaries for Financial Assistance




Asaduddin Owaisi














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of persons benefited in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh through financial Assistance and loan from the packages released till date during the Covid-19- pandemic by the Government district-wise and category-wise;(b)	whether the Government has issued any guidelines to banks to approve loans to various categories under the financial relief packages for Covid-19; and(c)	if so, the details of orders issued in this regard for Telangana and Andhra Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a) Government announced two packages namely, the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Yojana and a Special Economic and comprehensive package called AatmaNirbhar Bharat Abhiyan (ABNA) as part of the national economic response to the COVID-19 pandemic with special focus on relief to poor, Micro Small & Medium Enterprises etc. The details of coverage of beneficiary-oriented schemes under these packages in the State of Telangana and Andhra Pradesh are at Annexure-I, II and III.(b) and (c)Government has announced the Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS) as part of the ABNA in 2020 with the objective to help MSMEs/small businesses to meet their operational liabilities and resume businesses in view of the distress caused by the COVID-19 crisis. Under the scheme 100% cent credit guarantee for credit extended by the lending institutions is being provided by Government through National Credit Guarantee Trustee Company Ltd. (NCGTC). For this purpose, operational guidelines have been issued by NCGTC.Further, as part of ABNA Government has announced PM Street Vendor’s AatmaNirbhar Nidhi Scheme which is available for beneficiaries belonging to states/UTs which have notified Rules and Schemes under Street Vendors (Protection of Livelihood and Regulations of Street vending) Act 2014. Both Andhra Pradesh and Telangana have notified the rules.
