                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3217






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Super Speciality Blocks under PMSSY




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande













                                                    Bharti Pravin Pawar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government have sanctioned Super Speciality Blocks under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY) in the country including Maharashtra; (b) if so, the details thereof, State/ UT-wise;(c) the details of the colleges in Maharashtra where Super Speciality Wings have been set up; (d) whether it is a fact that there is shortage of specialist doctors in the said Super Speciality Blocks; and (e) if so, the reaction of the Government thereto and the efforts made to meet the above shortage of specialist doctors?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):    The Government has taken up 75 Up-gradation Projects in various parts of the country under different phases of PMSSY, out of which  Super Speciality Blocks have been sanctioned in 63 Government Medical Colleges (GMCs). Details are given in  the enclosed Annexure.  (c):   Construction of Super Speciality Blocks have been approved in following four GMCs up-gradation projects in Maharashtra under  PMSSY scheme : (i)      Government Medical College, Aurangabad;(ii)     Government Medical College, Latur; (iii)    Government Medical College, Akola; and (iv)  Sri Vasantrao Naik Government Medical College, Yavatmal. (d) & (e):     Deployment of manpower to run and operate the facilities created under PMSSY  in existing Govt Medical Colleges, is the responsibility of the State Government concerned. State Governments have already been requested to make necessary provisions thereof.
