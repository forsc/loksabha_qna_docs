                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1525






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Conservation of Monuments at Fatehpur Sikri




Rajkumar Chahar














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    the areas demarcated and selected by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) for the conservation and preservation of all the monuments located at Fatehpur-Sikri (UP);(b)  	the status of the preservation work being implemented at the monuments; and(c)	the efforts made by the ASI to clear the beehives from Buland Darwaza and other structures of historical importance in Fatehpur-Sikri?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) 	There are  9 Nos. of protected monuments of Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) at Fatehpur Sikri.  The conservation and preservation work of these monuments is executed by the ASI within the protected area of each monument which is well demarcated. (b)	Conservation work of protected monuments is a continuous process.  The conservation & preservation work of these monuments is attended regularly depending upon the availability of the resources and requirements of different sites and they are in a good state of conservation. (c)	The beehives from Buland Darwaza and other monuments at Fatehpur Sikri are cleared regularly by application of appropriate methods, keeping the monuments in presentable condition.
