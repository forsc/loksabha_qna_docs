                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3894






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Sainik School in Bijapur




Annasaheb Shankar Jolle














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether as per the Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Sainik School, Bijapur, the Government of Karnataka is responsible for providing funds towards maintenance, running expenditure, laboratory equipment, scholarships for the cadets, special requirements on actual need basis, pension and terminal benefits to all retiring employees of the school, etc.(b)  if so, the details thereof;(c)  whether the Government of Karnataka is not allocating funds towards pension and terminal benefits to all retiring employees of the school;(d)  if so, the details thereof; and(e)  whether the school is facing acute shortage of funds due to the increased costs and if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  Yes, Sir.(b)  Funds allotted by Government of Karnataka to Sainik School Bijapur over the last three years are as below:Sl. No.	Year	Budget allotted by Govt. of Karnataka		Scholarship (Rs. in lacs)	Running Expenditure (Rs. in lac)	Total (Rs. in lac)(a)	2016-17	249	121	370(b)	2017-18	249	124	373(c)	2018-19	249	124	373(c) to (e):  Funds are provided by the State Government under the MoA for the purposes of land, infrastructure, annual maintenance and scholarship.  Deficit in availability of funds may be bridged through funds from the Reserve Funds of Sainik Schools and Grant-in-aid from the Central Government.  The shortage of funds for pensionary purposes, on account of implementation of 7th Central Pay Commission’s recommendations has been met through additional funds of Ministry of Defence.*****
