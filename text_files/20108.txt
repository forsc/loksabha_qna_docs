                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1059






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Financial Assistance to Projects of Classical Language




Sumedhanand Saraswati













                                                    Balak Nath
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the Indian languages which have been awarded the status of classical language; (b) whether the Government has implemented any scheme for providing financial assistance to the various projects of classical language and if so, the details thereof; (c) the language-wise details of the funds provided to each classical language during the last three years; (d) whether the Government has received any proposal regarding projects of classical language during the last three years; and  (e) if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereon?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) :  Six Indian Languages namely Sanskrit, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam and Odia have been accorded  the status of Classical Language.-2-(b) to (e) :  Central Institute of Indian Languages (CIIL) works for the promotion of all Indian languages including four classical languages namely Kannada, Telugu, Malayalam and Odia.  Development and promotion of Classical Tamil is done by  Central Institute of Classical Tamil (CICT).  Sanskrit  is promoted by  Central Sanskrit University,  New Delhi. The details of funds  provided for various classical languages is given below: (in crore)No	Classical language	2017-18	2018-19	2019-201.	Kannada	   1.00	   0.99	1.072.	Telugu	   1.00	   0.99	1.073.	Tamil	  10.59	   4.65	9.80Funds are provided to Central Sanskrit University for their normal functioning  which includes for  promotion of  Sanskrit language.  Classical Malayalam and Classical Odia centres are recently  established .  No other proposal received in respect of Classical Language .***
