                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5054






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Appointment on Compassionate Grounds




Sumedhanand Saraswati














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether many army personnels are killed  in various accidents other than cross-border firing and encounters;(b)  if so, the details of such cases reported, State-wise;(c)  whether there is any provision to provide appointment on compassionate grounds to their dependents;(d)  if so, the details thereof and the number of appointments made on compassionate grounds so far, State-wise; and(e)  if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) to (c):  232 Army personnel have been killed in various accidents in 2019 till date.	State-wise details of Army personnel killed in various accidents are not maintained.	5% Vacancies are earmarked in Short Service Commission Women (Technical & Non-Technical) for widows of defence personnel, who die in harness.  This relaxation/earmarking is subject to clearing/passing SSB and medical fitness.  Widow of Defence personnel upto the age of 35 years may apply for these 5% vacancies.	Dependents of JCOs/ORs are given priority and enrolled as “Instant Enrolment” under Unit Headquarter Quota.	5% of direct recruitment vacancies are reserved for compassionate appointments in Group ‘C’ Defence civilian employees every year for dependents of combatants as well as Defence civilian who die in harness.(d) & (e):  Information is being collected and will be laid on the Table of the House.*****
