                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

796






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Satellite Centers of National Institutes




Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar













                                                    Bhagwanth Khuba
                                                








                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                








                                                    Shivkumar Chanabasappa Udasi
                                                








                                                    Pratima Bhoumik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA,YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA ANDHOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether it is a fact that the Government is in process to establish the Satellite Centers of existing National Institutes, if so, the details;(b) whether the Government has any plan to setup any of the Satellite Center in the Northeast India, if so, the details thereof; and(c)if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a):	National Institute of Ayurveda at Panchkula (Haryana), All India 	Institute of Ayurveda at Goa, National Institute of Unani Medicine at Ghaziabad (Uttar Pradesh) and National Institute of Homoeopathy at New Delhi are being 	established as Satellite Centers of the existing Institutes.(b):	No. (c):	Two National Institutes namely National Institute of Ayurveda and Homoeopathy at Shillong (Meghalaya) and North Eastern Institute of Folk Medicine at Pasighat (Arunachal Pradesh) already exist in the North East India.
