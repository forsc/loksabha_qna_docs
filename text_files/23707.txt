                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4782






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






MoUs to Expedite Cooperation in Defence Sector




Vishnu Dutt Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether India has signed a Memorandum of Understanding with America to expedite cooperation in defence sector; and(b) the efforts being made by the Government to sign an agreement of defence technology with the developed nations so as to get assistance in the modernization of defence forces and indigenious manufacturing along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a)  No such MoU with USA has been concluded to expedite defence cooperation in general.(b)  Defence Technology and Trade Initiative (DTTI) with USA has been established to cooperate on defence research and development.  Similarly, MoU with U.K on Defence Technology and Industrial Capability Cooperation was signed on 12 April, 2019 with the aim to enhance defence Industrial capabilities.*****
