                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4243






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Bridge on Dhela River on MoradabadKashipur Highway




S.T. Hasan














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether some part of the bridge on Dhela river after Bhojpur on Moradabad-Kashipur highway is in dilapidated condition which may lead to a cause for big tragedy; (b) if so, the details thereof along with the reaction of the Government thereto;(c) whether the Government proposes to construct a bridge on Dhela river and if so, the details and the present status thereof; and (d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d) The bridge on Dhela river is narrow with 5.5 m width. The bearings of the bridge and part of girder etc., are in dilapidated condition. The work of Detailed Project Report preparation has already been initiated for development of entire stretch of Moradabad-Kashipur highway. *****
