                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

348






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






BSNL Mobile Towers




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal













                                                    Arvind Kumar Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) mobile towers installed across the country along with the details of the services/facilities these are equipped with, State-wise including Haryana and the district-wise details of Gujarat State; (b) 	whether most of the BSNL mobile towers in Gujarat and Haryana are equipped to provide 2G services whereas mobile towers of other private telecom companies are providing 4G services and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government proposes to upgrade the BSNL mobile towers installed across the country including Gujarat, particularly in Bhavnagar and Botad districts; (d) 	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard along with the time by which the said upgradation is likely to be done and if not, the reasons therefor; and (e) 	the number of mobile towers upgraded in Gujarat during each of the last three years and the current year?	






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (e)	 A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT TO BE LAID ON THE TABLE OF LOK SABHA IN RESPECT OF PARTS (a) to (e) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *348 FOR 17TH JULY, 2019  ON “BSNL MOBILE TOWERS”.(a)	Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) is providing 2G, 3G and 4G (at few locations) mobile services in its Licensed Service Areas (LSAs).  State-wise detail of 2G, 3G and 4G Base Transceiver Stations (BTSs) installed by BSNL across the country including Gujarat and Haryana is at Annexure-I.District-wise details of BTSs  installed by BSNL in Gujarat State is at Annexure-II.(b) 	As on 31.05.2019, 4852 number of 2G BTSs, 3163 number of 3G BTSs and 844 number of 4G BTSs of BSNL are working in Gujarat State.  As on 31.05.2019, 2237 number of 2G BTSs, 2236 number of 3G BTSs and 170 number of 4G BTSs of BSNL are working in Haryana State.  Details of BTSs installed by BSNL and major private Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) in Gujarat and Haryana as on 31.05.2019 are as under:<pre>Name of TSP	Mobile Towers installed in Gujarat	Mobile Towers installed in Haryana	2G	3G	4G	2G	3G	4GBSNL	4852	3163	844	2237	2236	170Reliance Jio*	0	0	48606	0	0	18593Vodafone-Idea*	16430	15515	21662	4589	4622	5942Airtel*	9154	6147	13121	3744	2675	5771</pre>* Source: TARANG Sanchar Portal(c) & (d)	Subject to techno-commercial feasibility and availability of funds, BSNL is regularly adding new equipments for improving its mobile network and coverage in both urban and rural areas of all of its LSAs including Gujarat and Haryana.
