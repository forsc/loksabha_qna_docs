                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1402






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Heritage Railway Stations in Rajasthan




Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta













                                                    Chandra Prakash Joshi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Railways has decided to give heritage look to railway stations in the country; (b)  if so, the details of the selected stations for heritage look; (c) the details of the selected railway stations in Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh along with the details of works to be carried out there; and (d)  the amount allocated to Chittorgarh station for this purpose along with the details of the work and the present status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a)       At present, there is no policy of Ministry of Railways, to give heritage look to stations in the country. However, local Railway Administration may sometimes incorporate local ethnic and architectural elements in the station building.  (b) to (d)    Do not arise.*******
