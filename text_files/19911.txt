                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1241






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Kisan Credit Card Scheme




V. Kalanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the features of the PM-KISAN Scheme and whether the Government has linked this scheme with Kisan Credit Card Scheme;(b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	 whether any special drive has been under taken through this scheme to cover Animal Husbandry and Dairying Farmers within the country; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(DR.SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	& (b) The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) aims at providing financial assistance to landholding farmer families across the country, subject to certain exclusion criteria, to enable them to take care of expenses related to agriculture and allied activities as well as domestic needs. The Government of India had announced to cover all PM-KISAN beneficiaries under Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme in the budget announcement made for the financial year 2020-21. Accordingly, Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare launched a Kisan Credit Card Saturation drive to cover all PM-KISAN beneficiaries who don’t have KCC since February, 2020. As on 29.01.2021, 174.46 lakh KCC have been issued. (c)	& (d) A special saturation drive has been launched to provide concessional credit through KCC to farmers including PM Kisan beneficiaries, animal husbandry farmers (dairy, poultry, others) and fishermen from 1st June, 2020 on wards. During this drive as on 29.01.2021, a total number of 9,76,590 KCC have been sanctioned for animal husbandry, dairy and fisheries farmers. The details are given below:S No.	Type	Fresh KCC Sanctioned1	Crop loan with dairy	2,55,3952.	Crop loan with other allied activities	49,8763.	Dairy	5,86,3094.	Poultry	13,8345.	Others	43,9176	Fisheries	27,259	TOTAL	9,76,590Source: Department of Financial Services****
