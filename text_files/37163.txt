                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4251






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Pre and Post Matric Scholarships




Mohammad Jawed














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.4251TO BE ANSWERED ON 29.03.2022PRE AND POST MATRIC SCHOLARSHIPS 4251. DR. MOHAMMAD JAWED: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) the number of Scheduled Castes (SCs) students who have applied for Pre and Post Matric Scholarship in 2019-20 and 2020-21 along with the number of girls out of them; (b) the number of girls who have applied and received the scholarship; (c) the details of students who are receiving scholarship disaggregated along with the SCs and Other Backward Classes (OBCs); (d) the number of successful girl candidates; and (e) the number of students who were rejected along with the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI A. NARAYANASWAMY)(a) & (b):   The Post Matric and Pre Matric Scholarship Schemes for Scheduled Caste students are centrally sponsored schemes being implemented through the State Governments/UT Administrations who  invite applications from the students of their State, through their respective portals. Therefore, the data regarding the number of applicants in 2019-20 and 2020-21, including  girl students, is not available with the Ministry.(c) to (e): The number of SC and Other Backward Classes (OBC) students who received scholarships, in 2019-20 and 2020-21, including girls, as reported by the State Governments/UT Administrations is as follows:The details of the SC students are as follows:-Scheme	Total Beneficiaries in 2019-20 (in lakh)	Total Beneficiaries in 2020-21 (in lakh)Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme for SC	26.30	28.09Post-Matric Scholarship Scheme for SC	52.80	60.00The details of the OBC students are as follows:-Scheme	Total Beneficiaries in 2019-20 (in lakh)	Total Beneficiaries in 2020-21 (in lakh)Pre Matric Scholarship for OBC students	94.52	31.09Post Matric Scholarship for OBC students	40.94	44.80As  explained above in response to  (a) and (b), since the applications are invited and processed by the State Governments/UT Administrations only, the details  of rejected applicants and reasons therefor are not available with this Ministry.*****
