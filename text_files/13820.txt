                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2636






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Implementation of National Health Mission (NHM)




Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav













                                                    Nishikant  Dubey
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the status of implementation of National Health Mission (NHM) in the country during the last five years and the current year, year-wise and State/UT-wise including Maharashtra and Jharkhand;(b) the details of funds released under National Health Mission during the last five years and the current year, State-wise/UT-wise;(c) the details of components under which above said funds has been released component, State/UT and year-wise; and(d) the necessary steps taken by the Government for proper implementation and to put a check on misappropriation of funds and corruption in the said Scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	The Statements showing physical achievements and progress under NHM during the last five years and the current year, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra and Jharkhand, are given at Annexure-I to VI. (b):	Statement showing State/UT-wise release of funds under National Health Mission during last five year and current year is given at Annexure-VII.(c):	The State/UT wise details of components under which the above said funds have been released is given at Annexure-VIII to XIII.(d):	Public health and hospitals being a State subject, the primary responsibility for proper implementation and to put a check on misappropriation of funds and corruption in the schemes, vests with the State/UT Governments. However, the Government of India have taken several steps to monitor the implementation of NHM and utilization of funds by the States which inter-alia includes:  o	Performance Audit by the CAG,o	Annual Statutory Audits by CAG empanelled major Chartered Accountant audit firms;o	Concurrent Audits by Chartered Accountant audit firms;o	Financial review by Institute of Public Auditors of India (IPAI) in several States;o	Examination of quarterly Financial Monitoring Reports (FMR) furnished by the States;o	Visits by the teams of the Financial Management Group of the Department to the States/UTs for periodic review.o	Annual visits to States by Common Review Mission (CRM) which inter-alia, looks at financial systems and mechanisms. o	Implemented Public Financial Management System (PFMS) developed by the office of the CGA, Ministry of Finance for monitoring and management of funds on real time basis.o	Integrated monitoring visits by senior officials of the Ministry and National Health System Resource
