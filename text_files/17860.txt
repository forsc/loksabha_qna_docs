                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1587






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Watershed Projects




Bidyut Baran Mahato













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of sown area, irrigated and unirrigated area and rainfed area out of total cultivable area including its percentage in the country, State-wise; (b) 	whether the Government has taken several steps for optimum utilization of watershed projects for the development of rainfed farming in the country and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the number of watershed projects that have been sanctioned and completed during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise; and (d) 	the details of funds/subsidy allocated, released and utilized for watershed projects for exclusive growth of rainfed farming in the country; and (e) 	whether the Government intends to sanction new watershed projects for rainfed agricultural areas in Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh and Jharkhand and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):  	As per publication on Land Use Statistics for 2015-16 (latest available), State-wise details of  net area sown, net irrigated area and net un-irrigated area and their percentages, are at Annexure I.  The data on rainfed area is not centrally maintained.(b):   	Department of Land Resources (DoLR) has sanctioned 8214 watershed development projects in 28 States (except Goa) [now 27 States and UT of Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh] from 2009-10 to 2014-15 covering an area of about 39.07 million hectare under Integrated Watershed Management Programme (IWMP). IWMP was amalgamated as the Watershed Development Component (WDC) of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) in 2015-16.  The activities being undertaken through the watershed development projects inter alia include ridge area treatment, drainage line treatment, soil and moisture conservation, rain water harvesting, nursery raising, afforestation, horticulture, pasture development, livelihoods for assetless persons, etc.…..contd…2/--2-The Common Guidelines for Watershed Development Projects 2008 (Revised Edition 2011) provide for various mechanisms to ensure proper implementation, such as regular monitoring including GIS / web based online monitoring of process and outcomes, social auditing and independent and external monitoring by third party agencies and dedicated institutions with professional support at the State, District and project levels. In addition, the DoLR supervises the work through periodic review meetings, video conferences, regional review meetings and field visits etc.(c): As per the Common Guidelines for Watershed Development Projects-2008 (Revised Edition-2011), the period for completing PMKSY (Watershed Development) projects is up to 7 years. The Government is focusing on completion of all on-going projects and therefore no new projects under the WDC-PMKSY are sanctioned w.e.f.  2015-16. State / UT-wise number of watershed projects sanctioned during the period 2009-10 to 2014-15 and completed during each of the last three years and the current year is given at Annexure II.(d):  Under watershed projects, fund is being released primarily keeping at Center Stage the rainfed & degraded area development in the project areas and not exclusively for growth of rainfed farming in the country. Since inception, Rs. 19,185.26 crore (as on 16.09.2020) has been released as Central share and as per provisional unaudited information received from States about Rs. 24,670.93 crore (up to 31.03.2020) has been utilized. The expenditure includes Central share, State share and miscellaneous receipt utilized for implementation of watershed projects.(e):  Government of India is examining the prospects of launching new generation watershed projects for development of rainfed and degraded areas in the country with the target to cover 20 m. ha. by 2023-2024 by sanctioning new projects.
