                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

983






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






TV Relay Centres




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the number of TV relay centres established at present in the country, particularly in backward districts, State/UT and location-wise;b)	the expenses incurred on these relay centres during each of the last three years;c)	the number of relay centres not working properly and the number of such relay centres located in tribal areas;d)	the details of the steps taken to improve their functioning; ande)	the outcome thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) Presently, there are 23 Digital and 612 Analog terrestrial TV Relay Centres/ Transmitters of varying capacity located in various parts of the country, including backward and tribal areas. State/UT-wise and location wise list of TV Transmitters is enclosed as Annexure.(b) The expenditure incurred on TV Relay Centres/ Transmitters throughout the country for last three years is as under: -Year	Expenditure (Rs in crore)2016-17	392.72 2017-18	403.58 2018-19	501.57(c) to (e) As part of 11th and 12th Plan Schemes, Government has approved setting up of Digital Terrestrial Transmitters (DTTs)/ TV Relay Centres at 63 locations of which 23 no. of DTTs at 19 locations have already been commissioned and are functional.
