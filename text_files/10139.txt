                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3471






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Sale of Loss Making PSUs




Manoj Kumar Tiwari














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal for sale of loss making PSUs in the near future; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and (d)	the details of the implementation procedure and time limit for any such proposal in the near future?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI ARJUN RAM MEGHWAL)(a) to (c): As per information furnished by Department of Investment and Public Asset Management (DIPAM), profitability/loss of the Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) is not among the relevant criteria for disinvestment. Government follows a policy of strategic disinvestment of CPSEs, which are not in ‘priority sectors’. The Government has given ‘in-principle’ approval for strategic disinvestment of 33 CPSEs including subsidiaries, Units and Joint Ventures with sale of majority stake of Government of India and transfer of management control.(d): The procedure and mechanism of the strategic disinvestment of CPSEs as approved by Competent Authority involves constitution of Core Group of Secretaries on Disinvestment (CGD), Inter-Ministerial Group (IMG), Evaluation Committee (EC)/ Evaluation Sub-Committee (ECS) and Consultative Group (CG) to implement the decisions of the Competent Authority on strategic disinvestment of CPSEs. * * *
