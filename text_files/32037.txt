                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3622






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Teachers Training Institutes




Janardan Mishra













                                                    Narendra Kumar
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Subrata Pathak
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                








                                                    (Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is likely to formulate any action plan to lay down norms for Teachers Training Institutes across the country;(b) if so, the details thereof along with norms laid down in this regard;(c) the restrictions likely to be imposed on the institutes flouting the said norms;(d) the total number of teacher training schools/ colleges in the country, State-wise, especially in Rajasthan;(e) whether the National Council for Teachers Education gives accreditation to the B.ed. Colleges in States and if so, the details thereof; and(f) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) to (c) Yes, sir. NCTE has notified the National Council for Teacher Education (Recognition, Norms and Procedure) Amendment Regulations, 2021 in the Gazette of India on 26.10.2021 and is available at https://www.ncte.gov.in/website/PDF/regulation/509Regulations_2014.pdf.(d) The list is enclosed at Annexure-I.(e) to (f) NCTE exercises the powers in granting recognition for various teacher education programmes as vested under section 14 of the NCTE Act, 1993.***
