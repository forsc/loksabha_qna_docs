                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the funds allocated to various States under the Nirbhaya Fund Scheme during the last three years, State-wise, year-wise;(b)	whether the Government has taken note of the low utilization or under utilization of allocated funds under the said scheme and if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (c)	whether the Government maintains any data with respect to utilization of allocated funds under the scheme and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; (d)	the details of proposals received and sanctioned by the Government under the scheme along with the funds allocated thereunder; (e)	 the details of projects initiated, funds allocated and utilized under the scheme so far, State-wise; and (f)	the measures taken by the Government for faster roll-out of the scheme to ensure safety and security of women?
