                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4766






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Repairing of National Highways in Bihar




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the maintenance and repair works taken up on National Highways (NHs) include Ordinary Repairs (OR), Periodical Renewals (PR), Special Repairs (SR) and Flood Damage Repairs (FDR) and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether it is a fact that various service roads/ NHs in the State of Bihar including district Gopalganj have been damaged due to flood and need maintenance;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) whether it is a fact that road signs on NHs in the State of Bihar are not available/visible and the people are facing difficulties in identifying the areas and if so, the remedial measures taken by the Government in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	The development and maintenance of National Highways (NHs) is a continuous process.The Maintenance and Repair (M&R) of stretches of NHs, where either Development works have commenced or Operation, Maintenance and Transfer (OMT) Concessions/ Operation and Maintenance (O&M) Contracts have been awarded, are the responsibility of the concerned Concessioners/ Contractors till the Defect Liability Period (DLP)/ the Concession Period. M&R of balance stretches of NHs are carried out annually as per available budgetary outlay, inter-se priority and traffic density to keep such NHs in traffic worthy conditions.The Ministry implements M&R of NHs entrusted with State Governments/ Union Territory (UT) through works such as Periodic Renewals (PR), Ordinary Repairs (OR), Flood Damage Repairs (FDR), etc., through State Governments/ UTs as per extant policies.The M&R of NHs are implemented on the agency basis. The State Governments (State Public Works Departments (PWDs), Road Construction Departments (RCDs), etc.), National Highways Authority of India (NHAI), National Highways and Infrastructure Development Corporation Limited (NHIDCL) and Border Roads Organisation (BRO) are the main agencies of the Ministry. The responsibility of maintenance of NHs rests with the agency to which the NH stretch is entrusted.(b) and (c)	The details of NHs / service roads damaged due to flood in the State of Bihar along-with action taken on the same has been placed at Annexure.(d)	Road signs on NHs are provided as per the extant policy guidelines and site requirements. It is ensured that the damaged sign boards are replaced from time to time.
