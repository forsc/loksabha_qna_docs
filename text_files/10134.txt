                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3453






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Coal-based Industries




Gomati Sai














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of coal-based and steel-based industries at Raigarh and Jashpur Parliamentary Constituencies; and(b)	the details of the production capacity and efficiency, unit-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI ARJUN RAM MEGHWAL)(a & b)     As per information made available by Ministry of Coal and Ministry of Steel respectively, there are 67 coal based specified end use plants which have Fuel Supply Agreements (FSA) from Mahanadi Coalfields Limited (MCL) and South Eastern Coalfields Limited (SECL) and 8 steel-based industries (steel plants) functioning in Raigarh District. The unit-wise details of production capacity are given at Annex-I and Annex-II respectively.      In Jashpur, there is no coal-based specified end use plant which has FSA from MCL and SECL nor is there any steel-based industry (steel plant).*****
