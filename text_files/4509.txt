                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4182






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






NH Projects in Gujarat




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of National Highways (NHs) sanctioned in Bhavnagar and Botad districts (Gujarat) during the last three years and the current year;(b)	the present status of ongoing projects in these districts; and(c)	the reasons for delay with regard to the pending projects? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c): Details of National Highways projects   sanctioned  in Bhavnagar and Botad districts (Gujarat) during the last three years and the current year & present status is as under:Sr.  No.	Name of Project	Present Status1.	Periodical Renewal between km. 130/600 to 147/700 of NH-5.	Completed2.	Periodical Renewal from km 147/950 to 170/450 (Ranpur – Limbdi Section) on NH-51.	Work order issued3.	Periodical Renewal from km. 2/0 to 48/250 Mahuva to Amreli & Km. 49/6 to 57/8 of NH-351 (Mahuva – Savarkundla – Amreli – Bagasara – Vadiya – Jetpur road).	Tender process initiated4.	Limda / Dhasa – Limdi section of NH-8E (Length : 90 Km) 	DPR stage5.	Nari - Pipli – Bhavnagar (Length 33.3 Km) District Bhavnagar 	4 laning of Pipli-Bhavnagar section sanctioned & taken up.*****
