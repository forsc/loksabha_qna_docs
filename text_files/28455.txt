                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

160






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






National Education Policy




Ramesh Bidhuri














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any steps for effective implementation of NationalEducation Policy, 2020;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether any special guidelines/directions have been issued to States/UTs regarding the Policy;and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) and (b): As per National Education Policy 2020 (NEP 2020), implementation of the Policyrequires multiple initiatives and actions, which will have to be taken by different bodies in asynchronized and systematic manner. The policy provides different timelines as well as principlesand methodology for its implementation. Accordingly, Ministry has written to all its implementingagencies, regulatory bodies, State/UT Governments, other stakeholder Ministries/Departments, etcfor taking steps for implementation of NEP 2020.In pursuance of the goals and objectives of the NEP 2020 and to assist States, UTs and otherorganisations in this task, the Department of School Education & Literacy has developed anindicative and suggestive NEP Implementation Plan for School Education, ‘Students’ and‘Teachers’ Holistic Advancement through Quality Education (SARTHAQ)’.Further, various Committees/Expert Groups have been constituted by the both the Departmentsi.e. D/o Higher Education and D/o School Education & Literacy of the Ministry and otherimplementing agencies under its control for effective implementation of various aspects ofNational Education Policy 2020.On 29.07.2021, Ministry has launched several initiatives under NEP, 2020 like Vidya-Pravesh –School Preparation Module; Indian Sign Language as a Subject at secondary level; NISHTHA 2:0for Secondary Teachers; SAFAL (Structured Assessment for Analysing Learning Levels); OnlineModule on AI for Public Awareness; Setting up of Academic Bank of Credit; Guidelines onMultiple Entry/Exit in Academic Programmes offered in Higher Education Institutions; 1st YearEngineering Programmes in Regional Languages; Guidelines for Internationalisation of HigherEducation; National Digital Education Architecture (NDEAR) & National Education TechnologyForum (NETF) etc. Apart from these, several other initiatives have also been taken by Governmentof India.(c) and (d): After announcement of NEP 2020, a list of suggestive actionable points and variousactivities envisaged in NEP 2020 were identified and shared with State/UT Governments. A seriesof workshops/VCs have been held with States/UTs to discuss innovative ideas for NEPimplementation. In this regard, several communications have been made with State/UTGovernments requesting to take appropriate measures towards implementation of NEP 2020 videletters dated 30.07.2020, 16.09.2020, 06.04.2021, 29.07.2021, 10.08.2021 and 07.09.2021.States/UT Governments have also started taking initiatives towards implementation of NEP 2020.*******
