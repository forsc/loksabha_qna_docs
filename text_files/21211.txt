                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2332






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Allocation for Welfare of SCs




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2332TO BE ANSWERED ON 09.03.2021ALLOCATION FOR WELFARE OF SCs 2332. KUMARI SHOBHA KARANDLAJE: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the annual outlay for the Department of Social Justice and Empowerment in the FY 2021-22 is 28% higher and if so, the details thereof, scheme-wise; (b) the details of funds allocated to National Fellowship for SCs, National Fellowship for OBCs and EBCs, National Overseas Scholarship for SCs and OBCs in the Budget for 2021-22; (c) whether the allocation for welfare of Scheduled Castes for all Ministries during the year 2021-22 has been increased and if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the D/o SJE has restructured the existing schemes based on principles of operational efficiency, synergy and transparency into 19 schemes only and if so, the names of the restructured schemes; and (e) the details of the new scheme - Pradhan Mantri Anusuchit Jaati Abhyuday Yojana (PMAJAY), including the number of SC majority villages being developed, number of beneficiaries and the funds allocated?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a): Yes Madam. The proposed annual outlay for the year 2021-22 is Rs. 10517.62 crore.  It is 28.14% higher than the Revised Estimate for the year 2020-21 which is Rs. 8207.56 crore. The proposed annual outlay for the year 2021-22 has to be approved by the Parliament. The scheme-wise proposed annual outlay for 2021-22 is at annexure-I.(b): The proposed allocation  for the FY 2021-22 under the schemes of National Fellowship for SCs, National Fellowship for OBCs and EBCs, National Overseas Scholarship for the SCs and OBCs is as under:-Sl .No.	Name of Schemes	BE 2021-22 (Rs. in crore)1	National Fellowship for SCs	300.002	National Fellowship for OBCs and EBCs	100.003	National Overseas Scholarship for SCs	30.004	Interest Subsidy on Overseas Studies for OBCs and EBCs	30.00(c): Yes Madam. The budget allocation for Welfare of Scheduled Caste for all Ministries in the FY 2020-21 is Rs.  83256.62 crore. The proposed budget outlay for Welfare of Scheduled Caste for all Ministries for FY 2021-22 is Rs.126259.20 crore. The proposed allocation for Welfare of Scheduled Caste for all Ministries has increased by 51.65% in the FY 2021-22.(d): In pursuance of M/o Finance instructions, the existing schemes of the Department have been reviewed, rationalized and restructured. The restructured/ rationalized scheme has to be recommended by Standing Finance Committee (SFC)/Expenditure Finance Committee (EFC) and approved by Competent Authority viz. Minister of Social Justice and Empowerment/Finance Minister/Cabinet for their continuation during 2021-22 to 2025-26. The Detail of the proposed restructured schemes is at annexure-I.(e): At present the proposal for merger of the Scheme of Special Central Assistance to the Scheduled Caste Sub Plan (SCA to SCSP), Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAGY) and Babu Jagjivan Ram Chhatrawas Yojana (BJRCY) is at EFC stage. The merged Scheme is proposed as Pradhan Mantri Anusuchit Jaati Abhyuday Yojana (PM-AJAY). The merged scheme of PM-AJAY has been allocated Rs. 1800 cr. for 2021-22.
