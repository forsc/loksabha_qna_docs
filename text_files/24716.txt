                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

623






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Road Projects under Bharatmala Pariyojana




N.K. Premachandran














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to execute projects under Bharatmala Pariyojana in a time bound manner and if so, the details thereof;(b)     the details of roads in Kerala taken up under this pariyojana;(c)	whether the Government proposes to take up the Thiruvananthapurm - Kottarakara - Angamali road for development and if so, the details thereof;(d)	the details of alignment fixed for the roads in Kollam district;(e)	whether the Government proposes to develop the roads through green corridor and if so, the details thereof;(f)	whether the Government has taken up development of Kollam - Schenkottai NH under Bharatmala Pariyojana and if so, the details thereof; and(g)   whether the Government has fixed new alignment for the said road through green field and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) Yes Sir. At present, 4 projects comprising length of 52.6 km with cost of Rs. 3,694 crores are in different stages of implementation. 7 projects comprising length of 264 km with cost of Rs. 20,592 crores have been awarded in the year 2020-21. Moreover, 8 projects of total length 255 km with the cost of Rs. 26,917 crores have been planned for award in the year 2021-22.(c) & (d) Yes Sir. The alignment of Thiruvananthpuram-Kottarakara - Angamaly stretch passes through Kottarakkara and Punalur Taluks in Kollam District comprising length of 227.51 km has been approved.(e)            Yes Sir.(f) & (g) Yes Sir. Alignment of Kollam-Schenkottai is Greenfield for a length of 38 Km and Brownfield for a length of 21 Km.  The alignment has been approved and passes through Pathanapuram – Kottarakkara – Chirayinkeezhu & Kollam taluks of Kollam & Thiruvananthapuram Districts.*****
