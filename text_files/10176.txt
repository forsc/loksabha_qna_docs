                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3538






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Production of Seeds




K. Shanmuga Sundaram














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a):	the quantum of seeds produced by various States in the country including Tamil Nadu, crop-wise and State/UT-wise; (b):	 whether the country is self sufficient in the production of quality seeds and is able to fully cater to the demand of domestic market; (c):	 if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(d):	the steps taken by the Government to increase the production of quality seeds domestically?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The quantum of certified/quality seeds available in the country for the year 2019-20 is 431.01 lakh quintals against requirement of 387.31 lakh quintals as reported by States/UTs. The crop wise and state wise total quantity of seeds available in the country for the year 2019-20 including the State of Tamil Nadu is placed at Annexure I and II.(b) & (c):	Yes, Sir. The detail of requirement and availability of certified/quality seeds in the country in last three years and in current year is mentioned below:      (Quantity of seed in Lakh Quintals)S. No.	Year	Requirement	Availability1	2016-17	353.49	380.302	2017-18	371.38	419.413	2018-19	353.54	398.884	2019-20	387.31	431.01Contd….2/-- 2 -(d):	A well established system for production and distribution of quality seed of improved varieties to the farmers already exist in our country. The system is being implemented through Agriculture Department of States, National Seed Corporation, State Seed Corporations, State Agricultural Universities, Cooperative Societies, Other Central Government Organizations and Private Seeds Producing Agencies. The State Governments prepare Seed Rolling Plan three years in advance to estimate the requirement of seed in their state and distribute seed production targets accordingly to different seed production agencies. This system ensures timely availability of required quantity of seed to the farmers in different states.The Government of India also provides financial assistance to different States and Government Seed Producing Agencies for production and distribution of seeds and for other interventions related to seed sector to ensure timely availability of quality seed to the farmers. The various ongoing crop development programmes/schemes are National Food Security Mission, Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture, Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana, Sub-Mission on Seeds and Planting Materials etc. to increase the production of quality seeds domestically.
