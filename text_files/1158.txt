                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1021






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Construction of Highways in Rajasthan




Hanuman Beniwal













                                                    Rahul Kaswan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether bypasses sanctioned by the Union Government on various National Highways and 	State Highways in Rajasthan are not getting completed due to want of either financial 	approval or due to financial crunch; (b)	if so, the details thereof by indicating the commencement date, completion date and the 	date on which these works were stopped; (c)	whether the Union Government proposes to restart the work on the incomplete National 	Highways stopped for lack of funding and if so, the details thereof; (d)	the details and the length of highways in kilometer targeted to be constructed by the 	National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) in Rajasthan during the last three years; and(e)	the quantum of funds allocated for this purpose and the expenditure incurred thereon so far 	along with the time by which the said target is likely to be achieved?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)&(b) The Ministry of Road Transport and Highways is primarily responsible for development and maintenance of National Highways.  There are no such National Highways projects which are incomplete either due to financial approval or financial crunch on part of Government of India.(c)	No Sir, does not arise.(d)&(e) The details of length of Highway targeted for construction and actual constructed length by National Highways Authority of India for past 3 years is as below:Sr. No.	Year	Target for construction (in Kms)	Actual constructed length (in Kms)1	2016-17	496	7432	2017-18	473	6533	2018-19	539	474Total	1,508	1,870  		As such, the length targeted for construction in the state of Rajasthan for past 3 years was 1,508 km; however, the actual constructed length is 1,870 km.  Expenditure for an amount of about Rs. 6,042 crore has been incurred for this construction by National Highways Authority of India.*****
