                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3934






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






MSMEs in Rajasthan




Sumedhanand Saraswati














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of registered Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in Rajasthan,  District-wise; (b)	whether the Government is providing assistance to adopt technology for internal programme and external compliance of MSMEs;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and (d)	the steps being taken by the Government to provide assistance to MSMEs in Rajasthan?






ANSWER




                                                                         MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	    	As on 06.12.2019, the number of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) registered in Rajasthan on Udyog Aadhaar Portal after September, 2015 are 4.99 lakh. The district -wise details are given in the Annexure.(b)&(c):	 The Ministry has established 18 Tool Rooms for providing quality tools, training and consultancy in tooling and other support for upgradation of technology.(d): 		The Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) implements various schemes to support MSMEs in the country including Rajasthan. These include Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP), Micro and Small Enterprises-Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP), Tool Rooms and Technology Centers, Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFRUTI), Procurement and Marketing Support Scheme, Entrepreneurship Skill Development Programme (ESDP), Credit Guarantee Scheme for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE) and Credit Linked Capital Subsidy and Technology Upgradation Scheme (CLCS-TUS).
