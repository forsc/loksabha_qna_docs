                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

424






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Hostels for Backward Classes




Hemant Patil














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.424 TO BE ANSWERED ON 20.07.2021HOSTELS FOR BACKWARD CLASSES †424. SHRI HEMANT SRIRAM PATIL: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government is contemplating to construct hostels for the students of backward classes on the lines of hostels for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes students; (b) if so, the time by which their construction work is likely to be started; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a): The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment is already implementing the Centrally Sponsored Scheme for  Construction of Hostels for OBC Boys and Girls since 1998-99 to address the problem of educational backwardness of OBCs. Agencies eligible for financial assistance under the  Scheme are:- (i) State Governments and UT Administrations; (ii) Institutions or organizations set up by the Central Government as autonomous bodies under a statute (e.g. Central Universities, IIT, NIT, etc.).(b) & (c): As per guidelines of the scheme, the construction work of the Hostel has to be completed within eighteen months from award of work order or two years from the release of first installment of Central assistance, whichever is earlier.*****
