                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3540






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






PAN Card




S. Gnanathiraviam














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a large number of persons in the country has managed to obtain more than one PAN card;(b) if so, the number of such persons having more than one PAN card in the country; and (c) the action taken against them along with the number of such PAN cards surrendered/deposited? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a), (b) and (c) In some cases, persons have been allotted more than one PAN. However, the cases having duplicate PAN cards, as per record, have been de-duplicated.The number of PANs which have been de-duplicated is 12,12,527. This does not include PANs which have been re-activated over the period of time. The information relating to surrender of PANs voluntarily is not maintained separately in the data base.Further, in cases where misuse of the duplicate PAN card is reported, penalty proceedings are initiated in accordance with law. -------
