                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3286






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Students Police Cadet Programme




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has reviewed the implementation of the Students Police Cadet programme;(b) if so, the details thereof and the present status of its implementation;(c) the details of the States which has implemented the programme along with the funds allocated and utilized;(d) whether shortage of funds has been reported from any State including Kerala; and(e) if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY) (a) to (c) The Student Police Cadet (SPC) Programme was launched nationally on 21st of July, 2018. Programme seeks to build a bridge between the Police and the community through school students by inculcating values and ethics in them through classes in schools and outside.--2--L.S.US.Q.NO. 3286 FOR 16.03.2021The Union Government has partnered with State Governments & UT Administrations for the purpose. A State Level Monitoring Committee headed by the Additional Chief Secretary/Principal Secretary (Home)/Commissioner (Home) has been constituted along with Additional Chief Secretary/Principal Secretary (School Education) and Director General of Police to lay out broad policy objectives and operational guidelines and monitor the progress of the Programme.  The implementation of the Programme is also reviewed by the Ministry of Home Affairs and Bureau of Police Research & Development. The details of implementation status are at   Annexure-I.All the States/UTs except West Bengal are implementing the Programme. The details of funds released to the States and its utilization are at Annexure-II.(d) & (e) No specific additional budget proposal has been received from the States. *****
