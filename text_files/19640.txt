                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1134






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Lalit Narayan Mithila University Darbhanga




 Gopal Jee Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the admission process for the academic session 2020-21 has been closed in the Directorate of Distance Education of Lalit Narayan Mithila Univesity Darbhanga; (b) whether there is a proposal to restart admission process in various courses of the University through distance education in the interest of the students; (c) if so, the details thereof; (d) whether there is a proposal to construct the building for one more Kendriya Vidyalaya in Darbhanga; and (e) if so, the time by which the construction work is likely to be started?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): Lalit Narayan Mithila University, Darbhanga has informed that admission process in its Directorate of Distance Education has been closed for 2020-21.(b) & (c): As informed by University Grants Commission (UGC), there is no such proposal.(d) & (e): Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) has informed that at present two Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) are in Darbhanga i.e. KV No. 1, AFS, Darbhanga and KV No. 2, Darbhanga. Due to non transfer of land in favour of KVS by the sponsoring authority, KV No. 2 is functioning in the building of KV No. 1 in second shift.****
