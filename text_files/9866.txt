                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3251






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Vacancies in Kendriya Vidyalayas




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of vacant posts in teaching staff (PGT, TGT and SpecialEducators) across Kendriya Vidyalayas across the country includingKarnataka;(b) whether such vacancies have increased over the years and if so, the detailsthereof;(c) whether the Government has identified reasons behind the vacancies and ifso, the details thereof;(d) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to ensure zero vacancy inschools; and(e) whether there have been any modifications to the recruitment procedure tomaintain a good quality of teaching staff and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) As on 15.11.2019, details of sanctioned posts, staff in position and vacantposts of PGTs and TGTs in Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) located all over the countryare as under:-<pre>Post Sanctioned posts Staff in positions Vacant postsPGT 10812 9869 943TGT 16686 14876 1810</pre>There are no sanctioned posts of Special Educators in the KVs.(b) to (d) Filling up of vacancies is a continuous process and action is taken fromtime to time as per the provisions of the relevant recruitment rules for filling up of theposts. As the process of filling up vacancies involves various methods of recruitmentsuch as promotion, Limited Departmental Examination, Direct Recruitment etc. takestime, teachers are also engaged on contractual basis for temporary duration byKendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) to ensure that the teaching-learning process isnot hampered.Directions have also been issued to autonomous organizationsincluding Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan from time to time for reviewing theirvacancies and for taking prompt action for filling up of vacant posts in the vidyalayas.(e) No, Sir. KVS always adopts a fair and transparent procedure in recruitment inorder to maintain good quality of teaching in its schools.*****
