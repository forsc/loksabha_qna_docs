                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1619






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Setting up of Small Scale Industries




Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav













                                                    Prabhubhai Nagarbhai Vasava
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has released funds for setting up small scale industries under its ongoing scheme during the last three years and if so, the total funds released by the Government, State/year-wise; (b) 	if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	 the total number of small scale industries set up in various States during the last three years;(d) 	the details of the different units of micro, small and medium enterprises proposed to be set up in the State of Gujarat including present status thereof;(e) 	whether the Government has allotted land for setting up of all these units and if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and(f) 	whether any funds have been allocated so far by the Government for these units and if so, the details thereof and the target set for setting up of these units?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI  NITIN GADKARI)(a) & (b):	Yes, Sir. Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) is implementing Prime Minister''s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) since 2008. Khadi and Village Industries Commission is the Nodal implementing agency at national level. The scheme is being implemented through all Public Sector Banks, selected private sector Banks and co-operative Banks. Under this scheme, banks provide loan and the margin money subsidy is provided by Ministry of MSME through KVIC. The maximum cost of the project eligible for financing under PMEGP is Rs.25.00 lakh in Manufacturing Sector and Rs.10.00 lakh in Service Sector. 	The details of fund disbursed as margin money subsidy to set up micro units under PMEGP during the last three years State-wise is placed at Annexure-I.(c):	The total number of micro units setup in various States during last three years under PMEGP is placed at Annexure – II.(d):	A target to set up 3367 new micro enterprises under PMEGP scheme has been fixed during the FY 2019-20. Against this target, as on 31.10.2019, 2379 micro units have been established. (e):        No Sir. (f):	As mentioned in part ‘d’ against the target of setting up of 3367 new micro enterprises under PMEGP scheme an allocation of  Rs. 101.01 crore towards margin money subsidy has been fixed during the FY 2019-20.
