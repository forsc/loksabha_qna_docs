                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1803






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Development of New Tourism Circuit in Goa




Francisco Cosme Sardinha














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is planning to develop any new tourism circuit in Goa like churches apart from the proposed Eco-circuit under its Swadesh Darshan Scheme; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (b):	The Ministry of Tourism, under the Swadesh Darshan scheme is developing thematic circuits in the country in planned and prioritized manner. The projects for development under the scheme are identified in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations/Central Agencies and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, their adherence to relevant scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. Ministry has sanctioned following two projects in Goa:•	Development of Sinquerim-Baga, Anjuna-Vagator, Morjim-Keri, Aguada Fort and Aguada Jail during 2016-17 for Rs.99.99 crore.•	Development of Rua De Orum Creek - Don Paula -Colva – Benaulim during 2017-18 for Rs.99.35 crore.********
