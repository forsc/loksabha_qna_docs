                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

585






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






FCI Godown in PPP Moc




Manoj Kumar Tiwari














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any proposal to establish mini FCI Godown in PPP mode; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	if not the reasons therefore; and (d) 	the mechanism and nodal officer for the above said proposal, if any?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	No, Sir. There is no proposal to establish mini Food Corporation of India (FCI) godowns in Public-Private Partnership (PPP) mode.(b): 	Question doesn''t arise.(c): 	FCI constructs godowns through PPP mode as per storage gap assessment of minimum size of 5,000 MT in plain areas and 1,670 MT in hilly areas.(d): 	Question doesn''t arise.*****
