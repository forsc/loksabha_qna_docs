                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3878






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Recruitment through Advertisement




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the zone-wise number and details of recruitment for posts advertised by the Indian railways during the last five years;(b)	 the number of employees given permanent employment through these recruitment;(c)	 the number of recruitment tests out of these results of which are yet to be declared;  (d)	 the number of candidates awaiting appointment process after the declaration of result;(e)	 the time limit fixed for the result declaration of the tests results of which are yet to come including the reasons for it taking more than the fixed time; and(f)	the number of posts for recruitments in case of which qualifications/conditions have been amended after the application process and result declaration?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (f): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (f) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 3878 BY SHRI DEEPAK BAIJ TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 11.12.2019 REGARDING RECRUITMENT THROUGH ADVERTISEMENT(a):  The details are appended.(b):  During 2015-2016 to 2019-2020 (upto 30.11.2019), 45,507 candidates for various Group ‘C’ posts (excluding Level-1) and 55,013 candidates for various Level-1 posts were recommended for appointment on Zonal Railways / Production Units. In addition, 9,664 candidates have also been empanelled for various posts of Railway Protection Force (RPF) / Railway Protection Special Force (RPSF) for appointment on Railways. (c): Action on Centralized Employment Notifications (CENs) viz. 01/2019, 03/2019 and RRC-01/2019 for 1,40,640 combined vacancies is underway.  Declaration of result for CEN 03/2018 for 13,538 vacancies is under process. Part panels of 49,314 for all levels are under process at Zonal Railways / Production Units.(d):  Offers of appointment are given by Zonal Railways / Production Units to all the empanelled candidates subject to candidates satisfying all eligibility criteria, including character and antecedents.(e): No time-limit is prescribed for declaration of results in the Centralized Employment Notifications.(f):  No qualifications/conditions were amended after the applications by the candidates and result declaration. *****
