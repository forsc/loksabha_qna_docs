                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1723






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Assessment of PMAY-U




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the total number of applications received, 	processed and the amount of loans disbursed under different 	categories of the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Urban (PMAY-U) 	during the last three years, State-wise; (b) 	the details of the total funds released by the Government for 	yojana during the last three year, Statewise; and (c) 	the details of the total funds released by the Government and 	disbursed to the beneficiaries for the Yojana during the said 	period, financial institution-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	State-wise details of the total number of applications received, processed and the amount of loans disbursed under different categories under Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme [CLSS] of Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Urban (PMAY-U) during the last three years are given at Annexure -1.(b)	States/UTs wise details of central assistance released during last three years under PMAY-U including that under CLSS are at Annexure 2.(c)	Financial institution-wise details of funds released by the Government and disbursed to beneficiaries during last three years under CLSS are at Annexure-3.                                              *****
