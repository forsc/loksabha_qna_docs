                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4361






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Ayurvedic Central University




Devusinh Jesingbhai Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)  be pleased to state: (a) the details of guidelines worked out to establish universities of Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha Medicine system indicating the authority/officers nominated to formulate and introduce these guidelines; (b) whether the Government has any proposal to convert some of the selected AYUSH colleges to an Ayurvedic Central University; and (c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):   At present no such guidelines has been worked out by the Government to establish universities of Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha Medicine system.(b) & (c):  Presently there is no such proposal under consideration of the Central Government.………………….
