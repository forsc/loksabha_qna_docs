                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1649






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Appointments in Coir Board




Lallu Singh














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of appointments made in the Coir Board during each of the last years and the current year;(b)	whether compassionate appointments are being made in the Coir Board;(c)	if so, the number of the persons appointed on compassionate ground during the last three years and current year alongwith the directions prescribed for such appointments;(d)	whether applications for compassionate appointment are pending in the Coir Board; and(e)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken/to be taken/to be taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	The total number of appointments made in Coir Board during the last years from 2014 onwards are as follows:<pre>Year	Total Number of appointment2014	242015	NIL2016	NIL2017	12018	NIL2019	NIL2020	NIL2021	NIL</pre>(b) & (c):	Yes, Sir. Compassionate appointments are being made in the Coir Board. No appointments were made on compassionate ground during the last three years and current year. Only 5% of the total Direct Recruit vacancies in Group ‘C’ category are considered for filling Compassionate ground Appointment, as per rules.(d) & (e):	 9 applications as detailed below are under consideration of compassionate appointment under Coir Board as per rule.<pre>Sl.
