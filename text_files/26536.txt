                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2109






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Tribal Sub-Scheme in Telangana




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of amount spent by the Government in Telangana during the last three years under Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-scheme for development of tribals in the Integrated Tribal Development Projects area, district-wise;(b) 	whether there is any proposal to include any area in Telangana especially in Kamareddy, Sangareddy, Nizamabad & Medak districts under the above scheme that is pending with the Government; and(c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SMT. RENUKA SINGH SARUTA)  (a):	The details of the amount released by the Ministry of Tribal Affairs (MoTA) to the Government of Telangana under the scheme of ‘Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-scheme’ during last four years are given as under:Financial Year	Fund Released (Rs. in lakh)2017-18	4493.552018-19	2850.322019-20	5361.292020-21	4191.00Total:	16896.16As informed by Government of Telangana, the above amount has been spent for additional infrastructure in schools, MFPs for Farmer producers organization, Paramedic Trainings, Roads and Buildings, Nutrition Food, Sports schools, B.Ed and D.Ed Colleges, Employment and Livelihood Generation etc. ITDA-wise details of funds utilized during the last three years are at Annexure.(b) & (c): MoTA envisages to revamp the existing scheme of Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub-Scheme (SCA to TSS) for next five years i.e. 2021-22 to 2025-26 wherein 39 villages of these districts are covered.
