                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3585






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Contingency Fund under PMAY-G




Manjulata Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of RURAL DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state the time by which the administrative contingency under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) relating to the financial years 2017-18 and2018-19 amounting to Rs. 215.91 crore is likely to be sanctioned by the Ministry?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)To achieve the objective of “Housing for All” by 2022, in rural areas of the country, the Government has re-structured the erstwhile rural housing scheme Indira Awaas Yojana (IAY) into Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) w.e.f. 1st April, 2016. Under PMAY-G, Admin funds are released to the States/Union Territories (UTs) in two equal instalments. To ensure timely utilization and to avoid parking of Admin funds, releases are made only after 60% utilization of total available administrative funds with the State/UT. State/UT-wise funds released under Admin component of PMAY-G in the years 2017-18 and 2018-19 is given at Annexure.******
