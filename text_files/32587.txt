                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

99






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Mobile towers in Aspirational districts




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Union Cabinet has approved the setting up of mobile service/towers in the uncovered villages in aspirational districts of Odisha; (b) 	if so, the details of the amount of funds being spent on this project in Odisha including Kalahandi and Nuapada districts; (c) 	the number of villages where approval has been given for setting up of towers alongwith the number of persons likely to be benefitted there from; and (d) 	the details thereof and the time by which the said task is targeted to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) & (b) 	The Union Cabinet has approved a Scheme for provisioning of 4G Mobile Coverage in 7287 uncovered villages of Aspirational Districts of Andhra Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Maharashtra, Jharkhand & Odisha. Under this scheme, 3933 uncovered villages of Aspirational Districts of Odisha (including that of Kalahandi and Nuapada Districts) are included at an approved  cost of Rs. 3219 crore.(c) & (d) 	In Odisha, 2379 towers are planned to cover 3933 uncovered villages and the people living therein will be benefited. The project time-line is 18 months from the date of signing of the Agreement with the Implementing Agency for the project.
