                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4351






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






NMPB




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) the role of Kerala Forest department and other Forest Departments in implementation of National Medicinal Plant Board (NMPB) objective; (b) the details of the requirement of products from NMPB, State/UT and location-wise; (c) whether NMPB has been able to bring improvement in availability of medicinal plants, if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the other units besides Ayurveda are using plants to produce and market extracts; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):      Forests are the main source of medicinal plants and the conservation, resource augmentation and sustainable utilization of these resources is also one of the objectives of National Medicinal Plants Board (NMPB). To meet this objective of NMPB, the different State Forest Departments in the Country including Kerala Forest Department implements various projects sanctioned by NMPB on In-situ/ Ex-situ Conservation, Resource Augmentation and sustainable harvesting of medicinal plants.   (b):   The State/UT-wise detail of number of projects supported by NMPB on Conservation, Resource Augmentation and sustainable harvesting of medicinal plants till 2018-19 is at Annexure.Contd.......-2-(c):   Yes, the NMPB has been able to bring improvement in availability of medicinal plants in the Country. In this regard, the NMPB has supported more than 96738 hectare area till 2018-19 for conservation and resource augmentation of medicinal plants in wild. In addition, NMPB, Ministry of AYUSH has also supported more than 220476 hectare area till 2018-19 under cultivation of medicinal plants for the availability of medicinal plants.(d) & (e):	The information regarding other units using the plants to produce and market extracts is not available.………………
