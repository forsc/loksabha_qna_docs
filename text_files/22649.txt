                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3687






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Sriram Sagar Project Stage-II




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that Sriram Sagar Project Stage II in Telangana has been taken up under PMKSY with a targeted irrigation protential of 1,78,000 hectares on priority basis; (b) if so, the details thereof (c) whether the project is likely to be completed within the original deadline set for its completion, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) the funds allocated, sanctioned, released and utilized for this project during each of the last three years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b) During 2016-17, eleven ongoing projects of Telengana, including Sriram Sagar Project (SSP) Stage II, have been included in the 99 prioritized projects, to be completed in mission mode, under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana - Accelerated Irrigation Benefits Programme (PMKSY-AIBP) being implemented by this Ministry. For these projects, funding mechanism through NABARD has been provisioned by the Government of India, for both Central and State Share. Ultimate Irrigation Potential (UIP) of SSP Stage II is 178.066 thousand hectare.(c)  At the time of inclusion under PMKSY-AIBP, SSP Stage II was planned for completion by June, 2019. However, on account of delay in land acquisition, non-completion of structures on distributaries etc., completion for the project is now scheduled for March, 2021.(d)  Eligibility of total Central Assistance (CA) for the project under PMKSY-AIBP is Rs. 48.36 crore, against which CA released so far is Rs.39.29 crore. Further, the year-wise CA released, vis-à-vis expenditure incurred on this project during the last three years and current year (reported so far), is as under:Rs in croreSl. No.	Financial Year	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-211	CA Released 	13.242	0	9.049	02	Expenditure Incurred	49.28	35.76	10.435	15.95*****
