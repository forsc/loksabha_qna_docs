                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

182






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Identification of Tourist Spots of Odisha




Ramesh Chandra Majhi














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of TOURISM be pleased to state:(a)  	the number of tourist spots of Nabarangpur, Malkangiri and Koraput districts of ODISHA which have been identified by the Government during last five financial years;(b)   the funds allocated by the Government for the development of the said tourist spots;(c)  	if so, the details thereof; and(d)  	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 				(SHRI  G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c):	No project has been sanctioned for Nabarangpur, Malkangiri and Koraput districts of Odisha in the last five financial years. Ministry of Tourism has sanctioned following projects under the Swadesh Darshan, PRASHAD and Assistance to Central Agencies Schemes respectively in the State of Odisha:i.       Development of Gopalpur, Barkul, Satapada and Tampara under the theme of Coastal Circuit for an amount of Rs. 70.82 Crore.ii.      Infrastructure Development at Puri, Shree Jagannath Dham – Ramachandi - Prachi River front at Deuli under Mega Circuit for an amount of Rs. 50.00 Crore.iii.   Joint Development of Puri Railway Station in association with Ministry of Railways for an amount of Rs. 6.15 Crore.(d):	Projects under Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD are sanctioned after proposal is received from State Government. It is subsequently evaluated as per the Scheme guidelines.*****
