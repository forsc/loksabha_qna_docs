                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3840






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Schemes for Promotion of Agriculture




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the Centrally-Sponsored Schemes being implemented for the promotion of agriculture in Uttar Pradesh ; (b) the details of the amount allocated in this regard during each of the last three years and current year; (c) the amount of funds utilized under those schemes along with the targets set and the achievements made in this regard; and (d) whether some proposals received from the State Governments are pending with the Union Government and if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????  (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (c) Details of beneficiary oriented Centrally Sponsored Schemes being implemented in Uttar Pradesh, amount allocated and financial  achievements are given in Annexure.(d) 	Proposals from States related to farmers welfare pertaining to various schemes in the form of annual action plans/detailed project reports are received by various Divisions of the Department of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare.  These are processed for competent approval i.e. Executive Committee/State Level Sanctioning Committee etc.  Depending on the status of unspent balances, utilization certificates etc and as per laid down procedure ie. approval of Executive Committee/State Level Sanctioning Committee etc., these proposals are approved in coordination with the Finance Division. Receipt of proposals from the states is a continuous process and these are approved as per laid down procedure. Thereafter funds are released to the States. No proposals complete in all respects, from state governments are pending with the Department of Agriculture and Farmers’ Welfare currently.
