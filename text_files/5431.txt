                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5019






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Financial Assistance to Sugar Mills




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that the Government has provided financial assistance of Rs. 5.50 per quintal to sugar mills during the sugar season 2017-18 and Rs. 13.88 per quintal during the sugar season 2018-19 to compensate the cost of sugarcane;(b) 	if so, the details of the total funds provided under this head by the Government to sugar mills and the number of sugarcane farmers who got payment on time;(c) 	whether the Government has made any assessment in this regard and if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	whether it is a fact that the sugar mills are spending money on other heads which have been provided by the Government for payment of sugarcane farmers?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a):	With  a view to offset the cost of cane and facilitate timely payment of cane price dues of farmers for sugar season 2017-18, Government notified a scheme on 9th May, 2018 envisaging assistance to sugar mills @ Rs. 5.50 per qtl of cane crushed. Under a similar scheme notified on 5th October, 2018 for sugar season 2018-19, assistance is to be extended to sugar mills @ Rs. 13.88 per quintal of cane crushed.  (b) to (d):       The Central Government has so far extended assistance amounting to Rs. 392.76 crore to sugar mills under the scheme notified on 9th May, 2018. No funds have so far been released under the scheme notified on 5th October, 2018. As per provisions of both the schemes, banks shall credit the amount of assistance to the farmers’ accounts on behalf of the mills against cane dues payable and   subsequent balance, if any, shall be credited into mill’s account. Data regarding number of farmers benefitted under the scheme is not maintained by the Government.****
