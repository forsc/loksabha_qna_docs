                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3321






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Rural Health Research Units




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government proposes to establish Model Rural Health Research Units in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof including the number of such units established in the country, year-wise and State-wise;(c) the salient features of such research units;(d) whether the Government has any plan to set up more units in the country; and(e) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	The Government has been implementing a Central Sector Scheme for Establishment of Model Rural Health Research Units (MRHRU) since 2013-14.(b):	Details are annexed as Annexure.(c):	The salient features of such research units are :- I.	To develop state/area specific models depending upon the disease profile, morbidity patterns and local conditions, for transfer of technology for providing better health care services to the rural masses.II.	To train the health professionals under State Health Systems for use of modern field adaptable methods and develop models for translational purposes to improve health status of rural masses.III.	To undertake various research projects in close coordination with the State Government institutions and others that are relevant and beneficial to the rural population.(d) & (e):	These units are set-up as per proposals received from the State Governments in accordance with the guidelines of the scheme and this is a continuing process.
