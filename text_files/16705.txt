                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

358






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Beneficiaries under NFSA




Rahul Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the total number of beneficiaries under the National Food Security Act (NFSA) State-wise; (b) 	the details of allocation and lifting of additional foodgrains and pulses provisioned for National Food Security Act (NFSA) beneficiaries under the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY), Statewise; (c) 	the details of migrants not covered under the National Food Security Act or State PDS scheme, Statewise; (d) 	the status of distribution of food grains and pulses to 8 crore migrant workers and their families under the Atmanirbhar Bharat scheme; (e) 	whether NGOs and welfare organisations were provided subsidised food grains under the PMGKAY; and (f) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a): 	A statement indicating the State/UT-wise number of beneficiaries covered under the National Food Security Act, 2013 (NFSA) is at Annexure-1............2/--2-(b): 	Under the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana, about 121 Lakh Metric Tons (LMT) of foodgrains later revised to approx 119.32 LMT were allotted to approximately 80.96 crore beneficiaries for the period April to June, 2020. Hon’ble Prime Minister, in his address to the nation on 30.06.2020 announced extension of said package for a further period of 5 months i.e. upto November, 2020. Accordingly, allocation of foodgrains of 201 LMT for these five months i.e. July to November, 2020 was made for distribution to approximately 80.43 crore beneficiaries. Under the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package, distribution of pulses for three months from April to June, 2020 to all beneficiaries under the National Food Security Act, 2013 (NFSA) covering 19.4 crore households were made free of cost. The pulses have been provided at the rate of 1kg per household per month for 3 months covering 18.26 Crore beneficiary households. The scheme was extended for further five months i.e. from July to November, 2020 to all beneficiaries under the National Food Security Act, 2013 (NFSA) covering 19.4 crore households. 1 kilogram whole chana have been provided per household per month for 5 months.The status of allocation of foodgrains to beneficiaries covered under PMGKAY is at Annexure-2A & 2B. The status of allocation and lifting of pulses under PMGKAY is at Annexure 3.(c):  States/UTs collectively indicated a total population of about 2.8 Cr. migrants who do not have NFSA/ State ration cards and who may be potentially benefited from the distribution of free-of-cost foodgrains under the Atma Nirbhar Bharat Scheme (ANBS). The estimated numbers of migrants intimated by the States/UTs are at Annexure-4.(d):  	Under Atma Nirbhar Bharat (ANB) package, States/UTs have lifted total 6.4 LMT foodgrains against total allocation of 8 LMT from FCI godowns based on estimate of migrant population in their respective state. Total 2.67 LMT foodgrains have been distributed to 2.35 crore, 2.50 crore, 32.18 lakh and 22.38 lakh migrants in the month of May, June, July and August, 2020 respectively.Under the Atma Nirbhar Bharat Package, Distribution of 1 Kg whole chana from May to June, 2020 was made to migrant labourers households who are not covered under NFSA, 2013 or are without ration card in the State/UT in which they were stranded. This has covered 164.70 lakhbeneficiary households.
