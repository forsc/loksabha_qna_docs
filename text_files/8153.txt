                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1653






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Jal Jeevan Mission




S. Jothimani














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the measures taken/ being taken by the Government to provide financial and technical assistance to States under the Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM);(b) the details of the Functional Household Tap Connections (FHTC) installed under JJM in rural households during the last five years;(c) whether the Government will be able to cover FHTC to all rural households by its target of 2024; and(d) the details of allocation of funds for Tamil Nadu, district and State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) Under Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM), a budgetary allocation of ?10,000.66 Crore has been made for the year 2019-20 and first instalment has been released to eligible States/ UTs. Further, the planned outlay of JJM in five years is ?3.60 lakh Crore, out of which Central Share is about ?2.08 lakh Crore. After the launch of JJM, a conference of Minister in-charge of rural water supply of various States was held in New Delhi followed by five regional workshops to discuss various aspects of JJM and modalities of the implementation. In addition, regular review of the programme is also held with States/ UTs. (b) As reported, the number of household connection with Piped Water Supply installed during the last five years under erstwhile National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP) [now restructured and subsumed into JJM] in rural areas across country is as below:Year	No. of household connectionsprovided2014-15	17,10,1382015-16	12,94,0452016-17	11,92,6062017-18	6,32,6142018-19 	9,65,587(c) Jal Jeevan Missionaims at providing potable water to every rural household through Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) by 2024.(d) The State-wise fund allocation under JJM including Tamil Nadu for the year 2019-20 is annexed.*****
