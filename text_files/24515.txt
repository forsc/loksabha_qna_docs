                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

250






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Promoting Mechanisation in Agriculture Sector




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has established/ introduced any project to promote mechanisation in agriculture sector; (b) if so, the details thereof alongwith its aims and target; and (c) the details of budget provision made and utilised so far for it since its inception, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????             (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) and (b): 	The Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare is implementing Sub- Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM) with the major objectives and targets of increasing the reach of farm mechanization to small and marginal farmers and to the regions where availability of farm power is low; promoting Custom Hiring Centres and hubs for hi-tech & high value farm machines and creating awareness among stakeholders through demonstration and capacity building activities.(c)The statement giving details of funds releasedand funds utilized by States since inception of SMAM in 2014-15, State-wise is enclosed as Annexure-I
