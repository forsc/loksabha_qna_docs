                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

638






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Train from Bhavnagar Division




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the department of Railways has taken decision to normalize its services as earlier; (b) if so, the time by when all the train services under Bhavnagar division are likely to be resumed; (c) whether new trains are proposed to be operated from Bhavnagar division; (d) if so, the details thereof; and (e) the time by which the said trains are likely to be made operational?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a)  to (e) Due to Covid-19 Pandemic, Indian Railways had discontinued all regular passenger carrying trains w.e.f. 23.03.2020 and only special trains were operated. Further, Indian Railways is keeping a close watch on the situation and restoring regular train services in a phased manner gradually.  Besides, introduction of trains is an ongoing process on Indian Railways subject to operational feasibility, traffic demand, availability of resources etc.*****
