                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

748






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Madurai Smart City Projects




S. Venkatesan













                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the status of overall work in progress for Madurai Smart City Projects, project-wise; and(b) the time-line by which Madurai Smart City Projects will be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) Madurai was selected to be developed as Smart City in Round-2 of Smart Cities selection process on 20th September, 2016. So far, out of 14 projects worth Rs.977.55 Crore, Madurai Smart City has issued work order in all the projects of which 1 project worth Rs.12 Crore has been completed. The present status of these projects is given in Annexure-I.(b) Madurai Smart City is expected to complete its projects within 5 years from the date of selection.                                         *****
