                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2925






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Development of Darbhanga Airport




 Gopal Jee Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the amount of funds released and spent on various developmental works including infrastructural development of Darbhanga airport along with the time limit fixed for its proper development;(b) whether it is proposed to construct a passenger shed from main entry gate to Terminal building at Darbhanga airport for the convenience of passengers; and(c) if so, the time by which it will be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) to (c): So far, Rs. 81.785 crores have been spent on infrastructural development of the Darbhanga Airport The State Government has confirmed undertaking construction of shed at the Darbhanga airport. Further, infrastructural developments are linked to transfer of land by the State Government.
