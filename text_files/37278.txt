                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4305






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Decreased Agricultural Budget




Kalyan Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the budget for the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare as a share of the total budget has fallen drastically from 3.78% in 2021-22 to 3.36% in 2022-23; (b) if so, whether is it a fact that under PM-KISAN yojana, 12.5 crore farmer households are supposed to be provided Rs. 6,000 each which requires an allocation of Rs. 75,000 crore but only Rs. 68,000 crore has been allocated therefor; and (c) if so, the reasons behind the deficit in allocation and the steps proposed by the Government to support the 12.5 crore farmer families?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE????????????????????????(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) The budget for Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare as a share of the total Union Budget has increased from 0.5 % in 2013-2014 to 1.2 % in 2021-2022 andin 2022-2023.(b) & (c) The Budget Estimate for PM-KISAN has been increased fromRs. 65000 crore in 2021-2022 toRs. 68000 crore in 2022-23. Further on need/utilization basis, more funds can be provided in Revised Estimate stage.***
