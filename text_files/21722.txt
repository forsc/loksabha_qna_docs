                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2837






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Projects Approved under CAMPA Funds




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of projects approved along with funds released/employment generation reported using CAMPA funds under AtmaNirbhar Bharat Package in the year, 2020-2021, State/UT-wise;(b)	the total number of jobs created for vulnerable tribal population under projects utilising CAMPA funds in the last two years, State/UT-wise;(c)	 whether there is any time bound process and monitoring mechanism for completion of projects under Atma Nirbhar Bharat Package; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) & (b) On 12.05.2020 Hon’ble Prime Minister of India announced a special economic package of ?20,00,000 crore under Atma Nirbhar Bharat. This package also included ?6,000 crore employment push using CAMPA funds to create job opportunities including for tribal communities in semi-urban and rural areas. Against this, the National Compensatory Afforestation Fund Management and Planning Authority (National Authority) has approved Annual Plans of Operation (APOs) of 31 States/UTs amounting to ?7904.16 crore  as on 28.02.2021. As per information received from States/UTs 4,83,59,947 man-days have been generated till 05.03.2021 from 31 States/UTs. The information is attached at Annexure-I.(c) & (d) Yes Sir, Monitoring of projects are done as per established monitoring mechanism of the respective State Governments/UT Administration, which includes monitoring by the officers and staff of the State Government/UT Administration as well as specialized wings of the State Forest Department. External evaluation is also undertaken by the States/UTs. At National level a Monitoring Group has been constituted as per provision in the Compensatory Afforestation Fund (CAF),Act 2016.***
