                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2112






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Reservation to OBCs in Allotment of Retail Outlets




Vijay Baghel













                                                    Arun Sao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Other Backward Classes (OBCs) have been provided reservationby the Government in the allotment of retail outlets(Petrol Pumps) and if so, thedetails thereof;(b) whether the Government has framed any guidelines in this regard in allotmentof new retail outlets and if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details of total retail outlets allotted to the persons belonging to OBCsduring the last three years, location and State-wise, including Chhattisgarh?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURALGAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a)&(b) : Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) have informed thatadhering to the broad reservation principles of the Government of India,Public Sector OMCs had introduced 27% reservation for OBC category forthe first time in the Dealer Selection Guidelines (DSG) effective21.05.2014. Since then, 27% of locations advertised by Public SectorOMCs for new ROs dealerships are reserved under OBC category.(c) : The total number of petrol pumps allotted (LOIs issued) by Public SectorOMCs to persons belonging to OBCs during the period 2018-2021 (April-June 2021) in the country including Chhattisgarh is 6604. The details oflocations advertised in November/December 2018 are available athttp://www.petrolpumpdealerchayan.in.****
