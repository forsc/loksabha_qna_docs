                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

209






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Panchayati Raj Grants




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that Panchayati Raj grants under various schemes of the Government are not being released to State of Maharashtra in time during the last four years and in the current year;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the details of grants released under various schemes to the State of Maharashtra during the last four years, year-wise and scheme-wise; and(d) the details of expenditure incurred, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (d) The Ministry under the erstwhile scheme of Capacity Building- Panchayat Sashaktikaran Abhiyan (CB-PSA) implemented during the year 2016-17 & 2017-18 and ongoing scheme of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) being implemented w.e.f. 2018-19 funds have been released to State of Maharashtra. Year-wise details of funds released & utilized by the State of Maharashtra during the last four years and in current year are as under:Detail of fund released & utilized by the State of Maharashtra under the scheme CB-PSAYear	    Fund Released2016-17	    Rs.21.17 crore2017-18	    Rs.44.53 croreCumulative release since 2012-13 to 2017-18	Rs.188.12 croreCumulative utilization against the total fund released since 2012-13 to 2017-18 is Rs 139.14 crore  Year-wise detail of fund released & utilized by the State of Maharashtra in RGSA scheme from 2018-19 to 2020-21Year	Opening Balance	  Fund Released	  Total Available fund	  Fund Utilized2018-19	 Rs.48.98 crore	   Rs. 11.54 crore	Rs. 60.52 crore	  Rs. 26.44 crore2019-20	 Rs. 34.08 crore   Rs. 8.44 crore	Rs. 42.52 crore	  Rs. 37.29 crore2020-21	 Rs. 5.23 crore	   Rs. 66.76 crore	Rs. 71.99 crore	- *****
