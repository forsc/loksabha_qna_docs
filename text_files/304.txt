                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

275






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Atal Tinkering Labs and Innovation Mission




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to take a big initiative like Atal Tinkering Labsand Atal Innovation Mission to encourage research and innovation in the schools of thecountry;(b) if so, whether the Government proposes to provide the said facilities in the schoolslocated in Vadodara City of Gujarat; and(c) if so, the time by which it is likely to be implemented and if not, the reasonstherefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Atal Tinkering Lab (ATL) is a program run by Atal Innovation Mission (AIM)under NITI Aayog to foster curiosity and innovative mindset in young students acrossIndia to encourage research and innovation in schools across the country. The vision ofthe initiative is ''To create One million children in India as Neoteric Innovators''. Thedetails about various activities of ATL program are given on AIM websitewww.aim.gov.in. Till date, 8878 schools have been selected for ATL establishment, outof which 3020 have completed their compliance process and have been fundedsuccessfully.(b) & (c) Till date, 20 schools in Vadodara city have been selected for ATLestablishment, out of which 8 have completed their compliance process and have beenfunded successfully. The establishment of ATL is an ongoing process across the countryand is subject to compliance status of schools and availability of funds.******
