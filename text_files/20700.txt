                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2065






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Setting up of Handloom Clusters




Manne Srinivas Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to set up handloom clusters to benefit weavers in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof and progress made therein along with the funds sanctioned during the last three years including the current year as on date State/ UT-wise especially Mahabubnagar district of Telangana; and(c) the details and grounds under which proposals were accepted/rejected in this regard? 






ANSWER




MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (c):	The Government of India has been implementing Block Level Cluster, a component of National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP) and Comprehensive Handloom Cluster Development Scheme(CHCDS) since 2015-16 for holistic & integrated development of handloom weavers which inter-alia provides financial assistance upto Rs.2.00 crore per sanctioned cluster for implementation of various interventions such as Hathkargha Samvardhan Sahayata (HSS), product development, construction of workshed, project management cost, design development, setting up of Common Facility Centre (CFC), etc.  Since F.Y. 2015-16, 440 Block Level Clusters have been sanctioned in the country. Block level clusters are sanctioned based on the viable proposals received from State government concerned as per the extant guidelines of the scheme.  With regard to Mahabubnagar district of Telangana, one cluster has been sanctioned covering 235 beneficiaries during 2016-17 with total cost of Rs.104.60 lakh comprising the Government of India’s share of Rs.102.50 lakh.  Details of          State/UT-wise clusters sanctioned, funds released and beneficiaries covered from F.Y. 2015-16 to      F.Y. 2020-21(till January 2021) are annexed.
