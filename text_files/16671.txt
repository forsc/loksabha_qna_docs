                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

405






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Riverine Fishing Ports




Rajiv Pratap Rudy














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has conducted any survey to identify such riverine fishing ports where there is scarcity of infrastructure and other facilities, if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government has announced steps for development of inland fisheries in the country and if so, details thereof; (c) whether the Government is undertaking steps to boost economic activity along river banks and if so, details thereof; (d) the details of the proposals submitted for setting up of new inland fishing ports during the last three years and the current year, State and UT-wise and in district of Saran; and (e) the details of total fishing produce in the country, inland and coastal, State-wise, for the last seven years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a)	No, Sir.(b)	to (c): The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a new flagship scheme namely ‘Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana’ as a part of Atmanirbhar Bharat Covid-19 response with an estimated investment of Rs.20,050 crore for implementation for a period of  five years from FY-2020-21 to 2024-25 for the overall development of Fisheries Sector  in the country including Inland Fisheries. The PMMSY also envisages for support for establishment of fish landing centres in both Inland and maritime States/UTs to boost economic activity. Assistance will be provided based on the proposals received from States/UTs.(d) Proposals have not been received from States/UTs for setting up of new inland fishing ports during the last three years. While there is no precedence of taking up of establishing Inland fishing ports in the country, Fish Landing Centres  under ‘Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana’ at an estimated cost upto  Rs.25 crore can be taken up for establishing landing and berthing facilities including some of the post harvest  infrastructure as may be available in fishing harbours/ports.  (e) The details of total fish production, Inland and Coastal, State wise, for the last seven years from 2012-13 to 2018-19 is enclosed as Annexure.***
