                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

604






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Revenues of Trains in Salem Railway Division




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of number of passengers who travelled in the (1) Coimbatore-Mettupalayam passenger train (2) Coimbatore-Pollachi passenger train (3) Coimbatore-Madurai train in Salem railway division (Tamil Nadu State) and the revenue earned during each year during the past three years, year/train-route-wise, separately;(b)  whether the Government is reviewing the traffic and revenue in the above said trains;(c)   if so, the details thereof and the decision taken for extension of trains and introduction of Coimbatore-Madurai passenger train via Pollachi Dindigul; and(d)   if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.
