                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

809






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Allocation of Funds under HRIDAY




Sanjay Kumar Bandi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the quantum of funds allocated and released for Warangal City under Heritage City Development and Augmentation Yojana (HRIDAY), during the last five years, year wise; and (b)   the major works that have been completed so far and the schedule of the pending works to be completed? 






ANSWER




                                                                 THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                     (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE) (a):  The National Heritage City Development and Augmentation Yojana (HRIDAY) was launched on 21st January, 2015 and has ended on 31st March, 2019.  Under HRIDAY Scheme, funds were allocated to cities for the entire Mission period and not year wise. An amount of?40.54 crore was allocated to Warangal city under HRIDAY Scheme out of which ? 32.82 crore have been released as per the following details:-Financial Year	Funds released (? in crore)2015-16	             3.922016-17	            14.252017-18	             1.222018-19	            13.43(b):  The status of completed and on-going works in Warangal city under HRIDAY Scheme are as under:-S.No.	Name of the work	                              Present Status                                                             (as on 29.11.2021) 1	Bhadrakalilake foreshore development 	              completed2	Development work at 1000 pillar temple area	      completed3 	Development work at KazipethDargah	              completed4	Rejuvenation of pond at Padmakshi temple  and        development of Jain sites of Hanamkondahill 	       completed5	Revival and development of Fort Warangal	       likely to be                                                                completed by March, 2022                         *****
