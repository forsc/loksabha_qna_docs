                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4253






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






DISHA Committee




Sanjay Seth














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the targets fixed for the DISHA Committee and the objectives of its establishment along with minimum period of interval to organise its meetings;(b) 	the number of meetings of DISHA held in various districts of Jharkhand and the schemes discussed in these meetings along with the status of the same during the last three years;(c) 	the number of schemes being run for development of rural areas by the Ministry and the number of schemes functional in Jharkhand; and(d) 	the total amount of funds released for these schemes during the last two years, scheme-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)      District Development Coordination and Monitoring Committee (DISHA) are constituted at State and District levels to monitor the implementation of various schemes of the Central Government and ensure coordination among all stakeholders, including elected representatives and concerned officials for efficient and time-bound development of districts in the country. As per the DISHA Guidelines, the State level DISHA Committee meeting should be held at least once in every six months and the District level DISHA Committee meeting should be held at least once in every quarter.(b)     The details of number of meetings of DISHA held in various districts of Jharkhand during the last three years as well as the schemes discussed in these meetings are provided in Annexure-I. The schemes discussed in the meetings are currently operational in the State of Jharkhand.(c) & (d) The details of schemes being run by the Ministry of Rural Development for development of rural areas and functional in Jharkhand along with the funds released to the State under these schemes during the last two years are enclosed in Annexure -II.*****
