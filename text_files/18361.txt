                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2273






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






News Bulletins in Regional languages




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the various steps taken by the Government to increase the show-time on television and improve the broadcasting of news bulletins in regional languages at national level through ‘Doordarshan’ and ‘Akashwani’; (b)	whether the Government proposes to promote the broadcasting of such bulletins to attach more importance to the various regional languages of the country; and (c)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) to (c): Prasar Bharati has informed that the improvement in news bulletins including in Regional News bulletins is a continuous process. Prasar Bharati is currently broadcasting News bulletins in 77 regional languages/local dialects on its All India Radio (AIR) network.  A dedicated 24x7 multilingual Radio News Service has also been launched in Digital Radio DRM, DTH Satellite and Internet modes.Further, Regional News bulletins are being telecast on various Regional Language Satellite Service (RLSS) Doordarshan Channels.  News operations have been made automated at major DD Regional Kendra and news sets in studios have been revamped for enhancing look and feel of news telecast. More time slots have been provided for News bulletins and current affairs programmes in various regional DD channels.All News Units of All India Radio (AIR) and Doordarshan have been provided dedicated Digital Channels on platforms like YouTube to upload all bulletins and discussion programs in various languages.  The NewsOnAir Digital Platform of Prasar Bharati available on Android and iOS aggregates these news bulletins by language from across all regions. Language specific news websites are also available via www.newsonair.com. ***********
