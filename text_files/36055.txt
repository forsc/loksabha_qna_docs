                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3290






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Functional Post Offices




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the number of post offices functional in the country at present, district and State-wise; (b) 	the details of the number of new post offices set up in the country including Assam during the last five years and the current year, and State-wise; (c) 	the number of employees currently working in post offices across the country and the exact sanctioned strength for different posts therein; (d) 	the steps taken for strengthening the infrastructure and services of post offices across the country during the said period; and (e) 	the amount of funds allocated/utilized by the Government for the said purpose during the said period, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a)	Sir, at present, there are 1,58,595 post offices functional in the country. The district and State-wise details are at Annexure-I.(b)	5066 new post offices have been set up in the country including 12 new post offices in Assam during the last five years and the current year. The State-wise details are at Annexure-II.  (c)	There are 1,60,335 employees currently working in postal establishments across the country against the actual sanctioned strength of different posts totalling to 2,41,452.
