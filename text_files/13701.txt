                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2423






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Lack of Awareness about MSME Schemes




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal













                                                    Shankar Lalwani
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether due to the lack of awareness, the benefits of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) schemes are not reaching common people as a result of which the funds allocated in the budget are not being utilized properly;(b)	if so, whether the Government proposes to spread awareness about its schemes to boost the business of small entrepreneurs;(c)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (d)	the funds spent out of the funds allocated in the budget during the last five years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) to (d):  	Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) implements various schemes for organising Industrial Motivation Campaigns, Vendor Development Programmes, Entrepreneurship and Skill Development Programmes. Ministry also has provisions for expenditure on awareness about these schemes. The details of Budget allocated and utilized by the Ministry of  MSME during the last five years is as under:                                                          (rupees in crore)<pre>Financial Year	BE	Expenditure2014-15	3702.28	2766.602015-16	3007.42	2828.742016-17	3464.77	3626.412017-18	6481.96	6222.182018-19	6552.61	6513.122019-20	7011.29	5777.29(As on 29.02.2020)</pre>
