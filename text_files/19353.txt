                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

896






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Setting up of AIIMS at Darbhanga




 Gopal Jee Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has proposed to set up AIIMS at Darbhanga, Bihar, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has fixed any time limit/ schedule for the laying of foundation stone of AIIMS in Darbhanga; and(c) if so, whether said AIIMS has received approval by the Cabinet?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c) Setting up of a second AIIMS like Institute in Bihar was announced in the Budget speech of 2015-16. The Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, in consultation with State Government of Bihar has finalized the site for AIIMS at Darbhanga in Bihar.This project was approved by the Cabinet on 15th September, 2020 at an estimated cost of Rs. 1264 crore. No timeline has been fixed for laying of foundation stone.
