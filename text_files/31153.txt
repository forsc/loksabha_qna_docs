                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2889






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Paddy Procurement in Punjab




Jasbir Singh Gill














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the quantity of paddy procured during year 2021 from State of Punjab during the last five years; (b) 	the quantity of paddy procured all over India State-wise during the last five years; (c) 	the price of paddy procured at for the last five years for all States; (d) 	the reasons of delayed paddy procurement this season in Punjab; and (e) 	whether global rice commodity prices positively affect paddy procurement prices in India and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): to (c): 		State wise procurement of paddy including Punjab for Central Pool and Minimum Support Price (MSP) value for the last five years is at Annexure-I.(d): Paddy procurement period in Punjab was upto 30.11.2021. Procurement operations have been carried out smoothly in the state and there was no delay in paddy procurement.(e): 	The global rice commodity prices does affect the paddy procurement to certain extent on the principle of demand & supply of commodities as in case of better prices in global market will increase the procurement by private traders.**** ANNEXURE-IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN PARTS (a) TO (c) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.2889 DUE FOR ANSWER ON 15.12.2021 IN THE LOK SABHA.STATEMENT SHOWING STATE WISE PADDY PROCUREMENT FOR CENTRAL POOL & VALUE AT MSP 							Procurement Fig. in LMT , MSP in Rs.
