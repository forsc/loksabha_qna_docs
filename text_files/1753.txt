                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1669






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Growth Rate of Agriculture




Manish Tewari














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the details of the growth rate of the Agriculture  over  the  past  five  years, year-wise;(b) 	the details of the State/ region of the  country  which  has  been  the  fastest growing over the past five years in terms of the growth rate of Agriculture, year-wise;(c) 	the details of the States which have seen negative growth in Agriculture over the  past  five years,  year  and  State-wise; and(d) 	the details of the contribution of Punjab in terms of tonnage and percentage to the total procurement of all foodgrains carried out by the Union Government over the past five years, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????
