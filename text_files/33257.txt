                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

871






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Tribal University in Telangana




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the status of the detailed project report of the proposed Tribal University in Telangana which was promised in the State Re-organization Act; (b) the details of the proposed timeline by which the said University is likely to be set up;(c) whether there is any funds pending with the University Grants Commission for the said University; and (d) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR.  SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) to (d): 	The Andhra Pradesh Re-organisation Act 2014 inter-alia provides for establishment of a Central Tribal University in the State of Telangana. The Site Selection Committee constituted by the Ministry of Education has finalised the Site in Mulugu  (erstwhile Bhupalpally), Telangana. The Detailed Project Report has been prepared and the inter-ministerial consultations have also been completed. The proposal has been submitted for the financial approval of Ministry of Finance. Funds are allocated by University Grants Commission as and when the institution is operationalized.****
