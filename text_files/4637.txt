                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4274






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Construction of Houses in Karnataka




B. Y. Raghavendra













                                                    Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh
                                                








                                                    Nalin Kumar Kateel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government has introduced Housing for All  scheme in the country including Karnataka and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the total number of houses built there under in Karnataka during each of the last three years, district-wise;(c)	the total funds allocated and spent on the said scheme in Karnataka during the said period;(d)	whether the Government has set any target of number of houses for each district and State; and(e)	if so, the details thereof especially for Karnataka?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                        (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a): In pursuance of the Government’s vision of “Housing for All” by 2022, the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs is implementing Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY(U)] for providing assistance to States/Union Territories (UTs) including Karnataka  in addressing the housing requirements of the people belonging to Economically Weaker Sections (EWS), Lower Income Group (LIG) and Middle Income Group (MIG) categories in urban areas. Under PMAY (U), as on 08.07.2019, a total of 17,113 projects involving Central assistance of Rs. 1,30,293.00 crore for construction of 83,68,861 houses have been approved. Of the sanctioned houses, construction of 26,13,799 houses has been completed so far.In the State of Karnataka, a total of 1,792 projects involving central assistance of Rs. 9223.00 crore have been sanctioned for construction of 5,82,469 houses of which 1,50,726 houses have been completed so far. (b): The district-wise details of houses built in Karnataka under PMAY (U) during the last three years are given at Annexure.(c): During the last three years, central assistance sanctioned under PMAY (U)to the State Government of Karnataka was Rs. 8,237.00 crore of which Rs. 2,622 crore has been released. (d) & (e): Under PMAY (U), Government of India does not fix any target for construction of houses in a State/UT. Houses under PMAY (U) are sanctioned for eligible beneficiaries based on the project proposals submitted to the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs with due approval of the State/UT Governments. States/UTs are required to undertake a demand assessment and validate the same to determine the actual requirement of houses to be built under PMAY (U) during the period of “Housing for All by 2022” Mission.                  *****
