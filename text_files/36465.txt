                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3702






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






New Passport Offices




Gajendra Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to open new passport offices in Madhya Pradesh;(b)	if so, the details thereof including the details of the passport offices functioning in the tribal-dominated areas of Madhya Pradesh; and(c)	whether the Government has any proposal to open new passport office in Khargaun Badwani Parliamentary Constituency and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) to (c) 	In order to bring passport services closer to people residing in villages/remote/rural areas of the country, the Ministry, in association with the Department of Posts, decided in January 2017 to open Passport Kendras at the Head Post Offices (HPO)/ Post Offices (PO) in the country called ‘Post Office Passport Seva Kendra’ (POPSK) in each Parliamentary constituency where there is no Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) or POPSK. Accordingly, 18 POPSKs have been opened so far in Madhya Pradesh in addition to 02 PSKs. As far as Kharguaun Parliamentary Constituency is concerned, adequate space is not available in Khargaun Head Post Office for opening of POPSK.***
