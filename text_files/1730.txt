                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1626






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Execution of Kisan Samman Yojana




Bharti Pravin Pawar














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the measures taken by the Government for the execution of Kisan Sanman Yojana at bank level as many banks are not co-operative and not in a positive mode for the execution of the yojana; (b)	whether it is a fact that many banks are not co-operative in giving new loans to farmers in various regions of Maharashtra including Dindori and Nashik; and (c)	 if so, the details thereof along with the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	All nationalised, private sector and cooperative banks  and other financial institutions servicing bank accounts of farmers have been issued instructions by the Government at various levels through the Department of Financial Services (Ministry of Finance), through RBI, NABARD, State/UT Governments, State Level Bankers Committee (SLBC)/District Level Bankers Committee (DLBC), etc. to ensure implementation of the PM-Kisan Scheme, and that the money transferred under the PM-Kisan Scheme actually reaches the eligible beneficiary farmers and no deductions or adjustments are made therefrom against any outstanding loans or any other dues. The Government has regular interactions with the banks so that any roadblocks coming in the way of smooth implementation of the Scheme are removed immediately. (b)&(c):	The Department of Financial Services have informed that the Government fixes agriculture credit disbursement targets for the banking sector every year and banks have consistently surpassed these targets. The details of agriculture credit targets fixed by the Government and the achievement by the banks in respect of State of Maharashtra for the last two years (2017-2018 & 2018-2019), as reported by NABARD, are given as under:  (Rs. in Crore)Year	Target	Achievement2017-18	69650.00	72478.762018-19	81335.00	86809.11*****
