                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1546






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Administrative Contingency under PMAY




Anubhav Mohanty














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware that the administrative contingency relating to the financial years 2017-18 and 2018-19 amounting to Rs. 215.91 crore under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Grameen (PMAY-G) has not yet been received by the State Government of Odisha; (b) 	if so, the time by which the Government is planning to clear the pending amount of Rs. 215.91 crore for the financial years stated above; and(c) 	the reasons for the delay in releasing the amount of Rs. 215.91 crore to the State Government of Odisha?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c): The Framework for Implementation (FFI) of Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin (PMAY-G) provides for release of Administrative fund in two equal instalments to the State Government/UTs. The 1st instalment is released only after the State complies with conditions prescribed during release of previous instalments. Also, to ensure timely utilization and to dis-incentivise parking of such funds, subsequent releases of administrative funds is made only after 60% utilization of total available administrative funds with the State/UT. Further, release of 1st instalment of any financial year is contingent upon release of both instalments of previous financial year.Also, the Administrative fund under PMAY-G is shared between the Centre and State Governments in the ratio 60:40. The Central Assistance of 60% released from budgetary allocation is credited into State’s Consolidated Fund and the State Government thereafter transfers the Central Share along with matching State’s Share of 40% into State’s Nodal Account of PMAY-G. The figure of Rs. 215.91 crore includes both Central Share and State Share for the financial years 2017-18 (2nd instalment) and 2018-19 (both instalments). The details on status of release to Odisha are as follows:Year	                           Status of release2017-18	           An amount of Rs.51.76 crore has been released on 22.01.2020 as 2nd instalment of                    central share against which the State has to provide its matching share of                    Rs.34.51 crore.2018-19	           The total contingency fund as per the target allocated to the State of Odisha is                    Rs.129.64 Crore (Rs.77.78 Crore as central share and Rs.51.86 Crore as State                    share).                   Complete proposal, as per FFI of PMAY-G, has not been submitted by the State Govt.                    as yet for release of 1st instalment of central share of 2018-19.                                             ******
