                                                    840.  SHRI HANUMAN BENIWAL:DR. DHAL SINGH BISEN: Will the Minister of WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state: (a)     	whether the Union Government has set up Nirbhaya Fund to improve the safety and security of women in the country and if so, the details thereof;  (b)     	whether any financial assistance is provided under Nirbhaya Yojana by the Union Government to the respective States for ensuring safety and security of women and children;  (c)     	if so, the details of funds allocated and utilized by the States out of Nirbhaya Fund since inception , State/UT-wise, year-wise;  (d)   	the details of the projects undertaken by the State Governments under Nirbhaya Yojana along with the total expenditure incurred thereon, project-wise, State/ UT-wise including Rajasthan and Madhya Pradesh? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT        (SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (d):  Yes Sir. The Government of India had set up a dedicated fund called ‘Nirbhaya Fund’ for implementation of initiatives aimed at enhancing the safety and security for women in the country. Under Nirbhaya Fund, schemes/ projects worth Rs. 9288.45 Cr. have been appraised till date. An amount of Rs.5712.85 crore has been allocated and an amount of Rs.3544.06 crore has been disbursed/ released by the concerned Ministries/ Departments. An Empowered Committee (EC) of Officers constituted under Nirbhaya Fund appraises and recommends the proposals for funding under Nirbhaya Fund in conjunction with the concerned Ministries/ Departments/ Implementing Agencies. After appraisal by the EC, the concerned Ministry/ Department get / obtain approval of the Competent Financial Authority (CFA) to release the funds out of their respective budget and implement the project /scheme directly or through States/UTs / Implementing Agencies.
