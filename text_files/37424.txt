                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4553






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Rollout of 5G




Manish Tewari














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Cellular Operators Association of India has demanded that the Government fiberize significantly more towers for effective rollout of 5G and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether connection of mobile towers with optical fibers is needed to handle the massive surge in data that is anticipated with the 5G launch and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether currently only 35% of mobile towers in India are fiberized and if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether the Government is planning to rollout 5G by the month of August and if so, the details of the funds earmarked to ensure fiberization of mobile towers by then; (e) 	whether it is a fact that telecom operators have asked for easier payment terms for 5G spectrum and if so, the details thereof; (f) 	whether the Government has taken these demands into consideration and if so, the details thereof; and (g) 	the details of the status of 5G spectrum pricing recommendations expected from TRAI?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) to (d)	 The rollout of 5G mobile services is likely during year 2022-23 by Telecom Service Providers(TSPs). The investment for rolling out of 5G services by the TSPs depend on their techno-commercial considerations. The status of Base Transceiver Station (BTS) fiberized as on 31.12.2021 is given in Annexure.(e) to (g)	A reference has been sent to Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) regarding spectrum auction including regarding reserve price.  Some of the TSPs have represented seeking easier payment terms for 5G spectrum.
