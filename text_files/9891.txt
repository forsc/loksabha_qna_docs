                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3332






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Pre-March 2018 Quota Framework




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of pre-March 2018 quota framework;(b) the percentage of reservation for OBCs being implemented at the direct entry levelfaculty position in Universities;(c) whether it is true that the Law Ministry has recommended to strictly implement 27per cent reservation for OBCs at all direct entry level faculty positions in Universities;and(d) if so, the manner in which the Government is planning to take this issue forwardsince recruitment was stopped since March, 2018?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): 15% and 7.5% reservation was provided to the Scheduled Castes (SCs) andScheduled Tribes (STs) respectively in the direct recruitment of faculty and 27% to theOther Backward Classes (OBCs) in direct entry level faculty positions in Universities.The reservation rosters for the same were prepared by considering a University as a‘Unit’, according to the provisions of the University Grants Commission (UGC)Guidelines of 2006.(c) & (d): The Hon’ble Allahabad High Court in its judgement to W. P. No. 43260 of2016 dated 07.04.2017 directed that the reservation roster should be prepared byconsidering the Department as a Unit instead of University as a ‘Unit’. The CentralEducational Institutions (Reservation in Teachers Cadre) Ordinance, 2019 waspromulgated on 07.03.2019, to restore the University as a Unit, which wassubsequently repealed and the Central Educational Institutions (Reservation inTeachers’ Cadre) Act, 2019 was notified on 09.07.2019. Under the provisions of thisAct, 27% reservation is specified for SEBCs in direct recruitment of faculty.Instructions have already been issued to all Universities vide UGC letter dated04.06.2019 to actively fill all vacancies.*****
