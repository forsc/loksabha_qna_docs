                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4391






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






PMGSY in Kerala




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the length of roads constructed in rural districts of Kerala under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) during the last three years;(b) 	the number of gram panchayats provided access to roads under the PMGSY during the said period;(c) 	the percentage and number of gram panchayats in Kerala which currently lack access to roads;(d) 	the details of the funds allocated for further construction of roads in rural Kerala, if any; and(e) 	the steps taken by the Government to increase accessibility by roads in rural areas, including any timeline set for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) The length of roads constructed in the State of Kerala under the Pradhan Mantri Gram SadakYojana (PMGSY) during the last three years is as follows:-Year	      Road length ( in Km)2017-18	          3732018-19	          3142019-20	          126Total	          813(b) to (c) The unit for connectivity under PMGSY is a habitation and not a Revenue Village or Gram Panchayat. Hence, data is maintained in terms of habitation, and not revenue village or gram panchayats. A total of 404 habitations with the population size 500+ were sanctioned in the State of Kerala for all-weather road connectivity under PMGSY, out of which 402 habitations have already been provided road connectivity and balance 2 habitations are targeted for connectivity by March, 2022. A total of 32 habitations have been provided all-weather road connectivity in the State of Kerala during the last three years as per the year-wise details given below: Year	           No of habitations connected2017-18	                162018-19	                162019-20	                00Total	                32(d)  The allocation of funds to the States for implementation of PMGSY depends, inter-alia, on works in hand, execution capacity of the State and unspent balance of previous year(s) available with the State. The State of Kerala has been released a sum of Rs. 87.00 crore as Central Share during the current financial year 2020-21 for implementation of the scheme in the State.  (e) A total of 3,996 Km road length has been sanctioned to the State under various interventions/verticals of the PMGSY and 3,701 Km has been completed. Under PMGSY-III, Kerala has been allocated a target length of 1,425 Km. The timeline of PMGSY-III is till March, 2025. As on date, 104.56 Km road length with a total project cost of Rs. 82.86 crore has been sanctioned to the State under PMGSY-III. *****
