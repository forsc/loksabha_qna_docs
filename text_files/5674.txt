                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5147






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Updation of Government Websites




Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav













                                                    Krupal Balaji Tumane
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY to be pleased to state:(a)    whether Government has any mechanism for updation and  monitoring of the information  uploaded on the websites of the  Government and if so,   the details thereof;(b)  whether the Government has taken cognizance of the fact  that various Government departments are not updating their  websites with the information regarding latest developments  in  their respective departments:(c)    if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government   thereto; and (d)   whether the Government is taking corrective measures to  provide latest information to the people on these websites and   if so, the details thereof






ANSWER




                                                        a):	Yes, Sir.  Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India has formulated the Guidelines for Indian Government Websites (GIGW) available at https://web.guidelines.gov.in. GIGW have been adopted by Department of Administrative Reforms & Public Grievances (DARPG) and included in Central Secretariat Manual of Office Procedure. Each Government Department is responsible for monitoring the information in their respective websites and ensure that the contents are current and updated as per the ‘Content Review Policy’ to be framed for their respective website as outlined in GIGW guidelines.(b) and (c):   No, Sir. However, Department of Administrative Reforms & Public Grievances (DARPG) reviews the GIGW compliance periodically with Ministries/Departments.(d):	Yes, Sir.  As a part of GlGW guidelines it has been mandated that all Ministries and Departments  nominate a Web Information Manager (WIM). It is the responsibility of Web Information Manager to ensure that the content provided on the website is up-to-date and is reviewed as per the "Content Review Policy”.******
