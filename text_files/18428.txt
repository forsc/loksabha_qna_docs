                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2126






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






COVID Tests Rate




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar













                                                    Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh
                                                








                                                    Pradyut Bordoloi
                                                








                                                    Dean Kuriakose
                                                








                                                    Gaurav Gogoi
                                                








                                                    Anto  Antony
                                                








                                                    Kani K. Navas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government is tracking the testing numbers at the district level;(b) if so, the details of average number of tests conducted daily in the last one month, district-wise;(c) if not, the reasons therefor;(d) whether the rate of tests per 1000 persons at present is commensurate with testing levels  in countries with similar number of active COVID-19 cases;(e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(f) the average number of tests to detect COVID-19 per 1000 persons being undertaken, on average, since February 2020, month-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):	Test conducted for detection of COVID-19 are being reported to the ICMR data portal by the COVID-19 testing laboratories including the details of District where the sample collected and tested.District-wise details of tests conducted in the Month of August 2020 and average test conducted per day are placed at Annexure I.i)	Total Test Conducted by all district in the Month of August: 23966859ii)	Average test conducted by a District in a day: 1053  (d) & (e):	Yes:	WHO recommends 140 tests per day per million population. India’s testing is 875 test per day per million population (as on 19th September, 2020). Presently, the rate of COVID-19 test conducted per 1000 person in India is 0.875 test per day (as on 19th September, 2020).(f):	Month-wise test undertaken are as detailed at Annexure II.
