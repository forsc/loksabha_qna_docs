                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

406






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Expansion and Upgradation of Highways




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to take up the remaining 	one hundred kilometres stretch of National Highway (NH)-16 	from Narasannapeta to Ichapuram in Srikakulam for expansion 	into six lanes;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of the State Highways (SHs) being considered to be 	converted into NHs in the State of Andhra Pradesh;(d)	whether the Government proposes to convert SHs from 	Kalingapatnam-Srikakulam- Parvathipuram to Rayagada, from 	Gara-Alikam-Bathili Road to Gunupur and from Narasannapeta-	Polaki-Nowpada-Pundi to Parlakhemundi in the State of Odisha 	into NH; and(e)	if so, the details and the present status thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)  (a) to (e)        A statement is laid on the Table of the House.
