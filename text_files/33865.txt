                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1509






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2022






Diesel and Petrol Price Hike




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has intervened to stop the price rise of petrol and diesel in the country and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the price of petrol and diesel has not gone up during the last one and half months as per the price of international markets, if so, the reasons therefor; (c) whether the Government has any statistics regarding revenue generated through the sale of petrol and diesel; (d) if so, the details of revenue generation during the last twelve months, month-wise;(e) whether the Government has any statistics regarding consumption of petrol and diesel in the country; and(f) if so, the details of consumption during the last twelve months, month-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b):	Prices of petrol and diesel have been market-determined with effect from 26.06.2010 and 19.10.2014 respectively. Since then, the Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) take appropriate decisions on pricing of petrol and diesel in line with their international product prices, exchange rate, tax structure, inland freight and other cost elements.(c) & (d):	The details of revenue generation during the last twelve months, month-wise are given below:S.No.	Month	Total Excise revenue for Petrol and Diesel (in Rs. Crore)1	January,2021	36,6492	February, 2021	34,0493	March, 2021	37,3074	April, 2021	35,0385	May, 2021	28,7806	June, 2021	30,3627	July, 2021	34,7338	August, 2021	31,8339	September, 2021	30,43910	October, 2021	35,22011	November, 2021	27,04312	December, 2021	27,564		Source: Ministry of Finance.(e) & (f):	The details of consumption of Petrol and Diesel during January, 2021 to December, 2021 are given below:(Quantity in TMT: (P))Products	Jan	Feb	Mar	Apr	May	Jun	Jul	Aug	Sep	Oct	Nov	DecPetrol	2611	2472	2740	2386	1991	2409	2630	2692	2598	2750	2646	2816Diesel	6806	6569	7225	6676	5533	6195	6135	5609	5516	6618	6507	7305        (P):  Provisional,  Source : Petroleum Planning and Analysis Cell (PPAC)x-x-x-x-x
