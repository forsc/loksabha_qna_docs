                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1441






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Construction of ICPs




Raja Amareshwara Naik













                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Khagen Murmu
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                








                                                    Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama
                                                








                                                    Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has sanctioned the construction of 13 Integrated Check Posts (ICPs) in November, 2018;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the timeline of the project;(c) the current status and details of the ICPs being constructed along the border areas along with the facilities to be provided at each ICP, State-wise;(d) the funds sanctioned, released and utilised for the Integrated Check Posts (ICPs) during each of the last three years;(e) whether the Government proposes to take up construction of additional Integrated Check Posts; and(f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b)  On 17th December, 2018, approval was accorded inter-alia for Development  of  03  Integrated  Check  Posts  (ICPs), at Rupaidiha, Sutarkandi --2--L.S.US.Q.NO. 1441 FOR 11.02.2020and Sunauli at an estimated cost of ?847.72 crores by 31/03/2021 alongwith ‘in-principle’ approval for acquisition of land and preparation of detailed Engineering Report (DER) by Land Ports Authority of India (LPAI) for developing ICPs at the remaining 10 locations i.e., at (i) Ghojadanga, West Bengal (Bangladesh border) (ii) Changrabandha, West Bengal (Bangladesh border) (iii) Fulbari, West Bengal (Bangladesh border) (iv) Mahadipur, West Bengal (Bangladesh border) (v) Hili, West Bengal (Bangladesh border) (vi) Jaigaon, West Bengal (Bhutan border) (vii) Panitanki, West Bengal, (Nepal border) (viii) Banbasa, Uttarakhand (Nepal border) (ix) Bhitamore, Bihar (Nepal border) (x) Kawrpuichhuah, Mizoram (Bangladesh border). (c)	The current status and details of the ICPs being constructed along the border areas is annexed. The facilities provided at ICPs include Passenger Terminal with Customs & Immigration facilities, Health & quarantine facilities, Bank/Foreign Exchange Bureau, Public convenience, parking etc while Cargo Terminal has Customs facilities, Weigh Bridge, Driver Rest Area, Parking, Quarantine facilities etc. The ICPs also have CCTV surveillance, Accommodation for security personnel, Public Address System, ATMs etc. (d)	The funds sanctioned, released and utilized for the Integrated Check Posts (ICPs) during each of the last three years is given below;.--3--L.S.US.Q.NO. 1441 FOR 11.02.2020 (Rs in Crores)	Financial Year	Funds sanctioned and released	Funds utilised1.	2016-17	80.00	80.002.	2017-18	90.00	90.003.	2018-19	152.8	105.00(e) and (f) At present there is no proposal under consideration to take up construction of additional Integrated Check Posts.*****
