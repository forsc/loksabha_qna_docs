                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5135






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Promotion of Plywood Industry




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has made any plans to promote the plywood industry and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government has any schemes to offer to help existing industry to nurture the plywood business and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has any plan to reduce the GST on plywood for promoting the business; and (d)	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) :  	Subsequent to the announcement of Industrial policy by Government of India in 1991, most of industries are now delicensed except for four sectors [ Cigars and cigarettes of tobacco and manufactured tobacco substitutes, Electronic aerospace and  defence  equipment, Industrial explosives including detonating fuses, safety fuses, gun powder, nitrocellulose and matches and Hazardous chemicals]. In all other sectors entrepreneurs are free to setup industry in any part of country.       (b):  There are no specific schemes to promote the plywood industry. However, the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade under Ministry of Commerce & Industry has notified North East Industrial Development Scheme (NEIDS), 2017. This Scheme is meant for granting incentives to new industrial units established in North–East States which also includes Plywood Industry. Industrial Development Scheme ( IDS)  also exits for Himalayan States namely Jammu & Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh and Uttrakhand. Under the scheme incentives are provided to all eligible new and existing units and for their substantial expansion located anywhere in Himalayan states.(c)&(d):   Good & Services Tax (GST) on Plywood has already been reduced from 28% to 18% in November, 2017 on the recommendation of GST Council for promoting the industry.*******
