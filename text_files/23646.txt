                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4767






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Integrated Energy Management System




Pritam Gopinath Munde














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has unveiled a plan to save Rs.41,000 crore over ten years through an integrated Energy Management System;(b) if so, the result achieved in this regard;(c)  whether the Railways had planned in the year 2017 to electrify 24,000 km. of rail tracks over the next five years by doubling the annual rate of electrification from 2,000 km. to 4,000 km. in the next two years;  and(d)  if so, the result achieved in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) Yes, Sir. Indian Railways has unveiled a plan (Mission 41 K) to save ? 41,000 crores over ten years.(b) Indian Railways has saved about ? 19981 Crores in Business As Usual (BAU) mode since November, 2015 to December, 2020.(c) Yes, Sir. Indian Railways has embarked upon a major electrification program to electrify its Broad Gauge (BG) network by December, 2023.(d)  Total 16744 RKM has been electrified during last three years and current year upto 28.02.2021 (i.e. 2017-18 to 2020-21 (28.02.2021)).*****
