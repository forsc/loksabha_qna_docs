                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1066






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






National Power Training Institutes




Harish Dwivedi














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of National Power Training Institutes (NPTIs) working in the country, State-wise;(b) 	whether the Government proposes to establish such more institutes in the country; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR POWER, NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR SKILL DEVELOPMENT & ENTREPRENEURSHIP( SHRI  R.K. SINGH) (a) to (c) :	National Power Training Institute (NPTI), with its Corporate Office at Faridabad, Haryana, has eight branches/units working across the country, as per State-wise details given below:-  1.	NPTI (Northern Region), Badarpur, Delhi2.	NPTI (Hydro Power Training Centre), Nangal, Punjab3.	NPTI (Power System Training Institute), Bengaluru, Karnataka4.	NPTI (Hot Line Training Centre), Bengaluru, Karnataka5.	NPTI (Southern Region), Neyveli, Tamil Nadu 6.	NPTI (Eastern Region), Durgapur, West Bengal7.	NPTI (North Eastern Region), Guwahati, Assam8.	NPTI (Western Region), Nagpur, MaharashtraFurther, two new branches/units of NPTI, one at Alappuzha (Kerala) and another at Shivpuri (Madhya Pradesh), have been sanctioned.  ***************
