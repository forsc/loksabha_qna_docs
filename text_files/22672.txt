                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3879






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Coverage under Jal Jeevan Mission (Urban)




Rajiv Ranjan (Lalan) Singh













                                                    Ram Kripal Yadav
                                                








                                                    Dinesh Chandra Yadav
                                                








                                                    Santosh Kumar
                                                








                                                    Kaushalendra Kumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the Urban Local Bodies covered under Jal Jeevan Mission 	(Urban) in Bihar State, district-wise;(b) 	the amount to be given to each of the Urban Local Body; and (c) 	the action plan for liquid waste management in each Urban 	Local Body and the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS(SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a), (b) & (c).	Jal Jeevan Mission (Urban) has been proposed by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs as a Centrally Sponsored Scheme to provide universal coverage of water supply through functional taps to all households in all 4,372statutory towns/cities of the country and universal coverage of household-level sewerage/  septage management in 500 AMRUT (Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation) cities.	State-wise outlays and action plans will be decided after the proposed Mission gets approval of the Government and launched.                                *****
