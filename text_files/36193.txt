                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3524






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






NCRPB




Rajveer (Raju Bhaiya) Singh













                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the National Capital Region PlanningBoard (NCRPB) has completed Regional Plan, 2041;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of the major transport projectsundertaken by the NCRPB during the last five years andproposed to be taken in near future in Uttar Pradesh;(d)	whether the NCRPB has recently received anyroad development proposal from the State Governmentof Uttar Pradesh; and(e)	if so, the details thereof including the funds allocated to the projects in Uttar Pradesh during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                                                    THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                      MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                          (SHRI  KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)&(b):  No Sir. National Capital Region Planning Board (NCRPB) has stated that the draft Regional Plan-2041 has been approved in 41st meeting of NCR Planning Board held on 12.10.2021 and has been published for inviting public objections and suggestions on 09.12.2021 as per Section 12 of NCRPB Act, 1985. (c)to(e):	NCRPB does not undertake/implement any project/scheme but only provides loan based financial support for the projects of the NCR participating States and their implementing agencies. The details of the major transport projects financed during the last five years is annexed. No new proposal has been received from Uttar Pradesh seeking financial support for transport or road projects.                                    *****
