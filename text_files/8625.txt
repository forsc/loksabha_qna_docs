                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2133






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Buildings for Kendriya Vidyalayas in Maharashtra




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether many Kendriya Vidyalayas located in Maharashtra do not have theirown building;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the necessary arrangements are not being made to conduct classesfor higher education due to lack of space there for construction of buildingsand labs; and(d) if so, the time by which the Government proposes to make arrangements ofbuildings for the above schools?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) Out of 59 Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) in the State of Maharashtra, 5 KVsnamely BSF Chakur, Washim, Parbhani, Yavatmal & CRPF Teligaon do not havetheir own buildings.(c) Kendriya Vidyalayas Sangathan has informed that sanctions for statingclasses XI & XII in KVs are considered based on demand and availability of requiredinfrastructure in the concerned Vidyalaya.(d) Construction of permanent buildings for KVs is a continuous process, whichdepends upon identification of suitable land, completion of lease formalities in favourof KVS by the sponsoring authorities, submission of drawings/estimates byconstruction agency, availability of funds and requisite approvals etc. As such, nodefinite time frame can be given in this regard.*****
