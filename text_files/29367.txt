                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

693






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Road Map for Ethanol Blending




Pocha Brahmananda Reddy













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Talari Rangaiah
                                                








                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has announced a road map for ethanol blending in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government is aware of the impact of compatibility with ethanol fuels of on-road vehicles which are not developed to run on 20% ethanol blended fuel;(d)	if so, whether it would cause in increased fuel consumption and higher maintenance costs of vehicles; and(e) whether the Government is taking any steps to address the issue and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS  (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b):A “Roadmap for Ethanol Blending in India 2020-25” prepared by an Expert Committee under the Chairmanship of Additional Secretary, NITI Aayog was released on 5th June 2021. This report lays out a detailed road map for ethanol blending in India and also provides guidance to achieve the target of 20% ethanol blending in petrol by bringing all stakeholders together i.e. Ethanol producers, Oil Companies, Vehicle manufacturers and Consumers.(c) to (e):A joint study by Indian Institute of Petroleum (IIP) Dehradun, Automotive Research Association of India (ARAI) Pune and Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL) R&D on E20 as fuel with existing vehicles was undertaken under the aegis of Department of heavy Industry (DHI) and BIS has issued specification for E20 in 2018. As per the study, it was recommended to adopt E20 fuel in the compatible vehicles labeled for the same.******
