                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4199






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) 	the number of villages where milk producers are being benefitted by Dairy Processing and 	Infrastructure Development Fund (DPIDF), State-wise including Bijnaur Parliamentary 	Constituency;(b)	the amount spent and utilized to double the income of dairy farmers out of the first instalment of 	Rs. 440 crores provided to National Dairy Development Board (NDDB) under DPIDF along 	with the number of farmers benefitted, State/district-wise;(c) 	the details of financial assistance provided or being provided as loan to dairy institutions under 	the scheme, State/district-wise; and (d)	the percentage of interest subsidy being provided by the Government under the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) 	44217 villages are being benefitted under Dairy processing and Infrastructure Development Fund (DIDF). State-wise number of villages’ covered/ being covered under DIDF is at Annexure-I. No proposal has been received from Uttar Pradesh under DIDF; hence, Bijnaur Lok Sabha constituency is not covered under the scheme. (b)	The First Installment of Rs 440 crore as loan received by National Dairy Development Board (NDDB) under DIDF for onward disbursements. State-wise loan disbursed by NDDB to End Borrower (EB) and number of farmers benefitted through DIDF Scheme is given at Annexure I.  (c)	The State-wise loan disbursed to End Borrower (EB) under DIDF as on 22.03.2022 is at Annexure-II. (d)	Government of India is providing interest subvention at the rate of 2.5% p.a.under DIDF scheme ****
