                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

286






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Access to CCTNS




Shashi Tharoor













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Ranjeeta Koli
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Nisith Pramanik
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Jayant Sinha
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has decided to allow private companies to have access to the Crime and Criminals Tracking Network Systems (CCTNS) and other Government databases for conducting background checks;(b)	if so, whether all companies has been allowed to access the CCTNS;(c)	whether the Government will allow this access to those companies who pay for this information;(d)	if so, the details of the payment required and the criteria used to determine the same; and(e)	the safeguards that will be put in place to protect the data of citizens and prevent its misuse in the hands of private entities by the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI AJAY KUMAR MISHRA)(a)	No, Sir.(b) to (e)	Does not arise in view of reply to part (a).*****
