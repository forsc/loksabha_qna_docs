                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3469






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Cooperative Movement




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government will recognize/shortlist few cooperatives in the country that have truly been formidable players in the cooperative movement especially in Karnataka;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government will be having any further expansion within the States and pan India; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):		At present, no such proposal is under consideration. However, Laxmanrao Inamdar National Award for Co-operative Excellence were given on the occasion of “Laxmanrao Inamdar Birth Centenary Celebration and Sahakar Sammelan” held on 21st September, 2017 at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi. A list of Cooperative Societies winning the award (including cooperative society of the State of Karnataka) is at Annexure.(c) & (d):	Cooperative Societies is a State subject under entry 32 of the second list of the seventh schedule of the Constitution. Therefore, except the multi-state cooperative societies, all other cooperative societies are governed by the State Cooperative Societies Acts enacted by the respective States/UTs. The multi-State cooperative societies (MSCS) are governed under the provisions of MSCS Act, 2002 enacted by the Government of India. Further, National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC), a statutory corporation under the administrative control of Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare has launched “Yuva Sahakar-Cooperative Enterprise Support and Innovation Scheme 2019” in October, 2019, which aims at encouraging newly formed cooperative societies with new and / or innovative ideas.
