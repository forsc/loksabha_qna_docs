                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4280






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Four-Laning Project




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details and the present status of four-laning project on National Highway-30 from                                                      Raipur to Jabalpur;(b)	the target fixed for construction of this highway along with the work completed so far; and(c)	whether the Government proposes to make a bypass on Keshkal Ghati, Bastar on this route and if so, the details thereof?  






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)  & (b)  Only one four-laning project on NH-30 from Raipur to Jabalpur namely “4/6 Laning of Raipur-Simga Section of NH-200 (New NH-30) from Km 0+000 to Km 48+580 (Pkg-I) on EPC Mode” having length of 48.58 km for Rs. 513.00 Crores is under implementation with so far completed length of 41.6 km and targeted for completion by June, 2020. (c)	Yes sir, Keshkal bypass of length 11.38 km is a part of the under implementing project work “Rehabilitation and up-gradation of NH-43 from Km.130.00 to 180.000 (Kanker to Bedma) to two lane with paved shoulders under NHDP-IV on EPC basis” in the State of Chhattisgarh.*****
