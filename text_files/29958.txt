                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

148






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Closure of Companies




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has the data on foreign companies that have closed their  industrial operations in the country since 2014;(b) 	if so, the details thereof including the reasons for closure of several foreign industrial operations in the country;(c) 	whether the Government has the data on existing industrial operations of foreign companies in the country and if so, the details thereof, State/ UT-wise;(d) 	whether the Government has the data on industrial operations by foreign  companies that are attracted to ‘Make in India’ scheme and are still operating in the country; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (e):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. *****   STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF THE  LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 148 FOR ANSWER ON 08TH DECEMBER, 2021.(a) & (b): 	There are two types of foreign entities registered in India, (i) Companies who have registered a liaison/branch office/ Project Office in India but incorporated outside India (ii) Subsidiaries of foreign companies Incorporated in India. These subsidiary companies are registered in India under the provisions of Companies Act, 2013. During the period from 2014 to 2021 (till November 2021), 10,756 foreign companies have registered with Liaison/ Branch Office/ Project Office in India and with their subsidiaries.During the period from 2014 to 2021(till November 2021), 2,783 foreign companies registered with Liaison/Branch office/ Project Office and their subsidiaries have closed their operations in India for various reasons such as completion of business objectives/ projects, restructuring by Parent Company, amalgamation, and other management decision. The complete details of the foreign companies with Liaison/Branch Office/Project Office and their Subsidiaries along with reasons of closure as per filings on MCA21 done by these companies may be seen at the following link available on the portal of DPIIT: https://dpiit.gov.in/node/97263/ (c): 	The information with respect to total active foreign companies with Liaison/Branch Office/Project Office and their Subsidiaries, State-wise, may be seen at Annexure I.    The complete details of the Foreign Companies with Liaison/Branch Office/Project Office and their Subsidiaries, State-wise may be seen at the following link available on the portal of DPIIT: https://dpiit.gov.in/node/97263/ (d) & (e):  Initiative wise breakup of foreign companies is not centrally compiled.   ******
