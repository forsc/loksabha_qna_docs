                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

273






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Assistance to Farmers in Odisha




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Government has any plan to support farmers for the losses  suffered during pandemic and flood in Odisha; (b)  if so, the details thereof; and (c) whether the Government will extend support with farm loan waiver or provide seeds and fertilizers to the farmers,  if so, the details of the same?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? 	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (b)  During the COVID-19 pandemic, the agriculture sector had functioned smoothly. Government of India had taken all necessary measures to ensure smooth operation of agriculture related activities. An amount of Rs 500 crore has been released to Government of Odisha from National Disaster Response Fund, in advance, ‘on account basis’ for cyclone ‘Yaas’ during 2021-22. (c) 	Government of India has not announced any farm loan waiver in recent years. Under the component National Seed Reserve (NSR) of Sub-Mission on Seeds & Planting Materials (SMSP), seeds of short and medium duration crops varieties are kept to meet requirement during natural calamities and unforeseen conditions One per cent of the total seed availability at the national level is kept under NSR. Close monitoring of movement and availability of all fertilizers to real time coordination with State Governments/Fertilizer Companies etc. was ensured.  *****
