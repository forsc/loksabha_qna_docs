                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2008






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Light House Projects




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the light houses to be constructed under Light House Project under GHTC-India; (b)	the details of the guidelines for Light House Project;(c)	the details of the minimum amount of funds required for the said project; and the Central Grant-in aid for the said project; and   (d)	whether the said project is proposed to be implemented in Uttar Pradesh and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):	The details of Light House Projects (LHPs) proposed to be constructed under Global Housing Technology Challenge – India(GHTC-India) areat Annexure.(b):	Operational Guidelines of LHPs are available at GHTC-India website:https://ghtc-india.gov.in. (c):	The minimum amount of funds required for construction of LHPs at Rajkot (Gujarat), Chennai (Tamil Nadu), Indore (Madhya Pradesh), Ranchi (Jharkhand), Lucknow (Uttar Pradesh) and Agartala (Tripura) is Rs. 790.56 crore. The Central Grant-in aid for these LHPsis Rs. 360.24 crore.  (d):	Yes, Sir.  In Uttar Pradesh, LHP is proposed to be constructed at Lucknow with total project cost of Rs. 130.90 crore. This project consists of 1,040 number of dwelling units along with necessary infrastructure services.                          *****
