                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3149






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Compensation to Migrant Workers




Mohammad Jawed














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether any monetary compensation has been provided to the families of the migrant workers who lost their lives due to lockdown; and(b)if so, the details thereof including the compensation, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) & (b):	As per the information received from  the States/UTs, no loss of lives of migrant workers are reported from Andaman & Nicobar Islands, Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Chandigarh, Dadra & Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu, Delhi, Goa, Kerala, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, Puducherry, Himachal Pradesh, Punjab & Uttarakhand,  except 17 accidental deaths in Maharashtra. The monetary compensation has also been provided in some States. However, on receipt of complete data from all the remaining States/UTs, the information will be laid on the table of the house. ******
