                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3724






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Allocation of Funds for Institutions




Anil Firojiya














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3724 TO BE ANSWERED ON 17.03.2020ALLOCATION OF FUNDS FOR INSTITUTIONS †3724. SHRI ANIL FIROJIYA: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state  the details of the sectors for the development of which the Ministry has allocated funds along with the institutions to which funds have been allocated for the said purpose, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)The mandate of this Ministry is to implement the schemes of educational, economic and social empowerment of the people belonging to Scheduled Castes (SCs) and Other Backward Classes (OBCs), Senior Citizens, Victims of Drug Abuse, Transgenders, Denotified Nomadic, Semi-Nomadic Tribes (DNTs) and Persons with Disabilities.The State wise details of funds allocated/released  under the schemes implemented by this Ministry for the said purpose are given at Annexure.***** AnnexureAnnexure referred to in reply to the Lok Sabha unstarred Question No. 3724 for 17-03-2020 The details of the institutions to which funds have been released during the last three years.
