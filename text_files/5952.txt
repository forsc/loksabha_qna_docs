                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5464






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






International Flights from Calicut Airport




Ramya  Haridas














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of international flights being operated from Calicut international airport along with their destinations;(b) whether there is any proposal to change its category/level;(c) if so, the reasons therefor;(d) whether the Government is aware of the improvement works undertaken recently in the airport; and(e) if so, the details thereof and the amount spent thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) The details of number of international flights being operated from Calicut international airport along with their destinations is placed at Annexure-I;(b) There is no proposal to change the category/level of Calicut International Airport. ;;(c) Does not  arise. (d) & (e) Yes Sir, the following works has been taken up at Calicut Airport:;;(i) Construction of New International Arrival Block, Internal Modification of Existing International Passengers Terminal building and associated works.;New Arrival Block has been commissioned on 26.03.2019. The total cost of the project was Rs 120.19 crore and work was completed in January, 2019.;;(ii) Resurfacing and strengthening of Runway. ;The total cost of the project was Rs 56.11 crore and work was completed in February, 2017.;
