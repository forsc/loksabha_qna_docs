                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2507






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Social Welfare Hostels




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any scheme for providing financial assistance for construction of Social Welfare Hostels in the States and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the State Government of Jharkhand has requested the Union Government for release of funds for construction of Social Welfare Hostels in the State; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA) (a):. Yes, Sir. There are two Central Sponsored schemes under which financial assistance is provided for construction of Hostels: (i) Babu Jagajivan Ram Chhatrawas Yojna (BJRCY):Under this Scheme, Ministry provides financial assistance across the country for construction/expansion/repair and maintenance of SC boys and girls hostels. The State Governments, Union Territory Administrations and the Central & State Universities/ Institutions are eligible for central assistance for fresh construction of hostel buildings, for expansion of the existing hostel facilities and for periodic repair and maintenance of the hostels constructed under this Scheme. (ii) Construction of Hostels for Other Backward Classes Boys and Girls: The scheme aims at providing hostel facilities to student belonging to socially and educationally backward classes, especially from rural areas to enable them to pursue secondary and higher education.(b) & (c): Under the scheme Babu Jagajivan Ram Chhatrawas Yojna (BJRCY), the State Government of Jharkhand had sent a proposal during the year 2017-18 for release of central assistance  for construction of one SC boys hostel for 250 inmates. However, the proposal being incomplete, the deficiencies have been communicated to the State Government of Jharkhand to rectify the same. As regards scheme for Construction of Hostels for Other Backward Classes Boys and Girls, a list of hostels sanctioned to the State of Jharkhand is enclosed at Annexure.*****
