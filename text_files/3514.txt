                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Discontinuation of ICDS




Durga Prasad Rao Balli














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of funds released under Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) scheme during the last three years, State/UT-wise; and(b)	whether the Government has proposed to discontinue the ICDS scheme and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER
