UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3447






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Archaeological Survey of Ancient Temples




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the State-wise details of archeological survey being conducted by the Government in the country;(b)	whether the Government has formulated any scheme to get the archeological survey of ancient temples, some ancient historical forts and a number of ancient minarets situated in various States of the Country;(c)	if so, the details of archeological survey being conducted in Sitapur and Lakhimpur districts of Uttar Pradesh; and(d)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)  & (b)	Archaeological Survey of India conducts archaeological survey in the country. The details of the village to village survey conducted are at Annexure. The survey of ancient temples is conducted through Temple Survey Projects.  (c)  & (d) 	Archaeological survey and documentation conducted in district Sitapur of Uttar Pradesh in 2019 included Ancient temples of Naimishsaranya and surrounding areas of Chakra Tirth complex, Ancient temple of Lalita Devi, Hanuman Gadhi, Vyas gaddi, Area on the bank of Gomati river, Maharishi Dadhichi birth place, Dadhichi temple complex and pond.
