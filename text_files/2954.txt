                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2553






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Eradication of Animal Diseases




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government is taking steps to eradicate classical swine fever among pigs in the Country and if so, the details thereof;(b)	The steps being taken for the control of zoonotic diseases in economically important livestock and poultry and the funds  allocated during the last five years for the same, State and UT-wise; and (c)	Whether all the parts of the country (including rural and tribal areas) have access to proper veterinary hospitals and dispensaries, if so, the details thereof and the parts that do not have the same, State and UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY & DAIRYING (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	Yes Sir. The Central Government implementsClassical Swine FeverControl Programme (CSFCP) under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme “Livestock Health & Disease Control (LH&DC)” to supplement the efforts of the State/ Union Territory Governments for control & subsequent eradication of Classical Swine Fever especially in the North Eastern states in the country. Under this programme financial assistance is provided to the States/ Union Territory Governments for carrying out mass vaccination of pigs against the disease.(b)	Prevention, control and containment of livestock and poultry diseases including zoonotic diseases are undertaken by the respective State /Union Territory Governments. To supplement the efforts of the state government in this regard, the Central Government is implements a Centrally Sponsored Scheme “Livestock Health & Disease Control (LH&DC)”. Under Brucellosis Control Programme (BCP) and Assistance to States for Control of Animal Diseases (ASCAD), components of LH&DC, financial assistance is provided to the States/Union Territory Governments. The detail of funds released during last five years are annexed as Annexure-I & II.(c)	Veterinary hospitals and dispensaries are set up by respective States/Union Territory Governments. However, in order to supplement the efforts of the States/ Union Territory Governments, the Central Government implementsthe “Establishment & Strengthening of Veterinary Hospitals & Dispensaries (ESVHD)”, a component of the “Livestock Health & Disease Control (LH&DC)” scheme to provide financial assistance for establishment & strengthening of veterinary hospitals & dispensaries. At present, there are 12099 veterinary hospitals and 25263 veterinary dispensaries in the country to provide veterinary services. The State / UT-wisedetails of veterinary hospitals and veterinary dispensaries are annexed as Annexure-III.
