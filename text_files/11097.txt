                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

59






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Inflow of Tourists




Anto  Antony













                                                    Gurjeet Singh Aujla
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any statistics regarding number of foreign and domestic tourists visited various destinations in the country; (b) 	if so, the details of foreign and domestic tourists visited various destinations in the country during the last five years, State-wise along with the revenue generated during the last five years, year-wise; (c) 	whether the Government is aware that the annual footfall of foreign tourist to India has fallen drastically during the last financial year causing serious damage to the tourism industry and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor along with the steps taken by the Government to boost the tourism industry; (d) 	whether the agitations and violence held due to Citizen Amendment Act (CAA) has impacted the tourism industry along with the details of the countries which have imposed ban or issued advisory for its citizens visiting India; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b):	Details of Domestic Tourist Visits (DTV) and Foreign Tourist Visits (FTV) in the States/UTs for the years 2014 to 2018 are given in Annexure-I. Data on tourist visits to destinations and revenue generated from tourism are not centrally maintained in the Ministry of Tourism. However, Foreign Exchange Earned from tourism in terms of rupees crore are estimated to be 120367, 134844, 154146, 177874, 194881 and 210981 for the years 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018 and 2019 respectively.(c):	In alignment with the   practice being followed by United Nations World Tourism Organization (UNWTO), Ministry of Tourism, Government of India maintains data on Foreign Tourist Arrivals (FTA) calendar year-wise. The details of FTA for the last two calendar years are given below:<pre>Year	2018	2019 (Provisional)FTA(in Millions)	10.56	10.89</pre>(d) & (e):	No assessment has been done regarding impact on tourism of agitations held due to Citizen Amendment Act (CAA).The details of some countries imposing ban/ issued advisory to their citizen visiting India is attached at Annexure-II. *******
