                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1084






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Protection from Floods




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken or proposes to take any steps to protect Mallawan, Bilgram, Bharavan and Sandila areas of district Hardoi in Misrikh region of Uttar Pradesh from the flood arising out of the Ganga and Gomti rivers; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA) (a) to (c)  Irrigation and Water Resources Department, Government of Uttar Pradesh has prepared a scheme named “Flood Protection from Harpalpur Sandi Bridge to Mehandighat Bridge by construction of marginal bund on left bank of river Ganga in Mallawan Tehshil, Bilgram District  Hardoi, Uttar Pradesh”. The estimated cost of the scheme is Rs.280.382 crores and the length of the marginal bund is 36.5 kms.  This scheme envisages protecting eight villages of Bilgram Tehsil from floods. As far as river Gomti is concerned, State Government of Uttar Pradesh has informed that at present there is no problem related to erosion of banks of river Gomti in the Bharavan Tehsil, Sandila  area of District Hardoi of Uttar Pradesh.  *****
