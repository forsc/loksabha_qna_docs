                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

218






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Cold Storage Chain Scheme




Sumedhanand Saraswati













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Balak Nath
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the schemes being implemented by the Government for providing cold storage chains in the country;(b)	whether the Government is providing subsidy for setting up cold storage and if so, the details there of especially in Maharashtra and Rajasthan;(c)	the details of the number of cold storages to be set up in Maharashtra and Rajasthan;(d)	whether the Government has set any priority for food processing industries and if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(e)	the present system of cold storages in Maharashtra, Rajasthan and Haryana and whether these are sufficient to meet the requirement; and(f)	if not, the measures being taken to meet the shortage of cold storage?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a) & (b): The Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare (DoAC&FW) is providing assistance under Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) for development of Post Harvest Management (PHM) including establishment of cold storages.  The components of PHM are demand/entrepreneur-driven from among entrepreneurs, private companies, cooperatives, farmers group etc. through commercial ventures for which assistance @35% of admissible project cost in general areas and 50% in hilly and scheduled area is available as credit linked back ended subsidy through respective State Horticulture Missions. (c):	The government does not setup cold storage of its own. The components of PHM including cold storages is demand/entrepreneur-driven for which financial assistance is provided based on the Annual Action Plan, requirement and priorities of respective State Horticulture Missions.   1153 projects have been sanctioned under MIDH from 2014-15 to 2020-21 (up to December 2020) including 30 for Maharashtra and 20 for Rajasthan. (d):       The scheme of MIDH provides assistance for setting up of Food Processing Units in North East and Himalayan States.  The different component schemes under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojna (PMKSY) implemented by MoFPI are meant for supporting food processing sector only. MoFPI has also recently launched the scheme “Pradhan Mantri Formalization of Micro-Food Processing Enterprises.(e) & (f):     DoAC&FW has not conducted any survey for requirement of cold storage capacity in recent years.
