                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3558






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Inclusion of Jogulamba Town under Heritage Circuit




Ramulu Pothuganti














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme announced in January, 2015, many tourist circuits have been identified and became operational across the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise along with the funds allocated in this regard; (c) 	whether the Government is considering to include Jogulamba Town under the Heritage Circuit/Tourist Circuit; and (d) 	if so, the funds allocated for the State of Telangana under the Tourist Circuits?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	Ministry of Tourism under its schemes viz. Swadesh Darshan- integrated development of theme based tourist circuits in the country, PRASHAD- National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive and Assistance to Central Agencies provides financial assistance to State Governments/UT Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure and facilities in the country . Under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme fifteen thematic circuits have been identified for development namely; North-East Circuit, Buddhist Circuit, Himalayan Circuit, Coastal Circuit, Krishna Circuit, Desert Circuit, Tribal Circuit, Eco Circuit, Wildlife Circuit, Rural Circuit, Spiritual Circuit, Ramayana Circuit, Heritage Circuit, Sufi Circuit, and Tirthankara Circuit. The details of the projects approved and funds sanctioned state wise including Telangana under the scheme is attached in the annexure.The State government has sent a proposal for development of  "The Jogulamba Temple  in Alampur" Telangana under the PRASHAD scheme of the Ministry .The Concept presentation meeting was held on 22.08.2019 were in principle approval has been granted by the Ministry.*********
