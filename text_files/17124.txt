                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1043






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Tiger project in Sohagibarwa




Pankaj Chowdhary














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has launched or proposes to launch Tiger Project in Sohagibarwa reserved forest area of Maharajganj district of Uttar Pradesh;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and;(c) 	the time by which this work is likely to be commenced?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) No Sir.(b) & (c) Question does not arise.*****
