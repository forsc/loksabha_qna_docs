                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

455






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Maoist Violence




Ravneet Singh













                                                     Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the Government has recently held a meeting with the Chief Ministers of Maoist-hit States in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the details of the total number of incidents and deaths in the country due to Maoist related violence during the last three years, State/UT-wise;(d) whether the Government has formulated any comprehensive strategy to completely eradicate Maoist-related violence in the country; and(e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a & b) Yes, Sir. A meeting was held with Chief Ministers/ Ministers and Senior Officers of 10 Left Wing Affected States on 26.09.2021 to review the current scenario of LWE in the Country, security initiatives and development measures under taken to address the left wing extremism.--2--LS.US.Q.NO. 455 FOR 30.11.2021(c) The number of LWE related violence incidents and resultant deaths during last 03 years are given in Annexure-1(d & e) As per Seventh Schedule of the Constitution of India, subjects of Police and Public Order are with the State Governments. However, to address the LWE menace holistically, a National Policy and Action Plan was formulated in 2015 to address Left Wing Extremism (LWE). It envisages a multi-pronged strategy involving security related measures, development interventions, ensuring rights and entitlements of local communities etc.On security front, the Central Government assists the LWE affected States by providing Central Armed Police Force battalions, training, funds for modernization of State police forces, equipment & arms, sharing of intelligence, construction of Fortified Police Stations etc;On development side, the Central Government has taken various measures particularly for expansion of road network, improving the mobile connectivity, improving network of banks, post offices, skill development and education facilities in the LWE areas.--3--LS.US.Q.NO. 455 FOR 30.11.2021The GoI provides funds for capacity building of the LWE affected States, under various schemes like Security Related Expenditure (SRE) Scheme, Special Infrastructure Scheme (SIS), to fight the LWE menace effectively. Funds are also provided to the States under ‘Special Central Assistance (SCA)’ to fill the critical gaps in public infrastructure and services.
