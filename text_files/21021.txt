                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2202






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Van Dhan Kendres in Chhattisgarh




Santosh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a)   the details of the proposals approved by the Government till date to set up Van-Dhan-Kendras and the funds of allocated district-wise to set up these Kendras in Chhattisgarh State;(b)  the number of Van-Dhn vikas samuh constituted district-wise in Chhattisgarh State and the number of members on self help groups constituted under this scheme; and(c)  whether the Government is formulating scheme to identify the best performing kendras and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b) A total of 1770 Van Dhan Vikas Kendras (VDVKs) has so far been sanctioned in 22 States & 1 Union Territory for promotion of tribal entrepreneurship and livelihood generations wherein a total of about 5.27 Lakhs beneficiaries have been associated. This includes 139 VDVKs sanctioned in Chhattisgarh State.
