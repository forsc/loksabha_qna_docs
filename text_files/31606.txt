                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3018






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Urban Development Schemes




Anurag Sharma













                                                    Geeta Kora
                                                








                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Khan Saumitra
                                                








                                                    Jugal Kishore Sharma
                                                








                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the number of urban development schemes being implemented in the country including State of Uttar Pradesh especially in Jhansi and Lalitpur, Tripura, Jharkhand, Jammu and Kashmir and the current status of these schemes;(b)  	the funds allocated and utilized for urban development schemes during each of the last three years and the current year, State/Scheme-wise;(c)	the challenges faced by the Government while implementing the said urban development schemes;(d)	the steps taken by the Government to achieve the targets set under various urban development schemes;(e)	the details of funds allocated and utilised for urban development schemes during each of the last three years and the current year, scheme-wise;(f)	whether the Government is aware that major portion of the funds allocated to the States for urban development remains unutilised and if so, the details thereof; and(g)	whether the Government has fixed any time-line to complete these schemes and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                   THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                 MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                    (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)&(b) Urban Development is a State subject. The Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs assists the States/ Union Territories (UTs) including Uttar Pradesh, Tripura, Jharkhand and Jammu and Kashmir in development of urban infrastructure through its Missions - Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation 2.0 (AMRUT 2.0), Smart Cities Mission, Swachh Bharat Mission – Urban 2.0 (SBM-U 2.0) and Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY-U). The Central Assistance (CA) allocated and utilised by these schemes during the last 3 years and the current year State-wise are given in Annexure-I.  Status in Jhansi and Lalitpur, Tripura, Jharkhand and Jammu and Kashmir is given in Annexure-II.(c) to(g)	Details of funds allocated and utilized are given in Annexures I. The schemes will be implemented within the time frame set under the respective Missions - AMRUT by March 2023, SCM by June 2023 and PMAY-U by 2022. Swachh Bharat Mission – Urban 2.0 has been launched on  1st October, 2021 and committed liabilities for ongoing projects taken up under Swachh Bharat Mission – Urban (launched   in  the   year  2014)    continue  till   31.03.2023. The progress of the Missions / schemes is continuously monitored at the Central and State levels through meetings, video conferencing, field visits, institutional mechanisms comprising of Management Information System (MIS) and third party evaluation. Under PMAY-U, alternative and innovative technologies have been promoted under the scheme for rapid delivery of houses. Also an award mechanism with 150 days challenge has been introduced to boost the competition among states/UTs/Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) for completion of houses.                                      ****
