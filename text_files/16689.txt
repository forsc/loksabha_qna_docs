                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

447






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Relief Package for Differently Abled




Saikia Dilip













                                                    Ramesh Chander Kaushik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   Whether the Government is considering to give more relief package along with the existing concessions being given to the persons with disabilities for self employment in view of the global pandemic; (b) if so the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has conducted any survey or constituted task group for addressing  the economic status of the persons with disabilities; and(d) if so the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR)(a)  and (b):  Yes, Sir.  National Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation (NHFDC), a Public Sector Enterprise under the administrative control of Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD), Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment has taken following measures owing to the pandemic situation arising due to Covid 19:i)	It has extended moratorium of three months on payment of loan installments outstanding with implementing agencies, as per RBI guidelines.ii)	Also extended the Utilization period by another 120 days in respect of funds released to implementing agencies during the last quarter of last financial year 2019-20.(c) and (d): DEPwD has not conducted any survey or	constituted	task	group	for addressing the economic status of the persons with disabilities.****
