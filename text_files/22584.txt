                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

322






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Bharatmala Pariyojana




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the current status of Bharatmala Pariyojana at Kozhikode connecting Beypore port with National Highway (NH)-66 and NH-766;(b)	whether the Government proposes to take steps for connecting Beypore port at Kozhikode with inter-State NHs expeditiously and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has conducted any review meetings so far for this project at Kozhikode and if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	if not, whether the Government proposes to organise such meetings in near future?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (d)	Yes, Sir. A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) to (d) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *322 TO BE ANSWERED ON 18TH MARCH 2021 RAISED BY SHRI M.K.RAGHAVAN REGARDING ‘BHARATMALA PARIYOJANA’(a) & (b)  As on March 2021, there are two road projects that focus on connectivity to Beypore Port under Bharatmala and Sagarmala Scheme in the State of Kerala connecting junction of NH-66 and NH-766 at Malaparamab, Kozhikode. The details of both projects alongwith their status are provided below: <pre>Sl.No	Project Name	Implementing Agency	Project Status1	Beypore port connectivity to Malaparamab via beach	NHAI	The work for preparation of DPR for above mentioned roads has been awarded. The details of the fund requirement can be ascertained after completion of DPR. 2	Development of river side port road to Beypore port	NHAI	                                                                       </pre>c and d) Yes, the port connectivity projects are part of Bharatmala and Sagarmala schemes and the projects are regularly reviewed.                                             *******
