                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3500






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Funds Allocated to Bhagalpur for Fisheries




Ajay Kumar Mandal














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the details of the funds allocated to the Parliamentary Constituency of Bhagalpur for fisheries during the last three years along with the amount utilized; (b) 	the details of the facilities provided to the fishermen under various schemes for fisheries; and (c) 	the details of the progress made in generating employment opportunities and in fisheries production in the said Parliamentary Constituency during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) and (b): The Government of Bihar has reported that a sum of Rs.4.48 crore has been allocated for the development of fisheries in Parliamentary Constituency of Bhagalpur during the last three years, out of which, an amount of Rs. 4.10 crore has been utilized. The facilities provided to fishermen of the said constituency, as reported by Government of Bihar  include pond construction, training, fish hatchery, seed stocking, tubewell and pumpset, matsya vipnan yojana, fish inputs, renovation, fish-cum-poultry culture, biofloc and machua awas.(c): The Government of Bihar has reported that a total of 732 fishers were provided direct employment opportunities in the Parliamentary Constituency of Bhagalpur and 42,330 tonne of fish produced during the last three years in the said Parliamentary Constituency. ******
