                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1975






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Digital Calendar




Pocha Brahmananda Reddy













                                                     Chinta Anuradha
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has launched a mobile app for the digital calendar and diary of the Government of India;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the app will have information of the Government’s schemes, programs and events; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES {SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR}(a) and (b): Government has launched a digital Calendar and Diary app which is available for download on both Google Play Store and iOS Play Store with the name “GoI Calendar”. The digital calendar and diary can also be accessed on the website https://www.goicalendar.gov.in.(c) and (d): Information of Government’s schemes and programs can be accessed under the tab of “List of Important Dates” which has a list of 53 schemes and programs along with their launch dates. Further, each month in the Calendar App is dedicated with a Mission having information regarding various Government’s schemes and programs.*******
