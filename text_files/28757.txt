                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

86






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Funds for Promotion of Ayurveda




Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil













                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the details of the funds allocated to the States by the Government for various schemes aimed at promoting Ayurveda system of medicine during the last three years, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra; b)	the details of amount spent and amount not utilised by the States and Union Territories during the said period;c)	the progress in promotion and development of AYUSH drugs through clusters approach in the country; d)	the funds allocated and utilized under the cluster scheme during the last three years, State/UT-wise; and e)	the details regarding implementation of the said scheme in Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (e) A statement is laid on the table of the House. Statement referred in reply of Lok Sabha Starred Question No. *86 to be answered on 03.12.2021(a)	& (b) Ministry of AYUSH is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) through State/UT Governments and providing financial assistance to them for development and promotion of AYUSH system of medicine including Ayurveda as per the proposals received in their State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs). Under NAM funds are being released to State/UT Governments against the approved SAAPs. The status of funds allocated/spent and unspent during the last three years, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra is furnished at Annexure.(c)	Ministry of AYUSH (erstwhile Department of AYUSH) had implemented a Central Sector Scheme for AYUSH Clusters up to 12th Plan period (2012-17) for setting up of Industry Clusters. The Government had sanctioned funds to ten AYUSH Industry Clusters under the said scheme. Later, the Scheme was discontinued w.e.f. 01.04.2017. In six cases funds have been utilized and envisaged deliverables achieved. In other four cases, the deliverables were not achieved and the Ministry has taken legal recourse in the matter.(d)	Funds were allocated and utilized to complete on-going projects under Central Sector Scheme for Development of AYUSH Clusters during last three years as detailed below:Year	Name of the Industry Cluster/ Organisation	State/UT	Fund Released(Rs. in Lakhs)	Details of Utilization Received from the Organisation2018-19	M/s Sanskar Ayush Medicare Pvt.
