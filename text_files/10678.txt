                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4138






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Assistance to National Sports Federations




Locket Chatterjee














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry provides assistance to recognized National Sports Federations (NSFs) under the scheme of Assistance to National Sports Federations (NSFs);(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details of assistance provided by the Ministry to NSFs during the last year and the current year?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) & (b) Yes Sir. Under the Scheme of Assistance to National Sports Federations funds are provided for conduct of national championships, international tournaments in India, participationin international tournaments abroad, coaching camps, purchase of equipments, hiring of foreign coaches, etc.(c) The details of assistance provided by the Ministry to the NSFs during the last year and the current year (upto 19.11.2019) are given in Annexure.*****
