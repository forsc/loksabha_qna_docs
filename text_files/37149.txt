                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4240






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Crisis in Fishing Sector




Talari Rangaiah














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING ??????????, ??????? ?? ????? ?????? be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has noticed crisis in fishing sector; and (b) if so, the details of the steps taken by Government to save the fishing sector along with the break-up of the Government assistance, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING		(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) and (b) While as such, there is no crisis noticed in fishing sector, the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying, Government of India in May, 2020 has approved a new flagship scheme ‘Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana’ (PMMSY) with highest investment of Rs. 20,050 crore, for implementation over a period of five years from 2020-21 to 2024-25 in all States and Union Territories as part of AtmNirbhar Bharat Package in order to support the fisheries sector from the impact of Covid-19 pandemic. The State-wise details of the proposals approved under the PMMSY is provided at Annexure.  *****
