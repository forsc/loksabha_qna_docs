                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1855






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Highway Projects in West Bengal




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)      the details of highway projects undertaken by the Government in the districts of Darjeeling, Kalimpong and North Dinajpur during the last three years and the current year;  (b)      whether the Government has conducted review regarding the damages on National Highway (NH)-10 due to extreme weather during the last monsoon season; (c)      if so, the details of such damages and if not, the reasons therefor; and (d)      the steps taken by the Government for development/ expansion of NH-10 and other alternate highway projects in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)     20 highway projects amounting to Rs. 2732 crore have been undertaken by the Government in the districts of Darjeeling, Kalimpong and North Dinajpur during the last three years and the current year. The details of the projects are at Annexure-I.(b) & (c)     Yes, sir. Damages occurred to National Highways (NH-10) mainly due to land slides at km 3.2, km 6.60, km 11.20, km 14.60, km 22.40, km 23.50, km 24.90, km 26.60 and km 29.20 which have since been restored and opened to traffic.(d)     DPR preparation for permanent stabilization of 20 vulnerable locations on NH-10 in the state of West Bengal has been undertaken. Besides, 8 nos. of works costing Rs. 2408 crore are under implementation on NH-717A, which is alternate to the NH-10 in the state of West Bengal & Sikkim.
