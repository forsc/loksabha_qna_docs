                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4944






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






PPP in Rural Housing




Rebati Tripura













                                                    Anurag Sharma
                                                








                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Santosh Kumar
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to built houses in rural areas under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY-G)in bulk quantity through Public Private Partnership (PPP) model to ensure quick and Quality delivery of the houses under this scheme in the near future;(b)	if so, the details thereof, especially in North East, Uttar Pradesh including Jhansi, Delhi/NCR and other areas;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	whether there is any other scheme of the Ministry where PPP model will be introduced?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	As per the Framework for Implementation of Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana (PMAY-G), the house shall be constructed by the beneficiary himself/herself or get the house constructed under his/her supervision. No contractor shall be engaged by the State for construction of houses. In case the beneficiary is old/infirm or a person with disability and is therefore  not in a position to get the house constructed on his/her own, such houses shall be taken up as a part of the Mason Training Program. In case there are still some beneficiaries  left out, the State Government will ensure that they are assisted through the Gram Panchayats or a ground functionary to get their house constructed.(d):	Under Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY), a placement linked skill development program for wage employment, is being implemented in PPP Model all over the country through Project Implementing Agencies (PIAs) registered with this Ministry.  *****
