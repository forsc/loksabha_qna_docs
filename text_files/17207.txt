                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1062






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Medical College in Jharkhand




Annpurna Devi














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether medical college is being setup by the Government in Koderma district in Jharkhand;(b) if so, the current status of its construction; and(c) the deadline fixed for its completion?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c) A medical college has been approved at Koderma in Jharkhand under Phase-II of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme ‘Establishment of new Medical Colleges attached with existing district/referral hospitals at an approved cost of Rs.250 crore to be shared between the Centre and State Government in the ratio of 60:40. Out of Central Share of Rs.150 crore, Rs.127.59 crore has been released to the State Government of Jharkhand till date. The State Government is the implementing agency for the project. As per information provided by the State Government of Jharkhand, the construction of medical college building has started and timeline of completion is January, 2022.
