                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

286






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Advisory for Tourist in J&K




A. Ganeshamurthi














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that the Union Government has recently issued advisoryfor tourists in Jammu and Kashmir;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the total number of tourists who visited Jammu and Kashmir during the last 6 months, including foreign tourists; and(d) the income earned through tourism during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	 & (b):   The Union Government has not issued advisory for tourists in Jammu and Kashmir recently.(c)  &  (d):     The Government of Jammu & Kashmir has informed that a total of  34,10,219 tourists, including 12,934 foreign tourists, visited Jammu & Kashmir during last 6 months and an income of Rs. 25.12 crore has been earned through tourism during this period.******
