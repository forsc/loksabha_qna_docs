                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2132






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Creation of Sports Infrastructure in Jharkhand




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any measures to create basic sports infrastructure in Jharkhand;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the funds allocated, released and utilised by the Government for this purpose and the details of sports infrastructure created during the last three years?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) to (c) ‘Sports’ being a State subject the responsibility of creating sports infrastructure rests with the State / Union Territory (UT) Government. Central Government supplements efforts of the State / UT Governments for bridging gaps in sports infrastructure. This Ministry released Rs. 2.07 crore under Khelo India scheme to Sports Authority of India (SAI) during last three years for up-gradation of Hockey and Football Ground at SAI Training Centre, Hazaribagh in the State of Jharkhand and the entire amount has been utilized.*******
