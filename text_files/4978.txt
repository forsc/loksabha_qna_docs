                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4578






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Seats in Higher Educational Institutions




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware about extremely high competition for seats in CentralGovernment Higher Educational Institutions in Delhi;(b) whether the Government plans to open new Universities/Colleges/Higher Learning Institutions inDelhi and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has increased the seats in Higher Educational Institutions in Delhi, if so, thedetails thereof; and(d) the steps taken/being taken to ensure accessibility of higher education in Delhi?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) Yes Sir.(b) No Sir(c) Details are attached.(d) In order to increase access to higher education without the loss of quality, the Open and DistanceLearning Regulation have been issued by UGC which ensures quality education through distance modefor students desirous of studying at a Delhi based university. For the year 2018-19, the admission takenin IGNOU has shown an increasing trend as follows:-<pre>2017-18 Fresh 5740732018-19 Fresh 673696Growth – 99623, 17.35%</pre>This has increased the accessibility of higher education in Delhi.***2Detail of seats (intake) for institution in Delhi under MHRD are as under:-<pre>Institutions IntakeIntake18-19 17-18Delhi University 75618 75367Jawaharlal Nehru University 1959 1959Jamia Millia 10036 9359IIT Delhi 760 760NIT Delhi 226 200</pre>***
