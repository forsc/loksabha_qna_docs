                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4393






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Non-Technical Vacancies




Feroze Varun Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of vacancies in Non- Technical Popular Categories (NTPC);(b) 	the total number of candidates shortlisted in these categories; (c)	whether the Government is aware of  the protests by railway job aspirants against the recruitment rules; and(d) 	whether the Government has amended the rules and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a): 	35281 vacancies have been notified/modified in Non- Technical Popular Categories (NTPC) through Centralized Employment Notification (CEN) No. 01/2019.(b):	705446 Roll Numbers were shortlisted in these categories for 2nd Stage Computer Based Test (CBT).(c) & (d):	Candidates who appeared for 1St Stage CBT have raised some concerns after the results were announced and not against the recruitment rules. A Committee has been constituted to redress the concerns raised by candidates. The Committee has given its report which has been accepted. A notice containing decisions taken by the Committee and schedule for declaring revised result for all Pay Levels & of 2nd Stage CBT has been uploaded on official websites of Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) on 10.03.2022.*****
