                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1262






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Cold Storage in Maharashtra




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of applications received from Palghar district, Maharashtra to set up cold storage; and (b)	the number of applications which have been approved along with the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	As per report received from Government of Maharashtra, no application was received from Palghar District, Maharashtra up to the year 2021 to set up cold storage. However, for the year 2021-2022, 9 applications have been received on MAHADBT Portal from Palghar District, Maharashtra.(b):	No application has been approved by Government of Maharashtra to set up cold storage in Palghar District, Maharashtra.*****
