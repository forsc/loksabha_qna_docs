                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1934






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Dog Squads




Kaushal Kishore













                                                    Upendra Singh Rawat
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether dog squads have been deployed in Defence forces;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the number of such squads deployed as on date; and (c) the amount spent in training, food and other expenses like medicines on the squads?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) & (b):  Indian Army has Army Dog Units deployed in various operational areas/other locations under the various Regional Commands.  	Presently, there are 25 Army Dog Units (Full) and 02 Army Dog Units (Half).  The Army Dog Unit (Full) is authorised 24 Army Dogs and Army Dog Unit (Half) is authorised 12 Army Dogs.(c)  The total amount spent on training, food, and other expenses like medicines (excluding manpower) on the dog squads of Indian Army during 2018-19 is INR 1,24,11,450/- (Rupees One Crore Twenty Four Lakh Eleven Thousand Four Hundred Fifty only).*******
