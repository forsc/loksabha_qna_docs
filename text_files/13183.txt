                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2044






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






PM Kisan Scheme for Sericulturists




Mohammad Jawed














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the farmers engaged in sericulture have been covered under PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana in the country, if so, the details of the beneficiaries, State-wise; (b) 	whether any scheme has been formulated by the Union Government for upliftment of farmers engaged in sericulture in Bihar; (c) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (d) 	the amount of funds allocated to Bihar for sericulture during the last three years along with the amount utilized in this regard; (e)	 the details of the facilities provided to the farmers under the scheme related to sericulture; and (f) 	whether the Government has proposed to set up nodal board for the development of sericulture in Bihar and if so, the details of the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	 With a view to provide income support to all landholding farmers’ families in the country, having cultivable land, the Central Government has implemented a central sector scheme namely ‘Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)’ Scheme w.e.f. 01.12.2018. The scheme aims to supplement the financial needs of all landholding farmers’ families in procuring various inputs to ensure proper crop health and appropriate yields, commensurate with the anticipated farm income as well as for domestic needs. Under the scheme, an amount of Rs. 6000/- per year is released by the Central Government online directly into the bank of the eligible farmers under Direct Benefit Transfer mode, subject to certain exclusions. The responsibility of identification of eligible beneficiaries and uploading their correct details on the web-portal www.pmkisan.gov.in rests with the State / UT Governments. The financial benefits are released to the beneficiaries on the basis of the data of farmers received from States/UTs and their validation through Aadhar/PFMS. The details of the beneficiaries, State-wise, is at Annexure.Farmers engaged in the sericulture who are otherwise  eligible under PM-Kisan Scheme could get benefits under the scheme.(b) To (f):	 Ministry of Textiles thorough its Silk Board has launched a Central Sector Scheme namely “Silk Samara” for overall development of silk industry in the country including Bihar. Under this Scheme various beneficiary oriented components have been taken up for the welfare of the farmers and other stakeholders. Assistance under this scheme has been provided to the silkworm seed producers, cocoon producers, silk reelers and weavers covering activities. The details of funds released and utilized under Silk Samagra in Bihar are as under:(Rs. In lakh)2016-17	2017-18Funds released 	UC received 	Funds released 	UC received 370.61	155.64	301.33	86.21The Regional office, Central Silk Board, Kolkata is coordinating development of sericulture activities in Bihar through Directorate of Sericulture, Government of Bihar.
