                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2658






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






Scientific Social Responsibility




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has allocated funds to the Scientific Social Responsibility policy and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	the manner in which the Scientific Social Responsibility policy will be implemented and monitored; (c) 	whether the scheme has specific outreach programmes for the marginalized communities; and (d)	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE; MINISTER OF SCIENCE ANDTECHNOLOGY; AND MINISTER OF EARTH SCIENCES(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)????????? ?? ?????? ?????? ??????, ??????? ?? ???????????? ?????? ?? ?????? ??????? ????????. ???? ?????(a) to (d):   The Scientific Social Responsibility (SSR) Policy is in draft stage. The draft SSR Policy envisages utilisation of institutional resources with a mechanism of implementation and assessment and having an outreach towards all sections of the society, including the marginalised communities. *********
