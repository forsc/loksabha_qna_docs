                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

164






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Jagdishpur-Haldia Gas Pipeline Project




Locket Chatterjee














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the status of Jagdishpur-Haldia Gas pipeline project;(b) the tentative timeline for supply of CNG for the city of Kolkata; and(c) the plans and timeline for supplying piped cooking gas in Kolkata?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB) has authorized 2,655 Km longJagdishpur-Haldia-Bokaro-Dhamra Natural Gas Pipeline (JHBDPL) to GAIL (India) Limited toconnect eastern parts of the country. About 1,986 Km of pipeline has been laid as on 31.03.2021.(b) & (c) Bengal Gas Company Limited (BGCL) has been authorized for the development ofCity Gas Distribution (CGD) network in the Geographical Area (GA) of Kolkata MunicipalCorporation and parts adjoining districts of North 24 Parganas, South 24 Parganas, Howrah,Hooghly and Nadia. Supply of Natural Gas to the GA has started. Development of the CNGstations /PNG connections in a GA is done as per the terms and conditions of the authorization.******
