                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

584






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Proposal of Ban on Right Wing Organisation




Asaduddin Owaisi














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether as per the charge sheet filed by ATS of Maharashtra State Police, some right wing outfit like Sanathan Sanstha, Hindu Jangagurti and some other smaller outfit hatched conspiracy to undermine security and sovereignty of the country;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government proposes to ban such outfits in the interest of the nation to safeguard the unity and integrity of the country; and(d) if so, the steps taken or being taken in this regard?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):	 The subject refers to ATS of Maharashtra Police.  Since, “Public order” and “Police” are State subjects as per the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution, the above matter pertains to the Government of Maharashtra;(c):	No such proposal from the State or agencies has been received by the Central Government;(d):	Question does not arise.*****
