                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

415






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Remunerative Price




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya













                                                    Shantanu Thakur
                                                








                                                    Pradeep Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware that corn is not being exported despite its good production in Bihar and other States and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the farmers are not getting remunerative price for their produce and if so, the details of the same; (c) 	whether the Government is taking or proposes to take any concrete steps in this regard; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a): 	Corn to the extent of 7.07 lakh MT in 2017-18 and 10.31 lakh MT in 2018-19 has been exported. (b):	The Minimum Support Price (MSP) of corn (maize) for Crop Year 2019-20 has been fixed at Rs. 1760 per quintal and the average wholesale price of corn in major markets of the country is higher than the MSP.(c)  & (d):	To safeguard the interest of corn farmers, the Government announces Minimum Support Price of maize every year so that the farmers can get remunerative price of their produce. Further, export of corn/maize is free without any restriction.*****
