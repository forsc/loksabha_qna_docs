                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2543






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Online Investigation Tracking System




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has implemented Online Investigation Tracking System for Sexual Offenses provided to law enforcement agencies to track policeinvestigations;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the number of forensic laboratories in the country, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI AJAY KUMAR MISHRA)(a) and (b) Yes, Sir. The Ministry has launched an online analytical tool “Investigation Tracking System for Sexual Offenses” for States/ Union Territories to monitor and track time-bound investigation in sexual assault cases in accordance with Criminal Law (Amendment) Act, 2018. (c) Details on state wise number of Forensic Science Laboratories and Mobile Forensic Units for aiding investigation in such matters are given in Annexure. Apart from these, 7 Central Forensic Science Laboratories are located at Delhi, Pune, Bhopal, Guwahati, Hyderabad, Chandigarh and Kolkata.
