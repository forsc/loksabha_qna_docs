                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3897






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Singareni Coal Field




Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the steps being taken by the Government to extract coal in future from Singareni coal fields in Telangana State;(b)	whether the Government has reviewed demand and supply of coal for various requirements in Telangana and its export to generate revenue and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has taken steps to protect the lives of coal employees in Singareni Coal fields while working in tunnels and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the number of workers died and compensation paid to their families due to mishap in coal fields during the last three years and the current year?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES  (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a): In order to extract coal in future, Singareni Coal companies Limited (SCCL) has planned to open 12 additional coal mines (11 opencast mines + 1 underground Mine) in the next five years in Telangana State.(b): SCCL demand and supply of coal to Telangana State for the FY 2019-20 (up to 10.7.2019) is given below: tonne) (Quantity	in	lakhProgressive Sector	Annual Demand (FSA/MOU)	Progressive demand Supply Power	316.40	87.55	84.51Non Power	94.00	23.5	17.63Total	410.40	111.05	102.14At present, there is no plan to export coal out of the country from Telangana.(c): SCCL is taking the following steps to protect the lives of the employees while working in tunnels:I. Before starting the drivage of the tunnels, the rock types of the area/locality is thoroughly studied by the Exploration department and a report is prepared detailing the method to be followed for drivage of tunnels. A plan is also prepared for the same.II. Roof and sides are well supported with permanent structures like iron girders, concrete lining etc.III.	Good ventilation is provided by efficiently operated auxiliary fans.IV.	While the tunnels drivage is in progress suitable gradient is maintained for smooth operation of machines such as LHDs/SDLs etc.V.	Controlled blasting techniques are adopted to minimize the effect on roof and sides.VI.	Exclusive supervisory staff is engaged for tunneling works for ensuring the safety of the workers.(d): Number of workers died and compensation paid in SCCL mines during the last three years is furnished below:Sl. no.  year  No  of  workers  died  in  coal Amount	of	compensation	paid	to	their mines families (Rs. lakh) 1	2016	12	143.012	2017	12	124.823	2018	07	93.044	2019*	05	59.63*UP TO JUNE 2019*****
