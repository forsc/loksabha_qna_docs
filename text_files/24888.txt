                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

828






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






National Policy on Alzheimer‘s Disease




Feroze Varun Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has planned to bring a national policy on Alzheimer‘s disease in the country, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether any geriatric care units have been set up by the Government to treat Alzheimer‘s patients; and(c) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a):  No.(b) & (c): Geriatric healthcare services are being provided under National Programme for Health Care of Elderly (NPHCE) through District Hospitals, Community Health Centres, Primary Health Centres, Sub-Centre/Health & Wellness Centres under the umbrella of National Health Mission (NHM) throughout the Country.  Tertiary level care services, including for Alzheimer’s, are also being provided through Regional Geriatric Centres in selected medical colleges/institutes.  Two National Centres for Ageing (Centres of Excellence) each 200 bedded, at AIIMS, New Delhi and MMC, Chennai have been established for taking care of specific needs of elderly, with focus on long term care.  The specialised services would be available at these centres to Alzheimer’s patients as well.
