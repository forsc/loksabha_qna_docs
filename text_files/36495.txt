                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3744






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Replacement of Hippocratic Oath with the Charak Shapath




Anto  Antony













                                                    Mohammad Jawed
                                                








                                                    Adoor Prakash
                                                








                                                    Kumbakudi Sudhakaran
                                                








                                                    Karti P. Chidambaram
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government intends to replace the Hippocratic Oath with the Charak Shapath and the National Medical Commission has proposed this replacement;(b) if so, the details thereof and rationale for the same;(c) whether the Government has met with representatives of the Indian Medical Association (IMA) to discuss their opposition to this proposal; and(d) if so, the details thereof including the date of such meeting, list of Government representatives present, agenda discussed and the outcome thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (d): As informed by the National Medical Commission (NMC), there is no proposal of replacement of Hippocratic Oath with Charak Shapath.*****
