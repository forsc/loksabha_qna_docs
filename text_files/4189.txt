                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3787






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Census of Milch Animals




R.K. Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the census of milch animals has been done in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (c) whether the Union Government is implementing any action plan for conservation of milch animals to promote dairying; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY & DAIRYING (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)Yes,as per 19th Livestock Census the total number of milch animals in the country is 18.01 crore.(b) A statement showing State-wise number of milch animals is placed at Annexure I(c)& (d) In order to supplement and complement the efforts made by the State Governments, the Rashtriya Gokul Mission has been initiated in the year 2014 with an objective of development and conservation of indigenous bovine breeds and to undertake breed improvement programme for indigenous cattle breeds so as to improve their genetic makeup and increase the stock.
