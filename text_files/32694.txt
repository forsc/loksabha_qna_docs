                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

261






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Marketing and Liquidity Crunch in MSME




Manoj Kumar Tiwari













                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Kavitha Malothu
                                                








                                                    Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Subrata Pathak
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Dayakar Pasunoori
                                                








                                                    Gaddam Ranjith Reddy
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                








                                                    Prataprao  Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether MSMEs are facing problems of marketing and liquidity due to COVID pandemic;(b) 	if so, the extent to which Rupees three lakh crore package under Atmanirbhar Bharat helped this sector;(c) 	the status of disbursement of the above package, State-wise;(d) 	the manner in which the Government is facilitating marketing of MSME products;(e) 	whether the Prime Minister’s direction to clear all dues to MSMEs has not been implemented and still Rs. 6,900 crores are due to MSME sector and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(f) 	the details of the dues to be paid by private sector to MSMEs; and(g) 	the details of e-market linkages that the Government has for MSME sector ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a):	The lockdown imposed during the Covid-19 period had its impact on the overall economy including the MSME sector in the form of decline in sales, problems of relating to marketing, liquity, etc. (b) & (c):   The details of the state-wise beneficiaries under two loans schemes announced under Aatmanirbhar Bharat package viz. Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme (ECLGS) and Credit Guarantee Scheme for Subordinate Debt (CGSSD) are at Annexure I and Annexure II, respectively. (d):	The Ministry of MSME provides business opportunities to Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises to expand their market base through various initiatives, including Procurement and Marketing Support Scheme, Coir Vikas Yojana (CVY), Scheme for International Corporation, Public Procurement Policy for Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs).(e):	The Ministry of MSME has written to other Central Ministries/Departments/State Governments for the clearance of pending dues to MSMEs. The Government has taken many steps to get the dues payable to the MSMEs cleared by public sector units of State and Central Governments. After the announcement made by the Hon’ble Finance Minister under Aatmanirbhar Bharat, the Ministry of MSME has created a special sub-portal within SAMADHAAN portal to track delayed payments to MSMEs from the CPSEs/Government Departments/Ministries. From May 2020 to 27.01.2022, dues of Rs 88,391.94 crore have been settled through this sub-portal. Transaction in business is a continuous process in which some amount may remain stuck in the business process. As per the SAMADHAAN sub-portal, as on 27.01.2022, Rs 1,673.84 crore is pending for payment at the end of Government of India Ministries/Departments/PSUs.(f):	As per the data available on SAMADHAN Portal, as on 28.01.2022, the total dues to be paid by the Private Sector to MSEs are Rs 8,869.67 crore.  (g):	In order to provide an easy on-line access to all the procurement needs/demands from Government Buyers, an on-line portal Government e-marketplace (GeM) is in place. This portal is meant for the all the vendors, including MSMEs. More than 7 lakh MSEs are already registered on GeM portal. In addition to this, National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC) has also developed a Business to Business (B2B) portal viz. ‘MSME Global Mart’. Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) has also developed two online portals “www.khadiindia.gov.in” and “www.ekhadiindia.com” for providing e-market linkages to MSMEs.
