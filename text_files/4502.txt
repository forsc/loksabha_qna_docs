                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4152






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Phone Helpline Facility along NHs




Shivkumar Chanabasappa Udasi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether all the National Highways (NHs) in the country have phone helpline facility and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether the Government has received any request/proposal with regard to phone helpline facility and if so, the details and the present status thereof; and(c)	 whether the app-based cabs are provided with customer care center number in the country and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) NHAI is operating 1033 toll free helpline number for emergency/non-emergency assistance to the commuters on National Highways. (c) No Sir.*****
