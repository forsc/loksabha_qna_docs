                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2008






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Akola-Khandwa Gauge Conversion




Prataprao  Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Railways has given approval to convert Akola-Khandwa metre gauge railway line in Bhusawal region of Central Railway into broad gauge; (b)	 if so, the details thereof; (c)	 whether the Government has received any request from the public  representatives to construct Akola-Khandwa broad gauge rail line passing through remote Adivasi areas Tehsil Sangrampur, Jalgaon, Jamod in Buldhana district which is highly backward district in view of railway connectivity;(d)	 if so, the details thereof; and (e)	 the action taken/proposal to be taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	 to (e) A Statement is laid on the Table of  the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.2008 BY SHRI PRATAPRAO JADHAV TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 03.07.2019 REGARDING AKOLA-KHANDWA GAUGE CONVERSION.(a) & (b) Yes Sir. Akola-Khandwa section is a part of Ratlam – Mhow – Khandwa – Akola Gauge Conversion project and was sanctioned in  2008-09. The work of Gauge conversion from Ratlam-Indore-Mhow has been completed and commissioned. At present work of Akola-Akot  and Khandwa-Amalkhurd sections have been taken up. (c) to (e) Proposals from State Governments or various public representatives and other orgnaisastions for construction of railway projects are received at Divisonal Officers, Zonal Offices and in the Ministry from time to time. As receipt of such proposals is a continuous and dynamic process, compendium of such proposals is not maintained. However, due attention is given to the suggestions, subject to the overall availability of resources and feasibility of their implementation. Projects are sanctioned based on remunerativeness, operational requirements, missing links, strategic/scoio-economic considerations etc. Nevertheless, one letter in this regard has been received from Shri Prataprao Jadhav, Hon’ble MP, Lok Sabha, vide which, it was requested to re-route the alignment of existing line for passing the same through Buldhana constituency. However, Scope of present work is to only convert existing meter gauge track to broad gauge track within the available railway land on the existing meter gauge alignment. Accordingly, detailed estimate had been sanctioned and work has been taken up in Akola-Akot & Khandwa-Amalkhurd section. Railway Officials are following up the forestry clearance of Akot-Amalkhurd section.*****
