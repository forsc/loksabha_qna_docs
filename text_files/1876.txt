                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1630






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Welfare of Dairy Farmers in Karnataka




S. Muniswamy














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to give any special scheme/ packages for the welfare of milk farmers of Kolar district, Karnataka and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government has identified any areas for the implementation of this scheme and if so, the details of the same; and (c) the details of the district identified and the criteria for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (DR.SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a) This Department is implementing following dairy development schemes in the country including Kolar district of Karnataka primarily for strengthening of infrastructure for milk collection, chilling, processing and marketing in cooperative sector:-i.	National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD)ii.	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund (DIDF)iii.	National Dairy Plan-Phase I (NDP-I)	The details of scheme-wise projects approved for Kolar & Chikkaballapur District Cooperative Milk Producers Union Ltd. of Karnataka is as follows:-Scheme	Number of Projects	Total Outlay (Rs. in crore)NPDD	02	2.71DIDF*	01	158.90NDP-I	03	10.08		       *DIDF provides for loan assistance through NABARD(b) & (c) The above mentioned schemes are not area specific, but are applicable in Karnataka as well as the whole of India.****
