                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1407






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Post Offices Operating From Rented Buildings




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the maximum number of Post Offices in rural areas of Rajasthan are operating from rented buildings; (b) 	if so, the details of the Post Offices operating from Government buildings and the rented buildings, district-wise; (c) 	whether the Government proposes to allocate budget for the construction of Government buildings in mission mode for the Post Offices operating in rented buildings in various Districts of Rajasthan including Nagaur district; (d) 	if so, the details thereof and the time by when these buildings are likely to be constructed; (e) 	if not, the reasons therefor; and (f) 	the details of the policy of the Government regarding modernisation and provision of allocating Government buildings to the Post Offices operating from rural areas?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) 	Yes, Sir.(b) 	The details of post Offices operating from Government buildings and the rented buildings, district-wise are annexed.(c) & (d) 	The Government has allocated funds in the year 2021-22 to Rajasthan Circle for construction of Post Offices at  Gudamalani, Sindhari of Barmer Division and  Thoi of  Sikar Division.  Post Office buildings are constructed on the basis of availability of land and fund.(e) 	Does not arise in view of (c) and (d) above.(f)  	The Government has a policy for construction of Postal buildings with modern amenities on its available plots based on cost benefit analysis, functional requirement and availability of funds, including for Post Offices operating from rural areas.
