                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2451






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Development of Link Roads in Villages




Sukhbir Singh Jaunapuria














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether all the villages and dhanis of Rajasthan have been connected with link roads;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the number of villages and dhanis which are yet to be connected along with the time by which these are likely to be provided connectivity;(d)	whether any remedial steps have been taken to ensure that specific needs are taken care at the time of constructing roads in rural areas; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):	Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) is a one-time special intervention to provide rural connectivity, by way of a single all-weather road, to the eligible unconnected habitations in the core network. The unit of programme under PMGSY is a habitation and not a revenue village. In Rajasthan, against 16,450 eligible unconnected habitations, 16,252 habitations have already been provided all weather road connectivity. Out of remaining 198 habitations, 179 habitations have either been dropped or are not feasible as of now and balance 19 eligible and feasible habitations are targeted to be connected at the earliest.(d) to (e):	Specifications and standards available in PMGSY Guidelines, Operational Manual and relevant Indian Road Congress (IRC) specifications are taken into consideration at the time of sanction and construction of roads in rural areas.*****
