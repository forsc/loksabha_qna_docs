                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3915






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Town Committees




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of total number of Town Committees in Assam along with the number of elections conducted  thereof;(b) 	whether the elections of towns of BTC (Bodoland Territorial Council) area have not yet been conducted and if so, the reasons therefor;(c)	the amount of funds allocated by the Government for the purpose during the last five years;(d)	whether the deserving poor are not getting houses under urban housing schemes but the rich are getting more houses;(e)	if so, the reasons therefor along with details thereof with regard of BTC area;(f)	the amount of funds allocated by the Government under various schemes in 	different districts of Assam so far along with the details of utilisation thereof; and(g)	the details and the names of the Committee working for the implementation  of 	schemes?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	The conduct of elections to Town Committees is a state subject. The total number of Town Committees in Assam is 61. The details are given in Annexure I.(b) & (c)	Elections in towns of Bodoland Territorial council(BTC) were held in 2015. The conduct of elections is a State subject. (d) & (e) The selection of beneficiary under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) is done by the Municipal Board/Town Committee as per scheme guidelines. 7656 houses for EWS have been sanctioned in BTC areas.(f)	Under its Missions the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs releases funds to the States and not to the districts. The details of funds allocated to State Government of Assam under various Missions/Schemes and their utilization is given in Annexure II.(g)	Names of the Committees working for implementation of Schemes:Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation(AMRUT)(i)	State Level High Powered Steering Committee(SHPSC)(ii)	State Level Technical Committee(SLTC)Smart Cities Mission(i)	State level High Powered Steering Committee (HPSC)(ii)	Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV)Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban(SBM-U)Urban Development Department, Government of AssamPradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban(PMAY-U)(i)	 State Level Sanctioning & Monitoring Committee (SLSMC)(ii)	 State Level Appraisal Committee (SLAC)(iii)	 Urban Local Bodies / Municipal Boards / Town Committee(iv)	 Development Authority / Planning Authority.(v)	 District Level Project Steering Committee.                                *****
