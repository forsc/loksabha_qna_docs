                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

458






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






PMGSY-Phase I, II and III




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether all the States have achieved the targets under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), Phase-I and Phase-II, if so the details thereof, State-wise;(b) 	the details of the amount allocated and utilized by various States under PMGSY-I and PMGSY-II; (c) 	whether proposals have been received from the States for works under PMGSY-III and if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	whether the Government will give special consideration to the flood hit States including Kerala where the rural roads are completely damaged and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): As on 30th November, 2019, out of the 1, 78, 184 eligible and unconnected habitations of population size of 500+ in plain areas and 250+ in Special category States, Deserts, Tribal and Backward Districts (as per Census, 2001) identified for coverage under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY); 1,67,290 habitations have been connected through all-weather roads including 16,086 habitations connected by States out of their own resources.  4,247 habitations are dropped or are non-feasible. The State-wise details are given in Annexure-I.The details of road length sanctioned and completed under PMGSY-I and PMGSY-II, State-wise are given in Annexure-II and Annexure-III respectively.  (b): The details of amount released and utilized including State share under PMGSY-I &II are given at Annexure-IV.(c): The States of Andhra Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Gujarat, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan and Tamil Nadu have submitted their first batch of proposals under PMGSY-III. The proposals of the State of Chhattisgarh for 3,729.17 Km road length at a cost of Rs. 2,290.13 crore have already been sanctioned. (d): As per programme guidelines, ‘Rural Road’ is a State subject and PMGSY is a one-time special intervention to provide rural connectivity. Maintenance of rural roads constructed under PMGSY is the responsibility of the State Governments. The State Governments are required to undertake the maintenance of the entire Core Network, particularly the road works constructed/upgraded under PMGSY. All road works constructed under PMGSY are covered by initial five years maintenance contracts to be entered into along with the construction contract, with the same contractor, as per the Standard Bidding Document. On expiry of this 5 year post construction maintenance, PMGSY roads are required to be placed under Zonal maintenance contracts consisting of 5 year maintenance including renewal as per cycle, from time to time. Repair/reconstruction of roads damaged due to natural calamity is not covered under PMGSY.*****
