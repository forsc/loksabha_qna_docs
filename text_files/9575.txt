                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3052






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






National AYUSH Mission




Aparajita Sarangi













                                                    Gurjeet Singh Aujla
                                                








                                                    Krupal Balaji Tumane
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)   be pleased to state: (a) whether any new initiatives have been taken by the Government to provide more health cards and basic medical examination facility under the National AYUSH Mission (NAM) programme, if so, the details thereof; (b) the details of various components of centrally sponsored scheme under NAM for categorization and infrastructural strengthening of AYUSH in the country; (c) whether the Government has made sufficient fund allocation for promotion of AYUSH in the country, State/UT-wise particularly in Maharashtra during the last three years and the current year and if so, the details thereof; (d) whether the Government has sponsored institutions and also established/ likely to establish new academic institutes under NAM; and (e) if so, the details thereof, State/ UT-wise including Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):     Under various components of Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), there are provisions for financial assistance to make available necessary equipments in the AYUSH Hospitals & Dispensaries which may include essential diagnostics for providing basic medical examination facility to the public.  State / UT Governments may avail the financial assistance by projecting their requirement as per NAM guidelines. However, there is no provision for health card under existing guidelines of NAM.  (b) & (c):    Government of India is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) for development and promotion of Ayurveda, Yoga and Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha and Homoeopathy (AYUSH) in the country. The Mission inter-alia makes the following provisions which include upgradation and strengthening of infrastructure of AYUSH:(i) Co-location of AYUSH facilities at Primary Health Centers (PHCs), Community Health Centers (CHCs) and Districts Hospitals (DHs).(ii) Up gradation of exclusive State Government AYUSH Hospitals and Dispensaries.(iii)  Setting up of upto 50 bedded integrated AYUSH Hospital.(iv)  Upgradation of State Government Under-Graduate and Post-Graduate Educational Institutions.(v)  Setting up of new State Government AYUSH Educational Institutions in the States where it is not available in Government Sector.(vi) Strengthening of State Government/State Government Co-operatives/Public Sector Undertakings Ayurveda, Siddha, Unani and Homoeopathy (ASU&H) Pharmacies.(vii) Strengthening of State Drug Testing Laboratories for ASU &H Drugs. (viii) Support for cultivation of Medicinal Plant including processing and post-harvest management.However, there is no provision of categorization of AYUSH in the country under NAM. Funds allocated/released to the States/UTs by the Central Government for the promotion of AYUSH in the country during the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra is furnished at Annexure-I.(d) &(e):    Yes, as per the provision of NAM, the Government has provided grant-in-aid for establishment of AYUSH educational institutions in States/UTs where such educational institutions are not available in Government sector as per the proposals received through State Annual Action Plan (SAAP). The details of approval for setting up of new AYUSH educational institutions under NAM in various States of the Country are furnished at Annexure-II. However, no proposal has been received from the State of Maharashtra under this component.
