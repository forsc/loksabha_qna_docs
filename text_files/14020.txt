                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2928






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Infrastructure for Coal Industries




Anubhav Mohanty














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has provided any specific funds to develop infrastructure for coal industries to the State of Odisha;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether the Government expects the State Government of Odisha to develop infrastructure for Coal industries in the absence of Central aid and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a) & (b): Government through its Central Sector Scheme viz Development of Transport Infrastructure in Coalfields has provided fund to eight Road/Railway projects in Odisha which are mentioned below:(Figures in Rs. Crore) Sl. No.	Name of Projects	Length (KM)	Sanctioned assistance 	CCDAC released till date	Remarks1	Development of Railway infrastructure of Jharsuguda alignment from Gopalpur- Moharpur Block.	50.504	214.00	214.00	Work completed and it is 100% assistance2	Road from Basundhara East OCP to Gandhi Chawk (Brajarajnagar)	44.85	19.00	17.92	Work completed and it is 50% assistance3	Road fromTalcher to Deulbera Colliery	2.00	0.60	0.273	Work completed and it is 50% assistance4	Road from Belpahar OCP to Lajkura Siding	12	3.00	2.24	Work completed and it is 50% assistance5	Fly over across Bombay-Howrah Rly. Line	0.072	2.50	2.32	Work completed and it is 50% assistance6	Construction of road and culvert from Kalamchhuin and to hingula Mines	4.32	0.648	0.616	Work completed and it is 50% assistance7	Construction of approach road for Bhubaneswar OCP	4.00	0.15	0.094	Work completed and it is 50% assistance8	Widening & Strengthening  Road of Balinga – Taparia Road 	21.96	14.72	5.42	Work forclosed9	Road from sundargarh to Duduka road (Basundhara)	41.03	15.665	15.665	Work completed and it is 50% assistance10	Road from Jambubali to Angul via Natidi and Natar	15.72	2.495	1.19	Work completed and it is 50% assistance11	Widening of road from Derachowk to Lingaraj OCP	3.2	0.57	0.54	Work completed and it is 50% assistance12	Road overbridge from Talcher Balanda Rly Siding to Jagannath office.	0.755	1.735	1.735	Work completed and it is 50% assistance13	Road from Padmavatipur to Solada via Kandaberini	10.75	0.69	0.17	Work foreclosed and it is 50% assistance14	Road from Derachowk to Talcher colliery	2	0.6	0.55	Work completed and it is 50% assistance (c) In order to develop rail infrastructure in Odisha for evacuation of coal, a Rail Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) has been constituted among Mahanadi Coalfields Limited (MCL), IRCON and State Government (Govt. of Odisha). The stake of Government of Odisha in the SPV is 10%. ******
