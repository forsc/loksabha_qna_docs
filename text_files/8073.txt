                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1755






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Use of Plastic Waste in Development of Road




Feroze Varun Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has invested in research and development to increase usage of plastic in the development/ construction of roads in the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the manner in which the Government proposes to effectively implement its mandatory policy to make roads using discarded plastic waste along with the time frame proposed for the purpose; and (d) 	the length of road that has been constructed so far using discarded plastic waste across the country? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)  & (d) Indian Roads Congress (IRC) has formulated IRC SP: 98:2013 ‘Guidelines for the use of Waste Plastic in Hot Bituminous Mixes (Dry process) in Wearing Courses’ based on laboratory as well as field performance studies/investigations carried out in India.  To encourage use of waste plastic on National Highway (NH), Ministry has issued Guidelines for its use within 50 km periphery of urban areas having population 5 lakh or more. The Guidelines also stipulate to take up a stretch of at least 10 km as pilot project for assessment of its performance.  Waste plastic has already been utilised in wearing course of about 50 km of NH stretches. ****
