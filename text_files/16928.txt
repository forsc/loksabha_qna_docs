                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

547






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Encroachment by Slums near Railway Tracks




Kaushal Kishore














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the jhuggis/slum areas along the railway tracks in several States including Delhi have been instructed to be removed as per the recent Supreme Court orders; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has any data regarding the jhuggis/slums situated illegally along the railway tracks in different States including Uttar Pradesh; (d) if so, the details thereof;(e) whether any incident of deaths of migrant workers while sleeping on the railway tracks during lockdown imposed due to COVID-19 has been reported recently and the details thereof; and(f) if so, the reaction of the Government thereto and the compensation provided by the State Government/ Central Government to their family members?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (f): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. ******
