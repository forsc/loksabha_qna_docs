                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1556






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Scheduled Castes Sub-Plan




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1556TO BE ANSWERED ON. 20.09.2020SCHEDULED CASTES SUB-PLAN 1556.   SHRI S. VENKATESAN: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a)  whether any study has been done by the Government on the efficacy of implementation of Scheduled Castes Sub-Plan (SCSP) during the last 7 years i.e. 2014-20; (b)  if so, the details thereof; (c)  whether there is any proposal on the part of the Government to give legal framework for SCSP to avoid circumvention of guidelines; and (d)  if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RAMDAS ATHAWALE)(a):  No Sir. However, regular reviews are being held with all concerned Ministries/Departments to assess the progress of the schemes under Scheduled Caste Sub Plan (SCSP) now Development Action Plan for Scheduled Castes (DAPSC).(b):  Question does not arise.(c):  No Sir.(d):  Question does not arise.****
