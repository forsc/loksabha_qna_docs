                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

374






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Food Parks Scheme




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla













                                                    Nishikant  Dubey
                                                








                                                    Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government has implemented Food Parks scheme in the country to create modern infrastructure for food processing and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the details of parks identified/established/approved over the last two years along with financial assistance or funds provided to such parks; and (c)	whether any State Government has requested the Union Government to set up food parks and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a):	Ministry of Food Processing Industries is implementing Mega Food Park Scheme (MFPS) to create modern infrastructure for the food processing industries since 2008. This scheme is now a component of the new Central Sector Umbrella Scheme – Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY). The Mega Food Park Scheme (MFPS) aims at providing modern infrastructure facilities for food processing along the value chain from farm to market. The salient features of the scheme is detailed in Annexure-I.(b):	Total 42 Mega Food Parks (MFPs) were envisaged by the Government. As on date, Ministry has approved 39 MFPs in 24 states/UTs which are under various stages of implementation. Out of this 18 MFPs are operational. Details of MFPs made operational over the last two financial year and current financial years along with Grant-in-aid approved & released are at Annexure-II. Details of Mega Food Parks accorded final approval by the Ministry over the last two financial years and current financial year along with Grant-in-aid approved & released are at Annexure-III. (c):	Ministry has accorded final approval to 9 Mega Food Park Projects being implemented by State Government entities. The details of these projects are at Annexure-IV. At present no proposal of state government entity is pending for consideration with the Ministry under the scheme.*****
