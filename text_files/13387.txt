                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2287






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






User Development Fee




Pritam Gopinath Munde













                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government proposes to levy user development fee for redeveloped railway stations; (b) if so, the facts in this regard along with the reasons therefor; (c) whether the Union Government has assessed its impact on the poor railway passenger; and(d) if so, the response of the Union Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d):  A Group of Secretaries (GoS) was constituted by Ministry of Railways (Railway Board) for redevelopment of 50 railway stations. The GoS inter-alia suggested the levy of user fee for redevelopment of stations, which is presently under assessment at Ministry of Railways.  *****
