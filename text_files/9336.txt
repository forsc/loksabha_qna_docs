                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2966






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Construction Work on NH-927




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of construction work on National Highway-927 from Sarupganj to Ratlam via Kherwada, Udaipur, Dungarpur, Sagwada, Partapur and Banswara; and(b)	the time by which the work on the said sections is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	& (b) The upgradation of stretch of National Highway (NH) 927A from Swaroopganj to Kherwara (km 0.00 to km 148.00) is included in Annual Plan during Financial Year       2021-22, while work on the stretch from Kherwara to Wajwana (km 148.00 to km 251.00) via Dungarpur, Partapur and Sangwara is included in the current Annual Plan 2019-20. The work of 2-Lane with Paved shoulders is in progress in the stretch from Wajwana to Banswara (km 251.00 to km 275.00) and its completion is likely by 31.12.2019. The stretch from Banswara to Rajasthan/Madhya Pradesh Border (km 275.00 to km 313.00) has already been upgraded to 2-Lane on BOT (Toll) mode by State Government of Rajasthan. The remaining stretch from Madhya Pradesh Border to Ratlam has been                      de-notified as National Highway by the Ministry.*****
