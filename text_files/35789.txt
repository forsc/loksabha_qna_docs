                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2604






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Bids for City Gas Distribution Network




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board has invited bids for City Gas Distribution (CGD) network in the sixty-five Geographical Areas in 11th CGD bidding round; and; (b) if so, the details of the actual progress and current status of CGD network in Maharashtra, district and geographical area-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a)	Yes Sir, Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB) launched the 11th CGD bidding round on 17.09.2021 for development of City Gas Distribution (CGD) network in 65 Geographical Areas(GAs). PNGRB received bids against 61 GAs covering 215 (212 full & 3 part) districts including 8 GAs in the state of Maharashtra and Letters of Intent (LoIs) have been issued to all the successful bidders.(a)	The details of progress and current status of the GAs authorized till 10th CGD bidding round in the state of Maharashtra are at Annexure.****
