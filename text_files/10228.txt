                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3659






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Financial Assistance to SCs




Bhanu Pratap Singh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government provides financial assistance to the persons belonging to Scheduled Caste communities to set up and operate their own educational institutions for promoting education; and (b) if so, the details of the financial assistance released during each of the last three years and the current year, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b): This Ministry is implementing a Central Sector Scheme of Grants-in-Aid to Voluntary and Other Organisations working for Scheduled Castes, under which Grants-in-Aid is provided to Voluntary Organisations/NGOs/institutions like NSFDC  and NSKFDC working for the welfare of scheduled Castes, for running school, Hostels and Skill development programs for Scheduled Castes.
