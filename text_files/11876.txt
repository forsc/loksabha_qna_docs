                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

730






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






National Water Quality Sub-Mission




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the status of National Water Quality Sub-Mission (NWQSM) launched to provide potable drinking water in fluoride affected habitats;(b) whether the Government is aware of the low number of fluoride affected regions it has covered under the mission, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) the measures being taken to increase coverage under the said sub-mission; and(d) the number of fluoride affected habitations with Community Water Purification Plants?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATEIN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c) In March 2017, National Water Quality Sub-Mission (NWQSM) was launched as a part of National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP), which has now been subsumed under Jal Jeevan Mission, to provide safe drinking water to identified 27,544 Arsenic/ Fluoride affected rural habitations of the country. State wise physical progress of fluoride affected habitations under NWQSM is at Annexure-I.(d) Out 13,725 fluoride affected habitations identified under NWQSM, 1,507 habitations have been covered through Community Water Purification Plants. *****
