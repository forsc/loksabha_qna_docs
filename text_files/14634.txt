                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EARTH SCIENCES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3338






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Seismic Microzonation




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao













                                                    Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EARTH SCIENCES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF EARTH SCIENCESLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION No. 3338TO BE ANSWERED ON FRIDAY, MARCH 13, 2020SEISMIC MICROZONATION3338. SHRIMATI POONAM MAHAJAN:SHRI RAJESHBHAI CHUDASAMA:Will the Minister of EARTH SCIENCES be pleased to state:(a)	the details of agencies/institutions included in the list of Expression of Interest for Seismic Microzonation in the various cities; proposed by the Government;(b)	the results of the bidding for the tender of Seismic Microzonation of various cities; and(c)	the allocation made by Government for Seismic Microzonation during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGYAND EARTH SCIENCES (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a)	Following agencies/ institutions expressed their interest in conducting Seismic Microzonation of various cities:I.	M/s Fugro Geotech (India) Pvt. Ltd., MumbaiII.	M/s CENGRS GeotechnicaPvt. Ltd, NoidaIII.	M/s SeconPvt. Ltd, BangaloreIV.	M/s CE Testing Company Pvt. Ltd, KolkataV.	M/s Sarathy Geotech & Engineering Services Pvt. Ltd, BangaloreVI.	M/s Geo Designs & Research Pvt Ltd, VadodaraVII.	M/s Rawel Singh & Company, RoparVIII.	M/s Indian Geotechnical Services, New DelhiIX.	Centre for Urbanization Buildings and Environment (CUBE), IIT Madras, ChennaiX.	Institute of Seismological Research (ISR), GandhinagarXI.	Indian Institute of Technology-Roorkee, RoorkeeXII.	Indian Institute of Technology-Kharagpur, KharagpurXIII.	Kurukshetra University, KurukshetraXIV.	Indian Institute of Technology – Kanpur, KanpurXV.	Society for Innovation and Development, Indian Institute of Science (IISc) BengaluruXVI.	Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bengaluru(b)  Based on the competitive bids, seismic microzonation work for 4-cities namely Bhubaneswar, Chennai, Coimbatore and Mangalore has been awarded to following firms:Bhubaneswar:		M/s SeconPvt. LtdChennai: 			M/s Fugro Geotech (India) Pvt. LtdCoimbatore: 			M/s SeconPvt. LtdMangalore: 		M/s SeconPvt. LtdThe Microzonation work for 8 other cities namely, Kanpur, Lucknow, Patna, Dhanbad, Varanasi, Meerut, Agra, Amritsar shall be initiated soon after completion of necessary administrative and financial approval.  (c) An amount of Rs. 22.63 crores has been allocated during last there years. ****************
