                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

760






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Unutilised funds Under ICDS Scheme




Devendrappa Y














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the funds allocated under ICDS scheme remained unutilized by various States during the last three years;(b)	if so, the details thereof and reasons therefor, State-wise and year-wise; and(c) 	the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (b) : Any unutilized fund / excess expenditure incurred during a particular year under Anganwadi Services Scheme is adjusted in the allocation/release of the succeeding year. Details of the unutilized funds available / excess expenditure incurred as on 31st March of each of the previous three years is at Annex. (c): Due to various administrative reasons, some funds may remain unspent with the State/UT Governments.
