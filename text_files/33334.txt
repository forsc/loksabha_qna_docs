                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

841






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Protected and Unprotected Monuments in Maharashtra




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of protected and unprotected monuments in the State of Maharashtra District-wise;(b)	the details of revenue earned through the above monuments during the last three years, year-wise and monument-wise;(c)	the amount spent on safety and security of above monuments during the last three years, year-wise and monument-wise; and(d)	the efforts being made to increase the earning of revenue from above monuments?






ANSWER
