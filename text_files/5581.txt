                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5172






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






e-Catering




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the State and railway zone-wise details of revenue earned through e-catering services during the last one year, including district-wise details for Maharashtra; (b)	the State and station-wise details of existing set of catering service providers along with the details of subsidies provided/ expenditure incurred in relation to e-catering, if any; and(c)	whether the Railways intend to expand the services provided to other stations and include more Indian brands for providing e-catering services to passengers, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): State and Railway zone-wise details of revenue earned through e-catering services during the last one year are at Appendix-I. District-wise details of revenue earned through e-catering services during the last one year for Maharashtra are at Appendix-II.(b): State and station-wise details of existing set of catering service providers along with the details of expenditure incurred in relation to e-catering are at Appendix-III. It is further mentioned that no subsidies have been provided in relation to e-catering till date.(c): Yes, Sir. E-catering services have already been made available on 325 stations of Indian Railways. Proliferation of e-catering services on stations is being done as per the requirements of passengers. Many Indian service providers have been roped in to provide e-catering services which can be seen in Appendix-III and continuous efforts are being made to include more reputed Indian brands for providing e-catering services to passengers.
