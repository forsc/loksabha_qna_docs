                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1128






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Welfare Schemes for Women over 75 years




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has any welfare schemes particularly for women above 75 years, if so, the details thereof and if not, whether the Government proposes to introduce such schemes and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government proposes to make it mandatory for local police stations or any other authority to check on a fortnightly basis about the well-being and safety of such aged women, if so, the details thereof; and(c)	whether the Government is considering to provide free medical facilities to such aged women including supply of medicines, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (c)    The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment implements Integrated Programme for Senior Citizens (IPSrC) (a sub-scheme of National Action Plan for Senior Citizens), under which it provides grants in aid  for running and maintenance of, inter alia, Senior Citizens Homes (Old Age Homes) for 50 elderly Women.	The main objective of this Scheme is to improve the quality of life of the Senior Citizens by providing basic amenities like shelter, food, medical care and entertainment opportunities. ‘Krishna Kutir’ a Home for Widows has been set up by Ministry of Women & Child Development in Vrindavan, UP with a capacity of 1000 inmates to provide widows a safe and secure place of stay, health services, nutritious food, legal and counselling services irrespective  of age limit *******
