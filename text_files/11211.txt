                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

106






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






World Book Fair 2020




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) whether the National Book Trust in collaboration with India trade PromotionOrganization has organised 28 edition of World Book Fair 2020 in Delhirecently and if so, the details thereof along with the theme of the fair:b) the details of number of foreign exhibitors who participated in the saidevent;c) the details of amount of funds earmarked for the eventd) whether the fair acts as a gateway to the intellectual world of South Asiaand if so, the details thereof;e) whether foreign investors also took part in this book fair and if so, thedetails thereof; andf) the other steps taken by the Government to produce and encourage theproduction of good literature?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)****a) Yes Sir, The New Delhi World Book Fair (NDWBF), 2020 was organisedduring 4-12 January, 2020 at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi. The theme of theFair was "Gandhi: Writers Writer".b) In all 13 foreign exhibitors/publishers participated in NDWBF, 2020 from U.K.,Saudi Arabia, Spain, Germany, Iran, France, Poland, U.A.E., China, Nepal,Sri Lanka and Egypt.c) The funds earmarked for the event was Rs. 1484.00 lakh.d) In so far as South Asia is concerned, two countries viz. Sri Lanka and Nepalregularly participate in the NDWBF. Accordingly, National Book Trust (NBT),India has published the title Sri Lanka: A Land in Search of itself under theseries ‘Afro Asian countries’. Around 30 titles in Nepali language have alsobeen published by NBT for children to promote the language.e) No, Sir.f) Some of the important steps taken by the Government in collaboration withNBT are as follows:<pre>(i) on the occasion of the celebration of 550th Birth Anniversary of Guru NanakDev Ji, NBT has published 4 titles in Punjabi language to promote theteachings of Guru Nanak Dev JI.(ii) on the occasion of celebration of 150th Birth Anniversary of Mahatma Gandhithe title Gandhi "Warrior of Non-violence” has been published.(iii) Publication of various books in 50 Indian languages including tribal as well asdialects with a view to provide good literature to children in the mother tongue.(iv) Publication of around 300 books in Braille script done for the benefit of visuallychallenged in 5 languages including English.(v) Introducing new series like Nav Lekhan Mala, Mahila Protsahan Yojana, VeerGatha, Women Pioneers etc with a view to provide a window to new writersespecially with respect to books on and by women.</pre>
