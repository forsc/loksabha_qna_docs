                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4303






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Unregistered Food Processing units




 Chandra Sekhar Bellana













                                                    T.R.V.S. Ramesh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the Formalization of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (FME) Scheme; (b)	the details of the funding received by Tamil Nadu in 2020-21 and 2021-22; (c)	the details of the targets achieved under the scheme in Tamil Nadu in 2020-21 and 2021-22; (d)	whether it is true that there are nearly 25 lakh unregistered food processing units in the country and out of it more than 1.5 lakh such units are in Andhra Pradesh, if so, the details thereof;  (e)	the efforts being made by the Government to register and provide them financial, technical and other assistance through PMFME, PMKSY etc.;(f)	whether the National Portal for the FME scheme is fully functional and running;(g)	if so, the details of the number of new registrants of the scheme from the National Portal from each district of Tamil Nadu; and (h)	whether any new survey has been done on the number of unregistered food processing units since last one which was done in 2015-16 and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a):	As part of the Aatmanirbhar Bharat Initiative, Ministry of Food Processing Industries is implementing a Centrally Sponsored "PM Formalisation of Micro food processing Enterprises (PMFME) Scheme" for providing financial, technical and business support for upgradation of micro food processing enterprises in the country. Support to upgrade existing units or setting up of new micro units for individuals is provided through a credit linked grants-in-aid @35% up to a maximum grant of Rs. 10.00 lakh. The Scheme also provides support to food processing units of SHGs/ FPOs/ Cooperatives for credit linked grants-in-aid @35%, Seed Capital to SHGs, Incubation Centre, Common Infrastructure, Marketing & Branding and Capacity Building (b):	A total amount of Rs.15.32 crores as Centre Share has been released in 2020-21 and 2021-22 till date to Tamil Nadu for various components under the PMFME Scheme. (c):	The details of achievement under the scheme in Tamil Nadu during 2020-21 and 2021-22 till date are as under: (i). State Nodal Agencies (SNAs), State Level Approval Committees (SLACs) and District Level Committees (DLCs) have been constituted.  (ii). One District One Products (ODOPs) for all the 37 Districts have been approved in State. (iii). Seed Capital amounting to Rs.0.79 crore released for 281 Self Help Group (SHG) Members.  (iv). 4 Incubation Centers have been approved with an outlay of Rs.8.78 crore. (v). For capacity building, one  State Level Technical Institute (SLTI) has been nominated. 26 Master Trainers and 15 District Level Trainers have been trained. 33 District Resource Persons (DRPs) have been appointed to provide hand holding support to beneficiaries.   (vi). Loan sanctioned to 188 beneficiaries under the scheme.  (d):	As per the Annual Survey of Industries, 2015-16 and 73rd  Round Survey of National Sample Survey Organisation (NSSO), there are about 25 lakh unregistered/ unincorporated food processing enterprises in the country including Andhra Pradesh. The details of Statewise number of unregistered/unincorporated enterprises in the country are at Annexure-I.  (e):	Support is being provided under the PMFME scheme to upgrade existing units or setting up of new micro units for individuals  through  credit linked grant-in-aid @35% up to a maximum grant of Rs. 10.00 lakh. The Scheme also provides support to food processing units of SHGs/ FPOs/ Cooperatives for credit linked grants-in-aid @35%, Seed Capital to SHGs, Incubation Centre, Common Infrastructure, Marketing & Branding and Capacity Building.  The Ministry has also been implementing Central Sector Umbrella Scheme - Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana (PMKSY) for overall growth and development of food processing sector, including processing of agro- products and thus increasing the income of the farmers. The Ministry  provides financial support to entrepreneurs for setting up of food processing / preservation industries. (f):	National Portal for the PMFME scheme is functional and running for submission of applications by the interested Individuals and Groups for seeking support under the scheme. (g):	The district-wise details of the number of new registrants for establishing individual micro food processing units registered under the scheme in the State of Tamil Nadu are at Annexure-II. (h):	No new survey has been done on the number of unregistered food processing units by the Ministry. Data related to unregistered sector is collected, compiled and maintained by Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation.   *****
