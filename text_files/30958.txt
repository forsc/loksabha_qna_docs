                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2517






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Funds Sanctioned For Tourism in Rajasthan




Chandra Prakash Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the funds sanctioned and allocated along with the amount spent for tourism in Rajasthan during the last three years; (b) 	the year wise details thereof; and (c) 	the steps taken to increase the number of international tourists in Rajasthan?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b):	Ministry of Tourism under the schemes of “Swadesh Darshan”, “National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)” and ‘Assistance to Central Agencies for Tourism Infrastructure Development’ provides financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory Administration/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure in the country, including Rajasthan, in consultation with them. Details of projects sanctioned under the Swadesh Darshan, PRASHAD and Assistance to Central Agencies Schemes in the State of Rajasthan for development of tourism related infrastructure is given at Annexure.(c):	Ministry of Tourism, promotes India in a holistic manner, including Rajasthan and as part of its on-going activities, it releases global print, electronic and online media campaigns in important and potential markets overseas, under the ‘Incredible India’ brand-line, to promote various tourism destinations and products of the country. Promotions are also undertaken through the website (www.incredibleindia.org) and Social Media accounts of the Ministry. The promotions are undertaken through the “Domestic Promotion and Publicity including Hospitality Scheme” (DPPH) and “Overseas Promotion and Publicity including Market Development Assistance” (OPMD) Schemes of Ministry of Tourism.******
