                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1596






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Rural Development in Tamil Nadu




S.R. Parthiban














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any effective policy formulation is necessary for planning and development of rural areas, particularly in Tamil Nadu;(b)	if so, whether any effort has been made by the Government so far in this regard;(c) 	if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	if not, whether the Government has any future plan in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d): For development of rural areas, including those of Tamil Nadu, through employment generation, strengthening of livelihood opportunities, promoting self-employment, skilling of rural youth, providing social assistance, infrastructure development etc., the Ministry of Rural Development is implementing Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS), Deendayal Antyodaya Yojana – National Rural Livelihoods Mission (DAY-NRLM), Deen Dayal Upadhyay – Gramin Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY),  Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana – Gramin (PMAY-G), Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), Shyama Prasad Mukherjee National RuRBAN Mission, National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP) and Saansad Adarsh Gram Yojana (SAGY). Third Party evaluation of Ministry of Rural Development’s Schemes are also carried out regularly and follow up actions, as appropriate, are taken on the reports submitted.
