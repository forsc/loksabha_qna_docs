                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1893






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Inclusion of Cities under AMRUT




B. Y. Raghavendra














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of cities included under Atal Mission for 	Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) in the State 	of Karnataka;(b) 	the extent to which the project has been affected due to the 	present COVID period;(c) 	the criteria to make assessment of the pending works; and(d) 	the details of funds allotted and assessment of designated 	works in its comparison?






ANSWER




                                                                 THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                      (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a):	27 cities of the State of Karnataka have been covered under the Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) scheme. List of these cities is given at ANNEXRURE.(b):  	Implementation of projects under AMRUT scheme slowed down due to lockdowns imposed from time to time in the States/ Union Territories (UTs) to arrest spread of COVID-19 pandemic.  However, pace of implementation has picked up once restrictions got lifted.(c) and (d):	Pending works are assessed on the basis of progress achieved by States/ UTs in respect of each project. There is a dedicated AMRUT MIS portal for tracking the implementation of AMURT projects in the States/UTs. Data on progress achieved under each project are fed in the Portal by each States/ UTs on achieving respective project milestones. Implementation of projects are also assessed by Independent Review and Monitoring Agencies (IRMAs), which have been appointed by each State/UT. The approved plan size of AMRUT projects of the State of Karnataka amounts to ?4,952.87 crore, which includes committed Central Assistance of ?2,318.79 crore.  Central Assistance amounting to ?2,258.85 crore have been released to the Government of Karnataka so far for AMRUT projects. Against the approved plan size of ?4,952.87, State has grounded 401 projects worth ?5,119.95crore. Of these, works worth ?4,436.52crore have been physically competed which include 339 completed projects worth ?2,593.38 crore.State of Karnataka has taken up projects in excess of approved plan size, and therefore, the entire additional expenditure will be borne by the State itself.                      ******
