                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1318






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Pending Funds




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the details of funds towards various programmes/projects that are pending with the Union Government for release to the State Government for release to the State Government of Maharashtra; andb)	the time by which these funds are expected to be released by the Union Government?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE (SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY) (a) & (b) The Department of Expenditure, Ministry of Finance releases Central Finance Commission recommended grants-in-aid to States including that for the State of Maharashtra on the recommendations of the Administrative Ministries concerned subject to fulfillment of stipulated conditions associated with each component of the recommended grants-in-aid. There is no such pending recommendation from any Ministry as on 30.11.2021 regarding release of the 15th Finance Commission recommended grants-in-aid to the State of Maharashtra.********
