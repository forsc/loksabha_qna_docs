                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1184






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






GST Evasion




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that the tax evasion has increased after the implementation of Goods and Services Tax(GST); (b) if so, the details of evasion detected and the amount involved since 1st July 2017; (c) the details of FIR registered and arrest initiated in this regard, State wise; (d) whether the Government is also aware about the involvement of tax officials in the evasion cases; (e) if so, the details of such officers along with the action taken against them, State-wise; and(f) the details of measures taken by the Government to prevent/reduce GST evasion cases? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a): No, Sir. There is no evidence to suggest that tax evasion has increased after the implementation of GST.(b) & (c): The details of GST evasion cases booked by Central GST Authorities, State & UT wise, are as follows:                                (Amt. in Rs.
