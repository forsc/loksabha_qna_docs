                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

473






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Unspent MPLAD Fund in Jharkhand




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the quantum of funds that remained unspent under Members of Parliament Local Area Development Scheme (MPLADS) in Jharkhand during the last five years, MP/year-wise along with the manner in which how the unspent fund is utilized, constituency- wise; and (b) whether the Government asks district administration to provide regular information on progress of projects and if so, the details thereof work-wise, MP-wise and year-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING (RAO INDERJIT SINGH) (a) As reported by the concerned nodal district authorities on portal www.mplads.gov.in, the funds that remained unspent under MPLADS, Member of Parliament wise (sitting and former both), in respect of Jharkhand, as on 09.09.2020 for the last five years inter-alia covering the 16th, 17th Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha MPs is at annexure.   The funds released under the scheme are non- lapsable and the funds received by a district in a particular year can be utilized in the subsequent financial year(s). Therefore, unspent balance is not an unused fund but includes the funds of the sanctioned and ongoing works due to be paid on execution of works. The unspent funds are utilized for completion of the eligible works recommended by the concerned Hon’ble MPs upto the last day of the term of the Hon’ble MP as per para 3.11 of the MPLADS Guidelines.Funds that remain unspent with the district authorities after executing all the eligible works and closure of accounts in terms of provisions under 4.10.1 of MPLADS Guidelines, are transferred to successor/ distributed in terms of paras 4.7 to 4.10 of the said Guidelines, as the case may be.(b) Yes Sir, provisions exist on the MPLADS portal for the district authorities to provide work-wise, MP-wise and year-wise details on progress of projects regularly. The information in this regard is available in public domain on the website: www.mplads.gov.in.                                         *****
