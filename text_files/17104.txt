                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

954






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Promotion of Herbal Medicine




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA,YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether adequate steps have been taken by Government to promote/publicise herbal medicine among the people through newspapers and if so, the details thereof;(b) the details of the various States and regions identified by the Government to promote herbal medicinal plantations and allocation of funds for the purpose during the last two years; and(c) the further steps taken by the Government to promote herbal medicines in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a) to (c): 	The Ministry of AYUSH is concerned with the promotion of Ayurvedic, Siddha, Sowa-rigpa & Unani(ASU) and Homoeopathy  medicines. These are not categorised as herbal medicines. ASU&H medicines are promoted /publicised through various platforms and mediums. No promotion /publicity have been carried out by the M/o AYUSH for herbal medicines including newspapers.Ministry of AYUSH implements Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM).  Under ‘Medicinal Plants’ component of the NAM scheme, market driven cultivation of prioritized medicinal plants in identified cluster/zones is supported within selected districts of States and implemented in a mission mode.  The allocation of funds for the last two years has been summarised as below. Rs.in LakhsUnder National Ayush Mission(NAM) Scheme	FY 2018-19	FY 2019-20Total Fund allocation	4535.71	8931.90Total Fund Released	3740.90	3887.97State-wise details are given at Annexure.
