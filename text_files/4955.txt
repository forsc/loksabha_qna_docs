                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4491






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Textile Excellence Centre




Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)  whether the Government has any proposal to establish Textile Excellence Centres in different parts of the country, especially in Telangana; (b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (c):	The Government does not have any proposal to establish Textile Excellence Centres in the country, including Telangana. However, the Government has established eight Centers of Excellences (CoEs) for research and innovation in technical textiles as under:-S.No. 	Name of COEs 	Area 	S.No.	Name of CoEs	 Area 1. 	Bombay Textile Research Association, Mumbai 	Geotech 	5.	DKTE Society’s Textile and Engineering Institute,  Kolhapur 	Non-Wovens2.
