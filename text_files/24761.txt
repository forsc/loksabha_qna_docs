                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

609






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Open Defection Status




E. T. Mohammed Basheer














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is true that 44% of Indian people continue with open defecation; and(b) the time by which the country will become open defecation free?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) 	No, Sir.(b) 	All the villages in the country have already declared themselves Open Defecation Free (ODF) as on 2nd October, 2019.*****
