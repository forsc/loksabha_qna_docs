                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4484






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






DDU-GKY in Rajasthan




Narendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of rural youth from Jhunjhunu including other districts of Rajasthan provided skill training under the Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Gramin Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) during the last three years; and(b) 	the number of youth provided employment after completion of the training?
