                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2518






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






Rehabilitation of Manual Scavengers




Upendra Singh Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has the details of the manual scavengers  employed by the Railways as of September 2021;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government has taken any steps to rehabilitate and resettle the manual scavengers; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND		ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY				(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a)	to (d):  A statement is laid on the Table of the House.      *****  STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2518 BY SHRI UPENDRA SINGH RAWAT   TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 16.03.2022 REGARDING REHABILITATION OF MANUAL SCAVENGERS. (a) & (b)    There are no manual scavengers on Indian Railways.  Provisions of Prohibition of Employment as Manual Scavengers and their Rehabilitation Act, 2013 have been complied with by Railways and protective gear and devices including mechanized equipment to any person engaged in cleaning in passenger coaches, station area or railway tracks are being provided.  With the fitment of bio-toilets in all Broad Gauge coaches, no faecal matter is discharged on tracks.  Cleaning of washable aprons at platforms and coach toilets is done using high pressure jets and protective gears are provided to the workers.  Thus, there is no manual scavenging on Railways. (c) & (d)     Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment  has formulated a Central Sector Self Employment Scheme for Rehabilitation of Manual Scavengers (SRMS) which provides following benefits to the identified manual scavengers for their rehabilitation:-i.	One time Cash Assistance of ? 40,000/- to one identified manual scavenger in the family.ii.	Skill development Training of manual scavengers and their dependants upto two years with stipend @  ? 3,000/- per month during the training period.iii.	Capital Subsidy upto ? 5.00 lakh for those who availed loans for Self Employment Projects including sanitation related projects.iv.	Health insurance under Ayushman Bharat, Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) to the families of all the identified manual scavengers.      					                  ****
