                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4630






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2020






Billionaires




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the number of billionaires has been continuously increasing in the last ten years;(b) if so, the number of billionaires in the country during the said period and as on date, year-wise; and (c) the value of the total assets of the said billionaires during said period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR )(a) & (b): There is no legislative or administrative definition of the term billionaire under direct taxes. As per the information available with Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT), the number of individuals disclosing a gross total income of more than Rs.100 crore (one billion rupees) in a year in his/her return of income filed with the Income Tax Department since the Assessment Year 2012-13 to Assessment Year 2018-19 are given in the table below. Data on such individuals from 2008-09 to 2011-12 is not centrally available.Table: Number of individuals disclosing a gross total income of more than Rs.100 crore in a year in his/her return of income filed with the Income Tax DepartmentAssessment Year	Number of individuals2012-13	292013-14	272014-15	242015-16	322016-17	382017-18	612018-19	77(c): Wealth-tax has been abolished with effect from 01.04.2016 and, therefore, CBDT does not any more capture information about the assets of an individual taxpayer. Hence, no information about the total assets of individuals disclosing a gross total income of more than Rs.100 crore (one billion rupees) is centrally available.******
