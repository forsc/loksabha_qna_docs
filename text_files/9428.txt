                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2887






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Construction of Toilets




Jaisiddeshwar Shivacharya Mahaswamiji














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of toilets constructed in Solapur district of Maharashtra during the last two years and the current financial year; and(b) the details of the work carried out in Solapur district under Swachh Bharat Mission (rural) during the last two years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) and (b)    The number of individual household latrines (IHHLs) constructed under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) in Solapur district of Maharashtra during the last two years and the current financial year, as per the data reported by the State on the online Integrated Management Information System (IMIS) as on 02.12.2019, are as under:Financial Year	No. of IHHLs constructed2017-18	1,12,3632018-19	3,6672019-20	11,002Total	1,27,032All the 1,135 villages of the district have declared themselves Open Defecation Free (ODF).
