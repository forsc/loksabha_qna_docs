                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2499






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






Filling up of Vacant Posts




Kalyan Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether since the last seven years (2014-2021) regular employment in railways has been slashed by 40 per cent and over 50 per cent jobs are lying vacant in different departments of the Ministry; b)	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government to fill up these posts in a time bound manner;c)	whether after the post-COVID situation the Government is unable to maintain the railway tracks and rakes on regular basis due to shortage of manpower; andd)	if so, the action taken/likely to be taken by the Government for improving railway security, safety and providing amenities to passengers?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a): 	No Sir.(b):	Does Not arise.(c):	No Sir.(d):	Does Not arise.*****
