                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

197






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






National Institutes of Technology




Vincent H Pala














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the funds allocated to each of the National Institutes of Technology (NITs);(b)	the current status of NIT Meghalaya vis-a-vis other branches;(c)	whether the Government has any specific plans to improve the condition of NIT Meghalaya; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d): 	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. * * * * * * *STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (A) TO (D) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 197 TO BE ANSWERED ON 02.12.2019 REGARDING “NATIONAL INSTITUTES OF TECHNOLOGY” ASKED BY SHRI VINCENT H. PALA.(a):  The funds allocated to each of the National Institutes of Technology (NITs) during last three financial years are enclosed at Annexure. (b):   NIT Meghalaya is at present functioning from its temporary campus located at Laitumkhrah, Shillong with following branches of Engineering under UG, PG and Ph.D. programmes :-Under Graduate (UG)	Post Graduate (PG)	Ph.D.Civil Engineering (CE)Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)Mechanical Engineering (ME)	CECSEECEEEMEChemistryMathematicsPhysics CSEECEEEEMEChemistryMathematicsPhysicsHumanitiesSocial Sciences Currently 930 number of students are enrolled in various branches of study in the Institute.(c) to (d):	State Government of Meghalaya had provided land at Sohra, Shillong for the establishment of the permanent campus of the Institute where construction of building works have already commenced and is under various stages of completion. The Ministry of Human Resource Development from time to time releases grants to NIT Meghalaya to meet their expenditure. The Ministry of HRD has also sanctioned the requisite number of faculty and non faculty positions to the Institute for its smooth functioning.*****
