                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

938






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Conversion of SHs into NHs in Karnataka




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has received proposals from the State Government of Karnataka for declaration of State Highways (SHs) as new National Highways (NHs) since 2014 and if so, the details thereof, year-wise ;(b)	the details of new NHs declared during the above period and in 2018-19 in Karnataka ;(c)	the length of State Highways approved “In-Principle” for their declaration as new NHs subject to outcome of their DPRs in Karnataka since 2014, year-wise; and   (d)	the status of construction of Sringeri-Bahehonnur-Nadipur and Kundapur-Agumbe-Sringeri Road Highways and Chitradurga-Mangalore National Highway via Mudigere ? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)	Yes Sir, 37 Proposals for declaration of about 6800 Km of State Highways as new NHs have been received from the State Government of Karnataka since 2014 to till date.  The year wise details of NHs declared since 2014 including  the year 2018-19 for the State of Karnataka are as follows:Year	Length of Roads  notified as NHs (in Km.)2014-15	1382015-16	702016-17	2592017-18	8892018-19 	132 (c)	The year-wise length of State Roads identified “IN-PRINCIPLE” for their declaration as new NHs subject to outcome of their DPRs in Karnataka since 2014 are as follows: Year	Length of In-Principal approved NHs  (in Km.)2014-15	Nil2015-16	35222016-17	29992017-18	882018-19	Nil(d)      The  roads i.e Sringeri- Balehonnur-Nadipur and Kundapur- Agumbe-Sringeri  Road are at DPR stage and their declaration as new NHs depend upon final out come of DPR, inter-se priority and availability of funds. The stretch between Chitradurga-Mangalore via Mudigere  is part of NH-73, NH-173 & NH-369 and out of total length of 303 km, works in a length of 28.8 km have been completed and balance length is in traffic worthy condition. *****
