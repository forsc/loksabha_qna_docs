                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2365






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






University in Maharashtra




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to set up any university or educationalinstitution in Palgher of Maharashtra; and(b) if so, the details in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (b) : Education being in the Concurrent List, creation of new institutions is theresponsibility of both Central and State Government. However, recognising the needfor central support for achieving the aims of access, equity and excellence bystrategically funding the State higher education system a Centrally SponsoredScheme Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) is being implemented.RUSA follows a bottom-up approach wherein States and Institutions submitproposals as per their recognised needs in the higher education sector. Theseproposals are submitted on the Challenge Level Funding (CLF) portal and these areevaluated on certain parameters based on predetermined qualification criteria andweighted scoring. In view of the above, no proposal for creation of University oreducational institution in Palgher of Maharashtra has been approved under RUSA.However, under RUSA, the State of Maharashtra has been approved a total amountof Rs. 790 Crores under various components such as Enhancing Quality andExcellence in select Universities, Infrastructure Grants to Colleges and Universities,Research and Innovation, Faculty Improvement, Creation of new Universities byclustering of colleges or upgradation of autonomous colleges etc.****
