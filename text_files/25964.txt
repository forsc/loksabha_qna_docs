                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1692






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Upgradation of Airstrips




Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak













                                                    Raksha Nikhil Khadse
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CIVIL AVIATIONbe pleased to state:-(a) whether the Government is undertaking the upgradation and modernisation of major airstrip in various States of the country especially in Maharashtra considering the increase in traffic;(b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(c) whether the Government is aware that inadequate length of runway of some airports in Maharashtra makes landing and take-off difficult for an aircraft; and(d) if so, the details and name of such airports thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) & (b): The State/ Union Territories - wise details of upgradation and modernization of airstrips undertaken for operation of aircraft category of ATR-72 and above are as follows:;Maharashtra - Kolhapur;Madhya Pradesh - JabalpurUttar Pradesh - Kushinagar, AyodhyaTamil Nadu - Tuticorin;Andhra Pradesh - Tirupati, Kadapa;Jharkhand - Deoghar;Jammu and Kashmir - JammuDadra & Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu - Diu(c) & (d): The available runway length are meeting the requirement of aircrafts currently operating from the licensed airports in Maharashtra. The details of operational airports of Maharashtra and the runway length along with the suitability of aircraft is attached as Annexure. ;
