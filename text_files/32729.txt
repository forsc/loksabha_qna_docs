                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

294






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Benefits Under MSME Projects




Sanjay Seth














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	the details of institutions and persons benefitted under the MSME projects in Ranchi Lok Sabha Constituency and the number of projects thereof; (b) 	the details of schemes being run to promote micro and small industries across the country; and (c)  	the details and the number of such projects/ institutions/persons of Jharkhand being provided assistance by the Government?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a):  	Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) is implementing various Khadi and Village Industries (KVI) schemes/programmes in the country including Ranchi Parliamentary constituency. The details of beneficiaries benefitted in Ranchi Parliamentary constituency under various KVI schemes/programmes:<pre>	Year	Name of Schemes	District	No. of beneficiaries benefitted2008-09 to 2021-22 (as on 24.01.22)	Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)	Ranchi	No. of Projects - 2141, Margin Money (MM) -Rs. 4697.53 lakh, Employment (in no.) - 171282018-19	Kumbhar Shashaktikaran Programmes 	Village- Boria, Dist.- Ranchi	20 pottery artisans trained and benefitted.2018-19	Honey Mission	Village- Bero,Dist.- Ranchi	25 beekeepers trained and provided Bee-boxes with live bee colonies2021-22	Tamarind Processing Industry (ABFPI)	Village- Angara,Dist.- Ranchi	06 SHGs (60 persons) has been proposed to distribute Tamarind Processing Equipments and Tools 		Village- Thakurgaon,Dist.- Ranchi	Registered Khadi Institutions in Ranchi District – 06 	Khadi Artisans- 383<pre/>(b): 	The main schemes of the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises for promoting Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises are Prime Minister’s Employment Generation programme (PMEGP), Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI), Khadi Vikas Yojana (KVY), Gramodyog Vikas Yojana (GVY), Micro & Small Enterprises Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP), Market Promotion Scheme (MPS), A Scheme for Promotion of Innovation, Rural Industries and Entrepreneurship(ASPIRE), Technology Centres/Extension Centres (TC/EC) etc., details of the schemes are available on the Ministry’s website i.e. https://msme.gov.in/sites/default/files/FlipbookEnglishSchemeBooklet.pdf(c):	The details of beneficiaries benefitted in Jharkhand State under various KVI programmes and Schemes are given at Annexure – I
