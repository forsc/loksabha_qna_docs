                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

838






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






FASTag Transaction




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the  proportion  of FASTag  transactions  out  of total  transactions  in  the  country;(b) the number of FASTag transactions taken place in the fiscal years 2018-19 and 2019-20 in the country, month  and  year-wise;  and(c)  the  steps  being  taken  by  the  Government  to incentivise  higher  uptake  of  FASTag  in  the  country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) The proportion of FASTag transactions at fee plazas of National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) from 15th January 2020 to 31st January 2020 is around 66% amount-wise.(b) The month-wise FASTag transactions for fiscal years 2018-19 and 2019-20 (upto December, 2019) is at Annexure.(c) Government is providing 2.5% cashback in the Financial Year (FY) 2019-20 for user fee payment through FASTag. FASTag awareness program is being conducted through various platforms such as social media, print media, hoardings, advertisements, etc. Multiple channels and Point of Sale (PoS) have been operating including e-commerce platforms for easy availability of FASTag to customers. In December 2019, Government has released TV awareness campaign on FASTag featuring famous film actor with an attempt to bring in awareness and behavioural change amongst users towards user fee payment.
