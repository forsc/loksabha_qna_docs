                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3980






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Anganwadi Workers




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the total number of Anganwadi workers and Anganwadi Helpers in the country, State/UT-wise, district-wise;  (b) 	the number of Anganwadi centres operational in Bijnor Parliamentary Constituency; and  (c) 	the criteria under which the Government makes payment against the expenditure incurred on services being provided by Anganwadi?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) & (b)    As on 30th September, 2020, there are 13,25,335 Anganwadi Workers (AWWs) and 11,81,152  Anganwadi Helpers (AWHs) working across the country. State/UT-wise details of AWWs and AWHs is annexed. However, district-wise and parliamentary constituency-wise information of Anganwadi Centres and AAWWs & AWHs is not maintained at central level. (c)  On the basis of requirement of funds furnished by the States/UTs, Annual Programme Implementation Plan (APIP) of States/UTs is finalized at the beginning of the financial year. Funds are released on the basis of APIP and as per expenditure incurred under Utilization Certificates and Statements of Expenditure by each State/UT.  *****  Annexure ANNEXURE REFRRED IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 3980 FOR 19.03.2021 REGARDING “ANGANWADI WORKERS” ASKED BY SHRI MALOOK NAGAR.
