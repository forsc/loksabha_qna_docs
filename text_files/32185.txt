                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3651






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Loans Given to Telangana




Anumula Revanth Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of loan given to Telangana by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), External agencies under Externally Aided Projects and refinancing institutions of the Government during the last five years, year-wise;(b)	the details of repayment of principal and interest by Telangana to these lending institutions during the last five years, year-wise;(c)	the details of total public debt of Telangana which is outstanding as of 30th November, 2021; and(d)	the estimated amount of principal and interest to be paid by Telangana towards the outstanding dues during the financial year 2021-22, 2022-23 and 2023-24, lending institution-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	(i)  The Reserve Bank of India has not provided any loans to the Government of Telangana. However, RBI facilitates financial accommodation in the form of Special Drawing Facility, Ways & Means Advance and Overdraft to supplement the temporary mismatches between receipts and payments. Further, in the last five (5) years, no loan was extended by any external agency or refinancing institution to the Government of Telangana.        (ii)  Additional Central Assistance for Externally Aided Projects released to Telangana for the Financial Year 2016-17 to 2021-22 (upto 13.12.2021) is annexed as Table-I.(b)	Principal repayment and interest paid during last five (5) years on outstandings, upto 12.11.2021 is annexed as Table-II.(c)	The Public debt of Telangana as on November 30, 2021 stands at ` 2,37,747 crore (` 2,34,912 crore internal debt and ` 2,835 crore external debt).(d)	The estimated amount of Principal and interest payable by Telangana is annexed as Table-III.**** ANNEX.TABLE-I(` crore)Additional Central Assistance - Externally Aided Projects released to the State of Telangana        Year	Loan	Grant2016-17	956.12	30.722017-18	636.68	0.002018-19	433.36	0.002019-20	207.46	0.002020-21	-72.66	0.002021-22 (Upto 13-12-2021)	0.00	0.00TABLE-II(A)    Repayment of Loans by Government of Telangana to Multilateral / Bilateral  Institutions during 2017-18 to 2021-22 (upto 12.11.2021)(`crore)Name of Multilateral / Bilateral Institutions	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22Government of Japan (GoJP)	0.00	25.86	0.96	37.42	29.45International Bank for Reconstruction and Development (IBRD)	31.73	44.23	50.28	65.32	32.06International Development Association (IDA)	13.79	17.07	17.32	10.57	6.15Grand Total	45.52	87.16	68.56	113.31	67.66Note: This includes bifurcated loan liability consequent upon creation of Telangana, as per extant arrangements.
