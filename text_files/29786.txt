                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1442






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






National Cooperative University




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya













                                                    Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government proposes to establish a National Co-operative University;(b)	If so, the details thereof;(c)	Whether the Government has received any request seeking upgradation of the Vaikunth  Mehta National Institute of Cooperative Management (VAMNICOM), Pune into a Cooperative University;(d)	If so, the action taken thereon; and(e)	If not, the reasons therefor and the time likely to be taken for its early resolution?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COOPERATION???????? ?????? (SHRI AMIT SHAH)(a) & (b):     There is a need for establishment of a Cooperative University to firmly deepen the foundations of cooperative movement in the country. If any institution interested in cooperative sector comes forward for taking lead for creation of such university, the same may be considered.(c) to (e): 	Requests have been received for establishment of cooperative universities, including converting VAMNICOM into a cooperative university. Government has not yet taken any view in this regard.*****
