                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

404






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Teaching through Native Language




H. Vasanthakumar













                                                    T.N. Prathapan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the committee headed by Dr. K. Kasturirangan has submitted its report on DraftNew Education Policy, 2019 and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether an alternative version of NEP-2019 without the mandatory Hindi clause is likelyto be made and if so, the details thereof;(c) the action taken by the Government to allay the fears of non-Hindi speaking States and ifnot, the reasons therefor;(d) whether the Government is ready to make three language formula more flexible to avoidany sort of compulsions and if so, the details thereof;(e) the action taken by the Government to promote learning through native languages inschools along with language learning in the foundation classes as more fun and interactive;and(f) whether the Government has noticed that such impositions of monolithic culture orlanguage is likely to harm our pluralistic culture and legacy and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) : The Committee for the Draft National Education Policy under the Chairmanshipof Dr. K. Kasturirangan has submitted the Draft National Education Policy 2019 (Draft NEP- 2019) to the Ministry on 31st May 2019. There is no alternative version of NEP – 2019.Draft NEP 2019 is accessible athttps://mhrd.gov.in/sites/upload_files/mhrd/files/Draft_NEP_2019_EN_Revised.pdf and also atinnovate.mygov.in platform at https://innovate.mygov.in/list-nep/.(c) to (f) Of the many recommendations made in the draft, one proposal is to focus oneducation in the local language/mother tongue; multilingualism and the power of language.The Government is committed for equal development and promotion of all Indianlanguages. There will be no imposition of any language in educational institutions, nordiscrimination against any language. Further, the various sub-sections of Section 4.5 outlinethe importance of learning more languages for enhancing the cognitive abilities of children,bilingual approach for those whose language is different from the primary medium ofinstruction, exposure to three or more languages in schools and standardising sign language.Further, the draft proposes for flexibility within the same regarding the choice of languages.**********
