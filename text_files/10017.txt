                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3513






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Scheme under Animal Husbandry




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the details of various schemes being implemented by the Ministry;b)	whether in view of the fact that many Tribal people reside in Assam and there is vast scope for poultry, piggery, goat farming in the State, any schemes is being implemented by the Government for promotion of these occupations in the States, the details thereof;c)	the details of funds allocated and spent under various schemes during the last three years, State-wise including Assam;d)	whether providing veterinary service/treatment is a big problem in Assam and if so, whether the Government is taking necessary steps in this regard; ande)	the details of veterinary hospitals in the country, location-wise along with the details of the steps being taken by the Government for improving veterinary hospitals?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING                                                                  (DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	to (c) In order to complement and supplement the efforts of the States/UTs  for development and promotion of animal husbandry and dairy sectors thereby doubling the income of farmers, the Government is implementing various schemes to enhance production and productivity of bovines & small ruminants (sheep & goat), Piggery & Poultry across the country including Assam namely:I.	Rashtriya Gokul MissionII.	National Programme for Dairy DevelopmentIII.	National Dairy Plan-IIV.	Dairy Entrepreneurship Development SchemeV.	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development FundVI.	National Livestock MissionVII.	Livestock Health and Disease Control (Including National Animal Disease Control Programme (NADCP) for control of Foot & Mouth Disease and Brucellosis)	In addition, National Animal Disease Control Programme (NADCP) for control of Foot & Mouth Disease and Brucellosis is a new scheme for control of Foot & Mouth Disease and Brucellosis disease with 100 % central support to States with a financial outlay of Rs. 13,343.00 crore for five years (2019-20 to 2023-24) by vaccinating 100% cattle, buffalo, sheep, goat and pig population for FMD and 100% bovine female calves of 4-8 months of age for brucellosis. The details of fund allocated/released under these schemes during the last three years, State-wise including Assam are at Annexure-I.(d) & (e) The treatment of animals is a state subject. The Government is supplementing the efforts of State Government to establish new veterinary hospitals and dispensaries also strengthen/equip the existing ones as well as run mobile veterinary ambulances under the component of Establishment and strengthening of existing Veterinary Hospitals and Dispensaries (ESVHD) of LH & DC scheme. The list of the veterinary hospitals state-wise is attached at Annexure-II (Source: Basic Animal Husbandry and Fisheries Statistics, 2019)***
