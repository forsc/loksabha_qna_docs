                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

613






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Solar Parks Scheme




 Bhavana Gawali (Patil)













                                                    Krupal Balaji Tumane
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government has enhanced the capacity target under the Solar Parks Scheme;  (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government proposes to set up more number of solar parks in Maharashtra to achieve the target; and (d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) &(b) The Government has enhanced the target for setting up of solar parks from 20,000 MW to 40,000 MW capacity under the Scheme for “Development of Solar Parks and Ultra Mega Solar Power Projects”.(c) &(d)  Three proposals have been received from Government of Maharashtra for setting up solar parks. All the three proposals have been granted in-principle approval by the Government. The details are as follows: (i)	Dondaicha Solar Park (250 MW)(ii)	Patoda Solar Park (500 MW)(iii)	Sai Guru Solar Park (500 MW)*****
