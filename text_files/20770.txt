                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1930






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Basic Facilities in Anganwadi Centres




Pragya Singh Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















†1930:  SADHVI PRAGYA SINGH THAKUR:   Will the Minister of WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT be pleased to   state: (a)	whether it has come to the notice of the Government that many Anganwadi Centres lack basic amenities such as clean toilets and access to clean drinking water across the country and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including Madhya Pradesh;(b)	the total rental amount paid for running AWCs in rented premises during the last three years;(c)	the percentage of rental amount paid out of the total expenditure incurred on  Anganwadi centres during the said period, State/UT-wise including Madhya Pradesh;(d)	the policy in place regarding renting premises for providing Anganwadi facilities; and (e)	the provisions of penalty for the Anganwadi Centres for not adhering to the said policy? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (c):   Out of 13,86,990 operational Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) across the country, 4,36,613 AWCs do not have toilets facilities and 2,20,967 AWCs do not have drinking water facilities. State-wise details are placed at Annexure-I. 3,58,446 AWCs as detailed in Annex-II are run in rented premises. The details of the total amount paid as rent during last three years are as follows:(Rs. in lakhs)Financial Year	Total amount paid as towards rent2017-18	34,788.902018-19	49,220.932019-20	51,707.83  	 The  State/UT-wise Statement regarding the percentage of rental amount paid out of the total expenditure incurred on Anganwadi centres is placed at Annexure-II. Ministry has had sustained engagements with States to ensure that drinking water and infrastructural needs are met through convergence with the Jal Shakti Mission and schemes under Ministry of Rural Development.(d) to (e):      Anganwadi Centres not having their own or community buildings may be opened in rented premises with available space of at least 600 sq. ft. with adequate infrastructure facilities like toilet and drinking water.
