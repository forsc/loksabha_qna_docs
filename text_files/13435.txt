                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2161






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Women in Defence Services




Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel













                                                    Devendrappa Y
                                                








                                                    Khagen Murmu
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the number of women employed in the three wings of the defence forces, service-wise;(b) whether there has been an increase in the number of women joining the defence forces during the last three years and the current year;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the details of the schemes being introduced by the Government to incentivise women to increase their participation in the defence forces?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  The current number of women in the three armed forces is as under:Indian Army	6892Indian Air Force	1878Indian Navy	685(b) & (c):   Year-wise induction details of women in the three armed forces during the past three years and current year is as follows:Year	2017	2018	2019	2020Indian Army	949	819	364	102Indian Air Force	109	86	77	00Indian Navy	57	38	54	18 (in progress)(d)  Induction of women as soldiers in Military Police.  1700 such posts have been sanctioned to be spread over 17 years. 101 women have started their training in January this year at Corps of Military Police Training Centre, Bangalore.	All the branches/streams of Indian Air Force (IAF) are open to women officers.  Opportunities for career in IAF are publicised through print/electronic media in accordance with the policy on ‘Induction and Empowerment of Women in Armed Forces’.  The Government has commenced a scheme for induction of women Short Service Commission (SSC) officers in the fighter stream of the flying branch in the year 2015.  The IAF has also formulated the requisite gender neutral Air Hqr. Human Resource Policies (HRPs) enabling consideration of SSC officers for permanent commission in all ventures of the IAF.	Indian Navy has also progressively increased the avenues for women to join the Naval Force from 3 branches in 1992 to 11 branches in 2019.*****
