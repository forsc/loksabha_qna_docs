                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4226






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Registered Companies




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the number of new companies registered in the country in the current fiscal year; (b) the number of companies closed in the country during the said period; and (c) the increase/decrease in revenue to profit ratio of the companies  as per the MCA21 database?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE AND CORPORATE AFFAIRS	                                      (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)                                            ????? ??? ???????? ????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? 	                                     (???? ?????? ???? ?????)(a): 1,38,051 number of new companies registered in the current from April 2020 to February 2021. (b): 10,113 number of Companies have been struck off by invoking the Provisions of section 248 of the Companies Act, 2013 from April 2020 to February 2021.(c): The financial statements are filed with Ministry of Corporate Affairs for the statutory compliances to maintain the registry for public inspection and all documents are available at www.mca.gov.in. The financial statements are kept as filed and the financial ratios, like revenue to profit is not calculated. The number of filings may be different from year to year due to delays/ defaults in filing. Thus such financial ratios across the whole universe of filings are not comparable. *****
