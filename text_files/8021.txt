                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1636






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Revival of Micro Enterprises




Queen Oja














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Micro Enterprises which are traditionally manufacturing various useful important items of brass and bell metal but due to want of funds and infrastructure, the enterprises are going to disappear from Assamese Society and as a result of which upcoming generation would not be getting such brass and bell metallic items in the society, if so, the details thereof;(b)	 whether any steps have been taken by the Union Government to revive these Micro Enterprises for the larger interest of public in Assam in particular and North Eastern Region in general;  (c)	  if so, the details thereof and the steps taken in this regard; and   (d)	  the time by which it is likely to be done.






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) to (d):	 The Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises, has the following Schemes for supporting Micro enterprises including brass & bell metal sector:-i.	Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme  ii.	Credit Guarantee Fund Scheme for Micro and Small Enterprisesiii.	Interest Subvention Scheme for Incremental credit to MSMEs iv.	Procurement & Marketing Support (PMS) v.	International Cooperation scheme  vi.	Market Promotion and Development Assistancevii.	Scheme for Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI)   viii.	A scheme for Promotion of Innovation, Rural Industry & Entrepreneurship (ASPIRE)    ix.	Credit Linked Capital Subsidy and Technology Up-gradation Scheme (CLCS-TUS)    x.	Prime Minister''s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)
