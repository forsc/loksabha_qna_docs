                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1886






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






AYUSH Medical Colleges




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state:(a) the details of AYUSH medical colleges in the country and their intake capacity;(b) whether the Government has taken note of the propaganda that medicinal facilities in AYUSH stream are unscientific; and(c) if so, the details thereof and steps taken to verify such claims and the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF AYURVEDA, YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(AYUSH) (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a): The requisite information may please be seen in the statement attached at Annexure ‘A’. (b) & (c) The Ministry of AYUSH has taken cognizance of various misleading media reports and issued clarifications from time to time. These clarifications are available on (Press Information Bureau) PIBs website.
