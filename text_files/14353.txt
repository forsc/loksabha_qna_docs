                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3139






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






NH Projects in Kerala




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details and the present status of various ongoing projects of the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) in Kerala along with the time frame set for their completion;(b)  	whether it is a fact that many of these projects are delayed and resulting in cost-overruns and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor; and(c)  	the steps taken by the Government for their timely completion?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) Currently there are six projects being executed through National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) with an aggregate length of about 95 km in the State of Kerala, out of which, two projects have been delayed beyond scheduled completion period due to reasons such as financial crisis with Concessionaire/contractor, non-availability of encumbrance free land, public protest, change in scope etc. Regular review meetings are conducted to check the bottlenecks in the projects. In case of any issues with any departments, the field officers take up the issues with the concerned department.*****
