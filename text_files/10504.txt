                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI

STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

344






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Dams and Water Projects




Vishnu Dutt Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and the number of dams and water projects sanctioned by the Government under Bundelkhand Special Package provided for development of Bundelkhand region; (b) the quantum of funds sanctioned for dams and water projects under the said package particularly for Madhya Pradesh; (c) the number of projects completed and the time limit fixed for completing the remaining projects in Bundelkhand region; and (d) whether the Government has formulated any concrete plan to complete these projects at the earliest and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF JAL SHAKTI  (SHRI GAJENDRA SINGH SHEKHAWAT)(a) to (d) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.***** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF STARRED QUESTION  NO. †*344 TO BE ANSWERED ON 12.12.2019 IN LOK SABHA REGARDING “DAMS AND WATER PROJECTS” ASKED BY SHRI VISHNU DATT SHARMA HON’BLE M.P., LOK SABHA.(a) to (d)  The Central Government approved a Special Package for Bundelkhand region during November, 2009 amounting to Rs.7266 Crore (Rs. 3760 crore for Madhya Pradesh and Rs. 3506 crore for Uttar Pradesh). The Central Government further approved, an Additional Central Assistance of Rs. 200 Crore (Rs.100 Crore each for U.P. and M.P.) during May, 2011 to provide drinking water in Bundelkhand region. Central Assistance of Rs.6257.35 Crore (UP - Rs.3107.87 crore; MP- Rs 3149.48 crore) has been released from 2009-10 to 31.03.2018.
