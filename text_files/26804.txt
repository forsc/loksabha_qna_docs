                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2472






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






MSP of Redgram




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   whether MSP is not being fixed for redgram;(b)   whether redgram farmers from Tandur and other redgram-producing areas have been         demanding for fixation of MSP for redgram; and(c)   if so, the constraints faced by the Ministry in announcing MSP for redgram?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????      (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):     Government fixes Minimum Support Prices (MSP) for 22 mandated agricultural crops including red gram (tur/arhar) & gram every year, on the basis of the recommendations of the Commission for Agricultural Costs and Prices (CACP), after considering the views of State Governments and Central Ministries/Departments concerned and other stakeholders consultation. Details of MSP for red gram (tur/arhar) and gram fixed by the government is given below:                                                         (in Rs per quintal)Sl. No.	Commodity	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-221.	Red Gram (Tur/Arhar)	5675	5800	6000	63002.	Gram	4620	4875	5100	-b) & (c):   Government has been fixing MSP for red gram (tur/arhar) since 1978-79 and that of gram since 1975-76.******
