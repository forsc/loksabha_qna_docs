                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2787






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Mines in J&K




Hasnain Masoodi














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of major mineral mines and minor mineral mines operational in Jammu & Kashmir; and(b) the details of NOC’s granted by various Ministries/Departments for mining leases in Jammu & Kashmir?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a) As per the provisions of the Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) (MMDR) Act, 1957 and rules made there under, the State Governments grant the mineral concessions for the minerals located within their respective boundaries. As per the information provided by Indian Bureau of Mines, the details of the operational mines of major minerals in Jammu & Kashmir are at Annexure. For minor minerals, all policy and legislation for grant of mineral concessions, regulation and administration is covered in the jurisdiction of the State Government under the MMDR Act and the Rules framed thereunder. The details of operational mines of minor minerals are not centrally maintained.(b) It is mandatory for all lessees to follow the stipulated rules and regulations in regard to carrying out mining operations.
