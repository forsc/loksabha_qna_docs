                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

103






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Educational Loans




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the educational loans provided by the nationalized banks during each of the last three years, State/UTs-wise particularly in Bihar;(b)	whether it is a fact that the numbers of educational loans have reduced drastically in recent past and if so, the reasons therefor;(c)	whether the Government is contemplating any steps to increase the provision of educational loans; and(d)	 if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this direction?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a)	to (d): Disbursement of educational loans by Public Sector Banks (PSBs) has increased from  Rs 11,650 crore in Financial Year (FY) 2016-17 to Rs 13,638 crore in FY 2017-18 and further to Rs 15,126 crore in FY 2018-19. Details of the educational loans disbursed by PSBs during each of the last three financial years and till December, 2019 in the current financial year, State/UT-wise, are given in the Annexure. To facilitate easy processing and disbursal of loans, Government has launched a web-based portal namely, Vidya Lakshmi Portal. Students can view, apply and track their education loan applications online by accessing the portal.*******
