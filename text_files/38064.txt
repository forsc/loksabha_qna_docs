                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4663






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Four-Laning of Agar-Ujjain NH




Mahendra Singh Solanky














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to extend the existing four-	lane National Highway from Agra to Ujjain; and(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the length and extent up to 	which the said road would be extended?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (b)   Agra to Ujjain stretch (around 60 Km) is part of Ujjain to Jhalawar NH 552G (133.575 Km) and current lane configuration of the stretch is intermediate or 2 lane except 4 lane in Agra city portion. At present, the work of widening of Ujjain-Jhalawar road to 2 lane with paved shoulder is progressing with physical progress of 46%. *****
