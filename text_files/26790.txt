                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2421






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






FPOs linked with NABARD




 Bholanath (B.P. Saroj)













                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs) registered in States of 	Uttar Pradesh and Maharashtra so far including the number of such FPOs out 	of the said FPOs which have been linked with National Bank for Rural 	Development (NABARD) for getting necessary assistance; and(b) 	the total number of FPOs in Uttar Pradesh and Maharashtra which have been 	provided credit linkage through banks and the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	As informed by the Government of Uttar Pradesh, a total of 571 Farmer Producer Organizations(FPOs) have been registered in the State of Uttar Pradesh out of which a total of 282 FPOs are linked with National Bank for Rural Development (NABARD). 		Further, as informed by Government of Maharashtra, a total of 5320 Farmer Producer Companies have been registered till 31.05.2021. The National Bank for Rural Development (NABARD) has promoted a total of 352 FPOs in Maharashtra out of which 280 FPOs are registered. (b):	 As per the details received from various implementing agencies, a total of 36 FPOs in Uttar Pradesh and 05 FPOs in Maharashtra have been provided credit linkage by NABARD. Further a total of 03 FPOs in Uttar Pradesh and 30 FPOs in Maharashtra is provided credit linkage by Small Farmers Agribusiness Consortium (SFAC).  *****
