                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1657






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Apprenticeship Ambedded Degree/Diploma




Jaskaur Meena














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) whether the Government proposes to start apprenticeship embedded degree/diplomacourse in 150 higher educational institutions across the country and the details thereof andthe reasons therefor;b) the details of estimated funds for this purpose and the targets set in this regard;c) whether the Government have selected these higher educational institutions and if so, theState-wise details thereof;d) whether the Government have selected higher educational institutions of Rajasthan alsoand if so, the details thereof, district-wise and if not, the reasons therefor; ande) whether the Government have made any provision for the Scheduled Tribe (STs) underthis scheme and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): Yes, Sir. The Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) is working alongwith Sector Skill Councils (SSCs) under National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) todevelop courses and curricula for Apprenticeship/Internship embeddeddegree/diplomaprogrammes with core focus on development of knowledge, skills, aptitudeand on-job training with Industry exposure. These steps are being taken to enhance joborientation and employability of graduates/undergraduates students.(b) to (e): SSCs are working with Autonomous Universities/Institutions and StateGovernments for introducing the Apprenticeship/Internship embedded degree/ diplomaprogrammes. MHRD is working along with the SSCs to mobilize the Higher EducationalInstitutions to adopt the courses which are being developed by them. As a result, some of thesectors like Logistics & retail have commenced degree Apprenticeship programmes in 15institutions across the country. The detail of institutions is annexed. Further, variousinstitutions have expressed interest to take up the courses in near future. Media &Entertainment Sector Skill Council (MESC) have signed MoUs with 150 institutions toconduct skill oriented BSc., BBA, BVoc. Courses in media and entertainment sector. Also,Logistics Sector Council (LSC) has aligned with about 65 institutions to commenceapprenticeship embedded degree, diploma and engineering programmes. The State-wisedetails of institutions that will commence apprenticeship/ internship embeddeddegree/diploma programmes can be known only by the beginning of the Academic Year2020-21. The applicability of reservation policy on Institutions/ Universities, which willtake up these apprenticeship/ internship based degree/diploma courses will be as pernorms under which they have been established.*****
