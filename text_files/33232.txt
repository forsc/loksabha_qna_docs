                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

800






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Vacant Posts in IIT/NIT




Vijay Baghel













                                                    Sunil Kumar Soni
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of posts of Chairmen and others vacancies in the governing 	body of Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) and National Institute of 	Technology (NIT) in the country;(b) whether the Government has set any timeframe to fill these vacant 	posts; 	and (c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a):	At present, 08 positions of Chairmen in IITs and 22 positions of Chairmen in NITs are vacant. Further, 03 posts of Directors in IITs and 10 posts of Directors in NITs are vacant. Out of these order/offer of appointment have been issued with respect to 01 IIT and 10 NITs.(b) & (c): The nomination/selection of Chairmen and other posts is a continuous process. *****
