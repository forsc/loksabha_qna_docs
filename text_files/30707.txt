                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2247






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Refusal of Treatment of Cancer Patients from Private Hospitals by AIIMS




 Sunil Kumar Pintu














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the fact that All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi refuses to treat such cancer patients whose treatment is undergoing in any other private hospitals and they are not in position to take follow up treatment on account of financial crunch in those private hospitals;(b) if so, whether the Government proposes to pass such order so that the AIIMS, New Delhi could treat such patients on a mandatory basis; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) 	to (c)	All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi does not refuse any case of any treatable cancer, whether treated in any private hospitals or any other Government Hospital in Delhi or elsewhere.  However, there may be some waiting list because of large number of patients seeking treatment in the Institute, but patients requiring emergency radiotherapy are entertained on priority basis. *****
