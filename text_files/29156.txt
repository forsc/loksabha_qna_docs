                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

757






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Loss due to Floods




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the economic losses and deaths due to floods in the year 2021; (b) whether it is true that the economic losses due to floods have been increasing during the last five years and if so, the details thereof; (c) the details of the number of people in relief camps due to flooding in 2021, State-wise; (d) whether it is true that the Government’s share in funding of flood management programmes has reduced from 75 per cent to 50 per cent for general States and if so, the reasons for the same; (e) whether the Government has any plans to increase its funding share in flood management schemes and providing adequate budgetary support for the States; and (f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU)(a) to (c) Central Water Commission compiles the yearly flood damage data provided by the respective State Governments subsequent to conclusion of flood period. The data for the year 2020 is yet to be confirmed by the State Governments. The economic losses due to floods do not follow an overall increasing trend during the last five years (including the tentative figures of 2020). The statement showing damage due to floods/ heavy rains during the last five years (including the tentative figures of 2020) is given in Annexure-I. (d) to (f) : The funding pattern for flood management projects under Flood Management  component of Flood Magement and Border Area Programme during the XII Plan period and thereafter has been kept as 50 % (Centre): 50 % (State) for general States.
