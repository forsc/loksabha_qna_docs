                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1509






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






MGNREGS in Rajasthan




Subhash Chandra Baheria














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of people provided employment under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) during the last financial year 2019-20 in Rajasthan;(b) 	the number of people above 70 years therein;(c) 	the number of people above 70 years who were employed for 50 working days;(d) 	the details thereof, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)  The total number of people provided employment in Rajasthan under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA) during the FY 2019-20 is   80,59,270.(b)  The total number of persons above the age of 70 years provided employment in Rajasthan under Mahatma Gandhi NREGA during the FY 2019-20 is 2,60,030.(b)  The total number of persons above the age of 70 year provided 50 days of employment in Rajasthan under Mahatma Gandhi NREGA during the FY 2019-20 is 86,262. (d)  District-wise details are given at Annexure.***** AnnexureAnnexure referred in reply to part (d) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.1509 dated 20.09.2020
