                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2052






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Promotion of Floriculture




Rahul Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the steps being taken to increase production and promote adoption of high yielding varieties of flowers, aromatics and medicinal plants in India; (b) 	the steps being taken to develop infrastructure for post-harvest management and provide support to flower growers; (c) 	the details of funds allocated for programmes to boost floriculture in the last three years, State-wise; (d) 	whether the Government has identified floriculture hubs/clusters in different regions; and (e) 	if so, whether any cluster is proposed to be developed in Wayanad, given its untapped floricultural potential?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a):	 Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare promotes production and adoption of high yielding varieties of flowers and aromatic crops through centrally sponsored schemes like Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) & Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY). All States and UTs are covered under these schemes. Various research institutes under the control of Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) provide technical support to farmers for cultivation of flowers, aromatics and medicinal plants. Govt. is also promoting technologies and production of flowers through various demonstrations and trainings under Center of Excellences in different regions of the country. The list of such Centres of Excellence established for floriculture is given in annexure-I. The cultivation of medicinal plants is being promoted through National Medicinal Plant Board (NMPB), under Ministry of Ayush.Cond…2/--2- (b):	Under Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH), financial assistance is provided for creation of post harvest management (PHM) infrastructure for all horticultural produce including flowers. The component is demand/entrepreneur driven for which credit linked back ended subsidy at the rate 35% to 55% of admissible project cost is provided through respective State Horticulture Missions.(c):		 The details of funds allocated for last three years under Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) for horticulture development including floriculture is given at annexure-II.(d):     No.   (e):	 No cluster has been proposed in this region.
