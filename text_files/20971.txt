                                                    Nishikant  Dubey
                                                








                                                    Shankar Lalwani
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government has received any proposal from the various State Governments for cultural development during the last three years;(b)  if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Gujarat, Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh and Jharkhand;(c)  the current status of the proposals, proposal-wise and State-wise along with the details of proposals which are pending for approval and the reasons for pendency;(d)   the funds sanctioned/allocated for cultural development during the last three years, State-wise including above stated States; and(e) the various schemes, programmes initiated to protect and preserve the dying and vanishing tribal and folk art and culture along with the works undertaken by the Government for development of culture in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	Yes, Sir.(b)  to (d): The state-wise details of last three years are given at Annexure.(e)  Yes Sir, Ministry of Culture deals with the tangible and Intangible Cultural Heritage of India. This heritage encompass, inter alia, tribal folk, art and Culture, literature, archives, library, performing arts museum including music dance and drama, visual arts in the form of paintings, sculptures and graphics. All activities of the Ministry relating to promotion, preservation and conservation of the Cultural Heritage of the Country are implemented through a network of two attached offices, 6 subordinated offices and 34 autonomous organizations under its control and number of schemes directly operated by the Ministry for promotion and dissemination of Art & Culture.ANNEXURE REFFERED TO IN REPLY TO  PART (b) TO (d) OF LSUSQ NO. 2203 FOR 08.03.2021 REGARDING‘PROPOSAL RECEIVED FROM STATE GOVERNMENTS FOR CULTURAL DEVELOPMENT’DETAILS OF THE PROPOSALS RECEIVED UNDER SCHEME OF ALLIED CULTURAL ACTIVITIES & GANDHI HERITAGE SITE MISSION DURING THE LAST THREE YEARS                                                                                                                         (Rs.in Lakhs)Sl.No.	Details of Proposals received from State Govts.
