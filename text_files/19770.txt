                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1112






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






Proposal Received from Odisha for Infrastructural Development




Anubhav Mohanty














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received proposals for infrastructure development from the State Government of Odisha under the Financial Assistance for Tagore Cultural Complexes (TCC);(b)	if so, the details thereof(c)	the quantum of funds allocated under the scheme to specific agencies/institutions in the State of Odisha; and(d)	the details of all such funds allocated in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d) Yes Sir, in the year 2010-11 & 2011-12. The details of proposals for infrastructure development from the State Government of Odisha are attached at Annexure-I.ANNEXURE-IAnnexure referred to in reply to Part (a) to (d) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 1112 for 08.02.2021 DETAILS OF PROPOSALS RECEIVED FROM THE STATE GOVERNMENT OF ODISHA FOR INFRASTRUCTURAL DEVELOPMENT. Sl.No.	Name of the proposal    Name of Project Authority	Amount released/ allocated & purpose1.	Construction of Tagore Cultural Complex at Puri. Tourism & Culture (Culture) Department, Government of Odisha.	Rs.5,00,000/- for preparation of DPR.2.	Renovation of Rabindra Mandap Complex under TCC Scheme at Bhubaneswar. 	Tourism & Culture (Culture) Department, Government of Odisha.	Rs.5,00,000/- for preparation of DPR.3.	Construction of Rabindra Kala Katak under TCC Scheme at Cuttack. 	Tourism & Culture (Culture) Department, Government of Odisha. 	Rs.5,00,000/- for preparation of DPR.*****
