                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5006






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Construction of Houses in Maharashtra




Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav













                                                     Bhavana Gawali (Patil)
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of houses built in Maharashtra especially in 	Parbhani, Mumbai and Washim-Yavatmal during the last three 	years, city/project/urban housing scheme-wise; (b) 	the details of the cities in Maharashtra where the construction 	works of houses are currently going on, project/urban housing 	scheme-wise; (c) 	the details of beneficiaries under these schemes, city/project-	wise; (d) 	whether there has been any delay in the construction works 	related to these housing projects; and (e) 	if so, the reasons therefor including the details of the pending 	projects?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                 (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	The details about the number of houses built in Maharashtra especially in Parbhani, Mumbai and Washim-Yavatmal during the last three years, city/project/urban housing scheme-wise are annexed as Annexure – I.(b)&(c)The details of the cities in Maharashtra where the construction works of houses are currently going on, project/urban housing scheme-wise and the details of beneficiaries under three verticals of  PMAY-U are annexed as Annexure-II, Annexure III and Annexure-IV.(d) & (e) The houses sanctioned under PMAY-U are targeted to be completed by the year 2022.                                  *****
