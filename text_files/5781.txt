                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5435






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






NH Projects in Chhattisgarh




Vijay Baghel














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	the details of National Highway (NH) projects under execution in Chhattisgarh;(b)	the details of NH projects that have been completed in the current year along with the funds sanctioned/utilised for each of these projects; and(c)	the details of projects that have been delayed along with the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c)	There are 25 projects with a length of 985 km under execution in Chhattisgarh. None of the National Highway project has been completed in the current year. 17 projects have been delayed mainly due to land acquisition, utility shifting, tree cutting and poor performance of contractors etc.*****
