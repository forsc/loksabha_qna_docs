                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2292






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Maintaining of Heritage Nature of Lakshdweep  and Minicoy Islands




Thirumaa Valavan Thol














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that the Islands of Lakshadweep and Minicoy, is a land of only about 32.62 sq km and an icon of exclusive heritage zone, which if spoiled with non-native settlements cannot be restored back to original status; and(b)	if so, the plans of the Government to maintain the islands of Lakshadweep and Minicoy with its exclusive heritage nature?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)& (b) Archaeological Survey of India has conducted some explorations and excavations in Lakshadweep. No information is available about damage to Cultural heritage. *****
