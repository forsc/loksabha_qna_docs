                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3047






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Survivors of Gender Based Violence




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)   	whether the Government is considering to set up One Stop Centres across 10 missions around the world to provide assistance to Indian women who are survivors of gender based violence;(b)   	if so the details thereof;(c)   	whether it is also true that this proposal is being implemented in view of the large number of complaints received from overseas Indian women; and(d)   	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (d): The Indian Missions across the world have provisions for assisting distressed Indian women including survivors of gender based violence. However, to augment such facilities, the Empowered Committee (EC) under the Framework for Nirbhaya Fund has appraised a proposal to set up facilities in Missions/ Posts in countries having significant proportion of diaspora such as Bahrain, Kuwait, Oman (Muscat), Qatar (Doha), UAE (Dubai), Saudi Arabia (Jeddah and Riyadh), and at Australia, Singapore and Canada (Toronto) on the lines of One Stop Centres (OSCs) for the welfare of Indian women.  *****
