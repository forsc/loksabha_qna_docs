                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1792






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Accessibility of Solar Bulbs




Diya Kumari














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the list of programmes run by the Government to make renewable energy like solar bulbs, accessible to poor households; and (b) if so, the details thereof with special reference to Rajasthan?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a)& (b) The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy is implementing the following schemes to make renewable energy accessible to individual household including poor households in the country, including in the State of Rajasthan: (i) Rooftop Solar (RTS) Programme Ph-II where Central Financial Assistance (CFA) upto 40% of the benchmark cost is provided for installation of RTS plant upto 3 kW capacity and CFA up to 20% of the benchmark cost for RTS plant of capacity beyond 3 kW and up to 10 kW.(ii) Under the New National Biogas and Organic Manure Programme, CFA is provided for setting up of biogas plants of size from 1 cubic metre to 25 cubic metre. The CFA for installation of small biogas plant varies from Rs. 7500/- per plant of  size1 cubic metre  to Rs. 35,000/- per plant of size 25 cubic metre. *****
