                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4764






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Distance Learning Courses




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre













                                                    Devji Mansingram Patel
                                                








                                                    Pratima Bhoumik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the UGC has cancelled the recognition of certain distance learning courses offeredby State as well Central Universities in the recent past;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor; and(c) the steps taken by the Government to ensure that the students are not inconvenienced due tothe decision of the UGC?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): As per the advise received by University Grants Commission (UGC) from Departmentof Agricultural Research & Education and Indian Council of Agricultural Research & All IndiaCouncil for Technical Education (AICTE), some of the programmes of Central/State/State OpenUniversities/Deemed to be Universities in Agriculture and Agricultural Extension & Hospitalityand Hotel Administration at Undergraduate level have been stopped by UGC to run throughODL mode from the academic year beginning from the July, 2019. Further, UGC has alsoplaced some of the programmes of State/State Open Universities on hold for failing to meet theoverall academic facilities in such universities. A list of Central/State/State OpenUniversities/Deemed Universities whose some of the programmes have been withdrawn or puton hold is placed at Annexure-I.(c) : UGC has already issued instructions to the concerned Universities that the interest of thestudents already admitted in such courses upto the academic year 2018-19 will be protected byproviding all necessary support to these students till they complete their courses/programmes.****
