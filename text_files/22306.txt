                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3378






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Burnt to Death Cases




Kunwar Danish Ali














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of rape and burnt to death cases registered during the last five years in the country, especially in Uttar Pradesh; and(b)	the number of culprits arrested and punished during the said period, State-wise, especially in Uttar pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b)	National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) compiles and publishes statistical data on crimes in its publication “Crime in India”. Published reports are available till the year 2019. Specific data on cases registered for ‘rape and burnt to death’ is not centrally maintained. However, as per published information, details of cases registered, persons arrested and convicted for murder with rape/gang rape for the years 2017, 2018 and 2019 (data maintained only since 2017 for this category) are at Annexure.*****
