                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

790






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Construction of Bridge in Bijnor




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of work on the bridge connecting Mawana and Chandpur in Bijnor;(b)	the details of action taken so far against the officers and the former contractors held accountable for delay in construction of the said bridge;(c)	the time by which the work on said bridge is likely to be completed; and (d)	the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d) This Ministry is primarily responsible for development and maintenance of National Highways in the country. The bridge in question is not on National Highway.  *****
