                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1708






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Implementation of BADP




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of States where Border Area Development Programme(BADP) has been implemented; and(b) the details of the economic impact of this programme in the areas where it has been implemented?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a)  and (b):	Government of India is implementing the Border Area Development Programme (BADP) in border areas of 17 States. BADP is a major intervention for filling critical gaps in provision of essential social and economic infrastructure in the border areas to ensure equitable development in these areas. The works/projects undertaken under the BADP relate to construction of roads, bridges, safe drinking water supply, health, agriculture and allied activities, social sector activities such as creation of social infrastructure, capacity building and skill development, construction of toilets particularly for women, sports activities, promotion of rural tourism, border tourism etc. These activities help in providing additional job opportunities, improving quality of life and improving standard of living in the border areas. During the financial years 2015-16 to 2018-19 (four years), besides  various  other  infrastructure  related  projects, construction of 1935-2/…-2-LS.US.Q.NO.1708 FOR 02.07.2019 kms roads/ link roads, 2765 mtr bridges/ culverts etc, 520 numbers of sports fields/ stadia, 11 residential schools, 37 numbers tourism related projects, 34 warehouses and 52 livelihood and organic farming programmes were undertaken for improving the economic status of border population.*****
