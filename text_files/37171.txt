                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4257






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Funds for FAME-II Scheme




M. P. Abdussamad Samadani














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the funds allocated so far for the Faster Adoption and Manufacturing of  Hybrid and Electric Vehicle (FAME II) Scheme on an annual basis and the amount of funds utilized for the same;(b)	whether the Government has sanctioned charging stations to Kerala under FAME II scheme;(c)	if so, the number of charging stations set up in Kerala; and(d)	the details of other schemes sanctioned in Kerala thereunder along with the report of its progress?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a):	Sir, the detail of the budgetary allocation and utilization to implement Phase II of FAME–India Scheme is as under:Sl. No.	Financial Year	Budget Allocation	Fund Utilization1	2019-2020	Rs. 500.00 Crore	Rs. 500.00 Crore2	2020-2021	Rs. 318.36 Crore	Rs. 318.36 Crore3	2021-2022	Rs. 800.00 Crore	Rs. 729.30 Crore as on 28/03/2022.(b) to (d):	Yes Sir, Ministry of Heavy Industries has sanctioned 211 charging stations to Kerala state. In addition, this Ministry has also sanctioned 250 e-buses to Kerala state.******
