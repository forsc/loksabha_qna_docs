                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2424






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Loans provided by RBI




Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	The details of loans provided to Andhra Pradesh by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) till 30.11.2021; (b)	The details of principal and interest payments made so far by Andhra Pradesh Government and the outstanding to be paid in coming three years till 2023-24; and(c)	The details of loans provided to Andhra Pradesh by external agencies under Externally Aided Projects till 30.11.2021 along with the principal amount and interest, institution-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY) (a)	& (b) The Reserve Bank of India has not provided any loans to the Government of Andhra Pradesh. However, RBI facilitates financial accommodation in the form of Special Drawing Facility, Ways & Means Advance and Overdraft to supplement the temporary mismatches between receipts and payments.( c)	The details including principal repayments and interest payments of loans to Andhra Pradesh by Multilateral and Bilateral institutions as available upto  12.11.2021 are provided in Annex-I to V.
