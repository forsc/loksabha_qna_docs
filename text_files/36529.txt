                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3884






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






National Blood Policy




M.K. Vishnu Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is planning to introduce legislation to support the National Blood Policy; (b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	whether the Government proposes to bring the processing charges of blood and blood components under the ambit of National Pharmaceutical Pricing Authority (NPPA), if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(d)	whether the Government proposes to undertake a review of National Blood Policy, if so, the details thereof; (e)	whether the Government proposes to undertake a follow-up of the assessment of blood banks done by  NACO in 2016 in the current year; and (f)	if so, the details thereof?	






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (c): Government has notified the Drugs and Cosmetics (Second Amendment) Rules, 2020 to support the National Blood Policy. These Rules pertain to functioning of Blood Banks, Blood processing and related matters. The copy of the Rules is available at web link: https://cdsco.gov.in/opencms/opencms/system/modules/CDSCO.WEB/elements/download_file_division.jsp?num_id=NTc2MQ==The matter regarding processing charges of blood and blood components was taken up with the Department of Pharmaceuticals for bringing the same under Drugs Prices Control Order(DPCO), implemented by National Pharmaceutical Pricing Authority (NPPA). Department of Pharmaceuticals has informed that, there is no such proposal. (d) to (f): During a meeting with the State Blood Transfusion Councils (SBTCs) of all States and Union Territories (UTs), held in January, 2022, it has been recommended to take up a review of the National Blood Policy. There are no such proposals.*****
