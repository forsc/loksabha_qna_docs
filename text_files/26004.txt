                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1759






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Norms for Public Transport Vehicles




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)     whether the Government proposes installation of speed controller   mandatory in all the public transport vehicles;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether there is any proposal for raising the speed limit on National Highways; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) and (b)  The Ministry, vide SO 427(E) dated 27th June 1991, introduced Speed Control Devices for vehicles. Hon’ble Supreme Court, vide order dated 20th  Nov 1997, directed the Government to take immediate measures for installation of Speed control devices. Accordingly, the rules were further amended vide GSR 660(E) dated 12th Sept 2001, GSR 400(E) dated 31st May 2002, GSR 943(E) dated 31st  Dec 2012, GSR 290(E) dated 15th  April 2015, and GSR 424(E) dated 1st May 2017.(c) and (d) The Ministry, vide S.O 1522(E) dated 06th April 2018, increased the speed limits of vehicles on different kinds of roads in India. The issue relating to speed limits of the vehicles is   reviewed from time to time.*****
