                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2514






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Funds Released for Fisheries




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether more funds are being released for fisheries in the States where there are rivers; (b) 	the details regarding the funds released for fisheries during the last five years, State-wise; (c) 	whether more funds are being utilized in the districts which are adjacent to the rivers; and(d) 	whether the Government propose to manage such a system on priority basis with relaxation in the States where there is dearth of rivers by constructing water reservoirs for development of fisheries?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING:(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) and (b) :  The fisheries developmental schemes implemented by the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying inter-alia focuses on harnessing of fisheries resources of  various water-bodies including  rivers and reservoirs in a sustainable and equitable manner. Funds are released to the State Governments and Union Territories for development of fisheries based on receipt of the viable proposals.  State-wise details of funds released for fisheries development during past five years is furnished at annexure. (c) and (d) :	 The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a flagship scheme “Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY)- A scheme to bring about Blue Revolution through sustainable and responsible development of fisheries sector in India” with highest ever investment of Rs. 20,050 crore in the fisheries sector over a period of five years from financial year 2020-21 to financial year 2024-25 in all States/Union Territories. PMMSY inter alia provides support for promoting of aquaculture in inland water bodies including rivers and reservoirs for increasing the fish production. The activities taken up in such water-bodies primarily includes stocking of fingerlings, pen culture, cage culture, integrated development of reservoir etc. *****
