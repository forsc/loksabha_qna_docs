                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1766






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Coaching to Tribal People




Riti Pathak














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the financial assistance provided by the Government under the scheme related to coaching of people belonging to Schedule Tribes community during the last three/current year, State/UT-wise;(b)	whether the proposals of various States including Madhya Pradesh for sanction of funds under this scheme are pending with the Union Government;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and (d)	the date by which the said proposals are likely to be approved including the funds likely to be released for the States including Madhya Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                         (a):	Ministry of Tribal Affairs is not administering any scheme for providing coaching to Scheduled Tribes.  However, funds have been released under its scheme of “Grants under Article 275(1) of the Constitution” to State Governments to enable them to provide coaching to ST students. The details of funds released are as under:Year	State	Fund sanctioned/ released(Rs. in lakh)	Total(Rs. In Lakhs)2016-17	Telangana	312.50	312.502017-18	Nagaland	75.00	426.00	Rajasthan	351.00	2018-19	Karnataka	100.00	115.00	Mizoram	15.00	(b) No, Sir.(c) to (d) Does not arise in view of reply to (b) above.*****
