                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2614






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Completion of North Koel Project




Pradeep Kumar Singh













                                                    Sushil Kumar Singh
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the target fixed by the Government for completing the multi-purpose North Koel reservoir project of Bihar and Jharkhand;(b) the details of funds released by the Government in this regard so far;(c) the present status of the North Koel reservoir project including the volume of work completed;(d) whether the work is being done as per the schedule, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasonstherefor; and(e) the manner in which the Government is monitoring the said project to complete it on time?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU)(a) The Union Government, in August 2017, approved the proposal  to complete the balance works of North Koel Reservoir Project in Jharkhand and Bihar at an estimated expenditure of Rs.1,622.27 crore to be incurred during three financial years from the start of the project.(b) The fund released to the project till date is Rs. 721.22 crore.(c) There is 10% progress on Dam & Appurtenant works, 95% on Barrage and 75% on Left Main Canal.(d) The progress of works at the project has been affected due to several reasons like time taken in requisite clearance from Forest Department of Government of Jharkhand, agitation by the local villagers at the dam site and time taken by the Government of Bihar for concurrence of works to be carried out in Bihar portion. (e) A Technical Evaluation Committee (TEC) under the chairmanship of Member (WP&P), CWC, New Delhi has been constituted to monitor the completion of the balance works of the project. Besides, the progress of the project is regularly monitored in DoWR, RD & GR, Ministry of Jal Shakti.******
