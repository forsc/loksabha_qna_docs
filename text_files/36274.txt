                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3645






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Bio-Fuel Plants




Sudarshan Bhagat














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is giving a thrust to setup bio-fuel plants in the forest rich areas of the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c)	whether the forest-rich areas of Jharkhand including Gumla have been included in the said scheme and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS  (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c): The Government has notified the National Policy on Biofuels - 2018 (NPB) on 4th June 2018 which envisages production of biofuels from non-edible oil seeds bearing trees (Tree Borne Oilseeds (TBOs)) / crops such as Pongamia pinnata (Karanja), Melia azadirachta (Neem), castor, Jatropa Carcus etc. and cellulosic and lignocellulosic materials such as wood waste, agricultural and forestry residues, grasses, algae, etc. which are found in abundance in forest and agriculture areas. Government has also notified Pradhan Mantri JI-VAN Yojana for providing financial support to promote Second Generation (2G) ethanol production from cellulosic and lignocellulosic feedstock. The NPB & Pradhan Mantri JI-VAN Yojana are applicable all over the country including Gumla and forest rich areas of Jharkhand.******
