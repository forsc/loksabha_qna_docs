                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4134






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Setting up of Universities for Minorities




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Maulana Azad Education Foundation (MAEF) has constituted a committee to go into the modalities for establishment of institutions in different parts of the country with a view to facilitate educational development of the minorities and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the committee has submitted its report and if so, the main recommendations thereof; (c) whether the Government has studied the recommendations and if so, the action taken on these recommendations; (d) whether non-existence of universities to pursue higher education has affected minority students badly and if so, the steps taken by the Government to set up new universities for minorities; (e) whether the Government has received proposal for setting up of universities for minorities from different States of the country; and(f) if so, the State/UT-wise details thereof and the action taken by the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a):     Yes, Sir. MAEF in 52nd meeting held on 29.12.2016 had constituted a committee to go into the modalities for establishment of institutions in different parts of the country with a view to facilitate educational empowerment of minorities especially girls. (b) to (d ):   The Committee has submitted its report and has recommended for  three tier model of educational institutions for minorities having:-	211   Central Schools at the bottom level.	25     Community colleges at the middle level and	05     National institutes at the top level.(e ) to (f):  At present, there is no proposal in the Ministry of Minority Affairs to set up any University for Minorities. However, consequent upon the recommendation of the said committee, the Government of Rajasthan, on request of the MAEF, has allotted 15 acre land at Village Kohrapipli, Tehsil - Kishangarhbas, District - Alwar for setting up a  National Institute of eminence.***
