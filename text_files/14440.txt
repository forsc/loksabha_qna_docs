                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3115






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Funds for NGP




 Ramesh Chand Bind













                                                    Dulal Chandra Goswami
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds sanctioned by the Government under ‘Namami Gange Programme’ (NGP) during the years 2014-2015 to 2019-2020; (b) the funds spent in this regard, year and Statewise; and (c) the details of the projects started under NGP including Bihar and the cost involved in these projects so far?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)  During the period from 2014-15 to 2019-20 (up-to 29 February 2020), Government of India has sanctioned (final allocation) Rs.12,324.82 crore for the Namami Gange Programme. Out of this, Government of India has released Rs.8,917.02 crore to National Mission for Clean Ganga (NMCG) and subsequently, NMCG has released/expended Rs.8,342.14 crore in the said period.(b)  The year-wise details of funds released by the Government of India to NMCG under the Namami Gange Programme from the financial year 2014-15 to 2019-20 (up-to 29 February 2020) are given below:-	Financial Year	Funds released by Government of India to NMCG (Rs. in crore)2014-15	326.002015-16	1,632.002016-17	1,675.002017-18	1,423.122018-19	2,307.502019-20*	1,553.40Total	8,917.02               (* up-to 29 February 2020)Contd…P/2//2//	The details of year-wise and state-wise funds released and expended by National Mission for Clean Ganga from the financial year 2014-15 to 2019-20 (up-to 29 February 2020) are given below:-   (Rs. in cr.)S.N.
