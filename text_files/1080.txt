                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1091






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Construction of Houses under ‘Housing for All’ Vision




Sanjay Seth














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the physical target fixed by the Government for construction of houses under ‘Housing for All’ Vision by 2022 during the last three years in Jharkhand;(b) 	the details of houses actually built/constructed in Jharkhand during the said period; and   (c) 	the details of funds allocated thereunder to Jharkhand during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                     (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	State Govt. of Jharkhand has reported that the target of 1,67,359 Dwelling Units (DUs) was fixed for construction of houses under ‘Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) {PMAY(U)} - Housing for All by 2022’ Mission during the last three financial years in the State of Jharkhand.(b): 1,56,216 DUs have been sanctioned and 73,864 DUs have been constructed under PMAY(U) Mission during the said period in the State of Jharkhand. (c):	Under PMAY(U) Mission, Central Assistance of Rs. 2,280.43 crore has been sanctioned and Rs. 1,282.91 crore has been released to the State of Jharkhand during the said period.                                  *****
