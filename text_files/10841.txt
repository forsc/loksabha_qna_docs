                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4206






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Centre of Excellence in Rheumatology




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether it is a fact that the Regional Research Institute (Homoeopathy), Dr. GGH Medical College Campus, Gudivada, Andhra Pradesh, has been chosen to develop as a Centre of Excellence in Rheumatology; and (b) if so, the details thereof and the time by which it is likely to be completed? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):     Yes, the Regional Research Institute (Homeopathy), Dr. GGH Medical College Campus, Gudivada, Andhra Pradesh, has been chosen to develop as a Centre of excellence in Rheumatology.(b):   In accordance with the recommendations of Scientific Advisory Committee (SAC) of CCRH, following infrastructural strengthening has been taken up for developing Regional Research Institute (Homoeopathy), RRI(H), Gudivada as Centre of Excellence: 1.	IInd Floor construction has been completed and made functional.2.	Laboratory facilities are added like Fully Automated Bio-chemistry Analyzer, Ultrasonography, ECG, X-ray & Physiotherapy equipments.  3.	The institute has initiated Rheumatologic specialty clinic, the foot-fall of patients related to Rheumatology are increasing gradually.4.	The institute has taken up various research studies related to Rheumatology e.g. on Osteoarthritis, Rheumatoid arthritis, post chikungunya arthritis, chronic non specific low back pain, cervical spondylosis.  5.	Additional manpower requirements are identified and Council is in the process of submitting the same to Ministry of AYUSH for approval.6.	The Council proposes to develop the centre of excellence of Rheumatology at RRI, Gudiwada by 2021.…………………..
