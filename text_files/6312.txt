                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

35






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Release of Special Development Fund




Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has released Special Development Funds as promised to the Andhra Pradesh State during last five years from 2014-19;(b)	 if so, the details thereof along with district-wise break-up;(c)	whether the attention of the Government is drawn to the fact that some funds have not been released and are still pending;(d)	 if so, the reaction of the Government in this regard; and(e)	 the steps being taken by the Government for early release of the balance funds as promised?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) & (e): Following the recommendations of NITI Aayog in its report on “Developmental support to the successor State of Andhra Pradesh under Andhra Pradesh Re-organisation Act (APRA), 2014” dated 01.12.2015 Department of Expenditure has released Rs.1050 crore towards Special Development Package under Section 46(2) & (3) and 94(2) of APRA, 2014 to the successor State of Andhra Pradesh in three instalments of Rs.350 crore each @ Rs.50 per district for the 7 backward districts of the State. Special Development Funds have been released for the districts namely: Anantapur, Chittoor, Cuddapah,  Kurnool, Srikakulam, Vishakhapatnam and Vizianagaram.                                        *****
