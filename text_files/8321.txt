                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1845






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Health Centres under NHM




Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta













                                                    Chandra Prakash Joshi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the total number of Community Health Centres and Primary Health Centres set up in the States of Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan under National Health Mission (NHM) during the last three years; (b) the details of the funds allocated to the States of Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan under NHM during the last three years; and (c) the details of programmes being implemented to improve the healthcare facilities in the rural areas under NHM?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):    The total numbers of CHCs and PHCs set up/completed during the last three years 2016-17, 2017-18 & 2018-19, as reported by the concerned States, are as under:	CHC	PHCUttar Pradesh	18	4	Rajasthan	3	57Contd…….-2-(b):    The details of the funds allocated to the States of Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan during the last three years are as under:-Statement Showing Central Release under NHM for Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan during the last three years        							(Rs. in crore)	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19Uttar Pradesh	3,314.75	3,967.46	3,886.70Rajasthan	1,234.18	1,615.29	1,667.00(c):          The details of various health programmes being run by the Government under National Health Mission, State/UT wise is available in the form of Record of Proceedings at https://nhm.gov.in/index4.php?lang=1&level=0&linkid=449&lid=53……………….
