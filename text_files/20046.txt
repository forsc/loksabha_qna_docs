                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1155






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Renunciation of Indian Citizenship




Karti P. Chidambaram














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Indians who gave up Indian citizenship for the citizenship of another country during the last five years, year-wise;(b) the number of Indians currently living in foreign countries;(c) whether there has been an increase in the number of people seeking OCI cards;(d) if so, the details thereof;(e) whether the Government is considering any proposal for dual citizenship; and(f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)a) As per information available with the Ministry of External Affairs, the details are as follows : Year	No. of Indians who gave up Indian Citizenship and took citizenship of other countries2015	1,41,6562016	1,44,9422017	1,27,9052018	1,25,1302019	1,36,441-2-L.S.US.Q NO. 1155 FOR 09.02.2021b) As per the information available with the Ministry of External Affairs a total number of 1,24,99,395 Indian nationals are living in foreign countries. c) & d) Details of OCI Cards issued year-wise are enclosed as Annexure.e) No Sir. f) Does not arise in view of reply of (e) above.********
