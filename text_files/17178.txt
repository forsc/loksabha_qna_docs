                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1032






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






AYUSH Wellness Centres




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) the details of AYUSH Wellness Centres proposed to be set up by the Government during 2019-2020, State/UT wise and the present status of the same;(b) whether it is a fact that the Budget has been reduced to just Rs. 13 crores from the previously allocated Rs. 150 crores;(c) if so, the reasons therefor; and(d) the details of the steps taken by the Government for setting up of AYUSH centres in 2020-21?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a):	Under the existing provision of Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), 1632 units of AYUSH dispensaries have been approved for upgradation as AYUSH HWCs during the year 2019-20 and Rs. 140. 64 crore has also been released for the same. The State/UT wise status of approved units and released grant-in-aid is furnished as Annexure. However, implementation of the scheme comes under the purview of respective States and UT Governments.(b) & (c):	Yes. During 2019-20, after the approval of HWC Component under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission in March 2020, Rs. 13.10 crore only was earmarked under NAM for AYUSH HWC considering the budget ceiling of 10% of funds for utilization in last month of financial year.(d):	In year 2020-21 till date, a total of 1407 units of Health and Wellness Centres approved and Rs. 45.82 core has been released to the States and UTs as furnished in Annexure.
