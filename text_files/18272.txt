                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2035






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Firefighting Facilities at Ports




Kuldeep Rai Sharma













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    Manickam Tagore B
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the number of ports which handle the movement of petro-chemical products;(b)	whether the current firefighting facility at the ports does not support handling of LPG and other petroleum products;(c)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(d)	whether the Government proposes to augment firefighting facilities at major ports of the country and if so, the details thereof;(e)	the quantum of funds earmarked for the purpose; and(f)	the time by which the Government proposes to augment firefighting facilities at major ports in view of increase in LNG and LPG cargo?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c) All Major Ports have capacity to handle the movement of petrochemical products at their ports. Major Ports are having adequate fire fighting facilities for handling LPG as well as other petrochemical products.(d) to (f) All Major Ports upgrade the fire fighting facilities from time to time as per the increased LNG and LPG demand at their ports. Approximately Rs.263.75 Crore has been earmarked for the estimated cost of upgradation and augmentation of fire fighting facilities at the Major Ports.                                               *****
