                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3208






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Cotton Production in Maharashtra




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government is aware of abundant production of cotton in Maharashtra, if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government has deviced any plan to set up textile hub for such regions of abundant production, if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the current textile hub situated in Ichalkaranji in Maharashtra is in a dilapidated condition, if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether any funds are likely to be approved to re-establish such textile hub, if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	whether the Government has received any proposal from the State Government in this regard and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b): The Cotton Advisory Board (CAB) has estimated the cotton production in Maharashtra for the current cotton season 2018-19 as 77 lakh bales.  The details of cotton production in Maharashtra during the last three  seasons vis-à-vis current season is as under:-                                        (Quantity in lakh bales of 170 kgs each)Cotton season(October – September)	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19Cotton production in Maharashtra	76.00	88.50	83.35	77.00(c) & (d):  There is no such report in  this regard.  (e):  Government has not received any proposal from the State Government.  ***
