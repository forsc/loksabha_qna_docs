                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4179






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Registration of Property




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the measures being undertaken to improve India’s score in difficulty to ‘register property’ in World Bank’s Ease of Doing Business ranking; and(b) 	whether there is any proposal to move to Torren’s system of land titling on the lines of Urban Land (Certification of Title) Act, 2016 passed by the State of Rajasthan and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a):	 Digital India Land Records Modernization Programme (DILRMP), a central sector Scheme, is implemented by all States/Union Territories (UTs) which includes Computerization of Land Records, Computerization of Registration and Integration of Revenue Offices with Registration. The objective of DILRMP is to develop Integrated Land Information Management System in the country which will provide online single-window at-a-glance access to all available, relevant information to give a fair comprehensive position of any plot of land in question to the landowner, concerned offices/agencies and interested persons/entrepreneurs, etc.  This will ultimately facilitate online registration and mutation process.(b):	No such proposal is presently under consideration in this Ministry.                                *****
