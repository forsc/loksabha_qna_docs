                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4765






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Inspiring Results




Bhola Singh













                                                    Sakshi Ji Swami Maharaj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the steps taken to make the Higher Education Institutes like IITs, ISC, IIMetc. world class have not given very inspiring results;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether merely 24 Indian Institutions have been able to make a place amongselected 1000 educational institutions worldwide;(d) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and(e) the details of the efforts made/being made to improve the level of education in theother educational institutions of the country?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): In order to set up / upgrade 10 public and 10 private Institutions as worldclass teaching and research institutions, the Government approved an enablingRegulatory Architecture in the form of University Grants Commission (UGC)(Declaration of Govt. Educational Institutions as Institutions of Eminence) Guidelines,2017 for public institutions and UGC (Institutions of Eminence Deemed to beUniversities) Regulations, 2017 for private Institutions. The selected Institution underthis Scheme has been named as Institutions of Eminence (IoEs). The objective of thescheme is to bring selected IoE in top 500 of the world rankings in the next 10 years andin top 100 eventually overtime. In the month of October, 2018, 3 public Institutionsnamely Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay; Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi;and Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore have been declared as IoEs under thisScheme. Therefore, it would not be appropriate to judge the inspiring result of theseIoEs in such a short time period.(c) & (d): No, Sir. As per Quacquarelli Symonds (QS) World University Ranking-2019,(25) Institutions/Universities have found place in top 1000 world University rankings.Besides, as per Times Higher Education (THE) World University Rankings-2019, (33)Institutions / Universities have found place among the top 1000 world Universityranking. The details of these Institutions are at Annexure-I.(e): Apart from the scheme of establishment of ‘IoE’, the Government has taken severalother steps to improve the quality of higher education in the country. These steps, interalia,include promotion of autonomy to Higher Educational Institutions as well asencouraging Institutions to improve the quality of research through various schemesviz. Impacting Research Innovation and Technology (IMPRINT), Impactful PolicyResearch in Social Sciences (IMPRESS), Global Initiative for Academics Network (GIAN),Study Webs of Active Learning for Young Aspiring Minds (SWAYAM), Staff Training andResearch Institute of Distance Education (STRIDE), etc. The focus is on LearningOutcome based Curriculum (LOCF) to make learning oriented expectations and outcomeat the end of Under Graduate / Post Graduate level.*****
