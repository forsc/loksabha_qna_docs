                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1390






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Factory Activities




A. Ganeshamurthi














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that factory activities shrunk due to Covid-19 pandemic during the last 12 months and Purchasing Managers Index (PMI) for the manufacturing sector dropped;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Union Government has undertaken any relief measures to overcome the present situation;(d) 	if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) to (e): 	Industrial growth plays an important role in the development of an economy and Government of India is making continuous efforts for promotion of industrial growth. The industrial growth process is accompanied by the formation of new enterprises and closure of old ones, in response to the prevailing financial and economic scenario driven by economic shocks and stimulus. The fundamentals of the economy remained strong as gradual scaling back of lockdowns, along with the support of Atmanirbhar Bharat Mission placed the economy firmly on the path of recovery from the second half of FY 2020-21. The performance of Indian economy based on Purchasing Managers'' Index (PMI) and Index of Industrial Production for the last 12 months is provided at Annex-I.Government has announced various relief measures for several sectors including health infrastructure under the Atmanirbhar Bharat Packages announced on 13th May, 2020 to 17th May, 2020, 12th October, 2020 & 12th November, 2020 involving a total amount of Rs.29,87,641 crore (including amount provided for the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Package, Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana from July to November, and measures announced by RBI). Besides, a Rs 6.28 lakh crore Covid stimulus package has also been announced on 28.06.2021. The details of the measures are at Annex-II. These measures will provide necessary impetus to the existing projects and help in boosting the economy.Some of the recent steps taken by DPIIT to boost GDP growth and manufacturing sector are achieving integration of India Industrial National Land Bank (GIS Land Bank) in 17 states; covering over 400 industrial parks under Industrial Park Rating System 2.0. Steps like Startup India Seed Fund Scheme; Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups; Startup Ranking Framework; National Startup Awards etc has been taken to build a strong ecosystem for nurturing innovation and entrepreneurship; continuation of Footwear Leather & Accessories Development Programme (FLADP) 2021-26 for boosting leather sector. Details of measures taken by DPIIT to boost Industrial activities in country is provided at Annex-III.******
