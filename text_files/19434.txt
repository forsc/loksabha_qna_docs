                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

867






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Promotion of AYUSH Sector




Anil Firojiya













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                








                                                    Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether any scheme for the development of AYUSH entrepreneurship is being chalked out to promote AYUSH sector in the country, State/UT-wise particularly in Madhya Pradesh and Gujarat;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether any proposal has been formulated to include youth and scientists in nation building opportunities in various sectors like research, innovation, management, medical, higher education etc.; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a):	Yes,(b):	The Government have launched a Central Sector Scheme during 2020-21 to encourage private investors to invest in AYUSH sector to establish world class, state of the art super specialty  hospitals and  day care centres of the systems recognized under Indian Medicine Central Council(IMCC) Act, 1970  or Homeopathic Central Council (HCC) Act, 1973 for promoting export  of medical value travel services. Interest subsidy will be provided for construction of the hospital building and purchasing of equipments.(c):	Yes, 	(d):	The Government have established Research Councils and National Institutes in various parts of the country in order to undertake research and impart higher education under various AYUSH systems. The youths get opportunities for being recruited and serve in these organizations to ensure the optimal development, propagation and popularization of AYUSH systems to make them the preferred system of medicine.
