                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4269






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Promotion of Sports other than Cricket




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the schemes being implemented at present by the Government for promotion of sports other than cricket in the country; (b) the schemes being implemented to encourage and facilitate sportsmen excluding cricketers; (c) the details of beneficiaries of these schemes during the last five years and the current year, year and category-wise; and (d) the details of funds allocated and utilized for the purpose during the said period, year and sport-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR](a) and (b): Following schemes are being implemented by the Government for the promotion of sports in the country and to encourage and facilitate sportsmen:(i)  Khelo India; (ii) Assistance to National Sports Federations;  (iii)Special Awards to Winners in International sports events and their Coaches; (iv) National Sports Awards, (v) Pension to Meritorious Sports Persons; (vi) Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund for Sportspersons; (vii) National Sports Development Fund; and (viii) running sports training centres through Sports Authority of India (SAI). Details of these schemes are in the public domain on the websites of this Ministry and Sports Authority of India. (c):  The details of beneficiaries of these schemes during the last five years and the current year are provided at Annexure-I. Category-wise details are not maintained in the Department.(d):  The details of funds allocated and utilized for these schemes are provided at Annexure-II. Sports-wise details are not maintained in the Department.****
