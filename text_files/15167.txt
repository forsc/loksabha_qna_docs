                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3797






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Deaths due to Avalanches




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there has been an increase regarding deaths of civilians as well as paramilitary personnel due to avalanches in the recent months in the country; and(b) if so, the details thereof during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYA NAND RAI)(a) & (b) : During the last three years, 2 deaths (one in Indo-Tibetan Border Police in 2019 & one in Border Security Force in 2020) have taken place due to avalanche in Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs) and Assam Rifles (AR). As per the data compiled by the National Crime Records Bureau, number of deaths of civilians due to avalanche in Country during the year 2016, 2017 and 2018  are 19, 38 and 5 respectively.*****
