                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4826






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Ranking of IGI Airport, Delhi




Harish Dwivedi














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the IGI Airport, Delhi has been selected amongst one of the fifth best airports of the world and the best airport in Central Asia and India as per a recent survey;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the number of airports selected from India in the said survey?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) & (b): Indira Gandhi International (IGI) Airport, Delhi is ranked among the top fifty best airports globally by 2021 Skytrax World Airport Awards. Further, Delhi Airport has won the  ''Best Airport in India/Central Asia'', ''COVID-19 Airport Excellence'' and ''Best Airport Cleanliness in Central Asia and India'' awards in the 2021 Skytrax World Airport Awards.;(c): Hyderabad, Mumbai and Bengaluru Airports also feature in Top 100 Airports list published by 2021 Skytrax World Airport Awards. Participation in the survey for ranking of airports is upto the airport operators, to guage their performance against the benchmark standards. In India, most of the airport operators participate in the Airports Council International (ACI)-Airport Service Quality (ASQ) survey.  Airports namely IGI Airport, Delhi, Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport, Mumbai, Rajiv Gandhi International Airport, Hyderabad, Cochin International Airport, Cochin, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport, Ahmedabad and Chandigarh Airport, Chandigarh have won awards in different categories in  the ASQ Awards for 2021.******
