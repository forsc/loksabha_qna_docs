                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3962






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Deaths of Animals




Chandrani Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government has data of animal death due to accidents caused by vehicles in national parks and sanctuaries in the country, if so, the details thereof State-wise during the last three years particularly in Odisha;(b)	the details of animal deaths due to vehicles in Simlipal National Park; and(c)	whether the Government will consider construction of animal corridor in Simlipal National Park for safety of animals?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY(a)The management of forests and wildlife is primarily the responsibility of State Governments/UT Administrations. The State/UT wise details of animal deaths due to accidents caused by vehicles in National Parks and Sanctuaries are not collated in the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change. However, as per information received from the State of Odisha, the details of animal deaths due to accidents caused by vehicles in National Parks and Sanctuaries in Odisha are as given below:Year	Name of National Park/Sanctuary	Animal 	Number2018-19	Nil2019-20	Balukhand-Konark Wildlife Sanctuary	Spotted Deer	12020-21	Simlipal Wildlife Sanctuary	Barking Deer	1(b) and (c) As informed by the Odisha State Government, no deaths of animals have been reported in Simlipal National Park due to vehicles. The State Government has informed that no animal corridor construction has been proposed in Simlipal National Park as no National Highway, State Highway or District roads pass through the National Park.*****
