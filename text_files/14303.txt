                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3072






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Madurai Airport




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any proposal to develop and upgrade the Madurai Airport; and(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Development and upgradation of airports is a continuous and ongoing process. Airports Authority of India has undertaken extension of apron and allied works at Madurai Airport at an approved cost of Rs. 25.71 crore.
