                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3784






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Manual Scavenging




Kirron  Kher














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 3784TO BE ANSWERED ON 17.03.2020MANUAL SCAVENGING 3784. SHRIMATI KIRRON KHER: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) whether the provisions of the Prohibition of Employment as Manual Scavengers and their Rehabilitation Act are being implemented across the country; (b) the number of deaths caused due to manual scavenging/cleaning of sewers during the last three years, State/UT-wise; (c) the number of manual scavengers identified across the country, State/UT-wise and the number of such scavengers rehabilitated so far during the said period; (d) the funds allocated and utilized for the rehabilitation of the manual scavengers during the said period, State-wise; and (e) the other steps taken by the Government for the rehabilitation of such manual scavengers?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RAMDAS ATHAWALE)(a): Yes Sir,(b):  There have been no reports regarding deaths of persons due to manual scavenging. However, State-wise details of persons who have died while cleaning sewers and septic tanks during the last three years and current years as reported by the States and Union Territories (UTs) are at Annexure-I.(c): State-wise details of the number manual scavengers identified and rehabilitation benefits provided to them since 2013-14 upto 29.02.2020 are at Annexure-II.(d):  Being a Central Sector Scheme, there is no provision under Self Employment Scheme for Rehabilitation of Manual Scavengers for State-wise allocation of funds. State-wise details of expenditure incurred in providing rehabilitation benefits since 2013-14 upto 29.02.2020 are at Annexure-III.(e): Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment is also implementing a Scheme titled “Pre-Matric Scholarship scheme to the Children of those engaged in occupations involving cleaning and prone to health hazards”. Children of manual scavengers are also eligible for this scholarship. National Safai Karamcharis Finance and Development Corporation implementing various schemes for the welfare of safai karamchairs including manual scavengers and their dependents. NSKFDC has  been organizing health-cum-awareness camps to provide free medical checkup and medicines to the manual scavengers and  their families. 142 such camps have been organized by NSKFDC during 2017-18 to 2019-20.*****Annexure-I											Anneuxre-I referred in reply to part  (b)  Lok Sabha unstarred question No. 3784 for answer on 17.03.2020 regarding manual scavenging											Information received from States by the National Commission for Safai Karamcharis (NCSK) about number of sewer deaths and compensation paid from 2016 to 06.11.2019.
