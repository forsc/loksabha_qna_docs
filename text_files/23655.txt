                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4807






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Scrap Disposal




Kuldeep Rai Sharma













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to reduce the scrap generated through various rail projects and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether this scrap is not  reusable/recyclable and if so, the details thereof and the steps taken in this regard;(c)	the total quantity of railway scrap and the revenue generated therefrom during the last three years and the current year, year-wise;(d)	whether there have been instances of scrap being sold at prices below the reserve price; and(e)	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor along with the total loss incurred by the Railways as a result thereof and the other steps proposed to be taken by the Government for effective disposal of scrap?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY ANDCONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (e): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
