                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2764






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Portal for Complaints of Food Adulteration Cases




Arvind Dharmapuri













                                                    Meenakashi Lekhi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether there is a Government portal for registering complaints pertaining to presence of harmful chemicals in the food items; and(b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b): Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) has an online grievance portal namely ‘Food Safety Connect’ where a consumer can register complaint relating to the food articles , including   presence of harmful chemicals in food items . The portal to register complaint is https://foscos.fssai.gov.in/consumergrievance. On submission of complaint/ grievance, a unique reference ID is generated which is sent to the complainant by email/SMS for tracking status of grievance. The grievance received is forwarded to concerned Food Business Operator (FBO) /State Designated Officer for taking necessary remedial action.The status of consumer grievance is monitored by FSSAI through quarterly report submitted by State/UT Governments which are also taken up with States in the meeting of Central Advisory Committee (CAC), where Food Safety Commissioners of all States/UTs are represented.
