                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3300






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Federation of Indian Animal Protection Organisation




K. Subbarayan














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government’s attention has been drawn to a study conducted by the Federation of Indian Animal Protection Organization (FIAPO) in  collaboration with All Creatures Great and Small (ACGS) stating that unregulated fish farms in the country could be potential breeding ground for the public health catastrophe; and(b)	if so, the details of the findings in the report and the details of measures proposed to be taken to regulate and ensure safety of the fisheries produced in the farms in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a)  No Sir. The Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is not aware of the study conducted by the Federation of Indian Animal Protection Organizations (FIAPO) in  collaboration with All Creatures Great and Small (ACGS) which suggests unregulated fish farms in the country could be potential breeding ground for the public health catastrophe.(b)  Does not arise.***
