                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

646






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Repairing Works on NH-9




Kunwar Danish Ali














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the funds spent for the repairing works on National Highway(NH) No. 9 Garhmukteshwar, Brijghat during the last three years along with the name of company which was engaged for the said works; (b)	the number of accident happened on this NH during the said period;(c) 	whether the repairing work on this NH has been completed; and (d)	if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)	The fund spent for the repairing works on National Highway (NH) No.9 Garhmukteshwar Brijghat during the last three years is Rs. 35.44 Crore and the name of company is M/s Freyssinet Prestressed Concrete Co. Limited and BP Construction.(b)	About 185 no. of accidents occurred during said period. (c) & (d)      The repairing work on this NH has been completed. The rectification for new defects has been initiated.*****
