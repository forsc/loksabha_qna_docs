                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2923






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Supply of Safe and Adequate Drinking Water




Ramesh Bidhuri














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the steps taken by the Government to provide safe and adequate drinking water to every rural person in the country; (b) 	the details regarding percentage of people who do not have access to safe drinking water; (c) 	whether the Government has formulated any plan or mechanism regarding this problem; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d) 	Since August, 2019, Government of India in partnership with States, is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) – Har Ghar Jal to make provision of tap water supply to every rural household in the country by 2024. The estimated outlay of the mission is Rs. 3.60 lakh Crore, out of which Central share is Rs. 2.08 lakh Crore.As reported by States/ UTs, as on date, out of 17.02 lakh rural habitations, 13.46 lakh [79.09%] rural habitations have provision of potable drinking water with more than 40 litre per capita per day (lpcd) and 3.08 lakh [18.09%] rural habitations with less than 40 lpcd from sources at a reasonable distance, whereas 0.48 lakh [2.81%] rural habitations have drinking water sources with water quality issues.*****
