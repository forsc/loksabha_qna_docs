                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1630






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Swachh Survekshan 2020




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal













                                                    Arvind Kumar Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Government proposes to support those urban local bodies and address their problem of uncleanliness which lags behind in Swachh Survekshan, 2020 and if so, the details thereof; and(b) the details of efforts made or proposed to be made in this regard and outcomes achieved?






ANSWER




                                                               THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                          (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) and (b) : A cleanliness survey called Swachh Survekshan (SS) is carried out annually to inculcate healthy competitive spirit amongst Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) and States/ Union Territory Administrations by ranking them on the basis of their population.  The performance of the States/ULBs are reviewed regularly and those which could not perform well are guided through regular interaction with the officials, field visits, assisting them in preparation of Detailed Project Report (DPRs) along with releasing Central Assistance under Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U).                                       *****
