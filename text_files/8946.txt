                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2508






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Social Welfare Schemes




Kanakmal Katara













                                                    Giridhari Yadav
                                                








                                                    Rama Devi
                                                








                                                    Rajan Baburao  Vichare
                                                








                                                    Krishna Pal Singh Yadav
                                                








                                                    Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama
                                                








                                                    S. Venkatesan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of social welfare schemes being implemented in various States of the country particularly in Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu, Maharashtra, Gujarat, Bihar and Madhya Pradesh; (b) whether there is any special scheme for the weaker sections of the country particularly for Scheduled Castes, Women, Children and Weaker Sections/BPL people in the country; (c) if so, the names of such schemes and the details of the quantum of funds allocated and spent on said schemes during the last five years, State-wise; and (d) the monitoring mechanism to check whether the benefits under these schemes are reaching the needy persons?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c): The mandate of this Department is to implement of the schemes of educational, economic and social empowerment of Scheduled Castes (SCs) and Other Backward Classes (OBCs), Senior Citizens, victims of Drug Abuse, transgenders, Denotified Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Tribes (DNTs). The details of major schemes being implemented by this Ministry and quantum of funds allocated in States/UTs are given at Annexure-I and Annexure –II respectively.(d). Regular meetings/conferences, Field Visits, Video Conferences are held with the representatives of the State Govt./UT Administration and other implementing agencies for effective implementation and proper utilization of the funds released by the Union Government and to ensure  that the benefits under these Schemes reach to the needy persons.*****
