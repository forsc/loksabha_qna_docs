                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2174






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Andhra Pradesh Skill Development Authority under PMKVY




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       the details of the sectors identified by Andhra Pradesh Skill Development Authority under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana; (b)       number of job roles identified under the Centrally Sponsored State Managed component of Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana, sector-wise; (c)        the details of the placements offered in each sector every year in the last 6 years, year-wise, sector-wise and gender-wise; and (d)       the details of the candidates offered placements after successful training in the last 4 years?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)      Under Centrally Sponsored State Managed (CSSM) component of Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), 25 sectors have been identified by Andhra Pradesh State Skill Development Corporation (APSSDC) for imparting training in the State of Andhra Pradesh, namely, Aerospace & Aviation, Agriculture, Apparel, Automotive, Beauty & Wellness, BFSI (Banking, Financial Services and Insurance), Capital Goods, Construction, Electronics & Hardware, Food Processing, Furniture & Fittings, Green Jobs, Handicrafts & Carpet, Healthcare, IT-ITeS, Life Sciences, Logistics, Management, Media & Entertainment, Plumbing, Power, Retail, Telecom, Textiles & Handlooms, and Tourism & Hospitality.  (b)       The sector-wise number of 101 job roles under CSSM component of Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana, as on 10.07.2021, is given in Annexure I. (c)        The sector-wise, year-wise and gender-wise number of candidates placed under CSSM component of PMKVY since inception, as on 10.07.2021, is given in Annexure II.
