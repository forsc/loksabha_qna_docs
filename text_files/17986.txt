                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1685






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Recommendations by Bibek Debroy Committee




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	  whether the Ministry has seen the recommendations of the railway restructuring committee led by Shri Bibek Debroy which focus on developing only railway stations that serve more than 25,000 passengers daily;(b)	  if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) whether the recommendations will lead to the stalling of the development of several very small but crucial stations in Kerala, including Kannur, Palakkad, and others;(d) if so, whether the Government is going to consider not to implement these reforms and if so, the details thereof; and(e)  if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (e): Yes, Sir. Ministry has seen the recommendations of Railway Restructuring Committee led by Shri Bibek Debroy submitted in June 2015. There were general recommendations on development of stations and it would not lead to stalling of development of any station.  *****
