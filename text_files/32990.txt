                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

52






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






AYUSH Universities in Madhya Pradesh




Gyaneshwar Patil













                                                    Gajendra Singh Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether some AYUSH Universities are in the process of being set-up in Madhya Pradesh and if so, the details along with the locations thereof; (b) whether any plan is under consideration for setting-up of new AYUSH Universities in Madhya Pradesh and if so, the details and the locations thereof; (c) whether it is proposed to establish AYUSH Universities in the tribal dominated regions of Khandwa Burhanpur and Khargone-Badwani in future and if so, the details thereof; and (d) the time by which AYUSH Universities are likely to be established in the said regions along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a) to (d) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHASTARRED QUESTION NO. 52 * FOR 4TH FEBRUARY, 2022(a)	to (d) :At present there is no such proposal under consideration.  •	Central Universities are established by the Act of Parliament.•	State Universities and State Private Universities are established by the Act of the concerned State legislature.•	Institutions of Higher education are declared as Deemed to be Universities by the Government of India, on the advice of UGC, under Section 3 of the UGC Act 1956.•	Name of a University is included in the list of Universities maintained by the UGC on receipt of the Act as passed by the Parliament/concerned State legislature.At present total fifty Nine (59) Ayush Medical Colleges are functioning in the Madhya Pradesh including 07 Government Ayurvedic College, 01 Government Unani College and 01 Government Homeopathy College. The details are as follows:No. of Ayurveda Institute	No.  of Unani Institute	No. of Siddha  Institute	No. of Homeopathy Institute	No.  of Sowa Rigpa  Institute	No. of Yoga & Naturopathy  Institute27	04	0	25	0	03•	Madhya Pradesh Ayurvigyan Vishwavidyalaya, Jabalpur (Medical Science University) is the University and Governing body for all the Medicine, Dental, Nursing, Paramedical, Ayurveda, Homeopathic, Unani, and Yoga colleges in the state of Madhya Pradesh.•	The University has been formulated under the act as notified by Government of Madhya Pradesh. •	All Government Ayush Colleges and most of the private Ayush Colleges are affiliated with Madhya Pradesh Ayurvigyan Vishwavidyalaya, Jabalpur.*****
