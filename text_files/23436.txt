                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4583






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Beneficiaries under NFSA




Sunita Duggal














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the number of beneficiaries under National Food Security Act-2013 (NFSA) across the country, State-Wise; (b) 	whether any steps has been taken by the Government to increase the number of beneficiaries under NFSA; (c) 	if so, the details thereof; (d) 	the steps taken by the Government to modernize the storage infrastructure along with the current storage capacity, State-Wise; and (e) 	whether the Government has planned to install Automatic Grain Dispensing Machines in the country and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a): 	A State/Union Territory-wise statement of number of beneficiaries covered under the National Food Security Act, 2013 (NFSA) is at Annexure-I. (b) & (c): 	No formal proposal for increasing the number of beneficiaries under the National Food Security Act, 2013 is under consideration of the Government presently. As per Section 9 of the National Food Security Act, 2013 the total number of persons to be covered in rural and urban areas of the State shall be calculated on the basis of the population estimates as per the census of which the relevant figures have been published..........2/--2-(d):	In order to upgrade and modernize the storage infrastructure, Government of India has prepared an Action Plan for construction of steel silos on PPP (Public Private Partnership) mode in the country. Silos with capacity of 30.75 LMT have been awarded at various locations, out of which a capacity of 8.25 LMT is complete and the remaining is under various stages of completion. Further, silo capacity of 35.875 LMT in PPP mode has been proposed under Hub & Spoke model. Modernization of existing conventional godowns are being done by incorporating the provision of Cement Concrete Roads, Colour coated  profile sheet roofing and other Annual repair and maintenance works in food storage godowns. Central Warehousing Corporation takes up regular repair/ maintenance & upgradation of warehouses. Some of initiatives for modernization include a) use of galvalume sheets in place of asbestos. b) use of translucent sheets for lighting. c) use of turbo ventilators. d) Cement roads, etc. State wise details of the total storage capacity available with Food Corporation of India (FCI) and the State Agencies (both owned and hired capacity) as on 28.02.2021, is at Annexure-II.(e):	United Nations World Food Program (UN-WFP) is in the process of developing a Public Distribution System (PDS) foodgrain dispensing machine on pilot basis, with the support of this Department.*****ANNEXURE-IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN PART (a) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.4583 FOR ANSWER ON 23.03.2021 IN THE LOK SABHA.State-wise intended coverage under the National Food Security Act, 2013 Sl.
