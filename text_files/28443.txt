                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

116






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






National Achievement Survey 2021




 Rahul Ramesh Shewale













                                                    Girish Bhalchandra Bapat
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                    Pritam Gopinath Munde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the first National Achievement Survey 2021 of new National Education Policy,2020 has been conducted and completed in the recent past;(b) if so, the result of said survey;(c) the education system particularly before and after lockdown to evaluate the impact ofschool closures on students’ learning;(d) the response of the Government thereof;(e) whether the results of NAS 2021 will be prepared in the form of District Report Cards,State/UT reports and National report; and(f) if so, the details in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (f): The Government of India has been implementing a rolling programme of samplebased National Achievement Survey (NAS) aimed at classes III, V, VIII and X underSamagra Shiksha since 2001 with a cycle period of three years.NAS 2021 has been held on 12.11.2021 and has covered (a) Government Schools(Central Government and State Government); (b) Government Aided Schools; and (c) PrivateUnaided Schools.The objective of NAS 2021 is to evaluate children''s progress and learningcompetencies as an indicator of the health of the education system. National, State/UT andDistrict Report cards are released to enable analysis of results and remedial action at theappropriate levels.****
