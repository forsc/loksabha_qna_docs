                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2199






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Report on archaeological Findings in Adichanallur




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the steps taken by the Government so far to publish the report on the archaeological findings in Adichanallur, Thoothukkudi in the State of Tamil Nadu;(b)	whether the Government has started the work on constructing an on-site museum in Adichanallur and if so, the details thereof; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	The excavation report of Adichanallur has been submitted. Same is available at ASI Website. (b)	Yes, Sir. As part of the Iconic Site Project, a Museum-cum-site development plan at Adichanallur has been initiated by Archaeological Survey of India.  In this regard, few locations are identified for selecting a suitable land for construction of site museum. (c)	Question does not arise.*****
