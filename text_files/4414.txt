                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4086






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Restructuring of Army




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government plans to reform and restructure the Indian Army;(b)  if so, the details of reforms/restructuring that the Government is likely to bring in the Indian Army;(c)  whether the Government is going to bring down one lakh posts of Army as a part of the reform process; and(d)  if so, the details and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) to (d):  A Committee of Experts was set up by the Government in 2016 to recommend measures to enhance the Combat Capability and Rebalance Defence Expenditure  of Armed Forces.  A total of 65 recommendations pertaining to Indian Army were approved for implementation.  The recommendations of Committee of Experts entails rightsizing of certain units of Indian Army, thereby optimising the resources.  It involves redeployment and restructuring of various posts like Officers/Junior Commissioned Officers/Other Ranks and Civilians, better utilisation of supply and transport units, enhancement in standards of recruitment and improvement in the efficiency of National Cadet Corps.	This also includes optimisation of Signal Establishments, restructuring of repair echelons in the Army, redeployment of Ordnance echelons, better utilisation of supply and transportation echelons and Animal Transport Unit and closure of Military Farms and Army Postal Establishments in peace locations.*****
