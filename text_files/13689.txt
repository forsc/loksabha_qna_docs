                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2369






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Production Capacity in MSME Sector




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor













                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                








                                                    Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva
                                                








                                                    Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya
                                                








                                                    Pradeep Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware that the production capacity is decreasing in Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) sector and there is lack of internal capacity in the country;(b) 	if so, the steps being taken by the Government to tackle this situation; and(c) 	the measures being taken by the Government to overcome the problems faced in registration of industries thereunder?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	 As per the information provided by National Statistical Office, the Gross Value Added and Growth rate of MSME at constant prices (2011-12) are as follows:<pre>Year	Total MSME GVA (in Rs. Crores)	Growth rate of MSME (in %)2015-16	3489112	9.82016-17	3770652	8.12017-18	4090445	8.5</pre>(b):	Does not arise.(c):	Udyog Aadhar Memorandum (UAM), a self certification based online registration portal for MSMEs was introduced by the Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises on 18th Sept. 2015. As on 26.02.2020, more than 88.32 lakh units have been registered under UAM since inception.
