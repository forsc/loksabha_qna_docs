                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

105






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






National Skill Training Institute (NSTI)




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of National Skill Training Institutes (NSTI) and NSTI(W) in the State of Andhra Pradesh (AP) and the number of people trained therein since 2017, Year-wise;(b)	whether there are no such institutes in the State of Andhra Pradesh if so, the reasons therefore and whether the Government intends to open such Institutes in the state;(c)	the state-wise number of individuals trained under PMKVY 2.0 and PMKVY 3.0 since the launch of these schemes;(d)	the details of the number of individuals trained in AP under PMKVY since 2017, district-wise;(e)	the details of the funds allocated, utilization for PMKVY since 2019-20; and(f)	whether the Government has undertaken any special initiatives for the skill development of the tribal population in the state of Andhra Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        a)	Sir, there are33National Skill Training Institutes (NSTIs) spread in 19 States/UTs. Out of 33 NSTIs, 14 NSTIs are general and 19 NSTIs are exclusively for women. There is no NSTI in many states including the State of Andhra Pradesh.b)	All NSTIs impart comprehensive instructor training, includes hand-on-skills and training methodology for which trainees are selected through a national level entrance test, in which eligible trainees of all states including Andhra Pradesh can participate.c)	The state-wise number of individuals trained under PMKVY 2.0 and PMKVY 3.0 is placed in Annexure I.d)	The details of the number of individuals trained in Andhra Pradesh under PMKVY since 2017 is placed in Annexure II.e)	Under PMKVY (CSCM), there is no state wise fund allocation.
