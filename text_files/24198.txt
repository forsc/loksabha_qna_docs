                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

32






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Relief Package and Funds under Loan Gaurantee Scheme




Naranbhai Bhikhabhai Kachhadiya













                                                    Parbatbhai Savabhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the amount of funds announced for relief package by the Ministry recently to strengthen Indian economy to fight against Covid-19 pandemic;(b)	the amount of funds announced under Loan Guarantee Scheme for Covid affected areas; and(c)	the funds allocated or likely to be allocated for affected areas of Gujarat?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a)  to (c)  On 28th June, 2021, the Government had announced a package of Rs 6,28,993 crore to provide economic relief to people and business affected by COVID-19 pandemic. The measures announced in the package were sector specific with no State wise earmarking of funds. Rs 1.1 lakh crore was announced for Loan Guarantee Scheme for Covid affected areas. This includes Rs 50,000 crore for health sector and Rs 60,000 crore for other sectors.****
