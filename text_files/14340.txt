                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3110






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Road Projects under Bharatmala in Jharkhand




Pashupati Nath Singh













                                                    Annpurna Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of roads being constructed in the State of Jharkhand under the Bharatmala 	Pariyojana; (b)	the time-frame set to complete these projects; (c)	whether there has been delay in construction of these roads and if so, the reasons 	therefor; and (d)	the steps taken by the Government to complete these projects within the stipulated 	time frame?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)	Total four National Highway corridors with an aggregate length of about 784 km. had been identified for development under Bharatmala Pariyojana in the State of Jharkhand. The entire Bharatmala Pariyojana is targeted for completion in a span of five year. (c)  & (d)	No Sir, However, major impediments in the way of progress of National Highway Projects is delay in land acquisition, forest clearances, wild life clearances and utility shifting. These clearances are concerned with the State Government and are being pursued regularly at different level in order to expedite completion of the project. *****
