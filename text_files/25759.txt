                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ATOMIC ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1496






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Atomic Energy Generation




Manoj Kumar Tiwari














                        Will the Minister of





ATOMIC ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has achieved atomic energy generation target set for the current year so far;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the future targets of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES & PENSIONS AND PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH):    (a)	Yes, Sir. (b)	The target set for the current year 2021-22 is 41821 Million Units. The target for the period April to June 2021 was 10164 Million units against which, the actual generation was 11256 Million Units.(c)	Does not arise in view of (a) and (b) above. (d)	The targets for nuclear power generation are set on an annual basis, as a part of Nuclear Power Corporation of India Limited (NPCIL)’s annual Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Department of Atomic Energy (DAE).  The targets for future will be set on an annual basis prior to the commencement of the financial year.*****
