                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2184






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Trains from Shirdi to Delhi




Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has considered/proposes to consider to connect Shirdi city of Maharashtra to other cities especially to Delhi with any express/mail service; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c) At present, 22455/22456 Sai Nagar Shirdi-Kalka Express provides direct connectivity between Sai Nagar Shirdi and Delhi area. Besides, Sai Nagar Shirdi is connected with other major cities like, Kolkata, Chennai, Mumbai, Visakhapatnam etc. by direct train services. During the period 2014-2019, two pairs of trains originating from/terminating at Sai Nagar Shirdi has been introduced. Owing to operational and resource constraints, presently, there is no proposal to start new train from Sai Nagar Shirdi. However, introduction of trains is an ongoing process on Indian Railways subject to operational feasibility, traffic justification, availability of resources etc.*****
