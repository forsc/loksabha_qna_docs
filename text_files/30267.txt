                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2022






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Unique Identification Number for Houses




Pragya Singh Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   	whether the Government proposes to issue lease document of house or other property documents to all classes of people who have been staying in sewa basti for years in Madhya Pradesh including Bhopal Parliamentary Constituency;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor;(d) 	whether the Government proposes to issue any unique identification number for houses in line with Aadhar card to residents; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                                    THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                            (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) to (c): No, Sir. However, Government of Madhya Pradesh has informed that State is providing Patta to all eligible landless families including families living in Sewa Basti, as per provision of “Madhya Pradesh Nagariya Kshetro Ke Bhoomihin Vyakti (Pattadhriti Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Adhiniyam, 1984”. In addition to this, Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) is providing Central assistance to States/UTs under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana- Urban (PMAY-U) for construction of houses to all eligible urban families/beneficiaries.(d)	&(e): No, Sir. There is no such proposal.                             *****
