                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

596






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Railway Projects in West Bengal




Shantanu Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the steps being taken by the Government to complete Habra-Bongaon Phase-1 (Habra-Chandpara) and Machlandapur- Swarnpur Nagar Railway project in West Bengal;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c):  Habra-Bongaon (32 Km) section has already been doubled in two phases i.e. (i) Habra-Chandpara (22 Km) section and                       (ii) Chandpara-Bongaon (10 Km) section in the year 2012.However, execution of Machlandapur-Swarupnagar (15 Km) new line Material Modification project to Habra-Chandpara Doubling project could not be taken up due to non handing over of encumbrance free land to Railway by State Government.*****
