                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

614






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Development of Ayu Raksha Kit




Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel













                                                    L.S. Tejasvi Surya
                                                








                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                








                                                    Prataprao  Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Ministry has developed an ayurvedic kit namely ‘Ayu Raksha kit’ to improve the immunity of people and keep them healthy and if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether the Ministry is working in coordination with the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare to maximise the use of ayurvedic medicine during this challenging period of COVID-19 pandemic and if so, the details thereof and work done in this regard; (c)	whether the Ministry proposes to develop more such ayudvedic medicines to counter the threat of pandemic in near future and if so, the details thereof; (d)	whether Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences has transferred the technology of Ayush-64, an effective drug in treating mild to moderate cases of COVID-19 to 46 private companies; and (e)	if so, the details thereof, reasons therefor and the response of the companies in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL) (a)	Yes, Sir.  The Kit is a combination of four interventions viz. Chyawanprash, Samshamni vati, Ayush Kwath and Anu Taila. -2-(b)	To meet the challenge of COVID-19 pandemic, Ministry of AYUSH has proactively coordinated with Ministry of Health & Family Welfare on different issues relating to promotion of AYUSH System of Medicines.  In this context, a series of meetings are being held on regular basis between both the Ministries.  Pradhan Mantri Bharatiya Janaushadhi Pariyojana (PMBJP) – an initiative of the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare in this regard – has made provisions for making AYUSH Medicines like AYUSH Raksha Kit & Baal Raksha Kit for prophylactic care against COVID-19 and Ayu Care Kit for management of home isolated patients suffering from COVID-19 pandemic through Jan Aushadhi Kendras.  Further, Ministry of AYUSH has also distributed approximately 1.78 Lakhs Ayu Raksha Kits, Tablets Sanshamni Vati, Ashwagandha Vati, Sudarshan Ghan Vati and Yashtimadhu to 30,110 population of the country & AYUSH prophylactic medicines to approximately 5 lakh population.    (c)	Details of the initiatives taken by Central Council Research in Ayurvedic Sciences (CCRAS) to combat the threat of COVID-19 pandemic in future are as follows:-S.No.	Name of the Study & Details	Study Centre	Status1	A study of Ashwagandha administration in participants vaccinated against COVID-19 on safety, immunogenicity, and protection: A randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled, multi-centric clinical trial	AIIA New Delhi, NIA Jaipur, SDM Hassan, SPPU, Pune, Podar Ayurvedic College, Mumbai, DMIMS Nagpur, B.M.K. AyurvedicMahavidyalaya, Belgaum	Study Ongoing 2	A Randomized controlled trial to evaluate the efficacy of Ayurvedic interventions (Agastya Haritaki and Ashwagandha) and Yoga in long term effects of COVID-19	GMC, NagpurJ N Medical College, DMIMS, Wardha	Study Ongoing (d) & (e)	Yes, Sir.  The details are at Annexure – I. ******
