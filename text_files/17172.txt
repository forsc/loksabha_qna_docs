                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1009






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Central Council for Research in  Unani Medicine




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether it is a fact that there are more than 20 centres of Central Council for Research in Unani medicine across the country;(b) if so, the details of performance of each Centre, Centre-wise;(c) whether it is a fact that total sanctioned strength of these Centres is nearly 800 whereas the actual-position is not even half of sanctioned strength and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government has taken note of low strength in the said centres and efforts undertaken to fill-in the vacancies in a time-bound manner; and(e) the status of Unani Centre at Hyderabad and activities taken up by this Centre during the last five years and the current year, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a) & (b):	Yes, total 22 institutes are working under Central Council for Research in Unani Medicine across the country.  These institutes are engaged in different activities.  Details of centre-wise performance is attached at Annexure- I.(c) & (d):	The total strength of these Centres is 788, in which about 35% posts are vacant. The process for filling up the posts has already been initiated. Posts of ROs (Unani, Path, Clinical Pharm, Bio-Chem.), Assistant Research Officer (bio-chem.), Research Assistant (Bot, Chem), Investigator, Senior production Assistant, Urdu Translator / Assistant Senior Library Attendant, and others have been advertised and applications received in response have been scrutinized. Instruction to filling up the Post of Group C has been issued to the In-charges of the respective institutes and process is being initiated by the institutes.(e):	The Central Research Institute of Unani Medicine (CRIUM), Hyderabad has been upgraded to ‘National Research Institute of Unani Medicine for Skin Disorders’ (NRIUMSD) in Nov, 2019. The Institute has also received NABH Accreditation Certificate in February 2020. Activities taken up by this Centre during the last five years and the current year is attached at Annexure- II.
