                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3573






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






Telephone Services in West Bengal




Jyotirmay Singh Mahato














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has carried out the work of providing telephone service in all the districts of West Bengal;(b)	 if so, the details thereof district-wise; and(c)	 if not, the time by which the said work is likely to be completed in all the areas?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) & (b)	All the districts of West Bengal State have been provided with telephone facilities. District-wise number of Base Transceiver Stations (BTSs) providing mobile network coverage in West Bengal are given in Annexure.(c) 	Not applicable in view of reply at (a) & (b) above.
