                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1274






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Taxes on Petrol/Diesel




Sougata Ray













                                                    S. Jagathrakshakan
                                                








                                                    Anto  Antony
                                                








                                                    A.K.P. Chinraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the quantitative details of consumption of various petroleum products in the country and revenue generated in the last one year, month-wise and State-wise; (b) 	whether the pandemic caused decrease in consumption of petroleum products in the country, if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether India stocked enough petroleum products during the low priced time, if so, the details thereof; (d) 	the details regarding the tax and cess imposed on petrol and diesel (per litre) by the Union Government and State Government, if any and whether the Government has any details of profit making Oil Marketing Companies in the country, if so, the details thereof; (e) 	the total amount of crude oil imported along with its rates from various countries for the last 12 months, month-wise and country-wise along with the total amount spent by the Government for imports; and (f) 	whether the Government has any proposal to include oil in GST in near future, if so, the details thereof, if not, the reasons therefor?

Further, Article 279 A (5) provides that Goods and Services Tax Council shall recommend the date on which goods and services tax shall be levied on petroleum crude, high speed diesel, motor spirit, natural gas and aviation turbine fuel. Thus, while petroleum products are included under goods and services tax, the date on which GST shall be levied on such products shall be as per decision of the Goods and Services Tax Council.*****
