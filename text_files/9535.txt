                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Marketing Agencies of Handlooms and Handicrafts




A. Ganeshamurthi













                                                    T. R. Paarivendhar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of marketing agencies of Handlooms and Handicrafts functioning as on date, State-wise, particularly in Tamil Nadu; (b)	 the role and functions of these marketing agencies;(c)	 the total number of such marketing agencies working for Handlooms and Handicrafts across the country, State/UT-wise particularly in Tamil Nadu;(d)	 whether these marketing agencies have achieved anything for the development of Handlooms and Handicrafts; and(e)	 if so, the details thereof?
