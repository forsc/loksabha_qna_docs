                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4708






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Six-Laning of Nellore-Tada NH




Adala Prabhakara Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to convert the National 	Highway from Nellore to Tada from four lanes to six lanes;(b)	if so, the details and the present status thereof;(c)	the steps taken by the Government in this regard so far; and(d)	the time by which the said project is likely to start and 	complete?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (d)   The stretch from Nellore to Tada of National Highway 16 was upgraded to 4 lane configuration on BOT Mode with concession period upto May, 2031. There is no provision of capacity augmentation (upgradation to higher configuration) in the Concession Agreement.  Hence, at present there is no plan to upgrade the said stretch until the concession period is over.        *****
