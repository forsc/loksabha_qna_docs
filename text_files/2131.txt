                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1986






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Promotion of Defence Production




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to promote new and innovative defence production projects under Make in India programme;(b) if so, the details thereof and the details of the projects given approval during the last three years;(c) whether certain projects particularly manufacturing of small arms is not given approval even though the Ordnance Factory Board had given its approval and company validation was done, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(d)  the details of the projects lying pending during the last three years, year-wise; and (e)  the decision taken by the Government and the Ordnance Factory  Board (OFB) in this regard and steps taken to expedite the process of approval?
