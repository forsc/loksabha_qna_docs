Debt Recovery Tribunals




Arun Kumar Sagar














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of Debt Recovery Tribunals working in the country, State-wise;(b ) the number of cases disposed of by these tribunals along with recovery of the debt during the last three years and the current year, tribunal/year-wise; (c ) the number of cases pending in each tribunal at present; and (d) the time by which these cases are likely to be disposed of ? 






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State in the Ministry of Finance(a)	Government has set up 39 Debts Recovery Tribunals (DRTs) in the country. The details is enclosed at Annexure -1.(b)	The details of the number of cases disposed of by these tribunals and the amount recovered during the last three financial years and the current financial year is enclosed at Annexure-2.(c )   As on 28.02.2022, total 1,61,034 cases are pending for adjudication. The number of cases pending is enclosed at Annexure- 3.(d)  As per section 19(24) of the Recovery of Debts and Bankruptcy (RDB) Act, 1993, the application made to the Tribunal under sub-section (1) or sub-section (2) of section 19 of the said Act shall be dealt with by it as expeditiously as possible and every effort shall be made by it to complete the proceedings in two hearings, and to dispose of the application finally within one hundred and eighty days from the date of receipt of the application.  ***** 		Annexure 1Annexure referred to in reply to Lok Sabha UNSTARRED Q No. 5089  for 04.04.2022S.
