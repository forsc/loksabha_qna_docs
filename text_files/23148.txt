                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4275






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Higher Education Fund




K. Shanmuga Sundaram














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is having any proposal to create higher education fund?(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the funds to be provided by the Government and the method to raise further funds;(d)	whether the Government will permit Public Sector Banks to participate in the programme;(e)	whether the funds will be provided to individuals directly for higher education scholarship;(f)	if so, the details thereof; and(g)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): 	Higher Education Financing Agency (HEFA) is a not for profit organization set up in 2017, to finance infrastructure in higher educational institutions. Canara Bank was identified as the Joint Venture partner for the establishment of HEFA and to manage it. HEFA was established as a Section 8 Company with NBFC license. The number of projects sanctioned/ funded under the HEFA across educational institutions as on 31st January, 2021 is 92. (b) to (g):	Nil, in view of (a) above.  *****
