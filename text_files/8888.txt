                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2402






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Attack on Police Personnel




Kaushal Kishore














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that there has been a spurt in the incidents of attack on police personnel by advocates and also miscreants in various parts of the country especially in the NCT of Delhi;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the steps being taken by the Government to check this unhealthy trends in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c) : As per the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution of India, the primary responsibility with respect to ‘Police’ & ‘Public Order’ lies with the State Governments concerned.  In so far as NCT of Delhi is concerned, details of cases of assault on Police personnel by advocates and miscreants registered by Delhi Police during the last two years and the current year (up to 31.10.2019) are as under:Year	Assault on Police personnel by advocates	Assault on Police personnel by miscreants 	No. of cases registered	No. of persons arrested	No. of cases registered	No. of persons arrested2017	3	Nil	232	4372018	2	Nil	219	4582019 (up to 31.10.2019)	3	Nil	156	331*****
