                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4693






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






HOEC Consortium




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that Hindustan Oil Exploration Corporation (HOEC) consortium has successfully explored and is producing rich quantum of natural gas from the Dirok T.E area of Upper Assam and if so, the details thereof; and(b) the estimated time till when the production of gas would be carried out along with the details of the purpose for which the produced gas is being utilized?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM& NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b): Hindustan Oil Exploration Corporation (HOEC), as operator in Block AAP-ON-94/1, has made gas discovery named Dirok-1. After appraisal, discovery was developed for commercial production. As per Field Development Plan (FDP), in-place reserve is 314 Billion Cubic Feet (BCF) and Estimated Ultimate Reserve (EUR) is 209 BCF. The commercial production in Dirok started in August, 2017. Presently, the field is producing 38 Million Metric standard cubic feet per day (MMSCFD) gas and 950 barrels of condensate per day from 6 producing wells. As per approved FDP, production profile of the discovery is agreed upto 2025. The HOEC is supplying gas to Brahmaputra Cracker and Polymer Limited (BCPL) and condensate to Indian Oil Corporation (IOCL) Digboi, Refinery.************
