                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1069






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Upgradation of AIR Station




Janardan Mishra













                                                    Rodmal Nagar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has formulated a scheme regarding the upgradation of All India Radio (AIR) Stations having well equipped studios with state-of-the-art technology;(b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise indicating the details of the AIR Stations upgraded in Madhya Pradesh; and(c) if not, the steps being taken by the Government to save these studios from falling into a state of disrepair?  






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) to (c) Prasar Bharati has informed that there is a scheme namely “Broadcasting Infrastructure and Network Development” (BIND) which provides for Modernization and Digitalization of Studios, Modernization/Replacement of transmitters and Broadcasting equipment etc. Under this scheme upgradation and modernization of All India Radio(AIR) network is a continuous process. Out of 479 operational AIR stations throughout the country, 228 AIR stations have studio facility. State-of-the-art digital equipment have been provided at 98 studio centers recently. The state-wise details of AIR stations upgraded recently are given at Annexure. ******
