                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4370






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






ESI Hospital at Choudwar, Odisha




Mahesh Sahoo













                                                    Manjulata Mandal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the Government land will be provided for construction of a new hospital building of ESI hospital at Choudwar; and(b)the present status of the existing ESI hospital building at Choudwar?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a):	The Regional Office of the Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) Odisha has requested the district administration, Cuttack on 29.01.2021 for allotment of government land of about 5 acres area outside the protected Monuments zone of the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) for construction of a new building.(b):	The existing ESIC hospital at Choudwar is an old building and needs repairs.  The ESIC had sanctioned estimates for renovation of the hospital, but the No Objection Certificate could not be received from the ASI to enable the ESIC to carry out renovation work, as the hospital is within  protected monuments zone.******
