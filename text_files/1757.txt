                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1677






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






PM-Kisan Credit Card Scheme




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government has urged all the States and Union Territories (UTs) to expedite farmers’ enrollment process to get the benefit under the PM-Kisan Credit  Card Scheme;(b)	 if so, the details thereof along with the implementation plan and the targets that have been set for the same;(c)	 whether States have been identified where KCC penetration was found lagging; and(d)	 if  so,  the  details  to expedite  the process to achieve the targets?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	 Yes Sir.(b) to (d):	 As per the advisory issued by the Department for Kisan Credit Card (KCC) saturation drive, the implementation plan is as under :(i)     Camps to be  Organized Bank-wise / village-wise to collect Kisan Credit Card (KCC)  application forms from eligible farmers(ii)      The District Level Bankers Committee (DLBC) to decide the modalities of the camp and the date wise schedule(iii)    District  Collector and Lead District Manager (LDM) to  play a  key role in the campaign for all these activities carried out for KCC saturation(iv)     In the camps, the State machinery to facilitate  completion of  application forms which includes  documentation  like identity proof , Residence proof, Land records and one photograph(v)     Applications collected in the camps to be submitted to the Bank’s branch and KCC to  be issued within 2 weeks from the receipt of completed application.Contd…2/--2-(vi)    Details of Aadhar Card Number of the applicants to be captured(vii)    The progress  will be monitored by State Level Bankers Committee (SLBC).KCC enrollment is a continuous process and it is Government’s intent to cover every eligible farmer with KCC.  Union Government is closely working with States and  progress is being  monitored through Video Conference (VC) by Government of India, District level target monitoring by the States and visit of Central Teams to selected States especially where KCC penetration is less. ********
