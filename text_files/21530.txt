                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2661






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Digital Villages of Gujarat




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of Electronics and Information Technology be pleased to state: (a)	the details of villages made digital in the state of Gujarat till now, district-wise along with the funds allocated so far therefore; and(b)	The details of facilities being provided in those digital villages during the last three years;






ANSWER




                                                        a):   Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) had initiated Digital Village Pilot project titled “Pilot Project-Implementation of Digital Village” on 31st October 2018. 700 Gram Panchyats/villages across the country are being covered under pilot implementation which includes 33 Gram Panchayats (GP)/villages in Gujarat State (one each from the 33 districts of Gujarat). District wise list of the Gram Panchayats/villages is enclosed at Annexure.The Digital Village Project of MeitY is being implemented across India through CSC E-Governance Service India Limited as a project under the Digital India Scheme which is a Central Sector Scheme of Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology. The tentative funds requirement for the State of Gujarat to cover 33 Gram Panchayat/villages is approximately Rs. 326.70 lakhs.
