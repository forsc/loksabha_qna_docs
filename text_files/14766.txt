                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3474






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Drop Out Rate ofMuslim Students




Ritesh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the annual average drop-out rate in percentage of students from MuslimCommunity at Primary, Upper Primary, Secondary and Higher secondary levels inthe country during the years, 2017-18 and 2018-19, State-wise; and(b) the enrollment of minority children in surveyed schools in Uttar Pradesh duringthe last four years?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) As per the U-DISE data of Ministry of Human Resource Development for theyear 2017-18 (provisional), the State-wise annual average drop-out rate inpercentage of students from Muslim Community at Primary, Upper Primary andSecondary levels in the country during the years 2016-17 and 2017-18 is given atAnnexure I.(b) As per the U-DISE data of Ministry of Human Resource Development, theenrollment of minority children in schools in Uttar Pradesh during the last fouryears is given at Annexure II.*****
