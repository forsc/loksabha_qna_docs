                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3018






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Press Information Bureau




Bharti Pravin Pawar













                                                    Rama Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government reviews the work of Press Information Bureau (PIB);  (b)	if so, the number of times the work of PIB have been reviewed during the last three years and the outcome thereof; and(c)	the action taken by the Government on the said review outcome? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE: MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES                                {SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR}(a) to (c) : Press Information Bureau (PIB) is the nodal agency of the Government of India to disseminate information to the people through Media (print, electronic and digital) on Government policies, programmes, initiatives and achievements.Publicity programmes and other activities undertaken by PIB is regularly reviewed by Government at various levels.******
