                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3666






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






CSR Fund




Navneet Ravi Rana













                                                    Jyotirmay Singh Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) under the Ministry functioning as on date including the details of their net profit during the last five years and the current year, year and PSU- wise; (b) the details of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) fund of the PSUs fixed for each year, PSU-wise;(c) the details of non-governmental institutions and the governmental institutions to which CSR fund has been provided by the PSUs during the last two years and the current year including names of the various projects; and (d) the details of districts and States benefitted from CSR fund, district and State-wise including Amravati Parliamentary Constituency and West Bengal?






ANSWER




                                                               MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a)&(b)  The company-wise details of the net profit and CSR funds of Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) functioning under Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas during the last five years are given at Annexure. (c)&(d)    Public Sector Oil & Gas Companies have provided their CSR fund to various non-governmental entities and institutions such as Skill Development Institutes and various governmental institutions such as Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan, Artificial Limbs Manufacturing Corporation of India (ALIMCO) etc. The State/district/project/activities wise details of CSR activities undertaken and funds spent thereon during the last two years including in districts of Maharashtra & West Bengal are available on the respective websites of the Public Sector Oil Companies i.e. www.ongcindia.com, www.iocl.com, www.bharatpetroleum.com, www.hindustanpetroleum.com, gailonline.com, engineersindia.com, www.nrl.co.in, www.mrpl.co.in, www.cpcl.co.in,  www.balmerlawrie.com , oil-india.com & bcplonline.co.in.  ****
