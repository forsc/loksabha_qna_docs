                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5174






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






New Railway Line between RamatekParshivani-Khapa




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any decision to construct new railway line Ramatek-Parshivani-Khapa; (b) if so, the time by which the decision is likely to be taken; (c) whether the required funds have been released for this railway line, if so, the details thereof; (d) the present status of this railway line; (e) whether the Government has fixed any time limit for its completion; and (f) if so, the time by which this railway line is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b) Reconnaissance Engineering cum Traffic Survey has been sanctioned from Ramtek to Khapa via Parshivani(36 km) in 2017-18.  Decision to take up the project will be taken based on merits brought out in survey report including financial viability of the project. (c) to (f) Do not arsie, as the project is not sanctioned. *****
