                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1835






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Electrification under SAUBHAGYA




Sudarshan Bhagat














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether most of the houses in the country have been electrified under Pradhan Mantri Sahaj Bijli Har Ghar Yojana-SAUBHAGYA;(b) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and(c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF POWER AND NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY (SHRI  R.K. SINGH)(a) to (c) :	Government of India had launched Pradhan Mantri Sahaj Bijli Har Ghar Yojana – Saubhagya in October, 2017 with the objective to achieve universal household electrification by providing electricity connections to all un-electrified households in rural areas and all poor households in urban areas in the country. Under Saubhagya scheme, as on 31.03.2021, all the States have reported 100% electrification of all the willing un-electrified households, identified before 31.03.2019. As reported by the States, 2.817 crore households have been electrified since the launch of Saubhagya, up to 31.03.2021. State-wise details are given at  Annexure.**************
