                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4203






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Pending Proposals




Prataprao Govindrao  Patil Chikhalikar














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the number of proposals relating to youth affairs and sports activities sent for approval to the Union Government by the State of Maharashtra during the last three years;  (b) the number of proposals out of these approved so far and those lying pending for approval; and  (c) the time by which the pending proposals are likely to be approved? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)  FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS (SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)  (a) In so far as the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports is concerned, no proposal relating to youth affairs and sports activities has been received from the State of Maharashtra during the last three years.  (b) Does not arise.  (c) Does not arise.   ****
