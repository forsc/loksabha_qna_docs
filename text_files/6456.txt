                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

170






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






LIS




Sukanta Majumdar













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Government is aware about the difference in the curriculum ofLibrary and Information Science (LIS) studies across universities and highereducation Institutes in India;(b) if so, the details thereof and action taken thereon by the Government;(c) whether the Government has constituted any committee for introducing theuniform curriculum in LIS studies in India;(d) if so, the details thereof;(e) whether the Government had implemented the recommendations of NationalKnowledge Commission for improvement in LIS education in India; and(f) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) to (d): The University Grants Commission (UGC) constituted an ExpertCommittee to develop Learning Outcomes-based Curriculum Framework (LOCF) forthe Bachelor of Library & Information Science Programme. After having detaileddeliberations and wider consultations, the Expert Committee has developed LOCFfor Library and Information Science which is available athttps://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/9387546_Library-Science--Final-Complete-Report-12-Dec-2018.pdf .(e) & (f): Yes, Sir. The Ministry of Culture has informed that according to therecommendations of the National Knowledge Commission, National Mission onLibraries was set up on 4th May, 2012 for sustained attention for development ofLibraries and Information Science Sector.******
