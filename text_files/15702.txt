                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4169






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






PMAY Houses in Maharashtra




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of houses constructed under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) in the State of Maharashtra particularly in Nashik district during the last three years; (b) 	the total number of constructed houses that were handed over to the beneficiaries in Nashik district during the last three years; and (c) 	the total number of houses under construction and the time by which these are likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                       (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)& (b) Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban) [PMAY(U)], the details of houses constructed and completed/delivered to the beneficiaries in Maharashtra and Nashik district during the last three years are as follows -Details of houses	Maharashtra	Nashik DistrictSanctioned 	9,44,802	33,348Grounded 	2,82,531	14,690Completed/Delivered 	1,69,701	14,404(c) 	Details of the houses sanctioned and grounded for construction in Maharashtra and Nashik districtunder PMAY(U)since inception of the scheme in June, 2015, are as follows –Details of houses	Maharashtra	Nashik DistrictSanctioned 	11,95,425	39,904Grounded 	5,03,959	20,916The houses sanctioned under PMAY(U) are proposed to be completed by March, 2022.                                           *****
