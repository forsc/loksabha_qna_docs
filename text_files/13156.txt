                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1930






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Ban on Export of KP Onion




Y. S. Avinash Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Consortium of Indian Farmers Association representatives have recently met the Minister and submitted a memorandum to lift the ban on export of KP Onions and if so, the details thereof; and (b) 	whether the Government has taken any decision on this request, if so, the details thereof and the response of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Yes, Sir. A meeting was held with the representatives of Consortium of Indian Farmers Association on 22.1.2020 wherein they submitted a representation to lift the ban on export of KP Onions.(b):	Government vide notification dated 6th February, 2020 has allowed export of KP onion of 10,000 MT through Chennai Port for the period up to 31st March, 2020.*******
