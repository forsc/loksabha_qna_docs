                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2973






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






JICA collaboration for AIIMS, Madurai




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether it is a fact that the construction of AIIMS in Madurai, Tamil Nadu is based on the fund from Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) constituting 85 percent of the total cost for the establishment of this hospital and centre of medical sciences; and(b) if so, the details thereof and the reason behind receiving such funds from JICA?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b):   AIIMS at Madurai is being set up with loan from Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) for which negotiations have been concluded. Decision to fund the project through JICA loan is as per recommendations of the Expenditure Finance Committee and approval of Cabinet.
