                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

955






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Types of Aircraft




Ajay Nishad














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of various types of operational and non-operational aircraft in the fleet of national aviation company;(b) the average utilisation period of aircraft in present fleet of Air India;(c) whether several aircraft of Air India have completed their utilisation period and these are required to be replaced;(d) if so, the details thereof;(e) whether national aviation company proposes to procure new aircraft in place of old aircraft; and(f) if so, the details thereof and the quantum of investment is required for this purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) As on date, there are a total of 128 aircraft in the operating fleet (narrow body 79 and wide body 49) of Air India Limited.  Out of these 128 aircraft, 19 aircraft are non-operational/grounded due to shortage of engines and other components apart from the aircraft under daily scheduled maintenance as on 24.06.2019.;(b) The average utilization of aircraft in the fleet of Air India for FY 2018-19 is presently as under:;Type of Fleet                         Per day Average Utilisation;Airbus Fleet (A320 family)                 10.66 hrs.Boeing Fleet                                          11.14 hrs. ;(c) & (d) As on date, no aircraft in the operating fleet of Air India Limited has completed its utilization period.;(e) At present, Air India has no immediate plans to procure new aircraft in place of old aircraft.;;(f) Does not arise in view of (e) above.;
