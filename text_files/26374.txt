                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2141






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Reframing School Curriculum




Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to reframe all the curricula of education;(b) if so, whether the Government has taken any steps in this regard, so far; and(c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (c) The National Education Policy, 2020, inter-alia, provides that the National Curriculum Framework for School Education (NCFSE) will be developed. A comprehensive strategy document has been prepared for the NCFSE. As per the document the States/Union Territories’ will first prepare their draft Curriculum Frameworks through the process of district level consultations, mobile app survey and development of position papers by the State Focus Groups in identified areas/themes as per the NEP, 2020. These draft State Curriculum Frameworks (SCFs) will take care of cultural and geographical concerns of the States/UTs and provide inputs for development of National Curriculum Framework.****
