                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1937






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Weavers and Handloom




Jaisiddeshwar Shivacharya Mahaswamiji














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of beneficiaries who have availed benefits of HHS Scheme in Solapur district, Maharashtra during the last two years;(b)	the number of weavers and handloom workers enrolled under the Mahatma Gandhi Weaver Insurance Scheme in Solapur district;(c)	the number of beneficiaries to whom insurance benefit has been provided in case of natural death, accidental death and full and partial disability during the last two years and the current year; and d)	the complete details along with the names of the beneficiaries?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a):	There is no scheme by the name of HHS in the Ministry of Textiles.  However, there is a scheme called Hathkargha Samvardhan Sahayata (HSS) under which looms and accessories are provided to the handloom weavers.  Under HSS 12,983 number of weavers have been benefitted with 13,119 number of HSS items distributed during the last two years.(b):	Government of India is implementing converged Mahatma Gandhi Bunkar Bima Yojana (MGBBY) across the country including Solapur district for enrolment of handloom weavers/workers.  500 number of handloom weavers/workers have been enrolled in Maharashtra State during the last three year under MGBBY. (c) & (d):	The number of weavers/workers benefited in case of natural/accidental death, full and partial disability under Insurance Scheme through Life Insurance Corporation (LIC) of India during the last two years and current year is as under:Year	Natural Death	Accidental Death	Number	Amount (Rs. in Lakh)	Number	Amount (Rs. in Lakh)2017-18	2172	1303.20	54	40.502018-19	1023	728.60	0	02019-20(As on 31.10.2019)	337	406.40	0	0The details of beneficiaries are maintained by Life Insurance Corporation (LIC) of India.******
