River Restoration Projects

                                                    S.R. Parthiban
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has received any proposal from the Governments of Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu under the rivers restoration projects to restore the river systems and water bodies, if so, then details thereof; (b) the details of the list of projects given approval or to be approved and the funds earmarked/allocated for the same, especially in Chennai; (c) whether the Government has any plans to introduce state of the art technology in carrying out rivers cleaning and de-siliting process on its own or with Public-Private Partnership (PPP) mode or in collaboration with foreign countries; and (d) if so, the details thereof and the list of those projects approved and carried out during the last three years, year and State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) & (b) Ministry of Jal Shakti is implementing the scheme of ''Repair, Renovation and Restoration (RRR) of water bodies'', which at present is linked to irrigation and is part of PMKSY (Har Khet Ko Pani, HKKP). Details of the ongoing projects under the scheme of ''RRR of water bodies'' during XII Plan & onwards in Tamil Nadu are given at Annexure-l. Under this scheme, no projects are under implementation in Maharashtra during XII Plan & onwards.It is the responsibility of States/Union Territories (UTs) and Local Bodies to ensure required treatment of sewage and industrial effluents before discharge into recipient water bodies, including rivers, for prevention and control of pollution therein. This Ministry has been supplementing the efforts of States/UTs by providing financial and technical assistance in abatement of pollution in identified stretches of rivers (excluding river Ganga and its tributaries) through the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National River Conservation Plan (NRCP) on a cost sharing basis between the Central and the State Governments.Proposals for pollution abatement works/projects in identified towns/cities along polluted river stretches are received from the States/UTs from time to time for consideration under the NRCP, and are considered/ sanctioned based on their prioritization, conformity with the NRCP guidelines, availability of Plan funds, etc. Details of town-wise projects sanctioned under NRCP in respect of Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu, including sewage treatment capacity created, are given in Annexure-II.(c) & (d) Different river restoration works/schemes under this Ministry are implemented by the concerned States/UTs and their agencies through a well established system.
