                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4238






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Road Projects in Maharashtra




Bharti Pravin Pawar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and the present status of ongoing highway projects and road transport projects in Maharashtra at present, project/location-wise; (b) the details of projects running behind schedule, project/location-wise; and(c) the details of approved projects in Maharashtra which have not started yet, along with the reasons therefor project-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) There are total 215 on-going highway projects in a length of 8,684 km amounting to Rs. 94,637.87 Cr in the State of Maharashtra. There are total 145 projects running behind schedule in a length of 6,077.72 km amounting to Rs. 58,271.10 Cr in the state of Maharashtra. The details and present status of ongoing highway projects including projects running behind schedule at present, project/location-wise is annexed below at Annexure-I.(c) There are total 8 approved projects in Maharashtra which have not started yet. Out of 8 projects, 5 projects are with NHAI for which Letter of Acceptance has been issued and 3 projects are with State PWD in tendering stage. The details of these projects are annexed below at Annexure-II.
