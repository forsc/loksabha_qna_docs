Employment Schemes for Tribal Areas




Chandra Prakash Joshi













                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the names of the schemes being implemented by the Government  to promote / generate employment in the tribal areas of the country;(b)	the details of funds allocated under the said schcemes during the last three years, State/UT-wise; and(c)	the details of the works carried out by the Ministry for Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh in this regard during the last three years and the current year so far?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c) 	A statement is laid on the Table of the House. ***** Statement referred to in reply to parts (a) to (c) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No. 199 for answer on 02.12.2019 tabled by SHRI CHANDRA PRAKASH JOSHI, MP and SHRI RAM KISHAN, MP, regarding ‘Employment schemes for Tribal Areas’.(a)	The Government of India is implementing the following schemes for promoting/generating employment in the tribal areas of the country:I.	Grants under Article 275(1) of the Constitution.II.	Special Central Assistance to Tribal Sub SchemeIII.	Mechanism for Marketing of Minor Forest Produce (MFP) through Minimum Support Price (MSP) and Development of Value Chain for MFPIV.	Equity Support to NATIONAL SCHEDULED TRIBES FINANCE AND DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION (NSTFDC) / STATE TRIBES FINANCE AND DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION (STFDCs).V.	Institutional Support for Development and Marketing of Tribal Products / Produce(b) to (c) 	The requisite details are given in Annexure.*****  ANNEXUREStatement referred to in reply to parts (b) and (c) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No. 199 for answer on 02.12.2019 tabled by SHRI CHANDRA PRAKASH JOSHI, MP and SHRI RAM KISHAN, MP, regarding ‘Employment schemes for Tribal Areas’.Details of funds allocated during the last three years, State/UT-wise, scheme-wise:1.
