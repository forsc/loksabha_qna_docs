                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3807






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Processing of Fruits and Vegetables




Ajay Bhatt














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the ongoing schemes being implemented for storage and processing of fruits and vegetables;(b)	whether the Government has provided any support for food processing units and food parks in various parts of the country including the State of Uttarakhand; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c): Under the Central Sector Umbrella Scheme – PRADHAN MANTRI KISAN SAMPADA YOJANA - (PMKSY), the Ministry of Food Processing Industries  (MoFPI) is implementing the schemes of (i) Mega Food Parks, (ii) Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure, (iii) Infrastructure for Agro-processing Clusters, (iv) Creation of Backward and Forward Linkages, (v) Creation / Expansion of Food Processing & Preservation Capacities and (vi) Operation Greens with components for, inter alia, storage and processing of all agri and allied produce including fruits and vegetables. Under these schemes, credit-linked financial assistance as capital subsidy in the form of grants-in-aid is provided to the eligible applicants such as farmers, entrepreneurs, organizations like Central and State PSUs/Joint Ventures/Farmer	Producer	Organizations (FPOs)/NGOs/Cooperatives/SHGs/ Public and Private Companies etc. to set up food processing projects in all the States and Union Territories including in the State of Uttarakhand against the Expression of Interest (EoI) issued by MoFPI from time to time.The total number of projects supported for establishment of Mega Food Parks and food processing units in the country and in the State of Uttarakhand under the relevant schemes of PMKSY along with approved grant- in-aid are as follows:Scheme	Number	of projects Approved in	Grants-	in-	aid Approved(In Rs. Crore)	the Country	Uttarakhand	Country	UttarakhandMega	Food Parks	37	2	1719.20	98.12Creation/Expansion	of Food Processing & Preservation Capacities	219	11	821.41	50.49*****
