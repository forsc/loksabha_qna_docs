                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

674






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Loss of Life in Army




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number and the details of army personnel who have lost their lives on duty since 2014, and the compensation paid to their families; and(b) the number of soldiers lost in conflicts with neighbouring countries, including China and Pakistan, country wise, since 2014?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a)  (i) Details of Battle Casualties (Fatal) of Army Personnel since 2014 are as under:S. No.	Year	No. of Casualties(aa)	2014	78(ab)	2015	109(ac)	2016	133(ad)	2017	137(ae)	2018	117(af)	2019	107(ag)	2020 (till 14.09.2020)	57Total	738Note:  The ibid figures are exclusive of casualties under process for classification.   (ii)  The details of compensation paid to Next of Kin (NoK) are as per Annexure attached.(b)  Details of Battle Casualties (Fatal) of Army Personnel which occurred during conflicts with neighbouring countries, including China and Pakistan since 2014 are as under:Fatal Battle Casualties during Border Skirmish with ChinaS. No.	Year	No. of Casualties(aa)	2014	-(ab)	2015	-(ac)	2016	-(ad)	2017	-(ae)	2018	-(af)	2019	-(ag)	2020 (till 14.09.2020)	20Total	20	Fatal Battle Casualties during Cease Fire Violations by PakistanS. No.	Year	No. of Casualties(aa)	2014	-(ab)	2015	04(ac)	2016	07(ad)	2017	23(ae)	2018	19(af)	2019	13(ag)	2020 (till 14.09.2020)	09Total	75Note: (i)	The ibid figures are exclusive of casualties under process for classification(ii)	No Fatal Battle Casualties occurred with neighbouring countries except ibid figures*****
