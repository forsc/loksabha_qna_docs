                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2654






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Policy of DDA




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the policy of Delhi Development Authority (DDA) to provide land for Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) Stations and Electric Vehicle (EV) charging stations in view of deteriorating level of pollution in Delhi;(b)	the details of land allotted under this policy to set up CNG Stations and EV charging stations in Delhi during the last three years;(c)	the details of applications received by DDA for land allotment to set up CNG Stations and EV charging stations so far;(d)	the details of action taken on these applications; and(e)	whether it is a fact that DDA is taking laxity for allotment of land to set up CNG Stations and EV charging stations and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                                                    THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                         MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                             (SHRI  KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)	Para 12.13 of the Master Plan for Delhi-2021 (MPD-2021) has provisions for fuel stations, including CNG stations (https://dda.gov.in/sites/default/files/inline-files/Master_Plan_for_Delhi_2021_text_report.pdf).  Regulations for setting up fuel stations (including CNG) on privately owned lands in National Capital Territory of Delhi are contained in Delhi Development Authority (DDA) Notification No. S.O 1244(E) dated 08.03.2019(https://egazette.nic.in/WriteReadData/2019/199511.pdf).   Further, as per “Para 20.6.6 Parking” in the Transit Oriented Development (TOD) Policy notified by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (https://egazette.nic.in/WriteReadData/2021/228596.pdf), at least 20% of parking area shall be equipped with charging points for electric vehicles as per Unified Building Byelaws for Delhi 2016.  There is no provision for allotment of land for EV charging stations in MPD-2021. (b)	Land for 6 CNG sites have been auctioned during the last 3 years.  DDA has also accorded planning permission (NOC) in 28 cases for setting up CNG stations on privately owned land.(c)	70 applications were received for allotment of CNG Stations.  (d)	DDA has decided to allot CNG sites through open e-auction.  This will ensure objectivity, transparency and fair play in the allotment of CNG sites.(e)	No Sir.                                              ******
