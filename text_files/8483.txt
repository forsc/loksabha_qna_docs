                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

182






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Restoration of Old Temples




Guman Singh Damor














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the plan of the Government for restoration of old temples;(b)	whether  the  Government  is  likely  to  include  more  than  hundred  years   old   temples   numbering     more   than  500   in   Ratlam  of   Madhya  Pradesh under the said scheme;(c)	if so, the amount of funds to be provided for this purpose and the Department through which the restoration work is going to be carried out; and(d)  whether funds are likely to be provided for construction of a tribal museum for keeping the culture of tribal community alive in Ratlam which requires to be given special focus?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d) A statement is laid on the table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (d) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *182  FOR 02.12.2019.(a)Conservation work of protected monuments in the country including old temples is attended regularly by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) as per the requirements of different sites and availability of resources and they are in a good state of preservation.(b) & (c) Presently there is no such proposal. (d) Presently there is no such proposal.
