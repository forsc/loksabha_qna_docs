                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

569






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Status of JJM




Vijay Kumar Dubey













                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the status of Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) in rural communities across the country particularly in Uttar Pradesh and Tripura; (b) the details of the funds allotted under this mission, State-wise particularly for Uttar Pradesh and Tripura; and (c) the number of rural households in different districts of Uttar Pradesh who had been facilitated under JJM?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b) 	Since 15th August, 2019, Government of India, in partnership with States is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) - Har Ghar Jal to provide tap water connection to every rural household of the country by 2024.As reported by States/ UTs, out of 18.98 Crore rural households across the country, as of now, 7.76 Crore households (40.89%) have tap water supply in their homes. In Uttar Pradesh,31.37 lakh (11.91%) rural households andin Tripura, 2.42 lakh (30.27%) households are having tap water connections.The State/ UT-wise details of fund allocated, including those of Uttar Pradesh & Tripura, under the JJM for the year 2019–20, 2020–21 & 2021–22, is annexed.(c) 	The district/ village–wise status of tap water connection in rural households of all States, including those in districts of Uttar Pradesh is available on JJM dashboard, which is in public domain at:https://ejalshakti.gov.in/jjmreport/JJMIndia.aspx.*****
