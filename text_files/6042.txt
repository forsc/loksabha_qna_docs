                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5573






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Personal Health Identification Number




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government is considering to provide Personal Health Identification Number (PHIN) to each citizen on the lines of Aadhaar Card containing health history of each citizen, if so, the details thereof; and (b) the time by which the said PHIN is likely to be launched in the country including Gujarat?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	A committee was constituted under the chairmanship of Shr. J. Satyanarayana, former Chairman of UIDAI & former Secretary, MeitY to draft the National Digital Health Blueprint (NDHB) for India. The draft is currently placed in public domain for comments.The Committee has recommended designing and establishing Personal Health Identifier (PHI) in consultation with MeitY and UIDAI duly taking into consideration the regulatory, technological and operational aspects.(b):	The National Digital Health Blueprint (NDHB) is being finalized.
