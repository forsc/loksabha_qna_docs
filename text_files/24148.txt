                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

80






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Public Sector Steel Units




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of sick public sector steel units in the country at present;(b)	whether the Government has taken any steps to revive the said units;(c)	if so, the details thereof, plant-wise; and(d)	the time by which the said work is likely to be undertaken?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL		   (SHRI RAM CHANDRA PRASAD SINGH)(a)to(d): There are two steel manufacturing Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) under the Ministry of Steel, namely, Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL) and Rashtriya Ispat Nigam Limited (RINL). Both CPSEs do not fall under the category of sick CPSEs.********
