                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

444






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Proposals for Road Projects from Tamil Nadu




M.K. Vishnu Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of proposals received from Tamil Nadu for road construction projects during each of the last three years and current year; and (b) the number of proposals approved by the Government along with the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)&(b) Ministry is primarily responsible for the development and maintenance of National Highways in the country.  The details of National Highway proposals received from Government of Tamil Nadu and approved/sanctioned during each of the last three years and current year are as under:Year	Nos. of proposals  received	Nos. of proposals approved/ sanctioned	Length of sanctioned proposals(in Km.)	Sanctioned Cost (Rs. in Crore)2018-2019	24	20	147.63	862.112019-2020 	24	24	100.71	370.212020-2021 	38	37	229.81	1493.522021-2022(up to January, 2022)	56	36	473.67	1592.78 *****
