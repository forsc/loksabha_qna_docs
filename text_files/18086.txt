                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1721






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Speed Post Centres in Chhattisgarh




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of operational speed post centres across the country, as on date, State/UT-wise including Chhattisgarh; (b) 	whether the Government proposes to set up more such centres across the country and if so, the details thereof and the number of such centres proposed to be opened across the country, State/UT-wise including Chattisgarh; (c) 	if so, the steps taken by the Government in this regard; and (d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) 	The Department of Posts, under a new operational arrangement has changed the nomenclature of Speed Post Centres to National Sorting Hubs (NSHs) and Intra Circle Hubs (ICHs). As on date there are 90 NSHs and 151 ICHs across the country including Chhattisgarh. The State-wise details are at Annexure.(b) to (d) 	No proposal for creation of National Speed Post Hub is under consideration.
