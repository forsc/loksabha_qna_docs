                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

803






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Working Women Hostel




Pratima Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is implementing Working Women Hostels Scheme and if so, the salient features thereof; and(b)	the number and details of hostels sanctioned and presently operational in the country, State/UT-wise






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTRY OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPEMNT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) Yes, Sir. Working Women Hostel is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented by States with funding ratio 60:15:25 shared amongst Central Government, State/UT and the implementing agency (for North-East and Himalayan States, the ratio is 65:10:25). The Scheme aims to provide safe and affordable accommodation to working women with Day Care facility for the children of inmates. Working women who may be single, widowed, divorced, separated or married, but whose husbands or immediate family do not reside in the same area and for those women who are under training for job are eligible under the scheme. (b) A total of 971 hostels have been sanctioned since the inception of the Scheme.  On the basis of reports received from States/UTs, 497 hostels are found to be operational in the country.
