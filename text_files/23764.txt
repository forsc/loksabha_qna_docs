                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4720






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Toy Sector




 Chandra Sekhar Bellana














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has identified the by sector as one of the champion sectors, with a long term vision and plan to develop toy clusters in the country;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government plans to give a boost to domestic toy manufacturers; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) & (b):  	Yes Sir. The toy sector has been identified as one of the champion sectors. A number of measures have been identified to develop toy clusters in the country with a long term vision. Some of the measures identified are: Using toys as a learning resource;  Designing of toys based on Indian values, culture, and history; Organizing hackathons and grand challenges for toy designing and manufacturing; Public procurement of indigenous toys; Promoting Made in India toys; Promoting indigenous toy clusters; Monitoring quality of toys; Controlling imports of sub-standard and unsafe toys, etc. (c) & (d): 	A number of toy clusters have been identified for boosting domestic toy manufacturers. The details of the toy clusters are enclosed at Annexure. ****
