                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4847






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Funds released under PMAY




Chandra Sekhar Sahu














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of funds released under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) to the State of Odisha during each of the last three years;(b)	the details of proposals received from Odisha under the PMAY-G during 2018-19; and(c)	the details of the proposals which have been sanctioned under the said scheme during the said period?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The funds released under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G) to the State of Odisha during each of the last three years is as follows:                                      (Rs. in lakhs)S.No.	Financial Year	             Funds released1.	   2016-17	              149452.9342.	   2017-18	              312405.9033.	   2018-19	              329032.427(b) & (c):	During the year 2018-19, Government of Odisha had submitted proposals for release of funds for construction of houses under PMAY-G viz., 2nd instalment of 2017-18, 1st & 2nd instalment of 2018-19 against which Rs.129406.69 lakh, Rs. 97224.75 lakh and Rs. 97224.75 lakh have been released respectively. *****
