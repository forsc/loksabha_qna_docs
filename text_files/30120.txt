                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1787






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Budget allocation for BharatNet Project




Ramesh Chander Kaushik













                                                    Saikia Dilip
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of budgetory allocations made and its utilization presently for BharatNet project, State-wise including Haryana State;(b) 	whether targets fixed for the said project has been achieved; and(c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) to (c)	BharatNet project is implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity to all Gram Panchayats (2.6 lakh approx.) in the country. As on 01.11.2021, a total number of 1,66,088 Gram Panchayats (GPs) are made Service Ready. The scope of BharatNet on 30.06.2021 was extended up to all inhabited villages beyond GPs in the country. The target date for completion of the project is August, 2023.As on 30.09.2021, the State/UT-wise details of allocation / disbursement of funds (including Haryana) under BharatNet Project are given in Annexure-I.
