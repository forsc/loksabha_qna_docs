                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3712






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Status of Nirbhaya Fund




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the details and current status of the Nirbhaya Fund with regard to Dahod district of Gujarat;  (b) 	the increase in the amount of this fund with regard to Dahod district through the allocation made in the finance budget during the last three years; and  (c)  	the rank-wise position of the States in terms of spending the allocated amount under Nirbhaya Fund, State/UT-wise?  






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT        (SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)  (a) & (b) : In case of all schemes / projects under Nirbhaya Fund except under One Stop Centre (OSC) and Universalisation of Women Helpline (WHL) Schemes of this Ministry, the funds are released to the States/ UTs for implementation. Hence, the district-wise data with regard to overall allocation to Districts under Nirbhaya Fund is not maintained. However, under the Scheme of One Stop Centre (OSC), an amount of Rs. 88.75 Lakhs has been released to Dahod District of Gujarat. Under WHL Scheme, the fund is released to the Districts designated by the State for running the WHL. In Gujarat, the WHL is being run from Ahmedabad District.
