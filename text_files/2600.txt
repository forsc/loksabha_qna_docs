                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2370






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Funds to Odisha under Article 275(1)




Ramesh Chandra Majhi














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the amount of funds allocated to various States in the country by the Government as per Article 275(1); and (b)	 the amount of funds allocated to Odisha under Article 275(1) during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	& (b)	This Ministry is administering a programme of “Grants under Article 275(1) of the Constitution” wherein funds are released to 27 States, having ST Population. Under this programme, 100% funding is made by Government of India which is aimed to enable the States to meet the cost of such schemes of development as may be undertaken by the State for the purpose of promoting the welfare of Scheduled Tribes in that State or raising the level of administration of Scheduled Areas therein to that of the administration of the rest of the areas of that State.
