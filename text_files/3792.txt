                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3514






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Placement Cell in University




Pratima Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Universities in the country have a placement cell for everydepartment and if so, the details thereof, University-wise; and(b) the measures taken to generate employment for social science/arts graduates?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) & (b): The University Grants Commission (UGC) on 19th September, 2014requested all Central Universities to establish a Placement Cell for all disciplinesand co-ordinate with industry for placement of pass-outs. Universities areautonomous bodies established under Central/State/Provincial Acts. Universitywisedata regarding placement cell is not maintained centrally.Further, the UGC on 31st January, 2017 requested the Vice-Chancellors of allUniversities to review and revise curricula of all academic departments inUniversities at least once in every three years. This review and revision ofacademic curricula should focus on the existing and potential demand and supplyof skill sets to make the University/College students employable.******
