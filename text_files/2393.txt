                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2128






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Hybrid Simulator for Traffic Mapping and Modelling




Ganesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has received a report on hybrid simulation for traffic mapping and modelling for easing traffic congestion in big cities i.e. infrastructure projects like, roads, flyovers, underpasses and other rapid transit projects;(b) 	if so, the city-wise details including those in district Satna;(c) 	whether the said report has been prepared by a JV between Medulla Soft Technology Private Limited Transport Simulation Systems, Spain and if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	whether the Government has prepared any roadmap for easing traffic congestion in district Satna and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                         THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d) The Indian Academy of Highway Engineers (IAHE) has engaged M/s Medulla Soft Technologies Pvt. Ltd. in JV with TSS Transport Simulation System SL, for Consultancy Services for Traffic Performance Evaluation and Optimization of Highways in Delhi using Simulation Analysis. The Consultant has submitted Draft Final Report. To ease traffic congestion in Satna District, 4- laning of NH 30 (old NH 7) and a bypass on NH 75 in Satna town is in construction stage. *****
