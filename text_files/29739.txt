                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1394






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Floriculture in Andhra Pradesh




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Andhra Pradesh is the largest producer of flowers in the country in spite of limited area under cultivation;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether any special emphasis is given for protected cultivation through financial assistance; and (d)	if so, the details of the same?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ?? ????? ?????? ??????       ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b): As per Third Advance Estimates, Andhra Pradesh is the third largest producer of Flowers in the country during the year 2020-21. The Estimated Area covered under Flower Cultivation was 19.84 Thousand Hectare and Estimated Production of Flowers was 406.85 Thousand Tonne in Andhra Pradesh in the year 2020-21.  Andhra Pradesh contributed 15.62% of the total Flower production in the country during 2020-21. Andhra Pradesh has the highest production from per unit area among the major flowers producing States in the country.
