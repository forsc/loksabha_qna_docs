                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2088






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Extension of Metro Rail to Narela




Raj Hans Hans














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any proposal for extension of metro rail from Badli to Narela has already been finalised by the Government;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the reasons for delay in execution of the extension work; and(d) the time by which the project is likely to be executed?  






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                          (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a):	Urban Transport, which is an integral part of urban development, is a State subject.  Hence, respective State Governments / Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) are responsible for planning and development of urban transport infrastructure including metro rail.  The Central Government considers financial assistance for metro rail proposals in cities or urban agglomerates, on the basis of feasibility of the proposal and availability of resources, as and when posed by the concerned State Governments.  At present, Central Government has not received any proposal for extension of Delhi Metro rail from Badli to Narela.(b)to(d):	Do not arise.                              *****
