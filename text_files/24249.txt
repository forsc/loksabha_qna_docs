                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

195






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Expenditure Incurred for Implementation of Atmanirbhar Packages




A. Chellakumar













                                                    Pradyut Bordoloi
                                                








                                                    M. Selvaraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the quantum of revenue expenditure incurred in the 2020-2021 financial year to implement components of the Atmanirbhar Packages 1.0, 2.0 and 3.0;b)	The quantum of revenue expenditure incurred to implement components of the Atmanirbhar Packages 1.0, 2.0 and 3.0 upto March 31, 2020, December 31, 2020 and March 31, 2021 as a proportion of total Budget Estimates for the 2020-21 financial year; andc)	the quantum of capital expenditure incurred to implement components of the Atmanirbhar Packages 1.0, 2.0 and 3.0 upto March 31, 2020 and December 31, 2020 as a proportion of total Budget Estimates for the 2020- 21 financial year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (FINANCE)(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a)	to (c): The Government of India had announced Atmanirbhar Bharat Packages 1.0, 2.0, and 3.0 on 13th  May, 2020 to 17th  May, 2020,  12th  October, 2020  and 12th November 2020 respectively.  The Aatmanirbhar Bharat Packages comprise of various long-term schemes/ programmes/ policies announced with the objective of fighting COVID-19 and to make the country self-reliant, whose outcomes would be visible over a period of time.  The Ministry/Department have been tasked to formulate schemes/programmes announced under AatmaNirbhar Bharat Packages concerning them, take necessary approvals and meet the expenditure out of fund provided in Budget Estimates and seeking additional funds in Revised Estimates, wherever necessary.
