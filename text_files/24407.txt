                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

259






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






MGNREGS during Pandemic




P.P. Chaudhary













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Arjunlal Meena
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details and the number of people who have benefitted under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) since April 2020, State-wise, gender-wise, and discreetly mentioning new enrolments;(b) 	whether there has been an increase in enrolment in rural areas during and after the pandemic, if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the expenditure incurred from the start of the pandemic till now, the details on the usage of MGNREGS budgetary allocation during the last two years, State-wise;(d) 	whether some States have proposed changes in the scheme for its improvement and efficient implementation; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof including the feasibility of proposed changes?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)&(b):	Details of State-wise and gender-wise number of persons provided employment under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) in FY 2020-21 and FY 2021-22 (as on 16.07.2021) is given at Annexure-I. During financial year 2020-21, 1.87 crore new job cards were issued under Mahatma Gandhi NREGA which is 169% higher than the number of new job cards issued in the FY 2019-20, which was 69.53 lakh. The details of States/UTs-wise new job cards issued in FY 2020-21 and FY 2021-22 (as on 16.07.2021) is given at Annexure-II.(c):	The Budget Allocation and release of funds during last two financial years 2019-20 and   2020-21 is given as:                                                                (Rs. In crore)S. No.	           Revised Estimate	           Release of funds	         2019-20	2020-21	         2019-20	  2020-211.	       71,001.81	1,11,500	71,687.71	 1,11,700.86Under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS Scheme, no allocation of funds is made to States/UTs-wise, being a demand driven Scheme. Funds are released to States/UTs as per the requirements of funds against “agreed to” Labour Budget. States/UTs-wise detail of expenditure incurred during the last two financial years 2019-20, 2020-21and the current FY 2021-22 (as on 16.07.2021) under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS is given at Annexure-III.(d):	No Sir.(e):	Does not arise.********
