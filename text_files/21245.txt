                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2439






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Fisheries Sector in Chhattisgarh




Sunil Kumar Soni














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING ??????????, ???????  ??  ?????  ?????? be pleased to state:(a) 	the  details of the funds allocated and utilised under fisheries sector in the State of Chhattisgarh during the last two years ;and(b)	the  details of the guidelines issued by the Government to the State Government of Chhattisgarh for proper implementation of the fisheries subsidies provided by the Union Government?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING                                                                             (SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a): 	The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) on Blue Revolution :Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries provided  funds to the tune of Rs 4338.59 lakh during the last two financial years to the Government of Chhattisgarh for development of fisheries. The State Government has reported utilisation of the entire funds provided by the Department of Fisheries, Government of India. The details of  funds released during the last two years and utilized as reported by the Government of Chhattisgarh are furnished below:(Rs. in lakhs)S.No	Year	Central Funds released 	Funds Utilized 1	2018-19	1045.69	1045.692	2019-20	3292.90	3292.90(b)   :   Detailed guidelines on  CSS on Blue Revolution: Integrated Development & Management of fisheries were issued in the  year 2016  vide letter No. 27035-19/2015 (Fy) (IV) Vol.II to all States/Union Territories (UTs) including the Government of Chhattisgarh for availing benefits for developmental fisheries. The Government of Chhattisgarh has reported that the Guidelines have been followed for proper implementation of the fisheries subsidies. Besides, the Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying, Department of Fisheries in June, 2020 has also issued detailed Operational Guidelines of the newly launched “Pradhan Mantri Matasya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY)- A scheme to bring about Blue Revolution through sustainable and responsible development of fisheries sector in India” to all the States/UTs including Chhattisgarh for availing  its benefits as well as implementation of the scheme for development of fisheries in their respective  States and UTs. *****
