                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

205






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Road Over Bridge




Dharmendra Kumar Kashyap














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that there is a proposal to construct a Road Over Bridge (ROB) across the railway line at Faridpur railway crossing in district Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh;(b)	if so, the salient features of this project; and(c)	the time by which this project is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (c): The issue pertains to Level Crossing (LC) No. 352C at Km 1288/28-30 on Bareilly-Shahjahanpur section. This LC qualifies for replacement by Road over Bridge (ROB) on cost-sharing basis as traffic density is more than one lakh TVU. Accordingly, State Government of Uttar Pradesh was requested to submit the proposal for construction of ROB in lieu of LC along-with necessary consent for sharing of cost & closure of LC subject to feasibility at site.   No proposal in this regard has been received from State Government.*****
