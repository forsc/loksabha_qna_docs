                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5419






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Allocation and Utilisation of Funds under SBM-G




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the funds have been allocated under the Swachh Bharat Mission-Gramin (SBM-G) for the financial year 2019-20;(b) 	if so, the funds allocated/released/utilized during the financial year 2019-20State-wise; and(c) 	the component-wise expenditure of funds for the said period such as for toilets Information, Education and Communication (IEC), HR and Solid and Liquid Waste Management (SLWM) etc.?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) 	Yes, Sir.(b) 	Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) (SBM-G) is demand driven scheme, hence fixed State/UT-wise allocation of funds is not made under this programme.  During the financial year 2019-20 (as on 23.7.2019), funds amounting to Rs.4360.52 crore have been released to the States/UTs as Performance Incentive Grants under World Bank support to SBM-G.  State/UT-wise details of the same are given at Annexure.(c) 	Component-wise expenditure of funds under SBM(G) during the financial year 2019-20 as reported by the States/UTs on the online Integrated Management Information System (IMIS) of SBM(G) is as under:(Rs.in crores)Individual Household Latrines (IHHLs)	Community Sanitary Complexes	IEC	HRD	Admin Expenses	SLWM5395.27	54.00	34.81	1.48	6.79	23.44
