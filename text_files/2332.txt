                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2182






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Funding of Drinking Water Schemes




Arjunlal Meena














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposesto provide funds on 50:50 basis for thedrinking water scheme for the projectsbased on surface water sources byreconsidering the extant guidelines;(b) if so, the time by which it is likelyto be provided; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FORJAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)	Water is a state subject. It is for the States to plan, design, implement and execute drinking water supply schemes in rural areas.Funds under National Rural Drinking Water Programme (NRDWP) are released to all States for taking up Piped Water Supply Schemes (PWSS)as per guidelinesand the budgetary allocation under the programme. As per the extant guidelines, coverage component of NRDWP, funds are released to the States on 50:50 (Centre: State) sharing basis.(b)&(c):   Does not arise.
