                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

949






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Development of Animal Husbandry




Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                








                                                    Prataprao  Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the details of schemes being formulated by the Government for the development of animal husbandry in the country; (b) the details of steps taken by the Government to infuse new and latest technology in the field of animal husbandry to reach the farmers and achieve the target of doubling their income; (c) whether the Government also proposes to import high yielding cattle breeds and artificial insemination process to give a flip to dairy industry and if so, the details thereof; (d) the details of the funds allocated and released under the National Dairy Development Scheme in the country, State/UT-wise; and (e) the details of steps taken/being taken by the Government to set up new milk processing units in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI PARSHOTTAMRUPALA)(a) and (b)The Government is implementing various schemes to enhance production and productivity of bovines & small ruminants (sheep & goat), piggery & poultry across the country namely:i.	RashtriyaGokul Missionii.	National Programme for Dairy Development iii.	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fundiv.	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activities v.	National Livestock Missionvi.	Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fundvii.	Livestock Health & Disease Controlviii.	National Animal Disease Control Programme ix.	Livestock Census and Integrated Sample Survey	Under RashtriyaGokul Mission the following new technologies have been made accessible to the farmers for enhancement of milk production and productivity of bovines•	IVF Technology for Bovines•	Use of Bovine Sex Sorted Semen (c)       In order to improve milk production and productivity and genetic merit of bovine the Department has been implementing Progeny Testing and Pedigree Selection Programme under RGM for production of High Genetic Merit Bulls of Indigenous Breeds. High Genetic Merit Bulls of exotic breeds have also been imported under National Dairy Plan-I and Rashtriya Gokul Mission. In order to extend the Artificial Insemination coverage in the country, the Department has initiated Nation Wide Artificial Insemination Programme (NAIP).(d)The State/UT-wise details of the funds allocated and released under the National Dairy Development Scheme in the country are annexed.(e) The Government is implementing various schemes for promotion and development of dairy sector including setting up of milk processing units in the country namely:(i).	National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD): For creating/strengthening of infrastructure for production of quality milk, procurement, processing and marketing of milk and milk products by State Implementing Agency.(ii).	Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund (DIDF): For creation /modernization /expansion of dairy processing, chilling and value addition infrastructure.(iii).	Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activities (SDC&FPO): To assist the Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer organisations by providing soft loan for working capital and to provide stable market access to the dairy farmers.(iv).	Animal Husbandry Infrastructure Development Fund: For assisting Dairy Processing and value addition infrastructure.****
