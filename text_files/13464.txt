                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2135






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Expenditure on PM Visit




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of EXTERNAL AFFAIRS be pleased to state: the total expenditure on the foreign visits made by the Hon’ble Prime Minister during the last five years? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS [SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN]  Information is placed below: <pre>Financial Year Expenditure on chartered flights + hotline costs (services of TCL for hotline services discontinued  since June 2017)2015-16INR 121.85 Crore2016-17INR 78.52 Crore2017-18INR 99.90 Crore2018-19INR 100.02 Crore2019-20INR 46.23 Crore (bills for some visits not received)Total INR 446.52 Crore</pre>***
