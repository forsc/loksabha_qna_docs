                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2322






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Fill up Vacancies in Universities




Dibyendu Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to fill up all posts lying vacant in all theuniversities under University Grants Commission (UGC);(b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(c) whether UGC has given necessary approval and fixed a time frame to fill up the saidvacancies; and(d) if so, the suggestions and proposals from universities and or colleges in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d): Occuring of vacancies and filling up is a continuous process. The onus offilling up the posts lies on Central Universities which are autonomous bodies createdunder Acts of Parliament. However, the University Grants Commission (UGC)continuously monitors it with Universities.The University Grants Commission (UGC), vide its D.O. letter No. F.1-14/2019 (CPP-II)dated 4th June, 2019, has prepared the Guidelines for Recruitment of Faculty inUniversities, Colleges and Institutions Deemed to be Universities outlining the selectionprocedure and the time frame for recruitment. These guidelines have been circulated toall Universities to adhere to them. The Universities have also been requested to takesteps to ensure that the vacancies in the University as well as in the Colleges affiliatedto University are filled up at the earliest. A copy of the Guidelines circulated to allUniversities is available at https://www.ugc.ac.in/ugc_notices.aspx?id=2418.This Ministry has also requested all Higher Educational Institutions (HEIs) to fill upfaculty related data on National Higher Education Resource Centre (NHERC) portal athttps://nherc.in in order to fill up all vacancies in various higher educational institutionson an urgent basis.As informed by UGC, the State-wise details of vacancies in teaching and non-teachingposts in all universities under UGC are available athttps://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/2232390_State-wise-teaching.pdf andhttps://www.ugc.ac.in/pdfnews/6599300_State-wise-Non-teaching.pdf respectively.*****
