                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1527






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Promotion of IGNOU Courses




N.K. Premachandran














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to promote the courses conducted by Indira GandhiNational Open University (IGNOU);(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the other Universities and council recognize the degree, post graduate diplomagranted by IGNOU and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether the Government has noticed that PG diploma in clinical cardiology (PG DCC)granted by IGNOU is not recognized by Medical Council of India;(e) if so, the details thereof and the action taken by Government and IGNOU for the recognitionof Medical Council; and(f) if so, the steps taken/being taken by the Government to remove the difficulties?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): The fresh enrolment by IGNOU during 2018-19 was 673696 students against freshenrolment of 574073 students in the year 2017-18. This increase has been facilitated by the stepstaken to promote and establish quality norms in the field of open & distance learning byUniversity Grants Commission (Open and Distance Learning) Regulations, 2017.(c): Further, as per the provisions contained in University Grants Commission (Open andDistance Learning) Regulations, 2017, Open Universities can only run thosecourses/programmes which have due approval from the respective statutory regulators in thefield of higher education. Accordingly the courses currently run by IGNOU is recognised byother universities and are also recognised for employment.(d) to (f): The PG Diploma in Clinical Cardiology (PG DCC) granted by IGNOU is notrecognised by the Medical Council of India. IGNOU had taken up the proposal of recognition ofPG DCC with MCI, however, the request of IGNOU was not acceded to by MCI since the courseoffered by IGNOU was through distance mode. It is the decision of the government thatprofessional courses through distance mode is not to be allowed unless it is cleared by thecorresponding regulators.****
