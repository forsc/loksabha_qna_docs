                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EARTH SCIENCES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3034






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Sustainable Development Goals for Ocean Resources




Narendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





EARTH SCIENCES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has set sustainable development goals for the sustainable use and conservation of ocean resources;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the quantum of funds allocated for the purpose during the last three years, State/UT wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FORMINISTRY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGYAND EARTH SCIENCES(DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a)	Yes, Sir.(b)	National Centre for Coastal Research (NCCR), an attached office of MoES, provides scientific data for the Sustainable Development Goal-14 “ Life Below Water” targets 14.1 and 14.3. NCCR monitors 50 locations (coastal sites) covering the all the coastal states and Union Territories for parameters such as nutrients, dissolved oxygen, and pH. Coastal Water Quality Index (CWQI) has been developed based on these and shared with Ministry of Statistics and Program Implementation (MoSPI), for implementation of Sustainable Development Goals-National Indicator Framework (SDG-NIF).(c)	The budget allocated by MoES to NCCR for carrying out the research activities during last 03 years is given below.Year	Amount (Rs in crores)2018-19	3.402019-20	6.422020-21	4.33Total	14.15
