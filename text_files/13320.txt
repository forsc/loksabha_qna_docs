                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2149






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Railway Projects




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government has allocated 3024 crore for South Western Railway to implement railway projects for 2020-21;(b)	 if so, the details thereof including new lines sanctioned, doubling projects, electrification works and track renewal works approved;(c)	 the present status of Kadur to Chikmagalur, Mysore to Mangalore via Hassan and Birur to Talaguppa electrification projects;(d)	 whether there are still more than 3379 km of railway lines to be electrified falling fully/partly in the State of Karnataka till date and if so, the details thereof, zone-wise;(e)	 whether the estimated expenditure required for electrification of the said number of kms is to the tune of more than 3700 crore; and(f)	 if so, the quantum of fund allocated for this during the current year and whether it is sufficient to meet the target?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	 to (f) : A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.***STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (f) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.2149 BY KUMARI SHOBHA KARANDLAJE TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 04.03.2020 REGARDING RAILWAY PROJECTS (a) A gross outlay of ? 5337.78 crore has been provided to South Western Railway under Capital section of Demand No.83 for acquisition, construction and replacement of assets in Budget Estimates 2020-21.(b) : The details of sanctioned projects of New Lines, Doubling, Electrification and Track Renewals have been made available in public domain on Indian Railways’ official website www.indianrailways.gov.in-Ministry of Railways-Railway Board-About Indian Railways-Railway Board Directorates-Finance (Budget).  (c) : Electrification projects of (i) Kadur-Chikmaglur, (ii) Mysuru-Hassan-Mangalore incl Arsikere-Hassan and (iii) Birur-Talguppa have been sanctioned in September’ 2018.  All these projects are under process of tendering and are planned for completion by December’ 2023 in phased manner.(d) to (f) : Zone wise details of Railway Electrification Projects falling fully/partly in the State of Karnataka are appended.***
