                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2978






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






NHs Connectivity to Purnia




Santosh Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any proposal to connect Purnia with National Highways 	(NHs) and Expressways of other parts of the country in near future; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the steps being taken by the Government for timely completion of the said projects?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) 	Purnia is already connected with other parts of the country through East-West Corridor as well as through Khagaria-Purnia section of NH-31.  In addition to this, civil work on Madhepura-Purnia section (i.e. package-II of Maheshkhunt-Saharsa-Madhepura-Purnia stretch) was commenced in December, 2018 with scheduled completion period as 2 years.  The work for Package-I i.e. Maheskhunt-Saharsa-Madhepura section has also been awarded but appointed date has not been fixed due to non-availability of adequate land.  	Further, bidding for improvement & widening of Manihari-Katihar-Purnia (Narenpur-Purnia) section has been annulled due to non-availability of land.  	The concerned State Government has been requested to expedite the land acquisition.*****
