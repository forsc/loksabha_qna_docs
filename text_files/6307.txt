                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

25






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






World Bank Loan for Development Project




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is true that the World Bank has approved Andhra Pradesh and Telangana Municipal Development Project in 2010 with a loan amount of US $ 300 million; (b)	if so, the details and the status of the project; and(c)	the time by which the project is going to be commissioned?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (c):  The World Bank had approved the Andhra Pradesh and Telangana Municipal Development Project with a loan amount of US$ 300 million on December 10, 2009. The project became effective on March 23, 2010 and was implemented in the states of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana with total project cost of US$ 350 million. The project was closed on December 31, 2018.
