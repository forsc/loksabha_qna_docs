                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1226






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Setting up of the State-of-the-Art Museum in Kerala




Abdul Majeed Ariff













                                                    Kumbakudi Sudhakaran
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received any proposal from the Kerala State Government for renovation and setting up of state-of-the-art museum and culturally and archaeologically significant Napier Museum at Thiruvananthapuram and Krishnapuram Palace at Karthikapally in Alappuzha district of Kerala;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the status of the proposal; (c)	the action taken by the Government in this regard; and(d)	whether there are any proposals pending before the Government of India regarding the development of Krishnapuram Palace Museum and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d) Yes Sir, Government has received proposals from the State Government of Kerala seeking financial assistance amounting to Rs. 1273.142 lakhs for Napier Museum and Rs. 352.676 lakhs for  Krishnapuram Palace Museum, under Museum Grant Scheme. These proposals were considered as per the guidelines of scheme. At present, Ministry has not approved the above proposals.
