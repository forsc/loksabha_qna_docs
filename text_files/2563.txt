                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2439






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Promote Engineering Students




Ajay (Teni) Misra














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to promote the engineering students carrying outresearch work using new technique for taking up short term service in the education sector;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether there is any plan for providing the benefit of knowledge of technical educationobtained graduates to the children upto secondary level by making them teacher for shorttermperiod of four years under new education policy; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d): The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) encourages students to undertaketeaching as a career through schemes such as Post Graduate Scholarship and National DoctoralFellowship. Fellows of ‘Prime Minister Research Fellowship (PMRF)’ scheme are also expected toteach once a week in a neighbourhood ITI/ polytechnics/engineering college. The draft neweducation policy does not recommend for making technical graduates as teachers upto secondarylevel for short term period of four years.*****
