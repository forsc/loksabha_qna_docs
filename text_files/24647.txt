                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

612






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Sale of CWG Village Flats




Jyotsna Charandas Mahant














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether Delhi Development Authority (DDA) has sold CWG Village Flats in Akshardham, New Delhi through e-auction, tendering, Registration of Flats to Public Sector Undertaking/Banks and individuals etc.; (b)	if so, the details of flats sold through different schemes; and (c)	whether some CWG Flats have also been sold to Members of Parliament under the scheme of Registration and if so, the details  thereof?






ANSWER




                                                                  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                        (SHRI  KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)   Delhi Development Authority (DDA) sold/ disposed off CWG Village flats in Akshardham, New Delhi through the mode of direct allotment and first come first serve basis to Central Government Departments, Banks, Public Sector Undertakings, etc., while the mode of tendering/  e-auction was adopted to sell flats to individuals/ companies/ firms.(b)     Details of flats sold through different schemes/ modes are as under:S.No. 	Mode of sale / Allotment	   No. of Flats1	Tendering 	                       742	Through  E-Auction	                63	Allotted to Cabinet  Secretariat        64	On ‘First  Come  First Serve’ Basis    121	Total 	                               207(c)    Total 9 (Nine) flats were sold/ allotted to individuals through tendering/ e-auction. Of these, only one flat has been sold to Hon’ble Member of Parliament (Rajya Sabha).                                *****
