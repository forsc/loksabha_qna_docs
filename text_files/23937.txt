                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    POWER






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4906






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Electrification of Villages




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





POWER


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of villages or houses connected and yet to be connected with electricity line in Tamil Nadu, district-wise including Madurai; and(b) 	the details of average number of houses for which electricity is provided in these villages?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR POWER, NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR SKILL DEVELOPMENT & ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI  R.K. SINGH)(a) & (b) :	As reported by the State Government of Tamil Nadu, as on 28.04.2018 all inhabited census villages in the State stand electrified. Further, State of Tamil Nadu had declared on Saubhagya portal that all the willing un-electrified households in the State had been electrified as on 31st March, 2019. So far, a total of 2.37 Crore domestic service connections have been provided to houses (including huts) in Tamil Nadu. Further, State Government has reported that as and when an application for domestic service connection is received, the same is immediately being provided by the State.*********
