                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1125






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Health Schemes on Common Platform




Pritam Gopinath Munde














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Union Government is planning to bring health schemes on common platform;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether National Health Authority had a meeting with the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare to work out the arrangement on the lines of Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS) that covers all Central Government employees; and(d) the other step taken by the Government to bring all Government health schemes under a single umbrella?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	No. (c):	No.(d):	The hospitals under the administrative control of Ministry of Railways and Coal India Limited have been empanelled to provide services to the beneficiaries of AB-PMJAY. Memorandum of Understanding has also been signed with Employee State Insurance Corporation for onboarding their hospitals in the scheme.
