                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4861






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Grants to AIIMS Institutes




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government has been providing grants to the All India Institutes of Medical Science (AIIMS) in the country;b)	if so, the details of amount sanctioned and released during the last three years, AIIMS-wise; andc)	the details of steps taken for expansion, provision of new departments in the AIIMS, including AIIMS, Bhubaneswar during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (c): The Government provides grants-in-aid to All India Institute of Medical Science (AIIMS), New Delhi and new AIIMS, which have been approved under the Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY) and are functional or partially functional, to meet their operational expenditure.  The details of grants-in-aid  released to various AIIMS are at Annexure-I.  	Capacities of various AIIMS are strengthened based on requirements of the Institute, subject to availability of budgetary resources.   As far as AIIMS, Bhubaneswar is concerned, all the 18 speciality and 17 super-specialty Department envisaged for new AIIMS are functional.  One Satellite Centre of the Institute has also been approved at Balasore in Odisha.
