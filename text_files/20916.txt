                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2233






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Home Delivery of LPG Cylinders




Bhartruhari Mahtab













                                                    Chirag Paswan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the cases of overcharging for home delivery of domestic LPG cylinders aboveits MRP by LPG Distributors have come to the notice of the Government across thecountry particularly in small cities during each of the last three years and the current year;(b) if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise and Company-wise and the reasons thereforalong with the action taken/being taken by the Government in such cases so far;(c) the number and nature of complaints received by the Government against LPGDistributors across the country during the said period, State/UT-wise and Company-wisealong with the present status of such complaints;(d) whether the Government has received any complaint regarding non-supply of LPGcylinders to the consumers at regular basis by the LPG dealers across the countryincluding Bihar due to which the consumers have to bear the additional cost of transportingcylinders from godown and if so, the action taken against the guilty LPG distributors duringthe last three years and the current year along with the details thereof; and(e) the other steps taken/being taken by the Government to bring transparency in thefunctioning of LPG Distributors across the country along with achievements thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) State/UT-wise details of number of established cases of overcharging for homedelivery of domestic cylinders above the Retail Selling Price during the last three yearsand the current year i.e. 2017-18, 2018-19, 2019-20 and April-December 2020 are atAnnexure-I. In all such established cases, action is taken as per provisions of MarketingDiscipline Guidelines (MDG)/Distributorship Agreement.(c) Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) have received various complaints like delay inrefill delivery, unaccounted sale of LPG cylinders, supply of underweightcylinders/pilferage, release of LPG connection to ineligible persons, non-home delivery ofdomestic refills by LPG distributors etc. State/company-wise details of total establishedcases of irregularities against OMC Distributorships during the last three years and thecurrent year i.e. 2017-18, 2018-19, 2019-20 and April-December 2020 are at Annexure-II.In all such established cases, action is taken as per provisions of Marketing DisciplineGuidelines (MDG)/Distributorship Agreement.(d) OMCs have not received specific complaints regarding non-supply of LPG cylindersto the LPG consumers at regular basis by the LPG distributors across the countryincluding Bihar due to which the consumers have to bear additional cost of transportingcylinders from godown.(e) Major steps taken by the Government/OMCs to bring transparency in thefunctioning of LPG distribution across the country are:-? In order to regulate distribution of LPG, “Liquefied Petroleum Gas (Regulation ofSupply and Distribution) Order, 2000” has been notified.? OMCs have notified LPG Marketing Discipline Guidelines (MDG) to be adheredto by the distributors. The MDG is revised from time to time to cover all aspectsof LPG marketing and to keep a check on erring distributors? KYC initiative for all New Connections/Multiple Connections/Transferred/Inactivecustomers so that customers with proper identity and address proof are enrolledat distributors.? IVRS/SMS refill booking system has been introduced across the country in allregular distributorships, wherein customers get SMS on refill booking /generation of cash memo.? OMCs have also introduced e-Invoice so that customer can view the exact pricepayable .? Digital payment facility has also been provided to the consumers.? In order to have a convenient, easy and effective way to enable the citizen toprovide their feedback, a unique toll free telephone number 18002333555 is inoperation through call centres on industry basis.? OMCs have also introduced different colours for domestic and non-domesticLPG cylinders so that misuse of domestic LPG cylinders for unauthorised use iseasily identifiable.? In case the customer is not satisfied with the services of the existing distributor,facility of opting for another distributor operating in the same area is availableunder portability scheme.---------
