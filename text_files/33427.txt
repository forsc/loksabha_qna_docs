                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

878






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Bio Diversity Parks




Ganesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to develop Eco-Parks and Bio-Diversity Parks;(b)	if so, whether a proposal for providing financial assistance to develop an Eco-Park in the forest area in front of the World’s first White Tiger Safari at Mukundpur in Satna, Madhya Pradesh (MP) is lying pending with the Government;(c)	whether another proposal regarding developing a Bio-Diversity Park for the conservation of rare varieties and species of flora and fauna found in Narohills forest area in Satna district in MP is also pending with the Government;(d)	if so, the reasons for delay in sanctioning both the aforesaid schemes/projects; and(e)	the time by which both of these projects/schemes are likely to be sanctioned? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (e) Biodiversity parks, ecological parks etc., are established and developed by the concerned State Governments for protection and conservation of biodiversity. The Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change through its various centrally sponsored schemes provides financial assistance to the State Governments including Government of Madhya Pradesh for protection and conservation of forests and biodiversity.The State Forest Department of Madhya Pradesh has submitted an Annual Plan of Operations (APO) proposal to this Ministry in the month of January 2022 for availing funds under Compensatory Afforestation Fund Management and Planning Authority (CAMPA) scheme for the financial year 2022-2023 for establishment and development of eco-park and biodiversity park at Narohills of Satna district. ***
