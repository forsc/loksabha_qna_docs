                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3704






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Grant for Cold Storage for Potatoes




Mukesh Rajput














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to provide grant to potato growers for construction of cold storage; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	if not, whether there is any scheme for those potato farmers facing huge losses per year due to economic recession; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Government is implementing Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH), for holistic development of horticulture including setting up of cold storages for perishables including potato for which credit linked back ended subsidy at the rate of 35% in general areas and 50% in hilly and scheduled areas of project cost is available for individual entrepreneurs including farmers. The Mission also provides credit linked back ended subsidy for setting up of staging cold rooms in rural areas including potato growing areas at the rate of 35% in general areas and 50% in hilly and scheduled areas of project cost for 30 metric ton staging cold room with project cost of Rs. 15 Lakhs.(c) & (d):	Do not arise.*******
