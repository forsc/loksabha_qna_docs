                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

921






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Land Assets of AAI




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Airports Authority of India (AAI) proposes to manage large number of airports across the country by identifying best potential of their own land assets to improve the revenue stream, bringing better control and monitoring of its costs; and(b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir.  In order to enhance the non-aeronautical revenue potential of its land assets and to further improve the passenger facilities and convenience at its airports, the Airport Authority of India (AAI) is in the process of monetization of its city-side land by developing training centers, workshops, hotels, restaurants and rest rooms, warehouses, cargo complex etc. to the extent permitted under the provisions of AAI Act.
