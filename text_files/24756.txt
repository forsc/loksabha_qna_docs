                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

597






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Jal Shakti Abhiyan in Bihar and Gujarat




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam













                                                    Rama Devi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Jal Shakti Abhiyan has been implemented in Bihar and Gujarat; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the details of the targets set under the said Abhiyan in Bihar and Gujarat at present including the present status of the works done thereunder?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI AND TRIBAL AFFAIRS(SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU)(a)	 to (b) Ministry of Jal Shakti has taken up a nationwide campaign “Jal Shakti Abhiyan - Catch the Rain” (JSA:CTR) with the theme “Catch the rain, where it falls, when it falls” for creating appropriate rainwater harvesting structures in urban and rural areas of all the districts in the country including Bihar and Gujarat, with people''s active participation, during the pre-monsoon and monsoon periods. The campaign, primarily focusing on saving and conserving rainwater, was launched by Hon''ble Prime Minister on 22 March, 2021, the world water day. Creation of new and maintenance of old Rainwater harvesting structures; revival of traditional rainwater harvesting structures like stepwells etc; enumeration, geo-tagging, making inventory of water bodies, reuse and recharge of bore wells; watershed development and intensive afforestation preparation of scientific water conservation plans and setting up of Jal Shakti Kendras are part of the campaign.(c)	  The details of progress made under the Jal Shakti Abhiyan: Catch the Rain campaign upto 12.07.2021 as uploaded on JSA:CTR Portal (jsactr:mowr.gov.in) by the Central stakeholder Ministries/Departments in respect of their Key Performance indicators is annexed.  State Governments have also been request to upload the information regarding works undertaken by them with state and local funds under JSA:CTR campaign.***** ANNEXUREANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (c) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.†597 TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 22.07.2021 REGARDING “JAL SHAKTI ABHIYAN IN BIHAR AND GUJARAT”.STATE	Details of progress made	Water Conservation and Rainwater Harvesting	Renovation of Traditional Water Bodies Reuse and Recharge Structures	Watershed development	Intensive Afforestation	Awareness GenerationBihar	13570	2013	13717	7877	1836	1336Gujarat	1531	1691	359	5677	2567	25687*****
