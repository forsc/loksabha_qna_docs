                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3226






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Exports from Dantewada Mines




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of various minerals and iron ores produced from the Dantewada mines in Chhattisgarh under the National Mineral Development Corporation (NMDC) during the last three years and the countries to which the minerals acquired from the said mine are being exported;(b)	whether any contract has been signed to give the lease of Mine 13 under NMDC located at Dantewada District to a private firm and if so, the names of iron ores and other minerals obtained from these mines along with the quantity thereof;(c)	the details of the estimated quantity of minerals and iron ores from the said mine during the last three years before signing of the contract on the basis of which the contract was signed; and(d)	the number of mines in Chhattisgarh, lease of which has been given to private firms?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			            (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)	In Chhattisgarh, NMDC is producing Iron Ore from its 5 Mines viz. Deposit 5, 10, 11B, 11C and 14. The Iron Ore produced during last three years by NMDC from its mines in Chhattisgarh are as follows: -Year	Production (In Million Ton)2016-17	22.0082017-18	23.5652018-19	23.289NMDC has been exporting iron ore to Japan and South Korea through MMTC (MMTC being the canalizing agency for Govt. of India) under long-term agreement (LTA) and these exports are undertaken based on the decision of the Government from time to time. Details of exports are as follows: -Year	   Production (In Million Ton)	  Export to foreign countries with quantity (in Million Ton)		Japan	South Korea	Total2016-17	22.008	2.13	0.6	2.732017-18	23.565	1.99	0.61	2.602018-19	23.289	0.87	0.31	1.18 (b)&(c):  No contract has been signed to give the lease of Mine 13 to a private firm.  A Mine Developer cum Operator (MDO) has been appointed through transparent procedure for operation of the mine by NMDC-CMDC Limited (NCL), the lease will remain in the name of NCL. So far, no mining has started from Deposit 13.(d)  11 mining leases of Iron Ore have been given to private firms in the State of Chhattisgarh.
