                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

174






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Entry of Women in Sabarimala Temple




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering or is in the process of drafting any legislation, including an amendment to the Constitution, to overturn the judgement of the Supreme Court of India in Indian Young Lawyers Association & Ors. vs the State of Kerala & Ors. in writ petition (Civil) No. 373 of 2006 which allowed women of all ages to enter the Subarimala temple;(b)   if so, the details thereof; and (c)   if not, the reasons therefore?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E RMINISTER OF LAW AND JUSTICE, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)	to (c):  The matter is sub-judice before Hon’ble  Supreme Court.
