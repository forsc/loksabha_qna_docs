                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1337






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Custom Hiring Centres for Small Farmers




Narendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Custom Hiring Centres are proving very beneficial to the small farmers for agricultural activities; (b) if so, the details of the efforts being made by the Government to provide the benefits of Custom Hiring Centres to the maximum farmers; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? 	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c): 	 The Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare is implementing Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM) with one of the major objectives of promoting Custom Hiring Centres, Farm Machinery Banks and hubs for hi-tech & high value farm machines, in order to make machines and equipments available to farmers on rental basis. Since inception of the SMAM in 2014-15, more than 15400 Custom Hiring Centres, 360 Hi-tech hubs and 14200 Farm Machinery Banks have been established in different States. The Department has also launched a multi-lingual ‘FARMS’ (Farm Machinery Solutions) mobile app, which facilitates hiring and renting of farm machinery through a fair and transparent rental process.  All these initiatives are helping in making machinery accessible to small and marginal farmers.*****
