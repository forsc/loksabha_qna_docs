                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1496






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Status of Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) under the National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture; (b) 	the number of farmers who took up organic farming as part of the scheme; (c) 	the number of Participatory Guarantee Certification (PGS) provided to such farmers till now; and (d) 	the number of clusters and land area brought under the net of organic farming?






ANSWER




MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Under Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY), 29859 clusters comprising of 14.93 lakh farmers have adopted organic farming, covering an area of 5.97 lakh ha. Rs. 1138.45 crore has been released since 2015-16. The state wise details of area covered, farmers benefited and amount released are given at Annexure I.(c):	       655862 farmers have been certified under the Participatory Guarantee Certification (PGS).(d):	 A total area of 27.77 lakh ha has been brought under organic farming which includes 5.97 lakh hectares under Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY), 0.71 lakh hectares under Mission Organic Value Chain Development for North Eastern Region (MOVCDNER), 19.4 lakh hectares under National Program for Organic Production (NPOP) of APEDA and 1.70 lakh hectares under other initiatives. The State-wise details of the area covered under PKVY, MOVCDNER, NPOP and others are given at Annexure-Il.
