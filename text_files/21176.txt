                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2385






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Ethanol Blending Policy




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ethanol Blending Policy is useful for promotion of chemical industries;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has developed special policy for Ethanol based chemicalproducts imported from other countries; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS (SHRI D.V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a) & (b) Bulk of indigenously produced alcohol is being used for blending only andhence, ethanol based industry is primarily dependent on imports to meet theirdemands. As per with DGFT notification dated 24th May, 2019, import of ethanol hasbeen placed under restricted category as now license has to be obtained for its import.(c) & (d) There is no special policy for Ethanol based chemical products imported fromother countries.****
