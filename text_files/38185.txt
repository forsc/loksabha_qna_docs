                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5065






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Settlement of Insurance Claims




A. Ganeshamurthi














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of outstanding claims for natural disasters in 2020 and 2021 from the insurance companies, State/year-wise. (b)	the amount that was settled and its percentage out of the total claims for natural disasters;(c)	whether the Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority issues instructions to the insurers to expedite the process for receiving, verifying and settling claims; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE (a) and (b): Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority (IRDAI) has informed that no claim is outstanding with the Life Insurance Companies for natural disasters in the years 2020 and 2021. The catastrophe and State-wise details of claims for natural disasters in the years 2020 and 2021 from the life insurance and non-life insurance companies, are at Annex-I and Annex-II respectively.(c) and (d): Yes, Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India issues guidelines for life insurer & non-life Insurer from time to time to expedite the processes for receiving, verifying and settling claims pertaining to natural catastrophes. This includes guidelines issued to insurers in respect of insurance claims of victims of Cyclones Amphan, HudHud, FaniNivar, Nisarga, Taukate and Yaas, floods in Telangana, Andhra Pradesh and Maharashtra etc.*****
