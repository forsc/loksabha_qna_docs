                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3974






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Higher Education Regulatory Regime




P. C. Mohan














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to restructure the higher education regulatory regime;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether there are many vacancies in bodies like National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE) and All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE);(d) if so, the details thereof from 2012 to 2020, year-wise;(e) whether the post of Vice-Chairperson of NCTE is lying vacant for more than five years; and(f) if so, the details thereof indicating the date since this post has been lying vacant and the reasons for not appointing any official to this post?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) & (b): The Ministry of Education has announced the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 on 29.07.2020 after obtaining approval of the Union Cabinet. The NEP-2020 envisions setting up of a Higher Education Commission of India as an umbrella body with four independent verticals to perform distinct functions of regulation, accreditation, funding, and academic standard setting. Accordingly, the Ministry is in the process of drafting a Bill for establishment of the Higher Education Commission of India. (c) & (d): Details of vacancies in National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE) are at Annexure-I. As informed by the All India Council for Teacher Education (AICTE), the vacancy status readily available with AICTE is at Annexure-II. (e) & (f): The post of Vice-Chairperson, NCTE is lying vacant since 08.07.2011. *****
