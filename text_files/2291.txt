                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2260






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Airstrips in Small Town




Mukesh Rajput














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there is any scheme of the Government to redevelop the airstrips set up in small towns;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, whether there is any scheme to develop such towns which are located near tourist spots and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. Government of India, Ministry of Civil Aviation has launched Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Naagrik) in October, 2016 with the objective to facilitate / stimulate regional air connectivity to currently under-served and un-served airports in the country, including those in the small towns. The expansion of airports under the scheme is ''demand driven'', depending upon firm commitment from airline operators as well as from the State Government for providing various concessions.;(c): Does not arise.
