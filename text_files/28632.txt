                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

755






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Demand of Houses under PMAY-U




 Chandra Sekhar Bellana













                                                    Talari Rangaiah
                                                








                                                    Goddeti Madhavi
                                                








                                                    Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Sanjeev Kumar Singari
                                                








                                                    Pocha Brahmananda Reddy
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                








                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received a demand of 20.50 lakh  houses under (PMAY-U) for the State of Andhra Pradesh; and (b)	 if so, the details of the total houses sanctioned, district-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                                  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING & URBAN AFFAIRS                   (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)	& (b): Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban [PMAY-U], on the basis of project proposals received from State of Andhra Pradesh a total of 20,40,390 houses has been sanctioned. District-wise details of houses sanctioned are at Annexure.                           *****
