                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

420






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Smart City Projects in Uttar Pradesh




Haji Fazlur Rehman














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the details of the smart city projects launched by the Government in the country particularly in Uttar	Pradesh;(b) the amount of funds released for development ofsmart cities in Uttar Pradesh during the last four years	and the current year;(c) the amount of funds released for Saharanpursmart city during the last four years; and(d) the amount of funds spent on various wards andschemes out of the said funds, ward/scheme-wise?






ANSWER




                                                                          THE MINISTER OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                       (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI) (a)	to (d): Government of India launched the Smart Cities Mission (SCM) on 25 June 2015.  100 Smart cities have been selected through 4 rounds of competition from January 2016 to June 2018.  As per SCM Statement & Guidelines, Central Government will provide financial support to the Mission to the extent of ? 48,000 crore over five years i.e. on an average ? 100 crore per city per year.  An equal amount on a matching basis will be contributed by the State Government/Urban Local Body.  As on 21 January 2022, Smart Cities have tendered out 6,721 projects worth ? 1,88,507 crore; out of which work orders have been issued in 6,124 projects worth ? 1,62,908 crore; out of these, 3,421 projects worth ? 58,735 crore have been completed.  10 cities in the State of Uttar Pradesh i.e. Agra, Aligarh, Bareilly, Jhansi, Kanpur, Lucknow, Moradabad, Prayagraj, Saharanpur & Varanasi have been selected for development as Smart Cities.  The present status of Smart City projects and the funds released by the Central Government to the Smart cities in Uttar Pradesh, including Saharanpur,  are given in Annexure-I.                                     *****
