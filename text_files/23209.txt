                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4461






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Boat Insurance Scheme




Pinaki Misra














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the proposed personal and boat insurance scheme for fisherfolk under the Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana has been operationalised, if so, the details thereof and if not, the estimated timeline for its implementation;(b) 	the details of how the proposed scheme will interact with existing insurance coverage under the Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries scheme and the group insurance scheme run by the National Federation of Fishers Cooperatives Ltd.;(c) 	the total number of active fisherfolk and the total number of fisherfolk currently covered by the two aforementioned insurance schemes, nationally and State/ UT-wise;(d) 	the levels of insurance penetration in the fisheries sector as compared to animal husbandry and dairying; and(e) 	the details of steps taken by the Government to increase insurance penetration in the fisheries sector?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER     MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRYAND DAIRYING(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) to (e):   The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Fisheries Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a flagship programme of “Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY)- a scheme to bring about Blue Revolution through sustainable and responsible development of fisheries sector in India” with highest ever investment of Rs.20050 crore in fisheries sector for a period of 5 years with effect from the current financial year 2020-21. PMMSY inter-alia envisages insurance to fishers, insurance premium subvention for fishing vessels and the premium for fishers insurance is being finalised and the modalities for vessel insurance have been worked in consultation with State Governments and Union Territories. Under PMMSY, the insurance coverage has been primarily enhanced to Rs.5 lakh against death or permanent total disability and Rs. 2.50 lakh against permanent partial disability against the corresponding coverage of Rs.2 lakh and Rs.1 lakh provided under erstwhile Centrally Sponsored Scheme(CSS) on Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries. During last five years, under the CSS, an average of 41.09 lakh fishers was provided insurance cover annually in fisheries sector. In Animal Husbandry sector, it is reported that 7 lakh animals have been insured annually. The State-wise details of fishermen covered under erstwhile CSS on Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries during the last five years are annexed.*****
