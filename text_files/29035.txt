                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1011






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






COVID-19 Deaths of Indians Abroad




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has taken any steps to collect the number and details of Indian citizens who have succumbed to COVID-19 while staying abroad, if so, the details thereof, country-wise;(b) 	if not, whether the Government is considering to collect the said details through Indian embassies or high commissions in each foreign country;(c)	whether the Government intends to provide compensation to the family of Indian citizens who succumbed to COVID-19 while staying abroad as in the case of those who died in India as ordered by the Supreme Court recently; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reason therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) & (b) 	As per the details available with Indian Missions/Posts, the total number of Indian citizens who succumbed to COVID-19 is 4048. The largest number of casualties were reported from Saudi Arabia (1154), United Arab Emirates (894), Kuwait (668), Oman (551), Bahrain (200) and Qatar (109).  Country-wise details are attached at Annexure-I.(c) & (d)	Government issued guidelines through the National Disaster Management Authority, in pursuance of Supreme Court judgment regarding ex-gratia payment to Indians who succumbed to COVID-19.***
