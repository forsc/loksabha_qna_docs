                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

397






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Pradhan Mantri Credit Scheme Powerloom Weavers




Ganesan Selvam













                                                    Dhanush M Kumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is implementing Pradhan Mantri Credit Scheme for Powerloom Weavers and if so, the details thereof; (b) the number of Powerloom Weavers who have been benefited under the Scheme during each of the last five years in the country including States of Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra; (c) the other schemes/programmes being implemented by the Government to ensure adequate credit and to overcome the hardships being faced by the weavers/workers who are in unorganized powerloom sector in the country; (d) the funds provided under the said schemes/ programmes during each of the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise and Scheme/Programme-wise; (e) the cases of irregularities reported in implementation of the said schemes/programmes across the country along with the action taken thereon; and (f) the corrective steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (f):-  A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) to (f) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *397 DUE FOR ANSWERED ON 30.03.2022 RAISED BY SHRI SELVAM G AND SHRI DHANUSH M. KUMAR REGARDING PRADHAN MANTRI CREDIT SCHEME POWERLOOM WEAVERS(a):	Government had launched Pradhan Mantri Credit Scheme for Powerloom Weavers (PMCS) as one of the sub-components of PowerTex India Scheme to provide adequate and timely financial assistance to the powerloom weavers to meet their credit requirements, for investment needs (Term Loan) as well as for working capital, in a flexible and cost effective manner for a period of three years w.e.f 1st April, 2017 to 31.03.2020 and was further approved as a component of restructured ‘Textile Cluster Development Scheme’ (TCDS)upto 2025-26. 2. The details of PM Credit Scheme are as below:(i) MUDRA•	Eligibility: Existing individual powerloom units (or) New individual/ group enterprises involved in weaving activity are eligible.•	Financial Assistance:o	Margin money @20% of machinery cost with a ceiling of Rs.1 lakh for a borrower. Remaining amount of margin money will be borne by the borrower.o	Interest Subvention @ 6% per year for 5 years.(ii)	Stand-up India •	Eligibility:  New Powerloom units established by a person who belongs to SC/ST/Woman Entrepreneur and in case of non-individual units at least 51% of the shareholding and controlling stake should be held by either an SC (or) ST (or) Women entrepreneur.•	Financial Assistance: 25% Margin Money (Max. Rs.25 lakh) on the machinery cost, the borrower is required to bring in 10% of the Project Cost as his/her own contribution.(b): During past 5 years, total 99 powerloom weavers (6 in 2019-20; 88 in 2020-21; 5 in 2021-22) benefitted under Stand up India component of PMCS whereas no application was received under PM Mudra Scheme. From the states of Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu, no application was received under PMCS (Mudra and Stand up India) during last 5 years.(c) & (d):	Details of other schemes/programmes implemented to ensure adequate credit and to address the issues being faced by the weavers/workers who are in unorganized powerloom sector in the country, is given in the Annexure.(e) & (f): No irregularities have been reported under Pradhan Mantri Credit Scheme for Powerloom Weavers.
