                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4586






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Identification of Buddhist Circuits




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of Buddhist Circuits identified in Andhra Pradesh under Swadesh Darshan Scheme; (b) 	the details of projects sanctioned, approved and executed/being executed in each of the identified Circuit in Andhra Pradesh; and (c) 	the extent to which the development of above sites in Andhra Pradesh helps in attracting Buddhist tourism in the State, particularly from Eastern part of the world?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	The details of projects sanctioned by the Ministry of Tourism under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme- Integrated Development of Theme- Based Tourist Circuits to the State of Andhra Pradesh for development of tourism infrastructure are as below:(Rs. in crore)<pre>S. No.	Circuit/ Sanction Year	Project Name	Amt. Sanctioned	Amt. Released1	Coastal Circuit (2014-15)	Development of Kakinada Hope Island Konaseema as World Class Coastal & Eco Tourism Circuit	67.84	64.442.		Coastal Circuit (2015-16)	Development of Coastal Tourism Circuit in Sri Potti Sriramalu Nellore 	59.70	47.763.		Buddhist Circuit(2017-18)	 Development of Shalihundam-Thotlakonda- Bavikonda- Bojjanakonda- Amravati- Anupu	52.34	10.47Total	179.88	122.67</pre>All the projects are at various stages of implementation/completion. The projects, once complete, are expected to enhance tourist experience and increase tourist footfalls by attracting domestic as well as international tourists including the ones from Eastern part of the world.*****
