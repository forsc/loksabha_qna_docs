                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3310






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Allocation of Funds to AYUSH Industry Groups/ Clusters




Sunil Kumar Soni













                                                    Vijay Baghel
                                                








                                                    Arun Sao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has allocated funds for the development of AYUSH Industry Groups/Clusters;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the targets set and number of such groups/clusters developed at present, State-wise and district-wise, particularly in Chhattisgarh; and(d)	the number of groups/clusters targeted to be set up by the Government particularly in Chhattisgarh and number of clusters out of these constructed under the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. Ministry of Ayush (erstwhile Department of Ayush) implemented a Central Sector Scheme for Development of Ayush Clusters up to 12th Plan period (2012-17). The Government sanctioned funds to ten Ayush Industry Clusters under the said scheme. Later on, the scheme has been discontinued with effect from 01.04.2017. The details of Industry Clusters along with funds allocated are furnished at Annexure.(c) & (d): Since the scheme has been discontinued with effect from 01.04.2017, at present, the question of setting targets does not arise. Further, no Industry Cluster was developed in the State of Chhattisgarh.
