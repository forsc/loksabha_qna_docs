                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

988






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Universal Immunization Programme (UIP)




Adala Prabhakara Reddy













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Goddeti Madhavi
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Sanjeev Kumar Singari
                                                








                                                    Pocha Brahmananda Reddy
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                








                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has recently launched the nationwide expansion of Pneumococcal Conjugate Vaccine (PCV) under the Universal Immunization Programme (UIP);   (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether this nationwide rollout will be helpful in reducing Child Mortality Rate; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (d) Government has rolled out nationwide coverage of the Pneumococcal Conjugate Vaccine (PCV) under Universal Immunization Programme (UIP) in 2021-22.Nationwide rollout of Pneumococcal Conjugate Vaccine (PCV) leads to significant reduction in child mortality as it provides protection against Pneumococcal disease which is one of the leading cause of vaccine preventable deaths among children under five years of age.
