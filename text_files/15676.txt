                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4322






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Centrally Sponsored Road Projects




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and the present status of sanctioned/ongoing centrally sponsored road projects in the State of Jharkhand, project-wise;(b) the details of National Highways along with their length in the State; and(c) the total amount spent on their maintenance during the last three years, year-wise? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)The details and the present status of sanctioned/ongoing centrally sponsored road projects in the State of Jharkhand are enclosed at Annexure-I.(b) The details of National Highways along with their length in the State are enclosed at Annexure-II.(c) The total amount spent on their maintenance during the last three years including current year, year-wise, is as under:Financial Year	2016-17	2017-2018	2018-2019	2019-2020*Amount  (Rs. in Crores)	101.69	79.41	51.45	28.48*Expenditure from April 2019 to till date.
