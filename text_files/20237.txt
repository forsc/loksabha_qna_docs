                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1727






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Implementation of FASTag System




Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy













                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    T.R.V.S. Ramesh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has decided to implement the FASTag system fully with effect from January 2021 in  the  country;(b)  if  so, the  details  thereof.;(c) whether the hybrid lanes at all the Toll Plazas will also remain  operational; and(d) if so, the period till when these hybrid lanes are likely to remain operational?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) &(b)  The Government has mandated fitment of FASTag in M&N Categories of motor vehicles sold before 1st December, 2017 w.e.f. 01.01.2021.(c) &(d) As per the clarification issued by the Government on 31.12.2020, fee payment through FASTag as well as in cash mode in hybrid lanes of fee plazas on National Highways will continue till 15th February, 2021.*****
