                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1636






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.07.2019






Social Welfare Schemes




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry has implemented various social welfare schemes in the States including Bihar for the girl child; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether there are specific schemes for the girl child; and (d) if so, the details of these schemes along with the allocations made thereunder?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)  & (b):   The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment has Scholarship schemes for pre-matric and post-matric children belonging to Scheduled Castes and Other Backward Classes categories. There is one scheme for providing hostel facilities and hostels are constructed for both boys and girls separately. These schemes are gender-neutral hence include girls as beneficiaries as well.  These schemes are implemented throughout the country including Bihar.(c) & (d): The Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment does not implement scheme specifically for girl child.  However, the Ministry of Women and Child Development has social welfare schemes specifically for girl child which are implemented throughout the country including Bihar. The names of schemes and allocations made thereof is given in Annexure.****
