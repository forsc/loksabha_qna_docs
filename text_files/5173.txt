                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4696






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






MCA-21 Companies




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the list of companies included in mca21;(b)	whether the Government has reviewed the frequency of the list;(c)	 if so, the details thereof; and(d)	Whether the list has been updated in 2019 and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCEAND CORPORATE AFFAIRS                         (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR) ????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? ????? ??? ????????	             (???? ?????? ???? ?????)(a)to (d) As on 31st May,2019,  11,67,064 number of active companies are there in the Registry maintained in the MCA21 system as follows :-i.	63,986 - Public Companies Limited by Sharesii.	10,95,809 - Private Companies Limited by Shares iii.	6,955 - Companies Limited by Guarantees iv.	314 - Unlimited Companies	The list is updated real time based on the filings made by stakeholders in the MCA21 system.******
