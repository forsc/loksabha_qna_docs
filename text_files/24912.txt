                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

766






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






CSIR Loans for Research and Development




Ashok Kumar Rawat













                                                    Sadashiv Kisan Lokhande
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR) is providing soft loan for research and development under various projects; (b) 	if so, the details of the various schemes in this regard; and (c) 	the quantum of loan provided during each of the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY AND EARTH SCIENCES(DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a)&(b) Yes. Council of Scientific & Industrial Research (CSIR) provides soft loan for R&D activities to companies under the New Millennium Indian Technology Leadership Initiative (NMITLI) Scheme. The financial support to industry is in the form of soft loan with 3% interest.(c)	The quantum of loan provided during each of the last three years is given below:Financial Year	Loan given (Rs. in Lakhs)2019-20	---2020-21	  214.45 lakh2021-22	1203.03 lakh*****
