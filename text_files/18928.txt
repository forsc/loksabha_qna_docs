                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

371






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






BSNL 4G Services




Pragya Singh Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to start 4G services of BSNL in the country and if so, the details thereof; and (b) 	the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)&(b)  The Government approved the revival plan for Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL)  on 23.10.2019, which inter-alia includes administrative allotment of spectrum through budgetary allocation to BSNL for providing 4G services. BSNL has floated an Expression of Interest (EOI) on 01.01.2021 for prior registration/Proof of Concept (PoC) from Indian Companies interested in participating in BSNL’s upcoming 4G tender.BSNL has, meanwhile launched 4G services at limited scale by utilising existing spectrum which is being used for 3G services.  Approximately, 8,600 number of 4G sites are working in BSNL.************
