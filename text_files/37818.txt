                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4922






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Developmental Schemes and Projects




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the Developmental schemes and projects for AYUSH currently being implemented in various States/ UTs by the Ministry; (b) whether any project for Maharashtra is under consideration of the Ministry; and (c) if so, the details thereof district-wise; and (d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a)Ministry of Ayush is implementing Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) through the State/UT Governmentsto develop and promote AYUSH systems. Under NAM, Grant-in-aid is provided to the State/UT Governments as per the proposals submitted by them through State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs). Under NAM, the status of funds released as per the proposal received from States/UTs in their SAAPs from 2014-15 to 2021-22, State/UT-wise is furnished inAnnexure.(b) to (d) Ministry of Ayush has approved SAAP for the current year for different activities proposed by State Government of Maharashtra. However, grant-in-aid of central share could not be released during the current year due to non-compliance of conditionalities as issued by Department of Expenditure, Ministry of Finance, Government of Indiavide Office Memorandum No. F. No. 1(13)PFMS/FCD/2020 dated 23-03-2021. It is stated that Under NAM there is no provision for district-wise release of grant-in-aid.
