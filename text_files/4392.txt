                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4096






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Contractual Labour in BSNL




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has taken any decision to downsize the strength of contractual labour in BSNL and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and (b) 	the steps taken by the Government to overcome the shortage of contractual labour and clear the wages due to them?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) & (b) 	Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) has informed that austerity measures guidelines have been issued to circles for reducing costs.  Further, at this juncture no shortage of contractual labour is perceived.  Due to fall in revenue, payment of salary of employees and statutory payments are the first priority.  Thereafter, bills of contractors etc. are taken into consideration for payment.
