                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

675






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Impact of Demonitization on MSMEs




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	whether the Government is aware that there are many small and medium enterprises which 	have collapsed due to the demonetization and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	whether the Government has the data on the opening and closure of micro, small and medium enterprises since 8th November, 2016 and if so, the details thereof, State-	wise; (c)  	whether the Government has the data of growth in the micro, small and medium enterprises since 8th November, 2016; (d)  	if so, the details thereof; and (f)  	whether the Government has the data of employment generation happening in this  sector; and if so, the details thereof along with the situation of employment generation since 8th November, 2016?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE) (a) to (f): 	Yes Sir, The Government is aware of difficulties faced by Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) through various feedbacks received from industry and trade associations. The State-wise details of MSMEs which have registered on Udyog Aadhar Memorandum (UAM) and Udyam Registration (UR) since 8th November 2016 are given in Annex I & II. Total employment generation in the MSME sector, as per registration data available from UAM and UR, is 8,20,79,530.
