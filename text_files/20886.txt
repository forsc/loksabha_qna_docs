                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2129






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Import of Oil




Kotagiri Sridhar













                                                     Chinta Anuradha
                                                








                                                    Goddeti Madhavi
                                                








                                                    Pocha Brahmananda Reddy
                                                








                                                    Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu
                                                








                                                    M V V Satyanarayana
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the total value of oil and oil products imported by the country during the lastfive years country/year-wise;(b) whether the Government is taking measures to reduce the total import bill, if so, the detailsthereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c) the total savings in oil import value due to bio-ethanol blending in petrol, year-wise includingthe rate of bio-ethanol blending with petrol in 2020-21?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) The details of the total value of oil and petroleum products for the last five years are as under:Particulars 2015-16 2016-17 2017-18 2018-19 2019-20Import of Oil (in ? crore) 4,16,579 4,70,159 5,66,450 7,83,183 7,17,001Import of Petroleum Products (in ? crore) 65,361 71,566 88,374 1,13,665 1,25,742Total Imports (Oil & Petroleum Products)(in ? crore) 4,81,940 5,41,725 6,54,824 8,96,848 8,42,743(Contd..)- 2 -India imported crude oil & petroleum products from various geographical regions like Middle-East, Africa, Europe, North America, South America and South-East Asia.(b) Government has taken multiple steps to reduce imports through refinery processimprovements and various policies under Production Sharing Contract (PSC) regime, DiscoveredSmall Field Policy, Hydrocarbon Exploration and Licensing Policy, Setting up of National DataRepository, etc. Government has also provided functional freedom to National Oil Companiesand wider private sector participation by streamlining approval processes including electronicsingle window mechanism.Government has launched National Biofuel Policy, 2018, to boost availability of biofuels incountry and use of alternative fuels like ethanol, bio-diesel and bio CNG through EthanolBlending, Bio-diesel blending and Sustainable Alternative Towards Affordable Transportation(SATAT) initiative respectively.(c) Under the Ethanol Blended Petrol (EBP) Programme, Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs)have been directed to blend 10% bio-ethanol in petrol as per Bureau of Indian Standard (BIS)Specifications. The total savings in oil import value due to bio-ethanol blending in petrol is asunder:-Year 2018-19 2019-20 2020-21 (Provisional)Savings (in ? crore) 5035 4071 2264The percentage of ethanol blending in petrol is as under :-Ethanol Supply Year(ESY)#2018-19 2019-20 2020-21(as on 01.03.2021)Percentage 5.00 5.00 6.94#ESY starts from 1st December of a year to 30th November of the next year.*************
