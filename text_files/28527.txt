UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

492






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Mineral Blocks




Badruddin Ajmal













                                                    Diya Kumari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and the number of mine blocks of Iron Ore, minor minerals, Aluminium and Copper, Manganese, Bauxite and sand currently located in Assam; (b) the names and the number of mines which are in operational state and those that are not operational; (c) the reasons for closing mine specially mineral blocks in Assam; (d) the total number of mine blocks auction during the last five years in the country including the detail of the revenue Government earned from these mines blocks during last five years; and (e) the measures taken/to be taken by Government to curb illegal mining?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a) to (c): As per information provided by State Government of Assam, at present, there are no mineral blocks of iron ore, Aluminium and Copper, Manganese, Bauxite in Assam. As per information provided by Indian Bureau of Mines, a subordinate office of Ministry of Mines, 7 mineral blocks are in  Assam  of which two are working mines. Details of  major mineral mines in State of  Assam is  provided in Annexure I . However, in respect of minor minerals including sand , section 15 of the MMDR Act, 1957, empowers the State Governments to make rules for regulating the grant of minor mineral concessions. (d): Since the amendment to MMDR Act, 1957, a total of 125 mineral blocks have been auctioned so far across 9 States. Out of these 125 blocks, 35 blocks are operational in 3 States. As per information provided by State Government, details of revenue earned from these 35  auctioned blocks as auction premium  is provided in Annexure II.(e): Section 23C of the MMDR Act, empowers the State Governments to make rules for preventing illegal mining, transportation and storage of minerals and for the purposes connected therewith.
