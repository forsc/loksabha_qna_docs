                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

25






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Quality of Education




K. Muraleedharan














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that Kerala has topped with the best quality of schooleducation in the country and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether it is also true that the Government plans to collaborate with the WorldBank to offer performance linked grants as incentives for States with highranking and if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether it is also a fact that Uttar Pradesh was the lowest among 20 largeStates with regard to quality of school education and if so, the details thereof ?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) Yes, Sir. NITI Aayog released the School Education Quality Index (SEQI) on 30thSeptember 2019. The detailed report of indicators is in public domain and isavailable at www.niti.gov.in.(b) With a view to improve the quality and governance of school educationranging from Pre-School to Senior Secondary level, a project namely StrengtheningTeaching-Learning and Results for States (STARS) has been prepared incollaboration with the World Bank.(c) The State of Uttar Pradesh ranks third in the list of “Increment PerformanceRanking” for the large States.***
