                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

493






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






Growth of FMCG Companies




Sisir Kumar Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the growth of Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) companies has registered a decline in sales since 2018 till January 2020;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether there has been a decline in the demand for FMCG products in rural India also;(d) 	if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	the details of the reaction and proposal from the FMCG companies to the  Government along with the steps being taken to increase FMCG sales?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (e):  	Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) encompasses a diverse range of products. Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade under Ministry of Commerce and Industry does not maintain specific sales or manufacturing data regarding  Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) Sector. Ministry of Corporate Affairs has also informed that they do not maintain information relating to FMCG sector separately.  Companies usually diversify their activities and generate revenue from multiple activities. *****
