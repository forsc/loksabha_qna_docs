                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1605






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






PLI Scheme




Raja Amareshwara Naik













                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)    whether the Government plans to implement Productivity Linked Incentive (PLI) scheme to encourage manufacturing of Agriculture Machinery in the country; (b)    if so, the details thereof; (c)   whether the Government should consider bringing a 50% localization norm for farm machinery products empanelment in various financial assistance/subsidy schemes like Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM) specially in case of Rice Transplanters and Combine Harvesters for which the domestic manufacturers have sufficient installed capacity; (d)       if so, the details thereof; and (e)     the other steps being taken by Government for promotion of domestic production of agriculture machinery to become Atmanirbhar?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????      (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):    Presently there is no proposal for Productivity Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for agricultural machinery manufacturing in the country. (c) & (d):   The Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme implemented through the State Governments. The Guidelines of SMAM, provide an exhaustive list of machines and equipment along with applicable subsidy.Complete freedom to beneficiary is available for selection of agricultural machinery and equipment as per his/her choicein line with the prescribed guidelines.(e):   The Government as a policy to promote Make in India products endeavors and encourages domestic manufacturing agricultural machinery and equipment through Research & Development support, skill development, simplification of rules and procedures for testing of machines & equipment, preference to make in India products in public procurement, import restrictions under Foreign Trade Policy etc.*****
