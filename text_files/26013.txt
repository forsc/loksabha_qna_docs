                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1790






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Widening Work on Kishangarh-Gulabpurakhand NH




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of widening works from four lane to six lane of National Highway (NH) No. 79 and 79A from Kishangarh to Gulabpurakhand in Ajmer Parliamentary Constituency;(b)	the details of the small and big bridges, bus stop, service road etc. proposed to be constructed as part of the said widening project along with name of places and the criteria adopted for their construction;(c)	whether the Government proposes construction of a new Aariwal bridge at village Dilwara tehsil Nasirabad and village Baral Dvitiya/Sanjay Nagar tehsil Vijaynagar district Ajmer situated at the above mentioned NHs (79) in view of the problems being faced by the local population; and(d)	if so, the details thereof along with the time by which it is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	Present physical progress of 6-laning of Kishangarh to Gulabpura section is 80.65%.(b)	5 Flyovers, One Major Bridge, 19 Minor Bridges, 18 PUPs, 6 VUPs, 62.45 km Service road on left side, 64.25 km Service Road on Right side and 9 Bus Bays have been envisaged in the scope of work in accordance with the provisions in IRC Manual for 6-laning  project. (c)	Keeping in view the issues faced by the local population, it has been decided to take up Foot Over Bridges at these locations.(d)	The design and drawings of Foot Over Bridges have been initiated for completion by March, 2022. *****
