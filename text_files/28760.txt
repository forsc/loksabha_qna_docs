                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

330






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Decline in Automobile Sales




A. Chellakumar














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether manufacturing of automobiles has been reduced and the automobiles sales has also declined due to the spread of COVID-19 virus in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the measures being taken by the Government to tackle this situation?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEAVY INDUSTRIES(SHRI KRISHAN PAL GURJAR)(a) & (b):  Sales of automobiles have been affected in the last two years due to spread of Covid-19 virus in the country.  Production and sales of Automobiles have been impacted due to lockdown imposed to contain spread of Covid-19.(c):	The Government, as a policymaker, always attempts to sustain and improve momentum of the economy through a package of measures for comprehensive and continued development of the sector. The Government has notified the Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for the Automobile and auto component sector.  Also it has notified the PLI Scheme for Advanced Chemistry Cell (ACC) Battery Storage manufacturing in the country.**********
