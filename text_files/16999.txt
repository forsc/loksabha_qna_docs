                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

619






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Modernisation of Armed Forces




Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of budget estimates, revised estimates and actual expenditure on modernisation of armed forces during the last five years and the current year;(b) the details of investment made in the production of military equipment during the last three years and the current year and the Defence agreements signed for procurement of Defence equipment during the said period;(c) whether the Government has any proposal to allow the private sector to play a major role in the production of weapon system for the armed forces and if so, the details thereof and the percentage of funds allocated for procurement of indigenous equipments; and(d) the steps taken by the Government for modernisation of armed forces to improve weaponry like tanks and missiles and to reduce the dependence on import along with the contribution to the Defence sector under ‘Make in India’ for the purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) to (d): A statement is attached.*****
