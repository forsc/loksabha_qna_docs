                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4172






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






Medical and Dental Colleges




Jasbir Singh Gill














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE  be pleased to state: (a) the number of medical and dental colleges, State/UT/Government/private college-wise; and (b) the total number of doctors working as MD/MS/M.Ch. at present in the country, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):      There are 539 Medical Colleges and 313 Dental Colleges in the Country.  The State / UT / Government / Private college-wise break up of medical and dental colleges are in Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively. (b):     As per information received from the Board of Governors-Medical Council of India (BOG-MCI), a total of 3,34,774 doctors with  Postgraduate Medical qualifications (MD / MS / M.Ch / DM) in the country are  registered with the Medical Council of India and the State Medical Councils. The State / UT-wise details are in Annexure-III.………………
