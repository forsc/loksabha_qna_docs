                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

380






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Digital India and Broadband Connectivity




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the steps being taken by the Government for better implementation of Digital India Programme; (b) 	the action plan formulated by the Government for providing Broadband connectivity to each Gram Panchayat of the country; and (c) 	the district-wise population of Gram Panchayats in Rajasthan which are still deprived of broadband connectivity at present?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	Government of India has initiated the ‘Digital India Programme” with the vision to transform India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy. The Digital India programme is centred on three key vision areas namely Digital Infrastructure as a Core Utility to Every Citizen, Governance and Services on Demand and Digital Empowerment of Citizens. Digital India is an umbrella programme that covers multiple projects of various central Ministries/Departments & States/UTs and accordingly project-plans are charted out by the implementing Ministry/departments.(b)	Government of India is implementing BharatNet project in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity to all the Gram Panchayats (GPs) (approx. 2,50,000) in the country through optimal mix of media(OFC/Radio/Satellite). As on 15.01.2021, a total of 1,51,404 GPs including Block Headquarters have been made service ready.(c)	The district-wise details of Gram Panchayats in Rajasthan where connectivity is not there is at Annexure.
