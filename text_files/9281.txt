                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2835






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Freedom Fighters’ Memorials




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the list of memorials erected in the honour of freedom fighters along with expenditure incurred for each of them in the past five years;(b)	whether the Government has erected any memorial in the capital city, Delhi in the honour of freedom fighters from Andhra Pradesh and if so, the details thereof; and(c)	whether the Government proposes to erect  a National Memorial in the honour of women freedom fighters and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):	The National Salt Satyagraha Memorial at Dandi, Gujarat was constructed by Central Public Works Department (CPWD) for Ministry of Culture at a cost of Rs. 59.28 Crores and Dr. Ambedkar National Memorial at Delhi was constructed by CPWD for Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment at a cost of Rs. 99.64 Crores.(b): No such work has been done by CPWD.(c): There is no such proposal at present with Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs.                        *****
