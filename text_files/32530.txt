                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

98






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Export of Major Cereals




Sumedhanand Saraswati














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether export of major cereals like wheat and rice is on the rise during the last three years; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) whether it is also a fact that purchase from the farmers by the Government agencies is also on the rise; and (d) if so, the State-wise purchase thereof, agri commodity-wise during each of the last three years including current year?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY(SMT. ANUPRIYA PATEL)(a) & (b):  Yes, Sir. Overall, the export of cereals like wheat and rice has increased over the last three years and the current year.
