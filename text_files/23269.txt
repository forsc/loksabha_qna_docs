                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Funds to Industrial training institute




Keshari Devi Patel













                                                    Kanakmal Katara
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the steps taken by the Government to promote the setting up of small Industrial Training Institute (ITI) and technical education in various part of the country particularly in the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes dominated areas;(b)	if so, the details thereof with particular reference of Banswada-Dungarpur, Rajasthan and Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh; and(c)	the details of funds allocated / utilized under various heads for setting up of ITIs in the country State/ UT wise including Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	& (b) Setting up of Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) is a concurrent subject under the Constitution wherein the Central Government is entrusted with the responsibility of formulation of policy, laying down training standards, norms, whereas opening of ITIs, implementation of norms and day to day administration rest with the respective State Government Territory concerned including those for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes dominated areas.Details of existing ITIs in Banswada, Dungarpur and Prayagraj are as follows:Sl no.
