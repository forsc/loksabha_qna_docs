                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1816






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Participation of Players in Olympic Games




Krishna Pal Singh Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of number of players from Madhya Pradesh who participated in the Olympic Games and the number of Medals won by these players; and(b) the details of schemes being run by the Government to promote sports activities in the State, district-wise ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[KIREN RIJIJU](a) :The prerogative of fielding athletes and teams in international sports events, including the Olympics, is that of the National Sports Federations of the sports discipline concerned and the Indian Olympic Association (IOA). The Government has no role in such matters.Therefore, State-wise details of players who have participated and won medals in the Olympic Games, is not maintained by this Ministry. (b):Sports is a State subject and promotion of sports in a State is the responsibility of the respective State Governments.  However, to supplement the efforts of Central Government, Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports through Sports Authority of India (SAI) is implementing the following sports promotional schemes across the country to identify talented sports persons in the age group of 8-25 years and nurture them to excel at national and international competitions :•	National Centres of Excellence (NCOE)•	SAI Training Centre (STC)•	Extension Centre of STC•	National Sports Talent Contest (NSTC)Details of SAI Schemes athletes in the State of Madhya Pradesh are given at Annexure-I.Further, under the vertical State Level Khelo India Scheme (SLKIC), MP State Academy, Madhya Pradesh has been designated as Khelo India State Centre of Excellence.Under the vertical Support to National/Regional/State Sports Academies, 15 academies have been accredited and are being supported. List of academies accredited is placed at Annexure-II. *****
