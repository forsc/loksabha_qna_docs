                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1447






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Killing of Minorities by Terrorists




Gurjeet Singh Aujla














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that during the last Six months, several Kashmiri Pandits Hindus and Sikhs have been killed by the terrorists in Srinagar;(b) if so, the complete details in this regard;(c) whether it is also a fact that a sense of fear has gripped the minorities in the Kashmir Valley with several Government employees and teachers turning to otherparts of the country; and(d) if so, the steps taken by the Government to protect the minority community and announced compensation to the deceased families?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b):	Details of Kashmiri Pandits, Hindus and Sikhs etc. who lost their lives in the terror related incidents in Srinagar district during the last six months (from June, 2021 to Nov 27, 2021) are as under:-Date of terror incident	     Particulars of the victims                                 Remarks 05.10.2021	Shri M.L. Bindroo S/o Rakeshver Nath Bindroo R/o Indra Nagar, Srinagar 	Kashmiri                                                                                         Pandit	        Shri Virender Paswan S/o Ram Paswan R/o Sadpur, Baghalpur, Bihar	Hindu 07.10.2021	Smt. Supinder Kour W/o Rashpal Singh R/o Zazbugh Budgam	                Sikh	        Shri Deepak Chand S/o Lal Chand R/o Patoli Jammu	                Hindu16.10.2021	Shri Arvind Kumar Shah S/o Devinder Kumar R/o Targari, Bihar 	        Hindu-2-L.S.US.Q.NO.1447 FOR 07.12.2021(c):	In October 2021 approximately 115 Kashmiri Pandit families, mostly women and children, living in Kashmir have moved to Jammu region. These families are of Govt. employees, many of whom, move to Jammu in winter as part of movement of officials and the winter vacation in educational institutions.(d):	A robust security and intelligence grid is in place to ensure safety of minority community in Srinagar. In addition to group security in the form of static guards, day & night area domination and patrolling is being carried out in the areas where the minorities reside besides proactive operations against terrorists.         With regard to compensation, the Government provides ex-gratia relief/ compensation as per rules to the next of kin of any person killed by the terrorists and the same is provided in these cases too.*******
