                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4101






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Proposal to Mandate Sale of Electric Vehicles




K. Subbarayan














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Government’s attention has been drawn to the NITI Aayog’s proposal to mandate only electric vehicles in the sub-150cc category from the year 2025; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether top two-wheeler makers in the country have raised concern on the proposal and termed it unrealistic and ill timed as it comes just when the industry is preparing for a transition to BS6; and (d) 	if so, the details and the Government’s reaction thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ???????????? ( ???? ??? ??????) THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE &INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a)&(b): The Inter-Ministerial Steering Committee of the National Mission for Transformative Mobility and Battery Storage has discussed this proposal of NITI Aayog.  As per Ministry of Road Transport and Highways, the roadmap for implementation shall be finalised in consultation with stakeholders.(c)&(d): 	As per information received from Ministry of Road Transport and Highways, no representation in this regard has been received. *******
