                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3535






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Re-opening of Closed Road




Sanjay Bhatia














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the road of market committee from Ujah, Raslapur and Risalu 	in Karnal Parliamentary Constituency which leads to Bapauli grain 	market via Rasoolpur has been closed by highways authority;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor;(c)	whether the Government is aware that the farmers of that region have 	been facing grave difficulties for transporting their produce and to 	commute to their fields due to closure of this road;(d)	if so, whether the Government proposes to open the said road; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                         ¬THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (e)   No Link/Village road has been blocked during the construction of Project Highway. The Link Road at Km. 7.800 which goes to the village Bapauli is open for traffic. The work of construction of VUP at Km. 7.800 has been completed. Also, there is a major Intersection/Junction at Km. 13.700 through which the passage of traffic is smooth. Moreover, a median opening at Km. 5.800 has been provided near Minor Bridge (at Km 6.041) for the convenience of local villagers to cross the Main Carriageway and further goes to the slip road which connects the VUP.*****
