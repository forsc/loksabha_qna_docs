                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

298






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Rashtriya Gokul Mission




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of livestock registered under Rashtriya Gokul Mission till 2019 in Jharkhand, district-wise; (b) the number of beneficiaries of the Sub-Mission on Skill Development, Technology Transfer and Extension under National Livestock Mission in the State of Jharkhand, district-wise, and (c) the salient features of Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund and the details on the various projects and amount of concessional finances provided for projects in Jharkhand in 2019?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING (SHRI SANJEEV BALYAN)(a)	The details of the district wise animals registered under Rashtriya Gokul Mission till 2019 is given at Annexure-I.(b)	The Department of Animal Husbandry, Dairying & Fisheries is implementing National Livestock Mission (NLM) throughout the country including Jharkhand since 2014-15 with the objective to cover all the activities required to ensure quantitative and qualitative improvement in livestock production system and capacity building of all stakeholders. The mission includes sub-mission on Skill Development, Technology Transfer and Extension comprising nine components. National Livestock Mission (NLM) is a Demand-Driven scheme. Accordingly, the details of fund released to State of Jharkhand under sub mission on Skill Development, Technology Transfer and Extension is as follows:	FY	2014-15	2016-17S.N	Components	Financial (In lakh)	Beneficiaries(In no.)	Financial (In lakh)	Physical(In no.)1	Training and capacity building	50	10000	-	-2	Farmers field schools	-	-	100	18District wise data is not being maintained in the headquarters.  (c)	In order to address the infrastructure requirement for fisheries sector, the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying during 2018-19 created dedicated funds namely Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund (FIDF) with a total funds size of Rs 7522.48 crore, comprising of Rs.5266.40 crore to be raised by the Nodal Loaning Entities (NLEs), beneficiaries contribution of Rs 1316.60 crore and budgetary support of Rs 939.48 crore with a  loan lending period of 5 years from 2018-19 to 2022-23. The FIDF provides concessional finance to the Eligible Entities (EEs), including State Governments/Union Territories and State entities for development of identified fisheries infrastructure facilities.  The concessional finance is provided by the Nodal Loaning Entities (NLEs) namely (i) National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD), (ii) National Cooperatives Development Corporation (NCDC) and (iii) All scheduled Banks.  Under the FIDF, the Government of India provides interest subvention up to 3% per annum for providing the concessional finance by the NLEs at the interest rate not lower than 5% per annum. Under the FIDF, loan lending over a period of five years and maximum repayment is over a period of 12  years inclusive of moratorium of 2 (two) years on repayment of principal. No proposal has been received from Government of Jharkhand under FIDF.
