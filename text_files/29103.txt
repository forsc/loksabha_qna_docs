                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1095






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Jan Aushadhi Outlet in Uttar Pradesh




Arun Kumar Sagar














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has taken any steps to open Jan Aushadhi outlet for the poor in the backward areas in Uttar Pradesh particularly in Shahjahanpur Parliamentary Constituency; and (b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS(Dr. MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)                               (a)  & (b) There are about 1,177 Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi Kendras (PMBJKs) in the state of Uttar Pradesh as on 28.11.2021, of which 6 PMBJKs are functional in Shahjahanpur district. Out of these 1,177 PMBJKs functioning in the State, 62 are functional in aspirational districts identified by the NITI Aayog. District-wise list of PMBJKs functional in Uttar Pradesh is enclosed as Annexure-I and aspirational district-wise list of PMBJKs in Uttar Pradesh is enclosed as Annexure-II respectively.Pharmaceuticals & Medical Devices Bureau of India (PMBI), the implementing agency of Pradhan Mantri Bhartiya Janaushadhi  Pariyojana (PMBJP) has entered an MoU on 10.08.2017 with Health & Family Welfare Department, Government of Uttar Pradesh for opening of 1,000 PMBJKs. Till 28.11.2021, 140 PMBJKs have been opened in various districts in the state.Letters at various levels are sent regularly by the Department of Pharmaceuticals/ PMBI to the State Governments, including the State of UP for expediting opening of PMBJP kendras. PMBI has in July, 2021 issued Expression of Interest (EOI) inviting applications for opening of PMBJKs in 265 districts in the country, including 10 districts of Uttar Pradesh.
