                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3298






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Child Labour Survey




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the State Government of Chhattisgarh has sent a proposal to the Union Government on 5.5.2018 for releasing funds for conducting child labour survey/ identification in 27 districts of the State as per the new guidelines for centrally sponsored revision under Child Labour Rehabilitation Scheme 2016;(b)the details of the desired action taken so far on the said proposal; and(c)whether the Ministry is not serious on the said proposal of the State Government due to which child labourers are not getting desired benefits?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c):	As ascertained from the State Government of Chhattisgarh, they had submitted a proposal dated 05th May 2018 to the Ministry of Labour and Employment  for release of funds for child labour survey/ identification in 27 districts of the State. However, the said proposal has not been received in the concerned Division of the Ministry. A copy of the proposal has been called for from the State Government.*****
