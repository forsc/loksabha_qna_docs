                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2499






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Funds For Development of Forts in Telangana




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has received any proposal to allocate funds for development of forts including laying of cable car (ropeway), rappelling practice facility, laser shows, rock climbing school in the country including Telangana particularly Bhongir Fort, Kolanupaka, Phanigiri, Buddhavanam project at Vijayapuri near Nagarjuna Sagar, etc.; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and action taken thereon, State/Fort wise in the last five years and current year till date; and(c) 	the funds sanctioned/released/spent for each such project till date State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM                        		        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (c):	The Ministry of Tourism  under its schemes of  ‘Swadesh Darshan’ provides financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory Administrations/ Central Agencies etc. including Telangana for development of tourism infrastructure in the country. The projects sanctioned under the Swadesh Darshan scheme include development of tourism facilities such as   rappelling, sound & light shows, adventure equipment, cafeteria, conference hall, Illumination of forts, museums, etc. Presently, the Ministry of Tourism is not sanctioning any new projects under its Swadesh Darshan scheme. The details of the projects sanctioned under Swadesh Darshan scheme including that of Telangana is annexed.********
