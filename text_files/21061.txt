                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2291






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Impact of Pandemic on Taxi Operators




Rajmohan Unnithan














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has commissioned any study on the adverse impact of COVID-19 pandemic on the taxi operators working in tourism sector in India; (b) 	whether the Government of India has taken any steps to mitigate the adverse impact of the pandemic on the taxi operators in India; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a):	No formal study has been instituted to assess the adverse impact of COVID-19 pandemic on the taxi operators working in tourism sector in India. However, Ministry of Tourism, Government of India has engaged National Council of Applied Economic Research (NCAER) in January, 2021 to conduct study on “India and the Coronavirus Pandemic: Economic Losses for Households Engaged in Tourism and Policies for Recovery”. One of the objective of this study is to quantify the sector-wise and overall loss in income of the economy and of the household sector and also the loss in jobs, due to the impact of Coronavirus pandemic on the tourism sector.(b) & (c):	The Ministry of Tourism recognizes the importance of protective steps and relief measures to ensure the survival and revival of the tourism industry. The steps taken to mitigate adverse impact of the pandemic on tourism industry including travel and tour operators in India are at Annexure.*******
