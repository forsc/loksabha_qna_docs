                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2943






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Setu Bharatam Programme




Satyadev Pachauri














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of National Highways passing through the State of Uttar Pradesh which have been made railway level crossing free under Setu Bharatam Programme; (b) 	the funds allocated and released for the purpose during the last three years and the current year; (c) 	whether the work under this flagship programme is progressing in accordance with the time frame set by the Government; and (d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)         One  level crossing each on National Highway No. 91, 75E and 28B in the state of Uttar Pradesh have been taken up for construction of ROB under Setu Bharatam Programme.(b)        Funds are allocated and released to State Governments on yearly basis under different budget heads for all the NH works being executed by the State PWDs.  (c) & (d)    There has been delay in some of the ROB projects under Setu Bharatam due to delay in land acquisition, delay in utility shifting, delay in approval of GAD, poor response in bidding leading to invitation of bids multiple times, court cases, etc.*****
