                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3695






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Land Use for Central Vista




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received any proposal from Delhi 	Development Authority for allotment of  land use for Central 	vista, construction of residence for dignitaries and some 	departments; and(b)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                              (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI) (a) to (b) Land use have been changed by the Government in consultation with Delhi Development Authority (DDA) in respect of some of the plots falling in Zone-D and Zone-C for the purpose of Central Vista Project. These change of land use have been notified by the Government in the Gazette of India vide notification dated 20th March, 2020 and 28th October, 2020.                                      *****
