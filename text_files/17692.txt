                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1451






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Prohibition of Manual Scavengers and their Rehabilitation




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1451TO BE ANSWERED ON. 20.09.2020PROHIBITION OF MANUAL SCAVENGERS AND THEIR REHABILITATION 1451. SHRI NATARAJAN P.R.  Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the details of budget provision made/allocated/ utilised for the implementation of prohibition of manual scavengers and their rehabilitation during each of the last three years and the current year, State and year-wise; (b) the details and number of families of safai karamcharis benefitted during the above period; and (c) the details of unutilised fund held and their disposal as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(RAMDAS ATHAWALE)(a):   The Budget Allocation (B.E.), Revised Allocation (R.E) and Funds released under the “Self Employment Scheme for the Rehabilitation of Manual Scavengers (SRMS)” during the last three years and current year upto 15.9.2020 are as under:(Rs. in crores)YEAR	B.E	R.E.	FUNDS RELEASED2017-18	5.00	5.00	5.002018-19	20	85.76	85.762019-20	110	99.93	84.802020-21Upto 15.9.2020	110	-	0(b):  The target group of the SRMS are identified manual scavengers and  their dependents. Safai Karmacharis who are not manual scavengers and their families are not eligible for the benefits of the Scheme. The number of identified manual scavengers/dependents who were provided rehabilitation benefits under SRMS during the last three years and the current year upto 15.9.2020 are as under:YEAR	ONETIME CASH ASSISTANCE	SKILL DEVELOPMENT TRAINING	CAPITAL SUBSIDTY FOR SELF EMPLOYMENT PROJECTS2017-18	1171	334	1512018-19	18079	1682	1512019-20	13246	2532	1142020-21	13990	-	30(c):  The implementing agency of the Scheme, National Safai Karmacharis Finance and Development Corporation (NSKFDC) is having Rs. 11.80 crores as unutilised funds as on 15.9.2020 for providing rehabilitation benefits to  the remaining identified manual scavengers and their dependents during the remaining period of the current financial year.****
