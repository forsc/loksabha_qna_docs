                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4611






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Rajiv Gandhi National Fellowship




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has initiated a Central scheme under the name of Rajiv GandhiNational Fellowship (RGNF) for SC/ST candidates from 2005- 2006 onwards, if so, thedetails thereof;(b) whether the said scheme is implemented by UGC, if so, the total amount of fundsallocated to the Rashtrasant Tukdoji Maharaj Nagpur University (RSTM) by UGC under thesaid scheme during the years from 2007 to 2016, the details thereof, year-wise;(c) the details of candidates in RSTM, Nagpur University who were awarded the fellowshipunder the RGNF during the said years and how many of these awardees have successfullycompleted their M.Phil./ Ph. D. degree; and(d) whether the total amount of fellowship allocated under the RGNF Scheme has beendisbursed to their respective candidates after their successful completion of M.Phil./Ph. D.degree during the years 2007 to 2016 and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasonstherefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): Yes, Sir. The Rajiv Gandhi National Fellowship (RGNF) scheme for Scheduled Caste(SC) and Scheduled Tribes (ST) is formulated and funded by the Ministry of Social Justice &Empowerment and Ministry of Tribal Affairs (MoTA) respectively. The scheme providesfinancial assistance as fellowship, contingency and HRA etc. to selected candidatesbelonging to SC/ST Categories for pursuing M.Phil/Ph.D research on full time/regular basis inUniversities/Institutions/ Colleges in India.(b) & (c): Yes, Sir. The University Grants Commission (UGC) is implementing RGNF forSC/ST candidates. The RGNF for ST is fully transferred to MoTA from the selection year2017-18.As informed by the UGC, the scholars are selected based upon the State-wise slotsfixed by the respective Ministries. Thus, the fellowships are awarded to the scholarsirrespective of University/Institution/College.Funds amounting to Rs. 398.20 lakhs and Rs. 42.80 lakhs have been released toRashtrasant Tukdoji Maharaj Nagpur University (RSTM) under RGNF-SC and RGNF-STschemes respectively for the period 2006-07 to 2016-17. The total number of beneficiaries forthe same period has been 106 under RGNF-SC and 11 under RGNF-ST from RSTM.The UGC has further informed that these schemes are on tenure basis andscholarship is provided to the scholars for five years or till the submission of their Ph.D thesis,whichever is earlier. The fellowship is paid as long as there is confirmation from therespective institutions of satisfactory research work by the scholars but limited to maximum of5 years.(d): Rs.1123.91 crore and Rs.389.06 crore were released towards RGNF for SC and RGNFfor ST between the financial years 2007-08 to 2016-17 respectively.*****
