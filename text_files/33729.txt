                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

103






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2022






FDI Inflow




A. Ganeshamurthi














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of investment inflows into India through Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) during the last five years till date;(b) 	the details of various sectors mainly benefited from FDI including infrastructure sectors, in terms of investment amount and employment generated; and(c) 	the details and the share of FDI in the GDP of the country during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c):        A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.      ********   STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 103 FOR ANSWER ON 09TH FEBRUARY, 2022.
