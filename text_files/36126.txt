                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3564






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Development of Airports under UDAN Scheme




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of airports taken up for development under UDAN Scheme in the State of Maharashtra; and(b) the number of airports developed and the status of the progress of these airports?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) & (b):   An airport /airstrip /helipad /heliport /water aerodrome which is included in the awarded routes of Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik) and requires upgradation/development for commencement of RCS operations, is developed under "Revival of unserved and underserved airports" scheme. The Government of India has approved an amount of Rs. 4500 crore under "Revival of unserved and underserved airports" scheme for revival and upgradation of airports awarded under UDAN. ;Under the UDAN scheme, Airports Authority of India (AAI) has awarded 105 routes involving unserved & underserved airports/airstrips namely Jalgaon, Kolhapur, Sholapur, Nasik, Nanded, Amravati, Sindhudurg, Ratnagiri, Gondia in the State of Maharashtra to operate RCS flights. As on 21.03.2022, out of these nine (9) identified airports, six(6) airports namely Jalgaon, Kolhapur, Ozar (Nasik), Nanded, Sindhudurg and Gondia have been operationalized and  Selected Airline Operators have commenced RCS flight operations from these six airports . ;;******
