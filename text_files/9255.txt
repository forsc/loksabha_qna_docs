                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2911






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






CSR Funds of CPSEs




Nama Nageswara Rao














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) funds of Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) under the Ministry spent during the last two years;(b)	the details of areas where CSR funds were spent during the said period; (c)	whether any review or follow up has been made in the last two years to know the benefits accrued due to CSR initiatives by the Ministry; (d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (e)	the quantum of CSR funds contributed to PM’s fund during the last two years; and(f)	the quantum of CSR funds of the Ministry spent on amelioration of poverty and ensuring gender equality?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a)&(b):	The National Small Industries Corporation Limited (NSIC) is the only Public Sector Undertaking under the administrative control of the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises. The details of Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) projects/activities of NSIC and the areas where CSR were spent during the last two years are given at Annexure-I and Annexure-II, respectively. (c)&(d):	An evaluation cum impact assessment study for CSR projects /activities was conducted by NSIC in FY 2017-18 through the Society for Socio-Economic Studies and Services (SSESS), a National level Data Collection, Processing & Analytical Research & Development Oriented Institution. The report assigned ‘Outstanding & Great performance’ to the NSIC’s CSR projects.  (e)&(f): 	There was no contribution from CSR fund of NSIC to PM’s fund during the last two years. However, the details of CSR funds spent on amelioration of poverty and ensuring gender equality is enclosed at Annexure-III.
