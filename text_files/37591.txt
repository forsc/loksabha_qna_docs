                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4925






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Port Projects in Visakhapatnam




Girish Bhalchandra Bapat













                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                     Rahul Ramesh Shewale
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has inaugurated several port projects in Vishakhapatnam Port Trust in the recent past;(b) 	if so, the details and the salient features thereof;(c)	the extent to which these projects will enable the ports to cut down their logistics cost, improve supply chains and make local goods globally competitive in line with Maritime India Vision 2030; (d) 	the details of the expenditure likely to be incurred along with the funds allocated, released for these projects; and (e) 	the time by which these projects are likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)&(b) Yes, Sir. The details of the projects which have been inaugurated in recent past at the Port of Visakhapatnam are as under:-<pre>S.No.	Project details with salient features	Date of Inauguration1.	“Grade Separator from H-7 to Convent Junction”  which separates Port traffic from general traffic to provide hassle free movement .	23.09.20212.	“Mobile Container Scanner”  to check radioactive    material / contra band materials for smoothening the logistics for a swifter turnaround.	23.02.20223.	 “Sagarmala Convention Centre” for the benefit and welfare of Port employees and general public at large.	23.02.20224.	 “Multi-Beam Echo Sounder (MBES)” installed in Nirmal Survey launch which will help VPA to strengthen its Hydrographic survey operations.	02.03.2022                                                     </pre>(c)	These projects will help in lowering overall operational costs of ports, increasing efficiency, developing Indian Ports’ strategic importance in the South Asian region and welfare of general public at large. (d)&(e) The details of the project cost and the released amount as on date are as under:-<pre>S.No.	Project 	Project Cost(Rs. in Crore)	Amount released (Rs. in Crore)	Completion Status1.	Grade Separator from H-7 to Convent Junction			59.91	29.94	Completed2.	Mobile Container Scanner	30.0	15.89	Completed3.	 Sagarmala Convention Centre	25.0	24.02	Completed4.	 Multi-Beam Echo Sounder (MBES)	1.67	1.67	Completed                                                         </pre>                                        *****
