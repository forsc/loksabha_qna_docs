                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2109






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Compensatory Afforestation in Telangana




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has recently released money to Government of Telangana under Compensatory Afforestation Act after repeated requests from the State Government and direction from the Supreme Court;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the total money collected from State of Telangana since 2014 June under CAMPA and amount released to the State since 2014;(d)	whether the Supreme Court has directed the Government to release Rs. 54,000 crores of CAMPA funds to States;(e)	if, so the details thereof; and(f)	the details of pending projects under CAMPA from Telangana?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO) (a), (b) & (c)	It was Supreme Court direction, which resulted in piling up of CAMPA fund. Government passed on its own Compensatory Afforestation Fund (CAF) Act, 2016. Supreme Court okayed it. (d) & (e) Hon’ble Supreme Court vide order dated 28.01.2019 in IA No. 5891 of 2019 in Writ Petition (C) No. 202 of 1995 in the matter T.N. Godavarman Thirumulpad V/s Union of India and Ors. inter alia directed the Central Government to create the State Funds and to transfer the amount to the State Funds as per the provisions of the CAF Act (Annexure-I).(f) NIL
