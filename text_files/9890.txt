                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3330






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya in Gujarat




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya situated in Botad, Gujarat does not have itsown building due to which students are facing problems;(b) if so, whether the Government has any proposal to construct new building for the saidschool;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d): Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya (JNV) in Botad is presently functioning from itstemporary site in the building provided by the State Government since 2017-18. Land forconstruction of permanent building for JNV, Botad has been allotted by the DistrictAdministration and possession has been taken by the Samiti to construct a permanent building asper Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti norms.*****
