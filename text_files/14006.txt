                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2773






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






World Class Railway Stations under PPP




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of Railway stations in the country identified to be developed as world class railway stations under Public Private Partnership (PPP);(b) the funds allocated/released for the schemes during the last three years;(c) whether the works under the scheme have started in the country; and(d) if so, the details thereof including the number of stations which have already been developed under this scheme, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2773 SHRI M. BADRUDDIN AJMAL TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 11.03.2020 REGARDING WORLD CLASS RAILWAY STATIONS UNDER PPP(a) and (b): Station redevelopment is planned by leveraging real estate potential of sparable land and air space in and around the stations.  It is Railway’s endeavour to generally develop stations at no cost to Railways on PPP mode. For this, Railway conducts techno-economic feasibility studies of stations across the country.  Based on the outcome of these feasibility studies, stations are taken up for redevelopment in phases. (c) and (d): At present the works of station redevelopment have been taken up at Habibganj (Madhya Pradesh) on PPP mode.  *****
