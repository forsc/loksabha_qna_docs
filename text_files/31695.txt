                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3071






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Industries in Tribal Area




Durga Das Uikey














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the status of industries in tribal areas; and (b)	the work being done in tribal areas of Madhya Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a) & (b):	The Ministry of MSME implements a credit-linked subsidy scheme named Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) through Banks since 2008-09 with Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) as Nodal Agency for generating employment in the Country by setting up of micro-enterprises in the non-farm sector. Under PMEGP, Scheduled Tribes, OBCs, minorities, women, ex-servicemen, physically handicapped, beneficiaries belonging to North Eastern Region, hill and border areas, etc. are given subsidy of 35% in rural areas and 25% in urban area. The maximum cost of project is Rs.25 lakh in the manufacturing sector and Rs.10 lakh in the service sector. State wise number of units set up, Margin Money disbursed and Employment generated of Scheduled Tribe beneficiaries under PMEGP including Madhya Pradesh during the last three years is given at Annexure-ISince 2018-19, the financial assistance scheme for expansion/upgrading of the existing PMEGP/REGP/MUDRA units (for manufacturing units upto 1.00 Cr. and for service/ trading unit upto Rs.25.00 Lakhs with subsidy of 15% for non- NER and 20% for NER and hilly states) has also been introduced.	Ministry of MSME also runs an Udyam Portal for registration of MSME units. District wise total MSME units registered under Udyam Portal Since inception till 13.12.2021 for Madhya Pradesh is given at Annexure-II.	Under Micro and Small Enterprises – Cluster Development Program (MSE-CDP) of this Ministry, three Infrastructure Development Projects have been completed in tribal areas in Khargone, Sioni and Shivpuri Districts of Madhya Pradesh.
