                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4679






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Night Landing Facility at Airports




Jyotirmay Singh Mahato













                                                    Jugal Kishore Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether night landing facility is not available at several airports in the country including West Bengal and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government proposes to upgrade the technology to introduce night landing facility at the above airports;(c) if so, whether any time-limit has been fixed for this purpose; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) All the operational airports in the country having scheduled flight operations have night landing facility, except in 23 airports, details of which are given in Annexure.(b) to (d) The upgradation of airports, including provision of night landing facility is a continuous process, which depends upon operational requirements, traffic demand, commercial feasibility etc. and varies from airport to airport in the country.******AnnexureOperational Airports with Scheduled flight operations without night landing facility:S.
