                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

598






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Nanded Railway Division




Prataprao Govindrao  Patil Chikhalikar














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any plans to connect Nanded Railway Division with Central Railway;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government is aware that proper attention is not being given to the developmental works of Nanded Division Railway coming under South Central Railways due to which inconvenience is being caused to the public; and(d) if so, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)	[	(a): No, Sir. (b): Do not arise. (c) & (d): No specific case of neglect of Nanded Division by South Central Railway has come to notice of Railway administration. All possible efforts are being made by the Indian Railways to provide best services to the traveling public in the region.*****
