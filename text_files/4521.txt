                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4244






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Declaration of NH




Anil Firojiya














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	whether  the Akola-Burhanpur-Indore-Ujjain-Agar-Garoth road has been declared as National Highway (NH);(b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the time by which it is likely to be declared as NH;(c) 	whether the DPR of the said road and the  survey  for  alignment  has  been completed and if so, the details thereof;(d) 	if not, the time by which it is likely to be completed;(e) 	the details of the works carried out by the Government on the said road during the last  one  year  and  the  current  year; and(f)	 the  present  status  of  the process of acquisition of land for the purpose along with the time by which the said process is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) 	The road from Akola-Burhanpur-Indore-Ujjain-Agar-Garoth is a declared National highway except the stretches from Indore to Ujjain and Agar to Garoth. The details of declared stretches of National Highways from Akola to Garoth is at Annexure-I. (c) & (d) The status of DPR and alignment survey of the aforesaid road is at Annexure-II.(e)	The details of the development and maintenance works carried out on the aforesaid road during the last  one  year  and  the  current  year is at Annexure-III.(f)	The present status of land acquisition for the aforesaid road is at Annexure-IV.
