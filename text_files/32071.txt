                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3537






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Projects Formulated For Singheshwar Sthan Under Swadesh Darshan Scheme




Dileshwar Kamait














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware that ‘Singheshwar Sthan’ is a famous tourist and religious place situated in Madhepura district of Bihar; (b) 	whether any project has been formulated for the above ‘Singheshwar Sthan’ under Swadesh Darshan Scheme;(c) 	if so, details thereof; and(d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                          MINISTER OF TOURISM 				(SHRI G.KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d):	 Yes sir, Singheshwar Sthan situated in Madhepura district of Bihar is a tourist and religious place. 	Development of Tourism infrastructure is primarily the responsibility of the State Government.  However, the Ministry of Tourism under its ‘Swadesh Darshan’ scheme provides financial assistance to the State Governments/ Union Territory Administrations / Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure.  The projects under the schemes are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/(UTs) and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines,  utilization of funds released earlier etc. Singheshwar Sthan is not  included  in the  list of  sanctioned  projects  under  Swadesh Darshan  scheme.  ********
