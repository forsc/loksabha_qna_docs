                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

42






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Bank Loan Targets




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the public sector banks located especially in backward and rural areas of the country as on date, State-wise and Bank-wise;(b)	the details of the deposits and loans given during the last three years, State-wise and branch-wise; (c)	 whether the branches of banks have achieved their targets of loan disbursement especially in backward and rural areas;(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (e)	the steps being taken or proposed to be taken by the Government to achieve the said targets? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(a) to (e): Reserve Bank of India (RBI) collects, collates and publishes data on bank branch locations and bank deposits and loans. RBI data categorises location only as rural, semi-urban, urban and metropolitan. RBI data on deposits and loans is available for the bank as a whole. As per RBI information, State- and Union-territory-wise details of Public Sector Bank (PSB) branches located in rural and semi-urban areas are at Annexure 1, and the bank-wise details of deposits and loans outstanding of PSBs during the last three financial years are at Annexure 2. As regards branch loan disbursement targets, it is informed that branches are not given targets for loan disbursement. Banks target total credit outstanding and as per RBI data on domestic operations, gross loans and advances of PSBs grew by Rs. 5,32,053 crore during the financial year 2018-19, registering credit growth of 10.1%.  *****
