                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4200






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Incentives under Sub-Mission of Agro-forestry




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government has implemented the Sub-Mission of Agro-forestry (SMAF) under the National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA) to promote tree plantation on farmland for enhancing productivities of crops and cropping system;(b)  if so, the incentive provided in other schemes for horticulture/miscellaneous tree plantation;(c)   whether there is any provision to extend the SMAF in other districts of Odisha; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	    Yes sir.  Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare is presently implementing Sub-Mission on Agroforestry (SMAF) Scheme under National Mission for sustainable agriculture (NMSA) to promote tree plantation on farmland for enhancing productivities of crops and cropping system in 23 States/ UTs including State of Odisha.(b):    Under the SMAF scheme, 50% assistance to farmers is given through State Govt. for nursery development, boundary plantation and block plantation of prominent tree species. Incentives are also provided under Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) scheme for holistic growth of the horticulture sector covering fruits, vegetables, root and tuber crops, mushrooms, spices, flowers, aromatic plants, coconut, cashew etc. Assistance to farmers is given for quality planting material, drip system, integrated nutrient management, integrated pest management etc. (c) & (d):      The State Government of Odisha may implement SMAF scheme in any district of the State as per their requirement.*****
