                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

903






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Impact on Import of Crude Oil




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the oil production cuts by Saudi Arabia has impacted the import of crude oil in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) whether the State owned refineries have reviewed their oil import contracts in the current financial year and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b):	The details of total crude oil import and percentage crude oil import from Saudi Arabia by Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies during the last three years and current year are as under:- Particulars	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22 (April-October) (P)Total crude oil import – In MMT 	226.5	227.0	196.5	118.5Percentage share of crude oil import from Saudi Arabia out of total crude oil import%	17.6	18.3	18.0	15.9Source: Petroleum Planning & Analysis Cell, (P): ProvisionalOil production cuts by Saudi Arabia have not significantly impacted crude oil import to India and percentage share of crude oil import from Saudi Arabia.(c):	Pursuant to the decision of the Cabinet on 06.04.2016, the Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies formulate policies for import of crude oil in their best commercial interest and in accordance with the extant guidelines of procurement. Accordingly, Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies import crude oil as per refinery requirements. Further, the contract agreement for import of crude oil for a financial year is finalized in the previous financial year. ********
