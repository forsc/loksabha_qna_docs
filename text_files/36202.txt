                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3557






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Integrated Command and Control Centres




Midhun Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of Integrated Command and Control Centres (ICCCs) that have set up and are in the process of being set-up under the Smart Cities Mission including in the State of Andhra Pradesh;(b)	whether the Government has taken any steps to speed up the process of setting up of such centres in the all the Smart cities; and(c)	if so, the details therefor?






ANSWER




                                                                         THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE            MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                  (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) to (c): Government of India launched the Smart Cities Mission (SCM) on 25 June 2015.  100 Smart cities have been selected through 4 rounds of competition from January 2016 to June 2018.  So far, Integrated Command & Control Centres (ICCCs) have been operationalized in 76 Smart Cities.  List of Smart Cities which have operationalized ICCCs, including those in Andhra Pradesh, is given in Annexure-I. These ICCCs are playing an important role in ensuring better monitoring and efficiency in areas such as traffic management, crime management, solid waste management, water distribution management, grievance redressal, inter-departmental coordination, disaster preparedness and management, mitigation of environmental pollution etc.Following steps are being taken for speeding up implementation of ICCC projects in remaining Smart Cities:•	Fortnightly progress reviews with concerned stakeholders.•	Monitoring of project progress through centralized dashboard.•	City visits for fast tracking of the implementation.•	Regular communication in form of letters, video conferences etc.•	Technical support in planning and implementation stages.The period of implementation of SCM has also been extended upto June 2023 and all Smart Cities are expected to complete their projects, including ICCCs, within the stipulated time.                                       ******
