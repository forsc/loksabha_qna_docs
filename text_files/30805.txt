                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2429






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan in Telangana




Kavitha Malothu













                                                    Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta
                                                








                                                    Dayakar Pasunoori
                                                








                                                    Gaddam Ranjith Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of works such as construction of new classrooms, buildings, playgrounds,toilets, etc., taken up under Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan in Telangana in Secondary andSenior Secondary Schools since implementation of the scheme, year-wise and districtwise;(b) whether the targets set have been achieved during the last three years, if so, thedetails thereof district-wise, year-wise and component-wise;(c) whether the Government proposes to make NCC mandatory in all Secondary andSenior Secondary Schools as per the New Education Policy; and(d) if not, the reasons thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SHRIMATI ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) Details of civil works for science labs, new classrooms, buildings, drinking water,toilets (girls, boys & CWSN) etc. under Samagra Shiksha in Telangana in Secondarylevel since implementation of the scheme i. e. 2018-19 (year-wise and district wise) isenclosed as Annexure-A.There are no civil works sanctioned for senior secondary level from 2018-19 to 2021-22.(b) District-wise and year-wise targets and achievements of civil works sanctionedunder Samagra Shiksha in Telangana for secondary level during the last three years(2018-2019, 2019-2020& 2020-2021) is enclosed as Annexure-B.There are no civil works sanctioned for senior secondary level from 2018-19 to 2021-22.(c) & (d) NCC is entirely a voluntary programme and it is upto the schools to opt for it.The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has introduced NCC as an optionalelective subject in classes IX-XII in its curriculum. The schools may opt to offer NCCafter obtaining necessary approval from the Directorate of NCC.
