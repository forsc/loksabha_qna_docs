                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

874






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Smart City Project in Puducherry




 Vaithilingam Ve.














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether Puducherry is one among the city selected under “Smart City Project” and if so, the present status of the project there;(b)  the details of assistance from the Union Government so far;(c) whether there is any external assistance from abroad for this project; and (d)  if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS              (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)	& (b): Government of India launched the Smart Cities Mission (SCM) on 25 June 2015 for development of 100 cities as Smart Cities. Puducherry was selected for development as a Smart City in Round-3 in June 2017.  The status of Smart City projects of Puducherry are given below:-(Amount in ? crore)DPR Stage	Tender Stage	Work Order Stage	Work Completed	Total Projects	Total AmountNo. of Projects	Amount	No. of Projects	Amount	No. of Projects	Amount	No. of Projects	Amount		51	604.68	9	40.47	42	163.89	3	13.60	105	822.64(Source: Puducherry Smart City.As on 26.11.2021)So far, the Central Government has released ? 111 crore for Puducherry Smart City; out of which ? 40.04 crore has been utilized by them.(c)  & (d): The City Investments to Innovate, Integrate and Sustain (CITIIS) Program has been launched in July 2018 with AFD, European Union and National Institute of Urban Affairs (NIUA) for providing technical and financial assistance of € 100 Million to cities in implementing urban infrastructure projects that are integrated, innovation-driven and sustainable. Puducherry is one of 12 Smart Cities which have been selected through a challenge process for funding and technical assistance under the CITIIS Program. Under the program, Puducherry is implementing project named ‘Our Neighbourhood is your Neighbourhood too’ at a cost of ? 99.71 crore. Apart from this, Puducherry Smart City has reported that there is no other external assistance for its Smart City projects.                          *****
