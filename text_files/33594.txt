                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1016






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






National Population Register




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the progress made under phase-wise census and the collection of details to update the National Population Register (NPR); and(b) the details thereof till date State-wise and category-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b):	The intent of the Government for conducting Census 2021 was notified in Gazette of India on 28th March, 2019.  Subsequently, a notification under Citizenship Rules was published in the Gazette of India on 31st July, 2019 to prepare and update the Population Register along with the first phase of Census i.e.  Houselisting and Housing Census.  However, due to outbreak of COVID-19 pandemic, the conduct of Census 2021, updating of National Population Register and the related field activities have been postponed until further orders. ******
