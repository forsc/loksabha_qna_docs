                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4048






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Package to Entertainment Industry




Nusrat Jahan Ruhi














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the entertainment industry has been badly affected due to COVID-19 pandemic;(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the guidelines issued by Government to ensure safe and smooth functioning  of entertainment industry;(c)	 whether the Government proposes to announce economic package for the revival of entertainment industry and if so, the details thereof; and (d)	whether the Government proposes to compensate the entertainment industry by other methods like tax rebate and more and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR):(a) and (b) Yes, Sir. After the easing of restrictions post lockdown that were imposed to contain the spread of COVID-19 pandemic, Ministry of Information & Broadcasting circulated Guiding Principles and Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) on preventive measures for Media production in consultation with Ministry of Health & Family Welfare to restart shooting of Films and TV serials on 21.08.2020. Subsequently, Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) were issued by Ministry of Information and Broadcasting on 6.10.2020 to allow opening of cinemas/theatres/multiplexes upto 50% of seating capacity with continued safeguards of COVID-19 related protocols. Further, as per detailed guidelines issued by Ministry of Information and Broadcasting on 01.02.2021, opening of cinemas/theatres/multiplexes upto 100% of seating capacity with continued safeguards of COVID-19 related protocols has been allowed.(c) and (d)	A special economic and comprehensive package called Aatma Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan of more than Rs. 20 lakh crore equivalent to 10% of India’s GDP has been announced by the Government in the fight against COVID-19. Further, the opening of cinemas/theatres/multiplexes upto 100% of seating capacity along with other revenue streams such as food and beverage has also helped in the revival of the Entertainment Industry.*****
