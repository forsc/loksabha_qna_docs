                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

262






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Life Insurance Corporation of India




S. Ramalingam













                                                    T. R. Paarivendhar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the total number of offices/branches of Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC) functioning in the country and the number of officers/employeesworking therein, State-wise including Tamil Nadu;(b)	the total number of various policy holder who are paying premium across the country, policy-wise; and(c)	the total profit earned by the LIC for the last three years, zonal-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE FINANCE MINISTER(a) to (c): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.*** Statement as referred to in reply of Lok Sabha Starred Question no. *262 for answer on 15thMarch, 2021/ Phalguna 24, 1942 (Saka) regarding “Life Insurance Corporation of India” by SHRI RAMALINGAM S. AND DR. T. R.
