                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1126






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Kishori Health Cards




Ganesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    )       whether Kishori Health Cards for Adolescent girls are being maintained at the Anganwadi Centre under Scheme for Adolescent Girls to record the information about the weight, height, Body Mass Index (BMI), along with the services under the scheme; (b)      if so, the details and the outcome thereof as on date, State-wise including Satna district in Madhya Pradesh;(c)	whether the Government is implementing scheme for Adolescent Girls, a centrally sponsored scheme in Satna district, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	whether any complaint regarding functioning of these cards has been received by the Government from district Satna, if so, the details thereof as on date along with the action taken thereon? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) to (c)     As per the information available, some of the States are maintaining Kishori Health Cards for Adolescent Girls in Angawadi Centres (AWCs) to record the information about the weight, height, Body Mass Index (BMI) along with other services provided under the scheme. Government is implementing Scheme for Adolescent Girls across the country including Satna district of Madhya Pradesh. The details of achievements/outcomes made under the scheme are marked on Kishori Health Card and the card also carries important milestones of Adolescent Girls’ life including mainstreaming them into the schools etc. The information about the maintenance of Kishori Health Cards by the States/UTs is Annexed.(d)	As per the information received from the Government of Madhya Pradesh, Kishori Health Cards are not maintained in the State. Therefore, the question of complaint regarding functioning of these cards does not arise.***** AnnexureSTATEMENT REFERRED IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF LOKSABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 1126 FOR 22.11.2019 REGARDING KISHORI HEALTH CARDS ASKED BY SHRI GANESH SINGHS.
