                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3812






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the details of the Sustainable Development Goals targeted under the Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA);  (b)	the details of the measures undertaken by the Government to strengthen Panchayati Raj Institutions under the above scheme till date;  (c)	whether all Gram Panchayats in the country have been enabled with Panchayat Enterprise Suite and if so, the details thereof, State-wise; and (d)	whether any training workshops have been held by the Government to empower sarpanchs, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	(b) & (d) The main objective of Centrally Sponsored Scheme of Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan is to build capacity of Panchayati Raj Institutions to deliver on Sustainable Development Goals relating to poverty, public health, nutrition, education, gender, sanitation, drinking water, livelihood generation etc which fall in the realm of Panchayats. Under the scheme, financial assistance has been provided to States towards various activities relating to strengthening of Panchayats viz. Capacity Building and Training for Panchayat Elected Representatives (PERs), Panchayats Functionaries (PFs) and other stakeholders, administrative and technical support, training infrastructure, construction of Gram Panchayat Bhawans etc. As per the information available, 49.11 lakh PERs, PFs and other stakeholders including Sarpanchs, across States/ UTs have been trained under the scheme. (c)           A core software applications for Panchayats namely Panchayat Enterprise Suite (PES) has been developed by Ministry under Mission Mode Project. Current adoption Status of Panchayat Enterprise Suite applications (State-wise) is at Annexure.*****
