                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4945






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Grant for Small Food Processing Units




Vijay Kumar Dubey













                                                    Anurag Sharma
                                                








                                                    Santosh Kumar
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                








                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to provide grants for establishing small food processing cold chain units in near future;(b)	if so, the details thereof, especially in Uttar Pradesh including Jhansi and nearby areas, Delhi/ NCR and North Eastern States;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	whether there is any other Scheme also to benefit small and medium farmers through low investment processing units?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a) to (c):  Ministry of Food Processing Industries is implementing the Central Sector Scheme of Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition Infrastructure as one of the component of Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana with the objective of arresting post-harvest losses of perishables by encouraging the creation of primary processing, preservation and value addition infrastructure and transportation facilities (reefer/ insulated vans) across the country. To meet these objectives, the Scheme for Integrated Cold Chain & Infrastructure was launched in 2008. 292 projects have been approved as on 30.06.2019 and out of these, 154 projects have started commercial operations. Ministry does not support setting up of stand-alone cold storage projects. The details of cold chain projects approved by this Ministry till 30.06.2019 in various States / UTs including Uttar Pradesh and North Eastern States under the Scheme is placed at Annexure. No proposal has been approved in Delhi / NCR. (d):	There is no other Scheme in this Ministry to benefit small & medium farmers for setting up of small cold chain units.  *****
