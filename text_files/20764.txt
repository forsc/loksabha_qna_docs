Smartphones for Anganwadi Workers




Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    Ram Kripal Yadav
                                                








                                                    Krishna Pal Singh Yadav
                                                








                                                    Devji Mansingram Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    1878 #   SHRI RANJEETSINGH HINDURAO NAIK NIMBALKAR: 	    DR. KRISHNA PAL SINGH YADAV: 	    SHRI RAM KRIPAL YADAV: 	    SHRI SUDHAKAR TUKARAM SHRANGARE: 	    SHRI DEVJI M. PATEL:    Will the Minister of Women and Child Development be please to state: (a)	Whether the Government has formulated any scheme to provide ‘Make in India’ smart phones to Anganwadi workers to enable them to keep digital records in lieu of written records as it is time consuming for them to maintain written records of all Anganwadi beneficiaries;(b)	If so, the details thereof;(c)	Whether any time limit has been fixed to provide smart phones to all Anganwadi workers of the country including Rajasthan and Maharashtra;(d)	If so, the number of States where smart phones have been distributed till date; and(e)	The details of funds allocated and utilized in this regard for the year 2019-20, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT (SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (d) Anganwadi workers are empowered with the provision of smart phones procured through Government e-Market (GeM) for efficient service delivery. Ministry has been pursuing  with States to saturate access to smart phones for Anganwadi Workers and have recently,  in States review meetings, taken up the issue again. A total of 8.66 lakh smart phones have been procured  by 32 States/UTs including Rajasthan and Maharashtra. Of these, 6.42 lakh have been provided to Anganwadi Workers across 27 States/UTs.(f)	The details of funds allocated to the States/UTs and utilised are given at Anexure. ****  UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 1878Details of funds allocated to the States/UTs and utilised for all components including for procurement of smart phonesAnnexure(Rs.
