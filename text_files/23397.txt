                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4331






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






TRIFED Partnership with ABVKK




 Imtiaz Jaleel Syed














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether the Tribal Co-operative Marketing Development Federation of India proposes to partner with Akil Bhartiya Vanvasi Kalyan Kendra in setting up TRIFOOD Parks in different States to benefit the tribal population of the country; and(b) if so, the future plan chalked out by TRIFED for the welfare of tribals in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b) As informed by Tribal Cooperative Marketing Development Federation of India (TRIFED), an MOU has been signed between TRIFED and M/s Akhil Bhartiya Vanvasi Kalyan Kendra, Jashpur on 03.01.2021. The MoU envisages to establish Van Dhan Vikas Kendras and Tribal Entrepreneurship Development (TED) Parks in States, which intends to provide direct and indirect employment to the tribal families. *****
