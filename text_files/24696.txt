                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

498






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Construction of NHs in Jharkhand




Pashupati Nath Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether construction work on National Highways (NHs) in Dhanbad and Bokaro districts of Jharkhand is progressing as per schedule and if so, the details and present status thereof;(b)	if not, the time by which the said construction work is likely to be completed;(c)	the total number of bridges/culverts completed on the said NHs;(d)	whether the Government proposes to install solar panels along the six-lane and wider highways in the country and utilize that energy and if so, the details thereof, NH-wise; and(e)	whether any work has so far been undertaken in this regard and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)    The details of 2 projects falling in Dhanbad & Bokaro districts are as below:(i)   2/4 lane from Rajganj to WB Border section of NH-32 (Km. 0.000 to Km. 56.889) (Districts Dhanbad and Bokaro): The Scheduled completion date was 09-12-2019. However, due to issues related to LA, impact of Covid-19 and slow progress by the Contractor, the anticipated date of completion is 31-10-2021. The current physical progress is 93.90%.(ii)	Six laning from Khairatunda to BarwaAdda section of NH-2 (Km 360.300 to Km 400.132) (Dhanbad district): The Scheduled completion date was 07-01-2021, however due to issues related to LA, the impact of Covid-19 and slow progress by the Contractor, the anticipated date of completion is 31-10-2021. The current physical progress is 92.20%. (c)  Total 82 and 121 structures such as Bridges, RoBs, Culverts, VUPs/PUPs have been completed on the NH-32 and NH-2 respectively.(d) & (e)    National Highways Authority of India (NHAI)  has signed an MoU with Energy Efficiency Services Ltd.(EESL) [Promoted by Ministry of Power, Government of India as a Joint Venture of four reputed public- sector undertakings NTPC Limited, Power Finance Corporation Limited, REC Limited and POWERGRID Corporation of India Limited], wherein EESL will conduct the feasibility study in co-ordination with Regional Officers of NHAI for establishment of Solar Power projects on the available vacant land parcels with NHAI & rooftops of NHAI buildings/structures at Toll Plaza/ other NHAI owned buildings etc. and will submit the report to NHAI. No proposal has been considered till now.*****
