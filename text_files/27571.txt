                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3177






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Impact of Integrated Wool Development Program




Ritesh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the impact assessment of the Integrated Wool Development Program is underway; (b) if so, the details thereof along with the manner in which this scheme has impacted marginalised groups such as SCs, STs, OBCs and low-income women, State/ UT-wise; (c) if not, the time by which the Government plans to release the impact assessment report of this scheme; and (d) whether the Government plans to include in this report the details of benefits delivered to SCs, STs, OBCs and low-income women and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a):	The Outcome Review/Third Party Evaluation Study of ‘Integrated Wool Development Programme’ (IWDP) 2017-18 to 2019-20 of the Central Wool Development Board, Ministry of Textiles has already been completed.(b) to (d):	Under IWDP, there is no separate scheme for SC/ST/OBC and low income women. The schemes are implemented for overall development of wool sector and most of the schemes do not target individual beneficiaries. No targeted beneficiaries including SC/ST/OBC or lower income group were separately evaluated during impact study of IWDP schemes.***
