                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4008






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Fund for Digital Education




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there has been any endeavour to earmark a separate fund for digital education and awareness of our Tribal fraternity so that their lives may be made easier in the changed scenario; and (b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b): Yes Sir.   Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) through Education and Research Network of India (ERNET India), an autonomous scientific society under (MeitY), is executing a project  entitled “Establishment of Intelligent Educational Infrastructure (SMART) in Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRSs)”.   The 2  year project aims at setting up of digital classrooms in 175 Eklavya schools which are functioning from their own buildings. The project is being funded jointly under Schedule Tribe Component (STC) of MeitY (90% contribution) and Ministry of Tribal Affairs (MoTA) (10% contribution). The total outlay of the project is Rs.3629.75 lakh. *****
