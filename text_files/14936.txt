                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3529






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Reduction in CSR Spending on National Heritage, Arts and Culture




Ravneet Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) as per the latest available figures, whether there has been a substantial reduction in Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) spending on national heritage, arts and culture by companies;(b)   if so, the details thereof for the last three years;(c) whether the Government proposes to take any steps to meet the shortfall in funds availability for work related to national heritage, art and culture due to less CSR contribution by companies mandated to do so; and(d)   if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State (IC) for Culture & Tourism(Shri Prahlad Singh Patel)(a) & (b) Figures on CSR spending during the previous three years are available in the Annual Report of Ministry of Corporate Affairs for the year, 2018-19.(c) & (d) Ministry of Culture is running National Culture Fund since 1996 to encourage and invite CSR participation in furthering the cause of preserving tangible and intangible heritage of our country.
