                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2563






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Economic Development of PwDs




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the initiatives taken for self-employment and promote economic development activities for the benefit of persons with disabilities (PwDs)/divyangjan as on date; and(b) the funds utilised and the number of Persons with Disabilities benefitted particularly in Start up Schemes, state-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE & EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a) The National Handicapped Finance and Development Corporation (NHFDC), a Central Public Sector Enterprise (CPSE) under the administrative control of Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities has taken various initiatives for self-employment and promotion of economic development activities for the benefit of Persons with Disabilities (PwDs).    It includes concessional lending schemes, skill trainings, job connect and marketing support.   (b) The funds utilized and the number of Persons with Disabilities benefitted State-wise under NHFDC schemes for the purpose of extending concessional funds is at Annexure-I.*****
