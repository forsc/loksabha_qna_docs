                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2466






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Quality of Fertilizers




Nalin Kumar Kateel













                                                    Sumalatha Ambareesh
                                                








                                                    Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received complaints about the supply of inferiorand spurious chemicals by local manufacturer or imported into India;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has set any standard to maintain the quality ofchemicals;(d) if so, whether the Government has taken any action against the persons andcompanies involved in supplying spurious and spurious chemicals; and(e) if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS (SHRI MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a) & (b) No specific complain has been received about supply of inferior andspurious chemicals by local manufacturer or imported into India.(c) Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) formulates standards for chemicals and mostof the standards are presently voluntary in nature. To ensure manufacture and importof quality chemicals, Government initiated an exercise to make BIS standards asmandatory for some chemicals under Section 16 of BIS Act, 2016. Accordingly, D/oChemicals & Petrochemicals has notified 48 Quality Control Order (QCO) to make BISstandards as mandatory so far.(d) & (e) Not applicable in view of reply at (a) & (b) above.*****
