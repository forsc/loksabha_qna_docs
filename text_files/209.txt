                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

6






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






POCSO Act




Vinayak Bhaurao Raut














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    the total number of cases registered under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, 2012 along with the number of cases under investigation/sub-judice during the last three years in the country, State-wise including Maharashtra?  
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.*6 FOR ANSWER ON 21.06.2019 RAISED BY SHRI VINAYAK RAUT REGARDING ‘POCSO ACT’.As per information provided by Ministry of Home Affairs in accordance with the latest information published by National Crime Records Bureau for crimes against children during 2014, 2015 and 2016 respectively under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences Act, 2012 the details of number of cases registered, along with the number of cases under investigation/sub-judice during the last three years in the country are as follows:Year	Number of Cases
