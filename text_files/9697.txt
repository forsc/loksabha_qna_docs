                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3164






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






New Family Planning Centres




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the amount earmarked for setting up new family planning centres during the current five year plan, especially in the backward and rural areas of the country as on date;(b) whether the financial target fixed in this regard during the last three years have been achieved; and(c) if so, the details of the financial target fixed and family planning centres opened, State/UT-wise particularly those of backward and rural areas?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):	Under the National Health Mission, the Ministry of Health & Family Welafare follows a health systems approach wherein stand alone family planning centres are not established. Family Planning services are delivered as an integral component of the RMNCAH+N strategy (Reproductive Maternal Neonatal Child and Adolescent Health + Nutrition) through all levels of public health facilities across the country.
