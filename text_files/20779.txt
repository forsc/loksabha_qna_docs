                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1976






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Status of Implementation PMMVY




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)       the status of implementation of Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) in Uttar Pradesh;(b)       the details of cells set up at district-level in the State; and(c)       district-wise details of the beneficiaries under this scheme? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER FOR WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)  (a) 	Under Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY), maternity benefits amounting to Rs.1344.60 crores (including Central and State share) have been paid, as on 29.1.2021, to more than 31.86 lakhs eligible beneficiaries since inception of the scheme in Uttar Pradesh. (b) 	Out of total 75 districts of Uttar Pradesh, district-level PMMVY Cells have been set up in 54 districts of the State. 53 District Programme Coordinators (DPC) and 48 District Programme Assistants (DPA) has been appointed in these 54 district-level PMMVY Cells.
