                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1875






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Repairing of Dilapidated Roads




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether roads are in dilapidated condition in hilly regions of Sunabeda, Soseng, TH Rampur, Lanjigarh and M. Rampur blocks in Kalahandi district in Odisha; (b) if so, the remedial steps taken by the Government in this regard; (c) whether the said blocks are being covered under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS (SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (d) Ministry of Road Transport & Highways is mainly responsible for development and maintenance of National Highways in the country. The hilly regions of Sunabeda, Soseng, TH Rampur, Lanjigarh and M. Rampur blocks in the State of Odisha are situated in  Koraput, Nuapada and Kalahandi districts. The existing National Highways in these districts are maintained in traffic worthy and safe condition within the available resources. Pradhana Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana does not pertain to Ministry of Road Transport & Highways.******
