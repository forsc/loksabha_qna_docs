                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

64






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Frequency of Train




Deepak Baij














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of days on which the Visakhapatnam-Kirandul Night Express (18513/18514) under the East Coast Railways was being operated before corona pandemic;(b) the number of days in a week the said train is being operated at present; and(c) whether there is a continuous demand by the public representatives and passengers to operate this train entire week as it was earlier?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) Prior to discontinuation of all regular passenger carrying train services w.e.f. 23.03.2020, 18513/14 Visakhaptanam-Kirandul Express was being operated as a daily service. (b) At present, 18513/14 Visakhaptanam-Kirandul Express is being operated as a bi-weekly service. (c) Yes, Sir. Representations, including from the Hon’ble Member of Parliament, have been received for running this train as a daily service.*****
