                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1377






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Employment Increase In Organised/Unorganised Sector




Komati Reddy Venkat Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the employment increase; (b)if so, the details thereof organised and unorganised sector comparatively in the last five years sector-wise and if not, the reasons therefor, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b):	As per the Periodic Labour Force Survey (PLFS) conducted by National Statistical Office (NSO), the estimates of Worker Population Ratio (WPR) for persons aged 15 years and above defined as the number of employed persons to total population on usual status basis during the last three years has shown an increasing trend i.e it stood at 46.8% (2017-18), 47.3% (2018-19) and 50.9% (2019-20) respectively.
