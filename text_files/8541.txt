                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2181






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Pension  Scheme




Devji Mansingram Patel














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether a person is required to have important contribution in the field of art to be eligible for pension  scheme;(b)  whether there is any specific criteria for evaluation of the work of artists;   .(c)  if so, the details thereof;(d)  the manner in which the works of the applicant of artists are evaluated by the Government; and(e) the State-wise details of the number of applications received for pension scheme during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (I/C)FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) Yes Sir, the Ministry of Culture administers a scheme namely “Scheme for Pension and Medical Aid to Artistes” for improving the financial and socio-economic status of the old artist and scholars who have contributed significantly in their specialized fields of arts, letters etc. in their active age or are still contributing in the field of arts, letters etc. but due to old age they have to lead a miserable life or are in penury condition.  To be eligible for assistance under the Scheme, a person’s contribution to art & letters, etc must be of significance.(b),(c) & (d) Yes, Sir, the applicant artiste is required to be recognized by the State Government by getting pension of at least Rs.500/- per month from the concerned State Government/UT Administration and also recommended by respective State Govt./U.T. Administration. Further, all eligible applications are placed before an expert committee constituted for evaluating the works of the artists.
