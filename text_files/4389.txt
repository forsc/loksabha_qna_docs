                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4059






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Spectrum Auction and Public Wi-Fi




Faizal P.P. Mohammed













                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of spectrum auction held during each of the last three years; (b) 	whether the Government proposes to roll out five lakh public Wi-Fi hotspot on priority basis and if so, the details and the benefits thereof; and (c) 	whether the Government is planning to set up one lakh digital villages in coordination with the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)	One spectrum auction was conducted during the last three years, in October 2016, in which a quantum of 2354.75 MHz of spectrum was put to auction and a quantum of 964.80 MHz of spectrum was sold. The total bid amount for the spectrum auction was Rs.65789.12 crores.(b)	Cellular Operators Association of India, Internet Service Providers Association of India and Virtual Network Operators Association of India have jointly submitted a proposal for seamless and interoperable internet and broadband services through public Wi-Fi hotspots. As a part of the proposal, they have submitted a roadmap for achieving 5 lakh Wi-Fi hotspots within six months and 1 Million Wi-Fi hotspots within a period of one year. (c)	No such plan is presently under consideration in this Ministry. However the Ministry of Electronics and IT is implementing a Digital Village Pilot Project covering 700 Gram Panchayats (GPs)/villages with at least 1 Gram Panchayat or village per District per State/UT with an implementation period of 3 years and total budget outlay of Rs.98.32 crores.
