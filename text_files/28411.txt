                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

480






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






LHB Coaches




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the current status of Linke Hofmann Busch (LHB) coaches refurbishing facility at Bongaigaon to which the foundation stone was laid in February 2019; (b)    the details of the facilities that started operations and the annual capacity of the factory; and(c)  the total strength of permanent as well as temporary work force employed in the factory?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND                  ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY                                     (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) The work of LHB Coach Refurbishing Facility at Bongaigaon Workshop is under progress. Nearly, 80% of the facilities has been completed. (b) The facilities like LHB Bogie Repair Shop, LHB Coach Repair Shop, Coach Bio Tank overhauling facility and material testing facilities have started.Annual capacity is Periodic Overhauling of 504 LHB coaches.(c) The total number of permanent work force employed at Bongaigaon Workshop is 2200 as on date. However, no temporary work force has been employed. *****
