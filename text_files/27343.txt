                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

265






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.08.2021






Anganwadi Centres in Rented Buildings




 Bhavana Gawali (Patil)













                                                    Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    whether  it  has  come  to  the  notice  of  the Government  that  many Anganwadi  Centres  across  the country  lack  own  buildings,  basic  amenities  such  as clean toilets and access to clean drinking water; (b) 	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra; (c) 	the details of policy regarding taking premises on rent for providing Anganwadi facilities; (d) 	the total  amount  of  rent  paid  for Anganwadi Centres functioning from rented premises during the last three  years; (e) 	the percentage  share  spent  on  rent  out  of the total expenditure on Anganwadi Centres during the said period, State/UT-wise including Maharashtra, Jharkhand, Jammu and Kashmir and Bihar; and (f) 	whether the Government proposes to construct new buildings for Anganwadi Centres instead of hiring premises on rent, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons  therefor?






ANSWER




ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)  (a) to (f) A statement is laid on the Table of the House. ****** STATEMENT REFERRED IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 265 FOR ANSWER ON 06.08.2021 REGARDING ANGANWADI CENTRES IN RENTED BUILDINGS ASKED BY SHRIMATI BHAVANA PUNDALIKRAO GAWALI AND SHRI OMPRAKASH BHUPALSINH ALIAS PAWAN RAJENIMBALKAR (a) & (b) As on 31st March, 2021, out of total 13,87,432 Operational Anganwadi Centres (AWCs)  across the country, 6,54,428 AWCs are running from government buildings, 74,492 AWCs are running from Panchayat buildings and 2,28,749 AWCs are located in Primary Schools. Out of the  total 13,87,432 AWCs,  11,96,458 AWCs have drinking water facility and 10,05,257 AWCs have toilet facility. State/UT-wise number of operational AWCs, AWCs having Drinking water facility and AWCs having Toilet facility is at Annexure-I. (c) Under Anganwadi Services Scheme, cost norms for renting of Anganwadi Centre/Mini-Anganwadi Centre premises are as under: Rural/Tribal	Rs. 1,000/- p.m.Urban	Rs. 4,000/- p.m.Metropolitan	Rs. 6,000/- p.m. (d) & (e) The total amount paid as rent for Anganwadi Centres functioning from rented premises and the percentage share spent on rent out of the expenditure on Anganwadi Services (General) during the last three years, State/UT-wise is at Annexure-II. (f)        For construction of 4 lakh Anganwadi Centres across the country, revised joint guidelines have been issued on 17.02.2016 jointly by Ministry of Women and Child Development and Ministry of Rural Development under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS). Further, a joint advisory has been issued on 20.7.2017 by MWCD and Department of School Education and Literacy to all States/UTs to examine the possibility of relocation of Anganwadi Centres running in rented buildings to the campus of the nearby primary schools located in the habitation of AWC''s catchment to facilitate the transition of the children to the primary stage and ensure universal enrolment.***** Annexure-I Annexure referred in Reply To Part (a) & (b) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No.265 for answer on 06.08.2021 regarding “Anganwadi Centres in Rented Buildings” asked by Shrimati Bhavana Pundalikrao Gawali, Shri Omprakash Bhupalsinh Alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar indicating State/UT-wise Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) running from  Government  Building and AWCs with  Drinking Water & Toilet Facility, as on 31st  March, 2021 S.
