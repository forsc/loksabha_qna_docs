                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3408






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Monitoring of Funds




Bharti Pravin Pawar














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the execution of preservation and development projects with the funds received from the National culture fund are being monitored by the project-implementation committee;(b)	if so, the loopholes brought to the notice of the Government during the monitoring of the same;(c)	the action taken by the Government on its own level on the basis of these loopholes along with the outcome thereof; and(d)	the reaction of the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	Yes sir, the projects are implemented and monitored through a Project Implementation Committee (PIC) under the chairmanship of DG, ASI having a representative of NCF and the donor. The projects undertaken with the funds received from the NCF are also monitored by the respective field officers of ASI(b)        to (d) No loopholes have been brought to the notice of government.
