                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

986






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Ear Machines, Tri-Cycles to the Handicapped Poor People




Kripanath Mallah














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has made any scheme to provide materials like Ear Machine, Tri-Cycle and others etc to the Handicapped poor people within the country, if so, the details thereof;(b) the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized under this scheme within Assam during the last three years and the current year;(c) the target set and achievements made so far along with its response;(d) whether the Government organizes camps to provide such materials to the Handicapped poor people within the country; and(e) if so, the details thereof along with the number of camps organized in Assam so far, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK) (a)  Yes Sir. The Ministry is implementing a Scheme entitled ‘Assistance to Disabled Persons for Purchase/Fitting of Aids and Appliances (ADIP)’ to provide various types of aids and assistive devices including Hearing Aid and Tricycle to eligible persons with disabilities (Divyangjan) which can promote their physical, social and psychological empowerment by reducing the effects of disabilities and enhancing their economic potential. During the last three years and the current year, aids and assistive devices were provided to 10.85 Lakh Divyangjan across the country, involving a total amount of Rs.713.12 crore.  (b) & (c) Being a Central Sector Scheme, no state-wise sanction and allocation of funds is made under the ADIP Scheme.  Further, though there is no specific target fixed for States, the achievements and the response in terms of the  details of funds utilized and number of beneficiaries covered under the Scheme in the State of Assam during the last three years and the current year is as under:-Year	Funds utilized(Rs.in crore)	Number of beneficiaries covered2018-19	0.84	10512019-20	6.83	130002020-21	3.03	130832021-22	2.30	4145Total	13.00	31279 (d) &(e) Yes Sir. The aids and assistive devices under the Scheme are distributed by holding assessment and distribution camps throughout the country.  The details of camps held, funds utilized and number of beneficiaries covered during the last three years and the current year is at Annexure-I.  The district-wise details of camps held in the State of Assam during the last three years and the current year is at Annexure-II.
