                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1505






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Arjun Tank




 Imtiaz Jaleel Syed













                                                    Asaduddin Owaisi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Arjun Tank is awaiting the Government nod for inclusion in the Indian Army;(b) if so, the reasons therefor;(c) the total amount spent by DRDO in upgrading this tank;(d) whether validation trials were completed in 2018; and(e) if so, the time by which this tank is likely to be included in the Indian Army?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) to (e):  Quantity 124 Main Battle Tank (MBT) Arjun Mark-1 tanks have been inducted into the Indian Army since 2008 and two Armoured Regiments are fully operational with these tanks.  DRDO has further developed upgraded MBT Arjun Mark-1A tank.  This upgraded MBT Arjun Mark-1A tank has cleared validation trials in December, 2018.  The delivery schedule and other aspects of the procurements are governed by the terms and conditions of the contract/indent.                                                *****
