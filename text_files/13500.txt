                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2082






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Bharat Gold Mines Ltd.




S. Muniswamy














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    the necessary steps taken by the Government for the development of Bharat Gold Mines Ltd. at Kolar Gold Fields in Karnataka ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS  (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)Bharat Gold Mines limited (BGML) is a Public Sector Undertaking’ incorporated in April 1972 under the administrative control of Department of Mines with its office at Kolar Gold Fields (KGF), Karnataka. Its operation were closed with effect from 01.03.2001 under Section 25(O) of the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 in terms of Ministry of Labour’s order dated 29.1.2001.Presently, all operations of the Company are closed. All employees were given Voluntary Retirement by giving Special Terminal Benefit Package (STBP) and all the dues of the employees are paid. Supervision of all the assets of BGML is under the Company Bench of High Court of Karnataka under BIFR referral C.P. 180/2000. All the mining leases of BGML have expired. Govt. of Andhra Pradesh has determined all three mining leases. Govt. of Karnataka has not renewed the mining lease in KGF.*****
