                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2953






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Status of Project Sanctioned in Uttar Pradesh under PRASHAD Scheme




 Hema Malini














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual Heritage Augmentation Drive’ (PRASHAD) is a Central Sector Scheme with complete financial assistance by the Government of India aimed at infrastructure development to envisage world class infrastructural development of the sites with special emphasis on tourist facilities;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the projects sanctioned in the State of Uttar Pradesh under PRASHAD scheme have been completed;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	if not, the time by which these projects are likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM	                                   (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e):	Yes, Sir. Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRgvASHAD) scheme of the Ministry of Tourism aims at developing pilgrimage / heritage tourism infrastructure on need gap analysis basis, establishing tourism as major engine for economic growth, community based development by providing employment to the local population with their direct participation in tourism activities at the pre-identified destinations/places across the country. The status of the projects approved under the scheme for the State of U.P and for the other states, is annexed. *******
