                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3407






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Pending Rice Subsidy




 Chinta Anuradha














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether an amount of Rs. 4,282 crores is pending towards rice subsidy under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana from the Union Government to the State of Andhra Pradesh; (b) 	if so, whether the Government has issued any instructions for immediate release of the dues to the State of Andhra Pradesh; (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) 	the instructions issued by the Government to avoid future delays in releasing dues to the State for Public Distribution System?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) to (d):   Admissible amount of Provisional/Advance Food Subsidy is released to Decentralized Procurement (DCP) States such as Andhra Pradesh, based on their quarter-wise claims.         The claims from State Government towards food subsidy are processed  keeping in view the opening and closing balance of stock, procurement, allocation and distribution of food grains, Food Corporation of India (FCI) reconciliation, Utilization Certificate, Economic Cost of foodgrains etc., among others.         An admissible amount of Rs.7,110.11 crore, has already been released  against the claims of the State Government including their claim of Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY), details of which are annexed. ***ANNEXUREANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3407 FOR ANSWER ON 16.03.2021 IN THE LOK SABHASubsidy Released during Financial Year 2020-21 (including PMGKAY)Date	Amount (Rs.
