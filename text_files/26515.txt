                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2267






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Skill India Scheme




Krupal Balaji Tumane













                                                    Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the work done in various districts of Maharashtra particularly Ramtek under the ‘Skill India’ during the last three years; (b) 	the details of the amount allocated/ sanctioned/ utilized under the said Skill India Scheme during the last three years; (c) 	the number of people trained and employed under the said scheme in Maharashtra and particularly in the constituency of Ramtek; (d) 	the details of the proposed works in Maharashtra under ‘Skill India‘ scheme along with the time by which it is likely to be completed, district-wise; and (e) 	whether any time limit has been fixed to complete these works?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	The Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship has been implementing  Pradhan Mantri Kaushal VikasYojana (PMKVY),Jan Shikshan Sansthan (JSS) Scheme, National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS) and Craftsmen Training Scheme across the country including Ramtek under Nagpur District and other districts of Maharashtra for imparting short term training to youth and to make them employable.(b)	The details of fund released/disbursed in the State of Maharashtra under the SchemePMKVY, JSS and NAPS from 2018-19 to 2020-21 are given below:Rs. in croreS.No.	Schemes	Fund released/disbursed1	PMKVY 	378.862	JSS	26.933	NAPS	19.77 	Under CTS, there is no provision of fund allocation and release to States. However, the Ministry through Skills Strengthening for Industrial Value Enhancement (STRIVE) project (World Bank assisted-project), improve the relevance and efficiency of skills training provided through ITIs and apprenticeships. Under the STRIVE project, Rs. 51.50 crore has been released to the State of Maharashtra during the Financial Year 2018-19 to 2020-21.(c)	The district wise details of candidates trained and placed in the State of Maharashtra under the Scheme PMKVY, and candidates trained under the scheme JSS and NAPS are given at Annexure I, Annexure II and Annexure III. Since, Ramtek is not a district, segregated data for Ramtek is not maintained for the above mentioned schemes.While under the CTS, 3.34 lakh candidates have been trained in the State of Maharashtra from 2018 to 2020, out of which 742 candidates trained in the constituency of Ramtek.(d)	All of the above schemes are ongoing schemes of skill training.(e)	Does not arise, in view of (d) above.
