                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1249






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Scheme for Security of Border Areas




Ramesh Chander Kaushik














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the schemes implemented by the Government to secure the border areas of those States which are adjacent to the borders of Bangladesh, Bhutan, Myanmar and China; and(b) the funds provided for the security of these border areas after the year 2014, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a): 	Ministry of Home Affairs is implementing various projects for creation of infrastructure under Border Infrastructure and Management (BIM) Scheme to secure the border areas which inter-alia include construction of border roads, Border Out Posts (BOPs), border fencing and floodlights. -2-L.S.US.Q NO. 1249 FOR 09.02.2021(b):	Funds under the BIM scheme are allocated for different borders. Details of funds provided under the scheme to secure borders with Bangladesh, Bhutan, Myanmar and China since 2014 are as under:-(Rs. in crore)S. No.	Border	Funds provided		2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21 (Revised Estimate) 1.		Indo- Bangladesh	722.25	672.93	700.70	1152.80	1019.55	996.53	651.762.		Indo- Bhutan	10.34	33.82	20.76	20.02	11.34	10.31	9.883.		Indo- Myanmar	18.31	9.80	23.00	5.00	6.27	20.00	20.384.
