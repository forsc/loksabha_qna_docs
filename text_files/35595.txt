                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2972






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Central Institute of Buddhist Studies




Jamyang Tsering Namgyal














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a): whether the University Grants Commission (UGC) had declared the Central Institute of Buddhist Studies (CIBS), Leh as deemed University in 2016;(b): if so, the details thereof; (c): whether the Government has implemented UGC regulations in the CIBS;(d): if so, the details thereof along with the date of effectiveness; and(e): if not, the reasons therefor






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(Dr. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) & (b): Ministry of Education, on the advice of UGC, vide Notification No. 9-5/2001-U.3(A) dated 15.01.2016 had declared Central Institute of Buddhist Studies (CIBS), Choglamsar, Leh (Ladakh), Jammu & Kashmir as a Deemed to be University from the date CIBS disaffiliates itself for its courses/programmes from Sampurnanand Sanskrit University, Varanasi and Tibetan Medicines and Astrology Department, Dharmshala. (c) to (e): Presently, all Deemed to be Universities including CIBS, Leh are regulated by the UGC (Institutions Deemed to be Universities) Regulations, 2019, which came into effect on 20.02.2019 in supersession of UGC (Institutions Deemed to be Universities) Regulations, 2016. To implement the UGC Regulations, CIBS has taken the following steps:i.Memorandum of Association/Rules was submitted to UGC in accordance with UGC (Institution Deemed to be Universities) Regulations, 2019.ii.The Governing Bodies of the Institute like the Society, Board of Management and Finance Committee have been restructured as per the Regulations.iii.The post of Vice-Chancellor has been created in consultation with Ministry of Finance by upgrading the post of Director of CIBS.iv.The Recruitment Rules for the posts of Registrar, Deputy Registrar, Assistant Registrar, Finance Officer and Controller of Examination have been finalized as per the Regulations. *****
