                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3453






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Reclassification of Reservation in STs List




Y. S. Avinash Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to delay the reclassification of Reservation criteria to include some communities in the STs list and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government has taken any decision to reclassify the reservation criteria to include the fresh demands from the communities deprived of reservation and if so, the details thereof; and (c) the stand of the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                         (a):  There is no proposal for revision of the criteria followed for inclusion community/caste/tribe in the lists of Scheduled Tribes. (b):  No Sir.(c):  Does not arise.*****
