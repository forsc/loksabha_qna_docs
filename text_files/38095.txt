                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4780






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Road Projects in Odisha




Rajashree Mallick














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the road projects approved during the last three years and the current year in the State of Odisha which are of economic importance and have inter-state connectivity including those which are covered under the Central Road and Infrastructure Fund (CRIF), location-wise;  (b) the estimated cost of these projects; and  (c) the funds allocated and utilised for these projects during the said period? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS (Shri NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (c)    The projects sanctioned during the last three years and current financial year in the State of Odisha under Central Road and Infrastructure Fund (CRIF) including those which are of economic importance and inter-state connectivity are at Annexure. Sanctioned cost of the said  projects are also indicated in the Annexure. Funds are allocated to the State in consolidated manner for all such projects under CRIF. The details of allocation/accrual and utilisation under CRIF in Odisha during last three years and the current year are as under: Sl. No.	          Year	Allocation / Accrual of funds under CRIF (Rs. in Cr.)	Funds Utilized (Rs. in Cr.)1	2018-19	332.23	360.542	2019-20	308.94	282.163	2020-21	286.97	429.634	2021-22	298.53	360.90
