                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2260






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Establishment of CIPET




Nishikant  Dubey














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) Whether the Government proposes for establishing Central Institute of Petrochemicals Engineering and Technology (CIPET) in various States including Deoghar District of Jharkhand;(b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the steps taken/being taken by the Government for its speedy completion?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS (DR. MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a) & (b) Govt. of India has approved establishment of 15 New CIPET Centres in various states across the country including one at Ranchi (Jharkhand) during XII Five Year Plan in the years 2015 & 2016 and the status of its establishment, are as under:New Centres already established in own premises	Centres activities started / partially started, own building construction in progress	Land allotted by the State Govt., construction yet to start  	Land allotment from the State Govt. awaited01. Raipur (Chattisgarh)	07. Baddi      (HP)	12. Bhagalpur (Bihar)	14. Mumbai / Nashik  (Maharashtra)02. Vijayawada (AP)	08. Gwalior    (MP)	13. Bihta   (Bihar)	15. Jammu (J&K) 03. Agartala (Tripura) 	09. Ranchi (Jharkhand)		04. Benguluru (Karnataka)	10. Varanasi     (UP)		05. Jaipur (Rajasthan)	11. Dehradun (Uttarakhand)		06.Chandrapur (Maharashtra)			 In addition to the above, Govt. of India is in the process of establishing CIPET Centres at Ayodhyha (Uttar Pradesh), Navsari (Gujarat) and Sanand (Gujarat) with financial support from the State Government as per the Model II Guidelines for establishment of new CIPET Centre. Further there is no proposal for establishment of CIPET Centre at Deoghar District of Jharkhand.(c)  The progress of Establishment of New CIPET Centres are being reviewed regularly by the Governing Council of CIPET, chaired by the Secretary (C&PC) & President, Governing Council of CIPET. The State Government, also, monitors the progress through Regional Advisory Committee (RAC), for speedy completion of the project in the respective states. *****
