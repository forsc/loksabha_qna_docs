                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1591






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2020






Protectees under CRPF & SPG




Dayanidhi Maran














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the current protectees under Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) and Special Protection Group (SPG) in the country;(b) the list of persons for whom security has been withdrawn by the Ministry of Home Affairs or modified/ reduced/increased since 2014 and the reasons therefor; and(c) the list of persons for whom security has been recommended since 2014 and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a), (b) and (c):	At present, the number of protectees under CRPF and SPG cover, are as under:-CRPF :-  56SPG   :-  01Security is provided on the basis of threat assessment by Central Security Agency. It is subject to periodic review. Based on such review, the security cover is continued, withdrawn or modified. The details of individuals and their security cannot be disclosed due to security reasons.  *******
