                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1619






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Import of Chinese Goods




Krishna Pal Singh Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether import of Chinese goods/raw materials have been declining in Indian markets due to the effect of coronavirus;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government is identifying some other options for raw materials being imported from China; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF  COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY  (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a) & (b):	India’s imports from China have declined by 27.63% during April-August, 2020-21 over the corresponding period of the previous year. The month-wise details of imports along with the percentage change are given below: Period	Value of Import from China(in US million)	% Change over the corresponding period of previous yearApr-20	3,031.83	-42.98May-20	4,662.36	-22.80Jun-20	3,323.59	-43.75Jul-20	5,583.00	-9.77Aug-20	4,984.94	-21.782020-21 (Apr-Aug)	21,585.72	-27.63	Source: DGCIS, Kolkata (Data is provisional)(c) & (d):	The government is undertaking steps for creating/enhancement of domestic capacity, incentivizing domestic manufacturing through Production Linked Incentive Schemes, Phased Manufacturing Plans, purchase preference., timely use of trade remedy options, adoption of mandatory Technical Standards, enforcement of FTA Rules of Origin (RoO) and development of import monitoring system.*****
