                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4230






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Strengthening of Basic Facilities in Urban Bodies




Riti Pathak













                                                    Jugal Kishore Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) 	whether the Government is formulating any plan to strengthen basic facilities in urban bodies across the country including Madhya Pradesh and Jammu and Kashmir and if so, the details thereof; and(b) 	the funds sanctioned by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF MINISTRY OFHOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                           (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	& (b) No, Sir. However, Government has already launched various Urban Missions to improve infrastructure and facilities in the Urban Local Bodies (ULBs). These are:i.	Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U) to make every ULB open defecation free, develop system for scientific processing of Municipal Solid Waste and improve awareness for cleanliness in all ULBs. ii.	Providing all-weather housing for all in all the ULBs; already 104 lakhs houses out of assessed demand of 112 lakh houses have been sanctioned; out of this over 62 lakhs have been grounded for construction and over 32 lakhs have been completed and delivered to the beneficiaries. iii.	Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) for developing facilities like drinking water to every household, improving waste water treatment, developing non-motorised transport, drainage and green spaces in selected 500 cities.iv.	Developing 100 cities selected through four rounds of challenge as Smart Cities.v.	Developing Metro network in 27 cities; already 697 km metro network is operational in 18 cities.All these missions are continuing at present.                                                        *****
