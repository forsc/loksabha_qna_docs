                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2355






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Benefits of Soil Health Card Scheme




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Soil Health Card Scheme being implemented by the Government has led to savings in nitrogen fertilizer like urea;(b)	If so, the details thereof; and(c)	If not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR )(a)	to (c):   Soil Health Card (SHC) Scheme has been implemented since 2015-16 to promote soil test based balanced and integrated use of chemical fertilizers in conjunction with organic and biofertilisers.                  Consumption of urea depends on a number of factors like cropping pattern, cropped area, availability of irrigation, soil fertility status & recommended dose of fertilizers as per soil health card etc. The consumption of urea has been reduced from 155.81 kg per hectare in 2015-16 to 152.53 kg per hectare in 2019-20. Decreasing trend in  per hectare consumption of Urea has also been observed in the states of Andhra Pradesh, Kerala, Jharkhand, Odisha, Puducherry, Tamilnadu, Telangana, Uttarakhand  from 2015-16 to 2019-20 (As per DES and state data). ********
