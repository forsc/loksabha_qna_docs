                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

934






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Rural Road Network under PMGSY-III




Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of eligible habitations in the rural road network covered under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) since 2013, State-wise, particularly the State of Rajasthan, district-wise;(b) 	the total number of Gramin Agricultural Markets established since 2019 along with the details of farmers engagement with the same, State-wise, particularly the State of Rajasthan, district-wise;(c) 	whether the Government has any annual action plan to achieve the targets and disburse funds set under the PMGSY-III till 2024-25; and(d) 	the areas covered under rural road network as of January 2022 combining the work done under all three phases of PMGSY along with the details of the total area left to be covered, State wise including the State of Rajasthan, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI FAGGAN SINGH KULASTE)(a) The total number of eligible habitations in the rural road network connected under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) since 2013, State/UT-wise including Rajasthan State are given at Annexure-I. District-wise details of habitations connected under PMGSY in the State of Rajasthan are given at Annexure-II.  (b) The Government of Rajasthan has intimated that its Agricultural Marketing Division, Rajasthan has not established any Gramin Agricultural Markets since 2019. (c)  PMGSY-III was launched in the year 2019 for consolidation of 1,25,000 Km Through Routes and Major Rural Links. Against the allocated target, 77,129 km road length (61.70% of total allocation) has already been sanctioned and 25,120 Km completed. The State of Rajasthan has been allocated 8,662.5 km road length under PMGSY-III, out of which 5,821 km road length worth Rs. 3,121.64 crore including state share has been sanctioned. All the projects under PMGSY-III are targeted for completion by March, 2025. There is no dearth of funds required for completion of PMGSY projects. (d) All the projects under PMGSY-I & II have already been sanctioned to the states. Against total 6,95,493 km road length sanctioned under PMGSY-I &II, only  21,999 km length is balance for completion. All the projects under PMGSY I & II are targeted for completion by September 2022. Under PMGSY-III, against the allocated target of 1,25,000 km road length, 77,129 km has been sanctioned and 25,120 km has been completed. All the projects under PMGSY-III are targeted to be completion by March, 2025. The State/UTs wise details of road length sanctioned, completed under all three phases of PMGSY and Road Connectivity Project for Left Wing Extremism Affected Areas (RCPLWEA) are given in Annexure -III. The district wise details of road length sanctioned, completed in the state of Rajasthan under all three phases of PMGSY are given at Annexure-IV. ******
