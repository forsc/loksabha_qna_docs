                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4414






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Tamil Nadu Watershed Development Projects




T.R.V.S. Ramesh














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    LOK SABHA               UNSTARRED QUESTION No. 4414               TO BE ANSWERED ON 23.03.2021          Tamil Nadu Watershed Development Projects    4414.  SHRI T.R.V.S. RAMESH: Will the Minister of RURAL DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state:(a)	whether the Tamil Nadu Watershed Development Project-I and II has been taken up with financial assistance from the World Bank;(b)	if so, the details and present status of the project; and(c)	the time by which the project is likely to be completed and the benefits that the Tamil Nadu farmers would get out of this project? 






ANSWER




                                                                                                    ANSWER                     MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT                                       (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI) (a)As per information furnished by State Government of Tamil Nadu, no Watershed Development Project has been taken up with the financial assistance from the World Bank.  (b)& (c):	Does not arise                                              *****
