                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3310






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Prevalence of AIDS




Jayadev Galla













                                                    Devendrappa Y
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE  be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has noted that Andhra Pradesh is ranked second in the prevalence of AIDS in the country after Maharashtra as per the National Health Profile; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the special efforts being made by the Government to focus on Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka and Telangana where there are higher number of AIDS patients; and (d) the steps taken by the Government to ensure that the patients suffering from AIDS/HIV are not discriminated against in society and to track the patients through HIV diagnosis for care and treatment?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):    No. As per National Health profile 2019, Andhra Pradesh has been ranked at 8th position with HIV prevalence of 0.41 and Maharashtra has been ranked at 17th position with HIV prevalence of 0.26. The State wise data on HIV/ AIDS as per National Health Profile 2019 is attached in Annexure-I.(c):      National AIDS Control Program (NACP) is a central sector scheme of Ministry of Health and Family Welfare for prevention and control of HIV/AIDS in India, wherein technical, financial and commodity support is provided to all States. Andhra Pradesh Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka and Telangana, by virtue of their population and HIV/AIDS prevalence, have higher numbers of People living with HIV (PLHIV) and therefore are in receipt of proportionately higher quantum of support in terms of numbers of facilities, commodities and manpower for testing for HIV/AIDS and provisioning of free Anti Retro Viral Treatment for those infected. In areas where it is epidemiologically indicated that special efforts are required, comprehensive actions are undertaken in NACP. Contd…….-2-The State-wise specific details of programmatic efforts is detailed in Annexure II & III. (d)  The Government implements 360-degree multimedia campaigns to address Stigma and Discrimination of Persons living with HIV/AIDS through mass media, outdoor and folk media.The HIV and AIDS (Prevention and Control) ACT, 2017 also prohibits any sort of discrimination with regard to employment & occupation, unfair treatment in healthcare services, unfair treatment in educational establishments and services. The Act also has penal provisions for any discrimination practiced against a person with HIV/AIDS and breach of confidentiality.An online software platform is in place for tracking patients through HIV diagnosis for care and treatment. …………………..
