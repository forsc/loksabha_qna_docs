                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

439






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Construction of NHs in Haryana




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and the present status of National Highways (NHs) under construction in Haryana;(b) whether the said NHs are progressing as per stipulated time frame;(c) if not, the time by which the said NHs are likely to be completed; and(d) the details of the cuts/underpasses/culverts provided on NH-152D, 709E, 334B and NH-11 in Bhiwani and Mahendergarh districts? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.
