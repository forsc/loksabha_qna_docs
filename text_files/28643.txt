                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

534






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Funds for VCIC




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total funds allocated and utilized for the development of Visakhapatnam Chennai Industrial Corridor (VCIC);(b) 	whether any timeframe has been fixed for completion of the development project to avoid any delay and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the date since when VCIC has been in the initial stage;(d) 	whether the State Government has initiated the master planning and preliminary engineering for the Visakhapatnam node of VCIC; and(e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) to (e):    	Rs. 2.51 crore has been  allocated and utilized for project development activities including master planning and preliminary engineering in respect of Chittoor (Srikalahasti) & Kadapa nodes.  No time frame could be fixed for completion of the project at this stage. The state Govt of Andhra Pradesh brought the proposal to Govt of India for inclusion of VCIC under Industrial Corridor Framework in October, 2018.   State Government of Andhra Pradesh is developing Visakhapatnam node on its own.  As per information received, State Govt. has initiated master planning and preliminary engineering.******
