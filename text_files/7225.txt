                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

890






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






One Nation One FASTag Scheme




Subhash Ramrao Bhamre













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    A.K.P. Chinraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Government proposes to  implement  One  Nation  One  FASTag scheme  for  having  a  unified  electronic system  across  the  country  and  if  so,  the details and the objectives thereof;(b)  the total  number  of  FASTags issued by the Government to ease the traffic congestion at various Toll Plazas till now across the country;(c)  whether the Government has signed any MoU for integration of FASTags with GST e-way bill system and if so, the details thereof;(d)   the  details  of  the  benefits/advantages to the public after this move;(e)  whether  the  Government  has identified and resolved problems/issues that are  likely  to  lead  to  any  law  and  order problem after its implementation and if so, the details thereof; and(f)   the  other  steps  taken  by  the Government  to  popularise  the  use  of FASTags in rural areas?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) Yes Sir. To enhance National Electronic Toll Collection (NETC) programme coverage and to offer seamless services to consumers across the country, National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) through Indian Highways Management Company Limited (IHMCL) has launched scheme guidelines for inclusion of State Fee Plazas under NETC programme. NHAI has signed agreements with certain states.(b)  Appox. 66 Lakhs FASTags have been issued till 14.11.2019.(c)& (d)  Yes Sir.  An MoU was signed on 14.10.2019 between IHMCL and GST Network (GSTN) for integrating E-Way Bill system with FASTag. This has been done with a view to overcoming the existing challenge in track and trace mechanism for GST E-Way Bill (EWB) System and enhance the efficiency in its monitoring.(e)  The Trial run for dedicated FASTag lanes on pilot basis has begun on certain fee plazas. NHAI has appointed Nodal officers at all fee plazas.  Government has appointed Central Prabhari officers to monitor the progress. (f)  Advertisements and awareness campaigns are been carried out through various medium across the country.  Hoardings/banners have been fixed at fee plazas and many other locations.   Pamphlets are being distributed near Fee plazas and FASTag purchase are being facilitated near fee plazas.*****
