                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

259






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Waste Management in Trains




Heena Vijaykumar Gavit













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Sambhajirao Mane Dhairyasheel
                                                








                                                    Sujay Radhakrishna Vikhepatil
                                                








                                                    Shrikant Eknath Shinde
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the current system of disposal/storage of food plates after use in the train compartments tantamount to unhygienic conditions in the bogies and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Railways is planning to overhaul the aforesaid practice;(c) if so, the details in this regard; and (d) various measures taken/likely to be taken by the Railways for clean and effective waste management in trains?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c): No, Sir. Detailed instructions on Catering Waste Management System for collection and disposal of waste arising out of Pantry Car services have been provided to Zonal Railways and Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) vide Commercial Circular nos. 45 of 2011 and 55 of 2016. These circulars are available on Indian Railways’ website https://indianrailways.gov.in/. Compliance of these instructions is ensured through periodical inspections.(d): The various measures taken by Railways to ensure clean and effective management of waste in trains are as follows:(i) Waste generated during train journey is collected at the nominated stations. Waste collected at stations is disposed off to the nominated places/ authorised agencies of the Municipal/ local bodies.(ii) Provision of Dustbins has also been extended to non-AC coaches in addition to AC coaches.(iii) En-route cleaning of waste generated including emptying of dustbins is done under On Board Housekeeping Services scheme (OBHS) in important long distance Mail/ Express trains.	*****
