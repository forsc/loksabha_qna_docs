                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3967






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Renovation of Railway Stations in Rajasthan




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of railway stations standards of which have been improved or are in the process of being improved and beautification of the same has been carried out across the country by the Ministry during the last two years;(b) the State-wise/UT-wise details thereof;(c) whether the Union Government has presently stopped the ongoing development works at various railway stations across the country including Rajasthan;(d) if so, the details thereof;(e) whether the Ministry has prepared any roadmap for development and renovation of mismanaged railway stations in the country; and(f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (f):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.******
