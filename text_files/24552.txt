                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

396






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Assessment of Crop Damage due to Cyclone Yaas




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the central Government team has visited Odisha recently to assess the crop damage due to cyclone Yaas in the affected districts;(b) if so, the details of the crop damage in these districts;(c) whether the Ministry has sanctioned any financial package to the farmers of the affected districts; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????      (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d): 	An Inter-Ministerial Central Team (IMCT), constituted by the Ministry of Home Affairs, visited Odisha in June 2021 for an on the spot assessment of damages in the wake of Cyclone “Yaas” that struck Odisha in May 2021. As per the Memorandum submitted by the State Government, 2197.34 hectares of agriculture/horticulture crops sustained crop loss to the extent of 33% and above. As per the existing procedure, the report of the IMCT is considered by the Sub Committee of National Executive Committee (SC-NEC). Thereafter, the High Level Committee (HLC) approves the final quantum of financial assistance.******
