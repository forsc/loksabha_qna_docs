                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3118






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Works under PMAY-U




Ashok Kumar Rawat














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether work is being carried out under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Urban (PMAY-U) in the country, particularly in Misrikh Parliamentary Constituency of Uttar Pradesh;(b) 	if so, the details thereof particularly in Misrikh including updated status in Uttar Pradesh as on date; and(c) 	the details of the Central funds allocated including the utilization of the said funds and the number of beneficiaries during each of the last three years, State and year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                                   THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                 (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)	& (b): Yes, Sir. The details of houses sanctioned, grounded and completed under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana- Urban (PMAY-U) in Uttar Pradesh including Misrikh Parliamentary Constituency are at Annexure-I.(c): The details of the Central fund allocated including the utilization of the said funds and the number of beneficiaries during each of the last three years; State/ UT-wise and year-wise are at Annexure-II.                                              *****
