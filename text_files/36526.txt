                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3870






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Funds allocated under NHM in Chhattisgarh




Sunil Kumar Soni














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total amount of funds allocated to Chhattisgarh under the National Health Mission (NHM) since its inception, year-wise;(b)	the total amount of funds allocated and spent in various districts of Chhattisgarh under NHM, district-wise;(c)	whether the funds allocated under NHM are being utilized in Chhattisgarh as per the timelines drawn, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	whether the Government proposes to conduct audit of the funds spent and utilized under NHM in Chhattisgarh and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (d): National Health Mission (NHM) is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme under which the Government of India provides financial and technical support to States/UTs to strengthen their health care systems. The details of funds released Centrally under National Health Mission (NHM) to the State of Chhattisgarh, since its inception, year-wise is at Annexure-IA.District-wise details of funds released and expenditure under NHM since its inception, as reported by the State of Chhattisgarh is at Annexure-IB.Under NHM, to improve the financial utilization of funds the following steps have been taken by the Ministry:(i)	Performance Audit by the Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG) of India,(ii)	Annual Statutory Audits by CAG empaneled major Chartered Accountant Audit firms,(iii)	Concurrent Audits by Chartered Accountant Audit firms,  (iv)	Public Financial Management System (PFMS) in NHM to track flow of funds, (v)	Submission of quarterly Financial Monitoring Reports (FMRs) and Statement of Fund Position (SFP) by the States/UTs,(vi)	Review and monitoring of scheme components.
