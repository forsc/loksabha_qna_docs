                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2935






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Land for Airports




Ajay Nishad














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has requested various State Governments to release land for airports;(b) if so, the details thereof especially in Bihar;(c) the details of land released by the State Government so far;(d) whether the Union Government has formulated any policy with regard to land acquisition for construction of airports in the country; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) to (c):  The state-wise details of land requirements projected by the Airports Authority of India (AAI) to various State Governments/UTs for development of airports and the land handed over by the State Governments to AAI are at Annexure.;(d) & (e): The Government of India has formulated the National Civil Aviation Policy-2016, which inter-alia provides that the land for the development of the airport is to be provided by the respective State Government free of cost and free from all encumbrances.
