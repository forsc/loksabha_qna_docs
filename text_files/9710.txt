                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3139






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Cases of Prader-Willi Syndrome




Arjun Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of cases of Prader-Willi Syndrome reported in India;(b) the steps taken by the Government to provide treatment to the children afflicted by this disease;(c) whether there are any Group Homes or similar facilities to provide specialized care and supervision to those suffering by this disease; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (d):  Information regarding number of cases of Prader-Willi Syndrome is not maintained centrally.Government had formulated a National Policy for Treatment of Rare Diseases in the year 2017. However, the Government decided to review the Policy in the light of new information and updates available/received for its further improvement and effective implementation.  Accordingly, the Policy has been kept in abeyance vide non-statutory Gazette Notification dated 18-12-2018, till the revised Policy is issued or until further orders, whichever is earlier. In the meanwhile, a component for treatment of specified rare diseases, amenable to one time treatment, has been included under the Umbrella Scheme of Rashtriya Arogya Nidhi, for providing one-time financial assistance upto Rs.15 lakh to patients belonging to families living below threshold poverty line  and taking treatment in Government Hospitals. Prader-Willi Syndrome is not covered in the list of specified rare diseases for assistance under the Umbrella Scheme of Rashtriya Arogya Nidhi.
