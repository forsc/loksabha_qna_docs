                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2374






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Andhra Pradesh Disha Bill-2020




M V V Satyanarayana













                                                    Talari Rangaiah
                                                








                                                    Goddeti Madhavi
                                                








                                                    Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government intends to provide assent to the Andhra Pradesh Disha (Special Courts for specified offences against women and children) Bill, 2020;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the manner in which the Government plans to expedite verdicts in trials of the heinous offence of rape; and(d) the measures taken by the Government for women safety in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b): The Andhra Pradesh Disha (Special Courts for Specified Offences against Women and Children) Bill, 2020 reserved by the Governor has been received in January 2021 for assent of the President of India. As per practice bills received from States for assent of President are processed in consultation with the nodal ministries/departments. Accordingly, the consultation process with various ministries/departments concerned with Andhra Pradesh Disha (Special Courts for specified offences against women and children) Bill, 2020 has been initiated. Based on their inputs, Bill will be processed further. -2-L.S.US.Q NO. 2374 FOR 09.03.2021(c): 	The Criminal Law Amendment Act, 2018, prescribes timelines for investigation and trial in respect of rape cases.(d) : 	The Government has taken various measures for women safety which are briefly as under:(i) 	Emergency Response Support System provides  a pan-India single internationally recognized number (112) based system for all emergencies. (ii)	Cyber crime reporting portal has been launched.(iii)	Safe City projects have been sanctioned in 8 cities in 1st Phase.(iv)	An online analytic tool “Investigation Tracking System for Sexual Offence” has been launched.(v)	Upgradation of Forensic Science infrastructure has been undertaken.In addition to the above-mentioned measures, Ministry of Home Affairs has been issuing advisories from time to time to States/UTs to deal with crimes against women, which are available at www.mha.gov.in .******
