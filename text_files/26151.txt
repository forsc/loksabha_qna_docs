                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1991






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Damage caused by Elephants in Assam




Naba (Hira) Kumar Sarania














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of death of elephants and human deaths caused by elephants in Assam during the last three years; (b)	whether elephants coming form mountains of Bhutan are creating problems for many villages; (c)	if so, whether the Government has formulated any action plan to protect people and land from these elephants; (d)	the details of the compensation provided to the family of victims in case of death caused by elephants, State/UT-wise; (e)	whether the Government is aware that the relatives of the people killed by elephants face many problems in getting compensation; and(f)	if so, the details thereof along with the corrective measures taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) The details of total number of elephant and human deaths due to human elephant conflict during the last three years in the state of Assam are given below: Year.	Elephant Death 	Human Death 2018	78	922019	73	752020	74	77(b)&(c)	The movements of elephants from Bhutan have not resulted in any major problem for villagers. (d)(e)&(f)	The Ministry provides financial assistance to States under Centrally Sponsored Scheme, Project Elephant, which also includes a component of ex-gratia in case of human death by elephant.  The Ministry has enhanced the rate of ex-gratia for human deaths in 2018 from Rs. 2 lakhs to Rs. 5 lakhs.  The State Government also makes payment of ex-gratia to victims of conflict from their own funds which varies from State to State. The details of compensation paid in case of human deaths are not collated in the Ministry.  An Advisory has been issued by the Ministry for prompt release of ex-gratia by the State Governments/UT Administrations.  ***
