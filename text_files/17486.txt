                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1221






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Compliance of CAR




Kani K. Navas














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of companies that filed the voluntary Companies      Affirmation of Readiness to COVID-19 (CAR) Form;(b)	whether the Government has data of the above companies that failed to adhere to the safety standards; and(c)	if so, the details thereof along with the corrective steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCEAND CORPORATE AFFAIRS                                               (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR) ????? ??? ???????? ????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? 	                                (???? ?????? ???? ?????)(a) to (c): The Ministry deployed CAR (Companies Affirmation of Readiness towards COVID-19) Form as a confidence building measure to assess the readiness of the companies & Limited Liability Partnership (LLPs) to deal with COVID-19 threat in India.  207429 companies & LLPs filed this form.  It was purely voluntary as part of contribution of Corporates towards joining the movement to fight against the spread of the disease and as such no penalty or enforcement action was applicable.                                               *** *** ***
