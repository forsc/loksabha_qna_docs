                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1293






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Assessment of PMUY




Ajay Bhatt













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Sunil Baburao Mendhe
                                                








                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has set any parameters to assess the outcome of the Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) especially with respect to the health of women and air pollution and if so, the details thereof;(b) if not, whether the Government proposes to formulate any policy to assess the outcome;(c) the number of women who have benefited from the gas connection under the Ujjwala Scheme in Uttar Pradesh along with details thereof;(d) the number of women provided with gas connection till now in the parliamentary constituency of Pratapgarh district of Uttar Pradesh and Wardha and Amravati districts in Maharashtra along with the details thereof;(e) whether any annual targets were set under the Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana to provide gas connection in Uttarakhand, if so, the details thereof during the last two years; and(f) whether set targets have been achieved and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b) Government is undertaking third party evaluation of various continuing schemes before the end of 14th Finance Commission’s cycle.(c) Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) have released more than 1.47 crore LPG connections in the State of Uttar Pradesh under PMUY.(d) The details of LPG connections released under PMUY in the district of Pratapgarh in Uttar Pradesh State, Wardha and Amravati districts in Maharashtra State are as under :-DistrictNo. of connections released under PMUYPratapgarh, Uttar Pradesh2.17 lakhWardha, Maharashtra0.49 lakhAmravati, Maharashtra1.32 lakh(e) & (f) No State/UT-wise target was fixed to release LPG connections under PMUY. However, the target of providing 8 crore LPG connections under PMUY has been achieved on 7th September, 2019. OMCs have released 4.04 lakh LPG connections under PMUY in the State of Uttarakhand.
