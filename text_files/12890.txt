                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

153






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Availability of Books in Hindi Medium




Girish Chandra













                                                    Shyam Yadav Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of all those central universities where the books of all the courses are available both in English and Hindi languages;(b) whether the Government proposes to make books of all the courses available to the students of all the universities in Hindi language also because a lot of students complete their Intermediate Level studies in Hindi Medium;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT             (SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 153 FOR ANSWER ON 2nd MARCH, 2020 ASKED BY SHRI GIRISH CHANDRA AND SHRI SHYAM SINGH YADAV, HON’BLE MEMBERS OF PARLIAMENT REGARDING AVAILABILITY OF BOOKS IN HINDI MEDIUM.(a) to (d):  The Central Universities are autonomous institutions created under the Act of Parliament which are governed by their own Acts, Statutes, Ordinances, etc. and are competent to take decision on their academic matters such as  medium of instruction, academic content, courses, syllabi with the approval of their statutory bodies. Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) does not interfere with the routine academic/ administration matter of Central Universities.  But in the Conference of Central Universities Vice-Chancellors held on 18.02.2016, Vice-Chancellor of all Central Universities unanimously passed the resolution that in order to ensure that no student is deprived of Higher Education due to language limitation, universities to ensure instruction in  an Indian language as applicable to the state and English for this.*****
