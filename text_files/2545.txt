                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2400






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Kendriya Vidyalayas in Temporary Buildings




Su Thirunavukkarasar













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                








                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                








                                                    M. Selvaraj
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of total number of Kendriya Vidyalayas functioning in the country, city-wise/Parliamentary Constituency-wise, particularly in Tamil Nadu and Gujarat;(b) whether many Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) are running under temporary infrastructures in thecountry and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise and the reasons therefor;(c) whether running of Kendriya Vidyalayas under temporary infrastructure has adverselyaffected the study of students in many parts of the country;(d) if so, the corrective steps taken/ proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard;(e) whether the Government has granted permanent School buildings for various KVs in thecountry, especially in Tamil Nadu; and(f) if so, the details thereof during the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) At on date, 1206 Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs) are functioning in the country. City-wise/Parliamentary Constituency-wise, particularly in Tamil Nadu and Gujarat are at Annexure –I.(b) Yes, Sir. As on date 266 KVs are running in the temporary infrastructure provided by thesponsors. The State / UT- wise details are at Annexure-II.(c) As per the norms of KVS, due to lack of permanent buildings, teaching-learning processis affected.(d) to (f) Construction of permanent buildings for KVs is a continuous process, which dependsupon identification of suitable land, completion of lease formalities in favour of KVS by thesponsoring authorities, submission of drawings / estimates by construction agency, availabilityof funds and requisite approvals etc. The State/UT-wise details of permanent School buildingsgranted during the last three years and the current year are at Annexure-III.*****
