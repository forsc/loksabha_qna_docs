                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1446






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Schemes for Fishery and Dairy Industries




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh













                                                    Sumalatha Ambareesh
                                                








                                                    Nalin Kumar Kateel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the schemes introduced for development of fishery and dairy industries in the country during the last five years; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the target set to achieve under the schemes along with the details of the achievement during the said period; (d) 	the fund earmarked and spent under each scheme during the said period; and (e) 	the details thereof, State-wise and year-wise;






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) and (b): 	To complement and supplement the efforts of the States/UTs for promotion of fisheries and dairy sectors, the Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying hasintroduced various schemes across the country, such as (i) Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY), (ii) Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund (FIDF), (iii) National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD), (iv) Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund (DIDF), (v) Supporting Dairy Cooperative and Farmer Producer Organizations (SDC&FPO) engaged in dairy activities, (vi) Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme (DEDS).(c) to (e):	The Government is providing support under the above demand driven Central Sectors/ Centrally Sponsored Schemes based on the proposals received from the State/ UTs.The State-wise details of funds released and its utilization for development fisheries, and dairying industry during the last five years arefurnished atAnnexure-I, II,IIIand IV.*****
