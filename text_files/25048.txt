                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

923






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Farm Loans Waiver




S. Jothimani














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the total amount of outstandingfarm loans availed by farmers across the country,State-wise;(b) the details of the total number of farmers whohave outstanding farm loans, State-wise;(c) whether the Government has any plan to waiveoff the farm loans; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(a) & (b): State-wise details of number of accounts with total agriculture loan outstanding as on 31.03.2021, as reported by National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) are provided in Annexure to this reply.(c) & (d): There is no proposal under consideration of the Union Government to waive off farm loans.                                                                                                              .
