                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2920






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Special Trains in Bhubaneshwar




Aparajita Sarangi














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government runs tourism special or rail tour trains inBhubaneswar and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government is planning to increase the frequency of such trains or add coaches to the existing trains;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the details of the passengers travelling in tourism special or rail tour trains during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (c) IRCTC operates tourist special trains such as Bharat Darshan and Pilgrim Special trains from time to time on different itineraries based on market demand.  Two such Bharat Darshan trips have been operated in the last three years which covered Bhubaneswar. Details of the same are appended.   Frequency of these trains depends upon market demand. These trains have a standard coach composition.  (d)  672 tourists have travelled in Bharat Darshan tourist trains in the last five years to Bhubaneswar.  Details are appended.****
