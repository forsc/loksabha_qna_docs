                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3962






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Broadcasting Infrastructure and Network Development




Sumalatha Ambareesh














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has introduced the Broadcasting Infrastructure and Network Development (BIND) scheme in the country; (b) 	if so, the details and objectives of the scheme; (c) 	the present status of implementation of the said scheme; and (d) 	the details of funds allocated and spent for the scheme as on date?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) to (c) : Government has approved “Broadcasting Infrastructure and Network Development (BIND)” Scheme for Prasar Bharati as part of 12th Plan (2012-17) & Extension of the Scheme of Prasar Bharati (2017-20, which was further extended to 2020-21) at a cost of Rs. 1054.52 crores for 3 years. The scheme has components for modernization and upgradation of capital infrastructure in All India Radio and Doordarshan, as also content development. The objectives of the scheme include Augmentation and Replacement of transmitters and Broadcast Equipment, Digitization of studios, Coverage in sensitive areas, broadcasting on alternate platform, expansion of DTH and High Definition TV channels, etc. The present status of implementation of the scheme is given at Annexure.(d) : Year-wise details of allocation and expenditure during the last three years and current financial year (as on 31.01.2021) are as under:                                             Figure in Rs. CroresFY	Allocation	Expenditure2017-18	282.00	201.572018-19	304.94	222.582019-20	235.40	289.02*2020-21	173.90	131.1058**Including unspent amount of previous year.*****
