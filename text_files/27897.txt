Reservation for Socially and Economically Backward People




Mahabali Singh














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3612TO BE ANSWERED ON 10.08.2021RESERVATION FOR SOCIALLY AND ECONOMICALLY BACKWARD PEOPLE †3612. SHRI MAHABALI SINGH: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government had implemented the recommendations of Mandal Commission in 1990, if so, the details thereof; (b) whether there is any provision to give 27 percent reservation to the socially and economically backward people in Central Government jobs; and(c) if so, the details thereof regarding the number of people benefited from the reservation given in Governmental jobs from 1990 till date?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a) & (b): Yes Sir. As per recommendations of the Mandal Commission report, 27 percent reservation has been given to the Socially and Educationally Backward Classes (SEBC) (also known as the Other Backward Classes (OBC)) in direct recruitment to Central Government jobs and admission to Central Government Educational Institutions.(c): Department of Personnel & Training collects online data on representation and appointment of SCs/STs/OBCs w.e.f.01.01.2012 in the posts and services of the Central Government.
