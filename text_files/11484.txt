                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

308






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Fisheries University




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering to set up a fisheries university in view of the fact that India is a leading exporter of marine products particularly fish, shrimp, etc.; (b) whether it is appropriate to set up the said fisheries university in Andhra Pradesh as the State is the leader in fish and shrimp production in the country; (c) if so, whether the Government has ever thought of setting up such a university of national importance in the country with attached institution in various parts of the country; (d) if so, the details thereof; and (e) if not, whether the Government would consider the same?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING:(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) to (e):Setting up of Fisheries University is the State subject. As on date, there are three Fisheries Universities established in the countryof which two are by the State Governments inthe States viz; Kerala and Tamil Nadu and the third one is the Central Institute of Fisheries Education (CIFE) by the Indian Council of Agriculture Research (ICAR) at Mumbai, Maharashtra. Besides, there are 29 Fisheries Colleges functioning in the country in different States namely Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Maharashtra, Odisha, Bihar, Uttarakhand, Assam, Gujarat, Andhra Pradesh, West Bengal, Tripura, Rajasthan, Jammu & Kashmir, Uttar Pradesh, Punjab, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, Telangana and Jharkhand.*****
