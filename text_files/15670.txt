                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4323






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






National Highways Network




Harish Dwivedi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of National Highways (NHs) network in various States of the country as on date; and(b) the number of proposals received by the Government from the various States for expansion/widening of NHs during each of the last three years and the current year along with the present status thereof?  






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) State / Union Territory (UT)-wise details of National Highways (NHs) network the country as on date is placed at Annexure-I;(b) The details of proposals sanctioned by the Government for various States / UTs for development of NHs including widening of NHs during each of the last three years and the current year is at Annexure-II. The proposals received from States / UTs which fulfil the technical requirements are sanctioned as per traffic density and inter-se priority of works within the available resources and other proposals get automatically returned to States / UTs at the end of financial year. As such there is no pending proposal for the said period.
