                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

790






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Security of Projects under KRMB and GRMB




Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that all the projects under Krishna River Management Board (KRMB) and Godavari River Management Board (GRMB) are being handed over to Central Industrial Security Forces (CISF); and (b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI (SHRI BISHWESWAR TUDU)(a) & (b) The relevant provisions governing this aspect in the Gazette Notification dated 15.07.2021, regarding Krishna River Management Board (KRMB) and Godavari River Management Board (GRMB), May be mentioned.As per Clause 1(e) of Gazette Notification dated 15.07.2021 regarding Krishna River Management Board (KRMB), “On and from the date of commencement of this notification, the Central Industrial Security Force constituted under the Central Industrial Security Force Act, 1968 shall assist the KRMB in the day-to-day management of projects specified in Schedule-2 and any other work related to Security as assigned by the KRMB, on such terms and conditions as the Central Government may specify.” Similarly, as per Clause 1(e) of Gazette Notification  dated 15.07.2021 regarding Godavari River Management Board (GRMB), “On and from the date of commencement of this notification, the Central Industrial Security Force constituted under the Central Industrial Security Force Act, 1968 shall assist the GRMB in the day-to-day management of projects specified in Schedule-2 and any other work related to Security as assigned by the GRMB, on such terms and conditions as the Central Government may specify.”*****
