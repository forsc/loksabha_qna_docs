                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

276






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Kaleshwaram Irrigation Project




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has approved the Kaleshwaram Irrigation Project (KIP) in the State of Telangana and if so, the details of tendering process and physical progress thereof; (b) whether the KIP has created any new ayacut and if so, the details of additional irrigation water to be supplied by the project, the total area of additional land irrigated, the amount of water envisaged to be lifted in the main and additional KIP, location and district-wise along with the total cost incurred on the project so far; (c) the details of funding by the Union Government and its entities such as Power Finance Corporation, Rural Electrification Corporation and nationalised banks to the above project; (d) whether the Union Government has assessed the cost-benefit ratio of the Kaleshwaram main and additional projects and if so, the details thereof; and (e) the details of agencies entrusted with the execution of main and additional Kaleshwaram projects?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF JAL SHAKTI  (SHRI GAJENDRA SINGH SHEKHAWAT)(a) to (e) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. *****  STATEMENT REFFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF STARRED QUESTION No.*276 TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 16.12.2021 REGARDING “KALESHWARAM IRRIGATION PROJECT” ASKED BY UTTAM KUMAR REDDY NALAMADA.(a) Kaleshwaram Irrigation Project (KIP) has been accepted by the Advisory Committee on Irrigation, Flood Control and Multipurpose Projects of Ministry of Jal Shakti, in June, 2018. The project is being executed by the State Government of Telangana from its own resources. As informed by the State Government, tenders for the project were invited on e-procurement platform based on lump-sum contract system.Further, as informed by the State Government, physical progress achieved on KIP till 31.10.2021 is 83.7%.(b) As informed by the State Government, total command area proposed to be irrigated under KIP is 18,25,700 acres. Additionally, stabilization of an area of 18,82,970 acre of land is envisaged. The State Government has further informed that total amount of water envisaged to be lifted and supplied in the project is 240 thousand million cubic feet (TMC).New areas in varying proportion of thirteen districts of the State viz., Karimnagar, Rajanna Sircilla, Siddipet, Medak, Yadadri-Bhongiri, Nalgonda, Sangareddy, Nizamabad, Jagityal, Kamareddy, Nirmal, Medchal, Peddapalli etc., are proposed to be covered under irrigation by the project.The total cost incurred on KIP so far is reported to be Rs. 80,321.57 crore.(c) As per information provided by the State Government, details of funding provided by various agencies to the project, are given below:(Rs. in crore)Sl.
