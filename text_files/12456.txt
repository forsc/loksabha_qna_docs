                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1280






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Subsidy to JNU




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Universities receiving grants from the Government State-wiseand the funds disbursed and utilized by the universities year-wise from 2008;(b) the list of subsidies granted to Jawaharlal Nehru University and the cost tothe exchequer per year since 2008 to provide the subsidies;(c) whether the Government feels that the tuition, hostel and mess fee paid bystudents of Centrally Funded Universities has not kept up with times and if so,the action being considered by the Government:(d) whether the Government has a mechanism to check the appropriate usage ofthe funds by the universities towards the education of its students and if so,the details thereof; and(e) whether the Government has any mechanism to check the efficiency andlearning outcomes of students in Central Universities and if so, the detailsthereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): The Government provide grants to Central Universities through University GrantsCommission (UGC). The number of Central Universities receiving grants from UGCalongwith fund disbursed to and utilized by them year-wise from 2008-09 to 2018-19 isannexed.(b) & (c): All Central Universities including JNU is fully funded by the Government.Central Universities are autonomous bodies created under the respective Acts ofParliament and they are competent to take administrative and financial decisionspertaining to their Universities.(d) & (e): The accounts of Central Universities are mandatorily subjected to audit by theComptroller & Auditor General (C&AG) of India every year and the Annual Accounts dulyaudited are required to be tabled in both Houses of the Parliament. Further, theUniversity Grants Commission (UGC) reviews the financial performance of each CentralUniversity under the purview of Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) on aperiodical basis for ensuring proper utilization of funds and for providing further financialassistance to the Universities. All Central Universities have been brought under EATmodule of PFMS and their grants are being released only after verification ofexpenditure incurred.There is a mechanism of regular review of performance of Central Universities byMinistry of Human Resource Development, UGC and National Assessment andAccreditation Council (NAAC).***
