                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

455






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






PMKVY in J&K and NER




Jagdambika Pal 














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has launched any special projects under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) in Union Territory of Jammu and Kashmir (J&K) and North Eastern Region (NER); and(b)	if so, the details thereof along with the Detailed Project Report for these special projects?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)         A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) AND (B) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 455 TO BE ANSWERED ON 04.04.2022 ASKED BY SHRI JAGDAMBIKA PAL REGARDINGPMKVY IN J&K AND NER(a)       Under Skill India Mission, Ministry is delivering the skills to the youth through short term training (STT) and recognition of prior learning (RPL) under its flagship scheme Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) across the country. Special Projects (SPs) under PMKVY are those projects that primarily meet the skilling needs of marginalised or vulnerable groups (such as Scheduled Castes and Tribes, transgender, persons with disabilities, women, economically backward people, etc.) and those of difficult/remote geographies, hard to reach areas (such as LWE, aspirational districts, J&K, Ladakh, North East states, Island territories). Special Projects facilitates in reviving local livelihood through creative market linked entrepreneurship which further leads to economic upliftment of the vulnerable/marginalized communities.            Under PMKVY 2.0, Ministry has implemented 12 and 51 Special Projects in J&K and NER, respectively. Similarly, under PMKVY 3.0, Ministry is implementing 3 and 1 Special Projects in J&K and NER, respectively.
