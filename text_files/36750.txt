                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

349






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Training Providers under National Skill Development Corporation




Shiromani Ram














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)        the details of the training providers registered with the National Skill Development Corporation under the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) since 2019 and the details of the process of their registration, State/UT wise; and (b)        the number of beneficiaries who got employment out of the trained beneficiaries under PMKVY so far and the number of beneficiaries who are still unemployed? 






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)         A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.  STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) AND (B) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 349 TO BE ANSWERED ON 28.03.2022 ASKED BY SHRI RAMSHIROMANI VERMA REGARDING TRAINING PROVIDERS UNDER NATIONAL SKILL DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION. (a)       Under Skill India Mission, Ministry is delivering skills to the youth across the country through Short Term Training (STT) and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) under its flagship scheme Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY). Under PMKVY, short term training is being imparted through accredited and affiliated training centres (TCs) including Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendras (PMKKs). Ministry has the ‘Guidelines for Accreditation, Affiliation, and Continuous Monitoring of Training Centres’ with objective to carry out standardized mechanism to assess the infrastructure and capability of training centre. Centre Accreditation is a quality assurance process under which the TCs are evaluated against the required parameters. It is driven by a grading matrix and relates to the operation and service of training centre to its trainees. Since the implementation of PMKVY 2.0, i.e., 2016 as on 31.12.2021, training has been imparted through 17,882 TCs across the country. The State-wise details are given at Annexure. (b)       Under PMKVY, placement opportunities are being provided to STT certified candidates. Since the implementation of PMKVY 2.0, i.e., 2016 as on 31.12.2021, 40.56 lakh candidates have been certified under STT; out of which, 21.17 lakh candidates have been provided with the placement in various sectors across the country.
