                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

448






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Food Processing Enterprises




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of food processing enterprises in the country, category-wise (Large, Medium, and Micro) and State-wise;(b)	the total number of SHGs (Self –Help Groups) and producer cooperatives involved in food processing, State-wise; and(c)	the details of ongoing and upcoming projects and initiatives taken by the Government in Jharkhand, especially in districts of Hazaribagh and Ramgarh?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b): As per the latest Annual Survey of Industries (2017-18), the total number of food processing enterprises registered and operational is 40,160 and 34,562 respectively.  The state wise details of these food processing enterprises are at Annexure. The Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) does not maintain state-wise details of Self Help Groups (SHGs) and producer cooperatives operating in the food processing sector.(c):	The Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) is implementing a Central Sector Umbrella a scheme- PRADHAN MANTRI KISAN SAMPADA YOJANA (PMKSY) for overall growth and development of food processing sector including setting up of food processing industries in the country including in the State of Jharkhand. PMKSY is a demand driven programme and financial assistance to the eligible applicants is provided against the Expression of Interests (EoI) issued by the MoFPI from time to time. At present, no such project is being implemented under PMKSY in the State of Jharkhand.*****
