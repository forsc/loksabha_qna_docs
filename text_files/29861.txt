                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1517






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Agro-Processing Cluster




Thomas Chazhikadan














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    	the details of various initiatives of agro-processing cluster undertaken and the ongoing projects of such clusters, state-wise, including the State of Kerala, state-wise;(b) 	whether the Government has conducted any study in the context of developing agro processing clusters and mega food parks in the country and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether some modern infrastructure has been provided for food processing units and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(d) 	the details of ongoing and upcoming projects and initiatives taken by the Government regarding reduction of net-worth criteria for women and SC / ST entrepreneurs for agro processing clusters and units in it; and(e)	the number of projects approved under the infrastructure development of agro-processing clusters under Mega Food Parks Scheme in the State of Kerala?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a):	Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) has been implementing the Scheme for Creation of Infrastructure for Agro Processing Clusters (APC) under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY) to incentivize the setting up of agro processing clusters in the country including Kerala. The details of ongoing projects approved under the Scheme for Creation of Infrastructure for Agro Processing Clusters is at Annexure.(b):	The Government has got conducted a third party impact evaluation study of Mega Food Park scheme through M/s KPMG Advisory Services Pvt. Ltd, Gurgaon, Haryana. However, no specific study has been conducted in the context of developing ago-processing clusters.(c):	The Scheme for Creation of Infrastructure for Agro-Processing Clusters (APC) and Scheme of Mega Food Parks (MFP) aim at development of modern infrastructure to encourage entrepreneurs to set up food processing units based on cluster approach. The projects approved under these schemes have created / provided Basic Enabling and Core Processing Infrastructure to the food processing units such as roads, drainage, water supply, electricity supply including power backup, effluent treatment plant, parking bay, weigh bridges, common office space, food testing laboratory, cleaning, grading, sorting and packing facilities, steam generation boilers, dry warehouse, cold storage, pre-cooling chambers, ripening chambers, IQF, specialized packaging, other common processing facilities etc.(d): The details of ongoing projects approved under the Scheme for Creation of Infrastructure for Agro Processing Clusters is at Annexure. However, new projects are selected after issue of fresh EOI, there may be no details of upcoming projects.   As per the eligibility criteria prescribed in the latest guidelines of the APC scheme, the combined net worth of the Project Execution Agency (PEA) should not be less than 1.5 times of the grants-in-aid amount sought. However, the combined net worth of applicants proposing to set up units in these APCs and seeking financial assistance under the Scheme for Creation/Expansion of Food Processing & Preservation Capacities (CEFPPC) should be equivalent or more than the amount of promoter’s equity/ contribution.(e):	MoFPI has approved 2 agro processing cluster projects under APC scheme and 2 Mega Food Parks under Mega Food Park Scheme in the State of Kerala.*****
