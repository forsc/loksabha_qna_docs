                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3367






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Mine Water Utilization initiative




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of villages and States that are currently receiving water supply from the Government’s Mine Water Utilisation initiative;(b)	the quantity of water supplied to these villages since inception, year-wise; and (c)	the plan of the Government to expand the initiative for other purposes like irrigation?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COAL, MINES AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a) : State wise villages receiving water supply from CIL, SCCL and NLCIL are given at Annexure. (b) : The quantity of water supplied to these villages during last 4 years is given in the table below: Mine water supplied to villages ( Lakh Cu.m)Year	Community use for domestic and irrigation purpose	CIL	SCCL	NLCIL	Total2018-19	1627	585.01	154.45	2366.462019-20 	2231	607.88	161.34	3000.222020-21	2377	467.73	171.80	3016.532021-22 Till Feb-22	2057.39	486.27	191.64	2735.30 (c) : The target for FY 2022-23 is to supply 4000 Lakh Cu.m of mine water to nearby community for both domestic and irrigation usage.  ******
