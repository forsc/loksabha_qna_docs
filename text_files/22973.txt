                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3988






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Regional and Cluster Subsidy




Ramya  Haridas














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether India’s export-specific subsidy are violative of WTO norms, if so, the details thereof; (b) whether there are any policies to move away from export subsidy and focus on regional and cluster subsidies, if so, the details thereof; and (c) whether the Government policies encompass the need for technology upgradation, skill development and skill development subsidies, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (b):	Government has approved scheme for Remission of Duties and Taxes on Exported Products (RoDTEP) to all export goods with effect from 1st January 2021. The scheme is compatible to WTO norms as this scheme would refund to exporters the embedded Central, State and local duties/taxes to keep export zero rated in sync with global practices.(c):	Government of India is aware of need for technology upgradation and skill development in textiles sector and is implementing ATUF scheme for technology upgradation and SAMARTH for skill development.
