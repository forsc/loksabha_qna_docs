                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

899






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Promotion of Sports through Khelo India Programme




Vijay Kumar













                                                    Ganesh Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Khelo India Programme has been launched by the Government in order to promote sports in the country;(b) if so, the salient features thereof and the funds allocated thereunder during the current year;(c) whether the Government has set up and identified camps under the said programme and if so, the number and details of the sports academies identified, State- wise including district Satna;(d) the manner in which athletes are selected for these camps and the number of athletes selected from each State in India;(e) whether any Talent Identification and Development Committee for training is set for District Satna, Madhya Pradesh and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor along with action plan of the Government in this regard; and(f) the number of selected athletes who actually joined and attended training camps at selected academies, State-wise including district Satna?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) and (b) Yes Sir. The ‘Khelo India – National Programme for Development of Sports’ was launched in October, 2017 with the twin objectives of mass participation and promotion of excellence in sports across the country. The estimated allocation for the Scheme is Rs. 1756 crore, out of which Rs. 500 crore has been allocated for current financial year 2019-20. The Scheme has following 12 verticals:i. Play Field Developmentii. Utilization and Creation/ Upgradation of Sports Infrastructureiii. Sports for Peace and Developmentiv. State Level Khelo India Centersv. Annual Sports Competitionsvi. Talent Search and Developmentvii. Sports for Womenviii. Support to National/Regional/State Sports Academiesix. Promotion of Sports among persons with disabilitiesx. Promotion of rural and indigenous/tribal gamesxi. Physical Fitness of School going Childrenxii. Community Coaching Development(c) No training camps are organized under Khelo India Scheme. However, 99 sports academies have been accredited under Khelo India Scheme. State wise information of Khelo India Academies are at Annexure – I. No Khelo India Academy is accredited in district Satna.(d) The selection process for inclusion under Khelo India comprises of identification of potential talent, assessment of talent and inclusion for training under Khelo India. The list of Khelo India Athletes selected from different States is at Annexure – II. District wise Identification and Development Committee (TIDC) is not setup. TIDC are setup for different Sports discipline for entire country.(e) No Sir. Talent constituted based on sports discipline centrally.A total of 81 Khelo India Athletes have been selected to the Scheme from the State of Madhya Pradesh.(f) 2,741 Athletes have joined the scheme out of which 1,388 athletes are getting trained in Academies accredited by Sports Authority of India (SAI). The remaining are getting training through their own arrangements. There is no Khelo India Academy accredited in district Satna. State wise details of Khelo India Athletes under Academies is at Annexure – III.*******
