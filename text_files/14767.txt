                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3476






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Merger of SVPISTM




P. Ravindhranath














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether there is any proposal with the Government to merge Sardar VallabhbhaiPatel International School of Textiles and Management (SVPISTM) with CentralUniversity of Tiruvarur in Tamil Nadu;(b) whether the Government has also received any representation from theinstitution; and(c) if so, the details thereof and the action taken by the Government in thisissue?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c): Ministry of Textiles has informed that a number of representations formerger of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International School of Textiles andManagement (SVPISTM) with Central University of Tamil Nadu were received.After consideration of the proposal, it has not been accepted.******
