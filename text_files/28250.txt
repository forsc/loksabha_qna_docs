                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

44






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Debt Clearance by FCI




Kumbakudi Sudhakaran













                                                    Ajay Kumar Mandal
                                                








                                                    Mohammad Jawed
                                                








                                                    Behanan Benny
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total debt in Rupees owed by the Food Corporation of India (FCI) to the National Small Savings Fund (NSSF); (b)	whether the Government has a repayment plan in place to the debt owed to NSSF; (c)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (d)	whether the Union Budget 2021-22 reflects the accurate debt amount and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	As of now, there is no debt owed by the Food Corporation of India (FCI) to the National Small Savings Fund (NSSF).(b) & (c)	Not arise.(d)	The ‘Annexure-8’ of Receipt Budget of the Union Budget 2021-22 reflects NSSF’s investment in FCI for the FY 2019-20 and estimates for the FY 2020-21 and FY 2021-22. The details of the same is given below:Investment in Food Corporation of India (Rs. in crore)Particulars	Actuals 2019-20 (Prov.)	RE 2020-2021	BE 2021-2022Opening Balance as on 1st April	191000.00	254600.00	118712.00Additions during the year	110000.00	84636.00	0.00Less: Repayment during the year	-46400.00	-220524.00	-55000.00 Note: FCI has repaid outstanding NSSF loan amount of Rs.1,18,712 crore on 31.03.2021.
