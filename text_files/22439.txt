                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3620






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2021






GramNet




Anil Firojiya














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any provision has been made by the Government to connect all the villages in the country under GramNet through wi-fi and if so, the details thereof along with the manner in which it will be done; and(b)	 the details of the progress made by the Government in this regard, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	BharatNet project is being implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity to all the Gram Panchayats (approx. 2,50,000) in the country. Further, as part of this project, the last mile connectivity, through Wi-Fi or any other suitable broadband technology, is to be provided at all the Gram Panchayats (GPs) in the country.The provisioning of  Wi-Fi Services in about 1.10 lakh GPs has been assigned to CSC e-Governance Services India Limited (a Special Purpose Vehicle under Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology), and about 10,000 GPs of Rajasthan to Government of  Rajasthan/Raj COMP Info Services Ltd. (RISL). In 1407 GPs of BharatNet, the provisioning of connectivity over satellite media, along with Wi-Fi services, is being provided through Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL). Further, under State-led model, the States are responsible for utilisation of the BharatNet Network.(b)	Under BharatNet project, Wi-Fi hotspots have been installed in 1,03,400 GPs. The State/UT-wise details of Gram Panchayat where Wi-Fi Hotspots have been installed are attached as Annexure.
