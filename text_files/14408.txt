                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3120






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Regularisation of Unauthorised Colonies in Delhi




Ajay (Teni) Misra














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has fixed any time limit to regularise the unauthorised colonies in National Capital, Delhi and to provide them ownership right; and(b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                          (SHRI  HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)&(b):	The Government of India has launched a scheme named Prime Minister- Unauthorised Colonies in Delhi Awas Adhikar Yojna (PM-UDAY) for conferring ownership rights to the residents of 1,731 unauthorised colonies in Delhi. The scheme is a voluntary one. Residents of unauthorised colonies have to apply for ownership rights on public user interface of the PM-UDAY e-Portal. Ownership right is to be given within 180 days from the date of application.                                                ******
