                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1882






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Opening of New Ayurvedic Dispensary




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has any plan to open new Ayurvedic dispensaries in the country during current year; (b) if so, the details thereof, State/ UT-wise; (c) whether the Government has any proposal to develop temporary ayurvedic centres into full fledged ayurvedic dispensary during current year; and (d) if so, the details of these temporary centres?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (d):      As health is a state subject, opening of new Ayurvedic dispensaries and developing temporary Ayurvedic centres into full-fledged Ayurvedic dispensary in the country comes under the purview of respective State/UT Governments. However, under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), there is a provision of financial assistance for upgradation of AYUSH dispensaries. State/UT Governments may avail eligible financial assistance by projecting the same through State Annual Action Plan (SAAP) as per NAM guideline. ………………
