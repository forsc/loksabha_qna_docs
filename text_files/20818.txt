                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2045






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Homoeopathic Medical Colleges




Ratansinh Magansinh Rathod














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(AYUSH) be pleased to state:(a) the number and details of Homeopathy medical colleges set up during the last five years across the country, State/UT-wise including Gujarat; and(b) the steps taken by the Government to set up more Homeopathic colleges/institutes?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS AND ADDITIONAL CHARGE OF MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a):      The number and details of Homeopathy medical colleges set up during the last five years across the country, State/UT-wise including Gujarat is attached at Annexure.(b):  As per the provisions of Section 12A of Homoeopathy Central Council Act, 1973, any person intending to establish new homoeopathic medical college has to apply to Central Government as per the Establishment of New Medical College (Opening of New or Higher Course of Study or Training and Increase of Admission Capacity by a Medical College) Regulations, 2011 (Amendments Regulations, 2018) from 1st July to 31st August of any year.
