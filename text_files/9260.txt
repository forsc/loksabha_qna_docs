                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2772






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Collapsing of Buildings




Dhal Singh Bisen














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether incidents of building collapse have been reported from various parts of the country between July, 2019 to November, 2019 due to excessive rainfall;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise and district-wise; and(c)	whether any provision is being made for providing assistance by the Union Government and the State Governments jointly to build new houses for the families whose houses have been destroyed and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




(a)	& (b):  Urban Planning being a State subject, no such data is maintained by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs.(c):  	Does not arise.                                 *****
