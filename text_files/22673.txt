                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3760






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Assets of NYKS




Sushil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of total assets owned by Nehru Yuva Kendra Sanghathan (NYKS) and the monetary value of assets owned by it;(b) the funds allocated by NYKS to the States of Bihar, West Bengal, Assam, Mizoram, Meghalaya, Nagaland and Arunachal Pradesh respectively since 2010; programme-wise;(c) whether the funds were allocated to Arunachal Pradesh for Drug Abuse Programme between 2018 to 2021; and(d) if so, the details regarding utilisation of these funds?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) Items-wise details of Assets owned by NYKS and its monetary value, as per Annual Accounts of 2019-20 is at Annexure-I.(b) Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan implements various progammes and activities for rural youth, through its district level Nehru Yuva Kendras, from the Grants provided by the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports under NYKS, NYC, NYLP and NPYAD schemes. The funds allocated by NYKS to the States of Bihar, West Bengal, Assam, Mizoram, Meghalaya, Nagaland and Arunachal Pradesh respectively since 2010; programme-wise is at Annexure-II.(c) & (d) No funds were provided to Arunachal Pradesh for Drug Abuse programme between 2018 to 2021.*****
