                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2169






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Fellowship scheme for girls




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the provision was made earlier to enroll more and more girls into IITs and ultimately achieve 20 per cent enrolment by 2019-20;(b)	 if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	whether there is any proposal to extend the enrolment year to 2022 and the steps being taken by the Government to push research among girl students from IITs and IIITs;(d)	whether the Government is proposing any special Fellowship scheme for girls studying in these institutes;(e)	if so, the details thereof and, if not, the reasons therefor; and(f)	the details of funds sanctioned for IITs and IIITs in the country, with a particular reference to Andhra Pradesh during the last five years and for 2021-22?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) : 	With a view to improve female enrollment in the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs), the Government had decided to increase female enrollment in the B.Tech. programmes of IITs from 8% to 14% in 2018-19, 17% in 2019-20 and 20% in 2020-21 by creating supernumerary seats. Necessary instructions in this regard were issued to all the IITs for compliance. Against these, the female enrolment in IITs for B. Tech. Programmes was 15.3% in 2018-19, 18% in 2019-20 and 19.8% in 20-21.(c) to (e): The female enrolment of 19.8% has already been achieved in IITs. The girls are getting fellowship at par with male students in the IITs and IIITs (CFTIs).(f) : The details of funds released to IITs and IIITs in the country, with a particular reference to Andhra Pradesh during the last five years and for 2021-22 are as under-	Institutes	Funds released (in Rs.
