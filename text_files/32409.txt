                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3841






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






National Level Medical Institutes




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   the details of availability of National level medical Institutes specifically for physical Disabilities like Locomotor Disabilities i.e. Pt. Deendayal Upadhyaya National Institute for Persons with Physical Disabilities (Divyangjan) (PDUNIPPD) and for rehabilitation training and research i.e. Swami Vivekanand National Institute of Rehabilitation Training and Research (SVNIRTAR); (b) the number of disabled persons who have availed Inpatient/Outpatient treatment during each year of the past five years; and (c) the total number of beds available for mental health patients and the occupancy rate of these mental health facilities in these institutes?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT (SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a) There are nine National Institutes (NIs) working under the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD) (Divyangjan) specializing in different kinds of disabilities.  Details of these Institutes are at Annexure-I. The main objectives of these Institutes are to train rehabilitation personnel in the field of disabilities, to provide rehabilitation services to Persons with Disabilities and to undertake Research and Development. (b) Number of disabled persons who have availed inpatient/outpatient treatment during each of past five years are as under:-  Year	Number of inpatients	Number of outpatients2016-2017	1411	7853282017-2018	1538	7737822018-2019	1562	8527722019-2020	1653	8239322020-2021	906	487762Total	7070	3723576 (c)    No beds have been provided in these Institutes for mental health patients.*****
