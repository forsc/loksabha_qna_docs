                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1747






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Solar Charkha Clusters Project




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of the Solar Charkha Clusters Project implemented across the country; (b) 	the details of financial assistance provided by the Government to complete the project; (c) 	the details and the number of companies that are involved in the project; and (d) 	the roadmap and timeline for completion of the project?






ANSWER
