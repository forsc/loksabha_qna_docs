                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

106






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






PMGSY in Uttar Pradesh




Devendra (Alias) Bhole Singh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the targets fixed and achieved under phase-1 and 2 of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in Uttar Pradesh;(b) 	whether phase-3 of PMGSY has been launched with emphasis on strengthening the road infrastructure; and(c) 	if so, the details in respect of Uttar Pradesh alongwith the minimum width prescribed by the Ministry for the rural roads?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	to (c) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION No.106 TO BE ANSWERED ON 09.02.2021(a)	The status of road works and Long Span Bridges (LSBs) sanctioned and completed in respect of Uttar Pradesh under phase-I and II of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY), as per Online Monitoring Management & Accounting System (OMMAS) as on 4th February, 2021 are as under:-                                                                   (Length in km)	    Sanctioned	                                        CompletedPhases	  No of Road     No of LSBs  Road Length  No of road Works    No of LSBs   Road length	   worksPMGSY-I	   17,576	    0	      50,325	     17,562	           0	     49,343PMGSY-II      963	    2	       7,614	        962	           2	      7,509(b) 	The Government approved PMGSY-III in  July, 2019 for consolidation of 1,25,000 Km Through Routes and Major Rural Links connecting habitations, inter-alia, to Gramin Agricultural Markets (GrAMs), Higher Secondary Schools and Hospitals. Period of Implementations of PMGSY-III is 2019-2025.(c) 	The target length of road for the State of Uttar Pradesh under PMGSY-III is 18,937.5 km. So far proposal for a length of 6287.37 km (898 roads and 5 LSBS) has been sanctioned under Batch –I (2020- 21) of PMGSY-III for the State of Uttar Pradesh. The Low Volume Rural Roads are constructed with 3.0 m/ 3.75 m/ 5.5 m carriage way width depending upon the traffic intensity.  The roadway width required for rural roads for 3 m, 3.75m and 5.50 m carriageway width are 6 m, 7.50 m and 9 m respectively.
