                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

854






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Availability of Ayush Medicines




Shankar Lalwani














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state:(a) whether any scheme has been formulated to ensure the availability of high quality AYUSH medicines to the people of Madhya Pradesh;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details of the fund allocated to Madhya Pradesh for the medicinal conservation and development under his Ministry during the last three financial years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(DR. MUNJPARA MAHENDRABHAI)(a) & (b):	To ensure the availability of high quality AYUSH medicines to the people of the country including Madhya Pradesh comes under the purview of respective State Government/UT Governments, as Public health is a state subject. However, under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the National AYUSH Mission (NAM), there is a provision of financial assistance for the supply of essential AYUSH medicines through AYUSH facilities to the people of States/UTs including Madhya Pradesh. Grant-in-aid of Rs. 11525.82 lakhs has been approved to Madhya Pradesh under the supply of essential drugs under NAM from 2014-15 to 2020-21.(c):	The details of the fund allocated to Madhya Pradesh for medicinal conservation and development during the last three financial years is furnished in Annexure-I.
