                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4115






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Stoppage at Viluppuram




D. Ravikumar














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry has taken any steps to fulfil the demand of the people of Viluppuram for the stoppage of TEJAS train at this junction; (b) if so, whether the Ministry has any plans to introduce new super fast trains in Chennai-Madurai route; and (c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) No, Sir. Tejas Express is premium service train, with limited stoppages enroute. Therefore, stoppage at Villupuram  is not under consideration. (b) and (c) No, Sir. Villupuram is presently well connected to Chennai area and Madurai by 41 pairs of train services and 22 pairs of train services respectively, which inter-alia includes superfast services.*****
