                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3695






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






New Medical Equipment Park




Nitesh Ganga Deb













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Vijay Baghel
                                                








                                                    Arun Sao
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is implementing the scheme for ‘Promotion of Medical Equipment Parks’ in the country and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government proposes to set up new Medical Equipment Parks in the country during the year, 2022-23; (c) whether any proposal has been received from Chhattisgarh, Maharashtra and Odisha for setting up Medical Equipment Park in various districts including Sambalpur district in Odisha; (d) if so, the details thereof and the decision taken by the Government thereon; and (e) the details of funds allocated and released by the Government in this regard, State-wise including Chhattisgarh, Maharashtra and Odisha?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b): The Department of Pharmaceuticals is implementing a Scheme for ‘Promotion of Medical Devices Parks’ with a total financial outlay of Rs. 400 crore for setting up common infrastructural facilities in medical devices parks to be set up in four States/ UTs, with maximum assistance for one Park limited to Rs. 100 crore. The tenure of the scheme is for the period 2020-2021 to 2024-2025. The details of the Scheme along with the Guidelines can be accessed at https://pharmaceuticals.gov.in/sites/default/files/Guidelines%20of%20the%20Scheme%20Promotion%20of%20Medical%20Devices%20Parks_1.pdf. (c) to (e): Department has received proposals from 16 States/UTs, but no proposal was received from the State Government of Odisha. The proposals were evaluated as per the criteria laid down in the scheme guidelines and final approval for financial assistance of Rs. 100 crore each has been given to the proposals received from the States of Uttar Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Madhya Pradesh and Himachal Pradesh. The first instalment of grant-in-aid of Rs.30 crore to the Government of Tamil Nadu and Rs.12 crore to State Government of Uttar Pradesh has already been released. *****
