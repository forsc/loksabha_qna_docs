                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3410






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Promotion of Textile Industry




Chandra Prakash Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the work done in Rajasthan during the last three years to promote the textile industry; (b) the quantum of amount that has been spent in last five years; year-wise data; (c) whether the Government has plan to establish Shilp Gram in Rajasthan, particularly in Chittorgarh Lok Sabha constituency; (d) if so, the details thereof; and (e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) & (b):	Government is implementing various schemes viz the Amended Technology Upgradation Fund Scheme (A-TUFS), Schemes for the development of the Powerloom Sector   (Power-Tex), Scheme for Integrated Textile Parks (SITP), SAMARTH- The Scheme for Capacity Building in Textile Sector, Jute (ICARE- Improved Cultivation and Advanced Retting Exercise), Integrated Processing Development Scheme (IPDS), Silk Samagra, National Handloom Development Programme, National Handicraft Development Programme, Integrated Wool Development Programme (IWDP), Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for Textiles, Rebate of State and central Taxes and Levies (RoSCTL), National Technical Textiles Mission (NTTM) etc. catering exclusively for promotion and development of textile sector in the country including the state of Rajasthan.The quantum of amount released under major schemes of Ministry of Textiles in Rajasthan year-wise is given at Annexure.(c) to (e):	The proposal to set up Craft Village is considered by Ministry on the receipt of viable proposal from the concerned State Government. So far, no such proposal has been received from Government of Rajasthan for Chittorgarh. However, during 2020-21, Ministry of Textiles has sanctioned a Craft Village (Shilp Gram) at Amer, Jaipur, Rajasthan.ANNEXUREThe quantum of amount released under major schemes of Ministry of Textiles in Rajasthan year-wise:Year	 Amount released (Rs. in crore)2016-17	191.482017-18	91.482018-19	 32.46*2019-20	35.08*2020-21	69.17*Major portion of committed liabilities under erstwhile Technology Upgradation Fund Scheme (TUFS) scheme were released in 2016-17 and 2017-18 under Amended Technology Upgradation Fund Scheme (A-TUFS). ***
