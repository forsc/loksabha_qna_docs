                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1386






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Order Book of HAL




S. Ramalingam














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the State run Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) order books are empty beyond 2021-22 and need fresh orders from the armed forces for production of fighter jets, trainers and helicopters to prevent a halt from production at its facilities;(b) whether the Government has decided to import fighter jets, trainers and helicopters from other countries;(c) if so, the details therefor; and(d) the reasons for not producing these fighter jets, trainers and helicopters from HAL?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a)  Major part of the existing manufacturing orders of Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) will be completed by 2021-22.  Additionally, HAL is progressing the following projects that would sustain its operations beyond 2021-22:-	(i)  Light Combat Aircraft (LCA) MK-1-Delivery by 2022-23.	(ii)  LCA Mk-1A- A new contract for 83 aircraft signed in January, 2021.	(iii)  Hindustan Turbo Trainer (HTT) 40- Request for Proposal (RFP) for 70 trainers 	issued by Indian Air Force (IAF) in February, 2021.	(iv)  Light Combat Helicopter (LCH) - Documentation of 15 helicopters in progress.	(v)  Basic Certification of Light Utility Helicopter (LUH) granted by Services.(b)  No, Sir.(c)  Does not arise in view of (b) above.(d)  HAL has the capability to take up production of various types of fighter jets, trainers and helicopters based on the orders from Ministry of Defence (MoD).                                                 *****
