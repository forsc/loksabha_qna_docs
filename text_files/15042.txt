                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3894






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Fishing Harbour in Nellore




Adala Prabhakara Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is some delay in setting up fishing harbour at Juvvala Dinnalo, Nellore District;(b) 	if so, the reasons for the delay;(c) 	whether the Ministry has commissioned a feasibility study of the project, if so, the reasons for not moving ahead; and (d)	 the total amount of funds allocated for this project and the time by which it would be commissioned?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) to (d) : 	The Government of Andhra Pradesh has submitted a proposal for construction of a fishing harbour at Juvvaladinne in Nellore District  after completion of necessary feasibility studies, engineering and economic investigations and surveys. The Government of Andhra has estimated the cost for development of fishing harbour  at Rs. 288.80 crore and sought 50% central finance assistance amounting to Rs.144.40 crore under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS) on Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries. The Project Sanctioning Committee (PSC) in the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying has accordingly recommended the fishing harbour proposal for central financial assistance under the CSS. A time period of four years has been estimated for completion and commissioning of the fishing harbour at Juvvaladinne in Nellore District of Andhra Pradesh. *****
