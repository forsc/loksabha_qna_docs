                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3074






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Tourism Projects Approved in Jharkhand




Sanjay Seth














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the projects for development of tourism approved, sanctioned and executed in Jharkhand during the last five years including the current year, year-wise; (b) 	the details of the Tourism Projects completed so far and the time by which the remaining projects are likely to be completed; (c) 	the reasons for the delay in completion of the pending projects; (d) 	the details of funds allocated, released and utilised for the development of tourism sector in the State during the said period, year-wise; and (e) 	whether the State Government of Jharkhand has submitted any proposal to the Government and if so, the details thereof along with the present status of ongoing projects in the State of Jharkhand?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (e):	Development and Promotion of Tourist places is primarily the responsibility of the State Government/Union Territory (UT) Administration. However, Ministry under its schemes of Swadesh Darshan and PRASHAD provides financial Assistance to the State Governments/UT Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure and facilities in the country. The projects under the schemes are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. Funds are sanctioned and released for specific projects under the Schemes and there is no state specific allocations. Ministry of Tourism has sanctioned following two projects for Jharkhand. (Rs. in Crore)<pre>Sl. No.	Scheme	Sanction Year	Name of the Project	Amt. Sanctioned	Amt. Released	Amt Utilised1	Swadesh Darshan	2018-19	Development of Dalma- Chandil- Getalsud- Betla national park- Mirchaiya- Netarhat	52.72	15.07	4.252	PRASHAD	2018-19	Development of Baidyanathji Dham, Deoghar	39.13	20.58	9.00</pre>The projects under the schemes are under implementation.*******
