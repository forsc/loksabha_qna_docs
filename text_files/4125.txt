                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3786






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Storage of Foodgrains




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the quantum of foodgrain production in the country along with their procurement; (b) 	the details of the total and covered storage capacity available with the Government in the entire country as on date, State/UT-wise; (c) 	whether the optimal utilization of the said storage capacity is being done; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a): 	The details of the quantum of foodgrain production in the country along with their procurement at Minimum Support Price (MSP) by Government Agencies  for last three years and current year is at Annexure-I.  (b) to (d): The total storage capacity available with Food Corporation of India (FCI), Central Warehousing Corporation (CWC) and the State Agencies (both owned and hired capacity), is 862.45 LMT (as on 31.05.2019) comprising 739.76 LMT in covered godowns and 122.69 LMT in Cover and Plinth (CAP) storage.  The State-wise details are at Annexure-II.    	The available storage capacity with FCI is optimally utilized for storage of wheat, rice & pulses.
