                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3185






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Job Fairs organised in Uttar Pradesh




Devendra (Alias) Bhole Singh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government has formulated any scheme with the State Governments to organise job fairs across the country at the district level; and(b)	if so, the district-wise number of job fairs organised in Uttar Pradesh during the last three years and the outcome thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (b): Government is implementing the National Career Service (NCS) Project to provide a variety of employment related services like job matching, career counselling, vocational guidance, information on skill development courses, etc. Under the NCS project assistance is also provided to States/UTs for organising job fairs. As per the information received from the Government of Uttar Pradesh, Employment Department of the State Government is organising Job Fairs in each district. The district-wise details of Job Fairs organised in Uttar Pradesh during last three years is at Annexure.
