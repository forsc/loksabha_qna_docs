                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4913






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2019






Threat of Attack on Amarnath Pilgrims




Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of security arrangements made for Amarnath Yatra by the Central and State Governments;(b) whether there are intelligence inputs suggesting threats of terrorist attacks on Amarnath pilgrims;(c) if so, the details thereof and the response of the Government thereto;(d) whether incidents of stone pelting on the vehicles of pilgrims going to the holy shrine of Amarnath have come to the notice of the Government at present;(e) if so, the details thereof and the reaction of the Government thereto; and(f) the further steps being taken by the Government to make the pilgrimage to the holy cave of Amarnath safe?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (f):	The State Government has made adequate arrangements for safety and security of the pilgrims along the Yatra routes. These include deployment of local police 24x7 Nakas, Night Area Domination, use of technical gadgets like CCTVs, RFID tagging, deployment of mountain rescue teams and SDRF teams on strategic locations.  	All agencies are working in a coordinated manner and the intelligence inputs are shared among various agencies on real time basis. As per reports available, there is no instance of stone pelting on Amarnath Yatris. 	In addition to the deployment of Central Armed Police Forces (CAPF) Coys and  NDRF teams provided by the  Government,  various steps such as  QR-Coding/ -2/…-2-LS.US.Q.NO.4913 FOR 23.07.2019Bar-coding of Yatra Permit Forms at Access Control Gates and selective camps to track the movement of Yatris in the Yatra area, necessary arrangements for water supply, food grains, langar facilities at various places in the Yatra area have also been introduced by the State Government.*****
