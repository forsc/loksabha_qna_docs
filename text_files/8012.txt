                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1624






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Lighthouses Along Coasts




Aparajita Sarangi














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the lighthouses installed across the coast of India during the last five years, State-wise particularly in Odisha;(b)	the funds allocated for maintenance of these lighthouses during the last five years, State-wise;(c)	whether any guidelines/criteria has been laid down by the Government for coast line to have a lighthouse and if so, the details thereof;(d)	the details of the lighthouse projects approved by CACL during the last five years, State-wise including Odisha; and(e)	whether there is any plan to install more lighthouses along the coasts and if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	The details of the four Lighthouses setup along the coastline of India during the last five years, state-wise are given below:<pre>Sl. No.	Location of Lighthouse	State	Year of Installation1.		Achara Point	Maharashtra	20142.		Rava Port	Andhra Pradesh	20143.		Kolta Plam	Andaman & Nicobar (Union Territory)	20144.		Vembar	Tamil Nadu	2017                                                                  </pre>(b)	The work of maintenance of Lighthouses is carried out from the budget provisions under the Revenue Head for Directorate General of Lighthouses and Lightships (DGLL) as per the maintenance schedule. There is no state-wise allocation of funds. (c)	The establishment of Lighthouses is decided on the basis of traffic and need of ‘Aid-to-Navigation’ (AtoNs) in the proposed area on the recommendations of the Central Advisory Committee for Lighthouses (CACL) headed by the Secretary (Shipping). (d)	The details of the seven Lighthouse projects approved by CACL during last 5 years are as follows:<pre>                      Sl. No.	Location of Lighthouse	State	Year of Approval1.		Kelshi	Maharashtra	20142.		Arnala (improvement of Lighthouse)	Maharashtra	20143.		Valsad Khadi (improvement of Lighthouse)	Maharashtra	20154.		Dhanushkodi	Tami Nadu	20175.		Jaffrabad (improvement of Lighthouse)	Gujarat	20176.		Kuthankuli	Tamil Nadu	20177.		Chaumukh	Odisha	2017                                                                  </pre>(e)	 There is no plan to install more Lighthouses at present.                                               ****
