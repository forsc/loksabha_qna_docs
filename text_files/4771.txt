                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4472






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Green India Mission




Kanakmal Katara













                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the progress made in the implementation of ‘Green India Mission’ formulated to deal with the risks posed by climate change along with the names of the states which have achieved the targets fixed in this regard;(b)	the total funds released to States for the purpose during the last three financial years and the current year, State/UT-wise;(c)	whether the Government proposes to link ‘Green India Mission with the schemes of other Ministries like MNREGA; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	National Mission for a Green India is one of the eight Missions under the National Action Plan on Climate Change. It aims at protecting, restoring and enhancing India’s forest cover and responding to Climate Change. The implementation of Green India Mission started in the year 2015-16. A sum of Rs. 237.07 crore has so far been released under the Green India Mission for undertaking afforestation activities over an area of 87113.86 ha and providing alternative energy devices to 56,319 households. The State wise details of the achievements against the targets sanctioned is placed at Annexure-1. (b)	A total amount of Rs. 166.98 crore has been released to ten states during the last three financial years under the scheme. The State wise details are placed at Annexure-2.(c)	& (d) Convergence Guidelines of Green India Mission with Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) and Compensatory Afforestation Fund Management and Planning Authority (CAMPA) were issued in March and May, 2015 respectively.****ANNEXURE
