                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

266






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Process of Fund Transfer Order under MGNREGS




Dushyant Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any stipulated time limit to process the Fund Transfer Order (FTO) to transfer wages directly to the worker’s accounts after receivingFund Transfer Order (FTO) from the State Governments under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MNREGS), if so, the details thereof;(b) 	the details of average days of time taken to process the Fund Transfer Order‘s by the Government each month from 01.01.2021 to 31.10.2021 State-wise;(c) 	the details of the utilization of the allocated funds of MNREGS for financial year 2021-2022 Statewise and the steps taken by the Government to handle the payments of the States that used 100% funds, waiting for extra allocation; and(d) 	the details of the work demanded by the workers to work provided by the Government, State-wise in this financial year 2021-2022?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):       As per the provision of the Act, the beneficiaries are entitled to get wage payment within 15 days of closure of Muster Roll of the work. Government of India has also issued a detailed Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) to the States/UTs to ensure timely wage payment. (b):       State/UT-wise status of timely payment of wages as on 26.11.2021 is given at Annexure-I. (c):       Fund release to States/UTs is a continuation process and Central Government is committed in making funds available keeping in view the demand for work. The Ministry seeks additional funds as and when required for meeting the demand for work on the ground. There was a Budget allocation of Rs.73000 crore for Mahatma Gandhi NREGA for current financial year 2021-22 as Budget Estimate (BE). There is no State/UT-wise allocation of funds. State/UT-wise sanctioned/released funds for wage, material and administrative components under Mahatma Gandhi NREGA for financial year 2021-2022 (as on 24.11.2021) is given at Annexure-II.  (d):      States/UTs-wise details of employment demanded by workers, employment offered to workers and employment provided to workers  under the Mahatma Gandhi NREGS during the current financial year 2021-22 (as on 26.11.2021) is given at Annexure-III. *******
