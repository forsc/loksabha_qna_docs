                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

66






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Technological Upgradation of Fund Scheme




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the funds allocated and disbursed to the State of Tamil Nadu under the Technological Upgradation of Fund scheme(TUF), since 2019; (b) the district-wise details of those who were paid subsidies under the TUF in the State of Tamil Nadu, since 2019; and (c) whether the Government has taken any effective measures to ensure the timely disbursement of subsidies under the TUFS to its respective beneficiaries and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) & (b):	Under Technology Upgradation Fund Scheme (TUFS), there is no State-wise allocation of funds. District-wise details of subsidy released to the textile units located in the State of Tamil Nadu under TUFS/Amended TUFS, since 2018-19 are given in Annexure-I.(c):	With a view to streamline the procedures and to speed up the settlement of subsidy claims, the guidelines of ongoing Amended TUFS (ATUFS) were modified and issued on 02.08.2018 to make iTUFS online portal an end to end solution with features such as automatic registration of subsidy applications, use of digital signature, geo-tagging of assets, engraving of system generated Machine Identification Codes on machinery, claim tracking facility and direct release of subsidy to beneficiary through PFMS etc. Also, various policy clarifications and delegation of financial powers to office of Textiles Commissioner have been issued. Further, Inter Ministerial Steering Committee in its meeting held on 22.10.2021 has approved various measures and relaxations including automated/graded verification of machinery to expedite settlement of claims to ensure timely disbursement of subsidies under ATUFS.
