                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Department of Science and Technology has taken up the task of digital mapping of the entire country;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the present status of the project for digital mapping of the entire country by the Survey of India;(c) the details of funds allocated for the purpose;(d) the time by which the remaining States are likely to be covered; and(e) the time by which the project is likely to be completed?
