                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2691






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Elimination of Unmanned Level Crossings in Telangana




Sanjay Kumar Bandi














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is committed to eliminate manned level crossings across the country and if so, the details of manned level crossings removed in the state of Telangana during the last seven years, year-wise;(b)	whether the State Governments are supportive with matching grants, if any, while constructing RUBs/ ROBs on railway tracks, if not, the reasons therefor;(c)	whether one of the level crossings in Karimnagar constituency has been long pending; and(d)	if so, the reasons for not removing the same?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (d): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
