                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

47






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.09.2020






Development of Tillaiya Dam as Tourist Spot




Annpurna Devi













                                                    Ranjeeta Koli
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to launch a special drive for tourism development in Jharkhand with a view to attract tourism by developing to various important tourist places in the State; (b) 	if so, whether there is any plan for the development of Tillaiya Dam of Jhumari Tillaiya in Kodarma district as a tourist spot; and (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	Development and promotion of tourist places is primarily the responsibility of concerned State Government/ Union Territory Administrations. However, the Ministry of Tourism under ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ schemes provides financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory Administrations/Central Agencies for development of tourism infrastructure.The Ministry of Tourism under Swadesh Darshan Scheme, in the year 2018-19, has sanctioned a project ‘Development of Dalma – Chandil – Getalsud – Betla National Park – Mirchaiya – Netarhat under Eco-Circuit in Jharkhand at a sanctioned cost of Rs.52.72 Crore. In addition, the Ministry of Tourism under its PRASHAD scheme in the year 2018-19, has sanctioned a project ‘Development of Baidyanathji Dham’ with the sanctioned cost of Rs.39.13 Crore.********
