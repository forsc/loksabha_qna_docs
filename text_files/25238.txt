                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1010






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Giving up of LPG Subsidy




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of LPG connections given in total so far throughout the country as on31.03.2021;(b) the number of LPG customers who have responded to the call of “Give up subsidy”so far throughout the country as on 31.03.2021;(c) the total subsidy given for LPG connections in the financial years starting from 2015to 2021 year-wise; and(d) the savings in subsidy due to response to “Give up subsidy” call in the Financialyears starting from 2016- 2021 with details of number of options, year-wise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b) The total number of LPG customers as on 31.03.2021 across the country is28.95 crore. As on 31.03.2021, 1.08 crore LPG consumers have given up their subsidyunder “GiveItUp” campaign across the country.(c) The details of amount of subsidy/under-recovery on supply of domestic LPG from2015-16 to 2020-21 are given as under:Particulars Total Subsidy/ Under-recoveryon Domestic LPG ( ? in crore)2015-16 22,0292016-17 18,3372017-18 23,4642018-19 37,2092019-20 24,1722020-21(P) 11,896(d) As per available data, the estimated savings due to blocked/non-subsidiseddomestic/GiveItUp consumers from 2015-16 to 2020-21 is Rs. 57,768 crore.------
