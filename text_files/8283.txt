                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1971






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






NIFT Centres




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to establish more NIFT Centres in the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the student intake in the current NIFT Centres across the country; (c) 	whether the Government has any proposal to establish a NIFT Centre at Tiruchirappalli or any other place in Tamil Nadu, apart from the one at Chennai, if so, the details thereof; and(d)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) & (b): As per NIFT Policy for establishment of new campuses, the proposal is required to be submitted by the respective State Government along with following commitments:(i)	20-30 acres of land;(ii)	Rs. 150-200 crore for construction of Campus at the price rates of CPWD for 2011-12;(iii)	Rs. 30 crore for provision of equipments, machines and furniture at the price rates of 2011-12;(iv)	Annual revenue grants to adjust revenue deficit, till the Campus becomes financially viable. The student intake in current NIFT Campuses across the country is given in Annexure. (c):	No Sir. (d):	NIFT has not received any such proposal from State Government of Tamil Nadu for establishment of NIFT Centre at Tiruchirappalli or any other place in Tamil Nadu, apart from the one at Chennai. Moreover, as per NIFT Policy for establishment of new NIFT Campuses, only one NIFT Campus can be established in a State. One NIFT Campus is already functional at Chennai, Tamil Nadu.
