                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2342






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Slots of Jet Airways




Gaurav Gogoi














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a number of airline slots, previously occupied by Jet Airways are lying vacant ever since its closure and if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise;(b) whether the Government has started the process of slot allocation for the vacant slots and if so, the time by which the vacant slots have been targeted to be filled;(c) whether the Government has taken cognizance of the fact that vacant slots at non-metropolitan airports is impacting the number of flight operations and the overall connectivity to these States; and(d) if so, the details of the Government''s plans to mitigate the situation?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): Yes, Sir. The State/UT-wise details of vacant slots, previously occupied by Jet Airways, are at Annexure.;;(b) to (d): The Government has reviewed the situation arising out of sudden closure of operation by Jet Airways and allocated slots to other airlines up to the end of the Summer Schedule 2020  purely on temporary basis. Allocations so made are as per Standard Operating Procedure devised for the purpose to ensure transparency and equity. Airlines have been advised to mount the flights and most of the slots have been utilized by them.
