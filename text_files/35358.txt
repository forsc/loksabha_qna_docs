                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2485






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






Gorakhpur-New Delhi Vande Bharat Express




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Ministry has received any proposal to run Vande Bharat Express train between Gorakhpur and New Delhi; (b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the time by which the Ministry is likely to run Vande Bharat on the said route?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (c) Representations including that from the Hon’ble Member of Parliament have been received for introducing Vande Bharat Express between Gorakhpur and New Delhi. However, subject to operational feasibility, commercial justification, availability of rolling stock etc., introduction of train services including Vande Bharat Express is an ongoing process. Besides, Gorakhpur is, presently, well connected to Delhi by 24 pair of train services including services like 12571/12572 Gorakhpur - Anand Vihar (T) Humsafar Express,  12595/12596 Gorakhpur - Anand Vihar Humsafar Express and 12555/12556 Gorakhdham Express. *****
