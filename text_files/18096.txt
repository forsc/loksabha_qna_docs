                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1800






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






BharatNet Project




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to connect all the Gram Panchayats of the country with High-Speed Optical Fibre Cable Network under the BharaNet Project; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the number of Gram Panchayats which have been so connected, at present, State/UT-wise including Gujarat?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	BharatNet project is being implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity to all the Gram Panchayats (approx. 2,50,000) in the country through an optimal mix of media (i.e. Optical Fibre Cable/ Radio/Satellite). (b) 	As on 01 September 2020, by laying 4,50,997 km Optical Fibre Cable (OFC), a total of  1,56,108 Gram Panchayats (GPs) have been connected, out of which 1,41,327 GPs have been made Service Ready. In addition, 2558 GPs have been made Service Ready on satellite media.  (c)  	The number of Gram Panchayats which have been so connected, State/UT-wise including Gujarat, are given in Annexure-I.
