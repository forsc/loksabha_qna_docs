                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

718






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Construction of Roads




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether daily pace of construction of roads is 28 km per day in the country including Maharashtra and Rajasthan; (b)	if so, the details of the roads on which construction work has been undertaken during the last three years in Latur district in Maharashtra ; (c) 	the total length of roads in Latur district which is constructed and repaired along with the total amount allocated and utilized in this regard during the said period;(d) 	the present status of the construction work on Highways No. 63,361 and 750K; (e) 	whether many contractors have stopped their construction work on the said projects and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor; and (f) 	the time frame set for completion of the said projects along with the total cost thereof and the amount likely to be spent thereon in Latur district?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	The average construction of Highways was 28.04 Km per day during the FY 2019-2020 in the country.(b)	During the last 3 years, construction works on National Highways 63, 361, 361H, 548 B & 752Kin Latur District in Maharashtra have been undertaken. (c)	The total length of NHs approved for improvement and maintenance in last 3 years in Latur District is 507 Km out of which works in 277 Km length have been completed. The total cost of the projectsare Rs. 3675 Crore out of which Rs. 1484 Crore have been spent so far.(d)	The present status of the construction work on National Highways No. 63, 361 & 752Kin Latur District are at Annexure – I (e)	& (f )M/s MEP Infrastructure Developers Ltd have stopped  the construction work on the projects of Ausa to Chakur and Chakur to Loha section of NH-361 due to their financial problems.The process of substitution of Concessionaire has been initiated. The total bid project cost of these two projects is  Rs. 1848.73 Crore out of which work amounting to Rs.1372.14 Crore is in Latur District.
