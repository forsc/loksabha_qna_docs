                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

23






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Crop Damage due to Heavy Rains




Shrirang Appa Barne













                                                    Subrata Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the value of agricultural and horticultural crops damaged due to heavy rains in various parts of the country during the current rainy season; (b) 	whether the Government has conducted any survey in this regard and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has received any request from the State Governments for providing assistance to compensate for the loss suffered by farmers and if so, the details thereof along with the funds released, State/ UT-wise; and (d) 	the other steps taken by the Government to cover the loss of life, property and livestock suffered by farmers?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		 	  (SHRI NARENDRA  SINGH TOMAR) (a) to (	d):	A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 23  DUE FOR REPLY ON 30TH NOVEMBER, 2021.(a) to (d):      The State Governments undertake relief measures in the wake of natural disasters from the State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF) already placed at their disposal in accordance with Government of India approved items and norms. Additional assistance is extended from the National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF) as per established procedure. The assistance approved under SDRF/NDRF norms is provided in the form of relief.             Approximately, 50 lakh hectare agricultural area has been affected due to cyclonic storm/ flash floods / floods /landslides /cloudburst etc. during 2021, as reported by the State Governments. Ministry of Home Affairs deputed 19 Inter-Ministerial Central Teams (IMCTs), to 14 States and 02 Union Territories (UTs), as per the details at Annexure.
