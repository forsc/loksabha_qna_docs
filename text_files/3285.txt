                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3052






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






UDAN Scheme in Odisha




Ramesh Chandra Majhi














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether some proposals under UDAN scheme are under consideration for Odisha and if so, the details and the present status thereof; and(b) the time by which work on the said proposals is likely to be started?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b): In the first round of bidding under Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS)- UDAN routes connecting Jeypore, Utkela, Rourkela and Jharsuguda airports were awarded.  Out of these, routes connecting Jeypore, Utkela and Jharsuguda airports were cancelled due to the default by the airlines. These airports were offered under second and third rounds of bidding under RCS-UDAN.  However, no valid bids were received for Jeypore and Utkela Airport.;;Under UDAN third round, RCS route connecting Jharsuguda airport to Kolkata, Bhubaneshwar, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Delhi and Raipur airport were awarded. Operations on these routes, except Jharsuguda-Mumbai have been commenced. Jharsuguda-Mumbai route could not be operationalized as the Selected Airline Operator has suspended its operations.;;Route from Rourkela to Bhubneshwar and Raipur were awarded to M/s Alliance Air and M/s Turbo Aviation. However, operation could not be commenced due to non-readiness of Rourkela Airport.
