                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1700






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Continuation of PMGKAY




Rahul Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to continue the distribution of food grains under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY) after November 30, 2021 in the light of the continuing impact of Covid pandemic on vulnerable communities; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the State-wise and month-wise details of central allocation and food grain distribution to both Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) and Priority Households (PHH) from January 2021 onwards; (d) 	the State-wise and month-wise details of failed transactions, especially for NFSA ration card holders and the reasons thereof; and (e) 	the State-wise details of ration cards cancelled over the last two years and the key reasons for the same?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)  and (b): 	Yes Sir, Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY) has been extended for a period of four months i.e. December, 2021 to March, 2022......2/--2-(c):   	Under National Food Security Act (NFSA), the State-wise and month-wise details of allocation and offtake in regard to Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) and Priority  ouseholds (PHH)  are at Annexure-I(A) and Annexure-I(B).(d): 	Currently almost 87% of the transactions under NFSA on e-PoS are aadhar authenticated. The remaining transactions are non-authenticated but duly verified for rightful targeting through other mechanism specified for the purpose. States/UTs have been instructed to ensure that no genuine beneficiary is denied foodgrains under NFSA only for reasons of non-possession of aadhar or failure of biometric authentication during transaction.(e):  	The state-wise details of ration cards cancelled over the last two years (as reported by States/UTs) is at Annexure-II. Further, the States/UTs have highlighted the key reasons for such cancellation as due to the use of technology in PDS operations i.e. digitization of ration cards data, Aadhaar seeding, detection of ineligible/ghost/fake ration cards/beneficiaries, continuous de-duplication, migration/deaths of beneficiaries, etc.**** Annexure-I(A)ANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (c) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION No. 1700  FOR ANSWER ON 08.12.2021 IN THE LOK SABHAAllocation & Offtake of foodgrains under AAY+PHH(In thousand tons)  S.
