                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2039






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Conversion of SHs into NHs




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has received any request/ representation from the Government of Tamil Nadu to convert eight State Highways into National Highways including Thiruvannamalai- Kallakurichi, Valliyur - Thiruchendur, Palani - Tarapuram, Arcot - Tindivanam, Mettupalayam - Bhavani, Avinashi - Mettupalayam and Bhavani - Karur; and(b) if so, the details thereof; along with action taken by the Union Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a)&(b) Yes Sir.  This Ministry has received request/representation for conversion of 8 State Highways (SHs) having total length of 500 Km. into National Highways (NHs) in the State of Tamil Nadu.  Ministry considers declaration of State Roads into new National Highways from time to time based on the requirement of connectivity, inter-se priority and availability of funds.*****
