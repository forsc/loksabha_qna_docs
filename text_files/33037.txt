                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

651






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Opening of Ayurtech Centres




Ramesh Bidhuri














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government proposes to open centre of Ayurtech to enhance contemporary viability of Ayurveda in the country;b)	if so, the details thereof;c)	whether the Government has taken any steps/measures for providing adequate AYUSH treatment facilities, especially in rural areas of the country;d)	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; ande)	steps taken/ being taken by the Government for setting up of new AYUSH Hospitals in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a)	& (b) Yes sir, Ministry of Ayush has developed a concept of “Ayurtech” to integrate technological advances in Ayurveda for the purpose of research, diagnosis and therapeutic procedures to enhance contemporary viability of Ayurveda in the country. For this purpose Ministry has supported IIT Jodhpur under the Centre of Excellence (CoE) component of Central Sector Scheme namely "Ayurswasthya Yojana. The Project Sanctioning Committee has approved total grant-in-aid of Rs.10.00 crore to IIT, Jodhpur.(c)	(d) & (e) i.	Ministry of Ayush through its National Institutes and Research councils conducts OPDs for providing adequate treatments facilities including rural areas. Medical camps are also organized by these National Institutes and Research Councils in rural areas. ii.	Under National Ayush Mission ( NAM) there is provision of financial assistance for establishment of new 10 /30 /50 bedded integrated AYUSH Hospitals, Establishment of AYUSH Heath & Wellness Centres (HWCs) by upgrading Ayush Dispensaries and Sub centres, Up gradation of AYUSH Hospitals & Dispensaries, Supply of Essential drugs to AYUSH Hospitals & Dispensaries and Collocation at Primary Health Centres, Community Health Centres (CHCs) & District Hospitals (DHs) in the States / UTs including rural areas. Accordingly, State/UT Governments may avail financial assistance by submitting the suitable proposals through State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs). Under NAM, State / UT wise status of approved units regarding upto 50 bedded integrated Ayush Hospitals, AYUSH HWCs, Up gradation of Ayush Hospitals & Dispensaries, Supply of Essential drugs to AYUSH Hospitals & Dispensaries and Co-location at Primary Health Centres, Community Health Centres (CHCs) & District Hospitals (DHs) is furnished at Annexure-I. iii.	Ministry of Ayush has established following hospitals attached to National Institutes of Ayurveda, Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha and Homoeopathy in different states to provide system specific health care-S.No.	Name of the Institute	No. of Beds1. North –Eastern Institute of Ayurveda and Homoeopathy, Shillong, Meghalaya	iv.	60 bedded Ayurveda Hospital v.	40 bedded Homoeopathy Hospital iv. In addition, Ministry also supports the private sector to encourage them to establish Ayush super specialty hospitals under Central Sector Scheme AYURSWASTHYA YOJANA and Medical Value Travel component of Central Sector Scheme for Champion Services Sector. vi.	Under Centre of Excellence (COE) Component of Central Sector Scheme of AYURSWASTHYA YOJANA, financial support upto Rs. 10.0 cr. is provided to support establishment of advanced/ specialized AYUSH medical health unit in reputed AYUSH and Allopathy institutions and under Medical value Travel component of Central Sector Scheme for Champion Services Sector financial assistance up to Rs. 8.15 cr. is given to the private investors in the form of interest subsidy.*****
