                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2137






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Hospital in Chhatisgarh under PMSSY




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether any multi-super specialty hospital is being constructed in Bilaspur, Chhatisgarh under ‘Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana’ (PMSSY);(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the time by which the construction work of the said hospital is likely to be completed; and(d) the amount of funds sanctioned and allocated by the Government so far for construction of the said hospital?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (d):	Upgradation of Government Medical College, Bilaspur has been taken up through construction of a Super-speciality Block at a cost of Rs. 200 crore (Central Government share: Rs.120 crore and  State Government share: Rs.80 crore) under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY). The timeline for completion of the project is February, 2021.An amount of Rs. 83.91 [Rupees eighty three crore and ninety one lakhs only] [Rs.32.21 crore for Civil work and Rs. 51.70 Crore for equipment] have been released so far to Executing Agency for construction work and to Procurement Support Agency for Medical Equipment respectively by this Ministry.
