                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1198






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Restructuring of Cadre




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the number of physiotherapists, ESI hospitals, associate offices and autonomous organizations in the country; (b)the details of their prevalent hierarchy; (c)the details of setting up and restructuring of the cadre;(d)whether Physiotherapists are included with similar opportunities in the event of restructuring of the cadre; and(e)if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a):		Under the Employees’ State Insurance Scheme (ESIS) there are a total of 49 hospitals in the country run by Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC).  There are 60 posts of Physiotherapists sanctioned for these hospitals.(b):		As per the Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (Staff and Conditions of Service) Amendment Regulations, 2016, Physiotherapist cadre has a two-post hierarchy, namely;(i)	Physiotherapist and(ii)	Sr. Physiotherapist (c) to (e):The restructuring of all medical and para-medical cadres including that of Physiotherapists in the ESIC is done from time to time, taking into consideration the norms under Central Health Service (CHS) of the Government of India.********
