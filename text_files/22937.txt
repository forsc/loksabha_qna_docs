                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4114






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Externally Aided Projects




Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                








                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                








                                                    Nisith Pramanik
                                                








                                                    Jayanta Kumar Roy
                                                








                                                    Bhola Singh
                                                








                                                    Vinod Kumar Sonkar
                                                








                                                    Rajveer (Raju Bhaiya) Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Ministry has been implementing various externally aided projects in several States of the country; (b)	if so, the details of such ongoing projects including funding agency, costs, outcome etc. State-wise;(c)	the details of such projects under consideration and pending so far ;(d)	whether any proposals/projects of State Governments are pending with the Ministry and if so, the details thereof;(e)	whether there is any monitoring mechanism to evaluate outcome of these projects to avoid time and cost overruns; and(f)	if so, the details thereof and the steps being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) & (b) 	Yes,Sir. Several international institutions such as Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA), French Development Agency (AFD), Deutsche Gesellschaftfür Internationale Zusammenarbeit (GIZ), KfW Bank and the World Bank provide financial assistance for forest development projects aimed at increase in forest cover, conservation of biodiversity, rehabilitation of degraded forest areas, soil & moisture conservation measures, capacity building of forest officials and other stakeholders, creation of livelihood opportunities for forest fringe villagers and other measures for sustainable management of forests.  The details of externally aided forest development projects being implemented by various State Forest Departments are given in Annexure.(c) & (d)  There is no project pending with the Ministry.(e) & (f) The mechanism for comprehensive monitoring and evaluation of any project are included in the project design itself. It includes provision for concurrent, mid-term and post project evaluation for performance as well as impact of the project. TheProject Implementing Agency (PIA) also submits quarterly progress report that are shared with the funding agency. After the completion of the project, there is provision for assessment by reputed independent institutions or organisations to ascertain the achievements and fulfillment of project objectives. ****
