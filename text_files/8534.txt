                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2092






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2019






Schools in Assam




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the schools and educational centres set up under Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA) in Assamparticularly in Dhubri and Goalpara districts during the last five years;(b) the details along with funds released for the purpose during the said period, district-wiseand year-wise;(c) whether the Government proposes to establish more schools and education centres inAssam particularly in Dhubri and Goalpara districts under SSA keeping in itsbackwardness;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): The Department of School Education and Literacy has launched an Integrated Schemefor School Education – Samagra Shiksha from 2018-19, subsuming three erstwhile CentrallySponsored Schemes of Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA), Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan(RMSA) and Teacher Education (TE). 34 Primary Schools, 47 Upper Primary Schools, 1Residential school, 1 hostel and 24 Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalayas (KGBV) were approvedfor Assam during the last five years and the current year. This includes 1 Primary school, 1 UpperPrimary school and 2 KGBVs for Goalpara district and 3 KGBVs for Dhubri district of Assam.As per the information received from the State Government, year-wise details of specialtraining centres for Out of School Children during the period from 2015-16 to 2019-20 is givenbelow :Interventions Dhubri Goalpara2015-162016-172017-182018-192019-202015-162016-172017-182018-192019-20Residential Special Training Centres 7 7 6 6 6 3 3 3 3 3Non Residential Special TrainingCentres180 169 139 139 139 62 62 62 62 62Support to Religious Madarsas 15 42 0 37 37 15 80 0 13 13Seasonal centres for Migratory Learners 34 33 27 18 18 10 10 8 7 0Total 236 251 172 200 200 90 155 73 85 78The State has also informed that an Adarsh Vidyalaya (Model School), Matia wasoperationalised in Goalpara District from the academic year 2015-16.(b): Under Samagra Shiksha, Government releases funds to the State as a whole. Funds arenot released District-wise. Year-wise details of funds released to the State of Assam underElementary Education during the last five years and the current year are as under:-<pre>Sl.No Year Amount (in Rs.lakh)1. 2014-15 97782.192. 2015-16 100464.643. 2016-17 87652.304. 2017-18 123584.005. 2018-19 143658.616. 2019-20(as on 28.11.2019)103452.94</pre>(c) to (e): The State Government submits proposals for setting up of schools and otherinterventions in their Annual Work Plan & Budget (AWP&B) which are considered by thisDepartment as per the norms of Samagra Shiksha.******
