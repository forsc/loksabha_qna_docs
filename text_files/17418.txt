                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

844






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Reserved Items for Small Scale Industry




Ramya  Haridas














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  	the criteria to bring a product under the category of reserved items for Small Scale Industry (SSI);(b)  	whether the Government has made any changes in this criteria to benefit multinational as well as big companies;(c)  	if so, the details of the changes made; and(d) 	the response of the Government on the justification of these changes?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) to (d): 	All the items reserved earlier for exclusive manufacturing in the Small Scale Industry (SSI) sector under the provisions of the Industries (Development & Regulation) Act, 1951 have been dereserved vide a Notification dated the 10th April,2015.
