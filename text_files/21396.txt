                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

232






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Internet Services to Remote Villages




C.N. Annadurai













                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) has decided to provide internet services to 5000 remote villages using Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) satellites; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the details of the villages that have been identified for this purpose, State/ UT-wise; (c) 	the time by which these villages will be provided with internet services; (d) 	whether there is no internet facility in remote villages dominated by SC/ST population and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor along with the reaction of the Government thereto; (e) 	whether internet/broadband speed is very slow in remote areas dominated by SC/ST population where internet/broadband facility is available; and (f) 	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor and also the efforts made by the Government to overcome this problem?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to  (f)	 A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT TO BE LAID ON THE TABLE OF LOK SABHA IN RESPECT OF PARTS (a) to (f) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 232 FOR 10TH MARCH, 2021  REGARDING “INTERNET SERVICES TO REMOTE VILLAGES ”.(a) to (c)     The Department of Telecommunications (DoT) has targeted to provide internet connectivity to 5,519 Gram Panchayats (GPs) (4,112 GPs by Bharat Broadband Network Ltd. (BBNL) by November 2021 and 1,407 by BSNL by June 2021) on Satellite Media under BharatNet Project. As on 01.03.2021, 3,587 GPs (BBNL: 2,432 and BSNL: 1,155) have been connected. The State/UT-wise details are at Annexure-I.(d) to (f)    High speed internet facility is being provided in rural areas by the Government and the Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) through 3G/4G mobile connectivity and through wireline connectivity. As per data collected from the Telecom Service Providers (TSPs) in 2020, it is estimated that out of 5,97,618 inhabited villages (as per Census 2011) in the country, a total number of 5,58,537 villages have 3G or 4G mobile wireless coverage. The services cover the entire population including SCs/ST+s of the area. State/UT wise details are given in Annexure-II.In addition, to improve the internet speed particularly in rural areas, BharatNet project is being implemented to connect all the GPs (approx. 2.5 lakh) by broadband/high speed internet infrastructure in the country. As on 26.02.2021, a total number of 1,54,096 GPs including Block Head Quarters (BHQs) have been made service ready with broadband infrastructure.
