                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1373






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Delhi Riots




Mohammad Jawed














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government intends to investigate the actions of the Delhi Police during the North East Delhi riots between February 23 and 29, 2020;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) whether the Government has taken cognizance of videographic footage showing that the Delhi Police used excessive force and also failed to do their duty by silently observing the violence instead of intervening to stop it during the riots; and(d) if so, the action proposed to be taken by the Government against the officers concerned?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d): During the North-East Delhi riots, Delhi Police acted swiftly in an impartial and fair manner. Proportionate and appropriate actions were taken by Delhi Police to control the situation. Sincere, dedicated and incessant efforts made by Delhi Police brought the riotous situation to normalcy within a short span of time and also prevented the riots from spreading across to other areas of Delhi/NCR.  The police used appropriate force to control the crowd and disperse it.   Necessary legal and preventive actions were taken by Delhi Police on all complaints/calls received, as per law and procedures.*****
