                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1298






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Horticulture Products in Jharkhand




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the major horticulture products grown and marketed in Jharkhand; (b) the details of gains for Jharkhand under the Integrated Development of Horticulture Scheme, especially with regard to post-harvest benefits; and (c) the details of import and export of horticultural products for India in the last decade?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????  (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)  Details of the major horticulture products grown and marketed in Jharkhand are at Annexure I. (b)   Details of physical achievements including post harvest management in Jharkhand under the major components of MIDH from 2014-15 to 2020-21 is as under:•	Area Expansion: An additional area of 35,078 ha has been covered under horticulture crops.•	Nurseries: - 6 new nurseries have been established for production of quality planting material.•	Protected Cultivation: An area of 2487 ha has been covered under protected cultivation.•	Water Resources: -    173 water harvesting structures have been created•	Beekeeping: 41800 bee colonies with hives have been distributed.•	Post Harvest Management: Under the component of Post Harvest Management, 2673 units including pack houses, cold storage units, refrigerated vans, primary/ mobile processing units, ripening chambers, pre cooling units attach to cold storages and  mobile pre cooling units have been established.•	Training of Farmers: Under HRD component, 39430 farmers have been trained various horticulture activities.During 2014-15 to 2020-21, an amount of Rs. 291.36 crore has been allocated to Jharkhand and out of which an amount of Rs. 160.11 crore has been released as per request of the State Government.(c)	The major horticulture commodities being exported are Banana, Orange, Grapes, Mango, Onion, Potato & Tomato etc. and the major horticulture commodities being imported are fruit/vegetable seeds, fresh fruits and vegetables, processed vegetables and processed fruits and juices. During the last decade 200 Lakh MT of horticulture commodities worth Rs. 52601 crore have been exported and 112 Lakh MT of horticulture commodities worth Rs. 126589 crore have been imported.
