                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4222






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Expansion of Rajahmundry Airport




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Rajahmundry airport has become the destination for the arrival of eleven flights and the departure of another eleven flights every day and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether there has been a four-time growth in the number of flights in a period of eight months and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether there is a demand for air conectivity from cities like Delhi, Kolkata and Mumbai to and from Rajahmundry; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the time by which this demand is likely to be met?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a):  Rajahmundry Airport handles arrival of 9 flights and the departure of 9 flights per day.(b): No, Sir. No such growth trend in the number of flights has been observed over a period of 8 months.;(c): So far, no airline has expressed interest to connect cities like Delhi, Kolkata and Mumbai to/from Rajahmundry.;(d): Does not arise.
