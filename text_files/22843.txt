                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3807






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Prime Minister‘s 15 Point Programme




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of State and district-level committees formed in West Bengal under Prime Minister‘s 15 Point Programme for welfare of minorities;(b)	the status and the details of the committees constituted for implementation of above programme in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts; and(c)	the details of the expenditure incurred for the implementation of the programme from 2014 and till date?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	: The State Government of West Bengal has informed that District Level Committees have been constituted in all the districts in addition to the State Level Committee, as per details given in Annexure – I.(b)	: The details of District Level Committees constituted in Darjeeling and Kalimpong districts are given in Annexure – II.(c)	: Under the Prime Minister’s New 15 PP for the Welfare of Minorities, various schemes/initiatives are being implemented by various Ministries/Departments of the Central Government through out the country, including Ministry of Minority Affairs either exclusively or by earmarking of 15% of overall physical/financial targets, wherever feasible, for the welfare of six notified minorities viz. Christians, Sikhs, Jains, Muslims, Buddhists and Parsis.  The details of expenditure incurred in this regard for financial years 2014-15 to 2020-21 and till date are available on the website of the Ministry www.minorityaffairs.gov.in.****
