                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4326






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Toll Booths in Rajasthan




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the number and the details of toll booths set up by the National Highways Authority of India on National Highways in the State of Rajasthan along with the date since when these booths are functioning, location-wise; (b)	whether the facility of return toll journey is not being given to the commuters at these toll booths; (c)	if so, the reasons therefor along with the steps taken by the Government to provide the facility of return toll journey at the said toll booths; (d)	whether the Government has provided or proposes to provide the facilities of free passes and monthly passes to the local residents/villagers/ commuters at these toll booths; and (e)	if so, the details thereof along with the criteria adopted for the purpose? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	 Details of operational Toll Plazas in the State of Rajasthan is at Annexure.(b) to (e)	No Sir.   NH Fee Rules provides for discounted passes for local resident residing within a distance of 20 km from the existing toll plaza in case of Public Funded Projects provided further that no such passes be issued if a service road or alternate road is available for use. However in case of Public Private Partnership Project, exemptions are provided in accordance with provision of concession agreement.
