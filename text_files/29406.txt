                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1105






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






AIIMS in Nanded




Prataprao Govindrao  Patil Chikhalikar














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received any proposal/or proposes to establish AIIMS in Nanded;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor particularly keeping in view the non-availability of any AIIMS like hospitalin the region??






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (c)   The Government has plans to set up AIIMS in each State in a phased manner.   As far as Maharashtra is concerned, setting up of an AIIMS at Nagpur has already been approved. No proposal for setting up of an AIIMS at Nanded, has been received from the State Government of Maharashtra
