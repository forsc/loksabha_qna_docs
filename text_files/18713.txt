                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

11






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Development of Animal Husbandry in Karnataka




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government is providing any financial assistance for the various projects in connection with the development of animal husbandry in Karnataka; (b) if so, the details thereof during the last three years; and (c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING                                    	(SHRI GIRIRAJ SINGH)  (a) to (c) A Statement is laid on the table of the house.***** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY OF LOK SABHA QUESTION NO. *11 PART (A) TO (C) FOR ANSWER ON 02.02.2021 ASKED BY SHRI G.M. SIDDESHWAR, REGARDING “DEVELOPMENT OF ANIMAL HUSBANDRY IN KARNATAKA”.(a) Yes, Sir. (b) The details of financial assistance provided to Karnataka under the various schemes being implemented by Government of India for the development of animal husbandry in Karnataka during the last three years are as under:Fund Released to the State Government of Karnataka during the last three years(Rs. In lakh)S. No.	Name of the scheme	Years		2017-18	2018-19	2019-201.	Rashtriya Gokul Mission	857.00	164.38	756.472.	National Programme for Dairy Development 	260.00	1011.00	2018.003.	National Livestock Mission	310.10	169.93	919.304.	Livestock Health & Disease Control	1646.00	1931.42	2977.34Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund (DIDF) (as on 30.11.2020)  (Rs in crore) No of Projects	Project Cost	Loan disbursed	Total	Loan	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	Total8	1626.39	931.37 	396.15	227.31	69.114	692.574 (c) Does not arise.*****
