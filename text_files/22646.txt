                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3731






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






AMRUT in Odisha




Basanta Kumar Panda














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the projects in operation at present in Kalahandi 	and Nuapada districts under AMRUT scheme; (b) 	the funds allocated for these projects; and (c) 	the time by which these projects are likely to be completed 	along with their present status?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                   (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a).	Nine cities of the State of Odisha, namely Bhadrak, Baleshwar, Baripada, Bhubaneswar, Berhampore, Cuttack, Puri, Rourkela and Sambalpur,have been selected under the Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT). None of these cities are located in Kalahandi and Nuapada districts of Odisha and, therefore, no projects have been taken up under AMRUT in these districts.(b) and (c).		Do not arise.                                         *****
