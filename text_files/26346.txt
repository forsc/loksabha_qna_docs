                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2078






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Languages in Eight Schedule




Mala Roy














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of funds spent on the promotion of Hindi language during the last five years;(b) the details funds spent on the promotion of other Indian languages; and(c) the reason for such disparity, if any?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (c) The policy of the Government is to promote all Indian languages. Funds are allocated/released to institutions as per their utilization and requirements. For the promotion and propagation of Hindi language, there are 3 institutions viz. Central Hindi Directorate (CHD), New Delhi, Commission for Scientific and Technical Terminology (CSTT), New Delhi and Kendriya Hindi Sansthan (KHS), Agra. Central Institute of Indian languages (CIIL), Mysore promotes all languages including Scheduled and Non-Scheduled languages except Sindhi, Urdu, Sanskrit, Hindi, and Tamil, for which separate institutions are there. The details of funds allocated for promotion of all Indian languages during last five years is as under:- Rs.
