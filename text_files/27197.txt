                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2834






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Status of NBMMP




B. Y. Raghavendra













                                                    Annasaheb Shankar Jolle
                                                








                                                    Umesh G. Jadhav
                                                








                                                    Anurag Sharma
                                                








                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                








                                                    Rebati Tripura
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is currently implementing National Biogas and Manure Management Programme (NBMMP) in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise including Karnataka;(c)	the funds allocated, released and utilized under the said programme during each of the last three years and the current year; and(d)	the details of number of biogas plants that have been  established  under  NBMMP  in  the  State  of Karnataka; and(e)	the further steps being taken or proposed to be taken  by  the  Government  for  increasing  biogas production  along  with  introduction  of  new  viable technology in biogas plants under NBMMP?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a)&(b) The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) has been implementing New National Biogas and Organic Manure Programme(NNBOMP) in the country, including in the State of Karnataka. Under the scheme, Central Financial Assistance (CFA) of Rs. 7500/- to Rs. 35,000/- per plant for supporting family-size biogas plants (1 to 25 m3 biogas per day) was being provided. This scheme was valid till 31.03.2021. The scheme has been proposed for continuation beyond 31.03.2021 i.e. from financial year 2021-22 to 2025-26.(c) The details of funds allocated, released and utilized under the above NNBOMP scheme during the last 3 years including the current year (2021-22) have been given in Annexure-I.(d)The total number of family-size biogas plants established under NBMMP and its successor scheme NNBOMP is 5,12,729  in the State of Karnataka.(e)The Third-party evaluation of NNBOMP has recommended for its continuation with increased CFA. This and other recommendations of third party evaluation have been considered while proposing continuation of NNBOMP beyond 31.03.2021. For supporting new viable designs of biogas plants, MNRE had constituted an expert committee for evaluation of the new designs which were invited through a request for proposal during January 2020.  The designs recommended by the expert committee have been put under long term field performance evaluation across different regions and climatic conditions in the country.*****
