                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4842






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






AYUSH Survey




Pallab Lochan Das














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government is planning to conduct first AYUSH survey;(b)	if so, the parameters of the proposed survey; and (c)	total sample size proposed and States/UTs expected to be covered in the survey? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a)	& (b): National Statistical Office (NSO), Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation (MoSPI) is planning to launch the first survey on AYUSH in the NSS 79th round during the period July 2022 – June 2023. The major parameters on which information has been proposed to be collected in this survey are as follows: -(i)	Awareness of the households about AYUSH system;(ii)	Use of AYUSH system and (iii)	Expenditure incurred for AYUSH treatment by the households.(c): The survey will be conducted in about 15,416 First Stage Units (FSUs)consisting of villages in rural areas and Urban Frame Survey (UFS) blocks in the urban areas. All the States/UTs will be covered in this survey.***
