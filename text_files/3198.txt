                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2747






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Non-functional Mobile Towers




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of non-functional BSNL mobile towers on account of nonpayment of dues to the land owners; and (b) 	the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) & (b) 	Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) has informed that its 258 mobile towers are non functional on account of non payment of dues to the land owners.  BSNL has also informed that building owners are being pursued and payment will be made in due course of time.
