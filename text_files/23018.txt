                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3927






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Pre-Delivery Hubs




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	whether the Government proposes to set up/establish Pre-Delivery hubs for the poor and marginalized families staying in remote and far-flung rural areas of the country to reduce the maternal deaths and other complications due to the enroute delays and other associated difficulties in such areas; and  (b) 	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER FOR WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) & (b) The Government does not propose to set up or establish pre-delivery hubs. However, support is provided under National Health Mission for Birth Waiting Homes. These Birth Waiting Homes are located in hard to reach areas so as to facilitate pregnant women for reaching the nearby facility before the due date of delivery. So far approval has been accorded for setting up Birth Waiting Homes in 15 states under National Health Mission. *****
