                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3427






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.12.2021






Construction Work on Savagaon-Belagavi Road




Mangal Suresh Angadi














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the construction work on the Savagaon-Belagavi road (MDR) near Belagavi City of Karnataka has not been completed due to objections of the Defence officials under the pretext that it passes through the Defence Land;(b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the measures contemplated by the Government to enable the local authorities to begin the improvement/ construction of the said road in the interest of public?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE				 	       (SHRI AJAY BHATT)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE  (a) to (c): A proposal for transfer of 2.718 acres of defence land for widening of Belagavi Sanvgaon Mandolli Road (Nanavadi to Sanvgoan-Mandolli connecting road via Angadi Engineering College) was received from State Government of Karnataka on the basis of exchange of Equal Value Land. Identification of suitable land for transfer to Ministry of Defence in lieu of defence land is to be done by the State Government before matter is processed further for grant of working permission under Ministry of Defence Policy Letter No. 11015/2/2012/D (Lands) dated 02.02.2016. *****
