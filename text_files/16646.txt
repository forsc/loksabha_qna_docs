                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

282






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Rural Development Scheme in Bharatpur District




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any schemes has been started for rural Development in Bharatpur District during the last three years;(b) 	if so, the amount released thereunder; and(c) 	if not, whether any scheme is under consideration in this regard for the next three years and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): During last three years, though no new schemes have been started for rural development in the Bharatpur District of Rajasthan, ongoing Rural Development Schemes of the Government, started earlier than last three years, are being continued in the District. Details are provided at Annexure-I.(b): Does not arise.(c): The ongoing schemes will run till their validity unless decided otherwise, in Bharatpur District of Rajasthan. ****                                                                                      Annexure-IAnnexure referred to in part (a) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 282 due for reply on 15.09.20201. Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY)Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) was launched in the year 2000 to provide all-weather road connectivity in rural areas. Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Rural (PMAY-G) The erstwhile Indira Awaas Yojana(IAY) has been restructured as Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana- Gramin(PMAY-G) w.e.f 1st April, 2016, aims to provide “Housing for All” through provision of pucca house with basic amenities to all houseless households and households living in kutcha and dilapidated house in rural areas by 2022. The overall target is to construct 2.95 Crore houses during the period 2016-17 to 2021-22, wherein 1 Crore house were set to be achieved in Phase-I (2016-17 to 2018-19) and 1.95 Crore houses in Phase-II (2019-20 to 2021-22).Funds utilized in the district of Bharatpur, Rajasthan under PMAY-G is as follows:                                                          (Rs.
