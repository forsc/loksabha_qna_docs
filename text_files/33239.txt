                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

581






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Rogi Kalyan Samitis




Amar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the Rogi Kalyan Samitis (RKS) and Hospital Management Societies (HMS) set up by the Government in rural and urban areas of the State of Punjab and Haryana during the last three years, year-wise and parliamentary constituency-wise;(b)	whether the RKS and HMS set up, were in any way beneficial in reducing the workload on health infrastructure during the COVID-19 lockdown, if so, the details of the study or survey conducted resulting in such findings; (c)	whether the Government is taking any measures to set up such Samiti and societies in the Parliamentary Constituency of Fatehgarh Sahib, Punjab, if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the funds allocated for setting up the samitis or societies along with the office responsible for the implementation thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) and (b) The Rogi Kalyan Samitis (RKSs)/ Hospital Management Society (HMS) act as a forum to improve the functioning and service provision in public health facilities, increase participation and enhance accountability. The broad objectives of the RKS are to serve as a consultative body to enable active citizen participation for the improvement of patient care and welfare in health facilities. Year wise details of the Rogi Kalyan Samitis (RKS)/ Hospital Management Societies (HMS) set up by the Government in rural and urban areas of the State of Punjab and Haryana during the last three years as per NHM-MIS report (as on 30.09.2021) is placed at Annexure-I. The parliamentary constituency-wise data of RKS/HMS is not maintained centrally. (c) As per the information received from State Government of Punjab, Rogi Kalyan Samitis have already been constituted in DHs, SDHs, CHCs and PHCs falling under Fatehgarh Sahib Constituency. (d) Public Health and Hospitals is a State Subject, the responsibility of strengthening public healthcare systems including institutional implementation of such samitis or societies lies with the respective State/ UT Governments. Under National Health Mission (NHM), technical and financial support is provided to States/UTs for strengthening their healthcare systems based on the proposals submitted by them in their Program Implementation Plans (PIPs), subject to availability of resources. However, approvals under Untied Funds/ Annual Maintenance Grants/ Corpus Grants to RKS/ HMS under NHM to Punjab and Haryana during the Financial Year 2021-22 (as on 30.09.2021) is placed at Annexure-II.
