                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2749






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






LPG Supply in Maharashtra




Supriya Sadanand Sule














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of villages in Maharashtra where the supply of LPG is yet to be provided along with the reasons for non-provision of LPG supply in the said areas and the time by which LPG supply is likely to be provided in a time bound manner; (b) whether the number of LPG distributors in Maharashtra is relatively less as compared to other States, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor along with the number of LPG distributors in Maharashtra; and (c) whether the Government proposes to select/appoint more distributors to meet the demand of the people in Maharashtra and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) to (c) Appointment of LPG distributorships is a continuous process and locations for setting up of LPG distributorships are identified based on sale potential that makes them commercially viable. Public Sector Oil Marketing Companies(OMCs) are engaged in extending the reach of LPG in villages/rural area  all over the country including in Maharashtra by setting up new distributorships and enrolling new LPG consumers on an ongoing basis. As on 01.03.2022, State of Maharashtra has  2214 distributors catering to 2.98 crore consumers, out of which 1210 distributors with 1.30 crore consumers are catering to rural areas/villages.
