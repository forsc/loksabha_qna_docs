                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1290






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Setting up of Compressed Biogas Plants




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) the number of Letter of Intent (LoI) issued till date to private developers with respect toSustainable Alternative towards Affordable Transportation scheme;(b) the details of developers who have submitted Expression of Interest (EoI) in setting up ofcompressed biogas plants;(c) the details of challenges currently faced by the Ministry in procuring the interest ofentrepreneurs and developers to establish such plants; and(d) the steps taken to address those challenges?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d): Sustainable Alternative Towards Affordable Transportation (SATAT) initiativewas launched on 1.10.2018 to promote Compressed Bio Gas (CBG) as an alternative, greentransport fuel for efficient management of biomass and organic waste. Under SATATinitiative, developers have submitted 590 Expression of Interest (EoI) to Indian OilCorporation Limited (IOCL), Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited (BPCL), HindustanPetroleum Corporation Limited (HPCL), Gail (India) Limited (GAIL) and Indraprastha GasLimited (IGL). IOCL, BPCL, HPCL, GAIL and IGL have issued 479 Letters of Intent (LoI).Ministry has taken up with authorities concerned to include CBG projects under PrioritySector Lending, categorise CBG projects under White Category of Industries and to includeBio manure produced from CBG plants under Fertiliser Control Order.*****
