                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3859






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Irregularities in Procurement of Paddy and Wheat




S.R. Parthiban














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total quantum of wheat and paddy procured during the last one year and the current year, State-wise; (b) 	the details of the complaints received about the irregularities in procurement of paddy and wheat from various States; (c) 	the reasons for the irregularities thereof; and (d) 	the steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a): 		State-wise procurement of wheat and paddy during the last one year and the current year is enclosed as Annexure-I & II respectively.(b) to (d): 	During  2008-19 (upto June, 2019), total 14 complaints were received in Food Corporation of India (FCI) about the irregularities in procurement of paddy and wheat.            These complaints were mainly related to purchase of non-Fair Average Quality (FAQ) paddy by agencies, fake purchase of paddy, non adherence of Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) & terms and condition of Model Tender Form (MTF) by private players, collection of illegal money for opening of purchase centre and making access payment to contractor etc.    These complaints were investigated and appropriate action taken by respective Regional and Zonal offices of FCI as per the prescribed guidelines. Out of these 14 complaints, 08 were found non-authenticated.  ***Annexure-IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3859 DUE FOR ANSWERED ON 16.07.2019 IN THE LOK SABHA.
