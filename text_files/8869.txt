                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2382






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Assistance for BADP to West Bengal




Khan Saumitra














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the funds allocated to West Bengal under Border Area Development Programme (BADP) during each of the last five years;(b) whether the Government has received utilisation certificate from the State Government;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the details of projects initiated in various border districts of West Bengal under this fund?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (c):	The details of funds released under Border Area Development Programme (BADP) to Government of West Bengal during the last five years i.e. 2014-15 to 2018-19 and utilization certificates received from the State Government is given in the table below: --2--LS.US.Q.NO. 2382 FOR 03.12.2019        	    Position as on 27.11.2019                                                  (? in Crore)Financial Year	Fund released (Central Share)	Utilization Certificates received (Central Share)2014-15	97.39	97.392015-16	161.79	161.792016-17	108.32	107.332017-18	122.00	90.092018-19	85.40	46.13Total	574.90502.73(d):	The State Government of West Bengal  has utilized the BADP funds on the activities related to the following sectors, namely; (i) Road connectivity, (ii) Water Supply, (iii) Education, (iv) Sports activities, (v) Social Infrastructure, (vi) Health, (vii) Power, (viii)  Agriculture & allied sectors, (ix) Skill development, etc.*****
