                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2963






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






MoU between NSIC and CSC




P.P. Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC) has signed an MoU with Common Service Centres (CSC) e-Governance Services India Ltd.; (b)	if so, the details thereof and the type of services that would be provided to MSME sector; (c)	whether any new platform would be designed or existing CSC platform would be used for above services; (d)	if so, the details thereof; and (e)	whether a training programme for the same has been planned and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a)to(e):        An Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) has been signed between National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC), the PSU under this Ministry and Common Service Centres (CSC), e- Governance Services India Ltd. on 22-06-2019 for enhancing new offerings  for the MSME sector by synergizing each  others''  competence. As per MoU, CSC will facilitate enrolment of Village Level Enterprises (VLEs) on the NSIC portal www.msmemart.com in order to utilise plethora of services provided to MSMEs, facilitate various services of NSIC to other MSMEs, disseminate information regarding NSIC services to its network of VLEs, develop & manage App for data collection of NSICs, maintain the MIS of the project and to provide data/APIs for integration with NSIC portal. Further, NSIC will provide details of various Services offers to MSMEs, necessary support to CSC for registration and enablement of VLEs on the portal, CSC- e-Governance Services India Limited formats/ templates for reporting, incentive of Rs. 100/- to CSC on each successful application on monthly basis.  Both NSIC and CSC shall take up any other activity as mutually agreed including Credit Support Services to MSMEs. They will use existing CSC platform to provide services to MSMEs. So far, no training programmes for disseminating the schemes of NSIC have been worked out by NSIC and CSC.
