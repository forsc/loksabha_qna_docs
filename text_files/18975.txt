                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

411






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Integrating UNSC




Thalikkottai Rajuthevar Baalu














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                      (a)	whether the Government has drawnup any action plan to integrate United Nations Security Council (UNSC) debate with India’s broader national goals;  (b)  	if so, the details thereof;   (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor; and  (d) 	the steps taken by the Government to enthuse its foreign policy to cope up with the uni-polar world?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) to (c)	The Government of India has taken the 5S approach as a member of the United Nations Security Council (UNSC) during 2021-2022 - Samman (respect), Samvad (dialogue), Sahyog (cooperation), Shanti (peace) and Samriddhi (peace). The Government of India is committed to reformed multilateralism that reflects contemporary realities. India shall seek responsible and inclusive solutions to the global challenges of today, guided by dialogue and cooperation, mutual respect and commitment to international law.(d) 	The Government of India is committed to achieving its foreign policy interests in a multipolar world.***
