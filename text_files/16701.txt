                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

331






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Production and Procurement of Foodgrains




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total quantum of foodgrains produced in the country and the quantum of foodgrains procured; (b) 	the total available and covered storage capacity in the country with the Government, State-wise/Union-territory-wise; (c) 	whether optimum utilisation of above capacity is being made; and (d) 	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a): 	The Details of the quantum of foodgrains production during crop year 2019-20 in the country along with their procurement at Minimum Support Price (MSP) by Government Agencies is as under: (In Lakh Metric Tonnes) S. No.	Crop	Production*	Procurement1	Rice	1184.30	511.40#2	Wheat	1075.90	389.853	Coarsegrains         (Jowar, Bajra, Maize & Ragi)	453.90	4.31#4	Pulses	231.50	28.44*As per Fourth Advance Estimates of Production of foodgrains during 2019-20, published by Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare.# As on 10.09.2020...........2/--2-(b): 	As on 31.07.2020, the total available storage capacity with Food Corporation of India (FCI) and State agencies for storage of foodgrains meant for PDS/NFSA throughout the country is 781.22 Lakh MT (FCI – 416.52 LMT and State Agencies – 364.70 LMT).  The State-wise details of  available storage capacity are at Annexure-I. In addition to the above capacity, approx. 111.61 LMT capacity is also being used by State Government for storage of commodities other than Wheat & Rice and also for intermediary storage for PDS stocks.(c) and (d): 	Adequate storage capacity is available with FCI for the safe storage and management of procured food grains. The storage capacity with FCI and the stock held during the last three years & the current year (as on 30th June) is as under : -As on	Covered & CAP(In Lakh Metric Tonnes)	Capacity	Stock held	Utilisation (in %)30.06.2017	363.54	297.63	8230.06.2018	381.06	334.97	8830.06.2019	407.31	374.53	9230.06.2020	414.09	366.96	89The average utilization of covered capacity as on 1st July of every year has been more than 80% during the last three years & current year.  Hence, storage capacity is optimally utilized.****
