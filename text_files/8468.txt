                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1980






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Medical College in Bijnor




Malook Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has announced to set up a medical college in Bijnor town in Uttar Pradesh, if so, the details thereof;(b) the time by which the construction work of the said college building is likely to be started; and(c) the reasons for non-compliance of the announcement made in 2016 for setting up of medical college in Bijnor, Uttar Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):	One medical college for district Bijnor in Uttar Pradesh State has been approved under Phase-III of the Centrally Sponsored Scheme for “Establishment of new medical colleges attached with existing district/referral hospitals” at a cost of Rs. 325 crore with fund sharing between the Centre and the State in the ratio of 60:40.        The Phase-III of the Scheme has been approved by the Cabinet on 28.8.2019 to    establish 75 more new medical colleges by 2022-23. The implementing agency in this regard is the concerned State Government.
