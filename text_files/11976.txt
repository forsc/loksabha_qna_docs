                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

759






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.02.2020






Sports Infrastructure in Rajasthan




Ranjeeta Koli














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has made efforts to set up sports infrastructure in Rajasthan;  (b) if so, the details thereof; and  (c) the amount allocated so far for the said purpose alongwith the details of the infrastructure set up in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)  FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS  { SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }  (a) to (c) Sports being a State subject the primary responsibility of development of sports related infrastructure rests with the concerned State/Union Territory Government. The Central Government supplements their efforts by bridging the critical gaps. Under the Khelo India Scheme of the Government of India, a total of 38 sports infrastructure projects have been sanctioned in the State of Rajasthan so far. The details of the same are at Annexure-I. In addition to this, the Government has also released Rs. 618.82 lakh to Sports Authority of India in 2018-19 for providing Sports equipment and creation of sports infrastructure in 20 schools / institutions in Jaipur (Rural).   ANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. †759 TO BE ANSWERED ON 06.02.2020 REGARDING “SPORTS INFRASTRUCTURE IN RAJASTHAN” ASKED BY SHRIMATI RANJEETA KOLI, HON’BLE MEMBER OF LOK SABHA *****
