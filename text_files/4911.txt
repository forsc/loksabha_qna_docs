                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4516






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Children Enrolled in Anganwadis




Pratima Bhoumik













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                








                                                    Devji Mansingram Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the total number of beneficiaries including children enrolled under Anganwadis at present along with the funds released for distribution of cooked food to the enrolled beneficiaries under ICDS; (b) 	whether the Government has noted that a large number of ghost/fake beneficiaries have been identified across the country; (c) 	if so, the details thereof along with the number of such ghost/fake beneficiaries, State/UT-wise; and (d) 	the steps taken by the Government to weed out ghost/fake beneficiaries under the said scheme?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILDDEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	The total number of beneficiaries including children enrolled under Anganwadi Services under Umbrella Integrated Child Development Schemeas on 31st March 2019 and funds released for Supplementary Nutrition Program for the year (2018-19), is as under:-Beneficiaries of Supplementary Nutrition	Fund Released for SNP(2018-19)Rs. in LakhsTotal Children (6 months - 6 years)	Pregnant Women & Lactating Mothers	Total Beneficiaries	7,03,74,122	1,71,86,549	8,75,60,671	8,47,591.84(b)	     & (d): No such survey has been conducted by the Ministry. Supplementary Nutrition Programme is implemented as Direct Benefit Transfer Scheme. The beneficiaries under the Anganwadi Services Scheme are identified on the basis of Aadhaar which is used as identity document for delivery of services or benefits which brings in transparency and efficiency and enables beneficiaries to get their entitlements. The beneficiaries not possessing the Aadhaar card are assisted by the field functionaries to obtain the same. Till such time they get the Adhaar card, they are provided the Anganwadi Services on the basis of alternative identification document. *********
