                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2399






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






Railway Remote Terminal in Kerala




Rajmohan Unnithan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Railway Remote Terminal/Satellite Hub is essential for the comprehensive railway development of Kasaragod district, Kerala as well as for the extension of some of the trains from Kannur to Kasaragod/Mangalore;(b) if so, the facts thereof;(c) whether more than thirty acres of land at Kumbala Railway Station is in the hands of the Railways; (d) if so, the steps taken/to be taken by the Railways to develop Kumbala Railway Station to a satellite hub, due to its proximity to the national highway;(e) whether preliminary steps have been taken to make Kumbala Railway Station as a Terminal Station; and(f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)  (a) No, Sir.	(b) Does not arise.(c) Approximately 25.3 acres of land at Kumbala Railway Station is in possession of Railways. This land is being used for Railways’ operational purposes viz. track, station, buildings etc.(d) Does not arise, as satellite coaching terminals are need based.(e) No, Sir.(f) Does not arise.*****
