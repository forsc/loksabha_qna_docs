                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2255






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






New Trains in Karnataka




Sanganna Amarappa Karadi














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to announce some new trains in Karnataka, especially for the Koppal constituency; and (b) if so, the details of the trains that are likely to be provided?	






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) and (b) Indian Railways do not run train services on State-wise basis as railway network straddles across State boundaries. Koppal and Gangawati stations are at present served by two pairs of trains that is one pair of express services and one pair of passenger services.  However, introduction of trains is an ongoing process over Indian Railways subject to operational feasibility, traffic demand, availability of resources etc.*****
