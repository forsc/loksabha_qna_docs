                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3224






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Caste based Census




Kaushalendra Kumar













                                                    Dinesh Chandra Yadav
                                                








                                                    Santosh Kumar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any plan to conduct Caste based Census during the forthcoming census in 2021-22;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI) (a) to (c): The Questionnaires for the forthcoming Census are finalised in  consultation with various stakeholders. In Census, the castes and tribes which are specifically notified as Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes as per the Constitution (Scheduled Castes) Order, 1950 and the Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order, 1950 (as amended from time to time) are enumerated. The Union of India after independence, decided as a matter of policy not to enumerate caste wise population other than SCs and STs.*****
