                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4741






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Widening of National Highways




Arun Kumar Sagar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)     whether the Government has taken steps for renovation and        	widening of National Highways crossing through Shahjahanpur      	Parliamentarly Constituency of Uttar Pradesh;(b)     if so, the details thereof along with the funds allocated in this       	regard;(c)     whether the construction work is progressing as per schedule;(d)     if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(e)     the steps taken by the Government to complete the construction          work within the stipulated time period? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT & HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) & (b) Yes Sir.  Details of NH works crossing through Shahjahanpur and funds approved is at Annexure.(c) to (e)  The work of 4-laning of Bareilly-Sitapur section of NH-24 had been terminated by NHAI on 13.04.2019 due to poor performance of the Concessionaire. The balance work commenced from 17.12.2019. The work has been delayed due to COVID-19 Pandemic and variation in the quantities of BOQ. The work from Km. 262.000 to Km. 325.620 is expected to be completed by April 2022 and   remaining stretch from km.325.620 to km.419.590 is likely to be completed by December 2022.   The work of ROB at Ch 290+445 at Bareilly Sitapur section of NH-24 (Hulas Nagla, Katra) was delayed due to procedural delays by Railways which required fabrication of new girders and new approval process. The work of ROB is expected to be completed by April 2022.
