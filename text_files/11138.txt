                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

35






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Rozgar Mela




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Rozgar Melas conducted in the country, year-wise and State-wise since the launch of the Skill India Mission and the funds sanctioned and utilized for each of the Rozgar Mela along with the number of participants – job providers and job seekers;(b)	the number of such Rozgar Melas conducted with the support of the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) and Sector Skill Councils, the success of each Rozgar Mela – number of candidates who secured employment in each of the above Rozgar Melas;(c)	the number of Training Providers as on January 2020 and the number of TPs conducting Rozgar Melas, State-wise every six months as mandated in Skill India Mission; and(d)	the number of Training Centres, State-wise and year-wise who have been given reimbursement of last 20 percent of training payout upon successful placement of a candidate and the details of these TCs, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c)	Rozgar Melas are conducted by NSDC, PMKKs, SSCs and TPs  as a part of overall “Placement incentives & Job Melas / Rozgar Utsav” component of PMKVY (2016-20). The State-wise and Agency-wise number of Rozgar Melas conducted is given at Annexure.
