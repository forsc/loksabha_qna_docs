                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

235






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






Fees for KCC




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the various kinds of fees, surcharges, etc. collected by the Government and private banks from farmers to provide Kisan Credit Cards (KCC) before and after KCC approval; and(b)	 the fees, surcharge and other charges paid by the farmers for the renewal of KCC in the bank despite the amount paid by the farmers at the first approval along with the details of the provision made in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE ???? ?? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b): 	As per the instructions issued by Reserve Bank of India regarding Kisan Credit Card Scheme, processing fee, inspection charges and other charges may be decided by banks as per their board approved guidelines. However, Indian Bank Association has waived off all charges including processing, documentation, inspection and ledger folio charges as well as other service charges for KCC loans upto Rs. 3 lakhs, for the welfare of Small and Marginal farmers.*****
