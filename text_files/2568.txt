                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2445






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Kendriya Vidyalaya in Farrukhabad




Mukesh Rajput














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the reasons for delay in construction of another KendriyaVidyalaya approved forthe Parliamentary Constituency, Farrukhabad;(b) the name of the place where the said Vidyalaya is likely to be opened; and(c) whether the land has been acquired for the said Vidyalaya and if so, the detailsthereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) has informed that a proposal foropening of new KV at Pipargaon, district Farrukhabad, Fatehgarh had been receivedfrom the district administration. However, the proposal was not found to be meetingthe prerequisite as per norms of KVS, for opening of new KVs.(c) Does not arise.*****
