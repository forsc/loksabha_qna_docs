                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2908






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.03.2020






Passport Centre in Post Offices




Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has started passport centres in post offices in India; (b) if so, the details of the post offices where passport applications are received in the country and Gujarat in particular; and (c) the details of the passport centres at Sabarkantha district in Gujarat? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) The Ministry, in association with the Department of Posts (DOP), announced an innovative initiative on 24 January, 2017 to open Passport Seva Kendras (PSK) at Head Post Offices (HPO)/ Post Offices (PO) in the country called ‘Post Office Passport Seva Kendra’ (POPSK). The Ministry has operationalized 424 POPSKs till   5 March, 2020. (b) The details of 424 POPSKs in the country is at Annexure – I, out of which 23 POPSK are in Gujarat. (c) There is a POPSK functional at Himmatnagar in the Sabarkantha district in Gujarat. ***
