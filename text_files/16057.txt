                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4591






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Adolescent Friendly Health Clinics (AFHCs)




Arun Sao













                                                    Kaushal Kishore
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of adolescent friendly health clinics set up across the country, State/UT-wise and district wise;(b) the number of counsellors recruited in the clinics, State/UT-wise, particularly in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh; and(c) whether the Government proposes to set up such clinics in Primary Health Centres of villages, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	The State/UT-wise number of Adolescent Friendly Health Clinics (AFHCs) and the number of dedicated Adolescent Health Counsellors posted at the AFHCs, is placed at Annexure. District-wise data on AFHC is not maintained centrally.Lucknow in Uttar Pradesh has only 1 (one) Adolescent Health Counsellor in position.(c):	As per the guidelines, under Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram (RKSK), the Adolescent Friendly Health Clinics are established at the District and Block level onlyHowever, the healthcare providers at lower level health facilities including Primary Health Centres are trained in providing adolescent friendly health services.
