                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3801






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Roaming Facility for BSNL




Rodmal Nagar













                                                    Janardan Mishra
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has formulated a plan regarding roaming facility to be provided by Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL); (b) 	if so, the names of areas in the country where roaming facility of BSNL has been provided, State/UT-wise; (c) 	if not, the plan proposed by the Government in this regard; and (d) 	the names of the places outside the country like Nepal where roaming facility for BSNL has been provided?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS,  HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a) to (c) 	Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) has informed that it is providing roaming facility to its customers throughout the country except prepaid in-roaming and out-roaming in Jammu and Kashmir.(d) 	BSNL has informed that it is providing international roaming facility to its customers in 179 countries including Nepal.  List of countries where international roaming facility of BSNL is available is at Annexure.
