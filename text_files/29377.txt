                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Subsidy on Kerosene and LPG




Deepak (Dev) Adhikari














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the changes in subsidy for Kerosene and LPG given by the Government during each of the last five years along with the reasons therefor; and (b) the details of the consumption of Kerosene and LPG changes year on year during this period and the reasons therefor?
