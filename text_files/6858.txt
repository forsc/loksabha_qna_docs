                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

572






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Army Recruitment Camps




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of army recruitment made by the Army Recruiting Offices in various States during the last three years and the current year, year and State-wise;(b) whether it is true that only three recruitment camps each in 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19 have been conducted in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana which are much less when compared to many other States and if so, the reasons therefor;(c) whether it is also true that in 2019-20 only one recruitment camp was held in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana;(d) if so, whether there are any plans to hold more number of camps in these States and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(e) the criteria being adopted in holding the camps and number of youth selected during the above period, district-wise in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a): State-wise details of army recruitment made by the Army Recruiting Offices during the last three years are at Annexure-I.  (b): The recruitment in the Army is done regularly as per pre-planned schedule for every recruiting year, commencing from 1st April of a year to 31st March of the next year. Every district of the country is covered at least once in a year for recruitment. Accordingly, three recruitment camps each in 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19 were conducted in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. Adequate number of recruitment camps has been conducted to cover each district in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. (c) & (d): No, Sir; In 2019-20, four recruitment camps have been held in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. (e) Since July 2015, the recruitment process has shifted from ‘Open Rally System’ to ‘Online Registration’ by the candidates, wherein, candidates aspiring to join Army register themselves online on the Recruiting Directorate Website (www.joinindianarmy.nic.in), after which they are called for screening at nominated recruitment venues on specified date and time. District-wise details of number of youth selected during the years 2016-17, 2017-18 & 2018-19 in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana are at Annexure-II.*****
