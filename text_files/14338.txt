                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3105






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Road Projects




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the present status of various projects such as six lane bypass on NH-16 from 	Chinnavutupally to Gollapudi on Vijayawada-Gundugolanu section and from 	Chinakakani to Gollapudi including an iconic bridge on Krishna river on NH-16 in 	two packages, project-wise; and (b)	the time-frame set for completion of these projects?	






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)&(b) 6 laning of Vijayawada bypass for a length of 47.88 Km has been taken up in two packages namely  Chinnavutupally to Gollapudi and Chinnakakani to Gollapudi on Hybrid Annuity Mode. The Letter of Award for civil works for both the packages has been issued with scheduled date for completion as 24 months and 30 months from appointed date respectively. *****
