                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2341






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






PM-KISAN Beneficiaries in Maharashtra




Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar













                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                








                                                     Bhavana Gawali (Patil)
                                                








                                                    Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the number of farmers targeted and benefited under the PM-KISAN scheme in Maharashtra including the details of fund allocation for the same;(b) whether the target has not been achieved, if so, the reasons therefor;(c) the details of the number of beneficiaries along with the amount paid to them and the number of installments given under PM-KISAN scheme in Maharashtra since its inception, year-wise and district-wise;(d) the number of eligible farmers who remain deprived of availing benefits under PM-KISAN scheme in Maharashtra, district-wise;(e) whether the number of registered farmers has increased in Maharashtra under this scheme, if so, the details thereof; and(f) Whether the Government has any plan to increase income support under this scheme to help farmers across the country and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	There is a target to cover all the eligible landholding farmers under Pradhan Manatri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Scheme. It is a continuous and ongoing scheme, where farmer can get registered at any time.  As on 03.03.2021, 1,09,88,399 number of farmer families have been benefitted under the Scheme in Maharashtra based on the corrected and validated data received. There is no state-wise allocation of funds under the PM-KISAN scheme. Funds are released based on the verified data of eligible beneficiaries received from the States.(b) 	PM-KISAN is a continuous and ongoing Scheme, and as and when data of the eligible beneficiaries are received from the States, the requisite amount is transferred to the account of beneficiaries through Direct Benefit Transfer. Therefore, the question of achieving the target is not applicable.(c)	The details of the number of beneficiaries along with the amount paid to them and the number of installments under PM-KISAN Scheme in Maharashtra, district-wise is given at Annexure-I.(d) 	The eligible beneficiaries are not deprived of the benefits of PM-KISAN as it is a continuous and ongoing Scheme, and as and when data of the eligible beneficiaries are received from the States, the requisite amount is transferred to the account of beneficiaries through Direct Benefit Transfer.(e)	Yes, the process of registration of beneficiaries under the Scheme is a continuous process and ongoing process. Till date, 1,13,91,985 number of farmers are registered under the scheme in Maharashtra and the number keeps on changing as when new beneficiary is registered.(f)	 There is no plan to increase direct income support under this Scheme at present.
