                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2766






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Geo-spatial Mapping of Border Areas by Private Entities




Ravneet Singh














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)     whether the Government proposes to permit geospatial mapping of border and coastal areas by private entities, if so, the details thereof;(b)     whether this proposal have been opposed by the defence establishment for security reasons and if so, the response of the Government thereto;(c)     whether the Government undertook due consultation process with all the stakeholders including the armed forces while formulating the said proposal; and(d)     if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE; MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY; AND MINISTER OF EARTH SCIENCES(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)?????????  ??  ??????  ??????  ??????, ??????? ?? ???????????? ?????? ?? ?????? ???????  ??????(??. ???? ?????) (a):    Department of Science & Technology has issued Guidelines for “acquiring and producing geospatial data and geospatial data services including maps” on 15th February, 2021 which is applicable to all stakeholders, save as specifically provided for under the said guidelines, within the territory of India. (b) to (d):    The guidelines have been issued pursuant to recommendations received after due deliberations of the Committee of Secretaries which was represented by the Ministry of Defence also.  The Ministry of Defence consulted all  their stakeholders, after which the Committee of Secretaries recommended for the issuance of the guidelines.*****
