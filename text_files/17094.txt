                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

721






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Commercial Use of Drones




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to grant permission to drones intended for commercial and civilian usage;(b) if so, whether there will be centralised monitoring mechanism to ensure that all the procedures are followed and permission taken for flying drones; and(c) the details of the mechanism providing a way to monitor how drones collect, use, store and share data?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) Yes, Sir. Operation of Remotely Piloted Aircraft System (RPAS), commonly known as drones, is regulated by the Aircraft Rules, 1937 and Civil Aviation Requirements (CAR) Section 3, Series X, Part 1 issued by Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA).(b) The centralised monitoring mechanism namely Digital Sky Platform seeks to process the various activities pertaining to flying of drones viz issuance of Unique Identification Number (UIN), Unmanned Aircraft Operator Permit (UAOP), day-to-day permissions for the flights of Remotely Piloted Aircraft etc.;(c) The Digital Sky Platform is at present running on beta version. It seeks to regulate the activities relating to registration of drones and its operators and granting of permission for flying. However, the data collected by the drones during its flight is not monitored by the said platform.
