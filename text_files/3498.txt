                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3231






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






ANM & GNM under NRHM




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has recruited Auxiliary Nursing Midwifery (ANM) and General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM) workers on temporary/ contractual basis to work for healthcare services in the country under National Rural Health Mission (NRHM); (b) if so, the details thereof, Statewise including Rajasthan; (c) the district-wise number of employees working on temporary/ contractual basis particularly in Rajasthan; (d) whether the Government proposes to make such employees permanent; and (e) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):    Public Health and Hospital being a State Subject, all the administrative and personnel matters, including that of recruitment of Auxiliary Nursing Midwifery (ANM) and General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM), lies in the jurisdiction of the respective State Governments. Under National Health Mission, technical and financial support is provided for States/UTs for strengthening their healthcare system including support for engagement of nursing staff on contractual basis, based on requirements posed by the States in their Programme Implementation Plans (PIPs), within their overall resource envelope. Accordingly, State/UT wise, details of ANM and Staff Nurses supported under NHM for FY 2019-20 is given at Annexure. The district-wise number of NHM employees working on temporary/contractual basis including the State of Rajasthan is not maintained centrally.(d) & (e):  As stated above, public health being a state subject, all the administrative and personnel matters, falls within the purview of respective State Governments.……………….
