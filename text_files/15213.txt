                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3819






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Rural Roads in Andhra Pradesh




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total length of surfaced rural roads laid in Andhra Pradesh as on 2019, district-wise;(b) 	the percentage contribution of Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana in this regard; and(c) 	whether the ratio of surfaced roads to the total length of roads in the district of Chittoor is greater than that of other districts in the State and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) The total length of surfaced rural roads  in Andhra Pradesh as on 2019 is 28,502.59 km. District wise details is at Annexure.  (b)  A total of 14,214.193 km of rural roads have been constructed under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) which is 49.87 %  of total surfaced roads (28,502.59 km) laid in Andhra Pradesh. (c)   The ratio of surfaced roads to the total length of roads in the district of Chittoor (0.39) is greater than the districts of Anantapur (0.36), Guntur (0.32), Kurnool (0.30), Prakasam (0.29) and Y S R Kadapa (0.21).  District wise details is at Annexure.******    ANNEXUREAnnexure referred to in part (a) and (c) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 3819 to be answered on 17.03.2020
