                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2776






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Construction of Private Domestic/ Public Toilets




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of private domestic toilets i.e. Individual Household Latrines (IHHL) and Community/ Public Toilets (CT/PT) constructed in the country along with the details thereof, State/UT-wise; and(b) 	the number of private domestic toilets demanded by the people of the most backward classes in Uttar Pradesh during the last three years and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) :  State/ UT-wise details of Individual Household Latrines (IHHLs) and Community/ Public Toilets (CT/PTs) constructed in urban areas of the country is at Annexure-I and II.(b) : 	Benefit of IHHL is extended to all applicants who do not have private household toilets, irrespective of caste, class or religion. Data is not kept on the basis of any particular community/ group.                                        *****
