                                    DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2288






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Special Packages to Assam




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is providing funds to Assam under various special packages/schemes;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the expenditure incurred under each package since the announcement of such packages, year-wise; and(d) 	the details of projects sanctioned under each package, till date?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (Independent Charge) of the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region[Dr. Jitendra Singh] (a) and (b)	Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region (MDoNER) provides funds to the State of Assam under special development packages for Bodoland Territorial Council (BTC), Dima Hasao Autonomous Territorial Council (DHATC) and Karbi Anglong Autonomous Territorial Council (KAATC). Under BTC package of Rs.750.00 crore, 65 projects costing Rs.749.64 crore have been sanctioned against which, amount of Rs.711.19 crore has been released. Under KAATC package of Rs.350.00 crore, 20 projects costing Rs.187.28 crore have been sanctioned and Rs.95.71 crore has been released. Under special development package of Rs.200.00 crore for DHATC, 10 projects costing Rs.121.39 crore have been sanctioned and Rs.73.80 crore has been released.(c)	The details of funds released by MDoNER to Government of Assam under the special development packages are as under:-                                                               (Rs. in crore)<pre>Package	Funds released	Since inception upto 2014	2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	TotalBTC	573.61	30.27	30.00	50.00	23.46	0.00	3.85	711.19KAATC	1.72	10.51	5.15	3.44	0.00	39.62	35.27	95.71DHATC	0.00	0.00	37.24	0.08	23.37	12.00	1.11	73.80</pre>(d)	The details of projects sanctioned under special development packages, till date are as under:-<pre>S.
