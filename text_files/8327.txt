                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1946






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Ayurvedic Herbs Collection Centres




Sumedhanand Saraswati














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)   be pleased to state: (a) whether there is a huge demand for indigenous herbs across the country, if so, the details thereof; (b) the details and number of such Ayurvedic herbs storage and collection centres across the country, State/UT-wise;(c) whether the Government is considering to increase the number of such centres in the country especially in the hilly areas; and (d) if so, the details thereof along with the time by which such centres are likely to be set up?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):	Yes, there is huge demand for indigenous herbs/ medicinal plants across the country. As per study conducted by National Medicinal Plants Board (NMPB), Ministry of AYUSH, Government of India on Demand & Supply of medicinal plants in the country, the annual consumption of indigenous herbs/ medicinal plants is about 5 Lakh Metric Tonnes in 2014-15.(b) to (d):	Under ‘Medicinal Plants’ component of Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM), the Ministry of AYUSH, Government of India has been supporting various activities relating to cultivation, Post-Harvest Management and   marketing of medicinal plants/herbs throughout the country including hilly areas. Under ‘Medicinal Plants’ component of NAM Scheme there is also a provision to provide support for Storage Godowns/ Drying Sheds and establishing Herbs Collection Centres at the Village/District/State level based on the State Annual Action Plan (SAAP) submitted by concerned State. 	The State/UTs-wise detail of Storage Godowns/ Drying Sheds and Herbs Collection Centres supported under the NAM scheme till date is at Annexure -I.	In addition to above, the Ministry of AYUSH under its “Central Sector Scheme on Conservation, Development and Sustainable Management of Medicinal Plants” also provide project based support to Joint Forest Management Committees (JFMCs)/Block Management Committees (BMCs) etc. for creation of facilities like Storage Godowns, Drying Sheds for value addition of medicinal plants material. The State/UT-wise detail of Storage Godowns/ Drying Sheds supported to JFMCs/ BMCs etc. during last five years is at Annexure – II.
