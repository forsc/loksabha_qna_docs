                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4589






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Mahatma Gandhi Bunkar Bima Yojana




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the Mahatma Gandhi Bunkar Bima Yojana and objectives and eligibility set under the said scheme;(b) the number of persons provided assistance under the said scheme during the last three years, State-wise including Rajasthan, Jharkhand, Maharashtra; and(c) the efforts being made by the Government to provide benefit to more and more people under the said scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a):	The Government of India, Ministry of Textiles is implementing converged Mahatma Gandhi Bunkar Bima Yojana (MGBBY) for providing Social Security benefits like life, accidental & disability insurance coverage to handloom weavers/workers in the age group of 51-59 years across the country, who have already enrolled under the scheme on 31.05.2017. The annual premium of Rs.470/- is shared as under:- Government of India	Rs.290/-Weaver	Rs.80/-Implementing Agency 	Rs.100/-Total Premium	Rs.470/-Benefits: Natural Death	Rs.60,000/-Accidental Death	Rs.1,50,000/-Total Disability	Rs.1,50,000/-Partial Disability	Rs.75,000/-(b):	The State-wise number of claims settled under the above said scheme during the last three years including in Rajasthan, Jharkhand and Maharashtra is annexed as Annexure-I.(c):	To provide the benefits to all handloom weavers/workers in an effective manner, the Government of India has organized Hasthkala Sahyog Shivirs in handloom clusters across the country in association with State Governments and LIC for creating awareness among the weavers for enrollment under the Scheme. The claim benefits are provided by LIC directly into the bank account of beneficiaries through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT). 	Further, the Government of India, Ministry of Textiles through its Weavers’ Service Centres regularly organizes awareness programmes and camps to facilitate enrolment of handloom weavers/workers under Government Flagship Insurance Schemes.
