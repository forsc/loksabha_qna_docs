                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1877






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Funds for Green Environment




Sunil Kumar Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the funds allocated under various schemes for creating green environment during the last five years and the current year, State/UT-wise; and(b) 	the details of the recruitment for the said purpose during the last six years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	Funds are released / allocated to States/UTs from the Schemes pertaining to Centrally Sponsored Schemes only. The details of funds allocated under Centrally Sponsored Schemes of Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change for creating green environment during the last five years and the current year, State/UT-wise are as given in Annexure.(b)	No specific recruitment has been made for the said purpose in the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change. Implementing agencies of this Scheme are respective States/UTs. Ministries release/allocate funds to States/UTs as per their Annual Plan of Operation (APO) only. *****
