                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1699






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Sagarmala Project




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the percentage of work completed in Sagarmala project till date;(b)	whether any survey has been conducted about displacement of people after     completion of Sagarmala project; and(c)	if so, the details of the villages and people to be displaced from the Indian coastal belt after implementation of the project?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Under Sagarmala, 504 Project at an estimated infrastructure investment of Rs.3.55 Lakhs Crores have been identified. Out of these, 159 Projects have been completed.(b)	No, Sir.(c)	In view of item ''b'' above, the question does not arise.                                    *****
