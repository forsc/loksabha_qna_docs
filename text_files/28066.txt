                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3657






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






DISHA in Uttar Pradesh




Devendra (Alias) Bhole Singh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the progress made by District Development Coordination and Monitoring Committee (DISHA) inUttar Pradesh, district-wise;(b) 	whether the information is conveyed to the Government by the secretaries of DISHA officials related to irregularities and negligence by the officials in the schemes of the Government during the meeting of DISHA, if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	the number of cases in which action has been taken by the Government against the guilty officials during the last three years; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)	District Development Coordination and Monitoring Committees (DISHA) have been constituted in 75 Districts of Uttar Pradesh. District-wise details of the meetings of DISHA meetings held in Uttar  Pradesh is at Annexure-I. (b) to (d ) No such information is conveyed by the secretaries of DISHA Officials to the Government.  However, matter related to irregularities and negligence by officials in the implementation of schemes of the Government has been raised during the meetings of DISHA Committee of Kanpur Dehat. Details of the cases and action taken against the officials are at Annexure-II. ******
