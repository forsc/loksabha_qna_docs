                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2837






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Radhopur-Forbisganj Railway Line




Dileshwar Kamait














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Radhopur-Forbisganj (Bihar) railway line was damaged in the 2008 due to catastrophic destruction brought by Kosi tragedy and as a result the traffic has been completely stopped on this line;(b) if so, whether the Government proposes to restart the railway line to develop the basic traffic infrastructure in this important region; and(c) if so, the steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) Yes, Sir.(b) & (c) Traffic was restored after completing works on 20.09.2009 and continued till December, 2011 when traffic was stopped again for gauge conversion works in January, 2012. Progress of gauge conversion works dampened due to floods in river basin which caused breaches/washouts at number of locations. Target has been fixed as under:(i) Raghopur-Chhatapur Road – March 2020.(ii) Chhatapur Road-Forbisganj – March 2021.  *****
