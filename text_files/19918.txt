                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1323






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Funds allocated to Bihar for Fisheries




Ram Kripal Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the funds allocated to Bihar for fisheries and funds utilised during the last three years;(b)	the details of facilities provided to fishermen under fisheries schemes; and (c)	 the details of the development made in Bihar regarding employment generation and fish production during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRYAND DAIRYING(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) to (c) :  The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Fisheries Husbandry and Dairyingduring the past three years (2017-18 to 2019-20) has allocated 56.38 Crores during last three years to Government of Bihar for development of fisheries in the State under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme (CSS): Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and Management of Fisheries and state Government utilised Rs. 19.52 crore of the central funds released.  The central financial assistance had been provided to the Government of Bihar for establishment of (i) fin fish hatcheries hatcheries, (ii) brood bank, (iii) construction of new ponds, (iv) fish seed rearing unit, (v) development of wetlands, (vi) cage/pen culture, (vii) establishment of re-circulatory aquaculture system (RAS), (viii) construction of biofloc ponds for aquaculture, (ix) whole sale fish markets, (x) retail fish markets, (xi) State level, (xii) Disease diagnostic, (xiii) feed, soil and water testing lab (xiv) housing for fishers. The fish production in Bihar has shown an increasing trend during the past three years from 5.87 lakh during 2017-18 to 6.41 lakh tonne during 2019-20.****
