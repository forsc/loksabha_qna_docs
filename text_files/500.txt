                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

617






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Welfare Schemes for Senior Citizens




N.K. Premachandran














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to launch special programme for resolving the issues of senior citizens and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether it has come to the notice of the Government that a good number of senior citizens were deserted by their children; (c) if so, the action taken by the Government to protect the rights of senior citizens to stay with their children; (d) whether the Government proposes to introduce any scheme for ensuring the economic stability of senior citizens and if so, the details thereof; (e) whether the Government proposes to provide the service of experts in the field of psychology so as to resolve the psychological problems of senior citizens; and (f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a): Different Ministries & Department of Government of India run several Schemes and Programmes for the welfare of Senior Citizens, the details of which are at ANNEXURE.(b) & (c): It is understood from media reports that such instances happen. However data in this regard, is not maintained in this Ministry. Further, as per the Seventh Scheduled of the Constitution of India. ‘Police’ and ‘Public Order’ are State subjects, and as such the primary responsibility of prevention, detection, registration, investigation and prosecution of crime including crime against Senior Citizens lies with the State Governments/UT Administrations.(d) For economic stability, Schemes of Pradhan Mantri Vaya Vandana  Yojana (PMVVY) and Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme (IGNOAPS) under National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP) are being implemented by Department of Financial Service, Ministry of Finance and Ministry of Rural Development respectively.(e) &(f): As informed by the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, the Government of India is implementing the National Mental Health Programme (NMHP) since 1982 to address the burden of mental disorders including for Senior Citizens. The Government is supporting implementation of the District Mental Health Programme (DMHP) under NMHP in 655 districts of the country for detection, management and treatment of mental disorder/illness. The Government, under the National Mental Health Programme (NMHP), is also implementing manpower development schemes for establishment of Centres for Excellence and strengthening/ establishment of Post Graduate (PG) Departments in mental health specialities, with the objective to address the acute shortage of qualified mental health professionals in the country. Till date, support has been provided for establishment of 23 Centres of Excellence and strengthening/ establishment of 46 Post Graduate (PG) Departments in mental health specialities in the country.****
