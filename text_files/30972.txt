                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2472






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Courses in Industrial Training Institute




 Sunil Kumar Pintu














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Electronics and Information Technology courses are not offered in polytechnics and ITIs in the country especially in Bihar or if these courses are offered at all, there is not enough infrastructure there; and   (b)	if so, the measures being taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to start these courses in each such institute and make infrastructure available therein?; 






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Yes, Sir. Electronics and Information Technology courses are offered in polytechnics and ITIs in the country including Bihar, with proper infrastructure.     (a)	Does not arise.
