                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5368






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Activities/Programmes under Nehru Yuva Kendras




Vishnu Dutt Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) the details of the programmes and activities beingorganized/conducted under the Nehru Yuva Kendras (NYKs) inthe country;b) whether a large number of posts in NYKs at State and district level are lying vacant across the country;c) if so, the details thereof, State/ district-wise including Madhya Pradesh;d) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to fill the said vacant posts; ande) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to makethese NYKs more effective?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a): Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan(NYKS) through its 623 District level Nehru Yuva Kendras(NYKs) undertakes programmes and activities all over the country. Details of programmes and activities being organized/conducted under the Nehru Yuva Kendras (NYKs) are at Annexure-I.(b): Number of posts are lying vacant in Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan. Details are at Annexure-II.(c): State/district-wise details of vacant posts including Madhya Pradesh are at Annexure-III.(d): 824 posts out of sanctioned strength of 2273 posts were lying vacant for many years prior to 2014 and were deemed abolished. With the approval of Ministry of Finance all these 824 posts in different categories have been revived. NYKS is in the process of filling up these posts. As of now, 220 posts have been filled through an open competitive examination which includes 101 posts of District Youth Coordinator (DYC), 75 posts of Accounts Clerk cum Typist (ACT) and 44 posts of Multi Tasking Staff (MTS). The examination for another 225 posts has been conducted which includes 100 posts of DYCs, 73 ACTs and 52 MTS and advertisement has been issued for the other 337 approved posts. This includes 160 posts of DYCs, 58 posts of ACTs, 23 posts of MTS and 96 posts in other categories. The online application process has been activatedw.e.f. 19.07.2019. Departmental promotion is also in progress to fill up the promotional posts.(e) Following steps have been taken to make these NYKs more effective:  i. Data base of Youth Clubs has been created on NYKS website: www.nyks.nic.in along with details of members. This is in public domain. ii. Payments including monthly honorarium to all National Youth Volunteers (NYVs) are made through PFMS.iii. Number of districts has been increased from 29 districts to 58 Districts where United Nation Volunteers(UNVs) are taking up the project “Strengthening of NYKS and NSS”. iv. NYKS has commenced recruitment drive against various categories of posts revived/re-created with the approval of Ministry of Finance. Filling up of these posts and induction of young people in NYKS will make these NYKs more effective.*****
