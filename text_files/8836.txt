                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2364






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Procurement of Rice




Sanjeev Kumar Singari














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is procuring more quantity of rice than the required quantity, if so, the details of statistics of rice procurement in the last two years; (b) 	the details of the quantum of rice which got spoiled and the disposal of the same during the said period; (c) 	whether the Government is planning to reduce the procurement of rice; and (d) 	if so, the details of the plans for the purpose and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a): 	Under the procurement policy of Government of India (GOI), Minimum Support Price (MSP), period of procurement and quality specifications are declared before start of each marketing season (Kharif/ Rabi). Further, procurement policy of GOI is open ended, under which whatever paddy is offered by farmers within the stipulated period and conforming to the specifications prescribed in advance by GOI are purchased at MSP by the Government Agencies including Food Corporation of India (FCI) for Central Pool to avoid any distress sale by farmers. The details of rice procurement in last two years is annexed.(b): 	Quantum of rice accrued as damaged in FCI and its disposal during the year 2017-18 & 2018-19 is as under :Year	Accrual of damaged Rice in FCI (Ton)	Disposal of Damaged Rice in FCI (Ton)2017-18	1844	8302018-19	3793	2771(c) : 	No such proposal is under consideration.(d) : 	Question does not arise.****ANNEXUREANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.2364 DUE FOR ANSWER ON 03.12.2019 IN THE LOK SABHA.The details of rice procurement in last two years	 STATE/UT	KMS 2017-18 (in LMT)	KMS 2018-19*(in LMT) ANDHRA PRADESH	40.00	48.06TELANGANA	36.18	51.86ASSAM	0.35	1.01BIHAR	7.93	9.49CHANDIGARH	0.14	0.13CHHATISGARH	32.55	39.71GUJARAT	0.01	0.09HARYANA	39.92	39.42JAMMU & KASHMIR	0.13	0.09JHARKHAND	1.43	1.53KARNATAKA	 	0.59KERALA	3.29	4.65MADHYA PRADESH	10.96	13.95MAHARASHTRA	1.79	5.80ODISHA	32.87	44.48PUNJAB	118.33	113.34NEF(TRIPURA)	 	0.07TAMIL NADU	10.11	12.94UTTAR PRADESH	28.75	32.33UTTRAKHAND	0.38	4.62WEST BENGAL	16.73	19.79ALL INDIA TOTAL :	381.85	443.95 * As on 28.11.2019		*****
