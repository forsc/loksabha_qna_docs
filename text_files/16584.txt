                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

401






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Transfer of Funds under PM-KISAN Scheme




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total funds transferred to the farmers under the PM-KISAN Scheme that provides income support to land-holding farmers. families in the current financial year as on date and the last financial year;(b) 	the State-wise break-up thereof;(c) 	whether there has been an increase in the number of farmers registered in the State of Andhra Pradesh under this scheme;(d) 	if so, the details thereof;(e) 	whether the Government has any plans of increasing the income support under this scheme to help farmers across the nation; and(f) 	if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????            ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)  (a) & (b):	The State-wise details of the total funds transferred to the farmers under the Pradhan Mantri Kissan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Scheme since inception till 31-03-2019 and in the current financial Year (2020-21) as on 13th September, 2020 is enclosed at Annexure-I(c) & (d):	Yes Sir. Registration under the Scheme is continuously going on till all eligible farmers are enrolled.  As on date, 56,93,432 farmers are registered for the State of Andhra Pradesh.  Out of this 7,21,450 are registered in December, 2019-March, 2020, 65,024 in (April, 2020 to July, 2020) and 5802 in the current period. (e) & (f):	No Sir. There is no such proposal.
