                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3895






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Cancellation of Air Corporation License




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the  number  of  Air  Corporation  Licences cancelled by the Government during each of the last five years in the country, State-wise and the details of its  impact  on  passengers;(b)  whether  cancellation  of  Air  Corporation Licences  impacted  the  regional  connectivity  in  the country especially in Bihar; and(c) the number of passenger aircrafts which have started operating from Patna airport?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) & (b) The Air Operator Certificate (AOC) of airlines are not State specific. No Air Operator Certificates (Licences) of airlines have been cancelled by the government in the last 5 years. However, some airlines have closed/suspended their operations owing to various reasons.  List of such airlines which have suspended their operations in the last 5 years is enclosed at Annexure I. Details of number of domestic passengers who travelled during the last five years is at Annexure II. ;In Bihar, under the Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - Ude Desh Ka Aam Nagrik (UDAN), routes connecting Darbhanga and Patna were awarded to Selected Airline Operators (SAOs) till the completion of three rounds of bidding under RCS-UDAN. The SAO has commenced RCS flights from these two RCS  Airports. However, two routes which were awareded to an another SAO have been discontinued due to the suspension of the AOC. It is the responsibility of the  SAO to comply with all the regulatory requirements before commencing the operations under regional connectivity in the country.;(c) The details of Scheduled domestic airlines operating to/from Patna as per current Winter schedule 2020 is enclosed at Annexure III.
