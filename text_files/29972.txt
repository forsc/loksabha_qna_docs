                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

156






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Revenue from Railway Stations




Dharmendra Kumar Kashyap














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the annual revenue generated by the railway stations in Uttar Pradesh during 2016-17, 2017-18, 2018-19 and 2019-20, particularly Bareilly Junction;(b) the number of trains that stop at Bareilly Junction station; and (c) whether the Government is planning to privatize the Bareilly Junction station?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (c) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
