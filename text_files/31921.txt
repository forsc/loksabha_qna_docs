                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3665






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Funds Spent Under NCF Scheme




Bhagwant Mann














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the quantum of funds spent in the State of Punjab since the inception of the National Culture Fund (NCF) scheme; and(b)	 the quantum of funds allocated to various institutions/departments during the last three years and district-wise details of the allocated funds?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) & (b) National Culture Fund (NCF) is a Charitable Trust under Endowment, Act, 1890, focused on Conservation, Preservation and Restoration of Cultural Heritage (Tangible & Intangible) through Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) endowments or sponsorship. The Fund is open for endowments and sponsorship for heritage projects all over the country. No such sponsorship has been received for Punjab since inception. The list of projects completed under NCF in last three years is at Annexure.
