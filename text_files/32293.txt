                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3725






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Registered Criminal Cases in NCR




A.K.P. Chinraj














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of pick-pocket, cell phone missing/ snatching, bike and car thefts complaints being registered in National Capital Territory (NCT) region from 2014 to 2021;(b) whether any recovery of above said articles have been done so far along with the recovery percentage rate; and(c) if so, the details of the cases regarding above categories for which only complaint have been received but no FIR has been filed along with the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) and (b):	    ‘Police’  and  ‘Public  Order’  are  State  subjects  as  per the Seventh Schedule  to  the  Constitution  of  India. However, the details of the cases of pick-pocketing, cell phone snatching, two wheelers and four wheelers thefts registered and recovery made by Delhi Police during the years 2014 to 2021 (upto 30.11.2021) are at annexure.   (c):	Delhi Police has informed that they register criminal cases in all complaints disclosing cognizable offence, with an objective that no crime goes unreported. ******
