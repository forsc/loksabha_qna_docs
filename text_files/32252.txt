                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3684






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Statistics on Missing Girls




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any statistics regarding number of girls missing cases reported in the country; and(b) if so, the details of such cases reported, number of girls identified and return home, found dead during each of the last five years, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI AJAY KUMAR MISHRA)(a)	& (b) National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) compiles and publishes statistical data on crimes in its publication “Crime in India”. The published reports are available till the year 2020. As per published report, State/Union Territory- wise number of missing and traced girls (below 18 years) during the years 2016 to 2020 is at Annexure. The data of missing girls found dead is not maintained by NCRB.*****
