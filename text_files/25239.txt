                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1019






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Mobile Refuelling Units




Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi













                                                    Adala Prabhakara Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government had recently introduced Mobile RefuellingUnits for refilling vehicles in the country.(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the specific advantages of these Mobile Refuelling Units and whetherthe Government has determined a mechanism to evaluate thepotential demand of these Units; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI )(a) & (b) Yes sir, Government has made amendments in Petroleum Rules 2002 and MSand HSD (Regulation of Supply, Distribution and Prevention of Malpractices)control order, 2005 to facilitate Door to Door Delivery of Diesel through Mobilebowsers.(c) & (d) Door to Door Delivery (DDD) of diesel is done through Mobile Dispensers forsite refuelling of Stationary equipment, machinery and heavy vehicles locatedat specific customer sites, in dedicated industrial zones, equipment operatingin mines, construction sites, agricultural machinery, diesel generators, vehicleswhich cannot come to service stations for refuelling, etc. Mobile dispensersensure safety as per norms stipulated in the amendments to the PetroleumRules, 2002 and MS/HSD control order, 2005. Petroleum and Explosives SafetyOrganisation (PESO) has framed Standard Operating Procedure (SOPs) forfilling and dispensing the product through Mobile bowsers. Till date OMCs (OilMarketing Companies) commissioned 1607 DDD bowsers.*****
