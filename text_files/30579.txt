                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2070






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Works done under Smart Cities Mission




Rodmal Nagar














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of works done under Smart Cities Mission in Madhya 	Pradesh;(b) 	the details of funds sanctioned, allocated and spent for the said 	purpose so far;(c) 	whether the Government is satisfied with the pace of the works 	being carried out and if so, the details and extent thereof;  and (d) 	whether all the works are likely to be completed on time and if 	so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in 	this regard?






ANSWER




                                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                            (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) to (d):  Government of India launched the Smart Cities Mission (SCM) on 25 June 2015.  100 Smart cities have been selected through 4 rounds of competition from January 2016 to June 2018.  7 cities in the State of Madhya Pradesh i.e., Bhopal, Indore, Jabalpur, Gwalior, Sagar, Satna & Ujjain have been selected for development as Smart Cities.  Smart City-wise details of selection, progress of projects, funds released by Central Government & utilization thereof are given in Annexure-I.  The period of implementation of SCM has been extended upto June 2023 and all Smart Cities are expected to complete their projects within the stipulated time.                                    ****
