                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4755






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Urdu Teachers in Bihar




Ram Kripal Yadav














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the proposal regarding appointment of CTET qualified Urdu teachers recommendedby the State Governments of Bihar is under consider ation of the Government;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government is aware that out of the total requirement of 24 thousand Urduteachers only 11 thousand Urdu teachers have been recruited so far;(d) whether the case regarding applicants’ request of reducing merit cut off by 5 per cent is in theSupreme Court and if so, the details thereof;(e) whether there was a similar case in Tripura and at that time, the Government had appointedUrdu teachers by resolving the case and if so, the details thereof; and(f) whether the Government proposes to appoint Urdu teachers in Bihar on the lines of Tripuraand if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c) : No such proposal regarding appointment of Central Teacher Eligibility Test (CTET)qualified Urdu teachers has been received from the Government of Bihar. Further, as education isin the Concurrent List of the Constitution, the recruitment and service conditions of teachers comein the domain of respective State Governments and UT Administrations.(d) : The State Government of Bihar has no information in this regard.(e) : There is no sanctioned post of Urdu Teachers for Madrasa Education under the Directorateof Elementary Education, Government of Tripura and therefore, CTET qualified Urdu Teacherhad not been recruited in the State of Tripura so far.(f) : Question does not arise.*****
