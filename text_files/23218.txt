                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4600






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






PMMSY in Jammu and Kashmir




Jugal Kishore Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the State of Jammu and Kashmir has been provided benefits under Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY);(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the reasons thereof; and(d)	the cooperation being provided to the State of Jammu and Kashmir by the Government to promote fish farming?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND  DAIRYING                                                                                         (SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) to (d):    Yes, sir. The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a flagship scheme namely “Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY)”- to bring about Blue Revolution through sustainable and responsible development of fisheries sector in India” with highest ever investment of Rs.20050 crore in fisheries sector for a period of 5 years with effect from the current financial year 2020-21 in all the States and Union Territories(UTs) including UT of Jammu & Kashmir. The Department of Fisheries, Government of India during the current financial year(2020-21) has accorded approval to the fisheries development  proposal of Union Territory of Jammu & Kashmir at a total of Rs. 2509.62 lakh  with central share of Rs.1275.56 lakh and the approved proposal envisages construction of  raceways, hatcheries, construction of new ponds for fish culture, installation of Biofloc units, providing boats in replacement & nets for traditional fishermen and providing livelihood and nutritional support  for socio- economically backward fishers. Hence the Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying, Government of India has been providing necessary supports and cooperation to the UT of Jammu and Kashimir for promotion and development of Fisheries in the UT Jammu and Kashmir. *****
