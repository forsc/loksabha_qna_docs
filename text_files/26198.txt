                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1936






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram




Jaswantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:a)	The objectives of the Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram;b)	Whether students are screened in school under this programme; andc)	Whether the benefits of this programme is also being provided to adolescents in the tribal areas of Gujarat?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)a)	The objectives of Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram are as follows-(i)	Increase the availability and access to information about adolescent health(ii)	Increase accessibility and utilization of quality counselling and health services for the adolescents(iii)	Forge multi-sectoral and intra departmental partnerships to create safe and supportive environment for adolescents(iv)	Institute special strategies to target adolescents residing in geographic pockets which make them vulnerable to health and nutrition risks, such as tribal and out of school adolescentsb)	Screening of students in the schools is carried out under Bal Swasthya Karyakram.c)	Yes Sir, Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram covers adolescents universally, i.e. males and females; urban and rural; in school and out of school; married and unmarried; and vulnerable and under-served including the adolescents in the tribal areas of Gujarat.
