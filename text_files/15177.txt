                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3896






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Upliftment and Development of SC and OBC Women




Vijay Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. †3896 TO BE ANSWERED ON 17.03.2020UPLIFTMENT AND DEVELOPMENT OF SC AND OBC WOMEN †3896. SHRI VIJAY KUMAR: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has started any action plan for the upliftment and development of women belonging to Scheduled Caste (SC) and Other Backward Classes (OBC); (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the funds allocated by the Government for the upliftment and development of women belonging to SC and OBC during the last three years, year and State-wise; and (d) the percentage of upliftment and development of the women belonging to SC and OBC during the said period, State and year-wise?ANSWER






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RAMDAS ATHAWALE)(a) to (c): M/o Social Justice & Empowerment implements various Initiatives/Schemes/Policies for the Social, Economic and Educational Empowerment of the vulnerable section of the Society, including women belonging to the Scheduled Castes (SC) and Other Backward Classes (OBC). Although these schemes do not have specific fund allocation for women beneficiaries but some of these have special provisions for women. The details of special provisions for women beneficiaries under these Schemes are at Annexure-I.Under the Schemes at Sl. No. 1 to 6 of the Annexure-I, funds are not allocated/released to the States. The total funds allocated/released under these Schemes during last three year and current year up to 12.03.2020 are at Annexure-II.State-wise fund allocated/released during last three year and current year up to 12.03.2020 under remaining 2 Schemes are at Annexure-III.(d): No such data is maintained at this Ministry.****Annexure-IReferred to the part (a) to (c) of reply to the Lok Sabha Untarred Question No. 3896 to be answered on 17.03.2020 regarding “Upliftment and Development of SC and OBC Women” asked by Shri Vijay KumarSl.
