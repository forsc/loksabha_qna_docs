                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

40






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






NEAT




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre













                                                    P.P. Chaudhary
                                                








                                                    Pratima Bhoumik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that the Government is planning to implement and operationalise aNational Educational Alliance for Technology (NEAT) for using technology for betterlearning outcomes in Higher Education,if so, the details thereof; and(b) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to ensure that socially and economicallybackward populations can access the services under the aforementioned program?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a) and (b) Yes Sir. Department of Higher Education, Ministry of Human Resource Developmenthas launched the National Educational Alliance for Technology (NEAT) scheme for usingtechnology for better learning outcomes in Higher Education. The Scheme uses ArtificialIntelligence (AI) to make learning more personalized and customized as per the requirements ofthe learner. All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) has been designated as theImplementation Agency for NEAT. One of the mandates for AICTE while implementing NEAT isthat, AICTE would ensure that the solutions are made freely available to a large number of thesocially and economically backward students.*****
