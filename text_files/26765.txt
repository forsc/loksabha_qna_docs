                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2397






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Products under NHM




Manoj Rajoria














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of products included under National Horticulture Mission (NHM); (b) the districts of Rajasthan included in the National Horticulture Mission;(c) whether the Government proposes to include the famous betel leaf cultivated in the Masalpur tehsil of Karauli district and Bayana sub-division of Bharatpur district of Rajasthan under the National Horticulture Mission, which is lagging behind due to the increasing cost and lack of modern technique/technology; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????  (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a): 	National Horticulture Mission i.e. a sub-scheme of Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) covers fruits, vegetables, root and tuber crops, mushrooms, spices, flowers, aromatic plants, coconut, cashew and cocoa.(b):	 Out of the total 33 districts of Rajasthan, 24 districts namely Alwar, Jaipur, Ajmer, Kota, Baran, Jhalawar, Chittorgarh, Jodhpur, Pali, Jalore, Barmer, Nagaur, Ganganagar, Banswara, Karauli, Sawai Madhopur, Tonk, Udaipur, Dungarpur, Bhilwara, Sirohi, Jhunjhunu & Bundi, Jaisalmer are covered under National Horticulture Mission.(c) & (d): 	 Betal Leaf cultivation is not covered under National Horticulture Mission. However, the financial assistance is provided for development programmes for promotion of betelvine through Directorate of Arecanut & Spices Development (DASD). DASD provides financial assistance to State Agricultural Universities and ICAR Institutes to produce good quality nucleus planting materials of betel-vine for further multiplication and distribution to the farmers.*****
