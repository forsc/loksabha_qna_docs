                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4957






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Inadequate Medical Education Seats




Sushil Kumar Singh













                                                    Francisco Cosme Sardinha
                                                








                                                    Rita Bahuguna Joshi
                                                








                                                    Sougata Ray
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of undergraduate medical education seats in the country, State/UT-wise;(b) the ratio of students applying for NEET vis-à-vis the total number of medical seats;(c) the reasons for limited medical seats in the country and the impediments in setting up new medical colleges;(d) the reasons for the students going in large numbers to foreign countries to pursue medical education thereby causing foreign exchange loss;(e) whether the huge fee for medical education in India and lack of enough medical seats are behind such migration;(f) whether the Government proposes to rationalize fee and develop infrastructure in medical education and also amend policy related to admission in MBBS/BDS courses to reduce the number of students going abroad for medical education, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (f) : There are 89,875 undergraduate seats in the country.  Details are at Annexure. Further, as per information provided by National Testing Agency (NTA), total number of students registering for and passing NEET UG exam is as under:- Year	2021Number of students registered 	16,14,777Number of students passed	8,70,074No. of seats	89875Clause (i) of Sub-section (1) of Section 10 of National Medical Commission Act, 2019 provides for framing of guidelines for determination of fees and all other charges in respect of fifty percent of seats in private medical institutions and deemed to be universities which are governed under the provisions of the Act. Accordingly, National Medical Commission has framed the guidelines and the same was issued on 03.02.2022.
