                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3567






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Inclusion and Removal of Tourist Sites from UNESCO World Heritage Sites’ List




Khan Saumitra














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether Kailash Mansarovar has been removed from UNESCO tentative list of Heritage Sites;b)	if so, the reasons therfor;c)	whether India has nominated Ramappa Temples for WHC Session 2020 and Deccan Sultanate Sites for WHC Session 2021; andd)	whether the Ministry considers pursuing Bishnupur Temples, Neora Valley National Park and Shantiniketan for UNESCO world Heritage sites?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b) No Sir. However, the property titled “Sacred Mountain Landscape and Heritage Routes”, Pithoragarh, District-Uttrakhand, India has been withdrawn from UNESCO’s tentative list at the request of Director, Wildlife Institute of India citing the technical correction with respect to coordinates of the proposed property.(c) Yes Sir, India has nominated Ramappa Temples for WHC session 2020. For the year 2021, two dossiers namely ‘Dholavira: A Harappan City’ and ‘Monuments and Forts of Deccan Sultanate’ were submitted to WHC and as per Operational Guidelines, dossier of ‘Dholavira: A Harappan City’ has been prioritized.(d)  Yes Sir, all the three sites namely Temples at Bishnupur, Neora Valley National Park and Santiniketan have been included in the Tentative List, which is a prerequisite condition for further inscription process.
