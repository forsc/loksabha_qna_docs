                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4232






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Amount Spent on Preservation of Monuments in Odisha




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the amount spent by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) in the maintenance, preservation and beautification of the ancient monuments located in different States during the last three years;(b)	the name of the monuments in Odisha where these activities have been carried out during the above period;(c)	whether any new temples and ancient monuments in Odisha are left over without preservation or repairing, etc.; and(d)	 if so, the details thereof and the steps taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE & TOURISM (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a)  The details of expenditure incurred by Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) for conservation, preservation and environmental development work of protected monuments, located in different States of the country, during the last three years are as follows:Sl.No.	Year	Expenditure incurred (Amount Rs.in lakhs)1.	2019-20	43539.262.	2018-19	40502.013.	2017-18	41076.79 (b)  & (c) There are 80 nos. of protected monuments of ASI in Odisha.  The conservation, preservation and environmental development work of them is attended regularly by ASI as per the availability of resources and all of these are in a good state of preservation.  The details of these monuments are given in the annexure.(d) Does not arise.
