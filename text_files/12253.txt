                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

104






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






AICTE




Ajay Nishad














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government had recently conducted any review of the functioning of All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE);(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government proposes to bring radical changes in the functioning of AICTE in order to make it more transparent and dynamic; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a)	(a) to (d): A statement is laid on the Table of the House.STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a) TO (d) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. †*104 FOR 10.02.2020 ASKED BY SHRI AJAY NISHAD REGARDING AICTE.(a)	& (b):  Government had vide its notification dated 22.10.2014 constituted “All India Council for Technical Education Review Committee” under the Chairmanship of  Shri M.K.Kaw, Ex-Secretary, MHRD to restructure & strengthen the Technical Education”. Review Committee submitted its Report on 22.04.2015 and recommended many measures. (c) & (d): Efforts have been taken to make the functioning of AICTE more transparent & dynamic, this is a continuous process. These measures include-  preparation of outcome based curriculum, mandatory internship, mandatory induction programme for students, examination reforms, perspective plan for technical education, initiatives for Teacher’s Training and Start-up initiatives. AICTE has implemented e-governance system to conduct the various processes/activities. Approvals have been given on self disclosures basis with no human intervention.  This has brought more transparency and dynamism in the functioning.	********
