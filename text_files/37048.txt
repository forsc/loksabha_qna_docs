                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4167






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Women in CAPFs




Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah













                                                    Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal
                                                








                                                    Pushpendra Singh Chandel
                                                








                                                    Rodmal Nagar
                                                








                                                    Sarmistha Sethi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and number of women working in Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs), force-wise including Central Industrial Security Force (CISF);(b) whether there is negligible presence of women in CAPF including CISF and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government proposes to increase the number of women in CAPF including CISF and if so, the details thereof, force-wise;(d) whether it is a fact that the Government has taken a decision to include 20 per cent women in CAPFs and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the percentage of women deployed in CAPFs at present, the details thereof, force-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (e):	The posted strength and percentage of women personnel in CAPFs including CISF as on 31.12.2021 is given in Annexure. 	It has been decided to reserve 33% posts at Constable level for being filled by women in CRPF & CISF to begin with and 14-15% posts at Constable level in border guarding forces i.e. BSF,  SSB & ITBP.******ANNEXUREL.S.U.Q.
