                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4885






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.04.2022






Educational Institutes for Yoga




Ramapati Ram Tripathi














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a.	whether the Government proposes to set up educational institutes like Morarji Desai National Institute of Yoga, New Delhi in Uttar Pradesh and other States; andb.	if so, the time by which they are likely to be set up? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a): At present, there is no such proposal with the Ministry to set up educational institutes like Morarji Desai National Institute of Yoga (MDNIY), New Delhi in Uttar Pradesh and other States.(b): Does not arise in view of (a) above. ***
