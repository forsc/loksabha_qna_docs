                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

241






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Production of Wheat




Jugal Kishore Sharma













                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the production of wheat is being affected due to rising temperatures in the last few years during sowing or growth period of crops which is ultimately affecting the food security, if so, the details thereof; and(b) 	whether the Government has taken any steps to increase the production of adequate wheat since wheat contributes more than 71 per cent to the total foodgrain production in the country, if so, the details of wheat production during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	No Sir, Despite a rise in mean temperature, wheat production and productivity has not declined due to timely interventions by the Government to develop climate resilient varieties and replacement of old varieties with high yielding varieties etc.(b):	Yes Sir, Due to concerted approach taken by the Government through various scemes such as National Food Security Mission (NFSM), Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY), Soil Health Card (SHC), Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY), Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) etc. The details of wheat production during the last five years are as under:Year	Production(million tonnes)2014-15	86.532015-16 	92.292016-17 	98.512017-18	99.872018-19*	102.19                    *as per 4th advance estimates of Directorate of Economics & Statistics                                                                          ********
