                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3613






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Training to Youth




Rekha Arun Verma













                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of skills in which training is being imparted to youth in Uttar Pradesh and Maharashtra;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)	Under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) (2016-20), training is being imparted in different National Skill Qualification Framework (NSQF) aligned job roles, developed by industry led Sector Skill Councils, covering 37 different sectors. The top three sectors in Uttar Pradesh are Electronics and Hardware, Apparel and Retail and top three sectors in Maharashtra are Tourism & Hospitality, Beauty & Wellness and Construction.(c)	Question does not arise.
