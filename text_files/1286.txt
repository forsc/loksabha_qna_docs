                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1122






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






NIFT Campus in Andhra Pradesh




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether there is no National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT) Campus in Andhra Pradesh;(b)	if so, whether the ministry is considering for setting up one such campus in the proposed new capital of Andhra Pradesh;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a):	There is no NIFT Campus in Andhra Pradesh. However, a NIFT Campus is situated at Hyderabad, which is a joint Capital of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.(b) to (d): As per policy for establishment of a new campus of NIFT, the State Government is required to submit a proposal along with following commitments:(i)	20-30 acres of land;(ii)	Rs.150-200 crore for construction of campus at the price rates of CPWD for 2011-12;(iii)	Rs.30 crore for provision of equipments, machines and furniture at the price rates of 2011-12;(iv)	Annual revenue grants to adjust revenue deficit, till the campus becomes financially viable.The State Government of Andhra Pradesh has so far not submitted a complete proposal along with requisite commitments, in accordance with the policy guidelines, for setting up of a NIFT campus in their proposed new capital. *****
