                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1007






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Pneumonia Campaign




John Barla













                                                    Shantanu Thakur
                                                








                                                    Gitaben Vajesingbhai Rathva
                                                








                                                    Pradeep Kumar Singh
                                                








                                                    Nayab Singh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has initiated any steps under SAANS campaign to reduce child mortality due to Pneumonia;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the provisions made to impart training with an aim to neutralize Pneumonia; and(c) the sectors or people from which assistance is sought under the said programme?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b) The Ministry of Health and Family Welfare has launched SAANS (Social Awareness and Actions to Neutralize Pneumonia Successfully) Initiative to reduce child mortality due to pneumonia on 16th November 2019. • Under SAANS initiative, for facility and community based management of Childhood Pneumonia, “Childhood Pneumonia Management Guideline” was released.  •	SAANS IEC campaign ( November 2019 to January 2020)  was organized at National and States/UT level with use of mass media such as Television and Radio, Social Media, Interpersonal Communication etc by health workers. The States/UTs were supported with Banners and Posters to be displayed at communities and facilities for creating awareness on Pneumonia. •	Under the National Health Mission, States/UTs are supported for trainings, procurement of drugs and equipments, States/UT level orientations, monitoring activities and IEC.•	For skill based training, modules have been developed for training of Medical Officers, Staff Nurses, Community Health Officers and ANM    ( Auxiliary Nurse Midwife).“Childhood Pneumonia Management Guideline” has been integrated with Home-Based Care for Young Child Programme (HBYC) for training of ASHAs (Accredited Social Health Activist).•	National Training of Trainers was conducted in December 2019 for 34 States/UTs on Childhood Pneumonia Management Guideline.  (c)      SAANS initiative is for strengthening facility and community level activities both in urban and rural areas for early identification and management of Childhood Pneumonia cases by involving healthcare providers, families, caregivers, PRIs (Panchayati Raj Institutions) and others functionaries across all the States/UTs.
