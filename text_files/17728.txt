                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1397






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Records of Loan Waivers




Reddeppa Nallakonda Gari














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has a record of all the farm loan waivers announced and released to beneficiaries during each of the last six years; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; (c) 	whether the outstanding loan amount in the farm sector in the loan books of Public Sector and Co-operative banks have reduced year-on-year during the said period; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Government of India has not announced any farm loan waiver during the last six year.  	However, States announce their own Farm loan waiver schemes as per their own requirement from their own resources.  The list of States which declared farm loan waiver and brief details as compiled by NABARD is placed at Annexure-I.(c) & (d):	A statement of outstanding loan amount in the farm sector for the last 6 years as received from RBI in respect of Scheduled Commercial Banks and NABARD in respect of Cooperative Banks and Regional Rural Banks is placed at Annexure-II.
