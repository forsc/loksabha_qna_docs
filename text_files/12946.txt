Externally Aided Projects




Rebati Tripura














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the State Government of Tripura has forwarded a number of projects for financial grants under ‘Externally Aided Projects’ category;b)	if so, the details of these projects; andc)	the steps initiated by the Union Government for the sanction of externally aided projects in the case of Tripura?
