                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3415






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Wind Augmentation Purifying Unit




Sanjay Jaiswal














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has done an impact assessment of the WAYU air purifier prototypes that were installed in Delhi, if so, the details of its efficacy in real time situations; (b)	whether there are plans to have more WAYU units; and (c)	if so, the details of location identified and time frame fixed for installing them?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE; MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY; AND MINISTER OF EARTH SCIENCES(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)????????? ?? ?????? ?????? ??????, ??????? ?? ???????????? ?????? ?? ?????? ??????? ??????(??. ???? ?????)(a)	Yes Sir. The performance of Wind Augmentation Purifying Unit (WAYU) is evaluated for PM10 and PM2.5 monitoring at inlet and outlet of the device in the field. It is observed that device removes PM10 and PM2.5 by 28-49% and 9-25% respectively depending on amount of pollution load and device location. The device has potential applicability for limited area cleaning such as traffic intersections. (b)	Currently, there are no plans to have more WAYU units.(c)	Does not arise.		*******
