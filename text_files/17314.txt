                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

753






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Jal Jeevan Mission




Pramila Bisoyi














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of State-wise allocation and utilization of funds under Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) during the last three years, year-wise;(b) the budget allocation proposed for the current financial year and the details of the funds released till 31 August, 2020, State-wise;(c) whether the Government is considering to provide water supply facilities in institutions like Anganwadi Centers, schools and hospitals under the JJM; and(d) if so, the steps taken in this regard in recent years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)The details of State/ UT-wise allocation and utilization of funds under Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) in 2019-20 and under erstwhile National Rural Drinking Water Programme in 2017-18 & 2018-19 is at Annex-I.(b) The State/ UT-wise proposed central allocation and fund released in the current financial year under JJM till 31.08.2020 is at Annex-II.(c) & (d) To enable every rural householdand public institutions, viz.school, anganwadi centre, health centre, wellness centres, Gram Panchayat (GP) building, etc., in the country to have potable water through Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) by 2024, Government of India, in partnership with States, is implementing Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) with an estimated outlay of Rs. 3.60 lakh Crore.****
