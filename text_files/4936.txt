                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4434






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2019






Welfare of Journalists




Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the schemes for the welfare of personnel working in the electronic and print media along with the amount allocated/ utilized for the purpose in the last three years; and(b)	whether any schemes or proposals are under consideration to provide boarding facilities in circuit house, toll free travel and providing other facilities to the accredited reporters and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE & MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING                                {SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR}(a)   Ministry of Information & Broadcasting implements a ‘Journalist Welfare Scheme’ to provide one time ex-gratia relief on urgent basis to journalists /Media Personnel  or their families under extreme hardship on account of death of journalists and to the journalists in case of permanent disability, major listed ailments and in case of accidents causing serious injuries necessitating hospitalization. The amount allocated/utilized in the last three years is as under :- 		Year           Amount Allocated         Amount Utilized2016-17         Rs.   35,40,000/-            Rs.31,46,613/-		2017-18         Rs.   40,00,000/-            Rs.39,37,966/-2018-19         Rs.1,00,00,000/-            Rs.99,99,197/- (b) Journalist accredited with Press Information Bureau at its Headquarters are at present given following facilities :-(a) Railway concession for Government of India accredited media persons administered by the Ministry of Railways. (b)  CGHS Facilities for media persons administered by Ministry of Health & Family Welfare.******
