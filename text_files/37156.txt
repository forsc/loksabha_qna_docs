                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4245






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Drugs Seized by State Police




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and quantity of drugs seized as well as destroyed by State police for different kinds of drugs since March 2019, State and month-wise;(b) the details and the total quantity of drugs stored in different State police godowns right now, State-wise and drug-wise;(c) the number of incidents of pilferage, theft, and recirculation of drugs stored in State police godowns reported during the last 10 years;(d) whether any action has been taken on such reports and if so, the details thereof; and(e) the quantity of drugs with medicinal value seized by State police and sent to pharmaceutical companies manufacturing alkaloids or other substances since March 2019 along with the details of companies that have received such drugs, State and month-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b):	Month and State wise details of seizure and disposal of drugs by various state Police since March, 2019 to December, 2021 (both in english and hindi) as reported to NCB are available at following links:-https://narcoticsindia.nic.in/ImportantSeizure/seizure-english.pdf https://narcoticsindia.nic.in/ImportantSeizure/seizure-hindi.pdf https://narcoticsindia.nic.in/DisposalofDrugs/disposal-english.pdfhttps://narcoticsindia.nic.in/DisposalofDrugs/disposal-hindi.pdf  (c) to (e):	The details of such cases are not maintained by Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB) or National Crime Record Bureau (NCRB). *****
