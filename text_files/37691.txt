                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4646






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Centres of Excellences-Hydrogen




Khagen Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to set up a number of Centres of Excellences-Hydrogen (CoE-H) throughout the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) to (c)In the Independence Day speech on August 15, 2021, Hon’ble Prime Minister announced the launch of National Hydrogen Mission.Ministry of New and Renewable Energy has accordingly formulated a draft National Green Hydrogen Mission document. The draft Mission proposes support for Centres of Excellence for Research and Development on different aspects of Hydrogen. *****
