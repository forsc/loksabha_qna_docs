                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3202






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Cases Registered by NIA




Sunil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that National Investigation Agency (NIA) has registered cases of terror financing;(b) if so, the details of the action and impact thereof;(c) the steps being taken by the Government to strengthen NIA; and(d) the details and the number of terror incidents which have taken place in the hinterland of the country during the last five years, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) & (b) Since its inception, the National Investigation Agency (NIA) has registered 103 cases of terror financing, in which 786 accused have been arrested. Charge-sheets have been filed in 92 cases against 865 accused persons. Conviction have been secured in 16 cases, whereby 97 accused persons have been convicted. The conviction rate in terror financing cases investigated by the NIA is 94.11%.--2--L.S.US.Q.NO. 3202 FOR 22.03.2022(c)       The manpower and infrastructure requirements of the NIA are reviewed from time to time and steps are taken to enhance the capacity of the NIA so that it is able to carry out its      mandate effectively. The NIA, with its headquarters at New Delhi, has 18 branch offices in the country. Of them, 10 branch offices at Chandigarh, Ranchi, Imphal, Chennai, Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, Patna, Jaipur, Bhopal and Bhubaneswar along with 481 additional posts have been created since July, 2019. Besides, on the basis of requirements projected by the NIA, the Government also sanctions, from time to time, the posts of specialists including, legal, scientific, information technology experts etc.(d)        The number of the terrorist incidents in the hinterland of the country, during the last five years, is given below:Year	2017	2018	2019	2020	2021No. of terrorist incidents	-	01	-	-	01*****
