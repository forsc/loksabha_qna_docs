                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4530






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






WiFi in Gram Panchayats




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has intensified operationalisation of WiFi in Gram Panchayats (GPs) and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	the number of GPs with operational WiFi as on date, State/UT-wise including Gujarat; and (c) 	the details of the financial outlay for this year dedicated for GP internet services?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) to (b)	BharatNet project is implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity to all the Gram Panchayats (GPs) in the country. As on 28.02.2022, total 1,72,361 GPs (1,68,010 GPs on OFC and 4,351 GPs on satellite) have been made Service Ready in the country. In Gujarat, Phase-II of BharatNet is implemented under State-led model through the State Government under which, 7608 GPs have been made Service Ready out of 7668 planned GPs. Further, 22 GPs have been made Service Ready on Satellite media out of 26 planned GPs. Under Phase-I, a total number of 6593 GPs were taken up for implementation through RailTel Corporation of India Limited, out of which 6489 GPs have been made Service Ready. In total, 14,119 GPs have been made Service Ready in Gujarat, as on 28.02.2022.The work of providing broadband facility (Wi-Fi hotspots and Fibre to the Home connections) in Phase-I GPs, including that of Gujarat, is implemented with funding from Universal Service Obligation Fund (USOF). For BharatNet Phase-II, the respective Implementing Agencies are responsible for the utilisation of the created network.  Regular review & monitoring of the project is done with the Implementing Agencies for expeditiously making the Wi-Fi hotspots active/operational. The State/UT-wise number of GPs with active/operational  Wi-Fi, including in Gujarat, is given at Annexure-I.(c)	For BharatNet project, Rs. 7,000 crore is the Revised Allotment (RE) for 2021-22.
