                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3208






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Agricultural Growth




Kalyan Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether growth rate of the agricultural sector during 2021-22 was 3.9 per cent against 4.3 per cent in 2019-20;(b)	 if so, whether only Rs. 1.04 lakh crore has been allocated to just five schemes: Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN), Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY), Modified Interest Subvention Scheme, Market Intervention Scheme and Price Support Scheme and Pradhan Mantri Kisan Man Dhan Yojana in the recent budget; and (c) 	if so, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE????????????????????????	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	As per theSecond Advance Estimates of National Income 2021-22, released by National Statistical Office (NSO), Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation (MoSPI) on 28th February 2022, the annualgrowth rate of Gross Value Added (GVA) ofAgriculture and Allied Sectors in the country for the year 2021-22 is 3.3 percent at the constant (2011-12) prices.(b) and (c) : The details of budget estimates for 2022-23 in five schemes are given below.(Rs in Lakhs)SL. No.	Name of Central Sector Schemes/Projects	Budget Estimate (BE) 2022-231	Pradhan MantriFasalBimaYojna	15,50,000.002	Modified Interest Subvention Scheme (MISS)	19,50,000.003	Implementation of Market Intervention Scheme and Price Support Scheme(MIS- PSS)	1,50,000.004	Pradhan MantriKisanSammanNidhi (PM-KISAN)	68,00000.005	PradhanmantriKisanMandhanYojna	10,000.00Source: Budget Section, Dept. of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare*******
