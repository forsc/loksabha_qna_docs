                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1624






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Construction of Houses under PMAY-U in Odisha




Jayanta Kumar Roy













                                                    Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	The details of housing units constructed proposed to be constructed in Jalpaiguri, West Bengal and Bolangir, Odisha under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban(PMAY-U) till date, location/year-wise;(b)	 the details of the housing units sanctioned and percentage of those completed in Jalpaiguri, West Bengaland Bolangir Parliamentary constituency till date, location/ year-wise;(c)	  the funds sanctioned, allocated and utilized under this yojana for Jalpaiguri, West Bengal and Bolangir Parliamentary constituency till date, year-wise;(d)	  the details of houses under construction/getting delayed alongwith their locations, in the Jalpaiguri, West Bengal and Bolangir Parliamentary Constituency; and(e)	the reasons for the delay in the construction of such houses and the time by which these houses are expected to be completed?






ANSWER




            THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                          (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a)&(b): The year-wise and location-wise details of housing units proposed to be constructed/sanctioned and percentage of those completed in Jalpaiguri, West Bengal and Bolangir, Odisha Parliamentary constituency under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban [PMAY-U] till date are annexed as Annexure-I.(c)	:The year-wise and location-wise details of Central Assistance sanctioned/allocated, released and utilized under PMAY-U in Jalpaiguri, West Bengaland Bolangir, Odisha Parliamentary constituency are annexed as Annexure-II and Annexure-III, respectively.(d) to (e): The details of houses under construction in Jalpaiguri, West Bengaland Bolangir, Odisha Parliamentary constituency are annexed as Annexure-I. The respective State Governments of West Bengal and Odisha have informed that the main reason for delay in the construction of houses in these locations is financial difficulty faced by beneficiaries in contributing their share and disruption in supply of labour and construction materials due to COVID-19. The houses sanctioned under PMAY-U are to be completed within the mission period.                                          *****
