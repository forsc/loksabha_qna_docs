                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5259






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Tourism Development Schemes for Rajasthan




Narendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any scheme or proposes to provide financial assistance to the States for the development of tourism; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the details of projects selected and proposed to be selected for the development of tourism; (d) 	the details of the financial assistance sanctioned/being sanctioned for each project; and (e) 	whether there is any tourism development scheme for Rajasthan particularly for Jhunjhunu?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM				      (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e):	The Ministry of Tourism has provided financial assistance to State Governments/Union Territory Administrations under the ‘Swadesh Darshan’ and ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ Schemes for the development of tourism related infrastructure in the country. 	Proposals are received from State Governments/Union Territory Administrations from time to time for seeking financial assistance under the various schemes of the Ministry of Tourism. These proposals are examined in context of the scheme guidelines and financial assistance is extended for such projects subject to fulfilment of the stipulated provisions and availability of funds.	The details of the financial assistance/projects sanctioned under the above mentioned schemes to various States/UTs including Rajasthan is given at Annexure. It may however be mentioned that none of the sanctioned projects is in Jhunjhunu. ******
