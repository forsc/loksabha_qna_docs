                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2022






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Border Violation by Pakistan




Umesh G. Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the ceasefire and other border violations committed by Pakistani Army on Indo-Pak Borders during the last three months, date and place-wise;(b)  the details of casualties and other losses suffered on both sides, incident-wise; and(c) the reaction of the Government thereto and the remedial steps taken to prevent recurrence of such incidents?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) & (b):  Firm and decisive response is given by the Indian Army to the ceasefire violations on the Indo-Pak  borders. Ceasefire violations were 267, 234 and 221 during  March, April & May 2019 respectively.	Indian Army has suffered three fatal casualties during the period.	Regarding the casualties suffered on the other side there is no authentic figure available with this Ministry.(c)  All violations of ceasefire and infiltration are taken up with Pakistan authorities at the appropriate level through the established mechanism of hotlines, flag meetings, Directorate Generals of Military Operations talks as well as diplomatic channels between the two countries.*******
