                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3882






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Facility for Travellers on NHs




Chirag Paswan














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has provided basic trauma facilities for the travellers on all National Highways (NHs) in the country including Bihar; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	if not, the time limit fixed by the Government for providing such facilities; (d) 	whether the Gvoernment has provided proper lighting facilities on NHs in the populated areas along all NHs in the country and if so, the details thereof; and(e) 	whether the Government has provided the lighiting facility below the underbridges on all NHs and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	  The Government of India has taken initiatives towards setting up of trauma Care Facilities (TCFs) along the National Highways with an overall objective to bring down preventable deaths due to road accidents.(b)	& (c)  During the 11th five year Plan, under the scheme namely ‘Assistance for Capacity Building for Developing Trauma Care Facilities in Government Hospitals on National Highways’, financial assistance is provided to States/Union territories (UTs) for upgrading/strengthening Trauma Care Facilities in identified Government Hospitals/Medicals Colleges. 116 Trauma Care Facilities were funded in 17 States. During the 12th five Year Plan, the scheme continues as Capacity Building for Developing Trauma Care Facilities in Government Hospitals on National Highways’ and 80 more Trauma Care Facilities were funded. The Programme continues beyond 12th Five Year Plan as ‘National Programme for Prevention and Management of Trauma and Burn Injuries’ to support Trauma Care facilities identified during 11th and 12th five Plan. (d)  &  (e)     Lighting on the 4/6 lane National Highways is generally provided as   per guidelines given in IRC SP-84/87. Generally all the locations such as   built-up section areas, toll plaza areas, truck lay byes, rest areas, bus bay &   bus shelter locations, grade separated structures, interchanges, flyovers, underpasses (vehicular &pedestrian), overpasses and any other locations specified in the contract/ concession agreements read in conjugation with IRC   SP 84/87 are the locations identified to be illuminated.*****
