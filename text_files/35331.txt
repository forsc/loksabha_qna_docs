                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2379






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2022






ROBs/RUBs in Andhra Pradesh




Midhun Reddy













                                                    Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received proposals for construction of Road Over Bridges (ROBs)/Road Under Bridges (RUBs) in Andhra Pradesh during the last three years and the current year; and(b)	if so, the details of the projects approved by the Government and the least time frame for completion of these projects. 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (b): Yes, Sir. A proposal for construction of Road Under Bridge (RUB) in lieu of LC No. 104 on Katpadi-Tirupati section on cost sharing basis was received in 2018-19 which has been sanctioned in 2019-20. Work of Railway portion has been completed.  Work on approaches is mainly in State Govt. portion and is being executed by State Govt.*****
