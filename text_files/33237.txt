                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

823






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






EWS Quota in Private Schools




Vishnu Dutt Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether adequate measures are being taken to tame malpractices prevalent in large 	numbers in admission under EWS quota in private schools all over the country;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reason therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(DR. SUBHAS SARKAR)(a) to (c): Education is a subject of Concurrent List of the Constitution and schools, other than those owned /funded by Central Government, are under the jurisdiction of the State Governments. Thus, the matter relating to fees and its components in schools including admissions under EWS quota are regulated in terms of Rules and Instructions of the State Government concerned. The State Education Department is the Competent Authority to ensure implementation of Right to Education Act, 2009 and admission under EWS quota.The implementation of Section 12(1)(c) of the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education(RTE) Act, 2009 for the purpose of reimbursement is monitored through regular reporting mechanism from the States, Review meetings, Annual Work Plan appraisal etc. States and UTs are using different methodologies for grievance redressal such as Online Mechanism, Toll Free Number or offline mechanism for addressing grievances under RTE Act.  ****
