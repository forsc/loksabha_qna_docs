                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2317






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Welfare of Tea Workers




Kripanath Mallah














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)the features of the proposed special scheme for welfare of tea workers especially women and their children within Assam;(b)the details of its present status;(c)whether the Government has sanctioned any funds in this regard within Assam;(d)if so, the details thereof; and(e)the details of the target set?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (e):	As per announcement in the Union Budget 2021, a provision of Rs.200.00 crore has been made in the Budget 2021-22. The scheme thus announced is focused on Welfare of Tea Workers of Assam and West Bengal especially women and their children i.e. health, education, vocational training, skill development etc. *******
