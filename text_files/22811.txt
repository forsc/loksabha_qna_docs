                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3796






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






National Highways in Uttarkashi




Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details and the length of National Highways (NHs) passing through Uttarkashi region in Uttarakhand;(b)	whether the Government has taken special steps to promote cleanliness, safety/security on  these NHs and if so, the details thereof;(c)	the details of road accidents occurred on these NHs under the said region during the last three years; and(d)	the steps taken by the Government to prevent road accidents in the said region?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	The details and length of National Highways (NHs) passing through Uttarkashi region are as under:-Sl. No.	NH No.	Length (in km)(a)	94 (New No. 134)	95.43(b)	123 (New No. 507)	46.35(c)	108 (New No. 34)	133(b)	Yes Sir. Safety/security including steps to promote cleanliness on NHs are being taken as per guidelines issued by Indian Road Congress and instructions issued by this Ministry from time to time.(c)	During the last three years, road accidents on these NHs under the said region have been reported resulting in 26 fatalities and 19 injuries. (d)	Road Safety measures viz. removal of accident black spots, installation of signages, road marking, crash barriers, protection works etc. along with traffic calming measures have been provided to prevent road accidents.*****
