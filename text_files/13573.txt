                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2421






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Water Connection to every Household




K. Muraleedharan














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering to provide water connections to every household in the country and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the entire amount required for the said purpose has to be met from the Government sources; and (c) if so, the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) To enable every rural household in the country to have potable water at service level of 55 litre per capita per day (lpcd) through Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC)by 2024, Government of India, in partnership with the States, has launched Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) with an estimated cost of Rs. 3.60 lakh Crore out of which Central share is Rs. 2.08 lakh Crore.(b) & (c) The estimated amount of Rs. 3.60 lakh Crore is proposed to be shared between the Union and State Governments. In addition, to enable individuals, trusts, foundations, corporate and industrial houses, etc. to contribute in furthering the objective of the JJM, Rashtriya Jal Jeevan Kosh is being set up.****
