                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

669






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






IERMS for Women Safety




Shashi Tharoor














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Integrated Emergency Response Management Systems (IERMS) for the security of women passengers, have been established in 900 railway stations;(b)	  if so, the details thereof; State-wise;[[[(c)	  whether all the existing IERM Systems are functional as on date;(d)	  if so, whether IERM System has been installed in all railway stations in Thiruvananthapuram and if not, the time by when the system is likely to be installed; and(e)	  the details of the amount utilised for the IERM Systems since 2014, year and State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (e): It has been decided to provide Video Surveillance System (VSS) under Nirbhaya Fund on 983 Railway stations at an estimated cost of `500 crore for effective monitoring mechanism and speedier assistance to passengers especially women passengers in distress. A Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) defining role and responsibilities of on duty staff has been formulated to render assistance to passengers in minimum possible time. Number of Railway stations identified for the provision of VSS under Nirbhaya Fund are appended.The Integrated Emergency Response Management Systems (IERMS) is in tendering stage and bids have been invited. The SOP is applicable to all Railway stations including Thiruvananthapuram Railway station. ****
