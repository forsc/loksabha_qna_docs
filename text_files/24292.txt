                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

307






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Dairy Development




Harish Dwivedi













                                                    Jagdambika Pal 
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) the details of schemes being implemented by the Government to promote dairy development; (b) whether any financial assistance is also provided by the Government through the schemes being implemented for dairy development; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER)THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(DR. SANJEEV KUMAR BALYAN)(a)	to (c)  The Department has been implementing following dairy development schemes to promote dairy development across the country :-(i) Supporting Dairy Cooperatives and Farmer Producer Organizations engaged in dairy activities (SDCFPO)(ii) National Programme for Dairy Development (NPDD)(iii) Dairy Processing and Infrastructure Development Fund (DIDF)The details of the above Schemes are given at Annexure.
