                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2370






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Status of SBM




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	The details of the present status of Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM) and the level of implementation thereof in the State of Gujarat; district-wise;(b) 	whether the Government has made any plans to check review and audit the physical condition of the toilets constructed under the said campaign and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                             (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a): 	Under Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U) only urban areas of the country are covered.  As on 29.2.2020, in the State of Gujarat all the Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) have been declared as Open Defecation Free (ODF).  Similarly, the percentage of waste processing in Gujarat is 87 percent of the waste generated.(b) : 	The physical condition of the toilets constructed under SBM-U are evaluated by a third party  verification under ODF, ODF+ and ODF++ protocols and through annual sanitation survey called Swachh Sarvekshan, conducted throughout the country through an independent third party. The details of these protocols are available in public domain on portal of Swachhbharaturban.gov.in.                                                *****
