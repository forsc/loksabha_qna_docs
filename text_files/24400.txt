                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

315






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Additional Food Grains to NFSA Beneficiries




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has decided to allocate additional food grains to beneficiaries of the National Food Security Act (NFSA) for another five months from July to November, 2021; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether the additional foodgrain would entail a subsidy of Rs.64,031 crore, if so, the details thereof; (d) 	whether the total amount of food-grain to be distributed under the said initiative was estimated to be 204 lakh metric tonnes; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI) (a) to (c): 	Yes Sir, additional allocation of food-grain of approx. 198.78 LMT has been made under Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY) for distribution to approx. 80 Crore NFSA beneficiaries. The total financial implication for these five months i.e. July, 2021 to November, 2021 is estimated to be a maximum of Rs. 67266.44 Crore. The quantity of foodgrains, number of beneficiaries etc. may vary as per actual identification of beneficiaries done by States/UTs time to time during course of implementation of the Scheme.(d) & (e): 	Yes Sir, It entails a quantity of approx 204 lakh metric tons as per maximum coverage of up to 81.35 Crore persons under National Food Security Act, 2013 (NFSA) as per 2011 Census. ***
