                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3528






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






BIS Standards for Drinking Water




Raksha Nikhil Khadse














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government needs and proposes to make drinking water standards as per the standards notified by BIS as the recent samples tested across cities in the country failed on parameters of Total Dissolved Solids (TDS), turbidity, total hardness, total alkalinity, mineral and metals and presence of coliform and E. Coli which cause severe illness; and (b) 	the efforts taken/being taken by the Government in this regard to ensure safe tap water for the people?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI DANVE RAOSAHEB DADARAO)(a)  :	Yes, Sir.(b) : 	Hon’ble Minister has written to Chief Minister of all States/UTs to bring drinking water under mandatory Certification. Department of Drinking Water and Sanitation, Ministry of Jal Shakti has also been requested for taking action regarding the same. *****
