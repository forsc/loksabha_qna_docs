                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3442






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Rented Anganwadi Buildings




S. Venkatesan













                                                    Chandrani Murmu
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has taken note that Anganwadi Centres in the   country do not have their own buildings; (b)	if so, the number of functional Anganwadis and Anganwadis having their own building across the country, State/UT-wise including Odisha; (c)	the steps taken by the Government in this regard; (d)	the details of initiative taken to provide nutrition and helping the Anganwadi Centres in the country, State/UT including district-wise details of Tamil Nadu; and (e)	whether there is any proposal for the enhancement of salary of the Anganwadi workers and helpers?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)& (b):  Out of 13,80,796 functional Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) running across the Country, 6,05,474 AWCs have their own buildings including 44,671 AWCs in Odisha. The details of number of functional Anganwadis and Anganwadis having their own building across the country as per data available in Rapid Reporting System (RRS), State/UT-wise including Odisha are at Annexure.(c):	Government of India provides Central assistance for construction of new Anganwadi Centre buildings in rural area in convergence with Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) on reimbursement basis.  In the current financial year, Government has proposed construction of 58,670 more Anganwadi Centres across the country.(d) :	Under the Anganwadi Services Scheme, provision of supplementary nutrition is primarily made to bridge the gap between the Recommended Dietary Allowance (RDA) and the Average Daily Intake (ADI) of children and pregnant women and lactating mothers.
