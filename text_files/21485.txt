                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2533






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Halt of Trains




Sunil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of the Members of Parliament (MPs) who made the demands to re-operate the trains after lockdown in the country;(b)  whether the Government is aware that many MPs have written letters to Railway Minister, Railway Board, General Manager, DRM, etc. demanding the halt of trains at various stations after the nationwide lockdown; and(c)  if so, the details regarding the demands raised in the said letters and the number of demands fulfilled?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL) (a) to (c) In view of COVID-19 pandemic, Indian Railways discontinued all regular passenger train service w.e.f 23.03.2020 and Indian Railways is presently operating only special trains with limited stoppages keeping in view the suggestions and concerns of the State Governments for effective management of the pandemic.  Representations including those from many Hon’ble Members of Parliament for restoration of services and provision of stoppages have been received, a compendium of which is not maintained.  These representations/requests are considered / examined and action as found feasible and justified, are taken.   *****
