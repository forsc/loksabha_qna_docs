                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3189






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Proposals under UIDSSMT




Ramapati Ram Tripathi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)   the details of the proposals received from different States under Urban Infrastructure Development Scheme for Small and Medium Towns (UIDSSMT) duringthe last three years, till date, State-wise;(b)    the details of the proposals accepted and funds sanctioned for the said period, project and State-wise;(c)  the number of the pending proposals along withthe reasons therefor and the time by which these pending proposals are likely to be sanctioned;(d)   	whether some accepted proposals have not been completed within the stipulated time;(e)  	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor, project and State-wise; and(f) 	the steps taken/being taken by the Government in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE      MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                  (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a) to (c): Government of India launched the Jawaharlal Nehru National Urban Renewal Mission (JNNURM) including sub component namely Urban Infrastructure Development Scheme for Small and Medium Towns (UIDSSMT) in December, 2005 for a period of 7 years i.e., upto 31 March, 2012.  The UIDSSMT component was for infrastructure development with central assistancein small and medium towns of the States/Union Territories (UTs). However, the JNNURMwas extended for a period of two years i.e., upto 31March, 2014. As such, no new proposal was received from States after 31 March, 2014.(d)to(f): All the projects of UIDSSMT component of JNNURMin which 50% or more of the central assistance had been released and physical progress was 50% or more as on 31 March, 2014 or were sanctioned during the Transition Phase (1 April, 2012 to 31 March, 2014) of the Mission, were approved for funding under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) upto 31 March, 2017. After closure of the Mission, all the projects were handed over to the States/UTs.                                              *****
