                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Look Out Circulars against Foreigners




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has issued Look Out Circulars/Notices (LOCs) for the foreigners living illegally in various parts of the country during the last three years;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether LOCs were also issued in April, 2020;(d) if so, the details thereof; and(e) the further action taken or proposed to be taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (e) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House. --2--STATEMENT IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) to (e) of THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *05  FOR ANSWER ON 02.02.2021 REGARDING ‘LOOK OUT CIRCULARS AGAINST FOREIGNERS’.(a) & (b)  40,735 LOCs were issued against foreigners during the period January, 2018 to December, 2020. Out of these 512 LOCs were issued against overstaying foreigners.(c) &(d)  2627 LOCs  were issued against foreigners in the month of April, 2020. (e)  On detection at Immigration Check Posts at the time of departure from India, the foreign nationals against whom LOCs had been issued, were not allowed to leave India till legal proceedings against them were not completed.*****
