                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3115






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Issuance of FASTags




Sudheer Gupta













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether  the  Government  has issued  FASTags  to  ease  the  traffic congestion at various toll 	plazas on national highways across the country and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether all toll plazas do not have dedicated  lane  for  FASTag  holders  and if so, the details 	thereof;(c)  the  measures  taken  by  the Government to have a dedicated lane for such tag holders at all the 	toll plazas;(d) whether  some of  the toll  plazas are not accepting the FASTags and if so, the details thereof 	along with the reasons therefor; and(e)  the  corrective  measures  taken/being  taken  by  the  Government  in  this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) In order to facilitate smooth movement of traffic and reduce congestion on fee plazas, the Government has rolled out a cashless payment mechanism i.e. FASTag by implementation of Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) based Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) system on National Highways under which 22 banks have been engaged as issuer banks to issue FASTag to road users. At present, it is operable at 457 fee plazas on National Highways. (b) to (e) Currently, 87 fee plazas are not live with ETC system. Government is making efforts for the work of installation of ETC for such fee plazas. *****
