                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1531






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Tejas Express




S. Venkatesan














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Tejas express which runs between Madurai and Chennai Egmore is going to be provided a stop at Dindigul to cater to the tourists of Kodaikanal hill resort; and (b) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION   (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b) Yes, Sir. For the convenience for the passengers, it has been decided to provide stoppage of 02613/02614 Chennai Egmore - Madurai Tejas Special Express at Dindigul. *****
