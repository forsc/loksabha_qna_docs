                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3144






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.12.2021






Upgradation of Roads as NH in Chhattisgarh




Gomati Sai














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Raigarh-Gharghoda-Dharmjaigarh- Pathalgaon is presently the busiest route in Chhattisgarh due to use by several vehicles of various companies including SECL, NTPC etc.;(b) if so, whether the said route has been upgraded to National Highway (NH); and (c) if so, the details thereof; and (d) if not, the time by which the said route is likely to be upgraded as NH?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)   Yes Sir. (b) & (c)   The stretch from Raigarh-Gharghoda-Dharmjaigarh is not a National Highway (NH).The stretch from Dharmjaigarh to Pathalgaon is a part of declared NH-130A. (d)  The Ministry considers declaration of some State roads as new National Highways (NHs) from time to time based on requirement of connectivity, inter-se priority and availability of funds.*****
