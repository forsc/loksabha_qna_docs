                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4995






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Promotion of Coir Industries




Maneka Sanjay Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is implementing any scheme/project for setting up and promotion of coir industries in the non-traditional areas in the country and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the funds released under these schemes to various States during the last three years and the current year;(c)	whether any strategies have been framed for enhancing the skills upgradation and quality improvement for the youths in the country to open avenues for them in coir industries;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and(e)	whether any package has been provided by the Government for the development of coir industries in the country and if so, the details thereof? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	Coir Board is implementing Skill upgradation and Mahila Coir Yojana schemes for promotion and development of coir industry in the country including non-traditional areas. In addition, the scheme viz. Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) is also implementing in Coir Board. (e):	The Promotion of export of coir is one of the major activities of Coir Board.  The Coir Board is granting financial assistance to the coir units in the MSME sector under the International Co-operation Scheme of the Ministry of MSME with the objective of encouraging the MSMEs to enter into global market and to expand the export of Indian coir products. Ministry is also implementing a scheme titled ‘Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI)’.  The scheme organize the traditional industries and artisans into clusters to make them competitive by providing support for their long term sustainability and to provide sustained employment for traditional industry artisans and  rural entrepreneurs. As on 23.03.2021, 41 Coir clusters have been approved under SFURTI Scheme and 19 clusters have become functional.
