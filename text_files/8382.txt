                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1876






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Expansion of Ayushman Bharat Scheme




Khan Saumitra














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the number and percentage of population presently covered under the Ayushman Bharat Scheme, State-wise; (b) whether the Government is considering the expansion of Ayushman Bharat Scheme to cover all Indians; and (c) whether the Ministry has taken any steps after May, 2019 to bring West Bengal, Telangana, Odisha and New Delhi into the ambit of this scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) Services at Ayushman Bharat – Health & Wellness Centres are free and universal to all.           Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri – Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) aims to provide health cover up to Rs. 5 lakh per family per annum to around 10.74 crore families, which are approximately 40% of the total population. State wise details of beneficiaries covered are at  Annexure.(b):     No.    There is no proposal to expand the coverage of AB-PMJAY.(c) The Government has been requesting these State/UT Governments to join the scheme.………………….
