                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3044






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






Medical Research by ICMR




Jugal Kishore Sharma













                                                    Riti Pathak
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE  be pleased to state: (a) the details of major research projects started by Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) during the last three years and the current year; (b) whether the shortage/vacant posts of scientists and experts in ICMR has adversely affected its functioning and the progress of various projects initiated by it; (c) if so, the details thereof; and (d) the reformative measures taken/ proposed to be taken by the Government for capacity building and strengthening of ICMR and streamlining its functioning?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):     Details are annexed.(b) & (c):    No. However 30 scientists have been recruited recently and process to fill-up remaining vacant posts, has been undertaken.(d):       The knowledge and skills of scientific personnel are being regularly updated through their participation in various national and international level trainings, workshops and conferences. Upgradation of National Institute of Virology (NIV) unit at Gorakhpur has been done to a full fledged centre-Regional Medical Research Centre (RMRC), Gorakhpur. As part of streamlining of ICMR, number of institutes under ICMR, have been brought down from                  32 to 26.………………..
