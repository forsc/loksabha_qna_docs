                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3029






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Irrigation Projects




 Imtiaz Jaleel Syed














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of irrigation projects being funded by Union Government in Maharashtra and Telangana; (b) whether both the State Governments have requested the Union Government to declare some irrigation projects as National projects and to share the funding; (c) if so, the details thereof; and (d) the steps taken or being taken by the Union Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)  During 2016-17, ninety-nine (99) on-going Major/Medium irrigation projects under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) – Accelerated Irrigation Benefits Programme (AIBP) having ultimate irrigation potential of 76.03 lakh ha. and balance estimated cost of Rs.77595 crore [Central Assistance (CA) component of Rs.31342 crore] have been prioritized in consultation with States, for completion in phases up to December, 2019 along with their Command Area Development & Water Management (CADWM) works. Funding mechanism through NABARD has also been approved by the Government for both Central and State Share. Details of AIBP and CADWM components of the prioritized projects in Maharashtra and Telangana are as below:PMKSY-AIBP                 					    (CA in Rs. CA component amounts to Rs.3831.41 crore.  The targeted additional potential through these projects is 3.77 LHa.  These projects are planned to be completed in phases by 2022-23.  CA of Rs.500 crore and Rs.160 crore has been released/sanctioned to the Government of Maharashtra under this package during 2018-19 and 2019-20 (so far) respectively. (b) to (d)  One of the prioritized projects of Maharashtra namely, Gosikhurd Irrigation Project is a National Project. Further, Hon’ble Chief Minister of Telangana vide letter dated 11-02-2016 requested that Kaleshwaram Project be accorded status of National Project. However, no formal request in prescribed proforma has been received from the State Government of Telangana in this regard for consideration of the High Powered Steering Committee (HPSC) of Central Government constituted for implementation of National Projects.*****
