                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3881






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Irregularities by Multi-State Cooperative Organisations




Dilip Ghosh














                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware about the increasing illegal and irregular activities of the Cooperative Societies’ in the country, particularly in the State of West Bengal during the year 2012-2020, if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government is considering to take stringent action against such Multi-State Cooperative Organizations including any corrective steps taken/being taken by the Government to curb the same, if so, the details thereof; and(c)	whether the Government has established any mechanism to provide relief to the investors of such Societies and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COOPERATION???????? ?????? 	(SHRI AMIT SHAH)(a) to (c): Complaints have been received for non – repayment of funds against some of the multi-State cooperative societies, including those in the State of West Bengal. In case of delinquent societies, action for winding up is taken under section 86 of the Multi State Cooperative Societies (MSCS) Act, 2002. State-wise list of such societies is placed at Annexure - I.
