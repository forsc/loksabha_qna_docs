                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1132






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Green India Mission




Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the progress made under implementation of Green India Mission alongwith the targets achieved, State-wise;(b)	the total funds released during the last two years till March, 2020, State-wise;(c)	the number of such States that have been provided alternative sources of energy in project areas, State-wise; and(d)	the details of scheduled programmes undertaken during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a)	National Mission for a Green India(GIM) is one of the eight Missions under the National Action Plan on Climate Change. It aims at protecting, restoring and enhancing India’s forest and tree cover and responding to Climate Change. The implementation of various activities under the Mission started in the year 2015-16. A sum of Rs. 343.08 crore has been released to thirteen states during the period 2015-16 to 2019-20 for undertaking afforestation activities over an area of 142684 ha and distribution of alternate fuel energy devices to 33099 households. The State wise details of the achievements against the targets sanctioned is placed at Annexure-1.(b)	The total amount of Rs. 185.45 crore has been allocated during 2018-19 and 2019-20 to twelve states under the Mission. The State wise details of fund released during the last two years is placed at Annexure-2.(c)	33,099 alternative fuel energy devices have been distributed in eight states. The State wise details of alternative fuel energy devices are placed at Annexure-3.(d)	The afforestation activities are being carried out under  five sub-missions of National Mission for a Green India and include enhancing quality of Moderately dense forest cover, Eco-restoration of degraded open forests, Restoration of grasslands, Rehabilitation of Shifting Cultivation areas, Restoring Scrublands, Restoring/ planting Seabuckthorn, Restoration of Mangroves, Ravine reclamation, Restoration of abandoned mining area, Agro-forestry, Shelterbelt plantations, Highways/Rural roads/Canals/Tank Bunds plantation and Restoration of Wetlands as per the approved Annual Plan of Operation of the state. The afforestation activities have been carried out over an area of 142684 ha.****
