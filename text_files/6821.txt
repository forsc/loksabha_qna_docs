                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

524






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Construction of Railway Line




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has proposal for the construction of a third railway line between Rourkela and Jharsuguda in Odisha;(b) if so, the estimated cost and the details of funds sanctioned for that project; (c) the time limit fixed to complete the construction work of new railway line;(d) the estimated number of people likely to be displaced on implementation of that line; and(e) the steps taken to rehabilitate those people and the amount of compensation paid/likely to be paid to each affected family?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b):  Yes, Sir. Rourkela-Jharsuguda 3rd line (101 km) was sanctioned in the year 2015-16. The latest anticipated cost of the project is `1312.97 crore. An expenditure of `453.94 crore has already been incurred up to March 2019 and an outlay of `150 crore has been provided in the Budget 2019-20. (c): Due to large scale encroachments (801 in number) in Rourkela-Panposh section on railway land, the construction work for the above railway line has been affected badly and project is held up. (d): Nearly 4000 people of nearly 747 families are to be displaced due to requirement of removal of 801 Nos. (approx.) encroachments on Railway land on implementation of this line.(e): The land is of Railway and encroachers have encroached the Railway land illegally. There is no provision from the Railway side for rehabilitation of encroachers and the same has been advised to State Government.*****
