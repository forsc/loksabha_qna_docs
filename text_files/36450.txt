                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3864






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Pending Cases of Road Accidents and Insurance Claims




Rama Devi














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the cases pertaining to road accidents and settlement of related insurance claims take a long time for disposal in the courts; (b) if so, the reasons therefor; (c) the number of such cases pending in the country as on date, State/UT-wise including Bihar; (d) the number of cases pending for more than five and ten years; (e) whether the Government has taken any corrective steps in this regard; and(f) if so, the details thereof?	






ANSWER




                                                        The MINISTER OF LAW AND JUSTICE(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU) (a) to (f):	The information is being collected and will be laid on the Table of the House.******
