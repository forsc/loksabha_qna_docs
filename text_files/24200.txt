                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

38






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Direct Benefit Transfer




 Dr. T. Sumathy (a) Thamizhachi Thangapandian














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Controller General of Accounts maintains all the details of Direct Benefit Transfer;(b)	if so, the details of reports maintained;(c)	the list of all types of reports being stored by the Ministry on Direct Benefit Transfer; and(d)	the scheme-wise errors, amounts and numbers reported during Direct Benefit Transfer to beneficiaries during financial year 2020-2021.






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) to (d) : Public Financial Management System (PFMS) O/o Controller General of Accounts (CGA), Ministry of Finance maintains the data pertaining to the Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) transactions in respect of Central Sector Schemes (CS) and Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSS) which are on-boarded on PFMS by the Ministries/Departments of Government of India. There are 6 different DBT reports for monitoring the payment status by the user Ministries/Departments. The reports available on PFMS portal are; (i) DBT01: Beneficiaries Registration Status (ii) DBT04: District wise DBT Transaction summary (iii) DBT05:Scheme wise DBT Transaction Summary (iv) DBT07: State wise DBT Transaction Summary (v) DBT09: Scheme wise Performance Report for DBT Transactions (vi) DBT16: PAO-DBT Payment Status. The scheme-wise errors, amounts and numbers reported during Direct Benefit Transfer to beneficiaries are dealt appropriately by concerned Ministries/Departments.
