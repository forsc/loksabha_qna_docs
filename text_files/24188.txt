                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

213






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






National Mission on Cultural Mapping in West Bengal and Gujarat




Raju Bista













                                                    Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has undertaken a cultural mapping in West Bengal and Gujarat States under the National Mission on Cultural Mapping;(b)	the details of programs undertaken under the Cultural Mapping Mission;(c)	the number of artists and institutions registered, State/UT-wise including West Bengal and 	Gujarat under the mission till date and the benefits/assistance extended to the registered 	artists/institutions; and(d)	the details of the cultures or villages that were mapped in Gujarat and West Bengal State, 	especially in the districts of Darjeeling and Kalimpong; and(e)	whether the Government planning to expand the mission and if so, the details thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d) Ministry of Culture is working on the National Mission on Cultural Mapping (NMCM) for building a comprehensive data base of artists, art forms and other resources gathered from cultural organizations in the Ministry of Culture, other Ministries and also States including Gujarat and West Bengal on a robust IT-enabled platform.  So far 14.53 lakhs artists/artisans have been registered on NMCM portal through secondary sources State/UT-wise.  However, no direct benefits or assistance has been extended through this Mission to the registered artists/institutions.(e)    No such proposal is under consideration with the Government.
