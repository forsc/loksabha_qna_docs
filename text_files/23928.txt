                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

424






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






National Service Scheme




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of students engaged under National Service Scheme in the State of Maharashtra during the last three years, year-wise; (b) whether the State Government of Maharashtra has proposed to increase the said number; (c) if so, the details thereof; and (d) the steps taken by the Union Government to help the students’ holistic development and appreciating the diversity of the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI KIREN RIJIJU](a) to (d):	A Statement is laid on the table of the House. STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) TO (D) OF STARRED QUESTION No. 424 TO BE ANSWERED ON 25.03.2021 REGARDING NATIONAL SERVICE SCHEME ASKED BY SHRI SUNIL BABURAO MENDHE.(a)	The number of students engaged under National Service Scheme in the State of Maharashtra during the last three years, year-wise is as follows:<pre>S. No.	Year	Number of Volunteers1.	2017-18	3277002.	2018-19	3277003.	2019-20	327700  </pre>(b) No, the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports has not received any proposal from Govt. of Maharashtra to increase the said number.(c) Does not arise.(d)	The Union Government  helps the students through different ways for holistic development and  appreciating the diversity of the country. The National Service Scheme programmes also include the celebration of national days, comprehensive programme on functional literacy, Ek Bharat Shrestha Bharat, AIDS awareness programme and sustainable development with emphasis on watershed management & wasteland development, prevention of substance abuse etc. NSS Volunteers come forward with zeal to make these programmes a great success. Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports has organized 2nd edition of National Youth Parliament Festival 2021 from 24th December, 2020 to 5th January, 2021 at District and State Level through virtual mode and the finals at National level was organized on 11th & 12th January, 2021 at Central Hall, Parliament House, New Delhi. During COVID several activities were undertaken as shown in Annexure I.Further, the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India had instituted the National Service Scheme (NSS) Awards to recognize the voluntary service rendered by University/+2 Councils, Programme Officers and their NSS Units and NSS Volunteers. The details of these awards are given in Annexure-II.******
