                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2819






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Class Ten English Exam




Geetha Viswanath Vanga













                                                    Y. S. Avinash Reddy
                                                








                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has expressed regret for including a misogynistic comprehension exercise in its Class 10 English examination, 	and vowed to review its paper setting processes; and(b) whether the Government proposes to drop such question/s and all students  granted full 	marks for that section as one of the question paper contained a passage which blame a 	“feminist revolt” for teenage indiscipline and if so the details thereof and corrective 	steps/measures being taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) and (b): It came to the notice of Central Board of Secondary Education(CBSE)  that a passage in the question paper of English (Language & Literature) of  Class-X was not in accordance with the guidelines of setting question papers. Accordingly, the passage was dropped from the question paper and to maintain uniformity it was decided that full marks will be awarded to all the students for this question to all sets of question papers.*****
