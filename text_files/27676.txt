                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3258






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.08.2021






Oil and Gas Reserves




Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah













                                                    Pushpendra Singh Chandel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any special initiative to explore new oil and gasreserves;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the State-wise details of the domestic and foreign investment made in the oil andgas sector during the last three years?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b): Hydrocarbon Exploration and Licensing Policy was launched on 30thMarch, 2016 to award exploration acreages. So far, 105 blocks have been awardedwith an area of 1.57 lakh square kilometre. Further, the Government is facilitatingexploration by generating data through National Seismic Program, launched in2017, and disseminating through National Data Repository.(c) State wise details of investment made in oil and gas sector by upstreamcompanies are annexed.
