                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1368






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Inclusion of New Tourist Destination in Assam Under Adopt A Heritage Project




Pallab Lochan Das














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of tourist spots in Assam under adopt a Heritage 	‘Apni Dharohar, Apni Pehchaan‘ Project;(b) 	whether it is fact that many tourist destinations/ heritage 	/natural/tourist sites and monuments in Assam lack basic 	tourist amenities and facilities;(c)	 if so the details thereof;(d) 	whether the Government is planning to include any new 	tourist destination from Assam under the adopt a heritage 	project; and (e)	 the steps taken by the Government to develop tourist sites as 	tourist friendly destinations? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM			      (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY) (a):	No tourist spot in Assam is under adopt a Heritage ‘Apni Dharohar, Apni Pehchaan‘ Project.(b),(c)&(e):	The details of step taken by the Ministry of Tourism to develop tourist sites in the state of Assam as tourist friendly destinations is annexed.(d):	Adopt a Heritage Project details has also been shared  with the State Government of Assam with request to share the list of heritage monuments/standalone structures/tourist sites that can be promoted for adoption under the “Adopt a Heritage” project with brief about the site. The list is awaited from the State Government.*******
