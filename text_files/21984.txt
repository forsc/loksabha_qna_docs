                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3105






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.03.2021






Merger of ONGC




T.N. Prathapan













                                                    Aparupa  Poddar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the reasons behind the delay of the merger of Oil and Natural Gas Corporation(ONGC) with two oil refining subsidiaries, Hindustan Petroleum Corp Ltd (HPCL) andMangalore Refinery and Petrochemicals Ltd (MRPL) to Financial Year-24;(b) the benefits this merger will accrue therefrom; and(c) whether this merger aids India’s oil and gas exploration in the post-COVID-19economy and if so, the details thereof along with the foreseeable effects of themerger to import of oil and natural gas?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM & NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a): A detailed study was got conducted by ONGC on the issue of Merger of HPCLand its subsidiaries with ONGC. Merger of various organizations is based on therequirement of business, feasibility and economic considerations.(b): Integrated oil and gas companies have lower volatility in comparison to pureupstream or downstream companies. Integration also mitigates the risk of volatility inprices. There may however be some negative points also.(c): The integration has both positive and negative implications.*******
