                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

31






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






PLI Scheme




Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu














                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has approved the Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme in Food Processing Sector and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether any new investments have been generated by Food Processing Industries sector under the scheme thereof;(c)	if so, the investment amount and projected job generation, per State per product line thereof;(d)	the details of expenditure incurred against the funds allocated for the scheme in the FY 2020-21, per State and per product line;(e)	whether the Government has fixed any target for the increase in export value and job creation as a result of the scheme; and(f)	if so, the details thereof, per State and per product line?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a):	Based on the cabinet note of NITI Aayog on "Aatma Nirbhar Bharat Abhiyan for enhancing India''s Manufacturing Capabilities and Enhancing Exports" Government of India on 11.11.2020 approved for introduction of Production Linked Incentive Scheme in 10 sectors specific product lines including food processing sector. It was decided that final proposal of PLI Scheme for each individual sector has to be approved by the Government. The specific proposal of PLI scheme for Food Processing Industry is yet to be approved by the Government.(b) to (f): PLI Scheme for Food Processing Industry is yet to be approved by the Government. In the light of this, the question does not arise. *****
