                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2457






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Smart Schools




Rahul Kaswan














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has received any proposal to open ‘Smart Schools’ in all theStates including Rajasthan;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the salient features of such schools;(c) whether the Government has given administrative and financial approval to theseproposals; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b) : Government of Nagaland has submitted a proposal for one school each in 10Districts Headquarters to be upgraded as Smart Schools at unit cost of Rs. 25 lakhs perschool. No other State including Government of Rajasthan has sent any proposal to openthe ‘Smart Schools’.(c) & (d) : There is no provision for setting up of Smart Schools under Samagra Shikshaguidelines. The provision is only for setting up of Information and CommunicationTechnology(ICT) lab at a unit cost of Rs. 6.4 lakhs per school.***
