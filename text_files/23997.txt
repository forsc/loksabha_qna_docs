                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4916






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Amritsar-Jamagar Green Expressway




Jaskaur Meena













                                                    Nihal Chand Chauhan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details and the present status of Amritsar-Jamnagar Green 	Expressway being constructed  under the Bharatmala Pariyojana;(b)	the estimated expenditure likely to be incurred thereon along with 	the time limit fixed for completion of the said project;(c)	whether any challenges are being faced by the Government in 	execution of the said project and if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the details and the present status of other roads linked with 	Bharatmala Pariyojana in Rajasthan?






ANSWER




                                                         ¬THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d)  Amritsar-Jamanager Economic Corridor is included in Bharatmala Pariyojana Phase-I for development. Total length of this corridor is 1,225 km (917 km Greenfield and 308 km Brownfield) in the State of Punjab, Haryana, Rajasthan and Gujarat. Total estimated capital cost of the development of Amritsar-Jamanager Economic Corridor is Rs. 27,560 crore. The road works (except in the stretch from Amritsar to Bathinda of 155 km) have been awarded and are in different stages of implementation with target for completion by Year 2023-24. Government is facing difficulties in implementation of Sangariya-Santalpur Greenfield Corridor in the State of Rajasthan due to delay in the acquisition and possession of land specifically in Hanumangarh, Bikaner and Jalore districts. Total 44 road projects comprising length of 2,138 kms have been envisaged for development under Bharatmala Pariyojana Phase-I in the State of Rajasthan, out of which 40 projects of 1,887 kms have been awarded.*****
