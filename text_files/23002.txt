                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3994






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






COVID-19 Vaccination in Assam




Kripanath Mallah














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the salient features of the vaccine being used to combat the COVID-19 within Assam;(b) the number of districts covered so far within Assam under this vaccination process; and(c) the steps being taken by the Government for remote/inaccessible areas under this mission within Assam?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a): Two vaccines, namely Covishield manufactured by M/s Serum Institute of India and Covaxin manufactured by M/s Bharat Biotech International Limited, that have been granted permission for restricted use in emergency situation by the National Regulator i.e. Drugs Controller General of India [DCG(I)] are being used under the programme in Assam.(b):  All districts of Assam are covered under COVID-19 vaccination drive.(c):  As per the COVID-19 Vaccines Operational Guidelines, for remote areas, sessions can be organized for lesser number of beneficiaries ensuring no vaccine wastage, provision of special mobile teams, concurrent monitoring to be prioritised in such areas and other strategies as per the local context.
