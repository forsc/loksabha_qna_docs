                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

880






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






New Train from Ujjain




Pushpendra Singh Chandel














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received any proposal to     introduce a train from Ujjain to Ayodhya via Mahoba;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the time by which this new train is likely to be introduced?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY  (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c):  Representations, including that from Hon’ble Member of Parliament,  have been received for  introduction of train from Ujjain to Ayodhya via Mahoba.  Owing to operational constraints, introduction of train between Ujjain & Ayodhya via Mahoba is not feasible at present.  However, 3 pairs of trains connect Ayodhya & Ujjain, including services of Sabarmati Express which runs via Jhansi.  ( around 140 km from Mahoba).*****
