                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4206






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Construction of Delhi-SaharanpurYamunotri NH




Haji Fazlur Rehman














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the acquisition of land for construction of Delhi-Saharanpur-Yamunotri National Highway No. 709-B has been completed; (b) 	if so, the details and the present status thereof; and (c) 	the time by which the project is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) National Highway -709B starts from its junction with NH-9 near Akshardham (in Delhi) and traverses through Shashtri Park, Khajoori khas, EPE Crossing, Baghpat, Baraut, Shamli and terminating at its junction with NH-344 near Saharanpur.  The civil work of this road stretch has been envisaged in 04 nos. of packages.  The civil work of 02 nos. of packages i.e. EPE Crossing to start of Shamli Bypass and start of Shamli Bypass to joining at Saharanpur bypass has been awarded with scheduled completion period as 18 months and 24 months respectively from appointed date.  The total land required for these packages is about 390 Hectares, out of which 368 Hectares land is available with about 22 Hectares balance for acquisition.  The work for DPR/feasibility study of 02 nos. of packages i.e. Akshardham to Delhi/UP Border and Delhi/UP Boarder to EPE Crossing has been initiated. *****
