                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PANCHAYATI RAJ






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

269






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Funds Released for Panchayati Raj




S. Jothimani













                                                    Janardan Singh Sigriwal
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PANCHAYATI RAJ


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the budgetary outlay for Panchayati Raj institutions made for Financial Year 2020-2021;(b) the details of funds released for Panchayati Raj in the Financial Year 2019- 2020 and 2020-2021, State-wise;(c) whether there are dues pending in the allocation of funds to the State of  Tamil Nadu; and(d) if so, the reasons thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR PANCHAYATI RAJ(SHRI KAPIL MORESHWAR PATIL)(a)  A sum of Rs.690.00 crore (Revised Estimate) was provided to Ministry of Panchayati Raj (MoPR) for the year 2020-21, out of which Rs.546.94 crore was allocated for strengthening Panchayati Raj Institutions and incentivisation for their developmental works under the  Centrally Sponsored Scheme-Rashtriya Gram Swaraj Abhiyan (RGSA) and Incentivisation of Panchayats, a Central component of RGSA.  Under the Fifteenth Finance Commission Award, an amount of Rs.60750 crore was allocated to Panchayati Raj Institutions during 2020-21.  (b)    The State-wise release of funds under the schemes of Ministry of Panchayati Raj (MoPR) and out of awards of Fourteenth Finance Commission and Fifteenth Finance Commission are given at Annexure.  (c) No prior allocation of funds is made to any States under the Schemes of Ministry of Panchayati Raj. The funds under the RGSA Scheme is released based on the annual action plan submitted by the State concerned including the State of Tamil Nadu and funds under the scheme of Incentivisation of Panchyats is released to the best performing Panchayats selected through a national competition for the National Panchayat Awards of various categories.    For the State of Tamil Nadu, dues of Central Finance Commission Grants are not pending as the award period 2015-20 of Fourteenth Finance Commission (XIV FC) is already over and the Fifteenth Finance Commission (XV FC) Grants have been released upto the first installment of Basic (Untied) Grants for FY 2021-22.(d) Does not arise in view of (c) above. ******
