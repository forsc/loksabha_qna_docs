                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4041






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Funds under Centrally Sponsored Schemes




Ashok Kumar Yadav













                                                    Bheemrao Baswanthrao Patil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of funds allocated and utilised under various Central Area Schemes and Centrally Sponsored Schemes for Bihar particularly Madhubani district;(b)	the percentage of funds utilised for Bihar particularly Madhubani district out of the allocated funds;(c)	whether the funds utilised is less than the funds allocated and if so, the reasons therefor;(d)	whether any order has been issued to ensure the complete utilisation of the funds in future by reviewing the said reasons; and(e)	whether the Government has also released funds to Telangana State for various centrally sponsored schemes during the last three years and if so, the details thereof, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(SHRI PANKAJ CHAUDHARY)(a) to (d): The Union Government releases funds under the Centrally Sponsored Schemes (CSSs) to the State Governments. The district-wise or State-wise data on allocation and release of funds under different CSSs is not being maintained in Ministry of Finance except allocation of funds under different Schemes and overall release/expenditure under the Scheme as whole. The Schemes are implemented by the respective administrative Ministries/ Departments within the approved financial outlays as per Scheme design. However, as per Public Financial Management System (PFMS), an amount of Rs. 17,176.64 crore under the CSSs for the financial year 2021-22 (as on 23.03.2022) has been released to the State of Bihar.(e):  As per PFMS, releases under the CSSs during the last three years to the State of Telangana are given as below:                                                                                                                                 (Rs. in crore)                 Year	2018-19	          2019-20	 2020-21                Amount	11,844.53	12,200.60	14,414.21                                                                *****
