                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1123






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Development of Theme Based Circuit in Odisha




Anubhav Mohanty














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has had any proposal to develop theme-based circuits under the Swadesh Darshan scheme in the State of Odisha, more specifically eco-tourism circuits, during the last two years; (b) 	if so, the status of that proposal; (c) 	whether the Government is serious about taking up eco-tourism circuits; (d) 	if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	the time by which these tourism circuits are likely to be taken up for approval?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e):	Development of tourism is primarily the responsibility of the concerned State Government/UT Administration. However, for integrated development of Theme-Based Tourist Circuits in the country including Odisha, Ministry of Tourism, Government of India has launched the Swadesh Darshan Scheme in 2014-15. Under the scheme fifteen thematic circuits have been identified for development including Eco-Tourism Circuit. The sanctioning of new projects under the scheme is a continuous process. Projects for development under the scheme are identified in consultations with the State Governments/UT Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier.Ministry of Tourism has sanctioned a project Development of Gopalpur,	Barkul, Satapada and Tampara under Coastal Circuit theme of Swadesh Darshan Scheme for Rs.70.82 crore in 2016 in the state of Odisha.A list of Projects sanctioned under Eco-Tourism Circuit of Swadesh Darshan Scheme in the country is as detailed below. <pre>S.
