                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2313






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Fake Universities




Dean Kuriakose














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware that several fake universities are cheating thestudents in the country;(b) if so, the details and the number of such cases reported during each of the last threeyears and the current year and the action taken by the Government thereon; and(c) the remedial measures taken by the Government, including issuing of noticescautioning the public against such fake universities?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): Yes, Sir. On the basis of complaints received from the students, parents,general public and also through electronic print media, University Grants Commission(UGC) has released the list of 23 fake Universities which are offering Degreeprogrammes without recognition under Section 2(f) and Section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956.In addition, UGC issued public notice against the unrecognized status of Indian Instituteof Planning and Management (IIPM), New Delhi and illegal status of Bio-ChemicEducation Grant Commission, Nadia, West Bengal. Bhartiya Shiksha Parishad, Lucknow,Uttar Pradesh is also not recognized by the UGC under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956.The details of these fake Universities are available on its website www.ugc.ac.in.During the last three years and current year, the following Institutions have beenincluded in the list of fake Universities:<pre>i. North Orissa University of Agriculture & Technology, University Road Baripada,Distt. Mayurbhanj, Odisha-757003 (2016-17).ii. Viswakarma Open University for Self-employment, India, Rozgar Sewasadan,672, sanjay Enclave, Opp. GTK DEPOT, New Delhi-110033 (2016-17).iii. Sree Bodhi Academy of Higher Education, No. 186, Thilaspet, VazhuthavoorRoad, Pondicherry-605009 (2017-18).iv. Adhyatmik Vishwavidyalaya (Spiritual University), 351-352, Phase-I, Block-A,vijay Vihar, Rithala, Rohini, Delhi (2018-19).(c): The following steps have been taken by the UGC to warn people against fakeUniversities:i. Published public Notices on list of fake Universities/Institutes in Hindi andEnglish Newspapers.ii. Updated the list of fake Universities on its website.iii. Sent letters to State Chief Secretaries, Education Secretaries and PrincipalSecretaries to take action against fake Universities/Institutes located in theirjurisdiction.iv. Issued Public Notices on illegal status of Bio-Chemic Education GrantCommission, Nadia, West Bengal and IIPM, New Delhi.v. Lodged FIRs in Police station against Bio-Chemic Education GrantCommission.</pre>*****
