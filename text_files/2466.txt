                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2193






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Employment in MSME Sector




Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of persons for whom target has been set by the Government for providing employment in Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs); and(b)	the time likely to be taken to achieve these targets and the extent to which MSMEs are likely to be affected as a result thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI  NITIN GADKARI)(a)&(b): 	Ministry of MSME implements Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP), which is a credit linked subsidy programme, aimed at generating self-employment opportunities through establishment of micro-enterprises in the non-farm sector by helping traditional artisans and unemployed youth. Under this programme, subsidy of 25% to 35% of the project cost upto Rs. 25.00 lakh is provided as Margin Money Subsidy. Targets and achievements under the Scheme for the financial years 2017-18 and 2018-19 are given below.<pre>Financial Year	Margin money subsidy annual targets (RE) (Rs. in lakh)	Margin money subsidy utilized# (Rs. in lakh)	Number of projectsassisted	EstimatedEmployment generated  (No. of persons)2017-18	117000131240.07483983871842018-19	196880207000.5473427587416</pre>	# including un-utilized balance funds of previous year.Under the Scheme, a tentative target of assisting 73241 projects with an estimated employment generation to 585927 persons, has been fixed for the financial year 2019-20.
