                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2640






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2022






Construction of National Highway-927A




Kanakmal Katara














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any time frame has been fixed for the construction of Deval – Dungarpur - Sagwara National Highway (NH)-927A;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the funds sanctioned and released for the purpose;(c) the total number of saplings proposed to be planted against the total number of trees felled on both sides of the said NH during its construction; and(d) the steps being taken by the Government for maintenance of these saplings? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) Yes Sir. The scheduled date of completion of construction of Deval - Dungarpur - Sagwara National Highway (NH)-927A is 18.01.2024.(b)     Total Sanctioned amount for the project is Rs 290.18 crore and amount released so far is Rs 1.99 crore.(c) & (d) 1804 trees have been felled during construction of above mentioned project. An amount of Rs 51.95 lakhs has been released to the Forest Department, Government of Rajasthan for compensatory afforestation including maintenance of planted saplings.*****
