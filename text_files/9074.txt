                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2720






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Sewa Service Trains




V. Kalanidhi













                                                    Andimuthu Raja
                                                








                                                    Ajay (Teni) Misra
                                                








                                                    S. Ramalingam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to introduce Sewa Service Trains to ensure better last mile connectivity between smaller towns in short distance within the country and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government has identified the smaller towns to be covered under this service across the country including Tamil Nadu;(c) if so, the State-wise details of the routes on which these Sewa Services Trains are likely to be introduced along with the details of services proposed to be offered to the passengers including in North Chennai; (d) the details of the funds likely to be sanctioned for this; and (e) the time by which these are likely to be connected?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (e) To facilitate the travelling passengers and provide rail connectivity between smaller towns and important cities as also to meet the short-distance travelling requirements of the passengers, Indian Railways have introduced 12 pairs of “Sewa” services, w.e.f. 15.10.2019, over various origination-destination pairs, which include  03 pairs of “Sewa” services, namely 76801/76802 Karur – Salem Sewa DEMU, 56609/56610 Palani – Coimbatore Sewa Passenger and 56183/56184 Coimbatore – Pollachi Sewa Passenger, catering to the stations located in the State of Tamil Nadu. There is no proposal to introduce additional “Sewa” services, at present. However, introduction of trains services, including “Sewa” services, is an ongoing process, subject to operational feasibility, availability of resources etc.*****
