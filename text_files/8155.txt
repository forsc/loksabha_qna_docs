                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1681






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Nal Se Jal Scheme




S. Muniswamy














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the implementation status of the Nal Se Jal scheme;(b) the action being taken by the Government in this regard; and(c) the funds allocated for the said scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) Government of India has launched Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM), which aims at providing potable water to every rural household through Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) at service level of 55 litre per capita per day (lpcd) by 2024. As reported, as on 21.11.2019, about 18.41% [3.29 Crore] rural households have tap connection with piped water supply. (b) Under JJM, a budgetary allocation of?10,000.66 Crore has been made for the year 2019-20 and first instalment hasbeen released to eligible States/ UTs. Further, a conference of Minister in-charge of rural water supply of various States was held in New Delhi followed by five regional workshops to discuss various aspects of JJM and modalities of the implementation. In addition, regular review of the programme is also held with States/ UTs.(c) Planned outlay of JJM in five years is ? 3.60 lakh Crore, out of which Central Share is about ? 2.08 lakh Crore.*****
