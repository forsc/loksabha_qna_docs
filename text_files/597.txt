                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

535






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.06.2019






Course on Fishing




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to formulate a course/conduct a programme to make fishing more professional and skill based; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the time by which the said plan / programme is likely to be implemented?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	to (c) Central Institute of Fisheries, Nautical and Engineering Training (CIFNET), functioning under the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is already conducting a professional fisheries degree course, Bachelor of Fisheries Science-Nautical Science with four years duration and  two skill based courses namely (i) Vessel Navigator Course (VNC) and (ii) Marine Fitter Course (MFC) with duration of 2 years to cater to the needs of fishing vessel  operators like Skipper / Mate / Bosun / Chief Engineers / Engine Drivers of the fishing vessels. In addition, short-term skill based training programmes on safety navigation and fishing  including capacity building in deep sea fishing onboard handling of tuna are conducted for fishers and technocrats in the country. Besides, Fisheries research Institutes like Central Institute of Fisheries Technology (CIFT) and Central Institute of Fisheries Education (CIFE) functioning under Indian Council of Agriculture Research (ICAR) also conduct Training programmes and Post Graduate level courses on fishing.
