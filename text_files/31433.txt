                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2597






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Beneficiaries under MGNREGS in West Bengal




Khan Saumitra














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the beneficiaries under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) in rural areas are not getting employment as per the entitled amount, if so, the details thereof and the steps being taken by the Government in this regard;(b) 	the number of beneficiaries in West Bengal who have got employed in the month of June during each of years from 2018 to 2021 under MGNREGS;(c) 	whether the number of beneficiaries employed in 2021 are less as compared to the previous years;(d) 	the percentage of rural population currently unemployed; and(e) 	the details of employment given under the scheme to the migrant workers who have returned to their homes in rural areas due to COVID-19?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a):  Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven programme for the enhancement of livelihood security of the households in rural areas of the country by providing at least 100 days of guaranteed wage employment in every financial year to every household  whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work.  (b):  The details of households availed employment in the State of West Bengal in the month of June during the years 2018, 2019, 2020 and  2021 under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS is given below:  June, 2018	June, 2019	  June, 2020	    June, 2021  19,48,890       9,03,207	  37,31,510	    31,35,209 (c):   Month-wise details of households availed employment under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS from January, 2020 to November, 2020 and January, 2021 to November, 2021 is given at Annexure. (d):   There is no official system of estimation of current level of rural employment. As per data available from Center for Monitoring of Indian Economy(CMIE), rural unemployment rate  stood at 6.44 % for the month of November 2021.(e):       No separate statistics is maintained for returnee migrants who have availed employment under the Mahatma Gandhi NREGS. ******
