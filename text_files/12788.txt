                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1773






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Private Sector in Skill Training




Feroze Varun Gandhi














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is any participation of the private sectors in the skill training programmes that bring in the perspective of employer’s needs;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the details of the educational background of the people enrolled in skill development programmes?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b)	Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship is implementing the demand driven scheme, Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 2016-20 for short duration skill development training (Short-Term Training (STT) and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL)) in the country. In order to bring the perspective of employer’s needs, Sector Skill Councils (SSCs), which are industry-led bodies, have been made responsible for defining the skilling needs, concept, processes, certification, and accreditation of their respective industry sectors in accordance with National Skill Qualification Framework (NSQF).Additionally, under STT component of PMKVY (2016-20), an Employer led model has been envisaged to impart training which is placement linked and directly addresses the industry needs. Under this model proposals are invited either by employers directly or by consortium between employer with new/existing well performing training provider for enhanced employer participation. Currently, 90+ Training Provider have been allocated target of 70,000+ under the employer led mode under PMKVY (2016-2020)(c)	Under PMKVY (2016-20), the educational background of the candidate enrolled varies from uneducated to post graduate.
