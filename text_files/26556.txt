                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2107






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Restoration of Markandeshwar Temple in Maharashtra




Prataprao  Jadhav













                                                    Sanjay Sadashivrao Mandlik
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Subrata Pathak
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                








                                                    Bidyut Baran Mahato
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) has undertaken restoration work of various temples situated in the country and if so, the details thereof, Temple-wise;(b)	whether the ASI has initiated the restoration work of Markandeshwar Temple in Maharashtra;(c)	if so, the details thereof and  the time by which the said restoration work is likely to be completed; and(d)	the total expenditure involved therein along with the steps taken/ being taken by the ASI to complete the restoration works in a time bound manner?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	Conservation, preservation, restoration and environmental development work of the centrally protected monuments including temples in the country is a continuous process and the same is attended regularly by the Archaeological Survey of India(ASI) as per the requirement of the different sites and availability of resources.(b) &  (c)	Yes Sir, the conservation work of Markandeshwar Temple, Gadchiroli, Maharashtra has been taken in a phased manner as per the availability of funds and skilled manpower. The works is likely to be completed by 2022-23. (d)	The expenditure involved in the comprehensive conservation of group of temples at Markandeshwar, Distt. Gadchiroli  is Rs. 1,26,60,419/-. *****
