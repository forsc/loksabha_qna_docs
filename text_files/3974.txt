                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3651






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Destruction due to Storm




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether sudden dust storm, lightening and thundering that spread overmany districts of Uttar Pradesh and NCR on 6th June, 2019 had claimed many lives, injured many more and damage to houses, property and electricity have been reported from the affected areas;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the victims have been provided relief and monetary help by theGovernment; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (d): As per information received from Government of Uttar Pradesh, 29 human lives lost, 58 persons injured and 34 houses were damaged in 20 districts due to sudden dust storm, lightening and thundering occurred on 6th June, 2019. The Government of Uttar Pradesh has provided financial assitance to the victims as per State Disaster Response Fund norms. No loss was reported in National Capital Territory of Delhi. ******
