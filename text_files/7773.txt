                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LAW AND JUSTICE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1408






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Supreme Court Collegium




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





LAW AND JUSTICE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of recommendations of collegiums which are pending with the Union Government, as on date, and the reasons therefor; (b)	the number of recommendations of collegiums which have not been approved by the Union Government in the last three years along with the reasons therefor; and(c)	the names of all such persons recommended by the collegiums in the last three years for elevation as Supreme Court Judges, High Court Judges and Chief Justice(s) which were not approved by the Union Government?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF LAW AND JUSTICE, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) to (c) : There are 239 proposals  recommended by various High Courts Collegiums for appointment of High Court Judges, which are under various  stages of processing with the Government and Supreme Court Collegium as per the procedure  prescribed in Memorandum of Procedure (MoP). At present, no proposal of elevation of Supreme Court Judge and appointment of Chief Justice of High Court is pending.
