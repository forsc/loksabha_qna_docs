                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

66






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Development of Meerut under Swadesh Darshan Scheme




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of TOURISM be pleased to state:(a) 	whether the Government has proposed development of Meerut under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme; (b) 	if so, the details of the work proposed under the scheme along with work completed and those awaiting completion; and (c) 	the detailed list of developmental work carried out in Meerut under the scheme?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to  (c) The Ministry of Tourism under its ‘Swadesh Darshan Scheme’ has sanctioned 2 projects which include interventions at Meerut, the details of  which are annexed.
