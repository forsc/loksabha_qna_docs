                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1454






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Cooperation with Britain on Terrorism




Khagen Murmu













                                                    Ajay (Teni) Misra
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Britain has shown willingness to cooperate with India to combat global terrorism keeping in view the increasing threat and challenges of maritime security; and(b) if so, the details thereof and the extent to which maritime challenges and terrorism is likely to be checked as a result thereof, particularly in the Indian Ocean?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) & (b):  The matter relating to cooperation in Maritime Security was discussed during the 12th Meeting of the India-UK Counter Terrorism Joint Working Group held at New Delhi on 16th November, 2018.*****
