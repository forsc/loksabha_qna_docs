                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2415






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Special Cultural Fund For Organising Cultural Programmes in Jammu and Kashmir




Jugal Kishore Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any system to promote our invaluable culture for organizing cultural programmees particularly to promote cultural/tourism spots at district level in Jammu and Kashmir and establish Special Cultural Fund;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the co-ordination mechanism between the concerned officers and nodal agencies with respect to above objective including procedure for organizing such functions to utilise the allocated funds, promote tourism and business in Jammu and Kashmir State?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTRY OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d) The Ministry of Culture administers various scheme components such as Cultural Function Production Grant, Repertory Grant, Cultural Organizations with National Presence etc., under which grants are being given as financial assistance to the organizations to organise cultural events across the country including Jammu & Kashmir. Seven Zonal Cultural Centres across the country play a key role in promotion of culture through the programmes decided in consultation with their member State Government. Jammu & Kashmir is a member State of North Zone Cultural Centre (NZCC), Patiala and a part of the grant provided to NZCC is being utilized to organise cultural programmes in Jammu & Kashmir. However, the execution process is administered through state & not district.****
