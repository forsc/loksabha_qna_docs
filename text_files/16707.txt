                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

373






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Leakage in PDS




Shivkumar Chanabasappa Udasi














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there has been any move to assess the extent of leakage in Public Distribution System (PDS) from the point of procurement of grains till the point it reaches to the fair price shops; (b) 	if not, the reasons therefor and if so, the percentage of grains actually reaching the fair price shops during the last year; (c) 	the number of fake ration cards that have been found and cancelled as in December, 2013, State-wise; and (d) 	the percentage thereof vis-à-vis the existence of genuine ration cards as in December, 2013, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) & (b): 	Public Distribution System (PDS) is operated under the joint responsibilities of the Central and State/UT Governments. The Central Government through Food Corporation of India (FCI) is responsible for procurement, storage and allocation of foodgrains to States/UTs and for transportation of foodgrains upto the designated depots of the FCI in the States/UTs, whereas, the operational responsibilities for allocation within the State/UT, transportation up to Fair Price Shops (FPSs), identification of eligible beneficiaries/ households, issuance of ration cards to them, distribution of foodgrains through FPSs, supervision & monitoring of FPS operations, etc. rest with the respective State/UT Government. .............2/--2-	Further, this Department for the strengthening of PDS operations and to support the States/UTs in taking corrective actions, conducts periodic evaluation of PDS operations in States/UTs through multiple independent agencies, Monitoring Institutions (MI), etc. and no major gap has been indicated by any agency or MI in this regard in any State/UT. Besides, surveys conducted by independent agencies like Dalberg, MicroSave, etc. in various parts of the country have shown very high level of satisfaction of nearly 94% among the beneficiaries with regards to availability and distribution of foodgrains through PDS.(c) & (d):  	As per details available in the Department, a total of 46.65 Lakh ineligible/ duplicate ration cards were cancelled by States/UTs in 2013.
