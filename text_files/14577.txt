                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3390






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Increasing of Retirement Age ofAllopathy/AYUSH Doctors




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the decision on increasing the retirement age from 60 to 65 years for all the doctors (AYUSH and allopathy) was implemented in September, 2017 by the Government; if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the administrative control and financing of subordinate institutes of AYUSH, National Institute of Ayurveda, Jaipur and other autonomous institutes is being exercised by the Government; (c) whether the Government intends to increase the retirement age of AYUSH doctors (teachers and doctors) from 60 years to 65 years working in subordinate institutes like National Institute of Ayurveda, Jaipur and other autonomous institutes etc; (d) if so, the details thereof and by when it is likely to be done; and (e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a):  The enhancement of retirement age from 60 to 65 years for AYUSH doctors was implemented w.e.f. 27.09.2017 vide Ministry of AYUSH Order No. D.14019/4/2016-E.I (AYUSH) dated 24.11.2017 and for Allopathy doctors vide Ministry of Health & Family Welfare order No.A.12034/1/2014-CHS-V dated 31.05.2016.(b):  Yes.(c) to (e):   A consolidated proposal for increasing the superannuation age of teaching and non-teaching faculties of National Institutes under Ministry of AYUSH upto 65 years has been sent to Ministry of Health & Family Welfare for further action.…………………
