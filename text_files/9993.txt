                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3467






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Corruption in Jan Aushadhi Kendras




Pushpendra Singh Chandel














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether any incidence of corruption has come to light in the distribution of generic medicines through Jan AushadhiKendras in the country; (b) if so, the details thereof and the remedial measures taken by the Government in this regard; (c) whether the Government has received any proposal from private hospitals to review the prices of generic medicines; and (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




(a) & (b) No such complaint have been received by Bureau of Pharma PSUs of India (BPPI), the implementing agency of PradhanMantriBhartiyaJanaushadhiPariyojana (PMBJP).(c) & (d) Government has not received any proposal from private hospitals to review the prices of generic medicines. ******
