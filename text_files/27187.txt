                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2779






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Solar Parks




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government is setting up public and private solar parks in the State of Jharkhand for generation of renewable energy;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether construction of these parks is getting delayed, if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (d) whether the Union Government has formulated any plan to complete the construction of solar parks in time, if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(e) whether the Government has received any proposal for setting up of new solar parks in Jharkhand especially in Dhanbad, Bokaro, Giridih and Ramgarh districts; and(f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a)&(b) The Government has approved one floating solar park of total 150 MW capacity at Getalsud (100 MW) and Dhurwa (50 MW) dam in the State of Jharkhand.(c) As per sanction given by this Ministry, the time line for completion of the 150 MW solar park in Jharkhand is 04.09.2021.(d) The Solar Park developer is given time line of two years for completion of park. The           Ministry holds regular meetings with the Park developers and State Governments to facilitate      timely completion of the parks.(e)&(f) The Government of Jharkhand has submitted a proposal for development of solar parks of 20 MW each in Deoghar, Garhwa, Palamu and Simdega districts.*****
