                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2973






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Maintenance of NH-49




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether construction and repairing work to meet the requirement of heavy traffic on Jharpokharia-Keonjhar-Sambalpur NH-49 passing through Pallahara of district Angul has been proposed during the year 2019-20; (b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the time frame set for completion of the said work?	






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	Yes Sir.(b) & (c) The details are as follows:S. No.	Name of Package	Length (in km)	Sanctioned Cost (Rs. in Cr.)	Likely date of completion 1	4– lanning of Baharagora-Singara Section (except Badarma Wildlife Sanctuary portion) of NH -6 (EPC)	111.606	1282.94	31.03.20212	4 – laning of Singara –Binjabahal Section of NH 6 (HAM)	104.176	1313.20	26.03.20213	4- laning of Binjabahal –Teleibani Section of NH-6 (HAM)	78.318	1312.86	31.03.20214	4- laning of Telebani-Sambalpur section of NH -6 (EPC)	50.749	712.82	10.03.2021*****
