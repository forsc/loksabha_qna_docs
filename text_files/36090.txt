                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

303






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Haj Embarkation at Calicut International Airport




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to reinstate the Haj Embarkation at Calicut International Airport considering request of eighty per cent of the pilgrims in Kerala and if so, the details thereof; (b) whether the Government has noticed that a large number of beneficiaries were left out under National Merit-cum-Means Scholarship and if so, the steps taken by the concerned to resolve the issue; (c) whether the Government proposes to condone the delay caused in submission of applications for National Merit-cum-Means Scholarship of minority community due to cogent issues arising as a result of the pandemic; and (d) if so, the steps being taken by the Government to ensure that no child is left out from the benefit of minority scholarships due to delay in filing of application?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)	to (d):   A statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****
