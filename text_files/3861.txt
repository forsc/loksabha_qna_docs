                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3475






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Production of Steel




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether maximum growth has been recorded for the production of steel at the rate of 12 percent in January 2017 in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the demand of steel has increased and the steel price has also improved; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)&(b): Yes, Sir. Data on production of crude steel during January 2017 along with percentage change over January 2016 is given below which shows that a growth of 17 percent was registered during January 2017 over January 2016:-Month	India : Crude Steel Production	Qty.
