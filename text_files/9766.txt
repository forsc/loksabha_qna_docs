                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

289






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






Domestic Production of Steel




Chandeshwar Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is a fact that the share of domestic production in consumption of steel in the country has been declining whereas the share of import has been increasing during the past few years;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the Government is considering to increase customs duty to reduce the share of import in consumption of steel; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL			   (SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a)to(d):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the Lok Sabha.******** STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (A) TO (D) OF THE LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *289 FOR ANSWER ON 09/12/2019 ASKED BY SHRI CHANDESHWAR PRASAD, MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT REGARDING “DOMESTIC PRODUCTION OF STEEL”(a)&(b): No, Sir. Data on total consumption, share of domestic production and imports in consumption for the last three years is given below. This indicates a declining trend in the share of imports and a positive growth of share of domestic production in consumption of total finished steel.Year	Consumption (in MT)	% Share of Import in Consumption	% Share of DomesticProduction in Consumption	Total 	Share of Domestic Production  	Share of Import		2016-17	84.04	76.81	7.23	8.6	91.42017-18	90.70	83.22	7.48	8.2	91.82018-19	98.71	90.87	7.84	7.9	92.1Source: JPC, MT= million tonnes(c)&(d): Change in import duty is a budgetary exercise. ********
