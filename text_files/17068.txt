                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1020






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






Revenue Earned by DD/AIR




Gopal Chinayya Shetty














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	The details of revenue earned by the Akashvani Kendras and Doordarshan Kendras from the advertisements and serials and other programmes during each of the last three years; (b)	whether Doordarshan/Akashvani has any outstanding amount for telecast of advertisement against some private companies;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the steps taken or being taken to recover the said outstanding amount as on date, company-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE; MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING AND MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) Prasar Bharati has informed that the revenue earned by the Akashvani and Doordarshan Kendras from advertisements and serials and other programmes during each of the last three years is as under:   (Figures in Rs. Crore)FY	Akashvani	Doordarshan2017-18	465.41	607.082018-19	460.95	553.552019-20	305.23	348.83 (b) & (c): Yes, Sir. Details in respect of outstanding amount of Akashvani and Doordarshan for telecast of advertisement against the private companies are as under: `					   (Figures in Rs. Crore)Description	Principal amountAkashvani	  6.15Doordarshan	241.44Total	247.59(d)	Prasar Bharati has informed that they have a system in place to monitor the collection of outstanding dues regularly and they follow up with private companies for recovery of the outstanding dues. Various actions such as encashment of Bank Guarantee, invocation of arbitration, etc. are also resorted to.***********
