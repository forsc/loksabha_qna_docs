                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1926






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






UDAN Scheme in Bihar




Kaushalendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of airports identified under UDAN Scheme in Bihar;(b) the programmes being implemented for commencement of flights from these airports; and(c) the present status of the said programmes?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) to (c):  Ministry of Civil Aviation has launched Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik) on 21-10-2016 to stimulate regional air connectivity for making air travel affordable to the masses. The following Airports of Bihar are included in the RCS-UDAN document- Arrah, Begusarai, Bettiah, Bhabua (Kaimoor), Bhagalpur, Bihar Shariff, Bihita, Birpur, Buxur, Chhapra, Darbhanga, Dehri On Sonn, Farbisganj, Hathwa, Jehanabad, Jogbani, Katihar, Kishanganj, Madhubani, Monghyr, Motihari, Munger, Muzzafarpur, Naria, Panchanpur, Raxaul, Saharsa & Valmiki Nagar;After 4 rounds of bidding under RCS - UDAN, Darbhanga airport in Bihar has been awarded to Selected Airline Operator (SAO) for operating RCS - UDAN flights to Bengaluru, Delhi and Mumbai. SAO has commenced UDAN flights connecting Darbhanga airport to Bengaluru, Delhi and Mumbai.
