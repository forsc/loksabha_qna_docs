                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4559






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Textile Mills




Harish Dwivedi














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of textile mills functioning at present in the country, State-wise; (b) whether some mills have been closed down in Uttar Pradesh during the last three years and current year; and(c) if so, whether there is any proposal to restart these closed mills?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a):	As on 30.09.2019, there are 1347 operational Cotton/man-made fibre textile mills in the country. The details of State-wise number of operational mills are at Annexure.(b):	As far as NTC is concerned, no textile mill has been closed down in the State of Uttar Pradesh during the last three years and current year. As per the Non-Small Scale Industry (Non-SSI) units registered with Office of the Textile Commissioner, only one cotton and man-made fibre mill was closed in Uttar Pradesh, during year 2016-17. No mill has been closed during the year, 2017-18, 2018-19 and current year, 2019-20.(c):	There is no proposal for revival of the private closed mill at present. However, the Government is implementing several schemes to support the Textile Industry which include the Amended Technology Up gradation Fund Scheme (ATUFS); Scheme for Integrated Textile Parks (SITP); Integrated Skill Development Scheme (ISDS); Technology Mission on Technical Textiles (TMTT) etc. The industry could avail benefits under the above schemes for upgrading/ reviving textile units.
