                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4188






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2020






Maintenance of Government Quarters by CPWD




Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether there has been sharp decline in the maintenance of Government quarters in the country by the Central Public Works Department (CPWD) and if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether there has been rise in corruption among higher, medium and lower level officers of the CPWD during each of the last three years and if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether this is the main reason for poor maintenance of Government quarters and if so, the designation-wise details of officers involved therein; and(d)	the details of the registered and investigated cases of corruption during the said period and the number of accused officers? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                          (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	No Sir.(b)	An overall decrease has been observed in cases against officers/officials of Central Public Works Department (CPWD).(c)	Does not arise in view of reply to Part (a) and (b) above. (d)	The details of the registered and investigated cases in CPWD during last three years are as under :(i)	Details of complaints registered :Year	Numbers2017	1122018	1222019	  95(ii)	No. of officers to whom Charge Sheets issued:Year	Numbers2017	1232018	  642019	  24                                 *****
