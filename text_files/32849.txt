                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

297






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Sewage Treatment Plant under AMRUT




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware of Sewage Treatment Plant at 	Aavikkal under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban 	transformation (AMRUT), if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether the Government has given any financial support to 	Kozhikode corporation under AMRUT for the sewage treatment plant 	at Aavikkal;(c) 	if so, the details thereof; and(d) 	whether the Government is in receipt of any proposal under AMRUT 	for Kozhikode and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                                   THE MINISTER OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                 (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a):	Government of Kerala has taken up a 7 MLD (million litre per day) Sewage Treatment Plant (STP)project at Aavikkal under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban transformation (AMRUT).  The project was awarded on 22.4.2021 and initial survey has been completed.(b) and (c):	Projects under AMRUT are funded through Central as well as State and Urban Local Body contributions.  However, Central shares are not released project or city-wise.  Cost of the STP project at Aavikkalis ?71.57 crore.(d):	Government of Kerala has taken up 50 projects worth ?306.93 in the city of Kozhikode under AMRUT. Of these, 45 projects worth ?138.28 crore have been completed and 5 projects worth ?168.65 crore are under implementation.                             ******
