                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

518






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Solar Disconnect Photovoltaic Power Projects




Srinivas Kesineni














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the scheme of solar disconnect photovoltaic (PV) power projects for the islands of Andaman & Nicobar and Lakshadweep is still being operated;(b)	if so, the details of developments made so far and the expected deadline for the scheme thereof;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor;(d)	whether any solar related schemes are under consideration for the North East; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) to(c) The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE)’s scheme for setting up of distributed grid-connected solar PV power projects in Andaman & Nicobar & Lakshadweep with capital subsidy from MNRE was available till 31.03.2021. The scheme is now closed for new sanctions.A 20 MW solar PV project with 8 MWh battery energy storage system in Andaman & Nicobar Islands and a 1.95 MW solar PV project with 2.15 MWh battery energy storage system in Lakshadweep Islands have been supported under the scheme.(d)&(e) A number of solar related schemes like CPSU Scheme Phase-II, PM-KUSUM and Solar Roof Top Scheme Phase-II are available for beneficiaries across India, including the North East. PM-KUSUM Scheme has special dispensation for special category States including North East by way of provision for higher incentives. *****
