10.07.2019






Shortage of Coal to NTPC




Bharti Pravin Pawar














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the requirement of coal in the NTPC plants and the quantum of coal supplied during the last three years, plant-wise;(b) whether the electricity generation is reduced owing to shortage of coal supply to NTPC plants especially the plant situated in Nashik district; and(c) if so, the details thereof and the corrective steps taken by the Government in this regard?
