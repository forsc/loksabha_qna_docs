                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

361






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2022






Developments in Energy Sector




S. Muniswamy














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether 500 GW of energy would be generated taking into account the current developments in the new and renewable energy sector;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether a committee is being set up to achieve a power production of 500 GW by the year 2030;(d) if so, the details thereof;(e) whether this quantum of energy if generated would suffice and ensure electricity for every household in the country; and(f) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) &(b) In accordance with Hon’ble Prime Minister’s announcement at COP26, Ministry of New and Renewable Energy is committed to achieving  500 GW of installed  electricity  capacity from non-fossil fuel sources by 2030. (c) & (d) The Ministry of New and Renewable energy has constituted a committee for preparing a roadmap to achieve 500 GW of non-fossil fuel based energy capacity by 2030.(e) & (f) The projected power generation capacity in the country in 2030 would be 817 GW out of which 500 GW will be non-fossil fuel based capacity. *****
