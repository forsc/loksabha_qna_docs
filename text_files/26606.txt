                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2498






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.08.2021






Food grain under TPDS




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government makes allocation of food grains to the States under Targeted Public Distribution system in terms of National Food Security, if so, details thereof; (b) 	whether the people across the country have lost their livelihood due to heavy rain and suffering from scarcity of food, if so, details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government is providing food/ houses to the flood affected poor people under National Food Security Act, if so, details thereof; and (d) 	whether the Government has taken initiatives to rehabilitate the flood affected people of the country those who have taken shelter on national highways, if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)	& (b): 	Department of Food & Public Distribution makes allocation of foodgrains(wheat and rice) to the States/UTs under Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS) in terms of National Food Security Act(NFSA), 2013. Under the Act eligible households, which comprise of Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) households and priority households, are entitled to receive foodgrains @ Rs 1/2/3 per kg for nutri-cereals/wheat/rice respectively. NFSA is operated under the joint responsibility of the Central and the State/UT Govt. Central Govt is responsible for allocation and transportation of foodgrains upto the designated depots of the FCI. The operational responsibilities for allocation of foodgrains within the States/UTs, identification of eligible beneficiaries, issuance of ration cards to them, distribution of foodgrains to eligible beneficiaries rest with the concerned State/UT Govt. The details of allocation of foodgrains under TPDS during the last year and current year is at Annexure-I......2/--2-(c) & (d): 		In addition to NFSA, this Department also makes additional allocation of foodgrains for natural calamities based on the request of State/UT Govt. As per extant norms, in case of natural calamities, the allocation of foodgrains for 3 months of demand can be made to the States/UTs on the basis of the request received so that the relief measures are not affected. However, for the demands beyond 3 months the proposal must be recommended by Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare (in case of drought) and Ministry of Home Affairs [in case of natural calamities (other than drought) & law and order situations].
