                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2568






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.07.2019






Implementation of PMAY-G




Ganesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has approved the implementation of phase-II of the Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G), beyond March, 2019;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the salient features of PMAY-G, phase-II;(d)	the details of inclusion of additional eligible households, State/UT-wise including Satna District; and(e)	the details of the left out rural households who are houseless and/or living in dilapidated houses, subject to a ceiling?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	Yes Sir.(b) & (c):	The details of the approvals given and the salient features of Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana – Gramin (PMAY-G) Phase – II  are as follows:i.	Construction of 1.95 crore PMAY-G houses from 2019-20 to 2021-22. ii.	Target of construction of 60 lakh PMAY-G houses with financial implication of Rs.76,500 crore (Central Share of Rs. 48,195 crore and State share of Rs.28,305 crore) as per the existing norms of PMAY-G Phase-Iiii.	Continuation of the scheme beyond 2019-20 upto 2021-22 in the next Finance Commission cycle, after due appraisal and approval based on third party evaluation of scheme/programme as per the extant procedure.iv.	Inclusion of additional eligible households from the  final Awaas+ list into the Permanent Wait List (PWL) of PMAY-G, with a ceiling of 1.95 Cr.,  on priority for those States / UTs where PWL is exhausted and allocate target to these States / UTs with the approval of the Minister of Rural Development in consultation with the Ministry of Finance.v.	Borrowing for additional financial requirement through existing mechanism of Extra Budgetary Resources (EBR) till the validity of the scheme.(d) & (e):	Ministry of Rural Development has developed a mobile application “Awaas+” to capture the details of households (houseless households and/or households living in kuccha houses as defined by Socio Economic Caste Census, 2011) who though eligible for assistance under PMAY-G but could not be included in the Permanent Wait List (PWL). This mobile application “Awaas+” captured the details of the potential beneficiary households along with geo-tagged photo of their present dwelling and proposed site of construction. The States / UTs have  uploaded details of 3.53 crore potential households using the mobile application till the last date i.e.7th March, 2019.  After due scrutiny and validation, the eligible households would be included in the Permanent Wait List of PMAY-G for providing assistance.
