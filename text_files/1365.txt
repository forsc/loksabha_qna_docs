                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1301






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.06.2019






Broadcast and Distribution Policy




Manish Tewari














                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)      whether the Government is aware that as per the recommendation of TRAI dated 28 December, 2012 the Companies that are controlled by the Union, State Governments and their Departments are barred from the business of broadcasting and distributing television channels;(b) 		if so, the details thereof and the present status of said recommendation;(c)  	whether it has been accepted by the Government and translated into policy, if so, the details thereof together with the exceptions to the said policy, if any; and(d) 	whether there are any content or distribution entities in the TV space that have been licensed by the Government in the past five years, if so, the details of the said entities and the reasons for doing the same along with the full names and address of their respective promoters?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE AND MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING(SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a)	Yes Sir. (b) & (c) 	 The Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) in its recommendations on 28.12.2012 recommended that Central/State Ministries and Departments, Central/State Government owned companies, Central/State Government undertakings, Joint Ventures of Central/State Government and the private sector and Central/State Governments funded entities should not be allowed to enter into the business of broadcasting and/or distribution of TV channels. Ministry of Information and Broadcasting deliberated the said recommendations in the Inter-Ministerial Committee (IMC) set up to examine TRAI recommendations. The acceptance of the recommendations and its translation into policy has multi dimensional implications which requires consultation with various stakeholders.(d)	 In order to comply with the Order of Hon’ble High Court of Madras dated 20.12.2013 in MP No. 1/2013 in WP No. 34213 of 2013, a provisional Multi-System Operator (MSO) registration was granted to M/s Tamil Nadu Arasu Cable TV Corporation Ltd. (TACTV), a State Government Undertaking, on 17.04.2017 to operate in Tamil Nadu to provide digital cable TV services. The address of TACTV is as under:Tamil Nadu Arasu Cable TV Corporation Ltd.Dugar Towers,  6th Floor,No.34, (123), Marshalls Road, Egmore,Chennai-600008.A list of Promoters of M/s TACTV is annexed.
