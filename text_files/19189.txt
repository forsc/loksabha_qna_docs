                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

47






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Airports under RCS




Su Thirunavukkarasar













                                                    Prathap Simha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the names of airports which have become operational since the implementation of Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS);(b) whether a number of new airports are under construction under RCS;(c) if so, the details thereof along with the estimated cost and the present status thereof, project/State-wise; and(d) the time by which the new airports under RCS are likely to become operational?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (d): A statement is laid on the table of the House.; ; STATEMENT IN RESPECT OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 47 REGARDING "AIRPORTS UNDER RCS" TO BE ANSWERED ON 04.02.2021.; ; (a): Details of the airports operationalized under Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - UDAN (Ude Desh Ka Aam Nagrik) are attached as Annexure-A.; ; (b) to (d): Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs has approved the proposal for revival of existing unserved / underserved airports / airstrips/heliports/water aerodromes of the State Governments, Airports Authority of India, Public Sector Undertakings and Civil Enclaves at an estimated cost of Rs. 4500 Crore. An airport which is included in the awarded routes of Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik) and requires upgradation/development for commencement of RCS operations is developed under "Revival of unserved and underserved airports" scheme. Details of Airports which have been taken up for construction under the revival/development of airports scheme are attached at Annexure-B. ;
