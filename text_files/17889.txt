                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1780






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Digital Payment and Services Offered at CSC




Ramya  Haridas














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of Electronics and Information Technology be pleased to state:(a) whether the number of digital payments has increased in the country during the last three years;(b) if so, the details thereof and the number of digital payments registered during each of the last three years, State/UT-wise; and(c) the details of different key services like insurance, PAN card applications or ticket booking services availed at Common Service Centres (CSCs) during the said period, year-wise and service/head-wise?  






ANSWER




                                                        (a): Yes, Sir. Number of digital payments has increased in the country during the last three years ie since FY 2017-18.(b): 	State/UT-wise transaction details are not maintained, the total number of digital payments undertaken in the country during the last three years and current year is as under:Financial Year (FY)	Total number of Digital Transactions (Crores)FY 2017-18	2071FY 2018-19	3134FY 2019-20	4572FY 2020-21(till August)*	1634.92* Final data for July and August 2020 has not yet been published by RBI  (c) : The details of different key services availed at Common Service Centres (CSCs) during the  last three years, year wise and service/ head-wise are provided in Annexure.   Annexure   NUMBER OF TRANSACTIONS IN KEY SERVICES  DURING  2017-18 TO 2019-20 AVAILED AT CSCsType of Service	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20 	No. of Transactions (Fig in Lakh)BCA Banking (Business Correspondent Agent)	522.20	710.37	810.31Digipay + DMT (Domestic Money Transfer)	422.14	350.96	224.33Insurance (New Business)	3.49	6.59	15.24Insurance (Renewal)	11.77	16.74	22.19PAN -Application 	86.00	71.97	80.84EPIC Printing	26.24	23.26	12.97Bharat Bill Payment System (BBPS)	3.30	12.82	9.93FSSAI	3.94	7.03	4.48Electricity Bill Payment	56.40	96.18	164.20PASSPORT-Application	3.18	4.06	4.53E-Stamp	0.31	3.73	12.46Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Application)	3.68	3.13	2.01NPS & APY (Atal Pension Yojna)	0.31	0.27	1.08E-Recharge	76.52	39.32	47.15IRCTC Ticket Booking	2.33	5.93	11.43Other Tours and Travel	1.79	3.34	2.02(Data Source: CSC Portal)*****
