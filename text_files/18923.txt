                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

283






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Status of BharatNet Project in Gujarat




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal













                                                    Shankar Lalwani
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number and the details of panchayats in the country which have been linked under Bharatnet project; (b) 	the time by which the work of linking all the gram panchayats in the country particularly in the State of Gujarat with internet is likely to be completed; and (c) 	the present status of Bharatnet project particularly in the State of Gujarat?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	BharatNet project is being implemented in a phased manner to provide broadband connectivity at all the Gram Panchayats (approx. 2,50,000) in the country. As on 27.01.2021, under BharatNet project (Phase-I & II), a total number of 1,51,431Gram Panchayats (Including Block HQ) have been made Service Ready.  The details of the Service Ready Gram Panchayats (GPs) in the country are attached as Annexure-I. (b)	The BharatNet project was envisaged to be completed by August 2021. However, this time will now be extended as the pace of completion is affected by lockdown and restrictions on movement imposed by the various Governments due to COVID-19. (c)	In Gujarat, the total Number of Gram Panchayats (including BHQs) is   about 14,311. In the State, BharatNet Phase-I has been implemented by RailTel Corporation of India Limited (RailTel), whereas the BharatNet Phase-II is being implemented under the State-led model by the Government of Gujarat itself.  As of now, a total number of 13,266 Gram Panchayats (Including Block HQ)   have been made Service Ready in Gujarat.
