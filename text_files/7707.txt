                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1336






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Impact of Slowdown on Tax Collection




Prasun Banerjee














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has assessed the effect of slowdown on tax collection and fiscal deficit;(b) if so, the details and the findings thereof;(c) the manner in which the shortfall will be compensated for;(d) whether it is true that the estimated shortfall will be in the range of $2 million; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINSTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) to (e):   The revised estimates of tax collection and fiscal deficit are decided at the time of preparation of the budget estimates for the next year. The exercise for preparation of budget estimate for 2020-21 and revised estimates for 2019-20 is underway. At this stage, it may be pre-mature to assess the Revised Estimate against the Budget Estimate for FY 2019-20 with respect to taxes. The expected central taxes revenue for 2019-20 will be re-assessed and presented as Revised Estimate in the general budget, 2020-21.
