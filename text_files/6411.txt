                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

94






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Training Centres for Workers in Die-Making (Ornament)




Rajendra Dhedya  Gavit














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to open training centres for workers engaged in Die-making (Ornament) to make them skilled and trained in Palghar district of Maharashtra;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	whether the Government proposes to provide any special financial package for publicity and commercialization of Die-making (Ornament) vocation and if so, the time by which it will likely be provided?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship has no plan at present to open training centres for workers engaged in Die-making.(b) and (c)	In view of (a) above, question does not arise.
