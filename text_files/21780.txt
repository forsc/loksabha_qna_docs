                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2831






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2021






Block-level Handloom Clusters




Anumula Revanth Reddy













                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                








                                                    Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is planning to develop block-level handloom clusters in the country, if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (b) the details of block-level handloom clusters currently functional in Telangana; (c) the details of the places identified for the establishment of new handloom clusters in the State of Telangana; (d) the quantum of allocations made for each cluster and the amount so far released under this project; and (e) whether the Government has received any new proposal from the State Government of Telangana for setting up new handloom clusters in the State; and (f) if so, the details thereof and the response of the Government thereon?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a):	Block level clusters are sanctioned based on the viable proposals received from State Governments as per the extant guidelines of the scheme. A total of 440 Block Level Clusters have been sanctioned under National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP) and Comprehensive Handloom Cluster Development Scheme (CHCDS) in the country since 2015-16. State-wise details of Block Level Clusters sanctioned are annexed.  (b) to (d):	Details of  Block Level Clusters (BLCs) sanctioned under National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP) in the State of Telangana are as follows:- (Rs.in lakh)S. No.	Name of BLC and district	Amount released so far	Beneficiary covered1	Aler, Yadadri Bhuvanagiri	55.09	4082	Kamalapur, Warangal Urban	58.00 	6243	Veltoor, Wanaparthy	31.75 	2354	Armoor, Nizamabad	24.64 	1005	Shayampet, Warrangal Rural	22.20 	3026	Kanukula, Peddapally	31.25 	2727	Bhoodan Pochampally, Yadadri Bhuvanagiri	30.35 	3878	Vemulawada, Rajanna Sircilla	28.79	3249	Markandeya Bachannapet, Jangoan 	24.00 	159(e) & (f):	11 proposals of new Block Level Clusters were received from State Government of Telangana, which were examined and certain shortcomings were noticed in them. These shortcomings have been communicated to the State Government.
