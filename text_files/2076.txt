                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1950






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Savings Accounts in Post Office




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of saving accounts opened in the post offices of the country during each of the last three years and the current year; (b) 	whether the Central Government proposes to provide digital banking in these saving accounts and if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the services being offered to the post office account holders by the Department of Post; and (d) 	whether the Government proposes to formulate any new scheme in this regard in the future and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a)     Details of Savings Accounts opened in Post Offices of the country during the last three years and current year is attached as Annexure-1(b)     Yes sir, Internet Banking (intra-operable) facility has been launched for Post Office Savings Accounts. With this facility the customer would be able to view balances, view transaction details, open new Recurring Deposit (RD) / Time Deposit (TD) accounts, close RD/TD accounts, make PPF deposits, can transfer fund to another Post Office Savings Account and lodge stop cheque resuests etc. as additional facilities. Further, with the launch of IPPB (India Post Payment Bank), a customer can link his Post Office Savings Account with his IPPB (India Post Payment Bank) Account & can further avail digital banking services using IMPS(Immediate Mobile Payment Service) & NEFT (National Electronic Fund Transfer) for fund transfer & also make bill payments.(c)  The following services are being offered to the Post Office Account holders by the Department of Post.            (i)  Core Banking facility (CBS)           (ii)  Automated Teller Machine (ATM) facility.	(iii) Cheque facility	(iv) Fund Transfer from one account to another.            (v) Intra-operable Internet Banking.(d)    No, sir.
