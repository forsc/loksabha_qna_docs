                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1246






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Venture Education System




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware of the venture education system prevailing inthe State of Assam; and(b) whether the Government will consider to provide financial assistance to theseinstitutions until Assam State Government take a decision about provincialisation ofthese institutions?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): As per information received from State Government of Assam, VentureHigh Schools under Secondary Education Department as defined in the “The AssamEducation (provincialisation of services of Teachers and reorganisation ofeducational institution) Act, 2017” means High Schools imparting Education up toclass X and established by the people of the locality prior to 01-01-2006 which hasreceived permission from the State Government and recognition from the Board ofSecondary Education, Assam on or before 01-01-2006. Similarly, Venture HigherSecondary Schools as defined in the same Act, 2017 means High Schools impartingEducation up to class XII and established by the people of the locality prior to 01-01-2006 which has received permission from the State Government on or before 01-01-2006. State Government of Assam has reported that the financial assistance to theseventure schools has been discontinued for the last several years as per theintervention of the Hon''ble High Court. The matter is sub-judice.******
