                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1590






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






Damage to Agricultural Land by Flood




Nusrat Jahan Ruhi













                                                    Thomas Chazhikadan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that the country suffers from huge crop loss due to flood every year; (b) 	the details of the extent of agricultural land damaged by floods in the last five years, State/UT-wise and year-wise; and (c) 	the details of the estimated damage to agricultural output as a result of flooding during the said period, State/UT-wise and year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????      (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (c):    As per the information provided by the Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA), State/UT-wise and year-wise details of crop area affected due to hydro-meteorological calamities/hazards during 2017-18 to  2021-22 (as on 25.11.2021), as reported by the State Governments, are given at Annexure. The estimation in regard to damage to agricultural output etc. is done by the concerned State Governments.
