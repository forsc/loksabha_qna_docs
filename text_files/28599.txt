                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

642






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.12.2021






Steel Production




Nihal Chand Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the State-wise details of the percentage/ratio of total production and demand of steel in the country during the last two years;(b)	the details of the names of the countries from which India imports steel including the percentage thereof during the last two years along with the percentage it amounts to in terms of the total requirement of the country; and(c)	the details of the comprehensive steps taken by the Government to increase production of steel in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL		   (SHRI RAM CHANDRA PRASAD SINGH)(a) 	State-wise details of the percentage of total production and demand of steel in the country during the last two years are given at Annexure-I and Annexure-II respectively.(b)	The country-wise details of imports of finished steel during the last two years along with percentage thereof is given at Annexure-III.(c)	The Government has taken the following steps to increase production of steel in the country:-(1)	Notification of National Steel Policy 2017 which envisages inter-alia domestically meeting the entire demand of steel and high-grade automotive steel, electrical steel, special steel and alloys for strategic applications(2)	Domestically Manufactured Iron & Steel products (DMI&SP) Policy for promoting procurement of Made in India steel by Government agencies. (3)	Notification of Steel Scrap Policy to enhance the availability of domestically generated scrap. (4)	Issuance of Steel Quality Control Order to stop manufacturing and import of non-standardized steel. (5)	Notification of Steel Import Monitoring System (SIMS) for advanced registration of steel imports. (6)	Notification of Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme for Specialty Steel with an outlay of Rs 6,322 crores. (7)	Engagement with various stakeholders including industry association and leaders of domestic steel industry to address their issues by the concerned Ministries/Departments of the Central Government and State Governments. (8) 	Engagement with relevant stakeholders including from Railways, Defence, Petroleum and Natural Gas, Housing, Civil Aviation, Road Transport and Highways, Agriculture and Rural Development sectors to enhance the steel usage and overall demand for steel in the country.(9) 	Establishment of Project Development Cell in the Ministry to attract and facilitate investment in the steel sector.********
