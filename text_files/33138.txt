                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

542






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Vaccination Facilities under ICDS




 Bhavana Gawali (Patil)














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	whether the Government has reopened the Anganwadis with the full capacity under unlock plan and if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether the Government had taken any steps to provide nutrition and vaccination facilities under Integrated Child Development Service (ICDS) during lockdown phase, if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the Government has any plan to universalize the ICDS scheme and if so, the details thereof; (d)	whether there is any plan to include eggs in ICDS nutrition norms for enhancing nutrients; and (e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)	The decision for reopening or not opening Anganwadi centres is taken by the State Govt on the direction of the State Disaster Management Authority. (b)	Functioning of Anganwadi Centres during Covid-19 circumstances was permitted as per guidelines issued by Government on 15.04.2020 for distribution of food items and nutrition support by Anganwadi Workers, once in 15 days, at the doorstep of beneficiaries, i.e., children, women and lactating mothers to ensure continuous nutritional support. Besides medicines to be distributed as per requirement and special attention to be given to the timely medical interventions for SAM children. Further, steps have been taken to enable delivering of essential health services including vaccination services during the COVID 19 pandemic. States/ UTs have been guided for operationalization of immunization sessions at the Anganwadi Centres, ensuring compliance to all the necessary measures for prevention of COVID-19 infection.  Also, during Covid-19 period this Ministry has sought regular reports from States/UTs to ensure uninterrupted delivery of services to the beneficiaries.(C)	Anganwadi Services have already been universalized and has expanded to cover all habitations across the country.(d)&(e)    Anganwadi Services is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, implemented through State Governments/UT Administrations. Central Government prescribes the calorie and protein content of the food for SNP to be provided through the meal whereas the recipe/type of food to be included in SNP is decided by concerned States/UTs depending on local food and culturally appropriate habits**********
