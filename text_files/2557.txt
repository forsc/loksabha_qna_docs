                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Financial Aid for Research Support to Voluntary Organisations




S. Gnanathiraviam














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has details of Financial Aid for Research Support to Voluntary Organizations engaged in Cultural activities, State –wise during the last three years;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the details in regard of Tamil Nadu during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State (I/C) For Culture And Tourism (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) & (b)   Yes Sir. Financial aid is provided for Research Support to Voluntary Organizations engaged in Cultural Activities under the following schemes:-(i)	Scheme of Financial Assistance for the Development of Buddhist/Tibetan Art & Culture(ii)	Scheme of Financial Assistance for the Preservation & Development of Cultural Heritage of Himalayas(iii)  Financial Assistance to Cultural Organizations with National PresenceBesides, financial aid is provided for Research to Vrindavan Research Institute located in Vrindavan, Uttar Pradesh, which is a Voluntary Organization engaged in Cultural Activities. The state-wise details of financial aid provided during the last three years is annexed.(c)   No funds have been released to Voluntary Organizations under the said schemes in the State of Tamil Nadu during the last three years.******* Annexurei)    Details of Financial Aid for Research Support to voluntary organizations under the scheme for financial assistance for Development  of Buddhist/Tibetan  Art & Culture during last three years    2016-17Nil2017-18Sl.
