                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3814






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






PMGSY in Assam




Abdul Khaleque














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total length of completed roads under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana(PMGSY) in the country;(b) 	the total kilometres of roads completed under PMGSY in Assam, district-wise;(c) 	the number of RCC bridges sanctioned in rural Assam along with the status of completion;(d) 	the total kilometres of gravel roads converted to black topped/pucca road;(e) 	the mechanism of funding along with the amount spent out of the total allocation; and(f) 	the figures of all the above in the districts of Barpeta, Bongaigaon and Nalbari of Assam?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)        A total road length of 6, 21,818.290 Km has been completed under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) since inception till 12th March, 2020 in the Country.(b)        Since inception till 12th March, 2020, 24,825.540 km has been completed under PMGSY in the State of Assam. District-wise details are given in Annexure.(c)      A total of 1,958 Long Span Bridges has been sanctioned under PMGSY in the State of Assam, of which 942 bridges have been completed till 12th March, 2020. (d)       As per the available information, 416.81 km gravel roads have been converted into Black topped/Pucca roads under PMGSY.  (e)      PMGSY was started in the year 2000 as 100% Centrally Sponsored Scheme. However, the funding pattern was modified to 60:40 between the Centre and States for all States except for 8 North Eastern and 3 Himalayan States for which the ratio was fixed at 90:10 based on the recommendations of the Sub-Group of Chief Ministers on Rationalization of Centrally Sponsored Schemes. Since inception till 12th March, 2020, an expenditure of Rs 15,334.51 crore has been incurred for implementation of PMGSY in the State of Assam.
