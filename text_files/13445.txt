                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2240






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Recruitment Camps for Defence Forces




Rajkumar Chahar














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) the details of last recruitment camp/rally for the Defence Forces held in sports stadium Agra to enable participation of candidates from Fatehpur Sikri and adjoining cities;(b) the number of candidates from Fatehpur selected in the final round; and(c) the time by which the next camp/rally is planned to be held keeping in view the shortage of soldiers in the Defence Forces?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a)  No rally has been conducted by the Army in Sports Stadium Agra in the last five years.  However, in 2015, a rally was conducted at Eklavya Stadium, Agra.  The last rally for candidates from Fatehpur Sikri was conducted from 15-27 November 2018 at Eagle Ground, Mathura.(b)  No separate data is being maintained for Fatehpur Sikri.  However, 81 candidates were selected from Kiraoli Tehsil including candidates from Fatehpur Sikri.(c)  Next rally of Army Recruitment Office Agra is planned at Mathura in March, 2020.*****
