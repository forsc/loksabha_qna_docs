                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

223






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Autonomy of IIT &IIM




Asaduddin Owaisi














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) whether the Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) and the Indian Institutesof Management (IIMs) have sought autonomy;(b) if so, whether a seven member Committee was constituted in October,2018 to examine autonomy sought by IITs on the lines of IIMs;(c) whether the said Committee has submitted its report to the Government;(d) if so, the details thereof;(e) whether the said report has been forwarded to IITs for deliberation;(f) if so, the detailed recommendations made by the Committee; and(g) the time by which a final decision is likely to be taken in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (g): Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) are governed by the Institute ofTechnology Act, 1961 and Statutes framed under the Act, which inter aliaprovides autonomy in the functioning of the IITs, through Board of Governorsconstituted for the purpose. IIMs have been granted autonomy with effect from31/01/2018, as per the Indian Institutes of Management Act, 2017.In order to examine reconstitution of Board of Governors of IndianInstitutes of Technology (IITs) in line with those of Indian Institutes ofManagement (IIMs), a Committee was constituted vide Order No.35-5/2018-TS.Idated the 4th October, 2018. The Composition of the Committee was as under:-i. Prof. M. Anandakrishnan, former Chairperson, IIT, Kanpurii. Prof. Partha P. Chakrabarti, Director, IIT, Kharagpuriii. Prof. Bhaskar Ramamurthi, Director, IIT, Madrasiv. Prof. Devang Khakhar, Director, IIT, Bombayv. Prof. V. Ramgopal Rao, Director, IIT, Delhivi. Prof. Abhay Karandikar, Director, IIT, Kanpurvii. Prof. Ashok Jhunjhunwala, IIT Madras.The committee submitted its report on 5th February, 2019. The committee hasproposed amendments to the IIT Act to bring in the same level of autonomy tothe Board of Governors as conferred by the IIM Act. At present, there is noproposal under consideration of the Government to grant further autonomy toIITs on the lines of IIMs.*****
