                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5254






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






National River Conservation Programme




Alok Kumar Suman














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that Gandak river of Bihar has been identified under the National River Conservation Programme (NRCP); (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) the details of the funds released and spent for the conservation of the Gandak river under NRCP; (d) whether the Government has taken any steps to monitor the erosion by the Gandak river in the State of Bihar; and (e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c)   National River Conservation Program (NRCP) does not have any specific allocation for the Gandak  River in Bihar. National Mission for Clean Ganga (NMCG) has disbursed Rs 44.38 crore to the State of Bihar since the inception of NMCG in FY 2011-12 till 30.06.2019. Out of this an amount of Rs.16.20 crore has been specifically released for pollution abatement in river Gandak in Bihar. Another Rs.17.72 crore has been spent for pollution abatement in Gandak River, out of State share by the Government of Bihar.(d) to (e)   The Water Resources Department, Government of Bihar undertakes anti-erosion works before flood period for protecting  erosion as per requirement.
