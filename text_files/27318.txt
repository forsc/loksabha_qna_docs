                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2920






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Houses under PMAY-U




Omprakash Bhupalsinh alias Pawan Rajenimbalkar













                                                     Bhavana Gawali (Patil)
                                                








                                                    Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any data of houses provided to the poor people under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U) during the last three years and if so, the details thereof; (b) 	the details of the houses sanctioned particularly in Ramtek,	Washim-Yavatmal, Osmanabad and Parbhani districts of Maharashtra; (c) 	the total amount of funds allocated to Osmanabad district of Maharashtra under the said scheme during the last three years and the current year; (d)	 whether the Government allocated Rupees one hundred ninety crore for Osmanabad district under PMAY-U; (e)	 if so, the total amount out of the said amount released and the time by which the balance amount is likely to be 	released; (f)	 the number of applications pending with the Government for 	the allotment of houses to the poor families in the country including of Osmanabad in Maharashtra under PMAY-U, State-wise; and (g) 	the action taken by the Government for early approval of the said applications?






ANSWER




                                                                  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                             (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) Yes, Sir. During the last three years, i.e F.Y. 2018-19, 2019-20 and 2020-21, 74,36,097 houseshave been sanctionedunder the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Urban (PMAY-U). The beneficiaries include urban poor.  (b)	Under PMAY-U, 19,295, 8,195, 15,093 houseshave been sanctioned particularly in Washim-Yavatmal, Osmanabad and Parbhani districtsof Maharashtra.Regarding Ramtek no proposal has been received from the State Government.(c)	During the last three years and current year, Rs.128.49 crore has been sanctionedfor Osmanabad district of Maharashtra under PMAY-U.(d)&(e)	So far, out of Rs.128.98 crore sanctioned for Osmanabad district of Maharashtra under PMAY-U, the Union Government has released central assistance of Rs.34.00 crore to the State Government of Maharashtra. The central assistance is released subject to fulfillment of compliancesby the State Government as per scheme guidelines.(f) & (g) ‘Land’ and ‘Colonisation’ are State subjects. The Union Government through PMAY-U is augmenting efforts of States/UTs to meet the housing demand through financial assistance. The selection/Identification of projects and beneficiaries under the PMAY-U Mission comes within the purview of respective State/UT Governments.                                     *****
