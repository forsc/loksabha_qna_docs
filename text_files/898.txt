                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

869






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Crash of MI-17 Helicopter




Santokh Singh Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether it is a fact that an MI-17 helicopter was downed by friendly fire in Budgam on February 27, 2019 and if so, the details thereof;(b)  the number of persons killed in the crash;  (c)  whether any enquiry/investigation has been conducted in this regard;(d) if so, the details and the outcome thereof and the action taken thereon; and(e) the steps taken by the Government to avoid such incidents in future?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF DEFENCE                                    (SHRI RAJNATH SINGH)      j{kk ea=h    			           ¼Jh ?????? ???? )(a) & (b): A Mi-17 Helicopter of Indian Air Force crashed on 27.02.2019 near Budgam.  Total six service personnel and one civilian were killed in the crash.(c), (d) & (e): A Court of Inquiry is in progress to ascertain the cause of accident.  Every aircraft accident is investigated through a Court of Inquiry and remedial measures are undertaken accordingly to avoid such accidents in future.******
