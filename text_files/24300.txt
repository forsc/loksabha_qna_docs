                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

372






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.07.2021






Fishermen of Madhya Pradesh




Uday Pratap Singh














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has formulated/proposed any scheme to double the production under fisheries and double the income of fishermen of Madhya Pradesh, if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether the Government has taken any steps to provide training and financial assistance to fishermen in fisheries sector under Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan, if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise including the State of Madhya Pradesh; (c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and (d)	the year-wise details of funds released to promote fisheries in the State of Madhya Pradesh during the last three years? 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(Dr. L. MURUGAN)(a) to (c): The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a flagship scheme namely “Pradhan MantriMatsyaSampadaYojana (PMMSY) - to bring about Blue Revolution through sustainable and responsible development of fisheries sector in India” with an estimated investment of Rs. 20,050 crore for a period of 5 years from financial year 2020-21 to FY 2024-25 in all States/Union Territories including Madhya Pradesh as a part of AatmaNirbhar Bharat Package.Besides, a dedicated fund namely, ‘Fisheries and Aquaculture Infrastructure Development Fund’ (FIDF) with a total fund size of Rs.7,522.48crore has been set up for providing concessional finance for creation and strengthening of fisheries infrastructure facilities.The PMMSY inter-aliaaddresses critical gaps in fish production and productivity, quality, technology, post-harvest infrastructure and management, modernization and strengthening of value chain, traceability, establishing a robust fisheries management framework and fishers’ welfareincluding training and skill up-gradation to fisherman, fish farmers and other stakeholders.Under the PMMSY, the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying during the financial year 2020-21 and current financial year(2021-22) has accorded approval to the proposals of Government of Madhya Pradesh at a total cost of Rs.323.73crorefor assisting the fishermen and fish famers for development of fisheries in the State.(d):Year-wise details of central funds released to promote fisheries in the State of Madhya Pradesh during the last three years as below:Sl. No.	Year	Fund released(Rs. in lakhs)1	2018-19	711.422	2019-20	1859.473	2020-21	2379.16*****
