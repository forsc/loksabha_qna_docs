                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

218






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Assessment of PMAY-U




 Bhagirath Chaudhary














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the date on which the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY-U) commenced and the names of cities of Rajasthan and Gujarat where the persons were benefitted under various phases of this scheme as of now including the number of identified persons benefitted;(b) 	the details of the houses sanctioned and constructed and also the  amount of grant given in this regard in each district of these two States, phase-wise;(c) 	the number of beneficiaries in these States who have been provided houses in various  phases under the scheme till now including the number of houses provided and whether the fixed targets have been achieved and if so, the details thereof;(d) 	whether the Union Government and the State Government comply with the specific quality norms regarding construction of houses in these States; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor and the remedial measures taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	to(e) A statement is laid on the Table of the House. Statement referred to in reply to Lok Sabha Starred Question No. 218 to be answered on March 05, 2020 regarding Assessment of PMAY-U.(a) Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) has been implementing Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban (PMAY-U) Mission with effect from 17thJune, 2015to provide Central Assistance to States/Union Territories(UTs)for providing all weather pucca houses to all eligible urban households, especially Economically Weaker Section (EWS) families/beneficiaries. All statutory towns as per Census 2011 and townsnotified subsequently including Notified Planning / Development Areas are covered under PMAY(U).Names of cities and number of persons benefitted in the State of Gujarat and Rajasthan are at Annexure–I.(b) & (c) District wise details of the houses sanctioned, constructed and central assistance released so far in Gujarat and Rajasthan are given in Annexure–II and Annexure-III respectively. Under PMAY (U), States/UTs have undertaken demand survey for assessing the actual demand of houses. The indicated demand for houses in Gujarat and Rajasthan are 7.76 lakh and 6.15 lakh respectively. Against this demand, the number of houses sanctioned for construction in Gujarat and Rajasthan are 6,46,140 and 2,03,908 respectively. Mission guidelines provide flexibility to States/ UTs for appraising and approving project proposals based on demand assessed and to approach the Ministry only for release of Central assistance for projects approved at the State level.(d)&(e)  The implementation of projects under PMAY(U) Mission is the responsibility of the concerned State/UT and Urban Local Body (ULB). The State/UT authorities concerned engage Third Party Quality Monitoring Agencies (TPQMA) to ensure quality of construction. On the basis of quality assurance report by TPQMA and also reports of their own technical staff, States/UTs and ULBs take necessary action to ensure that standard quality houses and infrastructure are constructed under the Mission.                                                *****
