                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3478






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Shifting of Central University




Hasnain Masoodi














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the present status of shifting of Central University of Kashmir to its location at Ganderbal,Jammu and Kashmir and if so, the details thereof;(b) the steps are taken by the Government to expedite the under construction infrastructure ofthe University;(c) the status of implementation of Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan scheme in Jammuand Kashmir;(d) the funds allocated/sanctioned under different components of RUSA to Jammu and Kashmir;and(e) whether the Government has any proposal to establish a National Law University atAnantnag and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): Central University of Kashmir has started its operations from the allotted site at Tulmulla,Ganderbal by shifting three Departments in the first instance. Besides, nine Departments havebeen already shifted to District Ganderbal within a radius of 3 to 4 Km from Main site and arehoused in old buildings provided by the State Government. As on date, only four Departments arefunctioning from Nowgam Srinagar.(b): Government has approved financial assistance of Rs. 381.68 cr. through HigherEducation Financing Agency (HEFA) to complete the pending construction projects of the CentralUniversity of Kashmir. The University has to approach HEFA Board to get the fund released tothem.(c): The ratio of central funding under Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA) Schemefor the State of Jammu and Kashmir is 90:10. Under RUSA 1.0, an amount of Rs 299.01 crorewas approved with central share of Rs. 269.109 Crore. Out of which, an amount of Rs. Rs225.59 crore has been released to J&K. Under RUSA 2.0, an amount of Rs 192 crore wasapproved with central share of Rs. 172.80 Crore. Out of which, an amount of Rs. 62.40 crore hasbeen released to J&K.(d): The funds allocated/released under different components of RUSA 1.0 and RUSA 2.0 toJammu and Kashmir are as below:<pre>(Rs. in Crore)ComponentPhysicalUnitsApprovedTotalAmountApprovedTotal CentralShareApproved(90:10)CentralShareReleasedRUSA 1.0Preparatory Grants 8 7.2 7.2Management Monitoring Evaluationand Research MMER1% of total releases0.036Infrastructure Grants to Universities 2 40 36 36New Colleges (Professional) 3 78 70.2 46.8Infrastructure Grants to Colleges 22 43.54 39.186 24.99Vocationalisation of Higher Education(State as a Unit)1 7.47 6.723 0.7594Creation of Universities byConversion of Colleges in a Cluster2 110 99 99Upgradation of Existing DegreeColleges to MDCs3 12 10.8 10.8RUSA 2.0Infrastructure Grants to Universities 1 20 18 9Infrastructure Grants to Colleges 8 16 14.4 7.2Enhancing Quality & Excellence inSelect State Universities1 100 90 30New Model Colleges 3 36 32.4 10.8Upgradation of Existing DegreeColleges to MDCs2 8 7.2Equity Initiatives 2 5 4.5 2.25Faculty Improvement 1 7 6.3 3.15TOTAL 51 491.01 441.91 287.99</pre>(e): Presently there is no such proposal under consideration in the Ministry of HumanResource Development.******
