                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

325






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Electronic Charging Stations




Raghu Ramakrishna Raju Kanumuru














                        Will the Minister of





HEAVY INDUSTRIES AND PUBLIC ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has sanctioned Electronic Charging Stations under Faster Adoption and Manufacturing of Hybrid and Electric Vehicles in India (FAME India) Phase-II to the States in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the details of cities that have been identified in the State of Andhra Pradesh for this purpose?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF HEAVY INDUSTRIES & PUBLIC ENTERPRISES                  (SHRI PRAKASH JAVADEKAR)(a) to (c): Yes, Sir. Department of Heavy Industry has sanctioned 2,636 Electric Vehicles (EVs) Charging Stations in 62 cities across 24 States/UTs under FAME India (Faster Adoption and Manufacturing of Hybrid & Electric Vehicles in India) scheme phase II. In Andhra Pradesh, 92 Charging Stations to Vijayawada, 71 Charging Stations to Vishakhapatnam, 68 Charging Stations to Tirupati and 35 Charging Stations to Kakinada have been sanctioned under the Scheme.                      *****
