                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

218






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Shell Companies




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has developed any smart mechanism to identify shell companies in the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and(c)	the action taken so far against the shell companies during the last three years and the current year, State/ UT-wise including Bihar?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER FOR FINANCE AND CORPORATE AFFAIRS				(Ms. NIRMALA SITHARAMAN)????? ??? ???????? ????? ?????? 				(??????? ??????? ???????)(a)to(b):There is no definition of the term “Shell Company” in the Companies Act.It normally refers to a company without active business operation or significant assets, which in some cases are used for illegal purpose such as tax evasion, money laundering, obscuring ownership, benami properties etc. The Special Task Force set up by the Government to look into the issue of “Shell Companies” has inter-alia recommended the use of certain red flag indicators as alerts for identification of Shell Companies. The Government has undertaken a Special Drive for identification and strike off Shell Companies. ...2/--2-(c): The number of Companies de-registered for the last three years, State-wise is as given below including Bihar:S.
