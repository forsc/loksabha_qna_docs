                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

105






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Dropouts in Higher Education




Geetha Viswanath Vanga














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether a high dropout rate is being reported in Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Indian Institute of Management (IIM) and other higher education level courses;(b) if so, the details thereof during the last five years and the current year; and(c) the corrective steps being taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c) :      A statement is laid on the Table of the House. STATEMENT AS REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (c) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. *105  TO BE ANSWERRED ON 10.02.2020 ASKED BY HON’BLE M.P. SHRIMATI VANGA GEETHA VISWANATH REGARDING “DROPOUTS IN HIGHER EDUCATION”(a) No, Sir.(b) Details of dropout from Higher Education Institutions is as under:-<pre>Percentage of dropout in some institutesYear	IIT	IIM	Other Institutions2015-16	2.25	1.04	7.492016-17	1.60	1.06	8.562017-18	1.71	1.03	6.762018-19	1.46	0.50	5.362019-20	0.68	0.78	2.82</pre>(c) As can be seen from above, there has been decreasing trend in dropout rate over the last 5 years. This has been achieved by taking a number of corrective measures to minimise the dropout which include appointment of advisor to monitor the academic progress of students and peer assisted learning.***
