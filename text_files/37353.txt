                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4533






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






BRAP




Sushil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the status of ranking States based on the achievements of goals on the Business Reform Action Plan (BRAP);(b) 	the year in which this exercise was last conducted; (c) 	the reasons for the delay in carrying out this exercise;(d) 	the steps being taken by the Ministry to nudge States to adopt practices and reforms which attract investments;(e) 	whether the Ministry has made an effort to quantitatively gauge the impact of these reforms on the investments in the States; and(f) 	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons for stopping the Ministry from carrying out such an exercise?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a): 	Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), in consultation with the State Governments, conducts a comprehensive reform exercise called Business Reforms Action Plan (BRAP).  Ranking of States on the basis of implementation of BRAP started in 2015. Details of rankings last released are placed at Annexure 1. (b): 	The assessment of States/UTs under fifth edition, viz. BRAP, 2020 is in its final stage presently. (c): 		The reasons for delay in carrying out the exercise of BRAP, 2020 were: i.	Extension in timeline for implementation, submission of reform evidences, uploading of user data and submissions to queries raised, on the basis of requests received from States/UTs on account of COVID-19 surge, andii.	Implementation delay in Feedback Survey on account of COVID-19 surge curbs imposed by the State/UT Governments. (d): 	DPIIT assisted States/UTs in implementation of reforms by sharing national and international best practices through a number of Capacity Building Webinars and Regional workshops to help States/UTs in understanding reform requirements, objectives and other key points.(e) & (f): 	Effective implementation of reforms by States/UTs is the fulcrum of improved business ecosystem and attract investments. The reform achievements of States/UTs are captured quantitatively in the form of national rankings under the exercise of BRAP. The national rankings provide States/UTs an incentive to undertake reforms positively and transform themselves.****
