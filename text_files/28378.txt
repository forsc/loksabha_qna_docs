                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

15






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






National Policy for Skill Development




Dayakar Pasunoori













                                                    Kavitha Malothu
                                                








                                                    Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta
                                                








                                                    Gaddam Ranjith Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is in the process of revamping the National Policy for Skill Development and Entrepreneurship; and;(b)	if so, details thereof and whether the proposed revamp will improve the productivity to match global standards?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b) The Steering Committee of National Skill Development Mission in its 2nd meeting on June 29, 2021, has proposed to undertake the review of National Policy for Skill Development and Entrepreneurship. Alignment with global standards has always been a part of Policies and Guidelines of Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship. All NSQF Qualifications Packs and Curriculum contain references to global standards wherever needed.
