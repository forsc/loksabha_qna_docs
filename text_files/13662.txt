                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SHIPPING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2517






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Development of National Waterways




Ramesh Chandra Majhi














                        Will the Minister of





SHIPPING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any step on priority     basis to implement the development process for first two     phases of National Waterway (NW) No. 5   i.e. Talcher     Industrial area with Dhamara and Paradip Port, which     will connect industrial as well as coal mining area to     the port; and(b) whether steps are being taken by the Government to     develop NW-14, NW-22, NW-23, NW-64 and NW-96 and if so,     the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                         (a)	The Following development activities related to shipping and navigation have been taken up by the Inland Waterways Authority of India (IWAI) on the stretch of National Waterway (NW-5) form Talcher to Dhamra/Paradip, Pankapal and Mangalgadi.(i)        Monthly longitudinal Bathymatric survey (Thalweg Survey in Phase-I).(ii) 	Consultancy work for preparation of Detailed Project Report (DPR) including Front End 	Engineering Design (FEED) for construction of 4 weirs cum barrages with 3 navigational locks, 	2 check dams & 1 rubber dam with navigational lock.  (iii)	Consultancy work for preparation of Detailed Engineering Report (DER) including FEED for Modification/ Reconstruction of existing 09 Bridges on NW-5. (iv)	Detailed  hydrographic survey between Talcher and Pankapal for model study.   (b)	As per Feasibility Report (FR)/ Detailed Project Report (DPR),  development of shipping and navigation for the purpose of commercial cargo transportation on NW-14 (Baitarani River), NW-64 (Mahanadi river), NW-22 (Birupara-Badi Genguti-Brahmani river system), NW-23 (Budha-Balanga river) and NW-96 (Subarnrekha River) have not been found feasible by the IWAI. The FR/ DPRs of the aforementioned NWs have been shared by the IWAI with the State Governments for development of ferry/ cruise services  etc., in these NWs as per their requirements.                                   *****
