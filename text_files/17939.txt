                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1716






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Sainik Schools




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of Sainik Schools in the country at present, State-wise;(b) whether the Government is considering to open new Sainik Schools in the country in the next three years;(c) if so, the details thereof, location and State-wise; and(d) the average percentage of students from Sainik Schools selected to serve Armed forces, school-wise;






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE    (a) There are 33 Sainik Schools functioning in the country. State wise details of Sainik Schools in the country is placed at Annexure-I.(b) & (c) Government has signed MoA with the State Governments in respect of three new Sainik Schools namely Alwar (Rajasthan), Rudraprayag (Uttarakhand) and Warangal(Telangana).(d) The average percentage of students from Sainik Schools per year, selected to serve NDA/Naval Academy/ Military Academies is placed at Annexure-II.                              *****
