                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5395






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Awards/Incentives to Sportspersons




Gurjeet Singh Aujla














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has fulfilled its commitment to the sportspersons by giving awards and incentives as per policies and announcements during the last two years and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(b) whether the Khelo India programme has been as successful as was envisaged and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor along with the details of men and women participated and awarded under the ‘Khelo India’ youth games during the last three years; and(c) whether any player / players were not able to attend international sporting events abroad due to lack of air tickets or delay in confirmation of air tickets during last three years and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[KIREN RIJIJU](a) Yes Sir, the implementation of the Scheme of Special Awards to Medal Winners in International Sports Events is a continuous process. Information relating to disbursement of cash award to athletes and coaches in the last two years is at Annexure-I. Under Talent Search & Development vertical of Khelo India Scheme 2433 athletes are being supported with financial assistance @ Rs. 5 lakhs per annum.(b) Yes Sir, the Khelo India Scheme has helped in creating sports ecosystem with the twin objective of mass participation and promotion of excellence. The salient outcomes of Khelo India Scheme is at Annexre-II.(c) No sir, while there has been no incidence where players were not able to attend international sporting events abroad due to lack of air tickets or delay in confirmation of air tickets during last three years, India’s archery athletes could not take part in Archery World Cup (Stage 1) held from 22-28 April 2019 at Medellin (Colombia) due to sudden change in the departure time of KLM flight from New Delhi leading to non-availability of connecting flight from Amsterdam to enable athletes to reach the destination in time.*****
