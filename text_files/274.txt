                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

390






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Disinvestment of KIOCL Ltd




K. Muraleedharan














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	whether the Government has any proposal for disinvestment of KIOCL Limited;b)	if so, the details thereof; c)	whether the Government has received budgeted disinvestment target of 80,000 crores; d)	if so, the details thereof; ande)	the details of PSU’s gone for disinvestment during the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of Finance(Smt. Nirmala Sitharaman)a) & b) 	Yes Sir, disinvestment of a part of Government shareholding in KIOCL is being pursued to make the company compliant with the minimum public shareholding as required under SEBI rules and regulations.c) & d) 	Rs.84,971 crore proceeds have been received against the Budget Estimate of Rs.80,000 crore.(e)	The Details are available on the website of DIPAM: www.dipam.gov.in*****
