                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4591






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Setting up of PM Mitra Park




P. Ravindhranath














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the present status of the proposal for setting up of 7 Mega Integrated Textile Region and Apparel parks under Mega Integrated Textile Region and Apparel (PM MITRA); (b) whether the Government has also decided to complete the setting up of such parks within five years in the States including Tamilnadu and if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the Government has also identified the location for the same; and (d) if so, the details thereof and follow up steps taken by the Government to achieve the completion of such parks within the time frame along with the amount of funds allocated by the Government till now?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a) & (b):	The Government has approved setting up of 7 (Seven) PM Mega Integrated Textile Region and Apparel (PM MITRA) Parks in Greenfield/Brownfield sites to develop world class infrastructure including plug and play facility with an outlay of Rs. 4,445 crore in a period of seven years i.e. up to 2027-28. (c) & (d):	So far 17 Preliminary Project Reports (PPRs) from State Governments for setting up PM MITRA Parks have been received. The details of which are as under:-  S.No.	Name of the State	No. of proposals (proposed site)1	Karanataka 	2(Two) - (i) Vijaypura, (ii) Gulberga2	Andhra Pradesh	Kadapa3	Rajasthan	Jodhpur4	Odisha	Ganjam5	Gujarat	Navsari6	Madhya Pradesh 	4(Four) (i) Ratlam, (ii) Devas, (iii) Dhar & (iv) Katni7	Telangana	Warangal8	Punjab	Ludhiana9	Chhattisgarh 	Mahasamud10	Uttar Pradesh	Luck now/Hardoi11	Bihar	West Champaran12	Tamil Nadu	Virudhnagar13	Maharashtra	AmravatiThe PM MITRA Parks sites will be selected from the PPRs received from the State Governments through challenge matrix by Project Approval Committee. Milestones for making the PM MITRA parks operational have been laid down in the operational guidelines of the Scheme which will be duly monitored. ***
