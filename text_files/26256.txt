                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2050






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Medicinal Plants




Kanakmal Katara













                                                    Keshari Devi Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYUSH be pleased to state:(a) the details of the medicinal plants for which cultivation programme is being implemented under the National AYUSH Mission;(b) the financial assistance being provided by the Government for cultivation of medicinal plants indicating the names of plants for which assistance is being provided;(c) the facilities being provided by the Government to sell medicinal plants along with the scheme to provide easy market access in the country including Uttar Pradesh and Banswara - Dungarpur; and(d) the details of equipment being provided at special concessional rates for cultivation of medicinal plants?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF AYURVEDA, YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(AYUSH) (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a): The Ministry of Ayush, Government of India had implemented Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the National Ayush Mission (NAM) since 2015-16 to 2020-21. Under the Medicinal Plants Component of the National Ayush Mission scheme, the Ministry of Ayush had supported the cultivation of the prioritized medicinal plants species. The details of medicinal plants species are given in Annexure-I.(b): Under the Medicinal Plants Component of the National Ayush Mission (NAM) scheme, subsidy @30%, 50% and 75% of the cost of cultivation per hectare have been supported through State Implementing Agency. 86 medicinal plants species were supported for financial assistance on the basis of cost of cultivation per hectare under the Medicinal Plants Component of the National Ayush Mission Scheme during the financial year 2015-16 to 2020-21. Detail is given in Annexure-II. (c): To establish a platform for the trade of Medicinal Plants and to provide easy market access National Medicinal Plants Board (NMPB), Ministry of Ayush has launched “e-CHARAK” mobile application as well as a web portal for the promotion and marketing of medicinal plants/herbs. “e-CHARAK” is a platform for buying and selling of medicinal plants. The “e-CHARAK” application supports different local languages and it also provides a fortnightly market price of 100 Medicinal Plants from 25 herbal markets across the country.	In addition, provision for the development of infrastructure of Post-harvest Management facilities (Storage / Godowns, Drying sheds) and Collection Centres to facilitate easy market access (Rural Collection Centres, District Collection Centres and Retail Outlets) were supported under the Medicinal Plants Component of the National Ayush Mission Scheme. The detail of the number of the Post-harvest Management units and Collection Centres supported under the Medicinal Plants Component of National Ayush Mission scheme throughout the country including Uttar Pradesh and Rajasthan during the financial year 2015-16 to 2020-21 are given in Annexure-III.(d): Under the Medicinal Plants Component of the National Ayush Mission Scheme, there is no provision to provide equipments for the cultivation of Medicinal Plants.
