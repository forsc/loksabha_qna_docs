                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

263






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.11.2021






River Lake and Pond Fishing




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING ??????????, ??????? ?? ????? ?????? be pleased to state:(a) 	whether the Government has any special plans to motivate persons engaged in river/ lake/pond fishing and provide incentives for keeping them commercially alive in this field; and(b) 	if so, the details thereof and the incentive schemes provided to support river/lake/pond fishing in the country, particularly in the State of Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING		(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) & (b):The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying is implementing a flagship scheme “Pradhan Mantri Matsya SampadaYojana (PMMSY)- A scheme to bring about Blue Revolution through sustainable and responsible development of fisheries sector in India” with highest ever investment of Rs. 20,050 crore  for a period of five years with effect from the financial year 2020-21 to 2024-25 in all States/Union Territories(UTs) including Maharashtra.PMMSY inter-alia envisages development of fisheries in inland water bodies like rivers, lakes and ponds.  The PMMSY provides supportsto persons engaged in fishing in river/ lake/pondsfor procurement of boats, nets and such other implements for fishing, for procurement of fish transport vehicles like  refrigerated and insulated vehicles, three wheelers and two wheelers with ice boxes, construction of fish kiosks for marketing their catch,for construction of ice plants&cold storages for safe storage & reducing  losses, for construction of nursery ponds, rearing ponds& grow out ponds with inputs, Re-circulatory Aquaculture System (RAS) &biofloc units for diversifying into aquaculture, for undertakingcage culture in reservoirs, stocking of fingerlings in reservoirs&pen culture in open water bodies to increase their catch.The Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying during last financial year (2020-21) and current financial year (2021-22) has accorded approval to the fisheries development proposals of Government of Maharashtra at a total cost of Rs. 395.00 crore under the PMMSY. The approved project activities also include providing boats and nets for traditional fishermen,construction of rearing ponds, grow out ponds with inputs for aquaculture, construction of biofloc ponds,Re-circulatory Aquaculture System (RAS),stocking of fingerlings in reservoirs, installation of cages in reservoirs, integrated development of reservoirs,  post harvest and cold chain activities likeice plants/cold storages, refrigerated vehicles, insulated vehicles, motorcycle with ice box, three wheelers with icebox, live fish vending centres and fish kiosks.Besides, the Government of India has also extended the facility of Kisan Credit Card (KCC) to fisheries and animal husbandry farmers to meet their working capital needs for rearing of animals, poultry birds, fish, shrimp, other aquatic organisms and capture of fish. *****
