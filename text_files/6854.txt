                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

541






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Agreement on Manufacturing of LCA




Nitesh Ganga Deb














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has signed MoU for manufacturing of Light Combat Aircraft (LCA) and the Su-30 MKI jets by Hindustan Aeronautics Limited; and(b) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b):  The Government of India has not signed any MoU for manufacturing of either LCA Aircraft or Su-30 MKI Aircraft with Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL).	However, for procurement of total 40 LCA [20 in Initial Operational Clearance (IOC) and 20 in Final Operational Clearance (FOC) configuration], two contracts have been signed between Govt. of India and HAL.	For procurement of total 222 Su-30 MKI Aircraft under license production for Indian Air Force (IAF), six contracts have been signed by the Govt. of India with HAL based on the Inter-Governmental Agreement (IGA) inked between India and Russia.*****
