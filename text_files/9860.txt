                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3241






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2019






CBSE Syllabus




Abdul Majeed Ariff














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to include the views and thoughts of greatreformer Sree Narayana Guru in the syllabus of CBSE;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) and (b): NCERT has informed that the content related to the views and thoughts ofgreat reformer Sree Narayana Guru has already been included in NCERT textbook “OurPasts-III” for class VIII with a theme ‘Women, Caste and Reform’ under section onCaste and Social Reform. In this section under the sub heading Demand for Equalityand Justice, there is a mention of Sree Narayana Guru alongwith his photograph. Oneof his famous statement is also mentioned in the Chapter i.e. “orujati, orumatam,orudaivammanushyanu” (one caste, one religion, one god for humankind).(c): Does not arise.*****
