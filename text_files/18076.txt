                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1670






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Concerns of Employees of ITI Limited




Vellalath Kochukrishnan Nair. Sreekandan














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether employees of ITI Limited at Palakkad were not getting salary on time; (b) 	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (c) 	whether the Government has received any representation in this regard; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	Salary payment to ITI units including Palakkad Unit is done every month with an average delay of 3 weeks approximately.(b)	The delay is due to stress on cash flows.(c) & (d)  	A VIP reference dated 08.08.2020 from Shri V.K. Sreekandan, Hon’ble M.P. (Lok Sabha) was received in Department of Telecommunications (DoT HQ), which inter alia referred  to  non-payment of salary for the month of June & July, 2020 to employees of ITI Palakkad Unit. This was taken up with ITI Ltd., who informed that  the salary of June, 2020 & July, 2020  was delayed due to Covid-19 Lockdown and it was paid on 05.08.2020 and 21.08.2020 respectively.************
