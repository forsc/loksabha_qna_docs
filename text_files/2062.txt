                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1811






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Losses incurred by MTNL




Bidyut Baran Mahato













                                                    Sudheer Gupta
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether MTNL’s net worth has declined substantially and the current liabilities of the company has exceeded its total current assets and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and (b) 	whether the Government proposes to invest money to bail out MTNL and if so, the details thereof along with the other steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COMMUNICATIONS, LAW & JUSTICE AND ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY(SHRI RAVI SHANKAR PRASAD)(a) 	Details of networth, current assets and current liabilities of Mahanagar Telephone Nigam Limited (MTNL) for last five years are as under:(Rs. in crore)<pre>Particulars	2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19*Current Assets	   2,108.11 	   2,119.49 	      4,949.25 	      5,024.49 	      5,742.07 Current Liabilites	   3,452.40 	   3,888.11 	   10,580.75 	      9,055.39 	    11,558.60 Networth	   2,067.42 	        12.60 	 (3,366.70)	  (6,337.35)	  (9,734.94)</pre>* provisional and unaudited Low tariffs due to fierce competition in the telecom sector, high staff cost, debt burden and absence of 4G services in the data centric telecom market are the main reasons for losses of MTNL. (b) 	A consultant was engaged to facilitate the preparation of the revival/ restructuring plan of MTNL. Pursuant to the recommendations from the consultant and approval by MTNL Board, a comprehensive revival plan for MTNL is under preparation.
