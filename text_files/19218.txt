                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

547






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






Public Procurement Policy for MSEs




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the objectives of the Public Procurement Policy for Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs);(b) 	the total annual procurement from such MSEs, sector-wise, category-wise (buyers), and year-wise during the last three years; and(c) 	the total annual procurement from such MSEs in Jharkhand along the same parameters?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a):	The Public Procurement Policy for Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs) Order, 2012 has been notified by this Ministry, under Section 11 of MSMED  Act,  2006,  the same being effective  from 1st April, 2012, and has become mandatory w.e.f. April 2015. The policy was amended vide Gazette Notification No. S.O. 5670(E), dated 9th November, 2018. The said Policy, as amended, mandates 25% annual procurement from MSEs by Central Ministries/ Departments/ Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) including 4% from MSEs owned by SC/ST and 3% from MSEs owned by Women entrepreneurs.(b):	As per information reported by Central Public Sector Enterprises (CPSEs) on MSME SAMBANDH Portal, the details of the annual procurement of goods and services from MSEs, by CPSEs and year-wise during the last three years (as on date 01.02.2021) is given as under:<pre>Financial Year	Buyers	Total Procurement (Rs in Crores)	Procurement from MSEs(including SC/ST owned MSEs)(Rs in Crores)	Procurement from MSEs owned by SC/ST(Rs in Crores)	Procurement from MSEs owned by Women(Rs in Crores)2017-18	169 CPSEs	114,042.05	26357.46(23.11%)(No. of MSEs benefited  89631)	544.72(0.48%)(No. of MSEs benefited  2255)	-2018-19	165 CPSEs	153,477.05	40,399.54(26.32%)(No. of MSEs benefited  128124)	824.71(0.54%)(No. of MSEs benefited  4587)	232.56(0.15%)(No. of MSEs benefited  1410)2019-20	136 CPSEs	123,717.50	38,275.11(30.94%)(No. of MSEs benefited  156744)	685.21(0.55%)(No. of MSEs benefited  6313)	390.90(0.32%)(No. of MSEs benefited  3613)<pre>Information are available CPSE-wise, and not Sector-wise or Category-wise.(c):	As per available information, Sector-wise figures are not maintained.
