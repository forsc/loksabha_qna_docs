                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1100






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Skill Development Training




Khagen Murmu














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the number of institutes, which are currently functioning in Maldaha Uttar parliamentary constituency to provide skill Development Training;(b)	the number of youths, who have been benefited in Maldaha Uttar parliamentary constituency area by undergoing the said training?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Under Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), 52 training centres including 1 Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Kendra (PMKK) have been empanelled for imparting short term training courses in the Maldaha district of West Bengal. Out of which, 16 are operational. (b)       Under PMKVY, 10,460 candidates have been trained under short term training courses and 8,547 candidates have been oriented under Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) component in the Maldaha district of West Bengal.
