                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2773






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Adoption of Solar Energy




Sunita Duggal













                                                    Sanjay Bhatia
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to make all the Government buildings/offices self-reliant by adopting solar energy and other environment friendly renewable sources of energy;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the rate of success so far; and(c) the time by which target of hundred per cent coverage is likely to be achieved in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY AND POWER (SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a)to(c) The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy has requested all States /Union Territories to issue necessary directions to all Departments and Institutions of States/Union Territories for utilization of vacant rooftops of their buildings for installation of solar panels. All Central Ministries/ Departments have also been requested to issue necessary directions for installation of rooftop solar projects in all buildings owned by them. As per data available on the SPIN portal of the Ministry, an aggregate capacity of 517.78 MW of rooftop solar plants has been installed at 7897 Government buildings as on 02.08.2021.*****
