                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4596






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






UGC New Initiative – STRIDE




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the University Grants Commission (UGC) has approved a new initiative –Scheme for Trans-disciplinary Research for India’s Developing Economy (STRIDE) to boostresearch culture in the country;(b) if so, the details thereof, along with its objectives/salient features;(c) whether the scheme will strengthen research culture and innovation in colleges andUniversities and if so, the details thereof along with the areas identified for funding underSTRIDE;(d) the schemes being implemented by UGC, with a view to encourage research anddevelopment in the country; and(e) whether NITI Aayog has recommended for restructuring of the UGC and if so, the detailsthereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c): Yes, Sir. The University Grants Commission (UGC) has approved the Scheme forTrans-disciplinary Research for India’s Developing Economy (STRIDE) to boost researchculture in the country.STRIDE provides support to research projects that are socially relevant, locally needbased,nationally important and globally significant. The scheme supports research capacitybuilding as well as basic, applied and transformational action research that can contribute tonational priorities with focus on inclusive human development. It also provides creation,development and integration of new ideas, concepts and practices for public benefit andstrengthening the society. The focus on Humanities and Human Sciences provides to boostquality research on Indian languages and knowledge systems.(d): With the view to encourage research and development in the country, the UGC hasvarious schemes namely: BSR Faculty Fellowship, Emeritus Fellowship, Dr S.Radhakrishnan Post Doctoral Fellowship in Humanities (scheme initiated in 2015-16), DrD.S. Kothari Post Doctoral Fellowship, Post Doctoral Fellowship for SC/ST, Post DoctoralFellowship for Women, Junior Research Fellowship in Science, Humanities and SocialSciences, Basic Science Research Fellowship, Swami Vivekananda for Single Girl Childfellowship for Research, Maulana Azad National Fellowship for Minority Students(Scheme entrusted & funded by Ministry of Minority Affairs), National Fellowship for Studentsof Other Backward Classes (OBC) ( Scheme entrusted & funded by Ministry of Social Justice& Empowerment), National Fellowship for Scheduled Caste Students (Scheme entrusted &funded by Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment), Special Assistance Programme, MajorResearch Project and Minor Research Project.(e): NITI Aayog has recommended to replace the existing regulatory bodies with a singleapex regulator.*****
