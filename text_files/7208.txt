                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

851






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Repairing of Highways in Madhya Pradesh




Rakesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) the details and the present status of national highways which were damaged during the monsoon this year across the country, State/UT-wise;(b) the length of highways repaired/reconstructed and proposed to be repaired/reconstructed out of these highways in the State of Madhya Pradesh;(c) whether funds have been allocated/released for the said purpose and if so, the details thereof; and(d) whether any time frame has been fixed to complete the said reconstruction and repair work and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	This Ministry is primarily responsible for development and maintenance of National Highways (NHs). The works on NHs are accordingly taken up from time to time as per traffic density, inter-se priority and availability of funds to keep the NHs in traffic worthy conditions.	The State/UT- wise details of NHs damaged due to rainfall / floods during 2019-20 are placed at Annexure-I. Temporary restoration of all damaged sections of NHs have been taken up and permanent restoration under periodical renewal/ original work is planned as per inter-se priority and availability of funds to keep all NHs in traffic worthy condition. The M&R of stretches of NHs, where either Development works have commenced or Operation, Maintenance and Transfer (OMT) Concessions/ Operation and Maintenance (O&M) Contracts have been awarded, are the responsibility of the concerned Concessioners/ Contractors till the Defect Liability Period (DLP)/ the Concession Period.(b)	The details of length of NHs repaired/reconstructed and proposed to be repaired/ reconstructed in the State of Madhya Pradesh during current financial year 2019-20 are placed at Annexure - II(c)	The State/ UT wise funds allocated so far during 2019-20 for immediate restoration and for ensuring traffic worthiness of the NHs damaged due to rainfall/floods is placed at Annexure-III. (d)	The repair and reconstruction works are targeted to be completed in stages during current financial year 2019-20 and 2020-21..
