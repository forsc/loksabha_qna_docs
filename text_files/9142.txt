                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2644






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






Foreign Investment in India




Kotagiri Sridhar













                                                     Chandra Sekhar Bellana
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY  ??????? ??? ?????? ??????be pleased to state the number of foreign investors who have invested in India during each of the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????? ??? ?????? ?????? (???? ????? ????) THE MINISTER OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI  PIYUSH GOYAL)Data on number of foreign investors who have invested in India is not centrally maintained. However, the number of remittances related to FDI equity inflow reported in the country during each of the last three financial years are as under:<pre>S. No.	Financial Year	FDI Equity InflowNumber of Remittances1	2016-17	143992	2017-18	175513	2018-19	18355           Source: Reserve Bank of India.*****</pre>`
