                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

981






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Corporate Units




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the number of corporate units currently running in profits and losses respectively;(b) the efforts being made to strengthen corporate units more in this era of competition; and(c) the efforts being made to convert the current loss-making units into the profit-making ones ? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION; MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING; AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF CORPORATE AFFAIRS                                                                    [RAO INDERJIT SINGH](a) to (c): The Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) administers the provisions of the Companies Act, 2013 (the Act), the Limited Liability Partnership Act, 2008 and the Insolvency  and Bankruptcy Code, 2016.The term “Corporate Unit” is not defined in the Companies Act. However, as per the records maintained in the MCA portal, number of companies which are running in profits  during the financial year 2019-20 is 4,00,375 and number of companies which are running in losses during the financial year 2019-20  is 4,02,431.*****
