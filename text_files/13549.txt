                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2363






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Expansion of National Highways




Sukanta Majumdar














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government proposes to extend/ upgrade the National Highway (NH)-512 	from Gazole to Balurghat in West Bengal and if so, the details and the present status 	thereof; (b)	whether the bids have been invited for upgrading the said NH from existing 2 Lane to 4/6 	lane and if so, the details and the present status thereof; (c)	whether the Government has approached the Government of Bangladesh for extending 	NH-512 beyond border to Bangladesh; (d)	if so, the details thereof including the reaction of the Government of Bangladesh thereto; 	and (e)	the other important roads constructed in West Bengal including Balurghat region during 	the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) The road from Gazolle to Hili section of  National Highway (NH)- 512 in the State of  West Bengal is proposed to be upgraded under Bharatmala Pariyojna. Preparation of Detailed Project Report has been taken up. The bids for construction work are invited after finalization of DPR.(c)   No Sir. (d)   Does not arise.(e)   The details of important road works taken up/completed in the State of West Bengal during last five year is at Annexure.
