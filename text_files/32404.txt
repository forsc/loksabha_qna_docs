                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COOPERATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3836






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






NCCT Evaluation




Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya













                                                    Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COOPERATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether there is any independent evaluation conducted on National Council 	for Cooperative Training (NCCT);(b)	If so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government to create 	capable Human Resource for neo-cooperative movement in the country;(c)	whether financial audits are conducted on NCCT activities; and (d)	if so, the  details thereof including the audit paras still pending for action?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF COOPERATION???????? ?????? 	(SHRI AMIT SHAH)(a) and (b) Yes, Sir. An independent third party evaluation was conducted for National Council for Cooperative Training (NCCT) through NABARD Consultancy Services Pvt Ltd (NABCONS), New Delhi during the 12th Five Year Plan. The Government has continuously been making efforts towards capacity building in cooperative sector by conducting regular programmes for education and training through 20 NCCT institutes viz. [Vaikunth Mehta National Institute of Cooperative Management (VAMNICOM), Institute of Regional Cooperative Management (IRCMs) (5) and Institute of Cooperative Management (ICMs) (14)]. (c) & (d) Yes Sir, financial audits viz. Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG), statutory, internal and other audits are conducted periodically on various activities of NCCT.  Action on the audit paras is taken as per the stipulated norms.  *****
