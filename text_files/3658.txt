                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3287






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.07.2019






Ayurveda Colleges and Medicinal Plants




Mukesh Rajput













                                                    Kanakmal Katara
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)   be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government proposes to open Government Ayurveda Colleges in the most backward areas of the country; (b) if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (c) whether the Government also proposes to develop special medicinal plants/herbal parks in the country with the cooperation of multinational companies, if so, the details thereof; and (d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) & (b):  Presently there is no such proposal under consideration of the Government including for the most backward areas of the country.(c):   Currently, the National Medicinal Plants Board (NMPB), Ministry of AYUSH, Government of India has no proposal to develop special medicinal plants/herbal parks in the country with the cooperation of multinational companies.(d):  Presently, the NMPB, Ministry of AYUSH under its “Central Sector Scheme on Conservation, Development and Sustainable Management of Medicinal Plants” provides project based support to various Government and Non-Government organizations throughout the country to develop different types of Herbal Gardens viz. Home Herbal Gardens, School Herbal Gardens, Institutional/ Public Herbal Gardens and Herbal Gardens of State and National importance.However, as per the above said Central Sector Scheme guidelines there is no provision to develop medicinal plants/herbal garden in the country with the cooperation of multinational companies.……………
