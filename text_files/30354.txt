                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1728






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Reinstating Season Ticket




Kumbakudi Sudhakaran














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    )  whether the Government has taken cognizance of the complaints from the general public in North Malabar region that passengers availing season tickets are only able to board morning and evening trains and if so, the facts thereof;(b)  whether the Government has received any representation  from Member of Parliament on 5 November 2021 seeking to re-instate the season ticket arrangement for all trains;(c)   if so, the details thereof;(d)  whether the Government is willing to re-instate the season ticket arrangement for all trains;(e)  if so, the details thereof along with the timeline for implementing the request; and (f)   if not; the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY                              (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW) (a) to (f): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.  STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (f) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 1728 BY SHRI KUMBAKUDI SUDHAKARAN TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 08.12.2021 REGARDING REINSTATING SEASON TICKET(a): At present, season ticket holders of North Kerala are permitted to travel in unreserved coaches of 18 trains. (b) & (c): Representations are received at Stations, Divisions, Zonal and Railway Board level.  (d) to (f): With effect from 25.11.2021, unreserved coaches have been restored in 6 trains to cater the need of unreserved and season ticket passengers. Further, restoration of season tickets based on the Covid Protocol issued by State Governments/Central Governments is an ongoing and continuous process.                                                       *****
