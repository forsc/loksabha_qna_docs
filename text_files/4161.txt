                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3829






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Social Security Schemes




Ramya  Haridas














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that only 1.4 per cent of India’s GDP is spent on social security schemes and it is lower than the spendings in most countries in Asia; (b) if so, the details of the steps the Government would take to increase the percentage of expenditure on social security schemes; and (c) the allocations made and expenditure incurred towards social security schemes during the last five years, scheme and year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c): The Government has a priority to empower  weaker sections of the society through implementation of social security schemes. The detailed allocation and expenditure incurred under various social sector schemes run by Government of India under the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Ministry of Finance and Ministry of Rural Development is given in Annexure.****
