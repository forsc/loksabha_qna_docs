                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

268






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Setting up of MSMEs




Tirath Singh Rawat













                                                    T. R. Paarivendhar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has prepared any concrete industrial policy/action plan to set up Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) in the hilly districts of Uttarakhand so as to provide permanent employment and check migration keeping in view that people are continuously migrating from such places, particularly from Pauri Garhwal and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the Government has any proposal to set up more MSMEs in the country including industrially  backward  Perambalur region of Tamil Nadu and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and   (c)	the total number of MSMEs set up in the country including in Perambalur during the last three years ?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a) to (c):     A statement is laid on the Table of the House.Statement referred to in reply to parts (a) to (c) of Lok Sabha Starred Question No. *268 for answer on 11.07.2019(a) & (b):  The Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) implements various schemes and programmes for promotion and development of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) across the country. These include the schemes/programmes such as Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP), Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI), A Scheme for Promoting Innovation, Rural Industry and Entrepreneurship (ASPIRE), Credit Guarantee Scheme, Credit Linked Capital Subsidy Scheme (CLCSS), Micro and Small Enterprises - Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP), etc. 	These programmes and schemes are aimed at creating conducive environment for the MSMEs to grow across the country and promote employment generation. (c):  As per Udyog Aadhar Memorandum data, the total number of MSMEs registered in the Country and specifically in the Perambalur District in Tamilnadu during last three years is given in the Table below:<pre>	Year 2016-17	Year 2017-18	Year 2018-19	Total All India	2373223	1517784	2123472	6014479Perambalur District 	492	528	562	1582</pre>
