                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3604






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Regular Vice Chancellor




Ranjan Singh Rajkumar














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Manipur Central University is still functioning without a regular ViceChancellor;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether any enquiry is pendingagainst any former V.C. of the saidUniversity; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): The Hon’ble High Court of Manipur vide its Order dated 11.10.2018 in PublicInterest Litigation (PIL) No. 49 of 2018, has appointed ShriJarnail Singh, IAS Retd., andformer Chief Secretary of Manipur as an Administrator for discharging the functions of theVice Chancellor of Manipur University as provided under the Manipur University Act, 2005and such other relevant Statutes and his primary duty and responsibility is to bring normalcyin Manipur University.(c)& (d): The Committee constituted to enquire into the allegations made against theProf. Adya Prasad Pandey, Vice Chancellor, Manipur University has submitted its report tothe Ministry.******
