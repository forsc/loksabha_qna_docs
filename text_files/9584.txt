                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3156






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2019






AYUSH Wellnes Centres in Jharkhand




Chandra Prakash Choudhary














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH)   be pleased to state:(a) whether AYUSH Wellness Centres have been set up in Jharkhand, if so, the details thereof, district-wise; (b) the details of funds allocated for this work along with the funds utilized out of it; (c) whether the required number of doctors and equipments are functional in such AYUSH Wellness Centres in Jharkhand; (d) if not, the reasons therefor along with the assistance provided by the Government to the State Government to resolve it; and (e) the extent to which the said problems have been resolved with the assistance provided?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (e) As public health is a State subject, setting up of AYUSH Health & Wellness Centres (HWCs) comes under the purview of respective State/UT Government. The proposal received from the State Government of Jharkhand for upgradation of 100 AYUSH dispensaries to AYUSH HWCs was approved under Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM) and Rs 825.90 lakhs has been released to the State Government during the month of August, 2019 and this approved amount also includes the proposal of incentives for AYUSH doctors and purchase of equipments. However, at present these units are not functional as AYUSH HWCs. The details of AYUSH HWCs unit approved in the State of Jharkhand, district-wise is furnished at Annexure.
