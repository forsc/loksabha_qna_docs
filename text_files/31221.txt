                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2907






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Tickets for Passengers




(Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has made it mandatory to buy reserved tickets for the passengers of general coaches of trains; (b)	if so, the reasons therefor; and(c)	whether the Government has taken any steps to facilitate the passengers of general coaches and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMUNICATIONS AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY                               (SHRI ASHWINI VAISHNAW)(a) to (c):  In view of Prevailing Covid-19 conditions in various parts of the country, presently the general coaches in long distance Mail/Express trains are being booked on reservation basis as second sitting reserved.*****
