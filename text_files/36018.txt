                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3425






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Auction of Coal Blocks




Krupal Balaji Tumane














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether private players have won coal blocks auctioned recently in the country and if so, the details thereof;(b)	the details of companies and coal blocks won during last seven years by private players;(c)	whether the private players have started production from the coal blocks and if so, the details of the production and use of coal by private players; and(d)	whether the private players can sale coal in open market and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF COAL, MINES & PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a)	Yes, Sir. After of launch of Commercial Auction by Ministry of Coal in June, 2020, till date total of 42 coal blocks have been successfully auctioned which includes 39 coal blocks to private companies.  List of these 39 coal blocks may be found in the Annexure (Table-A) enclosed.(b)	In addition to aforementioned 39 coal blocks successfully auctioned to private players, 25 more coal blocks have been successfully auctioned to private companies before launch of commercial auction scheme.  List of these 25 coal blocks may be found in the Annexure (Table-B).  Hence, a total of 64 coal blocks have been successfully auctioned to private companies till date. (c)	Out of 64 coal blocks auctioned to private companies till date, 15 coal blocks have started production. Of these 15 blocks, 14 coal blocks have been allocated for captive use and one block is for sale of coal. Production from these 15 coal blocks till date (till February, 2022) is 49.141 Million Tonne.(d)	As per the agreement signed with the successful bidders of coal mines for commercial mining, there shall be no restriction to carry on mining operations for own consumption, sale or for any other purpose including but not limited to captive consumption, Coal Gasification, Coal Liquefaction and export of coal.  Further, Mines and Minerals (Development & Regulation) Act has been amended inter alia allowing 50% sale of coal on merchant basis annually from coal produced from captive mines.  ****
