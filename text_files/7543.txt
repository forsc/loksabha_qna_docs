                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1184






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Funds for Skill  Development Programme in Madhya Pradesh




Anil Firojiya














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether any grant/fund has been sanctioned by the Government for any city of Madhya Pradesh under the Skill Development Programme from the year 2015 till date;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d)	the district-wise number of youth who were imparted training in the said State under the Skill Development Programme from the year 2015 to 2019?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c)	Under Skill India Mission, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship is implementing a flagship scheme called Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 2016-20 with an objective to provide skilling to 1 crore prospective youth under Short Term Training (STT) courses and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) across the country including State of Madhya Pradesh for four years i.e. 2016-2020 with an outlay of Rs. 12,000 Cr.	PMKVY has two components known as Centrally Sponsored Centrally Managed (CSCM) being implemented by National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) and Centrally Sponsored State Managed (CSSM) being implemented by State Skill Development Missions of the States/ UTs popularly known as State- Engagement Component of PMKVY (2016-20).Under CSCM component of PMKVY 2016-20, there is no provision of State-wise or district-wise funds allocation. However, under CSSM component of PMKVY 2016-20, 25% of the funds and corresponding physical targets of PMKVY 2016-20 have been allocated to the States for the implementation of scheme through State Skill Development Missions across the State covering all districts. Under this component, after evaluation of proposal received from Madhya Pradesh State Skill Development and Employment Generation Board (MPSSDEGB), Government of Madhya Pradesh, Ministry has given in-principle approval of a total target of 84,058 candidates and corresponding financial allocation of Rs. 123.26 Cr. for FY 2016-20, out of which, Rs. 21.46 Cr. has been released to Government of Madhya Pradesh for the implementation of CSSM component of the Scheme in State of Madhya Pradesh covering all districts. After rationalisation, the fund allocated for State of Madhya Pradesh has been reduced to Rs. 92.44 Cr.(d)	The district-wise number of candidates trained in State of Madhya Pradesh under PMKVY 2016-20 is given at Annexure.
