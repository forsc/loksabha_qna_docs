                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PLANNING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1419






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.11.2019






Atal Innovation Mission




Uttam Kumar Nalamada Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





PLANNING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of funds allocated to States/UTs under the Atal Innovation Mission; (b)	the number of Atal Tinkering Labs, State/UT wise; and(c)	the number of Atal Incubation Centres that have been set up, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IMPLEMENTATION(RAO INDERJIT SINGH)----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------(a)	Atal Innovation Mission does not directly allocate any funds to States/UTs, but allocates Grants-in-Aid directly to the beneficiaries, the details of disbursements made by Atal Innovation Mission follow:1)	Atal Tinkering Labs: Rs.561.58 Cr2)	Atal Incubation Centres: Rs.97.26 Cr3)	Established Incubation Centres: Rs.42.65 Cr(b)	The number of Atal Tinkering Labs, States/UT wise is as under:State	No of ATLsAndaman and Nicobar Island	10Andhra Pradesh	349Arunachal Pradesh	23Assam	191Bihar	69Chandigarh	16Chhattisgarh	220Dadra and Nagar Haveli	3Delhi	146Goa	17Gujarat	323Haryana	175Himachal Pradesh	59Jammu and Kashmir	37Jharkhand	63Karnataka	336Kerala	248Madhya Pradesh	278Maharashtra	460Manipur	59Meghalaya	11Mizoram	20Nagaland	20Odisha	184Puducherry	14Punjab	121Rajasthan	215Sikkim	24Tamil Nadu	375Telangana	146Tripura	12Uttar Pradesh	305Uttarakhand	45West Bengal	106Grand Total	4680(c)	Atal Innovation Mission has given Grants-in-Aid to the following 38 Atal Incubation Centres (AICs):State/UT	Number of Atal Incubation Centres (AICs)Andhra Pradesh	2Assam	1Chhattisgarh	1Delhi	1Goa	1Gujarat	4Haryana	1Karnataka	3Kerala	1Madhya Pradesh	4Maharashtra	5Punjab	1Rajasthan	3Sikkim	1Tamil Nadu	4Telangana	2Uttar Pradesh	3Grand Total	38 Additionally, Atal Innovation Mission has given Grants-in-Aid to the following 9 Established Incubation Centres (EICs):State/UTNumber of Established Incubation Centres (EICs)Delhi	1Karnataka	3Kerala	1Tamil Nadu	1Telangana	2Uttar Pradesh	1Grand Total	9***
