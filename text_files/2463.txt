                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2224






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Promotion of Hockey




Sunil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the programmes being implemented by the Government to promote hockey in the country;(b) the locations in the country where hockey stadiums are being set up, State-wise;(c) whether the Government proposes to set up any new hockey stadiums incoming years;(d) if so, the details of the locations thereof; and(e) whether hockey is popular at Mahuadanr in Latehar district of Jharkhand and if so, the efforts being made by the Government to promote hockey there?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) Sir, while the responsibility for promotion of sports, including hockey, rests with State/Union Territory Governments, the Central Government, is supplementing their efforts by providing expert training and coaching facilities and assisting Hockey India (National Sports Federation for Hockey).(b) to (d) Sports Authority of India (SAI) under this Ministry has established 16 Hockey Astroturfs in its training centres and this Ministry has sanctioned 10 Astroturfs for Hockey under Khelo India Scheme. The list is at Annexure.(e) According to ‘Hockey India’ hockey is popular in Latehar district of Jharkhand. Training facilities have been made available at SAI Regional Centre, Kolkata and SAI Training Centres at Hazaribagh and Ranchi.*****
