                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

531






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






National Young Leaders Programme




Geeta Kora













                                                    Dileshwar Kamait
                                                








                                                    Vishnu Dutt Sharma
                                                








                                                    Jugal Kishore Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a)	the number of programmes organised in Madhya Pradesh, Jharkhand, Jammu & Kashmir and Bihar under National Young Leaders Programme during the last five years and the current year, State-wise;b)	the funds released to these States during the last five years along with the achievements of Nehru Yuva Kendras of Madhya Pradesh, West Singhbhum district of Jharkhand, Jammu district of J&K and Supaul district of Bihar during the last five years? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FORYOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS(SHRI KIREN RIJIJU)(a) The State wise details of number of programmes organized during the last five years under National Young Leaders Programme (NYLP) scheme (Neighbourhood Youth Parliament) through Nehru Yuva Kendra Sangathan (NYKS) is at Annexure-I. Neighbourhood Youth Parliaments could not be organized during the year 2020-21 due to Corona pandemic situation.(b) The details of the funds released during the last five years to the states of Madhya Pradesh, Jharkhand, J&K and Bihar to NYKS under NYLP scheme is at Annexure-II. The achievements of Nehru Yuva Kendras  in the state of  Madhya Pradesh, West Singhbhum district of Jharkhand, Jammu district of J&K and Supaul district of Bihar during last five years is at Annexure-III.	*****
