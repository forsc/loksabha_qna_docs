                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

965






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Construction of NH-31




Annpurna Devi














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the construction work of National Highway (NH) No. 31 between Barhi	(Jharkhand) and Rajauli (Bihar) was started four years back by the National Highways 	Authority of India; (b)	if so, whether the construction work on the said road has not been completed so far; 	and (c) 	whether the Government will ensure the construction of the said road in the fixed time 	frame and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c)	Barhi-Rajauli section of NH-31 has total length of about 47.40 Km.  The project stretch has been divided into two packages. Package-I i.e. Barhi to Koderma section of NH-31 of length 27.50 km has already been awarded in February, 2019.  Package-II i.e. Koderma to Rajauli section of length 20.00 kmhas not been awarded in want of requisite pre- construction activities and wild life clearance. *****
