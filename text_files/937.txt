                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

720






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.06.2019






Setting up of Atomic Park by Pakistan




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether Pakistan is preparing to set up an atomic park;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a)	The Government had seen press reports about Pakistan''s interest in setting up "Nuclear Parks” as a means to attract foreign investors in setting up private nuclear power plants. The stated reason for this was to meet the country''s growing energy requirements. (b) & (c)   Does not arise.***
