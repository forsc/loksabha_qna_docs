                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

816






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Facilities to Persons with Disabilities at Monuments




Bhartruhari Mahtab














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is contemplating a slew of measures for  facilitating harmonised and barrier-free environment for persons with disabilities (PwD) and elderly visitors visiting protected sites, monuments, libraries, and museums etc.;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the time by which such measures are likely to be implemented in the   country;  and(d)	the funds allocated for the same State/UT wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY) (a)  to (d) 	Yes, Sir. Site specific accessible structures/facilities viz. ramps & elevators, sufficient wheel chairs & space, free entry with attendants, train staff to assist, exclusive amenities (toilets, drinking water facilities etc.), tactile exhibits with braille and audio guide facility, tactile learning aids, reserved parking etc. are provided for Persons with Disabilities and elderly persons in Centrally Protected Monuments, Libraries and Museums of National Importance.Providing such facilities in protected monuments/sites, libraries and museums and upgrading them is a continuous process. ****
