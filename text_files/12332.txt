                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1194






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






Promotion of Tourism through International Financial Institutions




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has made any agreement with any International Financial Institution to promote tourism in India; (b) 	if so, the areas identified to promote tourism with foreign assistance; and (c) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	No sir. The Ministry of Tourism has not signed any agreement with any International Financial Institutions to promote tourism in India. However, certain tourist places/circuits in the country are developed by the State Governments/Union Territories (UT) Administrations with the assistance of various International Agencies/foreign banks. The details regarding foreign financial assistance for tourism projects of the States Government/Union Territories received from Department of Economic Affairs are at Annexure-I & Annexure-II.***********
