                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1903






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Smart City Projects in Rajasthan




Chandra Prakash Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the works undertaken in the State of Rajasthan 	under Smart City Projects;(b) 	the details of the funds sanctioned, allocated and expenditure 	incurred for the above projects till date;(c) 	whether the Government is satisfied with the progress of works 	being undertaken;  if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	whether all works would be completed as per the fixed time 	frame and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                                     THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                                (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a) to (d):  Government of India launched the Smart Cities Mission (SCM) on 25 June 2015. 100  Smart  cities  have been selected through 4 rounds of competition from January 2016 to June 2018.  4 cities in the State of Rajasthan i.e., Ajmer, Jaipur, Kota & Udaipur have been selected for development as Smart Cities. Smart City-wise details of selection, progress of projects, funds released by Central Government & utilization thereof are given in Annexure-I. The period of implementation of SCM has been extended upto June 2023 and all Smart Cities are expected to complete their projects within the stipulated time.                                       ****
