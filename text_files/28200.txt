                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3610






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Honour Killings




A. Ganeshamurthi













                                                    S. Ramalingam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware of the fact that large number of incidents of honour killings for inter caste and inter-religious marriages have been reported in various parts of the country;(b)	if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the total number of such honour killings for inter-caste and inter-religious marriages that have been registered, solved and are pending in the country during each of the last three years, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI AJAY KUMAR MISHRA)(a) to (c):	‘Police’ and ‘Public Order’ are State subjects under the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution of India. The responsibilities to maintain law and order, protection of life and property of the citizens, and investigation and prosecution of crime rests with the respective State Governments. The State Governments are competent to deal with such offences under the extant provisions of laws. National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) compiles and publishes statistical data on crimes in its publication “Crime in India”. Published  reports  are  available  till the  year  2019.  Specific  details  in this regard  are not  centrally   maintained.   However,  State/UT-wise   cases -2-LS.USQ. NO.3610 FOR 10.08.2021registered under Murder (Section 302 IPC) with the motive as Honour Killings for the years 2017 to 2019 are at Annexure. *****
