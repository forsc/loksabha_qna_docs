                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2174






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.03.2020






Short Supply of Coal




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether thermal power plants have been closed down by the Government in the entire country including Uttar Pradesh due to short supply of coal;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has taken steps to check the shortage of coal in the country; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF COAL, MINES AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a) to (d): Sufficient domestic coal has been supplied to power plants of the country and the coal stock at power plants end has increased from 25.37 Million Tonnes (MT) as on 26.02.2019 to 36.78 MT as on 26.02.2020 which is sufficient for 21 days consumption. Also, there is sufficient coal at the end of coal companies and as on 26.02.2020, the pithead coal stock with the Coal India Limited (CIL) was 42.43 MT and that of Singareni Collieries Company Limited (SCCL) was 2.26 MT.  The progress of coal offtake is reviewed on a regular basis and the coal supplies to Power sector are monitored regularly by an Inter-Ministerial Sub Group comprising representatives of Ministries of Power, Coal, Railways, Shipping, CEA, NITI Aayog, CIL etc.  *****
