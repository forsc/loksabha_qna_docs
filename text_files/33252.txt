                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

594






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Centrally Sponsored Healthcare Schemes




Girish Bhalchandra Bapat













                                                     Bhavana Gawali (Patil)
                                                








                                                    Chandra Sekhar Sahu
                                                








                                                    Pritam Gopinath Munde
                                                








                                                     Rahul Ramesh Shewale
                                                








                                                    Krupal Balaji Tumane
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the centrally sponsored schemes currently being implemented in health care sector across the country;(b) whether the Government has rationalized centrally sponsored schemes and central sector schemes in healthcare sector to make Government spending more effective and if so, the details of the schemes proposed to be rationalized and the steps taken to enhance the spending on health sector;(c) the funds sanctioned for various States and Union Territories including Bihar under such schemes during each of the last three years, State/ UT-wise;(d) whether funds are being allocated to various Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) under these schemes and if so, the names and details of such NGOs State/UT wise, particularly in Maharashtra including their registration number;(e) the funds released to such NGOs during the last three years; and(f) whether cases of fraudulent activities have been reported in the work allocated to the said NGOs and if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a)	to (f) The details of various Centrally Sponsored Schemes implemented in the Ministry along with details of funds released to various States/UTs is attached at Annexure. At present various Central Sector Schemes and Centrally Sponsored Schemes are implemented with separate budget lines for each component of the schemes under this Ministry. Government spending for these schemes are made for best output as per quarterly expenditure plan. Under major Centrally Sponsored Schemes of the Ministry, engagement of the Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) is conducted by the States and Districts at local level. All records pertaining to NGOs including funds provided, utilisation of funds and their performance assessment are maintained at the State level. The funds are released to States/UTs on the basis of Utilization Certificate.******
