                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.4572 TO BE ANSWERED ON 23.03.2021ALLOCATION UNDER SC/ST SUB-PLAN 4572. SHRI SHIVAKUMAR C. UDASI: SHRI SUMEDHANAND SARASWATI: MS. PRATIMA BHOUMIK: SHRI PRADEEP KUMAR SINGH: SHRI BHAGWANTH KHUBA: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has earmarked any allocation under SC-ST Sub Plan in the current year and if so, the details thereof, Ministry-wise; (b) whether it is a fact that utilization of SC and ST Sub-Plan funds is not upto the mark and a huge amount is lapsed every year, if so, the details of funds sanctioned and utilized during the last three years, State-wise; (c) whether the Government has any plan to put unutilized funds under SC-ST Sub-Plan to “Non-lapsable Central Pool of SCSP Funds (NLCPSF)”; and (d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
