                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1001






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2021






National Child Labour Project, Gwalior




Vivek Narayan Shejwalkar














                        Will the Minister of





LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)whether the child labour employees under the National Child Labour Project Scheme, Gwalior have not received their remuneration for the last twenty nine months;(b)if so, the reasons therefor;(c)the time by which the payment will be made; (d)whether the Government proposes any scheme regarding making these employees permanent who have been serving under the National Child Labour Project Scheme, Gwalior for more than twenty years; and (e)if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE (IC) FOR LABOUR AND EMPLOYMENT(SHRI SANTOSH KUMAR GANGWAR)(a) to (c):	Under the NCLP  Scheme,  Grant-in-Aid is released to the  District Project Societies under the chairmanship of District Collector/District Magistrate,  who in turn engage and allocate the funds to NGOs/Voluntary Agencies/ Civil Societies Organisation  etc. for running of Special Training Centres.  Ministry of Labour and Employment has   released the Grant-in-Aid of Rs. 51,36,500/- as 1st installment for the FY 2019-20  to the  District Project Society under NCLP, Gwalior. The 2nd installment is processed only after receipt of Annual Progress Report, Quarterly Progress Report, Utilization Certificate etc. (d) & (e):  At present, there is no such proposal under consideration of Ministry of Labour and Employment.   *******
