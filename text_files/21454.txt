                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PLANNING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2642






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Atal Tinkering Labs




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





PLANNING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the amount spent on Atal Tinkering Labs (ATLs) under Atal Innovation Mission (AIM) from the inception of the scheme till date, State-wise;(b)	the details of the number of schools that have ATLs, State-wise and distribution of such labs in Government and private schools;(c)	whether the Government has plan to expand the scheme to all schools in the country; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF PLANNING AND MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF STATISTICS AND PROGRAMME IPLEMENTATION(RAO INDERJIT SINGH)(a)	& (b) The information pertaining to the amount spent on Atal Tinkering Labs (ATLs) under Atal Innovation Mission (AIM) per state from the inception of the scheme till date; and  the details of the number of school that have ATLs, state-wise and distribution of such labs in Government and Private schools; is placed at Annexure A.(c)	Currently, AIM has no plan to expand the scheme to all schools in the country, as per budgetary mandate.(d)	The mandate of the Atal Innovation Mission is to set-up and establish 10,000 ATLs in schools across the country. The selection of 10,000 schools for the establishment of ATL is done and till date, 7138 schools have received the ATL Grant-in-Aid.*****Annexure A : State Wise number of ATLs and amount spent on ATLs.State	Count of Schools	Grant Amount (in Cr.)
