                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3346






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






Electrification of Anganwadi Buildings




Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the steps taken to improve the condition of Anganwadi centres across the country, State/UT-wise including Andhra Pradesh; (b) 	the number of Anganwadi centres that have been electrified across the country, 	State/UT-wise including Andhra Pradesh; and (c) 	the list of Anganwadi centres lacking functional toilet facility across the country, 	State/UT-wise especially in Andhra Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a)  	Providing modern and essential facilities to cater to the needs of women and children at Anganwadi Centres (AWCs) is a continuous process.  A number of steps in this direction have been undertaken in States including Andhra Pradesh, some of which are as under:(i)	Revised joint guidelines have been issued by Ministries of Women & Child Development, Rural Development and Panchayati Raj on 17.02.2016 for construction of 4 Lakhs AWC buildings across the country under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) in convergence with Anganwadi Services under Umbrella Integrated Child Development Services Scheme. (ii)	For upgradation of AWC buildings, there is a provision of Rs.2.00 lakh per annum per AWC building. Further, there is a provision of maintenance of Government owned AWC building @ Rs. 3,000 per annum per AWC building under Anganwadi Services.(iii)	Drinking water facilities have been provided in 11,31,837 Anganwadi Centres. State-wise statement (including Andhra Pradesh) is at Annexure-I.(iv)	Grants are sanctioned for purchase of water filter for serving safe drinking water, furniture, equipments, etc. (v)	Under the Swachhta Action Plan Rs.10,000/-per AWC are given for providing drinking water facilities and Rs.12,000/- per AWC for providing toilet facilities.(vi)	As on 09.03.2020, 7.41 lakh Anganwadi Workers (AWWs) and Supervisors have been provided with Smart Phones for IT-enabled monitoring under ICDS-Common Application Software (CAS).  State-wise statement (including Andhra Pradesh) is at Annexure-II.(vii)	Funds for ECCE have been enhanced to Rs 5,000 per AWC. Activity books have been introduced in the Scheme.  (viii)	For nutritional deficiency, norms prescribed in Schedule-II of NFSA, 2013, are followed: Sl. No.	Categories	Revised norms (per beneficiary per day)		Calories (K) Cal)	Protein (g)1.	Children (6-72 months)	500	12-152.	Severely malnourished children (6-72 months)	800	20-253.	Pregnant women and Nursing mothers 	600	18-20	To improve the condition of the AWCs across state of Andhra Pradesh, the following steps are being taken by the Andhra Pradesh Government:(i)	Out of 55607 AWCs (main & mini), 20868 AWCs are functioning in Own buildings. 7682 AWCs are functioning in School buildings and 3343 AWCs are functioning in community/rent-free buildings. Remaining 23714 AWCs are running in rented buildings.(ii)	During the year 2015-16, 2016-17 & 2018-19, 10584 AWC buildings were constructed, remaining 464 AWC buildings are under construction and 6000 buildings were sanctioned during 2019-20.(iii)	2065 AWC buildings were constructed under RIDF Scheme. 733 buildings were sanctioned during 2018-19. (iv)	Besides, Government of India has sanctioned funds for repairing of AWCs under up-gradation head as under:  (a)	4828 AWC buildings were taken up for repair during 2013-14 & 2014-15 @ Rs.1.00 lakhs per AWC.(b)	Repairs were sanctioned for 960 AWCs during 2017-18, 852 AWCs during 2018-19 & 600 AWCs during 2019-20 @ Rs.2.00 lakh per AWC. (v)	Government of India has sanctioned funds for maintenance of 18615 AWCs during 2018-19 and 30820 AWCs in 2019-20 @ Rs.3000 per AWC for minor repairs and white washing etc.(vi)	Government of India has sanctioned funds for construction of toilets for 9066 	AWCs @ Rs.12000/- per AWC and for drinking water facilities in 2628 AWCs @ Rs.10000/- per AWC during 2017-18 & 2018-19.(b)	State-wise data for electrified AWCs across the country is not maintained at Central Level.  However, as per information received from Andhra Pradesh Government, out of 55607 AWCs, 50653 AWCs have been electrified in Andhra Pradesh from District Authorities, Local bodies and from CSR funds.(c)	State-wise statement of toilet facilities (including Andhra Pradesh) is at Annexure-III.  In Andhra Pradesh, all 55607 AWCs are having toilet facility, out of which 40665 AWCs have separate toilet facility.
