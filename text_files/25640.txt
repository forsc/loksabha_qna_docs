                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1585






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.07.2021






Salt Pans




D. Ravikumar














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Ministry has any plans to lease 17000 acres of salt pan owned by Union Government for other commercial purposes in Tamilnadu and if so, the details thereof;(b) 	whether the Ministry has any plans to close salt pans in Marakkanam of  Viluppuram District, Tamilnadu and if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government has any plans to setup iodisation plant at Marakkanam and if so, the details thereof;(d) 	whether the Ministry has any plans to resume medical camps for workers and educational aid, as well as financial grants for meritorious students of their families; and(d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a):  	No Sir, The salt pan land owned by the Union Government is transferred to central government departments/ state governments and their agencies for public purpose only.(b) & (c):    	No Sir.(d)  & (e):  	As informed by the Salt Commissioner Organization (SCO), an attached office under Ministry of Commerce & Industry, the implementation of the two activities, namely, organization of health & eye camps for salt workers; and  providing financial assistance to meritorious students of salt workers, have been adversely affected in financial year 2020-21 due to Covid-19 pandemic. However, the activities will resume in current financial year and Government will organize two Health camps at Tuticorin, and Markanam, Tamil Nadu. Also the applications for grant of rewards to the meritorious students of salt workers during current financial year 2021-22 have been called for.******
