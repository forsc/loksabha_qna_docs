                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3101






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






ICAR Projects in Andaman and Nicobar Islands




Kuldeep Rai Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the projects being run/ implemented under the auspices of the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) in Andaman and Nicobar Islands during each of the last three years and the current year?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ?? ????? ?????? ??????		           (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	Central Island Agricultural Research Institute (ICAR-CIARI) established by Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR), at Port Blair conducts and coordinates Research and Development activities in field and horticultural crops, fisheries, animal husbandry, management of natural resources, agricultural engineering and social sciences.  The details of projects run/implemented at ICAR-CIARI, during each of the last three years and the current year in various areas of agriculture are given below:Year	No of Projects2018-19	852019-20	892020-21	1062021-22	81*           *As on 21/03/2022*****
