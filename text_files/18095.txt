                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1761






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.09.2020






Post Office ATM




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to connect Post Office ATM with Bank ATM to benefit people from rural areas of the country; (b) 	if so, the time by which the said connectivity is likely to be established; (c) 	whether the implementation of the said decision will result in creation of new employment; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)  	Sir, the Post Office ATMs have been made operational since 2014 and inter-operable with the existing ATM infrastructure of various Banks across the country since 30.12.2016(b)    Question does not arise in view of the position mentioned at (a) above. (c)    Question does not arise as ATMs were installed and made operational since 2014 and its inter-operability is not linked to employment generation. (d)    Question does not arise in view of the position mentioned at (c) above.************
