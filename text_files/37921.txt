                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5083






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






Financial Assistance to Kendriya Vidyalaya Students




Rajmohan Unnithan














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government did not provide any financial assistance including stationary allowance, transportation fare to Kendriya Vidyalaya Students under Right to Free of Compulsory Education Act;(b) whether the Government has taken note of the same and if so, the action taken/to be taken by the Government in this regard; and (c) if not the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) to (c) Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan has informed that the students admitted in Kendriya Vidyalayas under The Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 are exempted from payment of all kinds of fees upto Class –VIII. As per the availability of resources, reimbursement towards books, stationary, uniforms and transportation was made to the students admitted under The Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act, 2009 till 24.05.2018.  *****
