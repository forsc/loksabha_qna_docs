                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4803






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Demand and Requirement of Petroleum Products




Guman Singh Damor














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the details of annual requirement/demand of petroleum products and natural gas, State/UT-wise; (b)	the details and the number of petroleum and natural gas reserves in the country, State/UT-wise; (c)	whether the Government proposes to lay gas pipeline at district headquarters; and(d)	if so, the time by which work in this regard would to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a)	The consumption of petroleum products and natural gas for the financial year 2020-21 is 194.3Million Metric Tonnes (MMT) and 60.8 Billion Cubic Metres (BCM) respectively. The details of State-wise consumption of Petroleum products are annexed at Annexure-I (b) The state-wise details of balance recoverable reserves of Oil (including Condensate) and Natural Gas in the country as on 01.04.2021 are estimated as under: Location/ area	Balance Oil (including Condensate) Reserves (in MMT)	Balance Gas reserves (in BCM)Andhra Pradesh 	3.5	27.1Arunachal Pradesh 	3.6	3.1Assam 	110.8	144.3Gujarat 	86.3	45.6Jharkhand 	-	7.8Madhya Pradesh 	-	30.9Rajasthan 	35.3	57.7Tamil Nadu 	6.8	27.3Tripura 	0.1	22.2West Bengal 	0.023	32.2Eastern Offshore 	39	514.5Western Offshore 	189.9	228.6Total	475.3	1141.3(c ) & (d) As per PNGRB Act, 2006, the Petroleum & Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB) is the authority to grant authorization to the entities for development of City Gas Distribution (CGD) network in Geographical Areas (GAs). After 11th CGD bidding round PNGRB has authorized 268 GAs for development of CGD Network in the country. Letters of Intent have been issued for 21 GAs. Further, recently under 11th A CGD bidding rounds PNGRB has invited bids for 6 GAs. After the completion of 11th and 11th A CGD bidding rounds, the CGD network shall potentially cover 98% population and 88% geographical area of the country. The entities authorized by PNGRB lay Steel and Medium-density polyethylene (MDPE) pipelines based on technical feasibility of the route in the authorized GAs. As on 31.01.2022, a total of 3,52,961 Inch-kilometre of Steel and MDPE pipelines has been laid by the CGD entities across the country. Expansion of pipeline infrastructure is a continuous activity based on the gas demand assessment of various regions. ***
