                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

946






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.11.2019






Ayurveda Units in Army Hospitals




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal













                                                    Shankar Lalwani
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government is setting up Ayurveda units in the Army hospitals; (b) if so, the details thereof and the names of Army hospitals in which Ayurveda treatment facility is available at present; and (c) the number of Army hospitals in which the Government has set a target to increase Ayurveda system of treatment?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (c):       Ministry of AYUSH and Ministry of Defence have signed a MoU for the Integration of AYUSH under the health establishments of Ministry of Defence/Directorate General of Armed Forces Medical Services (DGAFMS).  Under the MoU, Ayurveda Units will be establishment at Palliative Care Centre, Base Hospital, Delhi Cantt., Army Research and Referral Hospital, Delhi, Air Force Hospital, Hindon, Ghaziabad and Five identified Ex-Servicemen Contributory Health Scheme (ECHS) Polyclinics in Delhi – NCR.  Hon’ble MoS (I/C) for Ministry of AYUSH and MoS for Defence inaugurated first Ayurveda unit at Palliative Care Centre, Base Hospital, Delhi Cantt. on 21.10.2019.  Second unit at ECHS polyclinic, Lodhi Road has also been made operational since 07.11.2019.…………………
