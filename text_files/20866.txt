                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2180






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Jiwan Kaushal Karyakaram




Sumedhanand Saraswati














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the salient features of the Jiwan Kaushal Karyakram run by the Government;(b) the name of the States where this scheme has been implemented; and(c) the details of the estimated funds earmarked and the targets fixed under the said Scheme?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ''NISHANK'')(a): The University Grants Commission (UGC) has developed life skills (Jeevan Kaushal) curriculum for undergraduate students at Universities and Colleges. This Curriculum covers the courses on communication skills, professional skills, leadership & management skills and universal human values. The life skills curriculum is available at https://www.ugc.ac.in/e-book/SKILL%20ENG/mobile/index.html . (b): The Jeevan Kaushal curriculum is suggestive in nature. Universities being autonomous bodies, the Vice-Chancellors have been requested by the UGC to consider the curriculum for introduction in their Universities and affiliated Colleges/institutions, at under-graduate level.  (c): The UGC has informed that the total estimated amount towards curriculum development and skill training in Universities and Colleges under the programme is Rs. 230.16 crores. ******
