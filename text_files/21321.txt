                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2523






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






New Value Addition Projects




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre













                                                    Ranjeetsinha Hindurao Naik-Nimbalkar
                                                








                                                    Mitesh Rameshbhai (Bakabhai) Patel
                                                








                                                    Shivkumar Chanabasappa Udasi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of various value addition projects in agriculture sector during the year2018-19 and the steps taken/being taken by the Government to ensure adequate value addition in agriculture sector;(b)	the total number of projects approved so far under the scheme for Integrated Cold Chain and Value addition, State-Wise including Maharashtra;(c)	the current status of implementation of the projects approved so far; and(d)	the steps taken by the Government to expedite the implementation of the approved projects?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI RAMESWAR TELI) (a):	Government does not setup projects for value addition to agri-horti produce at its own. Government supports such projects by way of financial assistance through its schemes, namely the “Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH)” of the Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare (DoAC&FW) and the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojna (PMKSY) of the Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI).  These schemes are demand/entrepreneur-driven from among entrepreneurs, private companies, cooperatives, farmers group etc.  Details of projects sanctioned during 2018-19 and subsequent years are given at Annexure-I. (b) & (c): The State-wise details of projects approved the scheme for Integrated Cold Chain and Value Addition, including Maharashtra and their current status of implementation is given at Annexure-II. (d):	Ministry is having online portal for monitoring implementation of the projects.Ministry regularly review the projects with the promoters at the level of Joint Secretary and Secretary.  Projects are also monitored by the Inter-Ministerial Approval Committee chaired by the Minister (FPI). Ministry also make interventions as and when necessary for resolving logistical and manpower related issues which poses problems, like covid situation.  *****
