                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4638






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Ordance Factory in Khamaria




Rakesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the employees of Ordanance factory Khamaria located in Jabalpur are being paid ‘overtime‘ without adding allowances; and(b) whether it is against the Factory Act; (c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) whether the Government proposes to make ‘overtime’ payment to the employees of this ordnance factory according to new procedure?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) Ordnance Factory Khamaria located in Jabalpur makes the overtime payment to employees in terms of letter of Principal Controller of Accounts (Fys) No. Pay/Tech-II/73 dated 08.06.2011 which excludes allowances of compensatory nature (HRA/Transport Allowance/Small Family Allowance etc.).(b) to (d):  The instructions issued by the Ministry of Labour and Employment vide O.M. No.   Z-16025/81/07-ISH.II dated 27.05.2009 and subsequent instructions issued by Department of Defence vide O.M. No. 14(1)/2000/D(Civ.II) dated 26.06.2009 exclude allowances of compensatory nature (HRA/Transport Allowance/Small Family Allowance etc.) are being used for the purpose of computing Overtime Allowance under the Factories Act, 1948.*****
