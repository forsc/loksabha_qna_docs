                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2117






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Construction of Roads under CRF




Khagen Murmu













                                                    Sukanta Majumdar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government builds roads under Central Road Fund (CRF) as per the recommendation of the States; (b)	 if so, the details of the roads constructed during the last three years in the country under CRF, State/UT-wise; (c)	 whether any proposal under CRF from the State Government of West Bengal is pending and if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	the steps being taken by the Government regarding speedy implementation and monitoring of funds under CRF?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)	Yes, Sir.(b)	The state wise details of State Roads (non-Rural) constructed under CRF for all the States/Union Territories, during the last three years are annexed.(c)	No proposal is pending under CRF for the state Government of West Bengal(d)	The State Governments directly execute the works sanctioned under CRF. Ministry monitors the progress of the works based on Utilization Certificate (UC) and releases the funds based on UC.
