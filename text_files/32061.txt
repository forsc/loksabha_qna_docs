                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3669






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.12.2021






Teachers in Uttar Pradesh




Dharmendra Kumar Kashyap














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total sanctioned strength of the primary junior and inter-college teachers in Uttar Pradesh;(b) the number of posts that have been lying vacant among these posts; and(c) the steps proposed to be taken by the Government for filling up these posts?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) to (c) : The State Government of Uttar Pradesh has informed that the total sanctioned strength of teachers in Junior Basic Schools (1st to 5th class) and Senior Basic Schools (6th to 8th class) run by Basic Education Board, Prayagraj is 5,80,084, out of which 4,53,594 teachers are working. In consecutive teachers recruitments from 2017, 1,20,554 teachers are recruited. The sanctioned strength of teachers in Government Secondary schools is 27,022, out of which 11,518 are lying vacant. Recruitment of 6,628 Secondary Teachers was completed during last 6 months.****
