                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

74






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Students-Teachers Ratio in EMRS




Meenakashi Lekhi














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of functional Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS) for the year 2018-19;(b)	the details of the student-teacher ratio in these schools;(c)	the details of the drop out ratio in EMRS;(d)	whether any steps have been taken by the Government to increase the number of functional EMRS; and(e)	the details of the steps taken to increase the enrollment ratio in such schools?






ANSWER




                                                         (a) The number of functional Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRS) for the year 2018-19 is 219.(b) & (c) As per the extant guidelines of Eklavya Model Residential School (EMRS), the schools have to be run as per norms of CBSE and in compliance with Right to Education Act.(d) & (e)The matter of making the schools functional and ensuring utilization of seats are pursued regularly with the State Governments. States have been advised to start facilities in suitable alternate building till the school buildings are completed. States have also advised to start admissions in new schools at the entry level of class VIth to ensure better enrolment and utilization of the infrastructure. *****
