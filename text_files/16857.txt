                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

566






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






Unused Land of Postal Department




Prataprao  Jadhav













                                                     Gopal Jee Thakur
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether ample land of Postal Department is lying unused in Buldhana and Shegaon post offices situated in Buldhana Parliamentary Constituency of Maharashtra State; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to build a commercial complex by developing unused lands; (d) 	if so, the details thereof; and (e) 	if not, the reasons therefor?  






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION AND ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)	No, Sir. There is no unused Postal land in Buldhana and Shegaon in Buldhana Parliamentary Constituency of Maharashtra State.(b)  	Nil, in view of (a) above.(c)	Nil, in view of (a) above.(d)	Nil, in view of (a) above.(e)	Nil, in view of (a) above.************
