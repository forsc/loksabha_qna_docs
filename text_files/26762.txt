EWS Reservation




Ritesh Pandey













                                                    Unmesh Bhaiyyasaheb Patil

                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.2310TO BE ANSWERED ON 03.08.2021EWS RESERVATION 2310. SHRI RITESH PANDEY:   DR. HEENA GAVIT:   SHRI UNMESH BHAIYYASAHEB PATIL:   DR. SHRIKANT EKNATH SHINDE:   SHRI DHAIRYASHEEL SAMBHAJIRAO MANE:   DR. SUJAY RADHAKRISHNA VIKHE PATIL: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the ten per cent reservation for Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) from General Category has been implemented, if so, the number of persons who have availed the benefit of the same in appointment to Government service till date; (b) the States where the said reservation is still to be implemented; (c) the steps taken to increase the number of seats in various Government institutions/Universities, thereafter, the details thereof, State/UT-wise;(d) whether many of these newly added seats have gone vacant, if so, the details thereof; and(e) the funds/additional funds provided to the Ministries for the implementation of EWS reservation?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SUSHRI PRATIMA BHOUMIK)(a): Yes Sir. Department of Personnel & Training who is handling the work of recruitment in Government service have informed that they are not having the information regarding the number of persons who have availed the benefits of EWS reservation in appointment to Government service till date.(b):  By virtue of Entry 41 of State List in the Seventh Schedule of the Constitution of India, the respective States are required to implement the provisions relating to State Public Services. As per available information the following States have implemented ten per cent reservation to EWS:- (1) Uttrakhand  (2) Gujarat  (3) Karnataka  (4) Jharkhand  (5) Maharashtra (6) Mizoram (7) Delhi (8) J&K (9) Goa (10)Assam (11) Telengana and (12)AndhraPradesh. (c) to (e): A total of 2,14,766 additional seats were approved to be created to in the central educational institutions; and an expenditure of Rs. 4,315.15 Cr was approved to be incurred to improve the infrastructure in the higher educational institutions as per the details given below:  	Number	Intake 2018-19	Total additional seats (number)	Total financial requirements(in Rs Cr)			Total Additional seats	2019-20	2020-21	Scholarships and maintenance	Salaries for additional teachers	Infrastructure creation	TotalIIT	23	26831	6708	2388	4320	237	212	552	1000.97NITs	32	26519	6631	4480	2151	7	0	663	670.25IIMs	20	4794	1363	620	743	20.45	54.52	272.6	347.565IISERs	8	2928	705	333	372	165.38	176.6	58.9	400.88Central Univs	40	209027	47223	20240	26983	230.30	248.95	957.48	1436.73IIITs	5	1787	446	213	233	1.58	4.06	29.50	35.14IIITs (PPP)	20	2361	593	270	323	7.44	6.47	0.00	13.91SPAs/CFTIs	10	4388	1097	439	658.2	183.00	0.00	71.70	254.70IGNOU		636940	150000	90000	60000	65.00	15.00	75.00	155.00 	158	915575	214766	118983	95783	917.14	717.83	2680.18	4315.15 All vacant seats are being filled by the Universities as per the prescribed procedure.*****
