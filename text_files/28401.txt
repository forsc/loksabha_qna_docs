                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

59






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2021






Enrolment in Schools




Pratima Mondal














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of school enrolment during the last three years, State-wise in rural and urban areas; and(b) the steps taken by the Government to carry on proper education system in rural areas during the pandemic?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        The MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a): The Department of School Education and Literacy (DoSEL), Ministry of Education has developed the Unified District Information System for Education Plus (UDISE+) system to record data on indicators of school education provided by all the States and Union Territories. As per Unified District Information System for Education Plus (UDISE+), the school enrolment for rural and urban areas in last three years, State- wise are at Annexure.(b): To provide uninterrupted schooling, Government has initiated various steps including online classes at the schools by employing various digital tools in the form of DIKSHA portal (https://diksha.gov.in/), SWAYAM Portal (swayam.gov.in), e-Pathshala (https://epathshala.nic.in/), NROER (National Repository of Open Educational Resources) portal (https://nroer.gov.in/welcome). Further, learning programmes have also been started in the form of offline learning tools in the form of SWAYAM Prabha, DD Channel, radio, community radio and CBSE podcasts, toll free numbers, missed call, SMS based requests for audio content, localized radio content for edutainment etc. Other steps taken by all the states are shown in this report - India Report Digital Education June 2020https://www.education.gov.in/sites/upload_files/mhrd/files/India_Report_Digital_Education_0.pdf******
