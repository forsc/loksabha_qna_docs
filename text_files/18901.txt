                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

437






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Re-routing of Trains




Pallab Lochan Das














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has decided to re-route train number 20505/06-New Delhi-Dibrugarh-New Delhi bi-weekly Rajdhani express via the Bogibeel bridge and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether Biswanath Chariali Railway Station at the headquarter of Biswanath district, has been not included for the halt/stoppage of the new Rajdhani Express; and (c) if so the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS, COMMERCE & INDUSTRY AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION   (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (c) To prevent the spread of COVID-19 pandemic, Indian Railways has discontinued all regular passengers carrying trains including 20505/20506 New Delhi-Dibrugarh Rajdhani Express w.e.f. 23rd  March, 2020. Presently, only special trains, with limited stoppages, keeping in view the concerns and suggestions of State Governments are being operated. Accordingly, 20505/20506 New Delhi-Dibrugarh Rajdhani Special Express is presently being operated with limited stoppages and this train is not having stoppage at Vishwanath Charali station. However, decision has been taken to re-route 20505/20506 New Delhi-Dibrugarh Rajdhani Express via Bogibeel Bridge. *****
