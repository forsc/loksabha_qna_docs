                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2267






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Universities Functioning in Rented Buildings




Farooq Abdullah














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the work on Central University Ganderbal has not been started ondesignated/identified site till date;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the University is functioning from rented buildings and if so, the details thereof;and(d) the time by which the works on identified site is likely to start and how many landdonors/locals have been engaged till date?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) & (b): The work on the land at Ganderbal has been started for construction of permanentCampus of Central University of Kashmir. The work of site office, single storeyed lightweight structure (Academic Block) and Pre Engineering Building (PEB) –I (Academic Block)have been completed. The construction work of PEB Block-II, Mini-Academic Block, 04Blocks of 02 class rooms each and Chain link fencing including gate around PEB Block I & IIhave been started.(c): Three Departments are already functioning from the main Campus of the University atGanderbal. The four buildings, namely, (i) Nowgam Campus-I, Srinagar, (ii) Boys Hostel(Namroz Commercial Complex), Ganderbal (iii) Boys Hostel Rajbagh, Srinagar, and (iv)Girls Hostel Gulshan Nagar Nowgam, Srinagar are rented buildings at Srinagar andGanderbal.(d): The land for establishment of the Central Universities is provided free of cost by therespective State Governments. The Central University of Kashmir is a statutory autonomousbody created under the Act of Parliament and recruitment for all the posts is made as per theprovisions of Act and Statutes of the University and UGC Regulations.******
