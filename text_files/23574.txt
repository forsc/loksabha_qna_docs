                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4655






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2021






Gold and Diamonds Mines/Reserves




Devusinh Jesingbhai Chauhan














                        Will the Minister of





MINES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of mines/reserves of gold and diamonds in the country as on date, State-wise;(b)	the quantity and price of gold and diamonds explored during the last three years and the current year, State/location-wise;(c)	whether the Government has taken note of the fact that some foreign companies engaged in the mining of gold and diamonds are not complying with the guidelines in this regard; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and the number of complaints received in this regard during the said period and the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MINES, COAL AND PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a): The data for state-wise reserves for gold and diamond as per the National Mineral Inventory (NMI) compiled by the Indian Bureau of Mines is at Annexure I. As per the information received by IBM, the state-wise details of mines of gold and diamond during 2019-20 are at Annexure II.(b): As per the information received from Geological Survey of India (GSI), an attached office of Ministry of Mines, a total of 2.734 Million Tonnes resources of gold have been identified by GSI in last three years in Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Karnataka, Jharkhand and Rajasthan. No resource of diamond could be reported from the exploration projects taken up by GSI in the last three years. The details of the exploration projects of present field season (2020-21) are at Annexure III.(c) & (d): The respective State Governments grant mineral concessions under the provisions of the Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) (MMDR) Act, 1957.
