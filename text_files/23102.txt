                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4148






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Programme for International Student Assessment




Vishnu Dayal Ram














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether India participates in the Programme for International Student Assessment and the Trends in International Mathematics and Science Study; and (b) whether there is an alternative mechanism for comparing India’s academic standing in comparison to other countries; (c) whether the Government proposes on collecting data regarding the gender gaps existing in achievements within the fields of Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM)?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) The Government of India has decided to participate in the Programme for International Students Assessment (PISA) 2022 to be conducted by the Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development (OECD). PISA is a triennial international survey that aims to evaluate education systems worldwide by testing the skills and knowledge of 15 year old students in Mathematics, Science and Reading.The schools in Union Territory of Chandigarh, as well as the Kendriya Vidyalaya (KVs) and Navodaya Vidyalaya (NVs) school systems would participate in the  PISA 2022.(b)  No, Sir.(c)  Department of Science and Technology is supporting projects on data collection in Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM). Recently, DST has published a report “Research & Development Statistics at a Glance-2019-20” which also includes gender gaps existing in achievements within the fields of STEM. ****
