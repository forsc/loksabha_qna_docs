                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

872






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Compensatory Afforestation




Adala Prabhakara Reddy













                                                     Chinta Anuradha
                                                








                                                    Beesetti  Venkata Satyavathi
                                                








                                                    Kotagiri Sridhar
                                                








                                                    Kuruva Gorantla Madhav
                                                








                                                    Pocha Brahmananda Reddy
                                                








                                                    Krishna Pal Singh Yadav
                                                








                                                    T.R.V.S. Ramesh
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has compiled list of locations where compensatory afforestation has been carried out for various projects in the country including Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu and Madhya Pradesh since 2014;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise; and(c)	the details of the CAMPA funds sanctioned to various States during each of the last three years including the current year, State/UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE(SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) The Central Government has launched a portal namely PARIVESH (“Pro Active and Responsive facilitation by Interactive and Virtuous Environmental Single window Hub”) for the purpose of makingproposals for non-forest use of forest land under Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980 (FCA, 1980). Compensatory Afforestation (CA) is one of the most important conditionsstipulated by the Central Government while approving these proposals. There is a provisions to upload the locations of the CA areas in the form of KML files, Survey of India Toposheet and DGPS maps, etc. in this portal for all States/UTs including Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu and Madhya Pradesh since 2014. (b) The State/UT wise details of area taken up under CA since 2014 is annexed as Annexure-I(c) 	The details of the CAMPA funds sanctioned to various States during each of the last three years including the current year is annexed at Annexure-II.***
