                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4574






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.03.2022






Amended Technology Upgradation Fund Scheme




Girish Chandra














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of capital investment subsidies issued under Amended Technology Upgradation Fund Scheme (ATUFS) between the years 2016 till 2021; (b) the total number of capital investment subsidies issued to Uttar Pradesh since 2016 till date; (c) the total number of energy-saving machines incentivised since the year 2016 till date; (d) the total subsidy released to attract investment in weaving, processing and garment sectors from the year 2016 till date, year-wise; and (e) the total amount of funds released under this scheme to projects in Uttar Pradesh from 2016 till date, year-wise?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a):	Total Capital Investment subsidy of Rs. 616.37 crore has been released in 2,487 cases under Amended Technology Upgradation Fund Scheme (ATUFS) since 2016 till 2021. In addition, a total of Rs. 6,799.14 crore has been disbursed as Interest Reimbursement/Margin Money Subsidy under the committed liabilities of old versions of TUFS, making the total release under ATUFS during            2016 - 2021 as Rs. 7,415.51 crore.(b):	Total Capital Investment subsidy of Rs. 26.76 crore has been released in 56 cases under Amended Technology Upgradation Fund Scheme (ATUFS) in the State of Uttar Pradesh since inception of the scheme in 2016. In addition, a total of Rs. 92.76 crore has been disbursed as Interest Reimbursement/Margin Money Subsidy under the committed liabilities of old versions of TUFS to the units in Uttar Pradesh, making the total release under ATUFS since 2016 as Rs. 119.52 crore.(c):	Total number of 525 energy saving machines have been incentivised under ATUFS since 2016. (d):	Segment wise and year wise details of subsidy released under ATUFS including committed liabilities since 2015-16 till 2021-22 is given in Annexure I.(e):	Year wise details of subsidy released to the units in the State of Uttar Pradesh since 2016 is given in Annexure II.
