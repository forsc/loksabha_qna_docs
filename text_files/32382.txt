                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3814






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






FDI in Broadcasting Service




Midhun Reddy













                                                    Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details along with the amount of Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) in Direct-to-Home (DTH) broadcasting services; and(b) the details of the FDI inflows to various services providers?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF INFORMATION AND BROADCASTING; AND MINISTER OF YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR) (a) and (b) : As per extant Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) policy, 100% FDI under automatic route is allowed in DTH Sector under Broadcasting Carriage Service sector. However, infusion of fresh foreign investment, beyond 49% in a company not seeking license/permission from sectoral Ministry, resulting in change in the ownership pattern or transfer of stake by existing investor to new foreign investor, will require Government approval. The details of the FDI inflows to various service providers are as under: -1.	M/s. Dish TV India Ltd. 		- 	Rs. 21,70,00,00,000/-(including Videocon)	2.	M/s Tata Sky Ltd.			-	Rs. 18,69,89,76,295/-3.	M/s Sun Direct TV Pvt. Ltd.	-	        Rs. 8,41,55,81,085/-4.	M/s Bharati Telemedia Ltd.	-	        Rs. 21,65,06,00,000/-Total					-	    Rs. 70,46,51,57,380/-
