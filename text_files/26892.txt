                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2733






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Coal Import




Francisco Cosme Sardinha














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    the quantity of coal that was imported at Marmagoa for the last one year and the quantum out of the above is lifted by Rail and road to other parts of India?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF  PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) Coal Cargo handled atMarmagoa(Mormugao) Port during the year 2020-21 was 9.296 Million Tonnes(MT). Detail of dispatch by Rail and Road during the year 2020-21 is given below:-   SL No.	Commodity	Rail	Road	Total1	Coal	8.749 MT	0.280  MT	9.029 MT*  *Difference in balance quantity from cargo handled is lying at the port. *****
