                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3294






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Employment at Panchayat Level




Riti Pathak














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has any scheme to provide employment opportunities at panchayat level; and(b) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)&(b):	 The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a demand driven wage employment focused programme which provide for the enhancement of livelihood security of the households in rural areas of the country by providing at least one hundred days of guaranteed wage employment in every financial year to every household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work. Gram Panchayat is the implementing agency under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme at Panchayat level and at least 50 percent of the works are to be implemented by Gram Panchayats. States/UTs-wise details of personday generated under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS during the last year and the current FY 2020-21 (as on 11.03.2021) is given at Annexure.****** Annexure referred in reply to parts (a)&(b) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.3294 dated 16.03.2021
