                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2181






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.03.2021






Target to set up EMRS




Kripanath Mallah













                                                    Rajiv Ranjan (Lalan) Singh
                                                








                                                    Chirag Paswan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has planned to establish more Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRSs) and if so, the details thereof including Assam along with the funds allocated and target set for the purpose;(b) the number of EMRS opened so far throughout the country and the location of these schools;(c) the State/year-wise funds allocated/expenses incurred on each EMRS during the last three years and the current year; and(d) the number of EMRS opened in Bihar along with the details of any further plan to open such school in Bihar?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)& b) As per budget announcement 2018-19, Ministry of Tribal Affairs is to establish 740 Eklavya Model Residential Schools (EMRSs) across the country including the State of Assam upto the year 2022. Out of these, 588 EMRSs have already been sanctioned till date. State/UT-wise list of these 588 sanctioned EMRSs is annexed at Annexure-I. Block and Village wise details of all these schools is available at Dashboard: www.dashboard.tribal.gov.in. Remaining 152 EMRSs are scheduled to be sanctioned during the year 2021-22.  As per the extant Guidelines of EMRS, existing capital cost for construction of EMRS in plain areas is Rs. 20.00 Crore with 20% increase in hilly and LWE affected areas. Besides, recurring grant at the rate of Rs.1.09 lakh per student per annum is also provided for running of EMRSs.(c)	State/UT and year-wise fund released for EMRSs during the last three years and current year is at Annexure-II.(d)	Three EMRSs have already been sanctioned in the districts of West Champaran, Jamui and Kaimur (Bhabua) and no further EMRS is scheduled to be sanctioned upto the year 2022 for the State of Bihar as per existing criteria of the scheme.
