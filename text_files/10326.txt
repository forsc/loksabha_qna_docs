                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3708






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Stoppage at Chirayinkil




Adoor Prakash














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of passenger earnings of railway stations under Thiruvananthapuram division in the last three years, station-wise;(b)	whether requests have been received for allowing stoppage for more trains at Chirayinkil and Kadakkavur railway stations under Thiruvananthapuram division;(c)	if so, the details thereof and the decision taken; (d)	whether the Government will consider allowing stoppage for Parasuram Express (16649/16650) and Maveli Express (16603/16604) at Chirayinkil and Kadakkavur railway stations; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	to (e) A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (e) OF UNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 3708 BY ADV. ADOOR PRAKASH TO BE ANSWERED IN LOK SABHA ON 11.12.2019 REGARDING STOPPAGE AT CHIRAYINKIL(a) The details of passenger earnings of railway stations under Thiruvananthapuram division in the last three years, station-wise is appended. (b) to (e) Yes, Sir. Requests/representations, including that from the Hon’ble Member of Parliament, have been received for stoppage of 16603/16604 (Mangalore Central-Thiruvananthapuram Maveli Express), 16649/16650 (Mangalore Central-Nagercoil Parasuram Express) and certain other trains at Chirayinkeezh and Kadakavur Railway Stations. These have been examined but not found commercially justified, at present. However, provision of stoppages is an ongoing process over Indian Railways.  *****
