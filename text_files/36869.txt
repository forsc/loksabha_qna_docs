                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3965






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.03.2022






Rashtriya Pravai Chatravriti Yojana




Chandra Prakash Joshi














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of beneficiaries under the RashtriyaPravasiChatravriti Yojana during the last three years;(b) the details thereof, State-wise; (c) the number of beneficiaries got enrolled in some foreign educational institution in the last three years and the details thereof, year-wise;(d) the amount sanctioned, allocated and utilized under the said scheme during the last five years and the details thereof, year-wise; and (e) whether the Government is considering to increase the number of scholarships in near future?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) & (b):  Ministry of Tribal Affairs is implementing the scheme of “National Overseas Scholarship Scheme" (Hindi transliteration RashtriyaPravasiChatravriti Yojana) for studies aboard for ST students, wherein 20 students are awarded scholarship every year.  The State-wise detail of students provisionally awarded scholarship under this scheme for last three year is enclosed as Annexure. 	 (c):  After selection, time of 2 years is given for getting him/her enrolled in a foreign University.   The number of NOS beneficiaries so far enrolled in foreign educational institution in the last three years is as below:-  Year	No of students selected 	No. of ST students joined universities abroad2018-19	20	162019-20	20	192020-21	20	2* Due to covid-19 pandemic and travel restrictions during the year 2020-21 less number of candidates have so far joined the universities abroad. Many a times, a student does not join even though selected for various reasons like not getting the admission in the desired University or if gets a good job in India or for some personal reason.    (d):      The year-wise details of budget allocated and utilization under the scheme of “National Overseas Scholarship for studies abroad” during the last five years is as below:- Rs. in Lakhs2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21BE	RE	Utilization	BE	RE	Utilization	BE	RE	Utilization	BE	RE	Utilization	B.E.	RE	Utilization100.00	39.00	39.00	100.00	100.00	100.00	200.00	200.00	200.00	200.00	200.00	200.00	200.00	476.00	476.00 (e):     Currently there is no proposal to increase the number of scholarships.
