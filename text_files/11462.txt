                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

333






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






E-PoS Machines




Ritesh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of the Electronic Point of Sale (E-PoS) machines installed by the Ministry in Uttar Pradesh and other States; (b) 	whether the Government has any information about the functioning and usage of these machines; (c) 	if so, the details thereof; (d) 	if not, th(a) 	the details of the Electronic Point of Sale (E-PoS) machines installed by the Ministry in Uttar Pradesh and other States; (b) 	whether the Government has any information about the functioning and usage of these machines; (c) 	if so, the details thereof; (d) 	if not, the reasons therefor; (e) 	whether there are any alternative measures in place in case the machines are not working properly to distribute the PDS benefits timely; and (f) 	if so, the details thereof?e reasons therefor; (e) 	whether there are any alternative measures in place in case the machines are not working properly to distribute the PDS benefits timely; and (f) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) to (f): The Department in association with all States/UTs is implementing a scheme on ‘End-to-End Computerization of Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS) Operations’ including automation of Fair Price Shops (FPSs) to bring transparency and efficiency in the allocation and distribution of highly subsidised foodgrains to beneficiaries covered under National Food Security Act, 2013 (NFSA). So far, nearly 88% FPSs in the country have been automated by installing ePoS devices at FPSs. A statement showing status of Uttar Pradesh including other States/UTs is annexed...........2/--2- 		For monitoring the ePoS operations and distribution of foodgrains in States/UTs, the Department has developed central Annavitran portal (https://annavitran.nic.in). Details on this portal are available in public domain.	Further, this Department vide letters dated 24/10/2017 and 08/11/2018 has advised all States/UTs that genuine beneficiaries/households shall not be denied from their entitled foodgrains under NFSA only on the ground of not possessing an Aadhaar number, or due to any technical failure of ePoS device including failure of biometric/Aadhaar authentication of beneficiary owing to network/connectivity/linking issues.**** AnnexureANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (f) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.333 DUE FOR ANSWER ON 04.02.2020 IN THE LOK SABHA.
