                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

316






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






Creation of Employment in Tourism Sector




Ramcharan Bohra














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the employment opportunities have been created in the tourism sector with international cooperation; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; and (c) 	the estimated number of employment opportunities that are likely to be created with international cooperation?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (b):	As part of International co-operation in tourism, the Ministry of Tourism engages in various consultations and negotiations with other countries for signing of Agreements/Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for bilateral/multilateral cooperation in the field of tourism.Employment generation in tourism sector is function of various factors including creation of a conducive environment and opportunities for movement of people across countries which are facilitated through international cooperation.(c):	The Ministry of Tourism has not commissioned any study to assess the number of employment opportunities likely to be created with international cooperation.*******
