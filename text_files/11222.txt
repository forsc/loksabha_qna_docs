                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

152






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Safety and Liberty of the Students




Kani K. Navas














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is aware about recent events of violence in variousuniversities of the country and proposed to obtain a comprehensive report regardingthe causes and impact of these events;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) the measures proposed to be taken by the Government for the safety and libertyof the students; and(d) if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (d) There have been reports of some violence in some universities in thecountry. Ministry of Human Resource Development was apprised of the factualposition by the concerned Central Universities. All the Central Universities arecompetent by their statutes to ensure proper safety and unhindered progress of thestudents.*****
