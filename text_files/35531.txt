                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2889






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Nagar Van Scheme




Chandra Prakash Joshi













                                                    Sangamlal Kadedin Gupta
                                                








                                                    Ram Kripal Yadav
                                                








                                                     Pratap Chandra Sarangi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of Nagar Van to be developed in the country, State-wise details thereof particularly in Odisha and Bihar; (b) 	the details of the areas identified for the same in Chittorgarh (Rajasthan), Balasore (Odisha), Patliputra (Bihar) and Pratapgarh (UP)?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) The Pilot Scheme of Nagar Van Yojana (NVY) envisages developing 400 Nagar Vans and 200 Nagar Vatikasin the country during the period of 2020-21 to 2024-25 with an objective to significantly enhance the tree outside forests and green cover, enhancement of biodiversity and ecological benefits to the urban and peri-urban areas apart from improving quality of life of city dwellers. So far, a total of 65 projects have been sanctioned in 22 States including Odisha and Bihar. The State-wise details of Nagar Van projects sanctioned under Nagar Van scheme are given in annexure.  (b) The concerned States have informed that no area has been identified for developing Nagar Van in Chittorgarh (Rajasthan), Balasore (Odisha), Patliputra (Bihar) and Pratapgarh (Uttar Pradesh)so far. *****
