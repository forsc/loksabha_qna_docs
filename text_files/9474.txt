                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2939






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Onboard Purchase




Pratima Bhoumik














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether several airlines are providing onboard service of selling food and beverages through credit/debit card and if so the details thereof;(b) whether airlines are accepting only VISA credit/debit cards for onboard purchase and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether airlines who are selling food/beverages or products onboard are not accepting RuPay Card and if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor; and(d) whether the Government has any plan to take any action against those airlines who are not accepting RuPay Card onboard and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (d): Selling of food and beverages onboard is commercial activity of the airlines. Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA), primarily being a safety regulatory body, does not regulate the commercial activities of the airlines.
