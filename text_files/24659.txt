                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

532






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Promotion of Khadi and Village Industires




Jual Oram














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the steps have been taken by the Government to promote Khadi and Village Industries in different States; (b) 	if so, the details of centrally sponsored schemes implemented in States; (c) 	the funds earmarked for the purpose; and (d) 	the extent of employment generated by such schemes in the Khadi and Village Industries?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NARAYAN RANE)(a):	Yes Sir.  Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) is playing a pivotal role to promote Khadi and Village Industries in the different States through the implementation of Khadi Vikas Yojana (KVY), Gramodyog Vikas Yojana (GVY), Prime Minister’s Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP), and Scheme of Fund for Regeneration of Traditional Industries (SFURTI).  	KVY provides support to the Khadi sector through market development assistance, interest subsidy on working capital, creation of physical infrastructure, modernization of sales outlets and training.  GVY focuses on development of Village based industries.  PMEGP is a credit linked subsidy scheme, for setting up of new micro-enterprises and to generate employment opportunities in rural as well as urban areas of the country.  Under SFURTI, traditional Khadi industries and artisans are organized into clusters, to make them more productive and competitive through setting up of Common Facilities Centre, procurement of new machinery, training, etc.   	The scheme-wise funds earmarked for the last three years and current financial year and the cumulative employment generated under the schemes are placed at Annexure.(b):	Ministry of MSME does not implement any Centrally Sponsored Schemes in the States.  (c) & (d):     Does not arise.
