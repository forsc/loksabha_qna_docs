                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2518






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Conservation and Maintenance of Monuments in Jaunpur




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware about the historical significance of Jaunpur district ; (b)	if so, whether the Government plans to undertake conservation and maintenance activities of the historical monuments in Jaunpur district; (c)	the amount of funds the Government plans to allocate for this purpose and the department through which the work is going to be carried out; and (d)	whether the Government plans to build a museum to showcase the culture and history of Jaunpur district; and (e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	Yes, Sir. There are sixteen centrally protected monuments in Jaunpur district. (b) & (c)	Regular conservation and maintenance works are undertaken as and when required by the Sarnath Circle of Archaeological Survey of India (ASI), and expenditure incurred for the same during last three years and current year are as under (in Rs): 2018-19	        2019-20	        2020-21	        2021-2213,28,970	46,26,415	94,57,263	63,25,788 (d) & (e)	At present, there is no such proposal with ASI.
