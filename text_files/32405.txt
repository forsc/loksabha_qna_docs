                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3837






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Recruitment Racket in CAPFs




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether recruitment racket was being operated in CRPF-CISF which has been exposed;(b) if so, the number of such cases came to light during the last four years;(c) whether the officers of the department have also been found involved in such a recruitment racket; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (d) :  While sporadic complaints in recruitment process are received, no information has come to the notice regarding recruitment racket operated in CRPF-CISF.*****
