                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4739






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2019






Pradhan Mantri Vidya Lakshmi Karyakram




Karti P. Chidambaram














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Pradhan Mantri Vidya Lakshmi Karyakram is launched to provide students asingle window electronic platform for scholarships and educational loans in the country; and(b) if so, the details of fund allocated/utilized for implementing the scheme, State/ UT-wiseincluding Tamil Nadu?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): Yes, Sir. Under the Pradhan Mantri Vidya Lakshmi Karyakram, Vidya Lakshmi Portal(VLP) was launched by the Government on August 15, 2015 to ensure that students canavail loans easily through single window system of banks for education loans. Students canview, apply and track the education loan applications online anytime, anywhere byaccessing the portal. All Public Sector Banks (PSBs) have been on-boarded on the Portal.(b): No fund has been allocated for implementing the Vidya Lakshmi Portal (VLP).*****
