                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2480






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2021






Western Ghats




M. K. Raghavan














                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the  Government  is  considering  to implement any scientific report on Western Ghats; if so, the details thereof; and(b) 	whether the Government has given approval for proposed Tunnelling of Western Ghats from Kodenchery to  Kalladi  through Western  Ghats;  if  so,  the  details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a)	Draft notification for declaration of Western Ghats Eco-sensitive Area has been issued by the Central Government vide S.O. 5135(E), dated 3rd October, 2018 on the basis of recommendations by the High Level Working Group (HLWG) under the Chairmanship of Dr. K. Kasturirangan. The recommendation of the HLWG involves six states viz. Goa, Gujarat, Karnataka, Kerala, Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu, covering an area of 59,940.7 sq.km. The draft notification has been issued for an area of 56,825.70 sq.km keeping in view the representation of Kerala. The validity of the draft notification is upto31st December, 2021.(b)	A proposal for "Construction of Twin Tunnel (2+2 Lane) with Four Lane approach (from existing roads) including approaches (from existing roads) for providing direct connectivity between Aanakampoyil¬ Kalladi-Meppadi in Kozhikode and Wayanad Districts of Kerala State" has been received in the Ministry on 16.11.2021 for grant of Terms of Reference in accordance with the provisions of the EIA Notification, 2006. ****
