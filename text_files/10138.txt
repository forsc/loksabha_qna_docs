                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3609






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2019






Widows and Senior Citizen Pensioners




Anurag Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether widows and senior citizen pensioners in the country have to face several difficulties due to discrepancies in the pension since the last one year and non-availability of a proper forum for timely redressal of their problem;(b)	if so, whether easy and simplified measures are being taken to resolve these discrepancies; and(c)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	to (c):	No instance has come to the notice of Government, where widows and senior citizen pensioners in the country have to face several difficulties due to discrepancies in the pension.******
