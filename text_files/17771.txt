                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1402






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Employment in Rural Areas




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Ministry has any scheme to provide jobs at panchayat level;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	the number of persons provided jobs under the Poor Welfare Employment campaign so far during the COVID-19 pandemic; and(d) 	the length (in Kilometres) of roads constructed so far under the Pradhan Mantri GramSadak Yojana last year along with the details thereof, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a)&(b):       The Ministry is implementing the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act,  2005 (Mahatma Gandhi NREGA), through States/UTs which assure upto 100 days of wage employment in a financial year to every rural household whose adult member volunteers to do unskilled manual work. Gram panchayat is the implementing agency under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme at panchayat level. Under the scheme ,during the current  financial year 203 crore persondays have been generated from April 2020- 17 September 2020.(c):    there is no “Poor Welfare employment campaign” being run by the Ministry. However under Garib Kalayan Rojgar Abhayan (GKRA), launched on 20 June 2020, a total of about 28 Crore Mandays Employment has been generated so far under GKRA in 116 districts selected for the Abhiyan in 6 States. (d):    The State-wise details of length (in Kilometres) of roads constructed so far under the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana during last year 2019-20, are given at Annexure.   Annexure referred in reply to part (d) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.1402 dated 20.09.2020ANNEXURE
