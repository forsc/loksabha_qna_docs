                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4112






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2020






Vacant Posts in Subordinate Offices




Chhedi Paswan














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of posts lying vacant in subordinate offices under the Ministries of the Union Government; (b) 	whether the Government proposes to make recruitments against these posts and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor; and (c) 	whether any plan in this regard is lying pending for implementation for the last five years?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE(DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a):  Group wise details of sanctioned strength of employees and numbers in position in various Ministries/ Departments of Government of India, as on 01.03.2018, are at Annexure-I. No separate data related to vacant posts in subordinate offices is maintained.(b) & (c):  Government has taken the following measures to fill up the vacant posts in Central Government through direct recruitment as well as by promotion:-i)     A Model Calendar for holding meetings of Departmental Promotion Committee (DPC) is in place since 1998, as amended from time to time,  for timely convening of DPCs/ preparation of approved select panels for filling up vacant posts on promotion basis.ii)    All Ministries/Departments have been advised to take advance action for reporting vacancy position w.r.t. direct recruitment (DR) to the concerned recruitment agencies for filling up the same in a timely manner.iii)   Recently, in January 2020, all Ministries/Departments of the Central Government have been requested to take time bound action to fill the existing vacancies in respective Ministries/ Departments and their attached/ subordinate offices.*****
