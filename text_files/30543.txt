                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2134






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.12.2021






Shortage of DAP Fertilizer in Haryana




Manish Tewari














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS be pleased to state:(a)	whether the Government is aware of reports of looting and violence in the state of Haryana over the shortage of DAP fertilizer and if so, the details thereof and the action taken to address the situation;(b)	whether the Government has assessed the adverse impacts of delay in sowing of crops caused by the said shortage, if so, the details thereof;(c)	If not, whether the Government proposes to conduct a study/survey in this regard and if so, the details thereof;(d)      the amount of DAP and NPK produced domestically along with its percentage to that of then total demand;(e)     whether the Government has any plans for ramping-up domestic production capacity and reduce import dependence, if so, the details thereof; and(f)      the details of current stocks of DAP in the states of Punjab and Haryana along with the details of amount of DAP used in these states in the last 10 seasons?                                           






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OFHEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS (DR. MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)___________________________________________________________________(a):	Sir, as per the inputs from the State Government of Haryana, no such incidence has been reported. The DAP availability has remained comfortable during ongoing Rabi-2021-22 season in the State of Haryana. (b) &  (c) :   In view of point (a) above, question does not arise(d)	:   Sir, during the period 2020-21, the amount of DAP and NPK produced  domestically along with its percentage to that of then total demand is per the following:(Fig: In LMT)Product	All India Requirement	Domestic Production	% domestic production to that of total demandDAP	         107.76	                  37.74	                          35.02%NPKS	         108.00	                  93.21	                          86.30%-2-(e)::   The following steps/efforts are made by DoF for DAP :Long Term Agreement: 1. An Agreement of Intent has been signed between four Indian PSUs and Russian company for supply of DAP/NPK and its raw materials in 2022. 2. Various efforts by DoF to ensure regular and sufficient supply of DAP and its raw materials to India at reasonable prices from various countries which is an ongoing process. Efforts made for enhancement of domestic production of DAP / NPK fertilizers & Raw Materials 1.  DoF granted permission to Madhya Bharat Agro product Limited Unit-II, Banda Sagar, MP for production of  1,20,000 MT per annum of DAP. 2. On exploration of minerals for raw materials for DAP & other fertilizers in India, discussion is in place with Ministry of Mines, GSI, MECL & PDIL.(e)	:  The closing stock of DAP (as on 06.12.2021) in the states of Punjab and Haryana is as follows:Punjab-       0.97 LMTHaryana-    0.16 LMTFurther, the details of amount of DAP used in these states in the last 10 seasons is as per Annexure-A. *********** -3-ANNEXURE-AHARYANALAST TEN SEASON-WISE DAP CONSUMPTION FROM 2016-17 TO 2021-22(UPTO 06.12.2021) PRODUCT	CONSUMPTION	CONSUMPTION	TOTAL CONSUMPTION  	A	B	(A+B) 	KHARIF 2016	RABI 2016-17	 DAP	2.50	2.75	5.25 	KHARIF 2017	RABI 2017-18	 DAP	2.70	2.76	5.46 	KHARIF 2018	RABI 2018-19	 DAP	1.85	3.63	5.48 	KHARIF 2019	RABI 2019-20	 DAP	1.73	4.16	5.89 	KHARIF 2020	RABI 2020-21	 DAP	2.08	4.24	6.32 	KHARIF 2021	RABI 2021-22 (UPTO 06.12.2021)	 DAP	1.93	2.53	4.46PUNJABLAST TEN SEASON-WISE DAP CONSUMPTION FROM 2016-17 TO 2021-22(UPTO 06.12.2021) PRODUCT	CONSUMPTION	CONSUMPTION	TOTAL CONSUMPTION  	A	B	(A+B) 	KHARIF 2016	RABI 2016-17	 DAP	4.08	2.95	7.03 	KHARIF 2017	RABI 2017-18	 DAP	3.77	3.08	6.85 	KHARIF 2018	RABI 2018-19	 DAP	1.76	5.26	7.02 	KHARIF 2019	RABI 2019-20	 DAP	1.46	5.83	7.29 	KHARIF 2020	RABI 2020-21	 DAP	2.02	5.71	7.73 	KHARIF 2021	RABI 2021-22(UPTO 06.12.2021)	 DAP	2.19	3.17	5.36*******
