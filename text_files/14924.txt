16.03.2020






Expenditure Incurred on Archaeological Sites in Gujarat




Poonam (Mahajan) Vajendla Rao














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of archaeological sites in Gujarat along with the location-wise details thereof;(b)	the details of such sites in Gujarat where the Government has handed over the conservation and maintenance work to Archaeological Survey of India (ASI);(c)	the details of expenditure incurred on conservation of archaeological sites separately during the last three years;(d)	whether the Government is considering to hand over some more archaeological sites to Archaeological Survey of India for conservation and maintenance; and(e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	203 archaeological monuments and sites are declared as monuments or ancient site of National importance in Gujarat.  Location-wise details thereof are at Annexure-I. (b)	Archaeological Survey of India is responsible for conservation and maintenance of all such sites/monuments. (c)	The expenditure incurred on conservation, preservation, maintenance and development of  environs of centrally protected monuments/sites in Gujarat  during the last three years, is as under:        Rupees in crores2016-17	12.012017-18	09.692018-19	08.52(d)&(e)	Declaring any monument/site as a monument of national importance (centrally protected) is a continuous phenomenon.
