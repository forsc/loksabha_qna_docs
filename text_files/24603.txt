                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

685






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Recovery of Dues from Air India




Gangasandra Siddappa Basavaraj













                                                    Rajmohan Unnithan
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CIVIL AVIATIONbe pleased to state:-(a) whether the Government is aware of the law suits filed by Cairn Energy and Devas Multimedia seeking to recover their dues from the Indian Government through the seizure of Air India’s overseas assets;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the likely impact thereof on the ongoing disinvestment process of Air India;(d) whether the Government has formulated any stragegy to overcome the crisis; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) and (b): Yes, Sir. Cairn Energy and Devas Multimedia  have filed lawsuits in the United States District Court for Southern District of New York (SDNY)  seeking declaratory and money judgement against Air India Limited (AIL) as an alter ego of the Republic of India.;;(c) to (e): There is no likely impact of the lawsuits on the ongoing disinvestment process of Air India Limited. As AIL is a separate corporate entity with its own management and board. The case is being defended by AIL.  The company has appointed lawyers to defend its interests.
