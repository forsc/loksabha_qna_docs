                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3881






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Funds for Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) in Goa




Francisco Cosme Sardinha














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total funds sanctioned for various schemes for the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) in Goa; (b)	the amount spent for various schemes in Goa from the said sanctioned funds; and (c)	the amount spent for the schemes outside Goa under the jurisdiction of Goa ICAR?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????         (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	The total funds sanctioned for various schemes for the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR), in Goa during the Financial Year 2019-20 is Rs. 1806.44 Lakhs and scheme-wise details are at Annexure ‘A’.  (b)	Out of the total funds sanctioned during Financial Year 2019-20, till 11.03.2020, an expenditure of Rs.1572.58 lakhs has been made and scheme-wise details of expenditure are at Annexure ‘B’.(c)	During the current financial year 2019-20, up to 11.03.2020 the ICAR-CCARI, Goa has made expenditure of Rs. 4.33 lakhs outside Goa.*******
