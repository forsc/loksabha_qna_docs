                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2912






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Projects under CADWP




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of projects included under Command Area Development and Water Management Programme (CADWP) from Andhra Pradesh during the last three years, year-wise; (b) the status of progress/completion of each of the identified project; and (c) the details of funds released and utilized by the Government to Andhra Pradesh during 2017 to 2021?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)  Under the ongoing Command Area Development and Water Management (CADWM) Programme in operation since 2016-17, seven projects of Andhra Pradesh have been included as per details given in Annexure-I.(b)  In terms of development of targeted Culturable Command Area (CCA), ‘NIL’ progress has been reported by the State government in respect of all seven CADWM projects. Barring Gundlakamma project where CADWM works have commenced, other six projects are at different stages of preparatory phase.(c)  Details of Central Assistance released for CADWM projects of Andhra Pradesh and utilization of funds by the State Government for the period from 2017 to 2021 (up to June 2021) is given in    Annexure-II.***** ANNEXURE-IAnnexure referred to in reply to part (a) of Unstarred Question No. 2912 to be answered in Lok Sabha on 05.02.2021 regarding “PROJECTS UNDER CADWP”.
