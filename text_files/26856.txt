                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2618






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Startup India Seed Scheme




Manne Srinivas Reddy













                                                    Kotha Prabhakar  Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has unveiled a Startup India Seed Fund Scheme(SISFS) to handhold and financially support early-stage startups across 300 incubation centres and the fund is expected to benefit around 3,600 startups and boost the startups ecosystem, particularly in tier-2 and 3 cities;(b) 	if so, whether the fund aims to provide financial assistance to startups for proof of concept, prototype development, product trials to ensure adequate availability of funds, especially for startups with good ideas and this scheme supports domestic entrepreneurs and their business ideas who often cannot take off due to the absence of funding at early stage;(c) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise including AP and Telangana; and(d) 	the details of the progress made/implementation status thereof along with funds sanctioned/spent as on date and the response received, city-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) & (b): 	Yes sir. The Hon’ble Prime Minister of India has announced the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS) on 16th January 2021. The scheme has been notified vide notification of the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade on the 21st January, 2021. Copy of the notification is available at website of this department: https://dipp.gov.in/sites/default/files/notification-Startup-29January2021.pdf.(c) & (d): 	The scheme has been operationalized with effect from 1st April 2021 and an online portal has been created for incubators and startups to apply.The list of incubators whose applications are at different stages on the online portal are given at Annexure.Till now, the following incubators have been sanctioned grants under the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS) as follows:-<pre>S.No.	Incubator Registered Name	State	Sanctioned amount (Rs.
