                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2829






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Progress of Swachh Bharat Mission




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)the details of the progress of Swachh Bharat Mission; (b)the funds released to Odisha for the same since inception of the mission, year-wise; (c) the number of households, public and community toilets constructed in Angul and Dhenkanal districts; and(d) the total amount of funds allotted to the districts of Angul and Dhenkanal since inception of the mission along with utilisation thereof?






ANSWER




                                                                           THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE                 MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                      (SHRI KAUSHAL KISHORE)(a): The details of the progress of the Mission are at Annexure.(b): The year-wise details of the fund released to Odisha under Swachh Bharat Mission-Urban (SBM-U) since the inception of the Mission, are as under:-Year-wise funds released to Odisha under SBM-U (? in Crore)2014-15	2015-16	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	2021-22	Total1.43	24.61	79.03	0.00	 40.77	100.45	22.12	0.00	268.41 (c) : The details of number of household, public and community toilets constructed in Angul and Dhenkanal districts are as under : DistrictName 	No. of Toilets constructed under SBM-U	       Household Toilets	Public Toilets	Community ToiletsAngul	              657	              48	    36Dhenkanal	   3,290	             145	    80(d) : Central Share (CS) fund is released to the State Government, which thereafter transfer the fund to Urban Local Bodies (ULBs), and hence the fund received by Angul and Dhenkanal District under SBM-U is not available with the Government.                       *****
