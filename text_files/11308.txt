                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

401






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Incident at Jamia Millia Islamia University




Deepak (Dev) Adhikari













                                                    N.K. Premachandran
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of action taken by the Delhi Police against students conducting protest against the Citizenship Amendment Act (CAA);(b) the details of cases registered against the students of Jamia Millia Islamia University in connection with the protest against the CAA during the last two months and the offences alleged against them;(c) whether the Delhi Police personnel entered the Jamia Millia Islamia University campus without the permission of university authorities;(d) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(e) the details of the action taken against the police on account of attack against the students in the said University along with the number of students injured; and(f) the details of the cases registered against students in various universities in connection with the protest against CAA?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e):	As reported by Delhi Police, three cases have been registered by Delhi Police against the mob, who were found involved in unlawful activities  and  defacing  the  public  property  while  protesting  against -2-L.S.US.Q.NO.401 FOR 04.02.2020Citizenship Amendment Act (CAA) in the area  at and around Jamia Millia University.  15 persons have been arrested by Delhi Police.  62 Police personnel and 127 persons, including 36 students, were injured.  As reported by Delhi Police, they entered the University Campus chasing the violent congregation of students/ mob, to nab/ disperse the offenders, control the situation and to protect the Government property as well as saving the lives of innocent students residing in the Campus area.    (f):   Central data base of such cases is not maintained.*******
