                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1179






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Nutrient Based Subsidy




Mahua Moitra














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Potassium and Phosphatic Fertilizers have been converted to Nutrient Based Subsidy, if so the details thereof; and(b)	whether there is any roadmap by the Government to convert Urea Fertilizer Subsidy to Direct Benefit Transfer or Nutrient Based Subsidy and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HEALTH & FAMILY WELFARE AND CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS(SHRI MANSUKH MANDAVIYA)(a) : Yes, Sir. The Government has implemented Nutrient Based Subsidy Policy w.e.f. 1.4.2010 for Phosphatic and Potassic (P&K) Fertilizers.  Under the policy, a fixed amount of subsidy, decided on annual basis, is provided on subsidised P&K fertilizers depending on their nutrient content. Under this policy, MRP is fixed by fertilizer companies as per market dynamics at reasonable level which is monitored by the Government.(b) : Sir, Currently there is no such proposal to convert Urea Fertilizer Subsidy to Nutrient Based Subsidy.                                                ******
