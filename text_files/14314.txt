                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3047






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.03.2020






Construction of Over Bridges in Bihar




Rama Devi













                                                    Giridhari Yadav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the proposals for construction of over bridges on National Highways in Bihar pending with the Union Government along with the reasons therefor, location-wise;(b) the steps taken by the Government for speedy disposal of the said pending proposals;(c) the details of the sites where the construction of over bridges is going on in the State at present; and(d) the time by which construction work thereon is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) There is no proposal pending for construction of over bridges on National Highways in Bihar.(b) Does not arise. (c) & (d) Details are Annexed.
