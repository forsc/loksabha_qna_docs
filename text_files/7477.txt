                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

110






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.11.2019






Historical and Religious Fairs




Mukesh Rajput














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government provides financial assistance to promote historical and religious fairs organised in different parts of the country; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the Government proposes to promote Kalpvasis’ ‘Ramnagariya Mela’ organized on the banks of the river Ganga during the month of Magha in Farrukhabad; and (d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d)	A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.  STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PARTS (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 110 FOR 25TH NOVEMBER, 2019(a) & (b)	Ministry of Culture does not provide financial assistance to promote historical and religious fairs organized in different parts of the country.  However, to protect, promote and preserve various forms of folk art and culture throughout the country, the Government of India has set up seven Zonal Cultural Centres (ZCCs) with headquarters at Patiala, Nagpur, Udaipur, Prayagraj, Kolkata, Dimapur and Thanjavur. These ZCCs organize various cultural activities and programmes on regular basis all over India. (c) & (d)	Uttar Pradesh is the member State of North Central Zone Cultural Centre (NCZCC), Prayagraj and provides cultural support to various festivals and fairs including Ramnagariya Mela at Farrukhabad. NCZCC sponsors cultural troupes of folk artists on specific demand of the Ramnagariya Mela Authorities. In the past also, NCZCC has participated in this Mela during the year 2013-14, 2014-15 and 2016-17.
