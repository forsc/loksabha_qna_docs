                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2960






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Civilian Flight on bio fuel




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has operated or proposes to operate any civilian flight on bio-fuel;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the total number of such flights being operated on bio-fuel;(d) if not, whether the Government will consider to operate the flights on bio-fuel due to heavy rise in the fuel price and to reduce the cost of flight operation;(e) if so, the details thereof; and(f) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) to (c) The Government has not operated any civilian flight on bio-fuel till date. However, Spicejet, a private Indian carrier had conducted a demonstration flight with Bombardier Q400 aircraft in August 2018 using bio-fuel blended with Aviation Turbine Fuel (ATF) (in the ratio of 25:75) in one engine.;(d) to (f) At present, the price of bio-fuels is higher than that of ATF due to very limited production. Hence, at this stage, operations using bio-fuels does not result in reduced operation costs.;
