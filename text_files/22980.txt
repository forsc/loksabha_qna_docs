                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4058






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Samarth Scheme in Andhra Pradesh




Kuruva Gorantla Madhav














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of persons skilled by imparting training after the launch of Samarth Scheme across the country, State/UT-wise particularly in Andhra Pradesh, district-wise during the last two years; and (b) the details and quantum of budget allocated and utilized under this scheme in the State of Andhra Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a): 	Samarth- skill development and capacity building in textiles sector is a central sector scheme targeted for training skill development and employment generation through direct participation of Industry, State Government Agencies, Industry Associations. With that regard, Ministry of Textiles has partnered with 92 textile manufacturers, 13 State Government agencies, 10 Industry Associations, 4 Sectoral organizations under Ministry of Textiles and a target for training of 3,30,379 beneficiaries have been allocated for implementation.	As far as the State of Andhra Pradesh is concerned, there is no State Agency which has partnered with Ministry of Textiles for participation in Samarth Scheme. The targets allocated to all State Government Agencies and Industry is annexed.(b):	There is no state- wise allocation of funds under Samarth. The cost of training programme under the scheme is released directly to the Implementing Partners based on physical milestones of training.
