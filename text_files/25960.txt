                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1661






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.07.2021






Second International Airport at Jewar




P. Velusamy














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CIVIL AVIATIONbe pleased to state:-(a) whether the Government has finalized the land deal for second international airport at Jewar in Uttar Pradesh;(b) if so, the details of the bidder and the expected date of completion as per their bidding;(c) whether the main bidder has given sub-contract or any other authority to implement the project and if so, the details thereof;(d) whether any Special Purpose Vehicle is being engaged to carry out the project and if so, the details thereof;(e) the estimated cost and the number of passengers expected to utilise the new airport in its completion; and(f) whether it will have any impact on the existing international airport in Delhi and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) : The State Government of Uttar Pradesh (GoUP) has informed that the task of acquisition of 1334 hectare of land for Phase-I of the project has been completed and the possession of the land has been obtained.;;(b) to (e):  GoUP has awarded the project to M/s Zurich Airports International AG for development of the airport. M/s Zurich Airport International AG has formed a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) -Yamuna International Airport Private Limited (YIAPL). The Concession Agreement has been signed on 7th October, 2020 between YIAPL and Noida International Airport Limited (NIAL), a State Government Company. As per the master plan submitted by YIAPL, the forecast for initial year traffic is 4 Million Passengers Per Annum (MPPA) for the airport against the initial capacity of 12 MPPA for Phase I . The approx. project cost of Rs. 8914 Crore for Phase-I is inclusive of land acquisition cost with Probable Date of Completion (PDC) in 2023-24. However, timeline for completion of the airport projects depend upon many factors such as land acquisition, availability of mandatory clearances, financial closure, etc.;(f): The IGI Airport, Delhi is an operational airport. Government of India has granted ''in principle'' approval for setting up of a new greenfield international airport at Jewar, Noida as a second airport to serve the need of Delhi National Capital Region. IGI Airport, Delhi will continue to remain operational.;
