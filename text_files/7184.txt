                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

896






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.11.2019






Jal Jeevan Mission




DNV Senthilkumar. S.













                                                    Hemant Patil
                                                








                                                    Mohanbhai Kalyanji Kundariya
                                                








                                                    Vinayak Bhaurao Raut
                                                








                                                    Shrirang Appa Barne
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the salient features and objectives under Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) along with its present status of implementation; (b) whether the Government is planning to offer 43-55 litres of water per person per day to every rural household by 2024 and if so, the details thereof along with the targets fixed for the purpose and the steps taken by the Government to achieve the said targets; (c) the total funds sanctioned under the mission since its inception; (d) whether the Government has conducted any survey to provide drinking water through tap to every household especially in rural areas and if so, the details thereof; and (e) the other steps taken by the Government to bring piped water to each and every rural household?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (d)      Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) aims toprovide potable waterat the rate of 55 litre per capita per day (lpcd) through Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC) to every rural household by 2024. As reported by States/ UTs 14.57 Crore rural households are to be provided with FHTCs by 2024 for which an estimated outlay of 3.6 lakh Crore has been envisaged under JJM for a period of 5 years.A budgetary allocation of ? 10,000.66 Crore has been made for the year 2019-20 and first instalment has been released to eligible States/ UTs. (e) For rural water supply, Government of India supplements the efforts of States by providing financial and technical assistance to States/ UTs. After the launch of JJM, a conference of Minister in-charge of rural water supply of various States was held in New Delhi followed by five regional workshops to discuss various aspects of JJM and modalities of the implementation. In addition, regular review of the programme is also held with States/ UTs.***
