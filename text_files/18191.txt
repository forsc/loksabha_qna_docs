                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1872






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






International Airport at Ayodhya




Rebati Tripura













                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has any proposal to start international airport in Ayodhya and other religious places;(b) if so, the details of funds allocated and used therein, State-wise;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the details about the concerned officials, mechanism and nodal officials involved therein, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c): At present, the State Government of Uttar Pradesh has requested Airports Authority of India (AAI) to develop the Ayodhya Airport for operation of 180-200 seater aircraft in phase - I and for wide body aircraft in Phase II. For the purpose of acquiring the land for the Ayodhya airport, Govt. of Uttar Pradesh has earmarked Rs.525.92 Cr. Declaration of an airport as an International airport depends upon the traffic potential and the demand from airlines for operation of international flights besides provision of Ground Lighting Facilities, Instrument Landing System, adequate runway length, Immigration, Health and Animal & Plant Quarantine services. Kushinagar airport in Uttar Pradesh has been recently declared as International Airport to cater the requirement of Buddhist pilgrims.(d): The acquisition of land for development of Ayodhya airport is being carried out by the District Magistrate, Ayodhya and AAI is expected to construct, develop and operate the airport.
