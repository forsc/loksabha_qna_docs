                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

850






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Inclusion of Agriculture/Tourism based Industries under MSME Sector




Sunil Baburao Mendhe














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has any proposal to include agriculture and tourism based industries under the MSME sector and if so, the details thereof; (b)	whether the Government has any proposal to encourage the producers and exporters of agricultural products with better infrastructure and also for adopting new techniques being used in other countries; and  (c)	 if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES (SHRI  NITIN GADKARI)(a): 	As per existing guidelines, some of the activities under agriculture and tourism based enterprises are covered in the ambit of MSME sector. 	The National Industrial Classification (NIC) 2-digit activity code 01- crop, animal production, hunting and related activities are not included in MSME except for the following sub-classes of activities at 5-digit level which are given below:-<pre>NIC Code	Activity01462	Production of eggs 01463	Operation of poultry hatcheries 01492	Bee-keeping and production of honey and beeswax01493	 Raising of silk worms, production of silk worm cocoons 01612	Operation  of agricultural irrigation equipment 01620	Support activities for animal production 01631	Preparation of crops of primary market i.e. cleaning, trimming, grading disinfecting  01632	Cotton ginning, cleaning and bailing 01633	Preparation of tobacco leaves  01639	Other post-harvest crop activities, n.e.c 01640	 Seed processing for propagation </pre>Similarly, following activities are also not included under MSME:-<pre>NIC Code	Activity02	Forestry and logging03	Fishing and aquaculture</pre>(b) & (c):  	Promotion of agricultural export is a continuous process. The Government has introduced a comprehensive Agriculture Export Policy 2018 to promote the agricultural export with the following vision:“Harness export potential of Indian agriculture, through suitable policy instruments, to make India a global power in agriculture, and raise farmers’ income.”  The Government has also brought out a new Central Sector Scheme -‘Transport and Marketing Assistance for Specified Agriculture Products’- for providing assistance for the international component of freight, to mitigate the freight disadvantage for the export of agriculture products, and marketing of agriculture products. The Department of Commerce also has several schemes to promote exports, including export of agricultural products, viz. Trade Infrastructure for Export Scheme (TIES), Market Access Initiatives (MAI) Scheme, Merchandise Exports from India Scheme (MEIS) etc. In addition, assistance to the exports of agriculture products is also available under the Export Promotion Schemes of Agriculture & Processed Food Products Export Development Authority (APEDA), Marine Products Export Development Authority (MPEDA), Tobacco Board, Tea Board, Coffee Board, Rubber Board and Spices Board.
