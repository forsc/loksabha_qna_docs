                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4268






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.12.2019






ICPD Declaration




Bhola Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether International Conference on Population and Development (ICPD)- 1994 declaration restrict the Indian Government to form a population control law/two child policy to stabilize the growing population of the country, if so, the details thereof; and(b) the section under which the population control law/two child policy has been restricted?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & ((b):	 India is a signatory to the International Conference on Population and Development declaration (ICPD) held in Cairo in 1994, which advocates honouring of reproductive rights of couples to decide freely and responsibly the number and spacing of their children. The Programme of Action (PoA) of the ICPD declaration recommends voluntary choice in family planning without any form of coercion. It lays down Actions for Governments in the field of Reproductive Rights and Reproductive Health.
