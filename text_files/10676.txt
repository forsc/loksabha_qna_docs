                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4130






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Inclusion of Games in List of Recognized Sports




 Dharambir Singh













                                                    Talari Rangaiah
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has formulated any proposal to include the game of sepak takraw in the list of recognized sports at national level;(b) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor;(c) whether Indian players have won bronze medal in sepak takraw in Asian Games and if so, the details thereof;(d) the details of the games included in the recognized list of sports at National/ International level and the norms for inclusion thereof;(e) whether the Government recognises the National Sports Federations in the country and if so, the details thereof along with the eligibility criteria fixed for the purpose; and(f) whether the Government proposes to promote Kickboxing in the country and if so, the details thereof?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS[SHRI KIREN RIJIJU](a) & (b): The Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports recognizes National Sports Federations (NSFs) for promotion and development of concerned sports disciplines. For promotion and development ofsport of Sepak Takraw at national level the Sepak Takraw Federation of India (STFI) has been recognized as NSF.(c): Yes Sir. Indian Men’s team won the Bronze medal in Sepak Takraw in the Asian Games-2018 held in Indonesia.(d) & (e): The government recognizes National Sports Federations at the national level for promotion and development of sports. The Government has laid down criteria for grant of recognition of NSFs in the National Sports Development Code of India (NSDCI), 2011. The list of recognized NSFs for the year is given in Annexure.(f) Promotion of specific sports discipline is the responsibility of the concerned NSFs. The role of the Central Government is to supplement their efforts. At present no NSF has been given recognition for Kickboxing sport by this Ministry.*****
