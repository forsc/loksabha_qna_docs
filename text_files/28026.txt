                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3624






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Demolition of Huts under PMAY-G in Odisha




Suresh Pujari














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the numbers of beneficiaries in Odisha in general and in the district of Bargarh and Jharsuguda in particular where work order was issued in favour of the rightfulbeneficiaries, who were asked to demolish their huts for construction of Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana – Grameen (PMAY-G) and after demolition told that their names have disappeared from the list;(b) 	the number of persons in Bargarh and Jharsuguda district in Odisha where houses under PMAY-G was allotted and amount paid to wrong persons against the name of rightful beneficiaries; and(c) 	the details of Identification of the erring officials along with the action taken against them if any for that gross negligence in duty?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a)& (b): No such complaints have been received in the Ministry of Rural Development.(c)	  Does not arise.*****
