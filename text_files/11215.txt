                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

130






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






NRC




Kapil Moreshwar Patil














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the students of various universities of the country including Jawahar Lal NehruUniversity and Jamia Millia Islamia University have recently carried out/are carrying out protestsagainst National Register of Citizens (NRC), National Population Register (NPR) and CitizenshipAmendment Act (CAA);(b) if so, whether the Government has taken any steps to explain to the students of the universitiesof the whole country in this regard;(c) if so, the details thereof; and(d) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to check the recurrence of such incidents in theuniversities?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ''Nishank'')(a) to (d) There have been reports of sporadic protests against NRC, NPA & CAA by a few studentsin some Universities including Jawaharlal Nehru University and Jamia Millia Islamia. The CentralUniversities are statutory autonomous organization established under an Act of Parliament and arecompetent to deal with such matters appropriately.*****
