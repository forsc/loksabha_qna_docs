                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1921






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.02.2021






Functions of Anganwadi Centres




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















1921.	SHRI BALASHOWRY VALLABHANENI: Will the Minister of WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT be pleased to state:(a)	details of services/functions of Anganwadi Centres in the country;(b)	whether as many as 25,000 Anganwadi Centres have become inoperational  in the country; (c)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(d)	whether there have been demands to increase the wages of Anganwadi workers and if so, the details thereof and the response of the Government thereto; and(e)	the number of new Anganwadi Centres announced under Atmanirbhar Bharat package, States/UT-wise?






ANSWER




ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)   (a)   The Anganwadi Services, a centrally sponsored scheme is implemented through the State Governments/UT Administrations. The Scheme aims at holistic development of children below 6 years of age and pregnant women & lactating mothers. It provides a package of six services comprising of (i) supplementary nutrition; (ii) pre-school non-formal education; (iii) nutrition and health education; (iv) immunization; (v) health check-up; and (vi) referral services. Three of the six services viz., immunization, health check-up and referral services are related to health and are provided by NRHM & Public Health Infrastructure. Inter-sectoral convergence is inbuilt and integral to the Anganwadi Services Scheme. (b) to (c)   The Scheme operates through a network of 7075 fully operational Projects and 13.87 lakh AWCs as on September, 2020.  Statement showing status of sanctioned/operational AWCs under Anganwadi Services is at Annexure. (d)    Yes, Sir. The Government has already enhanced the honorarium on 01.10.2018 to Anganwadi Workers (AWWs) from ? 3,000/- to ? 4,500/- per month; AWWs at miniAWCs from ? 2,250/- to ? 3,500/- per month; Anganwadi Helpers (AWHs) from ? 1,500/- to ? 2,250/- per month.
