                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4194






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Assessment of SBM




Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of work done in Rajasthan, Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh under Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM) during the last three years and the current year;(b) whether any proposal has been sent by the aforesaid States with regard to including any other area under SBM; and(c) if so, the details thereof along with the year-wise details of the amount released to the aforesaid States till date under SBM?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) 	Number of Individual household latrines (IHHLs) constructed in Rajasthan, Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh under Swachh Bharat Mission (Gramin) during the last three years and the current year are as under:-State	IHHLs constructed in 2016-17	IHHLs constructed in 2017-18	IHHLs constructed in 2018-19	IHHLs constructed in 2019-20(as on 15.7.2019)Rajasthan	27,91,196	21,29,328	1,45,082	17,427Maharashtra	19,16,460	22,52,876	2,26,710	2,01,645Uttar Pradesh	17,34,508	56,61,669	92,16,627	13,44,217(b) 	No, Sir.(c) 	Does not arise.
