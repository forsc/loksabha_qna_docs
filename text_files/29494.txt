                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1302






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.12.2021






Financial Support for Archaeological Excavations




 Dr. T. Sumathy (a) Thamizhachi Thangapandian














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has provided any financial support for conducting archaeological excavations and publishing of excavation reports in Tamil Nadu during the last three years and if so the details thereof; (b)	the various Steps taken by the Government to protect and preserve the archaeological sites in Tamil Nadu; and (c)	the total amount allocated for the same during the last five years, year-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER FOR CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a)	Government accords permissions to carry out archaeological excavations in states. Thirty permissions have been given to State departments of archaeology, universities, and research institutions in Tamil Nadu to conduct archaeological excavations during last three years. The report on excavations is published by the agency itself for which permission is given. During 2018-19 an expenditure of Rs. 69,253 was incurred by Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) for writing the excavation report of Adichanallur which was published in e-format. (b) & (c)	ASI through its two circle offices at Chennai and Trichy maintains all centrally protected monuments and sites of Tamil Nadu. Following is the expenditure incurred during last five years:Amount (Rs. in lakhs)2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21881.00	785.00	1037.00	1410.00	728.00*****
