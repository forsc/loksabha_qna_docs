                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

562






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Stoppage of Trains




Jaisiddeshwar Shivacharya Mahaswamiji














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether the Ministry of Railways is considering any proposal to provide a stoppage for Hussain Sagar Express, Bikaner Express, Secunderabad Hubli Express at Akalkot Road railway station under Solapur parliamentary constituency of Maharashtra; (b)  if so, whether a letter has been written to General Manager of Central Railway, Mumbai to provide such a stoppage to the said Trains at Akalkot Road railway station by the Divisional Railway Manager, Solapur; (c) if so, the details of decisions taken by the General Manager on this letter; and     (d) the time by which the said trains are likely to be provided a stoppage at Akalkot Road railway station?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY   (SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (d):  Request for stoppage of additional trains at Akalkot Road has been made, inter-alia, by the  Hon’ble  Member of Parliament. The same has been examined but not found operationally and commercially feasible, at present.  At present, 9 trains are stopping at Akalkot Road. These trains are adequately serving the needs of the passengers of this station.*****
