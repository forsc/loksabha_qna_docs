                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

81






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Survey to Benefits of Reservation




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a) 	whether the Government has conducted any survey to study the benefits of reservation; (b)	 if so, the details thereof;(c) 	if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) 	the details of the tribes who are availing more than 50 per cent benefits of the said reservation policy?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c):  Ministry of Tribal Affairs has not conducted any such surveys.(d):  Does not arise in view of above.*****
