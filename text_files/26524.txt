                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2194






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.08.2021






Development of Tourist Spots in Tribal  Areas of Gujarat




Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Union Government is developing tourist spots in tribal areas of Gujarat; (b) 	if so, the location-wise details thereof during the last three years; (c) 	if not, the reasons therefor; and (d) 	whether the Government has formulated any strategy/scheme to promote tourism by developing tourist spots in the tribal areas?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d):	Identification, Promotion and development of Tourism Destinations is primarily the responsibility of the State Government/ Union Territory Administration. The Ministry of  Tourism under  its  scheme  ‘Swadesh Darshan’ sanctions  thematic  projects pertaining  to  infrastructure  development. The projects are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/Union Territory Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable Detailed Project Reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier etc. ‘Tribal Circuit’ is one of the identified theme of Swadesh Darshan scheme. Under Tribal Circuit no project has been sanctioned in Gujarat. However, the Ministry of Tourism has sanctioned 03 projects for total amount of Rs. 179.68 Crore under Swadesh Darshan scheme and 03 Projects for total of Rs. 105.56 Crore under ‘National Mission on Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive (PRASHAD)’ in the state of Gujarat.  The scheme of Swadesh Darshan is presently under review. However, the details of projects sanctioned under the Tribal Circuit theme of Swadesh Darshan scheme is annexed.*******
