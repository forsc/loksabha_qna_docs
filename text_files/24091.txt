                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

22






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Faculty vacancies in all Centrally-funded IITs




Gaddam Ranjith Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether there are 170 faculty vacancies in all Centrally-funded Indian Institutes of Information Technology (IIITs) out of the total sanctioned strength of 410;(b) if so, the details of vacancies in each IIIT, IIIT-wise;(c) Since how long these vacancies are lying;(d) whether the Government justifies that it is not able to recruit faculty due to Corona pandemic;(e) the details of vacancies till January, 2019, IIIT-wise;(f) the steps taken by the Government to fill in the backlog and existing vacancies between January, 2019, and February, 2020?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) & (b): As on 13th July, 2021, 147 posts of faculties are lying vacant in Centrally Funded Indian Institutes of Information Technology (IIITs) out of total sanctioned strength of 410. Institute-wise details are as under:Sl. No.Name of the Institute No. of sanctioned posts No. of Vacant Posts1.IIIT Allahabad109282.ABV-IIITM Gwalior73373.PDPM-IIIT&M Jabalpur84214.IIITD&M Kancheepuram113505.IIITD&M Kurnool3111Total410147(c) to (f): Arising of vacancies and filling them up with suitable, qualified candidates is a continuous process. The IIITs have been taking measures to attract quality faculty, which include year-round open advertisements, invitation through search-cum-selection procedures to alumni/ scientists/ faculty, special recruitment drive, etc. A total of 204 faculty positions were lying vacant till January, 2019. However, 48 faculty posts were filled during the period after January, 2019 and till February,2020. Institute-wise details are as under:Sl. No.Name of the Institute Vacancy till Jan,2019 Filled between Jan,19 and Feb,201.IIIT Allahabad5152.ABV-IIITM Gwalior3403.PDPM-IIIT&M Jabalpur32214.IIITD&M Kancheepuram72105.IIITD&M Kurnool1512Total20448*****
