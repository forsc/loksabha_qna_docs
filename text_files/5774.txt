                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5417






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Construction of Bridge in Indore




Shankar Lalwani














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the  funds made available by the Union Government  to the Government  of Madhya Pradesh  for the proposed elevated bridge on old AB Road in Indore;(b)	the present status of work on the said bridge including the tender process thereof;(c)	whether fresh permission from the Union Government is required in case of any changes in the DPR of the said bridge; and (d)	if so, the details thereof ?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b)	Ministry has accorded administrative approval of the work of Construction of elevated corridor in Indore city from LIG square  to Navlekha square (Old NH-3) AB road (7000 mtr. length) at a cost Rs.350.00 Crore under Central Road Fund (CRF) on 08.03.2019. State Government of Madhya Pradesh has not yet accorded technical approval nor invited tender for the above said work.(c)	No Sir.(d)	Does not arise.*****
