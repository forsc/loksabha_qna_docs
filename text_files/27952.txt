                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3613






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Fish Landing Facility




 Vaithilingam Ve.














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Ministry of Fisheries is aware of Fish Landing facility at Moorthikuppam, Pudukuppam fishing villages, Puducherry is pending since 2009; and (b)	if so, the details thereof and the present status of the said project and the time by when it is likely to be implemented?






ANSWER




                                                                                                                                      ANSWERMINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING   	  (SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a)	 and (b): The construction of fish landing facilities at Moorthikuppam, Pudukuppam fishing village in Puducherry  is a  Puducherry  Government funded  project.   The Government of Puducherry  has reported that during  the year 2009, the work  for construction of fish landing facilities at Moorthikuppam, Pudukuppam fishing villages at a total cost of Rs. 10.99 crore  comprising of construction of piled jetty, training walls on the North and South side projecting into sea, stone revetments, retaining walls and dredging was assigned to the Puducherry Public Work Department (PWD), after obtaining clearance from the Ministry of Environment and Forest (MoEF), Government of India. It is also reported that out of the above said works, construction of piled jetty has been completed by Puducherry PWD and the remaining works were stopped and kept in abeyance as per the directions of Pondicherry Coastal Zone Management Authority. The Government of Puducherry further reported that they have now decided to resume the balance works and for this purpose, the UT Government is in process of formulation of necessary Techno-Economic Feasibility Report (TEFR) with the technical assistance of the Central Institute of Coastal Engineering for Fishery (CICEF), Bangalore.*****
