                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

36






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






Support to Online Games




M.K. Vishnu Prasad














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY   be pleased to State:(a)	whether the Government is providing any support to indigenously developed online gaming and if so, the details thereof; and(b)	whether the indigenous development of online games will generate employment opportunities in the country and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	and (b): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.  STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.*36 FOR 03.02.2021 REGARDINGDIGITAL SAFETY OF CITIZENS........(a):  Government of India constantly endeavors to leverage technology and innovation towards maximizing country’s potential & economic growth. One such area is online gaming which has become an accelerator of digital footprint in cyber space. With a large market of Indian online gaming enthusiasts and availability of digital infrastructure and engineering talent as well as a vibrant Startup community, India can be an engine for growth and innovation in online gaming across the world. Traditional games of India should also be promoted in online mode as there is huge market potential for this segment. Online gaming has created enormous opportunities but it is also fraught with challenges in the form of compliance with regulations, age verification and issues of betting and gambling. MeitY in partnership with Atal Innovation Mission – NITI Aayog launched Digital India Aatma Nirbhar Bharat Innovate Challenge to identify the best Indian Apps that are already being used by citizens and have the potential to scale and become world-class Apps in their respective categories. This Innovation Challenge was aimed at creating an ecosystem where Indian entrepreneurs and Startups are incentivised to ideate, incubate, build, nurture and sustain Tech solutions that can serve not only Indian citizens but also the world. The contest was hosted under 9 categories and Online Games was one such category. Out of the 326 entries that were received under this category, the top 3 winners selected were as follows:a.	Hitwicket Superstars: Cricket Strategy Game 2020; b.	ScarFall: The Royal Combat; c.	World Cricket Championship 2 (WCC2). The winners have been awarded prize money of Rs. 20 lakhs, Rs. 15 lakhs and Rs. 10 lakhs respectively. (b):  Yes, Sir. The indigenous development of online games is expected to generate employment opportunities as it has potential for growth, innovation, revenue generation and employment.*******
