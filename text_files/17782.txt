                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1439






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.09.2020






Funds for MGNREGS




Balashowry Vallabbhaneni














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of mandays generated under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (MGNREGS) from 01.04.2020 to 31.08.2020, State-wise along with the comparative figures of the previous three years;(b) 	whether the Ministry feels that the allocation of Rs. 61,000 crores is sufficient for MGNREGS this year;(c) 	if not, whether it has approached the Finance Ministry for additional funds; and(d) 	if so, the response of the Finance Ministry thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR) (a):     State/UT-wise details of persondays generated under the Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) from April to August, State-wise along with the comparative figures for last three years and the current FY 2020-21 are given at Annexure-I. (b) to (d):       Mahatma Gandhi NREGS is a demand driven wage employment programme. Fund release to States/UTs under Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme (Mahatma Gandhi NREGS) is a continuous process and Central Government is committed in making funds available to the States keeping in view the demand for work. During the Financial Year 2020-21, the Budget Estimate is of Rs. 61,500 crore for Mahatma Gandhi NREGS. In order to meet additional demand for work due to return of workers from urban areas to rural areas, Government has made an additional provision of Rs. 40,000 crore under Mahatma Gandhi NREGS under the Atma Nirbhar Bharat package as announced by Hon’ble Union Finance Minister during the current FY 2020-21. The Ministry seeks additional fund for Mahatma Gandhi NREGS as and when required for meeting the demand for work on the ground.Annexure-I referred in reply to part (a) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No.1439 dated 20.09.2020
