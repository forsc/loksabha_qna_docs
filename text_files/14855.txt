UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3589






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2020






Van Dhan Vikas Samuhs in Jharkhand




Jayant Sinha














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of Van Dhan Vikas Samuhs constituted and the corresponding number of members of the self-help groups formed under this scheme in Jharkhand, district-wise; (b)	the number of Van Dhan Vikas Kendras constituted so far under the Van Dhan Yojana in Jharkhand, district-wise; and (c)	whether the Government is planning to recognise the best performing Kendras and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c):	District-wise details regarding number of self-help groups and number of Van Dhan Vikas Kendras (VDVKs) set up under the scheme ‘Mechanism for Marketing of Minor Forest Produce (MFP) through Minimum Support Price (MSP) and development of value chain of MFP’ (MSP for MFP) in Jharkhand State are at Annexure.
