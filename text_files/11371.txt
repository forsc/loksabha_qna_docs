                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

330






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2020






Welfare Schemes in Jharkhand




Annpurna Devi














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. †330TO BE ANSWERED ON 04.02.2020Welfare Schemes in Jharkhand†330. SHRIMATI ANNPURNA DEVI:Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) the schemes of the Ministry which have been implemented in Jharkhand; (b) the amount allotted/disbursed for the said schemes during the last three years, scheme-wise; and (c) the reasons for not allotting and releasing the amount for the said schemes and the reaction of the Government thereto?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c): A list of schemes being implemented by the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment in Jharkhand, funds disbursed for implementation in Jharkhand under these Schemes during the last three years and reasons in case of non-release of funds, is attached as Annexure.****
