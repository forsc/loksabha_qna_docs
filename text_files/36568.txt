                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3713






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Harassment of Students in Poland




Deepak Baij













                                                    Balubhau (Alias Suresh Narayan) Dhanorkar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether Indian students faced harassment in the form of physical assault, extortion of money by Ukranian authrorities and Poland police as they attempted to leave the country from its land borders; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the number of students that have suffered injuries due to this harassment;(d) 	the number of students that have reported extortion of funds along with the total quantum of money lost;(e) 	whether there was any contact with Ukrainian/Poland border forces to allow the free exit of Indian students into neighbouring countries along with number of Indian officials present at the border; and'' (f)  	if so, the details thereof and if not, reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS [SMT. MEENAKASHI LEKHI](a) to (d)	There were difficult situations faced by Indian students as a result of heavy congestion and over-crowding at the Ukrainian-Polish border in the initial days after the conflict broke out. According to UN estimates, out of the 3.2 million refugees from Ukraine that have crossed over to its western neighbours, almost 2 million have crossed over to Poland. We have no reports of any injuries suffered by Indian nationals.   (e) & (f)	An MEA team of 8 officials including Russian speaking officers were deployed at the Ukrainian-Polish border points. Our Ambassador in Poland was constantly in touch with the Polish Border-Guard. Similarly, our Embassy in Ukraine was in touch with the concerned Ukrainian authorities. Their constant intervention at border check-points helped to address the severe challenges posed by over-congestion. Their liaison with local governments and community organizations was responsible for the boarding, lodging and medical attention of evacuees.***
