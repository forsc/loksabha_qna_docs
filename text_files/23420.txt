                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4525






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2021






Report of Misuse of PMGKAY




Harsimrat Kaur Badal














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the reports of misuse of PM Garib Kalyan Ann Yojana (PMGKAY) have come to Government‘s attention from Punjab; (b) 	whether the food security ration provided to poor under the Yojana did not reach the concerned beneficiary and was being diverted or distributed to only selected families; (c) 	if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	whether the Government has taken or proposes to take any action in this regard; and 	(e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION(SHRI   DANVE  RAOSAHEB   DADARAO)(a) to (c): 	As per information received from Government of Punjab, there is no report of misuse of the Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Ann Yojana (PM-GKAY) in the State, except in Gurdaspur. As per State, the District Controller of Gurdaspur had received 12 complaints against depot holders for giving less ration to beneficiaries.    (d) & (e): 	State Government has done the enquiry and had suspended the supply to these depot holders and four FIRs were registered against them.*****
