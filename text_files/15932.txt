                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4467






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.03.2020






Death of Forest Watchers




Kirron  Kher













                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ENVIRONMENT,  FORESTS AND CLIMATE CHANGE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number of forest watchers or other personnel in the forest services who have lost their lives in the line of duty across the country during the past three years, State-wise;(b)	whether the Government has chalked out any schemes /scholarship programmes to compensate the families and children of the forest guards who die on duty;(c)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor: and(d)	the other steps taken by the Government for the welfare of forest personnel who are injured or lost their lives in line of duty?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF ENVIRONMENT, FOREST AND CLIMATE CHANGE (SHRI BABUL SUPRIYO)(a) As per information received from various States and Union Territories, the total number of deaths of forest watchers and other personnel is reported as 81 during the last three years.  Tamil Nadu has reported 51, Kerala 07, Punjab 17, Madhya Pradesh 01, Arunachal Pradesh 04 and Rajasthan 01 deaths during the last three years.(b),(c) and (d) The State Governments/UT Administrations take various measures including providing ex-gratia relief / lump sum compensation, giving employment to the  dependent of deceased personnel on compassionate grounds in the case of death of their forestry personnel and bearing expenses on treatment of injured personnel or death related formalities as per their own schemes. *****
