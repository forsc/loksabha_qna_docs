                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2848






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.12.2019






Safety Audit




Satyadev Pachauri














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) has conducted a safety audit of airlines and if so, the details thereof; and(b) whether certain airlines have failed to clear safety audit on various parameters and if so, the details thereof and the action taken by DGCA against those airlines?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) Yes, Sir. The Directorate General of Civil Aviation( DGCA) regularly conducts safety audits of scheduled and non-scheduled airline operators in the country as per the Annual Surveillance Programme. The details of safety audits conducted by DGCA during 2018 and 2019 for Airlines are given in Annexure.;(b) Based on the findings/violations recorded during the audits/ surveillances/spots check, enforcement action is taken against the organisation/personnel responsible for lapse including withdrawal of DGCA approval to post holders. The enforcement action taken by DGCA consequent to safety audit conducted in 2018 and 2019 are:;1. Zoom Air: The Air Operator Permit was not extended.;2. Indigo:;       (i) Cockpit crew were suspended for non-reporting of incident.;;       (ii) Maintenance engineers were suspended for non-adherence to SOP.;       (iii) DGCA Post Holders approval was withdrawn for non-compliance of DGCA Regulations.;3. SpiceJet: DGCA Post Holders approval was withdrawn for non-compliance of DGCA Regulations.;
