                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CORPORATE AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

313






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






CSR Spending




H. Vasanthakumar














                        Will the Minister of





CORPORATE AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) the total Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) spending by the top 500 companies in the country in tourism, education, healthcare, rural development areas, etc. during the last three years and the current year along with the States/UTs where such spending was made;(b)whether the Government has advised the companies to make ground water development and pollution free water bodies as core areas of CSR investment in the coming years and if so, details thereof;(c) whether the companies are allowed/proposed to be allowed to carry forward the unspent CSR fund to next years; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF FINANCEAND CORPORATE AFFAIRS                                 (Smt. NIRMALA SITHARAMAN)????? ??? ???????? ????? ??????                                 (??????? ??????? ????????)  (a)	to (c): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*****STATEMENT REFERRED TO IN ANSWER TO PART (a) TO (d) OF LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO.313* (13TH POSITION) FOR 15th JULY, 2019 REGARDING CSR SPENDING.(a)	:  All data reported by companies on Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) is available on National CSR Data Portal at www.csr.gov.in. Details of CSR expenditure by the top 500 companies are at annexure-I and II. (b)	: As per Section 135 of the Companies Act, 2013 (Act), it is prerogative of the Board of a company to undertake any of the CSR activities enlisted in Schedule VII of the Act. The Government does not give any directions to Corporates to spend in any particular area or activity. (c) & (d) : As per Section 135 of the Act, every company during each financial year is required to spend at least two percent of its average net profits made during the three immediately preceding financial years.    ****** Annexure-IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO LOK SABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 313Development sector-wise CSR spending by the Top 500 companies (in Rs.
