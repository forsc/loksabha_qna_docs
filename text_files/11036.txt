                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

41






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2020






Generation of Funds




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	Whether the Government has fixed target of generating fund upto 1.05 trillion during the current financial year;(b)	if so, the details thereof and the area of sources from which the Government has decided to generate the fund upto 1.05 trillion;(c)	the details of the actual target achieved by the Government till now; and (d)	the steps taken by the Government to touch the target of 1.05 trillion fund generation 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE (SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR)(a) 	As per BE 2019-20, the Government has fixed the target of generating funds amounting to ?27, 86 349 cr for funding the expenditure requirements of an equivalent amount.(b)	Non-Debt receipts including taxes available with the Central Government, Non-Tax Revenues and Non-Debt Capital Receipts amounting ?20,82,589 cr and Debt receipts of ?7,03,760 cr are budgeted to be collected by the end of the Financial Year 2019-20.(c) and (d) Till 30th November 2019, Non-Debt Receipts totalling ?10,12,223 cr have been collected to fund a total expenditure of ?18, 20,057 cr.
