                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2503






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Committee for Study of Brahmaputra River




Pallab Lochan Das














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has set up a high level committee for study of Brahmaputra River and its tributaries and if so, the details thereof; (b) the details of recommendations and findings by the committee to tackle the problem of flood and erosion in the State; (c) whether the Union Government is considering to implement the recommendations made by the committee; (d) if so, the details thereof and the current status of any such implementation of recommendations; (e) the annual loss of property and life due to flood and erosion in Assam; and (f) the details of financial aid provided by the Union Government to the Government of Assam to tackle flood and erosion?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a)  A high level committee (HLC) for Management of Water Resources in the North East Region was constituted by Ministry of DoNER under the Chairmanship of Vice Chairman, NITI Aayog in October, 2017. The terms of reference of HLC included appraisal of the existing institutional arrangement for management of water resources in the North East Region, identification of Gaps in the existing arrangement of Water Resources, suggesting policy intervention spelling out actionable measures and chalking out plan of action for dovetailing of schemes/programmes of Union Ministries and State Governments. (b)  The details of recommendations and findings by the committee to tackle the problem of flood and erosion in the state includes immediate term, short term and medium term action points emphasizing on Integrated Water Resources Management covering all aspects of Water Resources. In addition, committee has also recommended to establish a Basin level authority namely North Eastern Water Management Authority (NEWMA).(c) & (d)  Water being a State Subject responsibility of it’s Development & Management including Flood & Erosion Management lies with the State Government.  Central Government provides Technical & Promotional assistance to State Government proposals as per their priority & resources.
