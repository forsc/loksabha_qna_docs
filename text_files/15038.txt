                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3788






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.03.2020






Assistance under Krishi Kalyan Abhiyan




John Barla














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the types of assistance provided to farmers under programmes like Krishi Kalyan Abhiyan which are run by the Government from time to time to help the farmers; (b)	 the number of times it is organised; (c) 	the basic facilities provided to the farmers under this scheme; and (d) 	the number of villages benefited or the number of villages in which it was organised during the last two years and the current year?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) to (d):	Krishi Kalyan Abhiyan (KKA) was launched with a focus to give a boost to agriculture and allied activities for augmenting the income of farmers in 117 Aspirational districts selected from all the States/ UTs in the country.  So far to Krishi Kalyan Abhiyans have been organized from 1st June 2018 to 31st July 2018 (extended upto 15th  August 2018) and 2nd October 2018 to 25th December 2018 (extended upto 25th January 2019) respectively.  Under each Abhiyan, 25 villages each with more than 1000 population were covered in each Aspirational district.  The details of activities carried out in Krishi Kalyan Abhiyan –I & II are as under:               S.No.	Activity	KKA-I Achievement(No. of  units) 	KKA II Achievement(No. of  units)1	Distribution of Soil Health Cards 	738058	7067872	Distribution of Mini Kits of Pulses and oil seeds or paddy	450863	1798813	Distribution of Horticulture/ Agro Forestry/ Bamboo Plant @ 5 per family	1519006	5605714	Making NADEP Pits in each Village (20 per village)	39742	157635	Training Programmes ( 3 trainings per village)	17810	90896	Distribution of 10-20 agriculture implements per village (10 per village)	33484	178307	100% coverage of bovine vaccination (FMD) in each village	1358724	14201738	100% coverage of Sheep and Goat for eradication of PPR	1078113	9334879	Artificial insemination saturation (100 animals per village)	131164	125533*10	Demonstration of integrated cropping practice (1 per district)	-	2633*11	Demonstration programmes on Micro Irrigation (1 per district)	-	2390*12	Technology demonstration on farmer’s field	-	250*13	Organizing awareness campaign for PMFBY (1 per village)	-	11565*14	Development/ Upgradation of Gramin Haats in Convergence with MGNREGA (1 per village)	-	183 * These activities were not covered under KKA-I.*******
