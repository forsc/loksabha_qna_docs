                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3263






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.03.2022






Textile Design Exhibition




Ratansinh Magansinh Rathod













                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of textile design exhibitions including handicraft exhibitions organised in the country particularly in the backward areas during the last three years and the current year; (b) whether the Government proposes to organise more such exhibitions in future and if so, the details thereof; (c) the norms/criteria to be followed by the Indian traditional handicraft industry/ artisans to participate in national/ international exhibitions/fairs; (d) whether the participation in such exhibitions/ fairs has declined during the last three years and the current year and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and (e) the number of promotional schemes being run by the Government to provide adequate financial assistance and conducive infrastructure to the traditional handicraft industry in the country?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR TEXTILES  (SMT. DARSHANA JARDOSH)(a):	1356 handicrafts and handlooms exhibitions have been organized throughout the country including backward areas during the last three years and the current year. (b):	Yes Sir, 430 handicrafts and handlooms marketing exhibitions are planned during 2022-23.(c):	Artisans having valid Pehchan Card are eligible for participation in domestic exhibitions/fairs. Further, awarded artisans including master craftspersons are nominated for participation in international exhibitions/ fairs.(d):	Yes Sir, the participation in exhibitions/fairs has declined during the last three years and the current year due to impact of COVID. In order to mitigate the effect of COVID various virtual/online events were organized.(e):	To provide adequate financial assistance and conducive infrastructure to the traditional handicraft industry, 2 schemes namely National Handicrafts Development Programme (NHDP) & Comprehensive Handicrafts Cluster Development Scheme (CHCDS) are being implemented by the office of Development Commissioner (Handicrafts).                        ***
