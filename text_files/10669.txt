                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3972






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Solar/Green Cities




Srinivas Kesineni














                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of progress made under the solar/green cities scheme in Vijayawada;(b) the funds allocated for Vijayawada since the inception of the scheme, year-wise;(c) whether the proposed goals of the scheme have been met or not and if so, the details thereof; and(d) the steps taken to make Vijayawada a solar/green city?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a) to (d) Ministry had given approval for developing Vijayawada as solar city in 2011 under the scheme “Development of Solar Cities”. Under this project, 5 Nos. of Solar PV projects of 100 kWp capacity each were installed at 5 different locations of Vijayawada Municipal Corporation. The details of the projects are placed in Annexure. Vijayawada Municipal Corporation had prepared Master Plan and created Solar City cell and stakeholders committee. They also organized seminar, exhibitions and other promotional activities for the promotion of solar city program. The year- wise funds sanctioned/ released to Vijayawada is as below :-Financial Year	Funds released(Rupees in Lakh)2010-11	12.202013-14	3.202014-15	75.002016-17	75.00Total	165.40*****
