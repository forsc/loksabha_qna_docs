                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2034






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Setting up of AIIMS Hospital in Khajuraho




Vishnu Dutt Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government is considering to establish a new AIIMS or any other institute like AIIMS in Khajuraho for serving 80 lakh people facing shortage of medical services in under developed region of Bundelkhand;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STTE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(Dr. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)a) to (c) The Government of India has plans to establish AIIMS in all the States in the country in a phased manner under the Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY).   As far as Madhya Pradesh is concerned, one AIIMS at Bhopal has already been established   and it is functional. There is no proposal under consideration of the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare to establish a new AIIMS or an  Institute like AIIMS in Khajuraho.*********
