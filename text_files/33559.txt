                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

981






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Implementation of PMAGY




Ashok Mahadeorao Nete














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. †981 TO BE ANSWERED ON 08.02.2022IMPLEMENTATION OF PMAGY †981. SHRI ASHOK MAHADEORAO NETE: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether work is being carried out under Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana (PMAGY), particularly in naxal affected and tribal dominated Gadchiroli-Chimur Parliamentary Constituency and other backward areas of Maharashtra; (b) if so, the details thereof, particularly the details of naxal affected and tribal dominated Gadchiroli-Chimur Parliamentary Constituency and other backward areas of Maharashtra including its up to date status; and (c) the details of central fund allocated for the said purpose including the utilization thereof during each of the last three years, year-wise and State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI A. NARAYANASWAMY)(a): Yes, Sir.(b): The details of implementation of Scheme of Pradhan Mantri Adarsh Gram Yojana(PMAGY) in villages selected in 25 districts of Maharashtra, including districts of Chandrapur, Gadchiroli and Gondia is at Annexure-I. These details are also available on the web portal of the scheme at the URL: pmagy.gov.in. (c): The details of central funds sanctioned, and the utilization thereof, during each of the last three years, year-wise and State-wise, is at Annexure-II.***** Annexure-I Annexure referred to in reply to part (b) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. †981 raised by Shri Ashok Mahadeorao Nete, Hon’ble Member of Parliament, regarding ‘Implementation of PMAGY’ to be answered on 08.02.2022.
