                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3649






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Construction of Roads under PMGSY in Assam




Badruddin Ajmal














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has received the list of roads for approval under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) for the years 2016-17,2017-18 and 2018-19 from the State Government of Assam and if so, the details thereof;(b)	if so, the details of projects sanctioned indicating the length of road, number of bridges and funds allocated under the PMGSY in Assam;(c)	whether the DPR of the projects under the PMGSY have been prepared; and(d)	if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Details of the proposals received and cleared under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) in respect of the State of Assam during the years 2016-17, 2017-18 and 2018-19 are as under:  Year 	       Proposal received	                   Proposal cleared*	   No. 	   Cost 	   No. of roads	   Length in km	    No. of bridges	Value in Cr2016-17	   663	  1283.53	      269	        854.46	           18	          551.912017-18	  3622	  6549.32	     2399	       7058.60	          727	         5356.242018-19	   368	  1395.94	      492	       1929.88	          324	         2284.30 * Some of the proposals received during 2016-17 and 2017-18 were cleared in the subsequent years. (c) & (d):	All the proposals under PMGSY have been approved on the basis of Detailed Project Reports duly vetted by the State Technical Agencies. *****
