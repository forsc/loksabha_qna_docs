                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

641






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Expansion of Gwalior Airport




Vivek Narayan Shejwalkar














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CIVIL AVIATIONbe pleased to state:-(a) whether the land of Potato Research Centre is likely to be acquired for the expansion of the Gwalior Airport and construction of a new terminal building to address the growing passenger traffic by introducing new flights for other routes, if so, the details thereof;(b) whether survey for the expansion of the Gwalior Airport is being conducted by a team of the Department of planning of Airport Authority of India and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has allocated any fund for the expansion of the said Airport and construction of a new terminal building; and(d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) to (d): A multi-disciplinary team of Airports Authority of India (AAI) including officers from Planning Directorate have undertaken a study of Civil Enclave at Gwalior airport to explore the feasibility of expansion of Civil Enclave. The land requirement and estimated cost of the project is subject to the outcome of the report of the feasibility survey. ;
