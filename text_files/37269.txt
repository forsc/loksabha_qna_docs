                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4299






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Funds under National Family Benefit Scheme




Thomas Chazhikadan














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government is aware of the non receipt of benefits under the National Family Benefit Scheme to the beneficiaries and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor;(b) 	whether any funds have been allocated to the States since 2016 under the said scheme and if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	if not, whether the Government proposes to take action to release the funds and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) to (c) 	Under National Family Benefit Scheme (NFBS), which is a component of National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP), the Below Poverty Line (BPL) households are entitled to get one-time lump-sum amount of Rs.20,000/- on the death of primary breadwinner aged between 18 and 60 years. Identification of beneficiaries, sanction and disbursement of benefit under the scheme is done by the States/UTs. The funds under the NFBS are released on the basis of number of beneficiaries reported by the State/UTs, subject to the ceiling number of beneficiaries. The details of funds released to the States/UTs under NFBS are given in Annexure.*******
