                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3768






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.07.2019






Kisan Call Centres




Harish Dwivedi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 the number and details of Kisan Call  Centres  operational  in  the  country, State-wise  particularly  in  Uttar  Pradesh; and(b) 	 the  steps  taken  by  the Government to ensure that such centres provide  sufficient  information  to  the farmers?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER  OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????     (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The Department of Agriculture, Cooperation and Farmers Welfare (DAC&FW), Government of India is operationalzing Kisan Call Centres (KCCs) from 21 locations in the country, covering all the States and Union Territories including Uttar Pradesh. The Uttar Pradesh KCC is located at Kanpur. The details of KCCs locations and States covered by each KCC are given at Annexure I.(b):	Dissemination of sufficient and appropriate information to farmers is ensured by-(i)	Answering of farmer’s queries by Farm Tele Advisors (FTAs) who are Graduates/ Post Graduates in agriculture and allied sectors;(ii)	Sending SMS to the farmers on the gist of the reply given to them over phone by FTAs;(iii)	Regular up-dation of knowledge of KCC Supervisors and FTAs through technical trainings provided before each cropping season; field visits of FTAs to farmer’s fields and their regular interaction with State officials; (iv)	Regular interaction of FTAs and Supervisors with officers of various subject matter divisions of DAC&FW through video conferencing; and(v) Obtaining feedback from caller farmer on performance of FTA at the end of every call.
