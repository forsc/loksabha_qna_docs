                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3960






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.03.2021






Malfunctioning in Vaccination Portal




Rakesh Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) whether the Government has taken cognizance of the fact that the second dose of the vaccine could not be administered to the frontline workers in Jabalpur inspite of their registration due to malfunctioning in vaccination portal whereas the certificate of vaccinationhas been issued on Arogya Setu app;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the remedial measures taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):	As per the information received from the Govt. of Madhya Pradesh, second dose of COVID-19 vaccine is being given to all the Front Line Workers (FLWs) from 8th March in Jabalpur district and 3249 Front Line Workers have been given the second dose of COVID-19 vaccine as on 17th March 2021. Certificate have also been issued to them after vaccination. (c):	There was an issue of slowing down of portal in some of the vaccination centres in some districts but this issue has since been addressed and the vaccination certificates were issued to the beneficiaries. After vaccination each beneficiary receives a SMS with a link on their mobile from which the beneficiary can download the certificate themselves.
