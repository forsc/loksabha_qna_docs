                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2043






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Urban Planning in Metropolitan Cities




Parvesh Sahib Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has carried out any study/research/survey on the need of new techniques in urban planning in Metropolitan cities and if so, the details thereof;(b)	whether the urban planning for Delhi is done in coordination with the urban transport system and if so, the details thereof; and (c)	the list of all international technical assistance and cooperation received in development of metropolitan areas in Delhi during the last five year?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                     (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a): Urban Planning is a State subject. However, for the guidance of the States / UTs, this Ministry has framed the Urban and Regional Development Plan Formulation and Implementation (URDPFI) Guidelines 2014. These guidelines inter-alia prescribe new techniques in urban planning using geographical information systems and remote sensing.(b): Yes Sir, urban planning for Delhi is done in coordination with the urban transport system. In the base lining phase of Master Plan for Delhi (MPD) 2041, DDA is consulting all the stakeholders including those related to the urban transport system.(c):  Nil.                *****
