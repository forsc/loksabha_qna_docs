                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2315






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Funds for Manual Scavengers




 Dr. T. Sumathy (a) Thamizhachi Thangapandian














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO. 2315TO BE ANSWERED ON 09.03.2021FUNDS FOR MANUAL SCAVENGERS 2315.   DR. T. SUMATHY  (a) THAMIZHACHI THANGAPANDIAN: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state: (a) whether the allocation of funds towards Scheduled Caste (SC) persons involved in manual scavenging has been reduced for the last few years and in the Budget 2021; and (b) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RAMDAS ATHAWALE)(a) & (b): The Central Sector “Self Employment Scheme for the Rehabilitation of Manual Scavengers” is to provide rehabilitation benefits to all the identified manual scavengers without any caste, religion or economic restrictions. The Scheme being demand driven, the allocation and expenditure is dependent on the number of identified manual scavengers available in each year. Details of Funds allocated, funds released and the number of manual scavengers who have been provided Onetime Cash Assistance (OTCA) during the last three and current years are as under:S. No.	Year	Funds Allocated (Rs. Cr)	Funds released (Rs. Cr)	No. of beneficiaries provided OTCA1	20017-18	5.00	5.00	11712	2018-19	85.76	85.76	180793	2019-20	99.93	84.80	132464	2020-21(upto 28.02.2021)	30.00	16.60	13990****
