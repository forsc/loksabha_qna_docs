                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4342






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Houses to SCs/STs under PMAY-G




Anubhav Mohanty














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of houses provided to the Scheduled Castes (SCs)/Scheduled Tribes (STs) under Pradhan Mantri Aawas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G) during each of the last five years and the current year, Statewise;(b) 	the total number of houses allotted to the SCs/STs during the said period, State-wise; and(c) 	the name of the States where houses under this yojana have been earmarked to be allotted in the coming years ending 31 December, 2024 including Odisha?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) & (b) The State-wise as well as total numbers of houses sanctioned for the members of the Scheduled Castes (SCs) & Scheduled Tribes (STs) under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) during each of the last five years i.e. from 2016-17 to 2020-21 and the current year i.e. 2021-22 (as on 23.03.2022) along with the total is given at Annexure. (c)         Out of the overall target to construct 2.95 crore houses, a target of 2.60 crore (approx.) houses (including a target of 26,95,837 houses to Odisha) has already been allocated to the States/UTs from the financial year 2016-17 till 2021-22.  The targets under PMAY-G are set annually on the basis of availability of eligible beneficiaries, achievements of targets allocated during the preceding years and proposal of the State/UT.  The timeline for completion of PMAY-G has been fixed as March 2024.  *****
