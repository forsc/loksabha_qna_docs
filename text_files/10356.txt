                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3764






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.12.2019






Indicator System




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether with a view to facilitate the passengers the work of installation and operation of new indicator at Thane Station in the Central Railway has been contracted out to a private company by the Railway administration;(b) if so, the number of years for which this contract has been awarded;(c) whether any survey has been conducted by the Central Railway administration regarding the extent to which passengers are being benefited since the work of operating the indicator system has been assigned to the private company; and  (d) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) & (b): The operation of Indicator Boards at Thane Railway Station has been contracted out to a private company for a period of two years.(c): No, Sir. (d): Does not arise.*****
