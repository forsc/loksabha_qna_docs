                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

574






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2021






PMAY in Odisha




Mahesh Sahoo














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of houses build under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojna in Odisha, district-wise for the last two fiscals; (b) whether the scheme was able to achieve its targets in last two financial years in Odisha and if so, the details thereof; and (c) whether the progress of the scheme is as per the desired speed and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                        (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	Under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Urban [PMAY - U], 60,659 houses have been constructed during last two fiscal years (2018-19 & 2019-20), district wise details of which are Annexed. (b)	& (c) 1,73,476 houses have been sanctioned so far in the State of Odisha since launch of PMAY (U) in June, 2015. Of the sanctioned houses, 1,19,243 houses have been grounded for construction, of which 79,126 houses have been completed. All the houses sanctioned under the scheme are to be completed within the mission period of PMAY – U i.e. by the year 2022.                                    *****
