                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1619






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Status of Kakinada-Vijayawada-Nellor Gas Pipeline




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the current status of Kakinada-Vijayawada-Nellore natural gas pipeline;(b) whether there is any time and cost overrun in the project;(c) if so, the details thereof along with the time when the project was started; and(d) the time by which the project is going to be completed and details of villages and number of households that are going to be benefited by the proposed line?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) to (d) Petroleum & Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB) is the authority to grant authorization to the entities to lay, build, operate or expand gas pipeline and for the development of City Gas Distribution (CGD) network in Geographical Areas (GAs) as per PNGRB Act, 2006. PNGRB has informed that they have authorized 667 Km long Kakinada-Vijayawada-Nellore Natural Gas Pipeline to IMC Limited, a private company, on 19.02.2018, with the completion schedule of 18.02.2021. IMC has completed techno-economic feasibility survey, detailed route survey and population density index of the pipeline. This pipeline is passing through the districts of East Godavari, West Godavari, Krishna, Guntur, Prakasam and Sri Potti Sriramulu Nellore of Andhra Pradesh. PNGRB has authorized East Godavari, West Godavari, Krishna and Sri Potti Sriramulu Nellore districts for development of City Gas Distribution (CGD) Networks. As per minimum work programme, the authorized CGD entities have to provide about 11.50 Lakh PNG domestic connections to household in these four districts.
