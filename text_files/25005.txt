                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

822






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.07.2021






Status of Malnutrition




Rama Devi














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    )    whether the Government has reviewed the status of malnutrition existing throughout the country;(b)       if so, the details thereof; and(c)       if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWER MINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a) to (c)  As per the report of the National Family Health Survey (NFHS) – 4, 2015-16, 35.7% children under 5 years of age are underweight and 38.4% are stunted indicating a reduction from the previous data captured in NFHS – 3, 2005-06, which reported 42.5% children under 5 years of age as underweight and 48% stunted.  However, the recently released report of NFHS-5 (2019-20) is available only for 22 States/UTs.
