                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

43






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2021






Increase in Crop Insurance Premium




Bharatiben Dhirubhai Shyal













                                                    Shankar Lalwani
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the insurance premium amount has been increased under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) by expanding its coverage; (b) 	if so, the details of the increased amount; and (c) 	the details of the area of land covered under PMFBY in Gujarat and Madhya Pradesh?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????    (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	Actuarial/bidded premium rate is charged by tahe insurance companies under the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY), but farmers have to pay maximum of 2% of sum insured for Kharif and 1.5% for Rabi food and oilseed crops and 5% for commercial/horticultural crops. Remaining part of actuarial premium is shared Central and State government on 50:50 basis except in North-Eastern States where it is 90:10 with effect from Kharif 20202 season. The premium amount payable to insurance companies also depends on sum insured and coverage under insured crops.(c):	29.44 lakh ha. and 110.25 lakh ha. of cropped area is covered in Gujarat and Madhya Pradesh respectively during 2019-20 under PMFBY.*****
