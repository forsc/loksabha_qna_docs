                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3100






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2022






Human Resource Development Support to Fisheries Sector




Kotagiri Sridhar














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING ??????????, ??????? ?? ????? ?????? be pleased to state:(a)	 whether the Government has established institutions for providing Human Resource  Development support to the fisheries sector; and(b) if so, the details of the selected institutes, State-wise and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) and (b): The Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR), Department of Agricultural Research and Education is the apex body for coordinating, guiding and managing research and education in agriculture and allied sectors including fisheries and aquaculture. The Central Institute of Fisheries Education (CIFE), a deemed to be University under ICAR with its headquarters at Mumbai, Maharashtra carries out human resource development through post-graduate and doctorate programmes in 11 disciplines. CIFE has two regional research centres at Kolkata (West Bengal) and Kakinada (Andhra Pradesh) which conduct certificate courses on different aspects of fisheries and aquaculture. Besides, other fisheries Research Institutes and their Centres under ICAR located in different States also provide human resource development support to the fisheries sector through training and demonstrations. A list of such institutes is given in the statement at Annexure.The Central Institute of Fisheries, Nautical and Engineering Training (CIFNET) and National Institute of Fisheries Post Harvest technology and Training with headquarters at Kochi in Kerala are functioning under the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying also impart training in various aspects of fisheries. CIFNET has its other units at Chennai in Tamil Nadu and Visakhapatnam in Andhra Pradesh. Moreover, the Department of Fisheries, Ministry of Fisheries, Animal Husbandry and Dairying has been implementing a flagship scheme namely “Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana (PMMSY)” with highest ever estimated investment of Rs. 20050 crore in fisheries sector for a period of 5 years with effect from the financial year 2020-21 which inter alia promotes activities like training, awareness, exposure and capacity building. National Fisheries Development Board (NFDB) has been identified as nodal agency for providing training and awareness programmes under PMMSY. These training programmes are implemented through Fisheries Departments of States/Union Territories, various institutes such as ICAR fisheries institutes, Fisheries Universities/Colleges of Fisheries, Krishi Vigyan Kendras (KVKs) and NFDB regional centres.                                 *****
