                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

716






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






National Water Policy




Parvatagouda Chandanagouda Gaddigoudar














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has finalized new National Water Policy (NWP);(b) if so, the details along with its features thereof; (c) the time by which the new Policy is likely to be implemented; (d) whether the Government proposes to set up National Bureau of Water Use Efficiency; and(e) if so, the details along with its proposed functions thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI  & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c) No. However, to address the present challenges in water sector, revision of National Water Policy has been envisaged  and a drafting committee has been constituted on 5th November, 2019 to revise the National Water Policy within a term of six months. Due to the work being affected due to COVID-19 pandemic, the term of the Committee has been extended up to 31st October, 2020.The said committee is working towards finalizing its report at present.(d) & (e) Yes. The proposed National Bureau of Water Use Efficiency (NBWUE) will have the overall responsibility of improving water use efficiency across various sectors, namely irrigation, drinking water supply, power generation, industries, cities and all other areas where water is used.*****
