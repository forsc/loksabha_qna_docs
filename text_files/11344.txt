Rural Roads Scheme in West Bengal




Raju Bista














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of Central budget allocations for Rural Roads development under various schemes in West Bengal during the last five years;(b) 	the budget allocation for development of rural roads in the district of Darjeeling, Kalimpong and North Dinajpur in such centrally funded schemes and the roads constructed with such funds during the said period; and(c) 	the length (in kilometres) of rural roads added in the district of Darjeeling, Kalimpong and North Dinajpur during the said period, as compared to previous five years?
