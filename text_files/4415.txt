                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4091






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Fire near Mazagon Dock




A. Ganeshamurthi














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  whether a fire broke out recently at an under construction warship of the Indian Navy near Mazagon dock;(b)  if so, the details thereof along with the number of casualties reported;(c)  whether the Government has ascertained the reasons for the fire break out; and(d)  if so, the details thereof and the action taken in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   ????? ????? ??????					(???? ??????? ????)   (a) & (b):  Yes, Sir.  A fire broke out onboard Yard 12704 at Deck 30 on 21 June, 2019 when tank cleaning was being carried out by a contractor’s worker using a portable electric grinding machine for cleaning.  One (01) casualty was reported.(c) & (d):  A committee has been constituted to investigate the cause of fire.  Preliminary investigations indicate that the fire might have been caused due to short circuit in the contractor’s portable electric grinding machine that was being used for tank cleaning work.  Action taken in this regard is as under:-(i)  Two concerned executives have been placed under suspension.(ii)  Work awarded to the contractor’s firm has been put on hold.(iii)  Fire safety and accident prevention system and organization onboard ship   have been further strengthened.(iv)  Efforts are afoot by MDL for speedy payment of compensation to the deceased.*****
