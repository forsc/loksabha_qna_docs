                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2421






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Procurement at Increased Rates




Ravneet Singh














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	The details of the different agricultural produce like wheat, paddy etc. which have been sold above Minimum Support Price (MSP) after the promulgation of the ordinances and consequent enactment of the three new farming laws, product-wise and State/UT-wise;(b)	the details of the total production, requirement and consumption of different food products including edible oil in the country during the last two years and the current year, product-wise;(c)	the details of the total quantity of food products including edible oil which have been exported from the country during the last two years and current year; product-wise; and (d)	the details of the total quantity of food products including edible oil which have been imported into the country during the last two years and the current year, product-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ??????? ??????   (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a):	The weighted average price per quintal of the different agricultural produce which are above MSP for the period from 05.06.2020 to 28.02.2021, product-wise and State/UT-wise is at Annexure-I.(b):	The details of the total production, requirement and consumption of different food products are not maintained. However, the details of Gross Value Added of different food products including edible oil in the country for the year 2015-16 to 2017-18, as per as per  latest data available from the Annual Survey of Industries released by Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation(MoSPI) for registered sector is at Annexure-II.(c) & (d):	The details of export and import of total food products including edible oil of the country during the last two years and current year in terms of value are at Annexure-III and Annexure-IV respectively.
