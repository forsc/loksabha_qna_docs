                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1369






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

01.07.2019






Foreign Operations of PSBs




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre













                                                    Devji Mansingram Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the total number of foreign branches and other operations of public sector banks (PSBs) abroad at present, bank-wise and country-wise;(b)	whether the Government has decided to close certain loss making foreign branches of PSBs and if so, the details thereof; and(c)	the fresh steps taken by the Government to rationalise unviable overseas branch operations of these banks?






ANSWER




                                                        FINANCE MINISTER (a): Bank-wise and country-wise number of foreign operations of Public Sector Banks (PSBs), as on 31.5.2019, is at Annex.(b) and (c): A reforms agenda based on recommendations made by Whole-Time Directors and senior management of PSBs was referred by the Government to PSBs for appropriate action as per approval of bank Boards. The agenda covered, inter-alia, rationalisation of overseas operations for cost efficiencies and synergies in overseas markets, based on competitive strength and viability.As per bank inputs, all PSBs having foreign operations jointly took the initiative to prepare a note in mutual consultation for rationalisation of their foreign operations for cost efficiencies and synergies in overseas markets, based on competitive strength, viability and commercial considerations, and 18 branches have been rationalised since 1.1.2018.***AnnexLok Sabha Unstarred Parliament Question No. 1369, for
