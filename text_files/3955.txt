                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3631






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.07.2019






Tourism Development Fund for Maharashtra




Sanjay(Kaka) Ramchandra Patil













                                                    Rajan Baburao  Vichare
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has taken any initiatives for promoting tourism in Maharashtra and if so, the details thereof along with the funds sanctioned and expenditure incurred during the last two years; (b) 	whether a provision has been made or proposed to be made by the Government regarding any Tourism Development Fund for development of tourism in the State of Maharashtra; (c) 	if so, the provisions made or proposed to be made by the Government for tourism development in Western Ghats range known as the biggest bio-diversity zone in the country; (d) 	whether there is any proposal of the Government to launch a special programme to increase the number of tigers in Sahyadri Tiger Reserve; and (e) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (c):	The Ministry of Tourism, under its different schemes has taken various initiatives for the development and promotion of tourism in Maharashtra as detailed below: Under its schemes of Swadesh Darshan, PRASHAD and Assistance to Central Agencies, the Ministry provides Central Financial Assistance to State Governments /Union Territory Administrations/Central Agencies for development of thematic tourist circuits in the country, for holistic development of identified pilgrimage and heritage destinations and for development of infrastructure. Fifteen (15) thematic circuits have been identified under the Swadesh Darshan Scheme, under which projects are sanctioned on pan-India basis. The projects are identified for development in consultation with the State Governments/UT Administrations and are sanctioned subject to availability of funds, submission of suitable detailed project reports, adherence to scheme guidelines and utilization of funds released earlier. Details of projects sanctioned under the above schemes in the States which fall under the Western Ghats region including the State of Maharashtra, are at Annexure As part of its on-going activities, the Ministry annually releases print, electronic, online and outdoor media campaigns in the international and domestic markets, to promote various tourism destinations and products of the country, including those in the State of Maharashtra and the Western Ghats. Promotions are also undertaken through the Social Media accounts and website of the Ministry. (d) and (e):	The National Tiger Conservation Authority, in collaboration with the Wildlife Institute of India has sanctioned a proposal titled ‘Tiger Recovery Strategy and Long Term Monitoring in Sahyadri Tiger Reserve’ for Rs.258 lakhs to recover the tiger population.*******
