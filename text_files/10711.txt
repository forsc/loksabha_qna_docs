                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3992






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

12.12.2019






Renovation of Bridges in Maharashtra




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the number and the details of bridges which require large scale repair or renovation in Maharashtra, district-wise;(b)	the steps taken by the Government to carry out repair or renovation of these bridges; (c)	the expenditure likely to be incurred thereon; and (d)	the time by which the said repair/ renovation work is likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (d) Work on 4 bridges, requiring large scale repair or renovation in the State of Maharashtra is being implemented by NHAI. The details of these bridges are annexed.
