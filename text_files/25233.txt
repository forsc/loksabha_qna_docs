                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

957






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Audit of Usage of LPG




L.S. Tejasvi Surya














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has conducted or plans to conduct a consumer-side auditof usage of LPG across States and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government intends to come up with policy changes in Pradhan MantriUjjwala Yojana (PMUY) to increase the usage of LPG and if so, the details thereof alongwith the number of citizens who are availing benefit under PMUY and the total amountof subsidy being given to the them, State-wise; and(c) the number of citizens State-wise who have surrendered the gas connections from2014 till date along with the State-wise amount of subsidy saved by the Governmentduring the said period and the details of the LPG connections provided before 2014?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) Performance audit of Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana(PMUY) was conducted byComptroller and Auditor General of India(CAG) with a view to ascertaining theimplementation effectiveness of PMUY. The CAG team covered the PMUY connectionsreleased from May 2016 to December 2018 and refill transactions upto March 2018.(b) Oil Marketing Companies have taken several initiatives to provide affordable LPGrefills to the PMUY beneficiaries which include deferring of recovery of loan fromsubsidy from the PMUY beneficiaries to encourage refill consumption for six refills or aperiod of one year whichever is earlier, swap facility to avail small package of 5 kg refillagainst 14.2 kg refill as per their requirement etc.Currently, there are 8 crore PMUY beneficiaries across the country. State/UT –wisedetails are at Annexure. The prices of petroleum products including LPG in the countryare linked to the prices of respective products in the international market.(c) As on 31.03.2021, 1.08 crore LPG consumers have given up their subsidy under“GiveItUp” campaign across the country. As per available data, the estimated savingsdue to blocked/non-subsidised domestic/GiveItUp consumers from 2014-15 to 2020-21is Rs. 72,586 crore. As on 31.03.2014, there were 14.52 crore domestic LPGconsumers in the country, which have increased to 29.11 crore as on 01.07.2021.-------
