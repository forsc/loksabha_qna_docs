                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3036






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Lift Irrigation Programme




S.R. Parthiban














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the existing Lift Irrigation Programme in the country;(b) whether the Government proposes to introduce new Lift Irrigation Programme in the country, particularly in Tamil Nadu; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR JAL SHAKTI & SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) to (c)  Water Resources Projects including lift irrigation projects are planned, funded, executed and maintained by the State Governments themselves as per their own resources and priority. In order to supplement the efforts of the State Governments, Ministry of Water Resources, River Development & Ganga Rejuvenation provides technical and financial assistance to State Governments to encourage sustainable development and efficient management of water resources through various schemes and programmes such as Accelerated Irrigation Benefits Programme (AIBP) etc. under Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sichayee Yojana (PMKSY).  		During 2016-17, ninety-nine (99) ongoing Major/Medium irrigation projects under PMKSY-AIBP, having potential of 76.03 lakh ha. have been prioritized in consultation with States, for completion in phases up to December, 2019 along with their Command Area Development & Water Management (CADWM) works. Funding mechanism through NABARD has been approved by the Government for both Central and State share.  No project of Tamil Nadu figures among these 99 projects.  Further, there is no scope to include new projects under the PMKSY-AIBP at present. Out of these 99 projects, nine (9) projects are mainly lift irrigation projects.
