                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2606






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

06.03.2020






AYUSH Wellness Centres




S. Venkatesan













                                                    Vijay Kumar Dubey
                                                








                                                    Manoj Kumar Tiwari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of AYURVEDA, YOGA AND NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY (AYUSH) be pleased to state: (a) whether the Government has any proposal to establish AYUSH Wellness Centres in the country, State/ UT-wise, District-wise including Madurai, Tamil Nadu; (b) if so, the details thereof and the time line decided in this regard; and (c) the system of medicines to be applied for treatment and medicines to be provided under existing AYUSH system of medicines?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE (IC) OF THE MINISTRY OF AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY, UNANI, SIDDHA AND HOMOEOPATHY(SHRI SHRIPAD YESSO NAIK)(a) to (c):     Yes, 12500 AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres (HWCs) are proposed to be established by upgradation of existing State Government AYUSH dispensaries and Sub Health Centres by the year 2023-24.A total 1037 AYUSH HWCs are approved in year 2019-20 under the existing provisions of Centrally Sponsored Scheme of National AYUSH Mission (NAM). State-wise status of approved Health and Wellness Centres is furnished at Annexure.For Tamil Nadu State, 48 AYUSH dispensaries are approved for upgradation into HWCs including 1 HWC at Pothumbu, Madurai District as proposed by the State Government.It is envisaged to provide the services and treatment from different AYUSH systems as per the demand and requirement for these systems in the community. In this regard essential AYUSH medicines are proposed to be provided to the beneficiary as per the HWC proposal approved in the State.………………..
