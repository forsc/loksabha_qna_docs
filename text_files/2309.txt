                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2082






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.07.2019






Digital Door Number to House




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it has come to the notice of the Government about giving Digital Door Number to every house in Andhra Pradesh which helps in finding out houses easy and if so, the details thereof; and(b)	whether the Government has studied this, first of its kind method in the country and proposes to implement this across the country for better identification of houses and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) OF THE MINISTRY OF HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS                            (SHRI HARDEEP SINGH PURI)(a)	‘Land’ and ‘Colonisation’ are State subjects. The Government of Andhra Pradesh has undertaken the initiative of giving a Digital Door Number to every house in the Urban Local Bodies and Municipalities in the State of Andhra Pradesh.(b)	No Sir.                                *****
