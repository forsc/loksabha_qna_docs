                                    COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2725






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Startup India Seed Fund




Arvind Kumar Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether many projects have been funded, employment generated and economic value created under Startup India Seed Fund; and(b) 	if so, the details of the scheme since its inception to till date? 
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        ???????  ??? ?????? ???????? ??? ????? ?????? (???? ??? ??????)  THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI SOM PARKASH)(a) & (b): 	Yes sir. The Hon’ble Prime Minister of India has announced the Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS) on 16th January 2021. The scheme has been notified vide notification of the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade on the 21st January, 2021. Copy of the notification is available at website of this department: https://dipp.gov.in/sites/default/files/notification-Startup-29January2021.pdf.
