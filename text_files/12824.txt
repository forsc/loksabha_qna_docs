                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1647






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Tribal University




Shantanu Thakur














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the State/UT-wise number of Central and State Universities in the country includingWest-Bengal;(b) the total number of tribal/Scheduled Tribes (ST) women in the Central and StateUniversities at present in the country, State/UT-wise;(c) whether the Government has implemented any schemes particularly for educated tribalgirls; and(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): The State/UT-wise number of Central and State Universities in the country includingWest Bengal is given in Annexure-I.(b): The total State/UT-wise number of Tribal/Scheduled Tribes (ST) women in the 43Central Universities under the purview of Ministry of Human Resource Development(MHRD) is at Annexure-II. State/UT-wise ST women enrolment in respect of StateUniversities is not maintained centrally in Ministry of Human Resource Development.(c) & (d): The University Grants Commission (UGC) is implementing the following schemesfor the benefit of SC/ST Students including women/girls:<pre>1) Residential Coaching Academies of Minorities /SC/ST and Women2) Establishment of Centres in Universities for Study of Social Exclusion and InclusivePolicy3) Establishment of Equal Opportunities Cells (EOCs)4) Establishment of Scheduled Caste and Scheduled Tribe Cells in Universities5) Coaching Schemes for Scheduled Castes/ Scheduled Tribes/OBC (non-creamy layer)/Minorities (i) Remedial Coaching at UG & PG level (ii) Coaching for entry intoservices (iii) Coaching for NET6) National Fellowship for Higher Education of Scheduled Tribe Students (FormerlyRajiv Gandhi National Fellowship for ST Candidates)</pre>***
