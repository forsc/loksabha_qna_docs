                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4267






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






Construction of Roads on both sides of Railway Crossings




Dulal Chandra Goswami














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there is any proposal to construct roads on both sides of railway crossing located near villages under Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY);(b) 	if so, the details thereof; and(c) 	if not, the steps taken by the Government to approve the proposal keeping in view the rural development?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) to (c) 	Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY) was launched with the objective to improve rural connectivity to provide access to economic and social services to the rural population. On a road alignment selected for construction/ up-gradation under PMGSY, the road work including its approaches on both sides of railway crossings falling on such alignment are constructed under PMGSY.  ******
