                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5522






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2019






Nursing Officers in Kalawati Saran Children’s Hospital




Bhartruhari Mahtab














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE   be pleased to state: (a) the number of proposals sent to the Government to increase the sanctioned strength of Nursing Officers in Kalawati Saran Children’s Hospital (KSCH) during each of the last three years and the current year along with the present status of such proposals, as on date; (b) whether it is a fact that the sanctioned strength of Nursing Officers has not been increased in KSCH till date;(c) if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; (d) the details of increase in patients recorded in KSCH since inception; and (e) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to increase the sanctioned strength of Nursing Officers in KSCH?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) to (c):      The strength of Nursing Officers in Kalawati Saran Children’s Hospital (KSCH) has been increased from time to time as per procedure and requirement of hospital.          A proposal for 14 posts of Nursing Officer from the Department of Pediatric Nephrology of Kalawati Saran Children’s Hospital was received from the hospital in October 2016. After receiving the approval, the hospital has carried out recruitment for these posts, in June 2019.           Further, action has been initiated, as per procedure, for creation of 260 posts of Nursing Officer in the Department of Neonatology of KSCH, for which the proposal was received in April, 2019.Contd…….(d):      Increase in patients recorded in KSCH since 1970 is as under:Year	IPD	OPD1970	7593	934851980	11987	2435931990	23949	2623121995	15384	2640202000	19542	3026452005	27293	2899922010	28840	2822052015	29262	2775872016	29443	2950402017	29595	2877082018	32909	282512(e):      The increase in the sanctioned strength of Nursing Officers in KSCH is an administrative matter and is undertaken from time to time, as per procedure and requirement of the hospital.………………….
