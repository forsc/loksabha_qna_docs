                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

556






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.02.2022






Allocation to Gujarat under NAM




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total funds allocated to Gujarat as grant-in- aid under various activities of the National AYUSH Mission (NAM);(b) the details of the activities organised for providing mass health facilities in Gujarat, district-wise; and(c) the number of integrated AYUSH hospitals proposed to be opened in Gujarat along with the amount of the fund allocated and disbursed in this regard? 






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH (SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a) The total funds allocated to Gujarat from 2014-15 to 2020-21 as grant-in-aid by centre under various activities of National AYUSH Mission is of Rs. 81.67 Crore.(b)  Under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the National AYUSH Mission (NAM), the Ministry of Ayush has approved 265 AYUSH Health & Wellness Centres and 36 Yoga Wellness Centers for providing mass health facilities in Gujarat as per the proposals submitted by the State Government of Gujarat through State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs). In Gujarat AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres under AYUSHMAN BHARAT are providing mass yoga sessions while Yoga Wellness Centres in Hospitals are providing Yoga sessions, disease wise Yoga treatment through seminars and camps. The District wise details of the AYUSH HWCs and Yoga Wellness Centres providing the AYUSH Health facilities in Gujarat is furnished at Annexure.(c)  Under the NAM there is a provision of financial assistance for setting up of upto 10/30/50 bedded Integrated AYUSH Hospitals. Accordingly, State/UT Governments including Gujarat may avail the eligible financial assistance by submitting the suitable proposal for setting up of upto 10/30/50 bedded Integrated AYUSH hospitals through SAAP as per the NAM guidelines.******
