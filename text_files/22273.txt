                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3262






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Vacancies in State Police Departments




T. R. Paarivendhar














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is a fact that around 5.31 lakh posts allotted under the State Police departments across the country are lying vacant;(b) if so, the details thereof alongwith the reasons/ impediments for not filling up these vacant posts;(c) whether the Union Government has any intention to fill these vacancies at the earliest;(d) if so, the time by which these vacancies are likely to be filled; and(e) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY) (a) to (e) “Police” is a State subject falling in List-II (State List) of the Seventh Schedule of the Constitution of India.  It is primarily the responsibility of the State Governments to fill up the vacancies in the police force in their respective States.  The Centre also issues advisories to the States for filling up these vacancies in the States and to bring in the requisite reforms in the police administration to meet the expectations of the people.--2--L.S.US.Q.NO. 3262 FOR 16.03.2021As per data on Police Organisations compiled by Bureau of Police Research & Development, sanctioned strength of police is 26,23,225, actual strength is 20,91,488 and 5,31,737 vacancies are there as on 01.01.2020.  The States/UTs wise sanctioned and actual strength of police is annexed.******
