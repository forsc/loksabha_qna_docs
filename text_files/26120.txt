                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1877






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

30.07.2021






Envenomation by King Cobra Bites




Suresh Kodikunnil














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a)   the details of deaths reported due to envenomation by King Cobra bites in the country forthe last three years, State/UT wise;(b)   whether the Government has inadequate facilities for developing antivenom for poison of King Cobra and for procurement and storage of such antivenom in select hospitals;(c)   if so, the details thereof including invoking of emergency procurement options to meet exigencies;(d)   whether the Government intends to grant solatium or compensation for the victim’s family owing to the extra risk undertaken and due to the rare nature of the incident; and(e)   if so, the details thereof including the compensation given to the family of a zoo employee who died due to cobra bite in Thiruvananthapuram?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH ANDFAMILY WELFARE(DR. BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (e) As reported by states the data pertaining to deaths/cases due to snake bites are at Annexure.Health is a state subject.  The Ministry of Health and Family Welfare has issued directions to all the States/UTs to include Anti Snake Venom Serum (ASVS) in the list of essential drugs of the States and to procure these drugs locally for supply to health facilities for use to the needy persons and support the same under National Health Mission.Treatment of snake bite cases and procurement and distribution of ASV in various states lies in the purview of various state governments and they can procure it from any of the following institutes.•	Central Research Institute (CRI), Kasauli•	King Institute of Preventive Medicine and Research (KIPM), Chennai•	Biological E Ltd., Hyderabad •	Haffkine Bio-Pharmaceutical Corporation Limited (HBPCL), Mumbai•	Bharat Serums & Vaccine Ltd., ThaneAs reported by the State Government of Kerala the following compensation was given to the family of the Zoo Employee who died due to King Cobra bite in Thiruvananthapuram:-i.	    Job to the deceased person''s wife in Government sector under the die-in-harness scheme by setting    aside seniority norms.ii.	   In relaxation to the Kerala rules for payment of compensation to the victims of attack by Wild    animals 1980 an amount of compensation of Rs.10 lakhs was made as compensation.iii.	   The education expenses of the son of the deceased person would be met by the Government till he     turns 18 years of age.iv.	  Construction of house for victim''s family under Kerala Government''s LIFE Mission programme.
