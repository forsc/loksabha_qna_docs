                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

250






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.07.2019






Beas Military Station




Jasbir Singh Gill














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the details of defence land infront of/outside/adjacent to the Beas Military Station on G.T. Road;(b)  whether a large number of trees in front of outside/adjacent to the military station, which were acting as cover to anti-aircraft gun morcha, have been reportedly cut for mining  resulting in disappearance of huge mounds and if so, the details thereof;(c)  the details of agencies/organisations which carried out the work of mining and cutting of trees thereby compromising on national security; and(d)  whether any action has been taken by the Government against those involved in mining and cutting of trees and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF DEFENCE                                    (SHRI RAJNATH SINGH) (a) to (d):  A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.*******
