                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2944






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.03.2022






Funds Allocated for Cultural Projects in Andaman and Nicobar Islands




Kuldeep Rai Sharma














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the funds allocated and utilized by the Union Government for various cultural assistance and projects in Andaman & Nicobar Islands during the last three years and the current year;(b)	the details of the ongoing cultural programmes and projects in Andaman & Nicobar Islands; and(c)	the details of the projects likely to be completed on time and the projects lagging behind schedule along with the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a): Government of India has released grant to National Council of Science Museums (NCSM), an autonomous organization of Ministry of Culture for setting up of a Sub-Regional Science Centre, Mayabundar in A&N Islands and an Innovation Hub at Science Centre, Port Blair in A&N Islands under the Scheme for Promotion of Culture of Science (SPOCS) of Govt. of India during the last 3 years:Year	Grant released to NCSM  2018-19	Rs 13.71 lakhs2019-20	Rs 177.35 lakhs2020-21	-2021-22	Rs 17.01 lakhs(b) & (c): After approval for setting up of Science Centre in A&N Islands, Letter of Intent for construction work was issued by NCSM to Public Works Department (PWD), A & N Islands on 5th March, 2019. However, the work at site has not been initiated due to the fact that PWD, A & N Islands is unable to finalise the agency for the work despite two attempts of tendering.
