                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2018






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.07.2019






Halt at Thiruthangal




Manickam Tagore B














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government will take necessary action to stop/halt the Chennai-Kollam (Quilon) Express and Ernakulam-Vailankanni Express at Thiruthangal Railway Station which is a very long pending demand of the general public and Traders Association of Thiruthangal; (b) if so, the details thereof; (c) if so, the time by which the action will be taken to stop these trains in Thiruthangal Railway Station; and (d) if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH  GOYAL)(a)	 to (d) Tiruttangal station is presently being served by two pairs of Express trains and three pairs of passenger trains. 16101/16102 Chennai Egmore-Kollam Express and 06015/06016 Ernakulam-Velankanni Special Express are having scheduled stoppage  at Sivakasi which is only 3 Km away from Tiruttangal. Additional stoppages to these trains in such a close proximity is operationally not expedient.*****
