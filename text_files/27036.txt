                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2563






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Vaccine to Diplomats




Mala Roy














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether foreign diplomats posted in India received COVID screening;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the extent of coverage of fully vaccinated foreign diplomats currently in India, along with the number and percentage thereof; and(d)	the details of places from where these vaccine were sourced from?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS     [SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) to (d)  As a public health measure, it was decided to offer vaccinations to foreign personnel of Embassies, Consulates, United Nations offices and their specialized agencies and other international organizations located in India.A total of 3751 such foreign personnel registered with the Ministry of External Affairs for COVID19 vaccination as per details below:<pre>Delhi2518UN ORG/IO/Other entities350Mumbai386Kolkata191Hyderabad79Bangalore35Chennai132Thiruvananthpuram19Goa7Guwahati10Chandigarh3Agartala21Total3751</pre>They were offered vaccination facilities at any of the vaccination centres in their respective cities. ***
