                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2649






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






Computerization of Post Offices




Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has undertaken any programme for the computerization and modernization of post offices in the country; (b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	the total number of post offices modernized/ computerized during each of the last three years, State/ UT-wise; and (d) 	the funds allocated and spent in this regard during the same period under the said programme, State/ UT-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS, EDUCATION ANDELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (SHRI SANJAY DHOTRE)(a)  	Yes Sir. The Department of Posts has modernized and computerized the Post Offices to provide better postal services in the country under the IT Modernization Project. (b)   	The details are as under: - i.	The project includes providing Central Server based integrated, modular and scalable solution for all the operations of Department of Posts including Mails, Human Resource, Banking, Insurance and Finance and Accounts. It also involves creation of IT Infrastructure like Data Centre, Disaster Recovery Centre, setting up of a Wide Area Network (WAN) and providing solar powered and portable hand-held computing devices (Micro ATM compliant) to all the Branch Post Offices.ii.	At present there are 25,409 Departmental Post Offices in the country and all are computerized. The Department has computerized and modernized 1,29,157 Branch Post offices by providing SIM based Handheld Point of Sale devices for carrying out postal and financial transactions using central server architecture under Digital Advancement of Rural Post Office for A New India (DARPAN) Project. iii.	Post Offices are providing Core Banking Services (CBS) to the Post Office Savings Bank (POSB) customers enabling them to transact their POSB accounts from any post office across the country.iv.	1000 ATMs have been installed, with at least one in each District Headquarters in the country. These ATMs are inter-operable with other banks. v.	Internet banking services, (e-banking) have been made available for POSB customers from 14.12.2018. Through the e-banking facility, POSB account holders can make deposits into Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana (SSY) and the Post Office Public Provident Fund (PPF) accounts online from their POSB savings account. vi.	The POSB customers have also been provided with the facility of Mobile Banking from 15.10.2019.vii.	Postal Life Insurance (PLI) services are being provided through Core Insurance Solution (CIS) which has been rolled out in all the Post offices and through this solution, the customers can make premium payment at any post office and has also enabled central processing centers to provide faster claim settlement and after-sales service.(c)  	Computerization of all the Departmental Post Offices in the country was completed in 2014-15. However, funds are regularly allotted to Postal Circles for procurement of computers and peripherals to replace the obsolete computer/hardware items as per requirement.  The Postal Circle wise details of Branch Post Offices computerized/modernized under Digital Advancement of Rural Post Office for A New India (DARPAN) Project is annexed as Annexure-I. (d)   	i) During the Financial Year 2017-18, Rs. 20.56 Crore were spent centrally. The Postal Circle wise details of funds allocated and spent to replace the obsolete computer/hardware items in 2018-19 and 2019-20 are annexed as Annexure-II. ii) The IT Modernization Project has been implemented centrally by the Department.
