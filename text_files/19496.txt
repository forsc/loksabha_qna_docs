                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TEXTILES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

839






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2021






Organic Cotton Producer




Margani Bharat














                        Will the Minister of





TEXTILES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether is it true that India is a major organic cotton producer in the world with more than 50 percent share globally and if so, the details thereof; (b) the details of the other organic cotton producers/ contributors across the world;(c) the quantity of organic cotton produced in India vis-a-vis Bt. Cotton during the previous three years including the current year, year-wise and State-wise; and(d) the details of foreign exchange proceeds accrued through export/sale of organic cotton and efforts being made to promote/strengthen organic cotton farming in India? 






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TEXTILES(SMT. SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI) (a):	Yes, Sir. Production of organic cotton in India is 1.23 Million Metric Tons which is 51% of global organic cotton production of 2.40 Million Metric Tons.(b):	The other organic cotton producing countries are China, Kyrgyzstan, Turkey, Tajikistan, Tanzania, USA, Uganda, Greece, Benin, Peru, Burkina Faso, Pakistan, Egypt, Ethiopia, Brazil, Mali and Argentina.(c):	The comparative figure of the quantity of organic cotton produced in India vis-à-vis Bt. Cotton during the previous three years including the current year, year -wise is not available.(d):	During 2019-20, organic cotton has not been exported from India under National Programme for Organic Production (NPOP). Regarding efforts made to promote/strengthen of organic cotton farming in India, it is stated that the organic farming promotion of different crops including organic cotton is being addressed by Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare through Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana.                                    ***
