                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    EXTERNAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2667






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.08.2021






Shikarpur Integrated Check Post




Mahua Moitra














                        Will the Minister of





EXTERNAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the latest status on the notification and development of the proposed Shikarpur Integrated Check Post (ICP) in Karimpur (West Bengal);(b)	whether there is any other ICP under development in West Bengal and if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether IOC, GAIL and H-Energy have been granted approvals by PNGRB to build a natural gas pipeline through Bengal to the Bangladesh border; and(d)	if so, the details of the approval in each case?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EXTERNAL AFFAIRS[SHRI V. MURALEEDHARAN](a) & (b)	Shikarpur in Nadia District of West Bengal has not been identified for the development of Integrated Check Post (ICP).  However, 7 locations in the State of West Bengal, namely Phulbari, Panitanki, Jaigaon, Ghojadanga, Mahadipur, Hili  and Changrabandha have been identified for the establishment of ICPs. Construction of ICPs depends upon  acquisition  of  land  parcels  at  the  above  named  locations  by  the Land Ports Authority of India, for which active response of the State Government is awaited.(c) & (d)	Petroleum and Natural Gas Regulatory Board (PNGRB) has granted authorization in July 2019 to Hoogly Pipelines Private Limited (earlier known as H-Energy Private Limited) for laying, building, operating or expanding natural gas pipeline emanating from Kanai Chhata (West Bangal) and terminating near Bangladesh border at Shrirampur (West Bengal). Further, PNGRB has accepted the Central Government Authorization issued to GAIL for Jagdishpur-Haldia-Bokaro-Dhamra Pipeline (JHBDPL) which also covers West Bengal.***
