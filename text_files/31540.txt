                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2704






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

14.12.2021






Investment in Food Processing Sector




Jugal Kishore Sharma













                                                    Geeta Kora
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the amount of funds invested by the Government in Jammu and Kashmir and Jharkhand in  the  food processing sector during the  last  three years; and(b)	the amount of Foreign Direct Investment received by  the  Government  in  the said  sector  during  the  last three years, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FOOD PROCESSING INDUSTRIES (SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL) (a):	Ministry of Food Processing Industries (MoFPI) does not set up food processing industries on its own. However, it  provides credit linked financial assistance (capital subsidy) in the form of grants-in-aid to eligible entrepreneurs selected on the basis of Expression of Interest under relevant component schemes of Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana (PMKSY). PMKSY is not region or state specific but demand driven and is implemented across the country including in the state of Jharkhand and Union territory of Jammu and Kashmir.  MoFPI is also implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme-PM Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises Scheme (PMFME) for providing financial, technical and business support for upgradation/ setting up of micro food processing enterprises across the country  including Jharkhand and Jammu & Kashmir. The details of projects approved under PMKSY since 2018-19 and funds released under PMFME is placed at Annexure-I.(b):	Foreign Direct Investment inflow in Food Processing sector during last three years along with state-wise details is placed at Annexure-II. *****
