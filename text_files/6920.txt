                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

667






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

20.11.2019






Stoppage of Trains




Pradyut Bordoloi














                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is aware that Jamunamukh Railway Station in Hojai District of Assam has been one of the oldest Railway Stations under NFR and if so, the reasons why Railways have kept out Jamunamukh Railway Station when scores of trains pass-through it; and (b)	whether the NFR will consider Jamunamukh Railway Station to be a short duration stoppage for the following trains:(i)	Kamrup Express (Train No. 15959-15960),(ii)	Silchar Fast Passenger (Train No.55615-55616),(iii)	Silchar Express (Train No. 15611- 15612),(iv)	BG Express (Train No. 15665- 15666),(v)	Avadh Assam Express (Train No. 15909-15910),(vi)	Rangiya Special (Train No. 15967- 15968 and 15927-15928), in the near future?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a)	 and (b) At present, Jamunamukh station is being served by 3 pairs of Express trains and one pair of passenger trains. These services are considered adequate for the present level of traffic offering at the station. Stoppage of additional trains at this station is neither operationally feasible nor commercially justified, at present. However, provision of stoppages of trains is an on-going process on Indian Railways.*****
