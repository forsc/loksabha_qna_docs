                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4146






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.07.2019






Monitoring and Repairing of Roads




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) has directed all its officials to strictly monitor road conditions and take timely action for repairing the damaged stretches;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether damages would be levied and appropriate action would be initiated against the contractor, if he fails to fulfil the contractual obligations of operations and maintenance; and(d)	 if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) & (b) Sir, supervision of works is being done through Authority Engineer/ Independent Engineer/ Supervision Consultants. National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) has modified Terms of Reference (ToR) for supervision consultants to include equipment based inspection and also devised a reporting system to be followed by them. Regular reporting of the damages /deficiencies is being included in the ToR in order to take timely action.(c) & (d) If Contractor/Concessionaire is at default in fulfilling its obligations, penalty/damages are imposed under the provisions of the agreement.*****
