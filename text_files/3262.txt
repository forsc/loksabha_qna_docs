                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3120






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Development of Sports Infrastructure




Chandra Prakash Joshi













                                                    Ravindra Shyamnarayan alias Ravi Kishan Shukla
                                                








                                                    Ramdas Chandrabhanji Tadas
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any measures to build basic sports infrastructure in Maharashtra, Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the funds allocated, released and utilized in this regard and the basic sports infrastructure created during the last three years and the current year?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) & (b) Sports being a State subject the responsibility for building basic sports infrastructure rests with the State Governments. The Central Government has provided sports infrastructure and training facilities at Sports Authority of India’s (SAI’s) training centres as per Annexure-I. In addition, this Ministry has invested Rs. 12.87 crore for sports infrastructure facilities in Shivchhatrapati Kridapeeth Sports Complex, Pune, during the year 2018-19.(c) The information is as per Annexure-II.*****
