                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2482






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Insurgent Groups in NER




Pradyut Bordoloi













                                                    Sisir Kumar Adhikari
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of armed insurgent/ guerilla groups presently operating in the North-Eastern Region (NER) and the number of groups with which the Government is having talks at present along with the complete details thereof;(b) the number of interlocutors appointed for these talks along with their names and the outfits associated with them, State-wise;(c) the details regarding when the talks were started along with the status of thetalks, organisation/outfit-wise;(d) the details of the timeline of these talks and the time by which these talks would be completed; and(e) the details of proposed commitment of the Government and the agreement between the parties on their rehabilitation, employment and their safeguards?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (e):	The details of major insurgent groups operating in North Eastern States are at Annexure–I.  Government of India has appointed separate interlocutors for talks with Naga insurgent groups and insurgent groups of other NE States including ULFA, NDFB, KLNLF of Assam and KNO and UPF of Manipur. Talks with these groups are in progress. ********
