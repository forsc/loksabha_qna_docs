                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1053






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2021






Implementation of AYUSH Schemes in Uttar Pradesh




Rajesh Verma














                        Will the Minister of





AYURVEDA,YOGA & NATUROPATHY,UNANI,SIDDHA AND HOMEOPATHY (AYUSH)


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the schemes being implemented by AYUSH in the State of Uttar Pradesh during the last three years, district-wise; (b) the details of the funds allocated under the said schemes; and (c) the number of AYUSH wellness centres in the State of Uttar Pradesh, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF AYUSH(SHRI SARBANANDA SONOWAL)(a) & (b)Ministry of Ayush is implementing the Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the National AYUSH Mission (NAM) though States/UTs including Uttar Pradesh for development and promotion of AYUSH systems and grant-in-aid is provided to them as per the proposals received in their State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs). Under NAM, as per the proposals received from the State Government of Uttar Pradesh through SAAP, grant-in-aid of Rs. 30151.699 lakhs has been released to themduring the last three years for various activities which inter-alia includesetting up of 23 fifty bedded Integrated AYUSH hospitals, establishment of 592 AYUSH Health &Wellness Centres and upgradation of 10 under graduate/post graduateGovernment AYUSH Educational institutions and upgradation of 149 AYUSH Dispensaries. However, under NAM, no grant-in-aid is released district-wise.(c)  The number of AYUSH Health and Wellness Centres (AYUSH HWCs) in the State of Uttar Pradesh supported under NAM,district-wise is furnished at Annexure.
