                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1908






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Eligibility for Doctorate of Medicine




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE   be pleased to state: (a) the shortage in numbers of Doctorate of Medicine (MD) degree holders in the country and the details thereof, State/UT-wise; (b) the details of age limit and eligibility prescribed for Doctorate of Medicine (DM) and Doctor of Medicine (MD); (c) whether the Government proposes to remove the age limit condition for doing DM on the lines of MD examination through written exams for all the branches in view of the shortage of DM degree holders in the country; and (d) if so, the time by which the said relaxation is likely to be made?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):   Shortage of doctors in public and private setup varies in different States. Health being a State subject, the primary responsibility to ensure availability of doctors in the State/UTs lies with the State/UT Governments and no data with regard to shortage of doctors is maintained centrally. However, the details of State wise postgraduate medical seats including Doctor of Medicine (MD) and Doctorate of Medicine (DM) is at Annexure. (b) to (d):   There is no age limit prescribed for doing Doctor of Medicine (MD) and Doctorate of Medicine (DM). ………………
