                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2406






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.07.2019






Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya in Bihar




Rama Devi













                                                    Giridhari Yadav
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is considering to increase the number of Jawahar NavodayaVidyalayas in Bihar;(b) if so, the details thereof and the location-wise details of the places in Bihar where JawaharNavodaya Vidyalayas are functioning at present;(c) if not, the reasons therefor; and(d) the reaction of the Government in this regard?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a): No, Sir.(b): The location-wise details of the places in Bihar where Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas arefunctioning at present are annexed.(c) & (d): Navodaya Vidyalaya Scheme envisages opening of one Navodaya Vidyalaya in eachdistrict of the country and all the districts in the state of Bihar have been covered under the scheme.*****
