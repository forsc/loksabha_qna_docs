                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

986






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






National Mission on Desalination




Kanimozhi Karunanidhi














                        Will the Minister of





SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is a fact that the Government is empanelling solution/technology providers under National Mission on Desalination; and (b) 	if so, the details thereof, indicating the details of institutions/technologies empanelled so far under the mission and the nature of their service?






ANSWER




                                                        (MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE; MINISTER OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY; AND MINISTER OF EARTH SCIENCES(DR. HARSH VARDHAN)????????? ?? ?????? ?????? ??????; ??????? ?? ???????????? ??????; ?? ?????? ??????? ????????. ???? ?????(a) & (b): Yes, Madam. Government has undertaken exercise to enroll solution/ technology providers under National Mission on Desalination. The exercise has resulted in enrolment of 46 solution/ technology providers representing industry, academia and R&D institutions. The activities/services of these organizations range from Research, Development and Demonstration to commercial deployment. The technologies proposed include reverse osmosis, electro dialysis, low temperature thermal desalination, solar assisted distillation etc. The details are at Annexure-I- 1 -
