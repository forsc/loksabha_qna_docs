                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

132






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.12.2021






National Livestock Mission Portal




Su Thirunavukkarasar














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has launched a National Livestock Mission portal recently;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the benefits likely to be accrued to the Animal Husbandry sector with the launching of this portal;(d) whether the Government has also announced a special package for the livestock sector; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE  MINISTER OF  FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING??????????, ??????? ?? ????? ??????(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) to ( e)  A Statement is laid on the table of the house STATEMENT REFERRED IN REPLY OF LOKSABHA STARRED QUESTION NO. 132 PART (a) to (e) ON NATIONAL LIVESTOCK MISSION PORTAL(a) & (b) Online portal for National Livestock Mission (NLM) has been launched on September 06, 2021 during the national level consultation meeting with State Animal Husbandry Ministers. 		Department of Animal Husbandry & Dairying (DAHD) is implementing National Livestock Mission, which aims towards employment generation, entrepreneurship development, increase  per animal productivity, feed and fodder development targeting increased production of meat, goat milk, egg and wool and mitigating the demand and supply gap of feed and fodder.  In order to facilitate the beneficiaries to submit their application under entrepreneurship programme and also to facilitate the State Government to submit their proposals for seeking assistance under National Livestock Mission, the online portal www.nlm.udyamimitra.in has been developed and launched.(c)  This portal will increase the effectiveness and transparency in the overall scheme implementation process.  The portal enables a customized “End to End digital workflow amongst Applicants to submit the application, State Implementing Agency (SIA) to screen and process the applications, Lenders to process the loan applications, State Level Executive Committee (SLEC) to approve and recommend the proposals for subsidy claim, and sending of subsidy to the beneficiary by Central Government directly to the beneficiary account through Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI). This portal enables the beneficiaries to reach to the handholding agencies for preparation of various scheme documents required for loan from the Bank and Knowledge sharing etc. The beneficiaries can also able to know the status of their application submitted in the portal. Thus the portal will provide a seamless “End to End digital journey” experience to the entrepreneurs, Lenders, State and Central agencies with full transparency and utmost ease. (d) & (e) The Central Government has approved implementation of a Livestock Sector Package consisting of several activities by revising and realigning various components of existing schemes in order to have focused development of animal husbandry and dairying across the country for next 5 years starting from 2021-22.  This package envisages Central Government’s support amounting to Rs.9800 crore over duration of 5 years for leveraging total investment of Rs. 54,618 crore for 5 years. ****
