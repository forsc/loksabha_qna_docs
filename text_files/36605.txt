                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3632






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.03.2022






Expressway Projects




Magunta Sreenivasulu Reddy














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the new expressway projects accorded approval by 	the Government during each of the last three years and the 	current 	year, State/UT-wise;(b)	the number of expressways completed so far along with the 	expenditure incurred thereon during the said period, State/UT-	wise;(c)	the number of such pending projects along with the reasons 	therefor;(d)	the time by which these pending projects are likely to be 	completed; and(e)	whether the Government proposes to establish Expressways 	Authority on the pattern of National Highways Authority of 	India and 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI) (a) to (d)   Ahmedabad-Vadodara Expressway, Eastern Peripheral Expressway and Delhi-Meerut Expressway have been completed and construction of Dwarka Expressway is in advance stage. As part of Phase I of Bharatmala Pariyojana, five Expressways of about 2500 km length with cost of Rs. 1.65 lakh crore, namely Delhi-Vadodara-Mumbai Expressway, Delhi-Amritsar-Katra Expressway, Lucknow-Kanpur Expressway, Bengaluru-Chennai Expressway and Ahmedabad-Dholera Expressway are in various stages of construction/award. All the works identified under Bharatmala Pariyojana Phase-I, including expressways, are targeted for completion by 2026-27.(e)	At present, there is no such proposal.*****
