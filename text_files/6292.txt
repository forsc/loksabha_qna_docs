                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

198






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Buildings Identified by ASI




Dulal Chandra Goswami













                                                    Shardaben Anilbhai Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the buildings of Archaeological importance identified under Indian Archaeological Survey (ASI) so far in the country; (b)	whether it has come to the notice of the Government that the buildings of Archaeological importance have been destroyed and these sites have been developed as hotels, clubs, complexes and marriage halls; and (c)	if so, the details thereof along with the action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR CULTURE AND TOURISM(PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a)	There are 3691 monuments/sites declared Centrally Protected under the Ancient Monuments and Archaeological Sites and Remains Act, 1958 and looked after by Archaeological Survey of India (ASI).  A statement indicating State/UT-wise number of monuments/sites is given at Annexure.(b)	No such incident has been reported at any centrally protected monument.(c)	Does not arise.
