                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

523






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Setting up New Greenfield Airports in the Country




Anto  Antony














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of CIVIL AVIATIONbe pleased to state:-(a) Whether the Government has received new proposals for setting up of new greenfield airports in the country;(b) if so, the details of such proposals received and the NOC given by the Government during the last three years and the current year, year and State-wise;(c) whether there is a proposal from the State of Kerala for setting up a Greenfield airport in Pathanamthitta Parliamentary Constituency; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the steps taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) & (b): During the last three years and the current year, the Government of India has received proposals for setting up of new greenfield airports at Mandi in Himachal Pradesh, Kasargod and Kottayam in Kerala, Pantnagar in Uttarakhand and Purandar (Pune) in Maharashtra under the Greenfield Airports Policy. "In- Principle" and "Site Clearance" approval given by the Government during the last three years and the current year under the Greenfield Airports Policy is at Annexure.;(c) & (d): No proposal has been received for development of an airport in Pathanamthitta District, Kerala from the State Government. However, Kerala State Industrial Development Corporation (KSIDC) has submitted a proposal for grant of ''site clearance'' approval for setting up of Greenfield Airport at Kottayam in Kerala.
