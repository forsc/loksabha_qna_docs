                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the State/UT-wise details of the Museum Grant Scheme;(b)	the number of new museums approved during the last five years, State/UT-wise;(c)	the details of the funds disbursed for the scheme and the amount utilized therefrom, State/UT-wise;(d)	whether any scheme has been prepared regarding digitisation and linking of Archaeological museums through a single portal; and(e)	if so, the steps taken/ being taken by the Government to update the records of the museums in digital format?

(c)	The State/UT-wise details of funds disbursed and amount utilized under “Museum Grant Scheme” during last five years (2016-17 to 2021-22) is at Annexure-III.(d)	& (e): The Ministry of Culture has taken up the task of digitisation of collections of ten museums under its domain through Jatan programme since April, 2014.
