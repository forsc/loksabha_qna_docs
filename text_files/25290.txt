                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1040






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

26.07.2021






Special Scheme for Promotion of Tourism in Tribal Areas




Gajendra Singh Patel














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether any special scheme has been implemented in the tribal areas to promote tourism; (b) 	the measures being taken to promote tourism in the tribal areas; (c) 	whether any new scheme to promote tourism is likely to be implemented in the tribal areas in near future; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF TOURISM 			        (SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) to (d):	Ministry of Tourism promotes India as a holistic destination including tribal areas under the Incredible India brand-line  and promotional activities in domestic and international markets are undertaken through its schemes of ‘Domestic Promotion & Publicity including Hospitality (DPPH)’ and Overseas Promotion & Publicity Including Market Development Assistance (OPMD). As part of its on-going activities, the Ministry releases print, electronic, online, social media platforms and outdoor media campaigns in the international and domestic markets, to promote various tourism destinations and products of the country including tribal areas. In addition under Domestic Promotion Publicity and Hospitality (DPPH) Scheme, Ministry of Tourism provides CFA for fairs & festivals and shopping festivals including tribal festivals. Submission of project proposal by the State Governments/ Union Territory Administrations and its sanctioning is a continuous process. Details attached at Annexure – I.Tourist destinations are identified by the respective State Governments / Union Territory for development and promotions, the Ministry of Tourism under its schemes of Swadesh Darshan, and Assistance to Central Agencies provides Central Financial Assistance (CFA) to State Governments/ UT Administrations for development of tourism infrastructure including tribal circuits in the country.  The details of the projects sanctioned under Tribal Circuit of Swadesh Darshan Scheme are as placed at ANNEXURE – II.*******
