                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1002






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.06.2019






Airport Tariff




Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has decided to have a pre-determined tariff structure linked to inflation for airports across the country;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the reasons for wide variation in airport tariff across the country; and(c) the steps taken by the Government to address the issues hindering investments in the Indian Aviation Sector?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c): In order to encourage private partners in the Infrastructure projects, the Government has decided to introduce an enabling clause in Airport Economic Regulatory Authority (AERA) Act, 2008 to adopt pre-determined tariff, if such tariff has been incorporated in the bid document, which is the basis for award of airport. This decision brings regulatory certainty to the private investors.;
