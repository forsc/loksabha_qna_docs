                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2062






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.11.2019






Fund for Schemes




Gowdar Mallikarjunappa Siddeshwara














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of funds granted/allocated under various schemes of the   Ministry to Karnataka during the last three years;(b) 	the details of funds released under those schemes to the State during that period; and (c) 	the details of funds yet to be released to the State ?






ANSWER

Funds under various schemeshave been released to the State of Karnataka, for which the Utilization Certificates/Statement of Expenditures have been received.AnnexureStatement referred to in reply to part (a) to (c) of the Lok Sabha Unstarred Question No. 2062 for 29.11.2019 by Shri G.M.
