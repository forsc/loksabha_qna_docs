                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

881






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.12.2021






Expansion of Shri Guru Ram Das Ji International Airport




Gurjeet Singh Aujla














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether Shri Guru Ram Das Ji International Airport at Amritsar is the largest airport in Punjab;(b) whether the Government has any plans to expand the integrated passenger terminal building and if so, the time by which it is likely to commence work; and(c) whether the Government plans to lease out the Amritsar Airport on Public-Private Partnership and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        Minister of State in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (GEN. (DR) V. K. SINGH (RETD))(a) & (b): Shri Guru Ram Das Ji International Airport at Amritsar is the largest airport in Punjab in terms of land area and runway length. Development and up-gradation of airports is a continuous process and is undertaken by Airports Authority of India (AAI) from time to time depending upon commercial viability, traffic demand, availability of land etc. ;(c): The Airports Authority of India (AAI) has identified Amritsar airport among 13 airports for operations, management and development of the airport through Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode in the public interest and for better management of the airport.
