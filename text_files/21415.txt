                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    DEFENCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2724






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.03.2021






MOUs Signed by the Government in Defence Sector




Shyam Yadav Singh














                        Will the Minister of





DEFENCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any agreement, MoU has been signed by the Government in the year of 2020 and in the current year so far in the defence sector; (b) if so, the details thereof; and (c) the details of the ammunition and war artillery purchased from other countries after the announcement of the Aatma Nirbhar Bharat?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE					(SHRI SHRIPAD NAIK)IN THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE   (a) & (b):  The following important Agreements/MoUs have been signed:-(i)  MoU between the Government of the Republic of India and the Government of the Republic of Finland on Cooperation in the field of Production, Procurement, Research and Development of Defence Related Equipment and Industrial Cooperation, singed on 15th January, 2020;(ii)  Arrangement between the Australian Department of Defence and Ministry of Defence of the Republic of India concerning Mutual Logistic Support, signed on 28th May, 2020;(iii)  Agreement between Government of the Republic of India and the Government of Japan concerning Reciprocal Provision of Supplies and Services between the Indian Armed Forces and the Self-Defence Forces of Japan, signed on 20th September, 2020; and (iv)  Basic Exchange and Cooperation Agreement (BECA) between the Ministry of Defence of the Republic of India and the National Geospatial Intelligence Agency (NGA) of the Department of Defence of the United States of America concerning Geospatial Intelligence, signed on 26th October, 2020.(c)  Since the announcement of the Aatma Nirbhar Bharat initiatives, certain emergent and operational requirements were met through procurement of some ammunition and war artillery.*****
