                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4873






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2021






Benefits of NRE Schemes




Dileshwar Kamait













                                                    Geeta Kora
                                                








                                                    Vishnu Dutt Sharma
                                                








                                                    Jugal Kishore Sharma
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the people are unable to get benefits of schemes  related to  new and  renewable  energy due  to lack of knowledge;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the  steps  being  taken  by  the  Government  to make people aware of the said schemes; and(d)	the  number  of  beneficiaries  under  the  New National  Biogas  and  Organic  Manure  Programme (NNBOMP) in Bihar, Jammu & Kashmir, Jharkhand and Madhya Pradesh, district-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (I/C) FOR NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY, POWER and MoS for  SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP(SHRI R.K. SINGH)(a)	No, Sir. (b)	Question does not arise. (c)	Information about MNRE’s programmes is disseminated via print, electronic and digital media. MNRE’s website is a particularly source of such information. Further MNRE’s implementing agencies in State also publicize MNRE’s Programmes. (d)The district-wise number of beneficiaries under the New National Biogas and Organic Manure Programme (NNBOMP) during the last three years and the current year ( up to 28.02.2021)  in the State of Bihar, Jammu & Kashmir, Jharkhand and Madhya Pradesh is given at Annexure –I. *****
