                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1094






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.09.2020






NCPCR Funds for NGO




Sanjeev Kumar Singari














                        Will the Minister of





WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















(a)	the funds provided by the National Commission for Protection of Child Rights (NCPCR) to Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs) during each of the last two years and the current year, NGO-wise;(b)	whether the cases of misuse of the funds meant for the welfare of children by certain NGOs have come to the notice of NCPCR during the said period; and(c)	if so, the details thereof and the  action taken thereon?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF WOMEN AND CHILD DEVELOPMENT(SHRIMATI SMRITI ZUBIN IRANI)(a) 	As informed by National Commission for Protection of Child Rights (NCPCR), the following funds have been provided by NCPCR to Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs) during each of the last two years and current year:S.No.	Year	Name of NGO	Amounti.	i	2018-19	Udyan Care	Rs.147500/- (Rupees One Lakh Forty Seven Thousand Five Hundred Only)		Cyme of Social Development	Rs.283200/- (Rupees Two Lakhs Eighty Three Thousand Two Hundred Only)ii.	ii	2019-20	Shikha Sanskriti Uthyan Nyas	Rs.300000/- (Rupees Three Lakhs only)		Indore Cycling Association	Rs.300000/- (Rupees Three Lakhs only)iii.	iii	2020-21	NIL	NIL(b) and (c) It has been informed by NCPCR that no such cases have come to their notice.*****
