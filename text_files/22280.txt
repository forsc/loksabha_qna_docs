                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3301






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.03.2021






Assistance to Farmers during Natural Calamities




Mahabali Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government provides any assistance to the farmers of Bihar during natural calamities;(b) if so, the details thereof along with the assistance provided to Bihar at the Central level during the last three years;(c) whether there has been undue delay in providing assistance and relief work during natural calamities in Bihar; and(d) the reaction of the Government thereto along with the norms and rules of procedure to provide assistance and relief work to farmers of the States during natural calamities?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a):	The primary responsibility of disaster management rests with the State Governments. The Central Government supplements the efforts of the State Governments and provides requisite logistics and financial support. For providing relief to the affected people, including farmers, in case of 12 notified natural disasters, State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF) -2-L.S.US.Q.NO.3301 FOR 16.03.2021is placed at the disposal of the State Governments, including State Government of Bihar. Additional financial assistance is extended from National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF), as per the established procedure in case of disaster of ‘severe nature’.(b):	Details of the funds, released to the State Government of Bihar during the financial year 2018-19 to 2020-21 from SDRF and NDRF, is as under:-(Rs. in Crore)Financial Year	Centre’s Share of SDRF Released	Fund released from NDRF2018-19	101.815	--2019-20	631.12	953.17 for Floods2020-21(as on10.03.2021)	1416.00	1255.27 for Floods(c) and (d):	    The distribution of relief assistance, to the affected people at the ground, is the responsibility of the State Government. The State Government provides relief assistance to the farmers in the form of input subsidy in case of damages to crops due to natural calamity as per the Government of India’s items and norms which are available on MHA website https://ndmindia.mha.gov.in/images/pdf/SDRF-NDRFNorms-dated8april2015.pdf.  *****
