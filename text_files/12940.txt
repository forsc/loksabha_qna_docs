                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FINANCE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1621






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.03.2020






Public Procurement




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





FINANCE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether it is true that India’s public procurement is currently 30 per cent of the GDP; (b)	if so, the details thereof; (c)	whether the Government is planning to bring a comprehensive legislation on public procurement;(d)	if so, the details thereof; and (e)	the measures taken by the Government to adopt or include the recommendations/observations of the Task Force on Sustainable Procurement in the public procurement policy/legislation?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FINANCE(SHRI ANURAG SINGH THAKUR) (a) &(b)	There is no official estimate in this regard.(c) &(d)	No Sir.(e)	The recommendations/ observations of the Task Force on Sustainable Procurement have not been received by the Government.
