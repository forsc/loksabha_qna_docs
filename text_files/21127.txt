                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2440






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.03.2021






Action Plan for Drip Irrigation




Ramapati Ram Tripathi














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government has started a new action plan for drip-irrigation on the basis of geographical survey conducted across the country including Uttar Pradesh; (b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (c)	whether the Government has sanctioned funds for the drip-irrigation schemes started in Uttar Pradesh; (d)	if so, the details thereof; (e)	whether the Government proposes to sanction and release funds especially for the alkaline soil of Deoria district in Uttar Pradesh; and (f)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????	(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):	The Department of Agriculture, Cooperation & Farmers Welfare (DAC&FW) is implementing a Centrally Sponsored Scheme of  Per Drop More Crop component of Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY-PDMC) from 2015-16 in all the States of the country which focuses on enhancing water use efficiency at farm level through Micro Irrigation systems viz. Drip and Sprinkler irrigation systems. Besides promoting Micro Irrigation, this component also supports micro level water storage or water conservation/management activities to supplement source creation for Micro Irrigation.	District Irrigation Plans (DIPs) are the cornerstone for planning and implementation of the PMKSY-PDMC.  Accordingly, the Annual Action Plan for implementation of the scheme is drawn by States from DIPs.  	An area of 53.73 lakh ha has been covered under Micro Irrigation (Drip irrigation 28.23 lakh ha & Sprinkler Irrigation 25.50 lakh ha) in the country through PMKSY-PDMC from 2015-16 to till date. Further, 4.85 lakh micro level water harvesting / secondary storage structures have been created under the scheme to supplement the micro irrigation. The State wise details of coverage of Micro Irrigation and no of micro level water harvesting / secondary storage structures created is Annexed.(c) & (d):	Under the PMKSY-PDMC, Central Assistance of Rs. 421.79 crore  has been released to the State of Uttar Pradesh from 2015-16 to till date including Rs. 100.00 crore  released during the current financial year.  (e) & (f):	There is no proposal as of now to sanction and release of funds for the alkaline soils of Deoria district in Uttar Pradesh.
