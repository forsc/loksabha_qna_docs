                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3069






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.07.2019






Transportation of Mortal Remains




Kumbakudi Sudhakaran














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has issued any guidelines for transporting mortal remains of Indians from foreign countries  and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has agreed to transport bodies at a flat rate against the practice of levying charges on the basis of the weight of the body including the coffin and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government has agreed to transport bodies of children less than the age of 12 years at a price half of the flat rate and if so, the details thereof; and(d) whether the Government is willing to transport mortal remains from the middle east for free and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (c) : No, Sir. However, Air India has issued two circulars dated 02.01.2019 with guidelines on the subject of carriage of Mortal Remains from  Gulf/UAE, which include the following:;;i. For carriage of mortal remains from Gulf/UAE to India, a fixed sum to be levied, on any direct Air India flight including Air India Express;ii. In case of mortal remains of children(below 12 years of age), 50 percent discount on the flat charges;;(d): Air India carries human remains free of cost from Gulf countries to India on receipt of request from the concerned Indian Embassy.
