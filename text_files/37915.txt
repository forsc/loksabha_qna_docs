                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5062






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.04.2022






SWAYAM PRABHA Programme




Prathap Simha













                                                    Umesh G. Jadhav
                                                








                                                    S. Muniswamy
                                                








                                                    L.S. Tejasvi Surya
                                                








                                                    Sanganna Amarappa Karadi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the expected date of launch of additional channels for decimating regional content under the PM e-Vidya and/or Study Webs of Active Learning for Young Aspiring Minds (SWAYAM PRABHA) and in case of phase-wise launch, the expected date of each phase, along with the number of channels being launched during that phase;(b) the number of channels to be launched and the funds allocated for the same for the present year in PM e-Vidya and SWAYAM PRABHA; and(c) the review mechanism in place for the effectiveness of the content being aired on PM e-Vidya and SWAYAM PRABHA?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION(SMT. ANNPURNA DEVI)(a) & (b) As per budget announcement for Financial Year 2022-23, 200 (Two hundred) PM eVIDYA Swayam Prabha DTH TV Channels were announced. 12 Channels in school education and 22 channels in higher education are already functional.(c) The content development for TV is done under a Standard operating Procedure (SoP). CIET, NCERT is the nodal agency and SCERTs of States and UTs coordinate the effort based on guidance by CIET, NCERT.**********
