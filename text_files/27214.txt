                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2802






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.08.2021






Renovation of Lighthouses




A. Ganeshamurthi













                                                    S. Ramalingam
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PORTS, SHIPPING AND WATERWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of total number of lighthouses under the control of the Union Government as on date, State-wise; (b)	whether it is true that the Union Government has undertaken renovation of lighthouse functioning in Mamallapuram in Tamil Nadu; (c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	the details of the funds spent for the renovation works?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	Sir, the State-wise details of total number of lighthouses under the control of the Union Government, as on date, is as under:-<pre>Sl. No.	State/Union Territory	No. of Lighthouses1	Gujarat	382	Maharashtra	263	Goa	34	Karnataka	95	Kerala	166	Tamil Nadu	227	Andhra Pradesh	188	Odisha 	69	West Bengal	310	Andaman & Nicobar Islands	3511	Dadra & Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu	112	Lakshadweep	1613	Puducherry 	2Total No. of Lighthouses	195                                         </pre>(b)	to (d)  Yes, Sir.The functioning of the Lighthouse at Mamallapuram(Tamil Nadu)has been improved by replacing the Metal Halide Lamp illuminant with high intensity LED illuminant developed under Atmanirbhar Bharat initiative and an amount of Rs. 6,01,800/- (Rupees Six Lakh One Thousand and Eight Hundred) has been spent for the renovation works.*****
