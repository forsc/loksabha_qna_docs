                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3637






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Problems Faced by Farmers




Kaushalendra Kumar














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether it is not true that our country is famous for its agriculture throughout the world;(b) whether it is also not true that our farmers are facing a lot of problems like lack of basic amenities of irrigation systems, short and long term loan facilities,  availability of high quality seeds and after that sale of their produce at MSP, if so, the details thereof;(c) whether it is not true that widespread exploitation of farmers at the hands of moneylenders is a common issue that immediately needs to be looked into;(d) whether it is a fact that farmers are committing suicide; and(e) if so, whether the Government has any plan to revive the agriculture sector in the country?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ?????? ( SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR )  (a) to (c): 	Agriculture is the backbone of the Indian economy. 54.6% of the total workforce is engaged in agriculture and allied sector (Census 2011) and it accounts for 18.4%(at current prices) of the country’s Gross Value Added (GVA) for the year 2019-20.The Government through its various schemes and policies is providing assistance to the farming community such as (1) irrigation facilities through the Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana. (2) Loan facilities are provided through the Interest Subvention Scheme. (3) Under Sub-Mission on Seed and Planting Material (SMSP) production and multiplication of quality seeds of all crops are promoted so that the required quantities of seeds can be made available in time to farmers. (4) Government fixes Minimum Support Prices (MSPs) of 22 mandated agricultural crops on the basis of recommendation of commission for Agricultural Costs & Prices (CACP) after considering important factors like cost of production, overall demand supply conditions, domestic and International prices etc.For institutional credit to farmers, the Modified Interest Subvention Scheme is being operated. In addition, PM KIsan SAmman Nidhi Yojana (PM-KISAN) is an income support scheme for farmers. (d): 	The National Crime Records Bureau (NCRB) under the Ministry of Home Affairs compiles and disseminates information on suicides in its publication titled ‘Accidental Deaths and Suicides in India’ (ADSI). These Reports on suicides upto 2019 are available on its website.(e): 	Agriculture is a State subject. However, Government of India supplements the efforts of States through appropriate policy measures and budgetary support. A list of various initiatives taken by the Government to revive the agriculture sector are at Annexure-I.
