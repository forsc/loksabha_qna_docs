                                    HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA

                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1636






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






SCM in Haryana




Brijendra Singh














                        Will the Minister of





HOUSING AND URBAN AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















(a) 	the amount spent so far on the projects under Smart City Mission (SCM) 	in Haryana along with the heads under which the money has been 	spent; (b) 	the number of projects completed in Haryana under SCM; and (c) 	the status report of all smart city works undertaken including the 	deadline for completion of the same?
