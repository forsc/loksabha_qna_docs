                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

02.02.2022






Wastage of Foodgrains




 Dharambir Singh














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether a huge quantity of food items gets wasted every year and if so, the plan to stop such wastage along with the details thereof; (b) 	the State-wise details of seven main food items wasted during the last five years along with the value thereof; and (c) 	whether any reasons for this wastage have been found and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a): 	No Sir.(b): 	The state-wise and year-wise details on the value and volume of foodgrains (wheat and rice), which got damaged in the godowns of Food Corporation of India (FCI) are as under: …..2/--2-(Fig in Tonnes)Region	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21 Bihar	0	1617	3568	0	5Jharkhand	0	45	31	0	12Odisha	1	0	2	87	1445West Bengal	0	0	12	0	79Assam	205	328	868	2	28NEF	105	92	58	27	0Nagaland	3	0	0	0	0Delhi	12	14	2	44	34Haryana	0	0	0	286	0J & K	0	0	0	0	8Punjab	8	211	318	56	25Rajasthan	125	1	0	2	84Uttar Pradesh	48	243	116	26	69Andhra Pradesh	6	0	0	640	0Kerala	88	2	113	32	14Karnataka	13	75	53	10	8Tamil Nadu	66	16	6	1	0Gujarat	119	6	19	694	34Maharashtra	7963	14	35	18	0Madhya Pradesh	0	0	5	0	0Chhattisgarh	12	0	7	5	6Country Total	8776	2663	5213	1930	1850Value in Crore (in Rs)	9.12	3.22	6.73	2.61	2.77(Figs are rounded off).(c): 	Yes Sir. Natural calamities like floods and cyclones are the major reasons for this damage of foodgrains.*****
