                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2697






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

04.12.2019






NLCI Ltd.




Thirumaa Valavan Thol














                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) 	the details of item-wise spending of CSR by Nayveli Lignite Corporation Limited (NLCI) during 2014-18;(b)	whether NLCI an important PSU is spending most of the CSR amount in other than service area; and(c)	if so, whether the Government will instruct the management of NLCI Limited to follow the guidelines and carry out CSR activities and the details thereof?






ANSWER




  MINISTER OF PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES(SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI) (a):The details of item-wise spending of CSR by Neyveli Lignite Corporation Limited (NLCIL) during 2014-18 are as follows :NLCIL:Sl.No.	CSR Activities	 	Expenditure (Rs. Crore)		2014-15  	2015-16  	2016-17  	2017-18 	2018-191	Health Care, Family Welfare and Sanitation	6.73	43.81	9.25	6.39	9.502	Providing Drinking Water Supply Facilities	0.90	0.13	0.17	2.07	1.513	Providing Education including Special Education, Vocational skills, Scholarships 	11.42	23.14	10.97	9.93	13.534	Women Empowerment / Gender Equality	0.09	0.21	0.17	1.32	0.375	Environmental Sustainability	0.04	0.12	0.18	3.03	2.436	Promoting Sports in Rural Areas	0.03	-	0.13	0.55	1.207	Water Resource Augmentation, Irrigation and Flood Control Works for Rural Development	10.97	2.87	7.00	9.62	15.098	Providing Link Roads/ Access for Rural Development	11.78	9.85	2.19	7.90	2.169	Other Community Assets for Rural Development including Construction of  infrastructure like Schools/ Libraries / Laboratories	0.24	0.17	7.11	-	-10	Heritage, Arts and Culture 	0.23	0.62	0.02	0.12	0.1411	Relief / Rehabilitation of areas Affected by Disasters and Welfare of SC,ST& OBC	4.05	1.01	-	2.66	3.5312	Research / technology incubation project	1.01	-	-	-	-Total	47.49	81.93	37.19	43.59	49.46 (b):As per NLCIL’s CSR Policy, NLCIL is spending most of the CSR amount only in the operating regions of NLCIL units.(c): Not applicable in view of reply to part (b) above. ******
