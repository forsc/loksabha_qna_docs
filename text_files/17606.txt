                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1333






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.09.2020






Profit/Loss of GAIL




Amol Ramsing Kolhe













                                                    Kuldeep Rai Sharma
                                                








                                                    Subhash Ramrao Bhamre
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    Manickam Tagore B
                                                








                                                    DNV Senthilkumar. S.
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to push to raise the share of natural gas in India’s energybasket to 15 per cent by 2030 from the current 6.2 per cent and if so, the steps taken by theGovernment to achieve this goal;(b) the details of profit earned/loss incurred by GAIL (India) Ltd. during the last three years andthe steps taken to enhance the profit;(c) whether the GAIL (India) Ltd. plans to invest large amount of money over the next five yearsto expand the National Gas Pipeline Grid and City Gas Distribution Network to help push forgreater use of environment friendly fuel and if so, the details thereof; and(d) the details of the projects being operated by GAIL (India) Ltd. in different parts of thecountry along with the present status/ progress of the said projects, particularly in Tamil Naduand Maharashtra along with the projects which are at planning stage?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI DHARMENDRA PRADHAN)(a) Natural Gas being an alternative cleaner fossil fuel has a major role in catering thegrowing energy demand in a sustainable manner. To increase the share of gas in the primaryenergy mix of the country, Government is progressively taking steps to enhance domestic gasproduction and develop requisite gas infrastructure. Details of major initiatives to increase theshare of natural gas in India’s energy basket are as under:Upstream Sector : Enhancing domestic gas production (a) issuing of Pricing Guidelines, 2014;(b)Marketing & pricing freedom to gas production from difficult & new field;(c) HELP Policy;(d) Discovered Small Field Policy etc.Midstream Sector: Developing additional gas infrastructure capacities by (a) Completion ofNational Gas Grid and (b) Establishing new LNG import facilitiesDownstream Sector : Development of downstream sector through (a) Development of CGDnetworks across country- presently covered 407 districts spread over 27 States/UTs for CGDdevelopment (b) Promoting gas in Refineries and in Steel industries, (c) Creation of free gasmarket and (d) Sustainable Alternative Towards Affordable Transportation (SATAT).(b) Profit After Tax (PAT) during last 3 years of GAIL (India) Ltd. is as under:Financial Year PAT (Rupees in crore)2017-18 4618.002018-19 6026.002019-20 6620.00GAIL (India) Ltd. is working towards developing gas sector as its profitability also depends ontransportation tariff and marketing margin and their profit will enhance by increasing the marketshare, reducing the cost and improving the efficiency .(c) GAIL (India) Limited is involved in expanding its natural gas pipelines and city gasdistribution networks over the next five years. The projects being undertaken primarily consistsof 17 natural gas pipelines and 35 CGD networks across various cities.(d) GAIL(India) Ltd. is implementing 114 projects at Pan-India level including projects in thestate of Tamil Nadu and Maharashtra, which are under different stages of implementation. Totalproject cost of all 114 projects is approximately Rs. 90,000 crore. Projects primarily consist ofnatural gas pipeline projects, CGD projects and other projects of GAIL and those of its jointventure/subsidiary companies. Details are available at GAIL(India) Ltd.''s website athttps://gailonline.com/BV-NarutalGas.html.*****
