                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TOURISM






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

220






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.06.2019






PRASAD Scheme




K. Muraleedharan














                        Will the Minister of





TOURISM


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spirituality Augmentation Drive (PRASAD) scheme; (b) 	whether the Government has included Pilgrimage Centre like Sabarimala & Guruvayur temple in Kerala under the Scheme; (c) 	if so, the details thereof; and (d) 	the amount sanctioned by the Union Government to the State in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR TOURISM (INDEPENDENT CHARGE)(SHRI PRAHLAD SINGH PATEL)(a) to (d):	Under the Scheme “Pilgrimage Rejuvenation and Spiritual, Heritage Augmentation Drive” (PRASHAD) Ministry of Tourism provides Central Financial Assistance for infrastructure development and beautification of Pilgrimage Centres/Tourist Places on receipt of suitable DPRs submitted by State Governments/Union Territories, subject to availability of funds, liquidation of pending utilisation certificate against the fund released earlier and adherence to the relevant scheme guidelines.  The project “Development at Guruvayur Temple, under the PRASHAD Scheme was approved by the Ministry at the cost of Rs.46.14 Crore in 2016-17. The amount Rs.36.91 Crore has been released so far.The details of the projects sanctioned under the scheme is annexed. *******
