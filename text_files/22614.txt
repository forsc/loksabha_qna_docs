                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3822






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Proposal for International Airport in Kanpur




Satyadev Pachauri














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to convert Kanpur domestic airport into international airport and if not, the reasons therefor;(b) whether the Government has received any proposal from the Government of Uttar Pradesh in this regard and if so, the details thereof;(c) whether the Government thinks it appropriate to convert Kanpur airport at par with international airport in view of the reputation of Kanpur earned at national and international industrial world; and(d) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): Declaration of an airport as an International airport depends upon the traffic potential and the demand from airlines for operation of international flights besides provision of Ground Lighting Facilities, Instrument Landing System, adequate runway length, Immigration, Health and Animal & Plant Quarantine services etc.(b): Presently, no proposal regarding declaration of Kanpur Airport as an International Airport has been received from the State Govt. of Uttar Pradesh. (c) & (d): Development / upgradation of airports is a continuous process and is undertaken by Airports Authority of India from time to time depending on the availability of land,  commercial viability, socio-economic considerations and traffic demand / willingness of airlines to operate to / from such airports.
