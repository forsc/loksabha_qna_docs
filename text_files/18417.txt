                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2086






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

23.09.2020






Purchase of Ventilators for COVID-19




Jasbir Singh Gill













                                                    Sudhakar Tukaram Shrangre
                                                








                                                    Mohammad Jawed
                                                








                                                    Doddaalahalli Kempegowda Suresh
                                                








                                                    Dean Kuriakose
                                                








                                                    Sumedhanand Saraswati
                                                








                                                    Sunil Kumar Singh
                                                








                                                    Saptagiri Sankar Ulaka
                                                








                                                    Devji Mansingram Patel
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the number of ventilators, PPE equipment and gloves purchased by the Government since January, 2020, month-wise; and(b) the quantum of funds spent on the aforementioned purchases, head and item-wise?(c) whether the Government has provided any special funds and multiple medical equipments to various hospitals and State/UTs Governments across the country to fight against     COVID-19 epidemic; and(d) if so, the details thereof and the number of these medical equipments supplied to hospitals State/UT wise and district-wise particularly Jharkhand, Latur district in Maharashtra and Sikar district in Rajasthan?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a) & (b):   The government of India has placed the following orders for supply of essential medical equipment   during the period January, 2020 to till date:Sl. No.  Items      		Quantity          Value                                                    		(in crores)1.	   	N-95 Masks   		45909199        491.152.	  	 PPE Coverall  	19222688      1963.413.	   	Ventilators       	600963        2568.40 (c) & (d):	Yes.  State-wise allocation of medical equipment as above is attached as Annexure-A.         The above medical equipments are supplied depending on requirements of States/UTs.  District-wise distribution is done by the States/UTs.         Regarding allocations of funds to different States/UTs,  the details are as under:I.	Rs. 1113.21 crores has been released to the States/UTs during 2019-20 under NHM for management and containment of COVID-19 pandemic.II.	As on 10th September, 2020, Rs. 4256.79 has been released to States/UTs during 2020-21 during under India COVID-19 Emergency Response and Health Systems Preparedness Package.
