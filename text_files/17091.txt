                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

714






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.09.2020






Air Routes under UDAN




Jagdambika Pal 














                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)  the  name  of  the  78  new  air  routes  approved under the UDAN scheme;(b) whether Sarnath, Kushinagar, Siddharth Nagar under  Buddhist  circuit  have  been  included  under  the said  scheme;(c) if so, the time by which these routes are likely to be made operational; and(d) if not, whether the Government has any plan to link Buddhist circuit with air routes?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a) to (d): Airports Authority of India (AAI), the Implementing Agency is in the process of awarding 78 routes identified in 1st phase of bidding received under the Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik) version 4.0.  RCS-UDAN  is a market driven scheme. The interested airlines based on their assessment of demand on particular routes submit their proposals at the time of bidding under RCS - UDAN from time to time. After four rounds of bidding under RCS-UDAN, the Implementing Agency has awarded RCS routes connecting Shravasti, Kushinagar, Gaya, Varanasi of Buddhist circuit to Selected Airline Operators (SAOs). The SAOs are obligated to commence RCS flight operations on a RCS-route, as part of individual route-proposal or a network proposal within a period of 180 days from the issuance of Letter of Award or within two months from the readiness of airport, whichever is later, unless any extension is granted by the Implementing Agency.
