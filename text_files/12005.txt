                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

92






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2020






Ranking of Hospitals




Arun Sao














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) whether the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), Delhi is not among the Top 10 hospitals of the country in the ranking of hospitals as per a survey conducted by the Ministry; (b) if so, the details thereof along with the reaction of the Government thereto; (c) whether 25 per cent patients in the country are not satisfied with the hospitals as per the said survey conducted on the basis of feedback from the patients; (d) if so, the details thereof along with the reasons therefor; and (e) the corrective steps taken/proposed to be taken by the Government to address the shortcomings and strengthen the medical services in the hospitals throughout the country?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (DR. HARSH VARDHAN)(a)	No such survey has been conducted by the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare.(b)	to (e) In view of reply at (a) above, the questions do not arise.
