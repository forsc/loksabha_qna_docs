                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

428






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.02.2021






National Policy on Software




Riti Pathak














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of Electronics and Information Technology be pleased to state:(a)	whether the Government has any national policy for the production of softwares;(b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	the number of people in the country whose aadhar data has been received by the Government and number of people whose aadhar is yet to be made State/UT-wise; (d)	whether the Government has any national policy with regard to Digital India; and (e)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	and (b) : Government has approved National Policy on Software Product-2019, on February 28, 2019 with a vision to create a robust Indian Software Product development ecosystem.  (c): As per the Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI), a total of 127.83 Crore Aadhaar have been generated as on 15th January, 2021. Further, the State/UT wise Aadhaar Saturation as on 15th January, 2021 is attached as Annexure-I.(d) and (e): The Government has approved a number of policies and initiatives under Digital India Programme. These include National Policy on Software Product 2019, National Policy on Electronics 2019, Updation and Usage Policy for the Sampark Database (IT Platform for Masses), Policy on Adoption of Open-Source Software for Government of India etc. Brief details on the major policies are as follows:?	National Policy on Software Product, so as to make India as a global player in development, production and supply of innovative and efficient Software Products, thus facilitating the growth across the entire spectrum of ICT.?	National Policy on Electronics 2019 is focused on electronics manufacturing in India for India and the world.?	Updation and Usage Policy for the Sampark Database (IT Platform for Masses) is towards communication to citizen in quick manner and at mass level. ?	Policy on Adoption of Open-Source Software for Government of India is for having strategic control over the Government applications. ******* Annexure-IState/UT wise Aadhaar Saturation
