Procurement of Foodgrains under Kharif Crops




Dharmendra Kumar Kashyap














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the details of quantity of foodgrains under kharif crops procured during 2021-22 till date, grain and State-wise; (b) 	the details of amount paid to the farmers as on date against procurement of kharif foodgrains during 2021-22 till date, State-wise; (c) 	whether some State Governments have recorded high procurement of kharif foodgrains during the current year; and (d) 	if so, the details thereof, State-wise?

Paddy at Annexure-I  II. Coarsegrain at Annexure-IIIII- Pulses at Annexure-III(c) and (d): 	Yes, Sir. Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Gujarat, Jammu & Kashmir, Madhya Pradesh, Uttrakhand, Tripura and Rajasthan have recorded highest ever paddy procurement during Kharif Marketing Season KMS 2021-22.
