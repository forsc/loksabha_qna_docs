                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3552






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Value Chain Development of Bamboo Products




Devji Mansingram Patel














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Union Government is implementing any scheme for value chain development of bamboo based products in Rajasthan including Sirohi;(b)	if so, the provisions for  entrepreneurs to set up micro and medium processing units under the said scheme;(c)	the total number of micro and medium processing units setup in the country till date including Rajasthan after the launch of the restructured National Bamboo Mission; and(d)	the details thereof, district-wise and State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????		(SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)	Government is implementing the restructured National Bamboo Mission (NBM) from the year 2018-19. However, the scheme is not implemented in Rajasthan.(b)	NBM has components for value addition units, waste management units and micro/medium processing units. The pattern of assistance under NBM is at Annexure-I.(c)	& (d)  So far, 375 numbers of product development and processing units including micro/ medium Processing units set up since the implementation of restructured NBM. The state-wise details of product development and processing units is placed at Annexure-II.
