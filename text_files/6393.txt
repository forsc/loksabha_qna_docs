                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

15






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






Fund Allocation of PMKVY in Maharashtra




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





SKILL DEVELOPMENT AND ENTREPRENEURSHIP


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	 whether the Government has implemented the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana in Maharashtra, if so, the details thereof;(b)	 the number of persons trained under this programme in Maharashtra during the last five years and the current year, district-wise and year-wise, along with the funds allocated for this scheme in Maharashtra during the last five years; and(c) 	whether the Government has launched any programme under skill development to certify skilled persons and if so, the details thereof including target, particularly in Maharashtra?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (c)	Under Skill India Mission, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship is implementing scheme known as Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) 2016-20 in the country including State of Maharashtra. This Scheme enables youth to take Short Term Training (STT) and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) courses through accredited and affiliated training centers (TCs) throughout the country. Under the scheme, till now 69,03,671 candidates have been trained in diverse sectors in the country. Out of these, 6,04,153 candidates have been trained in the State of Maharashtra. Detail of candidates trained and certified under PMKVY 2016-20, district-wise is given at Annexure. PMKVY 2016-20 has two components known as Centrally Sponsored Centrally Managed (CSCM) being implemented by National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC) and Centrally Sponsored State Managed (CSSM) being implemented by State Skill Development Missions of the States/ UTs popularly known as State- Engagement Component of PMKVY (2016-20). Under CSCM component of PMKVY 2016-20, there is no provision for State-wise allocation of funds. Under this component, funds are being released to NSDC which are subsequently released by NSDC on reimbursement basis at various stages of training (commencement of the training after validation, successful certification and placement) to the empanelled training providers and centres. Further, under CSSM component of PMKVY 2016-20, 25% of the funds and corresponding physical targets of PMKVY 2016-20 has been allocated to the States for the implementation of scheme through State Skill Development Missions. Under this component, Ministry has allocated Rs. 214.44 Crore funds to the State of Maharashtra for 4 years (2016-20). On the basis of performance of State, till now Rs. 85.77 Crore have been released under CSSM-PMKVY.AnnexureThe district – wise number of trained and certified candidates under PMKVY 2016-20 in State of Maharashtra, as on 11.11.2019 is given below:S.
