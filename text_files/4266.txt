                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COAL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4055






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

17.07.2019






Underground and Opencast Coal Mines




Kuldeep Rai Sharma













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Sunil Dattatray Tatkare
                                                








                                                    Nishikant  Dubey
                                                








                                                    Heena Vijaykumar Gavit
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





COAL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    a.	the total number of functional and non-functional underground and open cast coal mines in the country, location and State-wise;b.	the quantity of coal extracted from the functional coal mines during the last three years and the current year and the  details of the revenue earned by the Government from these mines;c.	whether the Government proposes to revive the non-functional coal mines;d.	if so, the details thereof, location wise and State-wise;e.	whether the Government proposes to open new coal mines and start mining in some new coal blocks during the current year and the next five years; andf.	if so, the details thereof, mine/ location/State-wise along with the names of the companies, public and private, to whom these mines are likely to assigned for development?






ANSWER




 MINISTER OF  PARLIAMENTARY AFFAIRS, COAL AND MINES  (SHRI PRALHAD JOSHI)(a)-(f): Information is being collected.
