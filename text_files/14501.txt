                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3247






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

13.03.2020






CGHS Wellness Centres in Telangana




Ramulu Pothuganti














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state: (a) the details of present Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS) wellness centres functioning/established in Telangana particularly in Nagarkurnool Parliamentary Constituency; (b) whether the Government is considering to open more such CGHS wellness centres at each Assembly segment of Kollapur, Kalwakurthy, Nagarkurnool, Achampet, Alampur, Gadwal, Wanaparthy, if so, the details thereof; (c) whether the common public other than Central Government employees including the State Government employees are also permitted to avail medical facilities in case emergency or normal routine check up in such centres; and (d) if so, the details thereof along with proposals to set up such CGHS wellness centres in the State of Telangana?






ANSWER




                                                         ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE (SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):There are 13 CGHS Wellness Centres in Telanagana at Hyderabad and Secunderabad. The list of the same is at Annexure.(b):  No.(c):    Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS) is a contributory health scheme of the Central Government Employees and pensioners and on payment of requisite subscription, they are eligible to avail services at CGHS Wellness Centres. However, in case of emergency, primary OPD treatment at Wellness Centres is provided to general public too.(d):     No such proposal is presently before the Government.
