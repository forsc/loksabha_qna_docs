                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

443






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.11.2019






Promotion of Pearl Culture




Rameshbhai Lavjibhai Dhaduk














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether steps have been taken in collaboration with Indian Council of Agriculture Research (ICAR) and the State Government of Gujarat to promote pearl culture at Porbandar as there is huge potential of pearl production at Porbandar and it can provide new opportunities of employment there: and(b)   if so , the details thereof; 






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE FOR FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING:(SHRI PRATAP CHANDRA SARANGI)(a) & (b). Yes Sir. Steps have been taken in collaboration with the Fisheries Institutes of Indian Council of Agriculture Research (ICAR) viz; Central Marine Fisheries Research Institute (CMFRI) and Central Institute of Fresh water Aquaculture (CIFA) and Government of Gujarat for promotion of pearl culture. Considering the scope for pearl culture, a sub-component of ‘pearl culture (marine & inland)’ is included under the CSS on “Blue Revolution: Integrated Development and management of Fisheries” being implemented by this Department and financial assistance is provided to the States for undertaking pearl culture. The CMFRI and CIFA also organize training programs for the farmers, entrepreneurs and researchers on various aspects of pearl farming. *****
