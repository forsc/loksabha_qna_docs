                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5114






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

24.07.2019






Under Construction ROBs




Vinod Kumar Sonkar













                                                    Raja Amareshwara Naik
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of the under construction Rail Over Bridges (ROBs) in various States on cost sharing basis, zone-wise;(b)	the details of the proposals sent by various State Governments location-wise including Bihar for constructing ROBs at level crossings along with the action taken on each of such proposals;(c)	the details of under-construction and new projects identified for constructing the ROBs during the last three years along with the details of the funds allocated, zone-wise;(d)	the details of the present status of each of these projects of ROBs; and(e)	the steps taken/being taken to complete the said projects within the stipulated time?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a) to (e): A Statement is laid on the Table of the House.
