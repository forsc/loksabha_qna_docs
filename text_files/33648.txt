                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1070






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.02.2022






Cultivation of Coarse Grains




Sanjay Seth














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is running any scheme to promote the cultivation of coarse grainsas these are an important source of nutrition for all;(b) whether any scheme has been launched to encourage the cultivation of coarse cereals during the last three years, if so, the details thereof and the details of the achievements madethere  under;(c) whether any steps have been taken by the Government to promote crop diversification, if so, the extent to which it has been effective till date and the benefits thereof, State-wise detailsthereof; and(d) the number of the farmers under Ranchi Lok Sabha Constituency, who were given funds under the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Kalyan Yojana during the last two years alongwith the amount disbursed thereof particularly the district-wise details with regard to Jharkhand?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ?????? ??????   (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b)  Yes, Sir. Government is implementing coarse cereals development programme under National Food Security Mission - Coarse Cereals to promote the cultivation of coarse grains like maize &barley. Maize development programme is being implemented in 237 districts of 26 states, 2 Union Territories, and barley in 39 districts of 4 states. In addition, the states have the flexibility to take up maize development programme under Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY) in the area of 50-100 km around distilleries to use maize as feed stock for production of ethanol. The production of Coarse Cereals (maize &barley) during last 3 years is as under;Crops	Production (Lakh tonne)Coarse Cereals 	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21*Maize	277.15	287.66	315.14Barley	16.27	17.22	16.73*As per 4th Advance Estimates 2020-21(c) The National Food Security Mission(NFSM),Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH)&Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY)support crop development programmes of various agriculture and horticulturecrops.In addition, the Department of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare is implementing Crop Diversification Programme(CDP) in original green revolution States viz: Punjab, Haryana and Western Uttar Pradesh, as a sub scheme of RKVY, since 2013-14 to divert the area of water guzzling paddy crop to alternative crops like pulses, oilseeds, coarse cereals, nutri cereals, cotton, agro forestry etc.Crop Diversification Programme has been extended to 10 tobacco growing states of Andhra Pradesh, Bihar, Gujarat, Karnataka, Maharashtra, Odisha, Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal to encourage tobacco growing farmers to shift to alternative crops/cropping system from 2015-16.(d)   The district wise number of beneficiary farmers under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) Yojana during last 3 years in the state of Jharkhand includingdistrict Ranchi
