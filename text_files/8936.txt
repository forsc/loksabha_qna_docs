                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2504






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






IAS and IPS Cadres for Puducherry




 Vaithilingam Ve.














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the total number of IAS and IPS posts/cadres sanctioned/allotted by the Union Government for posting in various posts for the Union Territory of Puducherry;(b) the total number of IAS and IPS cadre in various posts in the Union Territory of Puducherry; and(c) the total number of IAS and IPS officers presently working in the Union Territory of Puducherry as on date, postwise/ cadre-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a):	Total number of Senior Duty Posts sanctioned for IAS and IPS officers of AGMUT Cadre for posting in Union Territory of Puducherry is 14 and 05 respectively. (b) to (c): Post-wise/cadre-wise details (i.e. Senior Duty Posts and present incumbency) of IAS and IPS officers of AGMUT Cadre in Union Territory of Puducherry are as below:IAS	IPSGrade	Senior Duty Posts	Present Incumbent	Grade	Senior Duty Posts	Present IncumbentApex	00	00	DGP	00	01HAG	01	01	ADGP	00	00Super Time Scale	03	01	IGP	01	01Senior Scale	10	14	DIG	01	01Total	14	16	Senior Scale	03	03	Total	05	06In addition to above, 01 Junior Scale IAS officer is also posted in Union territory of Puducherry.  *****
