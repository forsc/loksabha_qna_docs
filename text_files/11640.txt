                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RAILWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

581






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.02.2020






e-Office Programme




Kuldeep Rai Sharma













                                                    Amol Ramsing Kolhe
                                                








                                                    Shriniwas Dadasaheb Patil
                                                








                                                    Supriya Sadanand Sule
                                                








                                                    Gautham Sigamani Pon
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





RAILWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Indian Railways has recently signed an MoU with the RailTel in order to implement Phase-II of e-Office programme, if so, the  details thereof;(b)	whether Railways has achieved the targets of e-Office in Phase-I, if so, the details thereof;(c)	 the amount of funds allocated by the Government during each of the last three years and the current year for the said project;(d)	whether the Government has issued any guidelines for the use of  internet and e-mail in office work for promoting paperless office work, if so, the achievements made in this regard; and(e)	the other steps taken by the Government to encourage paper-less work culture which saves operational cost and also reduces the carbon foot print?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RAILWAYS AND COMMERCE & INDUSTRY(SHRI PIYUSH GOYAL)(a): Yes, Sir. Indian Railways have signed Memorandum of Understanding with RailTel on 10.01.2020 for implementation of Phase-II of e-office programme in 34 Divisions, covering approximately 39000 users by end of June 2020.    (b): Yes, Sir. E-office has been implemented in all the 59 targeted Railway Units in Phase-I, covering more than 53000 users. Details are Appended.(c): `86.25 crores have been allotted for e-office Phase-I project during last 2 years as per details given below:- FY 2018-19 : `14.03 crores.FY 2019-20 : `72.22 crores.(d) & (e): Yes, Sir. For promoting paper-less office work, Ministry of Railways has issued instructions to implement E-Office over    Indian Railways. The use of E-Office by more than 53000 users, has resulted in reduction of consumption of paper, improved efficiency, ease of working and enhanced transparency in the system.*****
