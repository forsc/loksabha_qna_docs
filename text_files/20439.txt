                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





STARRED   


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

150






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

11.02.2021






Conversion of NHs into Four and Six Lanes




Subrata Pathak













                                                    Ravindra Kushawaha
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the targets fixed for converting the National Highways in the country into four lane and six lane during the year 2019-2020, State-wise;(b) the length of each such NH which has been converted into four lane and six lane, separately so far, State-wise;(c) the length of NHs on which such conversion work is pending along with the time by which it is likely to be completed;(d) the funds allocated by the Government to various States for this purpose, State-wise; and(e) the length of NHs in the country expected to be converted into four lane and six lane in the coming three years, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) to (e) A statement is laid on the Table of the House.
