                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CULTURE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

762






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

07.02.2022






Repatriation of Antiquities




Arvind Dharmapuri














                        Will the Minister of





CULTURE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of stolen antiques and historic artefacts that were repatriated back to the country during each of the last ten years from various countries, country-wise and year-wise;(b) whether the Government has taken any action against antique dealers responsible for the theft during the said period; and(c) if so, the details thereof including the number of antique dealers extradited to the country during the said period, country-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF CULTURE, TOURISM AND DEVELOPMENT OF NORTH EASTERN REGION(SHRI G. KISHAN REDDY)(a) Details of the antiquities repatriated to the country, during the last ten years, from various countries are attached at Annexure – 1.(b) & (c) The action against persons involved in theft/crime is taken up by Enforcement Agencies. Archaeological Survey of India provides all necessary help to Enforcement Agencies as and when required. Annexure – 1Statement giving a detail of the antiquities repatriated to the country, during the last ten years (2011-2021), from various countries in reply of part (a) of LOK SABHA Unstarred Qn.No. 762 for 07/02/2022:-Year	Country	No. of Antiquity2011	-	NIL2012	-	NIL2013	France	012014	Australia	022015	Canada	01	Germany	01	Singapore	012016	USA	11	Australia	032017    USA	08	UK	012018	USA	022019    UK	01	Australia	012020    Australia	03	UK	052021    UK	01	Canada	01	USA	157     Total -    200
