                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3497






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.08.2021






Coaching Classes for Competitive Examinations




Rajeshbhai Naranbhai Chudasama














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIAMINISTRY OF SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENTLOK SABHAUNSTARRED QUESTION NO.3497TO BE ANSWERED ON 10.08.2021COACHING CLASSES FOR COMPETITIVE EXAMINATIONS 3497. SHRI RAJESHBHAI CHUDASAMA: Will the Minister of SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT be pleased to state:(a) the details of the total number of SC and OBC students enrolled under coaching classes for different competitive examinations, State-wise; (b) the details of the total number of SC and OBC students who have passed in the examinations for which they enrolled in coaching class, State-wise; and (c) the total number of SC students provided with a pre-matric scholarship during the last three years?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI A. NARAYANASWAMY)(a) & (b):   This Ministry implements the ''Free Coaching Scheme for SC and OBC Students’ under which financial assistance is provided to the candidates to undertake coaching for various examinations. Under the Scheme, the Ministry empanels coaching institutes, with prescribed number of slots, to provide coaching to SC and OBC students. The State-wise details of number of students coached by empaneled coaching institutes, and successful students  as reported by these coaching institutes during the last three years is annexed.(c): The number of students provided scholarships under the scheme of ‘Pre-Matric Scholarship for SC students studying in class IX & X  being implemented by this Ministry during last three years are as under:Year	Beneficiaries  (Actuals as reported by States/UTs) 2018-19	26,30,3662019-20	28,09,5422020-21	30,53,739*****  AnnexureAnnexure referred to in reply of Point (a) & (b) of Lok Sabha Unstarred Question  No. 3497 dated 10.08.2021 regarding “Coaching Classes for Competitive Examinations”Number of students coached and number of successful candidates under the Free Coaching Scheme for SC and OBC studentsS.No	State	Total number of slots in empanelled coaching institute during last three years i.e. from 2018-19 to 2020-21	Total number of successful students as reported by coaching institutes during last three years i.e. from 2018-19 to 2020-211.
