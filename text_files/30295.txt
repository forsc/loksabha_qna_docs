                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1927






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Beneficiaries under PMUY




Dulal Chandra Goswami














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of beneficiaries included under the Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) in Bihar, district-wise; (b) whether the Government is providing refilling cylinders to the beneficiaries of PMUY in the States; and (c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a)   As on 01.12.2021, Oil Marketing Companies(OMCs) have released 98.50 lakh LPG connections under Pradhan Mantri Ujjawala Yojana(PMUY) which includes PMUY Phase-I and Ujjwala 2.0 in the State of Bihar. District-wise details are at Annexure.(b) & (c) Under Ujjwala 2.0, the beneficiaries are given deposit free LPG connections along with free first refill and stove.---------
