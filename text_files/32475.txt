                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3908






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.12.2021






Price of Soybeans




 Imtiaz Jaleel Syed














                        Will the Minister of





AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether farmers sold soybeans at Government-cut prices; (b)	if so, the details thereof;(c)	whether the import of soymeal results in pulling domestic soybean prices by 50%; and(d)	if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE???? ??? ????? ??????? ?????? (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a) & (b):  Government implements Pradhan Mantri Annadata Aay SanraksHan Abhiyan (PM-AASHA) for procurement of notified oilseeds, pulses and copra including soybeans of Fair Average Quality (FAQ) at Minimum Support Price (MSP) through Central Nodal agency directly from registered farmers after receiving the proposals from the State Government as per scheme guidelines. However, if farmer gets better price in comparison to MSP, they are free to sell their produce in open market.(c) & (d): In the last year, soyabean crop got damaged due to weather and there was continuous demand from livestock industry as soyabean meal was not available, so to protect livestock industry soyabean meal import was allowed. During the current harvest season of soyabean, the averge mandi prices is ruling above MSP in most of the soybean producing states. Mandi wholesale prices of Soybean during kharif season (September to December 2021) is at Annexure-I.
