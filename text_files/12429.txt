                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1223






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2020






New Fee Structure




Ritesh Pandey














                        Will the Minister of





HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the fee hikes in the M.Tech. course in IITs were allowed after aproper consultation with various stakeholders including students;(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) if not, the measures likely to be proposed by the Government to promotehigher scientific studies for the youth belonging to lower economic status,marginalized and vulnerable sections of the society, SCs, STs, and OBCsin the light of the new fee structure of the M.Tech. course in the IITs?
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF HUMAN RESOURCE DEVELOPMENT(SHRI RAMESH POKHRIYAL ‘NISHANK’)(a) to (c): An agenda regarding “Reform of the M.Tech. programme” in IITsincluding fee hikes came up for discussion in the 53rd IIT Council Meeting heldon 27.09.2019 at IIT Delhi. However, no decision has been taken by theGovernment in this regard.*****
