                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    TRIBAL AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

219






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.11.2019






ST List of Karnataka




Shobha Karandlaje














                        Will the Minister of





TRIBAL AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether various States including Karnataka have requested for inclusion of a number of tribes in the Scheduled Tribe list;(b)	if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(c)	whether Karnataka Government has recently sent a proposal to the Union Government to recognise the Siddis of Belagavi and Dharwad district as STs; (d)	if so, the details thereof and the action taken by Government in this regard;(e)	the time by which the proposal would be cleared and that community included in the list;(f)	whether Siddis are recognised as STs in some districts of Gujarat, Goa, Karnataka (Uttara Kannada district) and the UT of Daman & Diu and if so, the details thereof; and(g)	whether Siddis are included in the Centre’s list of Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs), if so, the details thereof and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        (a)	& (b) The Government of India on 15.6.1999 (further amended on 25.6.2002), has laid down the modalities for determining the claims for inclusion in, exclusion from and other modifications in the Orders specifying list of Scheduled Tribes. According to these modalities, only those proposals which have been recommended and justified by the concerned State Government/ Union Territory Administration and concurred with by the Registrar General of India (RGI) and the National Commission for Scheduled Tribes are to be considered for amendment of legislation. All actions are taken w.r.t. proposals of State/UT Governments for the communities demanding Scheduled Tribes status as per the modalities. A Statement of State/UT wise proposal for inclusion of communities in the list of Scheduled Tribes is placed at Annexure I.(c), (d) & (e)	Ministry of Tribal Affairs received a proposal for inclusion of “Siddi” community of Dharwad and Belagavi districts along with existing “Siddi” community (in Uttar Kannada district) at Sl. No. 50 of the list of Scheduled Tribes of Karnataka. In this regard, a bill namely “The Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order (Second Amendment) Bill, 2019” has been introduced in Rajya Sabha on 09.01.2019.(f)		A Statement showing list of Scheduled Tribes in the State of Goa, Gujarat, Karnataka and UT of Daman & Diu is placed at Annexure II.(g)		A Statement of State/UT wise list of Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs) is placed at Annexure III ***** Annexure IDetails of tribal communities requestedfor inclusion in the Scheduled Tribe list: -S.No.	States / UTs	Number of Proposals1.
