                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    COMMUNICATIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2980






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.12.2021






Deterioration of MTNL Services




Arvind Ganpat Sawant














                        Will the Minister of





COMMUNICATIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether it is true that MTNL mobile services have been seriously deteriorated in Mumbai and Delhi;(b) 	if so, the details thereof; (c) 	whether it is true that number of Base Transceiver Station(BTS) towers are not sufficient enough for the mobile services in Mumbai and Delhi; (d) 	if so, the number of additional mobile towers required to be installed; and(e) 	the amount required for additional installation of such towers?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR COMMUNICATIONS (SHRI DEVUSINH CHAUHAN)(a) & (b) 	As per Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI), MTNL is meeting the network performance related parameters for mobile services in Delhi and Mumbai. (c) to (e) 	As on 30.09.2021, MTNL is having 2775 BTSs in Delhi and 1553 BTSs in Mumbai. MTNL augments its mobile network based on techno-commercial considerations. *********
