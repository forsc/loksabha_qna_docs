                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1946






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.12.2021






Crude Oil Production




Hanuman Beniwal














                        Will the Minister of





PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the name of the places in the country where crude oil is produced;(b) the details of the crude oil produced/extracted during the last five years, State and year-wise;(c) the present status of the under construction refinery in the State of Rajasthan; and(d) the revenue earned from crude oil produced in Rajasthan during the last five years and the share of the Centre and the State in the said revenue?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PETROLEUM AND NATURAL GAS(SHRI RAMESWAR TELI)(a) & (b) State wise production of crude oil during the last five years is as under: -(In Million Metric Tonnes (MMT))State/Location	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21Andhra Pradesh	0.3	0.3	0.3	0.2	0.2Arunachal Pradesh	0.1	0.1	0.0	0.1	0.1Assam	4.2	4.3	4.3	4.1	3.9Eastern Offshore	1.0	0.8	0.7	0.6	0.7Gujarat	4.6	4.6	4.6	4.7	4.7Rajasthan	8.2	7.9	7.7	6.7	5.9Tamil Nadu	0.3	0.3	0.4	0.4	0.4Tripura	0.003	0.003	0.003	0.003	0.003West Bengal	 -	 -	 -	 -	0.0001Western Offshore	17.4	17.3	16.2	15.4	14.6Total	36.0	35.7	34.2	32.2	30.5(c)	The physical progress of Barmer Refinery and Petrochemical complex is 35.6% as of November, 2021.(d)	The details of revenue earned from crude oil produced in Rajasthan state during the last five years and shares of the Centre and the state are as under:- (Rupees in crores)Year 	2016-17	2017-18	2018-19	2019-20	2020-21	TotalState Government 	2,367.59	2,658.73	3,900.99	3,060.42	2,133.01	14,120.74Central Government 	7,094.00	7,482.16	9,902.12	7,177.19	6,171.40	37,826.87Total revenue earned 	9,461.59	10,140.89	13,803.11	10,237.61	8,304.41	51,947.61*****
