                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

170






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

21.06.2019






Upgradation of Medical Colleges




T.N. Prathapan














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE be pleased to state:(a) the measures taken by the Government to upgrade various medical colleges in Kerala, including Government Medical College Thrissur under Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY);(b) whether the Government proposes to upgrade Thrissur Medical College as Centre of Excellence due to its prominence and increased patient ratio; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(SHRI ASHWINI KUMAR CHOUBEY)(a):	Following 4 Government Medical Colleges in Kerala have been approved for upgradation under different phases of Pradhan Mantri  Swasthya  Suraksha  Yojana (PMSSY):i.	Government Medical College, Thiruvananthapuram under Phase-I at the cost of Rs. 120 crore (Central share: Rs. 100 crore; State share: Rs.20 crore).ii.	Govt. Medical College, Kozhikode under Phase-III at the cost of Rs. 195.93 crore (Central share: Rs. 120 crore; State share: Rs.75.93 crore).iii.	T. D. Government Medical College, Alapuzzha under Phase-III at the cost of Rs. 173.18 crore (Central share: Rs. 120 crore; State share: Rs.53.18 crore).iv.	Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences & Technology under Phase-V(A) at the cost of Rs. 230 crore (MoHFW share: Rs. 120 crore; DST share: Rs.110 crore).(b) & (c):	At present, there is no proposal to upgrade Thrissur  Medical College                     under PMSSY.
