                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3698






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

18.03.2021






Damages of Roads Due to Cyclone in Tamil Nadu




Andimuthu Raja














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government has conducted survey to ascertain the damages of roads due to recent cyclone Nivar/ Brevi in various parts of Tamil Nadu especially in Nilgiris district;(b) 	if so, the details thereof;(c) 	whether the Government has received any proposal from the State Government regarding sanction of funds; and(d) 	if so, the funds sanctioned by the Government for reconstruction and repair of such damaged roads in various parts of Tamil Nadu especially in Nilgiris district?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a)to(d)  On receipt of the memorandum from Government of Tamil Nadu, Inter-Ministerial Central Team (IMCT) had visited the affected areas and an assistance of Rs. 63.14 Crore was approved by the Central Government to the State Government covering all the affected Districts including Nilgiris for Cyclonic storm ‘Nivar’ of 2020, out of which Rs. 9.92crore is for immediate repair/restoration of damaged roads. Similarly, an amount of Rs. 223.77 Crore has been approved for Cyclonic Storm ‘Burevi’ of 2020 and out of which Rs. 17.95 Crore is for immediate restoration of damaged road.  *****
