                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2064






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.03.2020






Shortage and Unavailability of Fertilizers




Ajay Nishad














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number and details of fertilizer manufacturing companies in the country operating at present, State/ UT-wise; (b) 	whether the Government has received complaints regarding shortage and unavailability of fertilizers in the country and non-supply of the subsidized fertilizers on time by companies to farmers during the last three years and the current year; (c) 	if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor, State/UT-wise along with the action taken by the Government in this regard; and (d)	whether cases of black marketing/smuggling of subsidized fertilizers and supply of low quality/ adulterated fertilizers to farmers have come to the notice of the Government during the said period and if so, the action taken by the Government in this regard?






ANSWER




                                                         MINISTER OF CHEMICALS & FERTILIZERS( D.V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a):	The State-wise details of fertilizer manufacturing companies in the country operating at present  are at Annexure-I.(b):     No, Sir. (c):    Question does not arise. (d):   Government of India has declared fertilizer as an essential commodity under the Essential Commodities Act, 1955 and notified Fertilizer Control Order (FCO), 1985 and Fertilizer (Movement Control) Order, 1973. State Governments have been adequately empowered to ensure selling of fertilizers at MRP besides stopping black marketing/smuggling of fertilizers. A few cases of black-marketing/ smuggling of fertilizers were reported by State Governments during the last three years. In all the cases, State Governments have taken appropriate actions as per FCO.       Further, State Governments are empowered to conduct search, make seizures and take punitive action against any person violating provisions of FCO, 1985 and Essential Commodities Act, 1955.********
