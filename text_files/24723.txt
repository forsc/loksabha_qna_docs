                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

637






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.07.2021






Development of Road projects under CRIF




Thomas Chazhikadan














                        Will the Minister of





ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Union Government has received recommendations from the Government of Kerala for development of road projects on Mannoppally- Poovathilappu Road, Pala- Kozha Road, Pallom- Thottackad Road, Kanjiramkavala- Melukavu- Ilaveezhapoonchira- Chakkillavu- Koovapally Road, Thanneermukkam Bund Road and Kallara Vechoor Road under the Central Road and Infrastructure Fund (CRIF);(b) if so, the details thereof; and(c) the time by which these projects are likely to be completed?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF ROAD TRANSPORT AND HIGHWAYS(SHRI NITIN JAIRAM GADKARI)(a) to (c) Yes Sir, State Government of Kerala had submitted a list of 57 works costing Rs. 493 crore under Central Road and Infrastructure Fund (CRIF) vide letter dated 13/02/2020  for approval, which includes the roads mentioned in the question.  At present Rs. 1134.60 crore worth of works are under execution in Kerala, which is approx. 8.96 times the average amount available to State under CRIF. Hence these works will be considered once already sanctioned works are completed by State PWD.*****
