                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1435






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

10.02.2021






Vacant Posts




Jayadev Galla














                        Will the Minister of





PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether there are nearly 7 lakh vacancies of various groups lying vacant in various Central Government departments/Ministries, PSUs as of February, 2021; (b) 	if so, the details of vacancies of each department, group-wise and department-wise; (c) 	the details of vacancies lying vacant for the last three years and five years, department-wise and groupwise; and (d) 	the efforts being made to fillup all the vacancies in a time-bound manner?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF PERSONNEL, PUBLIC GRIEVANCES AND PENSIONS AND MINISTER OF STATE IN THE PRIME MINISTER’S OFFICE (DR. JITENDRA SINGH)(a) to (c):  The details of sanctioned posts and in position in various Central Government Ministries/Departments, group-wise and department-wise, as on 1.3.2016 and 1.3.2018 i.e. for the last three years and five years are at Annexure-I & II. The details of vacancies as of February, 2021 and that of PSUs are not available.(d): Instructions have been issued to fill up the existing vacancies in the concerned Ministries/Departments, their Attached and Subordinate Offices, in a time bound manner vide OM No. 43014/03/2019–Estt.(B) dated 21.01.2020.*****
