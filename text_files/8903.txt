                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2336






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

03.12.2019






Hostels for Divyangjan Students




(Dr.) Ram Shankar Katheria














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the number of hostels constructed by the Government for Divyangjan Students studying in schools meant for such physically handicapped from 2014 till date;(b)	whether the Government has received proposals relating to beautification of such hostels;(c)	 if so, the details thereof, State-wise;(d)	the number of proposals received from the State of Uttar Pradesh for beautification of such hostels; and(e)	the action taken by the Government on these proposals?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR) (a): 	Nil.(b): 	No, Sir.(c) to (e): Does not arise in view of (b) above.*****
