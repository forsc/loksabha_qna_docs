                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

344






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

15.09.2020






Facilities in Hospitals for Handicapped




Anil Firojiya














                        Will the Minister of





SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether any steps have been taken by the Government to ensure infrastructural facilities in hospitals for the handicapped for test, quarantine facilities and treatments of COVID-19;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) whether any measures have been taken by the Government for destitute/beggar/homeless during the lockdown period; and(d) if so, the details thereof, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND EMPOWERMENT(SHRI KRISHANPAL GURJAR)(a) and (b) Ministry of Health and Family Welfare has informed that for appropriate management of COVID-19 cases, a three tier arrangement of health facilities [(i) COVID Care Center with isolation beds for mild or pre-symptomatic cases; (ii) Dedicated COVID Health Centre (DCHC) with oxygen supported isolation beds for moderate cases and (iii) dedicated COVID Hospital (DCH) with Intensive Care Unit (ICU) beds for severe cases] has been implemented. Tertiary care hospitals under Employees’ State Insurance Corporation (ESIC), Defence, Railways, Paramilitary Forces, Steel Ministry etc. have been leveraged for case management. Details of these facilities is attached as Annexure.        In addition to above, Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities under this Ministry has requested to Chief Secretaries of States/UTs on 24.04.2020 regarding provision of basic accessibility features in COVID-19 testing and quarantine facilities as well as hospitals and health centres.(c) and (d)  Department of Social Justice and Empowerment under this Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment has formulated a project for persons engaged in the act of begging, which would cover identification, rehabilitation, provision of medical facilities, counselling, education, skill development with the support of State Governments/UTs/Local Urban Bodies and Voluntary Organizations, institutions etc. This is being implemented on pilot basis in 10 selected cities.        The 10 identified cities are Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, Kolkata, Hyderabad, Bangalore, Lucknow, Patna, Nagpur and Indore. Out of these cities, an amount of Rs 2.00 crore to Hyderabad, Rs 1.50 crore to Indore and Rs 1.50 crore to Patna has been released after receiving Action Plan from the States.        During lock-down, aforesaid Municipal Corporations of identified cities were requested to open feeding centre for providing free meals to beggars/destitutes and fund requirement for the same to be projected to the Ministry. As informed by the Municipal administrations of these Cities as many as 1.27 crore persons have been provided free meals.
