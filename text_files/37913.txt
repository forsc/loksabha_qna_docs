                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4810






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

31.03.2022






Prime Minister's  15 Points Programme




 Imtiaz Jaleel Syed














                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether under Prime Minister’s 15 Point Programme no scheme for minority welfare has been implemented in Aurangabad district of Maharashtra and if so, the reasons therefor; (b) whether it is a fact that no meeting has been conducted to implement any minority scheme during the last three years and if so, the reasons therefor; and (c) whether due to indifferent attitude of district administration minorities have been deprived of the benefits of various schemes under PM’s 15 points programme and if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




THE MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS(SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a)    No, Sir. (b) As informed by the Government of Maharashtra, the meeting of District Minority Welfare Committee could not take place due to Covid-19 Pandemic and Government restrictions. (c ) No Sir, various schemes covered under Prime Minister’s 15 Point Programme are being implemented in Aurangabad district of Maharashtra, such as Scholarship schemes- Pre-Matric Scholarship, Post-Matric Scholarship, Merit-cum-Means based Scholarship; skill development training, etc.  ******
