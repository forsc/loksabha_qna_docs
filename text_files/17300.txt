                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

657






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

16.09.2020






NeGP




Sushil Kumar Singh














                        Will the Minister of





ELECTRONICS AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    Will the Minister of ELECTRONICS & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY be pleased to state: (a).	whether the Government under the National e-Governance Plan (NeGP) has set up any G2B portal to serve as a one-stop-shop for delivery of services to the investors so as to address the needs of business and industry from inception through the entire life cycle of the business; (b).	if so, the details thereof, State-wise; (c).	whether the Government with DIPP has set up any special management team to facilitate and fast track investment proposals from Japan; and (d).	if so, the details thereof, State-wise?






ANSWER




                                                        (a) and (b): Yes, Sir. The Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT) had set-up a G2B portal as a part of e-Biz Mission Mode Project. The G2B Portal is an integrated platform providing access to a number of Government to Business (G2B) services such as filing of Industrial Entrepreneurs Memorandum (IEM) and Industrial License (IL) applications Online. (c) and (d): A specific desk “Japan Plus” had been set up by DPIIT on 8th October, 2014 to facilitate Japanese Investment in India. The desk consisted of 4 professionals from Indian side and representatives from Japanese side. At present, Japan Desk of Invest India, the National Investment Facilitation Agency of Government of India has been providing necessary support to facilitate and fast track investment from Japan. The team has been actively engaging with all the key stakeholders on a regular basis and has also created a repository of brochures, magazines and reports on available opportunities for Japanese investors. ********
