                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1802






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

08.12.2021






Procurement Fortified of Rice




Bhartruhari Mahtab














                        Will the Minister of





CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD AND PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	whether the Government proposes to procure fortified rice from various States during Kharif and Rabi season; (b) 	if so, the State/UT-wise details thereof; (c) 	whether the Government has launched a programme for distribution of fortified rice to children and lactating mothers in the country; and (d) 	if so, the quantum of the said rice likely to be distributed along with number of States/UTs proposed to be covered under the said programme?






ANSWER




                                                        A N S W E R  MINISTER OF STATE FOR MINISTRY OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT AND CONSUMER AFFAIRS, FOOD & PUBLIC DISTRIBUTION (SADHVI NIRANJAN JYOTI)(a) and (b):	Yes, Sir. The state wise target for procurement of fortified rice is at Annexure-I (c): 	In order to address anaemia and micro-nutrient deficiency in the country, Government of India approved Centrally Sponsored Pilot Scheme on “Fortification of Rice & its Distribution under Public Distribution System” for a period of 3 years beginning in 2019-20. Department of Food and Public Distribution in association with Ministry of Women & Child Development and Department of School Education & Literacy has started distribution of Fortified Rice under Integrated Child Development Scheme (ICDS) and PM POSHAN across the country.  (d): 	Action plan for Procurement of Fortified rice for distribution under ICDS/ PM POSHAN and other schemes is as below:Phase I:- 35 LMT of Fortified rice to be distributed till March 2022 in ICDS+ PM POSHAN schemes.Phase II:- 175 LMT of Fortified rice to be distributed till March 2023 in ICDS+ PM POSHAN +291 Aspirational & High Burden districts.Phase III:- 375 LMT of Fortified rice to be distributed till March 2024 in entire TPDS and OWSs.****ANNEXURE-IANNEXURE REFERRED TO IN REPLY TO PART (a)&(b) OF THE UNSTARRED QUESTION NO.1802 FOR ANSWER ON 08.12.2021 IN THE LOK SABHA.Sl.
