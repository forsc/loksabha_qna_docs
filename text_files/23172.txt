                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    Education






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4321






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.03.2021






Scholarships for Meritorious Students




Sanjay Haribhau Jadhav













                                                     Bhavana Gawali (Patil)
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





Education


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of various scholarships awarded to meritorious students under the Government schemes;(b) the total number of scholarships awarded to the students during the last three years and the current year,State/UT-wise;(c) whether a large number of scholarships were cancelled during the above said period and if so, the details thereof and the reasons therefor; and(d) the action taken or proposed to be taken by the Government against such institutions?






ANSWER




MINISTER OF EDUCATION(Shri Ramesh Pokhriyal ''Nishank'')(a)	: Ministry of Education implements various schemes to encourage meritorious students and support them in their studies by providing financial assistance. Details of scholarships administered by Ministry of Education are at Annex I. (b)	: The total number of scholarships awarded to the students during the last three years and the current year, State/UT-wise are at Annex II, III & IV.(c)	& (d) : All the eligible students who qualify into merit list, as per slots allocated to each States/Union Territories, are awarded scholarship by Ministry of Education. ****
