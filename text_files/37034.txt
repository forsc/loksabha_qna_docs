                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HOME AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

4153






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

29.03.2022






E-Visa Facilities




Gautam Gambhir














                        Will the Minister of





HOME AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details and the number of countries having e-Visa facilities with India;(b) whether the Government is working on any proposals to set up these facilities with more countries; and(c) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HOME AFFAIRS(SHRI NITYANAND RAI)(a) to (c):	Prior to e-Visa suspension in March, 2020 due to Covid-19 pandemic, e-Visa was available to the nationals of 171 countries under five sub-categories i.e. e-Tourist visa, e-Business visa, e-Medical visa, e-Medical Attendant visa and e-Conference visa. During Covid-19 pandemic, the e-Visa facility was suspended. Subsequently, the e-Visa facility was restored to the nationals of 156 countries out of these 171 countries. 	e-Visa facility is granted to nationals of various countries after taking into consideration, inter-alia, the issues of security, inbound tourism and investments, bi-lateral relations etc. Extension of this facility is an ongoing process which is done after appropriate consultations with all stakeholders.*******
