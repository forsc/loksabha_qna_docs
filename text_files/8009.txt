                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1816






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Construction of National and International Level Stadiums




Sukhbir Singh Jaunapuria














                        Will the Minister of





YOUTH AFFAIRS AND SPORTS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government proposes to construct national and international stadiums in Rajasthan, Haryana and Delhi;(b) if so, details thereof, State-wise;(c) the number of stadiums constructed by the Government during the last five years and the number of stadiums whose construction work has not been completed; and(d) the reasons for non completion of the construction work of stadiums and the time line for completion of their construction work?
                                                





ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE (INDEPENDENT CHARGE) FOR YOUTH AFFAIRS & SPORTS{ SHRI KIREN RIJIJU }(a) and (b) ‘Sports’ being a State subject the responsibility for providing sports facilities, including construction of national and international stadiums rests with the State / Union Territory Governments. Central Government supplements efforts of the State/Union Territories by focusing on training, coaching and support for bridging gaps in sports infrastructure. The Ministry does not have any scheme for construction of national and international stadium, including in the State of Rajasthan, Haryana and Delhi.(c) and (d) Do not arise.****
