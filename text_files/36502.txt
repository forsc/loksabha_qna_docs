                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

3789






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.03.2022






Medical Oxygen Plants




Janardan Singh Sigriwal














                        Will the Minister of





HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the number of oxygen plants installed and functioning in the country as on date, State/UT-wise;(b) whether the Government has any plan to set up medical oxygen plants in each district of the country;(c) if so, the details thereof along with the timeline fixed for the purpose;(d) whether the Government has launched any comprehensive mission to encourage entrepreneurship and generate employment in health sector keeping in view acute shortage of manpower; and(e) if so, the details thereof?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH AND FAMILY WELFARE(DR BHARATI PRAVIN PAWAR)(a) to (c): The Government of India has sanctioned 1561 Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) oxygen generation plants. These include 1225 PSA plants which have been installed and commissioned under PMCARES Fund in every district of the country. Additionally, 336 PSA Plants are set up by PSUs of Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas, Ministry of Power, Ministry of Coal, Ministry of Railways etc. As on date 1541 out of 1561 PSA plants have been commissioned. State/UT-wise details of PSA plants sanctioned and commissioned across the country is placed at Annexure.(d) & (e) The Government of India has provided the necessary financial and technical assistance to all States/UTs in building up the oxygen infrastructure. Under Emergency Response Package (ECRP)-I & II, the Government has provided Rs. 15,000 Crore and Rs 23,123 Crore respectively to the states for augmentation of health infrastructure, oxygen supported beds, isolation beds, ICUs & Human Resources etc.
