                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CIVIL AVIATION






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1630






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

28.11.2019






Smaller Airports under UDAN




A. Ganeshamurthi













                                                    T. R. Paarivendhar
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





CIVIL AVIATION


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government is implementing UDAN scheme under which various small airports are being connected with small flights and if so, the details thereof;(b) whether the Government has any proposal to start flight service under this scheme to Coimbatore-Thanjavur, Chennai-Thanjavur, Coimbatore-Hosur etc.; and(c) if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        The Minister of State (IC) in the Ministry of CIVIL AVIATION      (Shri Hardeep Singh Puri)(a): Government has launched Regional Connectivity Scheme (RCS) - UDAN (Ude Desh ka Aam Nagrik) on 21.10.2016 to stimulate regional air connectivity by making it affordable. Under RCS-UDAN, 688 RCS routes have been awarded connecting 137 small cities.  Out of which, 232 RCS routes commenced connecting 43 small cities till date. ; (b) & (c) : RCS-UDAN is market driven mechanism. Development of regional air connectivity routes is left to market forces so that airlines undertake assessment of demand and nature of supply required on particular routes. RCS routes between Tanjore(Thanjavur) - Chennai and Tanjore(Thanjavur) - Bangalore have been awarded to Selected Airline Operator under UDAN. However, no airline has submitted the proposal to operate RCS flight on the sector Coimbatore -Thanjavur and Coimbatore- Hosur till 3rd round of bidding under RCS-UDAN.
