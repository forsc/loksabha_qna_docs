                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    JAL SHAKTI






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1857






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

22.09.2020






Toilets Constructed under Har Ghar Souchalaya Yojana




Rajan Baburao  Vichare














                        Will the Minister of





JAL SHAKTI


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) the details of the number of houses where toilets have been built under ‘Har Ghar Souchalaya Yojana’ under Swachh Bharat Mission during the last three years, State-wise; and(b) the details of the amount received thereupon?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STATE IN THE MINISTRY OF JAL SHAKTI(SHRI RATTAN LAL KATARIA)(a) 	State/UT-wise number of houses where toilets have been constructed under Swachh Bharat Mission (Grameen) [SBM(G)] during the last 3 years, as per the data reported by the States/UTs on the online Integrated Management Information System, are given at Annexure-1.(b) 	State/UT-wise details of Centre share funds released under SBM(G) during the last three years are given at Annexure-2.
