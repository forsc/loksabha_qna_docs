                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MINORITY AFFAIRS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

2321






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

05.03.2020






Scholarship Schemes for Minority Students




Deepak Baij













                                                    Shyam Yadav Singh
                                                








                                                    Maneka Sanjay Gandhi
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





MINORITY AFFAIRS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the details of scholarships given to minority communities by the Ministry under pre-matric, post-matric and merit-cum-means during the last three years, community-wise;(b)	whether the Government allows SC/ST communities to avail post-matric scholarshipswhile joining courses under the management quota in self financing educational institutions and if so, the details thereof and if not, the reasonstherefor;(c)	the number of students belonging to minoritycommunity given scholarship to ensure that they geteducation without any hurdles during the last six years;and(d)	the details of scholarship amount givento the students and the dates on which it wasincreased?	
                                                





ANSWER




MINISTER OF MINORITY AFFAIRS (SHRI MUKHTAR ABBAS NAQVI)(a):	The details of Pre-Matric, Post Matric and Merit-cum-Means scholarships given to minority communities by the Ministry of Minority Affairs during the last three years, community-wise, are as follows:Year	Buddhist	Christian	Jain 	Muslim	Parsi	SikhPre-Matric Scholarship Scheme2017-18	131419	539847	59317	3718345	544	4247482018-19*	170892	613651	67106	4418314	854	4210372019-20*	175939	565117	61015	2888448	228	419985Post Matric Scholarship Scheme2017-18	3743	73814	14603	471057	64	580402018-19*	5192	87602	15244	514831	151	612452019-20*	3099	68936	12620	351911	35	53565Merit-cum-Means based Scholarship Scheme2017-18	583	17899	3096	88406	11	64572018-19*	495	19035	3335	90731	12	41632019-20*	202	16105	2482	65521	6	2837* - Provisional Data. Disbursement of Scholarship for 2018-19 and 2019-20 continues.(b)	Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment has stated that students admitted under management quota are not eligible for benefits under Post-Matric Scholarship Schemes for SCs. In so far as, ST students are concerned, Ministry of Tribal Affairs has stated that Scholarship is given for the study of all recognized Post Matriculation Post secondary course pursued in recognized institutions with the following exceptions:-Scholarships are not awarded for training courses like Aircraft Maintenance Engineer’s Courses and Private Pilot License courses, course at Training-Ship Duffering (Now Rajendra), course of training at the Military College, Dehradun and courses at pre-examination training centers of all India and Star Levels.”(c) & (d):	The number of students belonging to minority communities given scholarship and the amount sanctioned/released during the last six years are as follows:2014-15 to 2019-20 (As on 02.03.2020)Scheme Name	Physical	Financial(in Rs. crore)Pre-Matric Scholarship Scheme	31505702	6068.44Post Matric Scholarship Scheme	3993202	2360.02Merit-cum-Means based Scholarship Scheme	715586	1920.62The rates of Scholarships under all three Scholarship schemes for Minorities have remained same during the aforesaid years.*****
