                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    STEEL






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

44






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

19.07.2021






Oxygen Production by Steel Plants




Nalin Kumar Kateel













                                                    Talari Rangaiah
                                                








                                                    Midhun Reddy
                                                








                                                    Gajanan Chandrakant Kirtikar
                                                








                                                    Ganesan Selvam
                                                








                                                    C.N. Annadurai
                                                








                                                    Gautham Sigamani Pon
                                                












                        Will the Minister of





STEEL


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	the steps taken by Steel sector to fight the Covid-19 pandemic and bring relief to the people;(b)	whether Steel plants belonging to the Public as well as the Private sectors, had received instructions from the Government to supply the life-saving Liquid Medical Oxygen (LMO) to the country during the COVID 2.0 pandemic time recently;(c)	if so, the details thereof including the quantities supplied by them, State- wise during the last three months;(d)	whether steel plants have contributed major share of the LMO requirement of the country which stands at about 10 thousand MT/day and if so, the details thereof;(e)	the number of Oxygenated Beds established by the Steel companies in the country so far, State-wise; and(f)	the number of Oxygen Concentrators and Oxygen Compressors procured, and PSA Oxygen Generation Plants established by steel PSUs across the country so far?






ANSWER




                                                        THE MINISTER OF STEEL		   (SHRI RAM CHANDRA PRASAD SINGH)(a)	The steps taken by steel sector to fight the Covid-19 pandemic and bring relief to the people include:-(i)	Enhancing Liquid Medical Oxygen (LMO) production and supply;(ii)	Earmarking beds for Covid patients in hospitals within the steel plants;(iii)	Establishment of Jumbo Covid Care facilities using supply of gaseous oxygen;(iv)	Commissioning of Pressure Swing Absorption (PSA) units in steel plants;(v)	Arranging additional ventilators, Oxygen concentrators, CPAP machines;(vi)	Organizing vaccination drives.(b)&(c): The details of LMO supplied (state-wise) by steel plants during the period of April-June, 2021 are enclosed as Annexure –I.(d)	During peak of the pandemic in April-May, 2021, the supply of LMO which was only 538 MT on 1st April, 2021, went up to the peak supply of 4749 MT on 13th May, 2021.(e)	The details of the number of oxygenated beds established by steel companies, state-wise, are enclosed at Annexure-II.(f)	The details of the number of oxygen concentrators and PSA plants established by Steel PSUs are as follows:-Oxygen
