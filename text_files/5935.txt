                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

5466






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

25.07.2019






Exclusion of Producing Units of Job Orders in MSMEs




P.R. Natarajan














                        Will the Minister of





MICRO,SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a)	whether the Government is having any data on the number of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) available across the country particularly in Coimbatore and  if so, the details thereof, State/UT-wise;(b)	whether the Government is having any proposal to exclude the producing units of job orders in MSMEs;(c)	if so, the details thereof; and(d)	if not, the reasons therefor?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF MICRO, SMALL AND MEDIUM ENTERPRISES(SHRI NITIN GADKARI)(a): 	As on 19.07.2019, the number of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) registered on Udyog Aadhaar Portal are 71.39 lakhs. The number of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises registered in Coimbatore are 90367. The State/UT-wise numbers of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises registered on Udyog Aadhaar Portal in the country are given as annexure.(b) to (d):	 The Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises does not have any such proposal.
