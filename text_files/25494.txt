                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1252






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

27.07.2021






Financial Aid to Dairy Farmers




Hemant Tukaram Godse














                        Will the Minister of





FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                     (a) whether it is a fact that the State of Maharashtra has the least number of dairy units and has also received the least financial aid from the Central Government for setting up of dairies by the youth and women of the States of Maharashtra and Gujarat; (b) if so, the details of the number of applications received from all the States including the States of Maharashtra and Gujarat during financial years 2018-19 and 2019-20 along with the number of youth and women provided with financial grant-in-aid; and (c) the details of budget allocation for youth and women to set up dairies, State-wise including Maharashtra and Gujarat?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERTHE MINISTER OF FISHERIES, ANIMAL HUSBANDRY AND DAIRYING(SHRI PARSHOTTAM RUPALA)(a) Under the erstwhile beneficiary oriented Central Sector Scheme - Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme (DEDS) implemented by the Department through National Bank of Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) during the period from 2010-11 to 2019-20, Maharashtra has received Rs.143.54 Crore, which is 7.59 % of the total subsidy amount of Rs.1892.28 Crore disbursed across the country. The details of  total dairy units/number of beneficiaries including women beneficiary assisted under DEDS in Maharashtra & Gujarat  in comparison to all India is as follows :-All India	Maharashtra	GujaratTotal Unit	Amount(Rs in crore)	Total Unit	Amount(Rs in crore)	Total Unit	Amount(Rs in crore)421157	1892.28	27316	143.54	33729	198.78(b) & (c) Details of State wise back-ended subsidy disbursed including women by NABARD under DEDS during 2018-19 & 2019-20 is at Annexure.*****
