                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    RURAL DEVELOPMENT






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1251






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Implementation of PMAY-G in Maharashtra




Shriniwas Dadasaheb Patil














                        Will the Minister of





RURAL DEVELOPMENT


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) 	the total number of beneficiaries identified under the Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G) in Satara district of Maharashtra;(b) 	the target of houses to be completed in the year 2020-21; and(c) 	the present status of the above target in terms of percentage completion?






ANSWER




                                                        MINISTER OF RURAL DEVELOPMENT (SHRI NARENDRA SINGH TOMAR)(a)   Under Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G), a target of 14,166 has been allocated to the District Satara of Maharashtra.  (b)  The Satara district has been allocated a target of 5,083 under PMAY-G during the year 2020-21.  (c)  2,399 houses have been sanctioned in Satara District till now during 2020-21 which are at various stages of construction. *****
