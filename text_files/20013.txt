                                    GOVERNMENT OF INDIA
                                





                                    MINISTRY OF
                                    CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS






                                    LOK SABHA
                                





UNSTARRED 


                                    QUESTION NO:
                                

1193






                                    ANSWERED ON: 
                                

09.02.2021






Atma Nirbhar in Decorative Paint sector




Rajendra Agrawal














                        Will the Minister of





CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS


                        be pleased to state:-















                                                    (a) whether the Government has taken any steps to make India Atma Nirbhar in the Industrial and Decorative paint sector;(b) if so, the details thereof;(c) the imports of chemicals and petrochemicals in the ongoing fiscal year by the country (in value terms); and(d) the exports of chemicals and petrochemicals in the ongoing fiscal year by the country (in value terms) ?






ANSWER




                                                        ANSWERMINISTER FOR CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS (SHRI D. V. SADANANDA GOWDA)(a) & (b) The Chemical sector is delicensed and free from licensing regime except for three hazardous chemicals. Only Industrial Entrepreneur Memorandum (IEM) is required to be obtained from Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade for setting up new undertaking or to carry out substantial expansion in the chemical sector. Entrepreneurs are setting up units for Industrial & Decorative paints based on techno economic feasibility, demand and supply scenario and cost of feedstock/ rawmaterials and selling in the free market. 100% FDI is permissible under the automatic route to boost foreign investment in this sector. Paints manufacturing is facilitated under ‘Make In India’ programme of Government of India.(c) & (d) The imports and exports of chemicals and petrochemicals in the ongoing fiscal year by the country is as under:ImportExport2020-21(April 2020 to Nov. 2020)2020-21(April 2020 to Nov. 2020)Chemical and Petrochemical Products219463.29 Crore177163.67 Crore****
